Some fraudsters meet people on dating sites and then convince them to help fake car crashes or injury claims. The goal isn’t to steal money from the victim, but to use their identity to get insurance payouts. Victims can be added to a fraud register, hurting future insurance and credit. Report any suspicious requests, keep evidence, and say “call emergency services” if pressured to act quickly.
