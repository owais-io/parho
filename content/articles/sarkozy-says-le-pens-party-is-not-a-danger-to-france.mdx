In his new book, former French president Nicolas Sarkozy says Marine Le Pen’s far‑right National Rally is not a threat and he would not join a united front to stop her. He notes many of his past supporters could vote for her and urges a broad right‑wing coalition. Sarkozy talks about his own prison time and contrasts his earlier calls for Macron, showing a shift toward working with the RN. Le Pen may still face a 2027 ban.
