Glasgow hospital is looking into a serious mistake where a body was cremated instead of the correct one. The error was found only after the cremation, leaving another family without a chance to hold a funeral. NHS bosses apologized and said the mix‑up happened because staff didn’t follow the usual identification rules. The hospital has started a full probe, suspended involved staff, and promised to learn from the incident.
