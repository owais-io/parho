Business leaders warn that the UK will enter 2026 with a sharp slowdown as private companies cut investment and hiring. The CBI says output fell last quarter and firms expect more decline. Job vacancies are shrinking for a fifth month and unemployment reached 5.1%. Wages keep rising, but many businesses face high costs. Companies ask the government for support and simpler taxes.
