Labour said Kemi Badenoch is using the debate over U.S. and Israeli airstrikes on Iran to score political points. She accused Leader Keir Starmer of being too scared to act, calling for decisive action. Starmer’s party says Badenoch is turning a serious security crisis into a political game. The exchange came after the UK’s RAF joined defensive operations and an Iranian drone hit a base in Cyprus. Badenoch also promised tougher laws on mental‑health risk and anti‑social behaviour.
