Emmanuel Grégoire, a Socialist, won the Paris mayor race, beating former minister Rachida Dati and keeping the city in left hands. The far‑right National Rally lost many of its target cities, including Marseille and parts of the south. While the RN did win a few towns like Carcassonne and Nice, the overall results show a left‑leaning wave ahead of the 2027 presidential election. Grégoire said Paris will resist right‑wing influence.
