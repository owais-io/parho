The school in Minab was hit on Feb. 28, killing many children. President Trump blamed Iran, but he offered no evidence. Investigators found the blast near an Iranian guard base. Video and satellite images show a U.S. Tomahawk missile hit the area; only the U.S. uses Tomahawks, pointing to U.S. responsibility. Satellite photos confirm the school was separate, not a military target, and a viral “misfire” photo came from a snowy northern city, not Minab.
