New Zealand coach Brendon McCullum says England can still fight back from being 2‑0 down in the Ashes. He calls the team “glass‑jaw” – weak – and urges them to stay strong, tough and keep going. The squad will take a short break in Noosa before training in Adelaide, hoping fresh minds will help them tighten up in bowling and fielding. McCullum says players should not pity themselves but focus on improving.
