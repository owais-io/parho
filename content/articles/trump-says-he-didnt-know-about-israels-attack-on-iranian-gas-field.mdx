President Trump claimed he had no idea Israel struck Iran’s biggest gas field, a statement Israel’s Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu disputed. The strike on the South Pars field angered Gulf allies and raised fears of a wider war over energy. Trump’s comments reveal growing tensions between Washington and Israel over how to handle the Iran conflict. Meanwhile, Israel says it acted on its own and the U.S. did not direct it.
