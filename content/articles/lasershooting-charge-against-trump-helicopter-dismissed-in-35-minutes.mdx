Jacob Winkler was charged with pointing a laser at Marine One, the helicopter that carries President Trump. A jury in Washington, D.C., found him not guilty after just 35 minutes of deliberation. The case highlighted a string of failed prosecutions by the U.S. Attorney’s office led by former Fox host Jeanine Pirro, who had vowed to pursue harsh penalties. Lawyers said the quick verdict showed that policing poverty is a waste of resources.
