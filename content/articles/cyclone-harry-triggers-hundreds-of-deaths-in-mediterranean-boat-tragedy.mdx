Cyclone Harry slammed the Mediterranean last week, sending huge waves that caught thousands of migrants off guard. A boat from Tunisia went missing, killing an estimated 50 people in Malta and leaving only one survivor. Two one‑year‑old twins from Guinea also died near Sicily. Italian coastguards say up to 380 more could have drowned, and they’re still looking for eight other missing vessels.
