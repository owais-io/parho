The Trump administration’s “Make America Healthy Again” effort is stuck because the CDC has no confirmed director and the surgeon‑general seat is still empty. The CDC has been led only by an acting head for over 210 days, while the Senate has not yet confirmed Casey Means for surgeon‑general. Political battles over vaccine policy and leadership disputes have left key health jobs unfilled, slowing the administration’s plans.
