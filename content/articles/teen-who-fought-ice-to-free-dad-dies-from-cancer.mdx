A 16‑year‑old girl from Chicago, Ofelia Torres, died after battling a rare form of cancer. While she was in treatment, her dad was taken into ICE custody during the Trump‑era “Operation Midway Blitz.” Ofelia led a fight that got a judge ruling the detention illegal, and he was released. The family’s story drew attention to the human cost of hard‑line immigration crackdowns.
