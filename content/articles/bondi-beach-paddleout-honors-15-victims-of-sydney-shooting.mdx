On Bondi Beach, thousands paddled out to honor 15 people killed at a Hanukah celebration. Surfers, families, and the Jewish community gathered in the ocean, formed a circle around a buoy, and sang songs. The event brought hope, unity, and healing after the attack. The community also showed support through donations and blood drives. Local leaders and a rabbi spoke, reinforcing solidarity. Their gathering signaled that even in grief, the community can come together for peace.
