A 49‑year‑old man, Robert Davies, has been charged with murder, possession of an offensive weapon and affray after a court bailiff, Jeff Blair, was attacked at his job in Shildon, County Durham. Blair, a former police officer, died in hospital from serious injuries. The case has shocked the community and police are asking anyone with information to come forward.
