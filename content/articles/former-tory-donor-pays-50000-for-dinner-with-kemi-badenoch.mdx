Rami Ranger, a long‑time Conservative backer who was suspended for bullying allegations, won a £50,000 bid for a dinner with Kemi Badenoch at a Tory fundraising event. He will dine with the minister and a few friends, sparking outrage among party members who believe he should remain banned. Ranger has donated over £1.5 million to the Conservatives, lost his CBE, and was readmitted last year.
