Up to 10 cm of snow has already fallen and a bitter cold spell is set to hit the UK. Yellow warnings for snow and ice cover most of England, Wales, and Northern Ireland, while amber warnings warn of heavy snow in northern Scotland that could cause power cuts and road blockages. Travellers should expect delays, and health services warn of higher risks for older people.
