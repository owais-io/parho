Rita Buckley, a 74‑year‑old nurse, has been getting help for a brain injury at Buffalo Therapy Services. In August the clinic’s parent, Kaleida Health, announced it would shut two therapy locations because of federal health‑care cuts from Trump’s One Big Beautiful Bill. Patients now have nowhere to go, and the clinic staff have lost jobs. The bill is expected to cut Medicaid, hurt hospitals, and close more community clinics, sparking protests from patients and unions.
