Israel and the United States launched a new wave of missile and air strikes against Iran on Sunday, the second day of a campaign aimed at toppling Tehran's regime. The attacks hit government sites in Tehran and other cities, killing many civilians, including children. Iran said it will retaliate and warned of a wider war. The fighting has disrupted airports in Dubai, Doha and other Gulf cities, raising fears of higher oil prices and a regional crisis.
