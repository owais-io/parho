A shooting in the small New South Wales town of Lake Cargelligo left three people dead, including a pregnant woman. Police found the victims in a vehicle and in the town centre, and a fourth man was badly wounded and hospitalized. The suspect is still on the run in a vehicle, and a large search is underway. Residents are told to stay indoors and avoid the area.
