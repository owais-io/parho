Australia’s fans are buzzing as the Ashes move to Adelaide, where hot days and a day‑only test could bring back the old hard‑hit cricket vibe. They’re hoping for a 5‑0 win, a rare thing that used to feel almost mythical. The new “Bazball” style from England adds extra drama, but most Aussies just want to beat England, even if it’s just in a normal way. The pressure is on to smash the English side.
