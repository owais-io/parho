Luke Littler is the world champion and top‑ranked player in darts, and he’s back in London to defend his title. He’s knocked out most of his rivals in recent years, and only a few players like Ryan Meikle, Ryan Joyce, or Joe Cullen could give him trouble. The tournament also features rising star Beau Greaves in the women’s event and shows how darts is growing worldwide. The winner takes a record £1 million.
