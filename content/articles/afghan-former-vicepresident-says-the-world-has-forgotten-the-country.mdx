Afghan ex‑official Dr Sima Samar, who helped build a democracy after the 2001 war, is now living in exile. She says the world has stopped caring about Afghanistan, where the Taliban have returned and women are being stripped of their rights. She reminds us that when people ignored Afghanistan, the country fell back into chaos. She urges countries, especially Australia, to keep recognizing the former Afghan government and support women’s rights.
