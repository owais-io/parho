Three London boroughs—Kensington & Chelsea, Westminster, and Hammersmith & Fulham—were attacked by hackers. The councils shut down many computer systems to stop damage and called in the National Crime Agency and cyber‑security experts. Services like council tax checks and parking fine payments may be slow or unavailable for a few days while they fix the problem. Residents will get updates as the councils work to recover and protect their data.
