Swansea Ospreys fans are upset because the Welsh Rugby Union (WRU) plans to reduce the number of professional teams from four to three, potentially shutting down the Ospreys. The WRU also wants to buy Cardiff RFC, which would leave Ospreys the most likely team to disappear. Swansea council, players and supporters have launched legal challenges and protests, saying the change hurts Welsh rugby’s growth and local culture. The national team’s weak results add to the turmoil.
