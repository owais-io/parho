Liverpool coach Arne Slot said he has no issues with forward Mohamed Salah after a row over an interview. Salah was left out of a Champions League game but returned after a good showing against Brighton. Slot promised talks while Salah is away for the Africa Cup of Nations but said the matter is settled. He’ll use Salah when needed once he returns from the tournament.
