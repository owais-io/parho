Heavy rain turned rivers into flood‑bodies, forcing about 100,000 people in Washington and Oregon to leave their houses and leading the state to declare a full emergency. Roads, bridges and even rail lines were shut down, while the National Guard and California crews helped with rescues. Water levels are still high, and more rain is expected, so the danger of floods and mudslides continues.
