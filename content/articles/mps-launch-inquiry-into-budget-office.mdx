MPs have started a review of the Office for Budget Responsibility (OBR) to see how well it predicts the economy and stays unbiased. The all‑party Treasury committee will study the OBR’s forecasts, its independence and whether its ties to the Treasury need changing. The move follows a spat between the OBR chief and Chancellor Rachel Reeves over budget leaks. MPs want to understand the OBR’s role and make sure it helps the UK’s economy.
