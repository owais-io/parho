Rachida Dati, France’s culture minister and a former justice minister, is running to become Paris’s mayor. She promises more police, better housing for local workers, and a crackdown on crime. Opponents say she could turn the city into a right‑wing stronghold. The left, including Socialists and Greens, have joined forces to stop her. The election is close and could change how Paris is governed.
