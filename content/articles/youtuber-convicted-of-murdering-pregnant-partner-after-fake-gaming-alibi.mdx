Stephen McCullagh, a 36‑year‑old YouTuber from Lisburn, was found guilty of killing his pregnant partner, Natalie McNally, in December 2022. He tried to cover his tracks by livestreaming a video‑game session, claiming it was live, but police proved the footage was pre‑recorded. A Belfast jury handed him a mandatory life sentence after a brief deliberation, underscoring how digital alibis can be faked.
