Israel shut the al‑Aqsa mosque on Eid, the first time since 1967. They said it was for security amid tensions with Iran, but Palestinians say it’s a move to tighten control. Many were forced to pray outside the Old City, and the Arab League slammed the closure as a breach of rights. The news also notes Gaza’s still‑ongoing war and the hardship its people face.
