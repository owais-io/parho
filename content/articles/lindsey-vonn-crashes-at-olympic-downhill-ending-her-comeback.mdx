Lindsey Vonn, the former world champion, raced the Olympic downhill in Cortina d’Ampezzo despite a serious knee injury. About 13 seconds in, she clipped a gate, lost balance and fell hard, breaking her leg. She was air‑lifted and the race stopped. The crash marked the end of her long‑planned comeback, showing she kept pushing herself even when the odds were against her.
