Born in Ukraine, Danylo Yavhusishyn fled the war, found a new life in Japan, and turned to sumo. In just nine tournaments he rose to the top rank of ozeki, a record‑breaking climb. Now he hopes to become Japan’s first European yokozuna, the sport’s highest title. Aonishiki also wants to bring more fans from around the world to sumo and keep his family back home proud.
