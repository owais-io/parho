Gabriel Jesus, the Brazilian striker for Arsenal, says the club is getting closer to winning trophies after three consecutive runner‑up finishes. He wants to stay at Arsenal and extend his contract, even though he has no talks yet. Jesus just came back from a knee injury and wants to stay healthy. He hopes to help Arsenal lift silverware in the next few seasons.
