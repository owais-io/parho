After a tense start, US President Donald Trump and Colombian President Gustavo Petro will meet in Washington. The talks could calm the bitter feud that erupted when the US raided Caracas and when Trump accused Petro of drug links. Petro says he wants to stop cocaine smuggling and help Colombia meet its own goals, while Trump hopes to secure Colombia’s cooperation on drug spraying, migration and regional security. The meeting could also boost Petro’s image ahead of Colombia’s May elections.
