Chelsea is eyeing a £135m buy for Real Madrid's Vinícius Júnior, who is unhappy and could leave before his 2027 contract ends. The club also has Adam Wharton as a potential target if Crystal Palace releases him, with Real Madrid, Chelsea, Man City, Liverpool and Man United all interested. Other movers include Sacha Boey, João Gomes, Kevin Danois, Nathan Aké, Jahmai Simpson‑Pusey, Omar Marmoush, William Osula, João Cancelo and Luke O’Nien.
