City Football Group sold its 65% stake in Mumbai City FC and pulled out of Indian football after a review of risks and uncertainty around the Indian Super League (ISL). The 2025‑26 ISL season never started because the deal with the AIFF expired and no new sponsor was found, leaving players, coaches and fans in limbo. Losing CFG’s expertise and brand has hurt clubs, raised concerns about governance and warned investors that the sport still faces big problems.
