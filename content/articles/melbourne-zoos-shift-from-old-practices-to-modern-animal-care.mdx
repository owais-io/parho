Melbourne Zoo has changed a lot since it opened in 1862. Old shows, like orangutans smoking, are gone. Today the zoo focuses on keeping animals happy and healthy, giving them natural habitats and scents to play with. They also move big animals like elephants to larger parks and run breeding programs that released thousands of frogs and other endangered species. The zoo works with scientists to make sure animals feel safe and respected.
