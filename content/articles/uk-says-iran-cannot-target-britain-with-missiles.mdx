A UK minister says Iran has no ability or plans to hit Britain with missiles after firing two at a UK‑US base in the Indian Ocean. One missile missed the island, the other was shot down. The UK will not be drawn into the conflict and will defend its people and interests. UK forces have allowed strikes on Iranian sites that threaten allies.
