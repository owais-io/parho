The Pakistan government had almost cancelled its match against India because Bangladesh pulled out of the T20 World Cup, sparking worries about the sport’s big TV deal. After talks with the ICC, Pakistan and Bangladesh cricket boards, and Sri Lanka, Pakistan agreed to play. Bangladesh won’t face penalties and will host a future ICC event. The move keeps the tournament running and shows cricket can bridge politics.
