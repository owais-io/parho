Trump’s special envoy, Steve Witkoff, is heading to Moscow to talk with President Vladimir Putin about ending the war in Ukraine. The United States is hopeful a peace deal can be reached, but Europe worries the plan gives Russia too many concessions. Meanwhile, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy has just visited Ireland and is rallying support. The war continues, with Russia claiming advances in eastern towns like Pokrovsk, and concerns over abducted children.
