Robert Reich argues that while President Trump acted unilaterally and demanded allies’ help to clear the Strait of Hormuz, most U.S. allies—France, Canada, the UK—refused. He says the American public largely disagrees with Trump and continues to support the international system of NATO and the UN. Reich warns that the U.S. needs allies to tackle climate change, pandemics and other global challenges. He urges readers not to confuse the president with the people.
