Labour MPs have asked Ofcom, the UK’s media regulator, to give clear rules that protect men and boys from dangerous online influencers. They say the “manosphere” exposes young men to gambling ads, sextortion and violent porn, and that tech companies should face tougher guidance. The MPs argue that protecting women and girls alone isn’t enough – men and boys also need focused safeguards.
