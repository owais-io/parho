Donald Trump is being urged by Iranian protesters to act fast, but he's getting mixed signals. Some advisers warn that US air strikes could backfire, inflaming the regime’s narrative that the protests are a foreign plot. Iranian opposition leaders have sent letters asking Trump to intervene, while others call for restraint. Trump’s team has no clear plan, and military moves are stalled. The situation remains tense.
