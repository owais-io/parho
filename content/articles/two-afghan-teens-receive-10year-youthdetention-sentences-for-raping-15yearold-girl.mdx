Two Afghan asylum‑seeking teens, both 17, were found guilty of abducting and raping a 15‑year‑old girl in Leamington Spa. At Warwick Crown Court, they were handed youth‑detention sentences of about ten years each and ordered to be registered as sex offenders. The judge said the crime robbed the victim of her childhood and could never be undone. The girls' trauma has left them feeling unsafe and affected their schooling. The boys will likely be deported.
