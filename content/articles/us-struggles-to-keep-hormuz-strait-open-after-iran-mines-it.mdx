Iran has shut the narrow Hormuz Strait with attacks and mines, blocking more than 1,000 oil and gas ships. The US tried to escort vessels but lacks enough naval assets and air cover. This has raised insurance costs and energy prices. Military planners say the close distance to Iranian shores and fast drones make escorting difficult, and the US has no clear plan to secure the channel.
