The new maternity review shows the NHS still has big problems. Hospitals are too old, staff are short, and many black women are dying at almost three times the rate of white women. Mistakes are hidden, midwives and doctors clash, and old buildings need fixing. The government is looking for fresh ideas, but real change will take time and better leadership.
