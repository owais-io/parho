Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old ICU nurse at the Minneapolis VA, was shot by U.S. immigration officers after he tried to help a woman. Video shows him holding a phone, not a gun, and being taken to the ground before the first shots. Family and friends call him kind, helpful, and a patriot. The incident fuels anger over aggressive federal enforcement in Minnesota.
