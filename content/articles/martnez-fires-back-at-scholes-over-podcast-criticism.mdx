After Manchester United beat Manchester City 2-0, defender Lisandro Martínez said he didn’t care about former player Paul Scholes’ mocking comments on a podcast. Scholes and Nicky Butt called Martínez small and weak against striker Erling Haaland. Martínez replied that he would focus on the game and the fans, not on criticism. He praised new interim coach Michael Carrick and said the win showed United’s connection with supporters.
