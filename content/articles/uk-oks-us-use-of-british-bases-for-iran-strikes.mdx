Britain has shifted its stance on the Middle East war. Prime Minister Keir Starmer now allows the U.S. to use two British air bases – RAF Fairford and Diego Garcia – to hit Iranian missile sites, claiming it is legal self‑defence for allies. Britain has also moved aircraft to Qatar and Cyprus to guard against Iranian drones. If Iran keeps striking, the UK may step in further or retaliate directly.
