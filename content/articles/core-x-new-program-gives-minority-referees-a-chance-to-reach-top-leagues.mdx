Core X is a programme started by the Football Association and the Referee Authority to help Black, Asian and mixed‑heritage referees climb the ranks. 29 officials from across England meet in Loughborough for two days of practice, fitness drills, video review and equality talks. The programme gives them coaching and support, helping avoid the bias that has kept many under‑represented referees out of top leagues. Since it began, minority referees in the professional game are now about 7.5 %.
