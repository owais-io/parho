Defence Secretary John Healey criticised opposition MPs for trying to undermine the UK’s relationship with the United States, calling their actions “unpatriotic.” He pointed to their support of former President Trump’s criticism of the UK’s Chagos Islands deal and opposition to allowing US forces to use UK bases against Iran. Healey also discussed a drone strike on RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus, saying the UK has strengthened air defences and will continue to protect its bases and allies.
