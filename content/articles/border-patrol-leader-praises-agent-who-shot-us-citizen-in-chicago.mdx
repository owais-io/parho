Border patrol chief Gregory Bovino sent encouraging texts to agent Charles Exum after Exum shot Chicago woman Marimar Martinez, a U.S. citizen, five times during an immigration raid. Video later showed the agent had driven into her car, leading to the case being dismissed. The protective order on evidence was lifted this week, making the messages public. The incident highlights growing criticism of the Trump‑era border policy.
