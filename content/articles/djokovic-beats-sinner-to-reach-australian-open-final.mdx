Novak Djokovic, 38, won a five‑set thriller against two‑time defending champion Jannik Sinner at Melbourne’s Rod Laver Arena, beating him 3‑6, 6‑3, 4‑6, 6‑4, 6‑4 to reach the Australian Open final. The match lasted just over four hours and ended after 2 am. Djokovic’s win marks him as the oldest man to reach this final and boosts his quest for a record 25th Grand Slam title. He will face Carlos Alcaraz next.
