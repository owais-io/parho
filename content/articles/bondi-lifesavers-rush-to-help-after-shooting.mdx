Lifesavers on Bondi Beach usually save swimmers, but on a December Sunday they became first responders when a shooting broke out at nearby surf clubs. While a Christmas party was in full swing, shots rang out, and lifesavers opened their clubhouses, giving shelter and first aid to injured people. They treated dozens of victims before police and paramedics arrived, and their quick action saved lives and showed how surf clubs support the community.
