Palestinian activist Mahmoud Khalil, who was detained by ICE after protests at Columbia University, has filed a lawsuit against former Trump officials. He says the administration worked with far‑right anti‑Palestinian groups that shared his information and helped get him arrested. Khalil wants the government to hand over records of those conversations and is seeking money for false imprisonment and defamation. He was held for over three months and was released in June.
