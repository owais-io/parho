The ongoing war in the Middle East has shut large sections of airspace over the Gulf, forcing airlines to take longer, riskier routes. Flights now skirt north around Ukraine or head south through Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Oman, creating traffic bottlenecks and delays. Major hubs like Dubai and airlines such as Emirates, Qatar, and Etihad are partially shut, costing airlines fuel and time, and pushing up flight cancellations.
