Ukrainian and U.S. negotiators met in Florida to push for a peace deal, but Russia did not attend. Talks followed earlier sessions in the UAE and Geneva, focusing on finalising a comprehensive agreement. Meanwhile, Russian strikes killed four people in southeastern Ukraine and cut power in the Chernihiv region. The violence also hit Kyiv’s utilities, while Ukrainian forces shelled a building in Russia’s Belgorod region, killing four.
