A tragic fire at a bar in Switzerland's Crans‑Montana ski resort on New Year's Eve left 40 dead and 115 injured. Among those hurt was an Australian, who is receiving medical care and support from his government. The Australian government is offering consular help and keeping an eye on whether more Australians were affected. The fire was an accident, not arson.
