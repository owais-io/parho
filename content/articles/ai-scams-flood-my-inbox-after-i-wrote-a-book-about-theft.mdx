After writing a book about theft and fraud, I started getting emails from fake people. They claim to offer reviews, book promotion, and big money. The messages look real because AI can write personalized letters. I discovered the pictures were stock photos and the names were made up. Other writers have faced the same. My advice: think carefully, avoid clicking links, and never share your personal info.
