A 29‑year‑old activist in Peterborough is on day 42 of a hunger strike with other Palestine Action prisoners. They were jailed for alleged attacks on an Israeli‑arms factory and have been in custody longer than the usual six‑month limit. They want bail, an end to the group’s ban, and better communication rights. Doctors warn their health is slipping; the government has refused to meet with their representatives, raising fears the prisoners could die.
