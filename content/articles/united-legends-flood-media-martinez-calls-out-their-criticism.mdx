Manchester United’s old‑timers—Paul Scholes, Nicky Butt, Gary Neville and others—have become a regular feature on podcasts and TV, offering their take on the club’s fortunes. Their constant commentary often criticises the current manager and even players. When Lisandro Martínez was teased about his height compared to striker Erling Haaland, he shot back by inviting the ex‑players to his house. The joke made some of them look annoyed, but they remain unbothered.
