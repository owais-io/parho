Russia still wants Ukraine to hand over the Donbas region it controls, even though Kyiv has not agreed to pull back there. The three‑way talks in Abu Dhabi bring together Russia, Ukraine and the United States for the first time since Russia’s 2022 invasion. The Kremlin demands that Ukraine withdraw its forces and give up NATO aspirations, while the U.S. tries to broker a deal. Talks are stuck over land, security guarantees and Russia’s maximum demands.
