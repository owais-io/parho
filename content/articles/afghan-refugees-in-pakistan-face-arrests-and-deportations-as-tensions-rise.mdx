Afghan refugees living in Pakistan are now being stopped at airports, hotels and on the streets. With Pakistan’s new “open war” stance against the Taliban, police raids and deportations have surged. The Guardian story follows journalist Alma, who was denied a flight and a hotel room, and other refugees who report arrests, house‑to‑house searches and forced returns to Afghanistan—putting their safety at serious risk.
