A woman who claimed she was raped by Andrew Malkinson says she had doubts about naming him when she first told police. In court she admitted she was “not too sure” and that officers reassured her it was fine. New DNA tests found saliva from another man on the victim’s vest, linking it to Paul Quinn, who is now on trial. Malkinson was freed in 2020 after 17 years in prison for a crime he did not commit.
