Police have released court papers that say Naveed Akram and his dad planned the Bondi shooting for months. They brought a tennis‑ball bomb, pipe bombs and firearms to the Hanukah party but the bombs didn’t explode. The pair filmed an ISIS‑style video, trained with guns and even planted flags. Police found more weapons and bomb parts at their homes. Naveed faces 15 murder charges, his father died during the attack.
