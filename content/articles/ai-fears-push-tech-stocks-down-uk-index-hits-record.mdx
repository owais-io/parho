Investors panic over AI tools that could replace software work, causing a sharp drop in tech and data‑company shares worldwide. The slump hit the FTSE 100, but the market still reached a new record thanks to a big insurance takeover by Zurich of Beazley. Oil stocks gained as gas prices rose, and pharma firm Novo Nordisk fell after weak guidance. The move shows how AI worries can shake markets even as other sectors climb.
