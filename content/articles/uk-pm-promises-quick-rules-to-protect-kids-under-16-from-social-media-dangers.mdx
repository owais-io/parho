Prime Minister Keir Starmer says the government will act fast to safeguard children under 16 from harmful social media and AI chatbots. He will use two existing bills to give ministers the power to set rules in a few months, not years. Starmer says a ban could be considered but is not decided yet. He wants to hear from kids, charities, and experts before deciding.
