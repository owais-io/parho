Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said Russia violated a Trump‑made truce by launching a massive airstrike on Kyiv on a bitterly cold night. He accused Moscow of firing 71 ballistic missiles and 450 drones at energy sites, leaving over 1,000 homes without heat. Zelenskyy warned that this breach could trigger a swift response from Ukraine and its allies as new talks in Abu Dhabi begin.
