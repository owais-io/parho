Crystal Palace beat Burnley 1‑0 thanks to a goal from Daniel Muñoz. Burnley struggled to score and were kept quiet by Palace’s solid defence. The match was slow and both goalkeepers didn’t have much action. Palace’s coach Scott Parker switched in fresh forwards to keep the pressure on. Burnley’s manager, Oliver Glasner, praised his players’ effort despite the loss. The win keeps Palace in fifth place, just outside Europe spots.
