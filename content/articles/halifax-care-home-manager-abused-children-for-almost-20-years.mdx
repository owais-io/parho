A 92‑year‑old former care home manager in West Yorkshire, Malcolm Phillips, is accused of sexually abusing children at Skircoat Lodge from 1976 to 1994. His assistant, Linda Brunning, is also charged with helping him and assaulting a boy. The case shows how the manager used his power to isolate vulnerable children and exploit them, with the court hearing details of nightly assaults and intimidation.
