Victoria’s government will let police stop or move protests after a terror attack, following New South Wales. A new law will give the police commissioner the power to ban protests for a set time, similar to NSW’s 14‑day limit. Officers with rifles will guard the Boxing Day Test and may be used at other big events. The premier also plans faster anti‑vilification laws, tougher gun rules, and stronger action against antisemitism.
