A group of Democratic senators says the Trump administration pulled thousands of federal officers from tough cases—like child abuse, drug trafficking, and human smuggling—to work for Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). They argue this shift has weakened investigations into serious crimes. The senators want a full list of moved agents and the cases affected. ICE has hired 12,000 new staff and now holds the most immigrants in detention, many with no criminal record.
