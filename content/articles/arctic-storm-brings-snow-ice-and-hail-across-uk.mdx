An Arctic cold front is heading for the UK this weekend, bringing snow, sleet, hail and icy roads. Met Office has issued yellow warnings for wintry weather that could last until Sunday morning, and a cold‑weather health alert for vulnerable people. Major roads like the A66 are closed, and crews are clearing snow. Drivers are urged to plan routes and stay safe.
