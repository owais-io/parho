Lewis Hamilton finished his worst Formula One season, ending sixth in the championship and failing to score a podium (except a sprint win in China). He said he wants to unplug from the sport over the winter break, ditching his phone and media. Hamilton criticized Ferrari’s early stop on car development, but backs the team’s plan for the 2026 car. The 40‑year‑old will use the break to regroup.
