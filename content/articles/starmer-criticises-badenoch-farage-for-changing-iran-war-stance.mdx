Starmer took aim at fellow politicians Kemi Badenoch and Nigel Farage during a chaotic PMQs session, saying they’d suddenly changed their minds about the UK’s role in the Iran conflict. He accused them of backing the US war after first supporting it, slammed their comments about the RAF, and warned that their shift could hurt Britain’s interests. The debate also touched on rising energy costs.
