On 27 December, thieves drilled into a Sparkasse bank in Gelsenkirchen and emptied over 3,000 safe‑deposit boxes, a crime worth up to €300 m. The criminals entered through a car‑park exit, used a heavy drill and escaped in a black Audi and a white Mercedes. Customers, including a local carpet shop owner, are left stunned and worried about how the bank could be breached. Police are still hunting the suspects.
