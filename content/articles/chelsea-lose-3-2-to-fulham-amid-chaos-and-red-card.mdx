Chelsea suffered a 3-2 defeat to Fulham at Craven Cottage in a match full of drama. With Marc Cucurella sent off early, the Blues played with ten men and struggled against Fulham’s experienced squad. New coach Liam Rosenior watched the chaos from the stands, seeing his team’s defensive lapses and fans’ angry chants. Fulham’s Harry Wilson and Raúl Jiménez scored, while Chelsea’s poor form keeps them in eighth place.
