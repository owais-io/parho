In a surprise victory that could inspire Democrats nationwide, Analilia Mejia, a progressive activist and former Bernie Sanders adviser, is close to beating former congressman Tom Malinowski in New Jersey’s 11th‑district primary. With 93% of ballots counted, Mejia leads by 889 votes and has the support of figures like Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez and Elizabeth Warren. Her win shows how community‑driven campaigns can challenge entrenched politics.
