AI has been blamed for more than 1,000 Australian job cuts, with firms like Atlassian, Block and WiseTech announcing hundreds of layoffs. While companies say AI is cutting costs, experts argue only about 1 % of the cuts are actually AI‑driven; most are part of broader restructuring. Workers fear AI‑generated voiceovers and lost roles, but many sectors still need human staff, especially in call centres and entry‑level jobs.
