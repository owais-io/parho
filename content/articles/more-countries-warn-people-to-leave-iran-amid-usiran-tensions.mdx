More countries are urging citizens to leave Iran and nearby areas because U.S. and Iran tensions rise. Australia has told diplomats’ families in Israel and Lebanon to depart, and the U.S. embassy in Lebanon has removed non‑essential staff. Airlines like KLM and Air France‑KLM are cutting flights to Tel Aviv and other regional hubs. Other governments—Germany, India, Brazil, Singapore—also advise travelers to stay away or postpone trips.
