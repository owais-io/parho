Stuttgart won 4‑0 at Leverkusen, thanks to two goals from Jamie Leweling. The win was a big shock for Leverkusen, who were missing several players. Stuttgart’s strong start put them level on points with the league leaders and cut the goal‑difference gap. Leweling’s performance has drawn interest from clubs like Bournemouth, but Stuttgart want to keep him for the future.
