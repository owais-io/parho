US Treasury lifted Global Magnitsky sanctions on Brazilian Supreme Court Justice Alexandre de Moraes and his wife, after Brazil’s president Lula requested it. The judge had overseen former president Jair Bolsonaro’s 27‑year prison sentence. Trump added the sanctions when he slapped 50% tariffs on Brazilian goods. The decision signals a warming between Trump and Lula and leaves Bolsonaro’s supporters disappointed, while Bolsonaro remains in jail.
