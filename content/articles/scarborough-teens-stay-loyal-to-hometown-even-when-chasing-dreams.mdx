Scarborough's 17‑year‑old teens love their town and want to stay, even if they leave for jobs or training. They train at the Stephen Joseph Theatre and dream of acting, but bad roads and infrequent trains make it hard to reach jobs in bigger cities. Local arts groups and new jobs give hope, but transport and money still hold them back.
