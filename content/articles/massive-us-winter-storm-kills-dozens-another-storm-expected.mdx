A powerful winter storm has swept across much of the United States, causing at least 60 deaths nationwide, including eight in Tennessee, eight in Louisiana, and multiple fatalities in Kentucky, Mississippi, Texas and New York. The storm left about 370,000 people without power, especially in Tennessee and Mississippi, and caused snow, ice, and dangerously low temperatures. Weather experts say another big winter storm could hit the East Coast this weekend, warning that conditions could get even worse.
