Seamus Culleton, an Irish plasterer living in Boston, has been held by U.S. ICE for five months despite having a work permit. His wife, Tiffany Smyth, a U.S. citizen, is asking for his release because he says the Texas detention center feels like torture and he fears for his life. Culleton was arrested after overstaying a visa, but his marriage gave him a path to permanent residency. He wants to finish that process. The Irish government is helping.
