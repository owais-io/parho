Israeli air strikes on Tehran’s oil depots left the capital shrouded in thick smoke, soot, and acid‑rain fears. Six people died and 20 were injured at one site, while residents cough, burn in their eyes, and struggle to find masks. Authorities told citizens to stay indoors; fuel and food shortages worsened. Many residents tried to flee, but internet cuts and damaged infrastructure made escape hard.
