Mickey, a 45‑year‑old fracking worker in West Texas, started posting online offers to fix things. Instead of doing repairs, he discovered clients wanted sex and quickly became a popular escort, earning a six‑figure income. He says the region’s strict attitudes on sex created a hidden market that helped his business thrive. Mickey shares his experience on a new podcast, discussing relationships and consent.
