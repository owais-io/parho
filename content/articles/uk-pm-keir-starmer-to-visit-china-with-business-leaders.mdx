UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer is set to visit China next week with a group of big British companies like BP, HSBC and Rolls‑Royce. The trip comes after China got permission to build a new embassy near the Tower of London, a move that caused local protests. The visit is part of a plan to boost trade ties, but it follows past tensions over spy allegations and security concerns.
