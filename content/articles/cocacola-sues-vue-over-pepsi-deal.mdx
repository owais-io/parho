Coca‑Cola has filed a lawsuit against Vue Entertainment after the cinema chain chose PepsiCo to supply drinks in Europe. Vue’s 90‑plus cinemas had a 25‑year partnership with Coca‑Cola, but the new contract with PepsiCo ended that relationship. Coca‑Cola claims it is owed unpaid debts from the switch, while Vue says the money is under £100,000 and the dispute has been settled. The case shows how big brands fight over contracts.
