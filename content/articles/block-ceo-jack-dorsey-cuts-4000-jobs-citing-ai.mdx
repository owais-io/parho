Block, the fintech firm led by Jack Dorsey, announced it will cut 4,000 jobs—about 40% of its workforce—citing AI that can do more with fewer people. However, the company also faces a weak crypto market, has over‑staffed teams, and its stock has dropped sharply. While the share price jumped 20% after the news, analysts say the cuts are more about tightening costs than a true AI revolution. Other tech giants have followed similar moves.
