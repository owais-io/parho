UK defence secretary John Healey said that very few people will mourn the death of Iran's Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. He called the Iranian regime “evil” and blamed it for sponsoring terrorism in the UK. Healey did not criticize the US and Israeli airstrikes that killed Khamenei and said the UK had no role in them. He warned that Iran remains a growing threat and that the UK will act defensively.
