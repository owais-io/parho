Victorian opposition leader Jess Wilson pulled out of the Across Victoria Alliance conference in Horsham after premier Jacinta Allan accused her of “cosying up” to One Nation. The event, headlined by former National Barnaby Joyce—who switched to One Nation—was seen as controversial. Wilson’s exit, officially due to a “scheduling conflict,” was widely viewed as political pressure. Her replacement, Nationals leader Danny O’Brien, will speak, while the conference continues to address regional concerns like the emergency‑services levy and renewable‑energy rules.
