A federal judge decided Kari Lake could not legally run the US Agency for Global Media because she wasn’t Senate‑confirmed. The court voided her actions, including cutting over 1,000 jobs and other cuts she made. The ruling restores the agency’s former staff and stops the layoffs she ordered, giving the agency a chance to rebuild.
