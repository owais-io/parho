An 18‑year‑old who killed nine people in a Canadian school had made a Roblox shooting game before the attack. The game, set in a virtual mall, let players pick up weapons and shoot each other. Roblox removed the suspect’s account after the shooting and is helping police. The incident highlights worries about video‑game‑style violence and the link to real‑world shootings.
