Paul Henderson, a former Mail on Sunday editor, says he was never a “handler” for private investigator Gavin Burrows, who is accused of bugging homes and tapping phones. The high‑court case is part of a lawsuit by seven people, including Prince Harry, who claim the Daily Mail used illicit methods. Henderson insists the alleged relationship is fabricated and that he never ordered or helped with illegal spying.
