18‑year‑old Cooper Flagg broke a record by scoring 35 points in a Dallas Mavericks win over the Los Angeles Clippers. He made 13 of 22 shots, grabbed eight rebounds, and helped the Mavs beat the Clippers 114‑110. Flagg’s coach praised his calm play, and teammate Klay Thompson said he has “limitless upside.” This was the first time an 18‑year‑old scored that many in an NBA game.
