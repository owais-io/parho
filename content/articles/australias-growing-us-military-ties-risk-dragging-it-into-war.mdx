Australia is becoming more entangled with the U.S. military through submarine crews, the Aukus submarine deal, and expanded bases. Recent events show Australian sailors on a U.S. submarine that sank an Iranian warship, and the government has kept citizens in the dark. Analysts warn that deep ties make it hard to oppose U.S. actions, putting Australia at risk of being pulled into wider conflicts.
