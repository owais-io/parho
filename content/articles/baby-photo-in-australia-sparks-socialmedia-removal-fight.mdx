A controversial photo of a fetus, dubbed “baby Samuel,” has been shared by anti‑abortion activists across Australia. Hospitals and authorities are demanding the image be taken down and are investigating how it was taken and leaked. The picture has been promoted by political groups, including One Nation, and is being criticized as misinformation. The incident raises privacy concerns and highlights the heated debate over abortion laws in the country.
