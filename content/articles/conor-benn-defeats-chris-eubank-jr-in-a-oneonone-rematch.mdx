Conor Benn knocked out the fight against Chris Eubank Jr, winning all 12 rounds. He landed powerful punches and even knocked his opponent down twice. The judges scored the bout 119‑107, 116‑110 and 118‑108 in Benn’s favour. Eubank Jr looked tired and had trouble gaining weight after the weigh‑in. Benn celebrated a huge comeback after a tough three years, and said the feud is over. The crowd was quiet, and Benn said he would focus on higher‑level fights next.
