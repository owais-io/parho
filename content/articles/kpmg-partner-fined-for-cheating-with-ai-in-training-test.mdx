A KPMG partner in Australia was fined A$10,000 after using AI to cheat on an internal training test. The incident is part of a broader problem, with more than two dozen staff caught using AI tools to cheat since July. KPMG used its own AI detectors to find the cheating. The case highlights growing worries about AI‑enabled cheating in accounting firms and the need for better safeguards.
