After a serious knee injury, Lindsey Vonn skied a training run on the Olimpia delle Tofane course. She broke her ACL in a crash the week before and had to wear a brace. Despite fog delays and other crashes, Vonn finished the run in 1 minute 40 seconds and reached speeds of 75 mph. Her performance shows she can still race for a medal.
