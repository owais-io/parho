The UK’s consumer price index fell to 3.6 % in October, the lowest level in five months, although food costs are still climbing. The easing may give the Bank of England a reason to cut interest rates in December. Meanwhile, water company Severn Trent is swapping bosses – CEO Liv Garfield will leave, and Jame Jesic will take over while water bills are set to rise 21 % next month. Businesses and households are watching the coming budget for more relief.
