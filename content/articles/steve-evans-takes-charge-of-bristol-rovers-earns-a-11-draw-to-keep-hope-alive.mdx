Steve Evans has taken over as Bristol Rovers manager after a 10‑game losing streak that put the club in the relegation zone. In his first game, a 1‑1 draw at Crewe Alexandra, the team fought back from a goal down while playing a man less for most of the match. Evans said the point is a start and he’ll keep improving, hoping to lift Rovers out of the bottom of League Two and eventually reach League One.
