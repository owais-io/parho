After 144 years of construction, the last stone of the Sagrada Familia’s central tower was finally placed. The tower now rises 172.5 metres, making the church Barcelona’s tallest building. The ceremony, held on a windy Friday, marked a milestone for a project that has attracted 5 million tourists a year. Work on the remaining facades will finish in roughly a decade.
