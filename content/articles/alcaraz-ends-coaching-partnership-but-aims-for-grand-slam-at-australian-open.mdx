World No.1 Carlos Alcaraz has cut ties with long‑time coach Juan Carlos Ferrero, saying he will rely on his current team as he heads to Melbourne. He hopes to win the Australian Open and finish the career Grand Slam. His main rival, Jannik Sinner, is also in the hunt. The women’s side is open, with stars like Coco Gauff, Iga Świątek, and Elena Rybakina looking for titles. Fans will watch to see if Alcaraz can step up.
