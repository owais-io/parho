Smartmatic, a voting‑tech company, says the Murdochs cleared a plan for Fox News to repeat Donald Trump’s false 2020 election‑fraud story to win back angry viewers. Fox argues no such order was given and that hosts were just discussing news. In a Manhattan courtroom, Smartmatic seeks a summary judgment, while Fox wants the case to go to jury. The judge will review the broadcasts before deciding.
