A new survey of people in 29 countries found that almost one‑third of Gen Z men still think a wife should obey her husband, and many say the husband should have the final say in important matters. The same views are far less common among Gen Z women and older generations, especially baby boomers, who hold more liberal attitudes. Economic hardship and online misogyny may be pushing some young men back toward traditional, harmful masculinity, raising concerns about rising violence against women.
