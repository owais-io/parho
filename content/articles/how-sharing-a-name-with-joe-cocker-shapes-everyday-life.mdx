The writer, who shares the name John Robert Cocker with the late singer Joe Cocker, shares how the name shapes everyday interactions. After writing to the singer for his 70th birthday, he received a CD with a personal note, sparking a series of funny encounters—such as airport staff in Peru mistaking him for the musician. The story shows how a famous name can open doors, create awkward moments, and lead to unexpected conversations.
