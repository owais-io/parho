Arsenal boss Mikel Arteta says he wants every team that comes to the Emirates Stadium to suffer, especially after Aston Villa beat him 2‑1 with a last‑minute winner. Arsenal are unbeaten, but have struggled to finish games, relying on own‑goals. Villa are on a long winning run, beating Chelsea for an 11th straight match. Arteta says the loss will fuel his players, and he’s looking at defensive injuries that may need January signings.
