Pauline Hanson failed to declare a flight on Gina Rinehart’s private jet within the required 35‑day window, breaching Senate rules. The flight took her from Melbourne to Sydney in October after a Geelong event that Rinehart helped fund. Hanson only added the flight to her register on Tuesday, after the Guardian revealed the omission. The move could trigger a Privileges Committee inquiry.
