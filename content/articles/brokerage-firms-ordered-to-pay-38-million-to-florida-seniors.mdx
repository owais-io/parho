A panel of FINRA arbitrators has awarded $3.8 million to 13 Florida seniors who lost most of their retirement savings after a financial adviser put their money into risky structured products. The award names Charles Schwab, TD Ameritrade and the adviser as responsible. It comes amid a Trump‑era push to sell more high‑risk “alternative” investments to everyday investors. The decision is rare and signals stronger protection for retail investors.
