People often think of insects like wasps and mosquitoes as pests, but they’re actually essential for the planet. Wasps help pollinate plants and control harmful insects, while sharks, despite media fear, are key predators that help keep marine food webs balanced. The article argues we should stop judging animals by how annoying or dangerous they seem and instead value their ecological benefits. It reminds us that all species, even the unlovable ones, matter for a healthy Earth.
