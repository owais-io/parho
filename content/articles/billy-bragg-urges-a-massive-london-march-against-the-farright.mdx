Billy Bragg is urging people to join what could be the biggest multicultural march in UK history on Saturday, marching from Park Lane to Trafalgar Square to oppose the far‑right. The event will feature many celebrities, musicians and speeches from politicians and union leaders. Bragg says the march shows unity against hate and hopes turnout will beat last year’s “Unite the Kingdom” rally. He stresses empathy and solidarity as a counter to divisive politics.
