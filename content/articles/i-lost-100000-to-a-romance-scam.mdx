In 2017 I met an attractive man who said he knew bitcoin and lived in London. He convinced me to invest £34,000, promising huge returns. After I learned about my inheritance, he disappeared. Later I realized I had been scammed and lost all my money. The police investigated, but in 2023 they closed the case because they couldn’t prove anything. I was left shaken and worried about my children.
