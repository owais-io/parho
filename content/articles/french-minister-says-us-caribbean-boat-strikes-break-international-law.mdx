French foreign minister Jean‑Noël Barrot accused the U.S. of unlawfully striking suspected drug‑smuggling boats in the Caribbean, calling the attacks a breach of international law and a threat to regional stability. The claim was raised during a G7 meeting in Niagara Falls, where leaders also discussed Ukraine, Gaza and Sudan. U.S. officials have defended the strikes, saying they target drug traffickers linked to Venezuela’s president. Legal experts warn the actions could amount to war crimes.
