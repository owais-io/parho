Premier Chris Minns is urging Sydney residents to enjoy New Year’s Eve with friends and family, saying police will patrol the city to keep everyone safe. More than 2,500 officers, many with long‑arm weapons, will be on the streets, especially around Harbour, to show a strong protective presence. The move follows a recent Bondi attack and aims to reassure all communities, including Jewish and Muslim groups. Police may even bring in military support, but details are still unclear.
