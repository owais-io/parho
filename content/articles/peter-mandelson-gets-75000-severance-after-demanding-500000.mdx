Peter Mandelson, the former UK Labour minister, was dismissed as the UK’s ambassador to the US because of his close ties to Jeffrey Epstein. He originally asked the Foreign Office for more than £500,000 in severance, but the government cut the offer to £75,000. Cabinet papers show officials tried to keep the settlement low and smooth the process, while keeping the case quiet to avoid media attention. Mandelson remains under investigation for possible misconduct.
