AFL boss Andrew Dillon says he wants Australian rules football on the 2032 Brisbane Olympics stage. He also highlights the success of the Opening Round, which features games mainly in NSW and QLD and has drawn record crowds. Dillon praised the focus on those states for growing the sport and said the plan for a 2026 season is working. He saw potential in India and said Aussie rules should return to the Olympics like in 1956.
