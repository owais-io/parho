English footballer Jill Scott, Olympic champions Mo Farah, Alex Yee and Matthew Pinsent, plus 84 others, signed a letter warning that new planning rules could let developers take over sports pitches. They say this would hurt the poorest towns and stop kids from playing. The letter stresses play‑fields are essential for health, community and fairness. A public debate ends 13 January, and the government says it still has strong rules.
