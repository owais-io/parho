Labour MP Wes Streeting said he might be “toast” at the next general election after private WhatsApp chats with Peter Mandelson were released by the health secretary. The messages criticised the government’s growth plans and communication, and revealed a close friendship with the disgraced peer. Streeting still plans to stand again in Ilford North, saying he has community support and believes the economy has improved. The leaks fuel speculation about Labour leadership.
