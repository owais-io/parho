Trump hosted the Kennedy Center Honors, the country’s top arts award show, and bragged that the event was already getting “rave reviews” before it finished. He joked about turning the venue into a “Trump‑Kennedy Center” and talked about how the honorees showed persistence, like the movie Rocky. While many conservative leaders attended, Democrats were sparse. The performers celebrated icons like Stallone, Crawford, and Kiss, and Trump kept his speech short and self‑referential.
