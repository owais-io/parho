Memvid, a California startup, is hiring people to spend an 8‑hour day pushing chatbots until they give up or forget. The job pays $800 and doesn’t need a tech background—just someone who’s tired of chatbots repeating themselves. Workers ask the same questions, keep the conversation going, and record what happens. The goal is to expose how AI systems lose context and can hallucinate, a problem that’s hurting law and healthcare too.
