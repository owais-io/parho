Jannik Sinner fought through cramps and brutal 36°C heat at the Australian Open to beat 24‑year‑old Eliot Spizzirri. The match paused when heat rules triggered and the roof closed, giving Sinner a chance to recover. He rallied from a break down in the third set and won 4‑6, 6‑3, 6‑4, 6‑4, moving into the last 16. His mental toughness and luck were key.
