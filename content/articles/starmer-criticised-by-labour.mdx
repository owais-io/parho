Keir Starmer’s leadership has sparked a wave of criticism from inside the Labour Party. Letters from voters and party members accuse him of lacking vision, making too many U‑turns, and not uniting the party. Critics call for his removal, say his style is bland, and warn his tactics could doom Labour. The piece also highlights concerns over past appointments and the party’s relationship with the US.
