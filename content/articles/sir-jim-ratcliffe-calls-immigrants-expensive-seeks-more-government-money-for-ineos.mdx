Sir Jim Ratcliffe, the billionaire behind Ineos, said immigrants were too expensive and that the UK’s welfare system was draining money. He apologized after angry reactions. At the same time, he is asking the government for huge financial help for his chemical plants, a big stadium project for Manchester United, and other Ineos ventures. The company has already received hundreds of millions of pounds in state aid and is looking for more to stay afloat.
