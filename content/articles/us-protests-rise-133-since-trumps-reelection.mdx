From 4,588 protests in 2017 to over 10,700 in 2025, demonstrations have jumped 133% since Trump returned to the White House. Protests now pop up in 42% of Trump‑supporting counties and even in rural red states like Montana and North Carolina. They cover everything from trans‑youth health to anti‑ICE raids. The spread shows ordinary people are getting organized across the country, not just in big cities.
