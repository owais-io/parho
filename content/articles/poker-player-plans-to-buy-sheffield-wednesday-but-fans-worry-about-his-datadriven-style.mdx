James Bord, a former poker champion, is trying to buy Sheffield Wednesday with a consortium that also owns a data‑analytics company. The company has caused trouble at nearby Sheffield United, where it helped hire a bad manager and sign players who didn’t fit. Bord has stayed quiet about the deal, so fans are unsure what he will change. He promises to invest in data‑backed youth talent and focus on on‑field success.
