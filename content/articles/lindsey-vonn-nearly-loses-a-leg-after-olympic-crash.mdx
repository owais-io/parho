Lindsey Vonn nearly lost a leg after a crash at the Winter Olympics. She broke her tibia and suffered compartment syndrome, a painful condition that can damage muscles. Multiple surgeries in Italy and the US saved her leg, thanks to surgeon Tom Hackett. She’s now out of the hospital but still needs about a year to heal and decide on future surgery.
