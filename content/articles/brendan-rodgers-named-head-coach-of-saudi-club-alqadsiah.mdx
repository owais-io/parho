Brendan Rodgers, who left Celtic in October, has been hired as the new coach of Saudi Arabian club Al‑Qadsiah. The team, owned by the state‑owned oil company Aramco, wants to rise in the Saudi Pro League and hopes Rodgers’ experience will help. Rodgers is known for winning titles at Celtic and Liverpool. Al‑Qadsiah are currently fifth in the league, 10 points behind leaders Al‑Nassr. The move comes after Rodgers declined a Saudi job earlier this year.
