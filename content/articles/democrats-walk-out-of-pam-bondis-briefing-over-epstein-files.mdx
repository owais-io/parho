Democrats left a private briefing by Florida Attorney General Pam Bondi after she refused to testify under oath about the Justice Department’s handling of Jeffrey Epstein files. The House Oversight Committee had asked Bondi to testify under subpoena, but she said she would not give sworn testimony, sparking frustration from both parties. Democrats called the hearing a “fake” session, while Republicans insisted Bondi should just talk. The incident highlights tensions over releasing Epstein documents.
