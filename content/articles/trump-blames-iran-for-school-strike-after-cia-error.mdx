Trump said Iran was behind a deadly school strike, even though the CIA had originally told him the missile was American. Later the CIA admitted the missile was a U.S. Tomahawk, but Trump had already blamed Iran before reporters heard it. The Pentagon is still investigating the mistake, which killed at least 175 people. The school was close to a military base, and officials say the U.S. didn’t intend to hit civilians.
