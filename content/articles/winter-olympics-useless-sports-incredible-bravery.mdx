Even though winter sports aren’t popular or practical for most people, the Winter Olympics still capture our imagination. The Games highlight athletes’ daring, like Lindsey Vonn racing with an injury and skiers overcoming PTSD, and show how they push themselves simply because the sports exist. The article also notes problems such as political controversies, budget overruns, and unfinished venues in Italy, yet the excitement remains. It’s a mix of bravery, spectacle, and messy logistics.
