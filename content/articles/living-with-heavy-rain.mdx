The Guardian column talks about York's flooding, the fun and frustration of continuous rain, and offers practical ways to deal with it. From making rain‑aware art to dressing appropriately, the author suggests embracing the wet weather, making rain‑songs, and doing DIY crafts like Japanese rain‑charms. She also warns that flooding is linked to climate change and that more rain is likely, so having coping plans and protecting your home is key.
