West Ham beat Burton Albion 1‑0 after extra‑time, thanks to Crysencio Summerville’s winning goal. The match was played with only ten West Ham players after Freddie Potts was sent off. With the score level, Summerville slipped in during the 82nd minute, then struck a curling shot in extra‑time that went past the keeper. Burton’s efforts were stopped by the red card and West Ham’s defence. The win sends West Ham into the FA Cup last 16.
