Two‑year‑old Maya was taken by immigration agents in Minneapolis and her father was also detained. A judge ordered her release that night, but officials put them on a flight to a Texas detention center. The child was later flown back and given to her mother, but the father remains in custody. Lawyers say agents broke a car window, used crowd‑control tactics, and acted without a warrant.
