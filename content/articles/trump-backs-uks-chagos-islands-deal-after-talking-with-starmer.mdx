Donald Trump once slammed the UK’s plan to hand the Chagos Islands to Mauritius as foolish. But after a phone call with Prime Minister Keir Starmer, he said the deal was the best Starmer could make and praised the 99‑year lease of the Diego Garcia base. Trump also vowed to protect U.S. forces there. The move drew criticism from UK Conservatives, while Trump still talks about Greenland.
