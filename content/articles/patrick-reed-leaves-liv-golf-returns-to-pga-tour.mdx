Patrick Reed, the former Masters champion, has said he will leave the Saudi‑backed LIV Golf tour before the 2026 season and re‑join the PGA Tour as a past‑champion member for 2027. He will also keep playing on the DP World Tour. His exit follows a multi‑million deal signed in 2022, and it adds to the loss of top LIV players like Brooks Koepka.
