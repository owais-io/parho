Mandelson told Blair to keep Gordon Brown’s back‑bench supporters in check during the 2005 campaign. The two men were already at odds after Brown said Blair had promised to step aside. Mandelson warned that Brown’s allies might try to hurt Blair’s chances and that the media would focus on Blair, not on Brown. The plan was later kept secret until files were released to the National Archives.
