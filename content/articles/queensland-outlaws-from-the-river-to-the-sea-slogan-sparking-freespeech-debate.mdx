Queensland has banned the slogans “from the river to the sea” and “globalise the intifada.”  People who say them in public or online could face up to two years in prison.  The law was introduced after the Bondi beach terror attack and is meant to curb antisemitism.  Critics call it an Orwellian free‑speech crackdown, and legal challenges are already being planned.
