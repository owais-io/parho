Republican Senator John Cornyn and Democrat Greg Casar got into a heated argument outside Austin‑Bergstrom Airport over whether to fund the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). DHS has been shut down for two months because Democrats want new rules on immigration enforcement. The lack of funding has left TSA workers unpaid and slowed security lines, while both lawmakers blame each other for the stalemate.
