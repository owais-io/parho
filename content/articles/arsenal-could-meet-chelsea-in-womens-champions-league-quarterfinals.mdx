Arsenal are the current European champions and will face Chelsea in the quarter‑finals if they beat OH Leuven in the February playoffs. The draw shows the path each team must take to reach the final in Oslo on 23 May. Manchester United, who will play Atlético Madrid, could face Bayern Munich if they win their match. Barcelona, OL Lyonnes, Chelsea and Bayern are already in the last eight, while other teams must win in the playoffs to reach that stage.
