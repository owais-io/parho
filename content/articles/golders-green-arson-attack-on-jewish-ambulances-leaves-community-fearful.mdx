Early Monday morning, two Hatzola ambulances parked in Golders Green, London, were set on fire in an antisemitic attack. The blast caused panic and left the local Jewish community feeling unsafe and vulnerable. Residents and leaders said the incident highlights growing hostility toward Jews in the UK. While some people offered support, many fear that broader anti‑Jewish sentiment is rising and puts them at risk.
