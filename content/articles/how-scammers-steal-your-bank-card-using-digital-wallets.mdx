Criminals call you pretending to be your bank and ask for personal details and “fake” transaction confirmations. They pressure you to approve a digital‑wallet addition, which looks like a normal bank notification. Once approved, the scammer adds your card to their phone and quickly spends it on high‑priced goods. Banks now warn you never to trust unsolicited calls, use the official bank number, set account alerts, and report any suspicious activity right away.
