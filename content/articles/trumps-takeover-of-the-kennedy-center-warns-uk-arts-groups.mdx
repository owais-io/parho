Trump swapped the Kennedy Center’s board for his own supporters and said “woke” shows would be cut. Ticket sales dropped, big shows like Hamilton left, and staff quit. The place feels more like a museum than a theater. A senator is questioning the spending, and some artists are thinking of leaving. The politicisation shows how politics can hurt cultural places – a warning for the UK.
