Farage says his Reform UK party will team up with the Conservatives before the next general election, according to donors he spoke to the Financial Times. He called the move “inevitable”, but also warned about a quick pact. The Conservative party denies any talks and says it won’t partner with Reform. Some former Conservatives have joined Reform, and Labour warns the deal could bring back cuts. Donations remain higher for the Tories.
