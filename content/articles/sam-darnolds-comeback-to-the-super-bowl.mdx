After years of moving between teams, Seattle’s new quarterback Sam Darnold made a stunning comeback. He led the Seahawks to a 31‑27 win over the Rams, throwing 346 yards, three touchdowns and no turnovers, and earned the top seed in the playoffs. Darnold’s story—from a shaky start with the Jets to a breakout season with Minnesota—has become the headline of Super Bowl 60, showing how hard work can turn a career around.
