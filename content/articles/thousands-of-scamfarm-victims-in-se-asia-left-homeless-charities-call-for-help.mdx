Survivors of Southeast Asia’s cyber‑scam farms are left homeless and destitute. Amnesty International and aid workers say hundreds of thousands from more than 50 countries are trapped in camps in Cambodia, Myanmar and beyond. Many escape to street corners, hungry and without passports. Charities call for more funding, better medical and mental‑health care, and government support before victims can return home. The crisis is growing.
