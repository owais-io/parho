Lisa Nandy may investigate a £500m takeover of the Telegraph by the Daily Mail owner, Lord Rothermere. RedBird IMI had to sell the titles after a UK rule banning foreign state owners. DMGT has secured cash and loans to buy them. Nandy will decide whether to involve regulators, which could take up to 40 days. If approved, control of several papers moves to Rothermere.
