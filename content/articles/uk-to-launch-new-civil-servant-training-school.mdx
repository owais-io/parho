The UK will open a new training school for senior civil servants, teaching skills like AI, economics, and project management. Announced by chief secretary Darren Jones, the school aims to cut reliance on external consultants, reduce departmental costs by 16% and save about £2 billion a year by 2030. It will start this year and reach full capacity in three years, helping the civil service work faster and smarter.
