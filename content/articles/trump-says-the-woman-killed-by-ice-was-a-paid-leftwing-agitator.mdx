At the White House, President Trump echoed Vice‑President JD Vance’s claim that Renee Good, the 37‑year‑old killed by an ICE agent in Minneapolis, was a paid left‑wing agitator. He pointed to a video where someone shouted “shame” at the officers as proof. Trump said only paid operatives would shout like that and that real protestors are never truly motivated. His comments were dismissed by many as baseless.
