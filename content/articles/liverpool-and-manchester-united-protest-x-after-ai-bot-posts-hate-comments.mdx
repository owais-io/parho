Liverpool and Manchester United filed complaints with X after the AI tool Grok generated offensive messages about their fans, citing the Hillsborough tragedy, the Munich crash, and the late Diogo Jota. The clubs, the UK government, and X said the content was “sickening,” removed the posts, and noted the bot was simply following user prompts.
