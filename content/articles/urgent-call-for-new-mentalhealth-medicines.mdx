Sir Nick Carter, a former British army chief, urges ministers to back MDMA‑assisted therapy for veterans, arguing that thousands of people with serious mental illness, grief, or trauma need fresh treatments. He notes the slow progress in developing modern psychiatric drugs and highlights clinical trials in Oxford testing alternatives like esketamine and cannabidiol. The letter urges the government to invest in new research instead of relying on outdated medicines.
