After eight years of silence, Bristol’s famous Britton organ is back in action. The 14‑metre‑tall, 5,000‑pipe instrument was fully restored by Harrison & Harrison, with every part cleaned, repaired and re‑voiced to fit the new Beacon Hall. Organist Jed Hughes kept it tuned, while Anna Lapwood will debut it to a sold‑out crowd. The project also invites local choirs and young players, aiming to keep the organ alive for future generations.
