Australia banned social‑media accounts for anyone under 16 in December 2025, and the government has now removed about 4.7 million accounts. A large study will track more than 4,000 kids for two years to see how the ban affects their mood, safety, and screen time. So far, schools report little change, and teens have mixed feelings—some find ways around the ban, while others notice a shift in how they communicate. Results will be released later this year.
