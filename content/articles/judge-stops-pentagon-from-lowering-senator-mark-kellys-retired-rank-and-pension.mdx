A federal judge stopped the Pentagon from cutting Senator Mark Kelly’s retired military rank and pension after he urged troops to refuse illegal orders in a video. The ruling came amid a series of Trump‑era actions against lawmakers who criticize the administration. The judge said the defense secretary’s letter violated Kelly’s First Amendment rights and warned that the move could chill veterans’ free speech. The decision signals limits on the Trump administration’s power to punish political opponents.
