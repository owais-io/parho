A trust is restoring 4 Plaistow Grove, the house where David Bowie grew up from 1955 to 1968, and will let the public walk the floors by the end of 2027. The tiny bedroom will be set up just like it was in 1963, with rare pictures, books and even a photo of Little Richard on the wall. The project also plans workshops for young people and a fundraising campaign to finish the work.
