At a volleyball all‑star event in Kobe, player Yuji Nishida accidentally hit a judge with a serve. He immediately slid across the court, bowed, and knelt to apologize in a dramatic dogeza, a deep‑respect bow that touched the floor. The clip went viral, with fans calling it a ‘work of art’ and laughing. Despite the mishap, Nishida’s team won 3‑0 and he was named MVP.
