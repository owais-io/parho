Quentin Griffiths, 58, co‑founder of the fashion retailer Asos, died after falling from the 17th floor of a condo in Pattaya, Thailand. Police say the fall looks like a suicide with no evidence of foul play. He lived alone and was reportedly worried about lawsuits from his ex‑wife. Thai authorities sent his body for an autopsy and found related documents. Asos issued a statement of sadness.
