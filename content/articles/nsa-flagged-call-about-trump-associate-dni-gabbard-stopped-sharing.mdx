In spring, the NSA heard a foreign‑intelligence call that mentioned someone close to Donald Trump. The report was handed to DNI Tulsi Gabbard, who kept it from the NSA and from Congress. A whistleblower filed a complaint, and lawmakers say the delay shows the DNI is hiding the intel. The story has sparked a debate about how intelligence should be shared with Congress.
