UK woman Yvonne Ford, 59, died in 2024 after being scratched by a puppy in Morocco. She ignored the bite, thinking it was minor. Symptoms later appeared, confusing doctors. A psychiatrist suspected rabies, confirmed the diagnosis, and sent her to a specialist unit. She died days later. Her family now raises awareness and supports dog‑vaccination charities to help prevent future cases worldwide.
