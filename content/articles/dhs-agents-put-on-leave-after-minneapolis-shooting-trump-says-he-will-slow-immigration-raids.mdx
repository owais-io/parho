Two U.S. border patrol agents fatally shot Alex Pretti in Minneapolis over the weekend. The Department of Homeland Security put the agents on administrative leave, saying it is standard when a fatal shooting occurs. President Trump said he would “de‑escalate” the federal immigration crackdown in Minnesota, but he gave no details. Local officials in Minneapolis said they won’t help enforce federal immigration laws, sparking protests and a heated debate about federal‑state cooperation.
