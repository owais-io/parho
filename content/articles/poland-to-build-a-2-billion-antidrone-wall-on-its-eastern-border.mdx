Poland plans to finish a new anti‑drone system along its eastern borders in two years after Russian drones crossed its airspace in September. The wall will use guns, missiles, and jamming technology and will be added to an older line of defence. It will cost more than €2 billion, mainly from European funds, and is part of a broader “Eastern Shield” to stop future Russian attacks.
