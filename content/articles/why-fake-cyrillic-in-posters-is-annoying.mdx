Viv Groskop gets frustrated when movie posters and books use fake Cyrillic letters that look like Russian but are wrong. She hates seeing “STДLIN” instead of “STALIN” and other silly spelling mistakes that try to look exotic. She says it’s a cheap way to make something seem foreign and scary. The article notes many people share her irritation, especially on Reddit, and compares it to silly metal‑band umlauts.
