Nissan will shut down the remote‑control app for Leaf cars built before May 2019 and the e‑NV200 van by 30 March. Drivers will lose the ability to start heating or charge the car from their phone, though in‑car controls stay. Some owners complain that their cars, only a few years old, will lose a key feature with little notice. The move highlights a trend where car software may become unsupported as vehicles age.
