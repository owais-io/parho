Storm Ingrid slammed into south‑west England with heavy rain and big waves. In the town of Teignmouth, Devon, a section of its historic Victorian pier was washed away. The Met Office warned of yellow weather warnings across the region and the Environment Agency issued flood alerts. Heavy rain could reach 50 mm in some spots, and rail services were expected to face delays and cancellations.
