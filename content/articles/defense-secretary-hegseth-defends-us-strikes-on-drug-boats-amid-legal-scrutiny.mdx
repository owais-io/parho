Pete Hegseth, the U.S. defense secretary, defended recent drone attacks on suspected drug‑smuggling boats in the Caribbean. He said President Trump has the right to strike these vessels, calling smugglers “terrorists.” Critics say the attacks may violate international law and that the U.S. isn’t actually at war with drug cartels. The strikes have killed more than 80 people and faced backlash from Democrats, some Republicans, and legal experts.
