Arsenal won 2-0 at Brentford, with Mikel Merino scoring early and Bukayo Saka adding a late goal. The win kept Arsenal on top of the Premier League and gave them a clean sheet despite losing centre‑back Cristhian Mosquera to injury. Manager Arteta praised his squad depth and the rest he gave key players, but said the injury list – including Declan Rice’s calf problem – will make the next fixtures tough.
