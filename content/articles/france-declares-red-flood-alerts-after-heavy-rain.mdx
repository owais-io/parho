France has issued red flood alerts in three departments after Storm Nils left rivers overflowing. The Garonne River burst its banks, flooding homes and cutting villages off. Scientists say the soil is already saturated, so even a little rain can cause big floods. Officials will declare an emergency once the worst passes to speed up insurance claims. Climate experts warn that such heavy rains will become more common.
