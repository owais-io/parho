UK retail sales slipped 1.1% in October, the first drop since May, as shoppers hold back for Black Friday deals and worry about next week’s budget. Clothing stores fell 3.3%, while supermarkets and online sales also slipped. Consumer confidence dropped across all measures, and a services‑sector survey shows growth slowing. Retailers warn that holiday sales may remain weak as uncertainty lingers.
