The former mayor of New York City, Eric Adams, launched a cryptocurrency called NYC Token that saw its market value jump to nearly $580 million before crashing in a matter of hours. Investors say the sudden drop looks like a “rug pull,” a scam where creators dump their holdings. The company denies any wrongdoing, saying the moves were to “rebalance liquidity.” Adams has stayed silent, and details about the project’s team remain unclear.
