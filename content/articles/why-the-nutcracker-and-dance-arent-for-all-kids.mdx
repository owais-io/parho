The author shares how taking her son to a child‑friendly Nutcracker made her realise many kids miss out on dance because schools cut arts budgets. Few schools teach dance, especially in poorer areas, so children lose a natural outlet. Groups like Let’s All Dance and The Place bring affordable, professional ballet to kids, boosting focus, confidence, and teamwork. The article urges governments to fund dance as a creative subject for all.
