Roughly 600 survivors of a long‑running clergy abuse scandal in New Orleans are set to receive a total of $305 million after the archdiocese’s insurer, Travelers, agreed to add $75 million to a previously approved $230 million settlement. The deal still needs final approval, but it marks the biggest payout to date for this church, which has been in bankruptcy since 2020. The settlement also includes new rules to stop future abuse.
