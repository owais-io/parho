Trinity Rodman, the 23‑year‑old U.S. national team star, has signed a new three‑year contract with the Washington Spirit, making her the world’s highest‑paid women’s soccer player. The deal uses the NWSL’s new “High Impact Player Rule,” letting clubs spend extra money on top talent. Rodman chose to stay in the league instead of moving to Europe, keeping a key star for the Spirit and the NWSL.
