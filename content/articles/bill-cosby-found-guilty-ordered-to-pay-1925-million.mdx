The jury in California found Bill Cosby guilty of sexual assault in a civil case and awarded Donna Motsinger $19.25 million in damages. Motsinger, an 84‑year‑old former server, said she was drugged and raped by Cosby in 1972 after he gave her a glass of wine. The verdict follows changes to California’s sexual‑assault laws. Cosby, who served time in prison, plans to appeal. He denies the allegations.
