England’s cricket team lost badly to Australia in the first Ashes Test. Head coach Brendon McCullum told fans not to lose hope and to keep faith. He says the team must stay calm, stick to their game plan, and maybe play more friendly matches or team bonding to boost confidence. McCullum is confident that, if they stay focused and work together, they can turn the series around in Brisbane.
