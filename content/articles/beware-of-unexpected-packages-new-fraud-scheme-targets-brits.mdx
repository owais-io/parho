Fraudsters are using stolen postal details to create fake online orders in your name. They send cheap gifts and then leave five‑star reviews that look real. The tricks raise the sellers' ratings and can bring dangerous or low‑quality items into your home. If a mystery parcel arrives, don't open or use it. Check your accounts, report the fake order, and change passwords or enable two‑factor authentication.
