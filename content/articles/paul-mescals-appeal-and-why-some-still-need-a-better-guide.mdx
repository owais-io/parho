Adrian Chiles gushes about how irresistible Paul Mescal is, noting his talent to make Shakespeare feel visceral. He admits that while the actor’s acting feels almost like a physical experience, many fans—including himself—struggle to follow the words and plot. Chiles wishes for a simple “behind‑the‑scenes” guide, like a director’s commentary, to help people enjoy the performance without getting lost.
