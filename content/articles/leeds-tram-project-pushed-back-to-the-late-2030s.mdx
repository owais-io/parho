Leeds’ long‑planned tram system will now finish in the late 2030s, a 2‑3 year delay after a government review. West Yorkshire’s mayor says she’ll still lead the project by the end of the decade and is working with ministers to cut red tape and start construction sooner. Some critics fear the delay could threaten the scheme, but officials remain committed to delivering a modern transport network.
