In south London, Walworth Living Room is a community room where people can eat, get help with bills, and find friends. Fatma Mustafa, on Universal Credit, goes there three days a week to save on energy and groceries. Across the UK, ‘warm spaces’ have grown from 4,000 to almost 6,000, offering food and warmth in winter. While they help now, critics say they only patch problems, and experts call for government action to address poverty’s root causes.
