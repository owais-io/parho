Tennis Australia has boosted this year’s Australian Open prize pool to $111.5 million, a 16 % jump from last year. The winners of the men’s and women’s singles each get $4.15 million, up 19 %. Qualifiers now earn $40,500, and travel help is 67 % higher. The move follows a big rise in the sport’s revenue and shows the board’s commitment to supporting players, even as talks with players’ groups continue.
