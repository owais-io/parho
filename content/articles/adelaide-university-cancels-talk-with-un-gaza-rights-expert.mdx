Adelaide University shut down a planned talk with UN Gaza rights expert Francesca Albanese, sparking backlash from the festival and free‑speech advocates. Organisers say the university denied the venue because it didn’t follow internal approval steps, while the university claims it was unaware of the event until the last minute. The cancellation follows U.S. sanctions on Albanese and worries that the university is bowing to political pressure, stifling open debate.
