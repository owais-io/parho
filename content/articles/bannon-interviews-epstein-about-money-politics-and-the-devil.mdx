They talked about money, politics, science, racism and whether Epstein was the ‘devil’ Trump’s critics called him. Bannon pressed him on guilt, while Epstein tried to explain his financial work and deny responsibility. The clip was made for a documentary that aimed to soften Epstein’s image.
