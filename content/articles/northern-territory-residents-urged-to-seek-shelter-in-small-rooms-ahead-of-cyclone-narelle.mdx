Thousands in the Northern Territory are bracing for Cyclone Narelle, which is expected to hit with winds up to 195 km/h. Emergency officials have issued a top‑level warning and told people to stay inside the smallest, strongest rooms—like bathrooms—because traveling is dangerous. Many residents in Numbulwar have been evacuated, and emergency services are preparing for possible flooding in Katherine. The government has also activated disaster payments and is sending aid to Queensland.
