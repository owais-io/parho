Cyclone Narelle has intensified into a severe storm, heading for remote towns like Exmouth, Carnarvon and Denham in Western Australia. Meteorologists predict wind gusts up to 275 km/h and heavy rain, prompting evacuations and road closures. The cyclone may reach category 5 before landfall, making it the first severe storm to hit three Australian states in 20 years. It’s also a reminder of how rising ocean temperatures help cyclones grow.
