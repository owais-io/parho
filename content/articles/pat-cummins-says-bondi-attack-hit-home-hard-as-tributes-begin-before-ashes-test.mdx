Australia’s cricket captain Pat Cummins and England’s Ben Stokes were shocked by the Bondi beach terror attack that happened close to their homes. They said it was “horrible” and “hit home” because many Australians visit Bondi. The third Ashes Test will start with a moment of silence, black armbands, and flags at half‑mast to honor victims, especially the Jewish community. BBL games keep going with extra security, and police say there is no higher threat level.
