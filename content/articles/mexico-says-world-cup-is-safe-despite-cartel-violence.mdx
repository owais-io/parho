After a top drug boss was killed, gunmen blocked roads and attacked police, Mexico’s president says the World Cup is still safe. Military raids on the cartel leader, known as El Mencho, caused a fight that left him dead and many soldiers and gunmen killed. Violence eased by Monday, and games in Guadalajara and other cities are moving forward. FIFA and the government are reassuring fans that security is strong.
