Kash Patel, the FBI director, posted on X that the agency had detained a suspect in the Brown University shooting. The man was released hours later, and the shooter remains free. Local police said the evidence was weak and the alleged suspect isn’t a threat. Patel’s social‑media bragging has drawn criticism from both sides, with some calling for his resignation and saying the FBI is mismanaged.
