Spencer Pratt, known from reality TV, announced he will run for mayor of Los Angeles. He spoke at a rally on the first anniversary of the Palisades wildfire, blaming city and state officials for the disaster. He says the city’s system is broken and wants to protect everyday residents. He is running against candidates such as current mayor Karen Bass and former school superintendent Austin Beutner. Pratt’s campaign website is basic but includes a donation button.
