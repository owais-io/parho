Ofgem will lower the price cap by 7% on April 1, cutting the average annual bill for a typical home by about £117. Because the government is scrapping green charges, the reduction applies to everyone. Experts say the cheapest fixed‑price deals now are about 14% lower than the cap, saving households over £200 a year. Switching before April gives the biggest benefit.
