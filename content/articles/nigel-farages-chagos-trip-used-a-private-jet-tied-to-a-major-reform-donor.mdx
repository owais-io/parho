Nigel Farage tried to fly to the Chagos Islands on a private jet that seems to belong to Christopher Harborne, a huge donor to Reform UK. The jet and a boat from Harborne’s companies carried Farage and Chagossian activists, but they couldn’t get past a UK military base, so the trip failed. The move drew attention to Harborne’s massive £12m donation and raised questions about how much donors can influence UK politics. Calls for clearer limits on donations grew.
