Two Mexican‑born brothers who play mariachi were freed from ICE detention after a backlash that involved a Republican congresswoman and a Democratic representative. The 18‑year‑old and his 14‑year‑old brother had been held while their parents were still in a Texas family detention center. After Congress members visited the facility and highlighted the family’s legal status and good conduct, ICE released them. The case drew national attention to the harsh treatment of immigrant families.
