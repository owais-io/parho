A 49‑year‑old former Tory councillor in Swindon, Philip Young, has been charged with 56 offences, including 11 rapes, 13 drug‑induced assaults and many other sexual crimes. Five other men face similar charges. The victim is his ex‑wife, who waived anonymity. All defendants were released on bail with strict no‑contact rules. They will go to Swindon Crown Court on 23 January. They also face charges for collecting illegal images of children.
