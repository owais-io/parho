The Palace of Westminster could need £40 bn to fix and could take up to 61 years. Parliament has two main plans: move both Houses out of the palace (full decant) or move the Lords first and the Commons later (staged decant). A £3 bn start will refit the tower, add a Thames jetty and begin underground work. MPs worry the money and plan lack clear accountability.
