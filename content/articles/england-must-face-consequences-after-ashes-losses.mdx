England lost the first two Ashes Tests and are now on a beach break in Australia. Coach Brendon McCullum says the team needs to change and face real consequences. He wants honest talks, better batting decisions, and a clearer lineup, possibly dropping some players and adding others to bring calm. The side has to stop playing for fun and start winning or risk more defeats.
