Police officer Ben Parfitt helped 27 schoolchildren escape waist‑deep flood water at a caravan park in Katherine, NT. He then used a helicopter to rescue a couple and their German shepherd stuck on a 4WD roof. His actions made social media go viral. The floods, the worst in decades, caused many evacuations and other rescue missions, with helicopters and boats carrying people, animals and supplies.
