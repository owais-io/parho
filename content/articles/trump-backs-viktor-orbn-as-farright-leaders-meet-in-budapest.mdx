Trump sent a video message to Hungary’s prime minister, calling Orbán a “fantastic guy” and wishing him a big win in the upcoming election. The praise came as a group of far‑right leaders from across Europe, including Marine Le Pen and Matteo Salvini, gathered in Budapest for a “Patriots’ Grand Assembly.” Orbán, who has been losing in polls, faces a tough contest against a centre‑right rival. The event highlights the growing influence of far‑right politics in Europe and Orbán’s strained ties with the EU.
