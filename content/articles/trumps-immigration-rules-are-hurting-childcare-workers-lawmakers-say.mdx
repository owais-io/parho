Democratic lawmakers, led by Senators Warren and Duckworth and Rep. Quigley, wrote a letter to the HHS child‑care agency saying Trump’s immigration rules could cut the U.S. childcare workforce by about 15 %, leaving parents with fewer, pricier options. About 20 % of child‑care workers are immigrants, up to 70 % in some areas. The letter cites raids, deportations and TPS cuts that have forced staff to quit. Lawmakers demand an assessment and action plan.
