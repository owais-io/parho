Rojava in Syria has long been a rare place where men and women share power. But the Syrian army is now pushing in, threatening to roll back those gains. Women there warn of violence, betrayal by the West, and a grim future. The region’s fight for equality, justice, and democracy could inspire people everywhere, so the author urges us to speak up and support those women.
