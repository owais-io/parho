Ajit Pawar, the deputy chief minister of Maharashtra, died when his small plane crashed and caught fire on Wednesday. The crash also killed his two staff members and the flight crew. His death leaves a power gap in the state’s politics, where he had been a key figure for decades and recently helped the BJP win the 2024 elections. Party leaders and national politicians paid tribute, and discussions are now underway about who will replace him in the NCP.
