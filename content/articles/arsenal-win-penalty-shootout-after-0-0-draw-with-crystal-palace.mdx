Arsenal and Crystal Palace finished 0-0 in a tense match. In the penalty shootout, Arsenal won 5‑4, with goalkeeper Kepa Arrizabalaga saving a spot‑kick from Maxence Lacroix. Late in the game, Guéhi scored a stoppage‑time equaliser to force the shootout. Arsenal had a strong second half, while Palace fought hard but struggled after key players were injured. The win keeps Arsenal in the Cup competition.
