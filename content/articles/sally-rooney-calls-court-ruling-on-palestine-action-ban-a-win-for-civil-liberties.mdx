Sally Rooney, author of *Normal People*, says she is thrilled that a UK high court ruled it unlawful to ban the protest group Palestine Action under the Terrorism Act. The court found the ban too harsh and an infringement on free speech and assembly. Rooney will donate her book proceeds to the group and hopes the ruling will protect artists and activists. The Home Secretary will appeal.
