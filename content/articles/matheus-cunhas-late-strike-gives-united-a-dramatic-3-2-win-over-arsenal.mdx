Manchester United pulled off a thrilling 3-2 victory at Emirates Stadium, thanks to Matheus Cunha’s late curling goal. United went ahead early with an own goal, then drew level before taking the lead again. Arsenal fought back, but United’s confidence—boosted by a win over City—kept them in control. The result puts United back in fourth, while Arsenal remain anxious about their title hopes.
