Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has set up a four‑month review, led by former ASIO chief Dennis Richardson, to examine whether Australian intelligence and police agencies had the right powers, processes and sharing arrangements to stop the Bondi beach shooting. Critics say the review is too small and call for a full royal commission. ASIO will cooperate, and findings will be released by April 2026.
