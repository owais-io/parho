Everton’s striker Thierno Barry says he rediscovered his love for football while playing with kids on a Zanzibar beach after a rough spell in Switzerland. He’s now scoring again, with four goals in five Premier League games. Barry’s journey has seen him move from Belgium, Switzerland, Spain to England, overcoming early setbacks, red cards, and a goal drought. He credits patience, hard work, and family support for his recent form.
