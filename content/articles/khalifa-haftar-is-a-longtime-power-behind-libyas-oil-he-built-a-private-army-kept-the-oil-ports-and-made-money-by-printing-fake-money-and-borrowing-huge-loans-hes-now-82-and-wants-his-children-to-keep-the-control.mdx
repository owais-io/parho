Haftar grew up in Chad. He studied in Russia and joined the military in 1980. While in the army he became good at hiding his identity. He taught himself how to make a shadow state—one that looks like a government but is really run by a single man. In 2008 he fought against a group called the National Front for a Free Libya (NFSL). The NFSL was a big force that had to stay quiet. The group said Haftar had been good at keeping things secret.
