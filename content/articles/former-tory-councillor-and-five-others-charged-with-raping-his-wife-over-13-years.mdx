A former Tory councillor, Philip Young, and five other men have been charged with raping and drugging his wife over a 13‑year period. Young faces 56 sexual‑offence charges, including rape, drug‑assisted sex and child‑image possession. The other men were charged with various offences against the same victim. All are set to appear at Swindon magistrates court, as part of a long‑running investigation by Wiltshire police.
