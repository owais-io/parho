In 2026, Formula One cars will be smaller, lighter, and more agile. New hybrid engines split power roughly 50/50 between the internal‑combustion engine and electric motor, adding a lot of electricity but also creating turbo‑lag that teams must manage. Wings can now open on straights, and a new “overtake mode” replaces the old DRS. Cars will use flat floors, narrower tyres, and 100 % sustainable fuel, making drivers focus more on energy use and strategy.
