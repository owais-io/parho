Both Putin and Trump want more respect from other countries. Putin sees the war in Ukraine as a way to regain status, while Trump sees Europe as a threat to his idea of American power. Trump’s policies try to weaken the EU, back far‑right groups, and cut ties with NATO. Both leaders aim to replace the old liberal order with one that suits their own interests.
