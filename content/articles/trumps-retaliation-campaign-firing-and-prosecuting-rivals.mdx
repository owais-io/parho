Since returning to the White House, Trump has used federal power to punish anyone he sees as a foe. He’s fired prosecutors, dismissed FBI agents, and even threatened charges against lawmakers, judges, and the Federal Reserve chair. The move has scared career government workers, who worry they could be next. Analysts say the trend shows no sign of stopping, and they’re asking who will stand up.
