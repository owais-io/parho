At least 12 people died in a shooting at a Hanukah party on Bondi beach in Sydney. One of the shooters was also killed, a second is in critical condition and police are still looking for a possible third attacker. They’ve called the incident a terror attack. About 29 people were taken to hospital, two police officers are seriously hurt. Bomb‑detection teams are checking a nearby car for explosives. Prime Minister Albanese called it a hate‑based atrocity.
