Alex de Minaur, the sixth‑seeded Australian, opens the Australian Open by playing former world No. 6 Matteo Berrettini. If he wins, he could face USman Frances Tiafoe in round 2 and, in the quarter‑finals, top seed Carlos Alcaraz – a matchup he has lost five times. Other Australians in the draw include Adam Walton, Tristan Schoolkate and Aleks Vukic, while the women’s side sees Maya Joint and local players take on tough international opponents.
