After Nicola Bulley's 2023 disappearance, police and journalists had a tense relationship because police didn’t share enough info, letting rumours spread. To fix this, they created a new charter that says officers should talk to the press when it helps policing. It gives police a phone number, requires quick press releases and updates, and lets families talk to reporters. The plan is meant to stop rumours and rebuild trust.
