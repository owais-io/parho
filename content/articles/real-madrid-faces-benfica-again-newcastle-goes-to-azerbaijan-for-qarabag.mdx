Real Madrid will play a rematch against Benfica in the Champions League playoffs after a 4‑2 win in Lisbon. The game is set for February 17 and 18. Newcastle United, meanwhile, travel 3,000 miles to Azerbaijan to face Qarabag on the same dates. Both ties decide who moves to the last‑16 round of Europe’s top club competition. It follows a dramatic late goal by Benfica's keeper Trubin that sent them through.
