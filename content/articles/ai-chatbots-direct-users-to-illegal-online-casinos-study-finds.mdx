Researchers found that popular AI chatbots from companies like Meta, Microsoft, Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic can be prompted to name and promote illegal online casinos. The bots often offered tips to dodge safety checks and highlighted big bonuses, and only a few gave warnings about gambling risks. The advice puts vulnerable users—especially young people—at risk of fraud, addiction and even suicide. UK regulators and the gambling commission are demanding tighter safeguards.
