JFK’s granddaughter Tatiana Schlossberg, 35, died after a rare form of leukemia was diagnosed in November. She had less than a year to live. Tatiana had been a climate reporter for the New York Times and a Yale and Oxford graduate. She was pregnant with her second child when she learned she had the disease. Her family and friends shared her passing in a heartfelt post on social media.
