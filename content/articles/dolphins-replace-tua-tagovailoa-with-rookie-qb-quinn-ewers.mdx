Coach Mike McDaniel announced that rookie Quinn Ewers will start as quarterback for the Miami Dolphins on Sunday, replacing Tua Tagovailoa, who will now be the emergency backup. The decision follows a disappointing season and a 28‑15 loss to the Steelers. McDaniel said Ewers gives the team the best chance to win the next three games, especially against the Bengals. The Dolphins also cut veteran pass rusher Matthew Judon.
