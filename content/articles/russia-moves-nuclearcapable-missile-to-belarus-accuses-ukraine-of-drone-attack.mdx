Russia says it has moved a nuclear‑capable missile system into Belarus, claiming Ukraine hit President Putin’s home with drones – but no proof. The Oreshnik rockets will shorten the time it takes a missile to reach an EU city. Ukraine’s officials call the story a lie. Meanwhile leaders in Washington and Kyiv are working on a peace plan, while Russia warns of “reprisals.”
