UK lawmakers are pushing a ban on social media for people under 16, after concerns about kids’ mental health and addiction. Conservative leader Kemi Badenoch and Greater Manchester mayor Andy Burnham both support limits. Teachers’ unions and some Labour MPs want tougher rules and penalties for tech firms that let children sign up. The government says it already has laws to protect 18‑year‑olds, but it’s listening to parents.
