West Ham are preparing a £40m bid for Wolves striker Jørgen Strand Larsen as they fight to stay in the Premier League. The 25‑year‑old scored 14 goals last season but has only three this year, and Wolves want a high price. West Ham also looked at other forwards like Niclas Füllkrug and Coventry’s Ellis Simms, but Larsen is the priority. The club believes a new striker will boost their survival hopes.
