India beat the United States in the opening game of the T20 World Cup, thanks to a rapid 84 by Suryakumar Yadav. The Indians finished 161‑9, and the Americans struggled with early wickets, bad fielding and a few dropped catches. India’s score was just 29 runs higher than the USA’s, so the match was close but India held on. A few key wickets and a good bowling spell from Mohammed Siraj sealed the win.
