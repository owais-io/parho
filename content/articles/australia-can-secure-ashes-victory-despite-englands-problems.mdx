Australia’s Ashes win looked shaky with injuries, but they still beat England comfortably. Key Australian players like Smith and Khawaja missed games, yet the team managed a 4‑1 series win. England faced even more chaos – many players out and a reliance on fast bowlers. The upcoming Sydney Test could be played without a specialist spinner, and rain might turn it into a draw. Both teams are still hoping to finish strong: Australia wants to keep its World Test Championship win rate high, while England wants to salvage a 3‑2 result and keep its rebuilding project on track. Fans can expect a high‑pressure match, but weather and selection uncertainty could keep the game in the balance. With the pitch expected to favor fast bowlers, both sides will rely on pace and may adjust bowling plans as the match progresses.
