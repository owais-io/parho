A blaze broke out at the Le Constellation nightclub in Crans‑Montana, Switzerland, on New Year’s Eve. The fire ripped through a packed bar, killing about 40 people and injuring around 115, many of them teens and young adults from several countries. Investigators say there’s no sign of arson or terrorism, and the cause may be a spark from a candle or sparklers. Swiss hospitals are overwhelmed, and officials are reviewing fire‑safety rules.
