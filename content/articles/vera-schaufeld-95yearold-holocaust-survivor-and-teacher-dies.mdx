Vera Schaufeld, a 95‑year‑old Holocaust survivor and long‑time teacher, died after a life of teaching and sharing her story. She came to Britain on a Kindertransport in 1939, lost her family in the camps, then taught English to new arrivals in London and spoke widely about empathy and history. Honoured with an MBE, she worked with Holocaust groups until her death and inspired many.
