Storm Bram has struck the UK and Ireland, bringing heavy rain and strong winds that could flood homes and disrupt travel. Amber warnings are in place for south Wales, south‑west England and north‑west Scotland, where winds up to 90 mph are expected. Ireland will face an orange warning; rivers are already near overflow. Flood alerts cover more than 300 sites. Drivers and commuters may see road closures, train delays, and power cuts.
