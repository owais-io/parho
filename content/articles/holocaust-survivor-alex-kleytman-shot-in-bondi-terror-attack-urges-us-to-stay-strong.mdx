Alex Kleytman, a Holocaust survivor who lost his mother to a Ukrainian concentration camp, spent his life sharing stories of Jewish resistance. He moved to Australia, wrote books, and raised 11 grandchildren. In December 2024, he was fatally shot by a terrorist during a Hanukkah celebration on Bondi Beach. His wife said he died protecting his family. Kleytman urged everyone to stay strong and remember history.
