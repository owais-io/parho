Lev Parnas, a Ukrainian‑American businessman convicted of taking Russian money to influence U.S. politics, is now running as a Democrat for Florida’s 27th congressional district. He wants to unseat Republican María Elvira Salazar, a three‑term incumbent. Parnas says he will fight corruption and warns about a “cult” around former President Trump. Although he has a felony conviction, Florida lets people run for office.
