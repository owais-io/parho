47‑year‑old Benjamin Lewis admitted leaving dead deer and lambs near churches in the New Forest to provoke Christians. He pleaded guilty to five counts of religiously aggravated harassment and two thefts. Police found carcasses outside several churches from February to April. Lewis remains in custody while awaiting sentencing. Local authorities urge residents to report suspicious activity, noting similar incidents have occurred in the area before.
