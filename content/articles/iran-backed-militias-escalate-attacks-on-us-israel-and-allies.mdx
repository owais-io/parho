Iran-backed militias across Iraq and the region are stepping up attacks on U.S. and Israeli targets, using drones, missiles, and rockets. They hit U.S. bases in Jordan and Iraq, Israeli sites, and even Kurdish opposition infrastructure. In response, Israel and the U.S. have launched airstrikes and ground operations against militia strongholds. Iraq is trying to stay out of the fighting, but the conflict risks spreading and drawing in more regional players.
