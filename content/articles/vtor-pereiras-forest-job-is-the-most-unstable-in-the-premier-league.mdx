Nottingham Forest have had a chaotic season, firing three managers in one year. Owner Evangelos Marinakis has been quick to sack coaches after bad results, leaving the club in a survival battle in the Premier League. Vítor Pereira is being considered to take over, but the job is the most unstable in the league. Forest’s shaky performance and fan frustration show the pressure on the new manager to keep the club up.
