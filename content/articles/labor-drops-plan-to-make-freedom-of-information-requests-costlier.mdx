Australia’s Labor government has withdrawn a controversial bill that would have added fees and tightened rules on freedom of information requests. Finance Minister Katy Gallagher said the government still wants to fix the system, citing wasted time and money. Critics welcomed the move, calling it a win for democracy. The new reform plans remain to be drafted and debated in parliament.
