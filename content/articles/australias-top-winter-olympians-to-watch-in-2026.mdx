Australia has a huge 53‑man team for the 2026 Winter Games, the biggest Aussie squad ever, and it looks ready to beat its 2022 medal record. Star skiers Jakara Anthony and Matt Graham are back, while teenage talents Indra Brown and Valentín Guseli have already won World Cup medals. Snowboard stars Scotty James, Adam Lambert, and Bree Walker are in strong form. Aerialists Laura Peel and Danielle Scott are aiming for first‑time medals.
