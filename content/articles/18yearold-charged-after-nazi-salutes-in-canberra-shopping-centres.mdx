An 18‑year‑old was arrested for waving Nazi salutes at two Canberra malls and sticking hateful stickers there. He also was caught trespassing at the Australian National University and damaging property. Police seized phones, a computer, stickers and a video camera from his home. Australian federal police say anti‑Semitism is a serious crime and will keep fighting hate and division. The young man will appear in court on Friday.
