James Milner played his 653rd Premier League match for Aston Villa, tying the record set by Gareth Barry, but the team still had to settle for a 1‑0 loss. A late header from Tyrone Mings sent Brighton into a 13‑game winless streak. Villa coach Unai Emery celebrated the victory and the club’s 50‑point tally. Brighton’s manager Hürzeler called for a stronger effort after the defeat.
