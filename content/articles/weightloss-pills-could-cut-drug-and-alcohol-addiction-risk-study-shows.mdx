A new study of more than 600,000 US veterans found that medicines used for weight loss and diabetes, like Ozempic and Mounjaro, lower the chance of people starting to abuse alcohol, tobacco, cannabis, cocaine, nicotine or opioids. They also cut the risk of overdosing, hospital visits and death among those already using drugs. The research suggests the drugs may affect brain reward pathways, but more trials are needed.
