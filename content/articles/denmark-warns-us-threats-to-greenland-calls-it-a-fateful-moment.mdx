Denmark’s prime minister Mette Frederiksen said her country is at a crossroads after US President Donald Trump said the U.S. might take control of Greenland. The comment came before Denmark and Greenland’s foreign ministers were to meet Secretary‑of‑State Marco Rubio. Frederiksen warned that if the U.S. backs away from NATO and threatens an ally, it could unravel the alliance. Greenland’s leaders said they want decisions made by Greenlanders, not Denmark or the U.S.
