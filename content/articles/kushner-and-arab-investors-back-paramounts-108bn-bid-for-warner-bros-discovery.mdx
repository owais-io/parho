Paramount Skydance is offering $108 billion to buy Warner Bros Discovery, but the deal is backed by money from Jared Kushner’s investment firm and sovereign wealth funds of Saudi Arabia, Qatar and the UAE. Trump and his allies are involved, raising worries that foreign money could influence U.S. media. Congress and regulators may review the deal for national‑security reasons, while critics say the bid could give Trump a more friendly CNN.
