President Trump said on social media that the U.S. is ready to strike Iran again if an agreement isn’t reached. He warned the next attack could be worse and urged Iran to negotiate. The message follows the arrival of a large U.S. naval group, including the aircraft carrier Abraham Lincoln, near Iran. Trump’s comments signal a shift from anger over protesters to focus on Iran’s nuclear program.
