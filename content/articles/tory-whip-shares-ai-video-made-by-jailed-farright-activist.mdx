Tory chief whip Rebecca Harris has drawn criticism after sharing an AI‑generated clip from the satirical X account Crewkerne Gazette. The video, made by Joshua Bonehill‑Paine, a convicted far‑right activist jailed for antisemitic hate crimes, pokes fun at Conservative figures. Harris says the account is simply funny, but Labour MPs accuse her of promoting extremist content. The Conservatives say they were unaware of Bonehill‑Paine’s past and have not commented further.
