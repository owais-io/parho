After President Trump ordered a secret raid that captured Venezuelan President Maduro, thousands in Colombia marched to voice their anger. Protesters called Trump a threat, warned he might next target Colombia with military action, and shouted “Out with the Yanks!” They also blamed Trump’s actions on a drive to control Venezuela’s oil. Colombian President Gustavo Petro said he’d speak with Trump but urged vigilance. The protests spread to cities like Bogotá, Mexico City and Buenos Aires.
