Arsenal won the North London derby 4-1 against Tottenham, showing clear dominance. Eze, Trossard and Saka all scored, while Richarlison netted Tottenham’s only goal. The game featured standout performances from Eze’s skill and finishing, Trossard’s quality pass, Saka’s creativity, and solid defense from Saliba and Timber. Arsenal’s confidence grew, leaving Tottenham to regroup.
