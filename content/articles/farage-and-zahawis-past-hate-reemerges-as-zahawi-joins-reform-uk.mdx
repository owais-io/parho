Nigel Farage and former Conservative chancellor Nadhim Zahawi are now linked when Zahawi joined Reform UK. Their past tweets and comments show a long‑running feud. Zahawi called Farage “offensive” and anti‑British, while Farage called Zahawi “greasy pole” and a career climber. These old arguments raise questions about how well Zahawi will work with his new party leader. The feud could affect how voters see the new party and Zahawi's role.
