Doreen Lawrence, mother of murder victim Stephen Lawrence, is suing the Daily Mail publisher for using a private investigator to trick her into giving information. Former reporter Stephen Wright says the claims are false and that he never asked the investigator to deceive her. A court heard a recording where the investigator admitted she had bluffed her way to answers. Wright denies the allegations and says he was misrepresented by the lawsuit.
