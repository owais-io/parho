In February, President Trump asked the government to pull together and publish any documents about alien life and UFO sightings. The White House even registered domain names like aliens.gov, sparking jokes about a possible migrant registry. While the move has been praised by some who want more transparency, many are skeptical that any real evidence will surface. The promise remains a headline, but nothing concrete has been released yet.
