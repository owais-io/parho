Ben Stokes told England players they need to bring the same fighting spirit they used against India and show “dog” in Adelaide to keep their slim chances of winning the Ashes. He reminded them of the big crowds and hard games so far, said the team is ready and has changed a few players to fresh up. Stokes also said supporters should stay positive, and that the team has no doubt left.
