At the COP30 climate talks in Brazil, the conference president said richer nations should increase financial help and technology to aid poorer countries in moving away from fossil fuels. He argued the main split is over ending fossil‑fuel use, but countries can find middle ground by using existing money more efficiently. Brazil, an oil producer that also pushes for renewables, hopes to bring parties together.
