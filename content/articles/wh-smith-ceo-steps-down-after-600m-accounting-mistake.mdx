WH Smith's boss Carl Cowling has quit after an audit found big accounting mistakes in the U.S. business that made the company look more profitable than it was. The error cost about £600m in market value and sent shares down 42% in one day. A Deloitte report said the North American finance team had weak controls and overstated supplier income. WH Smith will now run its travel shops and look for a new permanent chief executive while rebuilding trust.
