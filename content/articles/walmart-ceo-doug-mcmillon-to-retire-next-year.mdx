Walmart’s long‑time boss, Doug McMillon, will retire in January 2025. John Furner, who rose from an hourly shift to president of Sam’s Club, will become global CEO. The move caused the stock to dip about 3%. Walmart employs 2.1 million people worldwide, had sales of $681 billion last year, and is worth around $800 billion. McMillon’s exit is part of a wave of leadership changes across U.S. retailers. He will stay on as an adviser until 2027.
