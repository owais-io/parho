Netanyahu has asked Israel’s president to grant him a pardon and stop his bribery trial, saying it would benefit the country. The president’s office received the request and will consult lawyers before deciding. Such a pardon before a conviction is almost unheard of, and Trump has also requested it. Critics say it could spark a political crisis, and opposition leaders vow to oppose the move.
