Workers are returning to UK offices in large numbers, pushing attendance to the highest level seen since before the pandemic. Banks like Goldman Sachs and JPMorgan Chase have set strict five‑day‑in‑office rules, but many staff still enjoy two days of remote work. Attendance rose to 44% in mid‑February, with big differences between cities such as Bristol, London and Glasgow. A petition against JPMorgan’s mandate shows ongoing employee pushback.
