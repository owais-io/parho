At a Medal of Honor ceremony, former President Donald Trump showed a red rash on his neck. White‑House doctors say it’s caused by a common skin‑care cream he’s been using as a preventative treatment for a week. The redness should fade after a few weeks. The report follows earlier sightings of bruised hands, drowsiness and a diagnosis of chronic venous insufficiency. Trump also mentioned taking extra aspirin and said his overall health is fine.
