Minnesota congresswoman Ilhan Omar criticized former President Donald Trump after he called Somali residents “garbage.” Omar said the insults were hateful and could spark violence. She also mentioned fraud cases involving some Somali charities in Minnesota and said the money didn’t go to terror groups. Omar warned Trump’s attacks are a distraction from his broken promises and called for investigations. She urged people to see the harmful impact of hate speech.
