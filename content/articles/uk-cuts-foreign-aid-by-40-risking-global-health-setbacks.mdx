The UK has slashed overseas aid by 40%, the steepest drop among G7 nations. Researchers warn the cuts could cause more than 22 million deaths in five years, especially for children under five. Aid to Africa will fall 56%, and climate aid will shrink 14%. While the government says it’s needed for defence spending, critics say it harms Britain’s reputation and reverses progress in child health and sanitation worldwide.
