Storm Leonardo has battered Spain and Portugal, killing two people, forcing over 7,000 people to leave homes, and flooding rivers. The heavy rain and winds have led the Portuguese government to keep a state of emergency until February. Some politicians, like far‑right leader André Ventura, want to postpone the second round of the presidential election, but officials say the vote will go ahead as planned.
