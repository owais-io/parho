Scott Bessent, the U.S. Treasury Secretary, made headlines at the World Economic Forum when he dismissed Denmark as “irrelevant” and defended President Trump’s push to claim Greenland. His blunt remarks, calling European allies’ opposition “hysteria,” drew ridicule on social media. The comments came as Trump threatens tariffs on countries opposed to the Greenland claim. Bessent’s outspoken style mirrors past clashes with figures like Elon Musk and his own ties to former financier George Soros.
