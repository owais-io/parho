Police have reopened the case of Morgan McSweeney’s stolen phone because they originally recorded the wrong address. McSweeney, a former senior adviser, lost a phone that may hold WhatsApp chats about Peter Mandelson’s ambassadorial appointment, which could be key to upcoming investigations. The error means the police will now re‑examine any CCTV or other evidence at the correct location. If the phone is never found, important messages may be lost.
