An autopsy shows the man who shot at Brown University and killed an MIT professor died by suicide on December 16, two days before his body was discovered in a Salem storage locker. Investigators linked him to a Rhode Island shooting that killed two students. They traced him through a Reddit tip, a rented car, and his past as a Brown student from Portugal. Portugal was surprised by the findings, and U.S. officials reacted by suspending the green‑card lottery.
