A letter to The Guardian says Palestinian activists who are hunger‑striking while in British custody are being treated cruelly, with little media coverage or political response. The writer is ashamed of the UK’s role in arms sales to the region and urges the government to show more respect and care for the prisoners. The piece also notes the lack of information about the risks of mistletoe, but the main focus is on the mistreatment of the strikers.
