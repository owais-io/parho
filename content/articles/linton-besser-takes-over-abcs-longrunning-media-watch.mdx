Linton Besser has been the new host of ABC’s long‑running show Media Watch for a year. He’s worked hard to keep the programme’s loyal tone while adding his own investigative style. With producer Mario Christodoulou, he’s tackled big stories, exposing misuse of power inside the ABC and other media scandals. The duo has already earned Walkley awards, and Besser says he keeps the show fair by being open and checking facts thoroughly.
