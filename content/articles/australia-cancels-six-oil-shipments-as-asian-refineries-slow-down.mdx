Australia has pulled six oil shipments that were set to arrive next month from Malaysia, Singapore and South Korea. Energy Minister Chris Bowen said the government is not looking at drastic steps yet, but it’s preparing for possible bumps in supply. He warned that Asian refineries may prioritize their own markets. Australia is holding extra fuel in reserves and is urging people to use less fuel. No rationing plans are in place.
