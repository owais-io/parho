In Dearborn, Michigan, a group of far‑right influencers staged an anti‑Islam protest, even trying to burn a Qur’an and shouting hateful slogans. City officials say the event was a cheap publicity stunt aimed at making money and clicks for the activists, who were also trying to promote a Florida Senate run. Dearborn, home to many Arab‑American residents, has faced repeated attacks from such groups. Local leaders urge residents to ignore the hate.
