The Trump administration has sued Harvard again, saying the university let anti‑Israel protests turn into antisemitic harassment of Jewish and Israeli students. The lawsuit claims Harvard violated civil‑rights laws and could lose billions of federal research dollars if it is found guilty. Harvard has counter‑sued, arguing the government is trying to control its academic freedom. The case could threaten funding for major research programs and affect the university’s independence.
