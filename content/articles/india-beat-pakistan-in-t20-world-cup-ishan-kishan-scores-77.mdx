India topped Pakistan in the T20 World Cup, winning by 61 runs. Opener Ishan Kishan made a brilliant 77 from 40 balls, driving India to a 176‑run target. Pakistan collapsed early, losing seven wickets before halfway and were all out for 114. Key wickets from Hardik Pandya and Jasprit Bumrah shut down the Pakistani batsmen. India now secures a spot in the Super Eights.
