GCHQ’s 2024 holiday card mixes a silly “robber” story with a set of seven puzzles that test math, logic and creativity. Students drew three winning pictures that appear in the card, and over 140,000 people downloaded it last year. The puzzles can be solved in teams and hide a final Christmas message. The card is free to download from GCHQ’s website, and answers will be posted on Dec 10.
