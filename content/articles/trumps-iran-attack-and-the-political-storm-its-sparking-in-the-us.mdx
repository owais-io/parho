Trump launched a military campaign against Iran, claiming he wants regime change, but there’s no clear evidence of an imminent threat. The war is tied to his plan to use a national emergency to push voter‑suppression measures before the midterm elections. Polls show low public support, and the conflict has already caused civilian casualties. Trump also pulled the U.S. out of the nuclear deal and has been deporting Iranian asylum seekers.
