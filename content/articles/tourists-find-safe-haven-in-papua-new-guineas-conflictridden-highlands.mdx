Ambua Lodge in Hela province shows tourists that even in a region known for tribal fighting, visitors can stay safely and enjoy wildlife and local culture. The hotel, run by the Melpa tribe, has welcomed thousands and is praised by the prime minister as a model of tourism. Nearby, Betty’s Place on the slopes of Mount Wilhelm offers adventure and authentic village life, but it still struggles with power and water. PNG wants more funding to grow this tourism boom.
