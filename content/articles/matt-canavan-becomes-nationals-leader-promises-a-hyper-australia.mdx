After Nationals leader David Littleproud resigned, Senator Matt Canavan was chosen as the party’s new head. A pro‑fossil‑fuel voice, he says the biggest threat to the Nationals is not the Greens or Labor but One Nation. Canavan pledges a “hyper Australia” – more jobs, more manufacturing, more Aussie‑style culture – but critics argue his plan is narrow and fuels climate worries. The piece mixes satire with serious politics.
