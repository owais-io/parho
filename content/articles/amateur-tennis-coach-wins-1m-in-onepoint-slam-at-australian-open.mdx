An amateur tennis coach from Sydney, Jordan Smith, beat top players in a new one‑point knockout tournament at the Australian Open and took a $1m prize. The event, called the One Point Slam, let pros serve only one ball and used rock‑paper‑scissors to decide who served each point. Smith’s win was a surprise and brought excitement to Melbourne Park, while the format got mixed reviews.
