Many workers are staying in their current roles because of economic worries and fears about layoffs and AI replacing jobs. A Monster.com study found almost half of employees are holding on longer than they might otherwise. While feeling safe is natural, clinging to a job can keep you from learning new skills or exploring better opportunities. Both employees and employers need to adapt or risk losing out in a changing market.
