Trump is building a short‑term family detention center at Alexandria airport in Louisiana. The site will hold migrant families and unaccompanied children for 3‑5 days before deportation, next to a runway used for mass deportations. Local officials say the lease is almost signed and the facility could open in 2‑3 months. Critics say the “voluntary” program is really a detention center, raising legal and health concerns.
