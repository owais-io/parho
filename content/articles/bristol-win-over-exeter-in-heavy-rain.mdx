Despite relentless rain, Bristol Rugby won 15‑13 against Exeter Chiefs, with Noah Heward scoring the only try. The match was a muddy, slow game that tested both teams’ resilience. Exeter struggled with bad kicks and could not play their key player Manny Feyi‑Waboso. Bristol’s defense was strong, keeping the score low. The win puts Bristol in third place and keeps them in playoff contention.
