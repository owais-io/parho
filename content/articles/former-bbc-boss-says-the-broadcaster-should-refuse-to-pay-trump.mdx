Donald Trump says he will sue the BBC for up to $5 billion after a Panorama documentary edited his speech in a way he says misrepresents him. Former BBC chief Tony Hall told BBC One he thinks the broadcaster should not pay Trump because the money comes from the licence fee and would be improper. The BBC has already apologized, pulled the clip, and is under investigation for similar editing errors.
