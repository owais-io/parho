Even after Iran turned the Hormuz Strait into a no‑go zone, Lloyd’s of London says it is still selling hull and cargo insurance for ships in the Gulf. War‑risk premiums have jumped to 1‑1.5 % of a vessel’s value, up from 0.25 % before the fighting. About 1,000 vessels are stuck on either side of the channel, and the UK and US are pushing to keep the route open and the prices fair.
