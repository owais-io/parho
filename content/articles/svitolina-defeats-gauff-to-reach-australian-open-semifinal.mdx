Elina Svitolina smashed Coco Gauff 6‑1, 6‑2 at the Australian Open, earning a semi‑final spot against world No 1 Aryna Sabalenka. The 31‑year‑old, who returned from maternity leave, is back in the top 10 for the first time since 2023. Gauff struggled with her serve and lost 26 unforced errors, eventually smashing her racket after the loss. Svitolina’s win marks a strong start to her 2026 season.
