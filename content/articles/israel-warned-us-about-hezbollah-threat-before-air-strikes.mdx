A leaked U.S. embassy cable shows that Israeli officials were already worried about Hezbollah’s growing military strength before launching air attacks in February. The memo says Israel doubted Lebanon could stop the group, and it also warned that Turkey was positioning forces in Syria that could threaten Israel. The cable was sent just a day before Israel and the U.S. struck Iran, after which Israel began its own raids on Hezbollah‑controlled areas.
