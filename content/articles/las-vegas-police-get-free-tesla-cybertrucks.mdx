Las Vegas police now have ten Tesla Cybertrucks, a donation from venture‑capitalists Ben and Felicia Horowitz. The trucks were fitted with police gear and cost about $2.7 million, but no taxpayer money was used. The move has sparked debate: some praise the modern look, while critics say the cars don’t improve public safety and raise concerns about the Tesla brand. Other U.S. cities have faced similar backlash.
