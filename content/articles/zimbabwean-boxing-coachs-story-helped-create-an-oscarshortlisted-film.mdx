Tobias Mupfuti was left homeless at eight in Victoria Falls. He learned boxing to protect himself, then started a free academy for kids. His life inspired the film *Rise*, which won 19 awards and made the Oscars shortlist. The movie stars Hollywood actor Tongayi Chirisa as Mupfuti. *Rise* shows how sport and school can pull children out of poverty, and it’s the first Zimbabwean film to reach the Oscars list.
