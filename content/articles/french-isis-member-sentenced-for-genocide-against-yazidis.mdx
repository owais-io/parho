A French ISIS fighter, Sabri Essid, was found guilty of genocide and crimes against humanity for killing, raping, and enslaving Yazidi women and children in Iraq. The Paris court used testimony from survivors and online chats that showed the buying and selling of Yazidis as slaves. This verdict is the first time a French ISIS member has been convicted for the Yazidi genocide, sending a strong message about justice and remembrance.
