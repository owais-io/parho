Rachel Reeves, the Labour chancellor, says the North Sea windfall tax should be removed but hasn’t acted on it yet. Union boss Gary Smith says the tax hurts jobs and slows Britain’s shift to clean energy. The tax, introduced after Russia’s invasion of Ukraine, is very high, making UK oil and gas uncompetitive. Reeves talks about a new scheme that would replace the tax when prices rise again. Industry wants clearer rules.
