Chelsea replaced Enzo Maresca with Liam Rosenior, who left Strasbourg where he had been coach. The 41‑year‑old signed a contract that runs to 2032. BlueCo, which owns both clubs, says Rosenior will keep Chelsea’s winning spirit alive. The move has sparked anger from Strasbourg supporters, who feel their club is being used as a feeder for the London club. Despite the backlash, Chelsea say the new coach will protect the club’s identity and win trophies.
