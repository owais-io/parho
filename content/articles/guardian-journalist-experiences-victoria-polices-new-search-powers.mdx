Guardian journalist Lisa Favazzo made a video about Victoria police's new search powers. Later, at a Sunday protest, police used those powers on her. Two officers stopped her, explained the rules, and searched her gear without a warrant. She was allowed to show her equipment, the search was quick, and only one knuckle duster was found. She says the experience was awkward but harmless.
