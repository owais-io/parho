An ICE agent shot and killed Alex Pretti, a nurse, in Minneapolis while he was trying to protect a woman from pepper‑spraying border patrol officers. Video of the incident spread quickly, sparking outrage across the political spectrum. Congress and state leaders condemned the shooting, and the White House softened its tone after a brief attempt to blame the victim. The event has intensified calls for accountability within the Trump administration and raised fears of authoritarian drift.
