Brooke Day, a 22‑year‑old Australian snowboarder, died after her backpack got caught on a ski lift at Tsugaike Mountain in Japan. The incident occurred while she was leaving the lift; the strap on her avalanche‑rescue pack tangled, pulling her into the air and causing a cardiac arrest. Family said she was a “safe one” among friends and a talented football player. The resort is cooperating with investigations and pledges safety improvements.
