The US Southern Command said it struck three drug‑trafficking boats in the eastern Pacific, killing eight people. Footage of the explosions was posted on social media. The hits are part of a broader effort that has already taken down more than 20 vessels and killed around 90 suspected smugglers. Some experts say the attacks may violate international law, while the Pentagon insists they are legal.
