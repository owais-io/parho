Sir Alex Ferguson says Manchester United might have to wait ten years for a league title. New head coach Ruben Amorim disagrees, saying the wait will be shorter and that players need to stop feeling entitled. He also talked about using younger players and said a recent t‑shirt controversy involving a teammate’s family member wouldn’t affect squad selection. The article also touches on a former player’s comments and a captain’s claim about the club hierarchy.
