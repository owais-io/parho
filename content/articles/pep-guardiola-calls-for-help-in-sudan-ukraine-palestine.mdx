Pep Guardiola, manager of Manchester City, has publicly condemned killings in Sudan, Ukraine and Palestine. He pointed out that a UAE‑backed paramilitary group in Sudan has caused over 150,000 deaths and called for help for civilians. Guardiola, whose club owner is a UAE prince, spoke about protecting children and urged people to act, saying we must stop letting people die. He highlighted war crimes worldwide.
