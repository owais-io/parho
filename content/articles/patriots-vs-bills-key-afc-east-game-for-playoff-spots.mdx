Patriots and Bills face off in a crucial AFC East game that could decide playoff positions. The Bills (9‑4) need a win to keep their playoff hopes alive, while the Patriots (11‑2) aim to stay top of the division. Bills have struggled with turnovers, especially against the Patriots’ strong pass defense, but their defense has improved. Patriots must stop the Bills’ running game, as they’ve been effective on the ground.
