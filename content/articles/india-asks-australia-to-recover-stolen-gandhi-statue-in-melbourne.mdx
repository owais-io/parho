The Indian government condemned the theft of a bronze statue of Mahatma Gandhi from a Melbourne community centre and urged Australian police to find the thieves. Victoria police think three people used an angle grinder to cut the statue. The statue, donated in 2021, has been vandalised before. Australian leaders say they support the Indian community and will help recover the statue.
