South Korea’s 17‑year‑old Choi Gaon stole the gold in the women’s Olympic halfpipe, beating American star Chloe Kim who finished silver. Kim, who had won the two previous Games, battled a shoulder injury and still scored a high opening run, but Choi’s third run after a fall earned the highest score and a new Olympic record. The win marks a generational shift as younger riders dominate the sport.
