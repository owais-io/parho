The U.S. and Israel began striking Iran after learning Israel was about to attack, saying the move was needed to protect U.S. forces. U.S. officials give different reasons, from stopping Iranian missiles to preventing a nuclear program. President Trump and others have framed it as a fight to destroy Iran’s military capabilities. The U.S. urges its citizens to leave Middle‑East countries and warns that the war could last longer than expected, with many casualties already reported.
