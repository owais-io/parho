Keir Starmer is finalising a list of 25 new Labour peers for the House of Lords, including former staffers and union leaders, to strengthen the party’s position. The announcement will come before Christmas. The move is part of Labour’s effort to rebalance the Lords against the Conservatives, who are also adding peers. Reform UK will receive none. Key names include Katie Martin and former union boss Paddy Lillis.
