UK officials are pausing a deal that would let a British firm ship high‑tech equipment to an Armenian company after a Guardian report linked the client to Russia’s war machine. The equipment can make carbon‑fibre material used in missiles and drones. Because of the links, ministers are re‑examining the export licence and have put the shipment on hold. No goods have left the UK yet.
