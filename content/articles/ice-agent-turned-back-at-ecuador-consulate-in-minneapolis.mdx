An ICE agent tried to walk into the Ecuadorian consulate in Minneapolis, but a staff member stopped him and told him he couldn’t enter. The embassy in Quito was informed, and the Ecuadorian foreign ministry filed a formal protest. The incident comes as ICE has been criticized for aggressive raids in Minnesota, and lawmakers say it violates diplomatic rules. Officials said the action could endanger U.S. diplomats abroad.
