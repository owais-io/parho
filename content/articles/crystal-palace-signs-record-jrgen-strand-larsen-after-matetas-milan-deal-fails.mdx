Crystal Palace signed Norway striker Jørgen Strand Larsen for a club‑record £48 million after the deadline‑day move of striker Jean‑Philippe Mateta to Milan collapsed because of a knee problem. The new striker will battle Mateta for a starting spot, as Palace have already spent more than £90 million this month. The team has won none of its last 12 games, and manager Oliver Glasner plans to leave next summer.
