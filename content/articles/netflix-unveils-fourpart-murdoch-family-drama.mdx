Netflix’s new four‑part docuseries, 'Dynasty: The Murdochs', drops March 13. It looks at Rupert Murdoch’s rise and the long‑running family fight over his media empire. Using documents, emails and texts, the series follows the court battle and settlement that keep Lachlan Murdoch in control until 2050. Interviews with journalists, former network staff and media commentators add context, covering Fox News’s growth and the News of the World hacking scandal. Fans of media dramas and ‘Succession’ will find it compelling.
