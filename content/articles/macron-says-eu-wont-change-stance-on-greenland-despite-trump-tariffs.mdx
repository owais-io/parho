Trump announced 10‑% tariffs on eight European countries from February and a further 25‑% from June if they oppose his plan to buy Greenland. French President Emmanuel Macron, Sweden’s prime minister and Norway’s leader all said no intimidation will change Europe’s stance. EU heads warned the tariffs would hurt transatlantic ties and could force the EU to pause the US‑EU trade deal. An emergency EU meeting is planned.
