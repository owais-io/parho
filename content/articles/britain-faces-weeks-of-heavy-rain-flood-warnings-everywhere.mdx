Britain is stuck in a rain storm that could last for weeks, with the Environment Agency warning 85 places in England that flooding is likely and 245 places that it could happen. The warnings also cover parts of Wales, Scotland and northern England. The Met Office says showers will continue into Wednesday, with heavy rain in many areas and only brief dry spells. There’s no sign of the weather changing soon.
