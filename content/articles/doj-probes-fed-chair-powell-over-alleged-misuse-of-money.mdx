The Department of Justice has opened a probe into Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell over alleged misuse of public money for a $2.5 billion renovation of the Fed’s Washington office. Powell says the investigation is a political attack for his refusal to lower interest rates, and he defended the Fed’s independence. Trump has been pushing the Fed to cut rates, while economists warn that politicising the bank could spark high inflation. Former Fed leaders and some senators have condemned the probe.
