Last weekend’s Premier League action gave fans a mix of drama and surprises. Chelsea and Arsenal drew 1‑1 after Chelsea’s Moisés Caicedo was sent off, but the Blues held on for a point. United’s set‑piece attack paid off, while Liverpool beat West Ham 2‑0 after a red card for Lucas Paquetá. Forest beat Brighton, Villa edged Wolves, City beat Leeds 3‑2, and Newcastle knocked out Everton 4‑1. Brentford also edged Burnley and Fulham beat Tottenham 2‑1.
