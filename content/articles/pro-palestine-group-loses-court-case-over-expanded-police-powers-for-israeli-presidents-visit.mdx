The Palestine Action Group lost a court case that challenged the NSW government's decision to give police extra powers for Israeli President Isaac Herzog’s visit. The court said the powers were needed to keep the president and the public safe. The powers let police search people, move crowds, and close places. Protesters who planned to march could still face fines if they break the rules.
