England won a T20 World Cup match against Sri Lanka, beating them by 51 runs. The English team scored 146 but struggled against spin, with few good innings from their top batsmen. Sri Lanka fell apart after a shaky start, losing all ten wickets for 95 runs. England’s Will Jacks was the star, taking a hat‑trick to finish the innings. England now move closer to the semi‑finals.
