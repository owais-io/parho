U.S. congresswoman Ilhan Omar says that Donald Trump’s harsh words and de‑humanising attacks are pushing people toward violence. Omar points out that threats to her life have risen whenever Trump speaks badly about her, and that the president’s rhetoric fuels a broader climate of hate. She notes that the number of threats against lawmakers has doubled in 2024. Omar warns that this shows how fragile our democracy is and calls for stronger protections.
