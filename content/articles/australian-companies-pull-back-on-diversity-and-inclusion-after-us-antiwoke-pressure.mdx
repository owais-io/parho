Recent research shows about 10% of Australian business leaders are scaling back or dropping diversity and inclusion programs after a wave of US anti‑woke sentiment. Some firms have even ended their membership with the national inclusion group, Pride in Diversity. While a few companies soften language or rename programs to avoid backlash, many still insist on staying committed to diversity, saying it’s vital for client success.
