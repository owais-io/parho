A group of readers write about how cricket in England has become an elitist sport, with high costs and limited TV coverage that drives away ordinary fans. They compare this to Australia, where cricket is a public pastime enjoyed by people of all backgrounds. The letters warn that without making the game more accessible, England’s national team and the sport itself could fade away.
