Trump is inviting the presidents of about ten Latin American countries—most of them right‑wing—to a summit in Miami. The meeting will focus on security, migration and counter‑drug efforts, and it shows Trump’s push to keep China out of the region. He’s leaving out left‑leaning leaders from Brazil, Mexico and Colombia, which has caused worry in those countries but they’re still attending to keep ties with the US.
