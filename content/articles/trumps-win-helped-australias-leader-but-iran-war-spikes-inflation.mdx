Trump’s re‑election helped Australian PM Anthony Albanese win the election, but the US’s war on Iran has pushed oil prices to a four‑year high. Higher oil costs fuel global inflation, prompting central banks – including Australia’s – to consider rate hikes. This raises living costs, hurts the Labor government’s second term, and could push voters toward populist alternatives.
