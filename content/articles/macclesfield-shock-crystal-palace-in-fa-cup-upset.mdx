Macclesfield, a semi‑pro phoenix club, stunned Crystal Palace by winning 1‑0 in the FA Cup fourth round. The match, held at Moss Rose, was a shock because Palace are 117 places higher in the league. Macclesfield’s victory was a historic moment, with fans celebrating late into the night. In the aftermath, the club remembered young player Ethan McLeod, who passed away last month, and the team praised the win.
