The US has banned travel visas for five Europeans who fight online hate and disinformation. Washington says the ban pushes back on European pressure on tech firms, while EU leaders, including France’s Macron, call it intimidation and warn of a quick EU response. The clash shows rising tension over digital rules and censorship between the US and Europe.
