Florida Governor Ron DeSantis is close to leaving office, but his final term is packed with new laws that many say could create a police state. Bills would let state officials label any group as a “terrorist,” set up a new counter‑terrorism unit that could spy on protesters, and block funding for schools that teach diversity. Civil‑rights groups say these rules would curb free speech and concentrate power in the hands of a few.
