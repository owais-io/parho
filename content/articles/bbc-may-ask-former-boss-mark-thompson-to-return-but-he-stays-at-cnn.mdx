After the BBC’s chief stepped down, the network is looking for a new leader. Report says former director general Mark Thompson has been quietly asked to return, but he says he will stay at CNN where he is leading a big modernization effort. Thompson left the BBC in 2012 and now heads CNN’s shift toward a subscription‑based model. Other candidates include Jay Hunt and Charlotte Moore.
