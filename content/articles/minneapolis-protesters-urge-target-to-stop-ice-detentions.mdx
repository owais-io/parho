Minneapolis activists staged a sit‑in at Target’s downtown headquarters after ICE agents violently detained two employees in a nearby store. They want the retailer to speak out against the agency and stop allowing ICE to enter without a warrant. The protest follows a long history of boycotts over Target’s DEI policies and sales decline. Local faith leaders, immigrant groups and activists are demanding concrete actions from the company.
