Border‑patrol agents shot nurse Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, sparking a fight over the incident’s story. White House adviser Stephen Miller called Pretti an “assassin,” a claim Trump denied and later apologized for. Miller was left out of a key Oval Office meeting and admitted the agents may not have followed protocol. Trump removed the local commander and sent a new leader, but is unlikely to dismiss Miller.
