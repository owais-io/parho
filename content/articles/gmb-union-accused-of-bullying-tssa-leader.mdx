Maryam Eslamdoust, the first female head of the TSSA transport union, says GMB union officials bullied her to try to oust her and weaken her union. She claims the GMB’s actions caused severe stress, that a male leader would have been treated differently, and that the GMB is trying to take over the smaller TSSA. The GMB denies the accusations and says it is working to fix its own culture after past sexism findings.
