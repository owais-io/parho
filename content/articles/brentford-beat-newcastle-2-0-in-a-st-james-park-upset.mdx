Brentford won 2‑0 at St James’ Park, ending Newcastle’s long winless run. Dango Ouattara scored first, then Keane Lewis‑Potter finished the game. Newcastle’s manager Eddie Howe had a tough match, with his team losing four of the last five games. The win keeps Brentford near the top of the table and dampens Newcastle’s hopes of a Champions League spot this season.
