Chelsea came back from behind to beat Wolfsburg 2‑1, earning a direct place in the Women’s Champions League quarter‑finals. Sam Kerr’s header gave them the win and they avoided a February playoff. In the same match, Manchester United defeated Juventus 1‑0, thanks to Jess Park’s first‑half goal, moving up to a top seed for the playoffs. Both clubs finish the group stage with strong results, setting up next‑round fixtures.
