A new movement called “Stealing Isn’t Innovation” has 800+ creators—actors, writers, musicians—joining together to tell AI firms like OpenAI that they can’t use their work without permission or payment. The campaign says that using copyrighted material from the internet without a license is theft. Some companies already license content, but many creators want fair deals and more transparency over how AI uses their art.
