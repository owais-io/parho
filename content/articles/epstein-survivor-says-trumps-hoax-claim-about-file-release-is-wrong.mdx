A former teenage victim of Jeffrey Epstein, Danielle Bensky, slammed President Donald Trump for calling the push to release Epstein’s files a “hoax.” She said Trump’s long social‑media rant turned a chance for justice into a political attack, hurting survivors’ hard‑won fight. Bensky, who was forced into abuse at 17, now teaches dance and wants the full files released to expose those who enabled Epstein and protect others. She and other survivors marched at the Capitol, holding photos of themselves to show their story.
