BP’s profits are falling as oil prices hit their lowest in five years, and shareholders want a clear plan for the future. The new chief executive will need to explain how the company will cut costs and shift away from fossil fuels, after activist investors pushed a resolution demanding a strategy for a world with less oil demand. BP’s latest projects and analyst optimism add pressure to the debate.
