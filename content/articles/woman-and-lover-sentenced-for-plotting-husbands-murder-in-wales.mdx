A 46‑year‑old woman, Michelle Mills, and her 47‑year‑old lover, Geraint Berry, plotted to kill her husband in Wales. They tried to murder Christopher Mills in a caravan, but he survived. Both were found guilty and each got 19 years in prison, plus extra time for other charges. A third man, Steven Thomas, received 12 months for helping. The case shows how dangerous secret affairs can become.
