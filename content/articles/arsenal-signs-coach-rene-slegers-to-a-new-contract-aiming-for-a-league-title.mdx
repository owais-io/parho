Arsenal has renewed head‑coach Renée Slegers’ contract for three and a half years, hoping to end the club’s decade‑long wait for a Women’s Super League crown. Slegers guided the team to a Champions League win last season and now faces pressure to lift the domestic title. The club is also hiring Jodie Taylor as its first women’s technical director to help rebuild the squad for the future. Fans expect the move to boost Arsenal’s chances against rivals like Manchester City and United.
