The Guardian has issued a correction about a previously published article that claimed the Drax power plant near Selby supplied 10% of Britain’s electricity in 2024. The actual output of the Selby site is only 5.3%, while the 10% figure refers to all of Drax’s plants combined. The notice also lists other recent corrections, but the main focus is the misreported percentage.
