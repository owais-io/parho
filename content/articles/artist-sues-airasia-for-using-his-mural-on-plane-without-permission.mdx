Ernest Zacharevic, a Lithuanian‑born artist living in Penang, sued Malaysia’s low‑cost carrier AirAsia in Kuala Lumpur’s high court. He says the airline put his 2012 street mural “Children on a Bicycle” on a plane and in ads without asking him. AirAsia admitted the mistake and pulled the image in December 2024, but has used his work in other campaigns. Zacharevic is seeking copyright and moral‑rights compensation.
