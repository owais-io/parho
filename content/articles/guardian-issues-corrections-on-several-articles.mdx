The Guardian apologises for a handful of mistakes in its recent print pieces. It clarified that hockey player William Nylander is Swedish, not American, and added a missing photo credit for Kate Peters. It corrected a mix‑up about the Tree Council picking the Bramley apple tree for a jubilee and noted the jubilee actually took place in 2002, not 2022. Other stories—ranging from the World Economic Forum to Queensland bike‑ride rules—were also amended.
