Katie McCabe, Arsenal’s long‑time left‑back, will leave the club when her contract ends next summer because Arsenal are looking to bring in younger players. The 30‑year‑old won multiple titles with Arsenal, including a Champions League trophy, and wants to keep playing at the top level. Arsenal may sign Barcelona’s Ona Batlle, who is four years younger. The move would mean a big change for the club and fans.
