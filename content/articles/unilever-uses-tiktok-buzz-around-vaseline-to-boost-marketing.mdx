Unilever is riding the TikTok craze that has turned Vaseline into a “life‑hack” hit. By listening to what people post online, the company is spending more of its ad money on creators instead of TV spots. Scientists test the hacks and share results, while marketers amplify the videos. The shift shows younger shoppers trust influencers more than traditional ads, though TV still matters for big events like the Super Bowl.
