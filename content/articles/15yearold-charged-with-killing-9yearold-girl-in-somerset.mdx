A 15‑year‑old boy is in court in Bristol charged with murdering nine‑year‑old Aria Thorpe in Weston‑super‑Mare. Aria was found stabbed to death on Lime Close. The boy was arrested in nearby Worle and is now in youth detention while the case moves to crown court. Police, schools and local councils are offering support to the grieving family and the community. Police are continuing to investigate, and community members are asking for privacy.
