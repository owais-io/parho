Ivory Coast and Cameroon, the defending champions, played a 1‑1 draw in their Group F clash at the Stade de Marrakech. Amad Diallo scored first for Ivory Coast, but Cameroon’s Tchamadeu equalised later. Both teams had already won their opening games, so the draw keeps them in the lead while still guaranteeing both sides a spot in the last‑16. The game was a tense, back‑and‑forth affair that finished in a level score.
