Ella Brockway, a journalist who loves finding hidden details, has joined The Guardian as a soccer correspondent in Washington, D.C., to cover the sport ahead of the 2026 World Cup. She explains how she treats sports stories like treasure hunts, hunting for interesting facts inside box scores, player habits, and locker‑room books. Her new role comes alongside two other hires, Pablo Iglesias Maurer and Jeff Rueter, who also cover U.S. soccer.
