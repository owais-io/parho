Ruben Amorim, the new Manchester United coach, said the team could switch to a four‑man defense instead of the 3‑4‑3 he’s used in all 59 games. He used that system in the 4‑4 draw with Bournemouth, but said a back four is ready if the game needs it. He also talked about adding new players, like defender Antoine Semenyo and midfielders Rúben Neves, Conor Gallagher, Tyler Adams, to strengthen the squad for the future.
