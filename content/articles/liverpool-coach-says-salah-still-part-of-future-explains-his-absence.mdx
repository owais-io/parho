Liverpool manager Arne Slot said the decision to leave Mohamed Salah out of the 2‑0 win over West Ham was a tactical choice, not a sign that the player is being dropped. Slot explained the tight schedule and a different defensive set‑up as reasons for the change, but praised Salah as a special player who will remain at the club. He also thanked fans for chanting his name after the win.
