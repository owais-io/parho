Liverpool are reportedly looking to sign Dutch defender Micky van de Ven from Tottenham, with a summer move likely. Leeds are eyeing Irish striker Troy Parrott from AZ for about £20 m, which could prompt a move for Joël Piroe to Celtic. Spurs want Curtis Jones from Liverpool, while other clubs like Arsenal, Juventus, and Barcelona are sniffing around players such as Federico Chiesa, Gabriel Jesus and Marc‑André ter Stegen. The transfer window is heating up across the Premier League.
