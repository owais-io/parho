Angus Taylor has challenged Sussan Ley to become the leader of Australia’s Liberal Party, and the choice of deputy will follow. Candidates include veteran senator Jane Hume, moderate Tim Wilson, young Zoe McKenzie, shadow minister Melissa McIntosh, veteran MP Dan Tehan, WA's Melissa Price, and Queensland's Ted O’Brien. The decision will shape the party’s leadership ahead of the next election.
