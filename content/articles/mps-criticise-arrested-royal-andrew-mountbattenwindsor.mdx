In a rare moment of open criticism, MPs in the House of Commons spoke out about Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor, a royal who has been arrested over alleged misconduct as a trade envoy. They called him rude, arrogant and self‑interested, saying his behaviour harmed Britain’s reputation. The debate also highlighted limits on questioning royals and raised concerns about how women’s safety is handled in government.
