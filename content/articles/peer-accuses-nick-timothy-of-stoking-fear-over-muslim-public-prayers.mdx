Conservative peer Tariq Ahmad says Shadow Justice Secretary Nick Timothy is stoking fear by calling Muslim public prayers un‑British and intimidating. Ahmad, a former counter‑extremism minister, said the comments hurt Muslims and threaten the party’s inclusive values. He urged the party to act, while supporters like Kemi Badenoch backed Timothy. The debate highlights growing tensions over Muslim worship in public spaces in the UK.
