After an ICE officer shot and killed a 37‑year‑old U.S. citizen in Minneapolis, Democrats on Capitol Hill slammed the incident and called for a full investigation. They are demanding limits on ICE’s power—like requiring warrants and banning mask‑wearing during arrests—and threatening to withhold the next DHS budget if changes aren’t made. The move could spark a government shutdown and has lawmakers debating how to hold the agency accountable.
