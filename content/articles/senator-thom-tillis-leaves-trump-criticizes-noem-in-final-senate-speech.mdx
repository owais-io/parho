Senator Thom Tillis, a Republican from North Carolina, has decided not to run again and will use his final months in the Senate to call out the Trump administration. He blasted Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem over immigration mishaps and criticized White House aide Stephen Miller. Tillis says he wants to focus on results, not fundraising, and hopes his criticism will protect voters in a swing state.
