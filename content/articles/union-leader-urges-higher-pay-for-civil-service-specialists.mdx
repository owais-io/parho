Union leader Mike Clancy says the UK government must stop capping civil service pay, or it will lose top technical talent. He argues that specialists need salaries that beat the private sector, even higher than the prime minister’s. Clancy also warns that weak funding, not rules, is the main cause of delays in environmental agencies. He urges fair pay and better resource allocation.
