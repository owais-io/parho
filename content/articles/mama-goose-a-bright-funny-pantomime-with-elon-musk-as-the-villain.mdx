‘Mama Goose’ is a bright, silly pantomime that puts Elon Musk as the villain. The story follows a hero team who hijacks a SpaceX rocket to save a goose named Gary who’s been taken by a fairy’s rival. The show mixes laughs, songs, and colorful sets, with lively actors like Duane Gooden and Ché Walker. The music and design sparkle, making it a festive, enjoyable theatre experience.
