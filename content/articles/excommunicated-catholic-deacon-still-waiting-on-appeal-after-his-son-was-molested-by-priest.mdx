A Louisiana deacon, Scott Peyton, quit after a priest molested his son. The bishop excommunicated him, and he appealed to the Vatican. More than a year later, no decision has been announced, and Peyton wants an update. He also seeks information about the molester’s status. The case highlights ongoing problems with clerical abuse and how the Catholic Church handles them.
