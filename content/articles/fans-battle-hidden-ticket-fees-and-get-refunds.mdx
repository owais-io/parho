Fans buying tickets for shows like Gorillaz and Pitbull have been hit with extra fees, such as a £1 restoration charge and higher booking fees. Gigantic, the ticket seller, admitted an error and refunded the difference. Many fans don’t know what the fees cover and only a third see the full cost before buying. The UK regulator is investigating so‑called drip pricing on event tickets, hoping to make prices clearer.
