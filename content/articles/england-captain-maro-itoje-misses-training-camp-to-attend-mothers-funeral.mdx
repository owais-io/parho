England’s Six Nations squad starts training in Spain without captain Maro Itoje, who is in Nigeria for his mother’s funeral. Coach Steve Borthwick has given him leave, and the team is preparing for the opening match against Wales. While Itoje is away, the side also deals with injuries across other positions, but they remain focused on getting ready for the tournament.
