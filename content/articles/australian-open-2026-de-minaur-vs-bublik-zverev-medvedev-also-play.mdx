At the 2026 Australian Open, Australian Alex De Minaur played a hard‑fought match against Kazakhstani Alexander Bublik, keeping his spot in the fourth round. In the men’s draw, Daniil Medvedev was upset by American Tien, losing the second set 0‑6. Alexander Zverev will face the winner of that tie, while world No 1 Carlos Alcaraz advanced to the quarter‑finals after beating Tommy Paul. The tournament remains full of drama.
