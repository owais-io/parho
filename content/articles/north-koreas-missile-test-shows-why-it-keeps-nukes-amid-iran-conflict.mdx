North Korea recently fired a missile from a warship, using the test to prove it’s building nuclear weapons for survival. The launch came as U.S. and Israeli airstrikes hit Iran, a country Trump warned might soon have nukes. Analysts say the Middle East war reminds Kim Jong‑un that nuclear power keeps his regime safe, and could either make talks harder or give him leverage if the U.S. softens its stance.
