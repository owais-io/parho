London Spirit is using money from a recent 49% sale to triply increase salaries for coaches, analysts and other support staff for the new Hundred competition. They believe better back‑room teams will lift the struggling men’s side and keep the women’s success. Director Mo Bobat says the budget jump lets them hire top talent from places like the IPL, and new players and a fresh logo have been unveiled.
