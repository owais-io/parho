Europe is sending emergency generators to Ukraine to keep homes warm during winter as Russia keeps attacking power lines. The EU has shipped 447 generators from its reserves, while Poland is adding more from its own stocks. Meanwhile Moscow still says Ukraine must pull back from the Donbas region, and a three‑way meeting in Abu Dhabi is set to try to end the war. The support shows Europe’s push to help civilians.
