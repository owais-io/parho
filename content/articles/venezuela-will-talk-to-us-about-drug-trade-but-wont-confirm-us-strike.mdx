Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro said his country is ready to negotiate a deal with the U.S. to fight drug trafficking, but he refused to comment on a reported U.S. drone strike on a Venezuelan docking area that Donald Trump said was used by cartels. Maduro claims the U.S. is trying to change Venezuela’s government and gain oil access. He says Venezuela can welcome U.S. oil investment and wants serious talks based on facts.
