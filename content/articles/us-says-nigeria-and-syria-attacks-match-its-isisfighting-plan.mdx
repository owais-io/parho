Republican Rep. Mike Turner says U.S. strikes in Nigeria and Syria match the country’s long‑running plan to defeat the Islamic State, a strategy that began during the Trump era. He argues the attacks show no new approach to force. Turner noted the Pentagon’s missile strike on ISIS camps in Nigeria came just before Trump called it a “Christmas present” for the group. He added the U.S. will keep fighting IS worldwide and cannot back Russia in Ukraine.
