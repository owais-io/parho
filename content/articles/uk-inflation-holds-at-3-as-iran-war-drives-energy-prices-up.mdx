UK inflation stayed at 3% in February, still above the 2% target, as the Iran war pushes up global energy prices. Food prices fell a touch, but experts warn this could change if the conflict drags on. The Bank of England kept rates steady, while the government offers energy bill cuts and food‑price support ahead. Core inflation rose to 3.2%.
