Over the weekend the Premier League saw some surprising results. Aston Villa beat Manchester United 2‑1 in a tight game, but United’s new coach Ruben Amorim still can’t turn wins into points. Everton lost to Arsenal 1‑0, while City comfortably beat West Ham 3‑0. Tottenham edged Liverpool 2‑1, and Newcastle drew 2‑2 with Chelsea after a controversial refereeing decision. These matches show the league’s unpredictability and growing pressure on managers.
