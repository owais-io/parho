Jimmy Lai, a 78‑year‑old Hong Kong activist who was sentenced to 20 years for sedition, said he will not appeal. The decision ends years of court battles and could open talks for his release. Western leaders, the UN and the UK have called the case politically motivated and urged China to free him. The move may start diplomatic negotiations about Lai’s freedom.
