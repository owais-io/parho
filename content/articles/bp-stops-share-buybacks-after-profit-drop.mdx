BP has stopped buying back its own shares after its 2025 profits fell below $7.5 bn, down from almost $9 bn last year. The move comes as new boss Meg O’Neill takes over and the company turns away from green projects, writing down its renewables business. Share prices slid 6 % and investors now want a clearer plan to keep cash flowing while the oil market weakens.
