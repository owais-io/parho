Gov. Gavin Newsom has hired former CDC chief Susan Monarez and former CDC medical officer Debra Houry to run California’s new Public Health Network Innovation Exchange (PHNIX). The pair left the federal agency after clashes with the Trump administration. PHNIX aims to upgrade the state’s health tech, data and funding systems and to counter the public‑health cuts made in Washington. Newsom’s move signals his pushback against Trump’s policies and strengthens California’s health‑care leadership.
