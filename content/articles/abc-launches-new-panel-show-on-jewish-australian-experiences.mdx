ABC has started a new weekly panel program called ABC National Forum, hosted by David Speers. The first episode will focus on Jewish Australians and the impact of the Bondi terrorist attack. The show aims to bring people together, sharing stories and ideas in a live studio audience, continuing the ABC’s tradition of town‑hall style debate after the long‑running Q+A ended. It’s part of the broadcaster’s effort to keep Australians talking and understanding each other.
