Newcastle United beat Aston Villa in the FA Cup fifth round after a game full of controversial calls. Villa were reduced to 10 men, a red card was shown, and there was a lot of debate about VAR use. Newcastle’s midfielder Sandro Tonali scored two goals, while Nick Woltemade added a late winner. Referee Chris Kavanagh and his assistants made several questionable decisions that angered both teams.
