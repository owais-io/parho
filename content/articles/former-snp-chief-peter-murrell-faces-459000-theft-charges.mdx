Peter Murrell, who ran the Scottish National Party for 22 years, is charged with stealing £459,046.49 of party money from 2010 to 2023. He allegedly used the cash to buy luxury goods, a motorhome, two cars, cosmetics, and even paid a parking ticket. Murrell will appear at Glasgow's high court next Friday. He was arrested in 2023 in a police probe into SNP finances; former leader Nicola Sturgeon was also questioned but cleared.
