Microsoft filed a supportive legal brief in a federal court in San Francisco to back Anthropic’s lawsuit against the Pentagon. Anthropic argues it shouldn’t be called a supply‑chain risk after talks fell through over a $200m contract to use its AI on classified systems. The company says it refuses to support mass surveillance or lethal autonomous weapons. Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Apple and OpenAI signed the brief, saying the ban could hurt AI suppliers and the government’s tech needs.
