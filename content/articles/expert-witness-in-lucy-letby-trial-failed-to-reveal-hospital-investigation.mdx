Cheshire police said they were not told that expert Prof Peter Hindmarsh was under investigation for serious concerns about his work before he testified in the Lucy Letby murder trial. Hindmarsh gave key evidence that Letby injected insulin into babies’ fluid bags. He only disclosed the hospital investigation and the termination of his contract in December 2022, after he had already given evidence. The prosecution and police are now reviewing the case.
