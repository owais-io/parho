Australia’s top aerials team almost won a medal at the Winter Olympics. Danielle Scott and teammates Abbey Willcox and Reilly Flanagan qualified third for the mixed‑team final, but a snow‑delayed event let them finish fourth, 23 points off the podium. 16‑year‑old Indra Brown’s half‑pipe final was postponed, and the team missed a chance for a seventh medal. Despite strong runs, the Australians were edged out by the US, Switzerland and China.
