A big fire in Victoria’s Otway National Park is spreading fast, and officials are telling residents of more than 1,000 homes to leave before sunset. The blaze, combined with a hot heatwave and strong winds, could reach towns like Lorne. Six major fires are burning across the state, and firefighters say conditions are extreme. The government has closed visitor sites and issued a statewide fire ban.
