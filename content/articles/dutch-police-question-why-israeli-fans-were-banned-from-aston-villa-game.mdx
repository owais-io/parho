Dutch police have said the intelligence that led to the ban of Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from an Aston Villa game in Birmingham was inaccurate. They disputed claims that the fans had thrown people into a river and that 5,000 officers were needed. A HMIC inquiry will release its findings this week, which could put West Midlands police chief under pressure. The ban followed a spike in antisemitism after a Manchester synagogue attack.
