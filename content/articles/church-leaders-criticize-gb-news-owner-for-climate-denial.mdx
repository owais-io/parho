A group of more than 100 church leaders, including former and current bishops, wrote to Paul Marshall, co‑owner of GB News, saying his comments on climate science and the channel’s attacks were misleading. They pointed out that Marshall’s hedge fund holds huge fossil‑fuel investments and asked him to disclose any conflicts. Marshall said he believes the planet is warming but disputes that human activity is the main cause and opposed the UK’s net‑zero policy.
