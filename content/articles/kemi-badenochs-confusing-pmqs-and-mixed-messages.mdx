Kemi Badenoch, a Conservative MP, has been mocked for her confusing answers during Prime Minister’s Questions (PMQs). Critics say she mixed up her positions on petrol prices, the UK’s role in wars, and even joked about managing Tottenham Hotspur. Her comments about supporting wars and then later saying she was backing the PM’s cautious stance left voters scratching heads. The article satirises her lack of clarity and the media’s reaction to her blunders.
