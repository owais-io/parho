In Los Angeles, a 20‑year‑old woman is the first witness in a big lawsuit against Meta and YouTube. She says the companies made social media addictive, which hurt her mental health and made her suffer from depression, anxiety, and body image problems. Her case is part of a group of lawsuits involving more than 1,600 people, aiming to set legal rules on how these platforms treat young users.
