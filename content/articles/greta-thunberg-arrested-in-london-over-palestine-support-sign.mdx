Greta Thunberg was taken to police in London after she held a sign that said she supported a group called Palestine Action and opposed genocide. She joined a protest outside an insurance company that has ties to an Israeli weapons maker. Police said she was arrested because the sign named a banned organisation. She was released on bail but the incident shows how protest signs can lead to charges.
