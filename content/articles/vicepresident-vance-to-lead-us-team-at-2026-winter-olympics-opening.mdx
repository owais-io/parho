Vice‑President JD Vance will head the U.S. delegation to the opening ceremony of the 2026 Winter Olympics in Italy. He’ll travel with his wife Usha, Secretary of State Marco Rubio, ambassador Tilman Fertitta, and Olympic champions like Jocelyne and Monique Lamoureux, Evan Lysacek, and Apolo Ohno. The ceremony on Feb 6 will take place at Milan’s San Siro stadium and three mountain venues. President Trump will not attend.
