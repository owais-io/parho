White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt told reporters that President Trump is fed up with endless meetings about Ukraine. He wants the war to end and has spent over 30 hours in recent weeks talking to Russians, Ukrainians and Europeans. Trump has criticized Ukraine’s president, saying he hasn't read the U.S. peace plan and pushing for elections. The U.S. also wants Ukraine to pull troops from Donbas and set up a “free economic zone.”
