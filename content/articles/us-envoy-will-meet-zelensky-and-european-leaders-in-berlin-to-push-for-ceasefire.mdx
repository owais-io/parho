Donald Trump’s envoy, Steve Witkoff, will meet Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky and European leaders in Berlin this weekend to push for a ceasefire. Germany will host talks and is sending soldiers to Poland to strengthen its eastern border. Russia says a pause will only happen after Ukraine pulls back from parts of Donetsk. Meanwhile, Russia launched drone and missile attacks, left a million Ukrainians without power, and struck a Turkish cargo ship. Ukraine also freed 114 prisoners from Belarus.
