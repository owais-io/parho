Tasmania’s $1.13bn AFL stadium on Hobart’s waterfront is close to getting the green light. Three independent lawmakers have said they will vote for it if certain conditions are met. This support means the stadium, which would let the Devils join the AFL in 2028, can now be approved by the upper house. The vote is scheduled for Thursday. The decision follows a review that found the stadium would boost local jobs and support grassroots sports.
