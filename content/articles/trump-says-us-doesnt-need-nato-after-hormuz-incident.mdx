Donald Trump said the United States does not need NATO after his allies refused to send warships to the Strait of Hormuz. He called the move a “foolish mistake” and blamed the United States for being shocked. The comment came amid tensions with Iran, a war that has killed thousands, and criticism from U.S. officials who quit or question the war. Trump’s remarks add to debate about U.S. defense commitments.
