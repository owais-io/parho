Missiles hit a girls’ school in southern Iran, killing 165 people and wounding 96, state media reports. The attack is the worst casualty of the US‑Israeli‑led campaign against Iran. Videos show the wreckage and grieving crowds. The US Central Command says it is looking into civilian harm. Iran’s health minister called it the most painful news, and Malala Yousafzai urged justice.
