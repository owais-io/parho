Macclesfield, a quiet Cheshire town, is buzzing because its football club, the Silkmen, just shocked the football world by beating Crystal Palace in the FA Cup. The town’s old silk mills and slow‑moving life now feel a surge of pride. After the original club folded, new owners rebuilt the team and stadium, and fans are ready for a big test against Brentford. Local markets, pubs and the Treacle Market add to the town’s charm.
