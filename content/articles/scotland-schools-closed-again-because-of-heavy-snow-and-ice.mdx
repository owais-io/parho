Hundreds of schools in northern Scotland are staying closed for a second day because the Met Office has issued heavy snow and ice warnings. Forecasts call for 5–15 cm of snow across the area, and travel links are already being hit – trains and flights have been cancelled. The storm may also bring snow, rain and strong winds to southern England later this week, but schools there remain open.
