After a heated press conference, Crystal Palace decided not to fire manager Oliver Glasner, even though he accused the board of abandoning him by selling captain Marc Guéhi to Manchester City just before a Sunderland game. Glasner said he doesn’t care if he stays till the end of his contract, which ends this summer. The club’s chair, Steve Parish, wants Glasner to finish the season as the team seeks to end a 10‑game winless run.
