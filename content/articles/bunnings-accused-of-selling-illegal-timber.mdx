An Australian environmental group says Bunnings may be selling timber that was illegally cut by the New South Wales forestry agency. The group asked the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission to investigate whether the chain’s claims about responsible sourcing are misleading. Bunnings says it only buys timber that meets forest certification standards, but the forestry company has a history of environmental violations. The case could force Bunnings to review its supply chain.
