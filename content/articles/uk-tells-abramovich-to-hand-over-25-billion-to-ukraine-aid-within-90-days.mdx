The UK has warned Roman Abramovich that he must move the £2.5 billion he earned from selling Chelsea to a Ukraine‑aid fund within 90 days, or it will go to court. The money is held in a UK account and can only be used for Ukrainian humanitarian help. Abramovich wants the funds to help all war victims, even Russians. The UK is ready to sue if he doesn’t comply.
