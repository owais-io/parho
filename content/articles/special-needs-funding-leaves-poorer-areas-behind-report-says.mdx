A Policy Exchange report shows that money spent on special educational needs has risen faster in wealthy councils, leaving deprived areas behind. Former education secretaries Ruth Kelly and Estelle Morris say this unfairly hurts families in poorer neighbourhoods and puts local councils under pressure. The government plans to spend £5bn to clear debt and is set to launch a new white paper to make support fairer and more sustainable.
