After a brief 276‑day stint, Liberal Party leader Sussan Ley said she will leave politics entirely. She lost a leadership vote to Angus Taylor, who is now party leader and promises to close the door on those who don’t share “Australian values.” Ley’s exit follows criticism from former PM Malcolm Turnbull, who called Taylor “the best‑qualified idiot.” Meanwhile, a NSW police watchdog will probe alleged brutality at a protest in Sydney, while other unrelated stories made the news.
