Trump is considering a strong military response to Iran’s crackdown on protests that have killed hundreds. He has mentioned air strikes, cyber attacks, new sanctions, and online propaganda. But targeting Tehran could harm civilians and boost the Iranian narrative that the US is behind the unrest. Iran has warned the US not to attack, and the situation remains tense. Trump posted on social media promising the US is ready to help Iran's “freedom.”
