**  
Brentford and Leeds played to a 1‑1 draw at the Gtech Stadium. Jordan Henderson netted Brentford’s goal after a deflected shot, his first for the club since 2021. Leeds answered with a header from Dominic Calvert‑Lewin, helped by a cross from Wilfried Gnonto. Substitutes Rico Henry and Gnonto added spark, but the match stayed tight. Both teams finished level, each earning a point in the league.
