Exiled Iranian women in the UK have taken to London streets, demanding that Iran become a free, secular country. They shout for a new republic led by a woman, such as Maryam Rajavi, and urge the UK to stop the Revolutionary Guard. The protests come as Iran’s people face violence and thousands are detained. While some hope the protests will topple the regime, others fear a new dictator could replace it.
