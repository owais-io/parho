After her parents’ garden turned into a wild forest, the narrator struggled to keep their home safe. A massive bamboo patch grew to 20 m tall and, after floods and a cyclone, was a dangerous mess. No contractors would tackle the job. A local man with a big excavator finally came, pulled out every stalk, and burned the remains to clear the sky. The story shows how the right help can bring order out of chaos.
