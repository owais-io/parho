Sky Sports launched a TikTok channel called Halo for female fans. The channel used pink graphics, captions about match‑a‑time, and featured male stars. Fans and women’s‑sports sites slammed it as patronising and sexist. After just three days, Sky Sports shut it down, apologised, and said it will work on better ways to include women fans. The move was welcomed by many in the women's sports community, who praised the quick response.
