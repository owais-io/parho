FIFA has struggled to sell TV rights for its new Women’s Champions Cup, the first global women’s club competition. The four‑match tournament starts in London on 28 January, featuring Arsenal, Corinthians and Gotham FC. Broadcasters like Sky and the BBC have shown little interest, so the rights remain unsold. FIFA tried to target key channels, but the small number of games and the tight football calendar make the event less appealing to buyers.
