Adrian Chiles talks about his first trip to Runcorn after years of vague fondness. He explains how a former university friend, an American Mormon passenger, and a sense of destiny made him curious about the town. During the visit he dropped his daughters off at the station, marveled at the brightly lit Silver Jubilee Bridge, and discovered the station’s resident cat. He left feeling that Runcorn truly has a special charm.
