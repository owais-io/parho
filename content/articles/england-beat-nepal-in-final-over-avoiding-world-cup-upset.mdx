England edged out Nepal by just four runs after a tense final over. Nepal had chased a modest 185, with Rohit Paudel and Lokesh Bam giving hope, but Sam Curran steadied England’s bowling. The match saw Nepal’s key bowlers take wickets early, while England’s batsmen, especially Will Jacks, scored the crucial runs. The win keeps England’s World Cup campaign alive.
