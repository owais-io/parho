A doctor who cared for a difficult patient for ten years explains why some patients feel hard to treat. A recent study finds one in six patients are seen as difficult, often because of mental health or pain issues. Less experienced doctors and those with burnout see more difficult cases. The paper says doctors need better training and more mental‑health help to ease the strain.
