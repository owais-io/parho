Texas congressman Tony Gonzales says he will not step down despite accusations that he had an affair with a staffer who later committed suicide. Republicans, including House Speaker Mike Johnson, have called for his resignation, but Gonzales says the story isn’t finished. New text messages show he asked the staffer for explicit photos and pushed for sex. The case could hurt his re‑election bid and the GOP’s control of his seat.
