President Trump signed an order that stops state governments from making their own AI rules. It creates a federal team that will fight any state laws about AI. Trump says a single national approval will speed up tech, but many states and rights groups say it gives too much power to big tech and could leave people unprotected. Critics worry the move ignores safety, privacy and fairness issues.
