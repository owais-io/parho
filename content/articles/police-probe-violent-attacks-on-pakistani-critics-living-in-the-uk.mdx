Scotland Yard’s counter‑terrorism team is investigating a series of three attacks on two Pakistani dissidents in England. One victim was punched and shot at his home, another had a window burst with gunfire, and a third assault involved arson and chemical spray. A 34‑year‑old man was arrested on suspicion of arson and firearms. Victims fear they were targeted for their criticism of Pakistan’s government, and the Foreign Office has remained silent.
