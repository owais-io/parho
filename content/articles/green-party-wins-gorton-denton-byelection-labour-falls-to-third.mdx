In the Gorton and Denton byelection, Hannah Spencer – a local plumber and Green Party councillor – won the seat, making the party the first in northern England to win a Westminster byelection. Labour finished third, losing its 13,000‑vote lead, while Reform UK came in second. The result shows a sharp drop in Labour support and signals a rising Green Party presence in a once‑Labour stronghold.
