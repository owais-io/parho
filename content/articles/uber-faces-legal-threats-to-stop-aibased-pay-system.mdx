A Dutch group called Worker Info Exchange (WIE) has sent Uber a warning letter, demanding the company stop using its AI‑driven pay system. WIE says the algorithm cuts drivers’ earnings and violates EU privacy rules. They want Uber to give drivers fair pay, bring transparency, and compensate those who lost money. Uber denies the claims, saying the study is flawed. The case could be filed in Amsterdam.
