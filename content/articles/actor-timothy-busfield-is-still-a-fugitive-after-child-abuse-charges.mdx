Timothy Busfield, a former Emmy‑winning actor and director, remains on the run after New Mexico police issued an arrest warrant for three counts of child sexual abuse. The accusations come from two 11‑year‑old twins who say he touched them during filming of the TV show “The Cleaning Lady.” U.S. Marshals are helping local police search for him. The case adds to earlier allegations against Busfield dating back to 1994.
