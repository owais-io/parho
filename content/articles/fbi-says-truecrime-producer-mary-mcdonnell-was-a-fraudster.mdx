Mary McDonnell ran Bellum Entertainment, the company that produced a popular true‑crime TV show. She tricked banks into giving her $15 million by faking documents that said she owned huge savings. She fled to Dubai before being charged. The FBI put her on its Most Wanted list and is asking the public to help locate her. She also owes former workers and the show’s lead, Nigel Bellis, more than $500,000.
