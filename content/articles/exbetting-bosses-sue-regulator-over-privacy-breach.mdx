Two former Entain bosses, Kenny Alexander and Lee Feldman, who face bribery and fraud charges, tried to buy a stake in online casino 888 and take control. The Gambling Commission intervened after learning they were suspects in an HMRC investigation and later announced a licence review. The pair claim the regulator leaked private information and harmed them, so they sued for breach of privacy. The regulator says disclosures were in the public interest.
