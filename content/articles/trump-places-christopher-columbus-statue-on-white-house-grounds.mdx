Donald Trump has put a 13‑foot statue of Christopher Columbus on the White House lawn. The bronze copy was made from the pieces of a statue that protesters tore down in Baltimore in 2020. Trump says Columbus was a hero, while many see him as a slave‑trader and oppressor of native people. The move sparks debate over how the US remembers its past.
