Catherine, the Princess of Wales, says we should stop shaming people who struggle with drug, alcohol or gambling problems. She encourages open chats, shows empathy, and supports the Forward Trust, a charity that helps people recover. A survey found many people know someone with an addiction and most think they need help, not judgment. She also announced a £100,000 grant to study how technology hurts parent‑child relationships.
