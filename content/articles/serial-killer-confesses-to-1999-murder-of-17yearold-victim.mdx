A serial killer who was already serving a whole‑life sentence for five murders has now admitted he killed a sixth victim, 17‑year‑old Victoria Hall, in 1999. The confession was made in a London court, where he also admitted a near‑kidnapping of a 22‑year‑old woman the day before. The judge will sentence him, giving the Hall family a chance to speak.
