Trump posted a private message from French President Emmanuel Macron that suggested a G7 meeting in Paris about Greenland, Ukraine and Syria. By putting the note on social media, he aimed to embarrass and intimidate other leaders. The move shows Trump’s habit of leaking confidential conversations to break diplomatic trust. It undermines the usual rules that keep such talks private and can make other heads of state wary of sharing information with him.
