At a Tulane University event, retired Army general Stanley McChrystal compared Donald Trump’s aggressive foreign moves to Dolly Parton’s song “Jolene.” He said Trump’s actions—from airstrikes in Nigeria, attacks on Venezuela and Iran, to threats over Greenland—show a pattern of “doing something just because we can.” McChrystal warned that this reckless “do‑because‑we‑can” style risks damaging U.S. alliances and global standing.
