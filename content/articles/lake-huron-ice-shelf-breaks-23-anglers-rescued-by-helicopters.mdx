In spring‑like weather, a group of ice fishers on Lake Huron were caught when a large ice shelf broke away from Georgian Bay. 23 people, including families, were left on floating ice as wind and heat pushed the ice off the shore. Ontario police sent two helicopters and an ambulance, taking two hours to bring everyone back to safety. The incident highlights how quickly warming temperatures can make ice dangerous.
