Enzo Maresca quit Chelsea on New Year’s Day after saying he’d talked to Manchester City about replacing Pep Guardiola. He’d already clashed with the board over tactics, rotation, and medical advice. The club had been unhappy with his poor results – only one win in seven games – and he tried to use interest from City and Juventus as leverage for a better deal. After his final press conference he left, and Chelsea will look for a new manager.
