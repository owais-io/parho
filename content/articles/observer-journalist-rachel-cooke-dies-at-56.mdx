Rachel Cooke, a 56‑year‑old Observer journalist, died from cancer after a diagnosis earlier this year. She spent 25 years at the paper, writing reviews, food pieces, and investigative stories. Colleagues called her fearless, funny and sharp, noting she could cover anything faster and better. She also published a food book, Kitchen Person. Cooke was survived by her husband, writer Anthony Quinn.
