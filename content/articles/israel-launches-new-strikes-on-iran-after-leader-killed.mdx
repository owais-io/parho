Israel has launched new airstrikes on Iranian targets, including Tehran’s government buildings, after the death of Iran’s supreme leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, in a US‑Israeli attack. Iran fired about 40 missiles at US and Israeli sites in retaliation. Khamenei’s funeral is being held, and his son is seen as the likely successor. The fighting has pushed oil prices up, cut shipping in the Gulf, and rattled global markets.
