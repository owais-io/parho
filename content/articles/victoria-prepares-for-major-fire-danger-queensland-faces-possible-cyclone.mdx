Queensland is bracing for a potential cyclone as a tropical low moves toward Townsville, with strong winds and heavy rain possible. While heat waves drive record electricity demand, rooftop solar is helping cut the load. In Victoria, extreme heat has triggered a total fire ban, closed the Hume Highway and cut regional trains. Residents are urged to stay cool, check on others, avoid outdoor work, and health officials warn of heat‑related illness and advise staying hydrated.
