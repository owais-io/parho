Gus Lamont, a four‑year‑old, vanished from his family’s Oak Park sheep station in September. Police ran a massive search covering over 800 km² but found nothing. After a week, the operation shifted to recovery. Authorities now say he neither wandered off nor was kidnapped and suspect a family member. The grandmothers are devastated, and lawyers urge privacy while police continue to seek clues.
