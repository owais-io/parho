The Justice Department is going through more than 5 million pages of documents tied to Jeffrey Epstein. The huge pile is pulling in about 400 lawyers, including prosecutors who normally handle other cases. Because of the size, the release of the files is now expected around January 20, a month past the December deadline. The team is carefully redacting parts that could hurt victims and is adding workers over the holiday break.
