Nicolás Maduro, the former Venezuelan president who was seized by U.S. forces in a dramatic weekend raid, is set to appear in Manhattan federal court on Monday. He faces serious drug and weapons accusations. The arrest came after a violent assault in Caracas that left dozens dead, heightening tensions between the U.S. and Venezuela. The court hearing will be a key moment in the international fallout from the raid.
