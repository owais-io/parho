Seattle is sticking to its plan to host LGBTQ+ Pride celebrations during next summer’s World Cup, even after the Egyptian and Iranian football federations protested. The city’s organizers say the events will happen outside the stadium and that FIFA has allowed rainbow flags in the arena. The plan, called the “Pride Match,” aims to show Seattle’s commitment to equality and welcome all fans and visitors.
