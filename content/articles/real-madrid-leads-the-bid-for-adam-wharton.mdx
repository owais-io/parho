Real Madrid is leading the chase to sign Crystal Palace midfielder Adam Wharton, setting a £65 million price tag. Other top clubs—Manchester United, Tottenham, Newcastle—are also eyeing the young winger. Palace said they might sell at season’s end. The transfer buzz also involves clubs like Liverpool, Roma, and Sunderland looking at different players, but the focus remains on Wharton’s move to Madrid.
