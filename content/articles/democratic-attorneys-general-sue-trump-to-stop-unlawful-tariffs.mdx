The 23 Democratic attorneys general are suing President Trump to stop him from imposing tariffs without Congress. They’ve filed over 50 suits against his administration, blocking policies that hurt food benefits, health programs and schools. Trump’s moves often break the law, so the AGs say they’re protecting state money and citizens. The AGs work together and try to bring Republican colleagues on board, but many red‑state AGs stay silent. Their lawsuits often win injunctions that stop the policies.
