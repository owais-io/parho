Leeds managed a 1‑1 draw at home with Brentford. Jordan Henderson gave Brentford the lead early, then Dominic Calvert‑Lewin headed a cross from Wilfried Gnonto to level the score. Both teams were missing key players, and a couple of substitutes – Jordan Henderson and Wilfried Gnonto – made the difference. The result keeps Leeds in the safety zone while Brentford remains in danger. Managers praised the resilience of their squads.
