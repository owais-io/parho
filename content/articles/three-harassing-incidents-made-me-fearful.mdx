Lucy Pasha‑Robinson writes about three separate times when men pushed or harassed her on empty sidewalks. She describes being startled by a man who shoved her from behind, and earlier episodes of verbal insults and physical pressure. The repeated encounters left her feeling unsafe, even on a quiet weekday morning. She shares how other women she knows have faced similar unwanted attention, and how such behavior breaks social norms.
