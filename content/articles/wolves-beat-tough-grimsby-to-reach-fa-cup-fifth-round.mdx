Wolves won 1-0 against Grimsby in the FA Cup, thanks to a late strike by Santiago Bueno. The match was played in rain and mud, making it a hard day for both sides. Grimsby, a League Two team that had a 10‑game unbeaten streak, looked like an upset but couldn't break through. Wolves now move into the fifth round while Grimsby head home with pride.
