A Nigerian transport plane had to make an emergency landing in Burkina Faso’s Bobo Dioulasso after a technical issue. The plane had been heading to Portugal, but the crew was denied permission to fly over Burkinabé airspace. Eleven Nigerian soldiers are now being held in the country. Burkina and its allies in the Alliance of Sahel States called the landing a hostile act. Nigeria says the crew is safe and plans to resume the mission.
