**  
Villa edged Tottenham 2‑1 in the third‑round FA Cup, with a late strike from Malen sealing the win and sending the visitors into the fourth round. Tottenham struggled after a string of injuries and a flurry of bookings, most notably Palhinha’s hard tackle on Rogers. The match was played in a somber atmosphere following the death of former Spurs legend Martin Chivers, with both teams making several changes.
