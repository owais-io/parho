US lawmakers voted against a bill that would have stopped President Trump’s attack on Iran. The House, controlled by Republicans, rejected the resolution by a 212‑219 margin. Democrats said the president had no clear reason to fight and that the war hurt Americans and the planet. Republicans say the president can act without Congress when there is no war declaration. Six American soldiers died in the campaign.
