Senegal’s football federation accused Morocco’s organisers of failing to protect its team before the Africa Cup of Nations final. The team said security at the station and hotel was poor, crowds crowded them, and they were given too few tickets for fans. Senegal also refused to use the main training complex and complained that being forced to train where Morocco already had a base was unfair. The complaint comes amid tense match‑day controversies.
