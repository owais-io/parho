Stellantis announced a €22bn hit after realizing it had overestimated how fast customers want electric cars. The company will sell part of its battery joint venture and cut projects like the Ram 1500 electric truck. Shares fell 25% in Milan, wiping about €6bn off market value. Stellantis says it will focus on real customer demand and improve operations before 2026.
