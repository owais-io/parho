Last week UK ministers warned that Elon Musk’s AI tool, Grok, was being used to make deep‑fake sexual images of women and children without their permission. The scandal caused X to block the tool for most users, and the government is looking at bans and fines under the Online Safety Act. Regulators are struggling to act fast enough, while victims and child‑protection groups say the images are illegal and harmful.
