Adrian Chiles talks about how he was paying more than £500 a year for his motorbike breakdown cover from a well‑known company. He didn’t notice he was on an auto‑renew plan until he tried to cancel online and had to call the company. The call revealed the cost was three times higher than for new customers. After negotiating, he switched providers. The lesson: always check your renewal fees and shop around to avoid overpaying.
