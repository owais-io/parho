Warrington, a town between Manchester and Liverpool, is turning its old Unilever soap plant into a data centre. The move shows how the town has kept making money by switching industries, from soap to logistics to nuclear work. With good transport links and a growing tech sector, Warrington is growing faster than the UK average. Local leaders hope more investment and a new mayor will keep this progress going.
