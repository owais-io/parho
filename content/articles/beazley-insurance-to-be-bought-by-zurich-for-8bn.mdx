Beazley, a specialist insurer that covers cyber risks, yachts, fine art and more, has agreed to be bought by Zurich for about £8bn. Shareholders will receive up to £13.35 per share – a cash offer of £13.10 plus a 25‑pence dividend. The deal, about a 60% premium, will pull Beazley out of the London market and merge its niche business with Zurich’s global specialty arm.
