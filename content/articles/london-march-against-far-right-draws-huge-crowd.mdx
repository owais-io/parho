Police say there will be a big police presence in Westminster on Saturday because thousands of people plan to march against the far right. The Together Alliance and the Palestine Coalition will lead two separate marches that will join at Whitehall. Some protesters will also take part in demonstrations against the Iranian regime. Police have set fixed routes and rally sites to keep the marches orderly and to protect people after a recent arson attack.
