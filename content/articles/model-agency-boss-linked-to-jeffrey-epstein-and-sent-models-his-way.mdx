Faith Kates, who ran the fashion agency Next Management for decades, had a close friendship with Jeffrey Epstein. Documents show she used her position to introduce models to him, sometimes arranging meetings and even giving him financial advice. Kates was involved with Epstein for nearly 40 years, sending models to his home and offering him loans she never received. The agency has distanced itself, saying it never knew about the relationship.
