Zelenskyy told students in Paris that the Middle East conflict, especially between Iran and Russia, is bad for Ukraine because it diverts world attention and resources from his war effort. He urged Iran not to help Russia and wants a free, independent Iran. He also highlighted the U.S. easing sanctions on Russian oil, which boosts Moscow’s budget, and reported new Russian attacks on Ukrainian towns. Meanwhile, Russia has named a U.S. scholar a “foreign agent.”
