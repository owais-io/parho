France is launching a parliamentary inquiry into its public broadcaster, questioning its neutrality and funding. The far‑right National Rally says the state stations are biased and wants them privatized. Critics point to private media boss Vincent Bolloré as giving the right more influence. Left‑wing MPs say the hearings are a political attack. The debate comes before the 2027 election, as the future of public media is at stake.
