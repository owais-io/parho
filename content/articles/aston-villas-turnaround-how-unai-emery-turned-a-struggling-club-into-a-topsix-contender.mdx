Since taking over in 2022, Unai Emery turned Aston Villa from a struggling side into a top‑six club. He brought fresh signings, big money, and a new style that made Villa win more home games than any team in Europe. They beat Manchester United in Emery’s first match and reached the Champions League quarter‑finals. Still, Villa have a bad record against United and Liverpool, and many fans think the team needs to break that curse to challenge for titles.
