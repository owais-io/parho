Former professor and judge writes that Trump’s decision to leave the Paris climate accord again shows a disregard for science and the risks of climate change. He calls it a “perverse” move that could hasten environmental disaster. The writer notes courts have confirmed the government’s role in fueling fossil fuels, but courts cannot change policy. Trump’s move is criticized as dangerous and short‑sighted for the planet and future generations today.
