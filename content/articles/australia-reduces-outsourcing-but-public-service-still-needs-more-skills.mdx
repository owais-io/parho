Australia’s new government has cut how much it spends on external workers, turning many contractor jobs into public‑service roles. The move saves money, but the real problem is a lack of in‑house expertise, especially in digital and complex program design. Slow hiring, uneven career paths and weak training mean that outsourcing still fills gaps, risking poor outcomes. True improvement will need a clear plan to build and keep talent.
