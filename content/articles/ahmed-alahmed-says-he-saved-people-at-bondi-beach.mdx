Ahmed al‑Ahmed, 43, is a father of two and a new Australian citizen. While walking at Bondi Beach, he saw a gunman shoot during a Hanukah party and stepped in. He wrestled the gun from the attacker and was shot himself, now in a hospital. His family say he did it because he could not stand people dying. The move earned praise from Aussie leaders and the US president.
