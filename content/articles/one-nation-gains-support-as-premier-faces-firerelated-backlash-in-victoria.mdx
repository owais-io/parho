One Nation says it is riding a wave of support after Victoria’s bushfires hit the spotlight. Premier Jacinta Allan has been criticised for how the state handled fires and a new emergency‑services levy. Protesters in towns like Alexandra blocked cars and spat out signs, demanding better help. The party’s secretary says the “tide is turning” and it will run candidates in every seat, hoping to win more votes before the election.
