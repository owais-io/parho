Teachers at two primary schools in Greater Manchester have started a nine‑day strike because pupils keep attacking staff and other children, creating a “culture of violence.” The union says school leaders have failed to protect teachers and have even threatened those who raise concerns. Despite the strike, the schools remain open. Parents are surprised by the claims, with some saying their kids enjoy school but worry about safety. The trust insists it is prioritising safety while respecting the strike.
