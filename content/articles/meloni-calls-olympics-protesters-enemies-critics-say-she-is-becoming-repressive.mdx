In Milan, thousands protested the Winter Olympics, saying it causes jobs, poverty, and damage. Most were peaceful, but a few clashes led to arrests. Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni called the demonstrators “enemies of Italy” and defended the police. Critics say her harsh security laws and new bills that let police detain protesters before they even march signal a move toward repression.
