In December, a car crash on Todwick Road left two teens badly hurt. Police mistakenly told Trevor Wynn’s family that he had died, but later tests proved Wynn was actually in hospital while 18‑year‑old Joshua Johnson was the one who died. South Yorkshire police have asked the Independent Office for Police Conduct to investigate the error and will give both families extra support. A few people have been arrested over the crash.
