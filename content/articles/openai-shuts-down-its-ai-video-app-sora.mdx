OpenAI announced it was shutting down its AI video app Sora, which had been live for six months. The app let people create short, realistic videos and hit number one on Apple’s App Store, but it faced backlash over violent, racist and copyright‑infringing content. The closure also ended a new partnership with Disney that would have provided access to over 200 licensed characters. OpenAI said it will share a timeline for backing up users’ videos.
