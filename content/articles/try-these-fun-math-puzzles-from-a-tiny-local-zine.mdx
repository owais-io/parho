Hyde Park Math Zine is a tiny, hand‑written zine that drops three puzzles in each of its 30 copies, mailed around Austin’s Hyde Park. Math teacher Kevin Gately created it to mix math with local community fun. The puzzles range from geometry riddles to probability challenges, all with clear answers. The article lists four sample puzzles and encourages readers to share their own puzzles and favorite fanzines.
