London Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood banned the annual al‑Quds Day march because police said it could spark serious public disorder. The protest, organised by a pro‑Palestinian group that has shown support for Iran’s late leader, has often faced counter‑demonstrations and police concerns about extremist ties. Critics say the ban stifles free speech, while the Home Secretary insists it was a security move.
