Paris Saint‑Germain’s women have lost only one league match, but the French football authorities turned three of their wins into defeats because a player, Florianne Jourde, didn’t have the right transfer paperwork. A complaint from a rival club triggered the ruling, and PSG was docked nine points. The club says the error was honest, and they are fighting back. The penalty pushed PSG down from second to fifth, 16 points behind the leaders.
