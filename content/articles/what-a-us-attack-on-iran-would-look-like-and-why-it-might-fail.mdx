The US has moved a carrier and jets to the Indian Ocean to be ready for an attack on Iran. Experts say the main goal would be to hit Iran’s leader, but it is hard to get accurate intel and the risk of Iran retaliating is huge. Even if the US kills the leader, Iran could still fight back with missiles and drones, and the world would likely see a bigger war.
