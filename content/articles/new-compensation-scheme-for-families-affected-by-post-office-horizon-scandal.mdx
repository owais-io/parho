The UK government has announced a new scheme to help relatives of post office workers hurt by the Horizon IT scandal. Families can now claim either a case‑by‑case personal injury payment if they have evidence of harm, or a fixed “recognition” payment without proof if they lost a post office to prosecution or bankruptcy. The move aims to give families the support they need quickly and without heavy paperwork.
