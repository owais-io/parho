On Wednesday, the three biggest cable news channels rushed to cover a fatal shooting by an ICE officer that killed a woman in Minneapolis. They used different headlines and invited former DHS and police officials to explain what happened. Some officials raised concerns about quick hiring and training; others blamed the city’s mayor or protestors for the conflict. Video of the shooting spread online, but networks cautioned viewers about its graphic nature.
