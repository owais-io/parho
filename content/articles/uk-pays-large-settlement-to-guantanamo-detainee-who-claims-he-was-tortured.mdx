The UK has paid a large settlement to Abu Zubaydah, a former Guantanamo detainee who says he was tortured while British intelligence helped the CIA ask him questions. A court allowed him to sue the government, and the payment was made even though the UK didn’t admit any wrongdoing. Zubaydah has spent years in Guantanamo without charges, and the case highlights the risks of cooperating with the US on torture.
