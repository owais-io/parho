White House first called Alex Pretti a gunman and a terrorist, but video shows he was tackled and shot many times. Public backlash forced officials to backtrack. Senior staff retracted their terrorist claims. President talked to the Minnesota governor, agreed to reduce federal agents, and let state investigators lead. The incident shows how quickly the administration shifts its position after criticism in Minnesota.
