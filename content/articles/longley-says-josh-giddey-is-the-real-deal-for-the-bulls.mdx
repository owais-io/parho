Luc Longley, who won three championships with the Chicago Bulls in the 1990s, says he sees Josh Giddey as the new heart of the team. Longley, still running basketball teams in Australia, remembers playing with Giddey’s father in the 1980s and feels a personal link to the young guard. Giddey’s strong play has helped the Bulls climb back into playoff contention, and Longley believes his energy and talent give the club a new fighting spirit.
