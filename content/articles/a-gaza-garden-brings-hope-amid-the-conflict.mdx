While Israeli bombs fell, one family planted a garden in Gaza as a quiet act of resistance and hope. The Guardian piece shares letters from readers across the UK and beyond—talking about trains, water shortages, and travel plans—showing everyday concerns during hard times. The garden, though small, stands out as a powerful symbol of resilience, reminding us that even in war, people can create life and optimism.
