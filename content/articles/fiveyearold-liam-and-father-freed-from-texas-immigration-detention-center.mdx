Liam, a five‑year‑old boy, and his father were freed from a Texas immigration detention center after a judge ordered their release. They had been held for over a week following an ICE raid on their home in Minneapolis. The court said detaining a child was wrong. The case drew national attention after a viral photo of Liam in a bunny hat. The release may spark hope for other detained families.
