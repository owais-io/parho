An 18‑year‑old man named Harper Dennis has been charged with murder after stabbing a 20‑year‑old cybersecurity student, Khaleed Oladipo, in Leicester. Oladipo was killed on Oxford Street when he was cut in the chest. Dennis will appear in court on Saturday and also faces two other weapon‑related charges that are not linked to the killing. The family describes Oladipo as a beloved son and football fan.
