Prue Leith, the veteran judge on Britain's hit show The Great British Bake Off, has decided to step down after nine years. She says she wants to spend more time gardening. Her move comes as older workers are staying in jobs longer, thanks to age‑discrimination laws and a rising state pension age. The article also notes that many seniors still work well into their 80s, showing that age is not a reason to stop working.
