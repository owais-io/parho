A cold polar wind is moving into the US, ending the unusually warm weekend in the South and Midwest. The high‑pressure ridge will bring freezing temperatures and possible snow, so people should swap shorts for hats. While the US cools, hot days hit Brazil and parts of South America. Meanwhile, Japan faces heavy snow and New Zealand is experiencing strong storms and rain. Weather across the world is very unsettled.
