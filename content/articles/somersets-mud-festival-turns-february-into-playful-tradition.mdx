Somerset's Quantock Hills team has started a Month of Mud festival to turn the wet February into fun. They want people, even adults, to get dirty, walk in the mud and make mud‑paint. The event includes hikes, art, storytelling and talks about how mud helps wildlife. After heavy rains and flooding, the festival reminds everyone that mud can be playful, healthy for the land and even a source of inspiration.
