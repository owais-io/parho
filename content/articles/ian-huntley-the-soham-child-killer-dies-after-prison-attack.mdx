Ian Huntley, convicted of murdering two 10‑year‑old girls in 2002, died in hospital after being badly injured in a workshop attack at HMP Frankland. The 52‑year‑old suffered severe brain damage from a spiked pole. He had been on life sentences for the murders and was taken off life support a week after the assault. The incident comes amid ongoing investigations into the prison attack.
