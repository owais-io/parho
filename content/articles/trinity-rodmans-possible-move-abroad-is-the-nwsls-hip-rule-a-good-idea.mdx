The National Women’s Soccer League is worried about top USWNT stars leaving for Europe, so it created a “High‑Impact Player” rule that lets teams spend an extra $1m on certain players. The players’ union says the rule violates contracts and wants a higher salary cap instead. Players say moving abroad gives them new challenges, like the Champions League, and can actually help the national team.
