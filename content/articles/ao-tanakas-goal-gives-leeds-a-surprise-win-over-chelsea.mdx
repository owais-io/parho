Leeds stunned Chelsea 3‑1 on a night that lifted them out of the bottom three. Manager Daniel Farke switched to a back‑three and saw first‑half goals from Jaka Bijol and Ao Tanaka. Chelsea answered with a Pedro Neto strike, but Dominic Calvert‑Lewin capped it off in the second half. The win boosts Farke’s job security and gives fans a bright moment after a tough spell to keep the team in the Premier League.
