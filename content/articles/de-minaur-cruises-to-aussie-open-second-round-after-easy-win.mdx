De Minaur breezed past Mackenzie McDonald 6‑2, 6‑2, 6‑3 in the Australian Open first round, saying he’s no longer content with just getting through to the second round. The 26‑year‑old added that players want a fairer slice of the money from top tournaments and that it isn’t about greed but better pay for everyone. He’s already eyeing his next match against Hamad Medjedovic.
