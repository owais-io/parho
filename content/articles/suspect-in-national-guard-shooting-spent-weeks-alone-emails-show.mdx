Rahmanullah Lakanwal, a 29‑year‑old Afghan refugee, was charged with killing two National Guard soldiers in Washington, D.C., last week. Emails from case workers show he had long periods of isolation, failed to keep a job, and struggled with mental health after moving to the U.S. in 2021. He had been warned for erratic behavior, but no one suspected he would become violent. He was shot and hospitalized by a guard during the attack.
