Lily Walters, a popular Instagram interior designer, tells people to make their homes “personal and slightly unhinged,” suggesting odd items like an alligator toilet flush or a stained‑glass traffic cone. The author shares her own collection of strange décor—a stuffed magpie, pewter goats, and other quirky items—along with stories of other homes’ oddities, like a wooden sheep or a dead bumblebee in a box. She notes that homes are already weird enough, so extra eccentricity isn’t needed.
