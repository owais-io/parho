Fans in Brooklyn and across New York are losing their phones at concerts, especially during metal and electronic shows where moshing crowds make it easy for thieves. Stolen phones can fetch up to $5,000 in China, so organizers warn people to keep devices in front pockets and not hold them up. The NYPD and venues like Live Nation are investigating, and security plans are still under wraps. Even global cities report thousands of phones stolen each year.
