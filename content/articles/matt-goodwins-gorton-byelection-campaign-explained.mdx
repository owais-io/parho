Matt Goodwin, a former academic turned TV host, is running for a seat in Manchester’s Gorton and Denton. He focuses on staged media events, rarely showing up on the ground. The campaign has been mired in controversy: a team member was suspended for racist posts, a candidate was pulled from a local hustings, and his party’s extremist ties raised red flags. Local voters feel he cares more about publicity than local issues.
