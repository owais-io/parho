In January, people rallied outside the Dilley center in Texas where many children are held with their parents. The protest was sparked by videos of kids screaming and a 5‑year‑old wearing a bunny hat who was soon released. Activists say the conditions at Dilley—lack of clean water, medical care and a measles outbreak—are cruel. They want Congress to stop family detention and shut down new centers, saying it causes lasting trauma.
