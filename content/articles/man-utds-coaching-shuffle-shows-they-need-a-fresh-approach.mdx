Manchester United have been scrambling to find a new coach after sacking Ruben Amorim. They first put former player Darren Fletcher in charge, then looked at Solskjaer or Michael Carrick, and finally hired Carrick. He will take on a City derby while the club is still stuck in a nostalgic “Ferguson‑era” mindset. The piece uses a Seinfeld episode to argue that sometimes you need to do the opposite of what feels normal.
