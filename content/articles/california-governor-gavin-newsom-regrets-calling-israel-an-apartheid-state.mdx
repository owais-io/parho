California Governor Gavin Newsom said he regrets using the term “apartheid” to describe Israel. In a Politico interview, he explained the remark was meant for a future scenario if Israeli Prime Minister Netanyahu keeps annexing the West Bank, not the current situation. Newsom still supports Israel but strongly opposes Netanyahu’s policies. His comments sparked backlash from pro‑Israel groups and sparked debate on US‑Israel relations.
