Arsenal won 3‑0 away at OH Leuven, earning a seeded spot in the Women’s Champions League playoffs. Olivia Smith, Beth Mead and an own goal from Saar Janssen gave the Gunners the win. The victory put them into the knockout phase despite a lower league finish. Fans from England packed the Belgian stadium, and the win keeps hopes alive for a chance to beat top teams later. Arsenal will face Atlético Madrid or Leuven again in February.
