Brazil’s Rocinha favela has turned its rooftop into a new tourist hotspot after viral drone videos showed visitors walking on a metal door and dancing on a red‑brick terrace. The trend drew millions of Instagram views and huge crowds, including celebrities. Local guides now lead tours, giving residents a new source of income and changing how people see the community. It’s boosting jobs, confidence and the city’s image.
