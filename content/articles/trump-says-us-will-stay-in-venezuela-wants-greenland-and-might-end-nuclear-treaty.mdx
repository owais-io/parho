Trump said the US will stay in Venezuela for a long time and that he doesn’t need international law. He wants the US to take Greenland and says Taiwan is safe for now, adding China would not attack it during his term. He also said he could let the US‑Russia nuclear treaty end and replace it with a new one. He talked casually, joking about weight‑loss pills and said his own morality is the only limit on his power.
