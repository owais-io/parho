The Trump administration is ending temporary protection for about 340,000 Haitians in the United States, with the status set to expire on Feb. 3. DHS says Haiti’s crisis has improved, but critics say violence and displacement are still serious. A federal judge stopped the earlier plan to cut the status, saying the agency didn’t follow the required steps. The move follows a trend of tighter U.S. immigration rules.
