A new study by Labour MP Liam Byrne found more than £170m was funneled to MPs, parties, media and thinktanks that support the UK’s right‑wing populists over the past five years. Most of the money went to three outlets – GB News, The Critic and UnHerd – and to a handful of billionaires such as Christopher Harborne, Paul Marshall and Jeremy Hosking. Byrne says the cash fuels a “media‑political complex” that boosts populist voices and calls for stricter rules on donations, especially from crypto.
