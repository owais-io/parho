In the Gorton and Denton byelection, Labour expected to win but finished third, losing a 13,000‑vote lead to the Greens, who won with a popular local candidate. Reform UK came in second, leaving Labour scrambling to explain the surprise loss. The result shows Labour’s support is shaky and highlights the growing appeal of both the Greens and Reform in the area.
