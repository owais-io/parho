Tottenham Hotspur fired coach Thomas Frank after a season of poor results and angry fans. Frank had been praised for his time at Brentford but couldn't lift Spurs out of the bottom of the Premier League, finishing 16th. Key defeats and a toxic atmosphere at the stadium led supporters to chant for his sacking. Ownership struggles, injuries and a hard‑hit squad also made it hard for Frank to turn things around.
