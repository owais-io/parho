An ICE agent shot and killed a woman in Minneapolis, sparking angry protests. Residents and activists shouted at the agents and called for ICE to leave the city. The mayor, Jacob Frey, told ICE to get out and said the agency was hurting families. Minneapolis police eventually took over, and ICE agents left after the crowd threw snowballs and used irritants. The FBI and state police are now investigating.
