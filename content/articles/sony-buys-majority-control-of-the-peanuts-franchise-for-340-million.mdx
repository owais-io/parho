Sony has bought 41% of the Peanuts franchise from WildBrain for about £340 million, bringing its total ownership to 80%. The remaining 20% stays with the Schulz family. The Peanuts characters, like Snoopy and Charlie Brown, are famous worldwide, appearing in comics, TV, movies, toys, and theme parks. Apple TV will keep streaming Peanuts until 2030 and plans a new animated film. Sony will use its global media reach to grow the brand.
