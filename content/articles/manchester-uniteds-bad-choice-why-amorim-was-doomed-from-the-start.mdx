Sir Jim Ratcliffe’s new Manchester United team hired coach Ruben Amorim, but insiders warned he wasn’t the right fit. Executives said the club lacked a clear hiring process, and former sporting director Dan Ashworth criticised the 3‑4‑3 system and said the squad wasn’t ready. Analysts also questioned Amorim’s track record and tactical flexibility. The club’s data team is now under fire after a new hire with no football experience.
