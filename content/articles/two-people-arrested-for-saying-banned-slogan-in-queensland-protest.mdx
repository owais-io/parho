Queensland just introduced strict hate‑speech laws that ban phrases like “from the river to the sea.” Two pro‑Palestinian protesters were charged on the first day the law took effect after a march outside the state parliament. The leaders, including Liam Parry, were arrested and later released on bail, while a young woman was given an adult caution. Both face court dates for using the banned words.
