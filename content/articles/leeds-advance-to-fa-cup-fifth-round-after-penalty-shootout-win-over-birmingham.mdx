Leeds United moved into the FA Cup fifth round after a tense 1-1 draw with Birmingham City that went to a penalty shootout. Goalkeeper Lucas Perri saved a key penalty, and Sean Longstaff scored the decisive spot‑kick for Leeds. Birmingham had a strong first half but could not finish the game, and their manager praised the players’ effort despite the loss.
