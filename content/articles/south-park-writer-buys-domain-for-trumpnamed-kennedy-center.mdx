South Park writer Toby Morton bought the website trumpkennedycenter.org after expecting Donald Trump to rename the Kennedy Center. He turned the domain into a satirical site that pokes fun at Trump, featuring strange ads and parody messages. Other satirists have also grabbed similar Trump‑related domains. The real Kennedy Center remains unchanged, but the joke site shows how humor is used to challenge political power. The move reflects a trend of using humor to criticize politicians.
