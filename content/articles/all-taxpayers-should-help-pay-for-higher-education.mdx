Higher‑education minister Rachel Reeves argues that university costs should be shared by all taxpayers, not just students. She sees education as a public good that supports safety, health, science, technology, and the creative arts. Reeves says it’s unfair for non‑students to shoulder the expenses that benefit everyone. The letter invites readers to share their thoughts and encourages broader public debate.
