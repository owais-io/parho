The 2026 Winter Games will be held in Italy, split between Milan and the mountains of Cortina d’Ampezzo. Sixteen sports and over 110 gold medals will be contested, from fast alpine skiing to tough biathlon races. Fans will see Mikaela Shiffrin on the slopes, Ilia Malinin in figure skating, and Italy’s Arianna Fontana in speed skating. Britain’s skaters Lilah Fear and Lewis Gibson hope to win the nation’s first medal since 1994, while hockey and curling add classic rivalries.
