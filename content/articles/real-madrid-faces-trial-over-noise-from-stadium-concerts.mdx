Residents living near Madrid’s Bernabéu stadium say the loud concerts by stars like Taylor Swift and Karol G have caused headaches, stress and disrupted daily life. A Spanish judge found enough evidence for a noise‑pollution case and has named Real Madrid’s CEO and the club’s venue company for trial. Real Madrid has canceled and rescheduled concerts and promised to meet council sound rules. The lawsuit could see the club face criminal charges.
