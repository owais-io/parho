AI shows that students have been outsourcing their thinking for years, using essay mills, shared past papers, and heavy tutor help. The tech just sped up an old shortcut. The real problem is how courses are designed: they prize polished writing over real reflection and struggle. Universities need to shift the focus to evidence of thinking and interpretation, not just final products.
