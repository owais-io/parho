A celebration at Stamford Bridge marked the third anniversary of Brown Girl Sport, an online community that lifts South‑Asian women and girls in sport. The event introduced a new supporters club for women of colour, partnered with Chelsea Women, and a new ambassador program. It highlighted how the group tackles racism, gives a voice to fans like Farah Chowdhury, and provides a safe space for people who often feel isolated.
