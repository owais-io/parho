Japan's latest GDP data shows only 0.1% growth, meaning it sidestepped a recession but still shows a weak economy. The yen has slipped as traders react to the numbers. Meanwhile, Switzerland grew 0.2% in its last quarter and 1.4% this year, staying out of a downturn. Markets are cautious— the Nikkei fell a touch and UK home prices are flat, giving buyers more choices.
