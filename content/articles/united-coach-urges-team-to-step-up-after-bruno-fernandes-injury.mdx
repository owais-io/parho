Manchester United’s coach, Ruben Amorim, said the team must step up after captain Bruno Fernandes hurt himself and will miss many games. Fernandes was taken off at halftime against Aston Villa with a soft‑tissue injury. Amorim told players to take on more responsibilities, especially with other key teammates away for the Africa Cup of Nations. The club will face Newcastle on Boxing Day, and the squad will need new leaders.
