Manchester United will officially appoint former midfielder Michael Carrick as interim manager on Tuesday. He’ll bring back Darren Fletcher to help coach the team and could add Jonathan Woodgate to the staff. Carrick previously guided the club to three wins in a short caretaker spell. With the squad short on midfield, the club is eyeing Rúben Neves for about £20 million. The goal is to lift morale after a recent FA Cup exit and push United into Champions League spots.
