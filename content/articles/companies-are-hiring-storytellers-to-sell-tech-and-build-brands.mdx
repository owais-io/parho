Companies are hiring storytellers to turn tech and product details into engaging narratives for marketing, sales, and internal communication. Google Cloud, Microsoft, Vanta, Notion, and even Marks & Spencer want people who can craft stories that highlight benefits, build brand image, and drive growth. The demand for these roles has doubled in U.S. job listings, with salaries reaching up to $274,000. The role blends tech knowledge with creative writing, marketing, and communication skills.
