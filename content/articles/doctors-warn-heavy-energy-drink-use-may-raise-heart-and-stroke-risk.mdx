Doctors in Nottingham say that drinking a lot of energy drinks can raise your chances of heart disease and stroke. A man in his 50s who drank eight drinks a day (over 1,200 mg of caffeine) had a stroke and severe high blood pressure. After he stopped, his blood pressure fell, but he was left with permanent numbness. The doctors call for tighter rules on selling and advertising these drinks, and for doctors to ask patients about energy‑drink habits.
