Google’s new AI feature, called Overviews, shows quick answers at the top of search results. A Guardian investigation found that some of these summaries give incorrect health tips, like telling pancreatic cancer patients to avoid fats, or confusing liver test numbers. Wrong advice can delay treatment or make people think they’re healthy when they’re not. Experts warn that such mistakes could put users’ lives at risk, even though Google says most answers are accurate.
