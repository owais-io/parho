A 31‑year‑old Customs and Border Protection employee in Minnesota, Alfredo Mancillas Jr, was arrested after state troopers found him unconscious and covered in vomit in his car. He was charged with third‑ and fourth‑degree drunken driving. The arrest happened amid the Trump‑era immigration push in the state. Homeland Security said it was reviewing the case. Mancillas was released on bail and faces a court date in March.
