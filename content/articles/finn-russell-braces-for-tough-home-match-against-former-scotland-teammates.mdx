Finn Russell is getting ready for a tough home game against Edinburgh, where he’ll face former Scotland teammates. He’s texting opponents to warn of a physical showdown and hopes the win will boost Bath’s chances in the Champions Cup knock‑out stage. The team is also fine‑tuning its attack after losing coach Lee Blackett, and the club feels extra pressure after last season’s three titles. Other clubs are also signing new players.
