King Charles announced he will need fewer cancer treatments in 2024, saying his doctors plan to move his care into a precautionary phase. He praised early detection and urged people to keep up with NHS cancer screenings, noting that 9 million Brits are behind. He thanked his medical team and highlighted a new online screening checker to help people find out which tests they qualify for. The king remains hopeful and supports all those fighting cancer.
