Ukraine says it faked the death of Russian Volunteer Corps leader Denis Kapustin, claiming Moscow had paid $500,000 for his murder. Military intelligence says it uncovered the plot, saved Kapustin’s life and exposed the agents. A video shows Ukraine’s chief of intelligence congratulating Kapustin. The story comes amid reports that Ukraine’s drones struck Russian energy sites, while both sides accuse each other of hitting civilians.
