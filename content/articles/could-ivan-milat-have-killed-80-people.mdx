A retired police detective, Hugh Hughes, thinks Keren Rowland—who vanished in 1971—was Ivan Milat’s first victim. Milat was jailed for seven backpacker murders and died in 2019. New South Wales MP Jeremy Buckingham wants a parliamentary inquiry into unsolved killings from 1965‑2010, arguing Milat could have killed up to 80 people and that corruption may have protected him. The inquiry will review past cases and police work.
