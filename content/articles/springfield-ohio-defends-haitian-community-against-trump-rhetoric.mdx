In Springfield, Ohio, Haitian immigrants face threats from Trump‑era rhetoric and possible enforcement. A local judge blocked the Trump administration’s attempt to end temporary protected status for 350,000 Haitians, but the fight continues. Churches, nonprofits and volunteers have built a support network, offering language classes, legal help, and emergency planning. The community rallies against hate, keeping hope alive amid fear and uncertainty.
