As people live longer, staying happy after 40 becomes urgent. In his book *Wise*, psychologist Frank Tallis says the key is to accept change, listen to your inner feelings, and stay flexible. He warns against over‑relying on tech and urges us to practice mindfulness, reconnect with nature, and pursue long‑hidden passions. Small, thoughtful habits can help us stay whole and healthy through midlife.
