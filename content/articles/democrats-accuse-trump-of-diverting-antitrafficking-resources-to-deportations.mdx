House Democrats have warned that the Trump administration’s mass deportations are pulling police, agents and funding away from the fight against child exploitation and human trafficking. They want the DHS and Justice Department to restore full staffing to anti‑trafficking units and release the Epstein investigation files. The letters cite a New York Times report that deportation drives have cut investigations into sexual crimes against children. Senators have asked for a full accounting of diverted officers.
