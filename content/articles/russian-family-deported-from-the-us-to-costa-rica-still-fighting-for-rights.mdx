During Trump's hard‑line immigration period, a Russian family was deported from the United States to Costa Rica. They were held in a detention center, treated poorly, and later released without compensation. A Costa Rican court said their rights were violated, but the government still refuses to pay. The family now lives in Monteverde, working jobs and hoping for justice, while the US and Costa Rica face scrutiny over the controversial deportation plan.
