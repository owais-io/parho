Mobile users are getting fake texts that say they have almost 13,000 reward points to redeem for gadgets. The messages look like official alerts from EE or Vodafone, but the links lead to fake sites that ask for bank details. The scam is urgent, promising rewards that expire soon. If you get such a text, don’t click the link, report it, and warn your bank if you shared any information.
