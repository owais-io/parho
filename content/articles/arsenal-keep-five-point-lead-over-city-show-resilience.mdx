Arsenal have kept a five‑point lead over Manchester City after a busy week that saw them beat Tottenham and Bayern and draw Chelsea, even without key defenders. They won 3‑1 against Bayern, showing they can stay calm after setbacks, and held their nerve to draw 2‑2 with Chelsea, keeping the top spot. City have lost some form, dropping points against Newcastle and Leeds. Arsenal’s depth and calmness show they’re ready for the title race.
