President Trump said the U.S. navy will block all sanctioned oil ships that leave or arrive in Venezuela. He claims the move is to stop the country from using oil to fund drug trafficking. The U.S. already has strict oil sanctions on Venezuela and recently seized a tanker carrying Venezuelan crude. Trump’s announcement comes as part of a bigger U.S. military build‑up in the Caribbean, which Caracas sees as a threat to its leader, Nicolás Maduro.
