Axolotls, a small Mexican salamander, have become a worldwide pop‑culture craze. They show up in toys, clothing, video‑games like Minecraft, and even on banknotes. Their cute, smiling face and the fact that they can regrow limbs make them a hit on social media. But in the wild, they’re endangered because of pollution and habitat loss. Conservationists are urging protection while fans enjoy their quirky charm online.
