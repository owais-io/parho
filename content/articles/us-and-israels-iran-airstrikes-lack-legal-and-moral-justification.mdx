U.S. and Israel have launched airstrikes on Iran, claiming they help free Iranians from oppression. But experts say the claims are weak. Unlike Iraq, there is no clear plan to change Iran’s government, and the attacks already hit civilians, even a school that killed 168 people. The letters argue the war ignores international law, shows no responsibility, and could set a dangerous precedent.
