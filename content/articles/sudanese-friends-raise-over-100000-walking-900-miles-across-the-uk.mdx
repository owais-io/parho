Giel Malual and his friend John Kuei, both from Sudan, walked 900 miles from Kent to Scotland in winter to raise money for a school for displaced children. They met thousands of friendly Britons who offered food, beds, and donations. The pair collected more than £100,000, far exceeding their £35,000 goal, showing widespread kindness and support for refugees across the UK.
