Brian Cole Jr., accused of planting pipe bombs outside the Democratic and Republican national committee headquarters in Washington, D.C., has agreed to appear in federal court after confessing. He says he was protesting the 2020 election and didn’t want to kill anyone, but the bombs never exploded. His lawyers want him released on GPS monitoring, citing no danger and his autism and OCD. Prosecutors argue he should stay in jail, facing up to 20 years.
