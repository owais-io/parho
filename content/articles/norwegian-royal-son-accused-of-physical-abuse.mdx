Marius Borg Høiby, the 29‑year‑old son of Norway’s Crown Princess Mette‑Marit, is on trial in Oslo for 38 crimes that include alleged rapes and assaults. His former partner, influencer Nora Haukland, says he punched her, often grabbed her by the throat, and tried to control her career and appearance. Høiby denies the charges and faces up to 16 years in prison. The case adds fresh scrutiny to the royal family.
