More than 3,100 protests are planned across the U.S. and 15 other countries this Saturday under the “No Kings” banner. The movement grew fast after launching in June, with a recent October rally that drew around 7 million people. Organisers expect Saturday’s event to be the biggest U.S. protest ever, but the campaign has no single leader or clear policy demands, raising questions about lasting impact.
