In 2025, President Trump repeated false climate statements. He said California’s delta smelt was worthless, diverting water to people. He blamed offshore wind farms for killing whales, despite no evidence. Trump promoted “clean, beautiful coal” while calling coal clean. He claimed the world used to cool and now cools again, ignoring scientific consensus. He also announced investigations into climate science, targeting scientists and politicians.
