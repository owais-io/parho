After losing his seat in 2025, Adam Bandt decided to quit politics and became the new chief executive of the Australian Conservation Foundation. He says he wants to grow a huge, non‑partisan movement that governments and companies will want to join. Bandt plans to use online campaigns, street rallies, and even DJ sets to make climate action fun and keep the focus on nature. He says the move shows the need to give ordinary people more power than big business.
