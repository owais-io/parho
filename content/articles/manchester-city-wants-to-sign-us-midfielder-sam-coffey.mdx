Manchester City are trying to sign Sam Coffey, a 27‑year‑old US midfielder from the Portland Thorns. The player is allowed to travel to England to finish the deal, which is thought to be almost done. Coffey could help City win their first Women’s Super League title since 2016. She has played 42 times for the USA, scored five goals, and won Olympic gold. Her move would be a loss for the US league.
