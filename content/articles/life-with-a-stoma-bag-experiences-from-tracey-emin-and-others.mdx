Tracey Emin talked about living with a stoma bag after surgery, sharing the challenges she faces. The Guardian asked others who have had similar operations to add their stories. Most people who get a urostomy—about 100 per week in the UK—find they can return to normal activities after a learning curve. The main changes are more frequent bathroom visits and no nighttime trips, which many find helpful.
