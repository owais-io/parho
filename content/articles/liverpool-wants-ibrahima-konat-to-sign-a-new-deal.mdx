Arne Slot, Liverpool’s head coach, says defender Ibrahima Konaté is essential for the team and the club is still trying to get him a new contract before it ends next season. Konaté’s form has improved after personal loss, and he’s been praised as reliable as Virgil van Dijk. Slot says talks are ongoing and Liverpool don’t want him to leave. He also mentioned needing to assess striker Mohamed Salah’s season.
