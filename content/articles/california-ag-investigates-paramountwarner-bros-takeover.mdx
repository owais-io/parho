California’s attorney general, Rob Bonta, has opened a probe into the planned merger of Paramount Skydance with Warner Bros Discovery, which Netflix has just pulled out of. The deal, worth about $111 bn, could trigger job cuts and higher prices, sparking concerns from Senator Elizabeth Warren, actor Mark Ruffalo, and the Writers Guild. Bonta says regulators in the U.S. and Europe must approve the merger before it can happen.
