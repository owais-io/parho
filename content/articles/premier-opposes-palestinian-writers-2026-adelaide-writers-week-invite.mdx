South Australian Premier Peter Malinauskas sent a three‑page letter to the Adelaide Writers’ Week board saying the festival should not invite Palestinian‑Australian author Randa Abdel‑Fattah for 2026. He said her past remarks, seen as antisemitic, could cause division after the Bondi terror attack. The board overruled the decision, later apologised, and will invite her in 2027. Abdel‑Fattah has threatened defamation action against the premier.
