The US and Israel used the AI program Claude, supplied by Anthropic, to plan and launch nearly 900 strikes on Iranian targets in just 12 hours. The AI cuts the “kill chain” from target spotting to legal approval to launch, shrinking decision time from days to seconds. While the speed gives the US a huge advantage, experts warn it may let human commanders become passive “rubber‑stamps” and risk mistakes. The rapid attacks also caused civilian casualties.
