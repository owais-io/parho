Sussan Ley has announced a temporary all‑Liberal shadow cabinet, leaving the Nationals to decide within eight days whether to rebuild the coalition. She has reassigned National portfolios to Liberal MPs in the meantime and warned that if the coalition isn’t re‑formed by 9 February, some Nationals will be moved out of the cabinet. Nationals leader David Little‑proud has declined an immediate meeting, citing a planned leadership vote, while Ley faces possible internal challenges.
