Eton College headteacher Simon Henderson said he was appalled and offered a public apology after former Russian teacher Jacob Leland was jailed for 3 years 3 months for sexually assaulting a boy at the school. Leland abused the pupil in his flat and during a school trip in 2012. The incident highlights the school’s responsibility to protect students and its commitment to safeguarding.
