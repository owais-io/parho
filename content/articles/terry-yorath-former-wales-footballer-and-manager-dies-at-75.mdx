Terry Yorath, a Wales football legend, passed away at 75. He played for Leeds, Coventry, and Tottenham, winning a First Division title and reaching a European Cup final. He later managed Wales from 1988 to 1993, lifting the team to its best‑ever world ranking and nearly reaching the 1994 World Cup. He also coached in Lebanon, led clubs like Swansea and Bradford, and faced personal loss after his son died. Yorath is survived by three children.
