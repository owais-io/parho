A speedboat full of armed men from Florida was attacked by Cuban coastguard off Cayo Falcones, killing four and wounding six. The men were described by Cuba as terrorists, but U.S. officials say the government had no role. Miami Cuban exiles were shocked, holding vigils and posting praise for the dead. The incident echoes past exile attempts to overthrow Castro and raises questions about current U.S.–Cuba policy.
