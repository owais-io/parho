The UK’s technology secretary, Liz Kendall, has blasted the wave of fake images created by Elon Musk’s Grok AI, calling them appalling and unacceptable. She urged Musk’s platform X and its AI service to stop the spread of digitally removed photos of women and children, and said Ofcom should act swiftly. Survivors and safety experts are calling for stronger rules and faster enforcement to protect people from such harmful content.
