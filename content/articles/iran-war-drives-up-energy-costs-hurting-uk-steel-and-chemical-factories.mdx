The war in Iran has pushed gas and electricity prices higher, putting pressure on UK factories that use a lot of energy. Somers Forge in the Black Country is already paying more for gas and has to cut back. Chemical plants face a double hit – higher energy costs and more expensive gas feedstock. Some firms risk shutting down or moving production overseas, and the crisis is also affecting European supply lines.
