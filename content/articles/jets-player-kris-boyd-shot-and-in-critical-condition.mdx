Kris Boyd, a New York Jets cornerback, was shot in his abdomen early Sunday morning on West 38th Street in Midtown. He was taken to Bellevue Hospital, where doctors say he’s in critical but stable condition. Police are looking for a suspect who fled in a BMW SUV. Boyd, who was drafted by the Vikings in 2019, had not played this season due to a shoulder injury. He has played for the Texans, Cardinals and Jets.
