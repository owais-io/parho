England captain Maro Itoje is targeting a 2027 Rugby World Cup win after the recent draw gave England a relatively easy path. They face Wales, Tonga and Zimbabwe in Pool F and could meet Italy, Australia, Ireland or Argentina in the last‑16. England are ranked third and have won 11 straight games. Both Itoje and coach Steve Borthwick said they’re building up through 2025‑26 to be ready. Other teams like Wales, Scotland and Tonga have mixed prospects.
