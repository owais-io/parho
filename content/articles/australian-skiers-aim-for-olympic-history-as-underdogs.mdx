Reilly Flanagan, a 21‑year‑old aerial skier, is on the brink of making history. If he qualifies for the Winter Olympics, Australia will compete in the mixed team event for the first time. Flanagan is still trying to meet the individual quota at upcoming qualifiers, while cross‑country skier Hannah Price also fights for a spot. Both hope to spark more interest in winter sports in a country that sees itself as an underdog.
