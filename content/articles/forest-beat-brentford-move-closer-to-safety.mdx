Nottingham Forest beat Brentford 2‑0, thanks to goals from Igor Jesus and Taiwo Awoniyi. The win lifts Forest to a safer spot in the Premier League and shows their growing resilience after a Europa League loss. Manager Sean Dyche praised the team's patience and teamwork, while Brentford’s coach urged his side to be more ruthless. Forest’s victory keeps them out of immediate relegation danger.
