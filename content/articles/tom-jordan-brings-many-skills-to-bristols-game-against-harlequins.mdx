Tom Jordan, a 27‑year‑old Scottish rugby player, has grown from an overlooked youth in New Zealand to a key player for Bristol Bears and the Scottish national team. He can pass, tackle, and kick, and he plays several positions. After moving to Scotland, he impressed at Glasgow Warriors and earned a spot in the national squad. Tonight he faces Harlequins, hoping Bristol can finish strong before the Six Nations break.
