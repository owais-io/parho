The BBC is about to pick a new director‑general, and many hope it will be a woman. But the station faces a serious problem: older female presenters vanish from shows while older men stay. A recent report shows women over 60 are pushed out or ignored. The new boss must change the culture, give fair chances, and stop women from being phased out.
