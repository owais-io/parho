After a wild 3-3 game at Old Trafford, manager Ruben Amorim said the team doesn’t need to sign more defenders. He praised the squad’s talent but said they must defend better. He blamed mistakes from Shaw, Dalot, and others for the four goals conceded. Amorim said United should change how they start the second half and stay united on the field.
