A major water outage has hit 30,000 homes in Kent and Sussex, leaving towns like Tunbridge Wells, Canterbury and Maidstone without running water for up to five days. Schools, libraries and even a hospital have closed or shifted to virtual appointments. Cold weather and Storm Goretti burst pipes and over‑topped the water plant, South East Water said. The government has called a major incident, met with the company, and promised a quick fix.
