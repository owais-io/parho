Weight‑loss drugs can cut pounds, but they’re not a silver bullet. The article explains that GLP‑1 medicines hit the brain’s reward system and can be part of a plan, but lasting change comes from good food, exercise, sleep and habits. Doctors, patients and readers agree that medicine alone can’t solve obesity; a full program that mixes drugs with lifestyle changes gives the best results.
