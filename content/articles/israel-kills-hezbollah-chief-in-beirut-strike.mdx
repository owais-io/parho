Israeli forces bombed a building in Beirut's southern suburbs and killed Hezbollah's top commander, Haytham Tabatabai. The attack also injured dozens and was part of a series of airstrikes Israel has been running to weaken the group. Hezbollah said the strike was a betrayal, while Israel said it would keep fighting to stop the militia from threatening Israel again. The incident heightens tensions after a ceasefire ended last year's war.
