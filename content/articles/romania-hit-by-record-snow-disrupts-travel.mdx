A severe storm dumped 40 cm of snow in Bucharest, far more than usual, shutting airports, delaying trains up to six hours, and blocking main roads. The heavy snow broke trees and power lines, leaving 200,000 homes without electricity. In France, the wet winter continues: Storm Pedro added wind and rain, worsening floods after Storm Nils killed two people. In the US, warmer‑than‑normal temperatures fuel wildfires in Colorado and Oklahoma.
