Marine Le Pen is on appeal in Paris, denying that her National Rally ran a fake‑job scheme to siphon €4.8 million from EU parliament funds. She claims she never asked MEPs to hire assistants for the party. The case could decide whether she can run in the 2027 election after a previous trial banned her from office for five years.
