Anthony Joshua beat YouTuber‑turned‑boxer Jake Paul in Miami. After a powerful right hand in round six, Paul’s jaw snapped and he could not continue. The bout was one‑sided; Paul had been knocked down four times, while Joshua struggled early and missed many punches. Joshua admitted the fight was tough after a 15‑month break. Paul posted a broken‑jaw X‑ray and vows to fight again, hinting at a future big match.
