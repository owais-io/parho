Last year’s Wakati Wetu arts festival in Nairobi used film, comedy and talks to push for reparations for slavery and colonialism. The African Union now declared 2025 the Year of Reparations and 2026‑2036 the Decade, and Ghana’s leaders are pushing the idea at the UN. European countries have been slow or reluctant to pay, but Germany offered aid while refusing to call it reparations. The movement hopes art can spark wider public support.
