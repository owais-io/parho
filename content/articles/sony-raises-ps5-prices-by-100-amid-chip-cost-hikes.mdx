Sony is raising the price of its PlayStation 5 by $100 in the US, with similar hikes worldwide. The jump comes as memory chip makers favor high‑margin data‑center chips to meet AI demand, tightening supply for consoles. The new US price for a standard PS5 is $649.99, the digital edition $599.99, and the PS5 Pro $899.99. The increase could cool console sales and hurt the video‑game market, prompting job cuts at Epic Games.
