US and Israeli officials aim to damage Iran's nuclear, missile and army. They hope a war could spark a popular uprising that ends the 47‑year rule. Experts outline four possible results, from a quick peaceful transition to a new interim government, to the regime surviving and making concessions, to a hardening and deeper underground nuclear program, to a chaotic civil war. The most likely outcome is the regime staying but becoming more ruthless.
