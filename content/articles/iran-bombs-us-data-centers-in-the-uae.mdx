During the US‑Israel‑Iran conflict, Iran fired drones that hit three Amazon Web Services data‑center sites in the UAE and Bahrain. The attacks sparked fires and shut down power, disrupting services for millions of people in Dubai and Abu Dhabi. It is the first known attack on a commercial data‑center by a state during wartime. The strikes also raised questions about the role of data‑centers in supporting military activities and the vulnerability of critical infrastructure.
