Early Thursday, Nottingham Forest dismissed Sean Dyche after 114 days in charge, sparking a quick search for a new head coach. The club is eyeing former Wolves boss Vítor Pereira, who left Molineux last November. Forest wants a manager with strong people skills, similar to Nuno Espírito Santo, to steer the team away from the bottom of the Premier League and prepare for an upcoming Europa League match.
