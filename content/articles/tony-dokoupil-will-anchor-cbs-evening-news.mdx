CBS News is changing its evening anchor. Editor‑in‑chief Bari Weiss chose Tony Dokoupil, a 44‑year‑old morning‑show co‑host, to lead the CBS Evening News from January 5. Dokoupil is known for asking hard questions, and his tough interview with Ta‑Nehisi Coates made headlines. The network hopes he can rebuild trust after two anchors left the show. He’ll start with a cross‑country tour to connect with viewers.
