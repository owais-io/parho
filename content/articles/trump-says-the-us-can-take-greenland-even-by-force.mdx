The U.S. president says taking Greenland is a national‑security priority and that using the military is always an option. Danish and Greenlandic leaders, joined by France, Germany, Britain, have demanded the U.S. respect their sovereignty and have asked for a meeting with the U.S. secretary of state. Most Americans and many politicians reject the idea, and Denmark says no force will be used. Trump’s comments have sparked tension across NATO.
