I’m a parent of a child with complex needs and no speech. I joined legal action after the government proposed changes that would weaken SEND plans and limit appeals. The education secretary says lawyers exploit families, but these lawyers are simply enforcing the law. The fight is about keeping the support a child is entitled to, not attacking the legal system. Families rely on legal help to secure the rights promised.
