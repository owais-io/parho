President Trump said the U.S. will let Nvidia ship its H200 AI chips to approved customers in China and other countries, lifting a ban that had been in place because of national‑security concerns. Nvidia’s CEO Jensen Huang has worked closely with Trump and plans big U.S. AI investments. Some lawmakers worry the sale could aid China’s surveillance and military programs and have requested hearings.
