President Claudia Sheinbaum has flown 37 cartel members from Mexican prisons to the United States to show the U.S. she is tackling drug‑trafficking. She says the move is a sovereign decision, but Trump keeps claiming Mexico is run by cartels and threatening tougher action. While the flights act as a pressure valve, experts worry the U.S. may eventually push for military help, which Mexico refuses.
