England coach Steve Borthwick has added George Furbank back into his 36‑man Six Nations squad after the full‑back missed the last autumn tests because of a calf injury. Furbank hasn’t played for Northampton since December. The team also includes three uncapped players—Greg Fisilau, Billy Sella and Emmanuel Iyogun—to fill gaps caused by injuries to several props. The squad will head to a warm‑weather training camp in Girona before the championship starts.
