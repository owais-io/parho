Apple’s new iPhone 17e is the company’s cheapest model. It gets a faster A19 chip, double the base storage to 256 GB, better battery life, and automatic portrait mode. The design stays the same as last year’s 16e, with a notch and a 6.1‑inch OLED screen. It lacks extra camera lenses, Wi‑Fi 7, and some smart‑home features. For budget buyers who don’t need a fancy camera, the 17e is a solid, affordable choice.
