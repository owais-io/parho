The Liberal Party of Victoria made a playful video with its leaders doing mock sports to mark the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Games that never happened. They used memes, silly stunts and the track Pump Up the Jam, hoping to show unity and win voters ahead of the state election. The clip sparked laughs and criticism, with some seeing it as political spin and others calling it a clever marketing move.
