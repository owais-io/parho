UK bond traders panicked when Chancellor Rachel Reeves said she would scrap a planned income‑tax hike in the autumn budget. 10‑year gilt yields jumped over 0.13 points to about 4.58%, the highest in a month, and the pound slipped against the dollar. The FTSE 100 fell more than 1%. The move raised doubts about Labour’s fiscal plans and even the party’s leadership, adding extra tension to already nervous markets.
