Comedian Russell Brand has been charged with two new rape and sexual assault allegations in a UK court, even though he is currently in Florida. The accusations date back to 2009 involving two women, and he had already faced earlier charges from 1999‑2005. He appeared via video link, said his name and age, and was granted bail to attend a hearing in London on 17 February. A full trial is set for later this year.
