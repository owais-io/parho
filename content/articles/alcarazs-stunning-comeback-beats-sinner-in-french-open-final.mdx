In June 2025, Carlos Alcaraz stunned the world by pulling off one of the greatest comebacks in tennis history. After Jannik Sinner dominated early, Alcaraz fought back from a huge deficit, saving multiple match points and winning five sets, including two tense tiebreaks. The match lasted over five hours, with Alcaraz turning the tide in the final minutes. It’s seen as a defining moment that could launch his career to legendary status.
