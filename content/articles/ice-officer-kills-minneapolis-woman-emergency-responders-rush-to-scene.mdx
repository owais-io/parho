A 37‑year‑old woman named Renee Good was shot by an ICE officer in her car in Minneapolis on January 7. Paramedics arrived at 9:42 am and found her unresponsive with multiple gunshot wounds. She was taken to a hospital but died about 10:30 am. Calls to 911 described shots fired at her windshield, and witnesses said ICE agents were on the scene. The shooting sparked protests, and Trump said he might use the Insurrection Act in Minnesota.
