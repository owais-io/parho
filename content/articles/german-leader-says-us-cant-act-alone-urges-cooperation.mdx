At the Munich Security Conference, German Chancellor Friedrich Merz told U.S. President Donald Trump that America can’t handle global security alone. He urged the U.S. to work with allies, especially NATO, and said Europe should build its own defence and even its own nuclear deterrent after talks with France. Merz criticised Trump’s tariffs and unilateral moves, called for stronger cooperation, and stressed Europe’s need to counter Russia.
