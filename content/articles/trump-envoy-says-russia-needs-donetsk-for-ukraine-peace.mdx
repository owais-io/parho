Trump’s special envoy, Steve Witkoff, told a senior Kremlin aide that a Ukraine peace deal would need Russia to control Donetsk and possibly swap some land. He also urged the Russian official to keep the talk upbeat and to congratulate Trump. Many U.S. officials and people involved in the war say the plan embarrasses the U.S. and could backfire. The conversation was recorded and released by Bloomberg.
