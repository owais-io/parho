Arsenal defender Gabriel Magalhães hurt his thigh while playing for Brazil, so he might miss the North London derby against Tottenham. Brazil has pulled him from the squad and he will be treated at Arsenal. Gabriel has played every minute this season and is a key part of a defence that has kept many clean sheets. Arsenal can rely on backup defenders and hope other injured players return soon.
