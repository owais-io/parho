The Daily Telegraph is now owned by Lord Rothermere after a £500m deal with Daily Mail and General Trust. RedBird IMI’s offer was pulled amid worries about foreign influence. Rothermere already owns nearly 10% of the paper, so the takeover felt inevitable. Critics warn the deal will pile even more right‑leaning voices onto the UK press and hurt media diversity. The government says it will review the purchase.
