Newcastle United’s long‑awaited Carabao Cup win over Manchester City gave the club its first trophy in 56 years and proved the team is now in a ‘must‑win or miss’ phase. Manager Eddie Howe said the club’s growth since the Saudi takeover has turned it into a serious contender. The match was hot, with a late goal from Antoine Semenyo sealing a 1‑0 victory and keeping the team’s momentum alive.
