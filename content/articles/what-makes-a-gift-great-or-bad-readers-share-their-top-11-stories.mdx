The Guardian asks people to recall the best and worst gifts they’ve ever received. Readers describe everything from life‑changing trips and handmade gifts to odd items like a plastic “bat’s head” and anti‑cellulite cream. The stories show how a good gift is thoughtful and useful, while a bad one feels awkward or unwanted. The piece reminds us to give something that matters to the receiver, not just what we like.
