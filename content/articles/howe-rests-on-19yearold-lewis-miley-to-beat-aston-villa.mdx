Newcastle United boss Eddie Howe is preparing for a big Premier League clash with Aston Villa. His key player, Bruno Guimarães, is sidelined after an ankle injury, so Howe plans to step in a 19‑year‑old midfielder, Lewis Miley, who has been showing promise. The win could keep Newcastle in a top‑four spot and help them stay in Europe. Howe also talks about the team’s tactics and how the young talent can help the club.
