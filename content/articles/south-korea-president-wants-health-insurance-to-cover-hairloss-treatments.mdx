South Korean President Lee Jae‑Myung has asked the government to add hair‑loss treatments to public health insurance. He says baldness is now a survival issue for young people, not just a cosmetic one. Doctors and conservatives say the money would be better spent on serious diseases. The proposal is controversial because the country’s insurance system is already under financial pressure. The debate shows how much pressure South Korea places on looks.
