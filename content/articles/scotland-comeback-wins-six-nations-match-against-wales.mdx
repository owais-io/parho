Scotland turned a 12‑point deficit into a win against Wales, ending the game 23‑17. The boost came from Finn Russell’s quick tries, while Wales’ early lead slipped away as injuries and pressure from the Scots helped shift momentum. It was Scotland’s first Six Nations victory since 2023, giving their fans hope and keeping them in the hunt for the championship.
