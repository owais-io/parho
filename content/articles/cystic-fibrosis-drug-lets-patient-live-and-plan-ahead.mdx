Nicole Adams, 34, has lived with cystic fibrosis since childhood. A new medicine, Kaftrio, developed by Queen’s University researchers, has turned her life around. After a severe lung infection, the drug lifted her out of the hospital and improved breathing in just days. With the treatment, Nicole now plans long‑term, even has a child, and feels she can look forward to the future.
