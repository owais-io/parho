The new head of MI6, Blaise Metreweli, said Britain is stuck between peace and war, especially as AI and tech fall into the hands of firms. She accused Russia of dragging out talks over Ukraine and warned that AI drones, lethal bioweapons and disinformation could change how wars are fought. While she avoided a detailed China critique, she said the country is a key part of global change. The spy agency must now learn technology and alert the government.
