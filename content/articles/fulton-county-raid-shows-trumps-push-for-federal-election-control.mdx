An FBI raid on Fulton County’s election office in Georgia shocked the nation. Officials seized ballots and records with no proof of fraud, and the move came after Trump urged Republicans to take control of state elections. The raid shows how Trump’s agenda—voter‑suppression bills, immigrant raids and erasing Black history—aims to silence minority voices and keep power in his hands.
