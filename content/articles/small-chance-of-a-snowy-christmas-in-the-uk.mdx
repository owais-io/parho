Experts say a wintry snap could bring a white Christmas in the UK, but the chance is only about 10%. Some parts, especially the south coast, might see a light flurry. Temperatures on Christmas Day are expected to rise to around 7 °C and drop to –3 °C at night. Historically, only a handful of years have had snow on the ground, and warming temperatures are making it rarer.
