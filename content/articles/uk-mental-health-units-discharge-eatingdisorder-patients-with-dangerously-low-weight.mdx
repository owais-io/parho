Patients with eating disorders are being sent home from NHS mental‑health wards even though their weight is critically low. An FOI request revealed that nine trusts discharged 119 patients with a BMI under 15, including one as low as 12.5. Doctors warn this can cause relapse or death, while the NHS says it follows guidelines. Critics see it as a failure to keep patients safe.
