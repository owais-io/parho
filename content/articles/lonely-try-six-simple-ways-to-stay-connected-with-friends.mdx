Lonely despite a full schedule? The article shows six easy tricks to feel more connected. Try sending a short voice note instead of a text, hop on a quick video call, or set a regular “standing” catch‑up like a weekly Zoom. Play online board games, send simple “thinking of you” messages, and set boundaries on constant texting. The key is to be proactive and flexible so you get the quality time you need.
