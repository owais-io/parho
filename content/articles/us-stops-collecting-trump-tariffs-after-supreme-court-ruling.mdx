The Supreme Court ruled that the tariffs Trump put in place under emergency powers were illegal, so the US Customs and Border Protection agency will stop collecting them. The trade negotiator says the overall policy stays the same, just the legal tools change. The decision hit markets – the dollar fell a bit, gold jumped, and the S&P 500 slipped slightly.
