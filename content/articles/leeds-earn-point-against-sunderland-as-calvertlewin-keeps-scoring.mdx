Leeds played Sunderland at the Stadium of Light and finished 1‑1. Dominic Calvert‑Lewin scored his seventh goal in six games, showing he’s back in form and keeping manager Daniel Farke’s job safe. Sunderland opened the scoring with Simon Adingra’s first goal for the club, but Leeds equalised through Calvert‑Lewin after a clever pass from Aaronson. The match was a reminder that promoted teams can compete well in the Premier League.
