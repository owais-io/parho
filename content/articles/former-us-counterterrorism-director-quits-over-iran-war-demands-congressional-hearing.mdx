Joe Kent, the head of the National Counterterrorism Center, left his post because he believes the US is fighting a war in Iran that poses no real threat. California’s Rep. Ro Khanna and Senator Patty Murray are urging him to testify before Congress to explain why the administration pushed the country into this conflict. The move sparks debate over the US’s involvement in Iran.
