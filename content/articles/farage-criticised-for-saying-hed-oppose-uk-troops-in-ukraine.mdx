Nigel Farage, leader of Reform UK, said he would vote against sending British troops to Ukraine. A cabinet minister accused him of echoing Russian propaganda and said his stance hurt national security. Farage made the comments on Times Radio, missing a parliamentary session. He has previously praised Putin and warned the West against provoking Russia. Labour politicians used his remarks to label him a “Putin puppet.”
