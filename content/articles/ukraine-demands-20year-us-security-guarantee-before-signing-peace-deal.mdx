Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelensky, told a Munich summit that the country will not sign a peace deal with Russia unless the United States promises a clear, 20‑year security guarantee. He said the U.S. had offered only 15 years, which is too short. Zelensky also asked for a definite EU‑entry date and criticized the U.S. and Europe for not being clear about their commitments. The talks involve U.S. officials, European leaders, and Russia, with Trump urging a quick agreement.
