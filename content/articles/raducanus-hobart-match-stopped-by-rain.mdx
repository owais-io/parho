Emma Raducanu’s first‑round match in Hobart was halted overnight by rain. The 22‑year‑old was up 6‑3, 2‑4 against Colombia’s Camila Osorio when the tournament paused. Raducanu, the top seed, has had a tough 2026 start after a foot injury that cut her 2025 season short. She only returned to proper training at the end of December and is still finding confidence on the court. She aims to finish the match on Wednesday.
