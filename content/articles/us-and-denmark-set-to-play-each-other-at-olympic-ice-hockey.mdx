At the Milan Cortina Winter Olympics, the U.S. and Denmark will play each other on Feb. 14 at the Santagiulia arena. The game comes amid heated U.S. politics over Greenland, where President Trump wants the island. Denmark’s leaders and thousands of citizens have protested, saying Greenland is not for sale. The U.S. team is ranked first in the world and includes many NHL stars, while Denmark is an underdog but has NHL players too.
