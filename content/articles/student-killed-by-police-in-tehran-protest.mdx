A 23‑year‑old student, Rubina Aminian, was shot in the head by police during protests in Tehran, according to a human rights group. The Kurdish woman left Shariati College to join the demonstration and was killed by a close‑range bullet from behind. Her family was prevented from burying her in Kermanshah and had to lay her to rest on a road. Officials say at least 538 people died, with over 10,600 arrests.
