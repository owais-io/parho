Mark Carney spoke with Donald Trump after the former president posted a rant saying the $4.6 billion Gordie Howe International Bridge would stay shut. Carney reminded Trump that Canada financed the bridge and that ownership is shared with Michigan, involving both Canadian and U.S. steel and workers. Trump’s false claim sparked ridicule from Canadians, while officials said the bridge will open in early 2026 as planned.
