Historic preservationists have filed a federal lawsuit against former President Trump to stop the demolition of the White House’s East Wing for a new $300 million ballroom. They say Trump broke federal laws by tearing down the building without required reviews or Congress approval. The court is asked to halt construction while the proper procedures are followed. Most Americans oppose the project, and it’s being funded by wealthy donors.
