Keir Starmer, the UK’s Prime Minister, has shifted from a quiet, diplomatic tone to a firmer stance after US President Donald Trump threatened to impose 25% tariffs on European allies. Starmer said Britain will not back down over issues like Greenland and Chagos and warned that US tariffs would hurt the UK economy. He’s ready to respond with retaliation if needed.
