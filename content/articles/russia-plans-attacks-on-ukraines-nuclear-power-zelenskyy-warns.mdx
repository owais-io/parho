Russia is preparing to hit Ukraine’s nuclear power plants and energy sites, the president said after a briefing with the defence‑intelligence chief. He warned that attacks in winter will hurt U.S. and allies’ efforts to end the war. Meanwhile, Ukraine is negotiating a peace deal in Miami that would need U.S. backing. Russia refuses any deal unless all of Donbas is surrendered. Recent strikes left houses and power plants damaged; Lithuania is sending equipment and money to help restore electricity.
