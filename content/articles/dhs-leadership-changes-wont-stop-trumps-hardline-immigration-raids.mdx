Kristi Noem was fired as DHS secretary, but the Trump administration’s aggressive immigration policy keeps going. Activists say violence and mass deportations still happen in cities, and the agency has no new oversight. Even after Noem’s exit, agents stay in Minnesota and other states, and the government plans to build more detention centers. The change in leadership won’t stop the hard‑line tactics.
