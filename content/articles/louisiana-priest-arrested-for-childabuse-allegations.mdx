Authorities in southwest Louisiana have taken a 37‑year‑old priest, Korey LaVergne, into custody on charges of indecent behavior with a child. He was booked after leading mass at St. Edward’s Church in Richard. The arrest comes while the Lafayette diocese faces lawsuits that could cost up to $162 million. The case adds to a long list of abuse claims and financial woes for the local Catholic community.
