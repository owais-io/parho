Zohran Mamdani, the city’s first Muslim mayor, was sworn in by Senator Bernie Sanders. He promised free childcare, free buses, a rent freeze for a million families, and city‑run grocery stores, but the plan will cost about $10 billion. To fund it, he plans to raise taxes on the richest New Yorkers and corporations. He’ll need state support and will face criticism from Donald Trump, while pledging to govern boldly and keep working people at the center.
