A House committee released videos of former President Bill Clinton and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton giving testimony about their encounters with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. Bill said he had ended his friendship with Epstein years before Epstein’s 2008 conviction, and that he had never seen evidence of trafficking. Hillary claimed she never even met him and criticized Republican oversight for lacking transparency. The footage shows both trying to explain their distance from the scandal.
