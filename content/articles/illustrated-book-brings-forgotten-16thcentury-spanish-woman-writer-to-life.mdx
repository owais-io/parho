Beatriz Bernal wrote Spain’s first female novel, a chivalric adventure called *Cristalián*. Though long overlooked, a new illustrated version by author‑illustrator Diego Arboleda makes the story fun for kids. The book shows strong female heroes and clever magic, breaking the old idea that only men could write such tales. It gives today’s readers a chance to enjoy a classic story written by a woman, long ignored in history.
