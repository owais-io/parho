Scammers on TikTok use AI‑made videos of Princess Leonor to lure users into paying fake fees, then demand more money and vanish. The Princess of Asturias Foundation says it never offers financial help and urges people to ignore such posts. Reports show fake phone numbers from the Dominican Republic and videos that have been viewed over a million times. TikTok denies the content violates its rules, leaving victims without protection.
