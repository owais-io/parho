On Friday, the Justice Department released FBI notes that expose how Jeffrey Epstein demanded under‑age girls for sex. The notes, part of a long‑awaited but heavily redacted release, show a 13‑page handwritten interview from May 2019. They reveal Epstein’s picky requests about age, race and “massage” set‑ups, and mention places in New York and a high‑school prom. The documents add new details to the story of Epstein, his associates and the ongoing investigations.
