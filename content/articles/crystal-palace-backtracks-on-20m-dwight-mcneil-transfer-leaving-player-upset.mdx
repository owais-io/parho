Crystal Palace decided at the last minute not to sign 26‑year‑old winger Dwight McNeil from Everton, after a £20 million deal seemed almost final. The club had already sent paperwork to the Premier League, but then changed their mind and made no contact with McNeil or his club. McNeil’s partner, Megan Sharpley, said the sudden radio silence broke his heart and raised worries about how clubs treat players’ mental health. Everton said they still support him.
