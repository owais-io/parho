A song performed by a virtual singer named Nava, created by London‑based artist Farbod Mehr, has turned into a rallying cry for Iranians amid crackdowns on protests and US‑Israeli air strikes. The track blends classic Iranian lyrics with a modern melody, reaching millions on Instagram. Although debates arose over whether Nava is real, many listeners connect with her voice as a symbol of resistance.
