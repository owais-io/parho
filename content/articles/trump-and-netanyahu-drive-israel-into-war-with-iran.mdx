Israel’s prime minister Benjamin Netanyahu has pushed his country into a war with Iran, and President Trump has largely supported the move. Israeli air raids on Iran’s South Pars gas field and other attacks triggered a spike in global oil prices and prompted Iran to hit Gulf oil facilities. Diplomacy in Geneva almost worked, but both leaders disagreed. The fighting is hurting civilians, draining economies, and leaving the U.S. uncertain about its role.
