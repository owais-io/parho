More than 300 demonstrations are happening across every state and Washington D.C. this weekend to protest ICE. Organized by the grassroots group 50501, the protests respond to recent killings by ICE agents and a broader pattern of force and abuse. Tactics include vigils, marches, banners, airport pickets, and campaigns to stop hotels and businesses from aiding ICE. Protesters also want Congress to cut ICE funding, create a code of conduct, and investigate the agency. They’re pressing for accountability and reform.
