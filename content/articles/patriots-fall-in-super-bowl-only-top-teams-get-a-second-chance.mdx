New England Patriots lost the Super Bowl to the Seattle Seahawks 29‑13. The game showed why only the very best teams get a second chance. New England’s offense struggled against Seattle’s tough defense, and their rookie offensive line was overrun. The Patriots’ defense did their part, but the weak pass protection left their young quarterback in trouble. The loss reminds fans that a single championship rarely guarantees a future run.
