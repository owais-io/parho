Tottenham boss Thomas Frank used a three‑centre‑back set‑up to surprise Borussia Dortmund, winning 2-0 in the Champions League. After a shaky start, the new plan gave the team extra defensive cover and let the attackers, especially Solanke, move forward. The win keeps Frank’s job safe while his club fights for European survival and hopes to finish the season in a play‑off spot.
