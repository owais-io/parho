EU foreign ministers met in Brussels to discuss several key issues. They warned about Russia’s bombings of Ukraine and called for stronger pressure on Moscow. The meeting also covered the United States’ push to secure Greenland, with Danish officials pushing back against US claims. Other topics included the Middle East, especially Iran, and the controversy over Danish flags at the US embassy in Copenhagen. Europe’s leaders stressed the need for stronger NATO ties and clearer rules for the global order.
