ACM will hold a Zoom meeting for staff to explain its response to the arrest of co‑owner Antony Catalano, who is charged with assaulting a woman. The company says it is “shocked” and has distanced itself from Catalano, who has been placed on leave and is seeking treatment. ACM says it will support its employees while the case is decided in court.
