During an anti‑ICE protest in Santa Ana, California, federal officers fired so‑called ‘less‑lethal’ rounds that hit two protesters in the face. One man, Kaden Rummler, was left blind in one eye after the blast. A second protester, Britain Rodriguez, also received a close‑range shot. The incident sparked anger over the use of force by federal agents, who are normally not tasked with crowd control.
