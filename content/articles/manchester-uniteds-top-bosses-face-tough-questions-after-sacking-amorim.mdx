Manchester United’s top bosses are in a mess after the club fired coach Ruben Amorim. He never changed his 3‑4‑3 style, and the team finished 15th and lost the Europa League final. Executives blamed his stubbornness and poor talk about the squad, the academy, and the transfer budget. They wanted a more attacking team and more midfield depth, but Amorim stayed set‑in‑his‑ways. Now caretaker Darren Fletcher takes over while the club looks for a new manager.
