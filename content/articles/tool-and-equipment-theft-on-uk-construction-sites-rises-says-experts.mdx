Security experts say theft of tools and equipment from construction sites is growing, especially during the Christmas shutdown when sites are closed. Criminal gangs are stealing copper, heavy machinery and expensive tools, costing the industry nearly £100 m a year. The thefts hit small builders hard, with some businesses going out of business. Measures like better lighting, CCTV and stronger laws are being pushed to deter thieves, but the risk is still high during winter.
