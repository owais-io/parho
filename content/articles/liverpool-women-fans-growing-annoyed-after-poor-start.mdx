After losing 3‑0 to Aston Villa, Liverpool Women’s fans are upset. The team has won no matches and scored very few goals, placing them at the bottom of the WSL. Manager Gareth Taylor faces criticism and a call for dismissal after just 10 games. The club sold a key player for £1m, but new signings haven’t helped. Fans hope a win in the next match will lift spirits.
