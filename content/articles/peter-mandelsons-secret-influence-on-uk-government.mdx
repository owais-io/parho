In this column, George Monbiot says Peter Mandelson used his role in the Business, Enterprise and Regulatory Reform department to push corporate interests. He allegedly worked with Jeffrey Epstein, helped privatise Royal Mail, blocked EU labour rules and weakened equality and environmental laws. The piece also criticises Gordon Brown’s PFI scheme, showing how the government gave businesses too much power while keeping risks for the public. Monbiot warns that UK politics still let the rich dominate public policy.
