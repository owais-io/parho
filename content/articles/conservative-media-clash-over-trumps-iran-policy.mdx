Conservative TV hosts and podcasts are fighting over Donald Trump’s plan to involve the United States in a war with Iran. Tucker Carlson, Megyn Kelly, Mark Levin, Ben Shapiro and others are trading insults and accusations. The dispute highlights deeper divisions in right‑wing media about Trump, Israel, and war. Some commentators say the shouting‑match is a symptom of the larger break‑up of the Trump‑MAGA alliance, while others point out it fuels ratings and subscriber growth.
