New evidence shows that border patrol chief Gregory Bovino praised agent Charles Exum after he shot Marimar Martinez, a U.S. citizen, in Chicago. The shooting happened during an immigration crackdown; video shows Exum firing five shots, but later footage suggests he may have driven into her car. Emails and texts reveal Bovino encouraging Exum, calling him a “legend” and urging him to keep going. Lawyers want the documents released.
