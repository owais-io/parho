Heavy rain sent weird sewage balls to Sydney beaches. The blobs came from a huge fatberg stuck in the Malabar sewage outfall. Sydney Water put up warning signs, started clean‑ups and is investigating the problem. The company plans upgrades to its treatment plants and a long‑term repair of the outfall. The incident shows how city sewage can spill onto the coast when things break.
