A huge heatwave is set to hit Australia’s southern states this week, with temperatures expected to rise into the mid‑40s and even higher in inland areas. The extreme heat is the strongest since the 2019‑20 “black summer” and will last for several days, especially in South Australia, Victoria, New South Wales and Queensland. Authorities warn of high fire danger, urging people to stay cool, hydrated, and protect vulnerable pets and families.
