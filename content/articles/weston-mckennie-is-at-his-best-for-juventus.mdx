Weston McKennie has exploded into a key player for Juventus. He’s scored four goals in eight games and can play almost anywhere on the pitch – from full‑back to striker. Coach Luciano Spalletti uses him to shape the team, favouring him on the right side. While the U.S. coach keeps him out of recent friendlies, McKennie's form shows he’s a must‑have for both club and country.
