Donald Trump said he could stop supporting Ukraine and wants its president to hand land over to Russia. He blasted Europe as weak, “decaying” and blamed immigration for its problems. In a long, sometimes confusing interview with Politico, Trump mixed up facts about the war, used far‑right language about immigration and insulted European leaders, calling them “stupid.” His comments suggest a shift away from the U.S. backing of Ukraine.
