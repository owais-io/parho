Two senior Democrats want an investigation into a huge stock‑buying spree by two fossil‑fuel billionaires tied to the former Trump administration. They bought more than a million shares of Venture Global just days after a meeting with White House officials that helped the company get an export permit for Europe. Senators say the timing shows a pay‑to‑play scheme and urge a probe. The company says all trades were legal.
