On Saturday, the United States and Israel launched an air attack on Tehran, targeting Iran’s top officials. The strike began in the morning when people were at work, hitting government buildings. Reports claimed the Supreme Leader was killed, though Iran later denied it. A school was also hit, killing over a hundred civilians. Iran fired missiles across the Gulf, striking the UAE, Bahrain, and Dubai. Both sides blamed each other as tensions escalated.
