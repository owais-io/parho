The Ashes match at the Melbourne Cricket Ground ended in a win for England, but the venue’s two‑day pitch drew criticism. Matt Page, the ground’s curator, apologized for the poor condition, and Australia’s chief executive stayed calm. Joe Root praised the team’s management, saying changes would be wrong. Ben Stokes, England’s captain, is expected to stay on, seeking backing from the board and players to keep improving.
