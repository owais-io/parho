A Swindon woman, Tarryn Baird, died by suicide after years of physical and sexual abuse from her husband, Christopher Trybus. A court trial now shows how his violence escalated, including rapes, beatings, and controlling tactics like phone‑tracking. Doctors and charities warned her of danger, but she feared police would worsen things. The jury heard diary entries and medical records, and Trybus faces manslaughter, rape and abuse charges.
