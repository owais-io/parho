Guardian's two football podcasts won top awards at the Football Supporters' Association ceremony in London. Women’s Football Weekly took the Women’s Football Media of the Year prize, while Football Weekly claimed the Podcast of the Year title after a three‑year streak. The event also named player of the year winners Declan Rice and Alessia Russo, and honoured other media, fan and club categories. Fans cast hundreds of thousands of votes each year.
