The UK’s broadcasting watchdog, Ofcom, has reversed a 2020 decision and will now investigate three complaints about climate‑change denial on TalkTV and TalkRadio. The move comes after pressure from the Good Law Project and a month‑long review that found broadcasters may have breached impartiality rules. Ofcom will look into whether the shows misled viewers and will publish results later.
