After a crushing 48‑7 loss to England, Wales captain Dewi Lake said the team had let fans down. The defeat, England’s 12th straight win, added to Wales’ 22‑loss streak in the last 24 Tests. Lake blamed the team’s mistakes and lack of accuracy, while coach Steve Tandy called the performance “bitterly disappointing.” England celebrated a hat‑trick by Henry Arundell and praised their defence, looking ahead to the next Six Nations clash.
