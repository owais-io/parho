England’s T20 World Cup campaign has been a slow start. After a tense match against Italy in Kolkata, the team admits nerves and lack of big‑moment experience have kept them from firing on all cylinders. With only a handful of World Cup appearances, players feel pressure. They look ahead to Super‑8s against Sri Lanka, New Zealand and Pakistan, hoping past home‑ground success will spark better performances.
