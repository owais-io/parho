France is set to add more nuclear bombs for the first time in decades, and will work more closely with eight European allies on nuclear and conventional weapons. President Macron said Paris could put nuclear‑armed jets in places like Germany and Poland, but he kept the final decision on nuclear use for himself. The move comes as Europe worries about US help fading and Russia, China and other threats rising.
