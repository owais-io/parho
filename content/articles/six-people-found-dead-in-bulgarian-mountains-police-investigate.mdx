Bulgaria’s mysterious mountain deaths: six people found dead in remote peaks, police say some died by suicide, others may have been murdered. The victims were part of a nature‑protecting group near the Serbia border, where illegal logging and smuggling happen. The investigation is slow, rumours spread, and the country’s low trust in authorities is hurting. The case shows Bulgaria’s struggle with truth and misinformation.
