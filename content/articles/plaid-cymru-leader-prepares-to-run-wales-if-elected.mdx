Plaid Cymru’s new leader Rhun ap Iorwerth says he’s ready to run Wales if the party wins the upcoming Senedd elections. He unveiled a 60‑page roadmap that focuses on cutting hospital waiting lists, easing the cost of living, boosting the economy, improving schools and tackling child poverty. The plan is built on a belief in Welsh independence and is designed to work with other parties in a minority government. The conference was lively, with volunteers and even a cheer‑leading show.
