Nat Metcalf, England’s netball captain, is back on the court after giving birth to her daughter Miller. She had an emergency C‑section, faced tough recovery, but got help from a community of athlete‑mothers. Now 33, a Commonwealth champion, she’s training and will play against Jamaica. She says it’s special to show her daughter she can still chase her dream. She hopes her return inspires other mums.
