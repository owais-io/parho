A shooting at a Mardi Gras parade in Clinton, Louisiana, left five people injured, including a six‑year‑old child. Crowd members ran for cover as gunfire rang out shortly after the parade started. Sheriff Jeff Travis said three armed men were taken into custody, but it’s unclear if they fired. Governor Jeff Landry called the incident horrific, and state police are now investigating.
