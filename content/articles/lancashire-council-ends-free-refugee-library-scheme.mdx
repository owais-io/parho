Reform‑UK led Lancashire County Council has decided to drop its 63 libraries from the free Libraries of Sanctuary programme, arguing it isn’t a good use of taxpayers’ money. The move surprised other councillors and drew criticism for targeting refugees and asylum seekers. Libraries normally offer free Wi‑Fi, language help and community events. The council is trying to cut costs, but the scheme was free to join.
