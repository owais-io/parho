Mark Robinson, the former North Carolina lieutenant governor, confessed that he lied about racist comments he posted on a porn site. He said he hid the truth to shield Donald Trump’s reelection, but the scandal exposed during his 2024 campaign caused staff to quit and Trump to distance himself. Robinson promised he would repeat the lie for political expediency. The controversy likely helped Democrat Josh Stein win the governor seat.
