The article shows the behind‑the‑scenes work of the Blackpool Illuminations. A small team builds and repairs the yearly light show that has been running for nearly 150 years. They use old tools and new tech like 3D printers and LEDs to cut power use and withstand stronger winds from climate change. The displays rotate to keep visitors coming back, and they add interactive walks and photo spots to boost local business. They hope the tradition continues for another 150 years.
