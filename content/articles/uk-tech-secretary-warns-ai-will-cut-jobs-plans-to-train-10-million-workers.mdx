Liz Kendall, the UK technology secretary, said that as AI spreads, some jobs will disappear, especially for new graduates in law and finance. She told leaders she’s not sure how many positions will be lost, but the government will train up to 10 million workers in basic AI skills by 2030. The plan includes online courses, support for women in tech, and new “future of work” teams with unions and businesses.
