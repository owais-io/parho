Police are searching two houses linked to former minister Peter Mandelson as part of an investigation that he may have shared secret market data with Jeffrey Epstein. The searches took place in Camden and Wiltshire, where Mandelson now lives. No arrest has been made; detectives are looking for phones, computers and documents. The probe comes after his 2024 dismissal as US ambassador and fresh evidence of ties to Epstein. The case is still under investigation.
