Sundar Pichai, Google’s boss, warns that AI tools can make mistakes, so people should not take their answers at face value. He says AI should be used with other tools and not as the sole source of truth. Google has added an “AI Mode” to search with its Gemini chatbot, and the next version is coming soon. Pichai also notes that no company, not even Google, will be immune if the AI market crashes.
