Europe's leaders say Hungary is sabotaging support for Ukraine by refusing to approve a new sanctions package and a €90 bn loan. The move comes just before the 4th anniversary of Russia’s full‑scale invasion. Hungary wants to keep the EU from hitting Moscow until Ukraine stops stopping Russian oil shipments. Poland and other members call the act political sabotage. Ukraine remains determined to win the war.
