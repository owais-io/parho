The Charlton Athletic club lost a long‑time fan, Norman Barker, who was famous around the ground as “Headphones Norm”. He died after a medical emergency at a match, which caused the game to be abandoned. Fans left flowers, candles and drawings outside the North Stand, and players wore black armbands. Barker had won the club’s Supporter of the Year award and was beloved for his friendly, upbeat spirit. He will be missed.
