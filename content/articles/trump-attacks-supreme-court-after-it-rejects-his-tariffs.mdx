The Supreme Court ruled 6‑3 that most of President Trump’s global tariffs were illegal, striking a blow to his trade policy. Trump angrily blasted the justices on his Truth Social platform, calling them fools and lap dogs, and later announced a new 10 % tariff on all countries. The court’s decision and Trump’s reaction are drawing attention from lawmakers and the public.
