Former Prince Andrew (Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor) was taken into custody by Thames Valley police on suspicion of misconduct in public office. The arrest follows a review of documents from the US Justice Department that show him sharing sensitive information with convicted sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein. King Charles said the law must take its course, while the former duke has been stripped of royal titles but remains in the line of succession. Police are searching homes in Norfolk and Berkshire.
