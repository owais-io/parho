Binance employees discovered that the exchange sent billions to Iranian‑backed groups, including Yemen’s Houthi rebels, despite sanctions. Over 1,500 Iranian‑linked accounts were used, and two accounts moved about $1.7 billion between 2024‑2025. Investigators warned management, but a few were fired or suspended. Binance says it obeyed laws and no staff were let go for whistle‑blowing. The founder, pardoned by Trump, has a history of legal trouble.
