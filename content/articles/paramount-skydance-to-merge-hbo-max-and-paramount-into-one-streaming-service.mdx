Paramount Skydance, the company that owns Paramount+, announced it will combine its platform with HBO Max after buying Warner Bros Discovery. The new service will bring popular shows like The Sopranos and Succession next to Paramount titles such as Yellowstone. CEO David Ellison said the merger could add more than 200 million subscribers while keeping HBO’s brand distinct. The deal faces regulatory scrutiny and critics warn about increased media consolidation.
