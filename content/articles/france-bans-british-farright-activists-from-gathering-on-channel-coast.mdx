French prefects in the north have banned British far‑right activists linked to the Raise the Colours group from gathering in Dunkirk, Calais, Boulogne‑sur‑Mer, Montreuil‑sur‑Mer and Lille. The ban, in force from Friday night until Monday morning, aims to stop an anti‑migrant protest called “Operation Overlord” and prevent clashes with migrants and local authorities. It follows an earlier interior‑ministry ban on some activists and shows France is tightening security around its Channel coast.
