Former UK Foreign Office minister Zac Goldsmith is launching a new sports radio station called trkradio, set to start next month just before the World Cup. He and his brother Ben have invested in the station, which aims to mix sports talk with music and broader content than usual football phone‑ins. Trkradio will compete with BBC Radio 5 Live, Radio 5 Sports Extra and TalkSport.
