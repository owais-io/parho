Liverpool’s match against Sunderland ended in a 1‑1 draw. The only Liverpool goal came when Florian Wirtz’s shot hit the crossbar and ricocheted off defender Nordi Mukiele into the net, giving Sunderland a win‑draw point. Federico Chiesa made a crucial block to stop Sunderland’s winner, while the Premier League champions struggled to break through and missed chances, leaving them in a disappointing home defeat.
