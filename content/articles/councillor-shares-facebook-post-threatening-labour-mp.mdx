A Reform UK councillor, Simon Evans, shared a Facebook post that threatened Labour MP Natalie Fleet with violence, including a fake quote and the words “you should be shot.” Fleet, who has spoken publicly about abuse, said such posts are common but worry her family and other women. Evans deleted the post, apologized, and Reform UK said it was an honest mistake, taking no further action.
