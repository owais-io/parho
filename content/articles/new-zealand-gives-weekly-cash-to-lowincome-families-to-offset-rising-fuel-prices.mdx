New Zealand will give about 150 000 families a weekly cash payment to help with petrol costs. From 1 April, families with children whose parents work and don’t get benefits can receive NZ$50 a week. The support lasts one year or until fuel prices fall below $3 a litre for four weeks. The plan aims to ease the burden on working families but does not help those on benefits.
