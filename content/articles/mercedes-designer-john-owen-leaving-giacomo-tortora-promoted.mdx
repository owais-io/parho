Mercedes chief designer John Owen will leave the team during the upcoming season, staying on until mid‑June to hand over his duties before going on gardening leave. The German outfit says Giacomo Tortora, the current engineering director, will become the new car‑design head. Owen has helped Mercedes win nine championships, and his exit marks the end of an era. Meanwhile, new Audi F1 team unveiled its car and plans to win a title by 2030.
