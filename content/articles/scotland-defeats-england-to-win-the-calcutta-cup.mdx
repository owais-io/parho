Scotland beat England 31‑12 in the Six Nations, ending England’s 12‑match unbeaten streak. Huw Jones scored two tries, while Finn Russell’s skillful play and the Scottish pack’s strong defence kept the hosts in check. The win lifted Scotland’s hopes for the championship and knocked England’s confidence, as their earlier win over Wales had given them a false sense of security.
