An FDA investigation says raw cheddar from California’s Raw Farm may have caused seven E. coli cases in California, Florida and Texas, most of whom are kids under three. The company says it’s wrong and hasn’t recalled anything. Earlier this year California pulled some Raw Farm milk products after bird‑flu was found in a sample. Health officials warn that raw milk can carry dangerous bacteria, yet its popularity is rising.
