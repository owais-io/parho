The UK regulator Ofcom has decided not to investigate 32 complaints about a GB News interview with Donald Trump, who denied climate change and made other controversial claims. Critics argue the regulator ignored possible bias and misinformation, noting the host did not challenge Trump. Ofcom said a surrounding panel discussion offered alternative views, but the move has raised doubts about its willingness to enforce impartiality.
