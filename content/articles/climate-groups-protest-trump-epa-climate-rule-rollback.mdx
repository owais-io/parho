On Wednesday climate activists and lawmakers marched outside the EPA to protest the Trump administration’s plan to scrap the 2009 endangerment finding that gives the EPA legal power to regulate greenhouse gases. They say the move is corrupt and will hurt the planet and public health. The EPA says it will save money, but scientists warn it could cost trillions in damages. Environmental groups plan to sue.
