Former footballer Joey Barton was sentenced to six months in prison, but it will be suspended for 18 months. He posted abusive messages from January to March 2024, calling pundits Lucy Ward, Eni Aluko, and broadcaster Jeremy Vine names that likened them to serial killers and accused Vine of paedophilia. The court said his comments were more than free speech and caused real distress. Barton must do 200 hours of community work, pay costs, and obey restraining orders that block any future posts about the victims.
