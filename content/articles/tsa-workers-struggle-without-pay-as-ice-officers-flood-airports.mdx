TSA workers have been working without pay for six weeks because the Department of Homeland Security is shut down. More than 400 officers have quit and thousands are calling out, causing longer lines at airports. To fill gaps, President Trump sent ICE officers to 14 airports, but they lack aviation‑security training, raising safety concerns and hurting morale. Workers worry about pay, costs, and the shutdown’s future.
