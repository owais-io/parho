Senate Democrats voted 52‑47 to block a DHS funding bill because of ICE’s actions, including two deaths in Minneapolis. They demand reforms such as body cameras and limits on ICE operations. The move will shut down most DHS services, but ICE and Border Patrol still get money. This action follows a Trump‑era crackdown and highlights the Senate's insistence on accountability.
