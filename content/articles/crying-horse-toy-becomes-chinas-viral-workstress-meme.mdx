A red horse plush from a Yiwu factory was meant to grin, but an error flipped its smile, making its nostrils look like tears. The ‘crying horse’ quickly went viral on Chinese social media, resonating with workers who feel exhausted by long hours. The toy’s popularity has spiked orders, with the company adding new lines and even selling it overseas.
