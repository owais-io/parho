Police say a 51‑year‑old man from Kettering drowned in the sea near Brighton Palace Pier on New Year’s Day. He was pulled out of the water, taken to the shore, but died before he could be treated. The case isn’t suspected to be foul play. The incident comes after other cold‑water accidents, including missing men in Devon. The RNLI warns people about the dangers of cold water and urges those who swim to check conditions and their health first.
