House Republicans rejected a Senate‑approved bill that would have funded most of the Department of Homeland Security but left out money for ICE and part of CBP. Speaker Mike Johnson will push a short‑term stop‑gap bill to keep DHS running until May 22. The decision risks keeping the 42‑day partial government shutdown, leaving TSA workers unpaid and travelers stuck in long lines. Democrats demanded reforms, and Trump has criticized the deal.
