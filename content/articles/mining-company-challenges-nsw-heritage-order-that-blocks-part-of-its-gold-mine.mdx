Regis Resources, a gold mining company, is asking a federal court to cancel a heritage protection order that blocks part of its Blayney mine in NSW. The company says the government didn’t follow proper steps to evaluate a Dreaming story that led to the order, and that the extra info was added too late. It argues the order would make the mine unworkable. The government defends its process and says the order protects important Aboriginal heritage.
