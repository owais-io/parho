Keir Starmer, the new UK prime minister, travelled to Beijing – the first British leader to do so since 2018. He hopes to bring clearer, steadier rules for trade and cooperation with China, while keeping close to the EU and the US. Chinese leader Xi said Britain should be more consistent. Experts say the trip will signal a calmer relationship, not big deals yet, but better understanding.
