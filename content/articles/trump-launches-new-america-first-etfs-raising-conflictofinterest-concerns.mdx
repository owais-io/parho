Trump Media has launched five new “America First” ETFs that trade on the NYSE. They cover U.S. companies, real‑estate, energy, defense and tech—including bitcoin. The funds are managed by Yorkville Advisors, a New Jersey firm linked to Trump. Critics worry the president’s stake in these products could create conflicts of interest, especially as he keeps money in a trust controlled by his son. Senators have asked regulators to slow a crypto‑bank charter linked to Trump’s companies.
