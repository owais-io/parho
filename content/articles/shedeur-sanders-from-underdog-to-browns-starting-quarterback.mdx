Shedeur Sanders, son of football star Deion Sanders, was a surprise fifth‑round pick who quickly became the Cleveland Browns’ starting quarterback. After proving himself in a few games, he earned the job for the rest of the season, sparking mixed reactions. Supporters say he deserves a chance, while critics question his skill and attitude. The story highlights how his famous family name fuels both praise and criticism, turning him into a modern “nepo‑baby” underdog in the NFL.
