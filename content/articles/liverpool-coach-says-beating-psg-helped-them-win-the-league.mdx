Liverpool coach Arne Slot says the early loss to Paris Saint‑Germain in the Champions League gave the club extra time to recover, which helped them win last season’s Premier League title. He points to Liverpool’s recent drop in form, injuries, and defensive lapses, and says the team must improve both scoring and stopping goals to win again this year. Slot is hoping for a win over Qarabag next Wednesday.
