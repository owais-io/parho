A Jewish bakery in Sydney called Avner’s Bakery has shut down for good after the terror attack at Bondi beach. The owner, celebrity chef Ed Halmagyi, said the bakery could no longer keep staff and customers safe because of ongoing antisemitic threats and vandalism that have hit the shop for two years. Police are investigating the threats, and a 17‑year‑old was taken into custody. The bakery’s closure shows how the attack has changed life for many.
