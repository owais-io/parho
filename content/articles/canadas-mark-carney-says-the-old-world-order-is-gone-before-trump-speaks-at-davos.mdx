At the World Economic Forum in Davos, Canada’s Mark Carney warned that the world is no longer following the rules‑based system led by the United States. He said great powers are competing fiercely and that middle‑sized countries like Canada must find new ways to stay safe instead of just building walls. Meanwhile, U.S. President Donald Trump is set to speak about seizing Greenland, sparking a row with European leaders and putting the North Atlantic alliance under strain.
