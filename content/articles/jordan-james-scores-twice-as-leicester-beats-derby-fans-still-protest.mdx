Leicester beat Derby 3‑1, with Jordan James scoring two goals to lift the team’s spirits. Despite the win, fans are chanting for the board to step down because staff wages were pushed back until New Year. Manager Martí Cifuentes thanked the crowd, saying the cheers were great, but the club is four points away from playoff spots and may face a points deduction for past spending breaches. Injuries to several players add to the club’s challenges.
