Police in Tumbler Ridge, a small town in northern British Columbia, say a mass shooting killed 10 people, including the shooter. The attack started at the local secondary school and spread to a nearby house. More than 25 people were hurt, some seriously. The shooter died from a self‑inflicted gunshot. Authorities have not released names, but the community is grieving and the prime minister has postponed travel. The incident is one of Canada’s worst school shootings.
