The US military announced it knocked out 16 Iranian vessels that were laying mines in the Strait of Hormuz, a narrow channel through which about 20% of the world’s oil passes. Iran has already dropped dozens of mines and warned it could block oil traffic if US‑Israeli attacks go on. The US said the strike protected shipping lanes and helped stop a wider conflict.
