Callum Wilson, who joined West Ham five months ago, is reportedly looking to leave the club because he’s unhappy under manager Nuno Espírito Santo. The striker has been used sparingly and scored only four goals in 18 games, which has frustrated fans and players alike. West Ham are 18th in the Premier League and risk relegation. The club may sell him before the transfer window closes, while they have also signed two new forwards, Pablo and Taty, to help.
