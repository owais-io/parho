Columnist Jan‑Werner Mueller says Trump’s inner circle, led by TV host Pete Hegseth, celebrates killing and encourages violence. Hegseth defends war crimes, talks about snuff‑like footage, and praises killing from a distance. The piece argues that Trump’s administration openly glorifies violence, ignores the rules of war, and turns war into entertainment. Mueller warns that this propaganda could legitimize illegal attacks and spread a culture of death.
