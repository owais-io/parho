Sir Gino, the highly‑rated hurdler trained by Nicky Henderson, was pulled up in the International Hurdle at Cheltenham after a suspected pelvic injury. The horse landed in the ambulance, and vets later confirmed a fractured pelvis. Despite the setback, Sir Gino remains stable and is being monitored for recovery. He was the favourite for the upcoming Champion Hurdle, and the race was won by The New Lion.
