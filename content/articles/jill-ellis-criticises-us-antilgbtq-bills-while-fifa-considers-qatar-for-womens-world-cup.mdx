Jill Ellis, former US women's national team coach, has spoken out against anti‑LGBTQ+ bills in the US while FIFA looks at Qatar as a possible host for the 2028 Women’s Club World Cup. She says the sport can raise awareness and must consider players’ rights when choosing locations. Though she hasn’t heard details about Qatar, Ellis supports a venue that could grow the game and challenge stereotypes.
