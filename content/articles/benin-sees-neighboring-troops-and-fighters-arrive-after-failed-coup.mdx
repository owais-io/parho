After a failed coup, Benin’s president says the situation is under control. West African neighbors sent troops from Ghana, Ivory Coast, Nigeria and Sierra Leone to back the government. Nigeria also sent fighter jets to take over Benin’s airspace and push out the rebels who had taken over state TV. The rebels, who called themselves a military committee, tried to remove President Talon. About 14 people were arrested, and the country is still preparing for its April election.
