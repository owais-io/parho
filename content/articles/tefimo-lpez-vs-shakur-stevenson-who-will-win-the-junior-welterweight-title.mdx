Teófimo López, the 28‑year‑old former world champion, is defending his junior‑welterweight title against Shakur Stevenson in a showdown that could decide who is the best American boxer today. López has a reputation for power and unpredictability, while Stevenson is known for his careful, defensive style. López says he wants to prove he can stay consistent and inspire younger fighters, while Stevenson hopes his discipline will win the fight. Fans expect an exciting battle.
