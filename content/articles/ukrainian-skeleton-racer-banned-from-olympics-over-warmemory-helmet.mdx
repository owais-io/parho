During the 2026 Winter Olympics, Ukrainian skeleton racer Vladyslav Heraskevych was kicked out after he wore a helmet showing the names of 24 athletes killed in the Russian war. The Olympic rules say no political messages are allowed, so the IOC removed his accreditation and forced him to leave the Games. He plans to appeal to the Court of Arbitration for Sport.
