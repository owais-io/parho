On Saturday, a US‑Israeli operation called Lion's Roar struck Iran, killing its leader and hitting many cities. Iran fired missiles at Israel and targeted Gulf countries, hitting places like Dubai and Bahrain. Hezbollah fired from Lebanon, and Israel struck back. A drone hit a British base in Cyprus. Flights were shut, oil prices jumped, and many civilians were killed or injured.
