Donald Trump said he would not impose tariffs on eight European countries because he had reached a deal about Greenland. He had earlier threatened to hit allies with 10% or 25% duties. Trump talked with NATO leaders and said the agreement would help U.S. missile defence in the Arctic. European politicians said the deal is unclear and warned they would not be blackmailed. No concrete terms have been released yet.
