A 22‑year‑old Russian man accused of raping a woman in London says he was not angry when the victim answered a FaceTime call from Donald Trump’s son, Barron Trump, in January. In court, he said he was exhausted and didn’t care about the call, arguing the woman was in a hysterical state and was “leading” Trump on. He denies all charges and insists the case is about whether he actually attacked her.
