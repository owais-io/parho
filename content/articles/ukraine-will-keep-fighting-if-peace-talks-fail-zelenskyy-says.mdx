President Zelenskyy says Ukraine will keep fighting if peace talks fail. He wants a U.S. leaders’ summit by the end of January to finish a plan that is 90% ready. U.S., EU, NATO and 15 other countries are in talks. Russia wants control of eastern Donbas, but Kyiv will not give up land. If negotiations fail, Ukraine will defend itself. Meanwhile, Russia blamed a U.S. strike on Venezuela.
