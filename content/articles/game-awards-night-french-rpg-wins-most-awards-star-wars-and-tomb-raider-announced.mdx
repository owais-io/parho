At last night's Game Awards in LA, the French RPG Expedition 33 swept nine trophies, including Game of the Year. The event mixed flashy ads and celebrity cameos, and also launched several new titles. Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic was revealed, along with two fresh Tomb Raider games. Indie hits like Coven of the Chicken Foot and new survival horror Resident Evil Requiem got trailers, while big‑budget releases like Control Resonant and 4:Loop were also shown.
