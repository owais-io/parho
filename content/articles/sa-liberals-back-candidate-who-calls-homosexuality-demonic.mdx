SA Liberals back Carston Woodhouse, a candidate who said same‑sex marriage is false, calls homosexuality “demonic” and claims gender transition is an illusion. Labor’s Blair Boyer called the comments shocking and extreme, noting a rightward shift. Liberal leader Ashton Hurn said she does not share Woodhouse’s views but respects free opinion. Woodhouse was picked in a last‑minute scramble for the safe Labor seat of Wright. The controversy shows the party’s internal conflict.
