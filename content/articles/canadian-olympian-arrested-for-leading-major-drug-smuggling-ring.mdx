Ryan Wedding, a 44‑year‑old former Canadian Olympic snowboarder, was arrested after turning himself in at the U.S. embassy in Mexico City. U.S. and Canadian police say he ran a drug‑trafficking organization that moved about 60 tonnes of cocaine into Los Angeles each year and was linked to murders and money‑laundering. He is in custody in California and faces charges of trafficking, conspiracy to murder and witness tampering. The arrest ends a decade‑long hunt.
