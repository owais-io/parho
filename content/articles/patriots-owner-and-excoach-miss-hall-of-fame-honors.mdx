New England Patriots owner Robert Kraft and former coach Bill Belichick both missed out on this year’s Pro Football Hall of Fame. Voters didn’t give them enough votes, even though they led the team to six Super Bowl wins. NFL commissioner Roger Goodell said they deserve entry, but the Hall decides independently. Rumors of personal drama or past scandals may have hurt their chances.
