A recent study found eight more universities have stopped letting fossil‑fuel firms recruit students, raising the total to 18 universities (12% of all UK higher education). People and Planet says this helps push the climate‑justice movement and keeps new grads from ending up in a dead‑end industry. The report also notes growing steps against the border industry and highlights Manchester Metropolitan University as the top climate‑friendly campus.
