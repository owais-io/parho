The U.S. and Canada are gearing up for a tense Olympic men’s hockey final, the first time NHL stars have played at the Games since 2014. Canada, the betting favorite, has won the most Olympic golds, but U.S. star Brady Tkachuk says the rivalry fuels their drive to win. The match is hot, especially after last year’s heated face‑off and Canada’s shaky captain, Sidney Crosby, who may miss the game due to injury.
