UK media are blasting Prime Minister Keir Starmer for refusing to support war in the Middle East, especially after Israeli warnings about Iran. Newspapers like The Telegraph, Express and Sunday Times attacked him, calling him a coward and accusing his party of extremist ties. Even BBC reporters are critical, and Starmer's own social media reach is limited. Experts say he needs to fight back or cut through the media with clearer, more persuasive stories.
