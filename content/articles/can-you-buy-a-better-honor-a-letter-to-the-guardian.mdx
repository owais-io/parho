A reader writes back to the Guardian about a recent story on selling honours. He says he got an email from a firm that would help upgrade his CBE, and wonders if this could become a fair way to raise money for the NHS—suggesting people pay a percentage of their wealth for MBEs or knighthoods. He asks readers to send in letters, hoping the Guardian will publish them.
