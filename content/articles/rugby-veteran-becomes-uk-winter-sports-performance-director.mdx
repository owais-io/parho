Kearnan Myall, a former professional rugby player, now runs the UK’s winter‑sports program. He uses his rugby‑spirit lessons—teamwork, mental focus and data science—to help snowboarders, skiers and freestyle athletes prepare for the Games. Myall blends meditation research, tech from McLaren and sports‑psychology support to sharpen athletes’ performance and mental health, aiming for the country’s medal hopes.
