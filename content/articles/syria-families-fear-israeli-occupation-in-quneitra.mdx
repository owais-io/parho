Syria families in Quneitra now live under Israeli troops that entered after Assad fell. Israelis built bases, run checkpoints, raid homes, take weapons, and arrest people suspected of ties to Iran or Hezbollah. Residents say their houses were bulldozed, and they must ask Israel for permission to hold gatherings or fix utilities. Local officials try to work with the soldiers, but many feel scared and unsure if they can stay.
