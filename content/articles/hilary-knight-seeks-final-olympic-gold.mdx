Hilary Knight, the oldest member of the USA women's hockey team, is heading to the Winter Olympics in Milan at 36 to win a fourth gold. The article follows her career, her role as a leader, and the sport’s growth from no professional league to the new PWHL. It also covers the fierce rivalry with Canada and how the Olympics reset everything. Knight looks forward to finishing her legacy while celebrating the sport’s progress.
