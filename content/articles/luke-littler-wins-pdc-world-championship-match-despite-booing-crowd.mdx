Luke Littler, 18, defended his title by beating Rob Cross 4‑2. The arena was noisy, with many fans booing the champion. Littler kept his cool, saying the boos didn’t matter. He averaged 107 points and even hit a 10‑dart break in the final set. Despite the crowd’s hostility, he won and will now face the next round of the championship.
