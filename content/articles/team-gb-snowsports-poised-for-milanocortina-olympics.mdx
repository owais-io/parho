Team GB’s snowsport athletes have returned to top form after strong X‑Games results, giving the country confidence for the Milano‑Cortina Olympics. 19‑year‑old Mia Brookes won gold in Aspen, while Zoe Atkin and Kirsty Muir also added titles. 2024 has seen new equipment, psychology support and more international coaches, helping the team overcome past funding cuts and COVID‑related setbacks. With 20 athletes ready, UK Sport sees up to eight medals.
