Iran has long suffered arrests, killings and disappearances under a hard‑line regime. Protesters once saw President Trump’s promise of U.S. help as a lifeline. But after Israel and U.S. airstrikes hit homes, shops, fuel depots and even a school, many now blame the foreign powers for causing civilian deaths and damage. The anger has turned into disbelief, with Iranians fearing the war will turn the country into a ruined Iraq.
