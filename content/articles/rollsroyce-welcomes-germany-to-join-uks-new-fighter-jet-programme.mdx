The chief of Rolls‑Royce, Tufan Erginbilgiç, said he would be happy if Germany helped build Britain’s next‑generation Tempest fighter jet. He believes more partners mean more sales and stronger defence. The programme is already a joint effort between the UK, Italy and Japan, and adding Germany could boost production and exports, but it still depends on governments deciding to join. This move could also help the UK stay competitive in defence technology and strengthen its economy.
