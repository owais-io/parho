Renee Good’s extended family said they want justice for her death by an ICE officer, but they asked people to focus on empathy and caring for the family, not hate. The family praised Good as a loving mother and spoke of her son now orphaned. They avoided political arguments, hoping her story will spark real change and protect other families. They chose not to name ICE or politics, wanting a united focus.
