Prosecutors have dropped aggravated burglary charges against 18 defendants accused of breaking into Elbit Systems’ UK factory after a jury cleared six others. The 18, who had been in prison since the August raid, now face bail for some and will still face criminal‑damage and violent‑disorder charges. The Crown Prosecution Service plans retrials on the remaining allegations. The case drew supporters outside the court chanting for Palestine.
