West Ham won 2‑0 at Burnley, thanks to goals from Crysencio Summerville and Taty Castellanos. The win keeps Hammers just three points behind the relegation zone, giving them a glimmer of hope. Burnley struggled all night, failing to score and losing fans’ support. Manager Scott Parker stayed calm but said he won’t quit, while coach Nuno Espírito Santo admitted more work is needed. The match shows how tense the fight to stay in the Premier League can be.
