Australia is stepping up gun control after a mass shooting on Bondi beach that killed nine people. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese says new laws will limit how many guns owners can hold and will only issue licences to citizens. Data shows Sydney suburbs hold a large number of firearms, with some owners having hundreds. The Jewish community is deeply hurt, especially after a Chabad event was targeted, and the government is urging better safety measures.
