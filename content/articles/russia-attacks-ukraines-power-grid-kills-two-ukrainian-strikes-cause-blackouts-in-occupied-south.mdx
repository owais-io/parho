Russia kept hammering Ukraine’s electricity network, killing two people and wounding six in the Dnipropetrovsk region. The attacks hit power plants in Sumy, Kharkiv, Dnipro, Zaporizhzhia, Khmelnytskyi and Odesa, and used more than 200 drones. Ukrainian drones also hit energy lines in Russia‑occupied southern Ukraine, leaving about 200,000 households without power. Ukrainian crews are repairing a backup line to the Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant, while officials promise quick fixes.
