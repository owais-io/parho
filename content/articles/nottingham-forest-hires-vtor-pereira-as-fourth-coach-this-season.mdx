Nottingham Forest has fired coach Sean Dyche after a string of poor results and an early FA Cup exit. They have hired former Wolves manager Vítor Pereira, who will lead the team for the rest of the season on an 18‑month contract. Pereira’s first game will be a Europa League match against Fenerbahce, followed by a Premier League home game against Liverpool. The club hopes he can lift the club out of the relegation zone.
