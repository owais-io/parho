A 9pm crash on Ardee Road near Dundalk killed five young people and injured three. A Volkswagen Golf hit a Toyota Land Cruiser, killing three men and two women. Survivors have non‑life‑threatening injuries. Irish leaders, including the Taoiseach and Deputy PM, offered condolences and called for witnesses to report dash‑cam footage. The incident has brought national sadness and reminded everyone of road safety.
