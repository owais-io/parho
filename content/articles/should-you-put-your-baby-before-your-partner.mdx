Polly Hudson writes about her experience with parenting advice from Gina Ford. Ford says parents who put their child first can end up hurting their relationships. Hudson laughs that she spent so much time making her son happy that she ignored her husband. She also complains about Ford’s app that sends reminders parents are failing. The article is a funny take on how parents juggle love for their child and for their spouse, and that there’s no one right answer.
