Dan Fox’s personal story shows how he learned Welsh with his mother and how Duolingo’s new Welsh course supports that journey. The course uses the official Welsh government standard, but many people still argue about the right way to speak. Despite no single spoken standard, Duolingo now has more than 700,000 users and proves learning Welsh is doable and fun.
