In Spain, police and Europol stopped three members of the Base, a banned neo‑Nazi terrorist group. Their U.S. leader, Rinaldo Nazzaro, who lives in Russia, said the arrests were political persecution and promised more retaliation. The Base uses social media to recruit, share weapon photos, and plan attacks, with possible ties to Russian spies. Earlier, the group was linked to killings in Ukraine and a plot in Luxembourg, and U.S. authorities had already arrested members.
