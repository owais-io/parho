West Ham won 3‑1 at Sunderland, with Jarrod Bowen and Mateus Fernandes scoring. Bowen also earned a penalty for a foul on the defender, and Fernandes finished off a missed clearance. The win keeps West Ham in the bottom three and moves them only two points behind relegation rivals Nottingham Forest. Manager Nuno Espírito Santo praised the team’s confidence and said they will keep fighting as they prepare to face Brentford next.
