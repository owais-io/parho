President Trump has started a 10% tariff on goods from every country after the Supreme Court declared many of his earlier tariffs illegal. The fee will last 150 days and sits on top of existing duties. Businesses say it makes planning tough, and Trump hinted he might raise it to 15% later, but that increase hasn’t happened yet.
