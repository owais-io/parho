Rob Reiner, the director of films like *Stand By Me* and *The Princess Bride*, died with his wife. Police say their son was arrested for murder. Fans gathered at his Hollywood Walk of Fame star to pay tribute and remember his work and activism. People noted his role in fighting for gay marriage and speaking out against Trump. The news shocked Los Angeles and highlighted how his legacy touched many.
