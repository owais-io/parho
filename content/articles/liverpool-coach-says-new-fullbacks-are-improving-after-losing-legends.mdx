Arne Slot says Liverpool are still learning to replace Trent Alexander‑Arnold and Andy Robertson. He backs new full‑backs Jeremie Frimpong and Miloš Kerkez, who have shown speed and helped in recent wins. Slot notes injuries and the need for time to work together. The team has gone seven games unbeaten and sits fourth in the Premier League. He believes the squad will grow stronger as players get used to each other.
