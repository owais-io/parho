The Supreme Court ruled that most of President Trump’s tariffs were unlawful, but the administration says its tariff policy will stay the same. Trade officials say they will use other laws to keep tariffs on many countries, even raising a global tariff to 15%. While the court struck down many deals, the U.S. will continue to honor existing agreements and hopes to protect American jobs.
