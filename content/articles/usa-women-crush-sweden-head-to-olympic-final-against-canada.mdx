The U.S. women's hockey team beat Sweden 5‑0 in the semi‑finals, keeping their unbeaten streak alive and setting up a gold‑medal match with Canada. The Americans scored early and never looked back, with key goals from Abbey Murphy, Kendall Coyne Schofield and Taylor Heise. Goaltender Aerin Frankel stopped 23 shots, keeping the score clean. The U.S. team, full of young talent, is now the clear favourite for gold.
