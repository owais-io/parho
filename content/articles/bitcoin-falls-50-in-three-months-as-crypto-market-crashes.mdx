Bitcoin dropped to $63k, half its all‑time high. The global crypto market lost $2tn since October. Companies that invested heavily in Bitcoin are now facing losses. Earlier this year, President Trump’s policies helped the price rise, but the market slump has reversed that trend. Regulatory worries have surfaced, with U.S. lawmakers questioning potential conflicts of interest involving Trump‑affiliated crypto firms. A congressman has launched an investigation into one such company.
