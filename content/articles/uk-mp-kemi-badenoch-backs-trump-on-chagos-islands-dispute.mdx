During a quiet night of parliamentary questions, UK opposition MP Kemi Badenoch defended President Trump’s stance on the Chagos Islands, a move that shocked fellow politicians and drew sharp criticism from Labour leader Keir Starmer. Badenoch argued for a quick solution but failed to explain why the UK’s long‑standing policy on the Chagossians was suddenly changing. Starmer accused her of opportunism and weak leadership, warning that the debate could damage the Tory image.
