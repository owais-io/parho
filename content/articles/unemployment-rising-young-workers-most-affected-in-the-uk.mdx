The UK’s employment rate is slipping below the government’s 80% goal, now at 74.9%. Unemployment has jumped to a four‑year high of 5.1%, up 0.8 percentage points from last year. Young people are hit hardest: 546,000 18‑ to 24‑year‑olds are out of work, the biggest number since 2015. Job growth is slowing, wages are rising more slowly, and the government’s plans to raise the minimum wage for younger workers are under review.
