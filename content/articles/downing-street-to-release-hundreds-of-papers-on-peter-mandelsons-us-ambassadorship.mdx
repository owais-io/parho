Downing Street will publish hundreds of documents about Peter Mandelson’s planned appointment as the UK’s ambassador to the United States. The first batch includes a Cabinet Office due‑diligence report that warned Prime Minister Keir Starmer of serious reputational risks linked to Mandelson’s past ties with Jeffrey Epstein. The files also contain internal correspondence and media reports, raising questions about the vetting process and Starmer’s judgment.
