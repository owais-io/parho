Bari Weiss, who had no TV experience, took the helm of CBS News in October. Within three months she pulled a controversial 60 Minutes story about an El Salvador prison, sparking a backlash from reporters and a letter to the owner. Critics say she lacks a clear plan, faces clashes over editorial choices, and has struggled to win viewers. Still, her boss stays supportive.
