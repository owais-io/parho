The Guardian has issued a correction notice, removing a comment that compared pro‑Palestinian activism to petty symbolism in a London article. The comment was meant to criticize misdirected actions, not the vandalism itself, and was taken down to avoid misunderstanding. It also rephrased a criticism of a big chain store’s impact on local cafés. Other pieces were amended, and readers can email complaints to guardian.readers@theguardian.com.
