Nathan Chasing Horse, the actor who played Smiles a Lot in Dances With Wolves, is due to be sentenced in Nevada next Wednesday after a jury found him guilty on 13 of 21 sexual‑abuse charges involving Indigenous women and girls. The case includes abuse of a 14‑year‑old. He denies the allegations and could face a minimum of 25 years in prison if he gets the lowest sentence. The conviction ended a long‑running case that also led to investigations in Canada.
