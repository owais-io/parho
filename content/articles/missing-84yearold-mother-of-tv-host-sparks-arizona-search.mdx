The 84‑year‑old mother of Today host Savannah Guthrie, Nancy Guthrie, vanished from her Tucson, Arizona home on Saturday night. Police treat the house as a crime scene and suspect foul play, saying she could not have left on her own. A helicopter, drones, dogs and even the homicide squad are hunting her. Family last saw her at 9:30 p.m.; her phone is with police. The sheriff urges anyone with clues to call him.
