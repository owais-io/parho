During a Thanksgiving‑style event in Watts, the Sisters of Watts—four sisters who grew up there—served food, fresh produce, and fun games to over 100 families. They’ve spent a decade running food drives, backpack donations, after‑school programs, and even helping ex‑prisoners start businesses and build. With a small budget and a recent $100,000 grant, they still rely on donations and community partners to keep the program running.
