Hong Kong voters turned out at a record low of 31.9 % for its “patriots‑only” election, the lowest since 2021. Only 20 of the 90 seats are directly elected and only pro‑Beijing candidates can run. The government warned against voting boycotts and arrested four men for encouraging people not to vote. Meanwhile, a deadly apartment fire in Tai Po left 159 dead, sparking demands for accountability and a crackdown on protests.
