Sam Altman, the CEO of OpenAI, told Jimmy Fallon he can’t picture raising his newborn without help from ChatGPT. The billionaire new dad said the AI answered questions about his baby’s quirks and offered reassurance. Altman’s remarks come as he balances a huge company, a potential $1 trillion IPO, and life as a surrogate dad with his husband. The story highlights how AI is reshaping everyday parenting.
