Women in 15 South African cities staged a silent lie‑down protest in black, marking 15 daily victims of gender violence. The event, led by Women For Change, pushed the government to call gender‑based violence a national disaster. The National Disaster Management Centre approved the move after a petition of more than a million signatures. President Ramaphosa said the country must act, and officials plan a detailed action plan next week.
