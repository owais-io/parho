Adrian Chiles shares how his view of Mondays transformed after a roller‑coaster career. He once hated school, scaffolding, and the early days of journalism, only to feel burnt out when gigs vanished. He realized a regular work routine—working Wednesday to Saturday—gives him the structure he needs, and now he looks forward to Mondays. The piece shows how routine can turn a dreaded day into a welcome start.
