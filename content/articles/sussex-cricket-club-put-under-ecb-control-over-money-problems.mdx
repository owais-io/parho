Sussex County Cricket Club is now under special measures from the England and Wales Cricket Board because of alleged financial mismanagement and breaches of sustainability rules. The ECB will monitor the club’s spending, approve big expenses, and keep a close eye on meetings. Sussex has a big operating loss and no permanent CEO. If more breaches are found, they could face a fine or lose points in this season’s County Championship. The club says it is working on a solution.
