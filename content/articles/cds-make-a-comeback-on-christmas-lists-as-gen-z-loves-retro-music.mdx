Compact discs are back on Christmas shopping lists, with sales up 74% at John Lewis. A wave of 90s nostalgia and deluxe releases from artists like Taylor Swift and Pink Floyd is driving the trend. Gen Z is buying more CDs than older generations, often online or in record shops, and many still value the physical format to support artists. Secondhand CD sales show classics like ABBA and Adele topping the list.
