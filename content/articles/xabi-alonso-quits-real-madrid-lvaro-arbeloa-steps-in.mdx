Xabi Alonso is leaving Real Madrid after a short stint of seven months, the club announced Monday. He departed after the team lost the Spanish Super Cup final to Barcelona, and Real Madrid said it was by mutual agreement. The club will use former teammate Álvaro Arbeloa – who coaches the B team – as a temporary replacement. Alonso's time saw a shaky start, a few wins but inconsistent form, and no clear progress in the league or Champions League.
