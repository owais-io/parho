Last night, Mansfield Town pulled off a surprise victory over Premier League side Burnley, ending the club’s 50‑year wait to reach the FA Cup fifth round. A free‑kick from Louis Reed sealed a 2‑1 win. Burnley struggled to find rhythm and were overwhelmed by Mansfield’s hard work and determination. The result leaves Burnley facing possible relegation and raises questions about manager Scott Parker’s future.
