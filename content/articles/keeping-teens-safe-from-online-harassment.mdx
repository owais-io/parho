A 15‑year‑old girl opens up about the daily misogyny she faces on social media. The letter argues that simply banning young users is not enough. It calls for better platform design, transparency, and accountability, while also involving teens in the conversation. Parents are urged to set limits and monitor use. The piece also warns that boys miss role models and could be influenced by toxic online content. Communities should step up support.
