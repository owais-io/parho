Jarred Shaw, an American basketball star in Indonesia, avoided the death penalty after being caught with 132 cannabis gummies. His arrest in May 2025 led to a 26‑month jail sentence and a $50,000 fine. Shaw, who suffers from Crohn’s disease, is now in a prison with poor food that worsens his health, losing weight and facing infections. He’s seeking medical care, compassionate release, and hopes to return to basketball after recovery.
