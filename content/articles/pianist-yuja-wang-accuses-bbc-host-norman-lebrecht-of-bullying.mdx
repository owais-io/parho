Yuja Wang, the Chinese pianist, posted an email from BBC Radio 3 presenter Norman Lebrecht on Instagram. Lebrecht had written to her about canceling a planned interview, and Wang said his tone was misogynistic and bullying. The BBC said it would stop working with Lebrecht. He apologized, claiming no bullying. The incident highlights tension between musicians and critics in the classical music world.
