Arizona Sheriff Chris Nanos says he didn’t hide evidence in the search for 84‑year‑old Nancy Guthrie. He claims the gloves found near her home were sent to a Florida lab that already had DNA work on the case. FBI says they wanted the gloves themselves. DNA at the scene doesn’t match Nancy or anyone close to her. A masked suspect was caught on video and a $100,000 reward was offered. The case is still ongoing.
