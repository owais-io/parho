Lime’s popular green e‑bikes have raised safety concerns after an inspection in Sydney found many bikes missing helmets, worn tyres, broken spokes and other damage. A mechanic checked 72 bikes, and almost a third of older models didn’t have a helmet, breaking the law. Most new bikes were fine, but loose parts and cracked baskets still pose risks. Lime says it checks bikes and plans stricter fines for riders who leave helmets behind.
