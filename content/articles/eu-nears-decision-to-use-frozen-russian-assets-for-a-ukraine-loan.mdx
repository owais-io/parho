EU leaders are almost ready to decide if they will use frozen Russian assets to create a loan that will help Ukraine pay for war damages. The plan faces pushback from some member states, especially Belgium and Italy, who worry about legal and political fallout. Despite opposition, the European Council can still pass the measure with a qualified majority. The debate will take place at this week’s European Council meeting in Brussels.
