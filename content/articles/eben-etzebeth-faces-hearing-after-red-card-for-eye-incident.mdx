Eben Etzebeth was sent off in South Africa’s 73‑0 win over Wales after a hit that looked like eye‑gouging. He will go to a disciplinary hearing on Tuesday, where officials may hand him a long ban – up to 12 weeks, or more if the offence is severe. He apologized, and his coach said the red card was justified. The final decision will come on Wednesday.
