El Mencho, a 59‑year‑old former Mexican police officer who helped start the Jalisco New Generation Cartel (CJNG), was killed by special forces on Sunday. The CJNG is Mexico’s most violent cartel, known for drone‑launched explosives and attacks on military helicopters. It supplies cocaine, fentanyl and meth to the U.S. and is seen as a national security threat. El Mencho had been convicted in the U.S. for drug trafficking.
