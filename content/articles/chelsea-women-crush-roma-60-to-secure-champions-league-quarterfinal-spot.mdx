Chelsea’s women ran away with a 6‑0 win over Roma, giving the team a big boost and moving them into third place in the Champions League group. Captain Millie Bright was named player of the match after an own goal and five Chelsea shots found the net. The victory puts Chelsea one game away from a guaranteed spot in the quarter‑finals, where they’ll face Wolfsburg next week.
