Trump’s government is paying almost $1 billion to French oil firm TotalEnergies to drop two offshore wind projects on the US East Coast. The money covers what Total paid for leases that Biden had reimbursed. In exchange, Total will stop developing new US wind farms and will focus on gas and oil projects instead. The move is seen as a blow to US wind energy, which is still expanding.
