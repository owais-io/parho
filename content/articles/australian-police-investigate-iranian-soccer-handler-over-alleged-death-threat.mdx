Australian police are investigating a complaint that an Iranian regime‑linked soccer handler threatened to kill activists during a Women’s Asian Cup match in Brisbane. The claim has sparked calls from the Iranian community for tighter visa checks on people linked to the Iranian Revolutionary Guard Corps, now named a state‑sponsor of terrorism. The case comes amid six Iranian women’s players seeking asylum in Australia and a parliamentary review of Iranian security activity.
