The Women’s Super League says the 2028 Women’s Club World Cup, set for Jan 5‑30, would hit the English season hard, cutting into five match days. Clubs and players are also against the timing, though the league won’t boycott the event. It wants the tournament moved to summer. FIFA hasn’t chosen a host yet, but talks are underway with Qatar. The dates clash with WSL’s winter break and other European leagues.
