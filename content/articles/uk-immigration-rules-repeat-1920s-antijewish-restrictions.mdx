Today’s UK Home Secretary, Shabana Mahmood, wants to lengthen the time immigrants need to gain permanent residency from 5 to 10 or even 20 years. The article says this echoes a 1924 policy that raised naturalisation times and made it hard for Jewish refugees to settle. It compares current anti‑immigrant media slant to the antisemitic propaganda of the 1920s, warning that repeating history fuels hate and harms minorities.
