The Kennedy Center changed its rules so only trustees appointed by Donald Trump could vote. This let the board add Trump’s name to the center’s title. The move sparked protests, artists cancelled shows, and some Congress members plan to reverse it. Trump, who became board chair in February, replaced many members with his supporters, including former ambassador Ric Grenell. Critics say the new voting rules break the center’s charter.
