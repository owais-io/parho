A new YouGov poll found that 50% of Americans would like the immigration‑law‑enforcement agency ICE to be abolished, while only 39% oppose it. The results show growing anger over Trump’s tough immigration tactics and recent killings of protestors by ICE officers. Democrats and independents are most in favour, while Republicans largely oppose, though a small share now supports ending ICE.
