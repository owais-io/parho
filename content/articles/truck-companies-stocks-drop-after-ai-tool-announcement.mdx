A small AI start‑up called Algorhythm announced a freight‑management tool that promised to boost shipping volumes by up to 400% without hiring more people. The news sent a panic wave through the trucking sector, with the Russell 3000 Trucking Index falling 6.6% and major names like CH Robinson and Landstar dropping 15%‑20%. The slump is the biggest since the 2023 trade‑war jitters.
