Senator Mitch McConnell, 83, was taken to a local hospital after feeling flu‑like symptoms over the weekend. The Kentucky senator, who has had falls and mobility problems in recent years, said he is doing well and thanks the staff. He missed two days of Senate business. McConnell has been a senior figure in Washington, leading the Republican caucus for many years and reshaping the federal court system.
