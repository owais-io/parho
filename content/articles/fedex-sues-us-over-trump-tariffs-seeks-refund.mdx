FedEx has sued the U.S. government after the Supreme Court ruled that President Trump’s import tariffs were illegal. The package shippers say they want a full refund of the duties they paid under those tariffs, which were estimated to cost companies $175 bn. FedEx’s case is the first by a major firm to seek money back, and other businesses may follow. The lawsuit focuses on customs, the CBP commissioner, and the U.S. as defendants.
