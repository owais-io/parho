By 2025 AI will change most people’s lives, but the hype around it is overblown. Big tech giants are pouring trillions into AI, betting on huge profits and a future where machines match or beat human intelligence. Some call this a bubble—good or bad. The US and China race for AI dominance with little global rules, raising risks of bias, misuse and job loss. When the bubble pops, we may finally decide whether AI serves us or controls us.
