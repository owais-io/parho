MIT professor Retsef Levi was chosen by Robert F. Kennedy Jr. to review the safety of COVID‑19 vaccines. Many scientists say Levi’s own studies are flawed and misleading. He claims the vaccines are dangerous, even though research shows they are safe and have saved millions of lives. Because Levi isn’t a vaccine expert, experts worry his review could push the US to pull back vaccine recommendations and spread doubt.
