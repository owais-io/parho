A woman who was sexually and physically abused by her parents for 14 years says a podcast by The Australian made her feel worse. The podcast, called Shadow of Doubt, interviewed the jailed parents and called the convictions a possible miscarriage of justice. It also shared private medical notes and didn’t let the survivor speak. The woman said the coverage broke her trust in the system and caused anxiety. She only spoke publicly after court orders were lifted.
