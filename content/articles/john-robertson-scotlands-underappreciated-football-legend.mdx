John Robertson was a highly skilled Scottish footballer and coach who never got the recognition he deserved. He helped Nottingham Forest win the European Cup, was praised by Brian Clough, and later coached Celtic with former manager Martin O’Neill. Despite his talent, he earned only 28 caps for Scotland and faced personal tragedies. After his death on Christmas Day, people are finally remembering his impact on the game.
