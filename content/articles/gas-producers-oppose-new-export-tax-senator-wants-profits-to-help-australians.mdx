Gas producers are fighting a proposed 25% tax on gas exports. Senator David Pocock wants the extra money to help Australians struggling with high energy bills. The government says the tax would scare away investment and hurt gas supply, which is needed for renewable energy. The debate comes as Middle East attacks push gas prices higher, making the industry’s profits surge. Industry leaders warn the tax could cause gas shortages and higher prices for households.
