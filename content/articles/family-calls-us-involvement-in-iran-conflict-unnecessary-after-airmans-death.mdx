A cousin of a 28‑year‑old Ohio airman who died in a KC‑135 crash over Iraq says the U.S. war with Iran was unnecessary and could have been avoided. He urges Americans to vote to push for political change, while his family laments the losses. The crash killed six U.S. soldiers, part of a larger conflict that has already taken 13 U.S. lives and drawn criticism over attacks like the bombing of a girls’ school in Iran.
