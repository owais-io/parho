Keir Starmer told the world that Britain will not send warships to the Strait of Hormuz, even after the US asked for help keeping the waterway open amid tensions with Iran. European leaders, including Germany and Italy, also said they would not deploy naval forces. Starmer said the UK will focus on minesweeping drones and support the UK’s own people with energy‑bill help.
