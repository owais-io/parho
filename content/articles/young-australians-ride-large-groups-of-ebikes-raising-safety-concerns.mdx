In Australia, teens are gathering in big groups on e‑bikes, riding through parks, golf courses and city streets. The groups—often called “fat bike” swarms—have caused damage and raised safety worries. Police have taken notice after incidents at a Sydney golf club and a Melbourne meet‑up, where riders rode too fast, on wrong sides of roads and without helmets. The NSW government plans to cut e‑bike power to 250 watts to curb the risk.
