Safyaan Sharif was called into Scotland’s T20 World Cup squad after Bangladesh was pulled out of the tournament. He arrived in India just two days before training and had to clear a visa delay because of his Pakistani background. Sharif says he’ll use his best bowling skill, the yorker, to help Scotland win the key match against England in Kolkata. He also jokes that if the team needs a chef, he’s happy to help out.
