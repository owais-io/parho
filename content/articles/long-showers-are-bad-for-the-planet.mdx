Many people think a long shower is harmless, but it uses a lot of water and energy. The writer talks about a friend named Peter who worries about saving water, and how he learned that water is scarce in places like Africa. He wonders what people do in a 30‑minute shower and suggests that comfy bathroom design could help. The letter urges us to stop using too many resources for comfort.
