Police across the U.S. are now using weapons and vehicles that were built for war, like armored trucks, drones and grenades. This “militarisation” has happened because the Pentagon gives police free military gear. The result is streets that feel like battlefields and a rise in violence. Lawmakers are calling on Congress to end the transfer of these weapons and to focus on calm policing instead.
