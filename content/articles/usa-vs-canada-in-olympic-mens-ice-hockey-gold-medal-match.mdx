The U.S. and Canada will face off for the gold medal in ice hockey at the Milano Cortina Olympics. Both teams are undefeated after beating Finland and Slovakia, and this will be the first final featuring NHL players. Canada’s comeback win over Finland and the U.S.’s 6‑2 victory over Slovakia set the stage. Key stars include Nathan MacKinnon, Jack Hughes, and Jack Eichel. It’s a historic rivalry and a chance for the U.S. to win its first gold since 1980.
