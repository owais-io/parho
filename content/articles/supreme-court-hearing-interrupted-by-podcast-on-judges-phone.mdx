During a UK Supreme Court session, a judge accidentally let a podcast episode play on the court’s microphone. The show, hosted by Tom Holland, featured a Jimmy Carter impersonation that made everyone laugh. The judge quickly silenced his phone, apologized, and the clip was recorded. It soon went viral, showing how a simple tech mishap can turn a serious hearing into a viral moment.
