Texas voters start early voting on Tuesday for the March 3 primaries. The big contest is the U.S. Senate seat. Republicans face a fight between long‑time senator John Cornyn and the scandal‑ridden attorney general Ken Paxton, who represents the Trump‑aligned grassroots wing. Democrats are choosing between civil‑rights lawyer Jasmine Crockett and former teacher James Talarico, each appealing to different parts of the party. The state’s result could shift the 2026 Senate balance.
