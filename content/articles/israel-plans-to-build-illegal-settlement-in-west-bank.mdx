Israel plans to build 3,401 homes in West Bank area called E1, a move seen as illegal. A tender issued in December sets a March deadline, so bulldozers could start soon. The project aims to split the West Bank and cut off East Jerusalem, undermining a Palestinian state. Despite U.S. and EU opposition, Israeli leaders say it shows “facts” not words. 20+ countries condemned the plan, and a UN court says the occupation is unlawful.
