On Capitol Hill, two protesters briefly disrupted a hearing where Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem was speaking. One shouted, “Stop ICE raids! The power of Christ compels you!” and held a sign calling for an end to deportations. The other held a poster saying “No ICE, no troops!” and shouted for ICE to leave the streets. Noem stayed calm, while Capitol police escorted the protester out. The incident comes amid growing pressure on Noem from Democrats to step down over her handling of Trump‑era immigration policies.
