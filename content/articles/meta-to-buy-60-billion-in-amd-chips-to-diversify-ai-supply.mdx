Meta has signed a five‑year contract to buy $60 billion worth of AI chips from AMD, a move that shows the social‑media giant wants more options for its data‑center hardware. The deal gives Meta a 10% stake in AMD and starts with 1 GW of the new MI450 GPUs, expanding to 6 GW over five years. By adding AMD to its mix, Meta is less dependent on Nvidia and is building a new $27 billion data‑centre in Louisiana.
