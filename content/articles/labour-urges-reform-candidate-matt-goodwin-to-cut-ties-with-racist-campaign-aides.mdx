Labour has warned Reform UK’s Matt Goodwin that he must distance himself from aides who shared racist posts and far‑right sympathies. An investigation by Manchester Mill found five campaign members posted hateful content, including slurs and support for extremist groups. Goodwin, fighting for a seat in Manchester, has already faced controversy over comments about women, migrants and other minorities. Labour insists he must remove these people and condemn their views.
