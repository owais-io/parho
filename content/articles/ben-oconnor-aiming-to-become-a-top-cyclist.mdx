Ben O’Connor, 27, is a rising star in world road cycling. He finished second at the 2024 World Championships behind Tadej Pogacar and has won stages in all three Grand Tours. After a tough first season with injuries and a crash, he’s now ready for more Grand Tour ambitions. He plans to focus on overall rankings in one race and stage wins in another. He’s set for the Tour Down Under to start his 2026 campaign.
