The Coral Adventurer cruise ship ran aground on a reef near Papua New Guinea’s city of Lae and couldn’t be freed by tugs. There were no injuries to the 80 passengers and 44 crew. Because the ship can’t be refloated, the operator cancelled the tour and will fly passengers back to Cairns. The incident follows a separate investigation over a passenger’s death in October.
