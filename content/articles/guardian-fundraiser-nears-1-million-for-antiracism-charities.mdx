Guardian readers have already donated over £950,000 to a Hope appeal that supports five charities working against racism, extremism, and hate. The goal is £1 million before the appeal ends at midnight Wednesday. The charities include Citizens UK, the Linking Network, Locality, Hope Unlimited, and Who is Your Neighbour? Many donors said they were moved by recent Guardian stories and want to help heal divisions and bring communities together. The editor urges anyone who can still give to donate before the deadline.
