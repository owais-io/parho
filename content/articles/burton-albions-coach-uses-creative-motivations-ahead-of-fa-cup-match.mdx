Gary Bowyer, Burton Albion’s manager, uses quirky tricks—toy tigers, tennis rackets, and Mike Tyson themes—to motivate his players. He believes vivid stories help them imagine fighting on the pitch. Bowyer’s weekly themes aim to build emotional attachment and resilience as the team faces West Ham in the FA Cup. He mixes fun drills, past upsets, and personal stories to keep the squad focused and inspired.
