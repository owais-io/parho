Russia’s top foreign‑policy aide said Europe and Ukraine’s tweaks to the U.S. peace plan are useless and could even prolong the war. While Ukrainian troops fight in the village of Grabovske, U.S. intelligence still thinks Putin wants all of Ukraine. Meanwhile, Trump’s negotiators and Ukrainian officials are meeting in Miami, hoping to fast‑track talks and prisoner swaps. France welcomed possible talks with Putin, and Ukraine recently struck a Russian oilfield with drones.
