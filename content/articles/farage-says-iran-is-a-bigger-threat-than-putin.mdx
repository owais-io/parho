Nigel Farage, leader of Reform UK, told the New Statesman that Iran poses a greater danger than Russia. He has long backed the US fight against Iran and now says preventing Iran from getting a nuclear weapon is worth the effort. Farage has also criticised the UK for not standing with Ukraine and offered to act as a back‑channel to the US, though his proposals have been rejected.
