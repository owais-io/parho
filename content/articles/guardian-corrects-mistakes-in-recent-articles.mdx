The Guardian is fixing errors in several recent pieces. It said a Palestinian teen was still in jail, but he was freed on Oct 13 after nine months in the Rakefet complex, as the cease‑fire took effect. Other corrections touch on a hurricane‑help flight to Jamaica, a guide on charging an electric car at home, and a story about a TikTok star’s death. Readers can send complaints to guardian.readers@theguardian.com or call +44 20 3353 4736.
