Stan Wawrinka, 40, is in his last year as a pro and is playing against younger players. He says he wants to give his all, but still cares deeply. Wawrinka has three Grand Slam titles, an Olympic gold and a Davis Cup win, and is famous for his powerful one‑handed backhand. He’s still driven, saying he won’t leave tennis with regrets. He’ll end his career at the Australian Open with a final tour.
