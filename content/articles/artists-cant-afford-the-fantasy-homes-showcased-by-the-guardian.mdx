Guardian’s latest “fantasy houses” look like a dream for many, but for visual artists it’s out of reach. The paper reports artists’ median earnings dropped 40 % since 2010 to just £12,500, making the featured properties unaffordable. Letters from readers echo the frustration, pointing out the absurd mismatch between house prices and artists’ pay, and the broader housing‑affordability crisis.
