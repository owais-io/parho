A 17‑year‑old girl and an 18‑year‑old boy in Dorset were arrested for an alleged indecent image. Their baby was taken into foster care. An inquest found the couple killed themselves at a reservoir, saying the baby’s removal and the arrest caused distress. The jury said police failed to act on reports of a controlling relationship and asked local authorities to explain their safeguards.
