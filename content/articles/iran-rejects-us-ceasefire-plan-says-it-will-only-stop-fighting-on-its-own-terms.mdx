Iran rejected a ceasefire plan sent from the U.S. via Pakistan and said it would only stop fighting when its own conditions were met. Iran’s foreign minister said the proposals were sent to senior officials but no talks will happen right now. Gulf states doubt that talks are underway, and Qatar said it is not mediating. The conflict has already caused more than 3,000 deaths in the region.
