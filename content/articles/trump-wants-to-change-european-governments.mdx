The new Trump national security strategy openly calls Europe a threat and says it wants to help far‑right parties change governments. It says the EU is weak and that the US will support parties like Germany’s AfD or France’s National Rally. The plan also pushes for a breakup of the EU, matching Putin’s view. Britain and other European leaders are still unsure, but the strategy shows the US is ready to back a regime change in Europe.
