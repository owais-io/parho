Tim Allan, the man who ran the press office for UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer, quit his post after just five months. His resignation followed that of Morgan McSweeney, the prime minister’s chief of staff. Both departures come amid a scandal over the appointment of former Labour minister Peter Mandelson as an ambassador, who has ties to convicted sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein. Starmer said he was sorry for the decision and that he was working to rebuild his team.
