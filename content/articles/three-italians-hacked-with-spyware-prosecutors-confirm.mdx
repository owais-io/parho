Investigators in Rome and Naples say that on the night of 14 December 2024, three Android phones were hit by spyware. The victims were a journalist, Francesco Cancellato, and two pro‑immigration activists, Luca Casarini and Giuseppe Caccia. The attacks look like one coordinated campaign, but officials haven’t yet found who did it. The case raises doubts about the Italian government’s role and hurts press freedom.
