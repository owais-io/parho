At a gala in Ottawa, former central banker Mark Carney joined actor Hudson Williams from the Canadian TV drama “Heated Rivalry”. After a warm handshake, Carney quipped, “Do the leg thing,” and Williams followed his lead, sparking laughter. Carney praised the show for its LGBTQ+ themes and said Canada’s film industry should keep bold stories alive. He joked he had helped fund the series, highlighting the country’s support for diverse voices.
