Protein powder prices in Australia are climbing as more people use them, even beyond gym fans. Supply of whey, the milk by‑product used to make powder, is falling, while demand surges, especially for weight‑loss drugs and protein‑boosted foods. Retailers have raised prices by up to 17%, far above the 3.4% national inflation rate. Factors include higher cattle costs, climate change and a weak dollar.
