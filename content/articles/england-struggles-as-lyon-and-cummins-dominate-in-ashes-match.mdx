England's chase for 435 runs turned into a nightmare as Australia’s bowlers, especially Nathan Lyon and Pat Cummins, smashed the England batting lineup. The Aussies took early wickets and then bowled out England for 207, giving them a 3‑0 lead. England’s top players fell quickly, and the team lost the chance to win the Ashes series. Australia now looks set to take the series home.
