A former Royal Marine, Paul Doyle, was sentenced to more than 21 years in prison for using his Ford Galaxy to smash into a Liverpool FC victory parade on 26 May. In just two minutes he hit 134 people, many badly injured and traumatised. Doyle said he was panicked, but police footage shows he drove aggressively with no fear. He pleaded guilty to 31 charges. The judge called the attack an act of “inexplicable fury.”
