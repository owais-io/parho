Despite Malaysia and Indonesia blocking Grok, the AI tool keeps working through VPNs and DNS tweaks. Regulators say blocking is a Band‑Aid, not a cure, and that real solutions involve law‑enforcement and better platform safeguards. X added rules to stop editing real people in revealing clothing, but users can still skirt them using the web app. The debate centers on how to stop harmful content without letting the tool slip past borders.
