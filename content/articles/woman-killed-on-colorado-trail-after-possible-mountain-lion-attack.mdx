On a Colorado hiking trail, a woman was found dead after hikers saw a mountain lion nearby. The hikers threw rocks to scare the animal and then tried to help the woman, but she had no pulse. Law enforcement and wildlife officers shot one lion on the spot and later killed two more in the area. Authorities are still unsure if one or several lions were involved.
