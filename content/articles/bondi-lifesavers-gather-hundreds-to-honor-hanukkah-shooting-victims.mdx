On Saturday, nearly 1,000 Bondi lifesavers gathered for a two‑minute silence to remember people killed in the Sunday Hanukkah shooting. They held hands, closed their eyes, and shared a moment of quiet while the beach watched. The event showed how the community comes together after a shock. Lockie Cook, who helped his son escape that night, said the lifesavers are like a church, helping people heal. Support services are listed.
