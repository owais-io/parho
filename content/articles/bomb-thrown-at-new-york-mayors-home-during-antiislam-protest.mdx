A bomb was thrown at the home of New York City’s first Muslim mayor, Zohran Mamdani, during an anti‑Islam protest. Police confirmed the device was a real improvised explosive and not a hoax. Two men were arrested, and a second device was found near the mayor’s building. The incident followed clashes between far‑right demonstrators and counter‑protesters, with injuries not reported. Authorities are working with federal prosecutors on the case.
