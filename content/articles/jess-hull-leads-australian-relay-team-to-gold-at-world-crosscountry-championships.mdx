Australian mixed relay team won gold at the world cross‑country championships in Tallahassee. Jess Hull, Ollie Hoare, Linden Hall and Jack Anstey ran a 22‑minute, 23‑second race, beating France, Ethiopia, Kenya and the US. Hull, an Olympic silver medalist, led the final leg. In the women’s race Lauren Ryan and Leanne Pompeani finished in the top 15, while the men’s team placed eighth overall. Australia’s team showed strong teamwork and growth.
