At the Isle of Wight Masters, British grandmaster Matthew Wadsworth took first place, beating German and Scottish opponents. He also won the event in 2025. The strong field could affect who joins England’s team for the upcoming Chess Olympiad. Meanwhile, young Scottish prodigy Freddy Waldhausen Gordon earned his IM title, and teenage talents like Supratit Banerjee are making headlines.
