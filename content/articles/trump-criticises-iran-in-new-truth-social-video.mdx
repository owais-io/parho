In an early‑morning video posted on his Truth Social account, former President Donald Trump talked about U.S. foreign policy and singled out Iran. He warned that the Iranian government is a threat, called for a strong military response, and said the U.S. should work to topple the regime. Trump compared his moves to past presidents, including Bush, and said the goal is to protect America and its allies. The piece criticises Trump’s approach and raises doubts about the strategy.
