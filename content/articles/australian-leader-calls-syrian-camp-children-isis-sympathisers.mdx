At a press conference, opposition leader Angus Taylor said the 23 children and 11 women living in a Syrian detention camp are “ISIS sympathisers” and urged they be denied entry to Australia. He criticized journalists and linked the group to the Islamic State. The comment comes amid a heated debate over repatriating Australian citizens from the camp, with the government saying it has limited options. Critics accuse Taylor of using children as political pawns.
