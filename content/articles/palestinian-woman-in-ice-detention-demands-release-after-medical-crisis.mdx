A Palestinian woman arrested at a protest in April has been held in ICE detention for almost a year. After a seizure and medical neglect, lawmakers and activists are demanding her release. She says she was chained in a hospital, denied family contact, and forced to eat non‑halal food. Officials argue she poses no threat, but the case highlights the crackdown on protest speech.
