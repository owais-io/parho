Ian Arnot, a 45‑year‑old LGBTQ+ activist, died after a battle with cancer. He spent 24 years at BT, rising to head of corporate communications, and helped launch and run charities that support gay people and those with HIV. In 2023 he became a chartered fellow of the CIPR and was set to join IHG before his diagnosis. Born in Kenya and raised in Scotland, he was a devoted public‑service advocate and recently married.
