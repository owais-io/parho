England beat Nepal in a tense match at the Wankhede Stadium, winning by just a few runs. The win is part of a pattern where England often snatches victories from close games, which gives them confidence for the T20 World Cup. Players like Jacob Bethell and Harry Brook said that learning to stay calm in pressure moments is key. Even when big names struggle, others step up to secure the win.
