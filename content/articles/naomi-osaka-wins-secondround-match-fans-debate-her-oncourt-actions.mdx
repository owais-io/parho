Osaka beat Sorana Cirstea 6‑3, 4‑6, 6‑2 at the Australian Open, but a row erupted over her behavior on the court. Earlier in the tournament she wore a jellyfish‑inspired outfit that drew praise and criticism. During the match Osaka shouted at the umpire after a missed serve, prompting a complaint from Cirstea. She apologized afterward and said she was just excited. Osaka is chasing a fifth Grand Slam title while managing health and media pressure.
