CNN is caught in a big ownership battle. Warner Bros Discovery, the parent company, could be sold to either Netflix or rival Paramount. President Trump wants a new owner that won’t criticize him, so he’s leaning toward Paramount. The fight shows how large media companies are merging, reducing competition, and putting public interest at risk. It highlights worries that a few big owners control too much news, hurting democracy.
