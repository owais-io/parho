Terry Yorath, who captained Wales and played for Leeds in the 1970s, has died aged 75 after a brief illness. The former midfielder was a key player for Don Revie’s Leeds team and later managed Wales, almost taking them to the 1994 World Cup. Yorath also survived the Valley Parade fire in 1985. His son Daniel died in 1998 from a heart condition. Fans and teammates mourn his loss.
