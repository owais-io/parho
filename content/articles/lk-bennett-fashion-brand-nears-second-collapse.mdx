LK Bennett, a high‑end fashion chain that started in 1990, has filed for administrators for the second time in six years. With about 280 employees and losses of £3.2 million, the company is under heavy debt, almost £22 million, and has breached lender rules. It was rescued by a Chinese owner after a 2019 collapse. Fans include the Duchess of Cambridge, but the brand is still at risk of closing.
