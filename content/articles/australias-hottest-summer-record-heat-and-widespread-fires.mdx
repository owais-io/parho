This summer, Australia hit record high temperatures, with cities like Melbourne and Adelaide reaching 45‑48 °C. The heat sparked almost 200 fires in Victoria, burning over 435,000 hectares and destroying more than 900 homes. Insurance losses hit about $1.6 bn. While solar power rose to 50 % of the grid, the Labor government still approved new coal projects, drawing criticism from environmental groups.
