Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK would not take part in the U.S. and Israel’s strikes against Iran, but he has allowed the U.S. to use British air bases for defensive bombing of Iranian missile sites. He insists the move is legal and needed to protect British troops and citizens in the Gulf. The U.S. president is unhappy, while UK lawmakers question the decision.
