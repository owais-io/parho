A severe winter storm across eastern North America caused at least 49 deaths, hit 312,000 homes with power cuts, and left 213 million people under alerts. The blizzard also dumped record snow in Toronto, while heatwaves in Australia broke temperature records and sparked fires. The mix of cold, wind, and rain led to widespread damage, flooding and dozens of weather‑related incidents worldwide.
