The Kennedy Center board in Washington DC has voted to rename the arts venue after former President Donald Trump, calling it the Trump‑Kennedy Center. The change follows Trump’s role in leading the board, which critics say was a takeover. The name change is controversial because federal law protects the Kennedy name, and some board members say it might not be legal. The move has sparked protests and debate about changing historic names.
