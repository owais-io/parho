This week, a big reveal said that the famous street‑artist Banksy is actually 52‑year‑old Robin Gunningham, but a long‑running mystery keeps people fascinated. At the same time, a fake post claimed novelist Elena Ferrante had died, when in fact it was a hoax by a hoaxer pretending to be her translator. The piece argues that both artists keep their names hidden not just for safety but because anonymity lets their work speak for itself and keeps the magic alive.
