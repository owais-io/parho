41‑year‑old ER nurse Suze Lopez in Bakersfield had a rare pregnancy that grew outside her uterus, hidden behind a large ovarian cyst. She only discovered she was pregnant when a routine test was positive, and weeks later her belly hurt badly. Doctors rushed her to the hospital, where a team of 30 surgeons removed the cyst and delivered baby Ryu. Both mom and baby recovered, and the family is celebrating their first holiday with a new child.
