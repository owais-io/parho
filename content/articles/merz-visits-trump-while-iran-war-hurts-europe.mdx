German politician Friedrich Merz flew to Washington after Israel’s attack on Iran, hoping to calm tensions and protect Europe’s interests. He met President Trump in the Oval Office, trying to shift the talk from Iran to issues that hurt Germany, like rising energy prices and U.S. tariffs. The war has hurt stock markets and European economies. Merz also faced pressure at home, as his party battles a rising far‑right and looming elections.
