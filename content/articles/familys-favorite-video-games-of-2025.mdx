This lighthearted yearly recap shows what games the author’s family loved in 2025. The eldest daughter danced with Just Dance and played Horizon Zero Dawn, the middle son raged Overwatch, the youngest stuck to Resident Evil and The Sims, and the wife stuck to Crash Bandicoot while scrolling. The dad jokes about his son’s Minecraft and shares his own favorite, Marvel Snap, plus games he wishes he’d played more or less. He also teases GTA 6 for 2026.
