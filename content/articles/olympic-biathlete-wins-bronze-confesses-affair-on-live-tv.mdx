Norwegian biathlete Sturla Holm Lægreid won bronze at the Winter Olympics, but his moment in the spotlight was cut short by a tearful TV interview. He admitted to cheating on his girlfriend six months ago and said it’s the worst week of his life. Lægreid also praised his performance, saying he used a club video as motivation. His confession shocked viewers and added drama to an already emotional race.
