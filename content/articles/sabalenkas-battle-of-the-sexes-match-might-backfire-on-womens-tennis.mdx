Aryna Sabalenka, who won four majors in 2025, is set to play Nick Kyrgios in a Dubai exhibition called a “Battle of the Sexes.” Many think it’s just a publicity stunt that could hurt women’s tennis. Critics say the match offers no real competition, draws on past controversies, and may give the wrong message about equality. They argue fans should focus on genuine matches instead of staged events.
