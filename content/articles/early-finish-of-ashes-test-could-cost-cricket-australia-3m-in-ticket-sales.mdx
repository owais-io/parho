Travis Head’s century and England’s fast batting finished the Ashes opener in Perth after just two days, cutting ticket sales for the final two days. Cricket Australia says it will lose more than $3 million in revenue and will refund those who bought day‑3 or day‑4 tickets. Despite the early finish, TV ratings were high and the series still looks set for a record‑breaking year.
