Cheltenham’s Trials Day started off strong, but it turned into a drama. The star horse Sir Gino was injured early in the day, leaving trainers worried. Later, a hole was found in the turf, so the final race was delayed while officials checked the ground. After a short break, the race went on, and Kripticjim won by a head based on video footage. Despite the chaos, the day finished safely.
