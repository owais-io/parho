At the 2026 Winter Games in Italy, the United States won a record 12 gold medals and over 30 medals in total, the most ever outside North America. Many athletes who aren't household names helped the tally, with strong showing in cross‑country skiing, alpine, speed skating and hockey. Snowboarding still strong, but competition has grown. The U.S. also earned medals in figure skating, bobsleigh, luge, and curling.
