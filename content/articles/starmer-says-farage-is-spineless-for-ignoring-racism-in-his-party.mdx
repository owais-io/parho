Prime Minister Keir Starmer has blasted Reform UK leader Nigel Farage, calling him “spineless” for not confronting racism in his party. He pointed to old allegations that Farage made hateful comments while at school—songs about the Holocaust, praising Hitler, and using the N‑word. Starmer also criticized Farage for ignoring a fellow MP’s racist remarks about advertisements. Farage denies the claims, saying memories are wrong, and has refused to discipline his MP.
