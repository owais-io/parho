Harry Redknapp, a former footballer and manager, is now a long‑time racehorse owner. His horse, “The Jukebox Man,” is set to run in the prestigious King George VI Chase. Redknapp talks about his East End roots and the old‑fashioned betting days that shaped his love of racing. He says the race feels like the Champions League, giving him hope for a big victory even though the horse isn’t the favourite.
