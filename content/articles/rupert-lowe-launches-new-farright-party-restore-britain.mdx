In Great Yarmouth, former Reform UK MP Rupert Lowe announced a new far‑right party called Restore Britain, promising mass deportations and a national revival. He also formed a local “Great Yarmouth First” group to contest county‑council seats. Other right‑wing groups, such as Advance UK and Tommy Robinson, backed Lowe, threatening to split the vote that helped Reform UK win seats in 2024. The split could weaken Reform in the next general election.
