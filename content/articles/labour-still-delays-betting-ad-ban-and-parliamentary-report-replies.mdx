Labor has not yet answered a committee report that asked for a phased ban on gambling ads, even after 978 days. The government also left dozens of other committee reports unanswered, with many overdue by more than a year. Critics say this shows a lack of transparency and respect for parliament. Meanwhile, changes to the Freedom of Information system face opposition, and lawmakers push for better oversight of political jobs and gambling harms.
