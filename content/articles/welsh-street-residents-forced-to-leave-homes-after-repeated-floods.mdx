In 2020, Storm Dennis turned the river next to Clydach Terrace in Wales into a flood that almost swept Paul Thomas away. The street, built on a floodplain, has been hit by heavy rain every year since. Local council RCT decided to buy 16 of the 18 houses for about £2.6 million and demolish them, making the residents the first UK “climate evacuees.” People are moving to other homes, some to Cardiff or council housing, hoping for safer living.
