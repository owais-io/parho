On a Texas highway, a welder named Rene Villarreal‑Albe pulled his truck in front of an unconscious SUV driver, slowly braking until both vehicles stopped. His wife filmed the moment and shared it online. A nearby nurse began CPR, and emergency crews arrived. The unconscious driver, who had a pulse but was critical, was taken to the hospital. Villarreal‑Albe earned praise as a local hero for his quick thinking.
