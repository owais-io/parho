Labour MPs are talking with the Green Party about moving over, but they want guarantees that they'll be supported for future elections. The Greens, led by Zack Polanski, have been meeting with a handful of left‑wing Labour backbenchers who are unhappy with Keir Starmer’s leadership. Although the Greens have grown quickly and won a recent by‑election, they say defections aren’t a priority right now, focusing instead on upcoming contests.
