A 71‑year‑old man named George Zinn is in jail after he pretended to shoot conservative activist Charlie Kirk. Zinn claimed “I shot him – now shoot me” after Kirk was killed by a sniper at Utah Valley University, but police found he was lying. He also admitted to holding child‑abuse pictures. Zinn pleaded no‑contest to an obstruction charge and got up to five years. For the child‑abuse cases he faces up to 15 years.
