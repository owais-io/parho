The New South Wales government is rolling out new rules that will require sellers to list a price guide on every property ad and make agents show how they calculate prices. If an agent underquotes a property, the penalty jumps to $110,000 (or three times the commission, whichever is larger). “Dummy bidding” will also face the same fine. These changes aim to stop misleading listings and protect buyers in a tight housing market.
