NYU Langone Health in New York has shut its program that gives gender‑affirming care to minors. The move comes after the Trump administration threatened to cut federal funding for such services and after the hospital lost its medical director. New York’s attorney general wants care to continue, and a judge has blocked the federal order. The decision follows a wave of hospitals pausing similar treatments and is praised by the White House, but opposed by advocates.
