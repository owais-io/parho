Russia launched a big drone and missile strike, using over 650 drones and 30 missiles, killing three people—including a 4‑year‑old—and leaving several Ukrainian regions without power just before Christmas. Polish jets were sent to protect their airspace. While Ukraine hit Russian oil plants, peace talks in Miami stalled, and Russia keeps attacking Ukrainian energy sites to weaken the population during the cold winter.
