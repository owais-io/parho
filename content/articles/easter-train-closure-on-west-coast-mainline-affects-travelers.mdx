Passengers planning to travel on the West Coast Mainline during Easter will find no trains from London Euston to Milton Keynes between Good Friday (3 April) and 8 April, and no service between Preston and Lancaster on 4‑5 April. Network Rail is carrying out a £400m upgrade, laying new track in Willesden and fixing stations, and chose the holiday period because it is the quietest time. Other routes will also see changes, so check schedules before you go.
