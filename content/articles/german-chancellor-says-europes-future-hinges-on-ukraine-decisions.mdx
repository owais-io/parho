Germany’s new chancellor Friedrich Merz warned that the choices European leaders make about Ukraine will shape Europe’s future. At the same time, French President Emmanuel Macron met China’s Xi Jinping, urging stronger talks to end the war and improve trade. China wants less friction with the EU over electric‑vehicle subsidies. Meanwhile, Russian President Vladimir Putin said peace talks were “necessary” but rejected some proposals. The EU is also debating how to use frozen Russian assets, with Belgium pushing back.
