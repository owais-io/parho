Russian bombs hit the Ukrainian city of Zaporizhzhia, killing five people and wounding three. The attack set buildings on fire and left rubble everywhere. Ukrainian President Zelensky says he will discuss a U.S. peace proposal with Donald Trump. The draft plan, seen by journalists, asks Ukraine to give up Crimea, Donetsk, and Luhansk, stay out of NATO, and cut its army size, in exchange for security guarantees and EU membership. Some Ukrainian officials call it unacceptable.
