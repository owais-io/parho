On Saturday, a shooting at Brown University killed two students and injured nine. Police had detained a person of interest, but released him later because there was no evidence. The case now has no known suspect, so authorities are combing video footage and using phone‑location data to find the killer. The campus is still under a temporary police presence, classes are canceled, and the community is coping with shock and grief.
