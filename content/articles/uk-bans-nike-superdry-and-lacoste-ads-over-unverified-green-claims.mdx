Ads from Nike, Superdry, and Lacoste were pulled in the UK because they used words like “sustainable” without any proof. The Advertising Standards Authority said the claims were vague and could mislead shoppers. Each brand must show clear evidence next time. Separately, a Betway gambling ad featuring Lewis Hamilton was banned for targeting under‑18s.
