House Democrats asked the Justice Department to explain why Gail Slater, the antitrust chief, was forced to resign after blocking a $14 bn Hewlett Packard Enterprise‑Juniper merger. They want details on Trump‑linked lobbyists’ influence and other big mergers like American Express‑CWT and Live Nation‑Ticketmaster. The move could spark a larger probe if Democrats regain House control.
