UK Foreign Secretary Yvette Cooper says the country is not being dragged into an Iraq‑style war after an Iranian drone hit an RAF base in Cyprus. The strike followed a US‑Israeli attack that killed Iran’s leader. While the UK will not support the strikes, it has agreed to let the US use British bases to hit Iranian missile sites, and ministers are ready to evacuate up to 300,000 Britons if needed. Cooper stresses a diplomatic solution.
