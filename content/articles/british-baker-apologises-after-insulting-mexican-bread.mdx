British baker Richard Hart caused a stir in Mexico after saying the country has no real bread culture and that its wheat is bad. He called the white rolls used for tortas “ugly” and said Mexican wheat is highly processed. Mexican food lovers outraged him on social media, and he later posted an apology, saying he didn’t mean disrespect. The comments touch a sensitive topic, as bread is a key part of Mexican daily life and tradition.
