Zelenskyy is flying to the US to meet Trump at Mar‑a‑Lago, hoping to speed up a peace plan with Russia. Ukraine faces daily missile threats, while the 20‑point deal is almost ready. Ukraine wants NATO‑style security guarantees, but Russia wants the Donbas region. Trump says he’ll review the plan, and talks are still far from finished, as the fighting continues.
