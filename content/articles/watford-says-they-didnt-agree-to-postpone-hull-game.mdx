Watford said they were ready to play against Hull, but the match was stopped 18 minutes before kickoff because the referee found the pitch unsafe. The club insists they didn't agree to the postponement, while Hull says the decision was made together and the pitch was actually fit to play. The halt added to a string of frozen‑pitch cancellations across the Championship, League One and Two.
