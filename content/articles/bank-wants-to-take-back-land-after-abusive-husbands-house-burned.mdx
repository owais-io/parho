Francesca Onody lost her husband, her cottage, and all her money when her abusive spouse set it on fire in 2022. After the blaze, the bank, Halifax, threatened to repossess the ruined land because of unpaid mortgage debt. She fought legal battles to claim the estate, won probate, changed the property deeds, and finally paid off the mortgage. Halifax stopped the repossession, cut fees, and gave her compensation.
