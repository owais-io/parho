New data shows a boost in how people feel about the NHS, the first rise since 2019. Labour says the system is getting better, but many problems still loom. Long waiting lists, a weak dental service, gaps in maternity and mental‑health care, and a fresh doctors’ strike show the work is far from done. Young voters are especially doubtful, and local‑devolution experiments are being tried.
