The article argues that the Australian Coalition is spreading misinformation that climate policies hurt the economy, using fossil‑fuel politicians to push a false narrative. The author explains that renewables are cheaper than coal and gas, yet the Coalition’s actions have kept prices high. He says the Coalition’s tactics mislead voters, slow the transition to clean energy, and harm regional communities most affected by climate change.
