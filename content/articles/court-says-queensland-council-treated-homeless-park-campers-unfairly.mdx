In Queensland, a state supreme court ruled that the City of Moreton Bay unlawfully evicted homeless residents from a park. The council used police, rangers, a bulldozer and an excavator to clear two tent cities after changing its local laws to ban homelessness. Residents were given only hours to leave, and the council seized personal items – even a deceased daughter’s ashes – without consent. The judge said the evictions were cruel, degrading and violated the right to a home and property.
