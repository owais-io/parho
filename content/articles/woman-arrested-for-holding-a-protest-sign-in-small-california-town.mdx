Jenny O’Connell-Nowain was taken to jail after sitting with a sign during a Shasta County board meeting. She was convicted of disrupting a public meeting, though she said it was a peaceful protest. The case highlights how the conservative‑led county has clashed with residents who speak out. Her husband was fired from a county job for speaking at meetings. The couple now faces jail time and legal battles, hoping to protect free speech.
