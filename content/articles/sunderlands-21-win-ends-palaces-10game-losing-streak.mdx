Sunderland defeated Crystal Palace 2‑1 in the Premier League, extending Palace’s winless run to 10 games. The match followed Palace’s sale of defender Marc Guéhi to Manchester City and manager Oliver Glasner’s announcement that he would leave at season’s end. Sunderland’s goals came from Enzo Le Fée and Brian Brobbey, while Palace struggled in midfield and couldn’t keep up, ending their long losing streak.
