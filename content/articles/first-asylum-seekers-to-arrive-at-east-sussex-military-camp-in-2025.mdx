England’s Home Office plans to send the first group of asylum seekers to a military camp in Crowborough, East Sussex, next year. The move aims to replace hotels used for asylum accommodation. Local residents and the council are upset, planning protests and a legal challenge because they say the camp lacks the facilities and safety needed for 540 men. The government says it will prepare the site and that it wants to end hotel use by the next election.
