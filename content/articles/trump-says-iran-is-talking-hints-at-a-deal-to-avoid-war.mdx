Trump said Iran is talking to the U.S., hinting at a deal that could stop a military strike. At the same time, the U.S. has sent a naval group near Iran’s coast, raising fears of a clash. Iranian leaders warned that any U.S. attack could spark a regional war. Diplomatic talks are ongoing, with both sides saying war is not their goal, and Iran wants negotiations, not conflict.
