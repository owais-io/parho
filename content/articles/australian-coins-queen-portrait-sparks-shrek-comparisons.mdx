Australian collectors slammed two new coins that celebrate Queen Elizabeth II’s centenary because the queen’s portrait looks more like a cartoon character than the real monarch. Social media users compared the face to Shrek, Mrs Doubtfire, and other funny roles, saying it doesn’t resemble the queen. The Royal Australian Mint said the design captures her essence and that the coins are limited editions. Some fans still love the art.
