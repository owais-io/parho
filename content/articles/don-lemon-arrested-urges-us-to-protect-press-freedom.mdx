Don Lemon, the former CNN anchor, was arrested after covering an anti‑immigration protest that disrupted a Minnesota church. He spoke at a Human Rights Campaign event in New York, saying the First Amendment is vital and must be defended. Lemon warned that when the press is threatened, democracy suffers. He said he is a journalist, not a protester, and that truth‑telling is a weapon of power. His arrest follows charges of conspiracy linked to the church protest.
