Russian missiles and drones hit Ukraine’s power grid, knocking out power in several regions and causing the Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant to lose off‑site power for the 11th time. Meanwhile, Ukraine’s peace talks are being pushed by Trump‑affiliated advisers in Florida, hoping a US‑led deal ends the war. At the same time, the US released a strategy that openly backs Europe’s far‑right parties, but the EU’s foreign‑policy chief said the US remains its biggest ally.
