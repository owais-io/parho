Qatar’s state‑owned TV network, beIN Sports, has signed a deal to broadcast the 2028 Los Angeles Olympic Games in the Middle East and North Africa. The contract, signed in Milan, gives beIN exclusive rights to show the Games on all its channels in the region. The move comes as Qatar pushes to host the 2036 Olympics, hoping to use its existing sports infrastructure from the 2022 World Cup and win IOC support. The bid also faces scrutiny over Qatar’s human‑rights record.
