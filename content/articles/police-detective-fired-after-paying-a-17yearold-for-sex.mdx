A Merseyside police detective who paid a 17‑year‑old boy for sex has been dismissed from the force. John Rigby pleaded guilty to paying for the child’s sexual services and also to illegally accessing police computers. The chief constable said the act damages policing’s reputation and that no officer should serve after such a crime. Rigby is in custody, awaiting a sentence, and is now banned from future police work.
