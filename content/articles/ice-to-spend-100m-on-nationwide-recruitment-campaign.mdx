ICE is planning a $100 million, one‑year advertising blitz to hire thousands of new officers for the Trump administration’s deportation push. The ads target conservative radio listeners, gun‑rights supporters, military fans and other patriotic groups, using symbols like Uncle Sam and promises of up to a $50,000 signing bonus. The agency is also paying influencers to promote the job to younger audiences. With over 220,000 applications already, ICE hopes to add 14,000 staff and increase arrests.
