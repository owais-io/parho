Football Daily has taken a lighthearted look at the year’s most embarrassing goals, calling them “kacktor” (German for “sh!t goal”). The article lists a handful of bad shots and own‑goals, from a Dortmund reserve player’s own‑goal to a Stockport keeper’s counter‑attack mishap. It pokes fun at German slang and the joy of watching bad football, promising more “worst” moments in an upcoming WDR video.
