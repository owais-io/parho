A former Metropolitan Police volunteer, who now goes by Gwyn Samuels, was sentenced to 24 years in prison for sexually abusing two women. The crimes started in 2018 when he befriended a 12‑year‑old girl online and later attacked her at a Christian festival. He also posed as a teenager to meet an 18‑year‑old woman and raped her. The judge warned that the offender poses a serious risk, and a lifelong ban was imposed.
