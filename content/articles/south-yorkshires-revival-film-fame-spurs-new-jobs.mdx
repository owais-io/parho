The story talks about how South Yorkshire, once a steel town, is rebounding. A local pub, Fagan’s, hosted the owners of Netflix’s hit Adolescence, which won awards and made the region famous. Now the area has big podcast festivals, a new weapons factory, and growing tech jobs. Politicians and businesses are investing, but the region still needs good transport so young people can keep the momentum.
