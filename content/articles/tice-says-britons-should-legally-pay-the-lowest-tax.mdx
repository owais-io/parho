Reform UK deputy leader Richard Tice said every Briton should try to pay the minimum tax they can legally. He dismissed a Sunday Times story that claimed he avoided almost £600,000 in corporation tax by using a REIT scheme. Tice said the scheme was legal and that people should aim to minimise tax payments. Labour called for an investigation into his use of the REIT.
