David McCann, the editor of the Scottish Times and Sunday Times, has been suspended after police arrested him following a search of his Edinburgh home. He faces charges for indecent online conduct and threatening behaviour, an investigation that is unrelated to his journalism work. McCann was briefly held overnight, then granted bail. He joined the Times in 2018 and previously worked for other Scottish newspapers.
