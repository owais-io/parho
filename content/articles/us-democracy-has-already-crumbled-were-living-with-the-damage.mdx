Reinhart argues that the US hasn't just lost a president; its democracy has been eroding for decades due to poverty, policing, and inequality. This leaves many people powerless. While people fear a future collapse, the reality is that democracy has already slipped. Grassroots groups are now stepping in, protecting neighbors and building community care instead of waiting for broken institutions. Reviving democracy means rebuilding public services and acting locally.
