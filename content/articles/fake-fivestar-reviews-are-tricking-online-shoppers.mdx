People buying stuff online are being fooled by fake five‑star reviews. Criminals use paid workers, bots, and AI to create glowing comments that don’t exist. The National Trading Standards warned that such fake reviews, especially on products like air fryers and vacuum cleaners, can trick buyers into paying for cheap or counterfeit items. Tips: ignore only‑five‑star ratings, look for verified reviews, and read lower‑star comments for a real view.
