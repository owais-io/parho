X says it will comply with UK law after critics slammed its AI tool Grok for creating sexual images of women and children. Prime Minister Keir Starmer called the photos “disgusting” and urged the company to act. Ofcom is investigating, and X has limited Grok’s ability to generate revealing pictures. Many Britons want a ban, and the government is considering stronger rules to stop non‑consensual AI‑generated images.
