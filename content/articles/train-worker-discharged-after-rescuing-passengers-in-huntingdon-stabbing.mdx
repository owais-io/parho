Samir Zitouni, a 48‑year‑old LNER crew member, was badly hurt while protecting passengers on a high‑speed train during a stabbing in Huntingdon on Nov. 1. He was in critical condition but, thanks to doctors, is now home recovering. His family says they appreciate the support but want privacy. The attacker, 32‑year‑old Anthony Williams, has been charged with multiple counts and will appear in court next month.
