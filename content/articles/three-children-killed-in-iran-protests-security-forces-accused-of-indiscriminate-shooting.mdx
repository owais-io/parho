In Iran, protests over the economy have spread to many cities. Human rights groups say security forces fired at crowds, killing at least three children and arresting over 40 minors. The crackdown has caused about 20 deaths and nearly 1,000 arrests in total. The violence has drawn international criticism, as authorities continue to brand protesters as rioters and use heavy weapons against demonstrators.
