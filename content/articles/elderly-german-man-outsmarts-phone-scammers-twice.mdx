An 85‑year‑old pensioner in Bavaria has caught phone fraudsters twice. In each case, callers pretended to be police and claimed a relative needed €10,000 and gold for bail after an accident. The man kept the scammer on the line, told his daughter to alert police, and set up a fake pickup spot. When the suspects arrived, police arrested them on the scene. Authorities warn seniors to be cautious and not share personal details.
