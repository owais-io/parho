A federal judge in Minneapolis is hearing a lawsuit that says Trump’s “Operation Metro Surge,” which sent 3,000 ICE agents to Minnesota, is an illegal occupation of the state. The case questions whether the agents’ presence violates the 10th Amendment and harms local law‑enforcement and residents. After three federal‑agent shootings, the state asks the judge to stop the surge, citing safety risks and overreach.
