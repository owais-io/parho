A hacker temporarily took over the ABC News Facebook page after a staff account was compromised, posting two adult photos and a picture of the prime minister eating a hotdog. The images were removed within minutes, comments were deleted, and ABC secured the account and is investigating the breach. The incident highlights the need for stronger social‑media security.
