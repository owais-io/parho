UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer ordered the destroyer HMS Dragon and anti‑drone helicopters to Cyprus after a drone struck a Royal Air Force base there. France is sending a frigate, and Britain’s move has drawn criticism for being late. The warship, which can fight missiles and drones, will join existing radar, jets and air‑defence gear. The decision comes amid tensions over Iran and recent U.S. strikes.
