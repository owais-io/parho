Ukraine’s top negotiator said the two‑day U.S‑led talks in Abu Dhabi were productive and focused on real steps. President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said the talks must bring peace, not give Russia a chance to keep fighting, and he expects a new prisoner exchange soon. Russian officials said they would keep fighting until Kyiv’s “decisions” change the war. Meanwhile, Russia used cluster bombs and drones, while Europe approved a €90 bn loan to help Ukraine.
