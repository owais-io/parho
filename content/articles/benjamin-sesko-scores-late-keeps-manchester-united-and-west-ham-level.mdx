Manchester United and West Ham finished 1‑1 after a late goal from Benjamin Sesko gave United the point they needed. The match stayed even until added time, when Sesko’s sharp finish levelled the score. West Ham were denied a deserved win after taking the lead early, while United missed chances to double up. Both sides look disappointed but will keep working to improve.
