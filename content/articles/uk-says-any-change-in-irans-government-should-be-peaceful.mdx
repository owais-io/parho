UK’s transport secretary said any change in Iran’s government must be peaceful. She said Britain will not get involved in US plans to use force after President Trump said he could back protesters with military help. Iran has seen protests over the economy, with police crackdowns and many deaths. The UK is watching closely and wants to stop more violence. The US is considering strikes, while Iran warns it will target US and Israel if attacked.
