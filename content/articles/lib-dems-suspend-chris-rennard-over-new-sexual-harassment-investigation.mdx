The Liberal Democrat party has suspended former chief executive Lord Chris Rennard after a new inquiry into sexual‑harassment claims he allegedly made in 2013. Party leaders say the earlier investigation was flawed and that Rennard should not sit in the House of Lords. Rennard denies wrongdoing, pointing to police and lawyer reviews that found no evidence. Three of the four complainants welcome the suspension, while the party pushes for easier peer expulsion.
