Police in Western Australia are still looking for a 32‑year‑old man who went missing while snorkeling near Ledge Point Beach, north of Perth. At the same time, New South Wales authorities are searching for a Nepalese man missing at Coogee Beach in Sydney, after a rip tide swept him out to sea. Several other drownings occurred over New Year’s, prompting warnings about dangerous surf and urging people to stay in patrolled beaches.
