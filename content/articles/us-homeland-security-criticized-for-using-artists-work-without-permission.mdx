The US Department of Homeland Security (DHS) shared a photo of a quiet beach that was created by 78‑year‑old Japanese designer Hiroshi Nagai. DHS posted it on social media to discuss 100 million deportations, but Nagai said the agency used his art without asking. DHS defended its choice, saying it keeps people informed. This isn’t the first time DHS has used art or songs without permission, which has upset many artists and their families.
