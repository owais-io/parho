On Tuesday afternoon a 13‑year‑old boy was stabbed at Kingsbury High School in Brent, followed by a 12‑year‑old. Police, led by counter‑terrorism officers, arrested the suspect on suspicion of attempted murder and recovered a weapon. The incident isn’t classified as terrorism, and detectives are working quickly to piece together what happened. The lower school closed, while officials from the mayor, Home Secretary, and Education Secretary offered support.
