Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has asked an independent watchdog to review MPs’ travel perks after a scandal involving a trade minister who paid over $2,200 for a trip that was called ‘official’ while he was at a wedding. The attorney general has also put her travel expenses under audit, and former senator Sussan Ley offered to help. Meanwhile, Brittany Higgins declared bankrupt, climate protestors won a class action against Victoria police, and a flamingo‑shaped cup controversy sparked a shop’s backlash.
