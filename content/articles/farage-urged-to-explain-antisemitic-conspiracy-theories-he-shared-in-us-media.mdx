Farage has been urged to explain antisemitic conspiracy theories he aired on US shows, including Alex Jones and others, which tied bankers and George Soros to global control plots. Jewish groups and MPs want him to deny these ideas, but he has stayed silent, saying critics are “manufactured.” New allegations of racist language from his school days add pressure. Politicians across parties say he must own his words and reject hate.
