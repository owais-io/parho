On Sunday, two gunmen attacked a Bondi event, killing 15 people, including 10‑year‑old Matilda. Her Ukrainian‑Australian parents spoke to a crowd of over 2,000, asking everyone to remember her name. The memorial at Bondi Pavilion read out the names of the victims, and donations poured in to help families. The community gathered to stand together against hate and support each other in the aftermath.
