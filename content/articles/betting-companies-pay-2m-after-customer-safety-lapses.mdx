Paddy Power and Betfair, part of Flutter Entertainment, agreed to pay £2 million to the Gambling Commission after a review found they failed to spot and stop risky gambling. One customer bet almost eight hours straight and spent over £86,000, losing £6,000, with no early help. The regulator said the companies’ warning systems were not sensitive enough. This is Flutter’s second fine in three years. The companies say they have upgraded their safety tools and will keep improving.
