FIFA president Gianni Infantino and Olympic Committee chief Kirsty Coventry both said Russia should be allowed back into international football and Olympic events. Their comments have sparked outrage, with critics arguing that the move ignores Russia’s war in Ukraine, its record on human rights and the corruption scandals that have plagued FIFA and the Olympics. The article argues that using sport to mask political problems erodes the sport’s moral standing.
