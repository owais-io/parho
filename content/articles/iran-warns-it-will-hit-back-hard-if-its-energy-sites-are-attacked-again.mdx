Iran’s foreign minister said the country would respond with full force if Israel or other countries struck its energy plants again. The warning comes after Israel bombed Iran’s South Pars gas field, leading to Iranian strikes on Qatar’s Ras Laffan gas complex. The comment came as gas prices jumped and markets fell worldwide. Iran says it only held back to respect a call for calm.
