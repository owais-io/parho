In a recent White House cabinet meeting, President Trump joked that Venezuela could build a statue in his honor, a comment that made the room erupt in laughter. Interior Secretary Doug Burgum joined in, noting Venezuelans already see Trump as a hero. The meeting also featured Trump’s off‑hand remarks about other politicians, criticism of Iran and the UK, and a playful jab about pens used to sign bills.
