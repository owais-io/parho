State‑sponsored hackers are turning the defence industry’s staff into prime targets, using phishing, fake job ads, and AI‑powered profiles. Google’s new report shows attacks now hit employees’ personal devices and hiring processes across Europe, the US, and even smaller suppliers. Groups from Russia, North Korea, Iran and China have spoofed company sites, sent tailored messages and impersonated recruiters to steal credentials. Ukraine has seen a 37% jump in incidents this year.
