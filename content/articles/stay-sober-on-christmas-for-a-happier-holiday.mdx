Dr. Antonia helps people quit drinking, and one client, Peter, wanted to keep his family drinks on Christmas. He feared jokes and feeling left out if he stayed sober. Dr. Antonia helped him imagine a different day: a quiet picnic with his wife and kids, no alcohol, a few thoughtful gifts, and an early bedtime. Peter told his family he wouldn't host, and they supported him. Staying sober made Christmas happier and less stressful.
