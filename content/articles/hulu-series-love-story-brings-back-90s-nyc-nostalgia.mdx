Love Story, the new Hulu series, revisits the romance and tragic death of JFK Jr and Carolyn Bessette. It shows 1990s New York in bright, stylish fashion and classic music, giving viewers a sentimental escape from today’s headlines. Critics praise its period details, but some family members say it’s inaccurate and shallow. Fans are hooked, while others are skeptical. The show taps into a broader Gen Z longing for simpler, pre‑internet days.
