Viking Football Club in Stavanger almost collapsed after relegation in 2017, but a new owner, fresh coaching, and a focus on local young players turned things around. They returned to Norway’s top league and are now regular title challengers. With a big win needed against Bodø/Glimt, the team, led by captain Zlatko Tripic, is fighting for its first league title in 34 years. Fans feel the excitement, but the result still depends on luck.
