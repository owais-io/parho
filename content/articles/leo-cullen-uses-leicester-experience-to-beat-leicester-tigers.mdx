Leo Cullen, who coached Leicester in the 2000s, now leads Leinster against Leicester Tigers. He has already beaten his former club four times and wants a third win this decade. The match also sees New Zealand star Rieko Ioane on his debut for Leinster. Cullen talks about the hard work he learned at Leicester and the friendly rivalry between the clubs.
