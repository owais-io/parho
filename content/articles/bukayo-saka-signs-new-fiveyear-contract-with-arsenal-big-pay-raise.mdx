Bukayo Saka has signed a new five‑year deal with Arsenal that will keep him at the club until 2031. The contract boosts his pay to about £300,000 a week, making him one of the club’s highest‑paid players. Arsenal wants to lock in its star forward after a strong season, while the club also looks to reward others like Declan Rice. Saka remains a key figure as Arsenal chase top honours.
