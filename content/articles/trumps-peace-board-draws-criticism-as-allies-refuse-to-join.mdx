Donald Trump has set up a new “Board of Peace” to raise money for Gaza and other conflict zones. The first meeting in Washington will feature a big fundraising push, with countries pledging more than $5 bn. But many European leaders, the Vatican, and other major allies declined to attend, saying the board’s funding and purpose are unclear and that it could undermine existing international organisations. The plan faces scepticism about whether it can deliver real aid.
