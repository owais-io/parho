Actress Cheryl Hines told a NewsNation interview that her husband, Robert F. Kennedy Jr., will not run for president in 2028. She added that although he once thought politics was not for him, he later decided to try again. The comments come as RFK Jr., a former Trump health secretary, has been discussed for a 2024 run and his controversial views on vaccines. Hines’ remarks suggest he will not seek the 2028 nomination.
