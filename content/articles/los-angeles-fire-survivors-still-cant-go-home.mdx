After the 2023 California wildfires, thousands of people in Los Angeles towns like Altadena and Pacific Palisades still can't return to their homes. The fires destroyed more than 16,000 houses, and many survivors are stuck in temporary rentals, high rent, or even abroad. Slow insurance claims, high repair costs and rising prices block rebuilding. Some families are moving away or waiting months for permits, while their trauma and debt grow.
