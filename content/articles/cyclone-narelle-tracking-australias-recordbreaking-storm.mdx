Australia is being swept by Cyclone Narelle, a powerful storm that is following a record‑breaking path around the continent. Meteorologists are tracking its progress every few hours, using real‑time maps that show wind speed and direction. The Guardian Australia team has shared a live tracking page and an animated map that updates every three hours, letting readers see the cyclone’s movement as it passes through Queensland and other regions.
