News Corp’s chief says the company is a valuable “input” for AI and has signed a $150 million deal with Meta to let the social‑media giant use its UK and US stories to train its models. The three‑year agreement covers major titles like the Wall Street Journal, the Times and the New York Post, but not Australian papers. Murdoch’s media empire also deals with OpenAI and is testing its own AI tools.
