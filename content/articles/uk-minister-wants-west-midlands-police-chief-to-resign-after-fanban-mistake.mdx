Health Secretary Wes Streeting says he is “shocked” that West Midlands Police Chief Craig Guildford still holds his post after a report found the force’s intelligence was wrong and exaggerated the threat that led to banning Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a football match. The report, written by Sir Andy Cooke, also said Guildford gave misleading evidence. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood lost confidence in him, and local officials and the Police & Crime Commissioner are calling for his resignation.
