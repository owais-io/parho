The Victorian Premier, Jacinta Allan, apologized in parliament to Indigenous communities for harms of colonisation and said the state will pursue a treaty. Victoria’s lower house passed the motion 56‑27. Meanwhile the Reserve Bank kept the cash rate steady at 3.6 %. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese gave a touching eulogy for former Labor minister Graham Richardson. In the news, 33‑year‑old climber Sasha DiGiulian became the first woman to summit El Capitán’s tough Platinum route.
