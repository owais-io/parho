In the Women’s Super League, the team with the strongest squad depth is most likely to win. Manchester City, Chelsea and Arsenal are using rotation to keep players fresh across many games. Managers say that having more than a core group helps handle injuries, fatigue and tight schedules. City’s bench has even scored winners, while Chelsea’s rotation keeps the team’s rhythm. A deep squad is now a must‑have advantage in the title fight.
