Reform UK announced a plan to set up a new “UK Deportation Command” that would detain up to 24,000 people at once and deport up to 288,000 migrants a year. The party also wants to end indefinite leave to remain, tighten surveillance, and ban churches turning into mosques. Human‑rights groups slammed the proposal as cruel and divisive, saying it would tear families apart and waste money. Reform’s spokesperson insists the UK will avoid violent protests seen in the U.S.
