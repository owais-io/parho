UN experts wrote to the UK government saying that L&Q, the country’s biggest social housing company, has repeatedly failed to keep its homes safe. They cite Sanjay Ramburn, a disabled man who lived with five family members in a flat in Forest Gate, London, for years with no electricity, multiple ceiling collapses and damp that hurt everyone’s health. L&Q offered little money and temporary housing, but the family kept asking for fixes. The government says it will act.
