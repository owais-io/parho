Sale lost at home to Glasgow in the Champions Cup, after a 14‑0 lead. Glasgow’s players, led by captain Kyle Steyn, turned the game around, scoring four tries to finish 26‑0. Sale had several England internationals missing and an early injury, while their coach said he’s not worried and will keep working. The defeat shows the team struggles in home matches this season.
