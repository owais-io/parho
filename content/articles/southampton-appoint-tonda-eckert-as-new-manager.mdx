Southampton have hired 32‑year‑old German Tonda Eckert as permanent manager after a bright interim spell. The under‑21 coach took over last month, winning four of five matches and boosting the team’s confidence. He’s keen on promotion and praised for his empathy and clear communication. The club has signed him to 2027. Southampton sit 14th, five points away from playoffs, and will face Birmingham on Saturday.
