During a second day of U.S.-brokered talks in Abu Dhabi, Russia launched a large missile and drone attack on Kyiv that killed one person and injured 23. Ukrainian officials say the strike came while diplomats were negotiating a settlement to Russia’s invasion. President Zelenskiy warned that air‑defence deals made with President Trump need to be fully implemented. The assault also damaged energy infrastructure, cutting power for hundreds of buildings.
