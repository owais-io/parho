Hidden‑market promoters are running social‑media giveaways that offer unlicensed weight‑loss medicines like retatrutide, Glow pens and melanotan as prizes. They use WhatsApp, Telegram, TikTok and Instagram, with countdown timers and fake fitness programmes to lure people. The drugs are not approved, carry serious health risks, and the marketing tactics are illegal and dangerous, prompting regulators to warn consumers.
