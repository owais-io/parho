The OECD says a sharp rise in oil prices and the US‑Israel conflict over Iran could hurt the world economy, pushing inflation up to 4% by 2026. It lowered growth forecasts for Europe, the UK and South Korea, and said the AI boom will be dampened by the energy shock. Australia’s growth is now expected to slow to 1.3% in 2026, with inflation reaching 4.5%.
