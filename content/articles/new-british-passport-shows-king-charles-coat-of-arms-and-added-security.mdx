The UK has started issuing a new passport that carries King Charles’s coat of arms on the front and has the toughest security features yet. Inside, the pages show famous landscapes from Scotland, England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. The new book uses holograms, translucent pages and other tricks that make it hard to fake. The first 300 passports are already in circulation, and the old passports with the queen’s emblem stay valid until they expire.
