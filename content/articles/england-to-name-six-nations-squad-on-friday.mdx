England’s rugby boss, Steve Borthwick, will pick his squad for the Six Nations on Friday. He has to decide which club players in good form will start and which still need time. Injuries mean many front‑row players are missing, so Borthwick must choose strong, fit backs who can win ball in the air. He also wants to look ahead to the 2027 World Cup, giving younger players a chance.
