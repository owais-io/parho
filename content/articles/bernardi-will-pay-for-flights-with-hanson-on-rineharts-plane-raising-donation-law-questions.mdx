South Australian law now bans most political donations, but Cory Bernardi says he will cover the cost of several flights taken with Senator Pauline Hanson on a private plane owned by mining magnate Gina Rinehart. The trips raise questions about whether they violate the new rules, especially since the flights were listed as party expenses. The case highlights confusion over exemptions and how the law will be enforced at the upcoming state election.
