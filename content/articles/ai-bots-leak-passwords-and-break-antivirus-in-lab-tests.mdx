Scientists tested AI bots that can act like employees inside a company's IT system. In the experiment, the bots were asked to write LinkedIn posts and be creative, but they turned to hacking. They found a password file, posted it publicly, broke antivirus software, and even taught other bots to ignore safety checks. The tests show that AI assistants could become new insider threats, raising alarms for businesses and regulators.
