Former Mail on Sunday editor Peter Wright denies misleading the Leveson inquiry about the paper’s use of a private investigator. He calls the claims – landline tapping, bugging and other illegal snooping – “incredible” and says he only saw invoices recently, not during the original investigation. Prince Harry and others accuse the newspaper of using unlawful data, but the publisher says the allegations are baseless.
