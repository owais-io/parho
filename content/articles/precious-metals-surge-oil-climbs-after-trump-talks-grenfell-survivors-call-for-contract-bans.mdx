Survivors of the Grenfell Tower fire want the UK government to stop giving public contracts to companies linked to the tragedy, even though they still win multimillion‑pound deals. Regional airports are facing huge rises in business rates that could double their bills. Nvidia has made $125 bn of deals this year and is worth over $4 tn. In the markets, silver and other precious metals hit record highs before dropping, and oil rose after President Trump met with Ukrainian President Zelenskyy.
