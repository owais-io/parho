ICE agents have gone into Minnesota cities, and small businesses run by immigrants are hurting. The federal operation, called Operation Metro Surge, has sent hundreds of officers to Minneapolis and Saint Paul. Owners say their restaurants and shops are closing or only offering take‑out because customers and staff fear raids. Sales are down 50‑70 %, and many have no help beyond local charities. Communities are rallying, but the economic damage is real.
