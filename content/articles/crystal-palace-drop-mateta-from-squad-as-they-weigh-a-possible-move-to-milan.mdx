Palace manager Oliver Glasner said striker Jean‑Philippe Mateta will miss the upcoming Forest game because he isn’t at his best amid speculation about a move. Milan is keen to sign him, but the deal is still off the table. Palace also received a £35 million bid from Forest and are not keen to sell to a Premier League rival. Meanwhile, Palace have loaned Aston Villa forward Evann Guessand and are holding off on new signings, while West Ham are eyeing defender Taylor Harwood‑Bellis.
