Police said the decision to stop fans of Israeli club Maccabi Tel Aviv from going to a Birmingham match was based on safety, not politics. Members of Parliament questioned the evidence and said the ban might be biased. The police pointed to online threats and past violence at other games. Critics claim the ban looks like antisemitism, and a government inspector has been asked to check the intel. The move was made after a synagogue attack, adding to the controversy.
