The article explains how right‑wing leaders in France, Germany, Italy and elsewhere want to reverse immigration by sending large numbers of Muslims back to their home countries. The plan, called remigration, was first promised by French politician Eric Zemmour and later adopted by Italy’s League and Germany’s AfD. It mixes deportations of undocumented people with the idea of removing any migrant. The movement is spreading as far‑right parties gain power, sparking protests and legal battles.
