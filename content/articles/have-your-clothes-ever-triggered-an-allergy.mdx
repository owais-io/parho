Some fast‑fashion clothing is treated with chemicals such as heavy‑metal dyes, antimicrobials, and wrinkle‑resistant sprays that can irritate skin or cause breathing problems. The Guardian is asking readers who’ve had such reactions to share details—what garments, where they were bought, any lasting effects or doctor visits, and how it’s changed their shopping habits. If you’re ready to speak up, they’ve provided a simple form to fill out.
