The Trump administration has sent a flood of federal officers to cities like Minneapolis, making it the biggest domestic immigration raid in history. Agencies such as ICE, Border Patrol, CBP, and even the FBI and DEA have been called in to arrest and deport people. ICE’s enforcement arm (ERO) has doubled its staff and budget, while its investigative team (HSI) is now helping with deportations. Local police sometimes refuse to cooperate, especially in Democratic or “sanctuary” cities.
