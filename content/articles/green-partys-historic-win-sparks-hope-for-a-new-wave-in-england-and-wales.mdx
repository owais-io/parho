A young plumber, Hannah Spencer, became the first Green MP in the north after winning the Gorton and Denton by‑election. Her victory shows voters are shifting away from Labour, and new polls suggest the Green Party could do even better in the upcoming local elections. The article argues that the Greens’ clear plans on water, housing and the cost of living have won voters’ trust, and that a nationwide Green wave is likely.
