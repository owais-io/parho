Andrew Rosindell, the Conservative MP for Romford, has left the party and joined Nigel Farage’s Reform UK. He says he was unhappy with the Conservatives’ support for the Chagos Islands deal that will give Mauritius control of the British‑controlled islands. Rosindell’s move is the third Tory MP to defect in a week and puts Reform UK’s MPs at seven. It’s a setback for the Conservatives and a sign the party is losing support.
