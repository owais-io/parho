Sally White bought a cheap houseboat in Noosa when house prices rose during the pandemic. New state rules limit boats on the river to 28 days, aimed at reducing congestion and safety risks. About 20 houseboat owners, including Sally, worry they could be forced off the water. The government says the rule is about safety, while residents argue it targets low‑income boaters and may be class‑based.
