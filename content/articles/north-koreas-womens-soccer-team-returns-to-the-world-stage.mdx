North Korea used women’s football to boost its image, winning three Asian Cup titles and drawing huge crowds. After a 10‑year absence, the national team is back for this year’s Women’s Asian Cup, while a generation of youth champions shows the country’s future. Their return signals the state’s long‑term investment finally paying off, with games against Uzbekistan first and China later.
