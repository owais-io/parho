Susie Dent, a well‑known word expert, says children’s vocabularies are shrinking because of more screen time. She urges parents to read, talk, and play word games to help kids learn new words. Dent’s new snack‑time campaign with Soreen malt loaf offers fun ideas like reading aloud, listening to audiobooks, and asking children to invent words. She also shares 10 unusual words that will spark curiosity and make language learning enjoyable.
