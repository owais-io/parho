Marika Pogany, 82, was a beloved Bondi beach volunteer who helped deliver meals to Jewish seniors and played bridge every Tuesday. She was killed in the deadly Bondi beach shooting that left 15 dead. Friends and the community praised her calm, kind nature and her lifelong service. She had survived a family history of Holocaust loss and remained close to Slovakia’s former president. Her memory is celebrated as a source of warmth for thousands.
