The US military still used Anthropic’s Claude AI to help plan and carry out a big strike on Iran, even though President Trump just ordered all federal agencies to stop using the tool. The AI helped with intelligence, picking targets, and running simulations. Trump and the defense secretary blasted the company, while Anthropic said its terms forbid violent use. Since the break, OpenAI has stepped in to provide AI support to the Pentagon.
