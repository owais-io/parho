A sexual assault survivor spoke out in a news.com.au interview, calling out The Australian’s podcast Shadow of Doubt, which interviewed convicted child sexual abuse offenders. The story went viral, drawing over 9 million TikTok views and 860,000 page views. The Australian fired back with a rebuttal and an editorial claiming mistakes, while news.com.au added commentary and later removed references to the podcast. The two Murdoch‑owned outlets are now at odds, with no clear resolution.
