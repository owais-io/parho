Democrats can send state resolutions—official statements—to Congress, a power that was used before to influence national law. They have been silent on this while Trump pushes harsh immigration rules and other policies. The author says using resolutions could pressure lawmakers on ICE and help Democrats win seats in mid‑term elections. The tool could also expose Trump’s abuses and rally voters against him.
