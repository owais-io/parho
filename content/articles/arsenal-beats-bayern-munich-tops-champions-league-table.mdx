Arsenal beat Bayern Munich 3‑1, with Gabriel Martinelli scoring the winner. The win keeps Arsenal unbeaten in the Champions League and puts them at the top of Group A. Bayern’s young striker Lennart Karl scored their only goal, but Arsenal’s early header by Jurrien Timber and a second from Noni Madueke sealed the victory. Arsenal stay strong heading into the knockout stage, while Bayern face a tough challenge to recover.
