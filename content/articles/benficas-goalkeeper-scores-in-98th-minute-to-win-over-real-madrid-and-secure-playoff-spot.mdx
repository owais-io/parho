Benfica’s goalkeeper, Anatoliy Trubin, scored a 98th‑minute header to win 3‑2 against Real Madrid in Lisbon. The late goal moved Benfica above Marseille in the new Bigger Cup table, securing a playoff spot. Trubin, who joined the club in 2023 from Shakhtar Donetsk, dedicated the goal to Ukraine. Mourinho praised the team’s resilience, and the win also keeps Real Madrid in the mix for the playoffs.
