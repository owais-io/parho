A surfer was taken to hospital after a shark bite off Port Macquarie, marking the fourth such incident in two days. Two other victims are still in critical condition. Health officials urge residents to avoid Sydney Harbour and coastal waters because murky, brackish conditions attract sharks and their bait fish. The Surf Life Saving NSW chief recommends using local pools instead.
