Maro Itoje has told England to stay sharp and fearless if they want to finally win at Murrayfield. He points to England’s 12‑match winning streak and Scotland’s recent poor form, saying the team must be aggressive, accurate, and confident on the Scottish soil. The aim is to break the six‑year drought and keep the grand slam chase alive.
