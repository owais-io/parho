Rapper Lil Jon confirmed that his 27‑year‑old son, Nathan Smith—also known as DJ Young Slade—has died. Police found a body in a pond north of Atlanta and are treating the case as an open investigation. Lil Jon said his son was a talented producer and NYU graduate. He thanked police and fans for their support and called his son “the kindest human being.”
