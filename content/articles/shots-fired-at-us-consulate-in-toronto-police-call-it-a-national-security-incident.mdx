Two men opened fire on the U.S. consulate in downtown Toronto early Tuesday. They fired several shots from a handgun but nobody was hurt, the building’s security said. Police called the incident a “national security incident” and are raising security at U.S. and Israeli diplomatic sites in Toronto and Ottawa. Investigators are looking into whether the attack is linked to recent synagogue shootings or protests.
