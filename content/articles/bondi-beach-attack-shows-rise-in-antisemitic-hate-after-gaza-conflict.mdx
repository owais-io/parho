Bondi beach attack, 12 killed, shows sharp rise in antisemitic hate after Gaza war. Rabbi Levi Wolff said the attack felt inevitable. Antisemitic incidents spiked worldwide—over 9,000 in the US, double last year in the UK, and triple in Australia. Experts say the Gaza conflict fuels extremist thinking, and groups like IS or al‑Qaida may inspire attacks, but no direct link to the Sydney shooters has been found.
