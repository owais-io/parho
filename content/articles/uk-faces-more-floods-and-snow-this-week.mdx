UK weather is set to keep flooding on the agenda for the next week. The Environment Agency has issued 73 flood warnings, mainly in the south‑west and Midlands, plus 177 alerts across England. The Met Office says blustery showers will hit Sunday, with sunny spells Monday and heavy rain, hail, thunderstorms. Snow is expected in the north, while the south may see rain and snow on Wednesday and Thursday. Earlier storms—Goretti, Ingrid and Chandra—caused flooding in Devon and Somerset.
