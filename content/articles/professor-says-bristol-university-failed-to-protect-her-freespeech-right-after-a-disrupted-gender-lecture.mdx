Professor Alice Sullivan says the University of Bristol let protesters shut down her talk about sex and gender. She wrote to the university and the regulator, claiming they didn’t keep her right to speak safe. The protestors shouted, set off fire alarms and climbed walls. Sullivan says the university could have chosen a safer venue and allowed students to attend. She says she may take legal action to defend free speech for all voices.
