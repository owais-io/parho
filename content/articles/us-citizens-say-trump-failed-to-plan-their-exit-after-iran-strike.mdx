US Americans living in the Middle East were angry that the Trump administration offered no quick exit plan after the US‑Israel strike on Iran. Many tried calling the State Department, but got no help for days and had to arrange their own travel, sometimes using other consulates. Only after a week were a few flights organized. Those who voted for Trump feel betrayed and say the government left them alone in danger.
