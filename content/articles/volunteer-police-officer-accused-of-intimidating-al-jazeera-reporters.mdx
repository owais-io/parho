A volunteer police officer in London is under investigation after video shows him trying to scare off Al Jazeera journalists who were covering a suspected antisemitic hate crime in Golders Green. The footage shows the officer calling a Palestinian reporter “donkey” and telling him to leave. Metropolitan Police have referred the case to their professional standards unit, and the incident came amid tensions around an attack on a Jewish ambulance service.
