Sir Simon McDonald, a former senior civil servant, warned that the government must carry out a thorough check before appointing Antonia Romeo as the new cabinet secretary after Chris Wormald departs. He says the vetting process should start from scratch and be very careful. Romeo is a leading candidate, though past spending questions were cleared. McDonald wants to discuss the vetting steps with the prime minister.
