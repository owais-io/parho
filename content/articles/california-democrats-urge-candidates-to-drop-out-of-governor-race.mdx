California’s Democratic Party sent a letter urging nine candidates in the governor race to drop out if they can’t win. Party chair Rusty Hicks warned that a crowded field could let two Republicans make the November ballot. Candidates like Xavier Becerra, Katie Porter, and Tom Steyer are among the nine, but the party gave no endorsement at its convention. Critics say the letter favors insiders, while Newsom acknowledged the concern.
