The opening Six Nations game at Stade de France saw France beat Ireland 36‑21. France’s forwards dominated the breakdown, while Antoine Dupont and Matthieu Jalibert kept the back line moving. In the Irish set‑piece, Caelan Doris was tackled early and the bench helped keep pressure on France. France’s try‑scoring was solid, and their defence held Ireland back after the early kicks. The result shows France still strong at the start of the tournament, but Ireland’s effort will spark a hard competition in the coming weeks.
