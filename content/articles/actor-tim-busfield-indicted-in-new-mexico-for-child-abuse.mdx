New Mexico prosecutors have charged actor Tim Busfield with four counts of child sexual contact. The case stems from incidents on the set of the TV drama “The Cleaning Lady” between November 2022 and spring 2024, involving a young boy and his brother. Busfield denies the allegations and has not pleaded yet. The case will go to trial through the district attorney’s special victims unit. If found guilty, Busfield could face significant prison time.
