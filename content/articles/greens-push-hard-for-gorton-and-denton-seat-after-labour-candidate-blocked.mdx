After Labour’s Andy Burnham was barred from standing, the Greens are launching a full‑scale campaign to win the Gorton and Denton by‑election. They plan to station many activists, raise money, and may field Trafford council leader Hannah Spencer as their candidate. The goal is to beat Reform UK and show Labour’s weakness, a win that would embarrass Prime Minister Keir Starmer. The campaign will use leaflets, door‑knocking, and online fundraising.
