Kyiv zoo staff are keeping animals warm despite severe power cuts and shelling. With only a few hours of electricity each day, they use generators and wood‑burning stoves to heat the gorilla Toni’s enclosure and other animals’ habitats. The zoo also patches bomb damage and has received food donations. Visitors, including evacuated children and soldiers, still come to see the animals, while staff work around the clock to keep the zoo running.
