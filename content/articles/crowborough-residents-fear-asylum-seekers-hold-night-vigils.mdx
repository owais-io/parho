Crowborough residents heard rumors that hundreds of asylum seekers would be housed at a nearby military training camp. The news sparked fear, and locals held night vigils and formed a self‑policing group. Police and council officials denied any plans and said no decision had been made. The tense night ended when no asylum seekers arrived, but the town remains on edge.
