Universal Music Group and other major labels are suing AI startups that use their music without permission. They’ve also made deals with some of those companies, saying they’ll protect artists. However, advocacy groups claim artists are being sidelined and losing income. Courts are wrestling with copyright law for AI, and activists urge new laws and unions to defend creators, warning that big‑company deals could hurt artists more than help.
