Iran has cut off the internet to stop protest videos from spreading, showing the government is under pressure. The protests began in December because of high inflation and a falling currency. Thousands have marched, burned cars and buildings, and some call for the regime to fall. The Supreme Leader now labels them sabotage and threatens harsh punishments. The government also faces pressure from the US, Israel, and a weak economy, weakening its power.
