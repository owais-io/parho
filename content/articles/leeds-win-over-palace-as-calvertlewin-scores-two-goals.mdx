Dominic Calvert‑Lewin scored a brace to give Leeds United a 3‑1 win at Crystal Palace. He became the first Leeds striker in 22 years to find the net in five straight Premier League games, putting the team six points clear of the relegation zone. Palace struggled with set pieces, while Leeds’ defensive solidity and set‑piece work secured the victory, sparking hope that Calvert‑Lewin could become a top striker for the club.
