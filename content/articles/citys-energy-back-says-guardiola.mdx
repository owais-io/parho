Pep Guardiola says Manchester City have finally found the spark they lost last season. After a trophy‑free year, City are now on an eight‑match winning streak and are chasing their seventh Premier League title. He credits a tougher training schedule, a fresh coaching team and lessons from the Club World Cup for the lift in energy. With a win against Nottingham Forest and a two‑point gap to Arsenal, City look ready to keep pushing forward.
