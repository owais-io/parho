José Antonio Kast won Chile's presidential election with 58% of the vote. He is a far‑right candidate who wants to expel 330,000 undocumented migrants, build walls, and has praised former dictator Augusto Pinochet. Experts say his win could weaken Chile’s democracy and follows a trend of hard‑line leaders in Latin America. Many worry his policies will increase state violence and curb human rights.
