Donald Trump hosted a meeting of 12 Latin American leaders at his golf club in Florida to create a coalition called Shield of the Americas that would fight drug cartels, especially in Mexico. He praised U.S. actions that helped kill cartel boss El Mencho and urged cooperation with Venezuela and Cuba. Trump also warned about Chinese influence in the region and praised allies for standing with the U.S. in tough moments.
