The war between the US and Israel has hit many of Iran’s ancient buildings. Damage has been seen at Tehran’s Golestan Palace, Isfahan’s Chehel Sotoon Palace, and other sites like Falak‑ol‑Aflak Castle. Glass, tiles, and mirrors shattered, and some museums were hit. Iranian officials blame Israel and say the attacks ignore international rules meant to protect cultural heritage.
