Hamilton Island, a popular holiday spot on the Great Barrier Reef, has been sold to the U.S. investment firm Blackstone for about $1.2 billion. The Oatley family, who bought the resort in 2003 for $200 million, agreed to sell it after getting regulatory approvals. Blackstone plans to invest in the island’s hotels, restaurants, golf course, marina, and community, while keeping the local workforce and businesses involved. It’s a key employer in the Whitsundays and a beloved destination for Australians.
