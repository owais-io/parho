Keir Starmer leaked a memo telling his cabinet to stop being too deferential to Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland. He said ministers must be ready to make spending choices even if the devolved governments disagree. The warning came after Welsh Labour MPs sent a letter asking Westminster to respect more devolved powers. The memo shows growing tensions in Welsh Labour and highlights how the coming elections in Wales and Scotland could shape UK politics.
