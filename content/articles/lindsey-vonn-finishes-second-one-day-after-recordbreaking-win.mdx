At 41, Lindsey Vonn broke a record by becoming the oldest woman to win a World Cup race. A day later she was almost as fast, finishing second in the downhill at St Moritz, just 0.24 seconds behind Germany’s Emma Aicher. Vonn’s mistake after a jump slowed her, but she still leads the downhill standings. Italy’s Sofia Goggia was third, and Breezy Johnson finished fourth.
