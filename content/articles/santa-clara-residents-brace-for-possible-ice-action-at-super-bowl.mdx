Santa Clara residents are bracing for possible ICE raids during this weekend’s Super Bowl. With almost half the city’s population born abroad, many fear being targeted just for their ethnicity. The NFL says no ICE teams will be there, and local officials have passed a law banning federal agents from using city property for immigration enforcement. Volunteers plan to patrol the stadium, and a letter from Congress calls on the DHS to stay away.
