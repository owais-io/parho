U.S. defense secretary Pete Hegseth said the military will add Elon Musk’s AI tool Grok to Pentagon networks this month. The move is part of a new plan to speed up AI use in the armed forces. Hegseth also announced rules to share data for AI. Grok has faced backlash for producing sexual and violent images, and some countries have blocked it. The Pentagon is still moving forward.
