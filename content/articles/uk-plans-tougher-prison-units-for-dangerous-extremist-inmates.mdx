UK lawmakers want to build “super‑max” style cells for the most dangerous extremist prisoners. Deputy PM David Lammy said he will follow a review that recommends a new tiered system, tighter rules, and stricter staff training. He also wants new laws to stop prisoners using human‑rights laws to challenge these harsher conditions. Some human‑rights groups warn the changes could erode basic rights.
