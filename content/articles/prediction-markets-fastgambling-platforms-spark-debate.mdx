Prediction markets let people bet on anything, from sports to politics, and are growing fast, with users as young as 18. Every week hundreds of millions of dollars change hands, and the sites claim to be investment platforms rather than gambling. Regulators disagree, citing risks like insider trading, gambling harm, and political influence. The Biden administration has cracked down on firms, while the Trump team has stepped up support. The debate over how to rule them is heating up.
