Brenden Aaronson scored twice for Leeds United in a 4‑3 win over Newcastle and hit a big goal against Manchester United, catching the eye of US coach Mauricio Pochettino. His strong form keeps Leeds near safety in the Premier League and gives him a better chance to be chosen for the U.S. World Cup squad. After a mixed spell in Germany, Aaronson’s goals and work rate are proving vital for his club’s survival and his own international hopes.
