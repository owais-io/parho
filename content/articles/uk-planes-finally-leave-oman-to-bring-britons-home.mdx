A delayed charter flight from Muscat took off with British citizens as the UK Prime Minister called the evacuation the biggest since Afghanistan. The move comes amid continued US‑Israeli strikes on Iran, with missiles and drones threatening the region. MPs and overseas Britons have complained about slow, chaotic departures, but the government is adding extra flights, including a new British Airways service from Oman to London.
