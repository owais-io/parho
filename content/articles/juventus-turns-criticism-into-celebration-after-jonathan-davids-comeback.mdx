After a bad penalty against Lecce, Juventus fans and pundits fired at striker Jonathan David. But the team and manager Spalletti backed him. Just days later, David scored twice in a 3‑0 win over Sassuolo, showing he can be a key player. Rumors that he was unfit to play had no backing. Juventus now sees David as a bright spot despite early doubts.
