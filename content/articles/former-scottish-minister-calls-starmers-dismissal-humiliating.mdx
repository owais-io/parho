Ian Murray, a former Scottish minister, says Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s move left him humiliated. He was dropped from his Scotland role and offered a tech post he only reluctantly accepted after a long debate. Murray says no one explained why he was moved or praised his past work. He and his wife feel unappreciated and angry at the lack of transparency. Many ministers feel the same after the reshuffle.
