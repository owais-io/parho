Sixteen‑year‑old Hanne from Sussex is stuck in Denmark after UK border rules meant she couldn’t board a flight home because she didn’t have a valid British passport. Her parents were unaware of the new rule, so she missed two weeks of school and now faces a six‑week wait to get a passport. MPs and lawyers say the Home Office’s policy breaks the child‑welfare duty and needs urgent change.
