New South Wales premier Chris Minns announced that Sydney will have a huge police presence on New Year’s Eve, including officers with machine guns and possible army help, after the Bondi beach shooting. He said the plan is to protect the Jewish community and other groups from anti‑Semitic attacks. The government wants people to still celebrate safely, even if the streets look more intimidating.
