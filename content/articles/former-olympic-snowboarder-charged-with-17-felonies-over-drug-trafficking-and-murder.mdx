Former Canadian snowboarder Ryan Wedding faces 17 felony charges, including drug trafficking, murder conspiracy, witness tampering and money laundering. He appeared in a Santa Ana court on Monday, pleaded not guilty and will stay in jail without bond. Law enforcement says Wedding ran a massive cocaine ring with the Sinaloa cartel, earning over $1 billion a year and linked to several murders. FBI officials compared him to El Chapo. The next hearing is Feb 11 and trial is set for March 24.
