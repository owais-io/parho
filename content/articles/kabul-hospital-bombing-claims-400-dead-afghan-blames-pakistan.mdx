Afghan officials say a Pakistani airstrike hit a drug‑treatment hospital in Kabul, killing about 400 people and injuring 250. The hospital, used for drug rehabilitation, was set on fire by bombs that collapsed parts of the building. Pakistan denies targeting civilians, saying it struck military sites. The attack comes amid heated border fighting between Afghanistan and Pakistan that has already killed dozens and displaced tens of thousands.
