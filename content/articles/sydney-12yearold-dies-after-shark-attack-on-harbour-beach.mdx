Tragic news from Sydney: a 12‑year‑old boy, Nico Antic, died in hospital after a shark bit both legs on a harbour beach. He was taken to Sydney Children’s Hospital on Jan 18 but passed away despite rescue efforts. The attack was part of a recent spike in shark bites on New South Wales beaches, which closed dozens of spots and raised safety concerns.
