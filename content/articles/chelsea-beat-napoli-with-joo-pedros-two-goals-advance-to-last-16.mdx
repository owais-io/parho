In the Champions League group match, Chelsea fought back from 2‑1 down to beat Napoli 3‑2. João Pedro scored twice, including a goal from a pass by Cole Palmer. Palmer’s brief appearance sparked the comeback, and Chelsea’s late goals sealed the win. Napoli, who needed a victory to avoid early exit, couldn’t keep up. The result puts Chelsea in sixth place and secures a spot in the knockout round.
