Charlie Curnow, a key forward for Carlton, was traded to the Sydney Swans just before the trade deadline. The move shocked fans who had seen him as a long‑term star. While Carlton’s coach and players said the switch was tough, Curnow’s new club welcomed him as a fresh start. The trade has sparked debate about how quickly players are released and how clubs handle loyalty.
