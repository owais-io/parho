A 33‑year‑old man named Gustavo DeJesus Torres is on the run after robbing six Chase branches in Queens, Brooklyn, Harlem and the Bronx over five days. He stole only $605 in total and nobody was hurt. Police have not caught him yet and are asking the public for clues. The robber is described as balding with a beard, sometimes wearing a mask, and he usually leaves on foot.
