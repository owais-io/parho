NBC anchor Savannah Guthrie says her fame may have attracted a kidnapper who took her 84‑year‑old mother from Arizona. The FBI found video of a man with a backpack at the house and has received two ransom notes. No suspect has been identified, and 13,000 tips have come in. Guthrie is still searching for answers and urges the public to help.
