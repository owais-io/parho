The Guardian apologises for a handful of small mistakes in recent stories. They clarified that Gaia Enria is the culinary consultant and Louis Lingwood (not Lingford) is the head chef at Osteria Vibrato. A quiz answer that listed ‘Guns N’ Roses (10)’ should have been (11) because the song refers to November. They also corrected the name of the former discount chain from Bradley’s to Bradlees. Other corrections touch on meningitis, museum‑ticket plans, and a quirky photo‑control story.
