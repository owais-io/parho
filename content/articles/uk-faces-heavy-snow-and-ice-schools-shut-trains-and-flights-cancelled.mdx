A big snow‑and‑ice spell is hitting most of the UK. Hundreds of schools have closed, and many trains, flights and even some buses are cancelled or delayed. The Met Office warns of ice and snow across Scotland, northern England, Wales and the north of Ireland, while the health agency has issued cold alerts for vulnerable people. Drivers, commuters and travellers should plan for longer journeys and stay updated on transport services.
