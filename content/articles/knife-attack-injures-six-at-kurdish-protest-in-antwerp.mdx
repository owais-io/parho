A knife attack at a Kurdish protest in Antwerp, Belgium, left six people hurt. Police caught the two attackers after they joined the crowd and started stabbing. Two victims were seriously ill in hospital; the others were less badly injured. The protest, which had about 50 people, was peaceful until the end when the attackers struck. Authorities say it’s an attempted‑murder case, not terrorism. Police are reviewing CCTV to find any other suspects.
