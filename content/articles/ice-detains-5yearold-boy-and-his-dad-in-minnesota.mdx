ICE officers arrested a five‑year‑old boy and his father as they were leaving school in a Minnesota suburb. The parents were taken to a Texas detention center, even though the child had no chance to fight back. The case sparked anger from school officials and the family, who say the father was legal and has an asylum claim. It highlights growing fears that ICE is targeting families in schools.
