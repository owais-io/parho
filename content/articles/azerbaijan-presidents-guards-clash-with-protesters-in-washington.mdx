While visiting Washington for Donald Trump’s first Board of Peace meeting, Azerbaijani President Ilham Aliyev’s bodyguards confronted and hurt protestors outside the Waldorf Astoria hotel. The demonstrators were demanding the release of political prisoners in Azerbaijan. Aliyev’s guards said they were protecting the president’s motorcade, but witnesses say they punched, kicked and chased people. Several protestors were injured. The incident echoes a similar clash involving Turkey’s president’s security team last year.
