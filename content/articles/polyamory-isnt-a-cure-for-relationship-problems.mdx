Polyamory is a type of open relationship where partners share love and sex with more than one person. A psychologist talks to a couple who tried it after years of secrecy and jealousy. He says it doesn’t magically fix broken bonds. Instead, it needs honest talk, clear rules, and a willingness to stay honest. When partners feel pressured or cheated, the problem stays or gets worse. Polyamory works only if both people truly want it and communicate well.
