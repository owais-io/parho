An 86‑year‑old man has been arrested for the 1993 murders of a married couple at their farmhouse in Llanharry, South Wales. The victims, Harry Tooze (64) and Megan Tooze (67), were shot with a shotgun. The case was first linked to Jonathan Jones, who was jailed in 1995 but released in 1996 after an appeal. Police reopened the investigation in 2023 and are still looking for more answers.
