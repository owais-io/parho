Pete Hegseth said the Pentagon will not release the complete video of a September strike in the Caribbean that killed two people on a burning boat. The attack is part of President Trump’s campaign to hit Venezuelan ships carrying drugs. Democrats want the footage for transparency and say it may show war crimes. The administration says the video is top‑secret, but lawmakers are calling for congressional approval of the strikes.
