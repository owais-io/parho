Trump says he will stop defense contractors from paying huge salaries to CEOs and giving dividends to shareholders until they speed up producing weapons. He wants to raise the U.S. defense budget to $1.5 trillion by 2027. The president claims the companies are spending too much on executive pay and stock buybacks, which he says hurts the military’s ability to get new equipment quickly.
