Brendon McCullum says England’s team is not a “loose ship” and that Harry Brook, while a strong on‑field captain, must improve his off‑field behaviour. He talked about a nightclub bouncer incident that led to fines for Brook and two teammates. McCullum says the England and Wales Cricket Board dealt with the matter internally and that discipline is important. The team finished well in Sri Lanka and is ready for the T20 World Cup.
