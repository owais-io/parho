Zelenskyy told the Munich Security Conference that Putin is a “slave to war” and said Russia’s strikes have damaged every Ukrainian power plant. He urged the US to give Ukraine 20‑year security guarantees before any peace deal, and pushed for a clear EU‑accession date. Ahead of talks with Russia and the US, he warned against half‑baked agreements. The meeting also highlighted rising casualties from drone attacks on both sides.
