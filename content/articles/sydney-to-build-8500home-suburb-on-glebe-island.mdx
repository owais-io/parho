Sydney is turning Glebe Island into its first new inner‑city suburb in over a century. The new Bays West will bring 8,500 homes, including 700 affordable units, and open the waterfront to residents. Port operations that handle cement, gypsum and sugar will stop by 2030, with some work moving to Port Kembla. The plan also keeps a White Bay cruise terminal, turns a power station into a cultural spot, and adds 25 % open space.
