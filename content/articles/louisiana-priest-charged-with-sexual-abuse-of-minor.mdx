A 37‑year‑old priest in Louisiana’s Lafayette diocese, Korey LaVergne, has been charged with three counts of indecent behavior with a 15‑year‑old boy. He was arrested in mid‑January, posted a $15,000 bail and pleaded not guilty. The case follows the long‑running clergy‑abuse crisis in the diocese, highlighted in the 1980s by another priest’s convictions. LaVergne faces up to seven years in prison and fines.
