Sean Ruiz, Wolves’ head of kit, keeps his office door open so players and staff can drop in for chats. He says the bond he has with teammates is like family. Ruiz spends days putting names and numbers on shirts, washing and sorting gear, and even loads a van with everything the team needs for a match. He learned the job after helping his dad with the Red Bulls. Now he and his crew make sure everything looks perfect before kickoff.
