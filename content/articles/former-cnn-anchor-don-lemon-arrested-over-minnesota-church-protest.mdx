Former CNN anchor Don Lemon was arrested in Minnesota for a church protest. Lemon, who left CNN in 2023 after controversial remarks and workplace allegations, has since moved to online platforms like X and YouTube. He’s a trailblazing Black and gay journalist who won awards but faced criticism for outspoken comments. The arrest brings his career controversies back into public focus.
