Electric bike sharing is exploding across Australia while e‑scooter rentals have fallen because of safety fears. In 2025 Sydney saw its fleet double, driven by Lime, and cities that once only ran scooters are now adding bikes. Several local governments have cut or paused e‑scooter operations after crashes and low usage, and new rules could bring the modes back under tighter control.
