Mark Lee Gardner’s new book, Brothers of the Gun, looks at the real stories of Wyatt Earp and Doc Holliday. He says the famous Gunfight at the OK Corral was messy and confusing, and every witness gave a different version. Gardner uses old court papers and diaries to show that Earp was a hardworking lawman with a tough past, while Holliday was a gambler who never really changed. The book pulls back the myths and shows the people behind the legend.
