Team GB made history at the Winter Olympics by winning two gold medals on Super Sunday. Charlotte Bankes and Huw Nightingale took the mixed snowboard cross title, while Tabitha Stoecker and Matt Weston won the mixed team skeleton, making Weston the most decorated British winter Olympian. The victories lifted Britain to 12th in the medal table. However, snowboarding star Mia Brookes crashed in qualifying and missed the final.
