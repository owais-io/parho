Brooks Koepka said he'll leave LIV Golf in 2026. He joined only because he feared injury. His exit hurts LIV, which had been trying to grow with big names. The PGA Tour welcomed the news and said it still offers the best competition. The move sparks debate about how returning players will be treated and if golfers can switch back without penalties. If Koepka returns, it could boost TV viewers and sponsor interest for the PGA Tour.
