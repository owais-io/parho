Robert Jenrick, a Conservative MP, abruptly left the party to join Reform UK, shocking local allies. Sam Smith, his long‑time friend and local councillor, learned the news during a council meeting. Jenrick was fired from the frontbench after evidence of a planned defection surfaced. He announced Reform as Britain’s solution, sparking debate among Newark voters: some back him, others accuse him of abandoning the community.
