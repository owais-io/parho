President Trump has asked European allies to send ships to the Strait of Hormuz after Iran shut it down in retaliation for U.S. and Israeli airstrikes. The move could cut global oil supplies, raising prices worldwide. Britain, France, Australia, Japan and the U.K. have said they will not send warships, leaving the U.S. alone to handle the crisis.
