Trump said White House doctors helped a congressman, Neal Dunn, stay alive, and that if they hadn’t, Dunn would have died by June. The comment was made during a press event and it made people upset. Many said it was a private matter that should not have been shared publicly. The incident shows how Trump talks about other people’s health, which many find unprofessional and inappropriate.
