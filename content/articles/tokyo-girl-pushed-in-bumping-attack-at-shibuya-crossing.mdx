A viral clip shows a Taiwanese girl in Tokyo’s Shibuya scramble crossing being pushed to the ground by a woman. The incident is part of a growing “bumping” trend where people shove strangers in crowded spots, often women. Experts say it reflects gender attitudes and stress in modern Japan. The trend has hit commuters, tourists and led to police arrests, and the Chinese embassy warned visitors to stay safe.
