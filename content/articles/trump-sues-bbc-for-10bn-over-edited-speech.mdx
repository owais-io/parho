Donald Trump has filed a lawsuit against the BBC, seeking up to $10 billion in damages after the broadcaster edited his January 6 speech. Trump says the edit falsely made him appear ready to march on the Capitol and calls it defamation. The BBC has apologized for the mistake but says the claim is baseless. Trump is demanding $5 billion on each of two claims—defamation and a Florida trade‑practice law violation.
