Trump has threatened a 10% tariff on goods from Denmark, Norway, Sweden, the UK, France, Germany, the Netherlands, and Finland unless Denmark sells Greenland. He could raise it to 25% if no deal is reached. The EU is considering using its “big bazooka” anti‑coercion tool to hit U.S. companies and products. Most EU leaders prefer dialogue, but a trade war could hurt both sides.
