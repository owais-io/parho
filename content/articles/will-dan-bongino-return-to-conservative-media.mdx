After a brief stint as an FBI deputy director, Dan Bongino said he'll leave the agency next month and is likely to jump back into conservative radio and podcasting. Trump and other media figures say he'll return to his popular show, and some believe his FBI experience will boost his credibility. Bongino faces scrutiny over past controversial comments, but most expect him to go back to the same format he used before.
