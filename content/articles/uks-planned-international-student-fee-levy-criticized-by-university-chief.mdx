The University of Manchester’s chief says a 6% fee on international students is a bad idea. He warns it would hurt the UK’s appeal to foreign students and cost universities more than £600 m a year. He thinks the money should fund student help in other ways, not make students pay extra. He also stresses that universities are already financially tight and need other support like fundraising.
