A Berlin power plant was set on fire by a left‑wing group called Vulkangruppe. They said they wanted to protest climate change, AI, and rich people’s energy use. The blaze broke high‑voltage cables, cutting power to about 35,000 homes and many businesses. The city has been working to restore electricity, but cold weather is slowing repairs. Berlin’s mayor says the attack is political, and police are investigating.
