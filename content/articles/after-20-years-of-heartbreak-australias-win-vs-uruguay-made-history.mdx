After losing close games for decades, this Australian fan finally felt hope when he attended a 2005 playoff against Uruguay. A friend gave him a ticket, and the match turned into a thrilling victory. The key moment was left‑foot scorer Marco Bresciano's goal, which steadied the team. Australia won on penalties, earning a spot in the 2006 World Cup—the first time in 32 years. The win brought joy and ended years of disappointment.
