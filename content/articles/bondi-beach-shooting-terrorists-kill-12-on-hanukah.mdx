A violent attack hit Bondi Beach in Sydney on a sunny Sunday, right when a Jewish community was celebrating Hanukah. Two gunmen opened fire from a bridge, shooting at people in the crowd. The attack left at least 12 dead and nearly 30 injured. Police say one shooter died, another is critically wounded, and they are still looking for a possible third attacker. The Prime Minister called it a hateful act of antisemitic terrorism.
