Emails released by the U.S. Department of Justice show that Prince Andrew, who was in the UAE with Queen Elizabeth II in 2010, wrote to Jeffrey Epstein asking him to help introduce the prince to UAE officials. The emails also reveal Epstein telling Andrew how to talk about himself to the UAE foreign minister and how to sidestep investment rules that apply to a royal trade envoy. Andrew denies wrongdoing, but the letters raise questions about his ties to Epstein.
