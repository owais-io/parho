Zoe Atkin is a British skier aiming for Olympic gold in the ski halfpipe. She studies psychology at Stanford to learn how to beat fear and stay calm. After many falls, she finally landed her signature 540‑degree trick and won the world championships. She also won the X‑Games, making her a top favourite for the Milano‑Cortina Games. Her older sister was the first British woman to win a Winter Olympic medal, which inspired her.
