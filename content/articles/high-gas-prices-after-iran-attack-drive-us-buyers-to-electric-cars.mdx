Gas prices hit a near‑three‑year high after the U.S. and Israel struck Iran, causing oil prices to rise. In response, people in the U.S. are suddenly more interested in electric cars—searches for EV models jumped 20%. Even used Teslas and hybrids look appealing because they’re cheaper than new electric models. The U.S. still lags behind other countries, and Trump’s policies have slowed EV growth, but demand is climbing.
