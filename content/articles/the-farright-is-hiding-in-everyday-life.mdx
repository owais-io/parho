Researchers in six European countries find that far‑right ideas are slipping into normal culture. From vegan cooking videos with Nazi symbols to popular music bands and “trad‑wife” posts, extremist messages hide in everyday trends. New tools like AI make it easy to produce such content quickly and cheaply, sometimes by bots. The study warns that people can be radicalised from their couch, so recognising these subtle signals is more important than ever.
