The U.S. will be in Group D at the 2026 World Cup with Australia, Paraguay, and a winner from a playoff between Turkey, Romania, Kosovo, and Slovakia. Australia is ranked 26th, strong defensively, and beat the U.S. 2‑1 in a recent friendly. Paraguay, ranked 39th, had its best World Cup run in 2010. The playoff teams could give the U.S. a tough challenge.
