JPMorgan’s boss, Jamie Dimon, received a total of $43 million last year, a 10% jump from 2024. His pay includes a $1.5 million base salary and $41.5 million in incentives, all approved by the board because of strong company performance and a solid balance sheet. The news came just hours after Trump sued the bank for $5 billion, and shares rose a touch that day.
