A folk‑pop track called “I Know, You’re Not Mine” by the AI‑created singer Jacub topped Spotify but was banned from Sweden’s official chart. IFPI Sweden said the song was mainly made with AI, so it fails chart rules. It has over 5 million streams worldwide. The publisher Stellar says AI was just a tool in their creative process. Fans and experts now urge clear AI labels on music.
