Lila Dominguez, a high‑school junior in Minneapolis, watched ICE agents storm her school on January 7 after a shooting that same day. She recorded the chaos, wrote a column calling for the agents to leave, and her story spread online. The raid left students scared, attendance fell, and the district closed schools for two days, switching many to virtual learning. Parents and teachers urged calm, while the school board demanded ICE stay out of schools.
