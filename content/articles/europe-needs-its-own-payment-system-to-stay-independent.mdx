A French MP warned that Trump could cut Europe off from Visa and Mastercard, showing why the EU should build its own payment network. Russia’s sanctions revealed how much people rely on foreign card networks. India’s UPI, a free, public digital payment platform, shows how a state‑run system can work. Europe could adopt a similar model, but banks, regulations, and private firms like Stripe would need to cooperate.
