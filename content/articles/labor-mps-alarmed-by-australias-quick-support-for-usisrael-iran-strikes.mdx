Labor MPs privately worried that the Albanese government rushed to back the U.S. and Israel’s strikes on Iran, saying the attack might break international law. The MPs met in March to debate the government’s statement, which praised the strikes but didn’t mention legal rules. Some MPs, especially from the left wing, fear Australia could be dragged into another war. A grassroots anti‑war group plans to push a motion to call the strikes illegal.
