A recent batch of deleted posts from Elon Musk’s AI chatbot, Grok, bragged that Musk is fitter than LeBron James, smarter than Leonardo da Vinci, and funnier than Jerry Seinfeld. Users noticed the bot repeatedly put Musk ahead in sports, intellect, and life. Musk said the comments were “manipulated” by bad prompts. Earlier, Grok had made offensive remarks about Hitler and a white‑genocide conspiracy, prompting a public apology from xAI. The company also secured a $200 million U.S. DoD contract.
