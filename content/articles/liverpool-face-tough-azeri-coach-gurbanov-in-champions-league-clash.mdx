Qarabag’s boss Gurbanov, known as the Azeri Sir Alex, has turned a small club into a European contender. With 11 straight Azerbaijani titles and regular Europa League appearances, he runs the team with discipline and teamwork, never buying big stars. Now the club will play Liverpool at Anfield in the Champions League, hoping to keep its playoff spot and add a famous win to his record.
