European housing prices are soaring, making it hard for young people to buy or even rent a home. Since 2010, prices have risen almost 60% on average, and rents are up 30%. In places like Ireland and Spain, young workers spend more than 80% of their wages on rent. Many still live with parents, and the crisis hurts the economy and fuels political unrest. The EU just launched a new affordable‑housing plan to help.
