Leonid “Leo” Radvinsky, the billionaire who bought and ran the adult‑content platform OnlyFans, has died at 43 after a long battle with cancer. Radvinsky bought the company in 2018 and owned most of it, earning billions from the site’s 20% fee on creators’ earnings. He had been negotiating to sell a majority stake, which would value the business at around $8 billion. His family asked for privacy.
