Donald Trump said the U.S. launched airstrikes against Islamic State fighters in northwest Nigeria, working with Nigerian forces. The strikes hit targets in Sokoto state, where the ISSP group is active. Pentagon officials thanked Nigeria for the cooperation. The attack comes amid violence that has harmed Christians, Muslims and others, and follows a recent mosque bombing. Trump has repeatedly urged the U.S. to intervene against Islamist groups.
