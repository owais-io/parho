Brigitte Bardot was a famous French actress who shaped 1950s and 60s culture. She became a symbol of sexual freedom for many women, but she also said hateful words about Muslims and immigrants. The French government celebrated her after she died, calling her a legend. Some people, like singer Chappell Roan, posted photos of her then deleted them after learning about her racist views. Bardot also fought for animal rights, but her hate speech led to several convictions.
