Shabana Mahmood, the Home Secretary, announced new immigration rules that would end permanent refugee status, cut support for certain asylum seekers, and offer money to 150 families to leave or face removal. Labour MPs say the plans mimic Trump and risk a Windrush‑style scandal. The proposals also extend the time before people can apply for indefinite leave to remain, which some argue is unfair. The move could split the government and spark a parliamentary rebellion.
