Trump got a bronze trophy from a coal‑industry group to honor his support for coal power. He told the crowd that the Pentagon will buy more coal and that the Energy Department will fund projects to keep coal plants running. The award shows the administration’s push to revive the coal sector, even though coal use in the U.S. has fallen sharply.
