Next's boss says the Middle East war could make clothes cost 4‑10% more if the fighting spills into autumn. Rising fuel and fabric prices could hit factories, but the retailer has extra stock and expects no profit hit. Despite worries, Next lifted its profit forecast after a sales jump in January. The company also says it will use more AI to cut costs.
