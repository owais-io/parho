Trump threatened to sue comedian Trevor Noah after the host made a joke linking Trump to Jeffrey Epstein at the Grammys. Trump posted an angry message on Truth Social, saying Noah had defamed him and that he would hire lawyers. Noah’s remark mentioned Epstein’s Caribbean island and the president’s desire for a new island. Trump denied any ties to Epstein and called Noah a loser. No lawsuit has been filed yet, but the incident shows Trump’s hostility toward entertainers.
