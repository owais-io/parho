Brazil’s highest court found two powerful brothers, former lawmakers, guilty of ordering the 2018 murder of city councillor Marielle Franco and her driver. The brothers received long prison sentences, and the case also punished police officers who carried out the hit. The verdict was a landmark step against political corruption and violence, giving hope to human‑rights defenders and showing that Brazil can hold powerful men accountable.
