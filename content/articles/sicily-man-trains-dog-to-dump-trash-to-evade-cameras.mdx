In Catania, Sicily, a man taught his small dog to carry trash bags and drop them on the road, trying to avoid the city's cameras that hunt illegal dumping. Police posted the video online and said the trick was clever but wrong. The owner has been identified and fined. The incident shows how Italian cities use surveillance to catch and punish illegal waste dumping, which has risen sharply in recent years.
