Streep and Hathaway are back for a new Devil Wears Prada 2 teaser. They’ll play the mean magazine editor and her former assistant again, with the movie coming out next summer. The cast also includes Emily Blunt, Stanley Tucci, Kenneth Branagh, and a host of new faces like Simone Ashley and Lucy Liu. The story follows Miranda Priestly as the fashion world changes, forcing her to team up with Emily. Fans are excited for the return of the iconic pair.
