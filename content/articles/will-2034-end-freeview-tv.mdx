Freeview is the UK’s biggest free TV service, used by 16 million households. Broadcasters say most people are now watching shows online and want to shut down Freeview by 2034. A campaign group says millions still need the cheap, easy‑to‑use platform and would be left behind. The government is weighing upgrading the tech, keeping a tiny channel line‑up or turning it off. The debate hinges on how many people can switch to internet TV.
