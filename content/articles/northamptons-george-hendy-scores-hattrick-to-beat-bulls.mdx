Northampton won easily against the Bulls, with George Hendy scoring three tries. The Saints also got two more from Ollie Sleightholme and one from Henry Pollock, making eight tries in total. The Bulls had a weaker squad because many of their key players were absent. The win gives Northampton two bonus‑point victories in the Champions Cup. Coach Johan Ackermann said his side lost in the second half and will face Bordeaux next month.
