The United Farm Workers union has stopped all planned events honoring Cesar Chavez after serious claims that he abused young women and minors. While the UFW says it has no direct evidence, it wants to investigate and give victims a safe way to share their stories. As a result, the union will not observe Cesar Chavez Day on March 31, and the Chavez Foundation has also expressed shock and pledged support.
