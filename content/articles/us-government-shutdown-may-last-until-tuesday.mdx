House Republicans believe they can close the partial shutdown by Tuesday, even though House Democrats won’t back the necessary votes. Speaker Mike Johnson plans to use a simple rule vote, but Democrats want a full debate and have raised concerns over immigration funding. The shutdown hinges on a revised DHS bill, and some Democrats oppose it, so the dispute could drag on.
