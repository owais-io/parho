Guardian gets more than 20 nominations for the 2026 Press Awards, covering 21 categories. Highlights include the Missing in the Amazon podcast, the Long Wave newsletter, and journalists like Charlotte Edwardes, Donald McRae, Simon Hattenstone, and Pippa Crerar. The Guardian is also up for best news website, best audience engagement, and its Saturday magazine is in the supplement race. Winners will be announced on May 21 in London.
