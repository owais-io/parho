Harlequins defeated Bayonne 50‑7 in the Champions Cup, scoring ten tries with nine converted by Marcus Smith. Bayonne sent an inexperienced side with many injuries and rested front‑line players, giving the English club an easy win. Young Australian Jonah Thompson started on the wing, but the match highlighted the Champions Cup’s format, where teams often play weakened sides, disappointing fans. Harlequins’ coach said clubs need to balance competitions but still want to play hard every week.
