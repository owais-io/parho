A new report warns that if we don’t act, over 220 million children worldwide could be obese by 2040. In 2025, about 180 million kids were already obese. The study also says more than 500 million children will be overweight, and 120 million school‑age kids could already have early heart‑disease signs. Countries like the US, China, and the UK have high rates. The report urges governments to use sugar taxes, limit junk‑food ads, and promote active lifestyles.
