An Australian man, Russell Allan Wilson, is believed to have died on 12 December while fighting with Ukrainian forces in the Donetsk region. The Australian government is checking reports and helping his family. Social media posts praise his bravery and note he was about to be married. The government warns Australians not to travel to Ukraine. Eight Australians have died there since 2022, and one, Oscar Jenkins, was captured but is now free.
