A 38‑year‑old man was arrested early Friday after he sprayed offensive graffiti on the Winston Churchill statue outside London’s Parliament. Police say the paint included words like “Zionist war criminal” and “free Palestine.” The Metropolitan Police seized the suspect within minutes. The statue has been cleaned and fenced off. The incident follows past vandalism, and officials say they will hold the vandal accountable.
