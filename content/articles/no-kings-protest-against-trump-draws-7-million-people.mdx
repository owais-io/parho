In 2025, about 7 million people marched in a No Kings protest against the Trump administration. This weekend, organisers plan over 3,000 events across the U.S., and the crowds could be even larger. Protesters are angry about Trump’s harsh immigration crackdown, the war in Iran, high gas prices, cuts to public services, and alleged corruption. The demonstrations unite a wide range of left‑leaning grievances in a single, massive show of dissent.
