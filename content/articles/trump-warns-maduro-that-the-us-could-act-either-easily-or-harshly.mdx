Trump warned Venezuela’s president Nicolás Maduro that the U.S. could act “the easy way” or “the hard way.” Meanwhile, Maduro addressed crowds in Caracas, promising to defend the country. The U.S. has sent a large naval force to the Caribbean, partly to fight drug smuggling and partly to pressure Maduro, who many think stole last year's election. Experts say the U.S. might soon strike Venezuelan territory, but it could backfire.
