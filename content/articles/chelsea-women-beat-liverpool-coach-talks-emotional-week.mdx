Chelsea Women won 2-0 against Liverpool at Kingsmeadow, thanks to Lauren James scoring twice. Coach Sonia Bompastor said it was an emotional week after the club fired long‑time head of women's football Paul Green. Despite the loss, Bompastor praised her players and the staff’s support. The win keeps Chelsea in third place and keeps their hopes of Champions League football alive, as they prepare for a FA Cup clash with Manchester United.
