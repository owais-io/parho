A London study shows kids who use social media for over three hours a day are more likely to feel anxious and depressed as they grow into teens. The researchers say late‑night scrolling cuts sleep, especially hurting girls. With a 2,350‑child sample, the link was strongest when kids used social media late. Policymakers debate bans for under‑16s, but evidence is still unclear.
