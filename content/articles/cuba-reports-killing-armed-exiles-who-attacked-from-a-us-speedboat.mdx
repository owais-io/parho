Cuba says its forces killed four exiles and wounded six others after a Florida‑registered speedboat fired on a Cuban patrol. The attackers were armed with rifles, handguns and homemade explosives, and some were wanted by Cuban authorities. The U.S. says it had no role and is investigating. The incident adds to rising tensions between Washington and Havana, following past clashes and new U.S. oil‑fuel policies.
