Sam Thomas and his stable are gearing up for the Welsh Grand National at Chepstow. After Steel Ally won the Noel Novice Chase at Ascot, Thomas will ride two horses, Jubilee Express and Shomen Uchi, hoping for another win like Iwilldoit in 2021. He praised the strong, battle‑hardened Jubilee Express and the classy but fragile Shomen Uchi. The team is proud to be a Welsh rider.
