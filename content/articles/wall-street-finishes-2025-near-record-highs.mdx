Wall Street ended 2025 close to record highs. The S&P 500 rose 16.4%, the Nasdaq up 20.5%, and the UK’s FTSE 100 had its biggest yearly gain since 2009. Tech stocks, especially Nvidia, drove the rally, helped by AI hype. Trump’s tariffs and a long government shutdown added worry, but the market kept growing. Many people feel their finances are getting worse, and the boom mainly helped the wealthy.
