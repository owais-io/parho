Facing rising hate and division in the UK, The Guardian is launching its 2025 charity appeal on Friday. The focus is on hope, supporting local charities that fight racism, help with housing, health, food banks, youth clubs and refugee programs. These groups rebuild libraries, parks and community centres, bringing neighbours together. Donations go to five partners like Citizens UK and Locality. The appeal aims to strengthen community pride and show that kindness can beat big problems.
