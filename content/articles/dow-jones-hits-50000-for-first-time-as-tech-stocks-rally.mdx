The Dow Jones industrial average climbed above 50,000 for the first time, driven by high tech prices, solid company earnings, and hopes that interest rates will fall. Even though worries about AI spending pushed some tech shares down, the market still rose, with the Nasdaq and S&P 500 gaining over 2% each. Investors shrugged off geopolitical tensions and stayed upbeat about the U.S. economy.
