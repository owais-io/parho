The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission has opened a probe into Nike, claiming the company discriminated against white workers and applicants. EEOC wants documents about Nike’s diversity plans and hiring data, including programs that may have offered race‑based benefits. Nike says it follows the law and has shared many pages, but the investigation continues as the agency pushes back against the company’s DEI initiatives.
