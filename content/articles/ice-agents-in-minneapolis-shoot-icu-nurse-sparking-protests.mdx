Federal agents from ICE and Customs were sent to Minneapolis, where they wore masks and tactical gear. During a protest, one officer shot and killed Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old ICU nurse. The killing happened just a mile from where George Floyd died. Protesters shouted at the agents and called for them to leave. Democrats want ICE funding cut and are warning the attack could signal a wider crackdown.
