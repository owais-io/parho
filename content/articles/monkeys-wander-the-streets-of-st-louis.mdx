Monkeys have been seen roaming the streets of north St. Louis. Health officials say more than one vervet monkey is on the loose near O’Fallon Park. They’re working with trained teams to catch the animals and move them to a licensed animal care center. Residents are warned not to approach or try to capture the monkeys. If you see one, call 314‑657‑1500 so authorities can locate them.
