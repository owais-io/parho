Big tech companies like Google, Amazon and Apple are blamed for privacy, misinformation, climate damage and political meddling. The article lists cheaper, greener, and privacy‑respecting alternatives: search with Ecosia or Mojeek, browsers such as Firefox, LibreWolf or Vivaldi, email services like ProtonMail or Tuta, office tools like LibreOffice, phones from Fairphone, and second‑hand stores such as Backmarket. Europe is also testing its own AI, social media and cloud options to reduce reliance on US giants.
