A conservative activist was photographed handing campaign checks to Republican lawmakers on the Wyoming Capitol floor. The act sparked criticism and revealed a split in the state’s GOP between moderates and hard‑right Freedom caucus members. The checks came from wealthy Teton County donor Don Grasso and were not tied to specific bills. The legislature opened an investigative committee and the sheriff’s office is looking into the matter, while some lawmakers say it is a smear, others say it could be illegal.
