A British anti‑disinformation activist, Imran Ahmed, says the Trump‑era U.S. government is targeting him for pressuring tech firms to be more accountable. He was listed as a non‑immigrant who could be barred from the U.S. after accusations that he tries to force platforms to silence American viewpoints. A court granted him a temporary restraining order to stop any removal or detention. He’s also faced a lawsuit from Elon Musk’s X, and says he will fight the claims.
