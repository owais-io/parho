Family courts in England and Wales fail to protect many children and parents. Experts say the real measure of any reform should come from listening to the children themselves, not just court results. By asking grown‑ups to recall how they were treated, we could see if changes help or hurt. The current system often treats both parents the same, letting abusive or manipulative parents keep control. Training professionals to spot power abuses is essential.
