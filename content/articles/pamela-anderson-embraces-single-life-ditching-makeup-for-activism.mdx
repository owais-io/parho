Pamela Anderson, who has been married five times, is now 58 and happily single. She says being alone is the key to loving herself. The former “Baywatch” star is also ditching heavy makeup, choosing a natural look after years of heavy makeup routines. Anderson says this lets her focus on her family, walking, and activism for animals and the environment. Her move signals a new, more authentic chapter in her life.
