Donald Trump has slammed NATO allies for not helping keep the Strait of Hormuz open, calling them “cowards.” While the U.S. plans to send three warships and thousands of troops to the Middle East, Trump says he might end the war on Iran but does not support a ceasefire. Meanwhile, NATO countries have pledged to protect shipping through Hormuz but have not given details.
