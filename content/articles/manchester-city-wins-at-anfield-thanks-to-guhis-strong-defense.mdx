After a tense match that went into extra time, Manchester City beat Liverpool at Anfield. Defender Marc Guéhi kept the Reds at bay, while key players like Haaland, Donnarumma and Silva helped secure the win. Coach Pep Guardiola blew a kiss to the sky in celebration, signalling a bright spot in the title race, which still feels open.
