The writer shares what it feels like to spot common scoters in Dundrum Bay, describing how they fly in groups and dive for shellfish. She remembers seeing them as a child in County Fermanagh, where they nested on Lough Erne. Unfortunately, that local population disappeared by 1993 because of hunting and other problems. While enjoying the beautiful sight today, she feels a mix of joy and sadness for the bird she once adored.
