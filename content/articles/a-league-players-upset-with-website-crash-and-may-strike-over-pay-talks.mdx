A-League players are furious after the league’s website went down for more than a day, showing what they say is the APL’s poor management. The players’ union warns a strike is possible if negotiations over wages and contracts fail to resolve. Many players say the APL’s new salary cap and lack of support hurt the league’s quality and future. The union wants big changes, like more player input and a new commission.
