After her mother died of rabies in 2023, 59‑year‑old Barnsley nurse Robyn Thomson vowed to stop the disease. She joined the charity Mission Rabies and, with her husband, travels to places where rabies is common, vaccinating dogs to break the cycle. They hit 10,000 vaccines in a day in Cambodia and plan to visit Malawi next year. Robyn wants to keep turning her loss into help for others.
