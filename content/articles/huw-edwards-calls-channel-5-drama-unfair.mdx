Former BBC anchor Huw Edwards has blasted Channel 5’s two‑part drama, *Power: The Downfall of Huw Edwards*, saying the show didn’t fact‑check him and may be misleading. Edwards was suspended in 2023 after a Sun report claimed he paid a teenager £35,000 for child‑sex images. The drama, which Channel 5 says tells the victim’s story, has been defended as raising online‑grooming issues. Edwards plans to release his own version of events.
