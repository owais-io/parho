Labour politicians in London are sounding the alarm that the May local elections could turn into a big blow for the party. New data from a modelling firm says Labour might fall from the top spot to fourth in the capital, losing most of its borough councils to the Greens and other rivals. Leaders say they must listen to progressive voters and fix key issues like housing and welfare.
