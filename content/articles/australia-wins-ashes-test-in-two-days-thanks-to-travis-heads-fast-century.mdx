Australia beat England in a two‑day Ashes Test, thanks to Travis Head’s rapid 123 runs. Head, along with Marnus Labuschagne, smashed the English bowlers while Mitchell Starc took 10 wickets in the match. England’s aggressive “Bazball” style backfired, as they collapsed for 164 in the first innings and were bowled out again. The win shows Australia’s dominance and England’s struggle to adapt.
