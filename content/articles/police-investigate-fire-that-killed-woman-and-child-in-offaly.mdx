Police are investigating a house fire in County Offaly that killed a 60‑year‑old woman and her four‑year‑old son. The blaze started around 8 pm on Saturday in Edenderry, killing Mary Holt and Tadhg Farrell. Another woman was badly hurt and is in hospital. Firefighters confirmed the fire was set deliberately. Police are asking anyone who was nearby to come forward with information.
