Many Black women report being dismissed, gaslit, or ignored by doctors. Stories include a young woman who had to prove a breast lump existed and a mother who nearly died after doctors ignored her pain. The article shows that Black patients often feel they must be their own doctors and that seeing Black doctors can improve trust and outcomes. It calls for better listening and education to reduce bias.
