King Charles is determined to keep working on climate issues, even after criticism, and has released a new Amazon Prime film that shows his long‑term environmental work. In the seven‑month, four‑continent documentary, the king talks about his “Harmony” philosophy, organic farming at Highgrove and projects around the world. He says the film is a personal call to action and hopes people will notice the need to protect nature.
