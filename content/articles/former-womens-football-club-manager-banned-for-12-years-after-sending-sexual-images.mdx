A former manager of a fourth‑tier women’s football club has been banned for 12 years by the FA after sending explicit pictures and videos to players and staff, and engaging in other sexual misconduct. The regulator found 23 of 24 charges proven, citing repeated abuse from 2022‑2024. The man denied wrongdoing but showed little remorse. The case highlights the need for zero tolerance of sexual harassment in sports.
