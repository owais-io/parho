Iva Jovic, 18, became the youngest American woman to reach the Australian Open quarter‑finals since Venus Williams in 1998. She won her first four matches without dropping a set, including a 6‑0 6‑1 demolition of Yulia Putintseva. Jovic also beat seventh‑seed Jasmine Paolini in her first top‑20 win. She will now face world No.1 Aryna Sabalenka. Jovic also played a lively doubles match with Victoria Mboko earlier in the day.
