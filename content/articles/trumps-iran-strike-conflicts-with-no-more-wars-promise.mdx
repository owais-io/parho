Trump promised to stop launching foreign wars, but his recent attack on Iran’s leader has shocked many of his supporters. The strike sparked anger from former loyalists, media hosts, and some Republicans, who see it as a betrayal of the “America First” pledge. Some still back the move, saying it stops Iran’s nuclear threat. The backlash shows a divide in Trump’s base over international conflict.
