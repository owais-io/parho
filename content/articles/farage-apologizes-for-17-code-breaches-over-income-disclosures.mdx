Nigel Farage, leader of Reform UK and MP for Clacton, has publicly apologized after a standards investigation found he failed to declare £380,000 of outside earnings on time. The breach involved work for GB News and social‑media payments, and he blamed a senior staff member for the administrative mistake. He said there was no intent to deceive and that he never used his MP role for money. The commissioner said the violations were accidental but serious.
