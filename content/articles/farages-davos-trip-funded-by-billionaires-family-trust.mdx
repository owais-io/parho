Nigel Farage, the Reform UK leader, attended the World Economic Forum in Davos, but the $10 billion family trust of Iranian‑born billionaire Sasan Ghandehari paid for his trip. He is listed on the forum’s programme as a member of parliament and a representative of the trust’s family office, HP Trust. The trust claims it invited him as an unpaid adviser, covering registration and hotel costs.
