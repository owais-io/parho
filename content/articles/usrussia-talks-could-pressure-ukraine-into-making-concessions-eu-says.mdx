EU foreign‑policy chief Kaja Kallas warned that the next meeting between Russian President Vladimir Putin and U.S. envoy Steve Witkoff could force Ukraine to make concessions. She fears the U.S. plan, which favors Russia, might let Moscow redraw borders and reward its war. Ukrainian President Zelenskyy and European leaders insist that only Ukraine can decide its borders and that peace must be fair and lasting.
