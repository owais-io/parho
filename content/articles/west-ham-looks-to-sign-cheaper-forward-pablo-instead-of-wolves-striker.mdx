West Ham are in talks to sign 21‑year‑old Pablo from Portugal’s Gil Vicente after pulling back from a £40m bid for Wolves striker Jørgen Strand Larsen. The Premier League club, fighting to avoid relegation, sees Pablo as a cheaper option who has scored nine goals and lifted his team to fourth place. The club also has interest in other forwards and is hoping to strengthen its attack as it remains 18th in the table.
