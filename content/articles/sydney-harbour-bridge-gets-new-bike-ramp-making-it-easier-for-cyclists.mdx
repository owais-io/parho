The Harbour Bridge now has a smooth ramp that replaces a steep 55‑step staircase. It lets e‑bikes, bikes with trailers, and older riders cross the bridge without struggling. The $39 million upgrade opened on Tuesday, and families, commuters, and delivery riders were among the first to use it. The new path is part of a growing network that connects North Sydney to the city and beyond.
