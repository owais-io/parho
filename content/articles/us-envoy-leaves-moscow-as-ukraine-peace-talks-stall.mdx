U.S. envoy Steve Witkoff flew out of Moscow after talks with Putin failed to move the Ukraine peace plan forward. Kremlin aides said nothing changed, and Ukrainian President Zelenskyy warned the U.S. might lose interest. Putin warned Europe that Russia was ready for war if it opposed Ukraine. Meanwhile Ukraine arrested a British spy and several Russian‑linked tankers were hit by attacks. The EU is still weighing using frozen Russian assets to help Ukraine.
