Blake Miguez, a Louisiana state senator running for Congress with Donald Trump’s support, was named in a 2007 rape allegation that was reported to police but never led to charges. The accuser said she didn’t want to press charges, and her father sent a letter to the campaign defending Miguez. The story surfaced months later, raising questions about how much the Trump team vetted his past.
