2025 was the UK’s hottest and brightest year ever, the Met Office said. This decade now holds the three warmest years, and all top ten warm years happened in the last 20 years. The average temperature hit 10.09 °C, beating 2022 and 2023. Heatwaves in spring and summer were strong. Sunshine was a record 1,648.5 hours, helping solar power meet more than 6 % of the country’s energy needs. Rain was lower than usual in many spots.
