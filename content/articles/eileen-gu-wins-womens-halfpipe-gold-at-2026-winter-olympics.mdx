Eileen Gu won the women's halfpipe at the 2026 Winter Olympics, beating teammate Li Fanghui and Britain’s Zoe Atkin. The Chinese skier scored the highest with a 94.75, while Li finished 93 and Zoe 92.5. The event was held after a snow delay and was part of a busy program that also included cross‑country, bobsleigh and curling. Fans celebrated a big win for China and excitement for the Games.
