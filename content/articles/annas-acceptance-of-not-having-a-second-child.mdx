Anna loved motherhood and hoped for a second baby, but after a year of negative tests she learned she has secondary infertility. The news shook her life, made her feel like a failure, and strained her relationship with her partner. Therapy helped her let go of the idea that a family must have siblings, taught her to handle people’s comments, and to find peace with being a single‑child family.
