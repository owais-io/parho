Karim Khan, the ICC prosecutor, says a British official threatened to cut the court’s funding if it issued an arrest warrant for Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu (and former Defence Minister Yoav Gallant). Khan says the UK warned the warrants were too harsh and that Israel wouldn’t cooperate. He also notes US officials warned of disastrous consequences and that he was later accused of sexual misconduct. Khan says he has acted neutrally.
