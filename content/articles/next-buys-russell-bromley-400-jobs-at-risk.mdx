Next has bought the old British shoe shop Russell & Bromley for £3.8 million. It will keep the brand and three flagship stores, keeping about 48 staff. The deal leaves 33 stores and nine concessions on their own, where around 400 jobs could disappear. Russell & Bromley, founded in 1879, is now part of Next’s growing list of fashion brands. The move shows how tough the UK retail sector is.
