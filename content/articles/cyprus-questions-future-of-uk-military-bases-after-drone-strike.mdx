Cyprus’s foreign minister said the UK’s two military bases on the island might change after a drone attack last Sunday that caused little damage. The attack, likely from Hezbollah, was intercepted and families were moved from the base. Cyprus leaders are upset about not being warned and want a conversation about the bases’ future. The UK says it remains committed to Cyprus’s security.
