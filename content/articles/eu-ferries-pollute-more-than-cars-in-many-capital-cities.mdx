A new study shows that ferries in 13 of Europe's 15 biggest port cities are releasing more sulphur pollution than the cars on the roads. The report, from Transport & Environment, found that ferries are a hidden source of bad air, especially in places like Dublin, Helsinki, Stockholm, Tallinn, Athens and Rome. The authors say electrifying ferries could cut emissions and give city residents cleaner air.
