British skier Kirsty Muir missed a bronze medal at the Winter Olympics, finishing fourth in women’s slopestyle by just 0.41 points. She had been a strong favourite after winning the X‑Games and had a solid final run, but a small mistake kept her from the podium. Muir is disappointed but still hopeful, looking forward to the big‑air event next week.
