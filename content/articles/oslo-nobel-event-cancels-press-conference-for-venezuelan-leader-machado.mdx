The Nobel Institute cancelled the planned press conference for María Corina Machado, the Venezuelan opposition leader who won the 2024 Peace Prize. The Oslo event was set for Wednesday, but organizers said they didn't know where she was. Machado hasn't been seen publicly since January and is thought to be in hiding. Her family is in Oslo, waiting. The Institute added it has no further information on her arrival.
