Trump’s war on Iran has cost the U.S. 30‑40 billion dollars and Israel 300 million a day. It hasn’t forced Iran to shut its nuclear program, and the U.S. struggles to keep the Strait of Hormuz open. Iran threatens shipping and could charge up to $80 billion a year. The long‑running conflict has dented Trump’s reputation and left the U.S. with a costly, uncertain future.
