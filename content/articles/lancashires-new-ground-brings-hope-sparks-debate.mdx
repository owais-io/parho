Lancashire’s county cricket club has opened a new second ground in Farington, near Preston, to give local kids a chance to play and improve the team’s performance. The site includes a professional oval for first‑team matches and a recreational pitch for community games, plus a future indoor dome and training nets. After a poor start to the season, the club reshuffled its coaching staff. Fans are excited but some worry the new site will replace traditional outgrounds.
