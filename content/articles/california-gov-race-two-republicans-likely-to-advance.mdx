A new poll shows Republican candidates Steve Hilton and Chad Bianco are leading in California’s governor race, each getting about 16% of likely voters, while three Democrats—Eric Swalwell, Katie Porter, and Tom Steyer—each get about 10%. With many Democrats still in the race and voters largely uninterested, the Democratic Party is urging weaker candidates to drop out, fearing a Republican win. Only one candidate withdrew, and a debate that was to feature several contestants was cancelled.
