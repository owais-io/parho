A woman in Melbourne discovered that after her heart issue she had to pay over $100 for each specialist visit. Her savings vanished, leaving her to pick between health and money. Many Australians feel the same. Experts say the system has no clear rules to keep fees fair, so doctors, hospitals and the government are debating ways to make fees transparent and give patients more affordable options.
