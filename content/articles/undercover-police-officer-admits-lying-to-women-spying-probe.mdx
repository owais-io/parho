Carlo Soracchi, an undercover police officer, admitted he lied to two women he had been involved with, fabricating family tragedies and using public money for romantic trips. He told managers the Italy trip was work, but it was for pleasure. His actions exploited the women's empathy and caused deep harm, prompting a spycops inquiry into his and other officers' conduct.
