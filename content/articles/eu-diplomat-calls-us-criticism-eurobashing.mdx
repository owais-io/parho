EU diplomat Kaja Kallas, speaking at the Munich Security Conference, said the US’s claim that Europe is being “erased” is wrong and called the criticism “fashionable euro‑bashing.” She praised Europe’s human‑rights record and press freedom, and warned the US that Europe wants to stay in the partnership, not just for Ukraine. US Secretary Marco Rubio said cooperation depends on Europe meeting demands on migration, trade and defence spending.
