Nurse Sandie Peggie, who worked over 30 years, said she was harassed when the NHS let a transgender doctor, Dr Beth Upton, use the women’s changing room at Victoria Hospital. The tribunal agreed Peggie was harassed by the health board but said the evidence against Dr Upton was not reliable, so her claim was dismissed. The case shows how the new Supreme Court ruling on biological sex is being applied, and it has sparked strong reactions from both sides.
