Local council in Hartlepool, County Durham, says the housing secretary, Steve Reed, treated a meeting about child‑care funding with arrogance and indifference. Council leaders accused him of calling it a “reward” and shrugging off the need for more money, calling his attitude moral bankruptcy. The council says its children are in crisis and the £3 million promised is far from enough. They want stronger national funding reforms.
