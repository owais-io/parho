US has banned visas for five European figures who pushed the EU’s Digital Services Act, sparking outrage across Europe. French President Emmanuel Macron and other leaders call the move intimidation and defend the EU’s rules on hate‑speech and disinformation. Washington says the act is a form of censorship that affects American tech firms. The clash highlights the growing friction between the US and Europe over how digital platforms should be regulated.
