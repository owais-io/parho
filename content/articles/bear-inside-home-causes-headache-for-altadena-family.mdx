A 550‑lb black bear is hiding in the crawl space of a house in Altadena, California. Homeowner Ken Johnson has tried to get it out with food, scented towels and special chemical traps, but the bear has stayed for more than two weeks. After the Eaton wildfire, several bears fled to nearby homes, and this one has become a nuisance. Wildlife officials are using cameras and sweet‑smelling lures to try to move the bear 10 miles away.
