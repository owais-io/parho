A Mexican immigrant died in a California hospital after being in ICE custody. Family members say he was denied medical care when he had chest pain and shortness of breath. The death is the ninth ICE‑custody death this year. ICE and the Department of Homeland Security have not commented. The Mexican government is demanding an investigation, and the detainee’s wife is filing a complaint and raising money for funeral costs.
