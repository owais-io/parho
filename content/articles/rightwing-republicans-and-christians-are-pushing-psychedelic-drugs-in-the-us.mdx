Once seen as a left‑wing movement, psychedelics are now promoted by conservative Christians, Republican leaders, veterans and tech billionaires. The drug ibogaine, from an African root, gets big funding in Texas and other states and is claimed to help veterans with PTSD and brain injuries after a single 12‑hour session. High‑profile names—Elon Musk, Conor McGregor—have spoken about their experiences. While many praise its healing, doctors warn it can be dangerous, with reports of deaths. The right is embracing psychedelic medicine.
