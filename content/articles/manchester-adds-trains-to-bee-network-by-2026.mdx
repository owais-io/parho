Manchester is creating an integrated transport system. By the end of 2026, trains on the Glossop and Stalybridge lines will join the Bee Network, letting passengers use one tap‑in card on buses, trams and trains. The network will cover all 17 stations by next year and the whole Greater Manchester area by 2030. New apps will give real‑time info, and the changes aim to make journeys easier, create jobs and boost the local economy.
