The UK defence secretary said the destroyer HMS Dragon will sail to Cyprus in the next few days, but the exact timing is unclear. The move follows a drone strike on a UK airbase in Akrotiri and has drawn criticism from Cyprus and from opposition MPs who say Britain was slow to respond. France and other allies already have ships in the eastern Mediterranean. The decision came after the UK has cut defence spending and reduced its number of warships.
