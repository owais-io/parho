Arsenal beat Leeds 5‑0 at Elland Road, putting them back on a winning track. The win was boosted by Noni Madueke, who supplied two goals from his first game for the club, and by striker Gabriel Jesus. Arsenal’s star winger Bukayo Saka was injured before kickoff, but the team’s depth helped them win convincingly. Leeds’ manager Daniel Farke said the defeat was frustrating, and Arsenal are now seven points ahead in the title race.
