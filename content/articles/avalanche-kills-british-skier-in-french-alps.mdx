A British skier, about 50 years old, died when an avalanche hit his ski run in La Plagne, a resort in the French Alps. The skier was off‑piste, without an avalanche transceiver and not being watched by a professional instructor. Rescue teams – 50 people, including medics, ski‑school teachers and dogs – searched for almost an hour before finding him under 2.5 metres of snow. The resort sent condolences to his family.
