Israeli troops went into eastern Lebanon to dig for the remains of missing pilot Ron Arad. They were spotted by the Lebanese army, triggering a fight with Hezbollah and villagers. Israeli jets fired at the town, killing about 41 residents and three soldiers. The operation ended with no Israeli casualties. The incident shows the high risk of the search and the ongoing tensions between Israel and Hezbollah.
