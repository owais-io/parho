After losing to Manchester United, Arsenal captain Martin Ødegaard says the club’s three straight runner‑up seasons are actually a motivator, not a curse. He stresses staying in the moment and ignoring outside noise. The team is eyeing a Champions League win and fighting to keep their league lead. Ødegaard sees the setbacks as fuel for more hunger and determination. They hope to finish the group stage undefeated.
