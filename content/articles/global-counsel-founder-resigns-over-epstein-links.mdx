Ben Wegg‑Prosser, former No10 aide and co‑founder of Global Counsel, stepped down as chief executive after emails revealed he and Peter Mandelson had consulted Jeffrey Epstein when the firm was set up in 2010. The scandal has cost clients like Barclays and raised doubts about the lobbying firm’s ties to Labour. Mandelson’s shares are being sold, and Rebecca Park is named as the new CEO.
