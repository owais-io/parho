Zack Polanski, the Green Party leader, says he has never used drugs or alcohol. He argues people should be free to use drugs if they wish, but they should be regulated and monitored by health professionals. Polanski believes prisons are the wrong answer and that the war on drugs hurts especially young black and brown people. He also shared that he used to breakdance, showing life can be enjoyable without substances.
