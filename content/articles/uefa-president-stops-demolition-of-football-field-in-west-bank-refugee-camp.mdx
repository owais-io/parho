Uefa president Aleksander Ceferin called Israeli officials and helped stop the planned demolition of a football field in the Aida refugee camp near Bethlehem. The pitch, built in 2021, is the only one in the camp and is used by residents, including young female players. After a campaign and talks with the Israeli Football Association, Israeli security forces decided not to remove the field. The move keeps a safe space for the camp’s community.
