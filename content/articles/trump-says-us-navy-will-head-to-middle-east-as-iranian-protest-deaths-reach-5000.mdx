Donald Trump said the United States is preparing a naval force for the Middle East after a deadly crackdown on Iranian protesters killed more than 5,000 people. He spoke on Air Force One, noting the USS Abraham Lincoln and other ships are moving to the region. The comments come as human‑rights groups report thousands of arrests and an internet shutdown in Iran. Iran warns any US attack would be met with strong retaliation.
