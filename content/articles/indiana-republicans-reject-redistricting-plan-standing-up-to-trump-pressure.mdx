Indiana lawmakers voted 19‑31 to reject a new voting‑map that would have added two more Republican seats. Key Republicans, including Senator Greg Goode, said the map went against their voters’ wishes. President Trump had urged the Senate to approve it and warned that the state could lose federal money if it failed. The rejection shows the GOP is not ready to give in to Trump’s demands.
