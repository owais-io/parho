Two 18‑ and 19‑year‑old men were arrested in New York after throwing homemade bombs at a protest outside the mayor’s house. They claimed to support the Islamic State and were charged with terrorism, possession of explosives and other crimes. Police said the devices, made of a dangerous homemade explosive, were defused safely. The arrests show the city’s ongoing effort to stop violent radical acts.
