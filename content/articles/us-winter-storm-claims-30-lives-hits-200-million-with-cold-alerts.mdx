Heavy snow and ice hit the U.S. over the weekend, killing at least 30 people from Texas to New England. Many states reported deaths from sledding accidents, hypothermia, and a snow‑plow hit. More than 200 million people are under cold alerts, and the storm caused over 670,000 power outages. Flights were cancelled and several schools and museums closed, while officials warn of another big storm coming soon.
