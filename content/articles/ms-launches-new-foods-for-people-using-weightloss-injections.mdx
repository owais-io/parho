Marks & Spencer has announced a new line of foods aimed at people taking weight‑loss injections such as Wegovy or Zepbound. The range, starting on Jan 5, includes salads, breads, yoghurt bowls and chicken dinners that are small but packed with nutrients per calorie. It comes as more shoppers use GLP‑1 medicines that curb appetite, making retailers rethink their menus. Similar products are already arriving at Morrisons, and the move follows growing demand for healthier, portioned meals.
