A 23‑year‑old Los Angeles resident, Jesus Javier Gomez Islas, claims he was blinded in one eye when a police launcher fired a green paint projectile at him during a January anti‑ICE protest outside the Metropolitan Detention Center. He filed a claim seeking $100 million from the LAPD, saying the city used a weapon called an FN 303 and failed to give medical help. The LAPD has not identified the officer or commented on the case.
