The DHS shutdown has left many TSA agents unpaid, and almost 500 have quit or stopped coming to work. Airports across the country now have hours‑long security lines, causing travelers to miss flights, spend extra money and in some cases cancel trips. People report chaotic queues at big hubs and even at small airports, while higher gas prices and a shaky economy make many rethink flying.
