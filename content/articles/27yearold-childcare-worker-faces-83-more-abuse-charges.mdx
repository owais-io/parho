A 27‑year‑old former child‑care worker in Victoria, Australia, has been charged with 83 new offences of sexual abuse after earlier accusations. Police say he may have hurt children as young as five months old at several centres. The cases prompted a health‑department test for about 2,000 kids, but no further testing is needed. The Premier promises stronger child‑care safety rules and support for affected families.
