A U.S. judge has ordered that a five‑year‑old boy and his father be released from a Texas detention center after ICE agents arrested them in Minneapolis. The child, Liam Conejo Ramos, was taken while he was on the driveway of his home, sparking outrage after pictures showed him in a bunny hat. The judge said the arrest was part of an ineffective quota system and that using a child as bait was wrong. The family is still awaiting asylum decisions.
