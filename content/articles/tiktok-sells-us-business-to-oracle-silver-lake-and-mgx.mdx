TikTok is selling its U.S. business to Oracle, Silver Lake and MGX, which will own half of a new U.S. joint venture. The deal, set to close on Jan 22, keeps TikTok running in the U.S. but raises worries about who controls the app’s algorithm, with Oracle linked to former President Trump. The sale follows years of talks to stop a U.S. ban and a last‑minute ownership plan.
