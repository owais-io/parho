Meera Naran lost her 8‑year‑old son Dev in a 2018 motorway crash. She spent years campaigning for stronger road safety, especially for smart motorways. Her fight led to the government pledging “Dev’s Law” – making safety tech like autonomous emergency braking mandatory in new cars. Ministers thanked her and said the plan will cut thousands of deaths by 2035. Naran says the rules turn her grief into life‑saving action for everyone.
