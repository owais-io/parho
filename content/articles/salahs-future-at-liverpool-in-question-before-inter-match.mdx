Salah could be left out of Liverpool’s upcoming Inter match after he publicly criticized the club and coach Arne Slot. He said he feels mistreated and no longer has a relationship with Slot. With 19 months left on his contract, the Egyptian star may attract interest from Saudi clubs. Liverpool said nothing publicly, and Slot must decide his place in the squad. Salah will still train and may face travel to Italy.
