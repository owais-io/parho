The Trump administration announced it will close the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) in Boulder, Colorado, saying it spreads “climate alarmism.” The move follows a broader push to cut climate science funding. Climate scientists, Colorado’s governor, and others say NCAR is essential for studying weather, including hurricanes, and helps protect people from storms and wildfires. The closure would affect about 830 staff, aircraft, and a supercomputer used for research.
