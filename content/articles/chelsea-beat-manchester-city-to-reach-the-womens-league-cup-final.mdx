Chelsea won 1‑0 over Manchester City thanks to a first‑half header by Wieke Kaptein. Goalkeeper Hannah Hampton made key saves, and City missed many chances. The win sends Chelsea to the League Cup final at Ashton Gate on March 15, where they’ll face Manchester United. Despite the victory, City’s coach said they should be sharper next time. Chelsea’s coach celebrated her run of finals since 2024.
