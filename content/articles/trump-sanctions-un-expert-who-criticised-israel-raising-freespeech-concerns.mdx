Trump has slapped sanctions on UN human‑rights officer Francesca Albanese for urging the International Criminal Court to seek arrest warrants against Israeli leaders. The U.S. bans her from opening bank accounts and warns anyone who helps her could be criminally charged. Academics fear this creates a chilling effect, as they hesitate to discuss Israel’s actions in Gaza. A lawsuit challenges the sanctions as unconstitutional and a threat to free speech.
