Paul Lim, 71, made darts history by becoming the oldest player to win a match at the World Championship. He beat Jeffrey de Graaf 3‑1 and will face former rival Luke Humphries in the next round. Lim says the win against Humphries five years ago mattered more than the age record. Humphries respects Lim but admits it’s tough to play a fan. Other players like Wessel Nijman, Gabriel Clemens and Gary Anderson also moved on to round two.
