The state party’s annual convention in San Francisco turned into a tribute to Nancy Pelosi, who is retiring after 20 terms. Thousands of Democrats gathered to applaud her record as a congresswoman and speaker, her role in redistricting that gave California 48 House seats, and her fight for women's and LGBTQ rights. Pelosi gave a short, emotional speech, thanked her staff, and urged the party to keep pushing for a Democratic win in 2024.
