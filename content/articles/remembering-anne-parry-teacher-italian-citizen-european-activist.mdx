Anne Parry (75) was a Liverpool‑born English teacher who moved to Italy in 1991, teaching at universities in Verona and Venice. She became an Italian citizen and fully embraced European life. After Brexit, she joined the European Federalist Movement, campaigned for the EU, ran for the Italian Senate, and supported Ukraine against Russian propaganda. She remained active until her death, leaving a daughter, granddaughters and siblings behind.
