Reform UK's new business spokesman, Richard Tice, says the party will scrap two important Labour laws – the Employment Rights Act and the Renters’ Rights Act. He argues they hurt jobs and make it hard for landlords. Unions, renters’ groups and charities say this would damage workers’ rights and push more people into homelessness. Tice also wants to cut net‑zero targets and boost fracking.
