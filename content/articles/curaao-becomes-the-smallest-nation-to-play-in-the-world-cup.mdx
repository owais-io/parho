Curaçao, a tiny Caribbean island with 156,000 people, has become the smallest country ever to qualify for the World Cup. Coach Dick Advocaat, who started after solving financial problems, brought experience and helped the team use players from the Dutch diaspora. The squad’s 0‑0 draw with Jamaica secured their spot. Advocaat may also become the oldest coach at the tournament. It’s a proud moment for a nation that feels its dream has come true.
