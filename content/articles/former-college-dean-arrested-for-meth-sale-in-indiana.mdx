Alan Jay White, a 59‑year‑old former dean of Indiana University Southeast’s business school, was arrested in Clarksville, Indiana. Police found 78 grams of meth and fake money in his home, saying the amount was too large for personal use. White denies ownership, saying any drugs or cash found were not his. The case highlights ongoing drug‑enforcement scrutiny and a reference to the TV show Breaking Bad.
