On Saturday, an ICE officer shot and killed Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, in Minneapolis. The city saw crowds in sub‑zero weather holding candles and chanting against ICE. Police and the National Guard set up a perimeter, then pulled back after the vigil. Residents set up barriers, shared videos of confrontations, and called for a stop to federal raids. The incident has sparked nationwide protests.
