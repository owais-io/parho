England collapsed to 518 for 7 at the Sydney Cricket Ground, and Jacob Bethell admitted the side is tired after playing five Tests in less than two months. He said the team must “graft” and keep pushing to recover, stressing that mental and physical fatigue will affect performance. Bethell was pleased to take his first Ashes wicket, but highlighted the need for better fielding, bowling plans and some spin.
