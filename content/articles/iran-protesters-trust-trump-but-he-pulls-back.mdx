Trump said he would help Iranian protesters, but when violence started, he reversed his statement and stopped any military plans. Many protesters, like Siavash Shirzad, went to the streets hoping for help and were killed. The government kept arresting people, showing forced confessions, and threatened more violence. Iranians inside and abroad feel betrayed because the US promised aid but pulled back.
