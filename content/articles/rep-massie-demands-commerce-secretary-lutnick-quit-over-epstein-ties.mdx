House member Thomas Massie has asked Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick to resign because emails show the secretary met with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein and discussed traveling to Epstein’s island. The files also reveal Lutnick invited Epstein to a Clinton fundraiser and sought his advice on a museum renovation. Lutnick, a Trump ally, has denied the links, but lawmakers are calling for his removal. The story highlights concerns over U.S. officials’ ties to Epstein.
