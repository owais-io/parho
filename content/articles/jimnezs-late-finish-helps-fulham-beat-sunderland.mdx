Raúl Jiménez scored a dramatic late winner to give Fulham a 1‑0 victory over Sunderland. The goal kept Marco Silva’s side away from the relegation zone, easing pressure on a club that has been uncertain about new signings and its future. Fulham’s win means the manager might stay on, but fans still worry about a lack of money and the need for better finishing. Sunderland lost another chance to climb the table.
