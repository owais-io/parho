Mikel Arteta argued Arsenal deserved a penalty when a Forest defender handled the ball with his arm in the last minutes. The referee didn’t award it, and the match finished 0‑0. Forest, who are only a few points above the relegation zone, showed strong defensive work, while Arsenal struggled to create scoring opportunities. The result keeps Arsenal close to the top, but Forest’s performance suggests they’re fighting to stay in the Premier League.
