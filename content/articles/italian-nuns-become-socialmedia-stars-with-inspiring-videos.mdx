For 22 nuns in an Italian retirement home, a 98‑year‑old sister felt lonely, so the youngest started posting daily videos on Facebook. The clips, showing prayers, jokes and simple challenges, quickly went viral, drawing 145,000 followers. They even toss cardboard boxes labeled “stress” or “anxiety” to throw out negativity. Fans send prayers; the nuns reply personally. Their online presence gives them purpose and spreads hopeful, positive messages worldwide.
