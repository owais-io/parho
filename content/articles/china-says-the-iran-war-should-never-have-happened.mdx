China says the war in the Middle East should never have happened, saying it would hurt the world. Foreign Minister Wang Yi told reporters at China’s parliamentary meetings that the U.S. and China should treat each other with respect, even as U.S. President Donald Trump prepares to visit Beijing. He warned that any new conflict would upset global trade and highlighted China’s stance on Taiwan and Japan.
