Peter Mandelson, the former UK business secretary, is accused of sending confidential market data to Jeffrey Epstein during the 2009‑10 financial crisis. New emails reveal he forwarded sensitive information that could have helped Epstein profit. Labour leaders, Gordon Brown, and others have demanded a full investigation, and the Metropolitan Police say they will review the evidence to see if a criminal case should be opened. Mandelson may also be asked to resign from the House of Lords.
