South East Water says 6,500 homes in Tunbridge Wells have no water, and many more get spotty supply. The company blamed the cold weather, which caused burst pipes. A month after a big outage, residents are again stuck without reliable water. The firm is running tankers and fixing leaks, but an MP warns the problem may last days. Critics say the company spends too much on dividends and not enough on fixing pipes.
