Jenna Turnbull, a 36‑year‑old civil servant, has been suffering from a condition called rejection sensitive dysphoria (RSD). Small comments or jokes trigger intense shame and physical symptoms like chest tightness, panic attacks, and obsessive cleaning. RSD is often linked with ADHD and can make everyday criticism feel like a huge blow. Jenna’s story highlights how the condition is being recognized, how it affects daily life, and the treatments that can help.
