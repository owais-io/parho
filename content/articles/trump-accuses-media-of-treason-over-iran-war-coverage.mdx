Trump has escalated his attacks on the press, saying news outlets could be charged with treason for reporting on his Iran war. He’s backed by FCC chair Brendan Carr and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth, who want media to sound “patriotic.” CNN’s boss defended the network’s reporting, while journalists at CBS say their independence is shrinking. The move threatens a free press and puts first‑amendment rights to the test.
