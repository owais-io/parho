Woolworths said its half‑year profit rose 16%, helped by tighter margins and a 3.6% sales increase. The company’s gains come just before the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission (ACCC) plans a lawsuit, accusing Woolworths and Coles of giving shoppers “illusory” discounts that mislead customers. Woolworths is also dealing with a $485m settlement for underpaid staff and a $400m cost‑cut plan.
