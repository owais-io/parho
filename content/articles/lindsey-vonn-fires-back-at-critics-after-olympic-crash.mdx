Lindsey Vonn, the 41‑year‑old U.S. skier, has responded to people who said she shouldn’t have raced in this year’s Winter Olympics after a crash that broke her tibia and ruptured her ACL. She posted on Instagram, reminding everyone of her world‑cup wins and how she earned her spot. Vonn said she has no regrets and that the decision was her own. Fellow skiers backed her, saying athletes should decide for themselves.
