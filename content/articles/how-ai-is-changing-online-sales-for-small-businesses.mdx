A quick survey is looking at how AI tools like ChatGPT and Google Gemini are changing the way people search online. Small businesses worry that these tools might hide them from customers and reduce online traffic and sales. The poll asks owners if they see less traffic, new opportunities, or new marketing tactics, and also asks customers if they’re finding it harder to locate independent retailers.
