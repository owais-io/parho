US forces attacked a suspected drug‑smuggling boat in the eastern Pacific on Friday, killing three men. It was the second such strike this week and brings the total U.S. strike deaths to at least 148 since September. Earlier in the week a strike killed 11 people. Critics, including the ACLU, say the hits may be illegal and amount to extrajudicial killings, raising legal concerns.
