Tottenham coach Thomas Frank urged calm after fans shouted for his sacking. The club drew 1‑1 with Burnley, keeping them mid‑table and in trouble. Frank said the team must tighten defence and keep fighting, but said the club was working hard to get better. Fans still want him out, but no decision has been made. Frank hopes to turn the draw into a win and stay in the Champions League.
