Fairphone’s sixth‑generation phone costs £499 and stays repairable and sustainable. It has a 6.3‑inch 120Hz OLED, 8 GB RAM, Snapdragon 7s Gen 3 and a 50‑MP camera good in bright light. The battery can be swapped at home and lasts about 35 hours. Made from recycled parts, it earned a 10‑out‑of‑10 repair score and will get updates until 2033. It also has a microSD slot and a Moments switch to reduce notifications.
