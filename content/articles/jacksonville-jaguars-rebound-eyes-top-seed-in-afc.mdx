Seven weeks ago the Jaguars were shaky and entertaining but inconsistent. After a win over the Broncos, they’re on a six‑game winning streak and have 11 victories, the most in a season since 2007. Head coach Liam Coen, new receiver Jakobi Meyers, and quarterback Trevor Lawrence have reshaped the offense, while the defense has become more aggressive. With two games left, Jacksonville could secure the AFC’s No. 1 seed and set the playoff path.
