Ukrainian vodka maker Nemiroff says its bottles are still reaching UK supermarkets, even after a container was bombed in Odesa. The company stopped selling in Russia, shifted to truck deliveries, and added generators to keep its distillery running. Sales in the UK jumped 24% last year, and the brand is now in major stores like Tesco and Sainsbury’s. Nemiroff is also supporting other Ukrainian businesses and the government’s new rail routes to keep exports moving.
