Swiss police are working fast to identify the 40 people who died in a bar fire at the Crans‑Montana ski resort on New Year’s Eve. The blaze, which started at about 1.30 a.m., also injured 115 people. The president announced five days of mourning and said the country must learn from this tragedy. Police say it may take days or weeks to match bodies with names, as they try to find the cause of the fire.
