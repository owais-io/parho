UK MPs say the government, Bank of England and FCA are not doing enough to manage AI risks in finance. They warn that AI could harm consumers and trigger a crisis if firms copy each other’s decisions. Over 75% of City firms use AI, yet no clear rules exist. MPs call for new stress tests, clearer guidance and accountability to protect customers and stability.
