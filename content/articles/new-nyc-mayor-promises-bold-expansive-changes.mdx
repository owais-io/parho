Zohran Mamdani, the city’s first Muslim mayor, vowed to govern with bold, expansive plans and to stay true to his democratic socialist values. He revoked several orders from the former administration and will re‑issue key ones. Mamdani’s agenda includes free childcare, a rent freeze and city-run grocery stores, but it will need state approval and higher taxes on the wealthy. He faces criticism from both Trump and some local Jewish groups.
