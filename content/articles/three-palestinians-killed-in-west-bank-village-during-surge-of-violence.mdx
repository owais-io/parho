Three Palestinians were shot dead in the village of Abu Falah, near Ramallah, after settlers vandalised olive trees. The killings are part of a week‑long surge of violence, with Israeli settlers and soldiers attacking Palestinian homes, crops and people. While Israel and the US focus on a new war with Iran, the West Bank sees more settler militias using the army for attacks. No arrests have been made, and the Israeli army says it will investigate.
