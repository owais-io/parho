People still debate whether Die Hard is a Christmas film, with polls split 44% against and 38% for. The article argues that a true holiday movie needs more than just decorations; it should carry a message about family, hope or togetherness. It then spotlights the new film *Pillion*, a queer biker romance set on Christmas that pushes that idea further. Other examples like *The Holiday*, *Catch Me If You Can* and *Eyes Wide Shut* show how Christmas can be used in many ways.
