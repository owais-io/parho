South Korean game publisher Krafton was forced by a US court to undo a plan that used ChatGPT to fire the leaders of its newly‑acquired studio, Unknown Worlds, to avoid paying a $250 million earn‑out. Krafton bought the studio for $500 million in 2021 and had agreed the studio would stay independent. The court said the leaders could only be dismissed for cause and that Krafton must still pay the earn‑out if the studio met its targets.
