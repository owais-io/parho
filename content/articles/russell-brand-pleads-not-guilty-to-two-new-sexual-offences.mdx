Russell Brand pleaded not guilty to two new rape and assault charges from 2009, after being charged in December. He appeared at Southwark Crown Court in a tiger‑print shirt, carrying a Bible and wearing a fedora. Judge Baumgartner said bail would be renewed and scheduled another hearing for March, with trial later this year. Brand had faced earlier accusations dating back to 1999, which he also denied.
