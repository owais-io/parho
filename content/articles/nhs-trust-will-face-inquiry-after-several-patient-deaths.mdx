The UK health secretary announced a public inquiry into Tees, Esk and Wear Valleys NHS Trust after several patient deaths, including three young women who committed suicide while in care. The inquiry will look at how the trust handled patients at risk of self‑harm. The trust has been fined before and has past investigations, but officials say those reviews were not enough. Families want truth and better safety.
