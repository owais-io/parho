New South Wales Liberals have chosen former journalist Kellie Sloane as their new leader, beating no rivals. She said she’ll fight the Labor government by focusing on families and the cost of living. Sloane, a first‑term MP, promised to keep net‑zero goals, handle rising energy prices, and support smart housing near trains. She has backing from senior party figures but faces questions about wages caps and transport funding.
