A green‑card holder and her two kids sued ICE after the agency violently detained them in Massachusetts. The lawsuit says ICE officers surrounded their car, smashed windows, handcuffed the mother, kicked her, and forced a 13‑year‑old son out. The mother suffered injuries and claims the officers didn’t identify themselves. She is seeking $1 million in damages. The case highlights concerns about ICE conduct in local courts and the impact on families.
