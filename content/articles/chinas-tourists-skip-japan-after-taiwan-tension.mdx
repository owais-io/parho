Japanese tourism from China fell 45% last December, after Japan’s prime minister warned her country could get involved if China tried to take Taiwan. China’s government urged its citizens to stay away, cancelling student exchanges and film releases. Despite the dip, Japan still tops the list of foreign visitors, attracting 42.7 million tourists last year. Officials hope Chinese travelers will return soon.
