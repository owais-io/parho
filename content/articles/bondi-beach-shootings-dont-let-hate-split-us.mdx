At Bondi Beach in Australia, a terrorist attack on a Jewish Hanukkah celebration left 16 people dead and 29 seriously hurt. Two gunmen fired from a bridge, targeting Jews, and a car with bombs was found nearby. The incident is part of a worldwide rise in antisemitic violence linked to the Israel‑Gaza conflict. Australia’s past gun tragedies add to the grief. The government urges everyone to stand together and not let hate divide communities.
