UK Foreign Secretary Yvette Cooper said Britain will not join Donald Trump’s new “board of peace” aimed at ending the Gaza conflict. She cited worries that the group is a Trump‑led rival to the UN and fears that Russian President Vladimir Putin, who is invited to the board, isn’t committed to ending the war in Ukraine. Cooper said the UK will keep talking with allies about peace and support Trump’s 20‑point Gaza plan.
