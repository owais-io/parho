Trump is still ordering U.S. airstrikes on Iran, but the reason isn’t clear. He says Iran’s missiles are down and its nuclear program is cut, yet the attacks are mainly to force Iran to let ships pass the Strait of Hormuz, where a lot of oil travels. The article compares this to Nixon’s Vietnam war, suggesting Trump wants an easy exit and the world is paying the price.
