California pipeline starts shipping oil again after Trump used emergency power. The Santa Ynez line had been shut since a 2015 spill that killed many sea animals. Sable Offshore, now the owner, has tried for a year to get permits but got none. Governor Newsom says the move hurts residents and threatens a lawsuit. Rising oil prices from the Iran conflict keep pressure high.
