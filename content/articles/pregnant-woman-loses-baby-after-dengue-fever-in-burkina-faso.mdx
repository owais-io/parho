In Burkina Faso, a woman named Gowend (not her real name) had dengue fever while pregnant twice. The first time she was sick, she fainted, went to the hospital, and later found out she had miscarried. The doctors couldn’t say for sure that dengue caused it, but she believes it did. The second time she fell very ill again, but she survived. Now she warns others about mosquitoes and dengue, hoping to protect her children and others in the community.
