Duke Charles James Spencer‑Churchill, 70, appeared in court and pleaded not guilty to three strangulation charges involving his estranged wife, Edla Marlborough, between Nov 2022 and Jan 2024. The incidents were said to have taken place in a garden, a laundry room, and on a bed. He was granted conditional bail and will return to Oxford Crown Court on Feb. 5 to formally plead. The Duke, who inherited his title in 2014, has been separated from Edla since 2024.
