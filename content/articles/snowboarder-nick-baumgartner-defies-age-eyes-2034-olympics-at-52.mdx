Nick Baumgartner, 44, is still racing snowboard cross at the Olympics, the fifth time he’s competed. He says age is just a number and he keeps training hard, using smart, injury‑free workouts. He hopes to keep racing until 52 in 2034 to inspire people. Baumgartner lives in Michigan’s Upper Peninsula, works construction jobs, and has a strong family and sponsor team backing him. His story shows that age can be a motivation, not a limit.
