Australia’s coach said they took a big risk by bringing back captain Pat Cummins for the third Ashes Test, but now he’s out for the rest of the series. The squad for the Boxing Day Test includes new pace bowlers and off-spinner Todd Murphy to replace injured Nathan Lyon. Steve Smith will lead the team, while the selectors look to keep the side strong for future games.
