Victoria Beckham has officially filed trademarks on the names of her children. The move has sparked debate about whether parents can own their kids’ names, especially for a celebrity with a global brand. Some see it as a natural extension of protecting a personal brand, while others think it’s unnecessary and even unfair to the kids and anyone else who shares the same names.
