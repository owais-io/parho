UK paediatrician Hilary Cass says social media shows unrealistic pictures of gender transition, making kids think it’s easier or more common than it is. She warns that early social changes can lock kids into a wrong path. Her review led to the NHS banning puberty blockers. New school guidelines will let teachers talk carefully with parents and kids before any social transition, and keep strict rules for pre‑pubescent children.
