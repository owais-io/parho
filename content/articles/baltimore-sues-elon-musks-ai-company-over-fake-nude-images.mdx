Baltimore’s mayor and city council have filed a lawsuit against Elon Musk’s AI firm, xAI, saying its chatbot Grok can produce fake nude images without users’ permission. The city says Grok put residents at risk by turning pictures into sexualized deepfakes, including child abuse material. The case is rare because a city is suing over consumer‑protection and public‑safety claims, not just personal harm. Musk says he didn’t know about the content.
