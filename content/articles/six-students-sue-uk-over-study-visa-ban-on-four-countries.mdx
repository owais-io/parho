Six students from Sudan and Afghanistan are suing the UK Home Office after it announced a ban on study visas for nationals of four countries. The ban, due to a rise in asylum claims, means they can’t enroll in UK universities even though they have offers from Oxford, Cambridge and Imperial. The students argue the ban is unlawful, racially discriminatory and harms their education and future careers.
