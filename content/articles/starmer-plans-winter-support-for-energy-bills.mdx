UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said his government will look for ways to help households with energy bills next winter, after a rise in prices caused by the Iran conflict. He plans to target support at the poorest families instead of a universal bailout and will use the existing price‑cap system. The government is also considering stronger powers for the Competition and Markets Authority to stop profiteering. The situation will depend on how long the conflict lasts.
