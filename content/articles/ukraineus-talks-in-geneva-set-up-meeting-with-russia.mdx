Zelenskyy said Ukrainian‑US talks in Geneva will help set up a March meeting with Russia. The talks focus on swapping prisoners, building a reconstruction “prosperity package” for Ukraine and exploring peace options. The meeting is part of a wider effort to find a solution to the war. The talks run ahead of a potential trilateral meeting involving Ukraine, the US and Russia.
