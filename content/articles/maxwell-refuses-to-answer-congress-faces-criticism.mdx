During a closed‑door session, Ghislaine Maxwell declined to answer questions from a congressional oversight committee, citing her right to remain silent. Rep. Robert Garcia blasted the move as a cover‑up and called for the release of Jeffrey Epstein’s files. Maxwell’s lawyer said she has a pending habeas petition and would speak if granted clemency. In a recent interview with Trump’s deputy attorney general, she answered every question.
