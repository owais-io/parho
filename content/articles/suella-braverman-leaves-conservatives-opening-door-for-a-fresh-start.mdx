Braverman has quit the Conservatives to join Reform UK, a move the writer says could help the party shed a divisive image. She argues that her exit, along with others like Jenrick, may give new leader Kemi Badenoch a chance to steer the party back to mainstream values and win over centre‑ground voters. The author also promotes a new centre‑right group, Prosper UK, as a platform for change.
