After the 2021 military coup, Yangon’s streets look busy but residents feel unsafe. The junta is holding elections that western observers say are fake. People fear speaking up, worry about being arrested or conscripted, and many have left jobs or the city. The economy has collapsed, inflation is high, and war spreads across Myanmar. Voter turnout is low and many believe the vote won’t change the junta’s power.
