A global outage at Cloudflare, a key internet security company, caused error messages on many sites after an unexpected traffic spike. Users couldn’t reach some websites, and tools like performance dashboards went down. Popular sites such as X and OpenAI were affected, and even Cloudflare’s Warp service in London was disabled. The company is working on a fix, but errors may persist for a while and the root cause is still unknown.
