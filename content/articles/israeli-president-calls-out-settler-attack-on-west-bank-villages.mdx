Masked Israeli settlers attacked two West Bank villages, setting cars on fire and injuring four Palestinians. The attacks also damaged a dairy factory and burned trucks. Israel’s president, Isaac Herzog, called the violence “shocking” and urged authorities to crack down. This is the first rebuke by top officials, as most have ignored the rise in settler attacks that have killed over a thousand Palestinians in recent years. Human‑rights groups say the violence is happening in a “permissive” environment.
