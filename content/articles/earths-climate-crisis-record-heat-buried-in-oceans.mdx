Scientists say the planet is holding a record amount of extra heat, most of which has gone into the oceans. That makes seas warmer, storms stronger, and sea level rise faster. Even though only a small part of the heat warms the air, it’s enough to make summers hotter and more dangerous. The world’s biggest climate deal, the Paris agreement, is now almost impossible to keep, and new heatwaves are coming.
