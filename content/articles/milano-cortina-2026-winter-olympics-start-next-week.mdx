The 2026 Winter Olympics in Milan and Cortina d’Ampezzo start next week. Eight‑plus events in skiing, skating, hockey, curling and more will be held in existing venues to cut costs and protect the environment. Star athletes like Mikaela Shiffrin, Ilia Malinin and Arianna Fontana are set to compete, while Russia and Belarus will participate as neutral athletes. The Games also face politics, with concerns over ICE security staff and the climate‑change impact on winter sports.
