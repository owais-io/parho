Dollar General will pay at least $15 million after lawsuits said shoppers saw one price on the shelf but paid more at checkout. The settlement covers 20,000 stores in many states. Customers can get refunds if they prove overcharged. The company also agrees to hire staff to check prices and hire outside auditors. It wants to avoid more lawsuits and help people who were hurt by the price errors.
