Family members of the alleged Bondi beach gunman are under a temporary court order that hides their names and addresses to keep them safe. A public defender asked for a permanent order, but News Corp said it was unnecessary because their details were already published. The court will decide next week. The order protects the families’ mental and physical safety after the tragic 15‑person shooting.
