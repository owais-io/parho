A survivor of the 2023 Nottingham stabbings says a repeat could happen unless mental‑health services are fixed. Wayne Birkett, badly injured, blames the local NHS trust for mistakes in caring for the killer, Valdo Calocane, who was discharged before the attack. A 2024 report found many errors, and the trust has been told to make major changes. Birkett and others will meet the health secretary to demand action.
