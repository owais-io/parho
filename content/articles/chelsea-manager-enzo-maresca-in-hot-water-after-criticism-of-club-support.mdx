Enzo Maresca, Chelsea’s head coach, said he didn’t feel fully supported after a recent win, sparking confusion and rumours that he might leave the club. He has also hinted at possible interest from Manchester City, which has only added to the tension. Chelsea’s directors are tight on decision‑making power, so Maresca’s comments could hurt his job prospects. The club may review his position at season’s end.
