During Bahrain’s pre‑season test, Max Verstappen slammed the new energy‑management system, saying it feels like “Formula E on steroids” and makes the cars “anti‑racing.” Lewis Hamilton echoed his frustration, calling the rules too complex for fans. Mercedes set the fastest laps, but Red Bull’s new power unit shows promise. Teams differ on whether the changes hurt or help racing, and an FIA meeting will address a Mercedes loophole before Melbourne.
