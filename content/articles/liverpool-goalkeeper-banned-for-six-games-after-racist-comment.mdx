Rafaela Borggräfe, Liverpool’s goalkeeper, has been banned for six games by the Football Association after an investigation found she made a racist remark. She has already served five of those games and agreed to take an education course. The remark was heard by staff during pre‑season photo‑ops. Liverpool looked into it, and the club says they supported the FA. The ban starts after the Arsenal game, and she’ll be available for the next match.
