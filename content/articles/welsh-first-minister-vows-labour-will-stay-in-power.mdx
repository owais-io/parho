Welsh First Minister Eluned Morgan has promised to keep Labour in power in Wales, even as polls suggest rivals like Reform UK and Plaid Cymru could win more seats in next year's Senedd election. She said Labour has been the most successful democratic party for a century and will stay if it keeps delivering good policies. Morgan highlighted plans for a new nuclear plant, AI growth zones, and clean energy, and warned that a change in government would bring instability.
