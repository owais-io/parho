Jeff Bezos, who bought the Washington Post, fired more than 300 staff, including a reporter in Ukraine, just as the paper faces pressure from President Trump. The cuts cut sports, culture and foreign desks, sparking protests outside the building. Journalists and former staff say the moves hurt American democracy and let Trump silence critical coverage. The layoffs show how wealthy owners can threaten independent news.
