At this year’s Munich Security Conference, US envoy Marco Rubio warned that the U.S. would pull back if Europe followed what he sees as a “civilisation‑declining” path. European leaders – from Germany’s Merz to France’s Macron and Britain’s Starmer – said they want more independent defence and a larger role in Ukraine talks. The meeting highlighted growing U.S.–Europe tension over Trump’s policies and NATO’s future.
