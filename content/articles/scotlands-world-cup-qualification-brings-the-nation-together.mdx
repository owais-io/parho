Scotland’s men’s football team won 4‑2 against Denmark, qualifying for the World Cup for the first time in 25 years. The final goal, scored by Kenny McLean in injury time, sparked huge celebrations across the country. Fans, young and old, felt united and hopeful, and the victory has boosted the economy and inspired more girls and boys to play football. Even ordinary people, who weren’t big fans before, are now talking about the game and feeling proud.
