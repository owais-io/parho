Democratic Rep. Ro Khanna and Republican Rep. Thomas Massie wrote to a federal judge, asking him to name a special master who would compel the Justice Department to hand over every file about Jeffrey Epstein, as required by the Epstein Files Transparency Act. They say the department has only released less than 1% of the documents and failed to meet deadlines, which could harm survivors and delay justice. The lawmakers want an independent monitor to ensure full, timely disclosure.
