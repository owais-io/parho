Benjy Taylor, the coach of Tuskegee’s men’s basketball team, was handcuffed by police after a game against Morehouse College. The incident happened when players from Morehouse’s football team joined the handshake line, which Tuskegee officials said was a security breach. Taylor tried to keep the situation safe and was taken off the court. He feels humiliated and is looking at a lawsuit.
