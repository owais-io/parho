Author Mark Cocker shares how a spring of snowdrops has filled his surroundings, from road verges to churchyards. He talks about a patch he planted from bulbs his mother grew, now blooming on his property. The flowers remind him of his mother’s love for gardening and his own family ties. He also links the blooming to science, noting that even hidden infrared light helps plants and people stay healthy.
