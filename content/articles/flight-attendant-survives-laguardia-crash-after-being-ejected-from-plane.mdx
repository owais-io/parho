A flight attendant survived a deadly crash at LaGuardia where a Jet collided with a fire truck. She was ejected more than 100 meters from the plane but still strapped to her seat. Both pilots died, and nine people were injured. The incident shows serious runway safety issues and sparked an investigation into air traffic control mistakes. The Canadian flight attendant has been praised as a miracle survivor.
