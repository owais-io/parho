The UK government plans to replace Lifetime ISAs with a new account aimed only at first‑time home buyers. Self‑employed people, who use Lifetime ISAs as a simple retirement saver, worry they’ll lose the 25% government bonus and have fewer options. While current accounts can still be opened, the future is unclear, leaving many self‑employed workers uneasy about their long‑term savings.
