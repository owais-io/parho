Actress Elizabeth Hurley has gone to court against the Daily Mail publisher, accusing it of putting microphones on her windowsill and tapping her home phone lines to gather information for stories. She is joined by other celebrities such as the Duke of Sussex and Elton John in a lawsuit that challenges the newspaper’s investigative methods. The publisher denies the allegations and says the claims are unsubstantiated. The trial is ongoing.
