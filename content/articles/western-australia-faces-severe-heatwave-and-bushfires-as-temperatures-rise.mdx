Western Australia is in the middle of a big heatwave, with temperatures over 40 °C. The heat has sparked bushfires that are destroying farms and towns. The Australian Bureau of Meteorology has warned that the heat will keep going for several more days. The hot weather is also affecting other parts of the world – Brazil’s power grid was hit by storms, California’s Central Valley is shrouded in fog, and a storm in Spain brought heavy rain and even snow to the Canary Islands.
