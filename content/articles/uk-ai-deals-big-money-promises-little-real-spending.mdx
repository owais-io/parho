A Guardian probe found that big UK AI deals backed by Nvidia firms Nscale and CoreWeave are more hype than help. Promised billions in new data centres and a supercomputer in Loughton are still just scaffolding or existing sites with imported chips. The government has no way to check the money, and the “investments” are often just chips moved into the UK to rent out. Shareholders even saw huge 350,000% returns on early shares.
