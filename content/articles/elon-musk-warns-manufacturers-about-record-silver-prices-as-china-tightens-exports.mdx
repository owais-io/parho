Silver prices hit a new record last month, jumping from $56 to $79 per ounce. Elon Musk said this hurts factories because silver is key for batteries, solar panels, cars and data centres. China will limit silver exports starting Jan 1, raising worries about supply. The price rise is driven by hopes of U.S. rate cuts and strong demand from investors and industry, pushing metals to record highs.
