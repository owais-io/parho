The AFL State of Origin return was a big success. Fans saw star players, high skill and real intensity. The game was heavily promoted and filled with top talent. But players still got hurt – Tom Green tore a knee, Jacob Weitering suffered a neck injury, and others risked more. The injuries make the future of the event uncertain, and the league must decide how to protect players.
