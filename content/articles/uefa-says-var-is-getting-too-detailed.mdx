UEFA’s top referee official, Roberto Rosetti, says the video assistant referee system is becoming too detailed and overused. He warns that slow‑motion replay and tiny mistakes are being scrutinised too closely, taking away from the game's flow. Rosetti says media pressure and fans want more intervention, but the technology should focus on clear, factual errors. He urges a return to the original purpose of VAR.
