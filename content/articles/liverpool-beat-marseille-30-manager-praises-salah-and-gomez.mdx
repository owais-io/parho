Arne Slot, Liverpool's manager, was delighted after his team beat Marseille 3‑0 in the Champions League. The win moved Liverpool to fourth in the group and keeps them in touch with the top spot. Slot highlighted Mohamed Salah for his professionalism and Joe Gomez for an outstanding performance after injuries. Salah played the whole match, returning from the Africa Cup of Nations, while Gomez's defensive work helped secure the victory.
