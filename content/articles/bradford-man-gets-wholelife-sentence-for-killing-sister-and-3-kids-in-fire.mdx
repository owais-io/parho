Sharaz Ali, 40, set fire to his ex‑partner’s sister Bryonie Gawith’s house in Bradford, killing her and her three children. He was driven by jealousy, drugs and alcohol, and told the court that the children were collateral damage. The judge gave him a whole‑life order, meaning he will never be released. His accomplice, Calum Sunderland, received life with a minimum of 18 years. Bryonie was praised for staying with her kids.
