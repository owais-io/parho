The Albanese government is under pressure to help Australians who are struggling to afford higher petrol prices, just as New Zealand will give almost 150,000 families a weekly cash payment to cover fuel costs. Critics say the Australian PM should consider similar cash relief, or offer free public transport and working‑from‑home options. Some lawmakers suggest an export tax on gas to fund aid, while welfare‑compliance rules are being questioned for adding extra costs.
