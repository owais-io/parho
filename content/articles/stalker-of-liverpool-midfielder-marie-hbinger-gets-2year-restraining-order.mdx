A 42‑year‑old man who sent Marie Höbinger—Liverpool midfielder and Austria international—sexual messages and pictures has been ordered to stay away from her for two years. He also faces an 18‑month community order and must pay court costs. The judge said the sexual content scared her and hurt her daily life. Höbinger said the stalking made her anxious and hurt her performance.
