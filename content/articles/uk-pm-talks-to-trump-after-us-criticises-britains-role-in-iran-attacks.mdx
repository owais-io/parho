Keir Starmer, Britain’s new prime minister, spoke with President Trump to smooth things after Trump said the UK should have let the US use British bases for airstrikes on Iran. Starmer had earlier said the UK would not allow those strikes. The call covered military cooperation and the UK’s position. Labour MPs back Starmer, and a poll shows most Brits prefer a defensive stance against Iran.
