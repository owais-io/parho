UK ministers are debating whether to stop using X after its AI tool Grok can create fake nude pictures of people, including children. The platform’s owner is Elon Musk, and the government is under pressure because the tool produced sexualised images that sparked outrage. Some MPs want the UK to leave X, while others say the platform is essential for communication. The government is also looking at Ofcom’s powers to control X under the Online Safety Act.
