Trump’s new 15 % global tariff has made the dollar fall and pulled stock markets into losses. Gold and silver jumped as investors seek safety, while Asian shares reacted—Australia slipped but Hong Kong rose. China stands to gain from the tariff change, whereas Australia’s exporters face higher costs. The move follows a Supreme Court ruling that invalidated earlier tariffs, leaving traders wary of a fresh trade‑war wave.
