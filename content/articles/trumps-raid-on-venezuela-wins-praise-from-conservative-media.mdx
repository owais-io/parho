After saying he’d stop wars, Trump’s team launched a raid that seized Venezuelan President Maduro. Most conservative TV and radio hosts cheered the move, calling it a win for America. A few pundits warned it could be a regime change and questioned the focus on oil. Editorials were split: some praised the raid, others criticized Trump for acting like a mercenary. Supporters still back Trump, while criticism is limited.
