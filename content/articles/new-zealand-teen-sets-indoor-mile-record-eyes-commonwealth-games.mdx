Sam Ruthe, a 16‑year‑old runner from New Zealand, broke the world record for an under‑18 indoor mile with a time of 3:48.88 at Boston University. The win also eclipsed the New Zealand national mile record. Sam already became the youngest person to run a sub‑four‑minute mile at age 15 and has held all New Zealand U‑20 middle‑distance records. He aims for Commonwealth Games selection and hopes to keep improving, though he says records aren’t his main goal.
