A 68‑year‑old ex‑police informant, Nick Gratwick, was sentenced to life imprisonment after being found guilty of 38 paedophile offences, including arranging child rapes in the UK and abroad. The judge called his crimes “utterly depraved.” Gratwick had also worked for police in the 1990s and 2000s, spying on environmental and animal‑rights groups while secretly helping police. His dual role was revealed during the trial.
