The Guardian letter argues that Britain should end its support for the US‑Israel military campaign against Iran. The writer says the war is illegal, causing huge loss of life and economic chaos worldwide. It also warns that using British bases lets the US carry out attacks that risk a wider conflict. The author urges the UK government to withdraw any participation and call for a ceasefire.
