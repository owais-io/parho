Jessica Plichta, a 22‑year‑old preschool teacher, was arrested after giving a live TV interview about a Grand Rapids protest against the Trump administration’s actions in Venezuela. She says the arrest was because she recently visited Caracas during the US blockade and spoke with Venezuelan officials. Police charged her with a misdemeanor, and another activist says local officers often target protesters. Plichta remains determined to keep speaking out.
