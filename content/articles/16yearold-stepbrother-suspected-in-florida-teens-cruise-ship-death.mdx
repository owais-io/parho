A 16‑year‑old boy is now a suspect in the death of his 18‑year‑old step‑sister, Anna Kepner, who was found dead under a bed on the Carnival Horizon cruise ship. Investigators say she died from asphyxiation after a bar hold, and she had bruises on her neck. The case is being studied by the FBI and Carnival, while the family is in court over custody.
