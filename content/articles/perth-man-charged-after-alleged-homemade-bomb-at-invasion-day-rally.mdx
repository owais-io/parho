A Noongar elder, Herbert Bropho, was speaking at an Invasion Day rally in Perth when a homemade bomb was thrown onto the stage. Police found a device, evacuated the crowd, and arrested a 31‑year‑old man from Warwick who was charged with intent to harm and making explosives. The man was denied bail and will court the same day. Despite the scare, the rally went on peacefully.
