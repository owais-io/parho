In the Gorton and Denton byelection, the Green Party won a surprising win, beating Reform UK and Labour. Voters rejected far‑right rhetoric and praised the Greens' focus on public services and climate. The result shows the Green Party’s membership boom and strong grassroots campaigning. It also hurts Labour, which lost many supporters, and signals a challenge for the Conservatives ahead of the next general election.
