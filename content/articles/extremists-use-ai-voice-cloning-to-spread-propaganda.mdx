Extremists are using AI to copy voices and translate propaganda, making it easier to spread their messages worldwide. Neo‑Nazi groups have produced English versions of Hitler’s speeches that get millions of views. ISIS and other jihadists use AI to turn Arabic text into English audio, and both sides use ChatGPT for images and planning. Law‑enforcement warns that these tools make extremist content more persuasive and harder to stop.
