Scientists studying the lone dolphin, Mimmo, in Venice’s lagoon say it’s humans, not the animal, that need to be better managed. They’ve tracked the dolphin’s movements since June, noting it’s healthy and adapting well. But the biggest threat comes from boats that can harm it with their speed and propellers. The team urges stricter speed limits and safe distances to protect Mimmo and other marine life.
