Tottenham have agreed to pay up to €40m (about £34.6m) to buy midfielder Conor Gallagher from Atlético Madrid. The 25‑year‑old, who has only made a few appearances in Spain, wants to return to the Premier League to increase his chances of making England’s World Cup squad. Spurs needed new midfielders after injured Rodrigo Bentancur. Aston Villa had also been trying to sign Gallagher but the Spanish club preferred a sale.
