Sam Forstag, a Montana smokejumper who lost his federal job when 300 Forest Service workers were cut, is running for Congress to unseat Republican Ryan Zinke. He says the cuts proved how easily workers are dismissed and will focus on jobs, public‑land protection, affordable housing and healthcare. With Democrats eyeing the seat, Forstag wants to bring working‑class issues back into Washington.
