Swiss academic Tariq Ramadan, 63, former Oxford professor, goes on trial in Paris for allegedly raping three women between 2009 and 2016. He denies the charges, having changed his story over the years. If found guilty, he could receive up to 20 years in prison. The case, part of the broader #MeToo movement, follows a 2024 Swiss conviction for a 2008 rape. Ramadan’s lawyers worry about his health and fair trial rights.
