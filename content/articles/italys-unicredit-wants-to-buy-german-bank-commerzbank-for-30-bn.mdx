Italy’s UniCredit has increased its stake in German lender Commerzbank to almost 30% and now wants to make a €30 bn offer for the rest of the shares. German law says a 30%+ holder must launch a takeover bid, but the German government and trade unions warn the deal could hurt jobs and national control. UniCredit plans to seek shareholder approval in May.
