Mayor Zohran Mamdani turned Manhattan’s Surrogate’s Court into a soccer hub for the Africa Cup of Nations. Fans gathered to cheer their teams, with moments of excitement and a little chaos when a fan sprinted to a screen to see a penalty miss. The event was part of the mayor’s plan to host free watch parties for big football matches, hoping to bring New Yorkers of African descent together in celebration.
