The author loves outdoor activities but hates skiing. She argues it’s a pricey hobby that forces you to buy heavy gear, pay high resort fees and fly long distances. Skiing also carries real injury risk, with many skiers returning home with knee or ligament problems. Comfort is low – cold, windburnt, pricey food – and après‑ski parties are noisy and exhausting. In short, she says skiing is a costly, risky, and uncomfortable pastime.
