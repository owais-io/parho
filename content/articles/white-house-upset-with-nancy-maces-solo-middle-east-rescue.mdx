White House officials are frustrated with Republican congresswoman Nancy Mace, who is leading her own rescue missions for Americans stuck in the Middle East. Mace flew to the region, talked to Saudi officials, and tried to use a Saudi plane for 300 people without telling the State Department. The administration says she’s complicating coordinated evacuations and using the situation for politics. The State Department has been running official flights to bring Americans home.
