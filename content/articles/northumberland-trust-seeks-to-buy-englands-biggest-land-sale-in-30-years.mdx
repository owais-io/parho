Northumberland Wildlife Trust wants to buy the 3,800‑hectare Rothbury estate, the largest piece of land up for sale in England in 30 years. The trust hopes to turn the land into a wildlife corridor that links coast to Scotland, restoring bogs and creating space for animals like beavers, pine martens and even bison. They need £30 million, have raised a third, and rely on local farmers and volunteers for support.
