Police have named the last 16 people who died in the New Year’s Eve bar fire at Crans‑Montana. The blaze, caused by candles too close to the ceiling, left 40 bodies and 119 injured, many needing burn care in hospitals across Europe. Authorities are investigating the bar managers for negligence. Switzerland will observe a national day of mourning, and the victims’ families are gathering for a memorial.
