Morocco, the host nation, won the quarter‑final 2‑0 against Cameroon. The hosts kept their nerves, scoring first with Brahim Díaz after a corner and adding a second from Ismaila Sarr. Coach Walid Regragui stayed calm amid earlier criticism, and the team used the loud stadium to press hard. Cameroon, who had been underestimated, fell to Morocco’s quick attack, leaving Morocco headed to the semi‑finals.
