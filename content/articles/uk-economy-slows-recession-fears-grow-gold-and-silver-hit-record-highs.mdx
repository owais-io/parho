UK growth slowed sharply this year, with Q2 GDP falling to 0.2% and Q3 to 0.1%, sparking fears of a recession. Household savings are still high but have dropped, and debt levels are near record lows, showing cautious spending. Auto output fell after a cyber‑attack at Jaguar Land Rover, and overall services growth was offset by lower production. Meanwhile, gold and silver hit record highs as investors chase safe‑haven assets amid global tensions.
