On Wednesday, over 2,000 ABC staff walked off the job for the first time in 20 years, demanding better working conditions. The national broadcaster’s TV news became a live feed of BBC News, while radio stations played music and re‑runs. Staff explained their strike was about secure, sustainable work and support from unions. The 24‑hour action showed the power of workers and kept audiences watching Parliament sessions uninterrupted.
