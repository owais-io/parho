Author Arwa Mahdawi spent a month trying to keep a constant smile like New York Mayor Zohran Mamdani. She explains how smiling can calm nerves but is hard for her. The article discusses research that shows smiling can slightly lift mood, and how people of color and women are pressured to smile and hide anger. She uses her experience to show the relief and discomfort of forced smiles.
