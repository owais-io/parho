Drax, Britain's biggest power plant, says its biomass fuel comes from waste wood, but insiders said the company might have used old‑growth forest wood instead. After a BBC documentary accused Drax of cutting old trees, the boss denied the claims and said they had evidence of sawmill waste. Court papers show senior staff privately warned that the evidence was weak. Drax has faced investigations and paid £25 m for data problems.
