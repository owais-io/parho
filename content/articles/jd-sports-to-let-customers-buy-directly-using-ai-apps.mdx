JD Sports will let shoppers buy its products with a single click using AI chat apps like ChatGPT and Microsoft Copilot. The move will launch in the U.S. soon and could spread worldwide. The company says many teens and young adults already use AI to find deals. Other retailers are also adding AI features, and Google is testing similar shopping bots. JD will announce results after the Christmas sales.
