Robert F. Kennedy Jr. announced a $100 million grant program to help people who are homeless and struggling with addiction. The money will go to eight cities, where the Department of Health and Human Services will run a new “Streets” project. It will move people from crisis to treatment, then to housing and jobs. The goal is to break the cycle of homelessness, addiction and emergency care.
