The final of the new Nations Championship, a 12‑team rugby competition that runs every two years, will be played at Twickenham in late November. The biggest seats for the finals cost £280, with a discount for multi‑day tickets. Fans can buy tickets for two matches, but not a single game. Prices range from £60 for Friday to £279.90 for Sunday’s finals, and there’s a resale market for swapping. It begins with 6‑vs‑6 on Friday and ends with 1‑vs‑1 on Sunday.
