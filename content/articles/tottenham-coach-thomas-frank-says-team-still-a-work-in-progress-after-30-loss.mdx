Tottenham lost 3‑0 to Nottingham Forest, and coach Thomas Frank said the side are still a work in progress and fixing things won’t be quick. Spurs sit in the middle of the Premier League and are just six points from Chelsea. Frank talked about the goalkeeper’s mistake, the defender Djed Spence’s reaction when he was subbed, and said he will look into it. He stresses the need for patience and steady improvement.
