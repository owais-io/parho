After the U.S. and Israel launched airstrikes on Iran, Russia has earned about €6 billion from selling oil, gas and coal. Most of the money comes from oil, as prices jumped 14 % over February. The figures arrive just as President Trump hinted at easing sanctions, which could let Russia sell at higher prices. The cash will help fund Russia’s war in Ukraine. The International Energy Agency says the war has cut Gulf oil output, widening global supply gaps.
