Trump accuses Colombia’s president Gustavo Petro of being linked to drug trafficking and threatens military action. Petro responds by rallying supporters and calling for a phone call, which calms the tension. The US has slapped sanctions, tariffs, and even revoked Petro’s visa. Petro’s government has seized tons of cocaine, but there is no proof he’s involved. The dispute shows how Trump’s hostility can stir political support in Colombia.
