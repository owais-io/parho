Wiegman says she chose the right goalkeeper for England. After England’s star keeper Mary Ear was dropped and Hannah Hampton took the spot, Ear told critics that Wiegman was unfair. Wiegman replied that she “makes decisions to win”, that she loved working with Ear but the team needed a different style. She said the choice helped England reach the Euro final. Ear now retired, and Hampton is injured for the next matches.
