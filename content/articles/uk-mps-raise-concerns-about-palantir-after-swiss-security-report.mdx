UK MPs are worried about the government's use of Palantir, a U.S. data‑software company, after a Swiss report raised doubts about data security. The report said that because Palantir is American, the U.S. government could see sensitive data shared with it. MPs called for clearer checks and more transparency. Palantir denied the claims and said it protects customer data. The debate has also spurred attention in Germany and other European countries.
