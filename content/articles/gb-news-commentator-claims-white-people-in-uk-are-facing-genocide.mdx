A GB News host said white people in the UK are being “genocided” by immigrants, sparking 24 complaints to the media regulator. Ofcom has not yet decided to investigate, but the comments drew criticism for being hateful and unchallenged. GB News says the remarks were the presenter’s own views, not the channel’s. The case highlights tensions around free speech and race‑related broadcasting.
