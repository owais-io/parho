After ICE officer Renee Good was killed, small groups called Visibility Brigades set up huge signs on bridges and overpasses across the U.S. They use short, shocking messages drivers can read in seconds. These quick, local actions are part of a network of over 250 brigades that aim to keep people aware of issues and push back against Trump‑era policies. They keep protests easy to repeat and spread their reach beyond their town.
