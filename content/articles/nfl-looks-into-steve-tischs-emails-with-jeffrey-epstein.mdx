The NFL is checking links between Giants co‑owner Steve Tisch and convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein after more than 400 emails appeared. Tisch says he only had brief exchanges about women, movies and charity and denies any wrongdoing. The league could still punish him under its personal‑conduct rules, even without a criminal case. Commissioner Roger Goodell said the NFL will review the facts before deciding on discipline.
