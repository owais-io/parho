Harvey is a five‑year‑old deaf‑blind child who has spent months fighting for an education plan that gives him extra help. He struggled in a busy nursery, missed schooling, and his mother had to quit her job to care for him. Now the government plans to change the special‑needs system and cut funding. Harvey’s family fears that the new rules might remove the support they’ve worked hard to secure.
