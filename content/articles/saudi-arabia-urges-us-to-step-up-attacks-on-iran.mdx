Saudi Arabia has urged the United States to increase attacks on Iran, according to a Saudi intelligence source. Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman told President Trump that the U.S.–Israeli campaign should not be shortened and should be intensified. Trump confirmed the crown prince’s role, saying the prince is a “warrior” working with the U.S. No Saudi troops are involved yet, but a political analyst says Riyadh might join if diplomatic efforts stall.
