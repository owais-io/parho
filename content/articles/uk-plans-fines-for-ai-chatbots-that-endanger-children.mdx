UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer announced new rules that could fine or block AI chatbots if they put children at risk. The move follows a scandal where a chatbot made sexualised images of real people, sparking outrage. The government wants all chatbots to follow the Online Safety Act, and will punish companies with up to 10% of global revenue. They also plan to tighten social‑media rules for under‑16s.
