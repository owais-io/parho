Senator Cory Booker slammed both Democrats and Republicans for letting President Trump use congressional war powers without enough oversight. He said Trump’s strikes in Nigeria, Venezuela and especially the Iran conflict have threatened world oil supplies, closed the Strait of Hormuz, and killed 13 U.S. soldiers. Booker urged Congress to stop the military campaign, warning that unchecked presidential action could lead to attacks on Cuba or North Korea. He called the parties’ inaction “feckless.”
