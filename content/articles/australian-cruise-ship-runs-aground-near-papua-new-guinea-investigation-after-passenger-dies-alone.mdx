The Coral Adventurer, a 120‑seat cruise ship, ran aground about 30 km from Lae, Papua New Guinea, on Saturday. No one was hurt, but the vessel is under inspection. The ship is linked to a separate investigation after an 80‑year‑old passenger, Suzanne Rees, died alone on Lizard Island during a stop‑over on the trip. The cruise line cancelled the rest of the voyage, refunded passengers, and is cooperating with authorities.
