MPs in a Home Affairs report slammed the government for a delayed and ineffective response to a ban on Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a match in Birmingham. West Midlands police used faulty, AI‑based intel and ignored better checks, the report said. The ban hurt trust in the Jewish community, led to the chief constable's resignation and calls to stop politicians in safety panels.
