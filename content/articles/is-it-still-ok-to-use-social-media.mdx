UK Labour ministers used X to defend the PM amid scandals, showing how politicians rely on the platform. But X has become a hub for sexualised images, deepfakes, and far‑right posts, while other sites like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and even Substack host hate‑speech and extremist content. Social media is addictive and a lifeline for many, yet it also spreads abuse and misinformation. The article asks whether it’s still ethical to keep using these sites, even as they help or harm us.
