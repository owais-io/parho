Trump has expanded the “global gag rule” to cut US foreign aid to any NGO that supports diversity, equity and inclusion (DEI) or trans‑rights programs. The new policy could take away about $30 billion in aid, affecting both international NGOs and U.S. groups working abroad. It follows a history of similar rules that shut down family‑planning services, and it is expected to hit health and human‑rights work worldwide.
