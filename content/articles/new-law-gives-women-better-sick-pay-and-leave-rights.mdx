Starting in April, a new Employment Rights Act will give almost 4.8 million women stronger sick pay and new parental leave rules. Many low‑paid women will get statutory sick pay for the first time, and fathers and partners can start paternity leave right away. The law also lets parents share up to 50 weeks of leave and improves protection for pregnant and menopausal workers. The changes aim to reduce pay gaps and help families.
