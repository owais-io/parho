Kemi Badenoch met Speaker Mike Johnson to push back on the Chagos Islands deal and North Sea oil. Johnson called President Trump, who then posted a harsh tweet attacking the Chagos agreement. UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer responded publicly, saying Trump is pressuring Britain to hand over Chagos to Mauritius. The exchange shows rising tension between the UK and the US over this long‑standing military‑base deal.
