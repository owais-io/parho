Author Sally Rooney has called on the British government to look after six prisoners who are on hunger strike because they say they’re being mistreated. The men and women, linked to the group Palestine Action, say they’re denied mail, kept in solitary and have not yet been tried. Rooney warns they are losing weight and health, and asks for better prison conditions, bail and the group’s ban to be lifted.
