Harry Brook hit a quick 36 in just 12 balls to help England chase a revised target in their second T20 match against Sri Lanka. The win gave England a 2‑0 series advantage and a confidence boost ahead of the World Cup. Tom Banton also scored 54 and Sam Curran finished the chase. England’s win shows the team’s strong batting lineup, with Brook now a key contender for the World Cup squad.
