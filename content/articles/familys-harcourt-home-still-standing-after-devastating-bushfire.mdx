Peter and Lynne Suelzle went back to Harcourt to find their house still standing, but many neighbors had lost their homes. The bushfire, which started near Fogarty’s Gap Road, blew through the town, burning about 50 houses and destroying power, water and radio services. The community stayed on guard, with some families evacuating and others staying to protect their property and animals. Emergency workers struggled to control the fast‑moving fire.
