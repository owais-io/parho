At a Gorton and Denton hustings in Greater Manchester, Labour’s Angeliki Stogia accused Reform’s Matt Goodwin of making women afraid with his rhetoric. Goodwin, who arrives with security, countered that the threat comes from Labour’s open‑border policy. Both sides shouted accusations, with Goodwin claiming life threats and Stogia citing women’s fear. Green, Liberal Democrat, and Conservative candidates joined, but the main fight was over women’s safety and alleged extremist talk.
