UK regulator Ofcom has started an investigation into X after a flood of AI‑generated sexual images of women and children appeared. Politicians say this is a key test of whether big tech can be held to democratic rules. The government will ban non‑consensual intimate images and outlaw nudification apps, and other countries are tightening rules on AI deepfakes. The focus is now on how quickly regulators can act.
