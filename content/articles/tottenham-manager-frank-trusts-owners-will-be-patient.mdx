After a 2‑1 loss to Fulham, Thomas Frank said the club’s new owners are patient and will give him time to build success. He criticised fans who booed keeper Guglielmo Vicario and said such behaviour hurt the team. Frank backed player Pedro Porro after the defender’s angry exit and urged supporters to stay united. He remains confident the owners are “intelligent” and that the team must keep playing well to win future games.
