The parents of two 19‑year‑old Melbourne girls who died after drinking methanol‑contaminated alcohol in Laos are outraged that the hostel staff who served the drinks were fined only $185. The girls died in November 2024, along with other tourists, after a night out at a Vang Vieng backpacker hostel. Families said they were kept out of the loop and criticised Australia’s handling of the case.
