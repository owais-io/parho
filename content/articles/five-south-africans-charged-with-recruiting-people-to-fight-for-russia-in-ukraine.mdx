Five South Africans were taken to court after police said they helped recruit friends to fight for Russia in Ukraine. The suspects were caught at Johannesburg’s OR Tambo airport while trying to travel to Russia. A woman was accused of arranging the trips, and police say the men were tricked into thinking they were training as bodyguards. The case is linked to a former president’s family, and the men are being held in custody until a bail hearing in December.
