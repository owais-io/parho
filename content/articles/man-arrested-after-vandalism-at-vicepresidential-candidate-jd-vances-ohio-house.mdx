A man was taken into custody in Cincinnati after breaking windows at the home of U.S. Vice‑Presidential candidate JD Vance. The Secret Service and city police found him trying to break in and had also damaged a protective vehicle. Vance was not at the house when the attack happened. Authorities are reviewing the case, and it comes amid a wave of increased security for politicians after recent violent attacks on public officials.
