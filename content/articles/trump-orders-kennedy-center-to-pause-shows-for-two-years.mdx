On Truth Social, President Donald Trump said the Kennedy Center will stop all entertainment events for about two years while it undergoes renovations. His move follows a big board shake‑up he led in February, which has sparked backlash from artists and board members. Performers like *Hamilton*, Issa Rae, and Ben Folds have pulled shows, and ticket sales have dropped sharply. The center is also being renamed “Trump‑Kennedy” in a controversial bid to rebrand the historic venue.
