Michael Carrick, who stepped in as Manchester United's temporary coach, praised his team's 2-0 win over Manchester City in the derby. He says the challenge is keeping that level of play for the remaining games, especially with only a chance to qualify for Europe left. Carrick wants the squad to stay calm and consistent, not repeat the emotional highs of the win. He will next face Arsenal.
