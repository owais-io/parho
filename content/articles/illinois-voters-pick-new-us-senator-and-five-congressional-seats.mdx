On Tuesday, Illinois voters will choose a new U.S. senator and fill five open congressional seats. The Senate race has ten Democratic candidates, including U.S. Rep. Raja Krishnamoorthi, Rep. Robin Kelly, and Lt. Gov. Juliana Stratton. Heavy money flows from outside groups like AIPAC, and the state’s blue‑leaning districts mean the primary winners are likely to keep the seats for years. State races, including Gov. Pritzker’s re‑election, are on the same ballot.
