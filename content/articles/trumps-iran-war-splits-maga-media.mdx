Media voices like Megyn Kelly, Mark Levin, Tucker Carlson, and others are trading insults and accusations, dividing supporters over whether the war hurts America. Most MAGA voters back the war, but the conflict shows a growing split that could shake Trump’s base before the mid‑terms in 2024.
