Actor Hugh Grant and other celebrities have signed a letter urging UK leaders to ban social media for people under 16. They back amendment 94a, which would lift the age limit and give parents more control. A poll shows 93% of parents think social media harms kids. Parliament will debate the ban next week, while the government plans a consultation on screen‑time guidelines.
