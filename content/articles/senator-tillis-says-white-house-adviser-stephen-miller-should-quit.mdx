North Carolina Senator Thom Tillis told CNN that White House adviser Stephen Miller should leave the Trump administration. He said Miller's focus on style over substance and his heavy influence over cabinet decisions are problems. Tillis also praised DHS nominee Markwayne Mullin as someone who could be free from Miller’s influence. The comment reflects growing criticism of Miller’s role in shaping the president’s policies.
