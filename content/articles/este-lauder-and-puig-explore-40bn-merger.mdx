Estée Lauder is negotiating a possible $40 billion merger with Spanish group Puig, owner of brands like Jean Paul Gaultier. Both companies confirmed talks but gave no details. Puig’s shares jumped 14% after the news, while Estée Lauder shares fell about 8% because of worries about a large deal during a turnaround. The merger could combine their fragrance and skincare strengths, helping both grow amid slowing consumer spending.
