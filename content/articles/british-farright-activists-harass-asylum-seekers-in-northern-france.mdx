British far‑right activists are attacking and intimidating asylum seekers living near Dunkirk, France. They paint hate‑filled graffiti, vandalise water tanks, and use drones and torches to scare people trying to cross the Channel. Groups like Raise the Colours and UKIP send volunteers in bright‑army gear, livestreaming the harassment. France has banned ten activists, but the groups keep recruiting thousands. The UK government says it has stopped many crossings, but the problem continues.
