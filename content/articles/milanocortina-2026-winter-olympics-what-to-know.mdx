The 2026 Winter Olympics will be held in Milan and Cortina d’Ampezzo, Italy, marking the country’s first Games in twenty years. The event covers 16 sports and more than 110 gold medals, from alpine skiing to biathlon, figure skating, speed skating, ice hockey, and curling. A medal table ranks countries by gold medals first, then silver, then bronze. Star athletes include Mikaela Shiffrin, Ilia Malinin, Arianna Fontana, and Team GB’s hope for a figure‑skating medal.
