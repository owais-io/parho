The IOC president said Russia could compete at the 2028 Los Angeles Games, which is a big hint that the ban might end. She emphasised that sport should stay separate from politics, a view that worries Ukraine. Ukraine’s football officials are outraged because the war has killed many athletes. Russia’s football ban still stands, though its young teams can play as neutrals.
