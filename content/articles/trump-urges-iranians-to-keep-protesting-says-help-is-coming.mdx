Trump posted on Truth Social telling Iranian protesters to stay on the streets and said “help is on its way.” He said he’d stopped meetings with Iranian officials, warned that airstrikes are one of many options, and threatened a 25% tariff on any country still trading with Iran. The move follows reports of thousands killed in the crackdown and has drawn a European response that condemns the violence but wants to avoid U.S. military action.
