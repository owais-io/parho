Labour leader Keir Starmer is being urged not to block former mayor Andy Burnham from standing in a Gorton byelection. Angela Rayner and several MPs say the seat should be chosen by local members, not by party bosses. Burnham, who has said he could run for leadership, would have to resign as mayor. The selection process is tight – nominations open Friday and close Sunday. Other candidates like the Greens and independent George Galloway could also run.
