Australia’s new law blocks kids under 16 from using social media. Some parents say the ban helps cut out time‑wasting and protects mental health, while others worry kids lose a useful way to stay in touch. After the ban went live, Guardian asked readers how it’s working out. More than a hundred answered, and the article shares 20 stories that show both the benefits and the problems families are feeling.
