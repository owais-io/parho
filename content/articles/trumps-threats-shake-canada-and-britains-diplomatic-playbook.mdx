Donald Trump’s foreign moves have rattled Canada and the UK. Trump once threatened to annex Canada, prompting Canada’s prime minister to rally against U.S. bullying and push for a new multilateral order. Britain, a middle power, balances ties with the U.S. and the EU. Prime Minister Keir Starmer keeps a calm, diplomatic tone with Trump, but critics say this may not solve deeper global crises.
