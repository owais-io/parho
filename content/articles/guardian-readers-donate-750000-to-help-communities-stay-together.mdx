The Guardian’s Hope appeal has raised more than £750,000 from readers who want to help charities that unite people, fight racism and hate, and build stronger communities. Five groups – Citizens UK, the Linking Network, Locality, Hope Unlimited Charitable Trust, and Who Is Your Neighbour? – run projects from affordable housing and youth clubs to food banks. Recent stories show Liverpool residents standing up against a racist attack and Belfast’s Circle of Change bringing people together across faiths.
