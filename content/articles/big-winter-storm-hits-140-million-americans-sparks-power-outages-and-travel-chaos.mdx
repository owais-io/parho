Across the U.S., a big winter storm hit Saturday, bringing heavy snow, sleet, and freezing rain. More than 140 million people, about 40 % of the country, received warnings. The cold made roads icy, caused power cuts, and knocked out nearly 14,000 flights. Three people died in New York due to the weather. States and cities issued emergency alerts and urged residents to stay safe.
