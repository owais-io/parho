A Georgia judge set a $1 bond for Alexia Moore, 31, who faces a murder charge for supposedly ending a pregnancy with abortion pills after the fetus had a heartbeat. The judge said the charge was “extremely problematic” and the bond may lead to a reduction or dismissal. Moore was released after posting $2,001 ($1 for murder, $1,000 each for drug charges). The case follows Georgia’s 2019 law banning abortions after a heartbeat.
