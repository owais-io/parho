Obama said he backs the Minneapolis protesters who opposed ICE’s heavy‑handed tactics. He explained that ordinary citizens can shape policy and that the protests exposed federal overreach, including tear‑gas use and targeting families. Obama said the backlash may have led the Trump administration to scale back enforcement in Minnesota. He urged Americans to keep standing up for their values.
