Storm Regina hit the Canary Islands, bringing strong winds and 5‑6 metre waves that triggered a yellow rough‑sea warning. In Tenerife, snow above 1,500 metres closed mountain roads. Across the US, the winter saw both record snowfall in the east and a rare 41 °C heat wave in Texas, making it the second‑warmest winter on record. Scientists expect an El Niño to bring more rain to Florida later this year.
