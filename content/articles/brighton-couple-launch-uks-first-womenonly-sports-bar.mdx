Lucy and Pippa Tallant have turned an empty pub into Crossbar, Brighton’s first women‑only sports bar. The couple created a welcoming space where women can watch football, rugby and other sports without the usual rowdy crowds. Social media buzz turned their 17,000 followers into a crowd that sold out the opening night in just four minutes. The bar aims to be a safe, friendly venue for fans of women’s sport and beyond.
