Andy Burnham, the mayor of Greater Manchester, still wants to become an MP again. Labour leaders have said he cannot stand in the upcoming Gorton and Denton by‑election because they think it would waste resources. Burnham is angry and says he will keep looking for another seat in 2027. Some party members want him to run, but others think it would hurt Labour’s chances. The conflict shows how split the party is right now.
