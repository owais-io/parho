A 13‑year‑old British boy, Alfie Hallett, was stabbed to death in his home in Casais, Portugal, by the ex‑partner of his mother. The attacker also died after stabbing himself and a gas leak caused an explosion that injured a police officer. Police said the family had a history of domestic violence. Alfie's basketball team paid tribute on social media, and the UK Foreign Office offered support.
