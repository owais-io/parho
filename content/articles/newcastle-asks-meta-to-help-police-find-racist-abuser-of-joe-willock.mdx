Newcastle has asked Meta, Instagram’s parent company, to give police the data they need to find the person who sent racist messages and threats to midfielder Joe Willock. After the team won 2‑0 against Crystal Palace, Willock posted screenshots of the abuse. The club says it is the second time in a year that the 26‑year‑old has faced such hate. Police are investigating with the UK Football Policing Unit, and anti‑racism groups urge faster action.
