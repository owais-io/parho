During a tour of a Michigan Ford plant, former President Trump was heckled by a protester who shouted harsh words. A short video shows Trump giving the heckler a middle‑finger gesture in response. Trump’s team said he gave an “appropriate” reply, but the White House hasn’t confirmed the gesture. Ford said it was a proud event but wouldn’t comment on the incident. The clip comes as Trump faces pressure to release documents tied to Jeffrey Epstein.
