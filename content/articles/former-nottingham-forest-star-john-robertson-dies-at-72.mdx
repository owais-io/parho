John Robertson, a celebrated left‑winger for Nottingham Forest, passed away on Dec 25, 2025, at 72. He was a key player in Forest’s European Cup victories in 1979 and 1980 and helped the club win the First Division and two League Cups. After playing, he became a respected coach, working with Martin O’Neill at several clubs. He died after a long battle with Parkinson’s disease and is survived by his family.
