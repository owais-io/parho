In China, a new app called Are You Dead? (soon to be renamed) asks users to tap a button every day. If two days go by without a tap, the app sends a warning to a contact. The idea came after a woman died alone in Shanghai, sparking worry that others might disappear unseen. The app is popular among city dwellers who live alone, giving them a quick check‑in to feel safer and less isolated.
