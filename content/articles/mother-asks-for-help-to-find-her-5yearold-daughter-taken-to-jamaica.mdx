Tau, 5, was taken from London by her ex‑mother‑in‑law, who flew to Jamaica against court orders. Since February, mother Samar Rodriguez has been searching for her in the storm‑hit town of Black River. UK and Jamaican authorities say they’re helping, but delays and unclear rules make it hard. The case is under the Hague child‑abduction treaty. Anyone with info is urged to contact the UK High Commission or the social media account @FindTauJA.
