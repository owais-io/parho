Philip Rivers, the 44‑year‑old quarterback who had been out of the NFL for almost five years, came back for the Indianapolis Colts after injuries to their main players. He played against the Seattle Seahawks, completing 18 of 26 passes, throwing a touchdown, and keeping the game close. Even though the Colts lost 18‑16, Rivers proved that his experience can still help a team, a rare comeback in NFL history.
