After losing its long‑time manager, key forwards, and goalkeeper, Brentford faced a risky summer. They hired TV pundit Keith Andrews as head coach, sparking doubts because of his lack of coaching experience. But the team has surprised everyone: a 7th‑place league finish, strong wins over top clubs, and hopes of European football. Players like Igor Thiago and new signings are performing well, showing Brentford’s resilient structure.
