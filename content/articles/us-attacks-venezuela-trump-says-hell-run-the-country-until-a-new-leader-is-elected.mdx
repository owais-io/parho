The U.S. launched a surprise strike on Venezuela, seizing President Nicolás Maduro and his wife. President Trump announced the U.S. will “run” Venezuela until a new leader is chosen. The move sparked sharp criticism from Democrats, other countries, and international law experts who say it violates rules. Some U.S. oil firms said they’re ready to invest in Venezuela’s oil after the attack. The story has divided opinions across politics and nations.
