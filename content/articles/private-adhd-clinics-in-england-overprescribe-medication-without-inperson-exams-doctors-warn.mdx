Doctors say private ADHD clinics in England are prescribing strong stimulants through video calls without proper physical exams, putting kids at risk. Some children even have hidden heart problems that weren't checked. NHS Greater Manchester has now forced face‑to‑face checks before medication can be started. The move aims to stop unsafe, online‑only prescribing and protect young patients.
