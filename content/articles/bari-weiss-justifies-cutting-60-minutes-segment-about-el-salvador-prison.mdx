Bari Weiss, CBS News' editor‑in‑chief, stopped a 60 Minutes report that investigated alleged abuse at an El Salvador prison where Trump‑era officials had sent migrants. She said the move was to keep reporting fair and rebuild trust, not to favor any political side. Critics called it censorship, and the reporter said the cut was political. The episode was later posted online, revealing torture claims. The decision comes amid a tense feud between CBS, Trump, and the network’s parent company, Paramount.
