Juventus are moving closer to the Serie A title race after beating Roma 2‑1, thanks to a string of good games and new coach Spalletti’s confidence. Meanwhile, Roma’s new signings—Evan Ferguson, Leon Bailey and Kostas Tsimikas—haven’t impressed coach Gasperini, who is thinking of cutting their loan deals. The season feels unpredictable, with Fiorentina winning their first game and Juventus still having a chance to climb the table.
