Mike Huckabee, the U.S. ambassador to Israel, said in an interview that Jews have a biblical right to take a large part of the Middle East. His words upset many Muslim‑majority countries, who called the remarks dangerous and against international law. The U.S. embassy said Huckabee was taken out of context. The comments reveal a split in U.S. politics over how much support Israel should get.
