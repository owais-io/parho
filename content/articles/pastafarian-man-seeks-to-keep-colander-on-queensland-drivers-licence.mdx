A Pastafarian man in Queensland wants to keep his driver’s licence photo that shows him wearing a colander as religious headgear. The state says it was issued by mistake and asks him to give it back for a new photo. He argues it’s a right to wear his faith’s symbol, just like a hijab or turban. The government says Pastafarianism isn’t recognized as a religion for licences.
