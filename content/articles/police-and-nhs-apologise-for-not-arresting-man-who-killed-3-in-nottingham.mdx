Police forces and the NHS have issued apologies after an arrest warrant for Valdo Calocane, who killed three people in June 2023, was ignored for ten months. The public inquiry heard that the warrant was issued in September 2022 after he missed a court appearance for assault. Calocane, who has paranoid schizophrenia, was later shot at a factory, then killed two university students and a caretaker in Nottingham. Both police and NHS said the failures were a “serious mistake.”
