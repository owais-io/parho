The United Nations says it has no connection to a London‑based marketing site that sells International Women’s Day (IWD) merchandise and sets yearly themes. The site, run by Glenda Slingsby’s company, has partnered with big names like Sainsbury’s, Barclays and the London Eye. UK organisations used its themes, causing confusion. UN Women confirms it chooses its own themes each year and has no ties to the website.
