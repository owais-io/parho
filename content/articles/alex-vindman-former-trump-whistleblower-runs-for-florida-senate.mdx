Alex Vindman, the former Army officer who warned President Trump about Ukraine and was later fired, has announced he will run for the U.S. Senate seat in Florida. He says he can’t sit on the sidelines and wants to fight corruption, lower insurance costs and end the state’s chaotic politics. Though Florida leans Republican and Democrats lack a strong base, he believes voters want change and he has growing support.
