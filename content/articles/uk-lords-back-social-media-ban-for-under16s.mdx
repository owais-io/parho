The UK House of Lords voted 266‑141 to reject Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s plan for a public consultation on whether to ban social media for people under 16. Lords, led by former minister Lord Nash, say the government must set the minimum age to 16 immediately. The decision comes after a Los Angeles jury ordered Meta and YouTube to pay damages for harming a young woman’s mental health. Families in the chamber shared stories of loss linked to online addiction.
