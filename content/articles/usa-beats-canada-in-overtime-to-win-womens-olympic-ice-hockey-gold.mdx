In the women’s ice‑hockey final at the Beijing Olympics, the U.S. edged Canada in overtime and captured the gold medal. With two minutes left, captain Hilary Knight tied the game with a long‑range goal, sending it to a 3‑on‑3 shoot‑out. Megan Keller then scored the decisive winner. The victory ends Canada’s streak of seven straight losses to the U.S. in Olympic finals.
