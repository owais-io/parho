Hannah Spencer, a new Green MP from Gorton and Denton, used her first Commons speech to call for tolerance, inclusivity and more working‑class people in Parliament. She highlighted her plastering background and that children had dressed as “Hannah the plumber” for International Women’s Day. She praised her predecessor’s work on health inequality, warned about the mistreatment of veterans, trans people, Muslims and disabled people, and vowed to make hope “normal again”.
