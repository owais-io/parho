The French navy boarded a Russian oil tanker in the Mediterranean that was likely part of a fleet that helps Russia sell oil despite sanctions. The ship, named Grinch, was flying a false flag and was taken to a port for checks. The move is part of a wider effort by France, the US and other European countries to stop Russia from making money from oil sales to places like India and China. The Russian government called the action piracy.
