On Friday, thousands of people in New York City marched in the cold to protest the Trump administration’s mass deportations. They chanted against ICE, the KKK and fascism, and shouted “Abolish ICE!” The protest was part of a nationwide day of action calling for work, school and shopping boycotts. The demonstrators also spoke out about killings in Minneapolis by federal agents and called for accountability.
