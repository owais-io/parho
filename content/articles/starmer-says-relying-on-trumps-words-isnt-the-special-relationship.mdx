In a heated session of PMQs, Starmer told MPs that the UK won’t get pulled into a war over Iran unless there is a clear legal basis. He explained Britain’s defence support for the US in the Middle East—aircraft, drones and ground systems—while stressing the importance of evacuating British nationals from conflict zones. Starmer criticised opposition criticism and stressed that the “special relationship” means protecting UK and US lives, not just echoing Trump.
