Foreign school parents are being tricked by cyber‑criminals into sending tuition fees to scam accounts. A study of 100 independent schools found each school faced at least one attack a year, with an average loss of £3,200. Fraudsters send fake emails from bursars, sometimes offering discounts, and use stolen email addresses. Parents should double‑check any unusual payment requests and report theft immediately.
