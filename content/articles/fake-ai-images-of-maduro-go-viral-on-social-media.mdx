After President Trump said the U.S. would strike Venezuela, fake AI pictures and videos of Nicolás Maduro appeared online. They showed him handcuffed, being escorted by U.S. agents, and even fireworks in Caracas. The posts mixed with real clips of U.S. planes and explosions, making it hard to tell truth from lies. Fact‑checkers found 7 misleading clips that have now been viewed over 14 million times. Influencers and right‑wing figures shared the fakes, spreading misinformation.
