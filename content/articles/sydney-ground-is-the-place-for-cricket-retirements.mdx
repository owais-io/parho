Sydney Cricket Ground has long been the place where many cricketers say goodbye. Usman Khawaja announced that the upcoming Ashes Test will be his last international game. England’s season may also mark the end of the fast‑paced “Bazball” style that has dominated under Brendon McCullum. With Ben Stokes still captain, England looks to rebuild after losing the Ashes in just 11 days. The final Test could signal a new chapter for both teams.
