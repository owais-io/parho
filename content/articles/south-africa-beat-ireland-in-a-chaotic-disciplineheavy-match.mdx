The match was a nightmare of cards and chaos. Ireland lost five players in the first half, with several yellow cards turning into reds. South Africa’s discipline and powerful set‑pieces kept them in control. Despite a rough first half, the Boks pulled ahead, scoring from penalties and a try, and held on for a 24‑20 win. It was a dramatic game that highlighted both teams’ struggles with discipline.
