A California prosecutor said AI was used in a criminal case filing, which had mistakes called hallucinations. The error was quickly withdrawn. Lawyers for a defendant, Kyle Kjoller, claim similar AI errors in other cases, but appeals courts denied sanctions. The district attorney said the mistakes were partly human error, and the office is now training staff and setting an AI policy. The case could reach the California Supreme Court.
