Nigel Farage has once again denied accusations that he was racist or antisemitic when he was a schoolboy. He says the allegations were made by people who simply disliked his politics, not because he actually abused classmates. Former classmates, including Jewish students, say he used hateful language and threatened them. Farage insists he never said or did such things and has not apologized. The dispute continues.
