From Jan 1, US national parks added a $100 surcharge for people who are not U.S. residents. Visitors are now being asked for passports at entrances, causing long lines and some tourists turning away. Park staff say the change has caused chaos, added work, and may hurt tourism. Environmental groups say the fee is illegal and have sued the park service. The move also comes as park staff are already stretched thin.
