Lakemba Mosque receives a third threatening letter before Ramadan, sparking police investigations and a surge in security guards. Muslim leaders say this is the most serious threat since the Cronulla race riots. With the holy month beginning, thousands will gather for prayers and iftar, but fears of hate attacks rise amid recent anti‑Muslim incidents. Authorities promise visible police presence and Operation Shelter patrols to keep the community safe.
