City & Guilds, the UK training charity, was sold to an overseas firm called PeopleCert in October. After the sale, the chief executive and finance director each received bonuses worth about £1.7 million and £1.2 million, plus higher salaries. The move comes as the new owner plans to cut costs, reduce staff in the UK, and move some jobs overseas. The charity still owns the training business’s brand.
