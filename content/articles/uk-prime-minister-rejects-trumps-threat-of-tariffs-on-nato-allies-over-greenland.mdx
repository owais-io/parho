UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Trump’s threat to impose tariffs on NATO allies over Greenland was wrong. He spoke with other NATO leaders, stressing that security in the North is a shared priority. Trump had warned eight European countries, including the UK, that tariffs could start in February. Starmer will not retaliate but will keep the UK’s ties with the US and other allies strong, insisting the future of Greenland is for its people to decide.
