A recent Israeli attack on Iran’s South Pars gasfield has strained relations between Washington and Israel. President Trump claimed he had no advance knowledge of the bombing and warned Israel against further strikes. Israeli officials argue the U.S. was briefed beforehand. The clash has triggered retaliatory hits on Gulf energy sites and sparked debate over U.S. policy being driven by Israel’s actions. Some U.S. officials have resigned over the war.
