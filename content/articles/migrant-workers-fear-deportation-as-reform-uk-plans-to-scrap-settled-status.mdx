Reform UK is gaining support and plans to scrap indefinite leave to remain, raising the skill‑worker salary threshold to about £60,000. This could cost many migrants the right to stay permanently. Doctors fear the NHS will lose staff, while engineers, designers and refugees worry about jobs, homes and uncertainty. The new rules would make it harder to settle, creating fear and stress for those who have built lives in Britain.
