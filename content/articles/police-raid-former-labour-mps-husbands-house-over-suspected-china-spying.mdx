Police searched the home of James Robinson, husband of former Labour MP Gloria De Piero, as part of an investigation into a possible Chinese spying ring. Robinson, a former Labour aide, said he was not detained or questioned. He is the fourth Labour‑linked person named. The raid follows arrests of three ex‑Labour staffers suspected of helping a foreign intelligence service. No indication De Piero was involved.
