Australia’s growing data‑centre industry could push up power bills, strain water supplies and slow emissions cuts. With 260 centres already in Sydney and Melbourne, experts warn the sector may triple its grid use in five years, boosting wholesale prices and greenhouse gases. Governments are pushing for on‑site renewable power and closed‑loop cooling, but water authorities fear high demand for drinking water.
