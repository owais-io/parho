Liverpool manager Arne Slot said he will skip the FA Cup game against Barnsley because right‑back Conor Bradley needs knee surgery and will miss the rest of the season. Slot had changed most of the squad last season to rest players and again will use a mix of starters and a young bench. He stressed the FA Cup is still important and defended his squad rotation choices. Bradley’s injury could affect his World Cup hopes.
