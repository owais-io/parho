Renee Good, a 37‑year‑old mother of three and a poet, was shot and killed by federal immigration officers in Minneapolis. She had no criminal record and was not involved in protests. Friends and her university say she was a kind, creative woman who moved to Minnesota last year. The incident sparked outrage, with many calling the “domestic terrorist” label from the administration false.
