The Guardian Weekly highlights this edition’s focus on the US‑Iran conflict, showing how the Trump era’s exit plans collapsed. It features essays about Iran’s new leader, the impact on Iranians, and U.S. military figures, plus an emotional Iranian perspective on life in Tehran. Other pieces cover war losses in Russia’s Komi Republic, the fading field of taxonomy, a kidnapped Spanish girl’s return, and an interview with singer‑songwriter Corinne Bailey Rae.
