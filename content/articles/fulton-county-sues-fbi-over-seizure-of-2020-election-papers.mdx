Fulton County officials are suing the FBI after the agency seized about 700 boxes of 2020 election documents, including original ballots, last week. The county says the FBI ignored a court order that let them keep only copies and took the originals illegally. Attorneys plan to file a motion to force the FBI to return the seized papers and explain its actions.
