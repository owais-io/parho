A video of a US military strike on a suspected drug boat that killed survivors is at the center of a debate. Republicans say the Pentagon should make the clip public, while Democrats want it released after claims of war‑crime orders. The attack, which took place on 2 September, killed 11 people, including two men who died in a follow‑up strike. Trump says he has no problem releasing the video, but the Pentagon has not yet committed.
