PSG and Paris FC are only 44 metres apart, but the clubs live in separate worlds. PSG, a trophy‑winning powerhouse, barely feels the presence of Paris FC, a newer Ligue 2 side that’s just been taken over by billionaire Arnault and Red Bull. The derby started with a 2‑1 win for PSG, showing the gap in strength. Both clubs have friendly ties, but their future matches could keep the rivalry fresh.
