A Man Utd supporter argues that firing manager Fernando Amorim was inevitable because he lacked experience and clashed with the club’s leadership. He claims Amorim tried to fix player attitude and infrastructure problems but was ignored. The fan regrets that the club didn’t stick with the former manager, Erik ten Hag, or give Amorim more time. He feels the club’s frequent managerial changes keep fans and the team stuck in a cycle.
