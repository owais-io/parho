During the U.S. Half Marathon Championship in Atlanta, a guide vehicle took the leading women’s runners off course with less than two miles left. Molly Born, who had been behind, sprinted past them and won, while Jess McClain, Ednah Kurgat and Emma Hurley finished much later. The organizers say the course was poorly marked and no changes to the finish order are allowed under the rules. The event also served as a qualifier for the 2026 World Championships.
