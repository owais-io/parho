Stephen Miller, Trump’s deputy chief of staff, drives the most hard‑line moves in the White House. He pushes nationalist ideas like “America for Americans only” and has shaped immigration rules that split families, cut birthright citizenship, and target migrants. Miller also helped plan foreign actions, such as the capture of Venezuela’s Maduro. The piece points out his long history of xenophobia and warns readers to pay close attention to his influence.
