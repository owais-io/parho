Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, was shot and killed by federal agents during a protest in Minneapolis. Video shows him being tackled, sprayed with a chemical agent, and then shot after being taken to the ground. The agents say they fired defensive shots, while protesters called the officers “rioters.” The incident sparked angry reactions from city officials, the state governor, and even President Trump, who urged an end to the federal presence.
