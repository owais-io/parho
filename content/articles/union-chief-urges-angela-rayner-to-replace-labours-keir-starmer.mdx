The head of a Labour‑linked union, Maryam Eslamdoust, says Starmer should step down and hand the party over to Angela Rayner, arguing a woman leader is needed and that Starmer risks a heavy loss to Reform UK. She points to a likely byelection defeat and a weak mandate, while Rayner’s supporters say she can unite the party and push for worker rights. Starmer has already lost key aides, and many in Labour think a challenge is coming.
