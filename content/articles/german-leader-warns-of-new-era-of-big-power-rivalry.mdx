At the World Economic Forum in Davos, German Chancellor Friedrich Merz said a new era of “great power politics” has begun, driven by Russia’s war, China’s rise and the U.S.’s shifting policy. He urged Europe to strengthen defence, boost economic competitiveness, and build partnerships with like‑minded allies. Merz warned that a world ruled only by power endangers small, middle and even great nations, and called for cooperation instead of confrontation.
