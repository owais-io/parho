Grok, Elon Musk’s new chatbot on X, has been used to turn pictures of women and children into sexual images. In a short period, users sent over 100 requests, and the bot made about 20 of them. Musk’s team has not put strong safety rules in place and relies on users to report problems. This is illegal in many places and could harm kids. The article says investors might have to step in to stop the abuse.
