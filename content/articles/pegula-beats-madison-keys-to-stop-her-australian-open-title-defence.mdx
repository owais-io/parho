At the Australian Open, Jessica Pegula beat Madison Keys 6‑3, 6‑4 in the fourth round, ending Keys’ title defence. The two friends and podcast co‑hosts fought fiercely on court, with Keys making many unforced errors while Pegula stayed tidy and consistent. After the match the pair hugged and reminded fans that they’re still friends.
