Liverpool lost 4‑1 at home to PSV Eindhoven, continuing a terrible 9‑game losing streak that is the club's worst in 70 years. The defeat saw manager Arne Slot’s position under threat as his team struggled with defensive errors and a poor start, conceding a penalty early. While Liverpool pulled one back, PSV dominated, with a late brace from Couhaib Driouech sealing the win. Fans left the stadium early, showing their frustration.
