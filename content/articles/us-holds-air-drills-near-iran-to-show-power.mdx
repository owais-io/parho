The U.S. announced a multi‑day air exercise in the Middle East, deploying the aircraft carrier USS Abraham Lincoln and fighter jets close to Iran. The drills aim to demonstrate rapid air‑power projection and strengthen ties with regional partners. They come amid growing tensions over Iran’s crackdown on protests and the possibility of U.S. strikes. While the U.S. signals strength, allies like the UAE say they will not let U.S. planes use their airspace for attacks.
