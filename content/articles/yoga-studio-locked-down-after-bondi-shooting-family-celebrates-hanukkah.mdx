After a gunman opened fire at Bondi Beach, a yoga studio on the same street was locked down. The writer, who had just left class, rushed back to shelter in the studio. Phones went dead and she worried her niece was missing. When the police cleared the area, her niece was safe in a nearby café. The family then lit Hanukkah candles, hoping for hope amid the tragedy.
