Former Jets linebacker Darron Lee has been charged with murder after his girlfriend died in Ooltewah, Tennessee. First responders couldn’t save her and suspect a stab wound. Lee is also accused of tampering with evidence, as police say blood was cleaned from the house. He will appear in court on February 11. Lee, a 2016 Ohio State star, played for the Jets, Chiefs, and Bills.
