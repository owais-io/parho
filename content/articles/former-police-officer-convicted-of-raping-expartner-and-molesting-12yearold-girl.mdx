David Carrick, a former Metropolitan police officer, has been found guilty of raping an ex‑partner and molesting a 12‑year‑old girl in the late 1980s. The case was heard at the Old Bailey after police discovered a note he wrote 35 years ago admitting the abuse. Carrick had already pleaded guilty to 71 sex offences, including 48 rapes. He now faces a new conviction and will be sentenced after the trial.
