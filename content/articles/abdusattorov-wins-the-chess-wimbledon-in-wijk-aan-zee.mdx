21‑year‑old Uzbek grandmaster Nodirbek Abdusattorov won the Tata Steel Wijk aan Zee tournament, often called “chess Wimbledon.” He finished with 9 points, edging his teammate Javokhir Sindarov who took second place. The victory pushes him up to world No. 5 and gives him a chance to qualify for the Candidates cycle. Abdusattorov had come close in previous years but finally broke through, and he’s already preparing for the FIDE Freestyle world championship in Germany.
