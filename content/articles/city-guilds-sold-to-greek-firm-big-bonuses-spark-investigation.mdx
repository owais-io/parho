City & Guilds, a long‑standing UK vocational training charity, was sold to Greek firm PeopleCert. The deal is under scrutiny after the charity’s chief executive and finance officer received £1.7m and £1.2m in bonuses. Investigations are looking into why the bonuses were paid and whether the new owners will cut UK jobs and raise fees. The sale also raises worries about the future of free‑market training and the charity’s £200m assets.
