Ukrainian skeleton racer Vladyslav Heraskevych has been using a helmet that shows pictures of fellow Ukrainian athletes who were killed in the war. Even though the International Olympic Committee (IOC) banned the helmet for being a political statement, he plans to wear it in competition. The Ukrainian team says the ban violates the Olympic Charter and has appealed. The IOC has offered to let him wear a black armband instead.
