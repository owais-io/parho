I was a child actor who grew up safe on set, but as a teen my picture was stolen online and turned into illegal sexual content. Now AI can make fake porn from any photo, making it far easier for predators to exploit kids. Experts say the problem comes from training data that includes bad images, and the tech companies aren’t doing enough to filter them. Legislation and new safety tools are urgently needed to protect children.
