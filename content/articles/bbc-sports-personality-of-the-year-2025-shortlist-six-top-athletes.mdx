BBC’s Sports Personality of the Year 2025 will be chosen next Thursday. The six‑person shortlist has England football stars Chloe Kelly and Hannah Hampton, rugby champion Ellie Kildunne, Formula One champ Lando Norris, darts world champion Luke Littler, and golf star Rory McIlroy. All won major titles this year, and two of the women could win for the first time in five years. The ceremony takes place in Salford on Dec 18.
