Brazilian midfielder Lucas Paquetá asked West Ham not to field him in an FA Cup game and wants to leave for his old club Flamengo. Flamengo offered £35 million, which was rejected, but they are willing to increase the bid. Paquetá has been unhappy in England, especially after a betting‑rule investigation and a tough season. West Ham, fighting relegation, may keep him for the rest of the year but could sell him if the offer arrives.
