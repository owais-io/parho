Many Iranians living outside Iran are scared and out of touch with what’s happening at home. Some think the US should step in to remove the current leadership, while others believe change will come from inside the country over many years. The protests in Iran have slowed, and deaths are still uncertain. People abroad hope the world’s pressure will protect civilians and bring new leaders.
