Hundreds of American contractors are stuck on a U.S. military base near Baghdad, with no clear plan to get them out. Iran‑backed militants in Iraq are allegedly planning a strike after Ramadan, and some Iraqi workers are reportedly giving them information. The contractors say they lack safety measures and evacuation orders, and the company is being warned that the U.S. government might lose a big contract if they leave. The situation reflects rising danger in Iraq amid the Iran‑US conflict.
