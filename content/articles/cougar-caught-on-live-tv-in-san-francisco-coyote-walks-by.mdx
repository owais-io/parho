While reporters filmed a 2‑year‑old mountain lion being tranquilized and freed in San Francisco’s Pacific Heights, a coyote casually strolled into the camera shot. The video quickly went viral, showing the irony of a city animal roaming free while a wild predator was removed. City animal control closed streets, used darts to sedate the cougar, and plans to release it back into the wild. Coyotes are common in the city’s parks, while cougars are rare but occasionally show up.
