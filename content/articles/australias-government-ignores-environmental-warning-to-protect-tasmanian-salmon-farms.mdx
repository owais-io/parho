Last year Australia’s Albanese government rushed through a bill that keeps salmon farms running in Tasmania’s Macquarie Harbour. The Environment Department had warned that the farms were harming the endangered Maugean skate and the world‑heritage area by dropping oxygen levels. Despite this advice, the government ignored the recommendation and passed a law that makes it harder for anyone to ask the decision to be reconsidered. Environmental groups say this is a major blow to the wildlife.
