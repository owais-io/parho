Trump and his team kept shifting their story about why the US started Operation Epic Fury against Iran. From defending the nation to promising liberation for Iranians, to claiming an imminent threat, officials gave conflicting reasons. The White House, Pentagon, and State Department each offered different explanations, confusing Congress and the public. The war’s purpose kept changing, leaving people unsure what the real goal was.
