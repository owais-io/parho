Eric Dane, the handsome surgeon Mark Sloan from the hit TV show Grey’s Anatomy, has died at 53 from motor neurone disease. He also starred in The Last Ship, Euphoria, and films like X‑Men: The Last Stand. Dane began acting in the early 90s, gaining fame for his charming, sometimes scandalous on‑screen characters. He married Rebecca Gayheart and had two daughters. His passing is a big loss for fans and the industry.
