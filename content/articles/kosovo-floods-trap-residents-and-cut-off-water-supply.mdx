Kosovo has been hit by heavy rain, flooding cities, villages and rivers. Homes were submerged, and rescue teams had to pull people out. Water and electricity were cut in several towns because floodwater damaged power stations. In the UK, a red wind warning was issued for Cornwall and the Isles as Storm Goretti brought gusts up to 100 mph. The same storm also threatened France with strong winds and high surf, and could bring snow to Germany and the Benelux.
