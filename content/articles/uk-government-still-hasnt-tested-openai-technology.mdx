The UK signed a deal with OpenAI to use AI across government, but after eight months no trials have taken place. A freedom‑of‑information request revealed only a small use of ChatGPT in the Ministry of Justice, while other projects with Nvidia and Nscale are stalled. Critics say the partnership lacks clear plans, transparency and a way to measure benefits.
