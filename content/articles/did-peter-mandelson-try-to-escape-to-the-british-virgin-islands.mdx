Peter Mandelson, a former UK minister, was arrested on suspicion of misconduct and police said he might flee to the British Virgin Islands (BVI). Speaker Lindsay Hoyle allegedly tipped the Met, but the BVI government officials claim no knowledge of any plan. The Met released Mandelson, saying he wasn’t a flight risk, and the BVI’s leaders say the island isn’t a safe haven for fugitives. The mystery remains.
