Iran’s foreign minister will meet Turkish officials in Ankara to discuss nuclear‑program concessions that could stop a war. The Turkish president has even suggested a video call between Trump and the Iranian leader. The US is ready to follow Trump’s orders, raising tension in the Middle East.
