Cristian Romero, Tottenham Hotspur's new captain, used his first interview to say the club is heading in the right direction. He praised new manager Thomas Frank and said the board should listen to the fans. After a tough season, Romero criticised the board for weak transfers and for not keeping the squad healthy, calling their moves “disgraceful.” His outspoken attitude has sparked debate about whether he is helping or hurting the club.
