England have moved on from a shaky start to the T20 World Cup. After training at Pallekele in Sri Lanka, the team says the results of the opening group are behind them. They are set to face New Zealand, Pakistan and Sri Lanka in the Super 8s. The players want a fresh attitude, better batting, and a stronger finish. Weather and a player injury have added extra pressure, but the squad is determined to improve.
