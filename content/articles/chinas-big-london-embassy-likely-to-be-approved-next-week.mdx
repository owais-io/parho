A huge new Chinese embassy near Tower Bridge in east London is likely to be approved next week, but Labour MPs worry about security risks and how it could affect Hong Kong and Uighur communities. The 20,000‑sq‑metre building has more than 200 underground rooms that could link to the City of London, raising fears of economic spying. MI5 says it has no security concerns, yet MPs want clearer safeguards before it goes ahead.
