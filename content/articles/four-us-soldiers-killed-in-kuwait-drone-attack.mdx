On March 1, 2026, four U.S. soldiers were killed when a drone struck the Shuaiba port in Kuwait. The soldiers—Capt. Cody Khork, Sgt. Nicole Amor, Sgt. Declan Coady, and Sgt. Noah Tietjens—were part of the 103rd Sustainment Command supporting the U.S. and Israel’s Operation Epic Fury against Iran. The attack follows the start of that campaign and is still under investigation.
