In a move that could hurt a fellow Republican, Donald Trump said he fully supports Congresswoman Julia Letlow’s possible run against Senator Bill Cassidy of Louisiana. Cassidy voted to convict Trump during the second impeachment and has since tried to regain his favor, even backing former health‑secretary nominee Robert Kennedy Jr. Letlow, a former U.S. House member, was the first Republican woman elected from Louisiana. Trump’s endorsement could spark a primary showdown that could split the GOP.
