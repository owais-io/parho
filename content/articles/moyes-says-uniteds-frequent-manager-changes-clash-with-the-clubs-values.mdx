David Moyes, former United boss, said the club’s constant reshuffling of managers goes against the traditions Sir Alex Ferguson built. He believes United needs a long‑term rebuild and that the recent turmoil shows a cultural shift away from the club’s values of stability and academy development. Current coach Ruben Amorim warns the “storm” isn’t over, even after a five‑game unbeaten run, reminding fans that quick results are still uncertain.
