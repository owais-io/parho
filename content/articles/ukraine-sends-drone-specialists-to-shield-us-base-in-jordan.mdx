Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, said Kyiv sent interceptor drones and crews to help guard a U.S. base in Jordan after Washington asked for support. The move is part of Ukraine’s effort to strengthen ties with Gulf and Middle‑East allies amid Iranian missile and drone attacks. Ukraine has built cheap, high‑speed drones that can stop Iranian Shahed drones, and it is now offering that expertise to the U.S. base.
