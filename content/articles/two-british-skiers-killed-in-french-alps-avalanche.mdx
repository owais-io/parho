Two British skiers died in a fatal avalanche in the French Alps. The men, Stuart Leslie and Shaun Overy, were part of a five‑person group off‑piste in Val d’Isère when snow swept them away. The French weather service had warned of a red avalanche alert, the third such warning in 25 years. A French skier also died, and another Brit survived. An investigation into possible manslaughter has begun.
