Bayern Munich beat Arsenal 3-2 in the Women’s Champions League after a late goal from Glodis Viggosdottir. Arsenal had taken a 2-0 lead early, thanks to Beth Mead and Stina Blackstenius, but Bayern fought back with Pernille Harder and Viggosdottir. The match left Arsenal with only one win in three games and reminded them of last season’s struggles. Over 400 Arsenal fans watched from Munich’s Allianz Arena, but the Bavarian side prevailed.
