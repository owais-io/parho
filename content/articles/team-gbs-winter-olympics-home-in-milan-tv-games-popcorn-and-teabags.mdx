Team GB’s 55 athletes at the Winter Games have turned a cramped Olympic Village room into a cozy home. They’ve brought 130kg of oats, 5,000 Aldi teabags, a Formula One simulator, TVs, jigsaw puzzles and even a piano. Healthy meals, plenty of popcorn, coffee and fruit are on hand, plus a gym and games. Strict hygiene signs remind athletes to wash hands and stay safe. It’s a mix of comfort and competition.
