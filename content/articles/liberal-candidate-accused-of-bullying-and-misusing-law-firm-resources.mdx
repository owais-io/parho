Grant Hutchinson, a Liberal candidate for the Croydon seat in Melbourne, is fighting back against accusations from his own law firm. Partners claim he bullied staff, threatened a lawyer, and used the firm’s resources for politics. Hutchinson says these claims are false and says he never used the firm for political work. A court case is ongoing, with mediation set to finish by March 2026. The dispute could affect his election chances.
