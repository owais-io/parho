A measles outbreak has infected more than 60 children in north London, with cases reported in seven schools and a nursery in Enfield. The spread is linked to low vaccination rates, and experts warn it could grow into a city‑wide outbreak. Temporary clinics are offering shots in schools, while a national vaccination campaign will launch next week to boost coverage and stop the virus.
