Labour MP Jess Phillips says the shock of the Jeffrey Epstein case must spark long‑term laws and support for victims, not just quick fixes. She wants the government to keep pushing on violence against women and girls, invest in prevention, and hold abusers accountable. Phillips urges NHS and schools to treat abuse like diabetes, and highlights the huge economic cost of domestic abuse.
