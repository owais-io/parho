Seven men were taken in southwestern Sydney after police thought they might be planning a violent act. Officers stopped two cars and found only a knife, no weapons. The men, aged 19‑24, were released on Friday while police investigate further. The police say there is no current danger to the public and are working with Victoria law‑enforcement to gather evidence.
