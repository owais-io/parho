In the recent Ashes Test in Brisbane, Australia’s wicketkeeper Alex Carey stunned the crowd with a flawless display behind the stumps, taking seven catches and standing up to fast bowlers like Ben Stokes. He also scored 63 runs, helping his team build a solid score. Carey’s performance marks him as the world’s leading wicketkeeper, following the path set by Rod Marsh and Adam Gilchrist. Though he never practiced standing up to quicks, his instinct made the moment unforgettable.
