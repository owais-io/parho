Six years after 14‑year‑old Noah Donohoe disappeared from Belfast, an inquest has opened to find out what happened. His mother told the court she was alarmed when he didn't answer his phone and that he had been emotional that day. Police say he died of drowning in a storm drain, and CCTV shows he rode a bike naked and left his backpack behind. Volunteers scoured the city and case expected to finish in March, with no sign of foul play.
