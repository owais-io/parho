An oncologist explains how researchers and patients keep getting tricked by fake papers. She talks about spam emails promising quick publication and fake journals that just want money. Studies show fraud in cancer papers is rising fast. Patients who read these papers may try wrong treatments like diets or herbs, hurting themselves. She says patients can still look up information, but they must check if the study is real and reliable. We need better checks and public awareness.
