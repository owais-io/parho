The war between Israel and Iran has shut the Strait of Hormuz, a key oil route, causing world oil prices to spike to about $100 a barrel. People from India to the UK are struggling: they’re driving less, using public transport, cutting heating oil, and canceling trips to save money. The International Energy Agency urges everyone to cut fuel use and switch to electric cooking, but many still rely on cars because of limited alternatives.
