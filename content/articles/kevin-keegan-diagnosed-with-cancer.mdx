Kevin Keegan, the former England manager and Newcastle legend, has been diagnosed with an undisclosed form of cancer. He is 74 and has been hospitalized for abdominal symptoms. The family says he is grateful to doctors and wishes privacy. Keegan had a celebrated playing and coaching career, winning titles with Liverpool, Hamburg and Newcastle. He will begin treatment and his family will keep the news private.
