Brentford won 2‑0 at Wolves, with Keane Lewis‑Potter scoring both goals. The defeat keeps Wolves on a winless streak and leaves them with just two points. Brentford’s keeper Caoimhín Kelleher also saved a late penalty from Jørgen Strand Larsen. Fans in Molineux remembered former player Ethan McLeod, who died in a car crash, and called for a new club chair. Wolves saw a change at the top, with chair Jeff Shi leaving and Nathan Shi taking over temporarily.
