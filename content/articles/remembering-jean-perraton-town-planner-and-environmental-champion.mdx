Jean Perraton (1944‑2023) was a planner and environmental activist who loved outdoor water sports. She led campaigns that turned the River Cam at Sheep’s Green into a safe, designated bathing water in 2024 and fought to clear invasive plants from the river. As president of the River and Lake Swimming Association, she wrote two books about swimming and wartime letters. Her career spanned planning work in the UK, Botswana, and local councils, always focused on protecting nature and community spaces.
