Storm Chandra may flood southwest England, bringing heavy rain and strong winds that could disrupt travel and cause river flooding. The Met Office has issued amber warnings for rain and wind, and the Environment Agency has 21 flood warnings in place. Earlier this month, Storm Goretti left many without power and knocked down trees. A kayaker and a swimmer were found missing in the area.
