Big elections in 2024 showed how fragile democracy is when people start calling each other enemies. Many people feel anxious and tired. Good journalism helps keep us connected. Stories from Gaza, Ukraine, and even Kabul’s water crisis show how human suffering spreads. The article ends by saying we can heal by recognizing the damage and working together.
