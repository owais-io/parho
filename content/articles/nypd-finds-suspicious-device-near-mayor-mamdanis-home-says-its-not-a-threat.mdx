A suspicious device was found near Mayor Zohran Mamdani’s home in Carl Schurz Park, but NYPD says it’s harmless. Police closed nearby streets while the bomb squad made it safe. The find follows a protest where teenagers threw homemade bombs that were never detonated. FBI confirmed explosives in a Pennsylvania storage unit linked to the same attack. The city is investigating the incident as ISIS‑inspired terrorism.
