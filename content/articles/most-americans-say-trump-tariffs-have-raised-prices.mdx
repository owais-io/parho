New poll shows 72% of Americans believe Trump’s tariffs have made prices higher. Across the political spectrum, 64% of Republicans, 77% of Democrats and 67% of independents say tariffs hurt consumers. Grocery costs have risen, with beef up 14% and coffee 18%. Despite this, Trump wants to keep tariffs going. The finding could trouble Republicans in the upcoming mid‑term elections as they try to win back voters.
