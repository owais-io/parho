Burnley and Everton played out a goalless draw at Turf Moor. The Reds had more chances and a higher expected goal score, but their shots missed the target. Burnley now sit 17th and still six points from safety, making a home win essential to stay in the Premier League. Everton, missing key players, were content to avoid a third straight defeat, but their lack of energy kept the score level. The result keeps Burnley's survival hopes dim.
