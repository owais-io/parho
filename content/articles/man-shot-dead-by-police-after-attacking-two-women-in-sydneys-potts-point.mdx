Police responded to an incident in Sydney's Potts Point after an armed man allegedly attacked two women. Tasers didn't work, so an officer fired a single shot, killing him. The women, aged 48 and 56, were taken to hospital with head and facial injuries. NSW ambulance paramedics confirmed the man's death on the scene. Investigations are underway, with an independent review ordered. The incident has raised questions about the effectiveness of tasers and police response protocols.
