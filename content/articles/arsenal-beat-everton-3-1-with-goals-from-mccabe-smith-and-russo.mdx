Arsenal won 3-1 at Everton, moving up to second place in the Women’s Super League. Katie McCabe opened the scoring with a half‑volley, and after Everton’s Honoka Hayashi equalised, Alessia Russo restored the lead. In the final minutes, Olivia Smith struck again to seal the win. This is Arsenal’s fourth straight victory and boosts confidence ahead of their next matches, including the League Cup quarter‑finals.
