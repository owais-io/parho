The UK’s top prosecutor, Stephen Parkinson, has insisted that nobody is above the law as new evidence links former trade envoy Prince Andrew (Mountbatten‑Windsor) to Jeffrey Epstein. Police are reviewing claims that Andrew misused his official role, shared confidential data and may have helped Epstein’s business deals. Parliament figures urge a full police investigation, and the Crown Prosecution Service says detectives will examine all evidence.
