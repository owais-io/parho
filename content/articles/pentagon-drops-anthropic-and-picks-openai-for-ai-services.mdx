The Pentagon ended its contract with Anthropic after a Trump‑era order and chose OpenAI instead. Anthropic had said its models couldn’t be used for mass surveillance or fully autonomous weapons, a stance Trump called “woke.” OpenAI signed a huge contract to provide AI for classified defense work. This shift shows how government deals can shape AI, raising debates about safety, ethics, and the need for clearer laws.
