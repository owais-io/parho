The Chicago United Center will now sell drinks that contain THC, a hemp‑derived cannabis ingredient, at events like concerts and hockey games. The drinks, made by brands such as Rythm and Señorita, will cost around $15‑$20. The move could open the door for other arenas to offer similar drinks, but sports leagues like the NHL currently oppose it. Experts warn about mixing THC with alcohol and emphasize safety.
