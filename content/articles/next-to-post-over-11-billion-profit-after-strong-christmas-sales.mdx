Next, the big UK fashion store, expects its profit for the year to be more than £1.1 billion after selling a lot more than people thought during the Christmas period. Sales jumped 5.9 % in the nine weeks to 27 Dec, better than the 4.1 % forecast. The company also did well abroad, with a 38 % rise in overseas sales. However, Next warns that growth could slow in 2026 because of job‑market pressures and higher living costs.
