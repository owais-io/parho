Actor Timothy Busfield, 68, was arrested on charges of sexual contact with minors in New Mexico. The charges stem from two boys who worked on the TV show “The Cleaning Lady.” A judge has kept him in custody without bond while the case moves forward. Busfield’s wife, actress Melissa Gilbert, said she supports him but asked for privacy during the legal process. Earlier accusations against him include a 1994 assault claim and a 2012 alleged fondling incident.
