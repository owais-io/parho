Australian media company News Corp says the ABC distorted Trump’s speech about the January 6 Capitol attack, claiming it was the same mistake the BBC made. The BBC had spliced two parts of the speech to make it sound like Trump was calling for violence, and the ABC did a similar cut. ABC says the edit was minor and didn’t change meaning. The dispute may lead to questions at the National Press Club.
