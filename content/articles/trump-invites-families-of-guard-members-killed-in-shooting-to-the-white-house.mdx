President Trump said he has invited the families of two West Virginia National Guard members—Sarah Beckstrom, who was killed, and Andrew Wolfe, who is in critical condition—to visit the White House. He expressed deep sadness and offered a meeting when the families are ready. Vigils were held in West Virginia to honor the two young soldiers, and authorities have charged an Afghan national with the murder.
