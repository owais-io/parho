After watching the final episode of Stranger Things, the writer sees how the show feels like an endless video‑game: long walks, many side‑quests, a huge final boss. He argues that TV and film copy games’ excitement but also their bloat, and notes how movies like Edge of Tomorrow and John Wick 4 already look like games. He also comments on the high cost of making new games and the dominance of a few big franchises.
