Wests Tigers are in crisis after CEO Shane Richardson and four board members were sacked by the club’s owners, the Holman Barnes Group. The shake‑up has shocked players and fans, who had seen a brief lift under Richardson’s rebuild. A jersey dispute and lack of communication were key reasons for the purge. Local mayor and supporters plan a rally, and the NRL may step in to keep the club afloat.
