A five‑year‑old boy named Liam Ramos was taken by ICE agents in Minneapolis after his father was arrested. The school released photos of the child in a blue hat, while agents used him to locate his parents. Liam was sent to a Texas detention center where conditions are harsh. The incident sparked protests and raised concerns about how ICE handles families and children.
