England lost their first game of the three‑match ODI series to Sri Lanka, falling 19 runs short. The match was the first win for England’s coach Brendon McCullum away from home, but the team failed to capitalize on a decent start. England’s top batters, Ben Duckett and Joe Root, hit half‑centuries, yet the Sri Lankan spinners, especially Dunith Wellalage, dismantled the batting lineup. It was England’s 11th consecutive away loss, highlighting their ongoing struggle on turning pitches.
