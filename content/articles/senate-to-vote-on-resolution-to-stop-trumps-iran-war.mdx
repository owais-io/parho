Republican senators are expected to reject a Democratic‑backed resolution that would stop President Trump from continuing the Iran war. The vote comes after Israel and the U.S. launched strikes on Iran, with Trump saying Iran tried to restart talks. The resolution would require Trump to seek Congress’s permission before any new military action. The outcome could affect U.S. troops, markets and global security.
