Artist Minne Fry, who died at 91, was a pioneering abstract painter from South Africa who made her mark in 1960s London. She started strong, then paused to raise three children and teach. In her 40s she returned to painting, showing work in London, Paris, and Johannesburg. She also made prints that earned awards, and critics praised her color and emotion. She is survived by her three children and grandchildren.
