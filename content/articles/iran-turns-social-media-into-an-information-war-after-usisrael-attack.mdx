Iran has shifted its social media tactics into an information war against the US and Israel after recent attacks. State actors flood X, Instagram and Bluesky with AI‑generated memes and fake videos that mock Trump and Israeli leaders, trying to turn American anger into support for Iran. The regime also blocks internet inside Iran, threatens ex‑Iranians abroad, and uses state‑run accounts to spread propaganda. Analysts say this cyber push is a key part of Iran’s survival plan.
