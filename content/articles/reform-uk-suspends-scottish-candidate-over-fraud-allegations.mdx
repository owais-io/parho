Reform UK dropped Stuart Niven, a Dundee City West candidate, after a report revealed he had been struck off as a company director for misusing tens of thousands of pounds in Covid grants. The party is also under fire for other candidates who made Islamophobic remarks and shared false claims about migrants. Critics say Reform’s manifesto promises are unrealistic and that its vetting process is weak. The scandal has hurt the party’s reputation ahead of the Scottish election.
