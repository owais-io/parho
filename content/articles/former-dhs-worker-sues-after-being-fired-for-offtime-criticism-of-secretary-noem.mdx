A former DHS IT worker, Brandon Wright, was fired after a video from a Bumble date criticizing Secretary Kristi Noem was posted online. He says the agency fired him because of his free‑speech comments and sued DHS, claiming a First Amendment violation. The lawsuit alleges the date was recorded by a woman linked to James O’Keefe, who pushed the conversation toward politics. DHS hasn’t replied.
