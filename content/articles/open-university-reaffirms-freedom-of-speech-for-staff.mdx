The Open University says it fully supports academic freedom and follows the 2023 Freedom of Speech Act. It clarified that a recent email with UK Lawyers for Israel was just about one case study in an intro history module that will soon be updated. The university stresses it has no ban on the phrase “Ancient Palestine”; staff can use any academically appropriate words. No external group has told staff how to teach.
