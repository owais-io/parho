Damien Martyn, the former Australian Test cricket star, is beginning to improve while he remains in an induced coma after a meningitis diagnosis. He was admitted to a Gold Coast hospital after getting ill on Boxing Day. Friend Adam Gilchrist said family and fans are grateful for the support and that recent tests are showing hopeful signs. He’s still in hospital, and more updates will follow.
