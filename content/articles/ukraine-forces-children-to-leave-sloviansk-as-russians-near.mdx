Ukraine is forcing children to leave the town of Sloviansk as Russian forces get close, showing the war is getting worse in the eastern Donbas. The city is still one of a few Ukrainian strongholds. At the same time, Kyiv launched over 280 drones at Russia and is waiting for a €90 billion EU loan that Hungary is blocking. The story also touches on moving US Patriot missiles and Hungary’s oil‑pipeline disputes.
