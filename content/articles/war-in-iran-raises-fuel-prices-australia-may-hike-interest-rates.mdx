The Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) says the war in Iran is pushing up fuel prices, which are making Australians poorer and raising inflation above its 2.5% target. Assistant Governor Chris Kent warns the central bank may need to raise interest rates again to curb rising prices and stop a high‑inflation mindset from settling in. The RBA plans a rate hike for May, and may raise rates again in June or September.
