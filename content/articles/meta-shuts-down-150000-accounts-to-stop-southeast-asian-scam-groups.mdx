Meta disabled more than 150,000 accounts across Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp after a joint operation with Thai police, FBI and other international agencies that arrested 21 people running scam centers in Cambodia, Myanmar and Laos. The crackdown targeted groups that lure users with fake romance and crypto offers. Meta also rolled out new safety tools that warn about suspicious friend requests, fake profiles and device‑linking scams, and let users report or block questionable accounts.
