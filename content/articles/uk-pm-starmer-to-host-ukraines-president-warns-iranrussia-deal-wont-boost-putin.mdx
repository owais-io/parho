UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer will welcome Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy next week. He says the UK must stop the United States and Israel from striking Iran in a way that would give Vladimir Putin extra money from the war. Starmer also warns Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich, who sold Chelsea, that he must give money earned from the sale to Ukraine’s war victims. Britain continues to send weapons to Ukraine.
