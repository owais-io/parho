A student in his 20s was stabbed to death in Leicester city centre. Police arrested an 18‑year‑old on suspicion of murder. Officers are reviewing CCTV and asking witnesses to come forward. The incident caused road closures on Oxford Street and nearby roads, disrupting traffic and hospital visits. The victim was taken to Leicester Royal Infirmary, where he died shortly after arrival. The case is still under investigation.
