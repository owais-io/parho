South Africa has told Israel’s top diplomat to leave the country within 72 hours after the Israeli embassy used its social media pages to post attacks on President Cyril Ramaphosa. In response, Israel expelled a South African official in Ramallah. The move comes amid a long‑standing dispute over Israel’s treatment of Palestinians, with South Africa accusing Israel of genocide. The tensions show the fragile diplomatic ties between the two nations.
