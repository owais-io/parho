Leeds Rhinos demolished Hull KR in Las Vegas, winning 50‑0. Star winger Maika Sivo scored four tries, including a hat‑trick, and helped the team dominate from the start. The game, part of the World Club Challenge, showed Leeds’ growing power in rugby league and left Hull KR, the Super League champions, stunned. The win underlined Leeds’ ambition to win major trophies this season.
