California’s new Drop website lets anyone in the state send a single request to 500 data‑broker companies to delete their personal info. Launched on Jan. 1, it follows the Delete Act law that created a free portal for residents. Data brokers collect details from public records, phone and web data and sell them for marketing and other uses. By stopping sales, Californians should see less spam and better privacy.
