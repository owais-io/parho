Melbourne tops Time Out’s global city ranking for 2026, but critics say the survey and expert voting methods are unclear. The list used 24,000 respondents and 100 city experts, giving Melbourne first place. Other rankings place it lower, showing that rankings can vary. Residents praised food, culture, and daily happiness, showing why the city feels special.
