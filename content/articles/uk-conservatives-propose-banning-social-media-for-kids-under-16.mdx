UK Conservatives are planning to ban social media for anyone under 16. Kemi Badenoch says the move would protect kids’ mental health and help them focus in school. She also wants phones out of classrooms. The teachers’ union NASUWT backs the ban, citing harmful content and school behaviour problems. Australia already made a similar ban last month, and other European countries are watching.
