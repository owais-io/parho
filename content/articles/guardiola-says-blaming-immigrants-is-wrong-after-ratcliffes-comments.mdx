Sir Jim Ratcliffe said the UK is being “colonised by immigrants”, sparking backlash. Guardiola, Manchester City’s boss, said blaming immigrants for a country’s problems is wrong and urged people to treat newcomers with respect. He highlighted his own travel and multicultural team, praising diversity at City. He also talked about his time playing in several countries and how this shaped his view.
