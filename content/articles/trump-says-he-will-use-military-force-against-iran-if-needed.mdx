The White House says President Trump would use U.S. military force against Iran if necessary. White House spokesperson Karoline Leavitt told Fox News that while diplomacy is the first choice, airstrikes are a possible option. Trump also warned that any business with Iran would face a 25% tariff. Iran is still dealing with huge protests, with many arrests and deaths, and the U.S. keeps threatening retaliation.
