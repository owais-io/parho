Mexico’s army killed the feared cartel leader “El Mencho,” killing 25 national‑guard soldiers. Trump blasted Mexico, saying it must do more to fight drug gangs and hinted he could send U.S. troops if they don’t cooperate. U.S. intelligence helped the raid, and the move may improve Mexico’s chances in U.S.–Mexico trade talks. The attack also comes as Guadalajara will host World Cup matches, adding pressure to keep the city safe.
