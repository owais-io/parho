South Australian police have gone back to Oak Park Station, the farm where 4‑year‑old Gus Lamont vanished on 27 September 2025, to look for more clues. A 75‑year‑old was recently arrested on unrelated gun charges, but the investigation now treats the disappearance as a major crime. The police say a person living with Gus is a suspect, and they’ll stay on the property for at least two more days to keep searching.
