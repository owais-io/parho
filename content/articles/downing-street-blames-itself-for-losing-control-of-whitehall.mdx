A former Downing Street adviser said the government’s lack of control over Whitehall is its own fault, blaming a “perma‑class” of politicians and lobbyists for wasting time on fringe issues, like freeing an activist. Experts say ministers still have power to change the system, but the Prime Minister’s team moves slowly. The remarks come after the adviser quit over past inappropriate messages.
