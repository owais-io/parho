After a slow start, Tottenham staged a comeback against Manchester City, with Dominic Solanke scoring the decisive goal that helped the Spurs secure a 2-1 win. Solanke's late strike, following a clever pass from Xavi Simons, overturned City’s early lead. The victory hurt City’s bid for the Premier League title, dropping them to sixth place. Tottenham’s win boosted their confidence and added pressure to Arsenal’s title run.
