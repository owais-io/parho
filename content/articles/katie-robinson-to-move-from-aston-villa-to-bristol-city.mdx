England winger Katie Robinson is set to leave Aston Villa and join Bristol City, a surprise move that shows the club’s ambition. The 23‑year‑old, who has five England caps and played in the 2023 World Cup final, recently finished a medical at her former club. Bristol City, backed by new owners, are targeting promotion and have also signed former Wales captain Sophie Ingle. They’re pushing to reach the top tier.
