The Jets had originally disqualified a fan, Ashley Castanio‑Gervasi, from a $100,000 field‑goal contest because she is a soccer coach. Fans, former player Damien Woody, and a congressman complained. The team said there was a misunderstanding and reversed the decision. Ashley will now try to win the prize, hoping to use the money to buy a home and support cancer charities. She plans to play at the game against the Patriots.
