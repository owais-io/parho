A silver steward who works at the French president’s Paris residence was arrested after stealing silverware and porcelain worth up to €40,000. The thief and two accomplices tried to sell the pieces on online auction sites like Vinted. Investigators found about 100 items, including Sèvres porcelain, Baccarat champagne glasses and a René Lalique statuette, in the steward’s home and locker. The recovered goods have been returned to the Élysée. The case highlights a string of recent thefts from France’s top museums.
