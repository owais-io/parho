Trump wants to delay his Beijing visit by about a month because of the Iran conflict. He says the trip may be postponed if China does not help keep the Hormuz Strait open for oil shipments. China is talking with the US and wants to avoid more tension, but the meeting could still happen. The leaders’ meeting is seen as key for US‑China relations.
