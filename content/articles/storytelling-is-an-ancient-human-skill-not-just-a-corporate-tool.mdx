Storytelling is a long‑standing human practice that builds connection, memory, and empathy. The Society for Storytelling has promoted oral storytelling for 30 years, showing how it helps kids learn language and adults stay emotionally healthy. Research finds stories boost thinking and resilience, and companies now use storytelling to strengthen their messages. It’s not just a job—stories are part of everyday life and keep us linked.
