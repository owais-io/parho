Spurs fans are complaining that the new stadium feels toxic. A small protest outside the ground showed frustration over transfer plans, injuries, results, and high ticket prices. Meanwhile, the team’s poor performances, especially against West Ham, have turned fans against manager Thomas Frank. The club’s board hasn’t fixed the problems, so supporters think the club is stuck in a bad mood and may even consider changing the manager. The atmosphere in London’s new arena has become negative.
