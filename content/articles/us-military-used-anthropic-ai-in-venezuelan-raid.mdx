The Wall Street Journal said the US military used Anthropic’s AI chatbot Claude during a raid on Venezuela aimed at capturing President Nicolás Maduro. The operation bombed Caracas and killed about 83 people. Anthropic says its model must not be used for violent or weapon‑related tasks, but it did not comment on the claim. Sources say the army worked with Palantir, a Pentagon contractor, to run Claude. The story shows how AI is being used in armed‑force planning.
