Aberdeen, once famed for oil, is becoming a tech city. In the 1970s it built the world’s first MRI scanner, and in the 1800s the first electric car was invented there. Today the ONE Tech Hub hosts dozens of start‑ups. Tech expert Timi Adegunwa says the city’s growth relies on skilled, inclusive people, strong government support, and a network that reaches worldwide. Aberdeen is turning into a future‑proof business hub.
