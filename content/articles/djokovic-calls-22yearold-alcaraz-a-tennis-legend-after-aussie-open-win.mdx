After beating world No. 1 Novak Djokovic, 22‑year‑old Carlos Alcaraz won the Australian Open and became the youngest man ever to finish a career Grand Slam. Djokovic praised him as a legend, saying he already has a huge place in tennis history. Alcaraz’s win also made him the youngest player to win seven Grand Slams and the first to win each major at a single age. He’s now planning a kangaroo tattoo to celebrate.
