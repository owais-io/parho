Prime Minister Keir Starmer is under pressure to fire his chief of staff after revelations that Peter Mandelson was appointed US ambassador while still linked to Jeffrey Epstein. Labour MPs, including Simon Opher and Harriet Harman, say Starmer was misled and urge a change of advisers. New documents will soon be released, and the drama could force Starmer to resign, though some ministers say he should stay.
