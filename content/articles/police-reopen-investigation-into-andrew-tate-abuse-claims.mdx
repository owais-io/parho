Hertfordshire police have opened a new inquiry into sexual abuse allegations against self‑proclaimed misogynist Andrew Tate. The decision follows criticism that a 2014‑2015 investigation was mishandled and an independent office now looks at possible police misconduct. Three women, who filed claims in 2013‑2015, say the case was wrongly closed in 2019. Tate denies the accusations, but a civil trial is set for June.
