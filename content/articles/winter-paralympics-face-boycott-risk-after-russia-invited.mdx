The Winter Paralympics in Milan are set to start, but inviting 10 Russian and Belarusian athletes has sparked anger across Europe. Ukraine, the Netherlands, Finland and others plan to boycott or skip the opening ceremony. The International Paralympic Committee is trying to keep the Games together, but the dispute shows how politics can spill into sport this year’s games for athletes.
