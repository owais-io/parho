Aston Villa beat FC Basel 2‑0 in the Europa League, thanks to a tidy finish from midfielder Youri Tielemans. The win keeps Villa third in the group and close to the top of the standings. Manager Unai Emery praised the importance of the competition and said the team will keep playing consistently. Villa used a mix of regulars and new players and will return to Premier League action against West Ham next week.
