A 56‑year‑old Minnesota woman died after a shark bit off her arm while swimming near St. Croix in the U.S. Virgin Islands. The attack happened on Jan. 8 and local lifeguards and bystanders rushed her to shore, but she lost too much blood and was taken to a hospital. She later died from her injuries. Shark attacks are rare—only a handful happen worldwide each year.
