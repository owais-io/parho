President Trump said he was having “very good” talks with Iran, but the Iranian government denied any negotiations. Trump said the talks could stop a planned U.S. attack on Iranian oil facilities and keep the Hormuz Strait open, which could calm oil markets. The talks appear to involve Pakistan and other regional allies, and U.S. diplomats like Jared Kushner are leading them. Iran’s officials say the claims are false and that the U.S. is just manipulating markets.
