UNRWA, the UN agency that helps Palestinian refugees, has been hit hard in Gaza. Israel has shut down offices, killed staff, and damaged buildings. Claims that the agency is biased have been spread, but they are false. No strong global help has come, and the agency might collapse. Its loss would hurt refugees and regional peace. The writer urges worldwide action to protect the agency.
