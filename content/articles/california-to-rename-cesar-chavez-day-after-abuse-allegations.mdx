California lawmakers are changing Cesar Chavez Day to Farmworkers Day after new claims that the labor leader sexually abused women. The state is also looking at renaming streets, parks, schools and buildings that honor Chavez. Some places may be named after Dolores Huerta instead. The move comes after media reports of abuse, and the Chavez family has expressed sadness. The change is part of a wider debate about how public spaces should reflect history.
