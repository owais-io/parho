Prince William has spoken out on the rising rate of suicide among UK men, calling it a “national catastrophe”. He urged more men to talk openly about mental health and shared his own struggles, saying that understanding emotions helps prevent crises. He announced a £1 million donation to a National Suicide Prevention Network and highlighted the role of charities and support lines in saving lives.
