Lando Norris, last year’s rookie champion, is ready to fight for a second title. He says he still wants to win, but he races in his own style – less aggressive than Red Bull’s Max Verstappen. With new rules that change how cars use energy, Norris says the season will feel more chaotic. He’s focused on mastering the new power‑unit and battery system before the season starts in Melbourne.
