Newcastle drew 1‑1 with Paris Saint‑Germain at the Parc des Princes, keeping their hopes of Champions League progress alive. Kvaratskhelia gave the hosts the early goal, and Joe Willock leveled before half‑time. A penalty for PSG was saved, and the match ended with both sides missing out on automatic qualification. Newcastle will join the knockout phase in February, while PSG will face either Qarabag or Monaco. The game showed Newcastle’s resilience and PSG’s lack of fire.
