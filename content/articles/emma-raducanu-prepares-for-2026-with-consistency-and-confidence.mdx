Emma Raducanu, Britain's top female tennis player, says she feels ready for a strong 2026 season. She focuses on consistent practice and enjoying the game. The 23‑year‑old has improved her fitness and teamwork with coach Francisco Roig. She will start the United Cup in Australia, where she will play against Japan. With Jack Draper out, the British team is adjusting, but Raducanu remains optimistic and excited.
