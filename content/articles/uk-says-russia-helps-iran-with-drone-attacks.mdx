UK defence secretary John Healey says Russia is guiding Iranian drone tactics. After Iranian drones hit a western base in Erbil, Iraq, he claims Russian advisers taught Iranian pilots how to fly them lower, making them more lethal. The drones are Shahed models, used by Russia in Ukraine. The attacks raise worries about the Strait of Hormuz, where Iran may try to mine the waterway, pushing oil prices up. The UK has only one warship in the area.
