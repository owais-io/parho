It celebrates Trish Savill and the Wombles groups, showing how community litter‑picking helps tidy streets, teaches kids and adults about the planet, and pushes politicians to act. Volunteers worldwide have lifted nearly 880,000 tonnes of rubbish in 212 countries, proving that a single clean‑up can inspire policy and hope for a cleaner future. Even local Green councillors now join the picks, proving that real change starts with hands on the ground.
