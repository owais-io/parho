The BBC Sports Personality of the Year award lists several standout athletes. Goalkeeper Hannah Hampton helped England win Euro 2025 with record‑breaking saves and also guided Chelsea to a treble. Chloe Kelly turned her comeback story into Euro 2025 heroics and a Champions League win. Rugby star Ellie Kildunne broke ground as the first women’s player on the shortlist with a World Cup win. Other nominees include darts legend Luke Littler, golfer Rory McIlroy and F1 champion Lando Norris.
