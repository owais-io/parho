The Vancouver Whitecaps are in trouble. The team is being sold, but no buyers want to invest because they earn too little money and have a bad stadium deal. The club’s lease at BC Place gives them only a small share of ticket sales, and the stadium is booked for many other events. MLS and the club warn that the team might relocate or even fold if things don’t change.
