Mary Kostakidis, former SBS presenter, was sued by the Zionist Federation of Australia (ZFA) over two social media posts about Hezbollah leader Hassan Nasrallah. ZFA says her posts violated the Racial Discrimination Act and hurt Jewish Australians. Kostakidis argues the case is a campaign to silence her criticism of Israel, and that “Zionist” isn’t a protected group. The court will hear the case in December.
