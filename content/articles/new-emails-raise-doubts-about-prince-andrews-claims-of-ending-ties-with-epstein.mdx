A fresh batch of Jeffrey Epstein emails, released by the U.S. House, shows Prince Andrew saying he was still in contact with Epstein in 2011, four months after he later claimed to have cut ties. The emails also confirm a photo of Andrew with a 17‑year‑old woman, supporting Virginia Giuffre’s allegations. Andrew has denied any wrongdoing, but the documents undermine his story and add to the controversy surrounding his relationship with Epstein.
