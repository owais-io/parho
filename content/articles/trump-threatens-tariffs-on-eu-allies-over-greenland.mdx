Trump announced he will impose tariffs on eight countries that support Greenland if they don’t sell it to the U.S. The move hurts the EU, which had signed a trade deal with the U.S. that many see as unfair. European leaders—from left to right—are angry and debate whether to reject the deal. The story shows how the EU is struggling to balance its alliance with the U.S. and its own interests.
