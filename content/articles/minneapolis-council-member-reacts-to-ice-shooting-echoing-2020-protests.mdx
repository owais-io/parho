Minneapolis council member Soren Stevenson, who lost an eye during the 2020 protests, is dealing with the recent ICE shooting of a local resident, Renee Good. His neighborhood feels scared, with many stores closed and people staying inside. Stevenson says the community is pulling together, helping each other and showing that they won’t give up. He wants the city to investigate the killing, keep ICE out, and protect residents.
