Toyota is using a game platform called Policy Drivers to get its U.S. workers to contact lawmakers and fight environmental rules it says are too strict. Employees play games, earn points, and win prizes for listening to company messages and sending letters to Congress. The company says it wants to protect its hybrid cars, but critics say the move pressures climate policy. Toyota says participation is voluntary, but the game makes lobbying feel like fun.
