Even though Trump says talks with Iran are “very good,” fighting in the Middle East is still rising. Iran fired missiles at Israel, Gulf states and Iraq, while U.S. and Israeli jets struck targets in Tehran and other places. Tehran says there are no talks, but countries like Pakistan and Egypt are trying to open lines of communication. The war has pushed oil prices above $100 a barrel, hurting global supply.
