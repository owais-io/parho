Prime Minister Keir Starmer is set to name a new ambassador to Washington from three candidates: Varun Chandra, Christian Turner and Nigel Casey. The pick comes as UK‑US ties are tense, especially over Trump’s push for a quick Ukraine peace deal and his criticism of European leaders. Starmer has already met with each finalist and plans to announce before year‑end. The role is key because the UK wants a steady partner amid rising US‑Europe tensions.
