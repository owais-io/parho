Actors, parents and MPs have created a group called Save Our Children’s Rights to ask Prime Minister Keir Starmer to keep the legal protections that help children with special educational needs (SEND) get the support they need. The group, backed by actress Sally Phillips, fears the upcoming schools white paper could cut how children qualify for extra help and remove the legal plans that set out their support. They have collected 130,000 signatures for a petition to Downing Street.
