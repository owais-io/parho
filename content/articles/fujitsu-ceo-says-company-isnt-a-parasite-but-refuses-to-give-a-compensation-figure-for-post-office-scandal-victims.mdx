Fujitsu’s European boss told MPs the company isn’t a parasite, but he still won’t give a figure for the money owed to Post Office Horizon scandal victims. The firm has made hundreds of millions from UK contracts while the government estimates payouts could cost £1.8bn. Labour MP Liam Byrne said Fujitsu was exploiting state contracts. Fujitsu said it would stop if the government demanded, and will set compensation only after the final inquiry report.
