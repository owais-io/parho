Labour’s NEC chair, Shabana Mahmood, says the party must stay calm and avoid drama while deciding if Andy Burnham, the Greater Manchester mayor, can give up his post and run for the Gorton and Denton seat. The NEC will meet in a small officers group to decide if he can stand, which could trigger a costly mayoral by‑election. Mahmood praises Burnham’s skills and says he can help the party, but stresses unity.
