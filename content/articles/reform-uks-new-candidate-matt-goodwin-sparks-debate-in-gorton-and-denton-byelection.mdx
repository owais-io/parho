Matt Goodwin, chosen by Reform UK for the Gorton and Denton byelection, is known for his hard‑right, anti‑immigration views. His selection turns the contest into a battle over race and belonging. Labour urges unity behind its own candidate, while the Greens target Goodwin’s record and focus on hope. Smaller parties like the Workers Party also join. How the main parties respond will shape the seat’s future.
