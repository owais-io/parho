After being removed as ambassador because of ties to Jeffrey Epstein, Peter Mandelson’s documents show that Prime Minister Keir Starmer let him take a new role and offered a £75,000 severance, even though advisors warned of risks. The papers reveal that Starmer ignored concerns about Mandelson’s links to Epstein, a lobbying firm, and his push for closer ties with China. The leaks could hurt Starmer’s reputation and make his party question his judgment.
