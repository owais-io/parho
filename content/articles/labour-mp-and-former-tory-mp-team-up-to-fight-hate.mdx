Labour MP Gurinder Singh Josan and former Tory MP Kris Hopkins, both volunteers with the charity Hope Unlimited, work together to stop hate and racism. They believe strong community spaces can keep people from falling into populist politics. After seeing racism grow in the 2000s and 2010s, they now give grants to local hubs where young people can meet, get jobs and share food. Their aim is to build hope, tolerance and trust in divided towns.
