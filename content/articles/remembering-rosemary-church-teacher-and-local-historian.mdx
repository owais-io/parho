Rosemary Church, 86, passed away after a life of teaching and preserving Faringdon’s history. She taught in Swindon and founded the Faringdon & District Archaeological and Historical Society in 1978, cataloguing graves, transcribing records, and publishing guides and photo collections. Outside work, she knits, makes jam and famous Christmas puddings. She left a legacy of community history resources, her family, and friends.
