Liam Rosenior has made it clear that Chelsea midfielder Enzo Fernández will not move to Paris Saint‑Germain or Real Madrid. Fernández has a long‑term deal until 2032 and has impressed under both Maresca and Rosenior, even playing hard while ill. Chelsea are not looking to sell him and are instead focused on keeping the squad healthy as they prepare for Champions League matches.
