A historic Queensland pub that was the filming location of the 1986 hit *Crocodile Dundee* has been sold to a new owner. 33‑year‑old Angus Brodie, a local cattleman, bought the Walkabout Creek hotel in McKinlay. He says he wants to keep the film’s legacy while making the bar more welcoming for families and travellers. The pub is still called the Crocodile pub, but it now hosts a mix of locals and tourists.
