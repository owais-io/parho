Michael Steele, former chair of the Republican National Committee, claims Trump got U.S. institutions to surrender quickly by targeting law firms, universities and other groups. He says voters will punish Trump in the 2024 mid‑terms and that Democrats should aim to flip 30‑35 seats. Steele urges Republicans to hold Trump’s allies accountable and warns the party must regroup once his influence fades.
