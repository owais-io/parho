Federal immigration agents seized a New York City council employee during a routine check‑in. City leaders, including Mayor Zohran Mamdani, say the move is an attack on democracy and have asked for the worker’s immediate release. The employee is a legal, work‑authorized immigrant until 2026. New York is fighting the detainment, while other states see similar ICE raids and protests, especially after a recent officer shooting in Minneapolis.
