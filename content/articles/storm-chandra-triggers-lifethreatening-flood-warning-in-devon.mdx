Storm Chandra dumped heavy rain and strong winds across south‑west England, prompting a red flood warning for the River Otter in Devon. The river reached record highs, forcing residents to move cars, pets and family, and fire crews rescued people from flooded vehicles. Schools shut, roads and rail links closed, and many areas were cut off by fallen trees. The storm also caused widespread power outages and travel chaos across the UK.
