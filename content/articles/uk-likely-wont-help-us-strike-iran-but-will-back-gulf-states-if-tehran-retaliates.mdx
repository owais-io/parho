Britain says it won’t support a US attack on Iran. Instead, it has moved Typhoon jets to Qatar to help the Gulf country defend against Iranian drones and missiles. The move was requested by Qatar and shows the UK is ready to back allies if Iran retaliates. The UK focuses on stopping Iran from getting nuclear weapons and is careful to follow international law.
