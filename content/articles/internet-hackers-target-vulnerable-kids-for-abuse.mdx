UK charity warns that a hacking community, called the Com, is preying on vulnerable children. Teenagers and young adults use Discord, Telegram and gaming sites to groom kids for sexual abuse, self‑harm and even live‑streamed attacks. The group also hacks big retailers. The report calls for governments, law‑enforcement and tech firms to act quickly. It highlights the danger to children with economic or mental health struggles.
