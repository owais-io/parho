Senator Kyrsten Sinema has been shown to have paid nearly $9,000 to Matthew Ammel, a former security guard she is alleged to have had an affair with. The payments were listed as payroll in her campaign committee’s filings. Ammel has received more than $128,000 from Sinema over the past year. His estranged wife has sued Sinema, claiming the senator’s gifts and travel ruined their marriage. Sinema, who left Congress last year, now works for a lobbying firm.
