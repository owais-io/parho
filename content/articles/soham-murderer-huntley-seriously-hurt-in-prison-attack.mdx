Ian Huntley, the man convicted of killing two 10‑year‑old girls in 2002, was badly hurt when he was attacked inside HMP Frankland in County Durham. A metal pole was used, knocking him unconscious. He was rushed to hospital and is now in a serious but stable condition. Police are investigating the assault, which happened on Thursday morning. Huntley is serving a life sentence for the murders.
