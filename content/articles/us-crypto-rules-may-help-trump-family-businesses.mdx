U.S. regulators announced new rules that treat most cryptocurrency tokens as commodities instead of securities, easing oversight and disclosure. The SEC and CFTC say only blockchain‑based securities stay under strict scrutiny. Industry experts say the move could bring more institutional money into the market and may give Trump family projects like the $Trump meme coin and World Liberty Financial’s tokens an advantage. Critics warn the gap leaves users less protected.
