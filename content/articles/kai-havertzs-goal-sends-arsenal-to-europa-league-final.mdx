Arsenal beat Chelsea 3‑2 after losing the first leg, thanks to a late goal from former Chelsea striker Kai Havertz, sending them to the Europa League final. The Gunners struggled early, missing key players, but their defence held as Chelsea pushed hard. Havertz broke the deadlock in stoppage time. Arteta’s team now face Manchester City in the final, hoping for a trophy after years of disappointment.
