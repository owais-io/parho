The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) stopped about seven big grants to the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) after the AAP spoke out against health secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr. The cuts hit projects that help kids with autism, sudden infant death, and other health issues. The AAP says the money will hurt families and plans to fight the decision. HHS said the AAP’s language didn’t match its priorities.
