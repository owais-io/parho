Flu cases in England are at a record high, and the NHS says anyone with flu or cold symptoms should wear a mask in public. The government says wearing a mask is a choice. People have mixed reactions: some say it’s a small inconvenience and helps others, while others think it’s unnecessary or uncomfortable. Some have sensory problems that make masks impossible, and many are unsure about the real benefit of masks.
