A Trump order says states can’t regulate AI, backing big tech. This move clashes with voters who want more rules, as polls show 70% favor regulation. The order forces politicians to pick sides before the 2026 midterms. Some states fight AI data centers because of pollution and costs. Big names like DeSantis, Sanders, Tlaib, and Klobuchar have voiced concerns. Candidates can use AI policy to win voters.
