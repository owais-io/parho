Prime Minister Keir Starmer says the government‑issued phone of former chief of staff Morgan McSweeney was indeed stolen and reported to the police. He rejects claims that the loss was used to push for documents on Peter Mandelson’s ambassadorial appointment. MPs want those papers, but no backup was made, raising concerns over device security.
