Concacaf’s president, Victor Montagliani, receives more than $3 million a year, mostly as bonuses, for what the organization says is only five hours of work each week. The tax filing shows a $2.1 million salary plus almost $900 k in extra payments. The total makes him the highest‑paid official in Concacaf. Other top officials also earn millions, while the U.S. Soccer president is paid $150,000 for five hours a week. Concacaf has not explained why the work hours are so low.
