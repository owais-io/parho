A new poll shows that more than half of U.S. adults think Donald Trump is making the country worse. 55 % say his actions are harmful, up from the same time last year. Democrats largely agree, while Republicans are split. Confidence in the checks‑and‑balances system fell to 32 %. Even many Republicans now question Trump’s mental fitness and ethics. The poll was taken right before his State of the Union speech.
