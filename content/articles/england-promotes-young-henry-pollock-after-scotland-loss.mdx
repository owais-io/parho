After a 31‑20 defeat to Scotland, England coach Steve Borthwick shook up the squad for a must‑win game against Ireland. He gave 21‑year‑old Henry Pollock a start, hoping his energy will lift the team. Other changes include Tom Curry and Ollie Lawrence returning to the front row, Tommy Freeman back on the wing, and Marcus Smith moving to the bench. The move is a clear effort to rebuild confidence after the Murrayfield setback.
