The T20 World Cup in Sri Lanka and India has lost a team: Bangladesh. The Bangladesh Cricket Board said the country’s players would not travel to India because of security concerns. The ICC replaced Bangladesh with Scotland at the last minute. The move follows a row where a Bangladeshi fast bowler was dropped from an Indian IPL team, and political tensions between the two countries have been high. The decision shows how politics can affect cricket.
