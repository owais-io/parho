Brighton won 2‑1 at the City Ground, with Maxim De Cuyper opening the scoring and Stefanos Tzimas adding a late goal after a mistake by Forest’s Morato. It was Brighton’s first win against Forest in over a year, moving them up to fifth in the Premier League. Forest had errors and failed to take chances, while Brighton’s coach Jürgen Hürzeler praised the team’s performance, showing they can compete again.
