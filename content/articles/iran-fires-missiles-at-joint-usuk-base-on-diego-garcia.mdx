Iran launched two missiles toward the joint US‑UK base on Diego Garcia after Britain said it could help the US strike Iranian sites. The missiles missed, one was shot down. Tehran says the UK move puts its people at risk and will defend itself. The US and UK have allowed limited defensive use of British bases while the UK faces criticism over the decision.
