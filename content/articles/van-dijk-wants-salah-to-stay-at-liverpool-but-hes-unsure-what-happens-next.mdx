Virgil van Dijk says he hopes Mohamed Salah remains at Liverpool but admits he doesn’t know what will happen. Salah was back in the squad after a fallout with coach Arne Slot, played in the win over Brighton, and will leave for the Africa Cup of Nations, possibly missing more than a month. Van Dijk stresses the team is united and will keep moving forward, even if the future is uncertain.
