Staff at Royal Stoke University Hospital are fighting a double crisis. Flu cases have hit record highs, and resident doctors are on strike. All 1,178 beds are full, with patients stuck outside in ambulances and in corridors. The hospital is on its highest pressure level, just shy of a critical incident. Doctors and nurses rush to move patients out, use virtual wards, and pull extra staff to keep people safe.
