The UK has said it is not in talks with Argentina to lift a ban that stops British companies from selling weapons to Argentina. Argentina’s president Javier Milei claimed the governments were discussing the restriction, but a UK spokesperson denied any such talks. The UK also reminded that the Falkland Islands, which Argentina briefly invaded in 1982, remain British territory after a 2013 vote by the islanders to stay part of the UK.
