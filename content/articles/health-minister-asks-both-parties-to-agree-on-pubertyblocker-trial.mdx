Health minister Wes Streeting wrote to opposition leader Kemi Badenoch, urging both sides to keep a shared approach to gender‑identity care before a new puberty‑blocker trial for children. He said the £10.7 m study, run by King’s College London, is needed to see if the drugs help, even though evidence so far is weak and some worry about side‑effects. He wants to move past politics, focus on evidence and child safety.
