Texas and Florida filed a lawsuit against the FDA to stop the abortion pill mifepristone after the agency approved a generic version. The states claim the FDA didn’t fully test the drug’s safety and that the approval was illegal. They also challenge the original 2000 approval and criticize rules that made the pill easier to get. The case is part of a wider push to limit abortion pills after Roe v. Wade was overturned.
