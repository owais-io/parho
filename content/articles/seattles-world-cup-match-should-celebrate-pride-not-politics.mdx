Seattle is hosting a World Cup match that was set to celebrate LGBTQ+ Pride. But the draw paired it with Egypt and Iran, where gay people face severe punishment. The teams protested, but the local committee said the Pride events would go on. The writer says the real World Cup is about local people, culture and fun, not just money or politics, and warns the 2026 tournament could be very exclusive if this spirit is lost.
