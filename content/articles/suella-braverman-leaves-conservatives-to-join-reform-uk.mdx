Suella Braverman, the former home secretary, has left the Conservative Party to join Reform UK. She was dismissed by Liz Truss and later by Rishi Sunak after accusations over her conduct and criticism of police at protests. Crace’s piece ridicules her decision, calling it a political stunt and highlights her anti‑immigration stance, support for veterans, and claims of patriotism. The article portrays her move as a self‑serving career play.
