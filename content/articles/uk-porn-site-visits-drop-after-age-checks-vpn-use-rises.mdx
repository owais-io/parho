In the UK, visits to porn sites fell after age‑verification rules went live on July 25. The most popular site, Pornhub, saw 1.5 million fewer visits in August, and other top sites dropped 18‑27 percent. But the use of virtual private networks (VPNs) to bypass the rules jumped from 650,000 to more than 1.4 million users, then slowly fell to 900,000. Ofcom says teens are more likely to report harmful content now, and the government is reviewing porn laws.
