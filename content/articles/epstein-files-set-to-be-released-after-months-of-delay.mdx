After a long wait, the Department of Justice is starting to make public a huge pile of documents about Jeffrey Epstein. The release is required by a new law that the U.S. Congress passed last year. Trump had said he would release the files but stalled, causing anger from his supporters. The documents include photos and paperwork that could show how Epstein worked with powerful people. Some sensitive information will stay hidden to protect victims and keep investigations fair.
