Senator Elizabeth Warren warned that President Trump’s attempts to take control of the Federal Reserve would damage the U.S. and its global standing. Speaking at the National Press Club, she criticized Trump’s push to override the Fed’s independence and said it could harm the economy. Warren also urged Democrats to focus on ordinary families, rebuild trust, and hold Trump accountable for rising costs and corporate abuses, calling for policies that lower everyday expenses.
