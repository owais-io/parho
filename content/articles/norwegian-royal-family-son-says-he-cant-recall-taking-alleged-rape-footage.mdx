At an Oslo court, Marius Borg Høiby – son of Crown Princess Mette‑Marit – said he can’t remember taking the photos and videos that prosecutors say show him raping a woman at a royal house. He denies the most serious charges and faces up to 16 years in jail. The case adds pressure to the Norwegian royal family amid scrutiny of the princess’s past ties. Victim claims she was drugged and unconscious during the incident.
