Joe Kent, the head of the National Counterterrorism Center, quit because he said the U.S. was going to war with Iran on false information. He blamed Israel and an American lobby for pushing the war, claiming Iran posed no real threat. Kent, a former special‑forces officer who lost his wife in a 2019 bombing, has a history of far‑right ties and lost two congressional races. He said the war would repeat Iraq’s mistakes.
