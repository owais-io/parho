A man in his 40s was arrested after he entered Manchester Central Mosque with an axe. Volunteers and police quickly restrained him, and no one was hurt. Police also searched for a second man who was not caught. The incident happened during Ramadan and drew a large police presence. Mosque volunteers urged worshippers to stay alert and travel in groups.
