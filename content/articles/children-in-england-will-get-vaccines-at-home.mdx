In a new £2 million pilot, NHS health visitors will travel to children’s homes across 12 areas of England to give routine shots, aiming to raise MMR and other vaccine rates that have fallen below 90 %. The scheme targets families who lack GP registration or face travel, language or childcare hurdles. If the trial boosts coverage, the service could launch nationwide by 2027, helping protect kids before primary school.
