Global, a large radio group, bought a majority stake in The Overlap, a popular YouTube football channel run by former Manchester United player Gary Neville. Neville will stay as co‑chair, and the new owners plan to expand the channel into a wider sports media network, similar to Gary Lineker’s podcast brand. The Overlap already pulls in 38 million monthly YouTube views, showing how YouTube sports content is challenging traditional broadcasters.
