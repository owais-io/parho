Brighton’s 18‑year‑old Charalampos Kostoulas scored a stunning bicycle kick in injury time, forcing a 1‑1 draw with Bournemouth after a disputed penalty decision. The referee had initially denied the spot‑kick, then reversed it after a monitor review. Kostoulas’s goal snapped Bournemouth’s long winless away streak, but both teams were frustrated – Bournemouth had injuries and time‑wasting tactics, while Brighton lacked a clear scorer. Brighton’s manager said the side must step up.
