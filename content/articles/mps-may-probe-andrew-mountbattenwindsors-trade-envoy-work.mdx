MPs may probe Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor’s work as a UK trade envoy after his arrest on suspicion of misconduct. The cross‑party committee will meet next Tuesday to consider whether his decade‑long role, which saw him use royal connections and spend public money on lavish trips, was inappropriate. The investigation will look at his links to repressive regimes, his friendship with Jeffrey Epstein, and whether he misused public resources.
