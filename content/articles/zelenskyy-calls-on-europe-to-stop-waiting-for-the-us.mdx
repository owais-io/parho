At the World Economic Forum in Davos, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy blasted European leaders for being passive and waiting for the United States to act on Ukraine and other crises. He called them “Greenland mode” after a US push to take control of Greenland, and urged Europe to take stronger steps against Russia, build a united defence and stop relying on the US. Zelenskyy also discussed his meeting with Trump and the chance for peace talks.
