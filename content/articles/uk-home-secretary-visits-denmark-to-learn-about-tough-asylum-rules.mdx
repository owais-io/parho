UK Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood spent two days in Denmark looking at the strict asylum camp Sjælsmark. She saw how rejected refugees live under curfew and strict rules. Mahmood said the UK should adopt tough policies to keep services from being overburdened. She blamed the rise of the right‑wing party for forcing hard‑line measures. Critics say Denmark’s camps hurt refugees’ chances to integrate. The visit comes after a local election loss for Labour.
