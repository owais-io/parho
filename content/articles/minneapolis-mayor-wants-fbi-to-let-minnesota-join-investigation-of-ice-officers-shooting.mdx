Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey has asked the FBI to allow Minnesota’s police and prosecutors to help investigate the fatal shooting of Renee Good by an ICE officer. He says the Trump administration jumped too quickly to call the incident “domestic terrorism” and that state officials should have a say. Frey also criticized the FBI’s and federal officials’ statements and highlighted new video footage that shows Good calmly talking to the officer before he fired.
