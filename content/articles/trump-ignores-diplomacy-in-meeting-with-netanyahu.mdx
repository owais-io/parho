Trump met Israeli prime minister Benjamin Netanyahu at Mar‑a‑Lago and spoke in a very casual, dismissive way. He said he would back Netanyahu if Israel attacked Iran and even talked about getting the Israeli president to pardon him for a corruption case. Trump also repeated a Russian claim about Ukraine and praised Turkey’s president. Netanyahu stayed polite but praised Trump as Israel’s best U.S. ally. Both leaders also touched on the Gaza peace plan and how to deal with Hamas.
