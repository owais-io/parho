A 15‑year‑old boy was sentenced to a minimum of 13 years in prison after he stabbed and killed 12‑year‑old Leo Ross in Birmingham. The attack happened on 21 January 2025 as Leo was walking home from school. In court, the judge said the teenager had a history of violent attacks and the case was not a random act. Leo’s family said the sentence didn’t bring justice.
