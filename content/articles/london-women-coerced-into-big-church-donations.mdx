Two London professionals, Sarah and Jennifer, said they gave away most of their money to the Universal Church of the Kingdom of God, hoping to receive health and wealth blessings. The Fundraising Regulator found the church broke its rules, taking large donations from a woman with mental‑health problems and failing to protect vulnerable donors. The church denies the claims and says it runs food banks and other charity work. The women want tougher rules for high‑control churches.
