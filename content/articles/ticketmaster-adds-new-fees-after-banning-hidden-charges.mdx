Ticketmaster removed a hidden processing fee to follow new U.S. rules, but it then raised other fees at many venues. Documents show the company added $2 service charges or similar to keep its revenue. The change happened at public venues across states like California, Arizona and Florida. While the FTC says the move could still mislead consumers, Ticketmaster says it’s simply adjusting to the law. The company also faces an antitrust trial.
