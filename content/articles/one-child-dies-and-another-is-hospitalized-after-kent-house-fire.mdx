A house fire in the village of Hamstreet, near Ashford, claimed the life of one child. A second child and an adult were taken to hospital for treatment, and three firefighters were hospitalized with minor injuries. Firefighters fought the blaze with six engines and an aerial vehicle, and the fire department is investigating the cause. Local support services have been set up for the community.
