Three people were arrested after two incidents outside the Crowborough training camp that houses asylum seekers. Police say the events involved shouting, striking a vehicle and blocking entry. The camp currently holds about 27 men, with the Home Office planning to move up to 500 men out of temporary hotels. Local residents and the Wealden council are unhappy and may take legal action. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood pledged to defend the policy.
