Manchester United will meet on Saturday to decide who takes over as caretaker until the end of the season. The main candidates are former coach Ole Gunnar Solskjær and former midfielder Michael Carrick. Current interim boss Darren Fletcher could also be considered. Solskjær, who led the team before, is the favourite, but Carrick is ready to work with him. The club’s executives will speak with each candidate at training before making a final decision.
