Two teenagers died in a crash between an e‑bike and a motorcycle in Greenbank, south of Brisbane. The bike was heading the opposite way when the motorbike, passing a car, hit it around 9 pm. Investigators say the e‑bike had no lights. The incident follows a state inquiry that recommends banning kids under 16 on e‑bikes and e‑scooters. The new rules would cap speed, require licences, and add insurance. Police are asking anyone with footage to come forward.
