Harlequins were up 17‑12 at half‑time but collapsed in the second half, giving up 31 unanswered points to Sale. The visiting side, led by flanker Jacques Vermeulen who scored twice, took control and ran five more tries, finishing with a 27‑12 win. Harlequins coach Jason Gilmore criticized the squad’s lack of drive, while Sale’s director praised the team’s resilience and the owners’ support.
