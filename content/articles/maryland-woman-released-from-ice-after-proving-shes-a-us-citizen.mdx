Maryland woman Dulce Consuelo Diaz Morales was held by ICE for 25 days. She says she was born in the U.S. and has papers to prove it. Her lawyers argued the case, and a judge stopped her deportation while the case is still pending. ICE says she is a Mexican citizen and didn’t have proper papers. Diaz Morales is now free, has dual citizenship, and will attend future immigration appointments.
