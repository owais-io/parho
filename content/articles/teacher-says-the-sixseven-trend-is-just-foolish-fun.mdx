She explains that the popular hashtag ‘six‑seven’ is just a silly trend made to get kids to click links and make money for influencers. In her classroom she says if you don’t know why something is funny, don’t spread it. The teacher says the word doesn’t help hope or learning; kindness and honesty are better ways to inspire students. She also worries that social media trends can distract kids from real learning.
