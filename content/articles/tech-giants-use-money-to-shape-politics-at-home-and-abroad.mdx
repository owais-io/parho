Tech giants are using money to shape politics at home and abroad. In India, Google, Microsoft and others are investing billions in AI infrastructure to help the country become an AI hub and keep it from being dominated by the US or China. At the same time, Californian tech billionaires are pouring tens of millions into campaigns, lobbying for policies that favor their businesses and fight billionaire taxes. The move shows how tech firms use wealth to influence policy.
