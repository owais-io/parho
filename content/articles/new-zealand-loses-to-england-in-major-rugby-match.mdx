In a tough game, New Zealand fell to England, showing cracks that hint the All Blacks may not be as intimidating as before. England’s aggressive play and solid defense, led by Sam Underhill, made it hard for New Zealand, who started strong but couldn’t maintain the lead. The loss follows a 43‑10 defeat to South Africa and raises doubts about the All Blacks’ future dominance. Rankings stay unchanged, but England edges Ireland in the standings.
