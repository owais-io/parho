Harry Brook celebrated his century by doing a Stone Cold Steve Austin move, and England captain Joe Root praised his calm leadership during the win over Sri Lanka. Brook’s celebration was a playful nod to the wrestler, showing humor while the team chased 358. Root highlighted Brook’s composure under pressure and teamwork, saying the partnership with him helped England secure the series.
