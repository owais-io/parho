British tennis stars Jack Draper and Emma Raducanu are still battling injuries as the 2026 season starts. Raducanu missed her first match after a foot injury but played in a team event, showing she’s recovering. Draper had to skip the Australian Open because of a bone bruise in his left arm and has been out for months. Both hope to be fully fit for upcoming tournaments and the Davis Cup.
