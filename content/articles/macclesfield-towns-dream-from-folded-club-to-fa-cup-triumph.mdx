Macclesfield Town was shut in 2020, but a local businessman bought the stadium and turned it into a new club. With a 4‑G pitch, a bar and a community focus, the team quickly rose through the leagues. New manager John Rooney led the side to an upset over Crystal Palace in the FA Cup. The win was a celebration for a town that rebuilt itself from the ground up.
