At the Munich Security Conference, Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK and other European countries must increase their defence spending and share more of NATO’s burden. He warned against relying too much on the US and urged stronger cooperation on European security and defence procurement. Starmer also criticised politicians who favour turning inward, saying Britain’s safety depends on a united Europe and a stronger NATO partnership.
