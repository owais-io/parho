Dana Eden, a 52‑year‑old producer who helped create the hit series *Tehran*, was found dead in a hotel room in Athens where the fourth season is being filmed. Greek police say the death was a suicide and have ruled out any foul play or political motives, despite earlier rumours linking it to Iran. The investigation will include a post‑mortem and footage reviews.
