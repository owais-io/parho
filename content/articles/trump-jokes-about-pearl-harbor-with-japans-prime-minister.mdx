During a visit to the Oval Office, President Trump laughed at Japan’s Prime Minister Sanae Takaichi, comparing the U.S. surprise attack on Iran to Japan’s surprise attack on Pearl Harbor in 1941. The joke shocked the Japanese prime minister, who had to explain Japan’s limits on military action. Trump’s comment drew cheers from his supporters but criticism from reporters and critics. The meeting also covered the need for help protecting the Strait of Hormuz.
