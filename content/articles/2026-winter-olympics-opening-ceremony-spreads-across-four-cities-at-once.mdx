At the 2026 Winter Olympics, the opening ceremony ran across Milan’s San Siro, Cortina, Livigno and Predazzo at the same time. The four venues told one story, making the event feel both huge and personal. The “Armonia” idea let athletes from different sports appear in the places where they compete, cutting travel and giving a fresh, fashion‑forward look. The U.S. delegation drew cheers, but a clip of the vice‑president caused boos. Nearby, activists protested the Games’ cost and politics.
