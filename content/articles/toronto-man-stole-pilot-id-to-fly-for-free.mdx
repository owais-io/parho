Toronto man Dallas Pokornik allegedly pretended to be a pilot to trick airlines into giving him hundreds of free flights over four years. He used a fake employee ID from a Toronto carrier, even though he hadn’t worked there since 2019. Prosecutors say he also tried to sit in cockpit jumpseats reserved for pilots. He was arrested in Panama, extradited to the US, and faces up to 20 years in prison and a $250,000 fine.
