Israel's recent airstrikes on Gaza have killed at least 30 people, including three children, after striking a police station, an apartment and tent villages. The attacks happened even though a ceasefire is in place, and Israel says they were responding to armed men from a tunnel in Rafah. Hospitals report the death toll keeps rising as the region's infrastructure is destroyed. The Rafah crossing is set to open, but Israel plans to allow only about 150 Palestinians through each day.
