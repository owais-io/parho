In a joyous moment at the Euros, Jude Bellingham scored a stunning bicycle kick to level the match against Slovakia. Fans erupted in celebration, some even getting carried away and hurting each other in the excitement. The writer reflects on how the goal sparked intense emotions, his own guilty feeling about celebrating too hard, and how football can bring people together. He argues that it’s fine to celebrate passionately, even if society sometimes sees fans as unruly.
