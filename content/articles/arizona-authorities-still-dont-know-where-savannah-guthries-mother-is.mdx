Arizona police say they still don’t know where Nancy Guthrie, the 84‑year‑old mother of TV host Savannah Guthrie, is after she vanished from her Tucson home. The case is treated as a crime scene, with signs of forced entry and blood found. Detectives, the FBI, and local volunteers are combing the area, but no leads have emerged. A $2,500 reward is on offer and the family is urging anyone with clues to come forward.
