Robert F. Kennedy Jr. announced a new plan that will require medical schools to teach 40 hours of nutrition courses by the fall of 2026. He says doctors need better nutrition knowledge so patients aren’t treated only with drugs. Schools that refuse could lose federal funding, while those that join will get public praise. So far 53 schools have signed up, though some top universities have declined.
