Mercedes team boss Toto Wolff says other Formula One teams should stop arguing over whether Mercedes' new engine is legal. He insists the engines meet the rules and blames rivals for missing a trick. The dispute centers on a higher compression ratio that could give a small speed advantage. Wolff is ready for protests but believes Mercedes are safe. He wants rivals to work on their own cars instead of complaining.
