Zack Polanski, a Green Party politician, has repeatedly apologised for a 2013 interview where he offered hypnosis to increase a woman's breast size. The BBC could not find the apology interview he claimed to have given the day after the Sun article. In another interview, he spoke of “successful projects” and said he thinks hypnotherapy could change body image, not size. Labour and Green sources say the controversy has faded as he focuses on policy.
