Southern Cross Austereo (SCA) used AI to help write news bulletins. A mistake caused the network to say reporter Dylan Hogarth was a man who hit police with a hammer. The claim aired on several radio shows, but the police had named a different person. SCA immediately apologized, aired a correction, and is investigating the error. The incident highlights how AI can spread wrong info in news.
