Independent journalists are stepping into the gap left by mainstream outlets that have been pressured by the Trump administration. Reporters like Marisa Kabas, who runs the reader‑funded newsletter Handbasket, have won subscribers with exclusive stories. Other independent sites cover trans rights, abortion, and police misconduct—issues often avoided by big media. They face legal risks but see a growing audience that wants trustworthy, untethered news.
