Loughborough University’s Sports Technology Institute has been working with adidas since 2002 to make footballs used in World Cup finals and to design sportswear that keeps athletes warm. Their research on how sweat spreads across the body helped create the “hot pants” used by Team GB at the 2012 Olympics and influenced the Climacool range. The partnership won a 2023 engineering award and is also shaping safer cricket helmets.
