Democratic House leader Hakeem Jeffries said on Sunday that Democrats will stop President Trump from trying to take control of this year's midterm elections. Jeffries warned that Trump’s push to “nationalize” the vote is illegal, noting the Constitution gives states control over elections. He praised Democrats’ success in blocking Trump‑led efforts to federalize the National Guard and redraw districts. Trump has filed lawsuits against states, and the FBI raided a Fulton County election office, seizing ballots.
