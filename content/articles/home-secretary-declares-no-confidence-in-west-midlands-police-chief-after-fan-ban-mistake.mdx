The home secretary said she no longer trusts the West Midlands police chief after a report found the force exaggerated intelligence that led to banning Israeli football fans from a game. The inquiry said the police made mistakes collecting and handling data, and that an AI error was blamed. The secretary also said she wants to regain the power to fire chief constables in future law changes. The case shows a serious lapse in policing oversight.
