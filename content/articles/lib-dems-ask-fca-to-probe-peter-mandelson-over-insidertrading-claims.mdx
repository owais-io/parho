Lib Dem MPs have asked the UK’s financial regulator, the FCA, to look into former business secretary Peter Mandelson. They say he gave Jeffrey Epstein secret government information that could have been used for insider trading. The claims come from emails that show Mandelson sent Epstein details about market‑moving events before they were announced in 2010. Mandelson has resigned from the Labour Party and the House of Lords, and the police are already investigating. The FCA has not yet commented.
