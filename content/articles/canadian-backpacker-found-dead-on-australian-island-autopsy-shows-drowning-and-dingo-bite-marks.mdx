A 19‑year‑old Canadian backpacker, Piper James, was discovered dead on K'gari (Fraser Island) in Queensland, Australia. An autopsy indicates she likely drowned in the sea, but also had injuries from dingo bites. Police are investigating whether the wild dogs pushed her into the water or caused her death. No evidence points to any human involvement, and the case is still under review.
