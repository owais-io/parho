The US and Israel have begun a bombing campaign called “Epic Fury” and “Roaring Lion” after the US ended nuclear talks with Iran. Since the first strike a week ago, more than 1,200 people in Iran have been killed, including a school in Minab. The attacks aim to destroy Iran’s nuclear program and pressure the government to change, but critics say the war is illegal and could spark wider regional conflict.
