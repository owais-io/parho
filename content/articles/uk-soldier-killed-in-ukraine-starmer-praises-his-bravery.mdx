British soldier George Hooley, 28, of the Parachute Regiment, died in Ukraine after a tragic accident while watching a defensive‑equipment test with Ukrainian forces. Keir Starmer called him courageous and honored the nation’s service. This is the first publicly confirmed British death in Ukraine. Britain keeps its small troop presence low to avoid Russian propaganda. The UK may soon join a coalition of 30+ countries to help stabilize Ukraine.
