The Alliance Defending Freedom's international arm, which the SPLC calls a hate group, has announced it will back a South Australian mother who says her daughter was shocked by a school presentation that talked about incest and bestiality. The presentation was run by Headspace, a youth mental‑health program. ADF says it is simply defending parental rights and ‘radical sex‑education’ in schools, not targeting LGBTQ people.
