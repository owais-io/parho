Seven police raids in London, Essex and the Midlands led to the arrest of three men who carried out “highly targeted” attacks on two Pakistani dissidents during the holiday season. The victims, including human‑rights lawyer Mirza Shahzad Akbar, were assaulted in Cambridgeshire and Buckinghamshire, and one was attacked at his home on New Year’s Eve. The men face charges of assault, weapon possession and arson conspiracies, while investigators keep an open mind on motive.
