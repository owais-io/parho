Prince George and his dad, Prince William, spent a day at a London homeless charity, helping make Christmas lunch. They dressed in aprons, tossed veggies, laid out Yorkshire puddings, and talked with volunteers. The visit is part of a tradition for William, who has been volunteering there since 1993. William says these experiences made him aware of people without homes and inspired his Homewards project to end homelessness. The pair also decorated a Christmas tree and enjoyed time with guests.
