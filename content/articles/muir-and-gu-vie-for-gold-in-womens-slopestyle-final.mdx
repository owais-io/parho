During the 2026 Winter Olympics, the women's freestyle skiing slopestyle final saw top athletes compete for medals. Defending champion Mathilde Gremaud led after powerful runs, while China’s Eileen Gu and Canada’s Megan Oldham fought for podium spots. Great Britain’s Kirsty Muir also delivered a solid performance, placing sixth. The event showcased high‑degree tricks, fast rails, and close scores, keeping fans on the edge of their seats.
