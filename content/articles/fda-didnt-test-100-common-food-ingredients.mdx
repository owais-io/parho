A new study by the Environmental Working Group found that more than 100 ingredients used in everyday foods, drinks and supplements were never reviewed for safety by the FDA. Companies rely on the old GRAS rule, which lets them skip testing if an ingredient is “generally recognized as safe.” Some of these unchecked substances could be harmful, sparking calls for stricter regulation and better oversight.
