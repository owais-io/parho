Citizen Lab's new report says Kenyan police used Israeli‑made Cellebrite spyware to break into pro‑democracy activist Boniface Mwangi's phone while it was in their custody last year. The tool could pull every message, photo, password and financial detail from the device. Mwangi, who plans to run for president, says the breach exposed his private life and shows how the tech is abused by governments worldwide. The company denies misuse, but evidence grows.
