Sporting’s new coach, Rui Borges, keeps winning, and he attributes part of it to a cheap Casio watch he never takes off. After the previous coach left for Manchester United, Borges has guided Sporting to a league and cup double last season and is chasing a third straight title. He rose from a small club in Mirandela to the top, keeping his superstitions and strong team spirit intact.
