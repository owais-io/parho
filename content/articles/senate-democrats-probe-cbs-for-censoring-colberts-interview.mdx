Senate Democrat Richard Blumenthal is investigating whether the FCC and Paramount’s CBS prevented Stephen Colbert from airing an interview with Texas Democratic candidate James Talarico. He says the move was a partisan censorship favoring Trump. CBS says new equal‑time rule forced the cut, while Colbert posted the interview on YouTube, which has over 10 million views. The probe also ties into Paramount’s bid to buy Warner Bros, raising free‑speech concerns.
