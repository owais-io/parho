When your car breaks down, you’ll want help that fits what you need and won’t cost too much. In the UK you can buy basic roadside help for around £15 a year, and add extras like getting your car back to home, a spare car, or travel costs. Prices rise with extra cover, so choose only what you’ll use. Many banks and car makers include coverage, and you can compare providers online for the best deal.
