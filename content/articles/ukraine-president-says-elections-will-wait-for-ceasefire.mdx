President Volodymyr Zelenskyy told reporters that Ukraine will hold elections only after a security guarantee is in place, mainly a cease‑fire with Russia. He said that if Russia agreed, hostilities could finish by summer. While the war continues, Kyiv and other cities have faced missile strikes, leading officials to boost air‑defence and protect key infrastructure. Zelenskyy also urged the US to press Russia harder for peace talks.
