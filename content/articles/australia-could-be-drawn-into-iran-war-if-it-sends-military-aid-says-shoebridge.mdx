Greens MP David Shoebridge warns that giving military support to Gulf countries would legally bind Australia in the Iran conflict, calling it a “deception and stealth” move favoring the US. He argues Australia has no strategic interest in the Gulf and should not send troops. The government is reviewing requests, while experts and opposition voices stress the need for clear parliamentary debate and caution against being labeled a war participant.
