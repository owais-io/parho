Eighty people who came to the UK by small boats and are now in a detention centre awaiting a return flight to France are saying they have been treated badly. They say the Home Office has left them without enough rest, medicine, or legal help, and that the conditions have caused serious stress and even self‑harm. The group has asked the UN and human‑rights bodies to look into what’s happening. New UK rules also let authorities take their phones.
