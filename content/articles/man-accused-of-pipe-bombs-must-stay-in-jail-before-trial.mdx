A federal judge said Brian Cole Jr., who is accused of making pipe bombs outside the Democratic and Republican party offices in Washington on the night before the January 6 attack, must stay in jail until his trial. The judge said Cole could pose a danger to the public and that no release conditions would keep people safe. He faces two federal charges. Police found bomb parts in his home and linked his phone and car to the sites.
