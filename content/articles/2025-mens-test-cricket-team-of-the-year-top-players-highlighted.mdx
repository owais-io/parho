The Guardian voted on the best 11 men’s Test cricketers of 2025. Travis Head led with 759 runs, while Shubman Gill topped with 983 runs and 13 votes. England’s Joe Root made history with 11 centuries, India’s KL Rahul and Jasprit Bumrah were key. South Africa’s Temba Bavuma captained a record‑winning side. Australia’s Alex Carey shone behind the stumps. The list shows the season’s biggest scorers, bowlers and leaders in a clear, easy‑to‑read order.
