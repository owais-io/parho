In 2022, a personal trainer discovered her Instagram exploded after a viral video. Today, @carolinescircuits has millions of followers and a best‑selling book, showing that people over 50 are a powerful force on social media. Research shows the 55‑64 age group drives the biggest growth on YouTube and TikTok. Influencers like Valerie Mackay and FitnessGeezer share life tips and fitness advice, building genuine communities and earning trust.
