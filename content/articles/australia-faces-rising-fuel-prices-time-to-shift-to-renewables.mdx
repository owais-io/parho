Australia’s fuel price surge—driven by wars in the Middle East and Ukraine—has pushed many to consider electric cars. The government is slashing A$16.3 billion in fossil‑fuel subsidies this year, and experts say mining subsidies should be cut the hardest. Home solar panels and batteries are already common, but more EV charging spots are needed. These moves could save money, cut emissions, and protect people from future price shocks.
