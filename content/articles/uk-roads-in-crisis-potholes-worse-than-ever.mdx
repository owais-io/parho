The UK’s roads are in crisis. Potholes are bigger, deeper and more dangerous than ever, especially in wet and freezing weather. Drivers and cyclists are stuck, losing tyres, suffering injuries, and facing long queues for repairs. The problem points to poor road maintenance and lack of funds, leaving locals frustrated and stuck without clear solutions or compensation. The article laments that the state isn’t fixing a preventable danger.
