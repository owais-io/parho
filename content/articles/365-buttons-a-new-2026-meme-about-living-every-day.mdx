In 2026, a TikTok trend called “365 buttons” started when user @flylikeadove talked about collecting a button for each day of the year to stay mindful and productive. The idea is simple: each button reminds you to make the most of your time, though the type of button doesn’t matter—just what works for you. The meme sparked millions of videos, but the core message is personal: do what feels right and keep going.
