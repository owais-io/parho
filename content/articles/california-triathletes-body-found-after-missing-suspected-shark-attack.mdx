Firefighters in California recovered the body of 55‑year‑old triathlete Erica Fox on Davenport beach, almost a week after she vanished while swimming from Lovers Point near Monterey. Local media and witnesses had reported a shark with a human body, sparking speculation she was killed by a shark. The Coast Guard, fire, and police conducted a large search before suspending it. Her husband and swim club held a memorial walk; investigators continue to look into the incident.
