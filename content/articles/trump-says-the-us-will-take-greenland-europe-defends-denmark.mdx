Trump said the United States would take Greenland, either by force or a deal, and warned that if the U.S. didn’t, Russia or China would. He mocked Greenland’s weak defense and said NATO would be affected but not in his view. Denmark’s prime minister said the threat was a turning point for the alliance. European leaders are unsure how to counter the move, weighing diplomacy, sanctions, or military options.
