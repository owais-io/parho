The Met Police now requires officers to disclose if they belong to the Freemasons or any similar hierarchy. The move comes after worries that such ties could hide corruption and damage public trust. Most officers back the rule, but the Freemason group says it is unfair and may sue. The new rule doesn’t ban membership, it only asks for a declaration to improve transparency.
