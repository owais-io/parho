Savannah Guthrie, a 54‑year‑old TV anchor, posted on Instagram asking anyone who knows where her 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy, might be to speak up. Nancy has been missing for 15 days, and a glove found near her home carries DNA that could link to a suspect. The FBI will search crime and genealogy databases. Pima County Sheriff says no family members are suspects, no arrests have been made, and the family is cleared.
