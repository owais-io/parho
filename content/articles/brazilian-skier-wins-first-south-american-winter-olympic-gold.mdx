Lucas Pinheiro Braathen made history at the Winter Games by taking gold in the men’s giant slalom, becoming the first South American to win a Winter Olympic medal. He surprised everyone with a fast first run, beat the Swiss champion by a half‑second, and then burst into tears at the finish. His win shows that talent can come from anywhere.
