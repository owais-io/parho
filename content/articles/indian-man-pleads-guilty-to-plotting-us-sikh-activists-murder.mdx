An Indian national, Nikhil Gupta, has pleaded guilty in New York to murder‑for‑hire and money‑laundering charges for planning to kill Sikh activist Gurpatwant Singh Pannun. Gupta was recruited by an Indian government employee and was extradited from the Czech Republic. Pannun, who works for Sikhs for Justice, says the case proves a Modi‑era plot to silence dissent. India denies any role. The case strains US‑India ties.
