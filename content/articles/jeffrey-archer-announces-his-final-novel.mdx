Jeffrey Archer, the famous British author who has sold over 300 million books, said his next book, Adam and Eve, will be the last one he writes. The 85‑year‑old will publish it in October. He explained that the story is bigger than any of his earlier works and he feels he cannot repeat it. Archer plans to stop after this novel, though he might still write short stories. The book mixes love, betrayal and war.
