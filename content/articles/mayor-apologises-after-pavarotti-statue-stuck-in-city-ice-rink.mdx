Pesaro mayor says sorry after a bronze statue of opera singer Luciano Pavarotti got trapped in a Christmas ice rink. The statue, displayed in the town centre, was meant to honour the late tenor, but the rink’s floor cut off its legs and made it look odd. Pavarotti’s widow was angry and upset. The mayor explained the statue can’t be moved safely and that no disrespect was intended.
