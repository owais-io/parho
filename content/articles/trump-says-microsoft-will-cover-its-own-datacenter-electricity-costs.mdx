President Trump announced he is working with tech firms to stop data‑center growth from raising U.S. power bills. He singled out Microsoft first, saying the company must pay its own electricity and property taxes and avoid any rate breaks. Microsoft’s chief explained the plan would also cut water use. Local communities, from Oklahoma to California, worry that big centers drain power, water, and hike costs.
