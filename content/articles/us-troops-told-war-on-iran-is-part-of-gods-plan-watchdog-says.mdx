US military commanders have been using biblical language to justify a war on Iran, says a watchdog. The Military Religious Freedom Foundation received more than 200 complaints from soldiers in all branches, including a NCO who says his commander urged troops that the conflict is “God’s plan” and cited Revelation. The complaints point to growing Christian‑nationalist influence in the armed forces. The Pentagon has not responded.
