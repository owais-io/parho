Delcy Rodríguez has become Venezuela’s interim president after Nicolás Maduro was detained. In her first speech she criticized the US and vowed to stay loyal to Maduro. Washington, led by former President Trump, thinks Rodríguez can be a useful partner and wants her to follow U.S. rules. Rodríguez must juggle support from the military and the Venezuelan people while keeping the U.S. happy. A hard balance ahead.
