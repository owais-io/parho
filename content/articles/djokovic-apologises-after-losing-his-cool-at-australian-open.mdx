Novak Djokovic won his 400th grand‑slam match at the Australian Open, beating Botic van de Zandschulp. During the second set he accidentally hit a ball near a ball‑girl’s head and got angry, causing a brief outburst. He apologized and said he had let his emotions get the better of him. Djokovic talked about needing more patience and calm, especially when he was younger.
