Ukraine’s sports minister said the country will skip the opening ceremony of next month’s Winter Paralympics because the International Paralympic Committee (IPC) lets Russian and Belarusian athletes compete under their own flags. He called the decision “outrageous” and said it gives war propaganda a platform. Ukraine wants the IPC to reverse the rule before the Games start on March 6. The move comes after Russia and Belarus were allowed to compete with their flags after a ban lifted in September.
