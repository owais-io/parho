Last week a 550‑lb black bear was removed from a crawlspace in Altadena, but that was just the start. New reports say bears are showing up under other homes in the area, and the Bear League has been called in to help residents. California’s wildlife agency says it could be the same bear or a different one, and the trend is growing in the Lake Tahoe region. Residents are urged to secure food and trash.
