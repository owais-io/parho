Sami al‑Saei, a 47‑year‑old father of six, says he was raped and beaten by Israeli guards while held without charge in February 2024. He described hours of forced penetration, beating, and no medical care. His story is part of a B’Tselem report that finds widespread sexual violence, torture and death in Israeli prisons after 7 October. Israel denies the claims, while many prisoners are held indefinitely without trial.
