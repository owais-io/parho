Former Labour minister and current independent MP Dan Norris has been arrested again, this time on charges of rape, sexual assault, voyeurism and upskirting. Labour suspended him after the arrest, and he has denied all allegations. Police say the alleged offences span from the 2000s to the 2020s and are still under investigation. Norris remains on conditional bail while the case unfolds.
