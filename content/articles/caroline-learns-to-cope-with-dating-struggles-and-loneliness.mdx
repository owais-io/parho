Caroline, a 30‑something woman, felt stuck in a cycle of failed dates, body worries and loneliness. Her therapist, Dr Guha, helped her stop obsessing over her looks, get back into hobbies, and practice being okay with feeling sad. Using simple brain‑training ideas and a focus on what matters most—community and boundaries—Caroline learned that worth isn’t tied to finding a partner. She now feels steadier and less lonely.
