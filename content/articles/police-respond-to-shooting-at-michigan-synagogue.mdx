A car crashed into Temple Israel in West Bloomfield, Michigan, and shots were fired. Police rushed to the scene while the building was on fire and local schools were put into lockdown. No casualties were reported yet. The synagogue’s school is part of the complex, and the Jewish Federation issued a lockout warning. A congresswoman urged residents to follow police instructions and stay away from the area.
