After the UK left the EU, leaders are still trying to decide how the two sides will trade. A senior EU official said the Commission is ready to discuss a customs union with Britain, a move that would let the UK use the EU’s 40+ trade deals. UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer wants closer links but says he won’t bring back the single‑market rules or free movement. Talks are ongoing about trade, science and emissions.
