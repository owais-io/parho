The UK will rejoin the EU Erasmus+ student exchange program in January 2027. Students and staff can travel abroad and get money to cover living costs. It started in 1987 and now covers university, work, and teacher training trips. The government wants feedback from people who might join or have joined before. Share why you’d participate or how it helped you.
