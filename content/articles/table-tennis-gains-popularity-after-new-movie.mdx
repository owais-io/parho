A new film about table tennis, 'Marty Supreme', has made the sport more popular in the US. After its release, clubs and the new Major League Table Tennis saw a jump in visitors and ticket sales. Young people are starting to play ping‑pong, and older fans are re‑joining clubs. Yet pro players still earn little, and the film shows the sport’s real skill and mental‑health benefits.
