A new trend of expert lip‑readers is turning casual conversations of royals and celebrities into instantly translated clips that spread online. The Guardian’s story shows how the royal household and talent agents are now warning their clients to watch what they say in public, even during walkabouts or award shows. Recent clips include Prince William, King Charles, Kylie Jenner and Selena Gomez, and they highlight the risk of private remarks going viral.
