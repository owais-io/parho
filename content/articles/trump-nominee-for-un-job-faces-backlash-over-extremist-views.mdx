Trump has nominated Jeremy Carl to be the assistant secretary of state for international organizations, a key UN role. Carl, a former interior aide, has a record of white‑supremacist, antisemitic and racist remarks, including support for the “great replacement” conspiracy. Democrats are calling for his rejection, saying his views could hurt America’s standing abroad. The Senate hearing could be a tough fight for Republicans.
