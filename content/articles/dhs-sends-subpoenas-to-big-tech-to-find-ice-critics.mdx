The U.S. Department of Homeland Security has sent subpoenas to Google, Meta and other social‑media companies, asking them to provide the names of users who post negative comments about Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). Robert Reich wrote a letter to Sen. Kristi Noem, saying the department oversteps its authority and threatens First Amendment rights by targeting critics rather than criminal activity.
