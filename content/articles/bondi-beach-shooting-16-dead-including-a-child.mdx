A tragic shooting at Bondi Beach in Sydney left 16 people dead, including a child, and many more injured. Two gunmen were involved; one was killed and the other was taken into custody. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese called it an “evil act of antisemitism.” The attack targeted a Jewish festival, sparking national grief and a call for unity. The incident is the worst mass shooting in Australia in decades and has prompted a national discussion about safety and anti‑hate measures.
