After a rough start at Tottenham, Xavi Simons is finally showing his talent. He has become the standout player in recent games, especially in wins over Dortmund and Frankfurt and a 2‑2 draw with Manchester City. His improved stats show he’s creating chances and scoring more. Simons credits better tactics, a supportive coaching team, and a personal routine that includes meditation and nutrition. He’s now a key figure in Spurs’ attack.
