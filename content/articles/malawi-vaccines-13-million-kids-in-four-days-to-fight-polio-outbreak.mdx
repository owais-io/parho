Malawi is tackling a new polio outbreak after the virus was found in sewage in Blantyre. WHO air‑lifted vaccine supplies, and health workers vaccinated 1.3 million children in just four days. The effort comes amid major aid cuts, so funding is tight. Local leaders, teachers, and micro‑influencers are used to counter misinformation and get parents’ permission, aiming to stop the disease and protect kids.
