Three siblings were found abandoned in east London between 2017 and 2024, and their parents have never been located. Two of the boys have already been adopted. The youngest, Elsa, was discovered wrapped in a shopping bag in 2024 and will be adopted after a judge said the situation is “extraordinary.” Police searched extensively but found no mother, and the media can now report the siblings’ connection.
