Asylum seekers who were sent back to France under the UK‑France 'one in, one out' agreement are now finding ways to return to Britain in lorries. The Home Office says returnees are quickly detained and sent back to France, but smugglers are forcing people into trucks to break the deal. Four people made the trip in the last two weeks, now in detention or hiding in London. Amnesty International says the deal should be scrapped because it empowers smugglers.
