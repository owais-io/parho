A Yemen separatist group that has taken over two oil‑rich provinces says Saudi Arabia has fired warning airstrikes near its forces. The Southern Transitional Council (STC) claims the raids followed attacks by its fighters that killed two people. Saudi Arabia has not confirmed the strikes but has urged the STC to withdraw and talks to keep Yemen united. The UAE backs the STC, while Saudi and other Gulf states want unity and a calm end to the conflict.
