The CPAC conference in Perth was aimed at rallying conservatives after the Liberal party’s weak election performance. Speakers, mainly Liberal MPs, blamed high immigration and rising costs for the housing crisis and inflation. They warned that without addressing these issues, voters—especially young people—are drifting to One Nation, the Greens and independents. The event highlighted the Liberals’ plan to regain lost support, but One Nation did not speak.
