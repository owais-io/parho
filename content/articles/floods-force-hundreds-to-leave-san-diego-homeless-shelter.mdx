San Diego got a month’s worth of rain in one day, breaking records. The heavy rain flooded the Bridge shelter, forcing about 325 people to leave the tent. They were moved to a nearby gym while rescue crews worked. This is the third time in seven years the shelter had to evacuate because of storms. Climate‑change‑driven floods and fires are making it harder for people to stay safe.
