Australia’s stock market made a 6.8% return in 2025, but investors are worried about sticky inflation, higher interest rates, and a possible AI bubble. Analysts expect rates to rise in early 2026, which could slow growth and hurt stocks. Geopolitical tensions and global inflation also keep markets on edge. Meanwhile, gold and silver prices have surged, while Bitcoin and other crypto are falling. Most investors see 2026 as a mixed‑but‑mostly‑positive year.
