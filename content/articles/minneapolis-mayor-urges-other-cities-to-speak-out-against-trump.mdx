Minneapolis mayor Jacob Frey has warned other mayors that their cities could face a wave of federal immigration agents if they don’t speak out against the Trump administration. He blamed ICE’s “Operation Metro Surge,” which sent thousands of agents to Minneapolis and led to the deaths of protester Alex Pretti and resident Renee Good. Frey said the presence of agents makes neighborhoods unsafe and that cities must stand up for democracy.
