ICE officers have been sent to cities like Minneapolis, Chicago, and Los Angeles, where they sometimes used force and detained many people. The raids caused deaths, family separations, and made many residents scared to go to work or school. Public opinion has turned against the Trump‑era tactics, and even some Republicans now say ICE has gone too far. The moves also hurt construction jobs and may damage the U.S. economy.
