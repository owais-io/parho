After a tense back‑and‑forth, Colombian president Gustavo Petro and former U.S. president Donald Trump met privately at the White House. The closed‑door meeting ended with smiles, autographs, and Petro wearing a red MAGA cap. The two joked about language and shared photos on social media. Trump said they reached an agreement on drug‑trafficking, while Petro highlighted Colombia’s record in fighting cocaine. No immediate changes were announced about Petro’s OFAC list status.
