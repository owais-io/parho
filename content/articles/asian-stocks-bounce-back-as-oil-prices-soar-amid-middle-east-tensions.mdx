Asian stock markets recovered after a slump caused by Middle East tensions, but oil and gas prices kept climbing. A US tanker in the Persian Gulf was hit by a missile, lifting Brent crude to $84 a barrel. Qatar paused LNG production, while China halted diesel and gasoline exports. Despite the rally, airlines like Wizz Air warned of losses, and investors are wary of supply‑chain risks.
