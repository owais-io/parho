Labour MPs are furious with Prime Minister Keir Starmer after he backed welfare reforms that backbenchers thought were too weak, and after the government released controversial documents about former minister Peter Mandelson. The opposition, led by Angela Rayner, called for an independent review of the files. Starmer’s decisions have left MPs feeling powerless and worried about his judgment. The situation shows the continuing strain on Downing Street as backbenchers push for more transparency and stronger leadership.
