A sudden US operation on Saturday captured Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and his wife. Trump announced at Mar‑a‑Lago that the U.S. would run Venezuela until a "proper" transition, citing concerns about other leaders. The raid knocked out power in parts of Caracas and involved special forces. Venezuelan officials and many world leaders condemned the move as illegal aggression. Trump said U.S. oil companies would fix Venezuela’s broken oil system.
