Tom Brady, the legendary quarterback, recently changed his stance on the Super Bowl. He first said he wouldn't back the New England Patriots after leaving them, causing fan backlash. Then he reversed his decision, pledging to support the Patriots and hoping they win the title. The move came as he works as a TV commentator and part‑owner of the Las Vegas Raiders. Fans and former teammates had mixed reactions.
