Manchester City boss Pep Guardiola is asking the Football League to change a rule that keeps midfielder Marc Guéhi from playing in the Carabao Cup final. Guéhi joined City after the competition had already started, so the rule says he can’t play. City beat Newcastle to reach the final, and Guardiola wants the rule to be reviewed so he can add Guéhi to the squad.
