Roman Abramovich sold Chelsea FC for £2.5 billion, but the money is still held in a UK bank because of sanctions. The UK government wants the cash earmarked for Ukraine, while Abramovich says he should decide how to use it for charitable causes. His lawyers argue the funds belong to his company and that the restrictions are delaying the donation. Britain threatens court action if he refuses to release the money.
