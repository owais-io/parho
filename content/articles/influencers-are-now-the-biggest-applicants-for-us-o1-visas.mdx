US O‑1 visas, which let foreigners work for a short time, have seen a 50 % jump in approvals over the past decade. Most of the new applicants are social‑media creators – TikTok stars, OnlyFans models and other influencers. They show their follower counts, income and brand deals to prove they’re “extraordinary.” Lawyers say the creator industry is a real job, while some critics question its legitimacy. The trend is reshaping how America defines the American dream.
