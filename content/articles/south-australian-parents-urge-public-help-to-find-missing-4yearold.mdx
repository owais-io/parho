Gus Lamont, a 4‑year‑old from a sheep station in South Australia, vanished on 27 September. His parents, Josh and Jess Lamont, are asking anyone with information to come forward. Police changed the case to a major crime after an initial search failed to find him, and now suspect someone who lived at the station but not his parents. The investigation continues, with police looking for clues and encouraging public help.
