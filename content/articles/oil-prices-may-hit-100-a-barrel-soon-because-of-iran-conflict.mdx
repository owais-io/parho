Goldman Sachs warns oil prices could climb above $100 a barrel soon because of a sharp cut in oil flowing through the Strait of Hormuz after Iran’s recent attack. Only about 10 % of the usual traffic can pass, so supply is tight and prices are already up more than 50 % this year. The risk of tankers stopping and storage limits in Gulf countries could push prices toward $150 a barrel.
