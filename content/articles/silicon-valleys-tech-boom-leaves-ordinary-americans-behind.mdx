Tech giants like Nvidia and Meta are pouring billions into artificial intelligence, trimming staff and cutting big projects such as the metaverse. Their AI focus leaves most Americans behind, with 65% not using chatbots at work. Meanwhile, the FBI is buying location data of citizens, raising privacy worries. Tesla’s Cybertruck has sparked safety fears after several fatal fires, pointing to design problems.
