The chancellor will speak to MPs about Britain’s response to soaring energy costs caused by the Iran war. She’ll say the government plans to strengthen the Competition and Markets Authority, push faster on nuclear power, and keep an eye on the upcoming end of the current energy price cap. The focus will be on targeted support for those most in need, not a universal cash handout.
