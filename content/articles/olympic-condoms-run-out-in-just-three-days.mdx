Athletes at the Winter Olympics found that the free condoms promised by organisers ran out in just three days, far less than the supply at previous Games. The current count was under 10,000 compared with 300,000 in Paris, while there are only about 3,000 competitors. Italian officials said they had been generous but the demand was higher than expected, and the situation was highlighted by a viral social‑media post from a figure skater.
