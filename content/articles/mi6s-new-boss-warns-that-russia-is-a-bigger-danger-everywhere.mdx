MI6 chief Blaise Metreweli will say Russia is a growing threat all over, from spy plots to hacking and fake news. She’ll call for the UK to use tech and human spies alike, and for the public to help keep Britain safe. Defence chief Richard Knighton will also warn that the war in Ukraine shows how serious the danger is. The speech comes as the UK talks with Europe and the US about the Ukraine crisis.
