Sir Jim Ratcliffe said the UK has been “colonised by immigrants,” a claim that almost got him charged by the FA. The governing body decided not to take action, only reminding him to be careful in interviews, and Ratcliffe offered a short apology. Meanwhile, Real Madrid’s Vinícius Júnior said he was racially abused in a Champions League match. Jose Mourinho’s comments about the incident were slammed by former Manchester City star Vincent Kompany, who called Mourinho a “huge mistake.”
