People on TikTok are decorating their homes with a 90s vibe for Christmas. The hashtag #90sChristmas has over 8,000 videos, showing bright lights, felt ornaments, and even VHS tapes of *Home Alone*. The trend is part of a bigger push for nostalgia, with experts saying it gives comfort in uncertain times. Other styles, like a Ralph Lauren look and 80s music, are also popular.
