Italian police arrested two brothers from the SS Lazio Ultras in Cortina d’Ampezzo, the ski town hosting the 2026 Winter Olympics. They’re accused of running drug networks, taking over nightclubs, and trying to force the town council to award construction contracts for Olympic works. The case shows how organised‑crime groups target big event projects. Anti‑mafia groups like Libera are demanding full transparency to stop corruption and protect the Games.
