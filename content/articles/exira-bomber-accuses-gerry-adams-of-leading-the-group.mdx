An ex‑IRA bomber has told a London court that former Sinn Féin leader Gerry Adams was a senior member of the IRA. The bomber and others who were hurt in 1973 and 1996 bombings want damages, but Adams denies any role. A former IRA member, who was imprisoned for bombings, said Adams was on the IRA’s army council and gave speeches at IRA funerals. The case is still ongoing.
