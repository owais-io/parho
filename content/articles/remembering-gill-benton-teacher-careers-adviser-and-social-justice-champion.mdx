Gill Benton, aged 81, was a dedicated teacher who later became a careers adviser. After leaving the classroom in 1981, she helped students across northwest England find their path to work and higher study. She led Wirral College’s guidance team, worked on EU‑funded projects to open universities to under‑represented students, and championed equality. A lifelong socialist and pacifist, she inspired others with kindness and faith in their potential.
