Aston Villa came from behind to beat West Ham 3–2 at the London Stadium. After losing the first half, Morgan Rogers scored a late rocket that sealed the win. The match showed Villa’s resilience after a rough start to the season, while West Ham struggled to keep a lead. Manager Unai Emery praised his team’s determination, and the result keeps Villa in the top‑four chase.
