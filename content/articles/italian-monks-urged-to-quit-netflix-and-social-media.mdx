In Tuscany, the head of a secluded monastery told nine monks to stop using social media and streaming services. He said these digital tools are designed to be addictive and distract from prayer and quiet contemplation. The prior wrote a long Facebook post warning that watching films online could turn monks into “cinephiles” instead of seekers of God. He encouraged them to focus on community and spiritual life, echoing Pope Francis’s caution about digital temptations.
