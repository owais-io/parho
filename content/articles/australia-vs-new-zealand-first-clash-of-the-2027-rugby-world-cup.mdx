In the 2027 Rugby World Cup draw, Australia (Wallabies) will face New Zealand (All Blacks) in Pool A. Australia is ranked 7th and had a tough year with 10 losses. They must finish at least second in the pool, then beat opponents in the knockout rounds, possibly including Japan. The match will set up a quarter‑final with South Africa if the reigning champions top their pool. Fans expect a heated rivalry, and the home crowd will be a big factor.
