US Coast Guard is chasing an oil tanker called Bella 1 that is trying to avoid a US blockade near Venezuela. The ship is linked to Iran and Hezbollah and is under US sanctions. This is the third ship seized in a week. The move is part of President Trump’s plan to pressure Venezuela. While officials say the seizures won’t raise US gas prices, they could push global oil costs up.
