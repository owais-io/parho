President Trump said he wants to take control of Greenland and even threatened to use force. He told reporters that NATO is only strong if the U.S. is part of it and warned EU members that he might hit them with tariffs if they oppose his plan. Europe called his moves “new colonialism,” markets fell, and lawmakers are trying to block his tariff threats.
