Texas immigration detention camp staff are accused of beatings, sexual abuse, and secret deportations to Mexico. A coalition of civil‑rights groups sent a 19‑page letter to ICE and the military, citing 45 detainee testimonies of mistreatment and poor conditions. ICE and the Department of Homeland Security denied the claims, saying detainees receive proper care. Advocates demand the camp close and call the situation a public health hazard.
