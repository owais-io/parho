The Colts are thinking about putting 44‑year‑old retired QB Philip Rivers back in the game because their starters are injured. Daniel Jones tore his Achilles, rookie Anthony Richardson broke his orbital bone, and backup Riley Leonard has a knee injury. Coach Shane Steichen, a close friend, asked Rivers to return and he replied, “Let’s freaking go.” The team will decide later in the week if Rivers will start against the Seahawks. Rivers says he’s excited and feels ready.
