Donald Trump’s allies at Paramount Skydance are almost finished buying Warner Bros Discovery, the parent of CNN. If the deal goes through, Trump could have a say in how CNN is run, after he’s already tried to weaken the network with lawsuits and criticism. Netflix pulled out of the bidding, and experts worry the purchase could reduce journalistic independence and raise antitrust concerns.
