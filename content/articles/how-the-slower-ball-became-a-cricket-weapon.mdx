The article tells the story of Franklyn Stephenson, a former Barbadian bowler who invented a slow, deceptive delivery called the “moon ball.” He learned to keep his arm action the same while dropping speed, tricking batsmen into wrong swings. Today, fast bowlers like Sam Curran use the same idea to surprise players in T20 matches. The piece explains how the slower ball keeps batters guessing and can turn a game.
