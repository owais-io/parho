A Republican motion to punish Stacey Plaskett for texting Jeffrey Epstein during a 2019 hearing didn’t pass. The vote ended 209‑214 against the censure. Some Republicans joined Democrats in voting no, while a few abstained. The failure sparked heated words on the House floor, with Rep. Lauren Boebert yelling at colleagues. Democrats had earlier halted a plan to censure another Republican, Cory Mills, after a similar ethics issue.
