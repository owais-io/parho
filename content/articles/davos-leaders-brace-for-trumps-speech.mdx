At the World Economic Forum in Davos, leaders from Europe and the United States are preparing for President Trump’s speech. Trump has threatened tariffs on countries that won’t let him annex Greenland, sparking fears of a new trade war. US officials urge calm, while European leaders, including Ursula von der Leyen and Emmanuel Macron, warn that Trump’s “dinosaur diplomacy” could undermine the world order and prompt sanctions. The tension highlights a growing split between the US and its allies.
