Australia has sent crisis teams to the Middle East to help its citizens stuck there as conflict over Iran escalates. Foreign Minister Penny Wong said the U.S. and Israel's attacks are for those countries to explain. Global markets fell after Trump offered U.S. navy escort for oil ships, and flights are being cancelled. The move follows a spike in travel chaos and a sharp dip in stock indices.
