Senegal beat Egypt 1‑0 in the Africa Cup of Nations semi‑final, with Sadio Mané scoring 12 minutes in. The game was full of fouls, time‑wasting and poor play, yet Mané’s powerful strike broke the deadlock. Senegal’s defense held firm and the win sends them to face Morocco in the final. Egypt’s coach was unhappy with the fairness of the match, and a few key players will miss the final.
