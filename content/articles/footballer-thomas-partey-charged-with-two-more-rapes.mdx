Thomas Partey, the Ghanaian footballer who plays for Villarreal, has been charged again with rape. A new woman has come forward, adding two more counts to the cases already filed against him. He will appear in court on 13 March, and a trial is set for November 2026. Partey denies all accusations and has been released on bail with restrictions.
