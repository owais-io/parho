President Trump denied he ever dozed off during White House meetings, saying any closed eyes were just blinking. He also said his health is perfect, despite taking a high dose of aspirin that makes him bruise easily. Trump defended his aspirin use, saying he wants thin blood to keep his heart healthy. He told the Wall Street Journal that doctors suggested cutting back, but he chose to keep the same dose.
