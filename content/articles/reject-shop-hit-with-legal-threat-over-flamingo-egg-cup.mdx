UK artist Hannah Turner says Australian retailer The Reject Shop sold flamingo‑shaped egg cups that look like her own design. She asked the chain to pull the items and pay for copying. The shop says it has no legal basis for her claim, but will stop ordering new ones and sell the remaining stock by January 2026. Turner wants money, but legal action is costly. Experts say copyright enforcement on such copies is hard.
