Kanaan, a Berlin restaurant run by an Israeli and a Palestinian, will shut its doors in spring. The owners say their story will live on as a TV miniseries called Breaking the Binary, produced by a German studio. The show follows them and their staff as they navigate cultural expectations, money issues, and personal doubts. Kanaan has symbolized unity, served mixed dishes like shakshuka lasagne, and faced threats after a queer brunch. The owners hope the series spreads their message of coexistence.
