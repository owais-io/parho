UK MPs want the Serious Fraud Office to look into two government insulation schemes that left thousands of homes with serious problems like mould, water damage and unsafe walls. Over 30,000 houses were affected, with many still needing repairs. MPs blame the old Conservative schemes, Eco4 and the Great British Insulation Scheme, for poor planning and slow action. Labour has promised new plans and a clean‑up, but many families still worry about costs and safety.
