Since Democratic‑socialist Zohran Mamdani was elected mayor, Miami’s high‑end beach area—known as Billionaire’s Beach—is getting a boom in interest from wealthy New Yorkers. Realtors report a jump in calls for pricey condos at places like the Ritz‑Carlton Residences, Four Seasons, the Perigon, and Cipriani. Prices range from a few million to over a hundred million dollars, and buyers are drawn by top‑notch amenities, endless ocean views, and a sunny climate.
