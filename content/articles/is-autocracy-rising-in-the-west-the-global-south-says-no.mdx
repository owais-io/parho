Many in Europe and the US are turning to far‑right leaders who promise to break rule‑by‑law politics. The article says that when people feel unheard and poor, they are more likely to back anti‑democratic forces. But in countries that have seen strong dictatorships, people still push for democratic change. The writer argues democracies must listen to ordinary people and keep free media and fair elections alive.
