Thousands of workers have escaped or been freed from Cambodia's scam factories after global pressure. Amnesty says at least 1,440 Indonesian and many Chinese workers were released, and videos show dozens of compounds emptied. The crackdown follows US and UK sanctions on Chinese‑born tycoon Chen Zhi. While releases are a win, experts warn workers may return to new scams if support and enforcement stay weak.
