US experts say that simply putting limits on how long kids can use screens isn’t enough. The American Academy of Pediatrics urges parents to talk openly with their children, keep an eye on the whole family’s digital habits, and use parental controls. They also call for rules on social‑media companies to stop pushing harmful or sexualised content to teens, and for safer community spaces for kids.
