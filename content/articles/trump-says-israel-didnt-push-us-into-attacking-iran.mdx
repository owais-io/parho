Trump has denied that Israel forced the U.S. to strike Iran. He said he acted because he believed Iran would attack first and wanted to pre‑empt retaliation. Senators, especially Democrats, are furious, arguing the decision should have gone through Congress under the war‑powers clause. Republicans largely back Trump, while critics say Israel’s interests, not American ones, drove the attack, sparking debate over U.S. foreign‑policy decision‑making.
