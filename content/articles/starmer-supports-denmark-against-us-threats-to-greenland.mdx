UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said he backs Danish leader Mette Frederiksen after she warned the United States not to try to take control of Greenland. Starmer agreed that only Denmark and Greenland can decide what happens on the island. He criticized US President Donald Trump for saying the U.S. might want Greenland for “national security.” The Danish government has rejected any U.S. claims and says any talks about Greenland must follow international law.
