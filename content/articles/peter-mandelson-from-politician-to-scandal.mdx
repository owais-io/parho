Peter Mandelson, a long‑time Labour politician, rose to high office but has faced repeated scandals. He served as a minister, trade secretary, and EU commissioner, but resigned twice over loans and citizenship deals. In 2024 he was named U.S. ambassador, but Epstein files exposed his ties to the convicted sex offender and alleged leaking of sensitive data. The revelations led to his dismissal, a business collapse, and his arrest in 2026.
