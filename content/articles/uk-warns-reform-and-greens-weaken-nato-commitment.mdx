At the Munich Security Conference, Yvette Cooper said Reform UK and the Green Party are undermining Britain’s pledge to NATO. She accused them of being too soft on Russia and not taking Russian interference seriously, citing former MEP Nathan Gill’s bribery conviction. Green leader Zack Polanski has floated leaving NATO, which Cooper called risky. The remarks highlight growing tension between Labour and the two parties.
