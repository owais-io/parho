Donald Trump said he is seriously looking at strong military options against Iran if the regime keeps killing protesters. He said Iran has reached out for talks but may have crossed a red line by killing people. Protesters in Iran, sparked by a falling currency, are demanding political reform and have faced a crackdown that has killed hundreds. Iran’s parliament warned Washington about possible miscalculations.
