Iran launched a missile that struck central Tel Aviv, damaging buildings and cars and injuring several people. Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu said Israel will keep striking Iranian targets in Tehran and Lebanon. Iran’s army vows to launch more missile and drone attacks unless Israel stops its strikes. The U.S. is coordinating with Israel, and the fighting has spread to Gulf countries, sparking regional alarm.
