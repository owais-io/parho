After a 5‑1 defeat to Manchester City, Chelsea Women’s coach Sonia Bompastor is under pressure. The club signed her to a new contract, but fans worry her criticism of squad depth might spark discontent. Some players seem uneasy, and the team has lost two league games in a row for the first time since 2015. With a big game against Tottenham coming, Chelsea needs to regroup and keep their Champions League hopes alive.
