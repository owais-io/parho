Racial and religious hate crime on buses, trains and other public transport is rising. Data shows more incidents last year than before. In Scotland, racial attacks have jumped. In England and Wales, Muslims face more religious hate. Stories of people being shouted at or sprayed with disinfectant on trains are getting out. People say support from other passengers helps. Authorities say they must act to stop it. Some avoid public transport because they fear attacks.
