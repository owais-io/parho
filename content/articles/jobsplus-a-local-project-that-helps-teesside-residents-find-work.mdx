JobsPlus is a pilot program in 10 UK neighborhoods, including Teesside, that uses local caseworkers and community champions to help people get jobs. They offer CV help, job‑search support, and a £400 bonus after eight weeks of work. Since starting in summer 2024, over 1,000 people joined and more than 270 found jobs. The scheme has already helped families, disabled people and young parents, but funding may end next March.
