Liberty, a 150‑year‑old London department store, is selling Bridgerton‑inspired scarves, dresses and bedding on its fourth floor to ride the wave of bold, richly patterned design. The collection comes as the store’s sales jumped 10% last year to £142 million, helped by its own‑label lines, exclusive partnerships and growing online sales. While rivals lose business, Liberty keeps customers with unique fabrics, a quirky layout and new jewellery plans.
