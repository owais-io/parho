An 83‑year‑old man from South Charleston, Ohio, was convicted of murder, felonious assault and kidnapping after he shot an Uber driver he mistakenly thought was involved in a scam. The driver, a Black woman named Lo‑Letha Toland‑Hall, was unarmed and had no threat. He is expected to be sentenced to life in prison, while prosecutors say the crime was a result of a phone‑scam plot.
