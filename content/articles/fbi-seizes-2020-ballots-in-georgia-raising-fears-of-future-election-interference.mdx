On Jan. 28, the FBI raided Fulton County’s election center in Georgia and took more than 700 boxes of 2020 ballots and related records. The move followed President Trump’s long‑standing claims of a stolen election. The raid, attended by top intelligence officials, signals a new level of federal pressure on state elections and warns officials that future election materials could be seized.
