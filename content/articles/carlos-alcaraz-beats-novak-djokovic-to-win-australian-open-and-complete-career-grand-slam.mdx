In a thrilling final at Melbourne, 22‑year‑old Carlos Alcaraz defeated Novak Djokovic 2‑6, 6‑2, 6‑3, 7‑5 to win his first Australian Open title. With the win, Alcaraz became the youngest man ever to capture all four Grand Slam titles, adding a seventh slam to his record. Djokovic, who had been chasing a record 25th slam, missed his chance to become the oldest champion.
