Spanish star Carlos Alcaraz won the Australian Open quarter‑final against Australian Alex de Minaur, defeating him 7‑5, 6‑2, 6‑1 in 136 minutes. Alcaraz’s powerful serve and groundstrokes overpowered de Minaur, who struggled after a strong first set. The win puts Alcaraz two victories from a career Grand Slam, making him the youngest man to do so if he wins the final. He will face Alexander Zverev in the semi‑final.
