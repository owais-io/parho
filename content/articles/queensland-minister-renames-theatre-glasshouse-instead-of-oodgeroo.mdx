In Queensland, the new arts minister decided that the upcoming Queensland Performing Arts Centre would be called the 'Glasshouse Theatre', overriding the board's recommendation to name it after famed Aboriginal poet Oodgeroo Noonuccal. The choice was made before a public vote and without consulting the board, sparking criticism from Oodgeroo’s family and Indigenous groups. The move also affects a state electorate that was slated to carry Oodgeroo’s name. Critics say the decision erases an important cultural legacy.
