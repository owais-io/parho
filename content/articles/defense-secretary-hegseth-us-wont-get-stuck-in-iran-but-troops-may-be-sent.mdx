Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth said the U.S. won’t get stuck in a long war in Iran, but he didn’t rule out sending troops if needed. He praised the precise air strikes that hit Iran’s missile and nuclear sites after Israel struck its leader. Hegseth said the goal is to destroy those weapons, not to build a democracy. Four U.S. service members died when a missile broke through air defenses. Trump also said he might send ground forces.
