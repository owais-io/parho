Last week the U.S. Supreme Court cancelled a big set of Trump’s tariffs that had raised the price of everyday items. The decision should ease the extra $1,000 a typical American household might pay in 2025. Small businesses, especially local shops, also feel relief because the tariffs had made parts and goods more expensive, hurting hiring and survival. The ruling reminds that trade rules should protect ordinary people, not just politics.
