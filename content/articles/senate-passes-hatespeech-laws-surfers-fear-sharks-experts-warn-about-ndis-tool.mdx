Meanwhile, surfers and swimmers in Sydney are worried about a spate of shark attacks that have closed beaches and cancelled events. Health experts are also sounding the alarm that the NDIS assessment tool hasn’t been tested on many disability types and could create problems. All three stories show lawmakers and experts trying to keep people safe.
