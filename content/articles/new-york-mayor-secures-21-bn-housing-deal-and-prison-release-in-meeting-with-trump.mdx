New York Mayor Zohran Mamdani met with President Trump and made a winning bargain. He asked for $21 bn in federal money to build 12,000 new affordable homes, and Trump agreed. Mamdani also got a student released from immigration detention and a request to drop four other cases. He used flattery and a fake newspaper headline to win Trump’s favor. Some progressives question the cost of the deal.
