Bluesfest, a popular Byron Bay music festival, was cancelled after the organising company went into liquidation, leaving ticket holders with a $23 million debt. Band Parkway Drive said fans were “gutted,” and the liquidator warned refunds were unlikely because the money was already spent. Ticket buyers can file claims, and some may get card chargebacks. Artists are unsecured creditors and are uncertain about payouts.
