On the fourth anniversary of Russia’s full‑scale invasion, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy delivered a video speech in Kyiv. He asked former U.S. President Donald Trump to visit the country, saying a trip would show the reality of the war and pressure Russia. Zelenskyy also reminded leaders that Ukraine has resisted Putin’s aims and will not surrender its people. The address included footage from the wartime bunker and scenes of memorials, and he welcomed European leaders to tour damaged infrastructure.
