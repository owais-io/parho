Israel has struck Iran’s biggest gas field, South Par, and Iran’s Revolutionary Guards have warned they will hit key energy sites in Saudi Arabia, the UAE and Qatar. Targets include refineries, petrochemical plants and gas fields. The threat has pushed oil and gas prices higher, with benchmarks spiking. Iran has already attacked Gulf facilities, cutting exports and raising fears of a wider energy crisis.
