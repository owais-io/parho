Aston Villa beat Wolverhampton 1‑0 at Villa Park, thanks to Boubacar Kamara’s left‑foot strike. The goal moved Villa up to fourth place, just one point behind Manchester City. Wolves have been struggling, losing their seventh straight match, but showed better form than in recent weeks. The win gives Villa confidence as they look ahead to the rest of the season, while Wolves face a tough task to turn things around.
