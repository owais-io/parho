The new farm bill, led by Republicans, adds rules that many say give pesticide makers a huge legal shield. The bill would delay safety reviews, let industry help decide endangered species protections, and let the USDA overrule EPA health rules for kids, farm workers and the public. It also blocks state lawsuits that claim companies didn’t warn about cancer risks. A wide coalition of health, consumer and farm groups is fighting back.
