Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said MPs will lose many travel benefits for family members after the Independent Parliamentary Expenses Authority (IPEA) advised changes. The new rules cap how often spouses can fly to Canberra and forbid travel between other cities. Only economy class will be allowed, and trips must be linked to official events. The changes aim to stop ministers from sending families to expensive sports events and to keep costs down.
