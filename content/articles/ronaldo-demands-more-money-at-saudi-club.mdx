Portugal striker Cristiano Ronaldo is upset that Al‑Nassr has received less money than rival Saudi clubs. He threatened to skip a derby, saying other teams backed by the state fund get better budgets. Experts point out Al‑Nassr has already spent nearly £100 m on new players, and Ronaldo’s own salary is the highest in the league. Missing a key game could hurt his chances of winning a title and reaching 1,000 career goals.
