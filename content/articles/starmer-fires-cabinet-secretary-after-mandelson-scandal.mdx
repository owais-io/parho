Starmer has fired the top civil servant, Chris Wormald, after the Peter Mandelson scandal. He plans to replace him with Antonia Romeo, the first woman in the role. Staff are angry about the abrupt move, calling it harsh and damaging morale. Critics say the appointment should go through a thorough vetting process. The decision shows Starmer is trying to reshape his inner circle.
