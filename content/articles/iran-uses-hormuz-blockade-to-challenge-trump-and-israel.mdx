Since Iran started hitting ships, about a fifth of the world’s oil can’t flow through the Hormuz Strait. Prices jumped and the U.S. and Israel are trying to curb Iran’s missile power. Iran’s blockade is a way to hurt the global economy and force a pause. Trump wants regime change, Israel wants Iran weakened, and Russia wants to keep oil profits. A quick end looks unlikely.
