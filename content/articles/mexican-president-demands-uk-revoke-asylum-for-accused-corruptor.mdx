Mexico's president, Claudia Sheinbaum, has written a formal complaint to the UK after Karime Macías, the former first lady of Veracruz, was granted asylum in Britain. Macías is wanted in Mexico for stealing millions of dollars from a state welfare office. Sheinbaum questions how someone accused of fraud can live in London, while the UK has not responded and the case remains in legal limbo.
