Former President Donald Trump says JPMorgan Chase closed his account after the January 6 Capitol riot and he plans to sue the bank. He claims the move is a political hit‑job and that the bank unfairly blocked his deposits. JPMorgan’s CEO Jamie Dimon denies the accusations, saying no job offer was made and that the bank does not de‑bank people for politics. Trump also criticized the Wall Street Journal and the Federal Reserve.
