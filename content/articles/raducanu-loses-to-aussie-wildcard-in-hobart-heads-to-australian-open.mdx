Emma Raducanu was beaten 6‑2 6‑4 by 20‑year‑old Australian Taylah Preston in the Hobart quarter‑finals. Rain and many unforced errors cost the British top seed, and the win was Preston’s best career result. Raducanu will now travel to Melbourne for the Australian Open, where she faces Mananchaya Sawangkaew in round one and could meet world No 1 Aryna Sabalenka by round three. Other British players also compete in the main draw.
