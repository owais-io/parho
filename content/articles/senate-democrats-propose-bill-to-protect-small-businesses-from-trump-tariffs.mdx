Senate Democrats introduced a bill that would let small companies import goods without paying the new tariffs Trump just announced. Senator Ed Markey led the Small Business Liberation 2.0 Act, which also bars price hikes caused by the tariffs. Small‑business owners say the tariffs have pushed their costs up about $1 million. Republicans control the Senate, so the bill may not pass.
