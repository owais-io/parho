People on online prediction markets bet that Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro would be taken into custody by January 31. One trader put $30,000 into a Polymarket bet. When news broke that Maduro had been captured, the trade paid off, earning him $436,000 in a few hours. The market’s odds jumped from a 5 % chance to almost 100 % in under a day. U.S. betting sites are now drawing huge amounts of money.
