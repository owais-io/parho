A GoFundMe campaign to help the family of Renee Good, a 37‑year‑old mother of three, raised $1.5 million after she was shot dead by an ICE agent during an immigration sweep in Minneapolis. The fundraiser, which started with a $50,000 goal, attracted about 38,500 donors and will place the money in a trust for the family. Good’s killing drew protests and criticism of the Trump‑era ICE policy.
