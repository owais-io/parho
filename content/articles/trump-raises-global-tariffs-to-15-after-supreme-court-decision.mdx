Donald Trump announced that he will raise a temporary worldwide tariff on U.S. imports from 10% to 15% after a Supreme Court decision said his earlier tariff was illegal. The new 15% rate is meant to be in place for 150 days before the administration must get congressional approval. European leaders and businesses warn higher tariffs hurt trade and raise consumer costs.
