Rural homes in Northern Ireland are being robbed of their heating oil as prices double after the Iran conflict. Delivery trucks are easy to spot, so thieves target houses with visible tanks. Stolen oil costs thousands of pounds, and the damage can leave families with broken pipes, fumes, and a huge bill. Police urge residents to lock tanks, use alarms, and hide their tanks behind fences or hedges.
