Ollie Watkins netted two goals in a dramatic 7‑0 win for Aston Villa over Brighton. Villa’s manager, Unai Emery, praised the team’s resilience after a rough start and highlighted Watkins’ important contribution. Brighton struggled defensively, losing their first home match of the season, while Villa continued their strong run of victories and looks to move closer to Champions League qualification. The game showed Villa’s solid defense and attack, leaving Brighton’s fans disappointed.
