Turkey’s foreign minister said a new peace‑keeping team in Gaza should first keep Israeli troops and Hamas apart, not immediately try to take Hamas’ weapons. He also noted that Indonesia and Azerbaijan want Turkey to join the UN‑backed force. Other countries, including Egypt and Norway, add that the team must monitor the ceasefire, not enforce it. Israel insists it won’t pull out until Hamas is disarmed. The debate highlights different ideas about how to restore peace.
