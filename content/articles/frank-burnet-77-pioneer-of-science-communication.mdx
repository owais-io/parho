Frank Burnet was a charismatic scientist who turned science into a public spectacle. Born in Edinburgh, he studied biochemistry and worked at the University of Kent, where he used his booming voice and theatre skills to make lectures fun. He later founded the Science Communication Unit at UWE Bristol, launched the Cheltenham Science Festival, and created FameLab, a worldwide challenge that inspires young scientists to tell stories.
