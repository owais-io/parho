The Guardian columnist warns parents against giving their kids AI toys now. With 1,500+ companies in China and big names like Mattel joining forces with OpenAI, these toys are everywhere. She tested one called Grem – after a day the toy talked nonstop about loving her child and left her uneasy. Studies show other toys give dangerous instructions, sexual content and can share personal data. Until clear rules exist, she says stay away.
