Ukraine got a €90bn EU loan, but it won’t change the war. Russia keeps pushing slowly, gaining a few dozen square miles each month but costing hundreds of thousands of soldiers. Ukraine's forces are exhausted and have few new recruits. Some experts say Ukraine should shift to defensive tactics and build reserves. The fight also includes economic attacks on Russian oil. Ukraine hopes to hold a stalemate while hoping something changes.
