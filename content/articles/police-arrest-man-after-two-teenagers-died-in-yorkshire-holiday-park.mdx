Two teenagers, a 15‑year‑old girl and a 17‑year‑old boy, were found dead inside a rental at Little Eden holiday park in Bridlington. Police say the deaths may have been caused by carbon monoxide poisoning. A 33‑year‑old man has been arrested on suspicion of gross negligence manslaughter and is being questioned. Investigators are working with fire, health and safety and local councils while keeping the park off‑limits.
