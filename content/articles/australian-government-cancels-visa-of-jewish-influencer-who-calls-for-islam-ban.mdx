The Australian government canceled the visa of Sammy Yahood, a Jewish influencer who has called Islam a “murderous ideology” and urged its ban. The cancellation came just hours before his flight, with Home Affairs Minister Tony Burke saying he was barred for spreading hate. Yahood and his supporters blasted the move as censorship, while the government cited new laws that let ministers reject visas for extremist or hateful views.
