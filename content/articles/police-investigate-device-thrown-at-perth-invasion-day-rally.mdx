Police in Western Australia are looking into a device thrown into a crowd at a Perth Invasion Day rally. The device had ball bearings, screws, and a liquid in a glass container. A 31‑year‑old man was arrested. The incident led to a security scare and an exclusion zone around Forrest Place. Similar clashes happened in Melbourne, Sydney, Canberra and Brisbane as thousands marched for Indigenous rights.
