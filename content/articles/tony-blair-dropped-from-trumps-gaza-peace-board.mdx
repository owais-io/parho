Former UK prime minister Tony Blair has been removed from Donald Trump’s planned Gaza peace board. Arab and Muslim countries said they did not want Blair on the group. Trump had said he would lead the council and had called Blair a “very good man”. Critics point to Blair’s mixed record in the Middle East and his role in the Iraq war. While Blair may still help in a smaller role, he won’t sit on the main peace board.
