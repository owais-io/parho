The Justice Department says DHS Secretary Kristi Noem decided to keep sending Venezuelan immigrants on flights to El Salvador, even after a federal judge ordered the flights back to the U.S. The judge had issued a restraining order to stop the deportations. Noem was told the planes had already left the U.S. when the order was issued, so the department claims the action was legal. The judge is still reviewing if the Trump administration broke his order. detainees returned to Venezuela in July.
