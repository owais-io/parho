NSW’s new government has confirmed it will start two‑way tolls on the Sydney Harbour Bridge in 2028, while keeping the $60 weekly toll cap. It will also scrap big admin fees that cost motorists money. Critics say this breaks the promise the government made to end “tollmania”. The premier says the plan was always the same as before. The cap will have a $5,000 yearly limit, and drivers will get digital reminders for unpaid tolls.
