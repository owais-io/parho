Nurses and activists gathered in Washington, DC, and across the country to honor Alex Pretti, a veteran‑hospital nurse who was killed by federal agents, and to demand that the U.S. stop funding immigration and border agencies. The vigil, held in freezing weather, joined protests in Minnesota, Portland, Oregon, Chicago, New York, and San Diego. Protesters called for an end to DHS, ICE and CBP funding and for better patient care.
