Hudson Williams and Connor Storrie, who play rival hockey players in the TV series *Heated Rivalry*, will carry the Olympic flame during the torch relay for the 2026 Winter Games in Milan. They’ll light the blaze that ends at San Siro Stadium on February 6, the opening day. HBO Max announced the pair, helping the network broadcast the Games in Italy and Germany. The actors, now popular after the show, are already in Milan for fashion events.
