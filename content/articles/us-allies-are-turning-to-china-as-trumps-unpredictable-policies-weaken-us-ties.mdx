Because Trump’s erratic policies have made the U.S. less reliable, many European and North American leaders are turning to China for trade and security deals. Ireland’s leader met President Xi and even talked about a classic Irish novel, showing China’s friendly outreach. Canada’s new prime minister cut tariffs on Chinese electric cars, while the UK and Finland are also visiting Beijing to boost ties. Some worry about growing dependence on China, but the trend is clear.
