Tommy Wright, who helped Everton win the 1970 league title, passed away at 81. The Liverpool right‑back played 373 games for the club and earned 11 England caps, including the 1970 World Cup. Known for his calm, attacking style, his career ended early due to a knee injury. After football he worked as a security guard, and fans remember him as a club legend.
