California is hit again by heavy rain from atmospheric rivers, with flood warnings in place through Friday. Western and northern parts could see 200‑300 mm of rain, while Los Angeles might get 100‑150 mm, making it one of its wettest Christmases. Flooding, mudslides and a winter‑storm warning for Yosemite could cause travel problems. Nearby Zimbabwe faces a level‑2 warning for rain, hail and wind, and Australia will see big temperature swings.
