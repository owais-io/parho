Lisa Cook, a Federal Reserve governor, has been accused by the Trump administration of mortgage fraud. Her lawyers argue the claims are baseless, saying the administration is cherry‑picking mistakes. Cook owns three homes – in Ann Arbor, Atlanta and Cambridge – and says her main home is in Ann Arbor. The Supreme Court restored her seat, and will hear the case in January. Her team also criticises officials who have similar property discrepancies.
