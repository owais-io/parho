Vice‑president Dan Vance says Minneapolis officials are causing the chaos by not helping federal immigration agents. He calls the city’s protests a “political stunt” and says the five‑year‑old child incident was blown out of proportion. Governor Tim Walz says his priority is people’s safety and blames the Trump‑era crackdown for the unrest. Vance wants the city to cooperate so ICE can arrest criminals more safely.
