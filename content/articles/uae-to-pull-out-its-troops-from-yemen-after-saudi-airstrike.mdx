The United Arab Emirates said it will withdraw all its forces from Yemen after Saudi Arabia bombed a port in Mukalla, claiming it hit a shipment of weapons for Yemen’s Southern Transitional Council (STC). The attack deepened a rift between the two Gulf monarchies, which both back different groups fighting the Houthi rebels. The STC now controls much of southern Yemen, and the conflict has killed about 377,000 people, with half the population facing hunger.
