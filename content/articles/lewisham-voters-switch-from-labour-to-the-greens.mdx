Local elections in Lewisham show a dramatic shift: thousands of voters who used to back Labour are now joining the Greens. In an assembly hall, around 600 volunteers gathered, the biggest turnout for the Greens ever. People say they leave Labour because of disagreements over Gaza, policing and housing. The move is led by new Green leader Zack Polanski, who promises fresh policies and a bigger focus on local issues.
