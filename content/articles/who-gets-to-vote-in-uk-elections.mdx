After the Green Party won a local election, Nigel Farage said only British‑born voters should be allowed to vote. This sparked a debate about who can vote in the UK. Some Commonwealth citizens and other long‑term residents can, while millions of people in the country cannot. A group called the Migrant Democracy Project explains the confusing rules and argues for a fairer system that lets more people vote.
