Keir Starmer said the Gorton and Denton byelection is a test of “true patriotism” and that Labour can beat Reform UK. Labour picked Angeliki Stogia, a local Manchester councilor, to run. The Greens have also chosen Hannah Spencer, but most see the contest as Labour vs Reform. The seat opened when former MP Andrew Gwynne resigned after a scandal. Labour wants to keep the strong local support.
