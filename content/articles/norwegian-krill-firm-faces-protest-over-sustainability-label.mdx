Norwegian krill firm Aker QRILL is being pushed to lose its Marine Stewardship Council ‘blue tick’ sustainability badge. Environmental groups say the fishery no longer meets standards because conservation rules set by CCAMLR have stalled and climate change is hurting Antarctic krill stocks. WWF‑UK and the Antarctic and Southern Ocean Coalition want a fishing pause and a full review of the certification. Aker says it’s working quietly to restore protections and keep the label.
