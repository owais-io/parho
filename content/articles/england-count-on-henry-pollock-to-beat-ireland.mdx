England are looking to a fresh spark for their Six Nations campaign. With only two home games left, a win over Ireland is crucial to keep title hopes alive. Henry Pollock will make his first start, and coach Steve Borthwick hopes he will disrupt the experienced Irish side. England also return key players like Ollie Lawrence and hope the veteran Maro Itoje can help keep the team focused after a shaky loss in Edinburgh.
