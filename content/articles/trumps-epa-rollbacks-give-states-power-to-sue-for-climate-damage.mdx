Trump’s EPA reversed a key ruling that let it regulate greenhouse gases. That move hurts the agency’s claim that state climate‑laws, like Vermont’s “climate superfund” that forces fossil‑fuel firms to pay for past damage, are pre‑empted. Environmental groups say the repeal means federal law can’t block state suits. More states are eyeing similar rules, and big cities are suing oil companies for climate harm.
