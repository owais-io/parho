On Sunday, a father‑and‑son pair opened fire at a Hanukkah event near Bondi Beach in Sydney, killing at least 16 people, including a child, and injuring many more. Police shot the older attacker and are treating the younger as critical. The government called it a terrorist attack, pledged tougher gun laws, and praised a local shopkeeper who stopped a gunman. The city is still in shock and recovery.
