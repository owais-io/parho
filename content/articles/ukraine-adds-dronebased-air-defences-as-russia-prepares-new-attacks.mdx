President Zelensky announced a new air‑defence plan that uses small mobile teams and interceptor drones to stop Russian attacks. The move comes after Russia hit Ukrainian power grids and struck Kyiv, cutting electricity in several regions. Zelensky warned citizens to stay alert and said intelligence spotted Russia targeting energy sites, including nuclear plants. Ukraine also faces the challenge of holding elections while fighting, with US and Russian officials meeting in Switzerland.
