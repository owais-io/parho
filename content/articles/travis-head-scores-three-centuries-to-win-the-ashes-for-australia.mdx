Travis Head hit three huge centuries and scored 629 runs in the five‑Test Ashes, helping Australia win 4‑1. He played in a wild, fearless way, using shots that look like magic, which left England feeling helpless. Head’s quick, fun batting style made crowds love him, and he keeps a relaxed attitude toward stats. His confidence and love for the game have turned him into a key star for Australia’s future.
