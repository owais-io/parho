US hockey star Hilary Knight spoke out on Saturday Night Live after Donald Trump joked that the women’s gold‑winning team would need to visit the White House. The men’s team had laughed, but Knight called the remarks “distasteful and unfortunate.” She and teammates, busy preparing for a July celebration, declined the White House invite and plan to celebrate the Olympic victory with rapper Flavor Flav’s event instead. Knight used SNL to defend her teammates and the sport.
