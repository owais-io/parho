Finnish police seized the cargo ship Fitburg after it was linked to damage on two undersea telecom cables in the Baltic Sea. The vessel, sailing from Russia to Israel, was caught with its anchor dragging along the seabed. Crew members from several countries were arrested and a probe is underway. The incident comes amid a series of similar attacks blamed on Russia as part of a hybrid war, prompting NATO to boost naval patrols.
