On Friday, the U.S. Justice Department released a small, heavily redacted set of documents about Jeffrey Epstein, instead of the full set the law requires by December 19. Many lawmakers, including Ro Khanna, Thomas Massie, and Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez, slammed the move, saying it breaks the Epstein Transparency Act and could lead to legal action or impeachment. The DOJ says it will keep releasing more documents, but critics call it a cover‑up.
