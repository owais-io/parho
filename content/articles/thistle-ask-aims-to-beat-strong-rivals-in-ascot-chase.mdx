Thistle Ask, a top two‑mile chaser, is the 7‑to‑1 favourite in Ascot’s Clarence House Chase. He’s improved quickly after joining trainer Dan Skelton, winning two races with fast times that raise his rating to 162. He’ll face strong rivals like Il Etait Temps, the unbeaten top‑rated favourite, and Jonbon, who has a new rider. The race looks like a showdown of rising talent and proven champions.
