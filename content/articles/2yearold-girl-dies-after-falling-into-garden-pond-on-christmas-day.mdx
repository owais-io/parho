A 2‑year‑old named Isobel Wallace died when she fell into a garden pond at her family’s new home on Christmas Day. Her parents say she was playing happily before she went missing for a brief moment. The pond had a temporary fence, but the accident still happened. Her family posted a heartfelt tribute online and a fundraiser has raised over £19,000 to cover her memorial, funeral and counseling.
