A fire broke out overnight at the Spit marina in Mosman, Sydney, around 3:30 am. The blaze forced the evacuation of four people from one yacht, and three of the luxury vessels sank while a fourth was badly damaged. Firefighters and the Port Authority quickly contained the flames, performed hazmat cleanup, and prevented wider pollution. Police say the fire is not suspected to be arson and investigations are underway.
