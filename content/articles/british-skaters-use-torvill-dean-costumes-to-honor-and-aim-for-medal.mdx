Lewis Gibson and Lilah Fear, a British figure‑skating pair, posted Instagram photos dressed in the original costumes of Torvill and Dean’s famous 1984 Boléro routine. They recreated the iconic poses and wanted to honor their idols. The duo has trained hard, moved from Nottingham to Montreal, and climbed from 10th place to podium‑contender status at the World Championships. They hope to win a medal at the Milan Olympic event.
