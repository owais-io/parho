Trump says America’s global power is over and wants to take control of the Western Hemisphere, starting with Venezuela. He claims the U.S. will seize Venezuela’s oil and keep it under American control. Trump wants to revive the old Monroe Doctrine to dominate Latin America, a region China is already expanding into. Jones warns this could turn the U.S. into an empire that relies on violence and brings more authoritarian rule to the area.
