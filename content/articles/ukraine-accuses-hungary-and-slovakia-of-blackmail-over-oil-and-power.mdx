Ukraine says Hungary and Slovakia are blackmailing it by threatening to cut electricity unless Kyiv restarts Russian oil flows. The two EU neighbours also plan to block a €90bn war loan. Kyiv blames Russia for drone attacks that hit energy sites and caused explosions in cities like Lviv and Kyiv. Meanwhile, the Ukrainian president calls for new talks with Russia and the U.S., while Britain urges troop deployment to show support.
