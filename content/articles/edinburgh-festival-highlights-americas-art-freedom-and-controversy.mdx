This year’s Edinburgh Festival, celebrating 250 years of the U.S. Declaration of Independence, will showcase an all‑American lineup. Director Nicola Benedetti says the show will explore America’s creativity, freedom, and its darker sides. Highlights include a piano‑and‑orchestra duet, a ballet about AI, new operas, jazz from Duke Ellington, a museum exhibit on slavery, and world‑premiere performances by major orchestras. Tickets go on sale soon.
