UK Home Office is looking into AROPL Studios, a company run by the Ahmadi Religion of Peace and Light sect, to see if it abused skilled worker visas to bring followers into the country. The group, known for strange beliefs and a popular YouTube channel, had 12 such visas issued between 2022‑25. Similar immigration problems led to Swedish deportations, and the sect says it has a legal right to stay in the UK.
