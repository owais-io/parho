She was kept as a domestic servant by a mother of ten for 25 years, living in a cramped, dirty house in Gloucestershire. The abuser, Amanda Wixon, beat her, forced her to scrub floors, shave her head and drink bleach. Wixon was jailed for 13 years. The victim now lives with a caring family but still suffers trauma. The case highlights how social services failed to spot and stop the abuse.
