At the Calcutta Cup pre‑match ceremony in Scotland, Princess Anne mistakenly told 26‑year‑old Leicester forward Joe Heyes that he was former England star Joe Marler. Heyes laughed and didn’t correct her, saying he didn’t want to stir up drama. The princess, patron of Scottish Rugby, had joked about Marler’s TV show, but it was a surprise for Heyes. He said the moment was still special.
