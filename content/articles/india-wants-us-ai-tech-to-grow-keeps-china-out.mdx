India celebrated its 80th independence day as a Delhi AI summit showcased its push to use U.S. AI tech. Prime Minister Narendra Modi said AI could transform the economy and society, and U.S. firms like OpenAI, Google and Anthropic signed deals to bring chatbots to India. The U.S. wants India away from China’s AI, while India wants to avoid becoming a tech‑dependent ally. Talks also raised concerns about cultural diversity and data sovereignty.
