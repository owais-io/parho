Shoppers may pay up to 70% more for Christmas chocolates, and turkeys can cost £15 extra. Which? found festive chocolate saw the biggest jumps, especially Lindt truffles, while turkeys rose about 28% due to bird flu and higher farm costs. Poor cocoa harvests also pushed chocolate prices up. Waitrose had the steepest supermarket rise, Asda the smallest.
