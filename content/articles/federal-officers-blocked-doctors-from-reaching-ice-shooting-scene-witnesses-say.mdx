ICE agents shot Renee Good in Minneapolis. Witnesses say federal officers blocked the road, preventing emergency medics from reaching her quickly. Ambulance arrived 15 minutes later, but officers’ vehicles blocked the street, so paramedics had to walk Good out to get near the ambulance. No federal medical help was given, even though DHS policy says officers must help. DHS hasn't replied, and the county is still investigating.
