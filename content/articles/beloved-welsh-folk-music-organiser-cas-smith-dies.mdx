Cas Smith, a beloved figure in south Wales folk music, died at 78. For over four decades she organized the Pontardawe festival, handling public relations, publicity, craft markets and directing the event. Friendly and welcoming, she hosted musicians and fans, even the Oyster Band at her home. A nurse by training, Cas moved from Norfolk to Wales in 1979, married, had a daughter, and later partnered with Malcolm Weindling. She is survived by daughter Claire and two grandsons.
