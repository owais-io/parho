President Donald Trump has fired Kristi Noem, the head of the Department of Homeland Security, and said she will become a special envoy. He named Oklahoma Senator Markwayne Mullin as the new DHS secretary, starting March 31. Noem's removal comes after complaints about her handling of immigration and an alleged personal relationship with a deputy. Democrats welcomed the change, saying it ends a disastrous tenure.
