A secret nursing school in Myanmar’s jungle trained 21 students while dodging military drones. The program was created after the 2021 coup, when hospitals were attacked and internet shut down. International partners filmed lessons in the UK and smuggled them in memory sticks. Students learn online and in makeshift clinics, often moving to avoid airstrikes. Their graduates earned a “Phoenix” nursing degree, showing hope amid war.
