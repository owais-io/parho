Trump’s new bill could give $50 billion to states that meet certain rules, one of which is using more AI in hospitals. Rural hospitals that adopt AI could get tech help, training and remote monitoring. Experts say AI might ease doctors’ paperwork and help attract staff, but there are concerns about money, safety and privacy. They warn that poor hospitals might rush into AI without the right support, increasing risk.
