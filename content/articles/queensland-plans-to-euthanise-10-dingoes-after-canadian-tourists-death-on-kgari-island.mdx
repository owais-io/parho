The Queensland government says it will euthanise a pack of 10 dingoes linked to the death of 19‑year‑old Canadian tourist Piper James on K'gari Island. Rangers observed aggressive behaviour and called the animals a public‑safety risk. The move was criticised by the island’s Indigenous Butchulla people, who said they were not consulted, and by scientists who warn the cull could damage the island’s small dingo population and its genetics.
