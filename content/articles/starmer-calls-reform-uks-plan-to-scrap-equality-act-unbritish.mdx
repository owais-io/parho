Prime Minister Keir Starmer slammed Reform UK’s proposal to repeal the Equality Act, calling it shocking and un‑British. He warned that removing the law would undo decades of protection for women and people of colour, and said the act is a core British value. Starmer also urged anyone with information on the Epstein case to speak out, and defended keeping local elections on schedule.
