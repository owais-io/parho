Ipswich’s women, known as the Tractor Girls, are looking to keep their FA Cup tie with Sheffield United going while they fight to stay in the second tier of women's football. After a big shake‑up, long‑time manager Joe Sheehan was replaced, with former player David Wright taking charge and new technical director Sean Burt bringing fresh plans. The club is adding support staff and is hopeful that a few new signings can turn tiny margins into wins.
