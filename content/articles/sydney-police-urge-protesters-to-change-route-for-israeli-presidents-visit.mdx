During Israeli President Isaac Herzog’s visit to Sydney, a Palestinian‑support group plans to march through the CBD. Police, led by acting commissioner Paul Dunstan, asked them to move to Hyde Park to avoid conflict. Premier Chris Minns used special powers to block the original route, and the group says they’ll sue. Police say the change keeps peace, while protesters worry the powers curb free speech.
