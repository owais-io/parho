The former mayor of DeRidder, Louisiana, Misty Roberts, was found guilty of raping a 16‑year‑old boy at her home during a July pool party. A jury convicted her of statutory rape and indecent assault, giving a potential 10‑ to 7‑year prison term. She had resigned after an investigation began, and the city has since elected a new mayor. The case underscores abuse of power in local politics.
