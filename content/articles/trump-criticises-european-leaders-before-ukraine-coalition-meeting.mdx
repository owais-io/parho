Donald Trump said European leaders were wasting time as a video meeting of the “coalition of the willing” about Ukraine’s war approaches. He questioned U.S. involvement and warned that their planned talks could be pointless. The call will include Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Britain’s Keir Starmer, France’s Emmanuel Macron and Germany’s Friedrich Merkel, plus many other supporters. Meanwhile, Trump’s peace plan is seen as friendly to Russia, and Ukraine faces ongoing attacks in Pokrovsk and Myrnohrad.
