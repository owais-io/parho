A 16‑year‑old boy in Sydney has been charged with multiple terrorism offences after police found online “guides” that teach how to carry out mass‑casualty attacks. He was also charged with weapons offences, and a search of his home uncovered a knife, a gel blaster and extremist material. The boy, who has depression and is in counselling, was denied bail and will face up to 15 years in prison.
