Nell Frizzell recalls her dad arriving in the UK 43 years ago. He tried to roast chestnuts, but actually brought poisonous horse chestnuts, and his British friends helped stop a potential disaster. This memory shows how newcomers need friends and a sense of belonging. Frizzell shares her own experience hosting asylum seekers, offering food, a bed, and everyday help. She urges people to keep sharing and welcoming, just as she did for her dad and the refugees she hosts.
