Paul Evans writes columns that mix poetry, nature observation, science, conservation, and biblical ideas, offering readers calm and a new look at life after death. The letter praises him as a post‑pastoral shaman, noting how his words make the writer want to honour a dead tree. It shows how his writing helps people feel grounded and connected to nature, and invites readers to share their thoughts with the Guardian’s Letters section.
