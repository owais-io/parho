The Syrian army told people in three Aleppo neighbourhoods to leave by early afternoon after fighting with Kurdish-led forces, the SDF, reached a third day. After the deadline, both sides started shelling, causing at least eight civilian deaths and many injuries. About 140,000 people have fled, and the violence threatens to spread as the government and the SDF still disagree over how the Kurdish group will join the army.
