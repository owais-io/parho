After changing its name to MS Now, the cable news network announced a fresh lineup of shows. “Morning Joe” will drop from four to three hours, and a new two‑hour slot from 9‑11 am will be hosted by Stephanie Ruhle. Ali Velshi takes over late‑night duties, while new hosts join the 7‑pm and 12‑pm programs. The shifts aim to boost daytime ratings ahead of the midterms and 2028 election.
