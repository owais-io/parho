The U.S. Justice Department has sued Harvard, accusing the university of withholding documents needed for a federal investigation into alleged racial bias in its admissions. The lawsuit says Harvard has ignored requests for applicant data for more than a year, slowing the probe. Harvard insists it is cooperating and defending its legal rights, while the Trump administration has also threatened to cut research funding and demand changes to diversity programs.
