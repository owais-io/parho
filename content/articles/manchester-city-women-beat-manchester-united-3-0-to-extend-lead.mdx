Manchester City Women beat Manchester United 3‑0 in a derby match at the Etihad, with goals from Rebecca Knaak, Bunny Shaw and Lauren Hemp. The win kept City at the top of the Women’s Super League and put them four points ahead of United. The defeat gave United their first back‑to‑back loss and raised questions about their thin squad. City played an easy, attacking game, while United struggled to keep up.
