Doctors in England will strike from Dec 17‑22 because they want better pay and job security. The British Medical Association says the government hasn’t offered real wages or enough training places. Doctors say they need a 26% pay rise to catch up with inflation since 2008 and more training spots. The strikes have already cost the NHS £300 m and disrupted many appointments. The union wants the government to act soon or risk more strikes.
