In December 2022, Stephen McCullagh, a 36‑year‑old from Lisburn, tried to hide the murder of his pregnant girlfriend, Natalie McNally, by uploading a pre‑recorded video‑game livestream to his YouTube channel. While viewers believed he was playing Grand Theft Auto at home, he was actually traveling to Lurgan, where he stabbed, strangled and beat McNally. A jury found him guilty of murder in a five‑week trial, ending his elaborate alibi.
