Families of prisoners who are on hunger strikes because of their support for Palestine Action are asking UK Justice Secretary David Lammy to meet with them. The strikers have been refusing food for weeks and several are now in hospital. Lawyers sent a letter saying the secretary is violating policy by not meeting the families. The government says it will keep the prisoners safe, but the families want a meeting to end the crisis.
