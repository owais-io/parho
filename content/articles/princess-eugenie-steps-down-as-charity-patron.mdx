Princess Eugenie said she will no longer be patron of the UK anti‑slavery charity Anti‑Slavery International after documents linking her father, Prince Andrew, to Jeffrey Epstein were released. No wrongdoing by the princess has been found. The charity removed her profile and thanked her for support. She has long campaigned against modern slavery and co‑founded a separate charity. Her stepping down comes amid scrutiny of the royal family's ties to Epstein.
