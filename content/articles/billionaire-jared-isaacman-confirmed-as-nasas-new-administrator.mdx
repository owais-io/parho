The U.S. Senate approved billionaire Jared Isaacman, a close friend of Elon Musk, as NASA’s new administrator. Trump nominated him and the vote was 67‑30. Isaacman wants NASA to accelerate plans to out‑race China to the Moon by 2028. He has paid for two private SpaceX crew flights. Earlier, the nomination was paused during a dispute between Musk and Trump. Acting chief Sean Duffy had hoped to merge NASA with his Transportation Department, but Isaacman blocked that.
