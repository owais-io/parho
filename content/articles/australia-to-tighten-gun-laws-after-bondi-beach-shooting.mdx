Australia’s Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said the government will introduce stricter gun laws after 15 people were shot dead at Bondi beach during a holiday event. The NSW premier also pledged reforms. The attack left 27 people injured, including a 10‑year‑old girl and a Holocaust survivor. Lifeguards and bystanders acted quickly, saving lives. Australians are being urged to donate blood and support the Jewish community, as leaders call for stronger anti‑antisemitism measures.
