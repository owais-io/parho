Zack Polanski, the Green Party leader, was caught up in a bizarre tabloid story called ‘Boobgate’. It turned out that he was just a hypnotherapist who once helped a client ask a Sun journalist if mind power could enlarge breasts. The column argues the story is a joke, not evidence of his character or politics. Polanski’s real focus is environmentalism, not body‑image gimmicks.
