The Yemen‑based Houthi rebels, backed by Iran, fired a missile at Israel for the first time, joining the Iran‑Israel war that began after Israel’s strike on Iran’s leadership. U.S. Secretary of State Marco Rubio said the fighting would end in “weeks.” Meanwhile, Iran has hit Saudi and other Gulf targets, threatening shipping in the Strait of Hormuz, while the U.S. and Israel continue air strikes on Iran’s nuclear sites. The conflict has already caused many casualties and heightened regional tension.
