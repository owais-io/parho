The 2025 wellness buzz includes “dopamine menus” that list mood‑boosting activities, and a book called The Let Them Theory that says let people be. Fitness fans keep buying creatine for extra energy, while influencers tout psyllium husk as a cheap weight‑loss aid. The idea of “fibermaxxing” – eating lots of fiber – is growing. Other trends are weighted vests for bone health, “little treats” for daily joy, and a “bird theory” that paying attention to your partner matters.
