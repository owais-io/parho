Liverpool’s new manager Arne Slot is hoping their upcoming game against Manchester City will show how much the team has improved after a rough start to the season. Despite a 3‑0 loss and a string of defeats, the Reds have started to press better, win back chances and use new signings like Wirtz, Ekitiké and Kerkez. The match will test whether the team can finally stop losing and regain confidence.
