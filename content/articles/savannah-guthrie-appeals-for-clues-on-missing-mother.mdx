Savannah Guthrie and her family are asking Tucson residents for any clues about her mother Nancy, who disappeared on Jan 31. The 84‑year‑old vanished from her home, and investigators found blood and a glove that turned out to belong to a restaurant worker. No arrests have been made, and a $1 million reward is on offer. Guthrie keeps the case in the public eye while the search continues.
