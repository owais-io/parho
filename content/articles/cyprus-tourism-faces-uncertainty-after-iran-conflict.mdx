The start of the tourist season in Ayia Napa is shaky as the US‑led offensive in Iran raises fears. Many tourists still come, but bookings drop and flights cancel because the island sits close to the Middle East. The British base at Akrotiri was hit, and the Foreign Office warned of possible attacks. Local business owners and visitors remain calm but wary, unsure how long the war will affect the season.
