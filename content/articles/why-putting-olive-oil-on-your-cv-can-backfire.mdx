The job market is tough, so people try to make their CVs stand out. A recent online argument erupted when a candidate put “olive oil” as an interest, and a social‑media user said it cost them an interview. The post sparked debate about what hobbies to list. Some say only mainstream activities help; others argue personal interests show personality. The bottom line is you should know who will read your CV before deciding what to include.
