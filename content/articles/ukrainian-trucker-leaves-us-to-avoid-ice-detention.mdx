A Ukrainian truck driver who had come to the US under a Biden program lost his commercial licence after new Trump‑era rules tightened licensing for immigrant drivers. Fearing arrest and detention by ICE, he returned to war‑torn Odesa. He’s not alone – hundreds of immigrant truckers in states like South Carolina and California are losing licences, sparking debate over safety, language and immigration policy.
