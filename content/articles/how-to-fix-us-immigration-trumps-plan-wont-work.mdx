Immigration remains a hot debate in the U.S. After an ICE agent killed a woman, Democrats worry that keeping the Department of Homeland Security funded could lead to another shutdown, so they push limits on ICE. Biden kept asylum rights but put many migrants in Mexico. The author says Trump’s plan to stop asylum is wrong. Instead, more judges and a time limit on deporting people who have lived in the U.S. for years could help create a fairer system.
