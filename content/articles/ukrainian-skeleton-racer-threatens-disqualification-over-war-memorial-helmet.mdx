Ukrainian skeleton athlete Vladyslav Heraskevych plans to run in the 2026 Winter Olympics wearing a helmet that shows pictures of people killed in Russia’s invasion. The International Olympic Committee says the helmet is a political statement and could disqualify him. Heraskevych says the helmet honors fallen athletes and is a tribute, not politics. He can wear a black armband instead, but he wants the helmet on the track.
