BP is selling its large refinery in Gelsenkirchen, Germany, to Klesch Group. The move is part of a $20 billion asset‑sale plan aimed at slashing costs. The refinery processes 12 million tonnes of crude a year, and BP expects the sale to save about $1 billion in operating expenses. The sale helps BP hit a 2027 cost‑cut target of $6.5‑7.5 billion and supports a broader reshuffle.
