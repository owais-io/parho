McCullum, the former New Zealand coach, slammed England’s “Bazball” style, calling it a bad word and saying the team trained too hard before a night match. His comments came after England lost the first two Ashes Tests and have become a laughing point for Australians. The piece argues that if England keep losing, McCullum’s criticism might be his last big talking point. It also looks at how the team’s prep and style are being questioned.
