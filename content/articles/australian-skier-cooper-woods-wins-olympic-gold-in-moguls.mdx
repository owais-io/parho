Australian freestyle skier Cooper Woods shocked the world by winning gold in moguls at the Milano Cortina Winter Games. The 25‑year‑old from New South Wales had barely made the world‑cup podium once and was ranked 20th before the race. He topped the second qualifier, then beat top competitors Mikael Kingsbury and Ikuma Horishima in the final. Woods said the pressure helped him perform and that the medal is a gift for his family and teammates.
