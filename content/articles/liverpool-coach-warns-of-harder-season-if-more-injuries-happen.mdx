Liverpool’s manager Arne Slot says the team faces a tough challenge if more players stay hurt. The club tried to sign Dutch defender Lutsharel Geertruida before the deadline, but the deal fell through when Sunderland couldn’t find a replacement. With key defenders out and a hip injury to Joe Gomez, Slot worries about the upcoming matches and stresses the need for players to stay fit. He also highlighted the new signing of Jérémy Jacquet for next season.
