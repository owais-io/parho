Police data shows violent or menacing threats to Australian federal politicians have almost doubled, from 555 in 2021‑22 to 951 in 2024‑25. The spike includes bomb threats, death threats, and online hate, even leading to the prime minister’s evacuation from his Canberra home. Since October, 21 people have been charged. The AFP has set up special teams to track high‑harm political threats and urges more security checks.
