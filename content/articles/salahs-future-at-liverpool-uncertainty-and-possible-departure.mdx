Mohamed Salah, Liverpool’s star forward, may leave the club after a rough season. He’s been left out of the squad, scored few goals, and is unhappy with the new system that demands more defence on the right. With his contract ending soon, the team’s new signings suggest they’re planning a post‑Salah era. The situation has created tension, and the club may let him go for free.
