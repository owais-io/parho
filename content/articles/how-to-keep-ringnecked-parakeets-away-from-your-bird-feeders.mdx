Ring‑necked parakeets are a growing problem in the UK. These colourful birds, originally from Africa, are crowding out native birds and eating flowers and fruit. Some London residents have found that hanging realistic rubber snakes near bird feeders stops the parakeets from coming. This simple trick lets local birds eat suet and seeds again, keeping gardens safer and the wildlife healthier.
