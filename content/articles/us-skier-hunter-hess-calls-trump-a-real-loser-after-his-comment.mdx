At the Winter Olympics, President Donald Trump called US half‑pipe skier Hunter Hess a “real loser.” Hess showed his own “L” sign and said he was proud of the label. He said the two weeks since the comment were the toughest of his life but that skiing and family helped him get through it. Despite a training injury and pain, Hess kept competing and said he’d never let the controversy stop him.
