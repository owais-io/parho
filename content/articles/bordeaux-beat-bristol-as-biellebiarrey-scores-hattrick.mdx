In Bristol, Bordeaux Bègles defended their Champions Cup title by beating Bristol with a 45-20 win. French winger Louis Bielle‑Biarrey scored a hat‑trick, including the winning try. The game was rainy, and Bristol made many handling errors, which Bordeaux capitalised on. The win keeps Bordeaux in the top spot for the tournament and makes their path to the final easier.
