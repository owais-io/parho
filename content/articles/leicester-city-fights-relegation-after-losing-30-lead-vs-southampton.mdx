Leicester City is fighting to stay in the Premier League after losing a 3‑0 lead at home to Southampton. Just days after a 6‑point penalty for financial misdeeds, the Foxes sit one place above the drop zone. Manager Andy King is only an interim boss, and other names like Steven Gerrard are rumored for the role. With only one win in seven games, the team must win the FA Cup match to avoid a double relegation scare.
