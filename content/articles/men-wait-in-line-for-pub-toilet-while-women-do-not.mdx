At a busy pub on Christmas Eve, a group of friends got caught up in a long line for the men's toilet while the women's room had no queue. The writer, Adrian Chiles, found the experience eye‑opening and a bit humiliating. He mused about how men rarely face such lines and how women often do, and wondered whether waiting times should be equal for both genders. The story highlights everyday gender differences in public bathrooms.
