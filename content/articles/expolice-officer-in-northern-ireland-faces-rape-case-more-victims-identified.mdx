Police investigators in Northern Ireland have found more people who may have been raped by a former officer. The ex‑police man was arrested in December 2025 for more than ten crimes, including rape, while on duty between 2000 and 2009. The ombudsman’s team is pulling all its resources into the case because it’s big and complex. It could affect other investigations, so other cases might take longer.
