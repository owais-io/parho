The US carried out air strikes on Iran without telling the UK the exact timing or plans. Britain, led by Prime Minister Keir Starmer, had refused to let US planes use British bases, but later changed its mind. The UK was warned about the operation through usual intelligence channels and even closed its embassy in Tehran. The move shows tensions in the special relationship and how quickly the UK adjusted its stance.
