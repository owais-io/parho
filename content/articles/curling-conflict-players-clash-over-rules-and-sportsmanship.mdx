The Olympic curling event has been rocked by a row between Canadian and Swedish teams over alleged illegal double‑touch moves. Canadian skip Marc Kennedy accused his Swedish opponent of cheating, sparking heated exchanges and viral footage of the controversy. Organisers added extra referees to monitor the hog‑line, but curlers complained it felt intrusive. The dispute has drawn huge online attention, turning the sport into a flash‑point for debate about rules and sportsmanship.
