Mitchell Starc, usually a fast bowler, also scored 77 runs in Australia’s second innings, helping the team move from 383 for 7 to 491 for 9. His partnership with Scott Boland built a steady score while England tried to chase. After batting, Starc bowled again, taking wickets and putting England under pressure. The combined effort gave Australia a comfortable lead and a win over England.
