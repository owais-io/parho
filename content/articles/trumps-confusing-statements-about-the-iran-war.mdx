Trump’s words about the U.S.–Israel campaign against Iran have been all over the place. Over the second week of fighting, he said the war was a short “minor excursion”, then claimed it was “very complete”, and later said it would end “soon” or “when I feel it in my bones.” Officials also said the strike campaign isn’t endless, but the messaging keeps shifting, confusing the public and hurting confidence in a clear timeline.
