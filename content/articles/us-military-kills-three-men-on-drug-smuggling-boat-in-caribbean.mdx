The U.S. military fired a missile at a boat in the Caribbean that was allegedly smuggling drugs, killing three men. It was the third attack in a week. A video shows the strike and the boat burning. The Southern Command said no U.S. troops were hurt, but human‑rights groups argue such strikes target civilians and are illegal. The action is part of the Trump administration’s push to stop “narco‑terrorists,” adding to over 150 deaths since September.
