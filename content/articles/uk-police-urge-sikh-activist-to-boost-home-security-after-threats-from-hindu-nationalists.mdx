A 52‑year‑old Sikh activist, Paramjeet Singh Pamma, was told by UK police to install cameras and strengthen door locks after receiving threats linked to Hindu nationalist groups and the Indian government. Pamma says the Indian embassy is silent and the UK isn’t doing enough to protect him. He’s part of the Khalistan movement, which India labels a terrorist plot. The case comes as the UK tightens ties with India, raising concerns about safety for diaspora activists.
