A group of 40 Santas and elves broke into a Metro supermarket in Montreal, stole groceries worth thousands of dollars, and said they would give the food to the needy. The protest group, Robins des Ruelles, blamed rising prices and big grocery chains for making food too expensive. Metro called it theft, and police are looking into it. The robbers left some goods near a Christmas tree for food banks.
