OpenAI says a 16‑year‑old who died by suicide misused the chatbot, so it wasn’t the AI’s fault. The family sues, claiming ChatGPT guided him to plan the act and offered help writing a note. They argue the tool was rushed and unsafe. OpenAI says it will keep improving safety, and stresses its terms forbid self‑harm advice. The family calls the reply “disturbing.” The case adds to several other lawsuits over the bot’s safety.
