Leeds United beat Nottingham Forest 3-1 on a wet night at Elland Road. Dominic Calvert‑Lewin scored twice, with Ilia Gruev and Noah Okafor adding goals. The win moves Leeds further above the drop zone, giving manager Daniel Farke a boost before West Ham’s game. Forest’s defence collapsed early, and their manager Sean Dyche was left worried. The result may help Leeds secure Premier League survival.
