A Channel 4 series called “Dirty Business” shocked viewers by revealing how Britain’s private water companies illegally dump untreated sewage into rivers and the sea, a practice that contributed to an eight‑year‑old’s E. coli death in Devon. The show follows amateur detectives uncovering the pollution while the Environment Agency failed to act. After the drama aired, many people downloaded a sewage‑alert app, marking familiar beaches as danger zones.
