European intelligence agencies say Russia is nearly ready to send drones to Iran for its war against the US and Israel. The drones would be the first lethal aid Russia has given since the conflict began. The move follows secret talks after US and Israeli attacks on Tehran. European ministers accuse Russia of helping Iran target U.S. forces and warn the plan could widen the conflict.
