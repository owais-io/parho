Michigan has hired 66‑year‑old Kyle Whittingham as its new head football coach in a five‑year deal. The former Utah legend, who won 177 games, takes over after Sherrone Moore was fired for an inappropriate relationship with staff. Moore was arrested on several charges and spent two nights in jail. Michigan is looking for stability after the scandal‑filled season. This move aims to rebuild trust and bring experienced leadership.
