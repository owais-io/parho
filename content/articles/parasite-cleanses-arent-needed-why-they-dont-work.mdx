Social media is full of posts about parasite cleanses that promise to detox your body. But experts say there's no evidence that these herbal supplements or diets actually work. Parasites are common in areas with poor sanitation, but most people in the US don't have them. The cleanses can be risky, cause side‑effects, and might just make you eat healthier. If you suspect a parasite, see a doctor instead of doing a DIY cleanse.
