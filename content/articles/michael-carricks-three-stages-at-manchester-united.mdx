Michael Carrick joined Manchester United in 2006, scored his first goal and helped the club win its 2007 title. He played 464 games, leaving in 2018 after setting up the winning goal in his final match and receiving a standing ovation. After retiring, he became a coach under José Mourinho, and later served as United’s interim manager, winning his first game in 2021.
