Google says that in the next decade quantum computers may be able to crack the encryption that keeps our data safe. It warns banks, governments and tech firms to start switching to “post‑quantum” security methods now. While fully powerful quantum machines are still years away, the warning highlights that data stored today could be read by a future quantum attack. The company urges industry to act early.
