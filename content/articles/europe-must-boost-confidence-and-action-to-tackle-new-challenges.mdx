Europe is struggling to keep up with the world, especially after a tough start to 2026. EU leaders are dealing with a €90 bn loan for Ukraine, US pressure over Iran protests, and a weak grip on global politics because of internal vetoes and a timid approach. The article argues the EU should act more decisively, invest in its own military, and strengthen social solidarity to protect its values and stand stronger against other powers.
