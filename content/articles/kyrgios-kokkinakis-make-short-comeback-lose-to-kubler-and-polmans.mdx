Nick Kyrgios and Thanasi Kokkinakis made a one‑night comeback in the Australian Open doubles. The pair, both nursing injuries, were defeated 6‑4, 4‑6, 7‑6(10‑4) by fellow Australians Jason Kubler and Marc Polmans. A tense third set saw both men limping and the match turn on a video‑review call. After the loss, Kokkinakis said he will take a serious break, and Kyrgios hinted at reshuffling his schedule.
