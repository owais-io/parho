At the Australian Open, Carlos Alcaraz fought a 5‑hour, 27‑minute clash with Alexander Zverev. The match, the third‑longest in Aussie‑Open history, saw Alcaraz suffer cramps in the third set but use a medical timeout to recover. He turned the tide in the fourth and fifth sets, winning 7‑5 to stay one step closer to a career Grand Slam.
