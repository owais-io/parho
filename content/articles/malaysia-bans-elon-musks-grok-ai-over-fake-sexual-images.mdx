Malaysia and Indonesia temporarily shut down the AI chatbot Grok, which is part of Elon Musk’s X social media platform. The ban comes after people used Grok to create fake, sexual pictures of women and children. The Malaysian commission said the tool could make non‑consensual deepfakes that hurt human rights. The ban also follows criticism from the UK, Australia and several European regulators, who want stronger safety rules.
