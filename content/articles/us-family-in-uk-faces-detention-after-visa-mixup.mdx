An American couple and their two children, who moved to the UK in 2019 on a skilled‑worker visa, have been forced into a dangerous immigration limbo after their application for indefinite leave to remain was rejected. It’s unclear if the rejection was due to a wrong form or a Home Office error. The parents are now on bail, barred from work, and facing possible detention, which has drained their savings and put the family at risk of homelessness.
