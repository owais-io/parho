Israel has begun a ground invasion of southern Lebanon, aiming to push Hezbollah back from the border. Heavy fighting is taking place around Khiam and several border villages. Hezbollah fighters use anti‑tank weapons, drones and artillery, holding their ground. Israeli troops have bombed bridges, roads and even medical centres to cut supplies, while building a buffer zone to keep Israeli communities safe. The clashes are causing civilian casualties and stirring anger across Lebanon.
