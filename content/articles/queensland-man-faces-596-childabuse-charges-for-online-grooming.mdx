A 27‑year‑old man from Maryborough, Queensland, has been charged with 596 offences that involve making and sharing child‑abuse images and grooming children online. Police say he used fake online profiles to trick kids aged 7‑15 into sending sexual pictures, and kept thousands of videos on his hard drive. Investigators identified about 259 victims so far, but may find more. The case is still under investigation.
