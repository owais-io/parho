The 2025 Africa Cup of Nations in Morocco exposed big problems with refereeing and field quality. VAR was slow, key penalties were missed, and a controversial referee decision forced Senegal to walk off the pitch. Politics may have hurt the officiating body, while bad weather and uneven pitches damaged player performance and safety. Fans and players say the tournament needs better referees, cleaner fields, and fairer conditions.
