Bayern Munich are discussing extending Harry Kane’s contract. The England striker, who joined in 2023, has already won the Bundesliga and is the league’s top scorer twice. He’s scoring 21 goals in 19 games this season, chasing Lewandowski’s record. Bayern say they’re patient and have no rush, while waiting for Kane’s reply. Barcelona also eye him after Lewandowski’s contract ends.
