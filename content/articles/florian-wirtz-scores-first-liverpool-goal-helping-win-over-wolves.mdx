At Anfield, Liverpool beat Wolves 3-1. Florian Wirtz scored his first goal for the club, the opener that set the tone. Liverpool also earned a goal from Gravenberch, and a second from Wirtz, giving them a solid lead. The game was emotional because the teams honored late striker Diogo Jota, with his family present. Despite Wolves’ efforts, Liverpool’s defense held and they sealed the victory.
