Reform UK plans to fire the top civil servants in every department and replace them with people more likely to implement the party’s agenda. Critics warn this could destabilise government, strip away expertise and make the civil service a political tool. The plan mirrors Trump‑style executive orders and follows the party’s promise to bring in outsiders, while big donations have helped it expand its policy teams.
