Shabana Mahmood, Britain’s home secretary, said the US claims that Europe is facing “civilisational erasure” because of Muslims are just political moves. She said the UK handles multiculturalism well and is proud of its diversity. Mahmood also warned that Trump’s strategy tries to push European politics toward a “western identity” and could spread anti‑Muslim sentiment. She defended the UK’s inclusive values and said the country can still welcome Muslim leaders.
