After an ICE officer shot and killed a woman in Minneapolis, videos spread online and drew a flood of reporters. The city’s strong local media, like the Star Tribune and Minnesota Public Radio, kept coverage steady, while national outlets such as CNN, MSNBC and CBS came to the scene. Journalists spoke about staying close to the action, handling new protests and additional shootings, and the challenges of covering a rapidly unfolding story.
