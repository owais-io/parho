Travis Head made an unbeaten 142 at Adelaide, giving Australia a huge lead and putting England in trouble. The crowd cheered as Head celebrated his fourth Test century in the city. England tried a new aggressive style called Bazball, but their batting was shaky and they were all out for 286. Australia built a strong position and are now 3‑0 up, looking to keep the Ashes.
