A federal judge named Aileen Cannon has ruled that the Justice Department cannot publish former special counsel Jack Smith’s report on President Trump’s handling of classified documents at Mar‑a‑Lago. Cannon said the report was prepared after she had dismissed the case and that releasing it would harm Trump and reveal sensitive grand‑jury information. The decision stops the report from being sent to Congress or the public.
