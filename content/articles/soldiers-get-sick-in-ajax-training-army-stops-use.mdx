The British army has paused training with its Ajax armored vehicles after about 30 troops fell ill from loud noise and strong vibrations. Some soldiers vomited and felt so unsteady they couldn't control themselves during recent exercises on Salisbury Plain. The Ministry of Defence says most soldiers are now fine, but a few are still receiving medical care. Testing will continue to fix the vibration and noise problems.
