A 43‑year‑old Swindon man, Christopher Trybus, faces charges of manslaughter, rape and controlling behaviour after his wife, Tarryn Baird, died by suicide in 2017. He told the court that learning of her death was “the worst day of my life” and described breaking down on the way home. Prosecutors presented an audio recording of alleged abuse and evidence that he deleted phone data. He denies all accusations; the case is ongoing.
