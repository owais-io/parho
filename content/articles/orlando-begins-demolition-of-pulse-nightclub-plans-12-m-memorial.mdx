Orlando has started tearing down the Pulse nightclub, the site of a 2016 shooting that killed 49 people. The city plans to replace the building with a $12 M memorial that will open in 2027. The new site will feature a reflection pool and quiet spaces for visitors. After years of debate and a failed private memorial proposal, the demolition marks the start of a new chapter of healing for the community.
