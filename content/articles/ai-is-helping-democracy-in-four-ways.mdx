AI is helping democracy in four ways. In Japan, a candidate used an AI avatar to answer thousands of voters and later built a civic app that lets people ask about bills. Brazil’s courts use AI to sort cases, cutting backlogs, while lawyers file more cases faster. German voters can chat with AI tools that explain party positions. In California, a nonprofit collects officials’ public statements and AI spots voting shifts linked to donations, giving reporters new story ideas.
