Federal agents have arrested 25 of 30 people charged in a January protest at a Minnesota church that targeted an ICE officer who is also a pastor. The demonstration upset a service and followed the deaths of two Minnesotans at the hands of ICE agents. Two journalists were also charged. Attorney General Pam Bondi said the DOJ would prosecute anyone attacking places of worship, while the church filed a lawsuit.
