An official report says MI5 let a top IRA double‑agent, known as Stakeknife, kill fellow republicans and then hid evidence that would have led to police investigations. The report criticised MI5’s delay in sharing documents and called it a serious failure. No one has been prosecuted for the murders, leaving families still in pain. MI5 has apologised and promised an independent review of how it handled the case.
