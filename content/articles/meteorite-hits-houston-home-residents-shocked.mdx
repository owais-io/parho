Residents in a Houston suburb said a rock fell through their roof, and local officials and NASA confirmed it was a meteorite. The fireball was seen in the sky and made a loud boom, then broke apart above the area. Pieces hit a home, leaving a hole in the ceiling. The incident comes after similar meteor events in Ohio and Atlanta. Officials are investigating.
