David Dinsmore, the new permanent secretary for government communications, will brief ministers on a new media strategy aimed at fighting far‑right content online. The plan will update how the government talks to voters, using social media, influencers and a new New Media Unit that tracks top stories on Facebook, TikTok and other platforms. Starmer’s team also plans to grow in‑house digital skills and invest in training MPs, after spotting millions of anti‑Labour videos on YouTube.
