Former President Donald Trump has filed a lawsuit against JPMorgan Chase and CEO Jamie Dimon, saying the bank unfairly shut his accounts after the January 6 riot. Trump claims the bank “debanked” him for political reasons and blacklisted him and his family. JPMorgan says it only closes accounts to manage legal risk. The case highlights tensions between politicians and banks over political bias.
