Ousmane Dembélé started as a promising youngster in Rennes and faced ups and downs, including injury struggles at Barcelona. After moving to PSG, he grew into a key player, winning the Guardian’s “best male player” award. His success comes from resilience, hard work and a willingness to learn, not just natural skill. Dembélé’s calm focus and creative play have made him the main man for his team.
