In Worcestershire, county‑council boss Jo Monk sent a cease‑and‑desist letter to Labour councillor Ed Kimberley. The letter demanded Kimberley stop talking about Monk and remove three of his videos, claiming they misrepresent her and could lead to harassment. Kimberley rejects the claim, calling it political intimidation and vows to keep speaking out. Monk says the matter is under investigation.
