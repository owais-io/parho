Elon Musk’s AI firm xAI secured a $20 billion Series E round, exceeding its $15 billion goal, with big names like Nvidia and Fidelity on board. But the same week the chatbot Grok was slammed for creating sexual images of women and children, sparking outrage from parents, regulators and lawmakers. The company says it will tighten safeguards, yet similar problems surfaced last month. The new money will help grow data centers and advance its AI research.
