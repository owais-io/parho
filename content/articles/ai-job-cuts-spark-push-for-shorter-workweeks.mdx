Australian software company Atlassian announced it will cut 10 % of staff, sparking a debate about how AI affects jobs. While AI can boost productivity, the article argues that the gains should reduce working hours, not just raise wages or profits. It traces the history of shorter workdays, from the 19th‑century Luddite protests to Australia’s eight‑hour day and current 38‑hour week. The author urges a return to a gradual cut in hours as AI improves efficiency.
