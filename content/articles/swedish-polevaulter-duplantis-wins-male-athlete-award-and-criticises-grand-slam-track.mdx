Armand Duplantis, pole‑vault champion, was named World Athletics’ male athlete of the year after 2025. He criticised Michael Johnson’s Grand Slam Track for excluding field events. The award went to him after breaking four world records and staying unbeaten in 16 races. The female athlete of the year was Sydney McLaughlin‑Levrone, who won the 400 m gold in Tokyo and has been unbeaten in 24 races. Other honours went to Nicola Olyslagers, Emmanuel Wanyonyi, Sebastian Sawe and María Pérez.
