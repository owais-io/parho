Trump spoke at the Davos business summit, saying he wants Greenland but will not use military force. He warned Europe that if they refuse, he could impose tariffs, but then paused those plans while talks continued. Trump also talked about a US missile‑defence base in Greenland and said the U.S. needs to own the island to defend it. European leaders and some U.S. senators reacted, with mixed feelings.
