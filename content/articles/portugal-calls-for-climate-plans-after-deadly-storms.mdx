Portugal is in chaos after a series of record storms that killed 16 people and left 33,000 homes without power. The floods forced the evacuation of 3,000 people and damaged the A1 motorway. The interior minister resigned, and scientists say the country is still built for 19th‑century weather. Experts have made roof‑repair guides, and the government faces heavy criticism. Meanwhile, similar storms hit Spain, prompting emergency alerts.
