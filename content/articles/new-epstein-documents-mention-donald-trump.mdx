A fresh batch of documents from the Justice Department about Jeffrey Epstein has just been released. The papers mention Donald Trump, saying he flew on Epstein’s private jet several times in the 1990s with other passengers, including women who could have known Epstein. The DOJ says those claims are false and unverified. A fake letter to a convicted abuser was also found. The files also touch on Prince Andrew and other unrelated matters.
