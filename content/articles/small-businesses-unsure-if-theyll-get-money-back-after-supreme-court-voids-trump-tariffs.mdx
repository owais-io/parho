The Supreme Court just ruled that Trump’s tariffs were illegal, opening a door for up to $175 bn in refunds. But small companies aren’t sure how to claim the money, and many worry the paperwork and legal costs could outweigh the benefit. Some have already raised prices, struggled to pay for production, and are hesitant to pursue refunds.
