Two journalists, former CNN anchor Don Lemon and reporter Georgia Fort, were taken into custody while covering a protest against ICE in Minnesota. Press‑freedom groups say the arrests are part of a broader crackdown by the Trump administration. Judges had already dismissed the charges. Critics call it a First Amendment attack, and lawmakers from both parties condemned the move. This incident raises concerns about press safety.
