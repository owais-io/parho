MI5 has issued a public apology and paid £200,000 to a woman named Beth, who says she was attacked with a machete and abused by an MI5 agent while he was infiltrating the far‑right. The agency admitted it misled courts, broke its own secrecy rules, and used a “no‑confirm, no‑deny” policy wrongly. The Prime Minister ordered an investigation, and MI5 vows to tighten record‑keeping and protect victims.
