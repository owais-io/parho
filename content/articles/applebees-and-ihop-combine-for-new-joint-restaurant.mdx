In Hawthorne, New York, Applebee’s and IHOP opened a new joint location with a big ribbon‑cutting ceremony and colorful balloons. The move is part of a plan to revive both chains, which have seen sales drop as competitors like Chili’s gain popularity. Locals are excited, hoping the new hybrid spot will bring families back to a familiar, affordable dining spot. Only time will tell if the fusion works.
