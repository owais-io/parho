A local derby between Sunderland and Newcastle ended in a 1‑0 win for the north‑east club, thanks to an own goal from Newcastle striker Nick Woltemade. The game saw Sunderland stay in the Premier League, while Newcastle's manager Eddie Howe was left frustrated after a poor performance. The match was also a tribute to former Sunderland star Gary Rowell, who died a day before. Fans celebrated in a tense but memorable encounter.
