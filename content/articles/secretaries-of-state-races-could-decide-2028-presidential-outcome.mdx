In 2026 midterms, voters will pick secretaries of state in 26 states, including key battlegrounds like Nevada, Arizona and Michigan. These officials run elections and certify results. After 2020, they were in the spotlight when they resisted Trump’s attempts to overturn the vote. In 2022, election deniers won some GOP spots, raising worries for 2028. Democrats are raising money to defend and flip seats while the Trump administration pressures states for voter lists. Secretaries of state could decide the 2028 outcome.
