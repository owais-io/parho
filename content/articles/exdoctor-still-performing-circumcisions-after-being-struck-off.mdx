A 55‑year‑old former GP, Zuber Bux, was removed from the medical register for a reckless circumcision that nearly killed a toddler. Despite this, he still advertises and performs circumcisions as a layperson. The case highlights a lack of regulation for the procedure, leading to calls from campaigners and the legal system to treat circumcision as potential child abuse.
