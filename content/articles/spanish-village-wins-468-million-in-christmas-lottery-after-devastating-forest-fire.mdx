After a massive forest fire in August that destroyed 55,000 hectares of woodland and left 10,000 residents of La Bañeza without homes, the town surprised everyone by winning €468 million in the Christmas lottery. The prize, split among 117 series sold locally, means each resident gets a share of the huge payout. Nearby Villablino, still grieving a mining tragedy, also bought a winning ticket and may receive millions. The win is seen as a lifeline after a year of disaster.
