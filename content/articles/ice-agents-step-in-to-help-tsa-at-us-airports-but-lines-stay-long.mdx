ICE agents have been deployed to help screen passengers at some U.S. airports because many TSA staff have quit. While some travelers reported shorter waits, others still faced hours of waiting. The new biometric checks have added some time, and the presence of ICE has raised privacy worries. Travelers are still advised to arrive early for flights. Congress is debating funding for the Department of Homeland Security, and President Trump is threatening an executive order to pay TSA workers.
