The FBI entered the Los Angeles Unified School District headquarters and the home of superintendent Alberto Carvalho as part of an ongoing investigation. Warranted searches were carried out at both locations, and authorities also checked a former Florida residence where Carvalho worked. The district said it was cooperating and had no extra info. No details of the case were released yet.
