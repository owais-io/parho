Judge Royce Lamberth ordered that more than 1,000 employees of Voice of America, the U.S. government’s international radio service, be brought back to work. The ruling came after a Trump‑era order dismantled the network and forced mass layoffs. Lamberth said the agency must show a plan to get VOA on the air again and called former head Kari Lake’s leadership unlawful. Lake plans to appeal the decision.
