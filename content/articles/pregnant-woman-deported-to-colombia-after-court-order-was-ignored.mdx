A 21‑year‑old pregnant woman was sent back to Colombia by U.S. ICE even after a judge tried to stop her. The judge said ICE must give her medical care and not deport her before Jan. 30. But ICE had already put her on a plane. Lawyers say this ignores rules that usually protect pregnant detainees and raise concerns about her health.
