Police in New South Wales are confident that Julian Ingram, who is suspected of killing a pregnant woman, her boyfriend and her aunt in Lake Cargelligo, is still alive. They think he may be getting help and supplies from locals as the heat hits 40°C. Authorities are searching the surrounding area, including Mount Hope, while he remains on bail for earlier domestic‑violence charges.
