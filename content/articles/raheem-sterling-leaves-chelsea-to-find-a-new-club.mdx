Raheem Sterling, 31, and Chelsea ended their contract by mutual consent. The former Manchester City star had been out of favour and was loaned to Arsenal last season. Chelsea’s new policy now favours younger, cheaper players, making Sterling’s high wages hard to justify. He’s looking for a club outside London, with interest from Fulham, Crystal Palace and West Ham. Chelsea thanked him for his contributions.
