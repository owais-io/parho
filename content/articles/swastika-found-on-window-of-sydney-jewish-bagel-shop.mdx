Police found a swastika etched on the window of a Sydney Jewish bagel shop before it opened. The owner, a Holocaust‑survivor’s granddaughter, reported the hate symbol after removing protective paper. The incident comes amid a wave of antisemitic attacks in eastern Sydney following the Gaza war, including arson and vandalism. Other Jewish bakeries have closed, and the community has urged support for local businesses and police action.
