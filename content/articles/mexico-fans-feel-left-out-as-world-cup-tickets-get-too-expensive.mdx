Mexico fans feel left out because World Cup tickets are very expensive and hard to get. The 2026 tournament will have only a few games in Mexico, while most are in the U.S. Many locals try to buy tickets through online lotteries but few succeed. Prices can reach over $1,300 for a regular seat, far above average wages. A small batch of cheap tickets has been offered, but most fans remain disappointed.
