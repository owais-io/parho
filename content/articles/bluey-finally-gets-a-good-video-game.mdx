Bluey finally gets a good video game. Earlier Bluey titles were hit‑and‑miss, with high prices, ads and bugs. Now a new game, Bluey’s Quest for the Gold Pen, was made in Brisbane by Halfbrick and the show’s creator. It’s a 10‑hour adventure, no subscription or ads, and focuses on curiosity and kindness. The move shows Aussie pride and a healthier model for kids.
