Usman Khawaja, 39, has decided to quit international cricket after the final Ashes Test at Sydney’s SCG. He thanked family and friends, praised cricket’s life lessons, and talked about facing racial stereotypes. The former Pakistan-born player, who made a late‑career comeback with two centuries, left the team while still in the squad, the second Aussie to do so in a decade.
