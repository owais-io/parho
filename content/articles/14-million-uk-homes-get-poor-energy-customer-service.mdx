A Citizens Advice survey of 16 energy suppliers shows that about 14 million households in the UK are receiving “below‑average” service. Half of the suppliers score under 3 out of 5 stars, with the average at just 3.09. Many customers—over 9 million—struggle to pay bills, and the report warns that vulnerable people are hit hardest. Top performers include Ecotricity, while TruEnergy ranks last. The study urges suppliers to improve response times, billing accuracy, and support for those in financial hardship, and calls on the government to step in.
