Spring Breakers in the U.S. are warned that their much‑anticipated trips could be marred by three problems: airport security delays, higher gas prices from the Iran‑related oil strikes, and storms that have already knocked out flights. A TSA pay standoff is keeping lines long, while a spike in fuel costs could add $300‑plus to travel bills. Even popular beaches are limiting alcohol to keep crowds in check.
