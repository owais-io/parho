Petrol prices in Australia have jumped, but most people are still driving and bus, train, and ferry use have barely moved. Traffic on major Sydney and Melbourne roads stayed flat, and the number of people tapping into public transport cards didn’t drop. Some lawmakers want free or cheaper rides to cut costs, but the government says it’s already covering most fares.
