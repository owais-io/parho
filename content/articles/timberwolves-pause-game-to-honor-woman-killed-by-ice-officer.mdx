Before a game against the Cavaliers, the Minnesota Timberwolves paused to honor 37‑year‑old Renee Good, who was shot and killed in her car by an ICE officer. The incident sparked protests across Minneapolis. After the moment of silence, Minnesota won 131‑122, with Julius Randle scoring 28 points. The event highlighted the city’s anger over rising ICE activity and called for more transparency.
