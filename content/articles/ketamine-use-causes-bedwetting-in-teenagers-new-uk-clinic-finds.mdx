An NHS clinic in Liverpool opened a new service to treat teens who’re wetting the bed because of ketamine addiction. Doctors say the drug damages the bladder, making kids feel the urge to pee all the time. Many 14‑15‑year‑olds have been using ketamine for a year or two. The clinic combines bladder care with addiction help, hoping to stop the cycle before serious surgery is needed.
