The BBC has been hit by a crisis after its chief and news head quit over a memo from a former adviser who said the broadcaster has a liberal bias. The memo accused the BBC of unfair coverage of the US election, Gaza and transgender issues. A parliamentary committee will hear from the adviser and board members on Monday to decide how much of the memo was ignored and whether the BBC’s leadership is fit to run it.
