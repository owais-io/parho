Osimhen, Nigeria’s top scorer, is a key player for the Super Eagles in Morocco, but his mood swings have made headlines. He quarreled publicly with teammate Ademola Lookman after a win, and he’s had earlier arguments with former players and coaches. Coach Éric Chelle says the problem will be handled inside the team. Fans hope he can focus on the upcoming quarter‑final and keep Nigeria’s AFCON hopes alive.
