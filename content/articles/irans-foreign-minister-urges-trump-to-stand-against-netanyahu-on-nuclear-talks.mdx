Iran’s foreign minister Abbas Araghchi told the Guardian that President Trump should go against Israel’s Prime Minister Netanyahu and support renewed nuclear talks with Iran. He says the region backs a deal and that Americans want peace, not more war. Netanyahu worries about Iran’s missile plans after U.S.–Israeli attacks. Araghchi argues the U.S. can help stop an unnecessary crisis and that Iran is still ready to negotiate if it isn’t forced to give up its rights.
