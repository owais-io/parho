George Mamalis, once a beach‑loving Instagram star, now runs a page full of conservative Christian messages. He’s a top player in Turning Point Australia, a group that grew from a U.S. campaign. The organisation wants to set up chapters in schools and universities and push minor right‑wing parties into power. Mamalis talks with far‑right figures, shares anti‑immigration and anti‑climate‑change content, and claims his tactics help win elections.
