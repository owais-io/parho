His & Hers is a six‑part Netflix thriller based on Alice Feeney’s bestselling novel. Produced by Jessica Chastain, it stars Jon Bernthal as a small‑town detective and Tessa Thompson as a former TV anchor who once ruled her hometown. Their estranged marriage adds tension as they chase a brutal murder of a teenage girl with a dark past. Packed with twists, a star‑filled cast and slick pacing, it’s a bingeable drama perfect for New Year viewing.
