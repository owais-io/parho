The chair of Fulton County’s commission, Robb Pitts, told reporters that he received a call warning him that federal agents might arrest state officials before the FBI raided the county’s election offices on Wednesday. The FBI seized about 700 boxes of 2020 election documents. Pitts is demanding the documents be returned and the sealed warrant be opened, claiming the case threatens the state’s constitution. No federal officials have been named for investigation yet.
