A 29‑year‑old former Uber driver, Jonathan Rinderknecht, is on trial in Los Angeles for starting a small blaze on New Year’s Eve that grew into the biggest wildfire in the city’s history. Prosecutors say he lit a fire that was later blown back into the area by strong winds, causing 12 deaths and 7,000 homes lost. Rinderknecht says he tried to help and blames the fire department. The case will decide if he’s responsible for the huge damage.
