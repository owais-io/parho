A man in Portland was shot by a Border Patrol agent during an immigration stop and has pleaded not guilty to assault and property damage charges. Luis Nino‑Moncada was injured in the arm, indicted, and entered his plea after agents said he drove a truck into a rental car. The incident follows protests over ICE tactics and recent officer shootings. Officials say the shooting followed the truck reversing and colliding with a rented vehicle.
