AI models use a lot of power, and data centres are growing faster than any other sector. In 2025 they could emit up to 80 million tonnes of CO₂ and use up to 760 billion litres of water—like the amount of bottled water people drink worldwide. In Australia this boom could raise electricity bills and hurt local communities. You can limit AI by stopping use or adding “‑AI” to searches, and groups want data centres to use renewables and recycle water.
