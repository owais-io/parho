Alan Milburn has started a big review into why more young people in Britain are not studying or working. He will look at mental health, disability, and poverty that keep 1 million kids stuck out of school, work or training. A panel of health, business and policy experts will give advice. He will publish an interim report in spring and a full report in summer, hoping to spark real change.
