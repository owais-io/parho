Ian Balding was a celebrated racehorse trainer who led the historic Park House Stables. He started training at 26 and guided the champion Mill Reef to win the Derby and the Arc in 1971. He also trained the sprinter Lochsong and many other top horses. Balding died at 87, leaving a legacy that lives on through his family and the racing community.
