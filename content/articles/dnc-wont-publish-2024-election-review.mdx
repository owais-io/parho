The Democratic National Committee has decided not to release its 2024 election review, calling it a distraction. Chair Ken Martin said the committee already learned from the study and is using those lessons to build future wins. The report looked at campaigning, fundraising, and outreach across all states, noting Democrats spent more but still lost. Republicans and some grassroots groups have debated the timing, but the DNC says the focus should be on upcoming elections.
