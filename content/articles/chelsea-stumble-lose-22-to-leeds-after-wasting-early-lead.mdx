Chelsea started strong, taking a 2‑0 lead, but kept making mistakes. They missed a penalty and let Leeds come back. With Okafor’s late goal, the match finished 2‑2. The defeat shows Chelsea’s ongoing problems – dropping points from winning positions and poor defensive play. Leeds’ fighting spirit earned them the draw and keeps them a few points clear of relegation. Fans hope the team corrects these errors soon.
