An international UN panel of 18 experts said that Donald Trump’s racist language and the United States’ hard‑border tactics are serious human‑rights violations. The group warned that Trump’s hate speech can spark discrimination and hate crimes, and it listed problems such as racial profiling by ICE, violent shootings of migrants, and growing numbers of people held in unsafe detention centres. The panel urged the U.S. to honour its anti‑racism treaty, while the White House called the report biased.
