After scoring his best score of the Ashes series, 78 not out in a rain‑shortened first day, England batsman Harry Brook told reporters that the pitch is “good” and that England is in a strong position. He and Joe Root added 154 runs together. Brook talked about how he handled Australia’s short bowling, his learning moments, and the need to be patient and keep the momentum going.
