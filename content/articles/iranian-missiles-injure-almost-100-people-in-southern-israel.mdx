Iranian missiles hit the southern Israeli towns of Arad and Dimona, leaving nearly 100 people hurt, including a 12‑year‑old boy and a 5‑year‑old girl. Air‑defence systems failed to stop the projectiles, and a missile struck close to a nuclear research centre. Israeli forces are investigating the failures, while the UN urges calm to prevent nuclear danger. Prime Minister Netanyahu vowed to keep striking enemies.
