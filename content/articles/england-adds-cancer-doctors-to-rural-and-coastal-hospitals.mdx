The UK government will station more cancer specialists in rural and coastal hospitals to tackle unfair differences in cancer care. The move aims to speed up diagnosis, boost early treatment and improve survival rates, which are lower in poorer regions. The plan also includes new training spots, faster treatment targets, and funding for tech like AI to spot cancer earlier.
