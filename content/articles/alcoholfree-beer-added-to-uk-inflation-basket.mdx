From next month, alcohol‑free beer will be counted in the UK’s inflation basket, along with hummus, pet grooming and other items, says the Office for National Statistics. The change reflects rising sales and shelf space for the product. The updated list will feed into the CPI, RPI and CPIH figures, while the Bank of England is expected to keep rates at 3.75% as inflation stays near its 2% target.
