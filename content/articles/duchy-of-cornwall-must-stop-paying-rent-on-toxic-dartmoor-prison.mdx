The UK government is being criticised for keeping a prison on Dartmoor that releases toxic gas, a decision that costs more than £100m over ten years. The lease sits on land owned by the Duchy of Cornwall, a private estate that pays the Prince of Wales personal income. The public accounts committee says the Duchy should stop collecting rent and let the money fund things like nurses or teachers instead.
