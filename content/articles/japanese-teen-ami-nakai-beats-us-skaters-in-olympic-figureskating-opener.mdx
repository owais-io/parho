Japan’s 17‑year‑old Ami Nakai led the Olympic women’s figure‑skating short program, landing a clean triple axel and scoring 78.71 to stay ahead of rival Kaori Sakamoto and others. Only American Alysa Liu scored a bit lower, 76.59, but still out‑shone most of the field. The rest of the US team fell behind, with injuries and dropped scores. Japan keeps a strong chance for medals on Thursday’s free skate.
