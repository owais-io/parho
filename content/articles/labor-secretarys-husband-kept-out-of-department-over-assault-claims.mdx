Shawn DeRemer, husband of Labor Secretary Lori Chavez‑DeRemer, was barred from the Labor Department after two women said he touched them without permission. Security footage showed him in the building during work hours, and police received a complaint. DeRemer denies the accusations. The incident sparked a criminal investigation, and staff at the department’s main building have been instructed to deny him entry. Meanwhile, Chavez‑DeRemer faces her own controversies, including alleged drinking and misuse of funds.
