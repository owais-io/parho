Manchester City have hit a low point during the winter, with key players out and a string of bad results. 29‑year‑old Rodri is still shaking off a long knee injury and hasn't been his usual self. Striker Erling Haaland has scored only one goal in eight games, and midfield talent Phil Foden looks weaker than before. Coach Pep Guardiola admits injuries and lack of form are hurting the team, and the club has signed a new defender to help.
