Ursula von der Leyen told EU ambassadors that the Commission can no longer act like a guardian of the old world. She said the EU must adopt a foreign policy based on realistic, interest‑driven priorities while still defending a rules‑based system. She criticised the current post‑war framework for not keeping pace with rapid change and warned that the EU’s credibility depends on adapting to new global realities. Her remarks came amid criticism over the Iran war.
