A 67‑year‑old Caltech scientist who studied planets was shot and died at his home in rural Los Angeles. Police arrested a 29‑year‑old man, Freddy Snyder, who was also charged with carjacking and burglary. The victim had spent over four decades researching the universe and was known for his work with NASA. The incident came a few months after another famous physicist was killed in a similar way.
