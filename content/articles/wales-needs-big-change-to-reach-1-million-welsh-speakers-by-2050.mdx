The report says the Welsh language is stable but not growing fast enough to hit the 1 million‑speakers target by 2050. It calls for major changes in schools, community support, workplace use, and better teacher recruitment. Data gaps between surveys and the census are noted. The new Welsh Language and Education Act aims to boost teacher numbers, but upcoming elections could shape the future of these plans.
