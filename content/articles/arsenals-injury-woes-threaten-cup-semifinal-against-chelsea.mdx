Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta says the team’s many injuries, especially to defender William Saliba, could hurt their chance to win the Carabao Cup semi‑final against Chelsea. Several defenders are out, so young centre‑back Christian Nørgaard might start. Arteta stresses finding ways to protect players and wants the team to learn from last year’s exit, hoping this season will bring a trophy.
