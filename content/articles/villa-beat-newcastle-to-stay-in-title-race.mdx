Villa beat Newcastle 2‑0, keeping them in third place and still in title race. Goals came from Emiliano Buendía, Ollie Watkins and a clever assist from Morgan Rogers. Newcastle struggled defensively, losing key players like Bruno Guimarães and Joelinton. Unai Emery praised his team's discipline and attacking play. Eddie Howe said his side were disappointed and need to improve. The win was a bright moment for Villa after a long winless run at St James’ Park.
