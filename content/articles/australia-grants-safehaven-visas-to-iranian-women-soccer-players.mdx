Iranian women’s soccer players at the Asian Cup faced threats from their government after refusing to sing the national anthem. Australia offered temporary visas to five players who feared persecution if they returned home. The Australian government protected them from Iranian officials who were watching the team. U.S. President Trump publicly praised Australia’s move, while the team’s future remains uncertain as more players might stay.
