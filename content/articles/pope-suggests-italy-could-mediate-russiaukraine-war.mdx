Pope Leo says Europe should help stop the war between Russia and Ukraine, and thinks Italy could act as a neutral mediator. He told reporters on his flight back from Turkey that the US wants a peace plan, but Europe’s involvement is key. Pope Leo also offered the Vatican to host talks and to encourage Italy to step in. He highlighted the war’s many dangers, including weapons and energy, and urged a just peace.
