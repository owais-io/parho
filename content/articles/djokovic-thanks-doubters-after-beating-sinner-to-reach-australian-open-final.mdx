Novak Djokovic won a five‑set match against Jannik Sinner, earning a spot in the Australian Open final. He said the praise and criticism from others gave him strength to prove them wrong. At 38, Djokovic will be the oldest man to reach the final of the tournament. He plans to face world No. 1 Carlos Alcaraz next week. His performance shows he still competes at the highest level.
