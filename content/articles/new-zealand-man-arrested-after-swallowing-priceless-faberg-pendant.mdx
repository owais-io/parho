A 32‑year‑old man in Auckland was caught inside a jewelry shop after he allegedly picked up and swallowed a Fabergé “Octopussy” egg pendant worth more than $33,000. Police arrested him on the spot and charged him with theft. The pendant, made of gold, diamonds, and sapphires, is still missing. The man has also faced charges for stealing an iPad and other items, and he will appear in court on December 8.
