Several women’s groups filed a complaint against France’s first lady, Brigitte Macron, after a video showed her calling feminist protestors at a Paris theatre “stupid bitches.” The incident happened backstage at the Folies Bergère when activists had disrupted a show by actor Ary Abittan. A group of 343 women, a symbolic number in French feminism, said the remarks were a public insult. The case highlights a clash between Macron’s public support for women’s rights and her private comments.
