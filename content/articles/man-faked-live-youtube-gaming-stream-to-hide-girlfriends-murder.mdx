A 36‑year‑old man from Northern Ireland tried to cover up his murder of his pregnant girlfriend by uploading a pre‑recorded gaming video to YouTube and pretending it was live. The court heard he filmed the video on Dec 14‑15, posted it Dec 18, and then went 17 miles away to kill her on Dec 19. The video was shown to the jury, and a forensic expert proved it was not live.
