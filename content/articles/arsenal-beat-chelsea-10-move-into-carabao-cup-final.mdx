Arsenal beat Chelsea 1‑0 in the second leg of the Carabao Cup semi‑final, thanks to a late strike from Kai Havertz. The win puts them 4‑2 on aggregate and into the final against Liverpool. The match was slow and rain‑slick, with Chelsea disrupting corners and Declan Rice finding space in a deeper role. Arsenal remain league leaders and are still chasing a historic quadruple, but it is a long shot.
