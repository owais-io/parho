Adrian Chiles, a TV presenter, talks about how pigeons and a falcon dropping a dead bird have seemed to bring bad luck to West Bromwich Albion. After a freak incident stopped a game, the team’s form worsened, managers were sacked, and promotion hopes faded. Even a brief win and a hopeful new coach didn’t stop the curse. He’s now worried that a bird of prey eating a pigeon could spell further misfortune for the club.
