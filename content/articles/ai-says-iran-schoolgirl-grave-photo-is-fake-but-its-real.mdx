During the Iran‑Israel war, a photo of a mass grave for schoolgirls in Minab went viral. People used Google’s Gemini and X’s Grok chatbots to ask if it was real, and both answered “no,” giving wrong locations and dates. Experts checked satellite maps and other footage and proved the grave is authentic. The incident shows how generative‑AI tools can spread false “facts” and waste investigators’ time, making real atrocities appear fake.
