Some beaches in New Caledonia are closed, and authorities have started killing sharks after a man was killed by a tiger shark. A court has asked the operation to stop, sparking a fight between safety and protecting animals. The mayor says large sharks near the coast make the cull necessary, while conservation groups say it is too harsh. Numbers so far show 7 tiger sharks and one bull shark caught.
