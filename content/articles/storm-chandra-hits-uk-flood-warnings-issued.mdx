Storm Chandra has brought heavy rain and strong winds to the UK. The Environment Agency and Met Office have issued almost 100 flood warnings in England, 200 flood alerts (meaning flooding could happen), and 24 alerts in Wales. A red flood warning, which signals danger to life, covers a river in south‑west England. Residents are encouraged to share their experiences and photos, but safety comes first.
