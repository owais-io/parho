Philosophy professor Idris Robinson says Texas State University broke his rights by ending his contract after he gave a talk on Palestine in North Carolina. The university was pushed by pro‑Israel social‑media critics who called him a terrorist. Robinson is suing for violations of the First and Fourteenth Amendments and wants his job back. The case shows a growing trend of universities disciplining faculty for speaking about Israel and Palestine.
