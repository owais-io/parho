Volodymyr Zelenskyy is on his way to Davos, Switzerland, after US President Donald Trump asked him to meet at the World Economic Forum. Zelenskyy had said he might not attend because of Russia’s attacks on Ukraine’s energy supply. The meeting, now confirmed for Thursday, will focus on security guarantees, postwar rebuilding, and funding from frozen Russian assets. Meanwhile, Russia claims Ukrainian drones hit a Russian oil terminal, killing three, while NATO urges European countries to supply more air‑defence weapons.
