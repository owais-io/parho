US Border Czar Tom Homan said the Trump‑era surge of agents in Minnesota, which caused the deaths of two citizens and drew protests, will be pulled back. He said about 700 agents were sent home, leaving around 2,000 for a brief period while security teams stay to keep peace. State leaders are skeptical, calling the operation harmful, and a plan for small‑business help is being proposed.
