Arsenal topped the Champions Cup by beating Corinthians 3‑2, earning their first global title. The win highlighted how the tournament’s timing and London location favor big clubs like Arsenal, while smaller teams struggle with travel and facilities. The Guardian also covered recent WSL action: Chelsea’s poor performance, Tottenham’s gritty comeback, and Everton’s long‑awaited Goodison win. All point to a need for a fairer set‑up.
