A British woman living in the Netherlands can’t visit her dying mother in England because new rules say dual citizens must show a UK passport or pay £589 for a certificate. She has a UK passport that just expired, so she’s stuck. MPs demand a grace period while the passport is processed. The Home Office says carriers may accept an expired UK passport, but it’s unclear.
