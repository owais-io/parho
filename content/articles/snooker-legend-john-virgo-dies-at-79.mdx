John Virgo, a famous snooker champion and popular TV commentator, died at 79 in Spain. He won the UK Championship in 1979 and later became known for his witty commentary on BBC shows, including Big Break. Friends and fellow players, like Ronnie O’Sullivan and Mark Selby, paid tribute online, calling him a great mate and a talented pundit. Virgo’s final comment on the BBC was in January 2024, and he is survived by his wife Rosie and children.
