A 62‑year‑old man in Gibraltar had his prostate removed by a robot that the surgeon operated from London. Using a high‑speed link, the doctor guided the machine with only 60 milliseconds of delay, and the surgery finished successfully. The patient recovered quickly and praised the new way of getting advanced care without flying to England. The team plans to show the technique to thousands of surgeons next month.
