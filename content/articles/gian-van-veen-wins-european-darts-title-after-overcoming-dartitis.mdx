Gian van Veen finally won the European darts title after beating world No.1 Luke Humphries 11‑9. He had struggled with dartitis—an anxiety that made his throws fail—since he was a teenager. The win shows he learned to focus on himself, not on pressure or criticism. He studied aviation engineering and now works in logistics. His triumph gives him a chance to join the Premier League and keeps him hopeful about the future.
