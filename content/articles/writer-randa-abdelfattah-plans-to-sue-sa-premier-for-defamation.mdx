Randa Abdel‑Fattah, a Palestinian writer, has sent a legal notice to South Australian Premier Peter Malinauskas claiming he defamed her by linking her to extremist terrorism. Malinauskas defended his support for her removal from the Adelaide Writers’ Week with a far‑right analogy. The festival saw most guests pull out, the board resign, and its director quit. Abdel‑Fattah has hired a lawyer and threatens to sue if he keeps making those statements.
