On Yom Kippur, a man named Jihad al‑Shamie attacked Manchester’s Heaton Park synagogue. He stabbed and tried to blow up the building, killing a 53‑year‑old worshipper and a 66‑year‑old man, and leaving the 39‑year‑old survivor, Yoni Finlay, with a chest wound. The incident sparked anger over rising antisemitism linked to the Gaza war, criticism of the UK government, and calls from Finlay for compassion and unity rather than more security walls.
