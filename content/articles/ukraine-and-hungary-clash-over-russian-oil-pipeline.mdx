Ukraine and Hungary are in a spat over the Druzhba pipeline that brings Russian oil to the two landlocked countries. Hungary sent a delegation to discuss reopening the line, but Kyiv said the group was unofficial. Ukraine says the pipeline was damaged by Russian strikes, while Hungary and Slovakia want it back online. Meanwhile, Ukraine is offering drone‑fighting expertise to Gulf states hit by Iranian drones, and G7 nations keep sanctions on Russian oil to protect global markets.
