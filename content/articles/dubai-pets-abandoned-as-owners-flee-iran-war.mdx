Many pets are being left behind in Dubai because their owners are fleeing the Middle East amid the Iran war. Animal charities say shelters are full and dogs and cats are in danger. The UK and UAE have an agreement that lets pets travel home without quarantine. Dubai is setting up AI feeding stations, but the crisis still overwhelms shelters and puts animals at risk.
