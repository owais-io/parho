The Gorton and Denton byelection in Manchester has opened, turning the seat into a three‑way showdown. The Green Party, led by Zack Polanski, says it is neck‑and‑neck with Reform UK, while Labour, who hold the seat with a 13,000‑vote margin, warn that a split left vote could hand the win to Reform. Candidates include Green Hannah Spencer, Reform’s Matt Goodwin, and Labour’s Angeliki Stogia. The result will decide if Labour keeps the seat or if the left splits let Reform win.
