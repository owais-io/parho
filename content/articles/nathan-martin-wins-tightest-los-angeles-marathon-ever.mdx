Nathan Martin, a 36‑year‑old high‑school coach, edged out Kenyan runner Michael Kamau by a blink to win the Los Angeles Marathon in just over 2 hours and 11 minutes. It was the race’s closest finish ever and made Martin the first Black American to win. Kamau collapsed after the finish, and the race was briefly hampered by traffic and a spectator stepping on the course. Organisers said the result stands.
