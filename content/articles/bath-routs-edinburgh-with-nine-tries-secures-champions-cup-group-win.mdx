Bath beat Edinburgh 41‑14 in a Champions Cup pool game, scoring nine tries. The win guarantees Bath tops the group and a home spot in the last 16. They dominated early with powerful forwards, especially No8 Barbeary, and the backs, Max Ojomoh and Joe Cokanasiga, finished strong. Edinburgh struggled after losing hooker Ewan Ashman. Bath will face tougher opponents next.
