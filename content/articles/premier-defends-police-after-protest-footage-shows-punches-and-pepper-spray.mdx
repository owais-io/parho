Sydney police clashed with protestors over Israeli president Isaac Herzog’s visit. Video shows officers pepper‑spraying and punching demonstrators, leading to 27 arrests. Premier Chris Minns says the police were put in an “impossible situation” by anti‑protest rules and defends their actions. Greens and protest leaders accuse the police of violence and unfair restrictions. The incident follows a 2024 Bondi terror attack that tightened Sydney’s protest limits.
