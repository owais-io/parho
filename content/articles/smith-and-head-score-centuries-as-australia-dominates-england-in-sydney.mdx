Australia’s top batsmen, Steve Smith and Travis Head, each scored centuries to help the hosts outscore England’s bowlers. The Australians posted a huge 518 for 7 in their first innings, taking a 134‑run lead. England’s bowlers struggled, and the crowd watched a dominant performance. With the support of a spirited crowd and a charitable event, Australia comfortably won the match.
