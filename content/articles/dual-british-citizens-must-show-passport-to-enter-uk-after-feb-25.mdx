From Feb. 25, the UK will require all dual British citizens to prove their identity with a British passport or a special certificate. If they only hold a second passport, they can pay £589 for a certificate or risk being denied entry. The rule follows new border checks that apply to everyone travelling to the UK. Many dual nationals are confused and worried about travel plans and costs.
