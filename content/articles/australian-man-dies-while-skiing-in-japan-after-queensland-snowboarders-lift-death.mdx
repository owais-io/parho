A 27‑year‑old Melbourne man collapsed while skiing in Niseko, Japan, and later died from a heart attack. He was part of a seven‑person group and received CPR, but local firefighters declared him dead at the hospital. The incident follows the death of Queensland snowboarder Brooke Day, who was killed when her backpack got caught in a lift at a nearby resort. Japanese authorities are investigating both deaths, and Australian officials are offering consular help to the families.
