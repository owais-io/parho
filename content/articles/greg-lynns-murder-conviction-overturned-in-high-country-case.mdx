Former Jetstar pilot Greg Lynn was found guilty of killing 73‑year‑old camper Carol Clay in 2020. His conviction was overturned by Victoria’s Court of Appeal after they said the prosecutor repeatedly broke court rules, making the trial unfair. The court said a new trial was needed. Lynn was not convicted of killing fellow camper Russell Hill. He remains in custody until the next court hearing.
