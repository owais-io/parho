At the 2025 Game Awards, a group of game‑worker activists from the United Video Game Workers union stood outside the venue in red shirts with signs that called out massive layoffs, the ending of a talent‑development program, and CEOs of big studios. They want better pay, job security, health care, and protection from AI taking jobs. The union says these fights will make games better for players too.
