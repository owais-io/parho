Ohio State University's president, Walter Carter Jr., has resigned after the board discovered he had an inappropriate relationship with a woman seeking university resources to help her personal business. The board said they were shocked, serious about the matter, and welcomed his resignation. Carter had led Ohio State since 2024 after serving as Nebraska's president. The university is also dealing with other controversies, including ties to Les Wexner and abuse lawsuits.
