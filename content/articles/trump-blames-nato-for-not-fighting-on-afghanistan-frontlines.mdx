Former U.S. President Donald Trump has accused NATO allies of not fighting on the front lines in Afghanistan. He said the countries sent troops but kept them back and questioned whether NATO would defend the U.S. if it were attacked. Trump called the alliance overrated and posted on Truth Social. NATO’s secretary‑general defended the alliance, saying member nations did stand with the U.S. in Afghanistan and would again if needed.
