Paul Silva, nephew of 26‑year‑old David Dungay Jr. who died in a NSW jail in 2015, is rallying people to protest the high number of Aboriginal deaths in custody. About 80 people met at Kempsey police station, and a larger Hyde Park rally is planned for January 18. New state laws, passed after a Bondi beach attack, could allow police to block these protests, sparking concern that First‑Nation voices might be silenced.
