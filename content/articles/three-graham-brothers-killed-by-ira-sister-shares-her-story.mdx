Three brothers from the Graham family were killed by the IRA in County Fermanagh between 1981 and 1985. Sister Pam Morrison kept quiet for decades because of fear and grief. Now she shares her memories and hopes for justice, even though no one has been convicted. The killings were targeted attacks on off‑duty Protestant soldiers. She also worked with the local SEFF group, making a memorial tapestry for all victims.
