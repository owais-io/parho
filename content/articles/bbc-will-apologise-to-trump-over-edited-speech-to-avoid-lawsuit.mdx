The BBC is ready to apologise to Donald Trump after a Panorama clip was edited to make it look like he urged the Capitol attack. The edit stitched speech parts from different times, misleading viewers. Trump has threatened a billion‑dollar lawsuit in Florida. BBC leaders say they will apologise and stand by their journalism. Legal experts think Trump’s chances of winning are low due to U.S. libel rules and no Florida audience. The BBC has insurance to cover legal costs.
