UK Energy Secretary Ed Miliband announced new rules to speed up building nuclear power plants, even close to homes and sensitive nature sites. The changes aim to cut costs and boost energy security, but critics say they risk harming the environment and are a dangerous deregulation. The plan follows a review that called for simpler, faster rules and will fund projects like Sizewell C and small modular reactors.
