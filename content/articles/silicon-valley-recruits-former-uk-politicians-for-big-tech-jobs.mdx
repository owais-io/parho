Tech giants like Nvidia and OpenAI are hiring former UK politicians, blurring the line between government and industry. A big Nvidia event in London saw Prime Minister Starmer and cabinet ministers mingle with the CEO. OpenAI hired former Chancellor George Osborne, and ex‑Prime Minister Rishi Sunak joined advisers at Anthropic and Microsoft. The move raises worries that tech firms could sway rules, so a parliamentary committee is watching closely.
