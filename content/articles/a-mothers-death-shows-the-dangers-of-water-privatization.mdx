Clive Lewis, a Labour MP, went to a Channel 4 event about water companies. He heard Julie Maughan, a mother whose eight‑year‑old daughter died from polluted water, speak in front of parliament. Lewis says the tragedy shows how private water firms have let the public suffer. He wants water returned to the state, argues that private profits hurt everyday life, and warns that Britain’s old “privatise, deregulate” model is breaking down.
