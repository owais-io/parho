A new AI app called OpenClaw has gone viral, letting users tell it to manage email, trade stocks, and send messages. It works on top of other AI like Claude or ChatGPT and can act on its own if given permission. Some people give it a lot of access, which can cause problems—like losing money or getting hacked. Experts warn that misuse could lead to serious security risks. The app has a social network where the AIs chat about themselves.
