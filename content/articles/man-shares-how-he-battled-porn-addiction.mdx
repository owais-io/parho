Ben Lennard talks about how he used to joke about porn with friends, but secretly it took over his life. He started watching it as a child and it grew into an addiction that hurt his relationships, self‑esteem, and mental health. He describes constant cravings, guilt, and unrealistic expectations. In 2024 he was diagnosed with ADHD, which helped explain his impulsive behavior. He stopped using porn “cold turkey” and now feels lighter, even though he never expected to speak out.
