The UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) is investigating an error that let customers of Lloyds, Halifax and the Bank of Scotland see other users’ transactions on their mobile apps. People reported that when they logged in they could read names, account numbers, and payment details of dozens of other customers. Lloyds said the problem was fixed quickly, but the incident has raised concerns about online banking security.
