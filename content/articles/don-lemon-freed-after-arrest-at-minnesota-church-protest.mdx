Don Lemon, former CNN anchor, was arrested for civil‑rights charges related to a protest outside a Minnesota church. He was released in Los Angeles the next day and says he will fight the charges. The DOJ says he interfered with religious freedom. Lemon and supporters claim it’s a First Amendment attack aimed at press freedom. Congress, CNN, and civil‑rights groups protested his arrest.
