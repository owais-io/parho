Celtic chairman Peter Lawwell has resigned after he and three colleagues were abused following the club’s League Cup final defeat. His exit comes as new coach Wilfried Nancy has already lost four matches, the worst start for a Celtic manager in years. Fans have been outraged by transfer missteps and the club’s Champions League exit. Despite the turmoil, the board says it still backs Nancy.
