Mike Lindell, the founder of MyPillow and a vocal supporter of Donald Trump, announced he’s running for governor of Minnesota. He’s a controversial figure because he spread false claims about the 2020 election and has faced big lawsuits and money problems. Minnesota voters face a crowded Republican race, while the current Democratic governor, Tim Walz, seeks a third term. The state usually leans Democratic, so the race could be close.
