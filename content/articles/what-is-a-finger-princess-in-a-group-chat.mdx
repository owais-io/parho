In many chat groups, a “finger princess” (or “finger prince”) is someone who keeps asking simple questions that the answer is already in earlier messages or on Google. The nickname began in Korea and has spread online. While some see it as a sign of entitlement, the main issue is laziness—never scrolling or searching before texting. The article suggests we should all take the small effort ourselves and be more mindful.
