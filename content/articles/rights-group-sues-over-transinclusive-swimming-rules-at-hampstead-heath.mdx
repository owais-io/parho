The City of London’s Hampstead Heath swimming ponds have allowed trans people to use the women’s pond since 2019, but rights group Sex Matters says the policy is illegal. They want the court to stop letting trans women and others use the women’s pond. The city says it is revising the rules after a Supreme Court ruling that focuses on biological sex, has gathered over 38,000 public opinions, and will publish a new policy in March.
