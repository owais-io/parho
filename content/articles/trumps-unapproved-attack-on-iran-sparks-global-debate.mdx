The letters in this Guardian piece argue that Donald Trump has started an illegal war against Iran without U.S. Congress approval and is urging Iranians to revolt. They point out the tragic death toll, including children, in a Tehran school. Critics worry that other countries might stay silent for fear of retaliation, and they question whether the U.S. itself should be pushing for regime change in Iran. One writer even notes Iran’s nuclear weapons.
