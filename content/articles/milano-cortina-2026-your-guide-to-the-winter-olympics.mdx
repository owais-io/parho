The 2026 Winter Olympics will be held in northern Italy from Feb 6‑22, spread between Milan and the Dolomites. The opening ceremony is at San Siro stadium, and events start early, covering ice sports, alpine skiing, snowboarding, and a new sport, ski mountaineering. Viewers can watch on NBC, Peacock, and local networks. The U.S. team has 232 athletes, featuring stars like Lindsey Vonn and Ilia Malinin. Italy uses existing venues and a six‑village athlete housing plan.
