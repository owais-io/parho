Researchers examined 50,000 health questions answered by Google’s AI “Overviews” and found the biggest source of information was YouTube, not reliable medical websites. About 4% of all citations came from videos. Only a small fraction of those links were from real doctors, raising concerns that people might get unsafe health advice from popular videos instead of trusted medical authority.
