Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth said the U.S. and Israel have destroyed Iran’s air force, navy and missile defense in just 13 days and hit more than 15,000 targets. He slammed the media for not praising the campaign and suggested TV headlines like “Iran increasingly desperate.” Hegseth also said the U.S. is investigating a strike on a girls’ school that killed 175 people. He criticised CNN and its potential new owner, David Ellison, and ended with a prayer for troops.
