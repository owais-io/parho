The UK media regulator, Ofcom, will now put Netflix, Disney+ and Prime Video under stricter rules. The platforms will be treated like traditional TV channels, with guidelines on accurate news and protection against harmful content. Ofcom can investigate complaints and fine companies up to £250,000 or 5% of UK revenue for breaches. The change, part of the Media Act 2024, applies to services with over 500,000 UK viewers, aiming to safeguard viewers and create a level playing field.
