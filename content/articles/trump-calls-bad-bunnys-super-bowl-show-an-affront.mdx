Former President Donald Trump blasted Bad Bunny’s Super Bowl halftime show, calling it an “affront” to American greatness. The Puerto‑Rican singer performed mostly in Spanish and celebrated Latino culture, earning praise from fans and other stars. Trump’s harsh remarks sparked online debate and highlighted the wider controversy over ICE’s immigration enforcement, which many say has targeted people unfairly. The story shows how a single entertainment event can ignite political backlash.
