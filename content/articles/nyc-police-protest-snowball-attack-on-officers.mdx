During a recent blizzard, a video went viral showing two New York City police officers in Washington Square Park being hit with snowballs while trying to control a large snowball fight. The officers were hurt and pushed people down. Mayor Zohran Mamdani called for respect, while Police Commissioner Jessica Tisch said the act was criminal and urged arrests. The police union also demanded charges, and detectives are investigating.
