The first day of the Australian Open drew a record 73,000 fans, far more than last year’s peak, and many had to stand in long lines at gates and courts. Organisers added extra security staff, urged people to book tickets in advance and download them before arrival, and highlighted new bar and shade areas. They also plan future upgrades to reduce crowding.
