The Supreme Court ruled that President Trump’s tariffs imposed under the International Emergency Economic Powers Act are unlawful. The decision does not stop Trump from using other laws to keep tariffs in place. Trade leaders in the UK, Canada, Germany, India and elsewhere say the ruling leaves partners unsure about future costs and refunds. The White House will seek new ways to maintain tariffs, so companies must stay alert.
