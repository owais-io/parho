Iran has cut off the internet, phones and landlines during protests. Two famous Iranian filmmakers, Jafar Panahi and Mohammad Rasoulof, say the shutdown is a tool to hide violence. They urge the world to help Iranians keep talking and to report what’s happening. Other artists and actors abroad are joining in, using hashtags like #IranRevolution2026 to support the protests. The regime’s move leaves people defenseless and hides the brutal crackdown.
