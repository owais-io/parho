Vincent Karremans, the Dutch economy minister, said he would do the same thing again after the six‑week fight over the Chinese‑owned chipmaker Nexperia, which almost shut down car production worldwide. He says the dispute is a wake‑up call about Europe’s dependence on China for vital technology. Karremans worked with the EU, US, China and German car makers, and he had no regrets about the steps he took.
