During the Senate hearing, Trump’s pick for DHS secretary, Senator Markwayne Mullin, said he would reorganise but not shut down FEMA. Officials worry he may continue cutting staff and funding, especially after his past climate scepticism and votes against disaster relief bills. He promised to find a FEMA head if confirmed, but critics say he lacks experience in homeland‑security committees and may keep the agency under‑staffed.
