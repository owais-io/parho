He was a devoted, modest Labour MP from Lancashire West who served from 1992 to 2005. A former schoolteacher, he stood against student fee hikes, opposed the Iraq war, and campaigned for animal rights and the banning of hare coursing. Known for his integrity, he was a trusted aide to ministers and avoided personal ambition. He died at 81 on 27 Dec 2025.
