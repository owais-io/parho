On January 15, a UPS driver named Willy Esquivel rushed into a burning condo in Santa Ana and carried 101‑year‑old Ann Edwards out of the house. Neighbors had tried to fight the fire and asked for help. Esquivel lifted Edwards to a nearby home, kept her calm while firefighters came, and then she was taken to the hospital. The rescue was praised as a quick, community‑spirit moment.
