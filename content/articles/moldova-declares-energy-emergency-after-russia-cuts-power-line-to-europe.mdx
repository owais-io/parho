After Russian strikes in Ukraine cut a key power line that brings electricity from Romania into Moldova, the country has declared a 60‑day energy emergency. Moldovan leaders are asking citizens to cut back on power use, especially during peak hours, while specialists work to clear crashed drones and repair the line, which could take up to a week. The move follows growing attacks by Russia on Ukraine’s energy infrastructure.
