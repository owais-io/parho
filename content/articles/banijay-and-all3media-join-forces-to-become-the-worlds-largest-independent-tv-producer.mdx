Banijay Group, a big French TV studio, and UK company All3Media are joining forces to create the world’s biggest independent TV production company. The new group, still called Banijay, will own popular shows like MasterChef, Survivor, The Traitors, Midsomer Murders, and Fleabag. The merger will bring about €4.4 bn of revenue and €690 m in profit for 2024, and is expected to save €50 m in costs. Banijay’s chief executive will run the new company.
