Venezuela says it has released 99 people who were detained for taking part in protests after its 2024 election, but human‑rights groups say this is far too few – about 900 people are still in prison. The government denies that it holds political prisoners and claims the release shows respect for human rights. Meanwhile, the U.S. has tightened a military blockade and airstrikes, and the opposition remains in exile.
