Anderson Cooper, who has worked on 60 Minutes for almost 20 years, said he'll step away from the CBS show to focus on his family. The move comes as CBS News undergoes a shake‑up under new editor Bari Weiss, who has stirred controversy with editorial changes. Cooper’s decision reflects a desire to spend more time with his kids as they grow up. His final episode is set for next month.
