Muslim artists, writers and chefs are gathering at Iftar dinners and festivals, sharing food and ideas that are putting a fresh voice on New York’s streets. Their meetings help them build influence, which now shows up in new restaurants, music clubs, art shows and even city politics. With leaders like Zohran Mamdani moving into office, Muslims are reshaping the city’s culture and fighting growing Islamophobia.
