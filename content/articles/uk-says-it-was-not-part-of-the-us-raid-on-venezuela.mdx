UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Britain had no role in the U.S. operation that captured Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and his wife and hit Caracas. He added the UK is helping 500 Britons in Venezuela and wants clear facts before judging. Other politicians, including UK opposition leaders, called the attack illegal and warned it could set a bad precedent. Starmer will wait for more details from the U.S. before reacting.
