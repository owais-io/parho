Ken Weetch, who served as Labour MP for Ipswich and taught history at Mayfield Boys School, left a lasting impact on his students and community. He was known for his passion for economic and social history, especially agricultural developments. His career bridged education and politics, and he was remembered as a dedicated public servant and mentor who inspired lifelong interest in history among his pupils.
