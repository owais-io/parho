Riverford, the vegetable‑box company, saw its sales climb 6 % to £117 million in the year to May 2025, matching the fastest growth in the UK organic food market in two decades. The boom comes as shoppers look for trusted, healthy food, pushing sales of organic meat and other products up. Despite higher wages and energy costs that cut operating profit, Riverford still gave staff a £1.1 million bonus and plans to keep growing as the market expands.
