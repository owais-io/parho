Two oil tankers that the U.S. has sanctioned, called Aria and Tia, are traveling east across the English Channel toward Russian ports. After the U.S. seized the tanker Marinera, the UK defence minister said the government will target Russia’s “shadow fleet.” The Ministry of Defence denied any plans to seize the vessels. The move shows the U.K. and U.S. are tightening pressure on Russia’s oil trade.
