Beth Gardiner, a British journalist, discovered that oil firms have spent over $180 billion on plastic factories since 2010. She explains how the industry keeps promoting recycling myths while actually expanding plastic use, making it harder to recycle and causing more pollution, toxic chemicals, and climate harm. The piece shows oil companies use money and lobbying to block rules and protect profits.
