Sergey Brin, former Alphabet president, has doubled his spend on the fight against California’s proposed billionaire tax, giving a total of $45 million to a Super PAC that opposes a 5% one‑time tax on residents worth over $1 billion. Co‑founder Eric Schmidt added about $3 million. The money goes to “Building a Better California,” which also backs a separate measure that would block retroactive taxes. The tax aims to fund education, food aid and health programs.
