Police found the body of 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian, who was kidnapped in February. The remains were discovered on Pitt Town Bottoms Road after a Toyota was traced back to the scene. The case involves a planned kidnapping that went wrong. Police say the man was taken by mistake, not the intended target. Two suspects have been arrested, and investigators are looking for more evidence.
