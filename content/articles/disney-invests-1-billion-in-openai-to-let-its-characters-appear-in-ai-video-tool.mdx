Disney is putting $1 billion into OpenAI so the AI company’s Sora video maker can use more than 200 Disney, Marvel, Pixar and Star Wars characters. The deal lasts three years and excludes real‑person likenesses. It comes amid protests from writers, actors and VFX artists worried that AI could replace jobs. Disney will also tap OpenAI’s APIs and may stream Sora videos on Disney+. The partnership shows a new, careful way for AI and entertainment to work together.
