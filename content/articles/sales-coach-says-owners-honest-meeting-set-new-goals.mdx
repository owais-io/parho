Sale's coach Alex Sanderson said a frank meeting with owners, players and coaches last week clarified the club’s goals for the upcoming Boxing Day match against Harlequins and the rest of the season. The team is seventh in the Premiership, has only two wins from seven games, and must start winning to reach the playoffs. The owners pledged continued financial support, while Sanderson highlighted returning key players and the need for consistent effort.
