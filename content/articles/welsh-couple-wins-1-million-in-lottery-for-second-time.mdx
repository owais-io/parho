Richard Davies and Faye Stevenson‑Davies from mid‑Wales have become lottery winners again, grabbing £1 million for the second time. Their first win was in 2018 with EuroMillions; this one came in the 26 November Lotto draw after a chain of lucky dip tickets. The odds of the same couple winning twice are more than 24 trillion to one. They use the money to help locals—donating cars, cooking at community kitchens, and volunteering their skills.
