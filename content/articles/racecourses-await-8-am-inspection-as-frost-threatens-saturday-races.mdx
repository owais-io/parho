Cold weather threatens Saturday horse races at Warwick and Kempton. A –2 °C to –3 °C frost is expected overnight, so officials will inspect the tracks at 8 am to ensure safety. Both courses are still “raceable” for now, but conditions could change. Key races include the Lanzarote Handicap Hurdle at Kempton and a novice chase at Warwick, featuring top runners like A Pai De Nom and Moon Rocket.
