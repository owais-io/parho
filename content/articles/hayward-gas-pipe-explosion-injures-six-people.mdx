In Hayward, California, a construction crew tore a gas pipe at about 7:30 am. When the gas leaked, it blew up at 9:30 am, sending a huge fire and debris into several nearby buildings. Six people were hurt and taken to hospital. Firefighters rushed the scene, putting out the blaze and protecting three damaged homes. PG&E said they cut the line before the blast, but the explosion still happened.
