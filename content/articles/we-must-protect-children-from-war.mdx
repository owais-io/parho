The letter urges the world to stop killing children in wars and to give them real help. It says that kids in places like Ukraine, Gaza, and other fighting zones suffer huge trauma – losing homes, schools, and friends, and hearing loud blasts. Doctors have found that therapy can help most of them recover, so the writer calls on governments to make stronger laws for kids and provide more treatment.
