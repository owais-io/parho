The rail regulator has brought back the 7 am Manchester‑to‑London train after pressure from passengers, sparking a mixed reaction. Some people celebrate the improved timetable, while others, in a series of letters, voice frustration over everyday mishaps—like dog injuries, tea delaying anesthesia, protesters targeting the crown jewels with apple crumble, and odd sports and building naming choices. The letters highlight how ordinary concerns can dominate public debate.
