Elena Rybakina won the Australian Open, beating world No. 1 Aryna Sabalenka 6‑4, 4‑6, 6‑4 in a dramatic final. After falling behind 3‑0 in the last set, Rybakina fought back and claimed her first Grand Slam title. The win lifts her to world No. 3 and confirms she is one of the best players on the tour today. Sabalenka, who has reached four major finals, was left disappointed.
