The Serious Fraud Office arrested six people and raided seven sites, including homes in the UK and one in Italy, after finding evidence of a £300m fraud at Home Reit, a former London-listed social‑housing company. Home Reit promised to give homeless people, veterans and others decent homes, but investors said the properties were often mouldy or broken, and charities stopped paying rent. The firm had aimed to grow into a £1bn business but collapsed after a series of scandals.
