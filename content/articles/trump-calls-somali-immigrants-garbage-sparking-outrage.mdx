Last week Trump made very racist comments about Somali immigrants in Minnesota during a cabinet meeting. He said they were ungrateful, a “garbage” and not fit to stay in the U.S., and also attacked Rep. Ilhan Omar. The remarks come as ICE agents raided the Twin Cities, hurting the Somali community. Trump has a history of racist statements and is using bigotry to rally his base as his popularity falls.
