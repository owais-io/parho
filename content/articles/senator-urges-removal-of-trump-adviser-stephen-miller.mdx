North Carolina Senator Thom Tillis says Stephen Miller should leave the White House, calling him a major problem in the Trump administration. He argues Miller’s focus on form over substance and his outsized influence over cabinet staff keeps officials from doing their jobs. Tillis wants Noem replaced with Markwayne Mullin, hoping Mullin will be free from Miller’s sway. Democrats, including House Minority Leader Hakeem Jeffries, say staff changes alone won’t fix the immigration agency’s violent behavior.
