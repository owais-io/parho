Chancellor Rachel Reeves said the government will not give a blanket energy‑bill subsidy. Instead, aid will be aimed at the poorest households, and she criticised the previous government's cheap, broad‑based support that mainly helped wealthier families. Reeves will review the planned fuel‑duty rise in September but has not pledged to delay it. She also warned companies against profiteering and said any help must stay within strict fiscal limits.
