The US military struck a drug‑trafficking boat in the Pacific, killing four people and raising the campaign’s death toll to 99. President Trump then announced a blockade of Venezuelan oil tankers, claiming the country uses oil to fund crime and that the US wants its oil back. Venezuela says it keeps shipping oil normally, while China and the UN urge calm. Congress rejected rules that would have required the president to seek approval.
