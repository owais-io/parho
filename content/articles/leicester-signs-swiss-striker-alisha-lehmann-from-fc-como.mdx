Leicester Women have added Swiss striker Alisha Lehmann to their squad after she left FC Como. Lehmann, 27, has played for West Ham, Everton and Aston Villa and has 64 international caps for Switzerland. She signed a deal that runs until 2028. Leicester’s January moves aim to bring experience to a team that’s been young so far. Lehmann’s arrival joins four other January signings, and she’s also a popular social‑media figure.
