After President Trump’s angry Truth Social posts—mocking Greenland, attacking the UK, and insulting a U.N. peace prize—Britain remained tense. In the same week, U.S. Speaker Mike Johnson visited London to deliver a brief, satirical speech. He admitted being a “useful idiot,” praised UK leadership for calm diplomacy, and hinted at a need to calm tensions. His remarks were seen as weak and self‑aware, underscoring growing rifts between the U.S. and its European allies.
