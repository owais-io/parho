In mid‑January, 12‑year‑old Nico Antic was bitten on both legs by a bull shark while swimming near Shark Beach in Vaucluse, Sydney. He was rushed to Randwick Children's Hospital and remains in critical condition. The family has set up a GoFundMe that has raised over $100,000 to cover medical and other costs. Several other shark attacks that week shut dozens of Sydney beaches and left many swimmers scared.
