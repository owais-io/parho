Sainsbury’s will eliminate 300 head‑office roles, mostly in technology and data, as it splits its tech teams for the supermarket and Argos. The company is also reshaping Argos delivery hubs so teams work regular hours instead of overtime, and is adding new local convenience store directors. The move is part of a push to use AI and robotics to cut costs amid fierce price competition from Aldi, Lidl, and a struggling Argos.
