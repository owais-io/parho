Marvin Bracy‑Williams, a top U.S. sprinter, tested positive for testosterone after a joke with coach‑owner Rae Edwards. An anti‑doping whistleblower tipped off authorities, leading to a targeted test and a 45‑month ban. Bracy‑Williams tried to help the investigation but was later found missing tests and got another sanction. The case also ties in coach Dennis Mitchell and raises doubts about doping in U.S. sprinting ahead of the 2028 Olympics.
