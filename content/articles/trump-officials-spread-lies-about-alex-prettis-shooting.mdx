After federal officers shot ICU nurse Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, Trump administration officials quickly released statements that blamed the victim. They claimed he was armed and tried to kill law‑enforcement, even calling him an “assassin.” Video shows Pretti had no gun and was disarmed before being shot. State officials and evidence say there was no violent attack. The Trump officials’ narrative ignores the facts and spreads false claims.
