A gunman fired from a footbridge at a Hanukkah celebration on Bondi Beach, killing 12 people and wounding 29, including two police officers. The attack, described by the Prime Minister as antisemitic hate‑crime, is the deadliest mass shooting in Australia in over 30 years. It follows earlier incidents such as the 1996 Port Arthur massacre, the 2018 Margaret River killings, and the 2022 Wieambilla ambush, which also left several dead.
