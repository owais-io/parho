Trump urged Iranians to keep protesting and promised “help is on its way,” suggesting a new push for military action against Tehran. He canceled all meetings with Iranian officials and warned U.S. citizens in Iran to leave. The U.S. State Department has urged Americans to plan a safe exit. Trump’s remarks follow a violent crackdown that has killed dozens of demonstrators, raising fears of a larger U.S. intervention.
