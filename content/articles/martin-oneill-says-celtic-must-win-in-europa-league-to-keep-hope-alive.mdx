Martin O’Neill, who has a long history with Celtic, says the club must fight for its spot in the Europa League. He wants a quick win in the next game to lift morale before the next round. The team has been struggling with injuries, new signings and a busy schedule, and O’Neill is careful about buying new players that future managers will accept. He also stresses that the board will support his transfer plans while looking for fresh talent.
