Canada is pushing the G7 and Middle‑East nations to work together to stop the Iran conflict. Foreign Minister Anita Anand says Canada has drafted a set of principles to keep the fighting from spreading, protect civilians, and reduce economic damage. Canada says it didn’t support the US‑Israeli attacks but condemns Iran’s retaliatory strikes. The country is also pushing for stronger trade ties outside the US and a new defence‑bank for European firms.
