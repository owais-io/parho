Liam Rosenior has verbally agreed to become Chelsea’s head coach, replacing Enzo Maresca. He left Strasbourg, where he led the team to a solid season, after talks with Chelsea’s owners, who also own Strasbourg. Rosenior said he has not yet signed a contract but expects it soon. He will bring two assistants with him and is excited to manage a top club, despite leaving a club he loves.
