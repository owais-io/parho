New Orleans is getting ready for a big federal immigration crackdown. Up to 250 border‑patrol agents will come for a new operation that aims to arrest about 5,000 people. The city’s crime rates are falling, but residents fear unfair arrests and racial profiling. Local leaders and immigrant groups are holding “Know Your Rights” workshops, posting ICE sightings, and planning to help those who might be targeted. State officials and the police are preparing to cooperate with the federal effort.
