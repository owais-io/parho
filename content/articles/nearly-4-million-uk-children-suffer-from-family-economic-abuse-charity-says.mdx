Surviving Economic Abuse found that about 4 million children in the UK have been hurt by economic abuse in their families. The study shows that 27 % of mothers with kids under 18 have had their finances controlled or blocked by partners, including cutting off bank access, stopping child benefits and refusing child‑maintenance payments. Many children miss basic items like food and clothes, and some even lose their pocket or birthday money.
