The new season sees clubs tightening squads. Manchester United, after missing out on a striker, want young Ivorian midfielder Yan Diomande from RB Leipzig for around £87 m. They also aim to bring back Portuguese midfielder Rúben Neves from Al‑Hilal, but many teams are eyeing him. Other transfer chatter includes Fulham’s failed bid for Ricardo Pepi and interest in Oscar Bobb and Federico Chiesa.
