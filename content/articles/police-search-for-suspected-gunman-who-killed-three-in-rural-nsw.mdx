Police in rural New South Wales are searching for 37‑year‑old Julian Ingram, who is accused of shooting his pregnant ex‑partner, her sister and another man in the town of Lake Cargelligo. The killings happened on Thursday, and the suspect was on bail for domestic‑violence charges. Authorities say he may have a gun even though he never had a licence. They are asking residents to stay alert while the hunt continues.
