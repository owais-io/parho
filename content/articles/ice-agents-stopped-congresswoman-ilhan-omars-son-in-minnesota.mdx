Congresswoman Ilhan Omar said her son was stopped by ICE agents in Minnesota after a Trump‑ordered raid on the city’s Somali community. She told a local TV station that after a quick visit to Target, ICE agents pulled his car over, checked his passport, and let him go. Omar warned her son about the risk of racial profiling and has written to officials, calling the operation “racially targeted” and “unnecessary.”
