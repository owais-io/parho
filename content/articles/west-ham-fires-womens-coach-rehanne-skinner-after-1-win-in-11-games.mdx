West Ham decided to fire women’s team manager Rehanne Skinner, who had only won once in her 11 league games. The club said the results were not good enough and a new coach was needed. Skinner, 46, had led West Ham to a cup semi‑final last season and had a long coaching career at Tottenham, Leicester and the FA. A replacement will be hired soon, ahead of the League Cup match with Manchester City.
