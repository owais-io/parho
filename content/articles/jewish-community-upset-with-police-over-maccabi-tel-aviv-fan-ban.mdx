West Midlands police banned Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a November match, sparking anger in the local Jewish community. Leaders say the police failed to explain why, did not consult the community, and may have acted politically. MPs and Jewish groups now demand the chief constable step down. A review of the police evidence is underway while the community remains confused and upset. The ban was justified by safety concerns, but critics say it inflamed tensions and was unproven.
