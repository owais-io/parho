After the pandemic, many bosses use software that marks employees inactive after a few minutes. Some cheat by placing objects on keyboards so the computer thinks they’re typing. Recent cases show police officers and other staff have been caught, with dozens forced to resign. Companies now use more advanced tools that track real activity, and some even forbid working from home. Workers also use AI tools to fake meetings while doing other things.
