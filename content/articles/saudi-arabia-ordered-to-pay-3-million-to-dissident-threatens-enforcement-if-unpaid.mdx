A London judge ruled that Saudi Arabia owes over £3 million in damages to Ghanem al‑Masarir, a Saudi comedian and activist who was attacked outside Harrods and had his phone hacked with Pegasus spyware. The court said Saudi agents acted unlawfully, and the king’s government must pay. Al‑Masarir warns he will chase assets abroad if the money isn’t paid. He still suffers depression and can’t work.
