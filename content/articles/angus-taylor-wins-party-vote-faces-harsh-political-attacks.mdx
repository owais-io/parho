Angus Taylor was recently chosen as the leader of the opposition and celebrated by his party. However, he faced a barrage of sharp insults from political rivals. The treasurer, Jim Chalmers, called him a “silver‑footed” figure, while former prime minister Malcolm Turnbull labeled him a “qualified idiot.” The article also lists other famous Australian political insults over the years, from “desiccated coconut” to “sook.” These jabs show how heated Australian politics can get.
