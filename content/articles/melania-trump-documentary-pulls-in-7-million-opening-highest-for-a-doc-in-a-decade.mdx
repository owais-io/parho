Amazon released a documentary about Melania Trump that made $7 million in its first weekend, the biggest opening for a documentary in over a decade. The film cost $40 million to make and $35 million to advertise, and Amazon says it will keep drawing viewers on its Prime Video service. Critics say the movie is more propaganda than truth, and many fans think it’s a political stunt.
