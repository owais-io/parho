Donald Trump’s White House press secretary, Karoline Leavitt, warned CBS that it would sue the network if it cut his interview. She demanded the 13‑minute clip be shown in full. CBS had recently settled a $16 million lawsuit over earlier edits. Despite the threat, the network chose to air the unedited interview. Trump also talked about Iran and the Federal Reserve, and the story highlights tension over CBS’s editorial independence under Bari Weiss.
