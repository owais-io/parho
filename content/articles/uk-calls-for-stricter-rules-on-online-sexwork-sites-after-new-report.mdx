A new UK anti‑slavery report says online sex‑work sites are a growing risk for trafficking. Researchers examined 12 sites that listed 63,000 ads and got 41.7 million visits, finding 92 % of listings show warning signs such as repeated phone numbers or “new to area” phrases. The report urges tighter rules, better victim support, age checks, and public debate, while noting the government is cutting funding for modern‑slavery policing.
