Michael van Gerwen, former world number‑one, says the past year has been a roller‑coaster. He’s gone through a divorce, watched his father battle cancer, and had to juggle being a dad to eight‑year‑old Zoe and five‑year‑old Mike. He still loves darts, but family comes first. Though his game has dipped, he’s optimistic about the World Championship and wants to win again.
