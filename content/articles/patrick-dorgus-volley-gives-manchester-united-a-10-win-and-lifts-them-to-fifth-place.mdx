Manchester United beat Newcastle 1‑0 on Boxing Day, thanks to a clean volley from Patrick Dorgu. The win pushes United up to fifth in the Premier League. With several key players out, the team relied on young squad members who performed well. Newcastle had many chances but failed to score. Manager Ruben Amorim praised the team’s spirit and the young defenders’ effort.
