A raccoon that smashed into a Virginia liquor store and got drunk on Black Friday became an internet star. A local animal shelter teamed up with a custom‑apparel company to sell shirts, mugs and stickers that show the “trashed” raccoon. All the money went to the shelter. By Tuesday the campaign had raised more than $254,000 and was close to selling 19,000 items. The money helps the shelter care for animals and keep the community safe.
