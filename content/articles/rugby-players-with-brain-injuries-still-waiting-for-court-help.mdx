Three big court cases about brain damage in football and rugby are still stuck in the early stages. Over a thousand former players, from amateurs to internationals, have joined the suit, hoping to get money for future care. Lawyers say the cases are tangled and the courts are slow, with delays blamed on the lawyers and the sports bodies. While rugby is adding safer gear and rules, the players keep waiting for a trial that may not happen until 2027.
