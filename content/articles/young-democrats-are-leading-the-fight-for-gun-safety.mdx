Young Democrats are using their own stories of gun loss to push for stricter gun laws. After school shootings and family deaths, voters want change. The movement has turned gun safety from a taboo to a top campaign issue. Candidates are losing NRA “A” ratings and instead take pride in fighting for limits on guns and better mental‑health support. Voters now back leaders who speak honestly about the problem.
