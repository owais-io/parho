Almost 400 millionaires and billionaires from 24 countries sent an open letter to leaders at the World Economic Forum in Davos. They want governments to raise taxes on the super‑rich to close the gap between the richest and everyone else. Signatories include Mark Ruffalo, Brian Eno and Abigail Disney. The letter says the few richest are buying influence, harming democracy and the planet.
