Denmark’s prime minister says any talks about Greenland must respect Danish sovereignty. Trump once threatened to use tariffs to force the U.S. to take control of the island, but he has since backtracked. European leaders are cautious, meeting to discuss the situation and how to protect Arctic security. The U.S. says it needs Greenland for national security, while the EU worries about a trade war and weakening NATO ties.
