Keir Starmer is being asked by both Conservatives and Labour MPs to launch a probe into a 2023 report he commissioned that said journalists covering the Labour Together think‑tank were being targeted. The report, written by PR firm Apco Worldwide, claimed the journalists’ emails came from a suspected Kremlin hack and even mentioned the writer’s Jewish background. Labour Together, linked to former chief‑of‑staff Morgan McSweeney, has now faced calls for an inquiry into its actions and the minister’s role.
