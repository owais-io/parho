Syrian army arrested 300 Kurdish fighters and moved more than 400 others out of Aleppo after fierce fighting. The Kurdish group, the SDF, agreed to leave under a ceasefire. Meanwhile, the US and allies bombed Islamic State targets in Syria as a reply to a recent attack that killed three Americans. The clashes killed about 21 civilians and pushed 155,000 people from their homes. The US is calling for calm and talks.
