Mark Bellchambers from West Sussex tried the Guardian’s online budget game and found that a simple plan could give the UK a £26 billion surplus, cut the basic income‑tax rate to 19 % and remove the two‑child benefit cap. The plan works by limiting pension relief, fixing capital‑gain loopholes, raising gambling tax, reforming council tax and levying banks – all fair moves for ordinary people. The article also includes a few short letters from readers.
