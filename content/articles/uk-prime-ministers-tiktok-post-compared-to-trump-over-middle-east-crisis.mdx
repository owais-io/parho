Keir Starmer posted a TikTok video about the Middle East conflict that many people say looks like Donald Trump’s style. The clip mixes footage of UK aircraft with a Dire Straits song and Starmer’s voice‑over. Critics say the choice of music and tone feels like war‑promotion. Starmer says he’s focused on defence spending and wants to protect Britain, but the post has sparked debate about how the UK leader uses social media.
