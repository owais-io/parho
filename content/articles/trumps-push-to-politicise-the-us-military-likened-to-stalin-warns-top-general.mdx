Retired Army general Paul Eaton says Trump and Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth are trying to make the US military follow the president’s politics, a move he compares to Stalin’s Soviet purges. He points out the quick firing of top officers, questionable war strikes, and the use of the National Guard in cities as signs that the military’s independence is under threat. Eaton fears long‑term damage to the army’s reputation and effectiveness.
