Green Party leader Zack Polanski said Britain has turned everyday needs—water, energy, housing—into profit for a few. He blamed decades of privatisation and austerity for high costs and called for £8.4 billion to help households cope with rising energy bills, especially if prices jump because of the Iran conflict. Polanski also wants windfall taxes on energy firms, rent controls, and a return of water to public ownership.
