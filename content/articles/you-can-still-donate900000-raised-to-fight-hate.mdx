Guardian’s 2025 charity appeal has already raised £900,000 to support groups that fight hate and bring communities together. The money will go to five partners – Citizens UK, Linking Network, Locality, Hope Unlimited, and Who Is Your Neighbour? – which run projects from school programs to food kitchens and community talks. Donations are still welcome until midnight Wednesday. Even a small contribution can help strengthen empathy and reduce intolerance.
