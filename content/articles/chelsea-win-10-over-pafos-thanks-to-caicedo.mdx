Late in the match, Chelsea beat Cyprus champions Pafos 1‑0 after Moisés Caicedo headed a cross in the 78th minute. The game was slow and Chelsea’s fans stayed quiet because they’re still upset with the club’s owners. Caicedo’s goal gave coach Liam Rosenior a win that keeps Chelsea’s Champions League hopes alive as they face Napoli next week. The result also kept Chelsea from drawing in Europe for the first time in 54 games.
