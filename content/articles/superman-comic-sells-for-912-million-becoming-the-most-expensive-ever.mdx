A rare 1939 Superman comic, found in a California attic, sold for $9.12 million, the highest price ever paid for a comic book. The copy was discovered in 2024 by three brothers sorting their late mother’s belongings, and it was in pristine condition, earning a 9.0 grade. Heritage Auctions handled the sale, breaking the previous $6 million record set by Action Comics No 1. It was the first solo Superman title and one of only seven copies graded 6.0 or higher.
