The Washington Spirit defeated the Portland Thorns 2‑0 to reach the NWSL championship in San Jose. Two Spirit players, Gift Monday and Croix Bethune, scored the goals, while forward Sofia Cantore missed a chance to double the lead. In the 90th minute, star striker Trinity Rodman made a brief return from injury, sparking rumors that she may leave for a higher‑paying European club. The win is Spirit’s fourth NWSL final appearance.
