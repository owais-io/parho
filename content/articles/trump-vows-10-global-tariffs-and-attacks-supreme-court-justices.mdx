Trump said he will raise worldwide tariffs by 10% after the Supreme Court blocked his earlier tariff plans. He blasted the justices, calling them unpatriotic and influenced by foreign and left‑leaning interests, and praised the three dissenters. Trump insists he has the legal power to impose the tariffs without needing congressional approval and will investigate trade abuses.
