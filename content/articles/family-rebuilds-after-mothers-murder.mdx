Stuart Green lost his wife, a Filipino lawyer, to a brutal shooting in the Philippines when his three kids were in the car. The children survived, but the family was shattered. Now living in the UK, Green writes a book, *The Regenerate Leap*, to turn pain into purpose. He shares how he helped his kids grow, including his eldest who now wants to be a lawyer. The book aims to help others turn tragedy into strength.
