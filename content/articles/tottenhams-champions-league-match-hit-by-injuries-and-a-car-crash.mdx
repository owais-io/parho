Tottenham Hotspur are scrambling for their Champions League game against Eintracht Frankfurt because key players are out. Pedro Porro is out for four weeks with a hamstring injury, and centre‑back Micky van de Ven is sidelined by a minor problem. Two forwards, Randal Kolo Muani and Wilson Odobert, were involved in a tyre blow‑out that delayed their flight. Coach Thomas Frank now has just eleven outfield players available and is hoping to finish the group in the top eight.
