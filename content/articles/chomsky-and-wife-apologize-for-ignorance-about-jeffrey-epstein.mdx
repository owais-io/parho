Noam Chomsky, the 97‑year‑old linguist, and his wife Valeria said they made a big mistake by not researching Jeffrey Epstein’s background. They had been friends with Epstein from 2015, attending dinners and talks, but did not know he had been convicted in 2008 or his later crimes. After Epstein was arrested again in 2019, they realized how wrong they were and apologized. The couple also explained that Epstein had tried to use their friendship for money and influence.
