Mexico’s president Claudia Sheinbaum warned that new U.S. tariffs on countries that send oil to Cuba could create a humanitarian crisis. Trump’s order threatens to cut Cuba’s oil supplies, which are already short, causing more blackouts and shortages of fuel, food, and medicine. Mexico, the biggest oil supplier to Cuba, is looking for other ways to help while keeping its own interests safe. The move could worsen Cuba’s already dire situation.
