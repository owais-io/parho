The Northern Territory’s new administrator, David Connolly, has sparked controversy after a 2024 speech that insulted Prime Minister Anthony Albanese and blamed Indigenous people for crime. Earlier deleted social‑media posts made similar offensive remarks. The appointment, chosen by Chief Minister Lia Finocchiaro, is now under scrutiny because the role should be neutral and unifying. Labor leaders and watchdogs say Connolly’s comments raise serious questions about his suitability.
