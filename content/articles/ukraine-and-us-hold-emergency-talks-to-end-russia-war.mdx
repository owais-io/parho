Ukraine and U.S. officials are meeting in Geneva this Sunday for urgent talks about a U.S. draft plan to end the war with Russia. The 28‑point proposal, leaked last week, asks Ukraine to hand over parts of the eastern Donbas, limit its armed forces, and abandon plans to join NATO. Kyiv says the plan is a surrender and wants big changes. Washington says the offer isn’t final and may be revised.
