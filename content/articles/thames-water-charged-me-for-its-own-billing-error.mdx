Thames Water sent me a nearly £2,000 bill backdated to 2020 because it stopped my direct debits by mistake. The company admitted the error and promised to wipe out charges older than 12 months, but it’s still trying to recover almost the full amount. The regulator says firms should waive such mistakes and will investigate if they don’t.
