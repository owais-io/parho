Card Factory, a UK card and gift store chain, said its profits will be lower than expected during the busiest time of the year. The company blamed shoppers spending less because of higher living costs and fewer visits to high‑street stores. As a result, its shares fell about 23% in early trading. The firm said it will keep working on cost‑saving plans and that its online arm, Funky Pigeon, is doing okay.
