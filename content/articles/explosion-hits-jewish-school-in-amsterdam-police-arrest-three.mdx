An explosion damaged a Jewish school in Amsterdam, but no one was hurt. Police say the blast was a deliberate attack and have arrested three teenagers. The incident follows a synagogue arson in Rotterdam and comes amid heightened security for Jewish sites. Officials are investigating the motive and asking witnesses to come forward. The city warns that antisemitism is on the rise.
