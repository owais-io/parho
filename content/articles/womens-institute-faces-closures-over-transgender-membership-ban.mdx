The National Federation of Women’s Institutes (NFWI) has banned transgender women from joining, citing a 2025 court ruling on the definition of a woman. As a result, at least 12 local WI groups are closing or threatening to shut down, citing a toxic, conservative culture and loss of members. Branches plan to start independent community groups, hoping to keep the volunteer spirit alive.
