Reyna, who has struggled for minutes at club level, finally shows his talent and confidence. He teamed up with winger‑right‑back Sergiño Dest, whose creativity and attacking flair add extra threat. With key players out, Reyna and Dest are seen as the American team’s bright spots heading into the World Cup.
