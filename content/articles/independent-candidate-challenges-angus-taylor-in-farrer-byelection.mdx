In the rural NSW seat of Farrer, former Liberal leader Sussan Ley is quitting, forcing a byelection. Independent Michelle Milthorpe, who ran second last year, will face a new Liberal candidate and test former MP Angus Taylor’s leadership. The contest could also bring the Nationals into the race and see Labor step aside. Local concerns like the hospital rebuild, climate, and rural services will drive voters, while the Liberals fight internal disputes.
