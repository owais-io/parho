Rachel Reeves will be at Davos this week, but critics say the UK should treat banks with less favour. She skipped a windfall tax last year and the Bank of England lowered capital rules to help banks lend more. Yet research shows a huge finance sector can hurt growth and raise crisis risk. Experts argue tighter taxes and rules, not more hand‑outs, are needed to protect the economy.
