Leicester Tigers defeated Harlequins 34‑7 at the Stoop, dominating the scrum and piling on penalties to keep the home side at bay. The win gives Leicester a top‑four Champions Cup place and sends Harlequins’ hopes of a strong European run into doubt. Even with a hefty injury list and a star player limping off, Leicester’s forwards outclassed the Quins. The match highlighted Harlequins’ struggles to live up to their high finances and reputation.
