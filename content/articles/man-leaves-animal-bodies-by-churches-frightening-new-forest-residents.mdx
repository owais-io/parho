Benjamin Lewis, a Totton resident, was arrested for dumping the carcasses of sheep, deer and lambs outside churches around the New Forest because he harbored hostility toward Christians. Police increased patrols, and he was charged with religiously aggravated harassment and theft. The incident has left locals uneasy, especially after a rise in animal‑cruelty cases in the area. Animal‑welfare groups warn that social media is helping such abuse spread.
