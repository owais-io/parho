Mary‑Ann Stephenson, the new Equality and Human Rights Commission chair, told critics to judge her work, not her past letters. She pledged to protect everyone’s rights and plans to work closely with LGBTQ+ groups. Her first job is to decide how public bodies should follow the Supreme Court ruling on single‑sex spaces. She says the draft guidance won’t discriminate on appearance and will keep collaborating with the government.
