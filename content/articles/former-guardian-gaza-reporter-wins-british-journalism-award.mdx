Malak A Tantesh, the Guardian’s former Gaza correspondent, won the British Journalism Awards’ New Journalist of the Year and the Marie Colvin Award for courage. She covered the Gaza war from inside, sharing her family’s losses and the aftermath of bombings. Judges praised her bravery, empathy and determination to report when many others couldn’t. The Guardian also earned awards in health, tech and features, while Channel 4 News was named News Provider of the Year.
