French Foreign Minister Jean‑Noël Barrot will ask US ambassador Charles Kushner to appear in Paris after the US embassy posted a comment on the killing of 23‑year‑old far‑right activist Quentin Deranque. Deranque was beaten to death in Lyon during a clash with left‑wing protestors. France says the embassy’s statement that “violent radical leftism is on the rise” is politicised and harmful. The meeting will also cover US sanctions on French officials.
