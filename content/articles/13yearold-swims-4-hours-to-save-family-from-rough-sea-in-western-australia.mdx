A 13‑year‑old swimmer named Austin Appelbee saved his mother and two siblings after a storm pushed their inflatable boats out to sea near Perth. He tried to kayak back, but the boat sank, so he swam about 4 km in strong waves, wearing a life jacket. After four hours, he reached shore, called emergency services, and helped the family get rescued by a helicopter and rescue team. All three survived thanks to their life jackets and his bravery.
