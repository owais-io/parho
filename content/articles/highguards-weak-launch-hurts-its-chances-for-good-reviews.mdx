Highguard, a new online shooter from Wildlight, was launched with a shaky reveal at the Game Awards, hurting its early reputation. The game mixes MOBA and shooter mechanics, with fantasy heroes and fast movement. Though it drew 100,000 players on launch day, players complained about lag, long matchmaking queues, and visual glitches. Negative early reviews and stiff competition from big titles like Fortnite and Battlefield mean the studio must fix bugs and build a lasting community to succeed.
