Brazil’s Corinthians defeated Gotham FC 1‑0 in the first semi‑final of the FIFA Women’s Champions Cup. A late goal from 40‑year‑old captain Gabi Zanotti sealed the win, leaving many expecting a Gotham‑Arsenal final. The match was tight, with both sides in pre‑season shape, but Corinthians’ defence and a late strike gave them the edge. Coaches praised effort, but Gotham said they were ready but unlucky. Corinthians now head to a final against Arsenal or other champions.
