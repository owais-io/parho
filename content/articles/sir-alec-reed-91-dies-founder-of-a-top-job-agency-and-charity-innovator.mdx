Sir Alec Reed, who founded one of the UK’s biggest job agencies, died at 91. He also started the Big Give, a charity website that now raises more money at Christmas than many big fund‑raisers. Reed’s company grew from a small office job to a large firm, with 18% of its shares supporting his charitable foundation. He launched schools, helped former addicts, and donated millions to causes like Ethiopiaid. He left a legacy of business success and generous giving.
