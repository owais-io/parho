The Justice Department says it has found more than a million new documents about Jeffrey Epstein. It will take a few more weeks to read them, redact victim names, and put them online. So far 750,000 have been shared, with 700,000 still pending, though many may be duplicates. Lawmakers are pushing the department to meet a December 19 deadline. The latest releases also mention Trump and other names, but some documents are fake.
