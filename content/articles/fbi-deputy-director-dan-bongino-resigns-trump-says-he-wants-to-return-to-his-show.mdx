Dan Bongino, the FBI’s second‑in‑command, said he will leave his post in January. He thanked President Trump, FBI chief Kash Patel and former attorney general Pam Bondi in a social‑media post. Trump praised him, saying he “did a great job” and that he likely wants to return to his podcast. Bongino had quietly told insiders he’d step down early next year.
