Kimi Antonelli, a young Mercedes F1 driver, received threats after Red Bull team members suggested he let Lando Norris overtake him in Qatar. The claim sparked a wave of abuse online. Mercedes called the comments wrong and reported the harassment. Red Bull later apologized, saying the footage showed a brief loss of control by Antonelli, not a deliberate move. The incident highlights the danger of online hate.
