UK oil and engineering company John Wood Group was fined £12.9m by the Financial Conduct Authority after it repeatedly misreported earnings from Jan 2023 to Nov 2024. The FCA said the company lacked proper controls and that management pressured staff to keep previous numbers. Wood Group is soon to be bought by a Dubai firm for £216m, down from an earlier offer of £1.58bn.
