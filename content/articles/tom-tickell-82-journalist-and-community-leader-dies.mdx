Tom Tickell was an 82‑year‑old journalist who wrote personal‑finance advice for the Guardian, Mail on Sunday and Telegraph. He also worked as a freelance writer and loved speaking at London’s Speakers’ Corner. Outside of work he served as a Mental Health Act Commissioner, prison visitor and monitoring board member. Born in Oxford, his family were writers, and he was known for his jokes, storytelling and love of family. He is survived by his wife, children, grandchildren, nephews and nieces.
