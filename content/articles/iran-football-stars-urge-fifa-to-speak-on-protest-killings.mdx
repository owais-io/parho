Prominent Iranian footballers, including former star Ali Karimi, have sent an open letter to FIFA president Gianni Infantino and national associations. They call on the organisation to condemn the killings and arrests of players amid the country’s protests, which have killed an estimated 18,000 people. The letter names several athletes who have been killed or threatened, and urges FIFA to use its powers to protect Iranian footballers.
