Tulsi Gabbard, the director of national intelligence, is under scrutiny after a whistleblower says she blocked the National Security Agency from sharing a report about a phone call between two foreign intelligence officers discussing someone close to Donald Trump. The report was kept sealed for eight months and was only handed to the White House chief of staff, raising concerns about transparency and congressional oversight.
