Oil has driven wars, coups and climate denial for decades. George Monbiot explains how Western governments pushed the 1953 Iranian coup to protect oil interests, sparking a revolution that toppled a democracy. He argues that the fossil‑fuel industry still funds politicians, media and lobbyists, keeping democracy weak and the planet hot. The article urges an urgent shift away from oil to stop wars, protect people and fight climate change.
