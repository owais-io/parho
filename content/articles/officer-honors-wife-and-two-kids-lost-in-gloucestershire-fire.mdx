Tom Shearman, a Gloucestershire police officer, lost his wife Nu and two kids, Eve and Ohner, in a cottage fire on Boxing Day. He shared his grief and thanked the community, which has raised over £300,000 for him. Shearman praised his wife’s craft work and his children’s passions, promising to honor their memories. He urged everyone to tell loved ones they’re cared for and to embrace family now.
