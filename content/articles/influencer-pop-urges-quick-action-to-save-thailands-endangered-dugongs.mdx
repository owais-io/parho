Pop Saksritawee, a Thai social‑media star, uses his online influence to spotlight the dugong—a sea‑turtle‑like mammal—threatened with extinction in Thailand. He explains how pollution, boat strikes, and habitat loss are pushing these gentle giants toward disappearance. Pop calls on government and locals to act fast, protecting mangrove wetlands and enforcing stricter fishing rules. His videos and posts aim to raise awareness and spark real‑world change before the dugongs vanish forever.
