Marjorie Taylor Greene, a Georgia congresswoman about to quit her seat, says she was “naive” for believing Donald Trump was a people‑oriented leader. She says her break began after the death of activist Charlie Kirk, when Trump’s hateful comments shocked her. Greene has since criticized Trump, called the Gaza war a genocide, and admitted she made mistakes. She’s now seen as a political outcast from both sides.
