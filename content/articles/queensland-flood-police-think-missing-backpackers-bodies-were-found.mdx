Police in Queensland say they have found the bodies of a 26‑year‑old man and a 23‑year‑old woman who disappeared while traveling to a flood‑hit area. The bodies were recovered from a silver car in Kilkivan, part of a search from Kilkivan to Mundubbera. These are the first flood deaths in the state. The region is still battling rising rivers, with warnings in towns like Bundaberg and Chinchilla.
