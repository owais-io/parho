Letterman says CBS News has become a mess under the new leadership after Paramount’s merger with Skydance. He criticized the new editor‑in‑chief, Bari Weiss, and the chaotic changes she made in the newsroom. Letterman also pointed out CBS’s decision to drop a 60 Minutes investigation and the overall decline in journalistic integrity. His comments come as CBS cancels shows and faces criticism from former employees.
