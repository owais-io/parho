A thinktank says Britain’s job market is getting worse, with unemployment rising to about 5.1% next month. The Resolution Foundation says employers are hiring fewer people, pushing many graduates and non‑graduates into joblessness. Young people are hit hardest, with almost a million 16‑to‑24‑year‑olds not in work, study or training. Economists warn unemployment could reach 5.5% next year, and the government must act fast to help the job‑hunters.
