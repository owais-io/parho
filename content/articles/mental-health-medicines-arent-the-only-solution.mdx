While new psychiatric medicines are slow to develop, many people now have effective talking therapies that can help. The UK already offers free treatment for veterans with PTSD, and research shows that science‑based conversations can improve mental health. Still, these methods aren’t widely available. The Human Givens Institute leads in creating proven, solution‑focused therapies, and more money is needed to expand and roll them out.
