Across the US, federal prosecutors are losing a growing number of cases that accuse protestors of attacking or obstructing officers. Many charges collapse after videos show that DHS and ICE officers gave false testimony. Defendants have been jailed or branded as violent criminals, only to see their cases dismissed or verdicts overturned. The trend has hurt courts, wasted resources, and left a lasting stigma on those who were accused.
