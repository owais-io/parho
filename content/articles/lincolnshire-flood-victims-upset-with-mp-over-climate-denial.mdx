In January, heavy rain flooded homes in Boston, Lincolnshire, leaving residents with sewage‑filled water and lost valuables. The area faces high flood risk and insurance problems. Local MP Richard Tice, a climate‑skeptic, denied climate change and has not visited residents after the flood, leaving many angry. He says focus should be on defence, not net‑zero goals, while the council has scrapped its carbon target.
