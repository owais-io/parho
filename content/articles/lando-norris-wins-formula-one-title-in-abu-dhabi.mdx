Lando Norris took his first Formula One title after finishing third in Abu Dhabi, even though Max Verstappen won the race. Norris stayed calm, made smart moves, and McLaren’s team helped bring the championship back to the team after a long dry spell. Piastri also fought hard but slipped behind. Verstappen finished only two points behind Norris. It’s a huge win for British fans and McLaren.
