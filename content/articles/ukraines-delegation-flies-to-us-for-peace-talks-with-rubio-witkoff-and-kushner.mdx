Ukraine’s president Volodymyr Zelenskyy sent a delegation to the United States for peace talks with U.S. officials. The meeting in Florida will include Secretary of State Marco Rubio, envoy Steve Witkoff, and Jared Kushner. The goal is to work on a plan to end Russia’s war against Ukraine. Meanwhile, French President Emmanuel Macron will host Zelenskyy in Paris to discuss sanctions and a ceasefire. Ukraine also attacked Russian oil tankers with drones, and drones struck Moldova and Ukrainian cities.
