Trump’s administration is boosting coal and LNG exports while blocking wind and solar projects. The move has sparked court battles and criticism from scientists, Democrats, and consumer groups. Prices for household electricity are rising faster than inflation, and states are fighting the federal orders that keep old coal plants open. Critics say the policies hurt families and the environment, and that cleaner energy is cheaper.
