A US disaster‑response firm called Gothams LLC sent a draft plan to White House officials that would let it run Gaza’s trucking and logistics for a 300% profit and a seven‑year monopoly. The proposal would charge for every truck entering Gaza and for warehousing services. Trump‑chaired Board of Peace and Kushner are involved, but experts say the terms are outrageous and no formal contract has been signed.
