2026 will bring a mix of races, snow, and crowds to Australia. In March, Australian driver Oscar Piastri will race in the Melbourne F1 Grand Prix, hoping to win at home. The Winter Olympics in Milan‑Cortina give Aussie snow‑sport stars a chance to win medals, while the 48‑team FIFA World Cup tests the Socceroos. The Australian Open in January lights up Melbourne with top tennis stars, and the Matildas will host the Women’s Asian Cup in March.
