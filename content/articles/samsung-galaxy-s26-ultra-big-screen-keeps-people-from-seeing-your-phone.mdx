The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is a top‑end Android phone that costs about £1,300. It has a huge 6.9‑inch screen that can lock out side‑glances, protecting your privacy. The phone packs four cameras, a 200‑MP main lens, and a stylus. It runs the new Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chip, lasts two days on a single charge, and gets software updates until 2033. Its high price and little new features beyond the previous S‑series are the main downsides.
