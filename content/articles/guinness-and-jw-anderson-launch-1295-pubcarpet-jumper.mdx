Guinness has teamed up with fashion designer JW Anderson to launch a high‑price collection that turns pub‑inspired styles into luxury wear. The 17‑piece line includes shorts, a £850 Irish wool jumper and a £1,295 unisex V‑neck that mimics a pub carpet. The collection is aimed at younger drinkers, boosting Guinness’ image as a lifestyle brand and helping it keep its spot as the UK’s top‑selling beer.
