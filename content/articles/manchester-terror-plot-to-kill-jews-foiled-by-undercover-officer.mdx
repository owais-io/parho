Two men, former Islamic State sleeper agents, planned to disguise themselves as Jews and shoot people at an anti‑antisemitism march in Manchester, then attack the wider Jewish community. They also wanted to target Christians. An undercover police officer pretended to help them buy weapons, but police caught them in a hotel car park in Bolton. Both men were arrested and later convicted for preparing a terrorist attack.
