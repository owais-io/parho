UK rural villages are hit by heavy rain and flooding more often, and the damage is piling up. Roads are washed out, power and internet are cut, and farms and small businesses suffer. Scientists say heavy winter rain is coming earlier, and local councils are under money pressure to fix drainage and build flood defences. Residents are being asked to share how the weather is affecting their work, homes and safety.
