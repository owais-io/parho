Authorities found several bomb‑like devices across Fort Washington Park in Maryland over the weekend, so the 341‑acre park was shut down. A Prince George’s County fire‑department bomb squad first cleared five devices, but more were discovered later. The scene is now a joint investigation involving the state fire marshal, ATF, FBI and U.S. Park Police. No danger to visitors, but anyone who sees a device should leave and call 911.
