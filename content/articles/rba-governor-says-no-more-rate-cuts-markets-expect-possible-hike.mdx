The Reserve Bank of Australia's governor said no more rate cuts, which shocked markets. Investors now expect a rate rise by May, after earlier predictions of a cut. The RBA’s statement showed more concern about inflation and private‑sector growth. The central bank focuses on keeping prices stable. New data next week may shape decisions. This shift shows how sensitive markets are to the RBA’s wording.
