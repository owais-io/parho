In 2026, big tech will spread data centers all over the world, especially in India, Southeast Asia, and Brazil, but power shortages and environmental protests are growing concerns. Self‑driving cars will start operating in many cities, with companies like Waymo, Baidu, and Uber rolling out robotaxis worldwide. Billionaires are set to get richer, especially with upcoming IPOs for OpenAI and SpaceX. AI is helping some jobs but hasn’t replaced most workers yet. Finally, phones and other gadgets will get new shapes, with folding screens and AI‑powered devices likely to hit the market.
