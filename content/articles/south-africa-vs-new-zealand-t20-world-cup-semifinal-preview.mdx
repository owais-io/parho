The semi‑final of the T20 World Cup pits South Africa against New Zealand in Ahmedabad. South Africa has won all its matches and has added key players back, while New Zealand has swapped Ish Sodhi for Jimmy Neesham and re‑added Matt Henry after his family break. The toss and a late‑game dew could give South Africa an edge, but both teams have deep experience in big games. The winner will move on to the final.
