Senators Bernie Sanders and Ro Khanna introduced a bill that would tax U.S. billionaires 5% per year. The plan could raise $4.4 trillion in ten years and give $3,000 checks to families earning $150,000 or less. Revenue would fund Medicare, affordable housing and more. The bill is unlikely to pass now, but could influence the 2028 Democratic race. A similar California measure faces opposition from Gov. Newsom.
