In the heated Derby d’Italia, Juventus got a red card after defender Pierre Kalulu was sent off for a tackle that many said was exaggerated. The decision sparked anger from the club’s top bosses, who blamed the referee and called the game unfair. Despite the drama, Inter won 1‑0, keeping their lead in the title race. The match shows how a single controversial call can affect a season.
