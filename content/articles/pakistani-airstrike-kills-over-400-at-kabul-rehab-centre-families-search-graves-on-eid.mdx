A Pakistani airstrike hit a drug‑addiction rehabilitation centre in Kabul, killing more than 400 people and leaving many injured. The UN says about 140 people died, while Afghan officials report over 400 deaths. Families, including the story of Sohrab Faqiri, went to the cemetery on Eid to look for their loved ones but found no marked graves. The strike happened after prayers and raised questions about the target and civilian safety.
