Many people who check and tweak AI text and images, like those on Amazon’s Mechanical Turk, are warning their families and friends not to use tools such as ChatGPT or image generators. They’ve seen the tech give racist or wrong answers, and the companies that hire them often give short deadlines and vague instructions. Because the models learn from this shaky data, the workers say the outputs can be unsafe and unreliable. They are sharing their experience to help people be more careful.
