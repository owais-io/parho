The 68‑year‑old Bishop of Lincoln, Stephen Conway, has been arrested on suspicion of sexually assaulting a man between 2018 and 2025. He was suspended from ministry while the Church of England investigates the claim. Police have released him on conditional bail. The diocese says it is supporting those affected and has put the Bishop of Grantham in charge of the diocese. No further comment has been made.
