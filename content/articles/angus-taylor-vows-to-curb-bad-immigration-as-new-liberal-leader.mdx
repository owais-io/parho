Angus Taylor has become the new leader of Australia’s Liberal Party after a partyroom spill. In his first press conference he said he wants to stop “bad immigration” and keep Australians’ way of life safe. He didn’t give details on how he will limit arrivals, but said he will focus on smaller government, home‑ownership for young people and a stronger economy. Taylor also criticised the party’s past mistakes and said the Liberals will not become a “One Nation lite” party.
