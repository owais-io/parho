Israel carried out airstrikes in southern Lebanon targeting Hezbollah sites as Lebanon faces a year‑end deadline to clear the group. The Lebanese army says it is almost finished with disarming Hezbollah, but Israel claims the group is still rebuilding. A recent drone strike killed a Lebanese soldier whom Israel says was a Hezbollah member. Talks between Israel and Lebanon, and a planned U.S. meeting, continue amid daily attacks to resolve tensions.
