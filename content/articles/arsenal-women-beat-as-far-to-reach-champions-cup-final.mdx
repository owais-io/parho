Arsenal’s women scored six goals in a 6‑0 win over Morocco’s AS FAR, sending them into the inaugural FIFA Women’s Champions Cup final. Four goals came before the break from Stina Blackstenius, Frida Maanum, Mariona Caldentey and Olivia Smith, and Alessia Russo added two more after the break. The win gives Arsenal a shot at a historic title and shows the growing gap between European and African club football.
