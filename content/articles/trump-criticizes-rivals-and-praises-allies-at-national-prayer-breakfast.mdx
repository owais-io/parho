At Washington’s National Prayer Breakfast, former President Donald Trump spoke, mixing faith and politics. He attacked rivals like former President Obama, mocked some lawmakers, and praised allies such as El Salvador’s President Nayib Bukele. Trump also repeated the claim the 2020 election was stolen and joked about his sleeping habits. The event showed how closely the Republican Party is linked with evangelical leaders.
