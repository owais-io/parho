Millions of working‑age adults are leaving the workforce because of long‑term health problems and a lack of help. Nick Pahl, chief executive of the Society of Occupational Medicine, says the problem is as much a public‑health crisis as an economic one. He urges employers, health services and job‑support agencies to work together, offering early care and reasonable workplace adjustments to keep people working or help them return.
