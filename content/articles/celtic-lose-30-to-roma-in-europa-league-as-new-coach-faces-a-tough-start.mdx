Celtic fell to a 3‑0 defeat by Roma in the first leg of their Europa League tie, with Evan Ferguson scoring twice for the Italian side. It was Wilfried Nancy’s first game as Celtic manager and his team struggled, showing softness in the first half and missing chances. Nancy admitted the loss was expected, noting injury issues and a long stretch of games for players. He vows to strengthen the squad and push harder in future matches.
