West Ham has brought in Chelsea centre‑back Axel Disasi on a season‑long loan, paying a £1.7 million fee. The move helps the Hammers defend against a possible relegation battle after a ban for Jean‑Clair Todibo and doubts about other centre‑backs. Disasi has been sidelined since April, but the loan gives him a chance to play and West Ham a fresh defender for the rest of the season.
