Brooklyn Peltz Beckham married Nicola Peltz in 2022 and both took the combined name Brooklyn Peltz Beckham. This move shows a new trend where husbands join their wives in hyphenating their surnames, which has been rare for men. The change has inspired others, such as swimmer Adam Peaty, who also hyphenated his name. The article notes that double‑barrelled names have a long history and could become the new normal.
