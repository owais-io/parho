The U.S. and Israel launched an attack on Iran’s leader, killing over 1,000 civilians, and Iran has started striking U.S. bases and civilian sites, even in Oman. Hezbollah drones hit a U.K. airbase and Azerbaijan reports attacks. The U.S. sank an Iranian warship near Sri Lanka, and energy markets feel the strain. With no clear victory goal, the conflict threatens wider war, higher energy costs, and political fallout in the U.S. and region.
