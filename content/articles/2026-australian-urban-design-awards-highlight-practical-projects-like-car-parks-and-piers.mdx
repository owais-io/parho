The 2026 Australian Urban Design Awards celebrated everyday places that put people first. Winners include a Campbelltown car‑park that doubles as a green, welcoming space and a St Kilda pier that blends protection, recreation and wildlife habitat. The judges praised projects that mix useful engineering with simple, beautiful design, showing that good urban change can be practical, inclusive and environmentally friendly.
