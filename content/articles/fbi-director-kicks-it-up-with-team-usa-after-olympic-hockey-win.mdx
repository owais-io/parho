FBI Director Kash Patel was caught on video drinking, spraying beer, and partying with the U.S. men’s hockey team after they won gold at the Winter Olympics. While the director says he was on an official trip, critics claim he misused his position and the FBI jet. This incident adds to a string of controversies surrounding Patel, who has been scrutinized for mixing work with personal celebrations.
