After months away from the field, Pat Cummins returned to the Adelaide Test and immediately made an impact. He used his mastery of Aussie conditions to bowl fast and precise, taking early wickets that broke England’s hopes of a big score. His return showed how important his skill is, as he turned a tough pitch into a weapon. Cummins’ performance gave Australia a huge boost and put England on the back foot.
