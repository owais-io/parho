The UK will charter a flight from Muscat, Oman, to evacuate vulnerable British citizens stuck in the Gulf after Iran’s attacks closed airspace. The government is working with airlines to increase departures from Oman and prioritise the most at risk. About 130,000 Britons have registered with the Foreign Office. The flight will leave in the coming days, with further details to follow.
