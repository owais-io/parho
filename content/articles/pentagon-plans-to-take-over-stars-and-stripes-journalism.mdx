Stars and Stripes, a daily newspaper for U.S. troops, has always been independent. The Pentagon says it will now take control of its newsroom, cutting out the civilian editor and ombudsman, and only covering war‑related topics. Press‑freedom groups say this is censorship and will turn the paper into a mouthpiece for the military. The move comes as the Trump administration has clamped down on critical media.
