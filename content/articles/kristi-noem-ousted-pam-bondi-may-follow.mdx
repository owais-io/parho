The U.S. secretary of homeland security, Kristi Noem, was fired after spending a huge amount of taxpayer money on a costly ad campaign urging migrants to leave on their own. She was replaced by Senator Markwayne Mullin. Meanwhile, attorney‑general Pam Bondi is facing pressure from Democrats, including a subpoena over her handling of Epstein files, and a possible impeachment. The piece also touches on other Trump‑era controversies, like a disputed Iran strike and broader cabinet instability.
