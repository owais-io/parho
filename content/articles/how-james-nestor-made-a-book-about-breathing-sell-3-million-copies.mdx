James Nestor wrote “Breath”, a book that turned simple breathing exercises into a bestseller. He spent years researching breathing patterns, even studying old skulls in Paris. The book hit the market during COVID, when people were desperate for breathing help, and it sold over 3 million copies. Nestor explains how proper breathing improves health, fights asthma and sleep apnea, and how modern habits like soft food and crooked teeth can hinder breathing.
