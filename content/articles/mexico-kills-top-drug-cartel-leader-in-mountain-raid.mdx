Mexico’s armed forces killed drug kingpin Nemesio Oseguera, known as ‘El Mencho’, after chasing his girlfriend to a mountain hide‑out near Tapalpa. The raid sparked gun battles, a helicopter crash and a surge of violence that blocked roads and set cars on fire across western Mexico. Seven of his men died and two soldiers were hurt. The hit was aided by U.S. intel, and the death has raised fears of a power vacuum that could lead to more bloodshed.
