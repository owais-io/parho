The UK has long treated Russia as its main enemy, but the rise of Donald Trump has made some Britons worry the US could become a danger instead. A recent poll shows 32 % now see America as a threat. Think‑tanks and even the government’s defence review keep focusing on Russia and other nations, yet they hint at a possible shift away from the “special relationship” that has defined UK‑US ties for decades.
