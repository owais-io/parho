Iran opened its airspace again after almost five hours of closure, a move that came while the US and Iran were on edge. President Trump said there was no plan to execute protesters, and Iranian officials denied any retaliation. The UN security council was called for an emergency meeting, and many foreign embassies closed or advised their staff to leave. The crackdown has killed over 2,500 people and arrested more than 18,000.
