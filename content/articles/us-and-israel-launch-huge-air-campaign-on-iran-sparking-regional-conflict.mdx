U.S. and Israeli forces have begun a huge joint air campaign against Iran, bombing dozens of sites, including homes of top leaders and weapons depots. The strike that hit a girls' school in southern Iran killed at least 51 people. Iran responded by firing missiles at Israel and U.S. bases in the Gulf, and striking U.S. forces in Iraq. The rapid escalation has turned the Middle East into a wider conflict, with many civilians at risk.
