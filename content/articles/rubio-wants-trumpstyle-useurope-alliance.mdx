At the Munich Security Conference, U.S. Senator Marco Rubio said America wants a new partnership with Europe, but only if Europe follows Trump‑style policies. He stressed tight borders, warned against “climate cults” and large welfare states, and said the U.S. will lead a new world order. Rubio called for reforms to the UN, not its abolition, and urged European leaders to join him in building a stronger, sovereign alliance. Critics say it’s not an equal partnership.
