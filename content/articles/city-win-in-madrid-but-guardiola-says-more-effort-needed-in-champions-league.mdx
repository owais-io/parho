Manchester City beat Real Madrid 2‑1 in a Champions League match, but coach Pep Guardiola said the win was not enough. He wants the team to raise their level in the knockout stages if they want to defend their 2023 title. Madrid manager Xabi Alonso is under pressure after poor form, with only two wins in eight games. Guardiola praised Madrid’s fight but insisted City must improve for later rounds.
