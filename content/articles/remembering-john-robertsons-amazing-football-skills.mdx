People still talk about John Robertson, the legendary footballer who played for Nottingham Forest. A letter shares a favorite memory of watching him at Arsenal’s Highbury in the 1980s. The writer describes how Robertson, known for clever moves, once nutmegged a defender in slow motion, surprising a home fan. He also recalls how manager Brian Clough praised Robertson’s passing that helped win a European Cup. Fans still feel his magic.
