BrewDog bought a 2,600‑hectare estate in Scotland’s Cairngorms for £8.5 million in 2020 and promised to plant a huge forest to capture carbon. After its chief executive was replaced and losses hit £37 million, the estate was sold to Oxygen Conservation for about £8.9 million, barely more than BrewDog paid. The deal also included carbon‑credit credits worth millions, sparking debate over the real value of green land deals.
