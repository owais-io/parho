Labour leader Keir Starmer had to decide whether to let Manchester mayor Andy Burnham run for a new parliamentary seat after a colleague resigned. Starmer could block him and look heavy‑handed, or allow the bid and risk losing the seat to an opposition party. The decision shows the tight spot the party is in, with polls slipping and critics questioning Starmer’s leadership.
