The Green Party has chosen Trafford councillor and plumber Hannah Spencer to run in the Gorton and Denton byelection. Spencer, who ran for mayor of Manchester in 2024, will be the party's first MP in the north if she wins. The Greens beat other candidates in a local vote. Labour and Reform UK also field candidates, and the seat was vacated after MP Andrew Gwynne stepped down.
