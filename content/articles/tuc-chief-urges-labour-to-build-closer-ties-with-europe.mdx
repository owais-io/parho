Paul Nowak, head of the TUC, says Labour leader Keir Starmer must work more closely with the EU, even considering a customs union, to improve trade and lower prices. He urges Starmer to focus on the cost of living and avoid political distractions in immigration policy. The union leader backs ending zero‑hour contracts and the new Employment Rights Act, but says more progress is needed, warning against rising racism and stressing the need for fair immigration reform.
