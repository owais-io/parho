Archbishop Mullally warned in a Christmas sermon that talking about immigration is splitting people and that we should focus on compassion and common humanity instead. She urged joy to open space in homes, churches, and conversations, and said money worries and inequality can pull us apart. Mullally, the London bishop, will become Canterbury’s first female archbishop in March. Her message called for kindness and welcoming others.
