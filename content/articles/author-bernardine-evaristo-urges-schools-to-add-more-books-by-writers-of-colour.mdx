Bernardine Evaristo says schools in England need more books by authors of colour. Only 1.9 % of GCSE English pupils study such books, up from 0.7 % five years ago. The Lit in Colour campaign has raised the share of set texts from 12 % to 36 %, but teachers say they lack time, resources and support to teach new titles.
