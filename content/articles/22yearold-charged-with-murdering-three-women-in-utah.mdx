A 22‑year‑old man from Iowa, Ivan Miller, has been arrested for killing three women in Utah. Investigators say he killed a woman at her home in southern Utah and then took two hikers to a trailhead near Capitol Reef National Park, where he killed them too. He fled in one of their cars, was tracked through Utah, Arizona and Colorado, and was finally taken into custody. Police say he admitted the killings were to get money.
