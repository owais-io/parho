Denmark’s Greenland says it will not let the United States take control. President Trump has said the U.S. wants to use Greenland as a military base to protect the Arctic from China and Russia. Greenland’s government says the island remains part of Denmark and NATO, and it wants a diplomatic solution. NATO and EU officials are talking about strengthening Arctic security, while some European leaders suggest offering Greenland EU membership to keep it safe.
