The DOJ has sued UCLA, saying the university allowed antisemitic harassment to happen during protests about Gaza. The lawsuit claims UCLA broke Title VII by failing to stop or fix hate attacks against Jewish faculty, staff and students. This is part of Trump‑era actions against colleges and follows earlier DOJ pressure on universities over diversity and transgender policies. UCLA has already paid a settlement and faces other suits, raising tensions with California.
