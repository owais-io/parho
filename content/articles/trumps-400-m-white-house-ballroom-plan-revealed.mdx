Donald Trump’s administration has released detailed sketches of a new ballroom that would add roughly 90,000 square feet to the White House. The $400 million project, funded by private donors such as Meta and Apple, has sparked backlash because the East Wing was torn down before plans were approved. Preservation groups sued, and the design team was changed. Trump says the ballroom will be safe and will host future inaugurations.
