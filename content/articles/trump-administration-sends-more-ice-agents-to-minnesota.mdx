The Trump administration is sending more immigration agents to Minnesota. They say it’s their biggest operation yet. ICE will visit homes and businesses to find illegal immigrants and people who stole public money. The state’s governor says it’s an attack on Minnesotans. Protesters are fighting back, saying the crackdown is unfair. The move comes after Minnesota faced fraud accusations. Local officials argue the surge could hurt businesses and make people feel unsafe.
