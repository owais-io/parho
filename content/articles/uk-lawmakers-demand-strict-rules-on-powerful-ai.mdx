Over 100 UK parliament members, including former ministers and AI experts, are demanding the government set hard limits on the biggest AI systems. They fear super‑intelligent AI could threaten national security and global safety. The group, led by the nonprofit Control AI, wants rules that include testing, shut‑off switches and international cooperation. The government says AI is already regulated, but critics say it’s too slow and too influenced by industry.
