Ethan Bundle‑Bell, 17, is the first person in his family to earn a Victorian Certificate of Education after choosing a practical vocational path at Braybrook Secondary College. He now looks forward to an apprenticeship in horticulture. Meanwhile, over 65,000 Victorian students are receiving their Australian Tertiary Admission Rankings, marking the first state to release these scores. The news comes after a mix‑up that mistakenly told some students they qualified for a university equity scheme.
