A baby Japanese macaque named Punch was born at Ichikawa Zoo in July. He was left by his mother and chased by other monkeys, so zoo workers gave him a stuffed orangutan toy to keep him calm. Videos of his struggles went viral, sparking worry about why moms abandon babies. Experts say first‑time moms and hot weather can cause abandonment. The zoo is now tightening visitor rules.
