Former UK prime minister Liz Truss launched a new YouTube show that promises to expose a “deep state” and promote a “MAGA‑style” revolution in Britain. She’s teamed up with U.S. journalist John Solomon and his network, Just the News, to tap into the same conservative media that helped Trump. Truss’s first episode got 67,000 views and 10,000 subscribers, showing the show’s modest start but also its connection to a global right‑wing ecosystem.
