A British family in Dubai was left on the ground while the UK was trying to evacuate people after Iranian attacks. The family tried to board a charter flight from Oman, but staff said most of them were not cleared. Only a toddler and a wheelchair‑using grandmother were allowed to fly. The family spent almost £4,000, received apologies but no real help, and are still stuck.
