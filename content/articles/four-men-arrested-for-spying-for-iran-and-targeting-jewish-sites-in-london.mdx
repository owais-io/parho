Police arrested four men in London and Hertfordshire who were suspected of spying for Iran and watching Jewish places and people. The arrests were part of a long‑running counter‑terrorism investigation. Three of the men are dual British‑Iranian, one is an Iranian national. Authorities say the spying could have been a step toward attacks on the Jewish community in Britain. The Home Secretary said the moves kept Britain safe and the Jewish community should stay alert.
