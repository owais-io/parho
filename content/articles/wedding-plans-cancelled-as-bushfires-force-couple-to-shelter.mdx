Lana Wolstencroft and her high‑school sweetheart Todd Byrnes were supposed to marry Saturday, but a huge bushfire in Victoria forced them to cancel the ceremony. They spent the day at a Mansfield showground shelter instead of the Kevington Hotel, helping to turn wedding flowers into bouquets for firefighting stations and packing extra food for relief centres. The couple and many friends are relieved the fire didn’t reach Mansfield, but they saw how badly other towns were damaged.
