Chiara Ferragni, a popular Italian fashion influencer, was found not guilty of fraud over two charity fundraising campaigns. In 2022 she sold a more expensive Christmas cake called pandoro, claiming the money would help a children’s hospital, but the hospital got only a small donation. In 2021–22 she sold chocolate eggs for Easter, also promising charity benefits. A court ruled the accusations were false, ending a two‑year legal battle.
