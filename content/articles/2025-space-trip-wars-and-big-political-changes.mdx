Katy Perry flew to the edge of space in an all‑female Blue Origin flight, but 2025 was otherwise grim. Wars in Ukraine, Sudan and Gaza continued, with suffering for women and children. In the US, a Trump‑style administration tightened rules on abortion and birth‑control aid, causing many to worry. Meanwhile, Italy made femicide a crime, France added consent to rape law, and Thailand became the first Southeast Asian country to legalise same‑sex marriage.
