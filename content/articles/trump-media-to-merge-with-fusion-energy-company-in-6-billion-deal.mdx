Donald Trump’s social‑media firm, Truth Social, is joining forces with TAE Technologies, a fusion‑energy startup, in a $6 billion merger. The two companies will split ownership 50‑50 and aim to build the world’s first large fusion power plant by 2031. Trump Media, which has struggled to grow its platform and is losing money, hopes the deal will diversify its business and tap growing demand for clean energy.
