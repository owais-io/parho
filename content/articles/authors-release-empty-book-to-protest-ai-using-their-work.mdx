Authors from around the world, including famous writers, printed a blank book with only their names to protest AI companies using their books without permission. About 10,000 writers signed the book, which will be handed out at a London book fair before the UK government reviews copyright rules. The book calls on the government to stop letting AI firms copy books without paying authors and to protect creative livelihoods.
