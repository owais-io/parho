Milano Cortina’s Winter Olympics are almost here, but protests in Milan over ICE agents have drawn attention. Organisers hoped Italy’s culture would keep the focus on sport, but demonstrations have pushed that goal aside. Meanwhile athletes are training hard: Lindsey Vonn is trying to race again after a knee injury, and NHL players will compete for the first time since 2014. Canada looks strong in hockey, and curling, ski jumping, and other events are set to begin soon.
