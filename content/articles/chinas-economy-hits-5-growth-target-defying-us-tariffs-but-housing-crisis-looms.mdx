China’s 2025 GDP grew 5 %, meeting its goal and beating expectations that US tariffs would slow growth. The country logged its largest trade surplus, proving it can find new markets. Yet home prices have dropped 20 % since 2021, hurting consumer confidence and risking a debt crisis. Analysts warn official figures may be inflated, and a weak domestic market could stall growth in 2026.
