Italian golfer Andrea Pavan, 36, fell three floors into a lift shaft at his private accommodation. The accident left him with vertebral fractures and a shoulder injury, requiring a six‑hour surgery. Though he is in serious but not life‑threatening condition, Pavan says he’s thankful to be alive and is keeping up with his kids on FaceTime. He must withdraw from the South African Open.
