After Trump said he didn’t know what body part was scanned in a recent MRI, the White House released a memo from the president’s doctor saying all tests were normal. The check, part of a routine review for a president in his late 70s, looked at his heart, blood vessels and abdomen and found no problems. Trump has been criticized for his late‑night posts, but the health report confirms he is physically fit.
