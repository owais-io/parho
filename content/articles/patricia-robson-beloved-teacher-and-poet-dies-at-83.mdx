Patricia Robson, an 83‑year‑old teacher, writer and activist, died from cancer. She spent her career bringing drama into primary schools, founding The Tree of Tales to let children act out history. After retiring she performed a one‑person show in schools and later led poetry groups for people with dementia. She published five poetry collections, even writing her last poem in a hospice. Her partner, son, and two granddaughters remember her as a warm, inspiring storyteller.
