Do Kwon, the South‑Korean founder of Terraform Labs, which created TerraUSD and Luna, has been sentenced to 15 years in prison for fraud. He helped cause a $40 billion collapse that hurt many investors. Kwon pleaded guilty to conspiracy and wire‑fraud charges, and the judge said his crime was very large, giving a longer sentence than the 12 years prosecutors wanted. He must also forfeit $19.3 million and may finish his sentence in South Korea if he follows the deal.
