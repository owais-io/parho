Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse from Minneapolis, was shot and killed by ICE agents while filming the officers in the city. Video shows him waving at passers‑by and recording the agents, not attacking them. The killings come after ICE's recent surge in the Twin Cities, which also killed resident Renee Good. White‑House officials called Pretti a “domestic terrorist” despite no evidence, sparking outrage and calls for investigations.
