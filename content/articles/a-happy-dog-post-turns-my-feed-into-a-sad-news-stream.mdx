After clicking a cheerful dog photo, the writer’s Instagram feed suddenly filled with sad posts about pet and human deaths. The algorithm kept showing her similar content because she kept tapping the happy images. She explains how social media can push people into negative emotions and make them feel grief for strangers. The story shows how one click can lead to a cascade of depressing content.
