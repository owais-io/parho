Two asylum‑seeking doctors who had been barred from working in the NHS because of a rule that only allowed certain jobs after 12 months of waiting are now allowed to apply for qualified positions. The Home Office changed the policy after a high‑court challenge, extending the list of NHS roles to include doctors and nurses. The change, coming into force Thursday, should help fill shortages and prevent skilled workers from losing experience.
