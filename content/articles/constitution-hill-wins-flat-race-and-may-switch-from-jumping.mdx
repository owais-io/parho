Constitution Hill, a nine‑year‑old hurdler who had fallen several times, shocked everyone by winning a flat race at Southwell. He beat his rivals by nine and a half lengths, giving his trainer Nicky Henderson and owner Michael Buckley a huge morale boost. The win suggests the horse might move to flat racing full‑time. Fans and bettors celebrated, and the crowded track showed growing interest in the sport.
