Taylor Adams, the 32‑year‑old Sydney Swans midfielder, was taken to St Vincent’s hospital after an altercation outside a club in Rose Bay, Sydney. Two men allegedly beat him, knocking him to the ground and cutting his cheek. He lost consciousness for a moment. Paramedics treated him on the scene and brought him to the hospital. He was discharged the next morning and will be monitored daily.
