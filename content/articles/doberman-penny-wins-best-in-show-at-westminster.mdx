Doberman Penny won best in show at the 150th Westminster Kennel Club event in New York, beating six other group champions. Judge David Fitzpatrick selected the four‑year‑old from Reseda, California. Handler Andy Linton, who has Parkinson’s, celebrated his second win after 1989. The show featured 2,500 dogs from 202 breeds and 17 countries, and paid tribute to late actress Catherine O’Hara.
