Democrats Ro Khanna and Republican Thomas Massie asked the Justice Department to disclose the names of six wealthy men who might be connected to the Jeffrey Epstein scandal. They announced the names in Congress: Leslie Wexner, the Victoria’s Secret founder, DP World CEO Sultan bin Ahmed, and four others—Nicola Caputo, Salvatore Nuara, Zurab Mikeladze, and Leonic Leonov. The move aims to expose possible hidden links in the huge Epstein file collection.
