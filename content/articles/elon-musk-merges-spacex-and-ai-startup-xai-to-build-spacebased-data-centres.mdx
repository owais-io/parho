Musk has announced a merger between SpaceX and his AI startup xAI, valuing the combined company at about $1.25 trillion. The deal will let SpaceX’s rockets carry satellites that could act as solar‑powered data centres for AI, reducing the need for expensive earth‑based infrastructure. While the idea could lower AI compute costs, experts warn about the technical hurdles and the impact on SpaceX shareholders.
