Donald Trump said a deal to end the war in Ukraine is almost finished after a two‑hour meeting with President Zelenskyy at his Florida home. He claimed the draft agreement is about 95 % ready and that both sides are close to agreeing. Trump said Russia’s leader wants peace too, but there are still hard questions about territory in Donbas and how the war ends. No sign that Russia will give up its demands, and Trump hasn’t promised Ukraine military help.
