Cyclone Ditwah hammered Sri Lanka, flooding Badulla and other towns. Heavy rains triggered landslides that buried homes, killing 410 people and leaving many missing. Villagers, like Layani Niroshani, lost everything and now must rebuild from scratch. Rescue teams and the military are helping, but thousands remain displaced, living in schools or on roofs. The disaster follows an already weak economy and could hurt tourism and food supplies.
