Two US lawmakers have asked former UK ambassador Peter Mandelson to give a recorded interview for the House Oversight Committee. They want him to talk about his close ties to Jeffrey Epstein and any knowledge he might have of Epstein’s crimes. The request comes after new documents and police investigations revealed Mandelson’s business and social links to Epstein. Mandelson has denied wrongdoing and must reply by month‑end.
