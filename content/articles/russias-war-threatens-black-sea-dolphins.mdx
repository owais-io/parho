Scientists say the war in Ukraine is hurting the Black Sea’s dolphins and the whole marine ecosystem. Shelling, oil spills and loud sonar from Russian ships are killing dolphins, and many have washed up on Ukraine’s coast. The war makes it hard to collect data or clean up. Researchers are trying to track pollution and hope to revive dolphin numbers after the fighting ends.
