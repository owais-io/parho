Senate Republicans are set to reject a Democratic‑backed resolution that would force President Trump to seek Congress’s approval for his Iran air campaign. Democrats say the bill would end U.S. involvement until Congress votes again. With 47 Democrats, they need five Republicans to win, but Senate Majority Leader John Thune argues Trump has the authority to act. A similar House proposal faces the same opposition, and Trump could veto it.
