In his column, Sean Ingle criticized how athletics keeps fans. World Athletics chief Jon Ridgeon replied that the sport is growing – income up 25% and social‑media views doubled. He said they are using data and tech to make races more exciting, like drone shots and sensors that track emotions. New ideas include a treadmill championship and letting athletes create their own online content. Ridgeon believes athletics can join the big sports if it keeps pushing hard.
