The UK government gave Palantir a £330 million contract to build a big data platform for the NHS. Human‑rights groups say the system could let government bodies share patient information and use it for things like immigration raids, similar to how the US uses Palantir for ICE. NHS trusts and doctors are urged to reject the deal, saying it hurts public trust and could turn data into a tool for surveillance.
