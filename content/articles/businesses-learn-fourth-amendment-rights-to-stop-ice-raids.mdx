In North Carolina, the immigrant‑rights group Siembra is teaching local businesses about the Fourth Amendment, which protects against un‑lawful searches. They hand out signs that say the store is private and that officers need a judge’s warrant. Over 250 businesses now carry the signs and have signed a pledge to support workers and refuse raids. The program also trains owners, spreads awareness, and is growing to nearby towns.
