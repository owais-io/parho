West Ham are in trouble again. After losing to Nottingham Forest, the team remains in the bottom four of the Premier League. Fans are upset and think the club’s new coach, Nuno Espírito Santo, hasn’t improved results. The previous manager, David Moyes, is no longer in charge, and the club still has to fight hard to avoid relegation. People worry the coaching change hasn’t helped yet.
