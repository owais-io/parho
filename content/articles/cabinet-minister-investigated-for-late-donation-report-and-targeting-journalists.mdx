Josh Simons, a Cabinet Office minister, is under investigation for hiring a PR firm to investigate journalists who wrote about a £730,000 undeclared donation to Labour Together, the think‑tank he led. He also failed to declare £1,250 gifts from donor Trevor Chinn until 32 days late, breaking Commons rules. Simons said the story was supplied by a hack linked to Russian propaganda, tying the case to earlier fines for Labour Together’s donation breaches.
