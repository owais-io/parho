Vonn crashed early in the downhill at Cortina, breaking her left leg and being airlifted to the hospital for surgery. The former Olympic champion had returned from a five‑year break and hoped to win a medal, but the fall ended her run. Her teammate Breezy Johnson had also been injured earlier, and the race saw a second crash that required an ambulance. The incident raised questions about the sport’s danger.
