Yvette Cooper, ex‑Labour leader, told the BBC that Britain must decide its own foreign policy instead of just following the United States. She criticised former PM Tony Blair’s claim that the UK should have backed Trump’s early airstrikes on Iran and said the country should learn from Iraq and act in its own national interest. Blair had urged current PM Keir Starmer to let the US use British bases, but Cooper disagreed.
