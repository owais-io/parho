A plan to build a $1.5bn Trump Tower on the Gold Coast has sparked debate. The project, led by developer David Young who has a history of bankruptcies, says construction should start in August and finish by 2032. City officials say a formal application is still needed. Critics point out that many Trump projects around the world never materialise, and the Gold Coast is trying to rebrand itself beyond flashy real‑estate. Whether the tower will be built remains uncertain.
