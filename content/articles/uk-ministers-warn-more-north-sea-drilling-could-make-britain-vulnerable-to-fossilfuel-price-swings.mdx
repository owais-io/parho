Ministers say new North Sea oil licences would tie the UK to unpredictable global markets. Conservatives want to lift the ban, fast‑track Rosebank and Jackdaw, and scrap the windfall tax. Labour stresses the need to cut fossil‑fuel dependence and invest in clean power, arguing that licences won’t curb prices. Both sides plan to protect households from rising bills while debating the country’s energy future.
