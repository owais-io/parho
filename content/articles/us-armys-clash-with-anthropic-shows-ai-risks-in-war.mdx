Anthropic is an AI company that focuses on safety. It has fought the U.S. Department of Defense over how its Claude model can be used. The Pentagon wants the AI for intelligence and possibly weapons. Anthropic refuses to allow mass surveillance or autonomous weapons. The dispute has made Anthropic a “supply‑chain risk,” and the company is fighting the decision in court. The case highlights the tension between AI safety and national‑security needs.
