England is stuck because winger Henry Arundell may be banned for the Ireland match after a red card against Scotland. Coach Steve Borthwick has only a few fast wings left, with Feyi‑Waboso injured and other options in doubt. After a heavy loss to Scotland, England need a win over Ireland to keep a chance at the Six Nations title. Ireland, meanwhile, are regaining form after a shaky start.
