After the Munich Security Conference, leaders discussed how Europe can strengthen its own defence while staying close to the U.S. Talks included a new European nuclear deterrent and tighter UK‑EU security ties. U.S. officials said they want to work with Europe, though some still question U.S. commitment. The conference also covered Greenland’s status, with the U.S. still showing interest, and pushed for long‑term U.S. security guarantees for Ukraine.
