Iran’s foreign minister says some Gulf countries are quietly backing U.S. strikes on Iran. He claims Saudi Arabia’s crown prince has talked with former President Trump, urging more attacks. Iran is also targeting Gulf states with drones and keeps the Hormuz Strait closed to enemies. Meanwhile, Iran’s military has tightened hard‑liner control, the country faces heavy air attacks and internet blackouts, and it urges Gulf Muslims to pick sides.
