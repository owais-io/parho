The Trump administration’s anti‑woke push has hit DEI hard, with the Supreme Court’s 2023 decision on affirmative action forcing many firms to pull back from diversity programs. Two NYU law professors, Kenji Yoshino and David Glasgow, explain that equality itself is not dying. In their book they offer practical ways to keep fairness alive—like removing bias, using “universality” and loopholes in admissions—to survive legal and political attacks.
