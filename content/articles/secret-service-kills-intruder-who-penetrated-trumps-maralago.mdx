At about 1:30 a.m. Sunday, Secret Service agents and a deputy stopped a 21‑year‑old man who had slipped into the inner perimeter of Donald Trump’s Mar‑a‑Lago club in Palm Beach, Florida. The man was holding a shotgun and a gasoline can. The agents fired and killed him. No law‑enforcement officers were hurt. The intruder, identified as Austin Tucker Martin from North Carolina, was missing before the attack. Investigators are looking into his motive.
