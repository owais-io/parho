Labour MPs are worried that their leader, Keir Starmer, is seen as a relic of the old ruling party and is dragging the party down. Some think he should step down before the next election, but others fear a replacement could cause chaos and hurt Labour’s chances. The party is still deciding on a new leader, but no clear candidate has emerged. Controversies such as the Mandelson scandal and internal divisions keep the debate heated.
