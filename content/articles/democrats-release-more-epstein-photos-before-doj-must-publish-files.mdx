House Democrats on the oversight committee released a new batch of unlabeled photos from Jeffrey Epstein’s estate right before the Justice Department’s deadline to publish its files. The images show strange text on bodies, famous names like Bill Gates and Noam Chomsky, and passports. They aim to pressure the DOJ to release documents under the Epstein Files Transparency Act, while the department rushes to redact thousands of pages. Meanwhile, Ghislaine Maxwell’s court filing seeks to overturn her conviction.
