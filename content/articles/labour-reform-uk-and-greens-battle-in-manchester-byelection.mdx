Labour’s Angeliki Stogia is fighting to keep her seat in Gorton and Denton against Reform UK and the Greens. The contest is tense because if the Greens split Labour’s support, Reform UK could win. Reform’s candidate is a TV pundit, while the Green hopeful is a local plumber named Hannah Spencer. Local voters worry about divided votes and national politics, making this a real test for the UK’s parties.
