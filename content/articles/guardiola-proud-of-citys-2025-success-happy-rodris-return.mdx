Manchester City’s boss Pep Guardiola says the club is his proudest moment of 2025, after turning a bad season into the Premier League’s best form. City tops the points‑per‑game chart, just ahead of Arsenal, and the team is already looking better with midfielder Rodri back after a hamstring injury. Guardiola also praised the January signings that lifted the squad, and hinted at adding winger Antoine Semenyo next winter.
