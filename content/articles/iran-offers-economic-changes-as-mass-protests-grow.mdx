Iran is trying to calm protesters by changing how it gives subsidies, but the protests now cover most of the country. Over 35 people have died and more than 1,200 have been arrested. Prices are still rising because of high inflation, and the government says the new plan will help ordinary people. The UN and the US have urged Iran to let people protest peacefully, while Israel says the protests could change Iran’s future.
