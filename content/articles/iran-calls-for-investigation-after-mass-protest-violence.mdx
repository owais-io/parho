Iran is now looking into the deadly crackdown on protests that killed thousands. Politicians, academics, and the media are demanding an independent inquiry and a quick internet reopening. The country faces huge food inflation and a weak economy, while some see a shift in foreign policy as needed. Inside the country, pressure is growing for free press and accountability, and there are fears the unrest could lead to deeper political change.
