Researchers using AI are producing a huge amount of weak, poorly written papers that are hard for reviewers to spot. This overload is confusing and slowing down the peer‑review process. Experts worry that the flood of bad work could erode scientific standards and make it harder to find trustworthy research. The problem isn’t just in AI; it spreads to other fields that rely on careful review. Everyone should stay alert and demand higher quality in published work.
