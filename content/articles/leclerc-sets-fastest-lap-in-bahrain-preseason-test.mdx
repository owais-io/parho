Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc clocked the quickest lap in Bahrain’s final pre‑season test, beating rivals by almost a second. The test used soft tires and low fuel loads, and Leclerc’s time was 1:31.992. Mercedes’ George Russell and Red Bull’s Max Verstappen were close behind, showing steady speed across all three tests. Aston Martin struggled, finishing with only six laps due to engine and battery issues. The results give Mercedes confidence heading into Australia.
