In early February, Ukraine and Russia will hold a second round of US‑mediated peace talks in the UAE. The talks come after a week of fighting, including drone and missile strikes on Kyiv and Kharkiv, that critics say undermine negotiations. Ukrainian President Zelenskyy said talks were constructive, while Russian attacks caused civilian casualties and infrastructure damage. US officials, including Jared Kushner, say the talks are a step forward, but many Ukrainians remain skeptical.
