New York Attorney General settled with Betar US after an investigation found the group carried out bias‑motivated assaults, threats and harassment against Muslim, Arab, Palestinian and Jewish people. Betar must stop encouraging violence and will face a $50,000 penalty if it breaks the agreement. The group, seen as an extremist group by the ADL, is shutting down its New York operations and says it denies wrongdoing. The settlement ends its presence in the city.
