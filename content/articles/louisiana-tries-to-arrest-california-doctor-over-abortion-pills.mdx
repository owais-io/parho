Louisiana police want to extradite a California doctor, Remy Coeytaux, who is accused of mailing abortion pills to a woman in Louisiana. The move is part of a broader fight between states that ban abortions and those that protect doctors through shield laws. If convicted, Coeytaux could face a fine and up to 50 years in prison. Louisiana says the pills were illegal drug trafficking and will keep targeting doctors who use shield laws.
