Teachers say that rude and threatening parents are making school jobs harder and hurting their mental health. In a survey, more than 90% of headteachers report dealing with “challenging behaviour” from parents, and 60% have faced verbal abuse in the last year. Parents often fight school disciplinary decisions, use legal‑style complaints and even AI‑generated letters. The teachers want the government to help parents act calmly and support children’s education.
