Super Rugby Pacific kicks off this weekend, and Australia’s four provincial teams must prove themselves if the Wallabies want to win the 2027 World Cup. The season opens with state‑vs‑state games, a chance for local talent to earn national spots. With NRL stars returning, a strong women’s sevens side, and the Wallabies needing a new playmaker, the focus is on fierce local battles that could spark a new era of success.
