The $200 million Kyle and Jackie O breakfast show was taken off air after a public dispute between hosts. Network ARN said Jackie Henderson no longer wants to work with Kyle Sandilands, ending their 25‑year partnership. Sandilands was suspended for 14 days for alleged misconduct. Both contracts were terminated early, and the country’s top breakfast program is now off the air. Speculation about a replacement show remains.
