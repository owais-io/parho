Rachel Reeves pulled out of a planned London Stock Exchange event after U.S. President Donald Trump announced tariffs on eight European countries until the U.S. can buy Greenland. The threat caused markets to dip, and Reeves joined a press conference with Prime Minister Keir Starmer instead. The LSE event went on without her, while traders rushed to safe‑haven assets like gold and silver.
