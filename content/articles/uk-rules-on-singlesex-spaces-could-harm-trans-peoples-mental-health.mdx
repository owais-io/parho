Fifteen major UK mental‑health charities have warned that new government rules on single‑sex spaces could hurt trans and non‑binary people’s mental health. The charities – including Samaritans, Mind and the Royal College of Psychiatrists – wrote to Equality Minister Bridget Phillipson about guidance from the Equality and Human Rights Commission. The guidance, still waiting approval, could let people be questioned about using bathrooms that match their gender identity, raising fears of discrimination and anxiety.
