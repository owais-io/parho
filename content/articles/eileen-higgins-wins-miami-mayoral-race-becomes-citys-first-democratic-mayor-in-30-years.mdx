Eileen Higgins, a 61‑year‑old Democrat and former county commissioner, won Miami’s mayoral race on Tuesday, becoming the city’s first Democratic mayor in three decades. She won 36% in the first round and then beat Republican Emilio González in the runoff. The win shows voters in the largely immigrant‑owned city are turning away from Trump‑backed policies. It’s seen as a boost for Democrats nationwide.
