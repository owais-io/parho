Iranian drones hit the UAE’s Shah gasfield and the shared South Pars field with Qatar. The strikes, possibly helped by Israel, could damage gas production for years, raising worries about higher oil and fuel prices worldwide. The attacks have pushed Iran to name other Gulf oil sites as targets and sparked alarm from Qatar, the UAE, and the US, who fear longer‑term energy shortages and political fallout.
