Israel has decided to ban many aid groups from operating in Gaza because they didn’t comply with new registration rules that ask for detailed staff information. The ban includes well‑known charities like ActionAid, the International Rescue Committee and Médecins Sans Frontières. Ten countries, including Britain, Canada and France, warned the crisis is getting worse, with people lacking shelter, medicine and clean water. They urge the UN to keep helping and to lift restrictions on items that could be used by Hamas.
