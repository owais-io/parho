**  
Saudi Arabia’s biggest oil company, Aramco, makes huge profits that fund the government and give the ruling family power. Because the economy depends on oil, Saudi officials fight to slow worldwide moves to cut fossil fuels. They use their influence in UN climate talks to block new rules, even as the country faces extreme heat and water shortages. While building solar and wind power at home, the main goal is to keep selling oil for as long as possible.
