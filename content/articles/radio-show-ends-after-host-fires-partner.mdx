Australian radio hosts Kyle Sandilands and Jackie O were in a heated feud. Sandilands criticized Jackie on-air, calling her unprofessional, and later wrote her a “love letter” to apologize. The dispute led to the Australian Radio Network pulling the breakfast show and ending Jackie's contract. Sandilands is suspended for 14 days and may lose his contract if he can’t fix the breach. The show also faces fines from the regulator for offensive content.
