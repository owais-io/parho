US Senator Marco Rubio visited Hungary, telling Prime Minister Viktor Orbán that the United States would support him politically and financially. He hinted at help if Hungary struggles and suggested keeping the country exempt from sanctions that restrict buying Russian oil. The visit comes before Hungary’s tough parliamentary election and raises EU worries that the US is trying to stir division among allies. Many European officials see Rubio’s message as a push to influence Hungary’s politics.
