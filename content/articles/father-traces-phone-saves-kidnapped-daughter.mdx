A 15‑year‑old girl from Porter, Texas, was taken by a man with a knife while walking her dog. Her dad used his son’s phone parental‑control app to trace the phone, found it 2 miles away in a wooded area. He reached the spot, saw his daughter and dog in a pickup truck with the attacker, and helped her escape. Police later arrested the suspect, Giovanni Rosales Espinoza, on kidnapping and child‑indecency charges.
