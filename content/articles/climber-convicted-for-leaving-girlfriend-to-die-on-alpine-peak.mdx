An Austrian court found amateur climber Thomas P guilty of manslaughter after he left his girlfriend, Kerstin G, on a dangerous winter climb. The judge said Thomas acted like an unlicensed guide, creating a legal duty of care that he failed to meet. The case may set a new precedent for how experienced climbers are held responsible for less‑skilled partners across Europe.
