A Guardian review shows no ICE deportation officer has died violently since 2003. Most of the 15 past deaths were from Covid, with only two caused by heat or a heart attack. Despite claims of a 1,300% rise in assaults, the DHS hasn't released evidence. Other ICE agents, mainly in the HSI unit, have died from violence, but not during routine deportations. Meanwhile, 32 migrants died in ICE detention facilities last year.
