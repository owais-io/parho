President Trump joined Israel in an air‑and‑sea campaign against Iran without getting Congress to approve it. Republicans in both houses supported him and blocked measures that would have forced the military to stop. The war has already cost billions, killed dozens of U.S. soldiers, and sparked debate about the president’s authority to wage war. Democrats oppose the action and argue the war is illegal and unnecessary.
