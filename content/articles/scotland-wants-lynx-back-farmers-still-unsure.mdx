About 61% of Scots want lynx back in the Highlands, but farmers and gamekeepers worry the cats will eat their sheep and deer. Six charities are talking with locals, trying to build a solid plan and figure out fair compensation. The Scottish government has been slow, and some rewilders even released lynx illegally last year. Politicians and the public are still debating whether and when to bring the predators back.
