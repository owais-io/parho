UK defence minister John Healey said recent drone strikes on a coalition base in Iraq showed Russia had helped Iran. He connected the Russia‑Ukraine war with Iran’s fight against US forces. The comments came after the Royal Navy ship HMS Dragon was sent late to Cyprus. Healey defended the head of the armed forces and hinted the UK might step up if Iran escalates.
