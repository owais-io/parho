Jeff Bezos, who bought the Washington Post in 2013, announced a huge round of layoffs that will cut almost half of its 790‑person staff. The cuts included the closure of half the paper’s foreign bureaus and the dismissal of hundreds of reporters. The move has sparked criticism that Bezos is prioritising his own interests and political goals over quality journalism.
