A 42‑year‑old protester who took part in a Sydney rally against Israeli President Isaac Herzog was arrested early Saturday by eight armed officers who burst into his home. Lawyers say the police used too much force, smashing the door while the man slept. The video shows the officers and the arrest. He faces several charges and will appear in court. The incident has sparked criticism of NSW’s tough anti‑protest laws.
