Arsenal have struggled badly in January, winning just once in five games. Fans and pundits are worried they might drop out of the title race. The team’s key scorers, Leandro Trossard and Viktor Gyökeres, have only five goals each – far below a typical champion side. After a loss to Manchester United, manager Mikel Arteta faces pressure to turn the season around before the final 15 matches.
