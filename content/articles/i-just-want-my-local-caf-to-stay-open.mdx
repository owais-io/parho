The writer talks about a family‑owned café on Hampstead Heath that could close if a chain buys it. Famous actors joined a petition to stop the takeover. She describes the café as a cozy, affordable place where people of all backgrounds eat together and feel welcome. The author says such cafés are disappearing as chains replace them. She urges the city to protect these community hubs and says she’s tired of trendy avocado toast.
