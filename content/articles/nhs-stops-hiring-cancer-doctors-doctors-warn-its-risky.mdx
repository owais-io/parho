NHS has frozen hiring for half of its cancer treatment centres and over a third of radiology departments, a move that doctors say will delay diagnoses and raise death rates. The Royal College of Radiologists warns the freezes risk dangerous delays and extra costs. Rural and deprived areas feel the hit hardest, while the NHS plans to hire thousands more staff in the next decade.
