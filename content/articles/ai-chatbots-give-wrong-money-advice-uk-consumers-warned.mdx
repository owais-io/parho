AI chatbots are giving British consumers incorrect financial and tax advice, says research. Tests of popular tools like ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, and Meta’s AI showed they suggested breaking savings limits, wrongly required travel insurance, and gave bad flight‑claim info. The consumer group Which? warned that using these bots for money matters can be risky. While some users found useful tips, many received outdated or misleading guidance, raising concerns.
