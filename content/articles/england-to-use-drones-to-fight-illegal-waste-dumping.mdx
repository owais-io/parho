England will deploy a 33‑person drone squad to hunt illegal waste dumps, following a £1.4 m fine and conviction of a leading waste‑crime boss. The plan uses drones with lidar to map sites and boosts the Environment Agency’s crime unit to 20 experts. The move aims to stop sophisticated waste criminals and protect landowners and wildlife, tackling a problem that costs the UK about £1 bn each year.
