James “Jimmy” Gracey, a 20‑year‑old University of Alabama student, drowned during spring break in Barcelona. Police say he fell into the sea while walking to a dock, with no evidence of foul play. CCTV footage and an autopsy confirm the accidental fall and injuries from hitting breakwater rocks. His family flew to Spain, and the university issued a statement of condolence.
