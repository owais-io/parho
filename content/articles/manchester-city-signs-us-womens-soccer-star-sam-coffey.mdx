Manchester City has signed 27‑year‑old US midfielder Sam Coffey on a three‑and‑a‑half‑year contract. The Olympic gold‑medalist has 42 caps and five goals for the U.S. team and won the 2022 NWSL title with Portland Thorns. City reportedly paid about £600,000. City’s women’s director said Coffey’s arrival shows the club’s ambition, while Coffey praised the team’s style and happiness. Meanwhile, Arsenal’s Michelle Agyemang is out after a knee injury.
