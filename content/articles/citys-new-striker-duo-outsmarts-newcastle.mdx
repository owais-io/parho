Pep Guardiola switched tactics by pairing fast forward Omar Marmoush with winger Antoine Semenyo instead of relying on Erling Haaland. The pair played on the flanks, opening gaps for midfielders like Reijnders and Foden. Their quick movements confused Newcastle’s defenders, helping City score three goals and proving a strong attack can work without Haaland.
