In a tragic event at an indoor ice rink in Pawtucket, Rhode Island, a shooter fired about 13 shots during a high‑school boys’ hockey game, killing three people and leaving three others critically injured. Police say the gunman died from a self‑inflicted wound. Officials suspect a family dispute may have sparked the attack, but details remain unclear. Victims’ names are not yet released.
