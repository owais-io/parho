An Australian crime boss who is believed to be behind a violent tobacco‑smuggling feud in Victoria, Australia, was arrested in Iraq. The man, Kazem Hamad, was taken into custody by Iraqi police after Australian investigators tipped them off. Authorities say he has links to drug trafficking and organised crime. The Australian Federal Police say the arrest stops a major threat to the country and weakens the gang’s network.
