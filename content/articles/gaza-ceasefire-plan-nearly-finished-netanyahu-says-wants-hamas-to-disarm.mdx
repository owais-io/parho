Gaza ceasefire plan is almost finished, says Israeli PM Benjamin Netanyahu. He says the first phase is near completion, but the next step must be Hamas disarming. Netanyahu will talk about it with President Trump in Washington. German chancellor said phase two must start soon. Meanwhile, Israel has pulled back to a ceasefire line, keeping 58% of Gaza, while casualties on both sides rise. Netanyahu rejects the ICC arrest warrants and opposes a Palestinian state.
