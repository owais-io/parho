After a close race with Chelsea, Liverpool have secured 20‑year‑old centre‑back Jérémy Jacquet from Rennes. The club will pay about £55 million, plus £5 million in add‑ons. Liverpool were eager to strengthen their back line because of injuries to Jeremie Frimpong, Conor Bradley and Joe Gomez. Jacquet can play both as a defender and a defensive midfielder, giving the squad more options.
