Trump said the U.S. might strike Iran’s Kharg Island oil hub again, claiming he’d do it “just for fun” while the war with Iran continues. He bragged the U.S. has already damaged the island and could hit it more times. Trump also questioned whether Iran’s new leader is alive and warned that the closed Strait of Hormuz is hurting global oil supplies and pushing prices up. He urged other countries to help secure the waterway.
