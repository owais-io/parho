Labour is introducing new rules to stop shady money in politics. Crypto donations are banned and overseas cash above £100,000 is capped. The party also wants tighter limits on how much anyone can give and will cut corporate money. Critics say the measures still let big donors like Elon Musk or foreign firms influence politics. Labour hopes these changes protect democracy and make funding more transparent.
