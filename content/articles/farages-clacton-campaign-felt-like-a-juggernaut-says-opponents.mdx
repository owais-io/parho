Candidates who lost to Nigel Farage in Clacton say his Reform UK campaign was huge and may have spent too much. A former aide claims the party didn’t declare costs for leaflets, banners, and even a bar refurbishment, putting them over the legal limit. Police are looking into the money reports, while Farage and Reform deny any wrongdoing. Opponents feel overwhelmed by the big, well‑funded effort.
