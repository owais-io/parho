An art show called “Happy Heavenly Birthday, Oscar” opened at the Black Panther Party Museum in Oakland to remember 22‑year‑old Oscar Grant, who was shot by police in 2008. Visitors can leave birthday cards and voicemails for Grant and other victims. It’s part of a national 1‑800 Happy Birthday project that collects voices for 15 people killed by police. The exhibit will tour the U.S. in 2026‑27, funded by the Mellon Foundation and others.
