After a truck filled with explosives rammed into Temple Israel in West Bloomfield, Michigan, the attack was stopped by security and the driver was killed. Michigan’s governor and a senator praised the guards and urged lawmakers to cut hateful rhetoric. Jewish groups across the US are demanding more money for armed guards and better building security. The incident follows rising antisemitism linked to the Israel‑Iran conflict.
