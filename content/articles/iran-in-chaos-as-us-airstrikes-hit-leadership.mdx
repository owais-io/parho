The US dropped bombs on Tehran, killing almost 800 people and striking the offices of Iran’s top religious leaders. The strike hit the building that selects a new Supreme Leader after Ali Khamenei was killed. Inside the city, fires and smoke spread while a power fight rages over who should lead next. Israel’s jets dominate the skies, and diplomatic talks with the US have stalled. Iran says it will keep fighting and rejects any talks that could weaken its security.
