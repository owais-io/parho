India’s top doctors say the new weight‑loss injections—Mounjaro, Wegovy and Ozempic—are being used too quickly and without enough control. They warn that the drugs aren’t a cure for the country’s rising diabetes and obesity problems and that side‑effects and abuse could harm patients. Prices are high, but cheaper versions may appear when patents expire, and doctors say only specialists should prescribe them while lifestyle changes remain essential.
