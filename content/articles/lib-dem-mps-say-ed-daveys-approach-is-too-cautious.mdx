Many Liberal Democrat MPs feel their leader, Ed Davey, is being too careful and not giving the party a clear national message. They want bolder policies, especially on the cost of living, and faster action before upcoming elections. Some praise Davey’s media skills but think he’s too academic. The frustration could grow if the party’s slow progress keeps up.
