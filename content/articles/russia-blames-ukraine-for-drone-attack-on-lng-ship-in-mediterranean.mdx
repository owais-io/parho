Russia says Ukrainian drones attacked the Arctic Metagaz LNG tanker, causing it to explode and sink 150 miles off Libya’s coast. The ship carried 61,000 tonnes of LNG and was under US and EU sanctions. Ukraine has not replied. Meanwhile, Ukrainian drones hit sites in Russia’s Saratov region, forcing airport closures and causing injuries. The incident comes as Russia seeks an energy boost amid Middle East tensions, while Ukraine plans to boycott the Paralympics over Russian athletes.
