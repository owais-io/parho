Queen Camilla opened up about a physical attack she suffered on a train when she was a teenager, saying she was furious and that the memory stayed with her for years. She linked the story to the 2024 murder of three women, the Hunt family, and praised the survivors’ bravery. Camilla used the platform to call for more conversation about domestic abuse and support for victims, and a new Hunt Family Fund will raise money for related charities.
