A Virgin Australia flight from Brisbane to Melbourne had smoke coming from a vape just before landing. Passengers and crew saw the smoke, so the pilot declared a “pan” to speed up the arrival. Firefighters arrived at the gate and safely removed the device. The airline’s safety policy now limits where power‑banks and vapes can be stored, after earlier incidents showed that cheap lithium batteries can spark fires.
