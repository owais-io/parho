Former presidents Barack Obama and Bill Clinton condemned the death of 37‑year‑old nurse Alex Pretti, who was shot by ICE officers in Minneapolis. They called it a tragedy and urged a full investigation, saying it threatens America’s values. The shooting has angered politicians on both sides, with some demanding accountability and even threatening to block homeland‑security funding if the agency is not held responsible.
