A new TikTok craze called “Chinamaxxing” has young users dressing up and doing Chinese activities like qigong and learning Mandarin. Some worry the trend feels like a cultural shift that makes them love China more than their own country. Conservatives see it as a threat, while others think it’s harmless curiosity. The writer suggests we give young people better chances at home instead of warning them.
