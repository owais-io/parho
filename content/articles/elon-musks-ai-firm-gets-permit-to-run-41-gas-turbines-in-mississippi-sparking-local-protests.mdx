Elon Musk’s AI company, xAI, has been approved to run 41 methane gas turbines at its large datacenter called Colossus 2 in Southaven, Mississippi. The turbines will power the AI supercomputers that run the Grok chatbot. Residents and environmental groups say the generators add dangerous air pollution and noise, and they’re already worried about health problems. The state agency rushed the decision, sparking protests and a lawsuit from the NAACP.
