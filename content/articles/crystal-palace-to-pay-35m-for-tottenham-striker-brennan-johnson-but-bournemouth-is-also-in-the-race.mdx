Crystal Palace have agreed a club‑record £35m fee to buy Tottenham forward Brennan Johnson, but the deal is being challenged by Bournemouth, who may match the offer. Palace need a striker after a heavy schedule and a break in their squad with Senegal winger Ismaïla Sarr away. Johnson has struggled for starts at Spurs, while clubs like Manchester City and others are also interested.
