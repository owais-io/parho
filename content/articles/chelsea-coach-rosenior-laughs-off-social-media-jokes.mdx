Chelsea’s new head coach, Liam Rosenior, says he doesn’t let online jokes bother him. After a blunder against Arsenal and some memes, he says life’s too short to worry about others’ laughs. He talks about staying serious in work, but also having fun. He’s building a team culture, with players meeting before games and working on mindset. He supports young players and keeps a light‑hearted attitude.
