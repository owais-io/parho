In the early 1990s, Trump signed two loans for Florida houses, saying they were his main homes, but he never lived there – he rented them out. The deals weren’t illegal, but Trump says the same thing he did could be fraud. He’s using the idea to charge political rivals with mortgage fraud, even as his own White House says nothing was wrong. The story shows a clash over how mortgage rules are applied.
