A teacher and a father were killed in a shooting at EZ’s Lounge in Oakland. The 33‑year‑old educator and her student’s teacher, plus seven other people were shot early Saturday morning. Police said the victims died on the scene or later in hospital, and they found multiple firearms. The community mourns, and city officials note Oakland’s low homicide rate and ongoing violence‑prevention programs.
