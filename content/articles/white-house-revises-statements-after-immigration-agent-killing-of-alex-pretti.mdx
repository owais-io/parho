Federal investigators are reviewing body‑camera footage from the fatal shooting of Alex Pretti by Border Patrol agents in Minneapolis. The White House has retracted earlier claims that portrayed Pretti as a threat, and the DHS says Border Patrol commander Gregory Bovino will no longer lead the agency’s operations in the city. The move comes as Trump’s administration looks to pull back the federal agent surge and address farm‑price worries in Iowa.
