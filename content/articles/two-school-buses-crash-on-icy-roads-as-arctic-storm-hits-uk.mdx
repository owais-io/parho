Two school buses hit icy roads during a cold blast, causing minor injuries to nine kids and nine adults. One driver and a child were sent to hospital, while another bus slipped into a ditch but no one was hurt. The storm, dubbed Storm Goretti, has left many schools closed and roads slow to clear. Officials warned of heavy snow, strong winds and power cuts, and will pay extra help for those in the cold.
