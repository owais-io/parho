Prunella Scales, famous for her role as Dottie in 1990s Tesco ads, used the money from those commercials to pay for her touring and stage work. She and her husband, Tim West, saved extra money each year, putting it into a fund that helped drama students and other charities. Their generosity made a big impact on British theatre, and many people benefited from their support. Scales' legacy lives on through the opportunities she created for others.
