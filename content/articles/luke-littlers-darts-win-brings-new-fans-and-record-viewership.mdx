Luke Littler, 18, won his second world darts title, drawing a record 2.5 million viewers on Sky. He beat Dutch player Gian van Veen 7‑1 and said he won’t change. The championship sold out, with fans from 20 countries and in costume, turning the venue into a lively festival. His success is helping darts grow, attracting younger fans and new sponsors.
