Qatar is talking with FIFA about hosting the inaugural Women’s Club World Cup in January 2028. The event would clash with many European leagues, forcing them to rethink winter breaks. Qatar has ready stadiums and a mild winter, but the tournament could raise concerns over the country’s human‑rights record and its limited history in women’s football. FIFA has not yet set a host or bidding rules, and other Gulf nations could also be considered.
