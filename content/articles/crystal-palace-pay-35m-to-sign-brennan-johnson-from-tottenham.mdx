Crystal Palace paid a club‑record £35 million to sign 24‑year‑old striker Brennan Johnson from Tottenham. Manager Oliver Glasner praised the quick deal and said Johnson’s pace and goal‑scoring will boost the squad. Johnson had limited starts at Spurs and scored their Europa League winner. Tottenham will need a new striker in January, while Palace eye more signings for the back and midfield.
