Aston Martin says it will lose more money in 2025 than expected, giving its fifth warning in just six months. To boost cash, it sold the rights for its name in Formula One to its own team for £50 million. The deal mainly comes from owner Lawrence Stroll, who controls both sides. The company still plans to deliver about 500 new Valhalla cars in 2026, but debt has jumped 70% and sales have slipped because of tariffs.
