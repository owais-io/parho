Everton manager David Moyes complains that referees are inconsistent and the PGMO team won’t explain their decisions. He was upset after Fulham earned a penalty that changed a game, while Everton didn’t get a similar penalty against Arsenal. Moyes also criticised a VAR‑reversed handball call in the Arsenal win and questions why some teams seem to get easier calls. He wants officials to be more consistent.
