At Wimbledon, Tennis Australia hosts an annual barbecue where former and current players, coaches, and journalists celebrate Australian tennis heritage. The event keeps the link between past champions—like Rod Laver, Ken Rosewall, and Lleyton Hewitt—and new talent alive. Legends talk about the Davis Cup tradition, mentoring young players, and sharing stories over food, reminding Australians that the sport’s history matters and new stars can learn from those who came before.
