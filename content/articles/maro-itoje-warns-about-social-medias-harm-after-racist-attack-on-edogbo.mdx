England captain Maro Itoje said social media can hurt people, especially after Irish player Edwin Edogbo was racially abused online. The comments came before England’s big Six Nations game against Ireland, where Itoje will play his 100th match. Itoje warned athletes and fans not to let online chatter dictate their lives. He also said we still need to fight racism, but progress is being made.
