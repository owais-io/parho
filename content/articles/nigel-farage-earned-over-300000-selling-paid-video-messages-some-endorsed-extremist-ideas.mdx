Nigel Farage has earned more than £300,000 by selling personalised video messages on Cameo, a pay‑per‑clip service. A Guardian investigation found that some of his videos promoted neo‑Nazi events and extremist slogans. Farage says the videos are for supporters and occasional mistakes can happen, but he denies endorsing violence. He remains very active on the platform, raising concerns about how paid political content can spread extremist ideas.
