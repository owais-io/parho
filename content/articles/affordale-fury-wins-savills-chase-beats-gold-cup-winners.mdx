Affordale Fury, a seven‑year‑old who has been hurt and taken long breaks, won the Grade One Savills Chase at Leopardstown. He beat Galopin Des Champs, the 2023 and 2024 Gold Cup champion, and I Am Maximus, the 2024 Grand National winner, by two‑and‑a‑half lengths. The win came after the horse’s last novice chase and shows his recovery. Trainer Noel Meade is delighted, and the race has left the Gold Cup betting wide open.
