Overdose deaths have fallen across the country since late 2023, and experts point to a little‑known cause: Crisis Intervention Training (CIT) for police. CIT teaches officers how to spot people in drug crisis, talk calmly, and connect them to treatment instead of jail. West Virginia and other states see big drops in fatal overdoses. Experts say “warm handoffs” to treatment and keeping people out of prison boost recovery and save lives.
