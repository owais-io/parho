US energy shares climbed after Trump said he’d help restart Venezuela’s oil output. Chevron, Exxon and Halliburton all gained, while Brent and WTI crude rose. The move could bring Venezuela’s huge reserves back into the market, but experts warn it will take years and billions to rebuild the damaged fields. Bonds in Venezuela have ticked up as traders hope for a new government. Oil‑related metals and Asian stocks also edged higher.
