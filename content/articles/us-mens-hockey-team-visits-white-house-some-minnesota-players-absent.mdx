After beating Canada for gold, the US men’s Olympic hockey team was welcomed to the White House by President Trump. Five players—Brock Nelson, Jackson LaCombe, Jake Guentzel, Jake Oettinger and Kyle Connor—did not attend. Most of those absent are from Minnesota or were busy with the NHL. The visit follows the team’s first gold medal since 1980, while the women’s team declined the invitation.
