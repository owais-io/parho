Donald Trump has announced plans for a huge 250‑foot arch, called the Independence Arch, that would dwarf the Lincoln Memorial and the Arc de Triomphe. He wants to build it on a small plot near Memorial Bridge in Washington, D.C. Architects and critics worry about the arch’s size and its impact on historic sightlines. Trump said the height matches the 250th anniversary of the U.S. Declaration of Independence.
