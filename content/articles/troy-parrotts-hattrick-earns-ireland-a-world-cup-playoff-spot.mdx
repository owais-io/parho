In a dramatic match at Budapest’s Puskás Aréna, Ireland beat Hungary 3-2, thanks to Troy Parrott’s hat‑trick, including a winner in injury time. The win gives Ireland a place in the World Cup playoff, while Hungary misses out. Parrott’s goals came after an earlier penalty and a late equaliser, turning a tense game into a historic Irish victory. The playoff will decide if Ireland reaches the 2026 World Cup.
