Trump’s decision to attack Iran has shown that the US can’t dominate the world. His military moves in the Strait of Hormuz have not stopped Iran’s navy or hurt shipping. He also pushed tariffs that hurt American consumers and gave China a chance to take control of rare‑earth minerals. China’s response and the lack of NATO support reveal that the US is not as powerful as Trump thinks.
