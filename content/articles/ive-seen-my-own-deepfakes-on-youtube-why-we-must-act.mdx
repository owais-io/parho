I recently discovered that deepfake videos of me are being shared on YouTube and social media. Even after trying to get big tech companies to remove them, the fake clips keep reappearing. I explain how this shows how tech giants control what we see and make us question who is speaking. I hope that this chaos will push us to focus on ideas, not identities, but real change needs political action.
