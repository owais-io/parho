Scottish Labour wants UK Labour leader to stay out of the Holyrood campaign so voters focus on local issues instead of Westminster failures. Leader Anas Sarwar says Starmer should stay in London, fix policy, and not campaign in Scotland. He says the party’s blunders have left voters angry and risk turning Holyrood into a protest vote against the UK government. Scottish Labour plans to highlight NHS waits, wages, and housing, while warning that Reform UK could siphon support.
