Elbridge Colby, the Pentagon’s policy chief, told NATO defence ministers in Brussels that Europe must step up its combat forces and take the lead against Russia. He said the U.S. will pull back conventional troops but will keep a nuclear deterrent. Colby urged European allies to focus on real fighting capability instead of paperwork and to make tough choices about equipment and readiness. He emphasized that the U.S. will still back NATO’s collective defence.
