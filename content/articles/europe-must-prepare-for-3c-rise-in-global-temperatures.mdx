A top climate scientist says Europe needs to act now to handle a possible 3°C rise in temperatures by 2100. The EU advisory board says current plans are too slow and weak. Heat waves, heavy rains and wildfires are already killing many people. The report urges stronger early‑warning systems, climate‑risk reviews and more money from governments and businesses. Europe can’t afford to wait.
