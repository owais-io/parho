Charlotte Bankes finished ninth again in the women's snowboard cross at the Beijing Winter Olympics, the same result she had in 2022. She said she was disappointed and felt she let her fans down. Two of the women who beat her went on to win gold and silver. Bankes said she struggled with the track and made a few mistakes. She hopes her mixed‑team partner can help her bounce back.
