Thousands of Iranians took to the streets on Friday, joining a wave of protests that began last week. The Supreme Leader warned that the government would not give in and said the crackdown would intensify. The state shut down the internet, cutting off information flow. More than 50 people have died and over 2,000 have been detained. Western leaders have called for restraint, while the protests have spread to major cities across Iran.
