As Europe ages, fewer young people mean fewer people paying into pensions that help retirees. A new poll in six European countries shows people know pensions will get too expensive but don't want to change the system. Politicians face a tough choice: keep promises to voters while finding a way to make pensions affordable. The Guardian's Europe correspondent explores how governments might tackle this ticking time bomb.
