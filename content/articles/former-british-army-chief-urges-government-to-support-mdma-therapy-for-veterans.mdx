Sir Nick Carter, a former top British army officer, is asking the government to loosen rules on the drug MDMA so it can be tested more cheaply for veterans with PTSD. Trials have shown it helped 71 % of participants. Carter says the cost of legal MDMA is huge—about £10,000 a gram—driving up trial expenses. He hopes the research could help veterans, first‑responders and the NHS.
