Alexander Isak was badly hurt in a heavy tackle by Micky van de Ven while Liverpool beat Tottenham. The injury included a broken fibula and ankle damage, so he had surgery and will miss several months. The club hasn't set a recovery time, but his absence hurts Liverpool because they already lost key players like Salah and Gakpo. This follows other squad injuries, adding pressure on coach Arne Slot.
