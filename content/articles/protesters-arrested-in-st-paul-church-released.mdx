Three activists who protested against ICE inside a St. Paul church were arrested and later freed by a federal judge who said the government failed to show detention was needed. The judge also released a third protester, William Kelly. The White House had posted a doctored photo of one protester crying, which the Guardian uncovered. The activists released a real video of the arrest, accusing the administration of using AI to distort their image and spread propaganda.
