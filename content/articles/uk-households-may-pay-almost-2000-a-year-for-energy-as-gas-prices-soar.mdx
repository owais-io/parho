The Iran war has sent UK gas prices up, pushing the government‑set energy price cap to about £1,972 a year for most households. This jump could raise bills by more than £330 from July. Petrol and diesel are also higher, and mortgage rates are climbing, adding to household costs. Energy experts suggest measures like working from home and lower speed limits to save fuel.
