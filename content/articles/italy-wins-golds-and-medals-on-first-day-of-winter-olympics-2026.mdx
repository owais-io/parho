Italy’s athletes started the Winter Olympics in Milan and Cortina with big wins. Francesca Lollobrigida broke the track record to win gold in the 3,000‑metre speed‑skating race. Alpine skiers Giovanni Franzoni and Dominik Paris added silver and bronze in downhill, while Swedish skiers out‑paced Norway in the women’s cross‑country skiathlon, taking gold and silver. The day also saw a handful of other medals and a brief health scare about norovirus.
