In January 2023, a 69‑year‑old Australian named Wayne Harvey died in a Bali hospital after a failed appendix operation. His son, Jake, had to remove tubes from the body and witnessed unhygienic conditions. He repeatedly asked the Australian consulate for help transferring his dad to a better facility, but the consulate ignored his pleas. The delay cost his dad a chance at better care. Years later, the ministry apologized, but Jake feels the consulate failed him.
