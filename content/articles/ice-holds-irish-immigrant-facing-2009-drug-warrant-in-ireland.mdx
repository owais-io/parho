Seamus Culleton, an Irishman, has spent five months in US ICE custody for overstaying a tourist visa, despite holding a valid work permit. A recent revelation shows an Irish court issued a warrant in 2009 for drug possession and alleged police obstruction. Culleton denies any crime, calls himself a “model immigrant,” and has fought deportation, even comparing detention conditions to a concentration camp. The case has drawn criticism from Irish politicians and raised concerns about ICE sweeps.
