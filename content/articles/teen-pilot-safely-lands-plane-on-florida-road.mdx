Niko Bray, a 19‑year‑old pilot, had to land his Cessna 150 on a busy Florida road when the engine failed. He chose a spot on Indiantown Road near a Home Depot, making the emergency landing in under two minutes. Nobody was hurt, thanks in part to a truck driver who slowed traffic. The incident is under FAA review, but Bray says it reminds him to cherish family and stay prepared.
