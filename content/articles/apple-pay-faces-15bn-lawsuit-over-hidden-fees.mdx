The UK consumer advocate James Daley has launched a £1.5bn class action against Apple, claiming the company’s Apple Pay service has charged hidden fees that raise bank costs and, in turn, consumer bills. Daley says Apple blocked other app developers from using its contactless tech, giving it a monopoly that harmed 50 million UK customers. Apple rejects the claim, saying it charges no fees and improves security. The case is now before the Competition Appeal Tribunal.
