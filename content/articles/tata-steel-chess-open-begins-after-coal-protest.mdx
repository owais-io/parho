At the famous Wijk aan Zee chess event, organizers were interrupted when protestors dumped two tonnes of coal in front of the hall and hung a banner that said “No chess on a dead planet.” The opening round started 90 minutes late. While top stars Carlsen, Nakamura and Caruana were missing, players like Hans Niemann, Vincent Keymer and Gukesh Dommaraju took the spotlight. Niemann made a quick 15‑move win, and other exciting games and blunders kept fans hooked.
