New South Wales is set to pass emergency laws that will cap gun ownership at four weapons per person and require licences to be renewed every two years. The bill also lets police ban protests for up to three months after a terrorist attack and stop face coverings. The move splits the ruling coalition: Nationals fear it hurts farmers, while Greens and civil‑rights groups say the protest rules are too harsh. Parliament plans to vote next week.
