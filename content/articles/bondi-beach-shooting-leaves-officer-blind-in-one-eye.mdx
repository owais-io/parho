A 22‑year‑old police officer, Jack Hibbert, was shot twice at a Hanukah celebration on Bondi Beach. He survived but now cannot see in one eye and faces a long recovery. The attack left two officers injured and raised questions about how many police were on duty—about 20 were reported. Leaders called for better security and a review of police weapons. Officials say the officers showed bravery but more must be done.
