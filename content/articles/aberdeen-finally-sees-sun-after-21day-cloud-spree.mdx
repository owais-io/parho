After three weeks of rain, Aberdeen in Scotland finally saw sunshine for the first time in 21 days. Residents spotted a brief burst of sun on Thursday, ending the longest dry spell since records began in 1957. The city had rained heavily, with 277 mm in Aboyne, and snow covered parts of the north‑east in early January. Weather forecasts say more cold air and a yellow snow warning will keep the weather chilly for a few days.
