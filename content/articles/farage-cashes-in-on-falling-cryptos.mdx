Nigel Farage made paid Cameo videos endorsing several memecoins, such as Stonks Finance and Trump Mania, for just £72‑£133 each. The Guardian found he sold over £1,000 for these clips, but all the coins he mentioned have since lost value. Farage, who says crypto frees people from banks, has a history of controversial public statements and has linked his party to crypto donations. His videos show a mix of hype and financial risk.
