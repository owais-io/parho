Sydney Mardi Gras organisers banned the activist group Pride in Protest after the group posted on social media calling the Jewish LGBTQ+ group Dayenu “pro‑genocide.” The posts broke the parade’s rules against harassment and hate speech, and Pride in Protest didn’t reply in the time allowed. The group says it was censored, while a Greens MP slammed the move. Dayenu said it was hurt and wants to talk.
