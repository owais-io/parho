Brisbane’s sports teams had a big year in 2025. The NRL’s Broncos won the grand final against Melbourne, thanks to star full‑back Reece Walsh. In cricket, Travis Head scored a huge century to beat England in Perth, creating a new series‑winning moment. The AFL’s Lions beat Geelong to win their first premiership since 2001, and local sprinter Gout broke records and headed to the World Championships. These wins made Brisbane the hub of Australian sport.
