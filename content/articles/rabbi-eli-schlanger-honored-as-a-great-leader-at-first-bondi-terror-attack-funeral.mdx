Rabbi Eli Schlanger, 41, was mourned in a Chabad synagogue after he was killed in the Bondi terror attack. He was a long‑time assistant rabbi and key organiser of the Chanukah by the Sea event. The funeral drew members of the Jewish community and senior politicians, including NSW Premier Chris Minns and former PM Scott Morrison. The service featured prayers in Hebrew and English, tearful speeches, and a candle‑lighting vigil to show resilience.
