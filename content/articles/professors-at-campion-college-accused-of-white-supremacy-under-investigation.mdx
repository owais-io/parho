Campion College in Sydney is under scrutiny after two professors, Stephen McInerney and Stephen Chavura, made public comments supporting a ‘White Australia’ policy and calling for Anglo‑Celtic Australians to become the majority. Education minister Jason Clare said the regulator Teqsa is investigating the college for potential breaches of anti‑racism rules. The college says the remarks were personal and protects academic freedom, but critics say racism has no place in higher education.
