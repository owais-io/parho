Geoffrey Shaw, a respected Aboriginal leader from Alice Springs and Vietnam‑era veteran, passed away at 79. He served in the Australian Army, fighting in Indonesia‑Malaysia and Vietnam, before returning home to help his community. Shaw helped start the Central Land Council and was a key voice for Aboriginal land rights. He also led the Tangentyere Council and received an Order of Australia for his service. He is remembered as a strong advocate for his people.
