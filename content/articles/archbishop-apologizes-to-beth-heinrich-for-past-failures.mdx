Beth Heinrich, who was abused by a church priest when she was 14, has spent decades seeking justice and an apology from the Anglican church. In March, Archbishop Jeremy Greaves will publicly apologize for former Archbishop Peter Hollingworth’s failures to act on the abuse. Heinrich hopes the apology will give survivors courage and help the church acknowledge its past mistakes.
