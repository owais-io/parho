More than 70 Australian schools closed after the ACCC recalled children’s sand that may contain asbestos. Products from China were found with asbestos fibres, but labs say the risk of inhaling harmful dust is very low. Parents should keep sand away from kids and follow safety tips while schools clean. Most schools in the ACT and Tasmania reopened, and authorities are reviewing customs checks.
