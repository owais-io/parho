900 people signed an open letter to the owners of internationalwomensday.com, demanding the site stop profiting from a social movement without tackling real women’s rights issues. The site, run by a marketing firm, has been mistakenly linked to the UN’s International Women’s Day and uses corporate themes like “Give to Gain.” British companies have adopted these themes, causing confusion. The owners say the site is one of many groups marking the day. The letter calls for transparency and real action.
