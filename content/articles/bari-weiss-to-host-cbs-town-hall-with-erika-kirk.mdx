Bari Weiss, the new editor‑in‑chief of CBS News, will run a town hall on Dec. 13 featuring Erika Kirk, widow of conservative activist Charlie Kirk. The talk will cover grief, faith and politics. It’s part of Weiss’s plan to bring more balanced voices to the network. The event isn’t public yet, but viewers can sign up online. Kirk has become a prominent figure in conservative media in recent months.
