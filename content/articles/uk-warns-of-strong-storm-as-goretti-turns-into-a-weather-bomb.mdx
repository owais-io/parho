Storm Goretti has turned into a “weather bomb,” triggering red and amber warnings across the UK. It could bring up to 30 cm of snow to Wales and the Midlands and 100 mph winds to Cornwall and the Isles of Scilly. The storm has already knocked out power for more than 25,000 homes and will disrupt roads, trains and flights, especially in the south‑west. Drivers should avoid highways, and people with health conditions are urged to stay indoors.
