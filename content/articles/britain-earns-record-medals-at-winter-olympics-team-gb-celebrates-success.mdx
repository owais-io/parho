Team GB had their best Winter Olympics yet, winning five medals – including three golds – and finishing 15th in the medal table. Zoe Atkin earned a bronze in the women's halfpipe, while Matt Weston won two golds in skeleton. The squad also took gold in mixed snowboard cross and silver in curling. The UK praised the performances and said the “Brits are coming again,” hinting at more future wins.
