A sudden cold wave hit the eastern US this week, breaking many low‑temperature records. In the deep south, 80 weather stations tied or set new minimums, and Jacksonville, Florida, saw a low of –3 °C. The cold also brought heavy snow to Illinois, Indiana and Wisconsin. The cold front left the US by Tuesday. Meanwhile, Typhoon Fung‑Wong moved north, hit Taiwan as a tropical storm, dumped 648 mm of rain in Suao, flooding homes, displacing 8,300 people and injuring 51.
