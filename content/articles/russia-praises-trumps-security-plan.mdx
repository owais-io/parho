Russia’s top spokesman said Trump’s new security plan matches Russia’s thinking and welcomes better US‑Russia talks. He also warned that a US “deep state” might try to block Trump’s ideas. Meanwhile, the US is pushing a peace deal for Ukraine that could let Kyiv give up some land. Ukraine’s president talks with US officials, but both sides doubt they can give the guarantees Ukraine needs to stay safe. The war’s winter is still a huge challenge.
