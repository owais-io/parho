Football’s governing body wants to seed the four richest Women’s Super League clubs so they can’t meet each other before the final. Fans and clubs say it will widen the money gap and kill the magic of random draws. The FA says the move will boost TV money and help reinvest in women’s football. No decision has been made yet, but a consultation is underway.
