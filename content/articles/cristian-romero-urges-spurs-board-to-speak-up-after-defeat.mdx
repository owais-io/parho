After a 3‑2 loss to Bournemouth, Tottenham captain Cristian Romero posted an Instagram message urging the board to speak up. He said the hierarchy only comments when things are good and that fans are supporting the team. The post was edited to remove a “lies” remark, but Romero still called for leadership to be more vocal. Tottenham have 5 points from their last 6 games, and coach Thomas Frank is facing pressure. The club has new leaders since the former chair left.
