SpaceX is looking at a merger with Tesla or a partnership with AI company xAI before it could go public. Elon Musk wants to float SpaceX at about $1.5 trillion, raising up to $50 billion. The idea is to use SpaceX rockets and Tesla’s batteries for space data centres that would power AI models. Tesla is also moving away from cars toward robots and AI, which could fit with the plan.
