After a sharp drop in the Iranian currency and years of economic hardship, thousands of Iranians, including a 19‑year‑old student named Mehnaz, have taken to the streets. The protests began with merchants closing shops and spread to 32 cities, demanding better living conditions and the removal of the regime. Police and security forces have fired on crowds, killing at least ten people. The government says it will listen, but activists say the regime will not change unless it is forced to.
