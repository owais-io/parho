Starbucks workers in more than 40 cities are on strike, hoping for a better contract. The union, Starbucks Workers United, wants higher pay and better conditions. The strike started on a holiday sales day and could grow if talks stall. Starbucks says most stores are open and is disappointed the strike happened. Politicians and city leaders are calling for a deal.
