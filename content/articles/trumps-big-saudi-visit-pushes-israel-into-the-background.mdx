Trump hosted the Saudi crown prince with a huge, flashy ceremony that showed how much he values Saudi ties. He bragged about selling the same top‑of‑the‑line fighter jets and AI chips to Saudi Arabia as to Israel, a move Israel dislikes. Trump ignored the Khashoggi murder and other human‑rights issues. The visit also highlighted a shift in U.S. policy, putting Saudi interests above Israel’s, but the change is still limited.
