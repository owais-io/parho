A coyote crossed about 1¼ miles of San Francisco Bay to reach Alcatraz Island, the first animal known to swim there. Video footage, shared by a tourist and a ferry captain, shows the tired animal paddling toward the former prison‑turned‑museum. Rangers looked for it but didn’t find it, and its fate is unknown. Experts think the coyote may have been searching for new territory because of crowded populations.
