West Midlands Police chief Craig Guildford is staying on even after Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood demanded he step down because a report found the force’s intelligence on Maccabi Tel Aviv fans was exaggerated and wrong. The report says the ban was based on faulty data, not antisemitism, and the chief has said he’ll wait for a formal dismissal decision. Meanwhile, politicians and the mayor want him gone and are calling for stricter rules on police dismissals.
