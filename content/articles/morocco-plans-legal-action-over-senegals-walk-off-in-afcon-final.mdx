Morocco says it will sue the African football confederation and FIFA after Senegal walked off the pitch during the 2025 AFCON final. The walk‑off followed a penalty awarded to Morocco, which Senegal players protested. The incident caused a 15‑minute delay and may have affected the outcome, as Senegal won 1‑0. Morocco’s federation claims the delay hurt their chances and will seek a ruling on the event.
