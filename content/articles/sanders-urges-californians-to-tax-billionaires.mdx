Bernie Sanders gave a fiery speech in Los Angeles, saying California should tax its richest residents. He slammed billionaire greed and said the tax would raise money for health care, schools and food programs. The union wants the bill on the November ballot, but the governor and tech billionaires like Sergey Brin are fighting it. It could bring tens of billions but may also make some rich Californians leave.
