North London has seen a measles outbreak linked to falling vaccination rates. A family shares the story of their granddaughter who, after a respiratory infection, developed severe measles and pneumonia, requiring readmission. Measles is highly contagious and can weaken the immune system, putting unvaccinated babies at risk. The letter urges councils to listen to parents, fight misinformation, and make vaccines easier to get so vulnerable children stay protected.
