Storm that came from the Pacific has dumped more than 11 inches of rain across Los Angeles County, flooding streets and cutting off highways. A 64‑year‑old man was killed when a huge tree fell on him in San Diego, and another woman was swept away in a creek. Authorities declared emergencies in several counties, ordered evacuations in flood‑prone areas, and are working to restore power and clear debris.
