In a New Year’s Eve address, China’s president Xi Jinping said China would bring Taiwan back into the country, calling reunification “unstoppable.” The speech followed big military drills around Taiwan, raising worries in the U.S. about China’s growing power. Xi also talked about China’s tech progress, cultural hits like a popular game, and its 5% growth goal, while highlighting recent global meetings and the historic war themes.
