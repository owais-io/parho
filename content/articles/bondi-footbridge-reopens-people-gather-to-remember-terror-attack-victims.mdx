On Thursday, the footbridge that was the site of the Bondi terror attack was reopened. People came to stand, pray, and leave flowers. A friend of the slain Rabbi sang a prayer, while others hugged and listened quietly. Police marked the spots where bullets struck, and a big memorial grew outside the pavilion for the 15 victims. The community is holding events to remember and support each other.
