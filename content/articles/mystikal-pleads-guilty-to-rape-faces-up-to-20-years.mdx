Mystikal, the 55‑year‑old rapper, pleaded guilty to a third‑degree rape charge that stemmed from a 2022 arrest. By admitting guilt, he avoided the harsher first‑degree rape and other charges that could have led to life in prison. The plea deal cuts the maximum sentence to 20 years. Mystikal’s legal troubles have deepened after earlier convictions for assault, robbery and domestic abuse, but he has yet to be sentenced.
