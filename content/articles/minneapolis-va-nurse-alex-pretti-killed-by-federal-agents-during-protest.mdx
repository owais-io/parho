Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old ICU nurse at the Minneapolis VA, was shot and killed by federal agents on Saturday while he was holding his phone and an empty hand, attempting to help a legal observer. His family says he was unarmed and peacefully trying to protect others. The incident follows a month of federal‑agent crackdowns in Minnesota, marking the city’s second fatal shooting.
