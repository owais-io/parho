The Guardian editorial says Labour’s former PM Gordon Brown told the cabinet secretary to investigate Peter Mandelson’s links to Jeffrey Epstein, noting that Mandelson received big payments from the financier and leaked secret banking‑crisis info to him. It criticises Keir Starmer for only calling for Mandelson’s peerage to be stripped, not for his removal or new law. The piece argues the scandal shows how Labour’s business‑friendly politics can let money corrupt public office.
