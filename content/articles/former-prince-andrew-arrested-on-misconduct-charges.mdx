Former Prince Andrew, now known as Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor, was taken into custody on 19 February for suspected misconduct in public office. Police say he may have shared sensitive information with billionaire Jeffrey Epstein while serving as a UK trade envoy. Officers searched addresses in Norfolk and Berkshire. Investigators are also looking into claims that a woman was trafficked to the UK by Epstein for a sexual encounter with Andrew.
