A 25‑year‑old woman named Alex Davies died in a segregation cell at Styal women's prison on Christmas Eve last year. Doctors had said she needed a hospital transfer, but she was kept in a solitary unit for 27 days and not watched closely. She tried to kill herself. An inquest found that neglect caused her death, and her family is demanding accountability. The prison says it will review its care of inmates with mental health problems.
