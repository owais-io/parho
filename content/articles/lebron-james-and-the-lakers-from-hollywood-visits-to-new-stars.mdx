LeBron James' move to the Los Angeles Lakers is explored in Yaron Weitzman’s book, which mixes Hollywood moments—like a visit from Will Smith—with the team’s ups and downs. It covers how LeBron saved the Lakers’ legacy, the 2019‑20 championship, the rough 2021‑22 season with Westbrook, and recent shake‑ups such as the Dončić trade and picking his son Bronny in the draft. The book also follows the Buss family’s sale of the franchise and LeBron’s record‑breaking career.
