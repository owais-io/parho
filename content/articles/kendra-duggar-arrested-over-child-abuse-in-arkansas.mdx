Kendra Duggar, wife of former reality‑TV star Joseph Duggar, was arrested in Arkansas on misdemeanor child‑abuse charges. Police say she faced four counts of endangering a minor and false imprisonment. Her court hearing is next Monday. The arrest comes a few days after her husband was taken into custody in Florida for allegedly molesting a child under 12. The two cases are unrelated, but the Duggar family has a history of scandals tied to their TV show.
