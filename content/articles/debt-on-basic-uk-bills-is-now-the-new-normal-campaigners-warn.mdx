StepChange says many low‑income families now live with growing debt on everyday bills. Rent and mortgage arrears jumped 15% and 22% last year, while energy debt still tops a third of clients. Even with lower energy prices, the average energy bill owed is rising. Two in five on universal credit, three in five renters. The charity urges the government to set affordable national tariffs to stop more people falling into debt.
