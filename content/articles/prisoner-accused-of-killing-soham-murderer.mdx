A 43‑year‑old inmate, Anthony Russell, is charged with killing former school caretaker Ian Huntley, the man who murdered two 10‑year‑old girls in 2002. The crime took place in HMP Frankland, County Durham. Huntley was attacked and later died after suffering brain injuries. The Crown Prosecution Service says evidence is strong enough for trial, and Russell will be heard in court by video.
