Volkswagen plans to cut 50,000 jobs by the end of the decade because sales in China and the U.S. are dropping and U.S. tariffs have hurt profits. The cuts will affect all 10 brands, including Porsche and Audi, and will happen in Germany. The company also cut 35,000 jobs in 2024. Lower EV targets and a shaky global economy are driving the move.
