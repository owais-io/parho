Talks between the US and Iran about Tehran’s nuclear program ended without a deal. The US is readying a big military build‑up in the Middle East, while Iran says it will keep some enrichment rights. Both sides keep saying the talks went well, but key issues like how much uranium can be enriched and the future of Iran’s highly enriched stockpile remain unresolved. A new round is expected soon.
