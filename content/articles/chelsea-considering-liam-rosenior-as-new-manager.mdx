Chelsea are close to hiring former Hull City coach Liam Rosenior as their new manager after Enzo Maresca left. Rosenior flew to London with his Strasbourg club's staff for talks. The club’s owners, who also run Strasbourg, need to balance both clubs' interests. The board likes him, and they hope he will take charge before Chelsea’s next game. Other candidates include Porto’s Francesco Farioli and former Flamengo coach Filipe Luís.
