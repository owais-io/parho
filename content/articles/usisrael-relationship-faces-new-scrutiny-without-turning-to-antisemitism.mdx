The recent joint US‑Israel strikes on Iran have made people question the relationship. US decisions were shaped by Israel’s moves, but only 21 % of Americans backed the strikes. Critics say we need to watch how the two countries work together. At the same time, attacks on Jewish people have risen, making it hard to separate criticism of Israel from hateful ideas. We must ask honest questions while fighting antisemitism.
