Police in Independence, Missouri, found a dangerous powder inside the packaging of five Barbie dolls sold at the Cargo Largo discount store. Testing confirmed the powder was fentanyl, a powerful synthetic opioid that can kill in tiny amounts. The five dolls were sold between March 19‑20, and all packages were recovered the same day. No injuries were reported, and authorities said the dolls themselves were not contaminated.
