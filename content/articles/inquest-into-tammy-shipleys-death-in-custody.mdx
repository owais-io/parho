Tammy Shipley, a 47‑year‑old mother, died in the Silverwater women’s correctional centre after being arrested for shoplifting. An autopsy said her death was due to hyponatraemia from low sodium, linked to her thirst and schizoaffective disorder. Her family said her mental‑health needs were poorly managed and that police failed to inform staff. A coroner’s inquest is still ongoing, questioning how she was treated in custody.
