Wolves held Manchester United to a 2‑2 draw in the Premier League, taking a point after the Red Devils missed eight front‑line players. The match saw Wolves win 2‑0 before United fought back to level it. Wolves’ goals came from Czech defender Ladislav Krejci and a late header from Joshua Zirkzee, while United’s equalisers came from Zirkzee and an own‑goal by a defender. United’s lack of cohesion and fluidity was clear.
