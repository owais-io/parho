On Saturday, the United States and Israel launched a large air campaign against Iran, aiming to weaken the Iranian regime. Iran quickly responded with strikes on U.S. bases and other targets across the Middle East. The attack caused explosions in Israel, Bahrain, Syria, Iraq, the UAE, Kuwait, and Saudi Arabia. The move has raised fears of a wider war, drawn in European leaders, and prompted the UN to call for calm.
