The Brisbane Broncos traveled to England for the World Club Challenge against Hull Kingston Rovers, but they lost 28‑20. The match was a tough test for a team already feeling the shock of Payne Haas’s sudden move to South Sydney. The Broncos had a shaky start with injuries and mistakes, but they fought back to stay in the game. Despite a strong second half, Hull kept the lead. The result shows how Haas’s switch is already hurting the Broncos’ confidence.
