Bristol council plans to offer temporary sites for people living in vans. It wants 250 spots by spring, spread across the city, including affluent neighborhoods. The aim is fairness and help van dwellers avoid being pushed to poorer areas. Sites will have water, toilets, rubbish, and outreach. About 600 people live in vans, many forced by high housing costs. Some already moved, enforcement expected next year.
