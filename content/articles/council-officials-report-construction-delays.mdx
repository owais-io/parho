Nearly two‑thirds of senior council officers say construction projects are running late. Around 40 % feel their authority can’t deliver, blaming money problems, new government reforms and a lack of skilled workers. Delays hit housing, regeneration and transport. Almost all want clearer, longer‑term funding. Labour promised big building plans and planning changes, but councils say policy uncertainty and rising costs keep projects stuck.
