Australian economists warn that if the country keeps warming, younger workers will pay up to $185,000 over their lives. Millennials would lose about $130,000, Gen Z about $165,000, and Gen Alpha up to $185,000 by 2070. Acting now could save each group $50–80 k. The study urges a carbon price and green‑job investment to protect future Australians.
