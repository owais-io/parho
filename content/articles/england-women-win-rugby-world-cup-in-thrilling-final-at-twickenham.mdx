England’s women’s rugby team, the Red Roses, won the 2025 World Cup at Twickenham in a sold‑out stadium of 81,885 fans. Canada scored first, but England took control and stayed on top even after a yellow card to Hannah Botterman. The final was loud and emotional, with fans cheering and young players dreaming of a trophy. The win is expected to boost the sport’s popularity and inspire the next generation.
