Lyft and Uber are teaming up with Chinese tech company Baidu to bring self‑driving cars to London next year. Tests with Baidu’s Apollo Go robotaxis are planned for 2026, with dozens of cars on the streets. The move has raised security worries because the cars could be used for spying or be shut down in an emergency. London is eager to be a global leader in driver‑less transport.
