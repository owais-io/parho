Britain is looking at using drones that can clear mines to reopen the Hormuz Strait, a key oil route that has been shut after Iran said it would target ships. The government says sending actual ships, as the U.S. president wants, could make the conflict worse. Instead, drones could safely trigger mines from a distance. The move comes as oil prices rise and the UK faces criticism for low defense spending.
