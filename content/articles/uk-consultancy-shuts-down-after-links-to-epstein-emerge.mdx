Global Counsel, a London‑based advisory firm founded by former politician Peter Mandelson, has gone into administration after several clients cut ties because of his connection to convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. The company, with about 100 staff worldwide, was forced to stop trading. Administrators at Interpath are reviewing assets and supporting UK employees, while co‑founder Benjamin Wegg‑Prosser has resigned.
