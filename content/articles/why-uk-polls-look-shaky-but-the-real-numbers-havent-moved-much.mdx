UK polls look shaky, but the real numbers haven’t moved much. Since last year’s local elections, Labour, Conservatives, Reform, Lib Dems and Greens have hovered around the same support levels, with only a few percentage‑point swings. A small change can shift a seat because the country is now a five‑party race. Voters are switching within the left and right groups, but the overall balance stays stable.
