Peter Mandelson stepped down as a member of the House of Lords after a batch of emails he sent to financier Jeffrey Epstein was revealed. The messages contained confidential UK government documents from his time as Business Secretary. Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the leaks show Mandelson betrayed the country, and he is looking into removing the peerage quickly. Police are investigating the emails and possible further scandals.
