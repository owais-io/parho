Former President Trump says the Ukraine war is very unfair—not just for soldiers who died, but also for Americans paying the cost. The White House notes he halted U.S. aid to Ukraine last year to push for peace. New talks in Geneva ended without a deal, leaving Ukraine’s president disappointed. While talks resume, both sides still argue over key issues, and the U.S. keeps working toward a lasting settlement.
