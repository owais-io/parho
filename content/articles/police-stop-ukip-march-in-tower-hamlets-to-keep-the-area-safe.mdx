The Metropolitan Police have barred a UKIP protest from marching through Tower Hamlets because they fear it could spark serious violence. The group could still protest elsewhere, but police say the local community’s past experiences with hate crime and unrest make the route too risky. Officials warned that anyone ignoring the order could be arrested. The move follows similar decisions in Birmingham and other parts of London.
