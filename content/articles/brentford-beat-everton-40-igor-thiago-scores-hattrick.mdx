Brentford routed Everton 4‑0 at Hill Dickinson Stadium, with Brazilian striker Igor Thiago scoring a hat‑trick. The win is a bright spot for the club’s poor away record this season, while Everton’s defence collapsed after losing key players. The Brazilians were clinical, calmly finishing each chance, and the hosts were praised for their structure. Everton’s manager, David Moyes, said his side needed to tighten up. Moyes said the team had conceded four goals in a shabby manner and should improve.
