Edward Harris, chair of Warwickshire county council, stepped down after Tamworth borough council discovered his two unlicensed rental houses were unsafe. The properties had no heating, hot water, fire alarms or proper cooking areas, putting tenants at risk. Tamworth called him a rogue landlord and issued emergency bans. The case damages Reform UK’s image as the party pushes to crack down on poorly managed HMOs. Harris apologized and promised to fix the homes.
