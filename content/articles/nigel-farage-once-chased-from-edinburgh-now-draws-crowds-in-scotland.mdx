Nigel Farage, who was once forced out of Edinburgh by angry protesters, is back in Scotland holding a sold‑out Reform UK rally in Falkirk. His support has grown as people react to the country’s asylum‑seeker crisis. While anti‑immigration chants echo, the SNP still looks set to win the Scottish election, but Reform UK could come second. The story shows how Scotland’s politics are now split over migration, not just independence.
