24 former volunteers from a search‑and‑rescue group are going to court on Lesbos to face charges that they helped migrants illegally enter Greece. The trial, starting after seven years of delays, will see human rights activists like Sarah Mardini testify. Amnesty International says the case threatens humanitarian aid. The defendants hope the court will see that saving lives is not a crime.
