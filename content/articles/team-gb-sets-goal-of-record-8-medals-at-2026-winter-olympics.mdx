Team GB hopes to win up to eight medals at the 2026 Winter Olympics in Italy, aiming for a record haul. This would beat their best finish of five medals in Sochi 2014 and Pyeongchang 2018. British officials cite world‑champion athletes like Mia Brookes, Zoe Atkin, and Kirsty Muir, and strong men’s teams in skeleton and curling, as reasons to be optimistic. They’re also preparing for a possible first figure‑skating medal since 1994.
