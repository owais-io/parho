Syngenta, a big crop‑chemicals maker, said it will stop producing the weed killer paraquat by the end of June. The decision comes after thousands of U.S. lawsuits claim the chemical causes Parkinson’s disease. Although the company says paraquat is safe when used properly, scientists have linked it to brain damage. The move is seen as a win for health activists who have pushed for a ban.
