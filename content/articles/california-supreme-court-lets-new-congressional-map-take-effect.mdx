California voters just approved a new congressional map that the state’s Supreme Court has now allowed to be used for the upcoming November elections. The decision blocks a Republican request to keep the old map in place. The move follows a similar ruling in Texas, where Trump’s push for new districts was also rejected. The court said the changes are aimed at countering partisan advantage.
