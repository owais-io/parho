Great British Railways (GBR) announced a new logo and branding on Tuesday, signalling the start of a nationalised rail system in Britain. The fresh design features the GBR name and a double‑arrow symbol, and will be painted on trains from spring 2025. The move comes with a bill to bring seven private operators back into public ownership, freeze fares in England, and create a single ticket‑app. Ministers said the changes will focus on better service and passenger safety.
