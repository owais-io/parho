Former CIA analyst David McCloskey says the U.S. helped Israel gather intel that led to the killing of Iran's top leader, Ayatollah Khamenei. He was surprised to learn CIA information fed into Israel’s targeting system, which uses CCTV, data and human spies. McCloskey explains Israel has deeper reach in Iran thanks to cultural ties, and he writes a novel about a Mossad agent trapped in Tehran.
