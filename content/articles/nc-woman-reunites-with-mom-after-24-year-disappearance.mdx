A woman from North Carolina has reunited with her mother, who vanished in 2001 and was found alive after 24 years. Amanda Smith, who was 14 when her mom disappeared, met her outside a courthouse and said she forgives her. The search involved many state and federal agencies, and investigators found her in another town after a tip. No criminal charges were filed. The family celebrated a new beginning.
