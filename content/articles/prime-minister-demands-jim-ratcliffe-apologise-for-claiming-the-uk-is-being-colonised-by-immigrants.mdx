Labour leader Keir Starmer has called on Manchester United owner Jim Ratcliffe to apologise after he said the UK is being “colonised” by immigrants. Ratcliffe’s numbers on population and benefits were shown to be wrong by the Office for National Statistics. The prime minister and Downing Street urged an apology, while Culture Secretary Lisa Nandy defended Ratcliffe’s point that the immigration system needs reform.
