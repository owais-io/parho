Les Wexner, the billionaire who built a fashion empire, is tied to the Jeffrey Epstein scandal. New documents show he gave Epstein power of attorney in 1991 and sent a birthday card with a breast drawing. Wexner denies knowing about Epstein’s crimes, but he still donates huge sums to Ohio State University, where his name marks several buildings. Students and lawmakers want the school to cut ties and remove his name.
