Veteran Janessa Goldbeck, who left the Marine Corps in 2019, now leads the Vet Voice Foundation. After Trump used National Guard troops in cities to enforce immigration rules, she and the foundation have helped local leaders stop or slow these deployments, offered expert witnesses in court, and trained activists. Goldbeck warns the president is trying to politicize the military and could use it in elections. She stresses that the Guard isn’t meant for policing or fighting Trump’s policies.
