Police and the FBI are still looking for Nancy Guthrie, the 84‑year‑old mother of Today host Savannah Guthrie, who disappeared in Arizona on Sunday. A new message linked to the case has been sent to investigators, and they are checking its authenticity. The authorities believe she is still alive and are offering a $50,000 reward for any information that helps find her or catch the kidnappers. No suspects have been named yet.
