Chief Justice John Roberts warned that calling out judges personally is dangerous, especially after President Trump’s recent social media attacks on judges who ruled against his policies. Roberts said critics can point out mistakes in rulings, but name‑calling and threats harm the court’s independence. He highlighted how such hostility threatens the rule of law and the ability of judges to make fair decisions.
