Researchers found that most TV shows portray CPR incorrectly, showing people giving breaths or checking pulses, while trained adults should do compression‑only. In a review of 54 episodes, only 16 showed the correct method. The shows rarely depict home arrests—common in real life—and focus on younger, white male victims. This misinformation may keep viewers from acting quickly. Experts urge learning proper CPR; free online tools are available.
