A photo that shows Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick standing next to Jeffrey Epstein on Epstein’s private island has sparked fresh calls from both Democrats and Republicans for Lutnick to testify. Senators demand he hand over all records of meetings and phone calls with Epstein, and the House oversight committee is considering a subpoena. Lutnick, who has denied wrongdoing, said he barely knew Epstein, but his past visits raise new questions.
