Trump ordered the diversity visa lottery to stop, saying it let the shooter who killed people at Brown University and MIT enter the U.S. The suspect, Claudio Neves Valente, came on a student visa, became a permanent resident, and later died by suicide. The lottery offers 50,000 green cards a year to people from countries with few U.S. residents. Roughly 20 million applied for 2025, and Portugal won only 38 spots.
