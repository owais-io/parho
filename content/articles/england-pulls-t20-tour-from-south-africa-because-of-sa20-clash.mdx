England’s plan to play a T20 series in South Africa next January has been scrapped because the dates clash with the SA20 domestic league. The country will still do a three‑test series in December and January, but the T20 and ODI games have been removed. England and Cricket South Africa worked out a new schedule that puts ODIs in smaller venues, keeping the overall tour going.
