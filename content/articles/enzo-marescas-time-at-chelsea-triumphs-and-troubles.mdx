Enzo Maresca was hired as Chelsea manager in June 2024 and signed a five‑year deal. He started well, beating Wolves 6‑2 in August and ended the season in fourth place. Chelsea won the Conference League in May 2025 and the Club World Cup in July 2025. But the team struggled with inconsistent form, lost fans’ support, and Maresca faced criticism. By January 2026 the club and Maresca agreed to part ways after a series of bad results.
