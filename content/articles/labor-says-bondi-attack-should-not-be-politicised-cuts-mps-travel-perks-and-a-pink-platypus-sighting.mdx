Prime Minister Anthony Albanese will not set up a royal commission into the Bondi beach terror attack, urging national unity and warning it has been politicised. He also said MPs’ travel allowances for families will be cut after an expenses watchdog review. In NSW, new laws banning protests for up to three months after a terror event face a constitutional challenge, while the ABC defends its journalists’ coverage of the attack. A Victorian fisherman spotted a pink platypus.
