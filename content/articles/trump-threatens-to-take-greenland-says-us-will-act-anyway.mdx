Trump says the U.S. will act on Greenland so Russia or China can’t take it, even if the island’s people don’t want it. Danish and Greenlandic leaders strongly reject the idea. Only a tiny fraction of Americans back a U.S. invasion, while 85% of Greenlanders oppose it. Trump also warned the U.S. might hit Iran if it shoots at protestors. The president’s statements have raised tensions with NATO allies.
