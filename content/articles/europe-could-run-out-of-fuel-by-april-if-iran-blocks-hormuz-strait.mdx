Shell's chief warned Europe could face a fuel shortage as early as next month if Iran stops shipping through the Hormuz Strait. The crisis has already forced jet‑fuel prices to double and may hit diesel and petrol later. Energy rationing in Asia has already started, and oil prices have swung from $114 to $100 a barrel. A prolonged shortage could spark a global recession.
