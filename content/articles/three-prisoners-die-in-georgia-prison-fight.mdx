In Davisboro, Georgia, a fight broke out at a medium‑security state prison early Sunday. Three inmates—Ahmod Hatcher, Jimmy Trammell, and Teddy Jackson—were killed, and about 13 more were hurt and sent to the hospital. Sheriff Joel Cochran said the fighting was between inmates and that a guard might have been wounded. The Georgia Department of Corrections said staff used non‑lethal weapons to calm the situation. No visitors were hurt.
