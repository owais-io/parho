The Guardian launched its 2025 charity appeal to help local communities. The campaign funds five grassroots charities that run training, discussion groups, and mixed‑faith youth projects. It aims to build hope, tolerance and community pride, countering social division. The appeal uses fresh marketing, even turning far‑right slogans into hopeful messages. Readers can donate online, join a telethon or buy books, and the Guardian bookshop adds 20p per sale to the fund.
