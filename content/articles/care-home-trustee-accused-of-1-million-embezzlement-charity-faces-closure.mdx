Ed Davey, leader of the Liberal Democrats, said a trustee of William Blake House stole £1 million and called for the charity commission to step in. Families of the 22 residents have raised concerns after the home faced a £1.6 million tax bill and almost shut down. The Charity Commission has opened a formal investigation into financial mismanagement, and a group of families wants to run the care home themselves.
