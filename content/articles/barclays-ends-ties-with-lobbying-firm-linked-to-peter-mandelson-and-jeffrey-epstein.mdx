Barclays has cut its relationship with a lobbying company founded by former minister Peter Mandelson after new evidence showed he had close ties to the late Jeffrey Epstein. The move follows a wave of scrutiny over the firm’s dealings and a scandal that led to Mandelson’s resignation from the Labour party and his dismissal as the UK ambassador to the US. Other firms, like Vodafone, are also reviewing their links.
