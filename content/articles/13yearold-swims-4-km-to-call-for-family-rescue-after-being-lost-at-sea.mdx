A 13‑year‑old boy, Austin Appelbee, swam 4 km in rough seas after his family was swept far from the West Australian coast. He left his kayak and life jacket, reached shore and ran 2 km to call 000. He told rescuers the family had drifted 14 km and were in danger. The call helped a search‑and‑rescue team locate and save the four‑member family.
