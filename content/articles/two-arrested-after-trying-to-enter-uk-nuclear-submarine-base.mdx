Police Scotland arrested a 34‑year‑old man, believed to be Iranian, and a 31‑year‑old woman after they tried to get into the Faslane submarine base in Scotland, home to the UK’s Trident nuclear submarines. They were denied entry and were taken into custody for suspicious activity. The incident is being investigated, but officials say it is not yet clear if it was an espionage attempt. The case follows other Iran‑linked surveillance complaints in London.
