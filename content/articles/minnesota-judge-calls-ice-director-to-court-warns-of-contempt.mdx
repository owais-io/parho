Minnesota Judge Patrick Schiltz has ordered the acting head of ICE, Todd Lyons, to appear before him, threatening contempt for ignoring court orders. The move follows months of clashes over the Trump‑era “Operation Metro Surge,” which has led to many lawsuits from immigrants claiming unlawful arrest and detention. Schiltz says the government’s non‑compliance is harming people and may prompt broader court challenges that could limit federal immigration enforcement in the state.
