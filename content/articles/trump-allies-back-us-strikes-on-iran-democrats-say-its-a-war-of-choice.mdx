Trump’s allies on TV and radio said the president was right to hit Iran after the 7‑day raid that killed its top leader, saying it stopped a long‑running threat. Democrats, however, called the attack a “war of choice” that should have been debated in Congress. They warn it could drag the U.S. into another Middle‑East conflict and raise questions about who will lead Iran next. The debate shows deep partisan split over how to handle Tehran.
