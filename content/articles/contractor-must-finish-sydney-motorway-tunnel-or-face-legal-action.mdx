The New South Wales government has warned contractor CGU to finish the nearly complete M6 motorway tunnel or face legal action. Two years after sinkholes opened during construction, work stopped when a hard rock fault was found. The minister says a technical fix is in the contract, but the contractor claimed they couldn’t deliver safely. The government will not give extra money and wants the company to complete the project.
