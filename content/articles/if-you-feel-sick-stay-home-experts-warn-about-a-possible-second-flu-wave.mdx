Experts warn people to stay home during Christmas if they have flu or COVID symptoms as flu cases could rise again next year. Flu hospital admissions are up 18% this week, and the NHS is under heavy pressure, with doctors on strike. The advice is to wash hands, keep rooms well ventilated, get a free flu jab if you can, and stay home if you feel sick.
