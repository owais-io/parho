In Minneapolis, Mayor Jacob Frey says federal officials are hiding the truth about the death of Renee Good, who was shot by ICE. He wants the state’s police to join the FBI’s probe. Frey, the governor, and other local leaders are furious after the FBI took over and cut off state access to evidence. The shooting sparked peaceful protests, and residents want a fair, transparent investigation.
