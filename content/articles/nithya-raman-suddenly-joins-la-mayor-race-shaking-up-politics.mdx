Nithya Raman, a 44‑year‑old city‑council member known for fighting for affordable housing, filed for Los Angeles mayor just hours before the deadline, surprising voters and her former ally Karen Bass. Raman’s late entry turns the race into a real contest after Bass was expected to win. Critics blame the mayor’s handling of wildfires and ICE raids. Raman says she wants urgent change and has already gathered volunteers for a grassroots campaign.
