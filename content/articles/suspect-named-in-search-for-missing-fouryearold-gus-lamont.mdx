Police in South Australia say a person who lives at the family’s outback station is now a suspect in the disappearance of four‑year‑old Gus Lamont. The boy vanished from his home in Oak Park on 27 September, and the police have carried out a massive search using drones, divers, mounted units and trackers across 60,000 hectares. The suspect is not Gus’s parents, and investigators are still looking for any clues to explain what happened.
