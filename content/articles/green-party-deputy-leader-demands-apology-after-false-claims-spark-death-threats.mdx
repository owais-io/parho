Mothin Ali, a Green Party councillor, received death threats after a news outlet falsely claimed he was supporting Iran’s regime. The claim surfaced when Ali joined an anti‑war protest in Parliament Square. Prime Minister Keir Starmer echoed the misinformation, prompting Ali to call for an apology. He said the allegations scared him, with threats growing. Parliament debated the incident, and Ali’s supporters defended him against the claims.
