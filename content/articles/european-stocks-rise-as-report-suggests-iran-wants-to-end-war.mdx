A New York Times story said Iran may secretly be trying to negotiate a cease‑fire in the Middle East. The news lifted European markets, with the FTSE 100 and Stoxx 600 climbing a few points. Oil and gas prices fell, and the US promised to escort tankers through the Strait of Hormuz. Despite the optimism, experts say a real peace deal is still far away.
