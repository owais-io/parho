New York’s mayor‑elect, Zohran Mamdani, will be sworn in underground on New Year’s Eve at a subway station under City Hall. The ceremony, led by Attorney General Letitia James, is meant to symbolize a fresh start for the city. After the underground oath, there will be a public ceremony on City Hall steps with Bernie Sanders and a block party. The event has drawn mixed attendance from former officials and sparked debate over Mamdani’s choice to head the FDNY.
