Police in Melbourne say a 29‑year‑old woman has been charged with murder and attempted murder after a house fire on Sunburnt Circuit in Clyde North left one man dead and another badly hurt. The woman was taken to hospital with minor injuries and is now in custody, set to appear in court. Two other people inside the home were not injured. The surviving man remains in hospital in a stable condition.
