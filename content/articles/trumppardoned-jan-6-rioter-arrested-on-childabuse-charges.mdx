A 44‑year‑old man who stormed the U.S. Capitol on Jan. 6 and was pardoned by President Trump is now arrested in Florida on serious child‑abuse charges. Authorities say he molested two kids, sent disturbing messages, and tried to keep them silent by talking about his pardon and money. He has pleaded not guilty. This is one of several cases where pardoned Jan. 6 rioters face new crimes.
