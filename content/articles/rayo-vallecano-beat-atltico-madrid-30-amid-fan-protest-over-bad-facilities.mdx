Rayo Vallecano beat Atlético Madrid 3‑0 in a match that was almost impossible to hold, as the club’s coach and captain quit, fans staged a boycott, and the home ground was unusable. They trained at Getafe and played in Leganés, with empty seats and poor facilities. Despite the chaos, the small club won, showing their grit while the owner’s mismanagement and bad pitch sparked outrage.
