In Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia, an 18‑year‑old shooter opened fire at the secondary school. Six people died, including three 12‑year‑old girls and a teacher, and more than 20 were hurt. The school went into lockdown while police rushed in and stopped the shooting, but the suspect was also killed. The town, with 2,700 residents, is in shock, and Canadian leaders have pledged support.
