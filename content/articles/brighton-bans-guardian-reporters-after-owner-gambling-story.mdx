Brighton & Hove Albion said it would no longer let Guardian reporters and photographers attend its matches. The club reacted after the newspaper published stories that raised questions about owner Tony Bloom’s gambling ties and a lawsuit that claims he used frontmen to place sports bets. Bloom denied betting on Brighton and said he hasn’t done so since buying the club. MPs want the FA to investigate the allegations, while the Guardian says the reporting is in the public interest.
