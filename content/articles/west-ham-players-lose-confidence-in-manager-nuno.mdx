West Ham’s manager, Nuno Espírito Santo, is losing the support of his players after a 3‑0 loss to Wolves. The team is in 18th place, four points below Nottingham Forest, and morale is slipping. Players feel Nuno is distant, gives confusing instructions, and sometimes plays them in the wrong positions. Despite the club backing him and making new signings, many are unhappy and hope for a change.
