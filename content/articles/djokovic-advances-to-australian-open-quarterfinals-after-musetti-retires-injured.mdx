Novak Djokovic moved on to the Australian Open quarter‑finals when Lorenzo Musetti pulled out with an injury. Musetti had taken a two‑set lead, 6‑4, 6‑3, but was hampered by a thigh problem and had to retire at 1‑3 in the third set. Djokovic said he was sorry for Musetti and praised his play, while the win gives Djokovic a key chance to keep his title defence alive.
