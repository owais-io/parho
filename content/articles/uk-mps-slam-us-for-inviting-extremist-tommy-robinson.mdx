UK MPs have blasted the Trump administration for hosting far‑right activist Tommy Robinson, calling it a sign of US interference in British politics. The visit, which saw Robinson meet US officials and right‑wing figures, raises questions about how he entered the US after a previous ban. MPs want an investigation into whether the US helped a dangerous extremist influence UK elections and policy.
