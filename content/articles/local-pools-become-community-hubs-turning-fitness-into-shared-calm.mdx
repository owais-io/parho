After a chilly first week in Bonn, the author found a free sauna that became a nightly escape. A few days later, meeting Afghan students turned the quiet space into a lively gathering. Years later in Melbourne, public pools and gyms offered the same idea – locals from all walks of life relax, chat, and feel at home. These shared spaces break the digital divide and turn fitness into community, making wellness feel like a collective breath of relief.
