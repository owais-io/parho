Donald Trump announced he will attend the White House Correspondents’ Dinner for the first time as president, ending his 2017 boycott. The event, normally attended by every president, will feature a mentalist instead of a comedian. Trump’s return to the dinner comes amid his media empire growth, with allies buying major outlets. The dinner marks a rare public appearance of the former president at a key press event.
