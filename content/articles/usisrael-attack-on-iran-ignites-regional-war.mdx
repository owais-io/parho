After a joint US‑Israeli strike that killed Iran’s top leader, Iran launched missiles and drones at US and Gulf targets. The attacks hit bases, embassies, and even oil facilities, raising oil prices and putting British citizens in the region at risk. Experts warn the fighting could spread and destabilise the Gulf, while the US and its allies scramble to defend themselves and plan for a potential exit.
