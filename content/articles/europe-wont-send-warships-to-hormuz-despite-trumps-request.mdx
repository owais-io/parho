European leaders said no to sending warships into the Hormuz Strait after US President Donald Trump asked for help reopening the waterway. Germany, the UK, Italy, and others declined, preferring diplomacy and saying the move was not theirs. Trump warned NATO could suffer if allies ignored the request. EU officials decided to keep existing naval missions in the Red Sea unchanged.
