A stranger pretended to be French musician Sébastien Tellier, showing up at parties, using his Chanel ambassador status, and even meeting Hollywood bosses while the real Tellier was busy elsewhere. Tellier wrote the song “Copycat” to describe the theft and turned the experience into a hit. He’s now 50 and dropping a new album, Kiss the Beast, featuring big names like Nile Rodgers and Kid Cudi to reach a wider crowd.
