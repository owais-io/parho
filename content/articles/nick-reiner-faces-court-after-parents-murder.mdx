Nick Reiner, 32, is charged with killing his parents, actor Rob Reiner and photographer Michele Singer Reiner. He first appeared in court Wednesday, didn’t plead, and the hearing is pushed to January. The couple were found dead in their Brentwood home, discovered by their daughter Romy. Police say Nick is responsible. He was arrested that night. Family mourns and asks for privacy. The case has shocked Hollywood.
