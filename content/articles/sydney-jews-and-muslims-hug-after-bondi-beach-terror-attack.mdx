After a terror attack on Bondi beach killed 15 people, Sydney’s Jewish and Muslim leaders met in Hyde Park. Rabbi Jeffrey Kamins and Imam Bilal Rauf gave speeches about unity and shared grief. They hugged, and the crowd cheered, showing that people from different faiths can come together. The vigil was part of a larger national response, with many memorials where Christians, Muslims and Jews prayed together and offered support.
