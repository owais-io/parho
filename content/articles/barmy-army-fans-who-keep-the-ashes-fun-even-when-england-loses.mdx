The Barmy Army is a group of English cricket fans who travel to matches, especially the Ashes. They started in Adelaide 30 years ago and grew from a few backpackers into a huge touring club. Even when England’s team is losing, the Barmy Army keeps the atmosphere lively with chants, parties and colourful shirts. They run tours, sell merchandise, and now have many female members. Their history shows fan enthusiasm can stay strong, no matter the score.
