The FBI is searching roads near Nancy Guthrie’s home in Arizona after she vanished a week ago. A man was arrested during a traffic stop and police inspected a nearby property. The agency released pictures of a masked intruder at the house and offered a $50,000 reward. Savannah Guthrie has posted pleas for her mother’s safe return, and investigators continue to hunt for clues.
