US forces struck Iran’s Kharg Island, a key oil‑processing hub, hoping to pressure Tehran. The hit could prompt Iran to cut oil output, and Gulf oilfields may shut too, worsening supply. With storage and pipeline options limited, oil prices have already spiked, and experts say full production could take weeks or months, keeping costs up for consumers and businesses.
