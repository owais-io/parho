The Church of England posted a video saying Christmas is for everyone. It came after far‑right activist Tommy Robinson planned a Westminster carols concert that many church leaders say misuses the holiday to push anti‑immigrant, nationalist ideas. Senior bishops, including former Archbishop Rowan Williams and Bishop Arun Arora, warned that the far right is exploiting Christian symbols and urged Christians to focus on compassion, hospitality, and inclusion. Other churches are holding services and a counter‑protest with singer Billy Bragg.
