New South Wales police posted CCTV footage of three men heading toward a suspected hide‑out in Dural, part of the investigation into the kidnapping and murder of 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian. Baghsarian was taken from his North Ryde home on Feb. 13, and his body was found near a golf club on Feb. 24. The footage shows a blue Hyundai sedan that may have been used in the case. Police are asking the public for help.
