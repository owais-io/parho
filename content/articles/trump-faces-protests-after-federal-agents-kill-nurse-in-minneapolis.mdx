On Friday, federal agents in Minneapolis fatally shot nurse Alex Pretti after a chemical‑agent spray and a struggle that left him on the ground. Video shows Pretti holding a phone, not a gun, contradicting Trump officials’ claims he threatened law‑enforcement. The killing sparks protests across U.S. cities and fuels calls to reform or abolish ICE. Democrats threaten to block DHS funding, while Minneapolis mayor questions how many more lives are needed to end the crisis.
