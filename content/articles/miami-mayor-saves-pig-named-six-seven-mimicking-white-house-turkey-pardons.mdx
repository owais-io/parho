Miami-Dade Mayor Daniella Levine Cava declared a pig named Six Seven “pardoned” in a ceremony at the Latin Café 2000 in Little Havana. The act was a playful copy of the White House turkey pardon, meant to start the holiday season with a light‑hearted nod to Miami’s food culture. The pig, donated by a firefighter, will live in a rural sanctuary away from the grill, while attendees ate vegetarian dishes. The event highlighted compassion and community spirit.
