An 82‑year‑old couple in Victoria had to pack up their home as fires threatened a heatwave‑driven day of catastrophic danger. They moved to a relief centre with their dog, leaving behind photos and needlework. Residents of Seymour stocked water, batteries and food, while others set up caravans for overnight stays. Fire officials warned of 100‑km/h winds and urged people to leave early. The state issued a total fire ban, and locals hoped rain would help.
