A big winter storm will hit most of the US this weekend, bringing heavy snow, ice, and temperatures below ‑30°F. It starts Friday, moving from the Southwest to the East Coast, affecting more than 200 million people. Major cities like Dallas, Nashville, Washington, and New York will see up to a foot of snow and dangerous ice. Power outages, travel chaos, and dangerous roads are warned, so people should stock up and be prepared.
