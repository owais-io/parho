Rising Middle East tensions are pushing up UK mortgage rates and bond yields, with banks raising prices and the Bank of England expected to hike rates again. Meanwhile, Brent crude oil is hovering around $110 after President Trump extended the deadline for Iran to open the Strait of Hormuz, keeping oil prices high. The higher energy costs are hurting the car industry, retail sales, and consumer confidence, as the UK faces a wave of economic worry.
