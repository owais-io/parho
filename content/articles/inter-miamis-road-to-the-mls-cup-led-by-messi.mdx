After years of planning, Inter Miami finally opened with Messi and a growing squad. Beckham and owner Mas built the club through legal fights and stadium dreams. Messi’s 2023 arrival gave the team a first trophy and a new buzz. The club added new leaders, built a mix of young and experienced players, and made a big playoff run. Now they’ll try to win the MLS Cup at home, with Messi set to stay until 2028.
