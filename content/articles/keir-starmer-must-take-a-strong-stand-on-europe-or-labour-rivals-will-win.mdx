Seven years ago, Starmer’s brief comment at a Labour conference showed he could sway the party’s view on Brexit. Now, as prime minister, he says Brexit is finished and warns a closer European tie could break US trade deals. Yet many Labour MPs want a customs union and tighter EU links. If Starmer stays weak, rivals could grab the European debate and win support.
