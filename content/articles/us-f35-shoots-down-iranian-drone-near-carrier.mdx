A U.S. F‑35 from the Abraham Lincoln carrier shot down an Iranian Shahed‑139 drone that was too close to the ship in the Arabian Sea, Central Command said. No U.S. crew were hurt and the carrier was unharmed. Earlier the same day, Iranian forces tried to approach a U.S.‑flagged tanker in the Strait of Hormuz, but were deterred. The incidents come as Washington and Tehran are preparing for possible nuclear talks and a tense U.S. military presence in the region.
