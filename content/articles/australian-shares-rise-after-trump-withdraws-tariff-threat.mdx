After US President Donald Trump said he’d back off a tariff threat that could have hit European allies, global stocks lifted, and Australian shares jumped. The ASX 200 briefly topped 8,860 points before easing. Australian stocks were helped by solid commodity prices, but a jobs report that could lead to an interest‑rate rise slowed the rally. The Australian dollar hit a 15‑month high against the US dollar.
