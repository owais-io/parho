The article says Erik Prince, founder of former mercenary firm Blackwater, has joined Ukrainian drone software company Swarmer as non‑executive chair. Swarmer, which has used AI to control drone swarms in the war with Russia, is looking to raise money with an IPO. Prince’s move ties him to Ukraine’s growing drone industry, which has already seen 100,000 combat missions. The U.S. and NATO are also investing heavily in drone tech for future wars.
