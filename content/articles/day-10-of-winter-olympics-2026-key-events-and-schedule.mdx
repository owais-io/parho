Day 10 of the 2026 Winter Olympics in Milan‑Cortina keeps the action coming with a packed slate of races and matches. The day opens with men’s slalom on the giant‑slalom course, where favorites like Clement Noel, Henrik Kristoffersen and Loic Meillard look to make the podium. Curling, bobsleigh, short‑track, ice hockey, figure skating and ski‑jumping follow, featuring teams from Great Britain, Canada, Sweden and Japan, plus veteran Kaillie Humphries in the monobob.
