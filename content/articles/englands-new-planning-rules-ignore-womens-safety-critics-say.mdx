The government released new planning rules that make no reference to women or safety after Sarah Everard’s murder. Experts say this ignores reports urging safer streets, better lighting and design. MPs Anna Sabine and Gideon Amos warned that ignoring women will embed risk into new developments. The government says planning and the VAWG strategy are separate, but critics call the omission arrogant and demand inclusion.
