British bishops are urging the UK to respond to a July 2024 International Court of Justice opinion that calls Israel’s occupation of the West Bank illegal. They say the government has ignored the court’s advice, helping Israel expand settlements, close roads, demolish homes and cut off water and electricity. The bishops ask Britain to refuse aid that supports these policies and to defend Palestinian rights.
