Adam Wharton joined Crystal Palace in January and has quickly become a key player, even though he hasn't scored yet. His play earned him a spot in England’s Euro 2024 squad, though he didn’t get any minutes. He helped Palace win the FA Cup and has delivered assists, showing good vision and confidence. With a contract until 2029 and a valuation over £80m, clubs like Manchester City, Liverpool, Real Madrid, and United are eyeing him, but Palace are keeping him for now.
