Northampton Saints beat Bath Rugby 36‑7 at Sixways Stadium. Even with many player changes, the Saints scored six tries, including a hat‑trick from centre Tommy Freeman. Bath, also with a heavily rotated lineup, could only score one try after a series of disallowed attempts. Northampton’s win moved them to the top of the Premiership table, while Bath struggled to keep pace.
