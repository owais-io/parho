World number‑one female chess player Hou Yifan, who is also a university professor, made a surprise appearance in the Global Chess League final in Mumbai. She helped her team Alpine SG Pipers beat the champions Triveni Continental Kings, winning the match with a series of four straight victories. The event is meant to be the chess version of the IPL. In the same final, Anish Giri crushed Wei Yi, and the world rapid championship has now started in Doha.
