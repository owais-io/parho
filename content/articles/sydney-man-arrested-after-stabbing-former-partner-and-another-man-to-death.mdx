Police in Sydney arrested a 47‑year‑old man after a violent incident on Sunday morning in Quakers Hill. The man allegedly stabbed his former partner and a second man to death. Emergency crews found a male victim with stab wounds on the street and a female victim dead in the kitchen. The suspect was taken to Blacktown police station, where authorities said a knife was recovered. The investigation is still underway.
