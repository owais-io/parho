An Amazon delivery van was stuck half‑submerged on the Broomway, a tidal mudflat route that leads to Foulness Island. The driver tried to reach the island when the bridge was closed and the mud track broke apart. A local farmer rescued the van, but the tide had already moved in. The Broomway is known for its shifting mud and past fatalities, making the rescue risky.
