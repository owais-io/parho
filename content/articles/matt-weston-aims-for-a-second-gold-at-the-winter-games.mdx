Matt Weston won Britain’s first gold in skeleton at the Winter Olympics and is now chasing a second medal in the new mixed‑team event. After a celebratory pizza dinner, he’s focused on the team race, which pairs him with Britain’s top female skeletoners. The event starts with a reaction launch, adding pressure and excitement. Weston and his teammates believe they can beat strong rivals like China, Austria, and Germany.
