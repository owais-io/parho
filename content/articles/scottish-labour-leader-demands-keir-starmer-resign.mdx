Anas Sarwar, the leader of Scottish Labour, called on UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer to step down, saying the current UK leadership is hurting the party’s chances in the May Scottish elections. Sarwar blamed policy mistakes and recent scandals, like the Epstein‑linked Peter Mandelson affair, for damaging Labour’s support in Scotland. While many UK ministers back Starmer, the call could spark a leadership showdown and impact the upcoming election.
