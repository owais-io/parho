Trump slammed the UK for refusing to let its bases be used in the US‑Israeli attack on Iran. He said the UK’s stance was a sign that the relationship had changed. UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK would only allow defensive use of its bases to protect allies hit by Iranian retaliation. The exchange shows tension over how much the UK will back US actions in the Middle East.
