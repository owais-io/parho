A 28‑year‑old Florida woman was arrested after she set up a fake GoFundMe in July, claiming she was the grieving father of 18‑year‑old Chloe Childress, a counselor who died in Texas’s Camp Mystic floods. She used Childress’s name and photos, asking for donations. When the real family noticed, detectives traced the page to her and charged her with online impersonation. GoFundMe shut the account, refunded donors, and banned her.
