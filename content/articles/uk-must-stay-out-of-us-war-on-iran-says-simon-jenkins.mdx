Simon Jenkins argues Britain must not get involved in the US‑led attack on Iran. He says Donald Trump lied about an imminent threat and used the war for political gain. While the UK used its bases, polls show most Britons oppose the bombing. Jenkins criticises UK politicians for backing the US, calls for Britain to keep its principles and stay out of another unwinnable conflict.
