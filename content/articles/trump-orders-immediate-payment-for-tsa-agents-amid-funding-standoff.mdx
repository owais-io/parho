Trump says he will sign an order to pay TSA agents right away after they’ve been unpaid for over a month because DHS lacks funds. The delay has left airports short of staff, causing long lines, flight cancellations and frustrated travelers. Trump hasn’t explained where the money will come from, and he also sent ICE agents to airports to help. Congress still hasn’t agreed on funding, and Democrats want reforms to ICE.
