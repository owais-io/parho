Rob Cross talks about what’s been happening in his life, from visiting a children's hospital to dealing with mental health and money problems. He says seeing the children made him realise how lucky he is and he needs to talk. His ranking fell, he lost money in taxes, and his manager is ill. Cross stopped his ADHD meds before the tournament, found it hard but still doing well. He looks forward to Christmas with his kids and to the next match.
