In Manchester, an Islamic State cell plotted a mass shooting at a synagogue but was stopped. Two men were convicted of terrorism. Jewish residents say they still live their daily lives, help neighbours, and keep their community close. Local leaders run a family centre that also supports Muslim families, while a neighbourhood patrol shares tips with police. The case highlights rising Islamist and far‑right hate, and calls for tighter security and solidarity.
