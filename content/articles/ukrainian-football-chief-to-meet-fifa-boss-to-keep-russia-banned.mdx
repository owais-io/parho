Andriy Shevchenko, head of Ukrainian football, will meet FIFA boss Gianni Infantino at UEFA’s Brussels congress to insist that Russia remains banned from international matches. Infantino said the ban could be reconsidered, angering Kyiv. Shevchenko says nothing has changed since Russia’s invasion and no return is possible under current conditions. The debate highlights how sports and politics collide amid Ukraine’s war.
