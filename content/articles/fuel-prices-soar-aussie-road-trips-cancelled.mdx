Fuel prices in Australia have jumped by up to 65 % after the Middle East war, sparking a shortage of petrol and diesel across the country. Retirees Jan and Earl Robinson, along with many families, have cancelled their Easter road‑trip plans to spare fuel for essential services. The Australian Tourism Industry Council is asking the government to prioritise fuel for regional travel routes. While some holidaymakers are cutting back or using public transport, the tourism sector fears a dip in demand.
