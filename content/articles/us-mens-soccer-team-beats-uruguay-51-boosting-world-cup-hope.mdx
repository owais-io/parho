The US men’s national soccer team, coached by Mauricio Pochettino, finished a rough first year with a stunning 5‑1 win over Uruguay. The victory, the biggest against a South American side, shows the team’s growing chemistry and confidence. Pochettino praised his squad for playing together and said the win is a sign of progress toward the World Cup. He highlighted the team’s focus on hard work, unity, and a new playing style.
