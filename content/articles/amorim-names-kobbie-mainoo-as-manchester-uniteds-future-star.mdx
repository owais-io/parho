Manager Ruben Amorim has said 20‑year‑old Kobbie Mainoo will be the next big player for Manchester United. The versatile forward can play many spots, but he’s been sidelined with a calf injury and hasn’t started in any of the club’s 17 Premier League games. Amorim said the club may loan him out if a new signing is made, and he expects Mainoo to step up when key players like Bruno Fernandes, Bryan Mbeumo and Amad Diallo are unavailable.
