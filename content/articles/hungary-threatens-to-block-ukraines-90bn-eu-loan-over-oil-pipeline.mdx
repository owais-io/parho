Hungary’s Prime Minister Viktor Orbán said he will veto the EU’s €90 billion loan to Ukraine unless the Druzhba oil pipeline that runs through Hungary is reopened. The pipeline was shut after a Russian strike and has angered Hungary and Slovakia, who want the oil to flow into Europe. The move comes as Ukraine says it is not losing the war, and Europe is looking at new drone programs to defend against attacks.
