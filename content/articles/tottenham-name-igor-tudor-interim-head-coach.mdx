Tottenham Hotspur has hired 47‑year‑old Igor Tudor to take over as temporary manager until the end of the season. Tudor says he wants to bring stability, focus and better results to the team after a string of shaky performances. The club hopes his experience and calm style will steady the club as they fight to stay in the Premier League.
