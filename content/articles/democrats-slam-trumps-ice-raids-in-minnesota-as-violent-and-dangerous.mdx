Democratic leaders in Minnesota, including senators, congresswomen, and state officials, held a hearing at the state capitol to protest the Trump administration's ICE surge. They accused federal agents of using deadly force, citing the shooting of Renee Good and the mass detentions of citizens. Lawmakers urged the FBI to let state investigators examine the case, called for independent investigations, and warned that the surge threatens civil rights and public safety.
