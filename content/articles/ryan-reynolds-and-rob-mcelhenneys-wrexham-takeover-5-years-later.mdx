Wrexham’s football club has changed dramatically since Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney bought it five years ago. The Hollywood duo’s investment has lifted the team from non‑league football to the Championship and turned the town’s pub into a tourist hotspot. Fans say the takeover boosted morale, even improving players’ mental health. The club keeps growing, with new stadium plans and a busy roster, but still faces limits in space and finances.
