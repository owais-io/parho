After a terror attack at Bondi that killed 15, Jewish rabbis urged Australians to do one act of kindness in memory of the victims. The 'One Mitzvah for Bondi' campaign asks people of all faiths to volunteer, donate, or simply help a neighbour. The NSW government, the premier and the prime minister praised the effort, and social media posts use #OneMitzvahforBondi.
