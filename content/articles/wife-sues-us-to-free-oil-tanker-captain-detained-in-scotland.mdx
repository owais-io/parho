The wife of oil‑tanker captain Avtandil Kalandadze says his arrest by US forces in Scottish waters is illegal. She has asked a Scottish court to review his detention after the ship – the Marinera – was seized while heading toward the Venezuelan blockade. Lawyers want the court to keep the vessel under Scottish jurisdiction and to stop the crew from being moved elsewhere. A judge has already blocked removal.
