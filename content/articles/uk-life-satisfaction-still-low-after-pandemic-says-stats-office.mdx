The Office for National Statistics says people in the UK are still not happier than before COVID‑19, even though the economy is growing. About 5% of adults say they are very unhappy with life, a level that hasn't dropped since the pandemic began. Trust in government is also low, and more people are worried about their health and money. Even with falling inflation, households feel cautious about the future.
