Mikaela Shiffrin dominated the slalom in Flachau, Austria, winning by 0.41 seconds over teammate Paula Moltzan. It was Shiffrin’s sixth win of the season and her 107th World Cup victory, extending her record. Moltzan earned her first podium of the year, while Austria’s Katharina Truppe finished third. With one slalom left before the Olympics, Shiffrin could win the season title if she keeps winning.
