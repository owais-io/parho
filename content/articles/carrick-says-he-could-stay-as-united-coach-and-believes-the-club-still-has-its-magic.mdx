Michael Carrick, the interim manager at Manchester United, has made clear that he is open to staying on permanently when the season ends. He insists the team still has “magic” and is far from soulless. United are weighing other candidates while Carrick works to boost player confidence amid a dip in form. He also dismissed a joke about his wife and noted a clause that could pay Casemiro more if he plays enough.
