Two Cuban mothers were forced out of Florida by U.S. immigration. One left with her U.S.‑citizen son, the other with a baby she couldn't take. Trump’s hard‑line policy says Cuba must be safe for Cuban Americans, but it ends legal pathways for newcomers and leaves families torn apart. About 45,000 Cubans already have deportation orders, with 550,000 more at risk. U.S. actions also target businesses helping Cuba.
