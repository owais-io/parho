Ring’s Super Bowl ad showed how a doorbell camera can help find a lost dog, but it also raised fears that the same tech could let the government watch people. The FBI used footage from a Nancy Guthrie home even though her camera wasn’t supposed to keep video. People, lawmakers, and privacy groups slammed Ring, saying the company may let law‑enforcement access data without clear rules. Ring says it only shares videos with officials when a legal request is made.
