On Tuesday evening in Kerzers, a Swiss bus caught fire after a passenger, later found to have set himself ablaze, doused the vehicle with a flammable substance. Six people died and five were hurt. Police say the man, from Bern and in his 60s, was marginalized and disturbed, and there is no evidence of terrorism. The investigation is still ongoing. The yellow PostBus, used for rural transport, was the vehicle.
