The NHS is facing a big strain as the UK Health Security Agency has put the whole of England on amber cold alerts. Hospitals are almost full—about 95% of beds are used—and many patients are sick with flu, chest infections, and stomach bugs. The cold weather is expected to make the situation worse. NHS leaders say they’re not out of danger yet and are urging people to look after older and vulnerable friends and family.
