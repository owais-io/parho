In a Welsh byelection, the right‑wing Reform UK party tried to win Caerphilly by spreading hate and misinformation. Ukrainian refugees, local voters and Plaid Cymru’s hopeful candidate, Lindsay Whittle, stood up instead. They highlighted how the party’s attacks on refugees were false and divisive. With a positive, community‑focused campaign, Plaid won the seat, showing that people could see through the lies and choose unity over fear.
