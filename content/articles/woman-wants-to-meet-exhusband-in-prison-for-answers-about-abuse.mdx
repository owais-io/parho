Gisele Pelicot, 73, says she needs to see her former husband in prison after he was found guilty of drugging her and inviting dozens of men to rape her. She wants answers about whether their daughter was also abused and about other crimes linked to him. The ex‑husband, Dominique Pelicot, got a 20‑year sentence in December 2024. Pelicot hopes a face‑to‑face meeting will give her the truth.
