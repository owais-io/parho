Freya Godfrey, a 20‑year‑old midfielder for London City Lionesses, almost missed her England call‑up. She ignored an unknown number, then heard a text saying “Hi, it’s Sarina.” She called the England coach, who congratulated her on being invited to the final 2025 matches against China and Ghana. Godfrey talks about her tough journey, injuries, and growing confidence, and says she’s thrilled to represent her country.
