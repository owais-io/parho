Jess Harwood, a comic artist and tour guide, says tech companies make us lonely by keeping us glued to screens. She decided to turn off her phone and talk to people she meets, like in a clinic, café or cricket match. Her conversations feel lighter and happier. She also runs women’s history tours in Sydney, which bring strangers together, and urges readers to step out, chat, and reclaim real connections.
