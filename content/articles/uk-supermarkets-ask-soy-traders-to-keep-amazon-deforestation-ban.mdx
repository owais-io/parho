UK and European grocery chains like Tesco, Sainsbury’s and Asda are writing to big soy traders, saying they need to keep a 2008 cut‑off that stops buying soy grown on newly cleared Amazon land. The traders have pulled out of the global agreement that helped protect the rainforest, and the retailers fear a new deal is needed to avoid more deforestation and keep shoppers confident.
