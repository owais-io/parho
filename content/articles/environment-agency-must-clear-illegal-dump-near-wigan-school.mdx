A 25,000‑tonne pile of illegal waste sits beside a primary school in Bickershaw, Wigan. The Lords environment committee chair, Lady Shas Sheehan, is demanding the Environment Agency clear the site because it poses a serious environmental and health risk. The dump even sparked a week‑long fire last summer, forcing the school to close. She says the Agency is already busy cleaning other dumps but should be allowed to tackle this one under ministerial direction.
