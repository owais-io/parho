Two former workers say Spanish singer Julio Iglesias sexually assaulted them in his Caribbean homes in 2021. The claims come after a three‑year investigation that collected testimonies from 15 former employees and documents showing a controlling work environment. One woman said Iglesias repeatedly touched and penetrated her without consent, while another said he kissed and touched her breasts. The case has sparked debate in Spain, with officials and politicians giving opposing responses.
