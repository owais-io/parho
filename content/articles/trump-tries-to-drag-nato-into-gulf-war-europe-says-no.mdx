Trump is asking NATO allies to help in a new war with Iran after he opened a conflict in the Gulf. European leaders say no, arguing the fight would raise oil prices, hurt Europe’s economy, and pull them into a dangerous war. They want to keep forces defensive, avoid Iran’s retaliation, and ensure European troops aren’t targeted, while still focusing on counter‑Russia security and protecting European interests.
