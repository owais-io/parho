North Korean leader Kim Jong‑un has praised Russia for sharing the fight in Ukraine, saying their countries have “blood, life and death” together. North Korea says it sent soldiers to help Russia, even losing troops. In Ukraine, Kyiv has been hit by missile blasts and air‑defence alerts. Russia and the EU clash over a peace deal, while Belarus is suspected of helping Russian drone attacks. Russia also claims to have taken a village in Zaporizhzhia.
