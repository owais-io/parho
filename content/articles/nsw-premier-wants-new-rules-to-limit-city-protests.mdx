NSW Premier Chris Minns wants to tighten rules on protests in Sydney’s CBD. A recent poll showed most Australians support giving police more power to control demonstrations. Minns is looking at changing the Form 1 system, which lets protest organisers share plans with police, and possibly banning certain slogans. Critics say stricter rules could curb free speech, while supporters argue they’re needed for safety after recent violent incidents.
