English Heritage has added special benches at its historic sites so parents can easily meet others and share their experiences. The benches have a sliding sign letting people say “up for a chat” or “need quiet”. Alongside this, the charity runs family‑friendly walks and other activities to help new parents feel less lonely and more supported. Studies show most new parents feel isolated or overwhelmed.
