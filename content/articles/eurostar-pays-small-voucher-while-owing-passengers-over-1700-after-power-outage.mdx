After a power outage in the Channel tunnel that left thousands of travellers stranded, Eurostar paid a £120 voucher to a customer instead of the £1,744 owed for food, accommodation and a refund. Passengers were told to book hotels, but the online system had no seats, forcing them to buy new tickets. The company claims the voucher is an apology and offers a slow claims process.
