Sainsbury’s boss Simon Roberts has been awarded a CBE in the New Year honours for his work turning the supermarket around during the pandemic and cost‑of‑living crisis, boosting its market share and share price. He’s also faced criticism for facial‑recognition trials, cutting 3,000 jobs, and a £5 million salary. Other business leaders, including Channel 4’s former CEO, HomeServe founder, and tech heads, were also honoured.
