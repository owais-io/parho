A 37‑year‑old mother was shot three times by an ICE officer in Minneapolis. Video shows the car was not hit, and the officer said he was protecting himself. Federal officials and some politicians say the shooting was justified, but many lawmakers and experts say ICE should have tried to calm the situation instead. The investigation faces questions about impartiality, and local police may have trouble pressing charges. The family can sue ICE in court.
