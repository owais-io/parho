President Trump criticised Australia for not taking part in US‑Israeli strikes on Iran. Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese replied that no direct request or advance notice was given from Washington. He highlighted that Australia has already deployed a Wedgetail surveillance plane and defence staff to the UAE to support Gulf states, but has not been asked to join the strike itself. The government says any future aid would depend on Australian national interest.
