People in cities from Munich to Los Angeles marched to show support for Iranian protesters who face violent crackdowns. Exiled prince Reza Pahlavi told 200,000 people he could lead Iran to a democratic future, while U.S. President Trump said a regime change would be best. Despite no diplomatic ties, the U.S. and Iran are in talks over nuclear issues. Human rights groups say thousands were killed in the protests.
