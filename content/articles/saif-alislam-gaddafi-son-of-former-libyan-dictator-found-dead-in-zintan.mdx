Saif al‑Islam Gaddafi, son of the late Libyan dictator, was found dead in the town of Zintan, 85 miles southwest of Tripoli, on Tuesday night. Officials say masked men shot him at his home. He once was seen as a reformer but later supported his father's violent crackdown. An international court had charged him with crimes against humanity. His death could stir unrest among pro‑Gaddafi supporters and deepen Libya’s divisions.
