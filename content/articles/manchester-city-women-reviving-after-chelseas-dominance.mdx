Manchester City’s women have turned the tide in the Women’s Super League. With a new coach, Andrée Jeglertz, they’ve stayed injury‑free, kept a tight defense and used powerful crosses to score many headed goals. Consistent line‑ups and strong recruiting have brought new talent like 19‑year‑old Iman Beney. They’re now 9 points behind Chelsea, but a win could keep the title within reach. The team’s calm leadership and unity are key to their revival.
