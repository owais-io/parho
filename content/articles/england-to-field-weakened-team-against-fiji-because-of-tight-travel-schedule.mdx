England’s summer rugby tour is packed. They’ll play South Africa in Johannesburg, Fiji in England, then Argentina in a week. To manage fatigue, coach Steve Borthwick plans to drop many key players for the Fiji game and use a mostly second‑string squad. Fiji, on the other hand, are hosting all their matches in the UK. The move aims to keep England’s main team fresh for later fixtures.
