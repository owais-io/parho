Marc Guéhi, 25, has joined Manchester City for £20 million after a deal with Crystal Palace. The England defender will help replace injured stars like Rúben Dias and John Stones. Guardiola praised him as a top central defender of the right age. Guéhi signed a five‑and‑a‑half‑year contract, earning about £300,000 a week, and Chelsea will receive 20 % of the fee. City head to a Champions League game in Norway.
