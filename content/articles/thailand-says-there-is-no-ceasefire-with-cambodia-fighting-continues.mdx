Thai leaders say there is no ceasefire with Cambodia, even though U.S. President Donald Trump said they had agreed to stop fighting. The two countries are still clashing along their border, with airstrikes and rocket attacks. At least 15 Thai soldiers and many civilians have been killed, and hundreds of thousands of people have been displaced. Trump’s claim was ignored by both Thai and Cambodian officials, who say peace will come only if the other side stops shooting.
