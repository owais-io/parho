After Liverpool lifted the Premier League trophy, celebrations were cut short by a car crash that hurt more than 130 people. The tragedy, followed by the death of young striker Diogo Jota, shook the club. On top of that, new signings have been injured or performed poorly, and the team has lost nine of its last twelve games. Manager Arne Slot faces a tough season as Liverpool struggles to stay calm and focus.
