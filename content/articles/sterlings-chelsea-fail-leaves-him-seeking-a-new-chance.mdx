Raheem Sterling joined Chelsea in 2022 for £47.5 m, hoping to revive his form after a dip at Manchester City. Coaching changes and injuries meant he never settled in, scoring only 19 goals in 81 games. Chelsea cut his contract early, freeing money and leaving Sterling a free agent. At 31, he still hopes to find a club where he can play regularly and reignite his career.
