Thirty years after the tragic shooting at Dunblane primary school, the town still remembers 16 children and their teacher who were killed. The Snowdrop campaign turned grief into Britain’s strictest handgun laws. A community centre opened in 2004 to give young people a safe place, and it now serves all ages. The story shows how mourning can spark lasting change and keep memories alive.
