BT said it lost 210,000 broadband customers in its third quarter and expects a total of 850,000 customers to leave the year‑long. Revenue fell 4% to £5bn and pre‑tax profit dropped 57% to £183m, largely because of losses from its stake in TNT Sports. Shares slipped 1%. Vodafone also fell 6% after missing revenue forecasts, though its churn rate is improving as it merges with Three.
