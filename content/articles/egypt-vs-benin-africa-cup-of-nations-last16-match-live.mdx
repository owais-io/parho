Egypt, who have won the Africa Cup of Nations seven times, are meeting Benin in the last 16. Benin made the quarter‑finals only once, when they reached the last eight in 2019 without a win in normal play, relying on a shoot‑out. Egypt, led by Mo Salah who scored twice in the group stage, aim to win their first title since 2021. The game will be played in Agadir at 4 p.m. GMT.
