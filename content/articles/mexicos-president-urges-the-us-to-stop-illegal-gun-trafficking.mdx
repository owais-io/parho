Mexico’s president, Claudia Sheinbaum, blasted U.S. President Donald Trump’s claim that Mexico is the “epicenter” of cartel violence. She said the U.S. must help stop the flow of illegal weapons—about 75 % of cartel guns come from the U.S. The Mexican leader also rebuffed Trump’s threat to send troops into Mexico. Analysts argue the real problem is U.S. demand for drugs and weapons, not Mexico itself.
