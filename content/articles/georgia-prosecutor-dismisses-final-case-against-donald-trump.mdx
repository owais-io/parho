The state prosecutor who took over after former Fulton County district attorney Fani Willis said the 2020 racketeering case against Donald Trump is finished. A judge ordered the case dismissed, meaning Trump no longer faces state charges in Georgia. Trump’s lawyer said the case was political persecution, and Trump posted his own remarks on social media. The case had accused Trump and 18 others of trying to overturn his 2020 election loss in Georgia.
