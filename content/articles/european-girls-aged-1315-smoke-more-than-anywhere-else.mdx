Europe’s girls aged 13‑15 are smoking at the highest rate in the world, WHO says. A third of European teens vape, and about 4 million youngsters in the region use tobacco products. Europe could remain the biggest global consumer of tobacco by 2030 unless stricter rules are enforced. Countries like Belgium, Denmark and the Netherlands are cutting back by banning flavours and advertising, showing a way forward.
