David Keohan, a Waterford construction worker, has brought back Ireland’s forgotten sport of stone lifting. Using Instagram and TikTok, he hunts and lifts historic boulders, telling the stories behind each one. His efforts have sparked a new community, inspired competitions, and even a Dublin gym with a sponsored stone‑lifting studio. Keohan hopes the tradition will join Ireland’s intangible cultural heritage list and earn UNESCO recognition.
