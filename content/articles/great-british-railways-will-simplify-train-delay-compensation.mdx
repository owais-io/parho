Great British Railways (GBR) will let passengers claim money back for delayed trains straight from the site where they bought tickets, ending the current maze of different operators. The new system will handle refunds for tickets bought from third‑party sites if both retailer and customer agree. The move aims to cut fraud, speed up payments and give passengers a clearer, single process for getting back money when trains run late.
