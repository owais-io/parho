In Glasgow, the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital is facing new investigations after two more patients died, bringing the total to nine deaths linked to contaminated water and ventilation that spread infections among cancer patients, many children. The Crown Office says it will keep families updated. The hospital has apologized, and the inquiry heard evidence that politics may have pushed the hospital to open before it was ready, raising concerns about patient safety.
