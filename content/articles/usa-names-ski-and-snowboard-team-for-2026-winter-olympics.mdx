The US has named a 97‑member ski and snowboard team for the 2026 Winter Games in Italy. Legendary alpine stars Lindsey Vonn, Mikaela Shiffrin and snowboarder Chloe Kim make the roster, while 48 newcomers earn their first Olympic spot. Vonn, 41, is back after a knee injury and is a medal contender. Kim, a two‑time champion, is hoping to win again despite a shoulder injury. The team includes a mix of young talent and seasoned veterans.
