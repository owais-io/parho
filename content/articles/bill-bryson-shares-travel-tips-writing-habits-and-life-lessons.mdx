Bryson says walking is the best way to discover a city, finding surprises like Sydney’s Harbour Bridge. He joked there are now twice as many moons as he knew, and he admits every book starts with finding his voice. He dislikes people saying “me and” and scoffs at unsolicited advice. He also joked he once imagined people could vaporise, but is learning to be kinder. His new book is out now.
