Bill Gates said he regrets ever knowing Jeffrey Epstein and apologized for his past meetings with the disgraced financier. The apology followed comments from his ex‑wife, Melinda French Gates, who called the relationship “muck” and urged investigations into their friendship. Gates denied any wrongdoing, said he never visited Epstein’s private island, and blamed the connection on a mistaken attempt to secure investment for science projects.
