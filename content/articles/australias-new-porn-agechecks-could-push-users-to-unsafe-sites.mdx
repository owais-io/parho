Australia has made porn sites check ages before letting people see adult content. To do this, users must show a photo of themselves or a government ID, and some sites now block Australian visitors. The move is meant to protect kids, but many people are frustrated. They’re using VPNs to get around the limits, and some creators are moving to other platforms. Critics say the law may push traffic to unsafe, illegal sites and hurt sex workers.
