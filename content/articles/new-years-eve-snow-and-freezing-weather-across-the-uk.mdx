Snow and near‑freezing temperatures are expected on New Year’s Eve across the UK. Edinburgh’s Hogmanay will still take place, but the popular Primrose Hill view in London is closed for safety. Weather warnings warn of snow in many regions, so extra layers and extra travel time are advised. Temperatures will be around 1‑2 °C in London and 2‑3 °C in Cardiff, with clear skies for fireworks but cold conditions for those on the ground.
