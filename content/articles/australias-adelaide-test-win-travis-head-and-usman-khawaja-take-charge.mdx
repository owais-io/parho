Australia won the Adelaide Test against England, ending the high‑energy 'Bazball' era. Travis Head made a 69‑ball century, while Usman Khawaja stayed unbeaten on 142, showing steady, sensible play. Their partnership gave Australia a big lead and highlighted local talent from South Australia. England's aggressive style faltered, and the win marks a shift to more traditional, solid batting for the Australian side.
