McLaren says it will keep the same rules for its drivers, Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri, during the 2026 Formula One season. The team has chosen not to give any driver a head start or tell them to hold back, even after last year’s close fight almost cost Norris a world title. Team boss Andrea Stella said the fairness policy will stay, and the new car is ready. He’s confident the team is stronger than ever.
