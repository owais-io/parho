Documents show former Barclays chief Jes Staley was one of Jeffrey Epstein’s estate trustees until 2015, even though he said he never accepted the role. The papers also include allegations that Staley sexually abused a woman, but prosecutors have not charged him. Staley has denied any wrongdoing and was sued by regulators over his ties to Epstein. The revelations raise questions about his statements in court.
