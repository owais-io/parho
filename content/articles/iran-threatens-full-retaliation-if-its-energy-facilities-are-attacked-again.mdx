Iran’s foreign minister said the country would use all its power against Israel and other Gulf rivals if energy sites were hit again, after Israel struck the joint South Pars gas field with Iran. Iranian strikes on Qatar’s Ras Laffan gas complex and other neighbors have sent global oil and gas prices higher and caused stock markets to tumble. Washington, Europe and Gulf states are watching closely, as the conflict could shut down the Hormuz Strait and disrupt trade.
