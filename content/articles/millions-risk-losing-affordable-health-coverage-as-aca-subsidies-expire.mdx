The deadline to sign up for ACA health plans ended last month, leaving many without clear help. Federal subsidies that kept premiums low have expired, and lawmakers can’t agree on new ones. Without subsidies, monthly costs could double. Stories from retirees, small‑business owners, and families with chronic illnesses show how higher premiums threaten their health and finances. Some are forced to drop coverage or look for jobs that provide insurance.
