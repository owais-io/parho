A community walk in North Shields shows how local groups can turn anger into pride. Citizens UK teamed up with a Catholic priest, schools, a bus company and the council to fix problems. Teenagers got a school counsellor and 8,500 mental‑health staff nationwide. Students won cheaper bus fares for under‑21s. Parents cleaned a park and secured a safe crossing. The effort fights hate and shows people can be heard.
