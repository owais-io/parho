A coalition of civil‑society groups has lowered the U.S. civic‑health rating from “narrowed” to “obstructed.” The change follows a year of Trump‑era executive orders, new laws, and crackdowns on free speech. The report says the government is using surveillance, bureaucracy, and hostile statements to weaken NGOs and other civil‑society groups, marking a sharp drop in the country’s civic space.
