Cyclist Simon Yates retires from professional racing at 33. He announced the move on social media, saying he feels proud and at peace after a 13‑year career that included a stunning 2024 Giro d’Italia win, a 2018 Vuelta title, 11 top‑level stage wins and 36 overall victories. Yates thanked teammates, staff and fans for their support, noting that the toughest moments helped shape his success.
