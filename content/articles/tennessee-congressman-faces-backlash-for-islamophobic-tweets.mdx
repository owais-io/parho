Tennessee Congressman Andy Ogles posted several hateful tweets saying Muslims don’t belong in America. His remarks, which linked Muslim names to violent incidents, drew fire from Democrats and anti‑hate groups. They called him an anti‑Muslim extremist and urged him to apologize. Ogles has also pushed bills to stop immigration from Muslim‑majority countries. His comments show how Islamophobia is rising in U.S. politics.
