Tehran says it will restart nuclear talks with Washington only if the U.S. speaks with dignity and respect. Foreign minister Abbas Araghchi told The Guardian that after Israel and the U.S. attacked Iranian sites, Iran still has no hidden nuclear sites and wants diplomacy, not military force. He warned the U.S. not to treat Iran as weak, said the 12‑day war showed the need for better dialogue, and that talks could resume if offers are clear.
