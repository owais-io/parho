David Hart, 22, was given a one‑year jail sentence in New York for making 95 fake bomb threats to UK places. He called hospitals, bars, supermarkets and even a police centre, mostly in London. Police said he knew his calls would be taken seriously. The threats led to searches and a hospital lockdown. Scotland Yard and US authorities worked together on the case.
