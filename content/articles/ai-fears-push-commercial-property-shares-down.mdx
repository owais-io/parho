Investors panic about AI cutting jobs and office demand, sending shares of commercial property firms like Savills, Regus, CBRE and others down. The sell‑off followed AI buzz from companies like Anthropic. Some analysts say the fall might be over‑reacted, but they warn that AI could reduce the need for office work and hurt profits. Despite losses, firms like CBRE still report solid revenue growth and expect long‑term benefits from AI.
