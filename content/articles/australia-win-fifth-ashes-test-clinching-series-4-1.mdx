Australia won the fifth Ashes Test at Sydney by chasing 160 runs with five wickets to spare, securing a 4‑1 series win. England struggled on both sides, missing chances and lacking bowl‑man consistency. Key Australian performers were Travis Head, Mitchell Starc and keeper Alex Carey, while England’s Ben Stokes missed the match. Jacob Bethell’s 154 was a bright spot for England, but Australia’s steady play decided it.
