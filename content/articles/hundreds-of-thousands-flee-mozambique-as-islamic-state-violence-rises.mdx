An Islamic State group has been fighting in northern Mozambique for years, forcing people to leave their homes. Since July, more than 300,000 people have fled, and the country has seen almost 1 million displaced over the last few years. The army and a Rwandan force that joined in 2021 are struggling to stop the attacks, which have killed hundreds of civilians. Aid is low, leaving many without food, shelter or medical help.
