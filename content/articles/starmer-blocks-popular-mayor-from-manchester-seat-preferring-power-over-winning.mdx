Starmer blocked Andy Burnham, a popular Manchester mayor, from standing as Labour’s candidate in a by‑election. He argues it protects his own position, but critics say it shows a focus on control rather than defeating the far‑right Reform UK. By preventing Burnham, Labour risks splitting the anti‑Reform UK vote, hurting its chances in a key seat where a united left could win.
