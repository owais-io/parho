Trump posted on Truth Social that he was glad former FBI director Robert Mueller had died. The comment sparked a backlash from both sides of politics. Republicans, Democrats, former officials and advisers called Trump vile, disgusting, and a hypocrite. Critics highlighted Mueller's service and role in investigating Trump. The outcry added to Trump’s record of speaking badly about the dead, drawing sharp condemnation.
