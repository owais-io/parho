New South Wales Premier Chris Minns said he would not apologise to the Muslim community after police forced men to stop praying during a protest against Israeli President Isaac Herzog. Muslim groups and Human Rights Watch called for an apology and an investigation, citing videos of police beating people kneeling in prayer. The premier blamed the police for acting in a riot.
