Rishi Sunak said he is British, English and British Asian after right‑wing voices doubted his identity. He joined a commission on community cohesion, warning that provocative, racist language is rewarded in media. Sunak shared how he and his siblings faced racism in Southampton and urged the UK not to slip back into open racism. He also touched on immigration, saying earlier limits should have been tighter, and praised his successor, a Black woman from Nigeria.
