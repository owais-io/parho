After a 22‑0 defeat by Ireland at Twickenham, England’s captain George Ford said the team will hold a “properly honest” and “uncomfortable” review to find the problems that led to the collapse. The loss exposed missed kicks, bad tackling and weak early pressure, raising questions about coach Steve Borthwick’s tactics and squad choices. England will use the findings to try and bounce back before their next game against Italy.
