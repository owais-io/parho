The South Australian premier says he didn’t pressure the Adelaide Writers’ Week board to remove Palestinian academic Randa Abdel‑Fattah. The board cancelled her talk because of “cultural sensitivity” after the Bondi attack. Some top arts figures wrote to the premier asking the decision be reversed, saying it hurt the festival’s reputation. The premier says he can’t direct the board but supports the board’s choice.
