A Ukrainian drone strike set fire to an oil tank and warehouses at Russia’s Taman port, wounding two people. Russian drones also damaged infrastructure in Ukraine’s Odesa region, cutting power and water. The attack came right before a fresh round of Geneva talks aimed at ending the war. Ukraine is pressing European allies for more air‑defence aid and wants security guarantees before any deal, while Russia seeks to lift sanctions and secure the Donbas region.
