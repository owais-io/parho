The U.S. Department of Homeland Security just signed a $140 million contract to buy six Boeing 737 planes from Daedalus Aviation. These planes will be used by ICE to fly people out of the country. Officials say the new aircraft will let ICE use cheaper flight routes and save taxpayers money. The deal is part of a larger effort under President Trump to speed up deportations.
