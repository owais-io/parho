Billionaire media owner Robert Allbritton says he’s glad about the Washington Post’s layoffs, because they let him hire top Post reporters for his new online outlet, Notus. He’s expanding Notus’s staff and wants to focus on Washington‑area politics and federal news. Allbritton also runs a journalism training program and is looking to rename Notus to include “Washington.” He says the Post cuts make buying it unlikely, but he’s ready to grow his own news brand instead.
