Israel’s airstrikes and tank shelling killed at least 21 people in Gaza, including six children and seven women. A medic was also shot down during a second strike in Khan Younis. The attacks hit Gaza City and Khan Younis. At the same time, Israel shut down the Rafah border crossing that had just reopened, stopping patients from leaving for treatment. The move was part of a fragile ceasefire that still sees daily fighting.
