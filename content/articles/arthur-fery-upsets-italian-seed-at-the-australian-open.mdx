British 23‑year‑old Arthur Fery shocked the crowd by beating 20th‑seeded Italian Flavio Cobolli 7‑6, 6‑4, 6‑1 in his opening match at the Australian Open. It was his first grand‑slam win outside Wimbledon and the start of a run where he hasn’t lost a set yet. Cobolli was dealing with an upset stomach, while Fery, a former Stanford college player, played calmly and took control from the baseline.
