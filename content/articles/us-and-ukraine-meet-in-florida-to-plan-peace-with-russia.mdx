Ukrainian negotiators met US officials in Florida to fine‑tune a 28‑point plan that could end the war with Russia. The US side, led by Secretary of State Marco Rubio, Special Envoy Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner, wants Ukraine to agree to conditions that include ending the war and ensuring future prosperity. Ukraine says it will not recognize Russian‑taken land or give up its own choices about NATO or the EU. The talks were hard but seen as a step forward.
