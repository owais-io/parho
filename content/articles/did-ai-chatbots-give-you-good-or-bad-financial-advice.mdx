Tech giants are spending billions on AI, and chatbots like ChatGPT and Google Gemini are becoming popular for everyday help. But when people ask them for money or debt advice, the answers can sometimes be wrong or misleading. A survey says 80% of Brits think AI should be regulated. The Guardian is asking readers who have used AI for finance to share their experiences—what was suggested, whether it was useful, and if it caused any problems.
