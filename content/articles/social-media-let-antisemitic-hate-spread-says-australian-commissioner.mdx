The Australian race‑discrimination commissioner, Giridharan Sivaraman, says social media firms let racist and antisemitic hate flow unchecked because it can make money. He says the government should adopt a national anti‑racism plan that includes legal protection and a task force to stop online hate. He also warns that after the Bondi beach attack, hateful messages could spark more violence, especially against Jewish people.
