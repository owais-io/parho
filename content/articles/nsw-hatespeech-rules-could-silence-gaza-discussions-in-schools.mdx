New rules in New South Wales let schools fire teachers for hate‑speech comments inside or outside class. The move comes after a terror attack and a new state hate‑speech law. Teachers and civil‑rights groups say the rules could hush debates about Gaza and Palestine, making classrooms feel unsafe for tough questions. The government insists it protects students, but critics worry it limits free speech.
