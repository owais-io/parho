Queensland's natural resources minister told a court that his earlier order to fight every new native title claim has been changed. The order, announced last month, forced the state to move many claims into court. The minister later said the state would not fight every claim, but the court heard that the government is still unsure which cases to take to trial. The move has left claimants and lawyers confused and many claims stalled.
