Cariad Lloyd and Rachel Parris met while doing improv comedy and started the group Austentatious. They now perform at London’s Vaudeville Theatre. Their friendship has lasted 14 years and they help each other through hard times, like losing parents and pregnancy struggles. They talk about life, laugh together and support each other on and off stage. The article shows how comedy can create lasting bonds.
