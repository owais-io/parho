Storm Claudia has caused severe flooding across Britain and Ireland, leading officials to declare a major incident. In Monmouth, Wales, fire crews are working with police, ambulance and other teams to evacuate residents and keep roads clear. The Met Office warns that rain will continue in England and Wales, while Northern Ireland expects drier weather. Power cuts and travel disruptions are already happening, and the UK Health Security Agency has issued a cold‑weather warning.
