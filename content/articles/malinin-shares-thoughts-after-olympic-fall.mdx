Malinin, 21, fell twice at the Winter Olympics, finishing 8th overall. He posted an Instagram video mixing happy moments with a clip of him holding his head in despair, explaining how pressure and online hate pushed him to a mental crash. He admitted nerves overwhelmed him, recalling traumatic moments. Despite the fall, he helped the U.S. win team gold. The story highlights athlete mental health.
