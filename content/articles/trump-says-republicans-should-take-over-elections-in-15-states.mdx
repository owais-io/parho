On a conservative podcast, former President Donald Trump said Republicans should “take over” elections in 15 states to stop what he calls illegal voting by undocumented immigrants. He claimed the party would lose otherwise, despite no evidence of fraud. Trump’s comments come after an FBI raid on Georgia ballots and as lawmakers consider bills tightening voter‑ID rules and limiting mail‑in voting.
