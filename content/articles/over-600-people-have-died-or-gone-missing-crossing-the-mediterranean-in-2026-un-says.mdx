The UN says at least 606 refugees have died or gone missing trying to reach Europe in 2026, the worst start to a year in more than a decade. A boat that left Libya capsized near Greece, killing 4 people. In Italy, dozens of bodies washed up on beaches, prompting bishops to blame politics. The far‑right government passed a bill to block boats, tightening the fight against irregular migration.
