An explosion hit a Shia mosque outside Islamabad on Friday prayers, killing 31 people and injuring more than 160. Police think a suicide bomber may have carried out the attack, and the Islamic State said it was responsible. Leaders in Pakistan condemned the bombing and promised full investigations. Hospitals are treating many wounded, and the government is calling for blood donations. The attack adds to rising violence across the country.
