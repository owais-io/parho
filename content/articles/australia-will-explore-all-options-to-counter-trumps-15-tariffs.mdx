After President Trump lifted a 10% tariff and then raised it to 15% on all U.S. imports, Australia says it will look at every possible response. Trade Minister Don Farrell said Canberra will work with its Washington embassy to understand the impact and decide how best to act. The Australian government says the new duties are unfair and hurt everyday consumers, while experts warn that uncertainty could slow economic activity.
