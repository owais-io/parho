California teens say the court’s decision could end the chance for trans kids to play on teams that match gender. They describe sports as a way to make friends, stay healthy, and feel normal, but bans in some states make them worry about being excluded or harassed. The Supreme Court case comes after 27 states passed rules that keep trans kids from school teams, while 20 still support them. The young athletes urge judges to keep the right to play.
