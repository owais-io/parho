After the Supreme Court struck down Trump’s tariffs, major US business groups are calling for quick refunds of the money they paid. The National Retail Federation and the Chamber of Commerce say the refunds could give $133 bn back to importers, boost hiring and lower costs for families. The ruling left the refund process unclear, and Trump has tried to re‑apply tariffs in other ways, making the issue messy and likely to take years to resolve.
