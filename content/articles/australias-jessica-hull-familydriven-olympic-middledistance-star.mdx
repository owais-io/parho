Jessica Hull has turned her family into her biggest advantage. She left the UK to train with her dad back in Australia and has since become one of the world’s fastest 800‑ and 1500‑metre runners. After winning Olympic silver in 2024 and breaking the world record in the 2000m, she now focuses on improving herself, not just beating rivals. Her husband and close friends stay in her inner circle, keeping the pressure low and the goals clear.
