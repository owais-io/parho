Trump urged Ukraine to reach a deal with Russia fast before U.S.-brokered Geneva talks, saying the war would end sooner if Kyiv complied. He warned that Russia had stalled in Abu Dhabi talks and that chances of a breakthrough are low. Trump’s envoy and son‑in‑law will join the U.S. delegation, and he blamed President Zelensky for slowing talks. He also criticized California’s green‑energy agreement with the UK, calling the governor a loser.
