In New Zealand’s Ruapehu region, the closing of a big pulp mill and other businesses has left hundreds of people out of work. The town of Ohakune and nearby Raetihi now have empty shops and many houses for sale. Residents are moving to other parts of the country or even to Australia. Local leaders say the community is trying to keep jobs and support each other, but the problem is still big.
