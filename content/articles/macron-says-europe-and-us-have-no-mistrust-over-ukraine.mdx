Macron says Europe and the US have no mistrust over Ukraine. He told reporters in China that the two sides must work together. A leaked call said Macron had warned the US could betray Ukraine, but he denies it. Meanwhile, European leaders and the EU are trying to raise money for Ukraine, debating whether to use frozen Russian assets. US talks with Ukraine and Russia continue, while the war keeps killing civilians.
