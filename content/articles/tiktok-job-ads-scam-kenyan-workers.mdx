Lilian, a Kenyan in Qatar, was tricked by a TikTok recruiter called WorldPath. She paid 150,000 shillings for a fake job in Amsterdam and never got the visa or a refund. Guardian says more than a dozen unregistered agencies use TikTok to scam job‑seekers with fake papers and promises. Kenyan youth, stuck in high unemployment, risk losing savings or taking loans to pay commissions. The government is blacklisting agencies, but new fake accounts keep popping up. Some victims have gone to court.
