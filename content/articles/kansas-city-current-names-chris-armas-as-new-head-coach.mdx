Kansas City Current announced former MLS boss Chris Armas as their new women's head coach. Armas comes after leaving Colorado Rapids and has managed New York Red Bulls and Toronto FC, plus assistant roles at Manchester United and Leeds. He also has college coaching experience. The 53‑year‑old takes over from Vlatko Andonovski, who stepped down to focus on the club’s sporting director job. The team hopes his experience will help them reach new heights.
