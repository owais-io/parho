Labour leader Keir Starmer told MPs he is willing to consider a ban on social media for children under 16, following worries about the time kids spend online and the harm it can cause. He had previously opposed such a ban but said he will look at Australia’s model and keep options open. Other parties, including Conservatives and Liberal Democrats, also support a review, and a decision may come in a few months.
