Anthony Joshua was in a car crash on the Lagos‑Ibadan road. His vehicle hit a stationary truck, killing two of his close friends – a strength coach and a trainer. Joshua was injured but is stable in hospital. Police say the crash may have been caused by speeding and reckless overtaking. The incident has shocked his team and the boxing community.
