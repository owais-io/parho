Reform UK says it is now Britain’s biggest party, claiming 268,000 members, overtaking Labour, which has dropped below 250,000. Labour's membership has slipped from a high of 500,000 to just over 333,000 this year. The party also received a record £9m donation from a Thai businessman, boosting its campaign funds. Meanwhile, the Greens have grown, while the Conservatives’ numbers have fallen.
