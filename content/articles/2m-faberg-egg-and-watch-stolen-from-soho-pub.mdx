A thief stole a £2m Fabergé egg and a gold watch from a Soho pub in London. He took them from Rosie Dawson, who works for a whiskey company, and also stole her laptop and credit cards. He was caught in Belfast, tried in court, and faces sentencing. The police still search for the missing egg and watch, which belong to the whiskey company.
