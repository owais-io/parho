Tanning apps that promise “safe” sun routines are being pushed to kids as young as four, raising alarm among health experts. Influencers and flashy ads lure teens into a new tanning craze, but doctors say no safe tan exists—UV exposure always damages skin and can lead to cancer later. The apps even suggest oils and accelerants, adding risk. Public health groups urge Australians to ditch these risky apps.
