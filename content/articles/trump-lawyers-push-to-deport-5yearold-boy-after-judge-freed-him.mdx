5‑year‑old Liam Conejo Ramos, who was released last month, faces new deportation action from the Trump administration. The DHS says it is seeking a standard removal order for the Ecuadorian boy and his father, even after a judge ordered their release. Lawmakers and supporters argue the move is retaliatory and violates legal precedent. The family plans to fight in court to protect their asylum claims and keep the child in the US.
