A U.S. citizen was forced into his Minnesota home by ICE agents without a warrant. He was handcuffed, left outside in only underwear, and taken to a remote spot for photos while it was freezing. The family says the agents were after alleged sex offenders, but no one in the house is on the registry. The incident angered local officials and the man plans a civil‑rights lawsuit.
