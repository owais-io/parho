After a surprise change in his position, President Trump now backs a House plan to make all unclassified documents about Jeffrey Epstein public. Democrats and Republicans, led by Ro Khanna and Thomas Massie, have been pushing the bill, and Marjorie Taylor Greene joined the effort. If the House passes the vote, the bill will go to the Senate and then to Trump to sign. The move could lead to a unanimous vote and new transparency.
