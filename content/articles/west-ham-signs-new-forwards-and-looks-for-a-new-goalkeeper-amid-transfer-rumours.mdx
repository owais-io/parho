West Ham have signed two new forwards, Pablo Felipe and Taty Castellanos, and are considering a new goalkeeper after an offer for Bento. Meanwhile, Manchester City are eyeing Marc Guéhi while Liverpool had interest in an England international. Other clubs are busy: Besiktas are after Burnley’s left‑back, Bournemouth want winger Jamie Leweling but rejected his bid, and clubs across the Championship and lower leagues are chasing players to strengthen their squads.
