Liverpool and Fulham drew 2‑2, with Liverpool's Wirtz praising the effort but wanting more control. Arsenal edged Bournemouth 3‑2, while Manchester United’s two‑man attack struggled, leaving United stuck at 1‑1. Tottenham and Sunderland also drew 1‑1, showing the league's unpredictability, highlighting the challenges managers face and players' performances.
