President Donald Trump called mail‑in voting “cheating” during a talk in Memphis, even though he himself sent a ballot for a Florida special election. Trump, who has repeatedly blamed mail ballots for fraud, said the system needs to change and is urging lawmakers to add strict rules, including proof of citizenship and limits on who can vote by mail. His comments add to the debate over election rules in the U.S.
