Finland’s new ski‑jump head coach, Igor Medved, apologized after being sent home from the Winter Olympics for breaking team rules by drinking alcohol. The Finnish Olympic committee confirmed his departure and said they would decide his future after the Games. The move was made to protect athlete focus and maintain team discipline.
