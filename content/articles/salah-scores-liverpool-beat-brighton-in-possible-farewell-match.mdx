Mohamed Salah made a tearful but triumphant return to Anfield, helping Liverpool beat Brighton 2-0. He came on early, set up a corner that let Hugo Ekitiké score, and almost scored himself. The win feels like a send‑off for Salah, who is heading to the Africa Cup of Nations soon. Coach Arne Slot praised the team’s unity, and the crowd sang his name as the match ended.
