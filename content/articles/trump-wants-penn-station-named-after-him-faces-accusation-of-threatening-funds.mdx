Donald Trump is pushing Congress to rename New York’s Penn Station and Washington Dulles Airport after him. He says he’ll release blocked federal money for a $16 billion tunnel project if the renaming passes, effectively threatening to hold the funds hostage. The move has sparked lawsuits, a judge’s order to release money, and a sharp backlash from voters who see it as self‑promotion and corruption.
