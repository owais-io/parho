EU leaders want to speed up talks with Britain to keep a new post‑Brexit deal alive. The main stumbling block is whether EU students should pay the same tuition as Brits, which could cost the UK a lot more. Both sides also wrestle with food‑safety rules and a new trade plan. Leaders say they need to “change gears” and finish agreements before the July summit, or the reset could collapse.
