Delta Air Lines flight 41 from Los Angeles hit heavy turbulence while descending into Sydney, injuring four crew members, including three flight attendants. They were taken to Royal Prince Alfred Hospital after the plane landed safely with no passenger injuries. The Airbus A350 carried 245 passengers and 15 crew. Ambulances arrived about 15 minutes after touchdown, treating five patients, all with minor injuries.
