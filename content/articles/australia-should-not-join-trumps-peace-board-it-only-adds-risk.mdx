Donald Trump has invited Australia to join a new “peace board” that already includes controversial leaders like Putin and Orbán. Analysts say the board offers no real benefits, no checks on power, and could drag Australia into legal and political trouble. Australian politicians are cautious; some want more details before deciding, while experts warn it would undermine the country’s rule‑based foreign policy.
