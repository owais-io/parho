During Trump's second term, his government cut diversity offices, tightened birth‑right citizenship rules, and denied transgender people the right to have their gender identity on passports. More than 100 lawsuits have been filed. The article follows four key cases: a detainee denied medical care, a protester held for speaking about Gaza, a trans woman who got a wrong gender mark on her passport, and a pastor fighting for immigrant worshippers.
