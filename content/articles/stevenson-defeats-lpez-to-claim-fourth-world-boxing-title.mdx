Shakur Stevenson outboxed Teófimo López in a 12‑round decision at Madison Square Garden, earning a fourth world title. He dominated the fight with a fast jab, smart footwork, and a calm, clinical approach, landing 165 punches to López’s 72. López couldn’t find his rhythm and was outclassed on every round, leading to a unanimous 119‑109 win for Stevenson. The bout drew a record crowd and showcased Stevenson's composure and skill.
