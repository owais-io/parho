Since the 1989 privatisation of water in England and Wales, profits have gone to investors instead of keeping pipes clean and safe. People now face higher bills, polluted rivers, and lost wildlife. The TV series *Dirty Business* exposed the tragic death of eight‑year‑old Heather Preen and the government’s failure to enforce rules. The article argues that removing the profit motive is the only way to protect clean water for future generations.
