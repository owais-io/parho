In Mount Gambier, a 20‑year‑old woman, Amelia Vanderhorst, was charged after she stuck large novelty googly eyes on the town’s $136,000 ‘Blue Blob’ sculpture. The eyes damaged the painting, leaving holes, and the city’s council called the act disrespectful. The case was moved to a January hearing while the lawyer negotiates. If convicted, it could be the first time someone is punished for a googly‑eye prank.
