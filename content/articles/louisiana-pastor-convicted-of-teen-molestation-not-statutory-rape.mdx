A Louisiana pastor, Milton Otto Martin III, was found guilty of molesting a teenage girl in his church but cleared of statutory rape. The 58‑year‑old faces up to seven years in prison and must register as a sex offender. The case hinged on one survivor’s testimony, while other church members backed the pastor. A civil suit now targets the church for failing to act on earlier abuse reports. Sentencing is scheduled for January.
