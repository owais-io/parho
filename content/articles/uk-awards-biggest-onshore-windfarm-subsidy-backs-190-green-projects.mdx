The UK government has granted the biggest onshore windfarm subsidy yet – the Imerys project near St Austell, Cornwall – as part of a package that gives 190 contracts for renewable energy. The deal also backs 157 solar farms, 28 onshore wind sites, 8 offshore wind farms and 4 tidal projects, enough to power about 16 million UK homes by 2030. Prices are set to keep solar and wind cheaper than gas.
