Trump tried to rename Washington’s Kennedy Center as the “Trump‑Kennedy Center.” The change sparked anger. Several musicians—jazz group Cookers, drummer Chuck Redd, folk singer Kristy Lee—canceled performances. The center’s president, a Trump appointee, threatened a lawsuit over Redd’s cancellation. The band said they quit because the name change stole freedom of expression. The backlash shows artists are opposing the politicization of culture.
