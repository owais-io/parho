Alyssa Healy started her cricket career feeling unsure and facing big family expectations. She scored over 3,000 runs and made 269 catches as a wicket‑keeper, winning six T20 World Cups, two ODI World Cups and many domestic titles. Her short Test career shows how few long‑form games women get. Healy also helped raise women’s cricket’s profile, getting a marketing deal and becoming a household name. Despite criticism, she remained resilient and now plans a home‑front farewell.
