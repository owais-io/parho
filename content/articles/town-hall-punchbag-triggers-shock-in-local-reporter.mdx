A journalist from the Melksham News was shocked when he found a photo of his face on a punchbag in the town hall. He showed the picture at a council meeting, and the mayor said it was unacceptable. The punchbag was apparently added by council staff. An investigation has been started, and the incident raises concerns about how reporters are treated in local government.
