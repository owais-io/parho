HelloFresh’s sales dropped sharply in 2025, prompting the company to cut 900 UK jobs and shut a delivery hub in Nuneaton. Revenue fell more than 11%, wiping out 93% of its market value since 2021. The firm is trimming staff, exiting Spain and Italy, and warns of another 6% revenue dip in 2026 as consumers shift toward real food and the US faces quality and production issues.
