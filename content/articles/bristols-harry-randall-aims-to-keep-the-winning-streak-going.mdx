Harry Randall has come back from a hamstring injury and marked his 150th game for Bristol. The club is on a five‑match winning run and is set to play Sale as they try to keep the momentum for the playoffs. Randall said the team’s hard work and improved defence have paid off, and he hopes to prove himself for England before the Six Nations. Coach Lam and the forwards are key to creating chances for the fast backs.
