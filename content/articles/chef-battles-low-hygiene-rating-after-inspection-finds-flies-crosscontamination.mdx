Gareth Ward, chef of the two‑star Michelin restaurant Ynyshir in Wales, says the local health inspectors’ one‑star hygiene rating is unfair. Inspectors flagged dead flies, possible cross‑contamination and raw lobster service. Ward feels the inspectors don’t understand his kitchen’s unique setup and vows to fight the decision. Ynyshir says the issues were minor, fixed quickly, and the council defends its inspection process.
