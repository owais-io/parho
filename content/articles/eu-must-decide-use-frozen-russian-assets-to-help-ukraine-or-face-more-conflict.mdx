EU leaders in Brussels are fighting over whether to use €210 billion of frozen Russian money to fund Ukraine for two years. Belgium and some other countries worry about legal problems and Russian pressure. Ukraine’s president wants the money to keep fighting. Germany, the EU commission, and most other leaders say it is the best way to pressure Russia and help Ukraine. Hungary and a few others say it is a bad idea. The meeting will decide the future.
