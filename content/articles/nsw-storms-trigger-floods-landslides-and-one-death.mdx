Heavy summer storms on Australia's east coast left one dead and many people in danger as flood waters rose. In Sydney's northern beaches, residents were evacuated from Narrabeen Lagoon overnight and later allowed back, while a landslide hit homes near Great Mackerel beach. New South Wales emergency crews answered over 1,400 calls, rescued more than 20 people from flooded cars, and volunteers helped clean up. A woman died when a tree branch fell on her car in Wollongong.
