World No.1 Carlos Alcaraz won 7‑6, 6‑4, 7‑5 over Tommy Paul in the Australian Open third round, moving into the quarter‑finals without losing a set. The win marks his third straight year reaching Melbourne Park’s last eight and his first time doing so without a set dropped. Alcaraz, who has already won two titles at each of the other three majors, now aims for his first Aussie Open semi‑final.
