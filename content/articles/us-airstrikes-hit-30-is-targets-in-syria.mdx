US conducted 10 airstrikes between Feb 3‑12 that hit more than 30 Islamic State sites in Syria. The attacks were part of Operation Hawkeye Strike, following an IS ambush that killed two US soldiers in Palmyra. Washington is leading the international coalition, working with the Kurdish‑led Syrian Democratic Forces, and has recently turned to Damascus as its main anti‑IS partner. The US is also moving detainees out of Syria and pulling back troops, closing its base at al‑Tanf.
