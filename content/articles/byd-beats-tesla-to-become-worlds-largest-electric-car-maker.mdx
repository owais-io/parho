In 2025, Chinese automaker BYD sold 2.26 million electric cars, outpacing Tesla’s 1.63 million and becoming the world's biggest EV maker. The win shows China’s growing influence in the auto industry. Tesla’s sales dropped after U.S. policy cuts and backlash over Elon Musk’s politics. BYD’s sales grew 28 % and its commercial electric vehicles doubled, highlighting China’s rising role in electric cars.
