Study shows nearly 2 million military people have died, been wounded or gone missing in the Ukraine war. Most losses are on the Russian side – about 325 000 killed out of 1.2 million total – while Ukraine lost 500 000‑600 000, with 100 000‑140 000 killed. UN says around 15 000 civilians have died, likely more, and dozens are missing. A recent Russian drone hit a passenger train, killing five people.
