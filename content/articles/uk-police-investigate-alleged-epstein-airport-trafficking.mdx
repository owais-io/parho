UK police are investigating claims that Jeffrey Epstein used private flights into UK airports to traffic women, after US court documents were released. Focus is on Stansted Airport and flights from Latvia, Russia and Lithuania. Four police forces – Essex, Thames Valley, Surrey and Bedfordshire – have begun checks, with a national group coordinating. The probe also touches high‑profile figures, including Prince Andrew’s brother, and could lead to criminal investigations.
