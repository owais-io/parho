New bushfires in Victoria are spreading because of a heatwave and a sudden wind shift. At least 20 homes in Longwood’s Ruffy area are on fire and three people are missing. The state’s biggest fire victims, Kinglake and Marysville, are told to be ready to leave. Meanwhile, 50 writers are boycotting the Adelaide festival after it dropped Palestinian scholar Randa Abdel‑Fattah. In a lighter note, a strange pasta dish from Toowoomba has become an internet craze.
