During a Senate hearing, DHS Secretary Kristi Noem stood by her claim that two Minnesotans killed by ICE agents were “domestic terrorists,” refusing to retract the statement. She also denied that federal agents follow arrest quotas and said the agency’s operations were “targeted.” Democrats blasted her for the killings and for spending on ads, while some Republicans asked about oversight. Several lawmakers called for Noem’s resignation or removal.
