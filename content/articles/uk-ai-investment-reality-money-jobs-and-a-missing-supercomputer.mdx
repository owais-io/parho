Investments in Britain’s AI boom look more like hype than real money. A Guardian investigation found that the promised $100bn Nvidia‑OpenAI deal vanished and the supercomputer site in Loughton is still just a scaffolding yard. Much of the “investment” is just buying chips from abroad, not new spending, and it creates only a few maintenance jobs. The UK’s AI push may be more about marketing than genuine growth.
