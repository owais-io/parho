Heavy rain triggered flash floods on Oahu, prompting the rescue of more than 230 people and evacuation orders for about 5,500 residents. Water levels at a key dam rose to almost its limit, raising fears of a collapse. No deaths were reported, but several people were treated for hypothermia. Officials are closely monitoring the dam and the weather as the situation unfolds.
