Arsenal needed a win to keep title hopes alive, but the women's team finished 0‑0 against Tottenham in a tense north‑London derby. They had more chances and possession, yet Spurs held firm and no goals were scored. The match drew a record crowd of 6,788 and marked Tottenham’s first win since the new manager took charge. Arsenal’s string of draws and losses has pushed them out of contention, and they face a Champions League match against Real Madrid next.
