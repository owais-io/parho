Donald Trump once threatened to take control of Greenland by force. At a Davos speech, he reversed course, saying he would not use military force and claimed a new deal was in place. The move follows a deal with NATO’s Secretary‑General and a Dutch leader, keeping Greenland under Denmark. Europe praised the U‑turn, showing it can stand up to Trump. The episode highlights how the US and Europe are negotiating power.
