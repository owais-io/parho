Daily Mail editor Paul Dacre says it was hurtful to be accused of spying on Stephen Lawrence’s mother, Doreen Lawrence, and other claimants. He told a UK court that the Daily Mail’s long‑running campaign helped bring Stephen’s killers to justice, and he denies any illegal hacking or bugging. The claimants say the paper used private investigators, but Dacre says the work was legal and that the allegations are “pre‑posterous”. The trial is still ongoing.
