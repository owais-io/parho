Police found a 70‑year‑old man dead in his car after floodwater reached Normanton in far north Queensland. While Queensland dealt with the floods, a different storm, Tropical Cyclone Hayley, hit Western Australia late Tuesday. The cyclone made landfall near Derby, bringing winds up to 140 km/h and heavy rain that could cause flash flooding. Residents were urged to stay inside, stock up on supplies, and move to evacuation centres if needed.
