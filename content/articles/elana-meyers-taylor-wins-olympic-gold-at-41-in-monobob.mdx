Elana Meyers Taylor, 41, finally earned her first Olympic gold in the monobob bobsleigh at the Milan‑Cortina Games. After competing in five Winter Games and collecting six medals, she beat Germany’s Laura Nolte in the last run. This win makes her the oldest‑ever female U.S. Winter Olympic champion and ties speed skater Bonnie Blair for most medals by a U.S. woman in Winter Games history.
