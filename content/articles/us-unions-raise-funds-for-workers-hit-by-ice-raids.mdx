US labor unions are raising money to help workers who have been targeted by Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). In Salem, Oregon, a SEIU member named Maria was attacked by ICE agents who smashed her car and left her on the street. Unions like SEIU, AFL‑CIO affiliates and Unite Here have collected over $300,000 to cover legal fees, groceries, rent and medical costs. They say ICE raids are hurting communities and businesses, and they’re standing up to protect members.
