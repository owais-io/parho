Xabi Alonso was hired to coach Real Madrid in 2023, but his ideas clashed with star players. He wanted a team‑first style, yet many players – especially Vinicius Jr and others – resisted, causing tension. Poor results in cup matches and the league added pressure. After just seven months, the club and Alonso agreed to part ways. The brief reign ended as player personalities proved too strong for his plans.
