Storm Goretti brought strong winds, heavy rain and up to 30 cm of snow to parts of the UK, leaving 380,000 homes in France and over 50,000 in the south‑west without power. Flights were delayed, Birmingham Airport stopped runway use, and many train services in Wales, Cornwall and Devon were canceled. Red and amber warnings warned of dangerous conditions, and motorists were urged to check routes before heading out.
