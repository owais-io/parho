California Governor Gavin Newsom filed a civil‑rights complaint against Dr. Mehmet Oz, the Trump‑era health agency chief, after Oz posted a video accusing Los Angeles Armenians of running a huge hospice‑care fraud scheme. Newsom says Oz’s claims are false, racist, and hurt the Armenian community, even slowing business for an Armenian bakery shown in the clip. The dispute is part of a larger feud between Newsom and the Trump administration over health‑care fraud allegations.
