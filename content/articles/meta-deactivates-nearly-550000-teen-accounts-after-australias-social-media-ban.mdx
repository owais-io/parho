Meta shut down about 544,000 teen accounts on Facebook, Instagram and Threads after Australia made it illegal for people under 16 to use social media. The ban covers 10 sites, and the company says it will keep improving its age‑checks. Teens have found ways to get around the rule and are moving to other apps that aren’t banned. Critics say the law isn’t working, and Australia may have to tighten enforcement. The UK is also watching the move.
