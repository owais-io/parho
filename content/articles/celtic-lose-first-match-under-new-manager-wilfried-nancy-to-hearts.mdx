Wilfried Nancy’s first game as Celtic manager was a tough start. He faced Hearts in a rain‑slick match that saw a last‑minute goal from Hearts’ Oisin McEntee. Celtic’s own Kieran Tierney scored just after the clock ran out, but the team struggled throughout and fans were disappointed. The loss shows how difficult Nancy’s job will be, especially after Celtic’s high hopes from last season.
