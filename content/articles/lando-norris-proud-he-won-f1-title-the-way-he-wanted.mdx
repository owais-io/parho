Lando Norris celebrated winning his first Formula One world championship, saying he did it the way he wanted – fair and not overly aggressive. He earned the title after finishing third at the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, keeping a two‑point lead over Max Verstappen. Norris praised his clean driving style and the support from his McLaren team and family. He’s proud that he proved a title can be won without being a “killer” driver.
