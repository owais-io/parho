Israel has objected to the White House naming leaders for a 'Gaza board of peace' that would run Gaza following the war. Said the appointments were made without its input and contradict its policy. The board would include Turkey’s president, Egypt’s president, UK prime minister Tony Blair, and Argentina’s president, with U.S. Secretary of State Mike Pompeo on the founding board. Israel’s Prime Minister urged the U.S. to involve him as the board plans to manage Gaza’s governance and reconstruction.
