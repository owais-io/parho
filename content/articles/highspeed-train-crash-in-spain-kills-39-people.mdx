Two high‑speed trains in southern Spain derailed and crashed into each other on Sunday night, killing 39 people and leaving 12 in intensive care. Roughly 300 passengers were on board. Emergency crews rushed to the scene in the town of Adamuz, pulling people out of twisted cars and treating injuries. Spanish officials say the cause is still unknown and are investigating the incident.
