Gun owners will face stricter rules, with licences only for Australian citizens and limits on how many weapons one can hold. The changes come after a 15‑person death and 40 injuries when a father and son opened fire on a Chanukah celebration at Bondi Beach. Prime Minister Albanese called a cabinet meeting, pledged to stop anti‑Semitic hate, and fast‑tracked a national firearms register. The government is also reviewing visa screening and hate‑crime laws.
