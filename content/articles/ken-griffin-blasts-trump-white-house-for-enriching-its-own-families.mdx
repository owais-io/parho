Ken Griffin, a top hedge‑fund boss, publicly slammed the Trump administration for helping its own families, especially Trump’s sons, with money‑making deals like crypto deals and big investments. He said the White House’s favoritism hurts ordinary business owners and shows the government is more about money than the public. Griffin, a long‑time Republican donor, said he might run for office, while the White House rebuffed his claims.
