Emergency doctors warn of a crisis as patients wait in hospital corridors. Dr. Ian Higginson says this has caused many deaths and that the situation should make people upset. He says fixing the problem needs more staff, better beds and help in communities. The government promises to stop corridor care, but the issue remains. Many patients wait over 12 hours and are on trolleys in hallways.
