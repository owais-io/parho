New Mexico’s Attorney General has ordered a fresh probe into Jeffrey Epstein’s Zorro Ranch after a public push for more answers. The state had closed the case in 2019 at New York prosecutors’ request, but new DOJ and FBI documents urge a review. A state truth commission will also examine the ranch, aiming to report by year’s end. Gov. Lujan Grisham backs the move for victim accountability.
