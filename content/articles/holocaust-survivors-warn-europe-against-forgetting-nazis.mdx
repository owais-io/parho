On Jan 27, Holocaust survivors gathered at Auschwitz and other memorials across Europe to honor the Nazis' victims. Survivors like Tova Friedman warned that rising far‑right politics and antisemitism could thrive if history is forgotten. They urged people to stand for democracy and to educate young people, after a survey revealed many Irish students knew little about the Holocaust. Survivors are also sharing their stories on social media to keep the memory alive.
