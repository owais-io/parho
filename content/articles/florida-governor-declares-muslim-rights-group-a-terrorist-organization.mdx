Florida’s Governor Ron De Santis has named the civil‑rights group Council on American‑Islamic Relations (CAIR) a foreign terrorist organization. The move echoes Texas Governor Greg Abbott’s similar order. CAIR says the claim is false, calls it an attack on free speech, and plans to sue. The governor claims CAIR is linked to the Muslim Brotherhood and Hamas, though neither is listed by the federal government. CAIR denies the ties and says the order is political and unjust.
