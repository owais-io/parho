Two men, one a Rams player’s brother, were arrested Saturday for allegedly taking a Lakers forward’s BMW in West Hollywood. The suspects, Samson Nacua and Trey Rose, were caught by hotel security after the car was found. They were released that night because there wasn’t enough evidence yet. Investigation continues, but no charges or lawyers reported yet. The Rams star Puka Nacua was already in the news this week. Police are still looking into how the car was taken.
