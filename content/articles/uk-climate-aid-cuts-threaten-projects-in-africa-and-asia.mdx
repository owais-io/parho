The UK is cutting money that helps countries in Africa and Asia protect nature and adapt to climate change. These cuts could stop projects that save mangrove forests, clean water, and help farmers grow better. Projects already run on very small budgets, so losing the money will make it almost impossible to keep going. Fiona Harvey says this shows the UK is not taking its climate‑leadership seriously and hurts the people most at risk.
