The article reflects on parenting and the loss of free play in modern kids, which can harm mental health. Australia’s ban on social media for minors gives them a chance to live without screens, to feel boredom and rest. This unplugged time can spark curiosity, improve memory, and help the brain grow. Parents are urged to encourage play, sun, good food, and quiet moments, rather than constant schedules.
