Australia hopes to build nuclear submarines through the Aukus pact with the U.S. and the U.K. But the U.K.’s submarine industry is stalled, with long delays, budget overruns, and a lack of skilled workers. Australia has poured billions into the deal, but the first U.K. submarine won’t be ready until the late 2030s, and the Australian boats aren’t expected until the early 2040s. With no clear schedule or capacity, Australia may end up with little or no new subs.
