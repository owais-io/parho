On Christmas Day, a 51‑year‑old woman and her 17‑year‑old stepson were swept more than 10km across Port Phillip Bay after setting out from Portarlington. They weren't wearing life jackets, but a police helicopter spotted them at 6:20 pm and air‑wing officers pulled them to safety. The pair are exhausted but thankful. That day Victoria also rescued a kayaker who had been lost for two hours and a mother with her daughters who were swept 1 km from shore.
