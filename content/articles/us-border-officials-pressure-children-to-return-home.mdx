U.S. border officials give unaccompanied children a warning sheet that says if they don't go back to their home country, they'll be held for a long time. It also warns their sponsors that they could be arrested or deported. Senator Ron Wyden says the document tricks vulnerable kids into leaving instead of seeking help. The policy is part of a Trump‑era push to deport children, and immigrant‑rights groups are suing over it.
