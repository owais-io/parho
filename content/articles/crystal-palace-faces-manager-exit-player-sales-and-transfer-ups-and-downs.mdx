In 2025 Palace won their first big trophy, but 2026 has been rough. They lost the FA Cup, sold captain Marc Guéhi to Manchester City, and manager Oliver Glasner is set to leave. A planned move for striker Jean‑Philippe Mateta to Milan fell apart, and the club backed out of a deal to buy Everton winger Dwight McNeil, drawing criticism. New signings, like Jørgen Strand Larsen, have broken transfer records.
