After a big scandal that forced out CEO Wayne LaPierre, the NRA has filed a lawsuit against its own charity, the NRA Foundation. It says the foundation’s former leaders are trying to take over the gun‑rights group and are diverting about $160 million of donated money for personal gains. The suit claims the foundation is misusing the NRA’s name and ignoring what donors wanted. The NRA wants a jury trial and a halt to the foundation’s use of its trademarks.
