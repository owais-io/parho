The ABC’s Four Corners broadcast a story from a former undercover agent who says father‑and‑son terrorists Sajid and Naveed Akram had been radicalised before 2019. ASIO denies the claims, calling them unsubstantiated and says Naveed was not a threat. The program aired despite ASIO’s warning of factual errors. ABC has not been contacted by ASIO, and the case may be examined in a new royal commission.
