Joy Davies (89) passed away after a life that blended science, social care and activism. A chemist with British Titan, she later earned a social‑services qualification and worked for Teesside County Council helping people with severe learning difficulties. She supported her husband’s tech studies by night shifts at a tea factory, ran training centres, and was a beloved village member near Thirsk. Her passions included theatre, gardening and local sports. She leaves two sons, a daughter and four grandkids.
