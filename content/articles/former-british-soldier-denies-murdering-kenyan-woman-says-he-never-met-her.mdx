Robert James Purkiss, a 38‑year‑old former British soldier, is being held in London after he was charged with killing Kenyan hairdresser Agnes Wanjiru, whose body was found in a septic tank in 2012. Purkiss says he never met Wanjiru and that there’s no evidence he was at the hotel where she disappeared. He claims he was a medic, had no blood on his clothes, and denies the murder. He faces extradition to Kenya.
