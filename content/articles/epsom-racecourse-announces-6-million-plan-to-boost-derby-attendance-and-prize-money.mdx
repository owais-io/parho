Epsom Downs is spending £6 million over the next five years to bring more people to the famous Derby. The plan adds £1 million to the winner’s purse, raises the total prize fund to £2 million, and will make admission free for kids under 18, free parking, and add new bleachers for a better view. It also moves the Coronation Cup to Saturday. The goal is to reach about 100,000 visitors over the two‑day festival by 2030, up from just 22,000 in 2025.
