Louis Rees‑Zammit left rugby to try a brief NFL stint with the Kansas City Chiefs and Jacksonville Jaguars. He never played a regular‑season game, was cut after an injury and coaching changes, and then returned to Wales. He says the experience made him stronger, faster and more versatile, and he has no regrets. Now he’s focused on being the best back for Bristol and the Welsh national team, especially ahead of the Six Nations.
