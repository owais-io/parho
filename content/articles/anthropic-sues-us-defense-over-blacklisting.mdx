AI company Anthropic filed two lawsuits against the U.S. Department of Defense after the Pentagon labeled it a “supply‑chain risk,” the first time the blacklisting tool has been used against a U.S. firm. Anthropic argues the move unfairly punishes the company for refusing to meet government demands and violates its First Amendment rights. The firm has supplied AI to the DoD, including for targeting decisions, and wants the court to reverse the label while it continues negotiations.
