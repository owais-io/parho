An NSW police officer unlawfully arrested a man, choked him, made rude remarks and then tried to delete the man's phone recording of the incident. The watchdog found the officer also removed CCTV footage and made false statements. The investigation, called Operation Somnus, says the officer in charge and a second officer fabricated evidence and lied in court. The watchdog recommends dismissing both officers and may seek criminal charges.
