Ali Khamenei, the Iranian Supreme Leader who governed the country for more than three decades, died on February 28, 2026. Born in 1939 in Mashhad, he studied Islamic theology and joined the revolutionary movement that overthrew the Shah in 1979. He served as Iran’s president from 1981 to 1989 before becoming Supreme Leader after Ayatollah Khomeini’s death. Under his rule Iran faced internal unrest and international tension. His death marks the end of an era.
