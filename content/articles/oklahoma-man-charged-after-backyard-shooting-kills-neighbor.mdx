A 33‑year‑old Oklahoma man named Cody Adams was arrested for first‑degree manslaughter after he shot a woman, Sandra Phelps, while target‑practicing in his backyard. Adams had a new handgun and was shooting at a Red Bull can. The yard didn’t have a backstop, so the bullet traveled several blocks and hit Phelps, who was with family at Christmas. He was released on a $100,000 bond.
