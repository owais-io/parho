Two 28‑year‑old men were discovered dead at a house in Sydney's north‑west suburb of Glenorie. Police said the case is not suspicious and are treating it as a tragic accident. The men are thought to be related, but the cause of death is still under investigation. Authorities have set up a crime scene and detectives are working on the case, and a coroner will review the findings.
