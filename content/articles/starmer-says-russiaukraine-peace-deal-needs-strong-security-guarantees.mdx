UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said a peace deal between Russia and Ukraine will only work if Western countries give Ukraine firm military guarantees. He warned that past agreements failed because they lacked such guarantees. Starmer also said the UK would support Ukraine with troops if it helps keep the country safe. He met with European leaders in Berlin and is urging Europe to spend more on defence.
