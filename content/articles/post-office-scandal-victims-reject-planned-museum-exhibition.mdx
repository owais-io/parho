People who were wrongly convicted in the Post Office Horizon scandal do not want a museum exhibit about their ordeal. They say the Postal Museum’s plan feels like a PR stunt and is too close to the Post Office. Victims say the museum is partly funded by the Post Office, which makes them uneasy. The museum says it will let victims tell their own stories. The inquiry wants to keep working with those affected.
