Trump said the U.S. would “rescue” Iranian protesters if the government kills them. Iranian officials slammed the comment, calling it reckless and threatening retaliation if the U.S. intervenes. The protests, sparked by a huge drop in the rial and long‑standing grievances, have seen at least seven people killed. Iran’s military is on alert, and officials warn that any foreign interference could trigger a dangerous conflict.
