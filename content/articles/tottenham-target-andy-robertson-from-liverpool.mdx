Tottenham Hotspur are in early talks to bring in Liverpool’s left‑back Andy Robertson after he loses his starting spot to Miloš Kerkez and his contract ends next season. The move could give Spurs a new defensive option while they rebuild after Thomas Frank’s exit. Meanwhile, other clubs such as Manchester United are looking at players like Kobbie Mainoo, and Brighton and Liverpool are dealing with injury concerns and potential loan moves. The January window is heating up for many teams.
