Donald Trump has named former Fed governor Kevin Warsh as his pick for the next Federal Reserve chair. Warsh, who left the Fed in 2011 after criticizing its stimulus policy, is a well‑known critic of the central bank’s slow rate cuts. Trump said he trusts Warsh to bring back strong monetary policy. The nominee will need a Senate vote. Markets reacted mildly, with the dollar and gold moving little.
