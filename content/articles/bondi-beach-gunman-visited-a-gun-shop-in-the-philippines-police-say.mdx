Police say the alleged Bondi Beach shooter, Sajid Akram, and his son stayed in a Davao hotel for four weeks before heading back to Australia. During that stay, the father went to a firearms shop, and the pair used local phones and made several hotel extensions. Police are gathering CCTV and other evidence to piece together their activities and contacts before the Sydney mass‑shooting that killed 15 people.
