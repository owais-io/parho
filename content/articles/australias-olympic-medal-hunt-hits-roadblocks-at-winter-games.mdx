Australia’s team at the Winter Olympics in Italy is still looking for its first medal, even though the country’s best hope, Jakara Anthony, was a favourite but missed the podium after a slip in the moguls final. Injuries to several skiers and snowboarders, including a broken neck and knee problems, have hit the squad hard. With no medals so far, Aussie fans and athletes are hoping for a better result in 2026.
