Jim Thomas was a community nurse who grew into a key leader at the charity Skills for Care. He always put people’s needs first, avoiding bureaucratic rules and finding simple solutions—like convincing an elderly man to keep his rural home with a proper toilet and water. He created projects that used everyday skills to help others live independently. Even after cancer, he kept cycling, consulting, and blogging about joy and life. He leaves his wife, two sons, and grandchildren.
