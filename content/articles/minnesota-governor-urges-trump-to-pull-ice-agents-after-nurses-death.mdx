Governor Tim Walz on Sunday urged President Donald Trump to pull federal agents from Minnesota after the shooting death of ICU nurse Alex Pretti. Walz said video shows Pretti was unarmed, contradicting federal claims that he was a threat. He accused agents of covering up evidence and called for humane immigration control. The Trump administration defended the agents and demanded law‑and‑order, sparking a showdown between state and federal authorities.
