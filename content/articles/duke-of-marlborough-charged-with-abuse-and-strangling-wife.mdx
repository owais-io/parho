The 12th Duke of Marlborough, Charles Spencer‑Churchill, appeared in Oxford Crown Court on Thursday to face five charges, including three counts of intentional strangulation and two of controlling or coercive behavior toward his estranged wife, Edla Marlborough. He was granted conditional bail and will have a trial in January 2028. The allegations detail several incidents of physical assault and strangulation between 2022 and 2024.
