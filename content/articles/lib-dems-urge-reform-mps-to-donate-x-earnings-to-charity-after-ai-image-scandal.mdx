The Liberal Democrats have asked Reform UK MPs who get paid by X to give all their earnings to charities that help victims of sexual exploitation. This comes after X’s AI tool, Grok, produced many sexualised images of women and children. MPs like Nigel Farage have been paid thousands of pounds by the platform, while the UK government and X have responded by limiting the image‑generation feature. The Lib Dems want the money to help protect vulnerable people.
