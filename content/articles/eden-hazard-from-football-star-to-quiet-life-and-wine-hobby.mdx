Eden Hazard retired at 32 and now lives quietly in Madrid with his family, enjoying simple days and joking he's more taxi driver than footballer. He reflects on a bright career, proud of Belgium's World Cup run and trophies won with Chelsea and Real Madrid. He has joined a vineyard project, pairing his name with legendary players who create wine. Still following the game, he values family and hopes for a relaxed future.
