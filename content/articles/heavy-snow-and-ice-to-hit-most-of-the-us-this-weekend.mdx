A powerful winter storm will bring record‑breaking cold, heavy snow, sleet, and freezing rain to the southern Rockies, plains, mid‑south, mid‑Atlantic, and New England. Expect 8‑16 inches of snow in parts of New York and widespread ice that could cause power outages and dangerous travel. Many states have declared emergencies, and officials warn of life‑threatening cold and frostbite risks.
