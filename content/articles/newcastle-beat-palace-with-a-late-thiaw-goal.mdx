Newcastle won 2‑0 at St James’s Park, with Bruno Guimarães and Malick Thiaw scoring. The game was tight, but Palace struggled with set‑pieces and missed chances. Manager Eddie Howe said it was a big step forward, especially after changing in Harvey Barnes. Crystal Palace coach Oliver Glasner was disappointed, blaming poor dead‑ball defense. Newcastle moved closer to the top of the table, while Palace kept a long winless run.
