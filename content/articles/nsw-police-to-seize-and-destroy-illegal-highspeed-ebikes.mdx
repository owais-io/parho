New rules let NSW police seize any electric bike that can go faster than 25 km/h. The bikes will be taken off roads and crushed, with police using portable speed testers. The move follows a surge of “souped‑up” ebikes that caused injuries and stunts on the Harbour Bridge. Transport ministers say the law will protect riders and stop illegal high‑powered bikes.
