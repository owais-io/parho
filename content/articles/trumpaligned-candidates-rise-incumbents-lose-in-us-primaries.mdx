Primaries in Texas, North Carolina and Arkansas show a shift. Trump‑aligned Republicans beat sitting members – Dan Crenshaw lost to Steve Toth, and other GOP lawmakers face runoffs. Democrats are also losing seats, with several NC state reps ousted. In Arkansas, a Democrat, Alex Holladay, beat a Republican, hinting at growing support. The results suggest voters are testing Trump’s influence and Democrats’ ability to rebuild after 2024 losses.
