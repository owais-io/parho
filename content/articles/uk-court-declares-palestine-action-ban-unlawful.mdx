UK judges ruled that the government’s ban on the protest group Palestine Action was unlawful and disproportionate. The court said the group’s activities did not meet the high threshold of terrorism required for proscription. The ruling means the Home Secretary must appeal, while police will stop arresting people for showing support. Hundreds of people arrested under the ban now face an uncertain legal future.
