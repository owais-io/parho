After a hectic holiday season, the writer feels upbeat and shares a handful of light‑hearted, doable New Year’s goals. He recommends watching more movies in a theater, dressing a bit better, proudly putting his name on things, and stopping the fuss over the McRib. He jokes about the long history of resolutions, noting most are tough or boring, and encourages choosing simple, positive habits that boost confidence and joy.
