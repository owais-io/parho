Bolsonaro underwent a second treatment to stop his long‑running hiccups. The procedure worked and he is doing well, doctors say. The former Brazilian president, jailed for a coup attempt, has been in hospital since Christmas after surgery for a hernia. The hiccups began months ago, so doctors blocked a nerve that controls breathing. He may leave the hospital on Jan 1 and return to prison.
