Brazil’s top foreign policy adviser warned that if the United States attacks Venezuela, it could trigger a war that spreads across South America, similar to Vietnam. He criticized Trump’s move to close Venezuelan airspace and the U.S. pressure on President Nicolás Maduro. The adviser said such an invasion could ignite anti‑American sentiment and draw global powers into the conflict. He urged a peaceful solution and warned against forced regime change.
