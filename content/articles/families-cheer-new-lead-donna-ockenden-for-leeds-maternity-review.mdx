Families of babies lost in Leeds are happy that Donna Ockenden, a former maternity reviewer, will head a new inquiry into the trust’s failures, where 56 babies and two mothers died in five years. The families pushed for her appointment and say they’re slowly regaining faith in the health secretary. Ockenden will focus on listening to families and making sure safer maternity care is delivered.
