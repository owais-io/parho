A purple‑haired AI character called Amelia, created for a UK school anti‑extremism game, has turned into a far‑right meme on X. Users keep making new versions, adding racist language and political slogans. The meme exploded after a single account shared it, reaching 11,000 posts in a day. A fake “Amelia” cryptocurrency also appeared, and even Elon Musk retweeted it, turning hate into money. The game’s creators say it was never meant to do this.
