Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese called 2021 Australian of the Year Grace Tame “difficult,” a comment Tame said was a misogynistic slur. She accused him of echoing former PM Scott Morrison and responded that the word is a code for a woman who won’t comply. Albanese apologized for any misinterpretation, and the exchange has sparked debate about gendered language and abuse survivors in politics.
