ICE arrests are happening quietly across the U.S. even while the media focuses on Minneapolis. In California, San Diego and Los Angeles, agents are doing quick “smash‑and‑grab” sweeps that finish in minutes, making it hard to record or protest. Similar rapid raids are happening in Portland, Charlotte and Washington, D.C., causing fear and disrupting work, school and medical visits. Local groups are stepping up with legal help, rights training and lawsuits to protect people.
