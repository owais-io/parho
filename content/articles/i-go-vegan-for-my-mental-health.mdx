Emma Beddington explains why she chose veganism, saying it boosts her mental health and gives her a sense of agency. She worries that veganism is losing ground as meat sales rise, restaurants close, and political voices back meat. She notes that plant‑based food can be processed but still healthy, and that small choices feel empowering amid climate anxiety. In short, she sees veganism as a personal, wellness‑driven choice that also helps the planet.
