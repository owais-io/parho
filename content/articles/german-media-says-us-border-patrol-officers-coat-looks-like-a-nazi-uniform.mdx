German newspapers criticized the long, olive‑green coat worn by US Border Patrol officer Gregory Bovino, saying it looks like a Nazi uniform. Bovino led immigration raids in Minneapolis and his jacket is not part of the official CBP uniform. German outlets Der Spiegel and Süddeutsche Zeitung compared the coat and his haircut to German officers, sparking backlash. Bovino says the coat is old and unremarkable, while the US says it is a standard winter jacket but not in uniform rules.
