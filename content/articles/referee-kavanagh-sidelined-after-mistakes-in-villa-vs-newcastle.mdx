The Premier League will keep referee Chris Kavanagh off the bench for the next round after he made three high‑profile errors in Aston Villa’s 1‑3 loss to Newcastle. He and one assistant were removed, while the other assistant will work at Nottingham Forest vs Liverpool. The game sparked a debate over how video‑assisted refereeing affects decisions, with Newcastle’s manager warning that VAR can make calls feel less instinctive.
