Two women in a California women’s prison say their former kitchen supervisor, Marcus Johnson, raped them in 2020. One woman’s claim was found true by the prison, but the other was not. Both are asking a district attorney to bring criminal charges. The case highlights how many staff abuse claims are never prosecuted, even though the state has a history of sexual misconduct in its prisons. The cook denies the allegations and was let go in 2020.
