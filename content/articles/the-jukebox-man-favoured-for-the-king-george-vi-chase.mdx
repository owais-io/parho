At the Boxing Day King George VI Chase, owners and punters eye the fourth‑favourite, Ben Pauling’s The Jukebox Man, who has a solid record and a fresh, easy run. The top‑rated pair Gaelic Warrior and Fact To File have impressive form but may be tired after a demanding finish earlier this year. Jango Baie and other contenders bring variety, but The Jukebox Man is the big name to watch.
