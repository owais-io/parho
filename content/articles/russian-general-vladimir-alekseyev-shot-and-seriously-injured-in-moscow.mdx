A top Russian military officer, Vladimir Alekseyev, was shot several times in a stairwell near his Moscow apartment and is now in critical condition. The attack happened a day after peace talks in Abu Dhabi, and no one has claimed responsibility. Russian officials blame Kyiv, calling it a terrorist act, while Ukraine says it had nothing to do with it. Alekseyev, who has led Russian intelligence and private military groups, is under US and UK sanctions.
