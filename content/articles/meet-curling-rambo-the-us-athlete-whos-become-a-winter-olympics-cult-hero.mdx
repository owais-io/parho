At the Paris 2024 Winter Games, Aidan Oldenburg of the US men’s curling team has turned heads. The 24‑year‑old, known for his wild hair, glasses and a red bandana, earned the nickname “Curling Rambo.” A juggler and environmental scientist by trade, Oldenburg blends quirky hobbies with sport, even tossing flaming torches into practice. Fans love the bandana look, and the team’s opener beat the Czech Republic, before falling to Switzerland.
