Arsenal won 4-1 over Tottenham in a North London derby. Eberechi Eze scored all three goals, showing effortless skill and calm. He looked almost like he was moving above the field, stopping in space to slot the ball home. The win lifted Arsenal into the top of the Premier League, giving manager Mikel Arteta a strong start to his season and keeping the title race open.
