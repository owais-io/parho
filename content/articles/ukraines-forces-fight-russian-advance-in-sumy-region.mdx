Ukraine’s army is fighting a new Russian push in the Sumy region, after Russia moved 50 people out of Grabovske and claimed it took Vysoke. Ukrainian troops say fighting is still happening in Grabovske and they’re pushing Russians back. In Florida, U.S. and Ukrainian officials had productive talks, but Russia says no talks with Kyiv are planned. A Ukrainian drone hit Russian ships on the Black Sea, sparking a fire.
