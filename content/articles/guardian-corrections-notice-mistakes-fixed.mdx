The Guardian is fixing several errors it published earlier. It clarified that BP’s oil output is 2.3 million barrels a day, not 2.3 billion. It corrected a note about a Birmingham University study, saying it appeared in BMJ Mental Health, not the main BMJ. It also noted other small errors in article titles and names. Readers can contact the Guardian to report more mistakes.
