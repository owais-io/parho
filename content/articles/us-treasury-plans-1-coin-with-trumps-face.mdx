The U.S. Treasury has drawn a $1 coin that will show former President Donald Trump on both sides as a celebration of the country’s 250th birthday. Trump also wants a new Washington Commanders stadium and a White House ballroom named after him. Robert Reich argues that putting Trump’s name on public money or buildings is like a dictator’s monument. He says democracy should remember him for the harm he caused, not honor him.
