A fire at Le Constellation bar in Crans‑Montana, a Swiss ski town, killed at least 40 people, many teens and young adults, on New Year’s Eve. The blaze started when candles were too close to the ceiling, investigators say. Residents, tourists and shop owners are mourning; candles and flowers are left at a memorial. Police are investigating the owners for negligence. The tragedy has shocked the community of 6,000 people, who are helping families and offering blood donations.
