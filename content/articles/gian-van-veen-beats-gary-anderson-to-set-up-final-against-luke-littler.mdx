Gian van Veen won a tense semi‑final against veteran Gary Anderson, securing a place in the World Youth Championship final against Luke Littler. The match was a back‑and‑forth battle, with Van Veen taking a 4‑1 lead but Anderson fighting back. Van Veen's calm and precise play, especially in the final legs, saw him win 6‑3. Littler, who dominated his semi‑final, is expected to be the favourite in the final.
