HiTZ Cricket is a training machine that can bowl up to 128 km/h and be set to any speed, spin or swing. Players control it from a computer, playing the ball many times or adding new variations so they must think like in a real match. The machine records every delivery, gives instant feedback and video clips, and costs less than a normal net session. It helps solo practice feel useful and gets players back into the game after a break.
