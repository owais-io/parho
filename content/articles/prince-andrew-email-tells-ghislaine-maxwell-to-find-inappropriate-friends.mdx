Emails from a man who looks like Prince Andrew, written from Balmoral, appear in new Epstein files. In them he asks Ghislaine Maxwell to find “inappropriate friends.” The emails also mention his departure from the Royal Navy and a planned trip to Peru with a third party. The documents do not prove any wrongdoing, but they add to the story that Andrew has been linked to Epstein. He has denied the claims.
