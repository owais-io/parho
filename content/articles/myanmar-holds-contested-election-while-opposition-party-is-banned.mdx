Despite being under military control, Myanmar has started voting in some areas, but the election is widely seen as a fake move to legitimize the junta. The biggest opposition party is banned, and Aung San Suu Kyi is jailed. Fighting, airstrikes, and arrests for political dissent continue, making many afraid to vote. The United Nations and Western leaders condemn the vote, while China and other allies watch it.
