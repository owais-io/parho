Arsenal won the first leg of the Carabao Cup semi‑final against Chelsea 2‑1. Ben White’s corner gave the first goal, then Robert Sánchez made a costly handling mistake that let Viktor Gyökeres score. Chelsea’s substitute Alejandro Garnacho hit a second late goal, but Arsenal still took the match away with a comfortable lead. The game showed Arsenal’s physical edge and Arteta’s focus on set‑pieces.
