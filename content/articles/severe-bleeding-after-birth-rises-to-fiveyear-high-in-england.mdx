The number of mothers who bleed badly after giving birth has jumped to the highest level in five years, with 32 out of every 1,000 births now affected – a 19% rise from 2020. Health groups blame older, heavier mothers and more complicated births. The Liberal Democrats call for a fast “maternity rescue plan” after a review said most hospitals are not safe enough. The government is starting a new review to fix the problems.
