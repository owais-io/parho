Denmark and Greenland’s leaders met with US officials after former President Trump said the Arctic island should belong to the United States. The talks ended with the US saying a takeover is unnecessary, and Denmark saying the two sides still differ on the issue. Meanwhile, the Islamist group Hizb ut‑Tahrir said it will not dissolve before a new hate‑speech law is introduced. In Australia, a tennis coach won a $1 million prize at the Australian Open.
