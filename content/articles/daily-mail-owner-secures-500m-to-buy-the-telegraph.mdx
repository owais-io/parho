The Daily Mail’s owner has arranged money to buy the Telegraph for £500 million. He will pay £400 million now, using more debt and cash, and the remaining £100 million in two years. The deal will be reviewed by regulators, and the government must approve it. If it goes through, the Telegraph will stay separate from the Mail, but both will share some resources. Other buyers are still interested.
