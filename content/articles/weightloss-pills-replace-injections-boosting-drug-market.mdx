People are switching from weekly weight‑loss injections to daily pills, making it easier and cheaper to lose weight. The new Wegovy pill from Novo Nordisk and other tablets are already hitting the market, with tens of thousands of prescriptions in the US. The pills mimic the gut hormone GLP‑1, but are simpler to take and don't need a fridge. While prices stay high and side‑effects exist, the market is growing fast.
