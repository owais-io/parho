During the Africa Cup of Nations final, Morocco faced Senegal. After a tense match, the game went to a penalty shoot‑out. Moroccan striker Brahim Díaz took a risky Panenka‑style kick, hoping to fool keeper Édouard Mendy. The keeper stopped the ball, and Morocco lost the match. Díaz was devastated, the team’s coach blamed him for the mistake, and Moroccan fans were furious, calling his choice careless.
