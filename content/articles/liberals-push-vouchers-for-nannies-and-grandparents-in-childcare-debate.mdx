The Liberal party in Australia is considering government‑funded vouchers that would let families hire nannies, au pairs or use grandparents to help with childcare. Critics warn that a voucher system could raise costs, reduce safety standards, and favour wealthier families. The move comes as Labor plans a universal centre‑based childcare system, and both sides see the issue as a key battleground for the next election.
