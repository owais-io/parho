The Nationals’ leader, David Littleproud, said the federal coalition is “untenable” after three Nationals shadow ministers quit, sparking a split. Liberal MPs now say Sussan Ley’s position is badly weakened and a challenge to her could come as early as February. Former leaders and politicians have warned the coalition’s future is in doubt, and the split may force Ley to fight for her job.
