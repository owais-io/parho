Oxford researchers urge the UK government to stop kids under 16 from using social media and to consider rules for AI chatbots that teens use. They warn that online addiction, mental‑health problems, and loneliness harm young minds. The letter calls for policies that cover all tech affecting kids, not just social media, to protect their development while still allowing healthy connections.
