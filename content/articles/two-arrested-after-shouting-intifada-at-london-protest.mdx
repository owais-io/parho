Police arrested two people who shouted “intifada” during a pro‑Palestinian protest in London. Five people were detained in total. Police said they would now arrest anyone chanting “globalise the intifada” or holding a placard with the phrase, after attacks on Jews in Manchester and Sydney. Some protest organisers say the move is political repression, while others support the crackdown. The courts will decide if the chant is a hate crime.
