Tottenham’s coach Thomas Frank left the game feeling frustrated after a 1‑1 draw with Brentford. The Spurs showed little creativity and struggled to create chances, which annoyed fans who booed him at the end, chanting “Boring, boring Tottenham.” Frank, who led Brentford to promotion a decade ago, said there are no quick fixes and the team needs time. He hopes supporters will understand the difficulties as the club rebuilds.
