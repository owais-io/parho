A woman in her 40s with learning disabilities was held captive in a Tewkesbury home for over 25 years. Her host, Amanda Wixon, forced her to do all the housework, beat her, and kept her from eating or cleaning. Police discovered the abuse in 2021 after a tip. Wixon was found guilty of false imprisonment and assault. The victim now lives with a foster family, goes to college, and is healing.
