Prince Harry and a group of well‑known people, including Elton John, are suing the Daily Mail for spying on them. They say the newspaper hacked voicemails, tapped phones, and even bugged homes, using private investigators. The case could cost £38 million and will be heard over nine weeks. Harry’s fight is part of a long record of him taking on the press after his mother’s death and the treatment of his wife, Meghan.
