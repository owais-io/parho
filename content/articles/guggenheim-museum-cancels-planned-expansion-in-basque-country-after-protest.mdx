Environmental groups and local residents in Spain’s Basque Country protested a plan to build a new Guggenheim museum branch on a protected nature reserve. The proposal, backed by the museum and regional governments, promised jobs but was seen as a threat to wildlife. After years of debate, the museum decided to cancel the project, citing environmental and planning obstacles, and will look for other ways to grow.
