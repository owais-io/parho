Trump said he will use executive action to pay the 50,000 TSA agents who have been working without pay because Congress hasn't passed a funding bill. He wants to stop long lines and flight delays caused by the staff shortage. The move comes after nearly 500 security officers quit during the partial government shutdown. The president didn’t say where the money will come from, but he said it will be paid immediately.
