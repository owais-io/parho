Trump has named Nick Adams, a controversial Australian influencer known for inflammatory online posts, as a special presidential envoy for tourism. Adams was previously rejected by the Senate when nominated as ambassador to Malaysia. His past remarks, which include Islamophobic and antisemitic language, sparked protests in Kuala Lumpur. White House officials say he will promote American values, but critics worry the appointment could damage the U.S. image abroad.
