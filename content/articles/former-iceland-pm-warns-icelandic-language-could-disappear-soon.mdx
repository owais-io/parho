Katrín Jakobsdóttir, Iceland’s former prime minister, says the Icelandic language might vanish in just a generation because more people are using English on social media and AI tools. Iceland has about 350,000 speakers, and kids are often talking in English. The government is trying to teach AI to work with Icelandic, but Katrín says a stronger push is needed to keep the language alive.
