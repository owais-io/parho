After a panic attack, author Emma Gannon hit burnout and decided to take a full year off. She cut most commitments, quit her podcast, stopped drinking, and spent the year resting, eating well, and listening to sad music to unlock feelings. She also spent time alone in nature, re‑visited old hobbies, and kept close friends who let her be herself. Now she’s more resilient, checks in regularly, and urges others to pause before they burn out.
