H&M opened a new, tech‑heavy store in Brighton this week, marking 50 years since it first opened a shop in Britain. The UK boss, Karen O’Rourke, says the high street is still alive and people want more than quick online deals. H&M is adding events, loyalty perks, and sustainability initiatives to stay ahead of fast‑fashion rivals. The chain plans to keep up 200‑plus UK shops for the next 50 years.
