UK bond yields climbed over 5% as the war in Iran triggered a worldwide sell‑off. Investors pulled money out of UK debt, pushing the 10‑year rate to its highest level since 2008. The Bank of England may need to raise interest rates faster than the US or eurozone to tackle rising inflation, which will strain the already‑weak UK economy and add pressure on households.
