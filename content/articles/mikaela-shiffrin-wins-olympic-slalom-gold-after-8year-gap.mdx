Shiffrin finally won her second Olympic slalom gold at Cortina, ending an eight‑year wait and proving she still dominates the sport. The 30‑year‑old had faced two huge crashes, a long mental slump and the death of her dad in 2020. She said the win felt like “being born again” and that she could finally accept loss and keep moving forward. Her victory also makes her the first U.S. skier to win three Olympic gold medals.
