Exeter’s manager, Gary Caldwell, got his players ready for an FA Cup clash against Manchester City, mixing humour with tight team talks. The match turned into a 9‑0 loss, but Caldwell praised the squad’s spirit, especially young striker George Birch who scored. He thanked supporters for their financial help and said the club will focus on the next 22 league games.
