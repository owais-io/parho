The Google Pixel 10a is a low‑cost Android phone that looks like the previous 9a. It uses the same Tensor G4 chip, 48‑MP + 13‑MP camera set, and a 6.3‑inch OLED display that’s a touch brighter. It offers a long battery, solid seven‑year software updates, and safety features, but it lacks newer AI tools, optical zoom, and has a plastic back. Overall it’s a solid bargain, though the 9a still beats it on price.
