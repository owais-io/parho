England beat West Indies by five wickets in a T20 World Cup match that acted as a quarter‑final, sealing a semi‑final against India. Sanju Samson scored 97 not‑out, guiding England to chase 196. West Indies had early runs but were undone by fielding mistakes and a quick wicket by Jasprit Bumrah. The win confirms England will play India in Mumbai, after a chaotic schedule and venue uncertainty.
