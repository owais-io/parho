Vilma Palacios, a 22‑year‑old nursing graduate, was arrested by ICE in June while getting a vehicle inspection sticker. She was sent to a processing center in Basile, Louisiana, where she’s been held for six months. She has no criminal record, was denied bond, and may be forced to return to Honduras while waiting for her case. She reports feeling hopeless, missing basic items like shampoo and menstrual pads, and has called for her release.
