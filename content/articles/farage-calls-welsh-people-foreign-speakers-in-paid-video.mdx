Farage made a paid video on Cameo where he called Welsh people “foreign speakers,” saying he’d be upset if a wedding had many Welsh guests. The clip, sold for £106, shows him mocking the Welsh language and culture. It comes just before Wales’ Senedd elections, where Reform UK wants to win the seat. The remarks risk hurting the party’s chances and add to a string of controversial Cameo videos that have raised questions about Farage’s views and the platform’s safety.
