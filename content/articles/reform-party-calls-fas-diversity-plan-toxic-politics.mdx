Reform UK sent a letter to the Football Association, calling its diversity plan “toxic politics” and saying it divides rather than unites. The party wants the FA to drop its 2024‑28 strategy that aims for 30% of England’s men’s coaching staff to be from ethnic minorities by 2028. The FA defended the plan as a way to tackle racism and widen opportunities. Meanwhile, Ipswich fans were angry at a Reform event at the club.
