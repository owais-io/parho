Two patients slipped out of a top Sydney psychiatric ward, one after a murder charge and another after a fatal car crash. Staff say the hospital is over‑full, understaffed, and under‑funded, leaving doctors with too little time for long‑term care. The state premier plans a full review of security and capacity, while unions warn the system is “broken” and needs urgent fixes.
