Former NATO commander Sir Richard Shirreff defended UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer after President Trump called him “not Winston Churchill” for refusing US use of British bases to launch strikes on Iran. Shirreff said Britain should avoid getting into a war with no clear finish line. The UK has also moved a destroyer to the eastern Mediterranean amid growing tensions. The comments sparked criticism from some UK politicians who say the PM is harming the country’s ties with the US.
