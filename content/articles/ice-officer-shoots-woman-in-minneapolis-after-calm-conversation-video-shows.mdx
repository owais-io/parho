A video from an ICE officer’s phone shows Renee Good calmly saying she was fine and not mad before he shot her in Minneapolis. The clip was shared by the White House and sparked debate. The officer says he feared for his life and acted in self‑defense, while many Minnesotans and Democrats call the shooting a tragedy. Protests gathered near the site, and lawmakers threaten to cut funding for ICE.
