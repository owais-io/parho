As the Winter Olympics get closer, snowboarding shows that success isn’t just about medals. Three‑time Olympian Lesley McKenna studied what makes the sport special. She found that riders chase “stoke” – a mix of fun, creativity, risk and community – rather than only rankings. The research shows how shared excitement, epic moments and learning from stories build motivation and a love of the sport.
