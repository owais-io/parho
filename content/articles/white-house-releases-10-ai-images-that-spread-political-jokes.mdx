The Trump administration has been using AI to create 10 funny yet controversial images that spread political messages and jokes. These pictures show Trump as a king, pope, Jedi, and even a penguin, and mix memes with serious topics like immigration and racism. The posts, called “slopaganda,” aim to provoke reactions and push propaganda quickly. Experts warn that even fake images can stick in people’s minds, making it hard to separate fact from satire.
