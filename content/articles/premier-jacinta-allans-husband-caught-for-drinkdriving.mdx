Premier Jacinta Allan said she was shocked and embarrassed when her husband, Yorick Piper, was caught driving under the influence on his way to the supermarket. He was stopped by police in Bendigo, failed a breath test, and had his licence suspended for three months and a $611 fine. The couple donated $1,000 to a road‑safety group and will pay for a minor collision’s repairs. Allan stressed honesty and said the incident was unplanned.
