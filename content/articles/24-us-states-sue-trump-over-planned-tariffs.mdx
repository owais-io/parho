A group of 24 Democratic‑led states, led by New York, has filed a lawsuit to stop President Trump’s proposed 15% tariff on imports. The suits say Trump lacks legal power to impose the tariffs and want refunds for the money businesses and consumers have already paid. The case will go to the U.S. Court of International Trade, and the White House says it will fight the claim in court.
