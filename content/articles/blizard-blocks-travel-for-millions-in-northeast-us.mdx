A huge snowstorm hit the Northeast, dropping up to 2 feet of snow and whipping winds that made roads nearly invisible. Flights were cancelled, many people lost power, and governors issued bans on non‑essential travel. Schools and some businesses stayed closed, and emergency services worked to help homeless residents and keep utilities running. The storm left millions stuck at home and disrupted daily life.
