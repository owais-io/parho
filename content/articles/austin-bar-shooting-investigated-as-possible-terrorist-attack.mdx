A gunman at an Austin bar killed two people and wounded 14. Police say the suspect, Ndiaga Diagne, may have acted as a lone terrorist after US airstrikes on Iran. He wore a “Property of Allah” hoodie and his Facebook posts show anti‑Israeli and pro‑Iran sentiment. FBI, local police and the FBI Joint Terrorism Task Force are probing his motives. Meanwhile, officials in Texas and cities have increased patrols and urged residents to be alert.
