Rosenior spent seven seasons at Hull, first as a player and later as a coach. He’s known for giving players confidence and studying top teams on his laptop. After helping Hull win promotion and reach an FA Cup final, he returned as head coach and kept the club in the Championship. He focuses on clear tactics, giving responsibilities, and building team spirit. His calm, detailed approach helped turn Hull into a solid, confident side.
