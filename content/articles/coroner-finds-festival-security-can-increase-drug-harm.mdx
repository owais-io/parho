A Victorian coroner has warned that police, security checks, and sniffer dogs at music festivals can actually make drug use riskier. The case involved a 28‑year‑old woman who died after taking MDMA and ketamine at an electronic dance event. From 2000‑2024, 18 people died in similar festivals, most were young adults and MDMA was involved in 15 cases. The coroner calls for updated safety guidelines and supports pill‑testing services.
