Dozens of actors, musicians and doctors have signed a letter asking Israel to open Gaza’s hospitals and let aid teams work. They say Israeli attacks and blockades have destroyed health services, killing workers and blocking medicine. The letter will be shown to UK and EU leaders and calls for immediate, unrestricted access for patients and medical staff. It also names a young girl whose death highlighted the problem.
