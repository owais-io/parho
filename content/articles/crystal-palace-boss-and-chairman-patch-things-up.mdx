Crystal Palace manager Oliver Glasner said he and chairman Steve Parish have reached a truce after a rocky period. The two spoke over dinner, agreeing to stick together and focus on ending the season on a positive note. Glasner admitted earlier tensions, like accusations over player sales, but now stresses teamwork and fan support. He remains committed to the club, hoping the partnership will help Palace recover from a winless run.
