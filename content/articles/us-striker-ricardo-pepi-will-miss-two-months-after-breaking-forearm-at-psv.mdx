Ricardo Pepi, the 23‑year‑old US men’s soccer striker, broke his forearm after scoring for PSV Eindhoven in a match against Excelsior. He’ll have surgery on Sunday and is expected to be out for about two months. The injury stops a good run of form for Pepi and raises doubts about his participation in US friendlies and the 2026 World Cup that starts on June 12. PSV says he’ll recover soon.
