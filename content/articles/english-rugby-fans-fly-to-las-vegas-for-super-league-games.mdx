This weekend, over 12,000 English rugby fans travel to Las Vegas for two Super League matches against NRL teams. Hull KR and Leeds Rhinos play at Allegiant Stadium, while the Australian NRL season kicks off. The event follows Hull KR’s World Club Challenge win and shows how the Super League is gaining global attention. NRL leaders want deeper ties with Super League, hinting at future London matches and wider worldwide plans.
