Farage, the former UKIP leader, used the Cameo app to film short videos for anyone who paid him, including extremist groups. The Guardian exposed him after he made messages for a Canadian white‑supremacist, a rioter, and others. He withdrew the service, citing security worries. His cash‑in‑hand approach mirrors Trump’s monetised personality, raising concerns that politicians may trade views for money, damaging public trust.
