Donald Trump said the world is safer now and launched a new “Board of Peace” at Davos, saying it could replace the UN. He signed it with 19 countries and wants to include Russia’s Vladimir Putin, who is still fighting in Ukraine. Britain, France, Germany and Norway said they would not join. Trump also talked about a Greenland deal that could give the US permanent access to the Arctic island.
