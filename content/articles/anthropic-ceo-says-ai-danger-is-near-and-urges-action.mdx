Dario Amodei, the boss of AI company Anthropic, wrote a 19,000‑word essay warning that powerful AI tools are almost ready and could outsmart humans in many fields. He says we must act now to keep AI safe, especially after incidents of deepfake sexual content and the risk of massive job loss. Amodei believes careful steps can help us avoid catastrophe and build a better future.
