Civil rights groups, including the NAACP and the Lawyers’ Committee for Civil Rights, have sued after the FBI seized almost 700 boxes of Georgia election data in a January raid. The lawsuit argues that the Trump‑era administration is misusing voter information to keep fighting the 2020 election. The groups want the materials returned and to stop any further misuse. The case was filed in the Northern District of Georgia.
