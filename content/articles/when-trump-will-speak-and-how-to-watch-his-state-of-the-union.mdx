Donald Trump will give his State of the Union address to Congress on Tuesday at 9 p.m. Eastern Time, his second big speech this term and the last before the 2026 mid‑terms. Major networks and the Guardian will broadcast it live, and it can also be watched on YouTube. Trump is expected to talk about tariffs, immigration and foreign policy, while Democrats plan a boycott rally and will give a rebuttal by Governor Abigail Spanberger.
