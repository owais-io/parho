In 2025, 129 journalists and media workers were killed worldwide, a record. The Committee to Protect Journalists (CPJ) says two‑thirds of those deaths, 86, were caused by Israeli forces, mainly Palestinians reporting from Gaza. The report also notes killings in Yemen, Sudan, Mexico, and Ukraine. Israel denies targeting journalists, saying it only hits combatants, but the CPJ says it has carried out more press killings than any other military.
