New rules in New South Wales could punish real‑estate agents who advertise a home for less than its real value. Agents could face fines up to $110,000 or three times their commission. The plan also wants listings to show the price the seller actually wants and a brief explanation of how that number was chosen. The changes come after a high‑profile case where an agent was accused of underquoting over 100 homes.
