Prince Andrew, the former Duke of York, was arrested this week over alleged wrongdoing while working as a trade envoy. Buckingham Palace says it won’t block the government’s plan to remove him from the line of succession after the police investigate. The move would need new laws and support from MPs, peers and Commonwealth monarchs. Meanwhile police are searching his former Windsor home.
