Russell Brand, the former comedian and TV host, has been charged with a new rape case and another sexual assault. He already faces five earlier charges, and police say more women have spoken out. Investigations began after a Channel 4 documentary and the Sunday Times report. Brand denies the accusations and says he has never been a rapist. He will appear in court on Jan 20 and may go to trial in June.
