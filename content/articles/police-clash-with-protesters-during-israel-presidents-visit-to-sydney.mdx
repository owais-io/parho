During a visit to Sydney by Israel’s president Isaac Herzog, thousands gathered outside Town Hall to protest. Video shows police punching and pepper‑spraying demonstrators, sparking criticism. New South Wales Premier Chris Minns defended the officers, saying his anti‑protest rules made the situation hard. Nine people were charged, and more may face charges. The police say they are ready for more protests in Sydney and Melbourne the next day.
