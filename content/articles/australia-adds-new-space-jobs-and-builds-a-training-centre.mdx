Australia’s Defence Force (ADF) has created two new space‑related roles—a space operations officer and a specialist—and is opening a brand‑new training building at HMAS Harman in Canberra. Successful applicants will get specialist training in space operations, which the ADF now treats as a core domain alongside air, land, sea and cyber. The move follows the recent appointment of Australia’s first female astronaut, Katherine Bennell‑Pegg.
