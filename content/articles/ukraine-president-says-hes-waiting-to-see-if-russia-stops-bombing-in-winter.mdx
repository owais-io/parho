Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, said he will wait to see if Russia stops its strikes on Ukrainian cities and energy sites during a harsh winter. President Trump claimed that Vladimir Putin agreed to a week‑long pause after a personal request. No formal ceasefire exists, and Russia hasn’t confirmed the pause, but attacks fell after Trump’s announcement. Ukraine and Russia plan to meet in Abu Dhabi for talks, while Iran tensions may affect the timing.
