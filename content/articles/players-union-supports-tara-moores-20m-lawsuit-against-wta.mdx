The break‑away players' union started by Novak Djokovic is backing Tara Moore’s $20m lawsuit against the WTA. Moore, a former British doubles champion, says she was banned for four years after a positive drug test that she claims was caused by contaminated meat. The union’s lawyers are helping her for free, and the union also has its own case against the WTA, ATP and the Grand Slam tournaments over prize money and rankings.
