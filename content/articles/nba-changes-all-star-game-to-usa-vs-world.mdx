Last year’s NBA All‑Star Game was criticized for being a lazy showcase. The league switched from the usual East vs West to a new “USA vs World” tournament. Three teams – U.S. Stars, U.S. Veterans, and International players – played short, intense matches. Players like Wembanyama and Edwards raised the energy, making the game feel more competitive and fun for fans.
