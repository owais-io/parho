Police banned fans of Israeli club Maccabi Tel Aviv from a Birmingham match, sparking concerns from Jewish groups that it looked antisemitic. The ban was justified by police intelligence on possible disorder, citing incidents in Europe. Prime Minister Keir Starmer called the move wrong. The decision is now under review by MPs and the police chief will appear before a committee.
