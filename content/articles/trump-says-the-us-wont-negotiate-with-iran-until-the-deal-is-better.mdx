Trump said the United States will keep fighting Iran and won’t settle for a cease‑fire unless the deal is better. He warned of more strikes on Iranian oil sites and said the U.S. might attack Kharg Island again. Iran, meanwhile, says it wants no cease‑fire and will defend itself. The fighting has closed the Hormuz Strait, cut oil shipments, and raised global fuel prices.
