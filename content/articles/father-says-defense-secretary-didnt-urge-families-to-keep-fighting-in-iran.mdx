Father says the defense secretary didn’t ask families to keep fighting in Iran. After a tanker crash killed six U.S. soldiers, Hegseth claimed relatives urged him to “finish the job.” The Ohio father says he had no such conversation and has spoken with Trump and the secretary about the war’s necessity. Other families also say the conflict is unwarranted, and the Pentagon says the talks were private.
