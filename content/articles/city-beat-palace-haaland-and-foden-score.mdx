Manchester City beat Crystal Palace 4‑1 at the City of Westminster. Erling Haaland opened the scoring and added a late penalty, while Phil Foden hit his sixth goal in four games. The win pushed City back within two points of Arsenal in the Premier League table. Manager Pep Guardiola said the result was good but his side still had room to improve. Palace struggled with injuries and a delayed flight, missing key players.
