A 13‑year‑old former student was arrested after he stabbed two classmates at Kingsbury High School in north‑west London. The attacks happened around lunchtime, leaving both boys seriously hurt but stable in hospital. Police found the suspect at a nearby mosque but say there’s no terrorist link; the case may stem from a personal dispute. Counter‑terrorism officers are investigating, while the school remains closed for safety.
