Trump issued an order telling the Department of Homeland Security to pay TSA agents right away, after they went unpaid for over a month because Congress failed to approve a bill that would fund the agency. The move comes amid a 42‑day federal shutdown that has left airport workers struggling to meet bills. Paychecks are expected to hit payrolls by March 30, easing long‑standing staffing and security delays.
