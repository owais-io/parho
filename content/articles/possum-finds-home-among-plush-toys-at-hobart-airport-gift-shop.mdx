While shopping in Hobart airport’s gift shop, a tourist discovered a live brushtail possum hiding among stuffed animals. Staff and customers were surprised, but the animal stayed calm. Airport staff safely removed it from the terminal, and the shop plans to name it and display a small memorial. The possum is a common Tasmanian marsupial that can live in both wild and human settings.
