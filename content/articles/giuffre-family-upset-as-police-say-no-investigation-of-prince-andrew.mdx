The Giuffre family is upset because the Metropolitan Police said it will not investigate Prince Andrew over claims that he had sex with Virginia Giuffre, who was trafficked by Jeffrey Epstein. The police say they looked at evidence, found nothing that points to a crime, and will not act unless new evidence appears. The family says they were not told the decision was coming and want justice for survivors.
