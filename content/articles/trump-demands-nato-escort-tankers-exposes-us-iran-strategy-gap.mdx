Trump is asking NATO and other countries to escort oil tankers through the Hormuz Strait as part of a new US effort to push back against Iran. But the United States has no clear plan for dealing with Iran’s threats. Iran has already hit several merchant ships and the US has mainly targeted Iranian naval and missile bases. Few allies have ready warships for escort duties, and many are reluctant to get involved.
