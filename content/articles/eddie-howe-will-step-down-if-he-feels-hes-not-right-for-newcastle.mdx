Newcastle United’s boss Eddie Howe says he’d quit if he ever doubted he was the right person to lead the club. His team are in 12th place after three straight league losses and are headed to a match against Tottenham, hoping to end the slump. Howe insists he’s confident but vows to put the club first and will resign if he thinks he can’t help the players.
