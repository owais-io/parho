Toby Carvery’s owners, Mitchells & Butlers, are facing eviction from an Enfield site after they cut down a 500‑year‑old oak tree in the car park. The council says the move was illegal and wants an apology and money to repair the damage. Legal action is underway, and the case may go to court later this year. A local toy company has offered to help pay for tree‑care costs.
