Jamaican bobsleigh team is chasing a medal at the 2034 Winter Olympics. Inspired by their 1988 debut, they now run a “Back to the Well” program that turns Jamaica’s sprinters into bobsleigh pilots. They’ve won eight North American Cup golds, but they have no home track and rely on private money. President Chris Stokes says resourcefulness beats riches, and the team wants to prove they belong.
