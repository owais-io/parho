Manchester City widened their gap to the top of the Women’s Super League to nine points after a 1-0 win at London City. Khadija Shaw scored a late goal in the 86th minute, keeping City on track for the title. It was the club’s 12th straight league victory, boosting confidence before a tough match against the West London side. London City fought back but couldn’t finish the game.
