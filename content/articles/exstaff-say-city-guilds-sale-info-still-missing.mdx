Retired senior staff at City & Guilds met with chair Ann Limb and chief executive Kirstie Donnelly on 2 Dec to ask why the organisation’s sale was kept from stakeholders, including pensioners. They said no official notice had been given. Limb apologized for the “oversight” and promised a communication, but the staff are still waiting for an update. The lack of transparency could hurt future members.
