Australian families of fallen soldiers are demanding reparations after Israel’s military bulldozed parts of a Gaza cemetery where their relatives are buried. The graves of World Wars veterans were destroyed in 2023, leaving families without clear answers about their loved ones’ remains. The Australian government, led by Prime Minister Albanese, has called on Israel to explain the actions, seek an apology, and restore the damaged graves.
