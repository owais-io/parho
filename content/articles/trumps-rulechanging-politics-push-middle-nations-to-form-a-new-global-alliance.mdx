Trump’s recent tweets and policy moves feel like he’s rewritten the rules of the game, and many observers say the world can’t keep playing by the old playbook.  The letter argues that the so‑called “middle powers” – countries that aren’t US, Russia or China – should unite to create a new coalition that can check the US’s power and promote peace and trade.  It also questions whether America’s checks‑and‑balances system is enough and calls for a fresh debate on stronger safeguards.
