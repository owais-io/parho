An eye doctor in Tehran says more than 400 protestors have been shot in the eyes and heads, many now blind or needing eye removal. Hospitals are overwhelmed after authorities shut down internet and mobile phones, blocking outside help. Doctors report security forces deliberately aiming at eyes and vital organs with both rifles and “less‑lethal” shotguns. Over 2,000 people have died and more than 16,000 are jailed amid the country’s biggest protest since 2009.
