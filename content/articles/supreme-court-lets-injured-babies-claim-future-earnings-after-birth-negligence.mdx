After a Supreme Court decision, babies hurt during childbirth can now ask for money to cover earnings they’ll lose because of their injuries. The ruling changes a 40‑year old rule that stopped children from claiming “lost years” damages. The NHS faces higher costs, with its medical‑negligence bill already at £60 billion. The case highlights the need to stop mistakes at birth and cover families’ future losses.
