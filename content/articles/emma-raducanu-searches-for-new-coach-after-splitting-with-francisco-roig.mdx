Emma Raducanu, Britain's No.1 tennis player, has said goodbye to coach Francisco Roig after a loss at the Australian Open. She is now looking for a new coach, following a long pattern of short coaching stints. The split comes as she struggles with her forehand and a foot injury. Raducanu will play the Transylvania Open in Romania next, with LTA coach Alexis Canter by her side.
