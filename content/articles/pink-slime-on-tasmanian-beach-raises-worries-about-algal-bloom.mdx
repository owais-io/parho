Scientists in Tasmania are looking into pink slime that appeared on a remote beach. They think it might be an algal bloom, a natural but sometimes harmful growth of tiny sea plants. Samples are being tested, but no results yet. The pink stuff could be a harmless species that feeds on small animals, or it could harm fish and crustaceans. The government is waiting for tests and warning the public.
