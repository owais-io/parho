New UK travel rules say dual nationals must show a British passport or pay for a certificate when boarding flights, ferries or trains. The Home Office said airlines can accept an expired British passport as a substitute. Many people with dual citizenship have expired passports and are worried they can’t travel to see family or attend events. Liberal Democrats want a short grace period to get new passports.
