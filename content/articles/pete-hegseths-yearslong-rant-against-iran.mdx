The Guardian shows how former U.S. defense secretary Pete Hegseth has called Iran a “mortal enemy” for years. He used books, TV programs and a 2018 speech in Jerusalem to warn about Iran’s nuclear plans and link the threat to Islamists. His hard‑line remarks mix anti‑Islamic rhetoric with calls for war and close ties to Israel, shaping U.S. policy.
