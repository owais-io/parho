ICE has tripled the amount of data it stores on Microsoft’s Azure cloud in the past year, according to leaked documents. The agency, which has increased its budget and staff, is using Microsoft’s cloud services, AI tools and productivity apps to help with arrests and deportations. Employees and tech workers are worried that Microsoft tech may be enabling aggressive immigration enforcement. The company says it doesn’t allow mass surveillance, but has faced internal protests.
