Alberta groups that want to leave Canada have quietly met with US officials, asking for help to become a US state. None of the activists hold elected office, and only about 18 % of Albertans support independence. The move echoes Quebec’s past separatist trips to France and raises fears of treason and foreign interference. Canadian officials warn the US might use the push to undermine national unity.
