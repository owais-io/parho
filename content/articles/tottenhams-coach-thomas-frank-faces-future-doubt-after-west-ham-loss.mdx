After Tottenham lost 2-1 to West Ham in stoppage time, fans shouted for coach Thomas Frank to be fired. The club’s chief executive sent a supportive letter asking for patience, while Frank said he’d stay and turn the team around. Tottenham have only won three of their last 15 league games and sit 14th in the table. The club added former Ajax boss John Heitinga to the coaching staff, but many doubt Frank’s future.
