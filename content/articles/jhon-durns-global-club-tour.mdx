Jhon Durán, the Colombian striker, has played in six countries by age 23 after leaving Aston Villa. He moved to Saudi Arabia’s Al‑Nassr, then spent half a season on loan at Fenerbahce before joining Russian side Zenit St Petersburg on another short‑term deal. Despite good goal‑scoring at Villa, Durán has prioritized money over legacy, making his career a rapid, money‑driven globetrotting adventure.
