England used a warm‑up match against the Lions to prepare for the Ashes. The match was played in a small park with few spectators, but it gave England’s No. 3 batsman, Ollie Pope, a big boost. Pope scored a confident 90 in the second innings after a century earlier, helping England win by five wickets. The team also saw useful contributions from Zak Crawley, Ben Duckett, and Joe Root, while the bowlers were largely tested.
