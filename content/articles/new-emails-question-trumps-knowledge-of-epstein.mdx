Emails from Jeffrey Epstein that Democrats released mention Donald Trump, raising doubts about his earlier claims of ignorance. An 2011 note calls Trump a “dog that hasn’t barked,” while a 2019 message to author Michael Wolff says Trump asked Epstein to leave Mar‑a‑Lago. Trump insists he never knew about Epstein’s abuse, but the emails suggest otherwise, adding fuel to the controversy over the former president and the Epstein scandal.
