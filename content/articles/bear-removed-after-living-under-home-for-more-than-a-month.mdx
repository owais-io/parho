Ken Johnson’s 550‑lb black bear lived in his crawl‑space for over a month. He asked California’s wildlife agency, but they couldn’t trap it. A non‑profit called Bear League used paint‑ball guns and an electrified mat to drive the bear out. The animal tried to return after two days but was stopped. Johnson can now fix the damage, including a broken gas line, after the bear had moved in following the Eaton wildfire.
