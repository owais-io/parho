A think‑tank warns that a flare‑up in the Middle East could raise oil and gas prices enough to wipe out the £300 a year boost in living standards that UK households are expected to see next year. The UK’s dependence on Middle‑Eastern gas makes it vulnerable if the Strait of Hormuz is blocked. The Foundation calls for a targeted social tariff to shield low‑income families, rather than a blanket subsidy.
