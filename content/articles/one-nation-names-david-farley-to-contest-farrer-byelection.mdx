One Nation has chosen 69‑year‑old irrigator David Farley to run in the Farrer by‑election after long‑time Liberal MP Sussan Ley quit. The 126,000‑sq‑km rural seat is up for grabs on 9 May. Farley won the party’s support over other local candidates. The move tests One Nation’s local reach and the new Liberal opposition leader Angus Taylor’s challenge. Other parties still haven’t named their candidates.
