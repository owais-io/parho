Two brothers from the same elementary school as five‑year‑old Liam Ramos were taken into ICE custody along with their mother, who has an asylum case. The family was moved to a Texas detention center, raising anger among school staff and local lawyers. Staff tried to protect the kids and comfort them. The case highlights the tension between federal immigration enforcement and the rights of families in Minnesota.
