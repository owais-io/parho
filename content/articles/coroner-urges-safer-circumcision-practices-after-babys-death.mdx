Coroner warns that more babies could die from unsafe circumcisions after a 6‑month‑old boy died from infection. Mohamed Abdisamad was circumcised in February, fell ill, and died in hospital. The inquest found no training, accreditation, or infection‑control rules for those who perform the procedure. The report urges the government to set up safety standards, after‑care guidelines, and proper record‑keeping, and to require professional accreditation for circumcisers.
