If you’re thinking about getting a cat, experts say use a mix of feelings and logic. Start by noticing your emotions and asking why you want a pet. Visualize future life with a kitten, and consider small steps like borrowing a cat or staying with a friend’s pet. Use experiments and future‑self thinking to see if a cat fits your routine. This approach can help you make a confident, realistic choice.
