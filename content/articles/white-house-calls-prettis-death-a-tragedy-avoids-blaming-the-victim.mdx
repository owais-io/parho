The White House press secretary, Karoline Leavitt, said the killing of Alex Pretti by ICE agents in Minneapolis was a tragedy. She avoided the usual victim‑blaming language that Trump’s advisers sometimes use, and said the president wants the investigation to run its course. Leavitt also mentioned a discussion with Minnesota governor Tim Walz about possibly pulling some federal agents out of the state.
