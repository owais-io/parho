FIFA has added a new $60 ticket tier to calm angry fans. Each match will have about 1,000 of these cheaper tickets, split between the two teams’ supporters. National football associations will hand them out to loyal fans. While the UK Prime Minister praised the move, he urged FIFA to cut prices further. The change comes amid criticism of the tournament’s high ticket prices and dynamic pricing.
