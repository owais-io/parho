Gregory Lynn, a 59‑year‑old former Jetstar pilot, was denied bail by a Victorian court as he seeks a permanent stay of his murder case. He was found guilty in 2024 of killing a 73‑year‑old camper in 2020, but the Court of Appeal overturned that conviction, saying the earlier trial had a miscarriage of justice. Lynn wants to be released while a new trial is arranged, but the judge said the seriousness of the charges keeps him in custody.
