Manchester City beat Real Madrid 3-2 in Madrid, thanks to Erling Haaland’s penalty goal. It was Pep Guardiola’s first win at the Bernabéu after a recent loss in the Champions League. Real’s coach Xabi Alonso saw his record drop to two wins in eight games, with key players missing. Madrid had chances, including a header off the bar, but City’s defense held strong, leaving Alonso’s future uncertain.
