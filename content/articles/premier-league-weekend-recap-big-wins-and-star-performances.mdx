Chelsea came back to beat West Ham 3‑2, with João Pedro scoring and setting up the winner. Arsenal thumped Leeds 4‑0, using new signings and Noni Madueke. Tottenham drew 2‑2 with Manchester City, while United edged Fulham 3‑2 thanks to Sesko’s late strike. Liverpool beat Newcastle 4‑1, and Aston Villa lost 1‑0 to Brentford. Key moments included standout plays from João Pedro, Sesko, and Szoboszlai.
