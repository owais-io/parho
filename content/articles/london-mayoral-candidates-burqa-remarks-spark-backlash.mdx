Laila Cunningham, Reform UK's 2028 London mayoral hopeful, said women wearing the burqa should face stop‑and‑search, calling them criminal. Her comments drew angry reactions from Muslim groups, the mayor, and MPs, who called the remarks hateful and divisive. Critics say the comments target a small number of Muslim women and could fuel hate attacks. The debate shows how political rhetoric about the veil can inflame tensions.
