In Australia, Indigenous leaders and activists report a flood of racist messages on social media, from threats of violence to slurs. The harassment has surged after the 2023 Voice to Parliament debate. Researchers at the Jumbunna Institute collect hundreds of hate reports each year. Politicians like Malarndirri McCarthy have announced a parliamentary inquiry into online racism. Many victims take extra safety steps, showing the real‑world danger of digital hate.
