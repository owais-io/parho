Because the U.S.–Israel war with Iran shut down Gulf airspace, many airlines can’t fly the usual Sydney‑to‑London route that stops in Dubai, Abu Dhabi or Doha. Cathay Pacific is now selling business‑class tickets for about £20,000 (A$39,577) to meet demand, even though the usual price is only £3,000‑£4,000. Passengers can also look at cheaper Asia‑via routes, but those have limited seats and long travel times.
