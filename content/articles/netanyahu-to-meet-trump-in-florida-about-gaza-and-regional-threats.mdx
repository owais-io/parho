Israeli prime minister Benjamin Netanyahu is flying to Florida to meet former U.S. President Donald Trump at Mar‑a‑Lago. They will talk about ending fighting in Gaza and whether Israel will launch new attacks on Hezbollah in Lebanon or Iran. The U.S. worries that Netanyahu is delaying the next stage of a peace plan that would hand Gaza over to a new Palestinian authority. Netanyahu’s upcoming election also makes him eager to keep Trump’s backing.
