After the FBI searched the LA Unified School District office and superintendent Alberto Carvalho’s home, the board put him on paid leave. They named assistant superintendent Andres Chait as interim head. The raids were linked to a failed tech company, AllHere, that had a $3 million contract to supply a chatbot. The investigation is still ongoing and the district says it will look into the situation.
