Yao Ming, the 7‑foot‑6 former basketball star, now serves as an independent delegate in China’s National People’s Congress and the advisory CPPCC. In 2024 he proposed a 24‑hour digital detox for kids to help them disconnect from screens. Though most furniture is made for average heights, Yao’s towering presence makes him hard to miss in the Great Hall, showing how athletes are getting involved in politics.
