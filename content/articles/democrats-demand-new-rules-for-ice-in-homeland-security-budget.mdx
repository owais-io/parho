Senate Democrat Chuck Schumer wants the Department of Homeland Security budget to include new rules limiting ICE. After a Minneapolis shooting that killed Alex Pretti, he demands a mask‑ban for ICE agents, body cameras, local police coordination, a conduct code and independent investigations. Republicans plan to vote on the bill soon. If the Senate can't agree, the bill must be passed again by the House, raising the risk of a government shutdown that could cut funding for TSA and FEMA.
