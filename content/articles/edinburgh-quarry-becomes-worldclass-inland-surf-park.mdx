Lost Shore is a £60m surf resort built in a former quarry outside Edinburgh. It uses Wavegarden Cove tech to create up to 1,000 waves a day, giving beginners and pros a great experience. The lagoon is fed by a nearby canal and cleaned naturally. The park also hosts a research lab that tests wetsuits and surf therapy. It drew over 100,000 visitors in its first year, adding £18m to the local economy and 130 jobs.
