The South Australian Liberal Party has dropped Carston Woodhouse, a candidate for the seat of Wright, after his controversial comments on a Christian podcast. Woodhouse said feminism and homosexuality were “demonic” and criticized gender transition. Party leader Ashton Hurn said the comments were unacceptable and removed him, even though the party said it was still the same candidate. The move was a surprise as the election is weeks away.
