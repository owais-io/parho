The Supreme Court has declared Donald Trump’s worldwide tariff program illegal, ruling that only Congress can set taxes and tariffs. Chief Justice John Roberts, joined by three conservative and three liberal justices, said the president misused the International Emergency Powers Act to impose tariffs on more than 80 countries. Trump angrily criticized the court. The decision could signal a shift, as the Court may be less inclined to side with Trump in future cases.
