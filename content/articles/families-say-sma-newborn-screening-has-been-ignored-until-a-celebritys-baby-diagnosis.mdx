Jesy Nelson’s twins were diagnosed with SMA1, a rare muscle‑weakening disease. Her story hit the news and the health secretary said he’d look into it. But families who have begged for newborn screening for years say the government has ignored them. England doesn’t test babies for SMA, though other countries do. Families talk about treatment costs, gene therapy successes and hope a new trial will change things.
