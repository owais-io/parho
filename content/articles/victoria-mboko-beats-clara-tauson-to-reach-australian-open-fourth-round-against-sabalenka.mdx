Victoria Mboko, 19, won a tense match against Clara Tauson at the Australian Open, earning her first fourth‑round spot in a slam. She survived match points and won 7‑6(5), 5‑7, 6‑3. The win puts her face‑to‑face with world No.1 Aryna Sabalenka, who beat Anastasia Potapova 7‑6, 7‑6. Sabalenka, now a Gucci ambassador, praised mental strength. Other highlights: Medvedev and the British doubles pair fell.
