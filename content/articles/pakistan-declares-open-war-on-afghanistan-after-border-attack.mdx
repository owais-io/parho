Pakistan has declared an open war against Afghanistan after a cross‑border attack. Islamabad launched air strikes on Kabul and other Afghan cities, claiming 133 Taliban fighters were killed. Afghanistan says 55 Pakistani soldiers died and that it captured some troops. Both sides report heavy casualties, but the numbers differ. Global leaders, including the UK, Russia and China, urge a quick ceasefire and dialogue.
