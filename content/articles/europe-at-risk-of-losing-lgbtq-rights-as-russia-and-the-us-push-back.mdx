Former Irish prime minister Leo Varadkar warns that LGBTQ+ rights in Europe are slipping as Russia and the United States push conservative agendas. He says Russia promotes a conservative Christian view and is spreading it across Europe, while the U.S. has introduced hundreds of bills that could roll back gay rights. Varadkar stresses that Europe must defend its values and that progress is not guaranteed. He calls for vigilance against any backlash that could affect other groups as well.
