An Oxfam trustee, Dr Balwant Singh, stepped down from the charity’s board because he said the organisation failed to govern properly and treated its former chief executive, Halima Begum, cruelly. Singh said the board had lost integrity, transparency and accountability and called for an external investigation. Begum was sacked in December after disputes with board members, and Oxfam has now ordered an independent review of its governance.
