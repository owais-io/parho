Ford CEO Jim Farley said Western car makers are fighting for survival against Chinese rivals. To stay competitive, Ford is teaming up with France’s Renault to build two small electric cars that could hit the road by early 2028. Chinese brands like BYD and Chery sell affordable, well‑reviewed EVs, putting pressure on Europe and the U.S. Ford has already cut thousands of jobs and slowed its EV plans. Meanwhile, BMW is replacing its chairman, Oliver Zipse, with Milan Nedeljković.
