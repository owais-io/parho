A UJS survey of 1,000 UK university students found that about one‑fifth would refuse or be reluctant to share a flat with a Jewish student. Nearly a quarter saw antisemitic remarks, and almost half witnessed support for the Oct. 7 Hamas attacks. Many students heard pro‑terror slogans, and two‑thirds said protests disrupted their studies. Only a small share reported the abuse or challenged it. The report calls for stronger campus action.
