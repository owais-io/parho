The Trump administration announced it would stop more than $250 million in Medicaid payments to Minnesota while it investigates alleged fraud linked to the state’s Somali community. Vice‑president Mike Vance said the pause would pressure Minnesota to clean up its programs. The decision also means new federal funding for durable medical equipment will be suspended for six months. Minnesota’s governor has denied the fraud claims and called the move politically motivated.
