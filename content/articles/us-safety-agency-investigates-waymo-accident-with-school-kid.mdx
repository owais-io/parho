A child was hit by a Waymo self‑driving car near a Santa Monica elementary school, sparking a federal safety investigation. The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration says the little boy crossed the street during school‑drop‑off hours and the Waymo vehicle, moving about 17 mph, slowed to under 6 mph before impact. Investigators will look at Waymo’s behavior around school zones, speed limits and post‑crash response. Waymo claims a human driver would have hit the child at 14 mph.
