Democrats are pushing Congress to hold a quick vote to limit President Trump’s authority to launch attacks on Iran. They say the president’s recent strikes and threats could spiral into a wider conflict. Lawmakers want to reassert their constitutional role in authorizing war, while Trump claims the strikes are tied to alleged election interference. The debate comes amid rising tensions and new US‑Israeli airstrikes on Iranian targets.
