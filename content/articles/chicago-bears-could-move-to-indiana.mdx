Chicago's oldest NFL team, the Bears, are looking to leave Soldier Field because lease terms are too expensive and the stadium is too small. Illinois lawmakers are trying to keep the team in-state, but Indiana recently passed a bill that could let the Bears move to Hammond, a suburb only half an hour from Chicago. Supporters say the new site would help revive a rust‑belt city, while critics fear a loss of city pride.
