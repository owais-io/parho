At the Consumer Electronics Show, Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang announced Alpamayo, an AI that lets self‑driving cars reason through rare, complex situations instead of just reacting. The tech, tested in a Mercedes‑Benz CLA demo, explains its decisions and can navigate roadworks and odd driver behaviour. Huang also previewed new chips that promise five times more power for AI, helping Nvidia stay competitive.
