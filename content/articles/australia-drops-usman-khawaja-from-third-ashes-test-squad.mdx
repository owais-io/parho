Australia left out opener Usman Khawaja for the third Ashes Test in Adelaide, raising questions about his future. Captain Pat Cummins is back after a tough back injury. The side is aiming to clinch a 3‑0 lead with Travis Head and Jake Weatherald opening, and Josh Inglis added at number seven. Nathan Lyon returns, while bowler Michael Neser misses the match. Khawaja, 38, may return later after his back injury.
