Saudi Arabia says the head of Yemen’s Southern Transitional Council, Aidarous al‑Zubaidi, disappeared after refusing to board a plane for peace talks. The Saudi coalition claims he fled with a large armed group. The council has removed him from leadership and is referring him for treason. This incident adds tension between Saudi‑led forces and the UAE‑backed separatists amid the ongoing Yemen conflict.
