Putin says Ukraine fired a sea drone that hit the Russian LNG tanker Metagaz, causing it to explode and sink off Libya. The 30 crew were rescued. The tanker was part of Russia’s “shadow fleet” that evades Western sanctions. Ukraine hasn’t replied, but the incident could worsen global gas markets. Putin also hinted Russia might stop supplying Europe’s gas, looking to new buyers.
