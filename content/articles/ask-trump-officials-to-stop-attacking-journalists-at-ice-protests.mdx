Journalists are being hit more often when covering protests about ICE, a spike that has worried news groups. After seeing 172 assaults last year, reporters and the Reporters Committee wrote to the Department of Homeland Security, demanding better rules and training so officers don’t strike or detain the press. Trump’s administration is hiring thousands more ICE agents, and a judge said police can’t stop journalists unless they’ve done something illegal. The DHS has not replied.
