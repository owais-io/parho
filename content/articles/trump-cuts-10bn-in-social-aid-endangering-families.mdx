The Trump administration has frozen $10 billion of federal money that supports childcare and families in need. Five states – California, Colorado, Illinois, Minnesota and New York – can’t access the Child Care and Development Fund, Temporary Assistance for Needy Families or the Social Service Block Grant until they submit detailed paperwork to prove there’s no fraud. Experts warn that hundreds of thousands of families could lose childcare, food and other aid, causing chaos, especially for working women.
