Cyclone Hayley struck northwest Australia on Tuesday night after a heatwave. The storm grew to a category 2 with 105‑mph winds and heavy rain, hitting near Lombadina. The Bureau warned of flash flooding and property damage. Two homes lost roofs, roads were closed, and clean‑up will take weeks. Australia’s cyclone season runs Oct‑May, with five storms so far this year. Meanwhile, heavy rain in southern Spain flooded streets and killed three people.
