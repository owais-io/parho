Brazil’s former president Jair Bolsonaro was allowed to serve his 27‑year prison sentence at home because of serious health problems. A Supreme Court judge gave him 90 days of house arrest after he was hospitalized with pneumonia, kidney issues and other ailments. The decision means he stays in his home instead of prison, pending a medical review. Meanwhile, his son Flávio is eyeing the 2024 election, sparking speculation about the family’s political future.
