ICE agents launched a big raid in Maine in January, targeting immigrants. Many say the operation used racial profiling and has scared workers, especially people of color. Labor unions and local residents have organized protests and support networks. Senator Susan Collins, a Republican up for re‑election, says the raid is over, but the state’s anger could hurt her chances in November’s Senate race.
