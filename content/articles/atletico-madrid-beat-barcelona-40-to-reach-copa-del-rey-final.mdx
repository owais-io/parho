In a dramatic first half, Atletico Madrid ran away with a 4‑0 win over Barcelona in the Copa del Rey’s first leg. An own goal by Eric García and goals from Ademola Lookman, Antoine Griezmann and Julián Alvarez gave the visitors a huge cushion. Barcelona’s coach Hansi Flick vowed to fight back, but the loss means they must win twice at home in the next match to stay in the tie.
