A New South Wales MP, Anthony D’Adam, wrote to Home Affairs Minister Tony Burke asking for an investigation into an Israeli report that lists him and other politicians as promoting antisemitic and anti‑Israel content. D’Adam says the dossier is a tool of foreign interference meant to scare and discredit critics of Israel. The report names 25 groups and five politicians, including D’Adam, Adam Bandt and others. He denies being antisemitic and wants to know if Australians helped compile it. Burke said the letter will be forwarded to the relevant agencies.
