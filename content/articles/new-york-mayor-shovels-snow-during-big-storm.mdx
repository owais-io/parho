New York’s new mayor, Zohran Mamdani, was everywhere during a big snow storm. He shovelled streets, appeared on TV, and even read to kids online. He worked hard to keep the city moving and helped people who were cold. Ten people died on the streets, so Mamdani said the city would do more to help the homeless and set up extra protection. He promised to keep everyone safe from the cold.
