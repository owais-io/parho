Patrick Reed won the Dubai Desert Classic, beating rival David Puig. Both players are part of the break‑away LIV Golf tour, so their win highlights the clash between LIV and the PGA Tour. Reed finished four strokes ahead and said his LIV contract has ended, hinting he could return to the PGA if he finishes high enough on the European Order of Merit. The victory also put pressure on the DP World Tour to review its entry rules.
