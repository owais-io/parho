The Green Party has picked Hannah Spencer, a Trafford councillor and plumber, to fight the Gorton and Denton by‑election in Greater Manchester. Spencer says the Greens are the only party that can stop Reform UK’s Matthew Goodwin from taking the seat and criticises Labour for losing voter support. She vows to bring hope back to the community. The result could test Labour’s lead and the popularity of Prime Minister Keir Starmer.
