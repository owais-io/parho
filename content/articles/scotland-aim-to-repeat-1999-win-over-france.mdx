Scotland’s rugby team is looking to recreate the surprise victory they scored against France in 1999. In that match, the Scots knocked out the defending champions and lifted the European title in front of a packed crowd. Now, with the 2026 championship approaching, the squad hopes to outplay a strong French side and win a second trophy. Fans and players alike are eager for a repeat of the historic win.
