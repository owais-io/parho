At a Bristol nursery, a 30‑year‑old teacher named Nathan Bennett was found guilty of raping and molesting five young boys. He gained their trust by being overly affectionate and even kissed them on the cheek. Parents had complained three times in six months before he was caught, but it wasn’t until a manager saw him on CCTV moving a child’s trousers that police intervened. He now faces a lengthy prison sentence.
