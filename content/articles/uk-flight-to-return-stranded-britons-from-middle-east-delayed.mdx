The first government‑chartered flight to bring UK nationals home from the Middle East didn’t leave because of problems loading passengers, a minister said. The plane will fly to the UK on Thursday instead of Wednesday. Over 138,000 Britons have signed up for help, mostly in the UAE. The delay comes amid war‑related missile attacks that have disrupted flights and left many stranded, including a woman in Qatar who feels abandoned.
