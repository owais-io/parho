Trump’s budget cuts have hit U.S. science hard, slashing staff and freezing thousands of research grants. Many scientists now think about leaving the country. Europe and the UK see this as a chance to welcome these experts. France, the UK, Canada and the EU have launched money‑backed programs to attract top researchers. The move could boost science and economies outside America.
