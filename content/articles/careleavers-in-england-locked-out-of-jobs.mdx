Many young people who leave England’s care system are struggling to find work because employers promise to hire but don’t change how they recruit. A charity says care‑leavers are three times more likely to be unemployed than their peers, with 40% of 19‑21‑year‑olds in the care system not working or studying. Despite government plans and a £1bn youth employment scheme, hiring gaps remain, so the charity is releasing a toolkit to help employers adapt.
