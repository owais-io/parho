Brother Dong, a former Chinese police officer now living in Frankfurt, drives 5,000 km each month to bring tapioca pearls to Ukraine. He opens bubble‑tea shops in Odesa and Lviv, giving locals a taste of home and a symbol of support while also distributing blankets, gloves and heaters. Dong is one of a few Chinese volunteers who, despite pressure from Beijing, want to stand with Ukraine against Russia.
