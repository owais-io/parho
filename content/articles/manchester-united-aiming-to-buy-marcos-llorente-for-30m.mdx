Michael Carrick is the new coach at Manchester United and the club is looking for a central midfielder. Rumours say the team might pay £30 million for Marcos Llorente from Atlético Madrid, or chase other players like Rúben Neves. Other clubs are also eyeing players – Tottenham and Newcastle want Sassuolo’s Tarik, Roma wants Donyell Malen, and Brentford may pay £13 million for Matteo Cancellieri. The transfer window is full of speculation.
