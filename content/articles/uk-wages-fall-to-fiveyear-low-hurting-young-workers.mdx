In January, UK wages grew only 3.8%, the slowest in more than five years. Young people (18‑24) saw unemployment hit the highest level since 2015, with many job‑seekers left out. Employers are hiring fewer entry‑level staff, and public‑sector pay rises have been cut, dragging down the overall figure. The Bank of England may keep rates steady at 3.75% as inflation and oil prices stay high.
