Donald Trump said the U.S. needs Greenland, sparking a fight with Denmark, which still runs the island’s foreign policy. Trump says the move is for national security and to block China from Arctic minerals and shipping routes. Denmark and Greenland’s leaders warn the U.S. takeover would break NATO and hurt their independence. Greenland is a strategic military base, rich in rare earths, and melting ice is opening new sea lanes. Trump’s long‑time interest in Greenland is now a real threat.
