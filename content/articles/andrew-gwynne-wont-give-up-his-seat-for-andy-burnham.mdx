Andrew Gwynne, the former health minister who was suspended after a WhatsApp leak, has denied rumours that he will give up his Gorton and Denton seat for Greater Manchester mayor Andy Burnham. The MP, who is still recovering from serious health problems, says he has no plans to step down before the May elections. Burnham has called the speculation rubbish, and Labour is dealing with internal pressure and upcoming election battles.
