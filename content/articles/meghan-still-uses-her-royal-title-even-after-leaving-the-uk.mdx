Meghan and Harry still use their royal titles even after stepping back from the British monarchy. A Harper’s Bazaar profile showed a house manager calling her “Meghan, Duchess of Sussex” in a private New York home, sparking criticism. Some say they love the perks of royalty but avoid the hard work that usually comes with it. Critics question whether their “humanitarian” awards are deserved, pointing out many real workers go unseen.
