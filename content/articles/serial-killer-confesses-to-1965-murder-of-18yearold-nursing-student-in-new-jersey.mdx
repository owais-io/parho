Richard Cottingham, 79, admitted he killed Alys Eberhardt, an 18‑year‑old nursing student, in 1965. The New Jersey police closed the case after his confession. Eberhardt was found beaten and dead at her home in Fair Lawn. Detectives reopened the case in 2021 and gathered new evidence that led to Cottingham’s admission. His confession brings closure to the family after six decades. He is already serving three life sentences for other murders.
