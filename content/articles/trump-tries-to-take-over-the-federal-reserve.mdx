Trump has openly targeted the Federal Reserve, visiting its Washington HQ to criticize a $3.1 billion renovation and push for lower interest rates. He has threatened to fire Fed governor Lisa Cook and is looking to replace Chair Jerome Powell, wanting a leader who will listen to him. The Supreme Court is weighing whether the president can fire a Fed official. Despite Trump’s attacks, the Fed stays calm, and markets keep trusting its decisions.
