Britain has been hit with almost nonstop rain since the start of the year, leaving 76 flood warnings in force. The deluge is hurting small businesses, especially in rural areas, where roads are blocked, crops fail and tradesmen can’t work. A thatcher in Devon says he is losing money because projects keep getting rained out. In Cornwall, a plant nursery has lost trees and must close parts of its site. Even London gardeners struggle with soggy soil.
