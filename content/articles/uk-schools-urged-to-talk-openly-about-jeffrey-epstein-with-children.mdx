Teachers and parents in the UK are being urged to talk openly with children about the crimes of Jeffrey Epstein. A free online seminar organised by Thrive will show how to handle kids’ questions and keep them away from harmful online content. Experts say that if adults don’t speak up, children will look for answers in untrusted sources, risking misinformation and danger.
