During a nearly 90‑minute talk to House Republicans, former President Trump celebrated the US‑led operation that captured Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro, calling it a “brilliant” win with no American deaths. He also mocked Maduro’s public dancing, blamed Democrats for not thanking him, and warned that the GOP must win the mid‑term elections or he will face impeachment again. Trump repeated false claims about a stolen 2020 election and urged voters to support his policies.
