The article says Iran’s main weapon is its readiness to keep the war going. Since the conflict started, the US has spent billions and is asking for even more money to fight Iran. Iran has attacked key Gulf energy sites, like Qatar’s Ras Laffan plant, and threatened to hit US bases if attacked. The war has hurt civilians, cost money, and split allies. It shows three possible ends: a long fight, a US declaration of victory, or a negotiated peace. It also covers how European leaders feel uneasy and how Israel is trying to push its own plans.
