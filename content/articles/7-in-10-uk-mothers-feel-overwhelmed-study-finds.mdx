A new survey shows 71 % of UK mothers feel overloaded and 47 % struggle with anxiety or depression. The study notes the UK has the highest rates of mothers feeling their career suffers from motherhood, and most women do most housework alone. Experts call for NHS staff to regularly ask about mental health and to give more talking‑therapy support. Funding for child‑care services is far below other European countries.
