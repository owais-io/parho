A 16‑year‑old from Florida has been charged with homicide in the November death of his 18‑year‑old stepsister, Anna Kepner, on a Carnival cruise ship. Investigators say she died from a bar hold that strangled her. The suspect, her stepbrother, was named after a court filing in a custody case and was allowed to drink while the ship was in international waters. Federal agencies are now looking into the case.
