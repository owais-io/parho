St Mirren beat Celtic 3‑1 in the Scottish League Cup final, giving the Paisley club its second cup win. It was the third loss for new Celtic boss Wilfried Nancy in as many games and highlights a growing crisis at the club. Fans and pundits blame his tactics and lack of experience. Nancy admitted the defeat was painful and will need to make quick changes.
