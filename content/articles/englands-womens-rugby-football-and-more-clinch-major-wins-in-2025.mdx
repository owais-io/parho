England’s women’s rugby and football teams won big in 2025, with the Rugby World Cup, the Euros, Arsenal’s Champions League title and a top‑five golf ranking. The victories drew record crowds and TV audiences, sparking new fans and players. Participation, coaching and refereeing numbers rose, and local clubs saw more supporters. The success has boosted confidence, raised the sport’s profile and set the stage for more events in 2026.
