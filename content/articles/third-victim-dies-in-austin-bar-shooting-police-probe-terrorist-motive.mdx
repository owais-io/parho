A 30‑year‑old fighter named Jorge Pederson is the third victim in a shooting at Buford’s bar in downtown Austin. The attack left more than a dozen injured and is being investigated as a possible terrorist act linked to retaliation for U.S. airstrikes in Iran. Police say the gunman, 53‑year‑old Ndiaga Diagne, may have acted alone but FBI investigators are exploring extremist ties. First responders reached the scene in 57 seconds, and the bar owner praised their quick action.
