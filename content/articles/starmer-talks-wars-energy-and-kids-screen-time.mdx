Prime Minister Keir Starmer spoke at a London school, saying the wars in Iran and Ukraine could change the world for generations. He also talked about how the fighting could raise energy prices and asked parents to keep children’s screen time short, suggesting new rules for social media for under‑16s. Starmer said the government would look at how to protect kids and keep the country safe amid global tensions.
