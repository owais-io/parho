A British F‑35 pilot in Jordan became the first RAF stealth‑fighter pilot to destroy a target in combat when his radar spotted two Shahed drones. He and nearby Typhoons fired ASRAAM missiles and knocked them out, preventing more drones from approaching the RAF base in Cyprus that had been hit earlier. The pilot said the mission was tense and he focused on safety, not celebration. Afterward he joked about having a beer and getting back to work.
