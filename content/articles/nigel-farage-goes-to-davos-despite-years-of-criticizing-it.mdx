Nigel Farage, who has spent years blasting Davos as a hub for globalists, will finally attend the World Economic Forum this week. He hopes to meet Donald Trump and use the event to warn the U.S. about potential tariffs on the UK and Europe. Though he is a rising UK political figure, his past comments about the WEF and “globalism” have sparked criticism. Still, Farage sees Davos as a key networking chance.
