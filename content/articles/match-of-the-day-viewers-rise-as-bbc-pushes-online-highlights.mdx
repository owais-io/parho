BBC’s new plan to stream Match of the Day highlights online has paid off. While the traditional TV show lost 770,000 viewers, the on‑screen clips released at 8 p.m. on the iPlayer added 1.5 million new fans. By December the total digital audience hit 61.9 million, nearly double the 33 million from last year. The move comes after long‑time presenter Gary Lineker left, and shows people are moving to online viewing.
