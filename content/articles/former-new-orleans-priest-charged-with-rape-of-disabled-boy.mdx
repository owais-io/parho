Mark Francis Ford, a 64‑year‑old ex‑Catholic priest, has been indicted on nine counts, including rape and kidnapping, for sexually abusing a disabled boy between 2006 and 2008 while he was a mentor through a church program. The case adds to a long list of clergy abuse scandals in New Orleans, and Ford faces mandatory life imprisonment if convicted.
