Scottish coach Gregor Townsend says the next Six Nations is a must‑do chance. Scotland have lost 11 straight to Ireland and finished low most years, but the team is healthy and many key players from top clubs are ready. Townsend wants to use the game against Italy in Rome to prove Scotland can compete for a title. He also talks about staying focused despite his future move to Newcastle.
