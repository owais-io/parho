Israel has returned the body of Sergeant Ran Gvili, the last Israeli soldier still held in Gaza after the October 7 attack. With his remains back, all 251 hostages from that day are now returned to Israel. The return is a key step in the U.S. ceasefire plan, which aims to stop fighting, disarm Hamas and move toward a stable, Palestinian‑run Gaza. The deal also includes returning Palestinian remains to Israel.
