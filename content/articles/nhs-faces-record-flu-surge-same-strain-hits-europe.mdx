The NHS is preparing for one of its worst flu winters as a new H3N2 strain has already spread across the UK and Europe. The virus arrived early, causing more people to get sick and hospitals to fill up. The yearly vaccine isn’t a perfect match, but it still helps prevent serious illness. Countries like Germany, France, Spain, Ireland and others are seeing rising cases and hospital admissions, especially among older adults.
