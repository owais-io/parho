Wes Streeting released private WhatsApp chats with former ambassador Peter Mandelson, prompting a government review. The Cabinet Office warned ministers not to share any messages that could trigger a Commons motion to release documents about Mandelson’s US ambassador role. The Met police are investigating claims that Mandelson passed sensitive information to Jeffrey Epstein while a business secretary. Officers searched two properties linked to Mandelson, and Keir Starmer said the release of documents must be handled carefully.
