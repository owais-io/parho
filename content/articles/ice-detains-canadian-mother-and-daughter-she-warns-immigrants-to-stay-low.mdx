Tania Warner, a 47‑year‑old Canadian, and her 7‑year‑old daughter are being held by U.S. ICE after a border stop in Texas. They say the officers ignored their valid paperwork and forced them to sign a “self‑deport” form. Warner says conditions are cruel—handcuffed, no privacy, no lawyer contact—and she warns other immigrants to stay low until the Trump era ends. Canadian politicians are trying to secure their release on a $15,000 bond.
