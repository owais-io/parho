US forces said they hit three drug‑smuggling boats, killing 11 people, in a campaign started in September that has already caused 145 deaths across 42 strikes. The hits were in the Caribbean and eastern Pacific. No US troops were hurt. Critics say the attacks are illegal because victims never faced a threat and had no chance to defend themselves. The strikes are part of a broader push against alleged narco‑terrorists.
