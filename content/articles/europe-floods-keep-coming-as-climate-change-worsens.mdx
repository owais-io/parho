In the past months, Spain, Portugal, France, and the UK have experienced a record number of flooding storms, killing dozens. Scientists say the rains are getting stronger because the planet is warmer. Yet some European leaders and far‑right groups are backing away from climate rules, showing that Europe needs better preparation for a hotter future.
