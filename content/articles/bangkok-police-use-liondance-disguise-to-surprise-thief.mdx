Bangkok police used a lion dance at a temple fair to surprise and arrest a thief who had stolen Buddhist artefacts. The officers had less than a day to plan, wearing masks and costumes so they blended into the crowd. They tackled the suspect amid the festivities and took him to prison. These playful disguises are part of a broader strategy to catch experienced criminals. The videos went viral, raising questions about their effectiveness.
