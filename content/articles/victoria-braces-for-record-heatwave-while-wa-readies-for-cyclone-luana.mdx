Victoria is facing its hottest summer on record, with temperatures above 40 °C and several out‑of‑control bushfires. Residents in towns like Gellibrand and the Otways region have been issued fire‑bans and evacuation warnings, while smoke is already affecting Melbourne and Geelong. The heat is set to peak at 48 °C by mid‑week. Meanwhile, Western Australia is preparing for Tropical Cyclone Luana, which is moving inland and could bring heavy rain and winds.
