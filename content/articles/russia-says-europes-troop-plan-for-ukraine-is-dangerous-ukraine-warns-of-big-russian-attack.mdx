Russia says a plan to send European troops to Ukraine is dangerous and will make those units targets. Kyiv says a big Russian attack could come soon, and the US embassy warns of possible air raids. German chancellor Friedrich Merz says a ceasefire is far away without Russia’s agreement. Meanwhile, Russia has struck Kyiv, Lviv and other cities with drones and missiles, cutting power and water. European governments are also under fire for selling Russian gas.
