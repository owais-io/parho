Playoffs in the MLS and NWSL have turned a season of ordinary games into a handful of thrilling, high‑stakes matches. Lionel Messi has been the star of Miami's run, while Gotham FC shocked the league by beating top teams to win the women's title. The article argues that playoffs add excitement and drama, even if they’re less fair, and that this is similar to the World Cup or Champions League. In short, the playoffs keep fans glued to the game.
