Alex Sarama, a 30‑year‑old Brit who grew a youth club in the UK, was laughed at for wanting to coach basketball. He moved to the US, worked in the NBA’s international office and with the Cavaliers, and now leads the Portland Fire, a new WNBA team. He plans to build the squad from scratch, using shared coaching methods and detailed playbooks. He’s hopeful the sport can grow in the UK and is excited to face rivals like Seattle.
