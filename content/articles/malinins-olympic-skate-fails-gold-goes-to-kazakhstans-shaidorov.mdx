Ilia Malinin, the 21‑year‑old “Quad God,” had a two‑year unbeaten streak and was the favourite to win Olympic gold. But in Milan’s free skate he fell, popped a quad axel, and finished eighth, letting Kazakhstan’s Mikhail Shaidorov win instead. The pressure of the Games made Malinin crash, showing how one bad moment can erase a season’s hard work.
