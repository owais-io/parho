Police are on the hunt for Julian Ingram, 37, who is believed to have killed his pregnant former partner, her boyfriend, and her aunt in Lake Cargelligo, NSW. A possible sighting was reported near Mount Hope, about 70km north of the crime scene. Law enforcement, with air support, is combing the wide area and checking local properties, while warning the public to avoid spreading unverified online rumors.
