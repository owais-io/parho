After Jeffrey Epstein was released from prison in 2009, he sent several thousand pounds to Peter Mandelson’s husband, Reinaldo Avila da Silva, according to emails released by the U.S. Department of Justice. The money was used to pay for an osteopathy course, and the letters show Epstein and Mandelson kept in touch for years. Mandelson later apologized for his ties to Epstein, who had been convicted of sex‑related crimes.
