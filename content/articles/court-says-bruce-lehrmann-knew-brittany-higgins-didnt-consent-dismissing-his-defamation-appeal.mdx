Australia’s federal court rejected Bruce Lehrmann’s appeal over a defamation case that found he raped Brittany Higgins. The court said Lehrmann knew Higgins didn’t consent and was not drunk when he took her to Parliament House. It agreed the original judge was right and ordered Lehrmann to pay costs. Lehrmann, who claims innocence, will likely appeal to the High Court. The ruling is a major win for Higgins and for defamation law.
