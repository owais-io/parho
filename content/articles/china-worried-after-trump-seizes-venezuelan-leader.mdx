Venezuela’s president, Nicolás Maduro, was seized by US forces after a meeting with a Chinese delegation. China says the move is illegal and worries about its $10 bn debt and oil ties to Venezuela. Beijing has long invested in the country, especially in oil, and wants a UN meeting to discuss the capture. China could use its rare‑earth power to pressure the US and may sue Venezuela if new leaders break agreements.
