Amazon said it will invest $200bn in AI, robotics, chips, and satellites this year, a jump from last year’s $125bn. The move follows Amazon’s Q4 earnings, which were a touch below Wall Street expectations, even as revenue hit $213bn and AWS grew 24%. Shares fell 9% after the news, and the company’s founder, Jeff Bezos, has also cut staff at his Washington Post newspaper. Amazon plans to use the money mainly for research and development, aiming for long‑term gains.
