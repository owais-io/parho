Channel 4’s new documentary about former UK prime minister Tony Blair gives viewers a rare look at how he sees himself. In the film Blair admits he feels like a “messianic” figure, joking that he thought he could walk on water. The film mixes interviews with former colleagues, family and critics, and asks if Blair’s self‑confidence was a strength or a mistake. Viewers get a nostalgic look at Blair’s political era.
