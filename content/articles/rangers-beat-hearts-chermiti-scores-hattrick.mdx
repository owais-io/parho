Rangers defeated Hearts 4‑0 at Ibrox, with Youssef Chermiti netting a hat‑trick. The win gives Rangers a big boost in the Scottish title race, putting them close to Celtic’s lead. Hearts’ manager, Derek McInnes, was disappointed but said his team remains competitive. Rangers’ coach Danny Rohl praised the team’s resilience, while the victory shows the club is serious about chasing the championship.
