At the Cheltenham festival, two top British‑trained horses, Haiti Couleurs and Lulamba, looked strong for the Gold Cup and Arkle Trophy. Haiti Couleurs, winner of the Denman Chase, finished solid in the King George VI Chase and is a favourite for the Gold Cup. Lulamba kept his unbeaten record and is the odds‑on favourite for the Arkle Trophy. Tutti Quanti dominated the William Hill Hurdle, raising hopes of a Champion Hurdle run.
