Ukraine and Russia held their first day of US‑led peace talks in Abu Dhabi, and both sides called it productive. Ukrainian President Zelenskyy said the talks must lead to a real end to the war, while Russia wants Ukraine to give up the entire Donbas region. The talks focus on steps like a prisoner swap and stopping attacks. Washington hopes to use the talks to stop fighting, but the parties still have hard demands.
