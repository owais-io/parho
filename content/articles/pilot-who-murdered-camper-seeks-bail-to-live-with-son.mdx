Former Jetstar pilot Gregory Lynn, 59, was found guilty in 2024 of killing 73‑year‑old Carol Clay at a campsite in 2020. A Victorian court said his conviction was wrong and ordered a new trial, which could start in July. Lynn’s lawyers now ask the court to grant him bail so he can stay with his son, offering more than $400,000 as surety. The prosecution says he still poses a risk.
