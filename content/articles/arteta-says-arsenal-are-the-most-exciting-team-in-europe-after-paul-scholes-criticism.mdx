Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta has brushed off former Manchester United midfielder Paul Scholes’ warning that Arsenal could be the most boring side to win the Premier League. Arteta insists that Arsenal are the most exciting team in Europe, pointing to their set‑piece goals and strong season start. He also highlighted the club’s progress in the Champions League and their current lead in the Premier League. The comments come before Arsenal’s Carabao Cup semi‑final against Chelsea.
