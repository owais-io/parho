Nigel Farage, who had urged the UK to help the U.S. and Israel push for regime change in Iran, now says Britain should stay out of the conflict, citing defence limits. Critics call this a U‑turn. A YouGov poll shows voters split on the issue. Party colleagues hold mixed views: some back U.S. support, others warn against war and its cost to ordinary Britons.
