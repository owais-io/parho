Israeli bulldozers have cleared a corner of a Gaza cemetery that holds war graves of British, Australian and other Allied soldiers. Satellite pictures show rows of headstones removed and a large earth berm built. The Israeli army says the work was defensive, claiming underground threats were found. The Commonwealth War Graves Commission says the damage is severe. The loss has angered veterans’ groups and locals who mourn the desecration.
