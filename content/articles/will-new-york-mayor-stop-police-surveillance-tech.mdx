Zohran Mamdani, the new mayor of New York City, once campaigned against police surveillance tools. The NYPD now uses a software firm called Ntrepid to create fake online identities for investigations. Mamdani can halt this, but he has shown more support for police since running. Activists fear the tech could target minority communities and urge the mayor to push for transparency and limit undercover accounts.
