A Los Angeles jury said Meta and YouTube caused a 20‑year‑old woman’s body‑image problems and thoughts of self‑harm after she used their apps from age six. The court said the companies knew their sites were addictive and ignored that knowledge. The decision could spark more lawsuits and shows that tech giants may be held accountable for the way social media affects young people.
