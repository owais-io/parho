Survey of UK students shows about a third think Reform UK politicians should be banned from campus talks, even though a majority say universities should never limit free speech. 41% of students who voted for Reform also want the ban, while 16% want Labour speakers barred. Only 18% support all parties speaking. The study highlights students’ mixed views on free speech and political bans, and adds new rules for the Office for Students.
