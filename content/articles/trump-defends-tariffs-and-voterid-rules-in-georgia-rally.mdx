At a steel‑processing plant in Rome, Georgia, former President Trump defended his tariff policy, saying it protects American jobs. He also raised his long‑standing claims of voter fraud, urging stricter ID rules and limiting mail‑in ballots. Trump criticized Democrats for blocking the Save America Act and linked their actions to cheating. He briefly touched on drug‑price promises, praised a local attorney general, and urged support for Republican candidates in upcoming elections.
