The chief of the Sydney Lyric and Capitol theatres said the Shen Yun dance shows will not be cancelled after bomb‑style threats hit a Gold Coast venue and the Prime Minister’s house. The threats, which the Falun Dafa group says come from China, have actually boosted ticket sales. The theatre plans to keep the shows, and security has been increased, while the Chinese embassy denies any involvement.
