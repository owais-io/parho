Bill Burnett and Dave Evans, former Apple engineers, say we’re all stuck in a meaning crisis. Their new book, *How to Live a Meaningful Life*, argues that big goals aren’t needed. Instead, focus on four tiny habits: feel wonder, get into flow, keep life coherent with your values, and join a supportive community. By practising these day‑to‑day, you can make every day feel richer without overhauling your life.
