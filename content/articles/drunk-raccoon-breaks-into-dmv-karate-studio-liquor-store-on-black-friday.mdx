A raccoon in Virginia broke into three local businesses— a DMV, a karate studio, and a liquor store— on Black Friday. The animal smashed bottles, ate snacks, and passed out drunk in a bathroom. After rescue, it was released near the sites. Officials say the raccoon is clever but hope it learns better habits, highlighting how city raccoons adapt to urban life.
