Maha, a health‑focused group, is asking Trump to remove EPA chief Lee Zeldin because he’s undoing rules on toxic chemicals and speeding up pesticide approvals. The group says Zeldin’s actions favor chemical companies over public health and could raise disease and medical costs. They want the president to hear their concerns and appoint someone who will protect water, food, and people’s health.
