Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy warned that rising US‑Iran tensions might push back a planned meeting between Ukrainian and Russian leaders in Abu Dhabi. He said the date or location could change. Meanwhile, US officials won’t attend, and a Russian envoy will fly to Miami instead. With a severe winter cutting energy supplies, both sides have slowed attacks, and Ukraine hopes for a pause in strikes.
