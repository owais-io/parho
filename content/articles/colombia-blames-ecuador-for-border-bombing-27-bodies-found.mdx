President Gustavo Petro says Ecuador bombed a Colombian border area, finding 27 charred bodies. Ecuador denies the claim, calling the attack a mistake. The dispute follows trade tariffs and drug‑war tensions, with both sides blaming each other for drug gangs. Ecuador added a security tax on Colombian goods and Colombia retaliated with its own tariffs, worsening the feud.
