Arsenal covered a goal‑keeping gap by loaning Czech international Barbora Votikova from Slavia Prague, while Everton finished the window by taking Slovenian midfielder Zara Kramzar on loan from Roma. Votikova has 56 caps and Champions League experience, and Kramzar’s deal will turn into a permanent signing next summer. The deadline day was calmer than last year, with a few other WSL clubs adding a handful of players.
