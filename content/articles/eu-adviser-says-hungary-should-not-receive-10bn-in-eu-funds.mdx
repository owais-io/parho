EU court adviser Tamara Ćapeta told the court that Hungary was wrongly given €10 bn of EU money after the Commission lifted a payment freeze. She said Hungary hadn’t made the judicial reforms it promised, so the Commission should not have released the funds. Parliament will decide soon whether to force the Commission to return the money.
