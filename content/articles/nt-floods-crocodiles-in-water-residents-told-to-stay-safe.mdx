Northern Territory towns are hit by record‑breaking floods. Rivers are rising past 15 metres, flooding streets and cutting off roads. A saltwater crocodile was even seen on a football field, adding danger. Officials warn residents to stay away from floodwater, boil tap water, and evacuate if needed. Schools closed, transport routes blocked, and many people are stranded as the storm continues.
