New polls show the Greens leading in the Gorton and Denton by‑election, with 22% of voters, while Reform UK has 20% and Labour 18%. Labour’s fall in support and its focus on stopping Reform are criticized. Monbiot says Labour should let the Greens, a more credible left‑wing party, take the fight against the right. He also highlights problems with Reform’s uncharismatic candidate, Matt Goodwin, who may hurt the party’s chances.
