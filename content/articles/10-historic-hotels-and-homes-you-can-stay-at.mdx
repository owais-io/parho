From Florence’s 15th‑century Casa Guidi, where the Browning couple once lived, to a 1970s Swedish ski‑resort with disco vibes, the list shows ten places that let you stay in history. In Kraków a retro hotel recreates the interwar cafés, while Sicily’s Palazzo Previtera offers 500 years of art and books. Prague’s Vintage Design Sax, UK Georgian cottages, a Tudor castle, a 90s Dorset home, a Victorian Dover suite, and a WWII air‑field B&B give guests a trip back in time.
