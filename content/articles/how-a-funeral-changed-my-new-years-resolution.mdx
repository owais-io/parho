On New Year's Eve, the writer reflects on tired, common resolutions. Her uncle—an Indian academic who kept his culture alive in Australia—died, and his funeral showed how many people were touched by his life. The experience made her realize that being seen and cared for matters more than the usual goals. Her new resolution is to make people, especially patients, feel seen and valued in everyday actions.
