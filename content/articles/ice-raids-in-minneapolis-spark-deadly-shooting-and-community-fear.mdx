In early 2026, ICE sent a huge team of masked, armed officers to Minneapolis. They raided stores, gyms, schools and tackled people, including U.S. citizens. On a Wednesday, a 37‑year‑old woman named Renee Good was shot in broad daylight. ICE said she tried to run over officers, but videos show she was not threatening anyone. The raid sparked fear, added to a year of 32 ICE‑related deaths, and caused a school to be attacked too.
