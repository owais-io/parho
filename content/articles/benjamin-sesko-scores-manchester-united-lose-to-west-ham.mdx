Manchester United went down 1‑0 to West Ham in a tense London Stadium match. West Ham took the lead with a quick pass between Bowen and Soucek early in the second half. Benjamin Sesko scored a beautiful sideline finish for United, but the late goal wasn’t enough. The game showed United’s resilience under manager Carrick, though the loss left them still chasing safety.
