Australian motorists are paying up to 25 cents more per litre of petrol after the US‑Israel war on Iran pushed global oil prices higher. Motoring groups NRMA and RACQ claim retailers are gouging customers. The ACCC has been asked to investigate, but says prices are set by the market. The rise could add a touch to inflation, prompt another rate hike, and strain household budgets.
