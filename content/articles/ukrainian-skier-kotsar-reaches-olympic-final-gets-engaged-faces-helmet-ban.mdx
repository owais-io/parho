Ukrainian free‑skier Kateryna Kotsar made the big‑air final at the Winter Games, then wrote “freedom of memory” on her glove to protest a ban on a fellow athlete. While celebrating, her boyfriend proposed, and she accepted, sparking a memorable Valentine’s Day. She also had to change her custom helmet after the IOC called it propaganda, leaving her with a simple flag instead. Kotsar remains focused on her rivals and on Ukraine’s hardships.
