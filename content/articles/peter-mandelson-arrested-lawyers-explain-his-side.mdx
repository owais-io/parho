Peter Mandelson was arrested after police said he might be leaving the UK. His lawyers say there's no truth to that claim and that he has agreed to talk with police next month. They want the evidence used to arrest him and say he is ready to cooperate and clear his name. The statement is a defensive move to control the story and fight the allegations.
