Trump’s decision to strike Iran has put the world’s oil supplies at risk, and the UK could soon face fuel shortages and higher prices. If the Strait of Hormuz stays blocked, British households might have to ration petrol and see energy bills rise by up to £500. Politicians are debating whether to give cash help or push the country faster toward electric cars and net‑zero to keep the economy stable.
