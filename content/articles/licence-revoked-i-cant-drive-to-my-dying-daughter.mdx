After a fall that left him with a minor head injury, a man was told by the DVLA he could stop driving for two weeks. When he resumed, his licence was taken back again and never returned. Because the licence was missing, he couldn't drive to his cancer‑suffering daughter, who later died. He asked the DVLA why the licence was revoked and why it was delayed, but received no clear answers.
