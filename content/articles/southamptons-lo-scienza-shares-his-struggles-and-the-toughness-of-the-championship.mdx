Léo Scienza, the young midfielder for Southampton, opened up about the loss of his father on his 20th birthday, a period that led him into depression and self‑destruction. After being rejected by top Brazilian clubs, he went to Sweden and played in the fifth‑tier, where money and living conditions were hard. The experience taught him resilience and the importance of therapy. Now he scores for Southampton in the Championship, describing it as a “bloodbath” but proud of his journey.
