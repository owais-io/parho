After a federal immigration agent fatally shot 37‑year‑old Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, Trump said the administration was reviewing the incident. Minneapolis mayor Jacob Frey announced a cut‑back in ICE agents in the city, and Trump plans to send a top border official to discuss next steps. The move follows national outrage, criticism from lawmakers and the public, and a pending court hearing on whether the federal agent surge should end.
