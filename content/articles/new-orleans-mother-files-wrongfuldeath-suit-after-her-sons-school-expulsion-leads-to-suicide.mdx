After a 17‑year‑old student was expelled from a Catholic school in Metairie, Louisiana, his mother sues the archdiocese and the school for wrongful death. The lawsuit says the school’s handling of bullying and the abrupt expulsion pushed the boy into suicide. It also claims the school didn’t follow its own rules or offer online schooling after the expulsion. The case highlights bullying‑suicide links.
