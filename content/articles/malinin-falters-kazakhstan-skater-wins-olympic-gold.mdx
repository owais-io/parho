The 2026 Winter Games ended with a shock: U.S. star Ilia Malinin, the top favorite, made two major mistakes in the final race and finished eighth. Kazakhstan’s Mikhail Shaidorov won gold with a clean program, becoming his country’s first Olympic figure‑skating champion. Japan’s Yuma Kagiyama took silver and Shun Sato earned bronze. Malinin’s fall shows even the best can stumble under pressure.
