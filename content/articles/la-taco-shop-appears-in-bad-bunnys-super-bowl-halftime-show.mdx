A popular Los Angeles taqueria, Villa's Tacos, popped up during Bad Bunny's Super Bowl half‑time show, catching fans by surprise. The star stopped at a handful of food stalls before heading to the taco stand, a move that thrilled locals. Owner Victor Villas, a first‑generation Mexican‑American, thanked the rapper and celebrated immigrant heritage. Mayor Karen Bass also praised the moment, calling it a proud celebration of diversity. The cameo highlighted the city’s multicultural spirit.
