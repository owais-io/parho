NSW Labor backbenchers plan to join a protest against Israeli President Isaac Herzog’s visit to Sydney, saying Israel’s actions in Gaza amount to genocide. They will march from Town Hall to Parliament, even though police have extended a ban on marches after the Bondi terror attack. Some MPs worry the ban could lead to arrests, while others support the right to protest. Jewish community leaders say the visit will help heal wounds from the tragedy.
