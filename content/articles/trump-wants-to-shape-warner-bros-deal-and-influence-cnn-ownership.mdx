Trump says he may be involved in reviewing Warner Bros’ sale. The studio could be bought by Netflix or by Paramount. He wants the buyer to also take over CNN and make changes there. Senators argue the president shouldn’t decide the deal. The Justice Department and state lawyers will review it for antitrust rules. The sale could tighten media ownership and change the entertainment industry.
