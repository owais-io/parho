The US has cut the Trump‑era tariffs that could have almost doubled the cost of Italian pasta in America. Instead of up to 92%, the new rates will be between 2% and 14% for 13 major brands. Italy’s pasta industry still faces a 15% EU tariff, and the final duties will be announced on March 12. The move follows talks between the US and Italian companies.
