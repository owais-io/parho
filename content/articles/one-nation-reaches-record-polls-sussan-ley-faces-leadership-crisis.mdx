One Nation has surged to a record 22 % in the latest Guardian Essential poll, stirring unrest inside the ruling coalition. With Prime Minister and opposition leader approval falling, many think the Liberal Party will face a leadership change. Sussan Ley, the Nationals’ deputy leader, is under pressure and may step down. Former PM Malcolm Turnbull says the coalition’s split shows how badly the parties are broken.
