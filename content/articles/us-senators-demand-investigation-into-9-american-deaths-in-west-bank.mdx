More than 30 U.S. senators have sent a letter to the Trump administration asking it to launch a full investigation into the February killing of a 19‑year‑old American in the West Bank, the ninth U.S. citizen killed there since 2022. They want a clear account of all nine cases and a briefing for Congress by April 5. No convictions have come from any incident, and senators say the U.S. has failed to protect its citizens abroad.
