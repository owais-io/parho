After leaving Chelsea, England winger Raheem Sterling has signed a short‑term deal with Dutch club Feyenoord. He became a free agent when his contract was ended in January. Sterling, who hasn't played competitively since his loan at Arsenal, said he chose Feyenoord because he can have a clear role and help the team. Feyenoord manager Robin van Persie welcomed him, hoping he will boost the squad.
