Police have named Sarah Forrester, 55, a mental‑health counsellor who died in a Swindon house last Friday. A 13‑year‑old girl was arrested on suspicion of murder but has been released on bail. Police say no other suspects are involved and the investigation is continuing. The Kelly Foundation, where Forrester worked, says she was devoted to her job and children. Family and community are grieving.
