Alex de Minaur won a quick 6‑4, 6‑1, 6‑1 victory over Alexander Bublik at the Australian Open, earning a spot in the quarter‑finals and a future match against top seed Carlos Al‑Caraz. The win came after a hot, packed arena and a heated match. De Minaur said he was focused on revenge and proving he can beat higher‑ranked players. He now faces the toughest test yet.
