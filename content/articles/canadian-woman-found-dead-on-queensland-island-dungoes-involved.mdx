On a popular Queensland beach, a 19‑year‑old Canadian hostel worker was discovered dead surrounded by a pack of dingoes. Police said the animals had touched her body, but they are waiting for a post‑mortem to determine whether she drowned or was attacked. Investigators are piecing together her last hour after she left the hostel for a swim. The incident has shocked the island community, and authorities are asking anyone who saw anything to come forward.
