Brian Hayes was a long‑time broadcaster on LBC, working with producer Lawrie Douglas from 1976. He was known for tackling tough topics like HIV/AIDS and the controversial Section 28 law that restricted discussions about LGBT people. During his shows, he welcomed callers to talk about these issues and even brought books about same‑sex families to spark conversation. Hayes was a steady voice against bigotry and misinformation.
