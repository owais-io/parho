Scientists argue that studies claiming microplastics in human tissue are not yet reliable, because the tests used are still being refined. They say good research has found plastic particles in people’s organs, and these particles can carry toxic chemicals that harm health. Experts call for stronger, transparent methods and better peer review. Meanwhile, the plastic crisis keeps growing, and scientists urge bold action.
