Since the U.S. and Israel launched attacks, Tehran has been bombed every day. More than 1,200 people, including children killed in a Hormozgan school, have died. Whole apartment blocks have collapsed, and families are forced to abandon their homes. Rescue crews fear second blasts, so they hesitate to reach the wounded. While markets stay open, traffic is quiet and people worry about walking or driving in the city.
