Harry Kane is only one goal away from tying Pelé’s record of 77 international goals. He’s scored 23 goals for Bayern Munich this season and 76 for England. Kane says he wants to win major trophies like the Champions League and World Cup before hoping for a Ballon d’Or. He also stresses teamwork and a strong culture for England. The England captain talks about building a winning culture under coach Tuchel and using set‑piece plays to boost the team.
