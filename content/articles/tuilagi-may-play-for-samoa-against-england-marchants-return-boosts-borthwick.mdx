Manu Tuilagi says he could play for Samoa at the 2027 World Cup, meaning he could face England in Australia. He will qualify under new eligibility rules, after ending his England career last year. England coach Steve Borthwick is happy that Joe Marchant is coming back to Sale, giving the side fresh talent for the 2025‑26 season. England’s next test is against Wales on Feb. 7.
