Iran’s biggest protests in three years have become deadly. Two people died after police fired on crowds in the city of Lordegan. Videos show gunfire and bodies with bullet wounds. Security forces are also arresting dozens of protesters, while activists say the crackdown is growing. The unrest started over the currency’s collapse and rising cost of living, and has spread to many cities across the country.
