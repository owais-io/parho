Liverpool let go of set‑piece coach Aaron Briggs because the team has been leaking goals from corners, free kicks and other set‑pieces. In the first half of the season the Reds have allowed 12 such goals – the most in the league – and scored only three. Manager Arne Slot was openly frustrated and said the issues were hurting the club. Briggs, who helped the team win the title last year, will be replaced by the current coaching staff.
