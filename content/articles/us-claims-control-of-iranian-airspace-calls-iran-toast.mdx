The United States says it now controls Iranian airspace after launching a joint U.S.-Israeli offensive. Pentagon chief Pete Hegseth bragged that Iran’s missiles, navy and leaders have been largely destroyed and that the U.S. will keep striking deeper into the country. He also said a U.S. submarine sank an Iranian warship, and the U.S. is ready to keep up the pressure.
