Australia beat England 4‑1 in the Ashes, thanks to Travis Head’s explosive batting and Mitchell Starc’s 31 wickets. Head’s 629 runs and fast centuries gave Australia a clear advantage. Usman Khawaja retired after the series, opening a spot in the middle order. Debate over Cameron Green’s role was sparked by Beau Webster’s solid finish. Ahead, Australia will face Bangladesh, South Africa and a T20 World Cup.
