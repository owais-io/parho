UN Secretary‑General António Guterres says the world can’t keep using GDP to judge success. GDP rewards pollution and waste, he argues, and hides inequality and climate damage. He wants new measures that count wellbeing, fairness and the planet’s health. Economists at a UN meeting are working on a dashboard that will let governments see if growth is truly helping people and the environment.
