After Manchester United beat Crystal Palace 2‑1, young defender Leny Yoro was taken off and looked upset on the bench. Coach Ruben Amorim said Yoro reacted badly because he overthinks his mistakes. He told the player to learn from the loss and that growing pains are normal for a 20‑year‑old. Amorim also praised teammate Mason Mount’s support, saying it shows a strong team culture.
