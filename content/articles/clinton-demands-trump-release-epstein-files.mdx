Hillary Clinton says the Trump administration is hiding Epstein documents and wants the Justice Department to speed up the release of about 3 million files. She and her husband Bill will testify before Congress next week, the first former president to do so in decades. Clinton wants the hearings to be public and says the files should be fully available. Trump dismisses the accusations, claiming he has been cleared.
