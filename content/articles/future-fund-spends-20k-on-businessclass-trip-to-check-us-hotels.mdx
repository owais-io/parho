Future Fund spent $20,000 to send its former executive assistant on a business‑class flight to the US to inspect hotels and try to get cheaper rates. The trip was meant to save the fund money; the chief said it cut annual hotel costs by $30,000. A senator asked why the work wasn’t done by phone. The fund also sent a chief to Disney’s exclusive club to study training ideas.
