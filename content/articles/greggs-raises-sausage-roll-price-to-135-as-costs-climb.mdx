Greggs has increased the price of a sausage roll by 5p to £1.35 and a latte by 10p to £2.25 because of higher wages, energy and packaging costs. The chain said it won’t raise prices further for now and expects inflation to ease. Sales at its own stores grew only 2.9% this quarter, and the company’s shares fell 9% after announcing a small profit drop and fewer new stores than planned.
