HSBC's new chief executive, Georges Elhedery, says the big shake‑up of the bank is almost done, even though profits fell a bit last year. The bank still beat expectations, earning $29.9 bn pre‑tax, but had $4.9 bn of one‑off charges. Elhedery raised the target return on equity to 17% by 2028 and said the bank will keep cutting costs and streamlining operations. Shares rose 2.5 % on the results.
