Arc Raiders is a fast‑paced multiplayer shooter where you fight robots and other players to collect loot and escape a ruined Italy. You start underground, venture out for supplies, fight deadly machines, and must reach an extraction point before time runs out. The game rewards gathering gear, upgrading your raider, and working with teammates. It’s easy to learn, but tough to win, and the use of AI voice acting adds a quirky touch. It costs £30.99.
