I’m moving house with friends, but none of us know how to do even simple repairs. We’re all hoarders and keep useless stuff—old kitchen gadgets, mismatched ribbon, Christmas cards we don’t remember. Trying to sort through years of junk is exhausting. The article shows how we cling to items, even when they’re broken or useless, and how moving finally forces us to confront this habit. It’s a humorous look at the mess we keep.
