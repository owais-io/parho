Nigel Farage, leader of Reform UK, paid to make short videos on Cameo where he praised the release of rapper Sean “Diddy” Combs and former Honduran president Juan Orlando Hernández, who is serving a 45‑year drug‑trafficking sentence in the U.S. The Guardian has exposed Farage’s past videos that promoted extremist slogans and a neo‑Nazi event. His spokesman says the clips are not political statements, but the controversy has already sparked criticism.
