Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said there is no link between Australia’s 2023 recognition of a Palestinian state and the deadly antisemitic shooting on Bondi Beach that killed 15 people. He urged unity and pledged tougher hate‑crime laws, a ban on Nazi salutes, extra security for Jewish sites and stronger gun rules. The attack has heightened tensions with Israel, where PM Netanyahu blamed Australia for rising antisemitism.
