Prince Harry says the Daily Mail group used illegal tactics to steal his private information. His lawyers point to 14 stories, some revealing flight details and personal secrets, that were supposedly gathered by a paid private investigator. Harry claims these attacks caused distress and paranoia. The court case pits him against the newspaper, with both sides arguing whether the stories were obtained lawfully. The trial is still ongoing.
