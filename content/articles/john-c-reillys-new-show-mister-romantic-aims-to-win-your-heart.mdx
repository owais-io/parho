Actor John C. Reilly is back on stage, this time as Mister Romantic, a one‑man show that blends jazz standards, pop songs, and comic moments. In a London run at Soho Theatre Walthamstow, he performs with a four‑piece band, speaking directly to the crowd and inviting them in. The act mixes humor, heartfelt singing, and audience participation to spread love and empathy. It’s a fresh, intimate musical experience that feels truly warm.
