Terence Crawford announced he is retiring while still unbeaten and at the top of boxing. He has won titles in five weight classes and is the only current champion in three of them. Crawford stepped away on his own terms, avoiding the usual fights for extra money or drama. He left the ring with no losses and no signs of wear. If he never fights again, his place as a great will stay secure.
