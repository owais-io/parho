Frederick Green, 20, has been charged with attempted murder for shooting Jets cornerback Kris Boyd in Manhattan after a night out. Boyd was wounded near his abdomen just after 2 a.m. and later recovered enough to visit the Jets’ facility. Green also faces assault and weapons charges. The attack happened when Boyd and teammates left a club, were taunted by a group, and a fight broke out.
