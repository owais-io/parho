The Gorton and Denton by‑election saw Labour finish third, a shocking result that shows deeper splits in UK politics. Sadiq Khan warns the party can’t take progressive voters for granted and must rethink its strategy. He stresses the need to unite with left‑wing allies, stay true to core values, and focus on real achievements like NHS, childcare, and affordable housing. Failure risks losing key seats and credibility.
