Fuzzy Zoeller, a legendary American golfer who won the 1979 Masters and the 1984 U.S. Open, died at 74. He was famous for his quick swings, humor, and memorable playoff wins, and he captured eight other PGA titles. Zoeller played in three Ryder Cups and later joined the senior tour, winning two events. He also designed golf courses. A controversial joke about Tiger Woods damaged his reputation. He is survived by his wife and four children.
