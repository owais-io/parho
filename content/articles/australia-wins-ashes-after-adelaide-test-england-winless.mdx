Australia clinched the Ashes after a tense third Test in Adelaide, thanks to Jason Starc's 4‑wicket haul that kept England in check. England struggled with dropped catches, erratic bowling and a lack of big scores, falling short by 83 runs in a high‑scoring chase. Australia, despite a few injuries, showed depth and solid fielding. With the series 3‑0, England remains winless in 18 Tests played in Australia.
