Jenni Murray, who hosted BBC Radio 4’s popular Woman’s Hour for 33 years, died at 75. A long‑time broadcaster, she interviewed famous women like Margaret Thatcher and Hillary Clinton, won awards, and was made a Dame. After her 2006 breast‑cancer battle, she spoke on social issues but faced backlash over comments on transgender people. Her warm, fearless voice left a lasting mark on British radio.
