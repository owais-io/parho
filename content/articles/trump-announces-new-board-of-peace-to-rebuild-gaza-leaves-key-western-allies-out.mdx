Donald Trump's new Board of Peace will meet in Washington DC to discuss rebuilding Gaza. The group, set up after the World Economic Forum, is meant to be a rival to the UN and includes about 24 countries—mostly Arab states and some Asian nations. Western powers like the UK, Canada, France and Germany are not on board. Israel and its prime minister are members, while Palestine is not. Members pledged $5bn for reconstruction, to be announced at the first meeting.
