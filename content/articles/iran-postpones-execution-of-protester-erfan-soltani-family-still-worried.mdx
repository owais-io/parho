The Iranian authorities have delayed the execution of 26‑year‑old protester Erfan Soltani, who was sentenced to death just four days after his arrest for joining demonstrations. His family, who had received almost no updates, were told at the last minute that the execution would be postponed, but no further details were given. Human rights groups warn that Soltani’s case shows how quickly the government is trying protesters, with many more people arrested and killed in the ongoing crackdown.
