Euro Car Parks was hit with a £473,000 fine by the Competition and Markets Authority after it ignored seven information requests. The CMA said the company blocked the regulator’s emails and only replied after a warning. This is the first time the CMA used its new 2024 powers to fine a firm for non‑compliance. The company also lost a court bid to stop the penalty being named.
