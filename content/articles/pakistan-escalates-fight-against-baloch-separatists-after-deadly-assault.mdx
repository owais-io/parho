On Saturday, Baloch separatists launched a huge assault across Balochistan, killing at least 31 civilians and 17 security staff. They hit military bases, police stations, banks, roads, and even the port of Gwadar. Pakistan’s security forces say they killed 145 militants in 40 hours. The Balochistan Liberation Army claims responsibility, blaming India for support. The attacks shocked a region that had been fighting for independence for decades.
