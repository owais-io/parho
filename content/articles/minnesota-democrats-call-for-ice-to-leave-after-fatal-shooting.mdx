After a federal immigration officer shot and killed a 37‑year‑old woman in Minneapolis, Minnesota’s Democrats demanded that ICE leave the state. They say the incident was caused by reckless shooting, not self‑defense, and call for a full investigation. The governor, mayor and state officials warned residents to stay calm. Republicans defended the ICE agent, saying he was acting in self‑defense. Trump also said the officer was justified.
