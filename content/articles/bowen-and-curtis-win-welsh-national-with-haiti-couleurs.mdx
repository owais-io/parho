Haiti Couleurs, trained by Rebecca Curtis and ridden by Sean Bowen, won the Welsh Grand National, giving Wales a strong chance for next year’s Cheltenham Gold Cup. The horse also sparked hopes of a Grand National treble, with odds now 14‑1. Bowen ran the race confidently, taking the lead early and never looking back. Curtis celebrated her first Welsh National win after a disappointing Betfair Chase. Both riders and the trainer felt it was a special, home‑grown triumph.
