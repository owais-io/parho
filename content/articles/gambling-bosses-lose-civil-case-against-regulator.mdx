Two former gambling bosses, Kenny Alexander and Lee Feldman, who are facing criminal charges, lost a civil lawsuit against the UK Gambling Commission. They claimed the regulator had invaded their privacy while reviewing 888’s licence because of their past roles at Entain. The court ruled the Commission didn’t breach their privacy, ordered the men to pay costs, and left them free to appeal.
