Looking for holiday fun? Here are eight easy‑to‑play party games that are perfect for family Christmas gatherings. Try Cake Bash for silly cake‑fighting, Heave Ho for stretchy teamwork, or the new Jackbox Party Pack 11 for trivia and doodle contests. If you love singing, Let’s Sing 2026 offers a karaoke jukebox, while Just Dance 2026 gets everyone moving. For teamwork, pick Lovers in a Dangerous Space‑time or Mario Kart World. Finish with Moving Out 2 and Spaceteam for laughs.
