Three men were taken into custody after they allegedly attacked Imam Ismet Purdic and his wife on a Melbourne freeway. Police say the trio, from a black hatchback, shouted racial slurs, threw objects, and punched the imam. The couple were forced into a service station before being assaulted. Victims say they suffered trauma; the attack was condemned by the premier and Muslim councils. The suspects face assault and damage charges.
