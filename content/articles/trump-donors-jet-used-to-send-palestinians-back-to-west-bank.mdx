ICE used a private jet owned by Trump donor Gil Dezer to fly Palestinian detainees from Arizona to Israel in late January and early February. The jet, which dropped the deportees in Tel Aviv before sending them to the West Bank, had been chartered for four earlier removal flights to African nations. Dezer says he only knew the dates, not the passengers. The flights are part of the Trump‑era hard‑line deportation push.
