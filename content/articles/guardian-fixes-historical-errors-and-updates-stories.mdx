Guardian’s fact‑checking team fixed a mix‑up about James Scott, Duke of Monmouth, who was actually a cousin, not a half‑brother of Mary II. It also corrected the timeline for King George VI’s knowledge of the Marburg Nazi files. The paper added updates on several other stories, from a Cuban power outage to a new epilepsy drug and Green Party membership figures. Readers can send complaints or suggestions to the Guardian’s editorial office.
