Usman Khawaja, the first Muslim to play for Australia, retired from Test cricket and used his final press conference to talk about racism he has faced, both on and off the field. He said stereotypes still linger and he was often criticised for things like playing golf. Khawaja also spoke about the Bondi attack and the need for unity, and how his faith has helped him. He hopes his story will inspire future players.
