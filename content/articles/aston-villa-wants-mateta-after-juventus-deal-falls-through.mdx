Villa are eyeing French striker Jean‑Philippe Mateta after a €30m offer from Juventus fell short. Palace was looking at their winger Evann Guessand as a replacement, but the deal didn’t go through. With Malen gone, Villa need firepower and are deciding whether to bid for Mateta. The move also ties into other Premier League transfer chatter, including talks about Tammy Abraham, Ethan Nwaneri and more.
