England is getting ready for the Ashes, mixing fun trips with serious training. Ben Stokes, England’s star all‑rounder, is the focus of the prep. After difficult Ashes tours in 2018 and 2022, Stokes has a strong summer with 20 wickets and better mental health. If he stays fit, he could turn the series into a career‑defining moment. His performance will be key to England’s chances in the test series.
