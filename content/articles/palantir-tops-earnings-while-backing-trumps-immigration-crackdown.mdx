Palantir announced record revenue of $1.41 bn, beating Wall Street expectations and showing a 66 % jump in government sales. Most of the money comes from contracts with the Department of Defense and Homeland Security, helping the Trump administration enforce immigration rules. The company faces backlash from privacy advocates who say its tools give ICE an unfair advantage. Still, Palantir says it protects data and follows the law.
