Slovenia will vote on March 22. The current left‑leaning coalition, led by Freedom Movement founder Robert Golob, did surprisingly well in 2022 but is now losing popularity. The centre‑right Slovenian Democratic Party (SDS) led by former PM Janez Janša is ahead in polls. SDS has a history of attacking the media and courts. The article warns that a new SDS government could push Slovenia toward illiberal, more authoritarian rules.
