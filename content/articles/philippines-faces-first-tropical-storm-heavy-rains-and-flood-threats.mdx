The Philippines is hit by its first tropical storm of the year, Ada (Nokaen), bringing heavy rain, strong winds, and risks of flooding, landslides, and volcanic mudflows. The storm will stay active until Tuesday, with warnings for surges and high waves. In the Mediterranean, Storm Harry brings heavy rain and strong winds to Spain, Italy, and Malta, sparking orange and red warnings. Meanwhile, parts of Eastern Europe are experiencing an unusually cold spell.
