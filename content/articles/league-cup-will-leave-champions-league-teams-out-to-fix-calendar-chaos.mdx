The League Cup is changing. Clubs that play in the Champions League will no longer join the cup next season, so only the top teams that stay in England compete. A new Swiss‑style group system will let the best eight move to knock‑outs. The move tries to ease players’ heavy schedules and fix a packed calendar, but it could hurt TV interest and leave some clubs without big chances.
