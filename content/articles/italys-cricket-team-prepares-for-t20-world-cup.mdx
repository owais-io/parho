Riccardo Maggio, Italy’s cricket boss, has packed blue jerseys for his squad as they ready themselves for the T20 World Cup in Sri Lanka and India. The team, made up of locals, immigrants, and players with Italian roots, has finally qualified – a first for the country. Despite cricket’s small profile in Italy, these players hope the tournament will spark interest and show Italians that the sport is for everyone.
