Caroline Ellison, 31, who once ran FTX’s trading arm and dated its founder Sam Bankman‑Fried, was freed from federal custody after serving almost 14 months of a 24‑month sentence. She pleaded guilty to wire fraud, money laundering and other charges that helped bring down the $8 billion FTX fraud. Ellison had cooperated with prosecutors, giving testimony against Bankman‑Fried. While she leaves prison, her case ends a major U.S. crypto scandal.
