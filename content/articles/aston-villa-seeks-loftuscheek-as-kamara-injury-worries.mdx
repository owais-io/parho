Aston Villa looks to add midfield depth with Ruben Loftus‑Cheek as they worry about Boubacar Kamara’s knee injury. The club plans to sign the former Chelsea player on loan or a short‑term deal. With injuries to captain John McGinn and striker Amadou Onana, Villa also seeks a new striker after selling Donyell Malen. Targets include Tammy Abraham, Jean‑Philippe Mateta and even England midfielder Conor Gallagher. This step comes as Villa chase a spot in the Europa League last 16.
