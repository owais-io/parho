Day 15 of the Winter Games was full of gold medals. Britain won ski cross and half‑pipe titles, Norway took the 50km mass‑start ski race, and the mixed ski‑mountaineering relay saw a new champion. The biathlon gave a Norwegian‑French showdown, while curling gold was contested by Scotland and Canada. Speed skating and ice hockey finals also delivered exciting finishes, and the two‑woman bobsleigh event ended with a German‑Israeli victory.
