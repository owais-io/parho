UK ministers are asking the public whether kids under 16 should be banned from using social media. They want to cut down on phone use and protect young people from addictive designs and harmful content. Some people say a ban could help kids focus better, while others worry it would be hard to enforce or drive teens to risky sites. The government wants feedback from kids, parents and teachers.
