TR‑49 puts you in the shoes of Abbi, a sharp‑talking code‑breaker who works with a mysterious machine that pulls clues from obscure books. Set in a Britain in the 2010s war, the game asks you to match letter‑number codes to book titles and travel to the pages where hidden messages lie. Your goal is to locate the crucial text “Endpeace.” It feels like a playful nod to AI data‑scraping and a bit of literary mystery, with some weak side dialogue.
