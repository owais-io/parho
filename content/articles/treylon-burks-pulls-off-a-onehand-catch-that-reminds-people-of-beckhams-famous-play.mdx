The Washington Commanders’ rookie receiver Treylon Burks made a dramatic one‑hand catch against the Denver Broncos. He fell backward in the end zone, snatched a pass from Marcus Mariota, and scored a touchdown that tied the game at 13‑13. The move reminded fans of Odell Beckham Jr.’s famous 2014 catch. Beckham praised Burks on social media, and this is Burks’ second career touchdown.
