Plaid Cymru leader Rhun ap Iorwerth told supporters at a huge party conference that the May Welsh parliament election will be a showdown between his party and Reform UK. He called it a choice between culture and ignorance, warning that Reform UK’s “Trump‑style” politics could harm Wales. He outlined plans for NHS hubs, school libraries, fair funding, and a new Wales bill, and said Plaid is ready to take power if it wins.
