Alex Honnold climbed Taipei 101 without a rope in a 91‑minute, 508‑metre ascent that was streamed live on Netflix. A cheering crowd in Taipei watched him pull himself up the building’s metal beams. The stunt sparked praise from local officials and criticism from some climbers who warned about the danger of broadcasting such a risky feat. Honnold said the climb should inspire people to chase their own goals.
