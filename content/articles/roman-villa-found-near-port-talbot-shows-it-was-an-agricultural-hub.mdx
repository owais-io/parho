Ground‑penetrating radar in Margam Country Park, outside Port Talbot, revealed the outline of a huge Roman villa – possibly the biggest in Wales. The find shows the area was a Roman farming centre, not a frontier. Its layout is similar to luxury villas in southern England and likely dates to the 4th century. Because the ground has stayed undisturbed, the remains may be well preserved. Researchers are keeping the exact spot secret to stop looters.
