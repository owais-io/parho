The BBC is starting a fast‑track investigation after the N‑word was heard during its live coverage of the Bafta film awards. The slur, shouted by activist John Davidson, was not cut out and stayed on the BBC iPlayer for about 15 hours. Director‑general Tim Davie has ordered a complaints unit review, while Culture Secretary Lisa Nandy and MPs have called the incident “unacceptable” and demanded answers.
