England’s captain says his team can beat any opponent and they’re aiming to get through to the T20 World Cup final after a shaky semi‑final. He stresses they don’t need a perfect game, just solid batting and a good bowling day. The side will face India at Mumbai’s Wankhede Stadium, where England’s bowlers and spin options will try to keep the hosts in check.
