A 16‑year‑old from Northumberland is on trial in Leeds for preparing terrorist attacks. Police found a cache of weapons – guns, explosives, knives, crossbows and nails – and notes showing hatred of Jews and black people. He joined a banned extremist group called Base at 13 and wants a white‑supremacist society. He watched violent videos, planned to target a synagogue and even build homemade bombs. He denies the charges and the trial is ongoing.
