The extra money that lowered health‑insurance costs for many people under the Affordable Care Act (ACA) stopped at the end of last year. Since then, premiums have jumped for at least 22 million folks who used those tax credits on ACA marketplaces. The article asks people how they’ve dealt with the higher costs, what it’s done to their 2026 budgets, and if they’ve changed their spending habits.
