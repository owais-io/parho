Storm moving up the East Coast will bring rain, ice and heavy snow to the northeast on Monday night and Tuesday. Places from the Poconos to eastern Maine could get 5‑10 inches, with higher spots in New England over a foot. State warnings are in place, and traffic may be hazardous. The storm follows a big mid‑west blizzard that hit Chicago and other cities, causing crashes and flight delays.
