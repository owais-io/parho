Barnaby Joyce, now a One Nation spokesperson, keeps claiming climate change is a hoax and that climate policies ruin the economy. His claims ignore real facts: energy price rises were caused by wars that cut fossil fuel supply, not climate laws. In fact, renewable energy is growing fast, solar and electric cars are cheap and safe. One Nation’s stance is a protest movement that rejects science.
