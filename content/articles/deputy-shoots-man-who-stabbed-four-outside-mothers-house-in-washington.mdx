A 32‑year‑old man in Washington was shot dead by a deputy after he stabbed four people outside his mother’s house. He had a history of substance abuse and mental‑health problems, and his mother had already obtained protection orders against him. Deputies were called to serve the latest notice when the man tried to leave, and the situation turned deadly. Police are still investigating why the orders were not served.
