British Prime Minister Keir Starmer is heading to China for the first time since 2018. The trip will let him talk to Beijing about trade, security and how Britain should position itself worldwide. China wants a smoother relationship and better market access, while Britain faces threats of cyber‑attacks and spy allegations. The visit will help Starmer decide whether Britain can work with China or needs to set limits.
