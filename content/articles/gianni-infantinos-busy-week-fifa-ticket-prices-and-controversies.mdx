Gianni Infantino, the head of FIFA, spent a hectic week juggling meetings and controversies. He flew to Rabat to discuss the Africa Cup of Nations, talked to Moroccan officials, and highlighted the high cost of World Cup tickets. Infantino also made statements about the safety of fans and the political role of football. Fans and supporters groups criticised his remarks, calling for cheaper tickets and better fan focus.
