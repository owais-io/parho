In Minneapolis, an ICE officer fired three shots at 37‑year‑old Renee Good, who was driving an SUV that was blocking the officers’ cars. The gunman shot her as she tried to escape, and she died. Trump‑era officials, including Kristi Noem, blame Good, calling the incident “domestic terrorism.” This killing is part of a broader Trump‑era push to deport many Somali residents. Protesters gathered to defend Good, but no officer has been charged yet.
