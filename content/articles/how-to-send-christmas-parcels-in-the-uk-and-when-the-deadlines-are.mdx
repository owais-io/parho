This guide explains the last dates you can post Christmas gifts with Royal Mail and other couriers. For the UK, second‑class parcels must leave by 17 December, first‑class by 20, and tracked options by 22 or 23. International parcels have earlier deadlines – for example, Spain and New Zealand by 8 December, the US by 12, Australia by 5. It also shows how to compare prices, choose the cheapest option, avoid banned items and customs paperwork. You can send small gifts as large letters for cheaper rates, and some online shops give free delivery above a set price.
