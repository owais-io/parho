Arizona Senator Ruben Gallego says that if President Trump tries to disrupt the November midterm elections, Americans should launch a national strike that would stop work and grind the country to a halt. He warns Trump could try to take over ballot boxes, stop the count or surround polls. Gallego says workers, pilots, teachers and others should not show up to work if democracy is threatened. He argues that protecting elections is as important as protecting the economy.
