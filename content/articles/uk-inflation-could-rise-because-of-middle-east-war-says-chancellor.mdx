UK Chancellor Rachel Reeves warned that the war between the United States and Iran could push inflation higher. She said a quick de‑escalation is the best way to keep energy prices from spiralling. The government will monitor fuel pumps and heating oil to stop profiteering, and may step in to protect households. The debate over a planned 5‑pence fuel‑duty rise is still on, with MPs split.
