Former Australian prime minister Malcolm Turnbull told the Guardian that new Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese should openly acknowledge how Donald Trump has shifted the global order. Turnbull compared Albanese to Canadian finance minister Mark Carney, who warned at Davos that the US‑led system is breaking apart. He urged Albanese to give a speech explaining how Australia will navigate a world where the US is less reliable and great‑power rivalry grows.
