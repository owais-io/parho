Arsenal Women have won their way to the Champions Cup final after a 6‑0 win over Moroccan side AS Far. Coach Sarina Slegers said the team is proud of its diversity, citing hijab‑wearing player Nouhaila Benzina as a role model. She’s confident but respectful of the unknown South‑American club Corinthians, and believes the match will be a big event for players and fans alike.
