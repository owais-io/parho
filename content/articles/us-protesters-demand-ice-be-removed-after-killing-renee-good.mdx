In Philadelphia on Saturday, two groups of a few hundred people marched to the ICE detention centre, demanding the agency leave the city. They also criticized President Trump’s policies in Venezuela, calling the government a regime that kills its own people. The protests were part of a nationwide weekend after ICE killed Renee Good in Minneapolis, with similar marches in Boston, San Francisco and Minneapolis. Police largely stayed out of the way, but tensions and arrests were reported in other cities.
