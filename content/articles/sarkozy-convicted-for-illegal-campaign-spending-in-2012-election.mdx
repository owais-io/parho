Former French president Nicolas Sarkozy has been found guilty of illegally overspending during his 2012 re‑election campaign. France’s top court rejected his last appeal, making the verdict final. He spent roughly €42.8 million—nearly double the legal €22.5 million limit—on large rallies. Sarkozy, who lost to François Hollande, now faces a one‑year sentence, half suspended, which could be served with an electronic tag instead of jail.
