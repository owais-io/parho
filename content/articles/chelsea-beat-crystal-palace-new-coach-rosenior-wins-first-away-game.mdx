Chelsea beat Crystal Palace 2-1 in a Premier League match, giving new coach Liam Rosenior his first away win. Brazilian striker Estêvão Willian scored first, then João Pedro provided an assist, and Enzo Fernández sealed it with a penalty after Adam Wharton was sent off. Palace now face 17 straight Premier League matches without a win. Chelsea remain unbeaten in the league under Rosenior.
