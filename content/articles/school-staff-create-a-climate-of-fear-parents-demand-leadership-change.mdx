Parents at Mossbourne Victoria Park Academy want school leaders to step down after a report found harsh, fear‑based discipline. An Observer investigation revealed years of abusive tactics, even a “healthy fear” training session. Despite top Ofsted ratings, inspectors missed the problems. Parents and experts say schools must focus on student well‑being, especially mental health, and give special‑needs kids proper support. They call for a major overhaul of how schools are run.
