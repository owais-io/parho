After a pre‑dawn attack on Caracas, the US captured Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and his wife. They were flown to New York and will face federal court. President Trump said the US will run Venezuela until a safe transition and will play a big role in its oil industry. The move sparked strong protests from Venezuela, Russia, Cuba and others, who say it violates international law.
