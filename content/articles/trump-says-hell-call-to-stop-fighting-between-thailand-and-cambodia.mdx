Trump said he'll make a phone call to end the renewed fighting between Thailand and Cambodia. The clash restarted after a ceasefire he helped arrange fell apart. The fighting has killed people and forced over 500,000 refugees. Thailand blames a landmine, while Cambodia denies it. Both sides accuse each other of breaking the U.S.-backed truce and promise to keep fighting until a deal is reached.
