Swedish navy jamming a drone near France’s Charles de Gaulle carrier shows Russia’s use of hybrid tactics against NATO allies. French officials dismissed it as provocation, while Moscow shrugged off criticism. The incident comes as Denmark warns of Russian disinformation in its upcoming election and Hungary uses the Ukraine war to distract from domestic woes. Other points include a pipeline dispute, a temporary nuclear ceasefire, and reports of African recruits fighting for Russia.
