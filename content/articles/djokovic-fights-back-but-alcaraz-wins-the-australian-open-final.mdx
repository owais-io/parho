In the Australian Open final, 39‑year‑old Novak Djokovic battled a younger Rafael Alcaraz in a dramatic match. Fans chanted “No‑le” to keep the older star alive, but Djokovic’s hip and age‑related decline proved too much. Alcaraz seized the moment, breaking key points and winning the title, giving him a chance to complete the career Grand Slam.
