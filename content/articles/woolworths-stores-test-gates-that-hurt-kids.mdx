Woolworths is testing new security gates at six stores that have low bars. Parents in Sydney and Melbourne say the bars can hit children’s arms, legs and faces, and even push back if kids grab them. Some shoppers feel unsafe and want the gates removed. The supermarket says the gates aim to stop theft but says it will review customer feedback.
