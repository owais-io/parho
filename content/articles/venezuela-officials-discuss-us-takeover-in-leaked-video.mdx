A leaked recording of a nearly two‑hour meeting shows Venezuela’s acting president Delcy Rodríguez and other cabinet members telling influencers what happened after US forces seized former President Nicolás Maduro. They say they were given 15 minutes to decide whether to obey Washington or face death. The clip reveals the regime’s scramble to control the narrative, keep its base together, and decide how to keep power while dealing with the US.
