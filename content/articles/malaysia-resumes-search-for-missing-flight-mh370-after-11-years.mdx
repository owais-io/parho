Malaysia will restart its long‑awaited search for missing flight MH370, a B777 that vanished over the Indian Ocean in 2014. The government has hired Ocean Infinity, a U.S. robotics firm, to scour a 15,000‑square‑kilometre area for 55 days, paying only if wreckage is found. Families and fans have long campaigned for answers, hoping this fresh effort brings closure for all those who lost loved ones and for the global aviation community.
