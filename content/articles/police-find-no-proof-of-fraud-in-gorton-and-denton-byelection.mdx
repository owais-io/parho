Police investigated claims of cheating in the Gorton and Denton by‑election after Nigel Farage’s accusations. They found no evidence of illegal voting or coercion, even after reviewing CCTV and speaking with polling station staff. Observers had reported family‑voting concerns, but no proof was found. The Green Party’s Hannah Spencer won the seat, ending Labour’s long hold. Reform UK leader called the report a “whitewash,” but police stand by their findings.
