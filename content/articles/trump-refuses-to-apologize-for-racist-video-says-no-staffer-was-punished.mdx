Trump posted a video on Truth Social that turned Barack and Michelle Obama into cartoon apes, sparking backlash. He denied disciplining the staffer who shared it, blamed the clip on a misinterpreted “Lion King” reference, and claimed the video was already widely seen. Despite critics calling it offensive, Trump has not apologized and insists he made no mistake.
