Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy says there are still real chances to end the war with dignity. He wants another round of talks in Geneva with Russia and the United States, and he urges Europe to stay involved. Meanwhile, the EU is set to adopt a new sanctions package against Russia, even though ambassadors have not yet agreed on it. On a security note, Ukrainian and Moldovan police arrested 10 people who were planning to kill Ukrainian officials on Moscow’s orders. Germany is also pushing for stronger European intelligence cooperation, and several other European countries are reviewing their defense and sanctions plans as the fourth anniversary of Russia’s invasion approaches.
