The Xbox 360 launched in 2005 and quickly became a game‑changing console. It brought online play, achievements, and a single gamer ID to millions, creating a social hub that rivalled Sony’s PlayStation. With Xbox Live Arcade, indie titles like Geometry Wars and Braid found a home, and the sleek controller set a new comfort standard. Though the later Xbox One missed the mark, the 360’s legacy lives on in every gamer’s memory.
