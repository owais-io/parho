Russia launched a big drone and missile attack on Ukraine’s biggest cities, Kyiv and Kharkiv, on Saturday. The strikes hit homes, hospitals, and power lines, leaving thousands without heat and electricity and causing one death and dozens of injuries. The assault came while peace talks were happening in the UAE. Ukrainian leaders said talks were useful and welcomed more meetings, but Russia still wants to keep the eastern Donbas region.
