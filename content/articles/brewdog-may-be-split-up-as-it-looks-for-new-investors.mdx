BrewDog, the Scottish craft‑beer maker known for Punk IPA, has hired consulting firm AlixPartners to explore a sale that could break the company into parts. After shutting its distillery brands, the firm is focusing on its core beer business and has already cut jobs. With about 1,400 staff, 72 bars and four breweries worldwide, the company is looking for new investors to keep it afloat.
