House Speaker Mike Johnson often answers reporters with “I don’t know” when asked about Trump controversies—from pardons to jet gifts. Critics say this shows he’s not doing his duty to stay informed and hold the president accountable. Even with a big staff, he claims ignorance or says the issue isn’t his job. His habit has drawn fire from both sides of Congress, raising concerns about his leadership and loyalty to Trump.
