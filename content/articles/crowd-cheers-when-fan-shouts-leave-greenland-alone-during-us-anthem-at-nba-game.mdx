At an NBA match in London, a spectator shouted “Leave Greenland alone” while the US national anthem was sung, prompting cheers from some fans. The comment reflected tension over US President Donald Trump’s recent threats to impose tariffs on European nations if the U.S. is allowed to buy Greenland. Trump said he would raise tariffs on Denmark, Norway, Sweden, France, Germany, the UK, the Netherlands and Finland. European leaders, including UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer, rebuked the move.
