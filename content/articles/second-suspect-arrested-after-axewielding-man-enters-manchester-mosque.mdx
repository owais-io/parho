A man with an axe and knife entered Manchester Central Mosque on Feb. 24. Worshippers hit him with a fire extinguisher, and police arrested him. The first suspect, a white man in his 40s, was taken for weapons and drug charges. A second suspect, a black man in his 20s, was also arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to commit assault. No one was hurt, and police say there’s no wider threat.
