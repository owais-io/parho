In the morning, officials found a package in the Arizona Supreme Court’s mailroom that tested positive for a homemade explosive. The building was emptied around 10:30 a.m., and staff were sent home. State police, the Phoenix police, and federal agencies like the Bureau of Alcohol, Firearms and Tobacco helped investigate. Nearby buildings, including the Department of Education, were also cleared while authorities searched the area.
