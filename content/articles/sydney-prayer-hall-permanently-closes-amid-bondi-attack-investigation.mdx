An Islamic prayer hall in Bankstown closed for good. It was linked to cleric Wisam Haddad, who has been under police eye after the Bondi beach shooting. The local council said the centre never had permission to be a mosque, only a medical clinic. New state laws let councils shut down unapproved places of worship. The centre said it was acting independently and would seek proper approval.
