Mark Davis, a Florida House hopeful, bought the domain nazis.us and set it to redirect visitors straight to the DHS site. He says he did it to expose the Trump‑era crackdown and to show that even a “nobody” can speak up. The move drew mixed reactions—some praised his courage, others mocked it. DHS quickly blocked the redirect, but the domain still points to the homeland security website.
