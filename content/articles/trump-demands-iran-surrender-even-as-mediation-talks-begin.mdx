He ignored new mediation efforts announced by Iran’s president, who said some countries were working on a peace deal. The U.S. claim has hurt diplomatic chances and stoked fears of a wider oil price jump. Meanwhile, Iranian reformists want a new supreme leader to open doors for negotiations.
