Nvidia’s venture arm and the UK’s National Wealth Fund have put $50 million into Oxa, a British company that builds software for self‑driving industrial vehicles. Oxa, based in Oxford, raised a total of $103 million and is using the money to grow commercial solutions, robotics and AI, and to expand overseas. The firm focuses on towing and cargo transport in ports, airports and factories, and its co‑founder says the off‑road market is more affordable than passenger cars.
