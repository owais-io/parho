Iran has warned it will attack any U.S.‑linked sites in the Middle East after former President Donald Trump said several countries would send warships to keep the Hormuz Strait open. The U.S. and Israel have bombed Iran’s oil hub Kharg, while Iran has launched missiles at Israel, the UAE and other targets. Oil prices are spiking, and the fighting has killed thousands in the region.
