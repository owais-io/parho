When Israel and the U.S. started bombing Tehran after the Iranian leader died, many Iranian Americans felt a spark of hope that the regime could change. But the war has brought thousands of deaths, damage to historic sites, and no clear end. Opinions now split: some support a monarchy, others fight the regime, while many just want peace. The war has made most feel sad and unsure about Iran’s future.
