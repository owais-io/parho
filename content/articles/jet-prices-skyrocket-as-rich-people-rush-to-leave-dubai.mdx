After Iranian drones and missiles hit Dubai and shut its main airport, wealthy travelers are racing to fly out on private jets. Jet prices have jumped, with a small jet to Istanbul costing around €85,000, and Moscow charters about €20,000 per seat. Limited commercial flights are available to neighboring Oman and Saudi Arabia, but many passengers are stuck on cruise ships or hotels, while some officials face political backlash.
