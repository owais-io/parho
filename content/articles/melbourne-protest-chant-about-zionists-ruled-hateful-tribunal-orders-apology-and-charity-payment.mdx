Victorian tribunal ruled that pro‑Palestine protester Hash Tayeh breached the Racial and Religious Tolerance Act after shouting “All Zionists are terrorists” at a Melbourne rally. The judge said the chant was strongly linked to Jews, likely to stir hatred, and Tayeh had no valid defence. Vorchheimer, a Jewish protester, asked for an apology, a ban on similar comments, and a $20,000 charity payment. A hearing will set the final penalty.
