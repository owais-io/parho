Shoppers alone can keep local shops alive. The writer says that big supermarkets and online buying are pulling people away from small shops. But he argues that people can reverse this by choosing to shop locally. Community projects like art displays, markets and better town planning have already helped places like Melton Mowbray and Brixton. The piece urges readers to spend money in their neighbourhoods instead of on big retailers.
