Sumo wrestling, a Japanese sport known for its giant wrestlers, is suddenly popular in Britain thanks to TikTok videos and a recent tournament at the Royal Albert Hall. Britons, including teens, are wearing mawashi and joining clubs. A new British Isles Sumo Championship will start soon, with matches lasting about 10 seconds and featuring weight classes for men and women. The sport is praised for being fast, fun, and open to everyone.
