Two Sámi DJs from a remote Norwegian village—Alice Marie Jektevik and Petra Laiti—run the collective Article 3, mixing traditional joik and throat‑singing with electronic beats. Their shows, held in forests and clubs, aim to create safe, inclusive spaces for Indigenous fans. The duo credits a growing sense of Sámi pride among young people and the challenge of cross‑border borders for artists. They’ll play at the Barents Spektakel festival.
