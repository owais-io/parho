At the University of Florida, a College Republicans chapter was suspended after a video showing two members giving Nazi salutes was posted online. The group says the suspension violates their First‑Amendment rights and has sued the university, claiming the action was retaliation for their political views. The case highlights growing tensions between right‑wing student groups and campus policies against hate speech.
