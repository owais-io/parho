The 2026 Cheltenham Festival is starting, and British trainers hope to end a decade‑long slump where Irish jumpers have dominated. With big crowds at earlier meetings, the betting market now shows several UK‑trained horses as top contenders for the Grade One races, especially the Gold Cup. Trainers like Nicky Henderson, Paul Nicholls and Dan Skelton are backing their best runners, raising hope that Britain can finally lift the trophy back home.
