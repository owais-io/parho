Three men who escaped a Georgia jail used a fake name to book a Lyft, then hijacked the ride and drove for ten hours to Florida. The driver was held hostage, threatened, and dragged to a rental house in Miramar where the men used her credit cards. Police tracked the vehicle with license‑plate readers and arrested the trio in Florida. The jailbreak happened during a routine security check; the jail is now being upgraded.
