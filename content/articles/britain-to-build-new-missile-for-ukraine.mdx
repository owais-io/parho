Britain is starting a project called Nightfall to build a ground‑launched missile that can carry a 200‑kg warhead up to 500 km. Three teams will get £9 m each to produce three missiles in a year for test flights. Ukraine already uses U.S. ATACMS and its own Sapsan. Russia is still bombing Kyiv, and Ukraine has hit Russian oil sites and Lukoil platforms. The Pope also prayed for Ukraine.
