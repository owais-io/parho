Nickie and her Threshold Choir help people in their final hours by singing gentle, lullaby‑style songs. The choir creates a loving, safe atmosphere for those near death, offering comfort and companionship. Their music guides patients through the dying process, giving peace and support to both the person dying and their families. In short, the choir brings calm, love, and hope to those at the end of life.
