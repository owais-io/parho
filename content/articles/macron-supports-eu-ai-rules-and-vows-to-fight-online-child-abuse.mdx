Macron, France’s president, said Europe will tighten AI rules to protect children. Speaking at a Delhi summit, he reacted to a chatbot that produced sexualised images of kids and announced France will ban social media for users under 15. Tech leaders from Google, Meta, Microsoft, OpenAI and India’s prime minister were also there, discussing how to keep AI safe for families.
