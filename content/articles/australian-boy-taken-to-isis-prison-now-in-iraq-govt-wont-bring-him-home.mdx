An Australian boy named Yusuf was taken to ISIS prisons when he was 12. After ISIS fell, he survived bombings, beatings and illness in Syria. He was moved to Iraq this month. His family and Australian politicians want him back, but the government says it will not repatriate him. The move has caused political fights in Australia. Lawyers say the boys were treated poorly and could face harsh punishment in Iraq.
