Former tennis champion Sam Groth, who is the deputy leader of Victoria’s Liberal Party, said he will not run in the November state election. He blamed growing fights inside the party and the strain from a defamation lawsuit against News Corp for pushing his family into the spotlight. The decision also came after another Liberal MP, Wendy Lovell, announced her resignation. Groth says the pressure made him decide to step back and spend more time with his family.
