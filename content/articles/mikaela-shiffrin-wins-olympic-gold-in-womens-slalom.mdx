Mikaela Shiffrin won her third Olympic gold in women’s slalom at the Winter Games, beating competitors by 1.5 seconds. After a long wait for a medal, she celebrated with tears and hugs, reflecting on her late father's influence. The win also made her the first American to win three alpine gold medals. The article also notes China’s Su Yiming winning men’s slopestyle on his birthday, Xu Mengtao defending her aerials title, and a curling win for Great Britain.
