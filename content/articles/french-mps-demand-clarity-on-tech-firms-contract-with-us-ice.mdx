French lawmakers are demanding answers after Capgemini, a major French tech firm, signed a multimillion‑dollar deal with US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) to help track and remove migrants. The contract includes “skip tracing” services and large bonuses. MPs say this could violate human rights, especially after ICE’s recent controversy in Minnesota. Capgemini says the deal isn’t active yet and wants more transparency.
