U.S. Ambassador Mike Huckabee told Fox News host Tucker Carlson that, based on a biblical passage, Israel could rightfully claim much of the Middle East. Huckabee said it would be fine if Israel “took it all.” His comments, made while touring Israel, sparked backlash. Israeli officials denied any detention of Carlson and said the remarks were controversial, stirring debate over religious‑political claims in U.S. foreign policy.
