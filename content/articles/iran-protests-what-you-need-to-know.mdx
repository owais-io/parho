Protests in Iran have turned violent, with reports of over 2,000 deaths and tens of thousands arrested. Protesters say the government is using police and the military to crush dissent, shooting many. U.S. President Trump has warned Iran and said he might use force if the killings continue. European leaders and Russia have condemned the crackdown. The internet is blocked, but some private services are helping. The situation remains tense, and the future of the regime is uncertain.
