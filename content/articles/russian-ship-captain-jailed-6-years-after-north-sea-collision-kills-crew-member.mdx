A 59‑year‑old Russian captain, Vladimir Motin, was sentenced to six years in prison for gross negligence manslaughter after his container ship, Solong, crashed into an oil tanker, Stena Immaculate, in the North Sea. The impact caused an explosion that killed a 38‑year‑old Filipino crewman. A judge said Motin failed to keep a proper lookout and the death was “wholly avoidable.”
