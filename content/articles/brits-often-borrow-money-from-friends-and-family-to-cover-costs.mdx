Many Brits borrow money from friends and family to cover costs, says a new survey. Over 4,000 adults were asked. About 26% borrowed from family and 15% from friends in 2023, while a quarter used Buy‑Now‑Pay‑Later loans. People rejected by banks and younger, lower‑paid workers or new parents were most likely to seek help from loved ones. A quarter of households said they couldn't pay a £500 bill without borrowing.
