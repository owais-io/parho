The former court clerk in the 2023 Alex Murdaugh murder trial, Becky Hill, pleaded guilty for showing sealed crime‑scene photos to a photographer and lying about it. She also faced charges for taking bonuses and promoting her book using office funds. The judge sentenced her to three years of probation, noting a harsher sentence would have followed if jury‑tampering evidence had been proven. Hill apologized, saying she had no excuse for her mistakes.
