Harry Brook, England’s white‑ball captain, said he feels lucky to keep his job after fighting a nightclub bouncer the night before a New Zealand ODI. He apologised, was fined about £30,000, and said he has work to regain players’ trust. Brook denied a drinking culture, saying the team can choose. The incident sparked a review of England’s on‑tour behaviour. Fans and officials are watching to see if changes happen.
