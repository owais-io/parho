Christchurch has turned a decade of disasters—earthquakes, fires, and a mosque attack—into a story of recovery and growth. The city is now attracting people from across New Zealand with cheaper homes, job opportunities, and a lively arts scene. Businesses are opening faster than the national average, and new sports and cultural facilities are coming online. Mayor Phil Mauger says the community’s spirit and new city life make it “the capital of cool.”
