A 5‑metre deep hole opened next to two tunnelling machines at A J Burkitt Oval in Heidelberg, a Melbourne suburb. No one was hurt, but the area was shut off and construction has been stopped while officials investigate. The incident is part of the larger North East Link road project, which will add a 10‑km stretch with tunnels to cut travel times. Work will resume once the cause is found.
