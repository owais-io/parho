England coach Steve Borthwick is putting his confidence in 21‑year‑old Henry Pollock for the Ireland game, saying the energetic back‑rower can bring excitement to Twickenham. Pollock will replace Ben Earl and fill the No.8 spot as England look to bounce back from a loss to Scotland. Borthwick praised Pollock’s drive, self‑motivation and his knack for big plays, hoping he will keep the crowd cheering.
