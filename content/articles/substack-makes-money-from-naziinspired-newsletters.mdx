Substack, a popular newsletter platform, is making money from newsletters that spread Nazi ideas, white supremacy, and Holocaust denial. A Guardian investigation found that the site takes about 10% of the revenue from these paid newsletters, which attract millions of readers. The platform’s algorithm pushes users to more extremist content, raising fears that hate online fuels real‑world violence. Experts call for stronger regulation and clearer policies to curb this harm.
