A federal court shut down the Biden‑era Save plan, an income‑driven loan program that let many borrowers pay nothing. The ruling comes as the Trump team moves loan management to the Treasury and says it will guide borrowers to new plans. With 7.7 million people already in default, experts warn confusion will grow. Students are urged to log into StudentAid.gov, see who their servicer is, and switch to a different repayment option, like IBR or the upcoming RAP plan.
