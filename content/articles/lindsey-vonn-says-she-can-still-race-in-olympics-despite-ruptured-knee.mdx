Lindsey Vonn, the 41‑year‑old American skier, says she is still ready to compete at the Milano‑Cortina Winter Games even after a complete ACL tear in her left knee. She had a crash at the World Cup in Crans‑Montana, but doctors say she can get back on the slopes. Vonn will try to race the downhill, super‑G and team event, hoping to finish strong for her comeback season.
