Ukraine’s foreign minister told Putin to stop wasting the world’s time, while Britain’s and Estonia’s leaders urged a ceasefire and talks. U.S. President Trump discussed peace talks but said progress was unclear. Ukraine reports battlefield gains and is pushing back Russian forces. Europe and the UN are offering aid and demanding the return of abducted children. Australia and New Zealand are helping buy weapons and sanctioning Russia.
