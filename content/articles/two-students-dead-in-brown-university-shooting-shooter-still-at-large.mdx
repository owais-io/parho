On Saturday, a shooting at Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island left two students dead and eight injured. The attack happened in the Barus + Holley engineering building during finals. Police put the campus in lockdown and ordered a shelter‑in‑place order. The suspect, a man wearing black, has not been caught and no weapon was found. Authorities are still searching for him and asking anyone with clues to come forward.
