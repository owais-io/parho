At Anfield, Chelsea women demolished Liverpool 9‑1 in the League Cup quarter‑final. Johanna Rytting‑Kaneryd scored a hat‑trick, and Sam Kerr added two goals. Liverpool struggled early, turning their mistakes into quick goals. Manager Gareth Taylor said the team was punished for errors and that injuries and a lack of substitutes hurt them. Liverpool are out of the competition, while Chelsea moves on to the semi‑final against Manchester City.
