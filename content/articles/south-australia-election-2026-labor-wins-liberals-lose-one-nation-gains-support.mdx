South Australia’s 2026 election is shaping up to be a win for Labor, a blow for the Liberals, and a chance for One Nation to grow. Premier Peter Malinauskas is likely to win, while Liberal leader Ashton Hurn faces a heavy defeat. One Nation, led by Pauline Hanson, has surged in polls and could win a seat in the upper house. The election also highlights voters’ frustration with major parties and a focus on rural seats.
