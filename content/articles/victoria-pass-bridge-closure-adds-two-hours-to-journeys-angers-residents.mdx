Transport for NSW shut the Victoria Pass bridge on the Great Western Highway after cracks were found. The 12‑mile stretch is closed for at least three months, forcing 12,000 daily commuters to take long detours that add up to two extra hours, especially during peak times. The move has upset residents and local businesses, many of whom have lost sales and face longer travel times. The state says safety is top priority and will study the damage before reopening.
