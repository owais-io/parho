A new report shows that first‑time buyers now have the most choices for low‑deposit mortgages in over a decade. 537 deals let people borrow 95% of a home’s value, almost twice as many as earlier this year, and some lenders offer up to 100% loans.  Rates for 2‑ and 5‑year fixed‑rate mortgages are around 4.5%.  Many buyers still haven’t explored these options, so the market may be a lifeline for those struggling to save a deposit.
