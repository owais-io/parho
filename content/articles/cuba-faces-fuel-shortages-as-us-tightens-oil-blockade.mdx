Cuba is struggling to get gasoline after the U.S. strengthened its oil blockade on the island. With fewer shipments, many petrol stations are closed and flights are cancelled. People are missing fuel for cars and generators, causing power cuts and transport delays. The U.S. warns countries that send oil to Cuba with higher tariffs, saying the Cuban government threatens U.S. security. Residents are sharing how the crisis affects everyday life.
