Jack Doherty, a 22‑year‑old livestreamer known for pranks, was kicked out of the Waste Management Phoenix Open after he paid a fan to shout during a golfer’s setup. Tournament security and police removed him, and the PGA Tour says anyone breaking the Fan Code of Conduct can be ejected. The tour hasn’t officially confirmed the ban, but reports suggest Doherty may be barred from future PGA events for the foreseeable future.
