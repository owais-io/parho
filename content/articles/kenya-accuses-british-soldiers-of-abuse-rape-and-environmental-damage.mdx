A Kenyan parliamentary report says British soldiers at a base near Nanyuki committed many wrongs. The soldiers allegedly raped, abused, and abandoned children. They also caused serious damage to the environment, destroying wildlife habitats and sparking huge fires. The report says the British unit refused to cooperate, and justice for victims has been blocked. Kenya recommends new rules, a support team for victims, and ways to hold foreign soldiers accountable.
