Meg Turner, 79, was a dedicated nurse and caregiver who spent over 30 years in nursing and elderly care. She trained in 1964, worked in hospitals across Sussex and Somerset, and later became a matron and training teacher in Wales. After retiring in 1999, she stayed active in local groups, loved poetry, craft, and word games. She died with arthritis, leaving her husband, two children and four grandchildren.
