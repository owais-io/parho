Amy Scott, the Bondi Junction police officer who bravely shot the attacker in April 2024, was recently diagnosed with an aggressive breast cancer. A charity fundraiser launched for her and her family quickly gathered over $230,000 in a single day. Support poured in from the NSW police, politicians, and the community, all cheering her courage and wishing her strength through her fight.
