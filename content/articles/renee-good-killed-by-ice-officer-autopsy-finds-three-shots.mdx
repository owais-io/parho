A private autopsy found that Renee Good, a 37‑year‑old mother, was shot three times—once in her forearm, once in her breast, and once in her head—by a federal ICE officer in Minneapolis. The family’s lawyers say the officer fired in “self‑defence,” but video shows Good was driving away. No criminal probe of the officer has been started, sparking anger from state officials and protests. The Department of Justice says the officer is cleared, but the investigation is stalled.
