ABC radio host James Valentine, known for his quirky style and 25‑year run on Sydney’s Afternoons program, has stepped down after a cancer diagnosis. The 64‑year‑old revealed oesophageal cancer in 2024, underwent surgery, but new tumours forced him to retire again in June. Fans called in to share tears and memories. Valentine will focus on his health, and the station will celebrate his legacy with a special retrospective.
