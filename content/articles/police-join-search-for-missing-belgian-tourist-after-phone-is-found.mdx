Tasmania police are now officially helping a private search for Celine Cremer, a 31‑year‑old Belgian tourist who vanished near Cradle Mountain in June 2023. A volunteer discovered her Samsung phone near the Philosopher Falls track, indicating she may have left the trail, dropped the phone, and become lost. With bad weather still a concern, the search will focus on the area around the phone, as no other evidence of Celine has been found yet.
