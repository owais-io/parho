Channel 5 will air a two‑part drama about former BBC anchor Huw Edwards, who was convicted of child‑sexual offences. The series, starring Martin Clunes, will show how a 17‑year‑old was groomed and how Edwards’s career collapsed. He received a suspended six‑month sentence for handling indecent child images. Channel 5 claims it has behind‑the‑scenes access and wants to reveal the human cost. The channel is known for using controversial stories to attract viewers.
