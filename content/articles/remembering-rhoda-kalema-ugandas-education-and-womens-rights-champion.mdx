Rhoda Kalema was a fearless woman who helped Uganda rebuild after years of war. She fought for free primary schooling for all kids and for women to vote and win seats in parliament. Her work, done with a group of other brave women, made big changes, but the media hasn’t talked about them enough. We remember her for her courage and her big impact on Uganda’s future.
