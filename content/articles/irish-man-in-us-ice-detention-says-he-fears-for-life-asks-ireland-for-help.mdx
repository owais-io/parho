An Irish plasterer, 42, has been held in a Texas ICE detention center for five months even though he holds a valid work permit and no criminal record. He says the conditions are like torture and that he fears the staff. He has asked Ireland’s government to intervene and even wants the Irish prime minister to bring his case to President Trump. The Irish embassy is trying to help.
