MPs voted 307-173 to reject a bill that would ban children under 16 from social media. The bill, introduced by a Conservative peer, had support from campaigns like actor Hugh Grant. While supporters say parents need help protecting kids, charities warn a ban could push teens into unsafe corners of the internet. The government will consult on next steps.
