Starmer, whose brother died last Christmas, says people should show kindness this holiday. He urges everyone to reach out to lonely or struggling friends and family, echoing the work of volunteers and public‑sector workers. He also says the cost of living remains a worry for many. The message came as other leaders – Kemi Badenoch, Ed Davey, and Zack Polanski – also shared holiday wishes and calls for compassion. King Charles will give his own speech on Christmas Day.
