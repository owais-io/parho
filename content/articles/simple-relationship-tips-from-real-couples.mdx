The article shares practical advice from Guardian readers on keeping relationships strong. Tips include finding common hobbies, keeping a balance between shared and personal interests, lowering unrealistic expectations, laughing together, learning to have tough conversations, and listening more than speaking. It also stresses the importance of honesty, giving each other space to grow, forgiving past mistakes, respecting differences, and building a friendship first. These ideas help couples stay connected and supportive.
