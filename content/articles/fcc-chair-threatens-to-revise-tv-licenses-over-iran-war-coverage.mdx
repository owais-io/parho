FCC Chair Brendan Carr said TV stations could lose their licenses if they broadcast what he calls “fake news” about the Iran conflict. Republican Senator Ron Johnson defended the First Amendment and opposed government control over media. Democrats, including Senators Warren and Murphy, slammed the warning as censorship. The FCC’s power is limited, and many media experts say the threat is illegal and unconstitutional.
