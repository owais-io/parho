Chelsea won over Everton, but coach Enzo Maresca’s vague remarks about the past 48 hours left fans puzzled. He hinted at problems inside the club, though he didn’t say who. The team shows talent but keeps slipping, partly due to injuries, rotation, and a cautious manager.
