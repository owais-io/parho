23 people died in suspected suicide bombings in Nigeria's Maiduguri, killing many more. Attacks hit a post office, market, and hospital entrance during iftar. The bombings are blamed on Boko Haram or its splinter group. The city, once calm, has seen fewer attacks since 2021. More than 100 people were injured. Nigerian officials, including President Tinubu, have ordered a military response to fight the attackers and restore security.
