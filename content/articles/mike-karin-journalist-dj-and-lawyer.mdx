Mike Karin, 59, was a journalist, DJ and lawyer who died unexpectedly. He began at Loaded magazine in the 1990s, helped launch its website and later wrote for the Times, GQ and Vogue. Moving to Thailand, he became a partner at Siam Legal, handling high‑profile cases, including a commercial dispute that reached the UK Court of Appeal. A passionate vinyl DJ, he lived in the US and Thailand before his death. He is survived by his wife Joanne and family.
