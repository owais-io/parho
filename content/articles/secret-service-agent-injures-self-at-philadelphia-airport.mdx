A Secret Service agent assigned to protect former First Lady Jill Biden accidentally shot himself in the leg at Philadelphia International Airport on Friday morning. The gun discharge happened around 8:30 am and caused a non‑life‑threatening injury. No one else was hurt. The agent is receiving medical care and is being reviewed by the agency’s Office of Professional Responsibility for the negligent firing.
