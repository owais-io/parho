In Europe, air pollution and traffic kill many people, but a handful of cities show how a healthy environment can boost life quality. Copenhagen’s bike lanes and clean air let people commute without cars. Vienna shares parks and cheap housing, lowering health gaps. Barcelona’s superblocks cut traffic and heat, while London’s parks need safer walkways. Experts say smart planning and fewer cars make cities livable and healthier.
