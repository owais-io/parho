On Sunday, the Chicago Bears will face the Green Bay Packers in a crucial game that could decide who makes the NFL playoffs. A win for either team gives the other a bad loss. A Bears win could close the division. Green Bay is missing star defender Micah Parsons, so they’ll need to hold the ball longer. Chicago wants to prove they can win big. The outcome will also influence the playoff picture for teams like Detroit and Indianapolis.
