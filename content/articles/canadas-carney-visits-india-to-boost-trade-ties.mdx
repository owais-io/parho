Canada’s new prime minister, Mark Carney, flew to Delhi to meet Indian leader Narendra Modi. The two leaders want to grow trade, especially energy, because both feel pushed by the United States. Canada hopes to double non‑US exports and may sign a $2.8 billion uranium supply deal. The visit also follows a tough past visit by former Canadian PM Justin Trudeau and recent security tensions between the two countries. Both sides hope the talks will open new markets and reduce dependence on the US.
