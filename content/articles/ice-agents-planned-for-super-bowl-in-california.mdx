ICE is expected to send agents to Santa Clara for next month’s Super Bowl. The move would be the first time the agency focuses on immigration at a major sports event. Local officials and community groups worry it could make people feel unsafe. DHS said it will keep the game secure while respecting the Constitution. The plan follows criticism of ICE’s actions in other states.
