Napa Valley wineries are seeing a sharp drop in sales as older wine lovers drink less and younger drinkers turn to other drinks. Big brands are cutting jobs and closing facilities, while small, family‑run wineries are changing their focus to organic farming, better employee treatment, and online marketing. Tourism is also hit by fewer international visitors, but some winemakers remain hopeful and keep experimenting to stay afloat.
