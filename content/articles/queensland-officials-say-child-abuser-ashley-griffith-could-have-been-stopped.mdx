Queensland's top officials say the child abuser Ashley Griffith could have been caught earlier. A report shows that over 20 years, authorities missed 18 chances to stop him, including five key moments. Griffith, who abused 73 children (mostly 3‑5 year olds) while working in childcare centres, was sentenced to life. The state will now speed up a new safety system to protect children.
