X said it had shut down 800 million accounts in 12 months to curb manipulation, especially by state actors like Russia, Iran and China. The platform, with 300 million monthly users, said it keeps fighting fake networks and spam that try to steer the conversation. X’s executive told MPs the problem is still big, but most remaining accounts are real.
