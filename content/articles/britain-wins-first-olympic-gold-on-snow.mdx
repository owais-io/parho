Charlotte Bankes and Huw Nightingale lifted Great Britain to its first gold medal on snow at the 2026 Winter Olympics. In a wild mixed‑team snowboard cross race, the pair battled bruises, a broken binding and a close finish against France, Australia and Italy. After a rough start and a beer‑soaked pre‑race break, they edged out the competition, earning a historic win for the UK’s Olympic team.
