London City Lionesses beat Sunderland in the FA Cup, keeping their place in the competition and avoiding a shock exit. A clear win with a header from Wassa Sangaré early in the second half gave them the lead, and they held on with good possession. Sunderland, a lower‑tier team, had a chance for a surprise but were outplayed. The match showed top‑tier teams can stay safe in cup matches.
