Callum Chick joined Northampton Saints from Newcastle and has been praised for his hard work and quick recovery after a turnover. He says the team’s focus on chasing lost chances and working hard off the ball turns chances into points. Chick enjoyed the supportive town and fans, who are more rugby‑centric than football. He believes rotating players keeps the squad fresh for the long season and the hope of a title.
