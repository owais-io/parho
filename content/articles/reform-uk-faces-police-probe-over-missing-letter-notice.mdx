Reform UK is under police scrutiny after it sent out campaign letters that didn't include the required legal notice saying the party had paid for them. The letters, written by a 74‑year‑old pensioner, were sent to voters in the Gorton and Denton byelection. Greater Manchester police will investigate, and the Electoral Commission says the omission is a legal offence. The printer says it was a printing mistake.
