Guardian’s latest corrections notice clears up several mistakes from a recent article. It says the fertiliser shipment in the Strait of Hormuz was 855,000 tonnes, not 855. The ship that left Portsmouth is HMS Dragon, not HMS Destroyer. It also fixes quotes about a Jimmy Kimmel joke, Israel’s strikes, and a few other headlines. Readers can send further complaints to the Guardian’s email.
