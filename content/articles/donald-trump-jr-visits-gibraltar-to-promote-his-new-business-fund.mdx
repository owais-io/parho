Donald Trump Jr. flew into Gibraltar and met at the local law firm Hassans to pitch his investment fund 1789 Capital. The visit came after the Trump family’s business earnings spiked since the president returned to office, sparking concerns about conflicts of interest. The fund focuses on tech, AI, and other high‑profile ventures, and has already grown to more than $1 billion. Gibraltar’s own political drama adds to the scrutiny. The meeting happened while Gibraltar’s chief minister faces a security probe.
