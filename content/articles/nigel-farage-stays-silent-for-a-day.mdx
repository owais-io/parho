In a rare moment of quiet, former UKIP leader Nigel Farage did not give his usual press briefing, letting the headlines go to other politicians. Conservative MP Kemi Badenoch used the slot to discuss the rape‑gangs inquiry, calling for cross‑party action but also blaming opposition parties. Meanwhile, Labour leader Keir Starmer launched a Substack newsletter to reach younger voters, signaling a new digital strategy.
