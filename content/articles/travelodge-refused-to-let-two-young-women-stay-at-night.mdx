In Cambridge, two 22‑year‑old women were told no one could stay at a Travelodge because staff insisted they were under 18, even though the girls had valid IDs. The hotel refused passports and student cards, leaving the guests without a place to sleep at nearly 10 p.m. The parents complained, but the hotel gave only a brief apology and a refund. The story highlights unclear ID rules and poor customer care.
