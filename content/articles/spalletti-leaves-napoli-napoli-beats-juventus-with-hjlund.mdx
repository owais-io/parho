After leaving Napoli, coach Luciano Spalletti joined Juventus and faced his former club at Stadio Maradona. He was booed by Napoli fans, but the team won 2‑1 thanks to a goal from Rasmus Højlund and a superb cross from David Neres. Napoli’s new 3‑4‑3 line‑up and key player Neres have revived the side, which now tops the league. Spalletti said he’d keep building on his past successes in this match.
