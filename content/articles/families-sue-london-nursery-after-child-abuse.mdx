Forty‑six families whose children were abused by paedophile Vincent Chan are suing the former Bright Horizons nursery on Finchley Road, north London. They say the nursery repeatedly ignored warnings about the teacher, who molested girls aged 2‑4. The families want the nursery and Camden council held accountable and are seeking a review of child‑safeguarding practices. Chan faces a February court hearing on 56 charges.
