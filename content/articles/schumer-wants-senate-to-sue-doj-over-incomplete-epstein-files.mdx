Schumer says the Senate will sue the Justice Department for not giving the public all the documents about Jeffrey Epstein. The law passed last month says the department must hand over every file by December 19, but it only released some of them. The partial dump has upset lawmakers, victims, and even the Clintons. Congress can pressure the department, but real enforcement may need a lawsuit or even impeachment.
