In a high‑court case, the Daily Mail publisher, Associated Newspapers Ltd, is said to have lost thousands of invoices showing payments to private investigators. The claimants – including Prince Harry, Elton John and Doreen Lawrence – say the missing records prove the paper used unlawful techniques. Lawyers found a small box of documents by accident, but the publisher claims it has turned over 2,700 records. The judge must decide how much of the hidden evidence can be used.
