A father and son are accused of using legally obtained guns to carry out a deadly shooting at Bondi Beach, killing 15 people and wounding dozens more. The 24‑year‑old son was arrested at the scene and hospitalized, while his 50‑year‑old father was shot dead by police. Australian officials say the pair were not part of a wider terrorist network, but the incident has sparked talks about tightening gun laws.
