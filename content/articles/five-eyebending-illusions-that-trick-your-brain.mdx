French‑American inventor Olivier Redon shows five eye‑bending pictures that trick the brain. The first looks like a can, but it’s actually the inside of an upside‑down can. The second is a red box made from two X‑shaped cardboard pieces that flips your view. Two dinosaur photos are taken from different angles to make them seem the same size. The fifth is a transparent‑sided cube that hides the real faces. These simple setups turn ordinary photos into playful puzzles.
