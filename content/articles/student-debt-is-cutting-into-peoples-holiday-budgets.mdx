A new survey shows 40% of borrowers can’t even cover basic things because of student loans, and 52% can’t save for retirement. Ben L., a biotech worker with $95,000 in private debt, pays $1,850 a month and can’t afford a vacation or gifts. The Trump administration is ending the Save income‑based plan, forcing many borrowers to higher payments. Erin O., a nonprofit worker, may see her monthly cost double, making holiday spending even tighter.
