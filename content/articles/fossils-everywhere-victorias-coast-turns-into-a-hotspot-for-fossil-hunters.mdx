Scientists and beach‑goers alike are finding fossils along Victoria’s coast, from ancient whale bones to tiny shark teeth. The Jan Juc beach has produced new species of early whales and large sharks, while other spots near Melbourne and the Bass Coast offer dinosaur tracks and plant fossils. Local experts say anyone can join in by watching for odd rocks, taking photos, and reporting finds. Fossil hunting is a fun, easy way to learn about Earth’s past.
