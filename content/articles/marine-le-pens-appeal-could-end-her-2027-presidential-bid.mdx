Marine Le Pen’s lawyers say she never ran a scheme to steal EU funds, but an appeal court will decide if she can run in France’s 2027 election. Prosecutors want the five‑year ban to stay, arguing she orchestrated a big embezzlement scheme from 2004‑2016. The verdict, due July 7, will tell if she can run again or if her 30‑year‑old deputy will take her place.
