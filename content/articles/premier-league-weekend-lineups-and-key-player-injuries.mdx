Premier League teams are preparing for Saturday and Sunday matches with several key absences. Aston Villa and Nottingham Forest are missing several squad members, while Brighton, Burnley, Wolves, West Ham, Bournemouth, Arsenal, Leeds, and Manchester United each have multiple injuries or suspensions that could change their starting XI. The lineups feature notable gaps such as Wolves missing S Bueno, West Ham missing Todibo, and Man United with a thigh injury to Maguire. Fans should watch for final squads before kickoff.
