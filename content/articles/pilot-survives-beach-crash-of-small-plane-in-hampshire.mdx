A light aircraft crashed into the sea off Lee‑on‑the‑Solent, Hampshire, this Saturday, but the pilot escaped on his own and was later checked by ambulance and coastguard teams. Firefighters and police worked to pull the plane out of the water, and the coastguard said the pilot was the only person on board. Witnesses saw the plane hit the rocks and the pilot run onto the beach. The rescue teams were quick and calm.
