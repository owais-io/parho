Vice‑president J.D. Vance angrily defended an ICE officer who shot 37‑year‑old poet Renee Good in Minneapolis. He blamed the press for misreporting the incident, saying Good was reckless and part of a left‑wing network. Vance called the coverage “disgrace” and said it endangers law‑enforcement officers. He also touched on other topics like Venezuela and Greenland, but the main focus was the media’s handling of the Good shooting.
