The Metropolitan Police have opened a criminal inquiry into former business secretary Peter Mandelson after documents from the Jeffrey Epstein files suggest he may have shared confidential government information with the jailed sex offender. The case focuses on potential misconduct in public office and possible market‑abuse offences, as well as claims that he received money from Epstein. The investigation follows warnings from the Cabinet Office and former prime minister Gordon Brown, who called the alleged disclosures “unpatriotic.”
