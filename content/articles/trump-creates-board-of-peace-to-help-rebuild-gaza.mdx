Trump has set up a seven‑member “Board of Peace” to steer Gaza’s reconstruction after the cease‑fire with Hamas. He will chair the group, which includes US officials, former UK prime minister Tony Blair, Jared Kushner and World Bank chief Ajay Banga. They’ll focus on governance, rebuilding homes, attracting investment and raising money. Critics say the board’s name clashes with Trump’s past actions, and Israel has already demolished many buildings in the area it still controls.
