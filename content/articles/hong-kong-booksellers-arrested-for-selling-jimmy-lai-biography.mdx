Police in Hong Kong detained a bookstore owner and three workers because they sold a biography of jailed media figure Jimmy Lai. The book, titled *The Troublemaker*, was written by a former Apple Daily director. Authorities say the book is “seditious” under the city’s national security law. The arrests highlight growing restrictions on free speech and the crackdown on media in Hong Kong. The bookstore has closed for a day, and no charges have yet been announced.
