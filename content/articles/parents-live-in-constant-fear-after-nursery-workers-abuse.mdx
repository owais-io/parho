Parents of one of Vincent Chan’s victims say they live in constant fear over the long‑term damage to their child. Chan, a former nursery worker, was given 18 years in prison after pleading guilty to 56 charges, including taking indecent pictures of children and spying on adults. The judge called his acts “wicked and perverse.” Parents feel no responsibility and rely on NSPCC helplines for support.
