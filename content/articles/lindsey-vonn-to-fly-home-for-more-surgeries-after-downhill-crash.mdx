Lindsey Vonn, 41, crashed in the Olympic downhill and broke her left tibia. She will fly back to the U.S. for further surgery after having two operations in Italy. Vonn also has a torn ACL and a titanium knee replacement, but she says she has no regrets about taking the risk. Fans and teammates support her decision and hope she recovers quickly.
