Manchester United beat Tottenham 2-0 at Old Trafford. Bruno Fernandes scored in the 81st minute, and Michael Mbeumo added a second goal. Tottenham’s manager Thomas Frank suffered another defeat in his fourth straight league loss, and defender Cristian Romero was sent off early in the match. United dominated the game, keeping their four‑game winning run alive.
