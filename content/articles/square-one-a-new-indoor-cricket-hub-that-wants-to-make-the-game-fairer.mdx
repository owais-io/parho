Square One is a huge indoor cricket centre built in an old tennis hangar near a state school. It uses colour‑coded nets and micro‑chipped balls to give kids a clear, data‑driven way to learn and improve. The team—former players, coaches, scientists and entrepreneurs—aims to open 150 similar centres across England to break down barriers like racism, sexism and cost, and help more kids discover cricket.
