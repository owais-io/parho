Dubai’s usual calm was shaken when Iranian missiles and drones struck the city, hitting the Fairmont Palm, airports, the Jebel Ali port and even the Burj Al Arab. About 137 missiles and 209 drones were launched, most intercepted by UAE air defences. Fires, smoke and a few casualties followed, but authorities quickly told residents the situation was under control and life could resume. Many tourists and influencers posted that Dubai was still safe, even as airports stayed closed.
