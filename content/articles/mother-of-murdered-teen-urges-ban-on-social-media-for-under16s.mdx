The mother of a murdered teen, Brianna Ghey, has asked Prime Minister Keir Starmer to ban social media for kids under 16. She says her daughter’s eating disorder and self‑harm were worsened by TikTok content and that online addiction made her vulnerable. A group of bereaved parents wants a parliamentary vote on the ban, while the government considers different safety options.
