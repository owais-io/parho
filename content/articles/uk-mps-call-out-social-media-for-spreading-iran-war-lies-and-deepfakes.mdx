UK MPs slammed big tech like X, TikTok and Meta for spreading Iran war misinformation, political deepfakes and AI‑made nudity of minors. They said the companies claim to do a lot but it makes no difference. MPs called for stricter rules on child access, identity theft and faster platform action. The hearing ended with a warning that unchecked online harms could disrupt the upcoming election.
