A 34‑year‑old lawyer from New York, Joshua Nass, who helped get a Trump pardon for a businessman, has been charged with trying to extort $500,000 from a former client and that client’s son. Prosecutors say he threatened a hired hitman to beat the son or threaten his family, and even offered money to the thug. Nass was arrested, released on a $5 million bond and faces up to 20 years in prison.
