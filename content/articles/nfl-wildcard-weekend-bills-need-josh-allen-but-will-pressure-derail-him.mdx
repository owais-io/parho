This preview covers the NFL wildcard weekend’s seven games. It explains what each team needs to win and names key players to watch. The Rams must defend tightly against the Panthers. The Bills need to protect Josh Allen from a strong Jaguars defense. The 49ers want to run the ball against the Eagles. The Chargers will face a hard‑hitting Patriots line, while the Texans and Steelers will battle in the red zone. Predictions favor the Jaguars, Eagles, Patriots and Steelers.
