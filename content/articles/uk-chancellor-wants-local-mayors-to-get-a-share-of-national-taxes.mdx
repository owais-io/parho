Rachel Reeves, the UK chancellor, said the Treasury will draft plans to give England’s mayors a cut of national tax revenue, starting with income tax. She also announced £2.3 bn in city investment funds for regional leaders and said they could keep future business‑rate income. The aim is to give local governments more money and decision‑making power to boost regional growth.
