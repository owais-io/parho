Close Brothers, a UK banking group, plans to cut around 600 jobs and roll out AI quickly to save costs. The cuts, about a quarter of its staff, will happen over 18 months as the firm faces losses and a huge £300 m compensation bill from a car‑loan scandal. Shares fell sharply after a short‑seller report, and investors remain skeptical as the bank tightens its balance sheet.
