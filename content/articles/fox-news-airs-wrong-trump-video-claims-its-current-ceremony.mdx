Fox News mistakenly showed a December clip of President Trump at a ceremony where he wore a golf hat, claiming it was from Saturday's Dover Air Force Base event where six soldiers who died in Kuwait were honored. The network later admitted the mistake. This incident, repeated on multiple programs, raised concerns about accuracy and editorial oversight in cable news coverage.
