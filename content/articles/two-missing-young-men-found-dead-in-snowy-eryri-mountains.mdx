In early February, police launched a big search in Wales’ Snowdonia region after two 19‑ and 20‑year‑old men went missing. Mountain‑rescue teams worked through snow and ice on Snowdon’s slopes all night. By Thursday morning, they found both bodies on the Eryri range. Families were notified, and the coroner has been informed. The operation showed how difficult it is to locate people in winter mountain conditions.
