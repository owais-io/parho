John Lohan, a 59‑year‑old firefighter with 30 years of service, was killed when a tree fell on him during a bushfire in Nerong, NSW. The fire also destroyed 16 homes in Koolewong, sparking an arson investigation by the police and forensic teams. Fire crews are still working to control the blaze and evacuate nearby residents, while air quality remains poor in the region.
