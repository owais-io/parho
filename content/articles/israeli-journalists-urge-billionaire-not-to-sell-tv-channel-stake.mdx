Israeli journalists fear losing independent TV coverage after Sir Leonard Blavatnik offers to sell a 15% stake of Channel 13 to telecom owner Patrick Drahi. They worry Drahi would control the channel and curb criticism of the government, similar to what happened at the Washington Post under Jeff Bezos. A rival tech group has offered more money, but the sale is still moving forward. Journalists hope Blavatnik changes his mind.
