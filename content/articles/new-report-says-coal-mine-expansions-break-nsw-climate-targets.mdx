The NSW Net Zero Commission has warned that approving new coal mine expansions would violate the state’s legal emissions goals. The report highlights that coal mining is the biggest climate problem in NSW and that demand for coal is falling. It calls for a stop to new approvals, better planning that looks at all emissions, and help for workers to switch jobs. Industry leaders argue the findings are flawed and fear job losses.
