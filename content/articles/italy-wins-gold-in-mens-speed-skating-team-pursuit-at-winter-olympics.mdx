On the ice in Cortina, Italy’s speed‑skating trio – Davide Ghiotto, Andrea Giovannini and Michele Malfatti – beat the United States by more than four seconds to win the men’s team pursuit. The win gave Italy its first gold in the event since 2006 and thrilled the home crowd. French skiers also surprised everyone, taking gold in the men’s biathlon relay after a comeback. Heavy snow forced the postponement of some snowboarding events, and the medal table has Italy in second place.
