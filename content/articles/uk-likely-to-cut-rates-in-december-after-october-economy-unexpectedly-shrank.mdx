Card Factory warned that sales are below expectations, cutting its profit forecast and its shares fell 26%. Meanwhile the UK economy shrank 0.1% in October, the first quarterly fall since December 2023. The Office for National Statistics blamed budget uncertainty and weak consumer confidence. With growth weak, the Bank of England is expected to cut the interest rate to 3.75 % in December, a move seen as “nailed on.”
