Tech stocks crashed, especially Nvidia, pulling global markets down. Investors worried about China’s weak investment numbers and a big Chinese economy slowdown. In the UK, the pound fell after the chancellor said there’d be no income‑tax hike. US markets dipped too, while traders eyed whether the Fed will cut rates next month. The result was a shaky week for stocks, bonds and the pound.
