Iran's top leader, Ayatollah Khamenei, said thousands of protestors were killed, calling some deaths “inhuman” and blaming the United States. He demanded harsh punishment for the demonstrators and showed footage he said proved foreign support. Human rights groups report over 3,000 people have died and more than 22,000 were arrested. The protests began in late December over the falling rial, spread to demands for the government’s end, and are still met with a brutal crackdown.
