Dr Ghassan Abu‑Sittah, a surgeon cleared by the Medical Practitioners Tribunal Service of antisemitism and supporting terrorism, says the General Medical Council (GMC) is seeking a “politically acceptable” decision. After the GMC appealed the tribunal’s ruling, he is now raising funds to defend himself again. Abu‑Sittah claims the appeal is driven by political pressure and unfairly targets his professional reputation.
