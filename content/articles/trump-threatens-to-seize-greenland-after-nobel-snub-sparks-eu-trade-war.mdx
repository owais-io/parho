Donald Trump sent a text to Norway’s prime minister saying he was angry at being snubbed for the Nobel Peace Prize and that he now wants full control of Greenland. He threatened to raise tariffs on several EU countries and said he could even use force to take the island. The move has pushed the EU and NATO into a standoff, and Brussels is meeting to decide how to respond.
