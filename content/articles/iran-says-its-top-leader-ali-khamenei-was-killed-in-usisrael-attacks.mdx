US and Israel launched a series of missile attacks on Iran. State media said the supreme leader, Ali Khamenei, was killed along with members of his family. The strikes also hit Iranian missile sites, the Revolutionary Guard and nuclear facilities. Iran says the attacks will trigger a new phase of war and vows retaliation. The move has escalated tensions across the Middle East and drawn global diplomatic calls for restraint.
