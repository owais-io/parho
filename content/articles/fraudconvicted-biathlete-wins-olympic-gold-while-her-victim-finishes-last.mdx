Julia Simon, who was fined for using her teammate’s credit card, won gold in the 15km biathlon at the Paris Games. Her victim, Justine Braisaz‑Bouchet, ended up 80th in the same race. Simon was suspended for six months but still competed, focusing only on her win. The case highlights growing ethics problems in French biathlon.
