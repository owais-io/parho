Ben Affleck sold his AI post‑production company, InterPositive, to Netflix for an undisclosed amount. The tool uses a film’s own footage to fix shots, correct lighting, and remove wires, rather than creating new scenes. Affleck, who once doubted AI could write films, now says it’s a useful innovation. Netflix plans to use the tech to boost creative freedom and hires Affleck as a senior advisor.
