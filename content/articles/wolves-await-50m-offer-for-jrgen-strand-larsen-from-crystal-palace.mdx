Wolves are ready for a £50m bid from Crystal Palace for striker Jørgen Strand Larsen. Palace is in advanced talks and may offer £45m plus add‑ons. Wolves’ agent, Jorge Mendes, has seen higher offers, but the club wants the best deal. The 25‑year‑old, who joined Wolves for £23m and now has a contract until 2030, could leave for the right price. Other clubs, like Leeds, also eye him.
