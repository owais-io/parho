The chief of Woro village in western Nigeria says gunmen stormed his home, killing two of his sons, burning shops and homes, and taking his second wife and three daughters. The attack lasted from 5 p.m. to 3 a.m., leaving 162 dead and many injured. Nigeria’s president condemned the raid, sent troops, and blamed Boko Haram and other jihadists. The incident is part of a wider surge of violence and kidnappings across the country.
