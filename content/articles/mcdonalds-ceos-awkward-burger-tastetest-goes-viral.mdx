Chris Kempczinski, the head of McDonald’s, posted a video tasting the new Big Arch burger. His shy, overly formal demo—calling the sandwich a “product” and taking a tiny bite—made many viewers laugh. The clip was shared online, sparking jokes about how awkward he looked. The Big Arch burger will roll out nationwide on March 3.
