Prime Minister Anthony Albanese spoke on the Karl Stefanovic Show, saying One Nation politics are driven by mining billionaire Gina Rinehart, not working‑class Australians. He slammed Senator David Pocock as a “grievance promoter” and criticized the party’s anti‑worker policies, like opposing wage rises and penalty‑rate laws. Albanese also rejected harsh anti‑immigration comments made by other guests and defended Australia’s non‑discriminatory immigration policy. The podcast was marred by a brief antisemitic comment thread that was later deleted.
