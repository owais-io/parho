Israel has shut all crossings into Gaza, cutting off food shipments. The move follows an attack on Iran and has left the strip with only a week’s worth of fresh food. Prices for basics like flour and sugar have tripled, sparking panic buying. Humanitarian kitchens are running low, and aid is only gradually being allowed back in. Palestinians fear a return to famine while families struggle to afford essentials.
