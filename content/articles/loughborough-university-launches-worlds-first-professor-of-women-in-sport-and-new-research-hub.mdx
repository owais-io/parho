Loughborough University has set up a new Women in Sport hub and just appointed the world’s first professor for the field. The hub brings together scientists, coaches, athletes and businesses to study and improve women’s sports from grassroots to the pros. Researchers are looking at health, coaching jobs, pregnancy exercise and kids’ play gaps. The goal is to give women better support, gear, jobs and confidence in sport.
