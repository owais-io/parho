Oil fell while stocks in Asia and Europe climbed after U.S. President Donald Trump sent a 15‑point peace plan to Iran, boosting hopes of a Middle East ceasefire. Iran also said it would allow “non‑hostile” ships through the Strait of Hormuz, easing fears that the vital oil lane had been closed. The news lifted markets, but oil prices swung back up as talks stalled.
