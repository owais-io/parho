Baba Vanga was a Bulgarian mystic who lived in the 20th century. Today, many people claim she predicted 9/11, Covid‑19, and even a future world war. In reality, she never said these things. Her name is now a tool for click‑bait and for spreading pro‑Russian stories. The Bulgarian foundation that protects her legacy says most of the so‑called prophecies were made up or stretched to fit political agendas.
