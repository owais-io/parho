Manchester City won 5‑0 against Newcastle at the Etihad, securing a place in the League Cup final at Wembley. Omar Marmoush scored twice and Tijjani Reijnders added another, while Anthony Elanga gave Newcastle a consolation goal. City’s manager Pep Guardiola made several changes and used a 4‑2‑2‑2 formation, exploiting Newcastle’s weak back line. The win means City will face Arsenal in the final.
