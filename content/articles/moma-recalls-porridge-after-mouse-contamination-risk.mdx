Several of Moma’s porridge pots and sachets have been recalled because a possible mouse contamination was found at the company’s factory. The brand is asking customers not to eat the affected products and to return them to the shop for a full refund. The recall covers seven pot flavours and two sachet flavours, and the Food Standards Agency has issued a warning. Only the listed items are affected.
