On Wednesday, Iran launched new attacks on Gulf shipping lanes and Dubai’s airport, while U.S. and Israeli warplanes struck Iranian sites. The fighting has spread across the Middle East, with Israeli bombs hitting Hezbollah targets in Lebanon and Iranian rockets hitting Israel. The conflict threatens global oil flows, as Iran blocks the Strait of Hormuz and calls for higher prices. Thousands of Lebanese people have fled the fighting, and the war has killed many civilians and military personnel.
