A Labor MP and an Animal Justice MP announced the birth of their daughter, Lilah Poppy Purcell, who arrived a bit early at 37 weeks. The name mixes their party colours – Lilah (purple lilac) for Animal Justice and Poppy (red) for Labor. Both MPs said the baby is a reminder of why they fight for a kinder future. Purcell will pause work until February and then run again in the 2026 election.
