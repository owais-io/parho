China carried out the death penalty on 11 men who were part of Chinese criminal groups operating scam centers in Myanmar. The executions followed sentences in Wenzhou for crimes like murder, fraud, and running illegal casinos. Beijing has tightened cooperation with Southeast Asian countries to shut down these scam hubs, which use trafficked workers for romance‑based internet scams worth billions each year. The UN has warned the industry could generate up to $40 billion annually.
