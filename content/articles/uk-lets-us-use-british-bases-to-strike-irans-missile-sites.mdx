The UK has agreed to let the US use two of its bases to carry out defensive strikes against Iranian missile sites. Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Iran’s attacks risk British people abroad, so the UK will help by allowing US jets to strike missile launchers. The move comes after a suspected drone hit on a UK base in Cyprus. Some politicians say it breaches international law, and the UK has not yet formally condemned the US action.
