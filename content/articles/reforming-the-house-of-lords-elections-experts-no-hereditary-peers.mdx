The letter proposes a new House of Lords that mixes indirect elections by local councillors, temporary seats for national leaders and regional mayors, and appointed experts. It would drop hereditary peers and bishops, keep only a few elected members, and have an independent commission to vet appointments. The aim is to create a modern, legitimate upper chamber that supports local politics and better reflects the UK’s devolved system.
