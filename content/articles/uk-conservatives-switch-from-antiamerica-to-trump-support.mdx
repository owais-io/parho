After WWII, Britain saw itself as a senior partner to the US but later many conservatives disliked America. Today the UK right has turned around. They love Trump, copy American culture‑war tactics, and even welcome US help to oust leaders like Keir Starmer. Big names like Musk and Trump see Britain as a key spot for global finance, so UK conservatives give up their independence and embrace a Trump‑style alliance.
