Midwife Donna Ockenden, who is running the biggest NHS maternity review in history, bills NHS England up to £26,000 a month for her advice. She charges £850 a day for 7½ hours of work. The company’s invoices can go above £300,000 when extra costs like staff, office space and insurance are added. Ockenden says the fees cover her long hours and the review will finish in June 2026.
