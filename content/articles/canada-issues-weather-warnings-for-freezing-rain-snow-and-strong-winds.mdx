Atlantic Canada is under a winter storm that brings heavy snow, freezing rain and strong winds. Newfoundland saw up to 40 cm of snow and 74‑mph gusts, with blizzard‑like conditions lasting until Tuesday. Nova Scotia and parts of New Brunswick face freezing rain and up to 25 cm of snow, causing power outages, falling branches and hazardous roads. In Australia, Cyclone Koji produced heavy rain, wind and flooding, while a record heatwave hit New South Wales, reaching 43 °C.
