Zelenskyy says the US and Ukraine are close to finishing a peace plan that will be sent to Russia in the next few days. He told reporters that the U.S. will vote on security guarantees for Ukraine, but Russia has not yet seen the documents. Russia also says it will not allow NATO troops in Ukraine. If Russia rejects the plan, Ukraine will ask the U.S. for more weapons and sanctions.
