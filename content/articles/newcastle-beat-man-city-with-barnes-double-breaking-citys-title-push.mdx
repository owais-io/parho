Newcastle’s win over Manchester City lifted them from 15th to 10th in the league. Harvey Barnes scored twice, and the team kept a solid shape, stopping Erling Haaland and stopping City’s rhythm. Manager Eddie Howe celebrated a turning point against Pep Guardiola, who saw his side drop points after a shaky first half. The victory keeps Newcastle on the rise and puts a dent in City’s title hopes.
