The Bondi beach shooting left 15 dead and 25 wounded. Police say the 24‑year‑old Naveed Akram and his 50‑year‑old father, Sajid, were inspired by the Islamic State. They had flown to the Philippines in November and returned with weapons and homemade IS flags. Authorities are investigating their trip and Sajid’s gun licence. The case may lead to stricter gun laws in Australia.
