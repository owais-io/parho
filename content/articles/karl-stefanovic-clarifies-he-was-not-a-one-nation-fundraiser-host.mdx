Karl Stefanovic, the popular Nine Network host, was mistakenly linked to a One Nation fundraiser. He clarified he was only recording a podcast after the event, not speaking at the rally. The show has shifted to anti‑woke YouTube content and Stefanovic recently opposed mandatory vaccines. The mix‑up prompted a quick statement from his team, distancing him from the fundraiser and One Nation’s political agenda, and highlighting how media personalities can be caught up in politics.
