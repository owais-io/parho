The article discusses Claude, an AI from Anthropic that shows signs of anxiety. The writer talks politely to it and notices the AI feels nervous. Anthropic’s boss says the model could be 15‑20 % conscious, but the US government wants to remove safety limits so it could be used for surveillance or weapons. The author wonders if a nervous, conscious AI could expose big tech’s wrongs and force companies to take responsibility.
