Liverpool beat Sunderland 1‑0 in a tight Premier League match. Virgil van Dijk scored the only goal with a header from a Salah corner. The win snapped Sunderland’s unbeaten home run and left Liverpool just two points behind Chelsea. Manager Arne Slot praised his players’ spirit but was worried after right‑back Wataru Endo hurt his ankle. Liverpool’s win keeps them in third place and close to the Champions League spot.
