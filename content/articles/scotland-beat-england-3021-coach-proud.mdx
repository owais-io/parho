Scotland beat England 30‑21 in the Calcutta Cup, giving fans something to smile about for the next year. Coach Gregor Townsend praised the team’s best‑ever first‑quarter play and said the victory will be remembered under his guidance. England’s coach was disappointed after a red card and a slow start. Scotland will next play Wales with some injured players ahead of the next test.
