Peter Mandelson was taken into custody on Monday because detectives thought he was going to leave the UK for the British Virgin Islands. He says the arrest was based on a false claim and that he had been told he could just be interviewed later. After being released on bail, he and his lawyers demanded evidence that justified the arrest. The case also recalls his earlier sacking as ambassador and the government's review of his ties to Jeffrey Epstein.
