Nancy Guthrie, 84, vanished from her Tucson home on Feb. 1. No ransom has been paid and no suspect is named. The FBI raised its reward to $100,000 and released a sketch of a man 5‑10 ft tall with a black backpack. Police have sifted through thousands of tips and found shaky CCTV footage of a masked figure. Rumors of fake ransom emails and drug‑cartel links have surfaced, but the case remains unsolved.
