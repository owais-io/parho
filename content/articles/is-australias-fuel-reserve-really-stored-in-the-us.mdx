A lot of AI tools say Australia keeps a big fuel reserve in the U.S., but that’s not right. The real reserve is in Australia and is called the Minimum Stockholding Obligation (MSO). It keeps enough petrol, diesel and jet fuel on hand for 20‑27 days. The government can lower that requirement to sell more fuel, especially to regional areas that have run short. The US storage was tiny and not a strategic reserve.
