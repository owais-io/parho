Storm Goretti has knocked out roads, trains and flights across the UK and parts of Europe. There are six weather warnings—five yellow and one amber—outstanding. The Met Office says it’s a “weather bomb,” with up to 30 cm of snow in Wales and the Midlands and wind up to 100 mph on hills and coastlines. We’d love to hear how it’s affected you, but keep safety first and share photos if you’re comfortable.
