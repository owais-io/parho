Trump has set up a Gaza peace board to steer reconstruction and governance after the war. Chaired by the president, the board includes former British prime minister Tony Blair, US senator Marco Rubio, special adviser Jared Kushner, and others such as a World Bank chief and a property developer. The group will handle reconstruction, investment, and security while a Palestinian technocratic committee runs day‑to‑day operations. The plan faces aid shortages, lingering violence, and disputes over Hamas’s role.
