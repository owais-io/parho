New York Mayor Zohran Mamdani reversed an earlier mayor’s rule that used the IHRA definition of antisemitism and banned certain boycotts of Israel. Israel’s foreign ministry said this move was “antisemitic gasoline on an open fire.” Mamdani said he would protect Jewish New Yorkers and fight all hate, while some groups praised the change and others worry it weakens anti‑antisemitism measures. He also promised to keep an office to fight hate.
