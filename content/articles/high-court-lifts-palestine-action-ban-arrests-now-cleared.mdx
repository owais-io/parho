UK judges decided that the ban on Palestine Action was unlawful, so the 3,000 people who were arrested for showing support are now technically cleared. Many of those arrested feel vindicated, but the government will appeal the decision, so their trials could still go ahead. Police will stop detaining people for protests, but will keep collecting evidence. People are worried about what will happen next, while they also comment on the Gaza crisis.
