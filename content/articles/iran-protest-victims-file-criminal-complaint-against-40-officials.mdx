A group of people hurt during Iran’s 2022 Women, Life, Freedom protests has filed a criminal complaint in Argentina against 40 Iranian officials. The complaint says Iran’s security forces used live ammo, paintballs, and metal pellets, carried out mass arrests, torture, and even executions. Victims like Minoo Majidi, who was shot in the back, and Kowsar Eftekhari, blinded by paintballs, seek justice and truth. Argentina’s courts can investigate crimes worldwide, so the case may lead to real action.
