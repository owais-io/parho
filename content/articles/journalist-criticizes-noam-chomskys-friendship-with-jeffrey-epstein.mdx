Vijay Prashad, a journalist who co‑authored books with Noam Chomsky, wrote a public letter saying he is “heartsick” over new documents that show the 97‑year‑old linguist was friends with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. The letter explains Prashad was unaware of the friendship and is upset that Chomsky has said little about the relationship. Chomsky has not replied, and his age makes it hard for him to explain himself.
