Greenland's PM says they choose Denmark over the US as Trump pushes to take the Arctic island, sparking a crisis. Denmark, the EU and NATO refuse to let Washington buy Greenland, calling the move illegal and threatening the alliance. Greenland’s residents feel uneasy while leaders meet at the White House to discuss the future. The island’s self‑governance and mineral wealth make it a hotspot for global power plays.
