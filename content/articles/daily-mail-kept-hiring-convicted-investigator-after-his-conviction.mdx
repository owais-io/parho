Steve Whittamore, a private investigator jailed for illegally gathering personal data, claims the Daily Mail and Mail on Sunday kept hiring him after his 2005 conviction. He says he supplied criminal records, phone details and other secrets to the papers. Seven people, including Prince Harry, are suing Associated Newspapers Ltd for the same unlawful work. The papers deny wrongdoing, and the former Mail editor Paul Dacre said he banned investigators in 2007. The court hearing is ongoing.
