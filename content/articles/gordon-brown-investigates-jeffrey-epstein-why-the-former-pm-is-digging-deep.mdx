Gordon Brown, the former British prime minister, has been digging into the Jeffrey Epstein scandal, especially the links between Epstein and former deputy prime minister Peter Mandelson. He wrote a Guardian article calling the revelations “hard to find words” for, and has shared a five‑page memo with police about Epstein’s flights from UK airports. Brown is pushing authorities to investigate how the UK may have enabled Epstein’s abuse, and he plans to keep the pressure on.
