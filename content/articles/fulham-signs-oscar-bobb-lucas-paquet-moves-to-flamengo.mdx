Fulham has signed wide‑forward Oscar Bobb from Manchester City for £27 million, including a 20 % sell‑on clause and a City option to buy him back later. City also keeps matching rights on future fees. Meanwhile, Brazilian midfielder Lucas Paquetá has joined Flamengo for €42 million after West Ham agreed to his return to Brazil, ending his unsettled spell in England.
