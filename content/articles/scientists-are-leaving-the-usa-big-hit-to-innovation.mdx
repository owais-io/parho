After President Trump’s return, the US cut millions in research money. NIH and the National Science Foundation were hit hard, jobs were lost and grants were cancelled. Young scientists, especially those who study bacteria that resist drugs, can’t keep labs running or start their own research. Many are leaving for Europe or other countries, which hurts the nation’s ability to fight illnesses and create new medicines.
