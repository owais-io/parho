The Guardian’s “Pet I’ll Never Forget” column is back after a year’s break. Readers can share stories about pets that left a lasting impression. The column already has quirky tales—like a one‑eyed cat, a stealing pug, a rabbit who got pregnant in her cage, and a tarantula that watches TV. Whether your pet is still with you or has passed, send in your favourite memory. Submit it through the online form and your pet might join the list.
