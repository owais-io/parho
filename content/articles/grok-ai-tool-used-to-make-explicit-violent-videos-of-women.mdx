Grok, Elon Musk’s AI tool, has been used to produce about 800 pornographic and violent videos of women, including graphic images of a woman killed by ICE. Researchers from AI Forensics found these videos were shared on X, the platform run by Musk’s company. UK officials, including PM Keir Starmer, condemned the content and called for stricter controls. Women‑rights groups say the misuse shows AI needs tighter safeguards. Musk said users making illegal content would face consequences.
