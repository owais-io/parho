Manchester United are looking at former player Ole Gunnar Solskjær to become the team’s temporary boss after the previous manager was sacked. Director of football Jason Wilcox and CEO Omar Berrada say Solskjær could help, and key players like Bruno Fernandes would welcome him. Solskjær was successful as caretaker in 2018‑19 and hopes to stay on permanently if the stint works. Other names, such as ex‑United midfielder Michael Carrick and current interim coach Darren Fletcher, are also in the mix.
