Donald Trump decided to launch a large air and missile strike against Iran, calling it Operation Epic Fury. He ordered the attack from Air Force One after a speech in Texas. The strike used U.S. and Israeli forces and hit many targets, including the Iranian Supreme Leader’s compound. Trump said this would give Iranians a chance to overthrow their government. The move was controversial and marked a shift from Trump’s earlier anti‑war stance.
