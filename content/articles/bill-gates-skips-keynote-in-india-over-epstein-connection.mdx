Bill Gates decided not to give his keynote at the AI Impact Summit in Delhi because of growing scrutiny over his past ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The billionaire, who is on the sidelines of the event, said he was worried the controversy would distract from the summit’s focus on AI for good. His foundation will still be represented, but the speech was cancelled just before it was to begin.
