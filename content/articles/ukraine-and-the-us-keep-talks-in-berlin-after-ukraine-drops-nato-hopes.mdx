After talks in Berlin, Ukrainian President Zelenskyy and U.S. officials will meet with European leaders such as Britain, Denmark, Finland, Norway, the Netherlands, Poland and Sweden. They say progress was made, but issues about Ukraine's eastern borders and security guarantees still need answers. Moscow has rejected these proposals. The EU will soon decide whether to use frozen Russian money to fund Ukraine. Czech Prime Minister Babiš also says no.
