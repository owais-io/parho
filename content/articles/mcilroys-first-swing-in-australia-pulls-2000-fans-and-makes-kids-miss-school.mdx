Rory McIlroy’s first swing in Australia drew a huge crowd, with 2,000 fans waiting at the start line. Some parents even let their kids skip school for the “special day.” The event marked his return to the Australian Open after a decade, and he was greeted by cheers and gasps as he hit his first tee shot. McIlroy said the tournament deserves a top spot on the calendar, and his presence is helping revive the Open’s popularity.
