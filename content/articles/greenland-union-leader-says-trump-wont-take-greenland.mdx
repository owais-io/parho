Jess Berthelsen, head of Greenland’s biggest trade‑union, says the country will never be taken over by the U.S. He rejects President Trump’s claim that Russian and Chinese ships are prowling Greenland’s waters and that the territory is a security risk. Greenland is a self‑governed part of Denmark, and most locals want independence. European leaders have backed Greenland, and the union says threats from Washington hurt cooperation and future plans.
