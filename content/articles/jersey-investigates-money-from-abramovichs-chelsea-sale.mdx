Jersey officials are checking if the £2.4 bn from Roman Abramovich’s 2022 sale of Chelsea FC is money from crime. The cash, frozen after UK sanctions over the Ukraine war, sits in a Barclays account. The investigation could affect how the money is released to Abramovich, who denies wrongdoing. Meanwhile, Chelsea’s new owners have a £150 m safety net against penalties if the club broke financial rules under Abramovich. The case adds another layer to the battle over the sale’s proceeds.
