President Trump has been giving his close aides and visitors a set of Florsheim dress shoes. He orders them after guessing their shoe size and pays out of his own pocket. The gifts go to senators, cabinet staff and TV personalities. While the shoes cost about $145 each, the gesture has sparked talk about favoritism and the administration’s lack of diversity. Trump’s shoe‑giving habit has become a quirky, regular event in the Oval Office.
