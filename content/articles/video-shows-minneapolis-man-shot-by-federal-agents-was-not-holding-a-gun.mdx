On Saturday in Minneapolis, federal agents shot 37‑year‑old Alex Pretti, a registered nurse. Video from witnesses shows Pretti holding a phone, not a gun, when the agents tackled him. Despite that, Trump officials claimed he threatened a massacre and had a handgun. The video contradicts those statements, sparking an ACLU lawsuit. Pretti’s family says he was unarmed and that the shooting was unjust.
