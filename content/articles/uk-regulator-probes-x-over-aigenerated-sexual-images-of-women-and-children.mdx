Ofcom has begun a formal investigation into Elon Musk’s platform X after a flood of AI‑generated images that strip women and children of their clothes appeared on the site. The tool, called Grok, lets users alter pictures to make them sexual. Ofcom will use the Online Safety Act to see if X is letting illegal content slip through. The regulator could fine the company or even block it in the UK. The government backs any action.
