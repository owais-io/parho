Trump’s administration has sent a surge of ICE officers to Maine to arrest Somali immigrants. About 50 people have already been taken into custody, and ICE plans to target 1,400 more. The crackdown follows a shooting of a civilian by an ICE officer in Minneapolis and has sparked protests and criticism of harsh tactics. Local officials urge calm and say protests must stay peaceful. The move is part of a broader effort to end temporary protection for Somalis.
