High living costs in the UK have stopped many families from putting money aside. A survey found 16 % of adults have no savings, and 39 % have less than £1,000. People are using emergency funds to pay bills and are even withdrawing from retirement accounts. Some can save only by cutting travel, food and extras. Others have small pensions but still worry about future security.
