Lithium‑ion batteries are now a serious fire hazard that fire chiefs say outpaces safety rules. A blaze in Glasgow’s Central Station, sparked by a vape shop’s battery, shows how easily the batteries can start fires. The number of battery‑related fires has jumped in Scotland and London, especially with e‑bikes and scooters. Poor disposal and lack of strict safety standards have caused costly fires at bin lorries and recycling plants. Experts call for tougher regulations and better public education.
