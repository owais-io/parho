Greg Squire, a former postman turned Homeland Security agent, spends years undercover on the dark web hunting child abusers. After seeing a brutal video in 2008, he and his team track forums with over a million users, rescuing victims like Lucy and stopping predators such as the “Lover Boy Only” case. The work drains him emotionally, but therapy and support help him cope. He urges everyone to stay vigilant.
