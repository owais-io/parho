WPP, a large UK advertising group, will sell assets and cut jobs to save £500m by 2028 and stay competitive with AI. CEO Cindy Rose says the company will simplify its structure, eliminate duplicate roles, and focus on AI projects. The plan follows falling revenue, profit, and the loss of its FTSE 100 spot, and aims to keep clients and drive future growth.
