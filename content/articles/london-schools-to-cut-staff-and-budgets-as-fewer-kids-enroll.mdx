A new report says London schools could lose £45m over four years because fewer children are enrolling. Both primary and secondary schools will see staff and programme cuts as funding is tied to pupil numbers. Inner‑London schools will feel the hit hardest, with year‑7 places falling 7.6% and reception 6.4%. The cuts could affect special‑needs support, GCSE options and extra‑curricular activities.
