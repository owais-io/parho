The T20 World Cup is getting faster than ever, with runs coming at a record‑breaking rate. India, who beat South Africa in last year’s final, are the clear favourites because they play every match at home and have an unbeatable batting line‑up. England and Australia are the main challengers, but India’s recent form and home advantage make them the hardest team to beat. Meanwhile, Bangladesh and Pakistan have left the tournament, adding to the drama.
