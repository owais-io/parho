Floods in the Northern Territory have forced the evacuation of towns like Nauiyu and Katherine. Residents are being warned not to enter flood waters because crocodiles are roaming everywhere, officials say. The Katherine and Daly Rivers are at record high levels, and crews used helicopters and boats to move people to higher ground. The government is offering financial help while the rain continues.
