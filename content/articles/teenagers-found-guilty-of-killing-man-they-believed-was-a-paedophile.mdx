Two teenagers were found guilty of killing a 49‑year‑old man in Kent. They had been texting the man, who used a fake name, and thought he was a paedophile. The teens lured him to a beach and threw rocks and a bottle at him, killing him. The court said the attack was not pre‑planned to kill but became violent. The teens will be sentenced in April.
