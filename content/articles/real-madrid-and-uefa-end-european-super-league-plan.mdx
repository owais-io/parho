UEFA and Real Madrid have decided to drop the European Super League idea. The two sides agreed on principles that keep club football fair, focus on long‑term stability, and improve fan experience with tech. The deal also settles legal disputes that began when Real Madrid sued UEFA for blocking the breakaway league. With the agreement in place, the Super League project is officially finished, and Real will return to normal European competitions.
