The US bombed a group in north‑west Nigeria called Lakurawa on Christmas Day, saying it was an Islamic State cell. Nigerian and US officials say several fighters were killed, but exact numbers are unclear. The attack happened in a rural area where other violent groups, like bandits, also operate. Some say Lakurawa is a symbolic target to give US president Trump a way to claim he is fighting jihadists that hurt Christians.
