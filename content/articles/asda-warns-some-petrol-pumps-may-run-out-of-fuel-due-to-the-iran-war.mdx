Asda’s boss says a few of its petrol stations might temporarily run short of fuel because prices have spiked after the Iran conflict. The rise in oil costs has made demand outpace supply, so motorists may see brief shortages when a delivery is delayed. The UK government may step in if prices keep climbing, but fuel suppliers say overall supply remains stable.
