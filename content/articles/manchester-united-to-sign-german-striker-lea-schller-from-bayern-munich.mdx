Manchester United is about to sign German striker Lea Schüller from Bayern Munich. The 28‑year‑old has scored over 100 goals for Bayern and 54 for Germany, and she has been a top scorer in the German league for seven straight seasons. Her move gives United a big boost in attack as the club also added Swedish right‑back Hanna Lundkvist and kept midfielder Hinata Miyazawa on a new deal.
