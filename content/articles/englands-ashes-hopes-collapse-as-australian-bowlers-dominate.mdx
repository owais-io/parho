After a tough second day in South Australia, England’s chances of winning the Ashes were basically gone. They were 158 runs behind with only 213 for 8 when Australia finished with 371 all‑out. Australian bowlers—especially Pat Cummins, Mitchell Starc, Scott Boland, Cameron Green and Nathan Lyon—raced through England’s lineup, using tight lines and movement. England’s hopes collapsed while the Snicko technology caused on‑field confusion and boos from fans.
