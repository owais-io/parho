After a US‑Israel strike on Tehran, Iran’s deputy foreign minister warned European countries that backing the attack could make them targets for Iranian retaliation. He said Iran will keep the Strait of Hormuz open but will hit any US or Israeli ships. The UN reports over 1,300 Iranian civilians have died, and the UN chief calls for urgent diplomacy. Meanwhile, oil prices have spiked above $90 a barrel, adding pressure on global inflation.
