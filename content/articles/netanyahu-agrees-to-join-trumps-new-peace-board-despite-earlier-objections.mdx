Israel’s leader Benjamin Netanyahu said he will join a US‑backed “board of peace” that President Trump wants to use to oversee conflicts worldwide, even though his office had earlier criticized the board’s makeup. The board, originally aimed at a Gaza ceasefire, is now being offered to dozens of countries. Some diplomats fear it could undermine the United Nations, and some nations, like Norway, have said they won’t join.
