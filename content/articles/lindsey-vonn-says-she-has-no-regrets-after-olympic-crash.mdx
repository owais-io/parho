Lindsey Vonn crashed in the women's downhill at the Olympics, breaking her tibia and needing more surgeries. She had already hurt her knee a week earlier. Vonn posted that she didn’t regret racing, saying it’s a risk sport and she was still aiming for a gold medal. Some fans worried she was too injured, but teammates and other skiers defended her choice. Her father wants her to retire.
