Cuba says the US seized the Skipper oil tanker near Venezuela’s coast is piracy and a violation of international law. The US says it’s stopping Venezuelan oil that would go to Cuba. The tanker had about 2 million barrels of Venezuelan crude, headed to Cuba, but after off‑loading 50,000 barrels it was taken by the US and sent to Texas. Venezuela also calls the action theft. The move is part of wider US sanctions and military build‑up in the Caribbean.
