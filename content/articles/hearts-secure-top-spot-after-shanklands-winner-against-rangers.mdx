Hearts beat Rangers 2‑1, with Lawrence Shankland scoring the late winner. The victory keeps Hearts at the top of the Scottish Premiership, nine points ahead of Rangers. Manager Derek McInnes said he is confident in his team, noting their defence and attacking play. Rangers’ Youssef Chermiti scored a 95th‑minute goal, but it was too late. The win shows Hearts are a serious title contender and the first club outside Celtic and Rangers to challenge for the league in decades.
