Australia’s disability funding scheme, the NDIS, is being reshaped to curb rising costs. New rules trim therapy and travel payments, cap funding periods, and introduce an online “robo‑planning” tool that could reduce personal oversight. Appeals will be limited, and a 2026 program will move younger kids with mild‑to‑moderate delays off the NDIS. Families like Bonnie’s fear losing the one‑to‑one care their loved ones rely on.
