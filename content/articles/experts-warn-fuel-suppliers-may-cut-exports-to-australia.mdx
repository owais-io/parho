Australia may face fuel shortages because Malaysia, South Korea and other countries might keep more oil for themselves. Energy minister Chris Bowen says supplies are still coming, but he warns that governments can ration fuel if things get worse. Some states, like New South Wales, are planning emergency plans and checking fuel stocks. Meanwhile, politicians are urging Australia to drill for its own oil to avoid future gaps.
