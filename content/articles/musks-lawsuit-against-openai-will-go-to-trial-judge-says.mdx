A U.S. judge has cleared the way for Elon Musk’s lawsuit against OpenAI to go to trial in March. Musk says the company broke its promise to stay nonprofit by switching to a for‑profit model, and he wants money for what he calls “ill‑gotten gains.” He contributed $38 million to OpenAI and claims the shift helped enrich its leaders. OpenAI says the case is baseless and calls it harassment. Microsoft, a partner in the company’s deals, is also involved.
