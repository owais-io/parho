Trump said the Iran war is almost finished, but costs are rising. The US‑Israeli air campaign has hit Iranian targets, while Iran has fired missiles and drones at Israel, the Gulf, and the US. The fighting has stopped oil ships in the Strait of Hormuz, sending prices up and hurting global markets. France sent a navy to protect oil lanes, and many civilians in Lebanon and the UAE have been hurt.
