Queensland police arrested a 52‑year‑old man for stealing priceless Egyptian artifacts from the Abbey Museum in Caboolture. He was caught on Russell Island after police found most stolen items in a camper van at a ferry terminal, including a 2,600‑year‑old wooden cat sculpture. The heist happened at 3 a.m. Friday, when a window was smashed and several ancient pieces were taken. The two‑day search ended with his arrest and charges.
