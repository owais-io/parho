Trump Media, the owner of the Trump‑backed Truth Social platform, announced a $6 billion merger with TAE Technologies, a fusion‑energy start‑up. The deal would combine a social‑media company that’s been losing money with a company developing fusion power plants that could supply cheap electricity for data centres. Shares jumped 27 % after the news. The new company would split ownership 50‑50 and aim to build the first large‑scale fusion plant by next year.
