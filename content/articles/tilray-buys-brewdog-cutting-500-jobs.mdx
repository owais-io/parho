Tilray, a US cannabis and drinks company, has bought BrewDog's UK and Irish brewing assets for £33 million. The deal will keep about 733 jobs but close 38 pubs, meaning nearly 500 jobs lost. Early investors in BrewDog’s “equity for punks” scheme will get nothing. The sale follows years of losses and a toxic workplace culture that led to the founder’s departure.
