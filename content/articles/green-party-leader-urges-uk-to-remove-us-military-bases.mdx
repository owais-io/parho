Green Party leader Zack Polanski says Britain should pull U.S. troops out of its bases and consider leaving NATO. He argues the UK relies too much on American military help and fears Trump’s influence. Polanski also backs a wealth tax to fight inequality and wants the UK to give up nuclear weapons. His remarks come amid growing trade and foreign‑policy tensions between the U.K. and the U.S.
