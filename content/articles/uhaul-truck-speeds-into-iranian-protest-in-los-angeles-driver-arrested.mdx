On Sunday, a U‑Haul box truck drove into a crowd of Iranian‑rights protesters on Veteran Avenue in Los Angeles. The truck’s windows and mirrors were shattered after demonstrators attacked it. Police stopped the vehicle a few blocks away, and the driver was taken into custody. One protester was hit, but no serious injuries were reported. The truck bore a banner denouncing Iran’s regime, and authorities are investigating the motive amid protests.
