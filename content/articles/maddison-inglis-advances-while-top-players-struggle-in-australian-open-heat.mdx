Maddison Inglis, a 28‑year‑old qualifier, made the fourth round of the Australian Open after Naomi Osaka withdrew with stomach pain. The win earned Inglis $480,000, almost a quarter of her career earnings, and a big boost to her ranking. Meanwhile, stars like Jannik Sinner, Novak Djokovic and Stan Wawrinka battled cramps, a near‑disqualification and a final match at Melbourne Park. The heat made for a tough day on court.
