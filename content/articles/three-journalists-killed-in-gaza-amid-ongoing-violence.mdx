On a Wednesday, Israeli forces killed 11 Palestinians in Gaza, including three journalists and two 13‑year‑old boys. The journalists were filming a displacement camp when a strike hit their car. Two boys were killed in separate attacks, one in a drone strike, the other by troops. Israel says it targeted drone operators, but the incident is being investigated. Gaza hospitals are treating the victims.
