West Ham are adding new attacking talent by signing 21‑year‑old Pablo from Gil Vicente for £18.3 m and agreeing to buy Lazio striker Taty Castellanos for £26.1 m, a combined spend of about £47 m. Manager Nuno Espírito Santo wants more goals to keep the club in the Premier League. They dropped a more expensive Wolves striker and are also eyeing short‑term deals for other forwards, while still needing a regular striker.
