UK Foreign Office minister Hamish Falconer told MPs the plan to hand over the Chagos Islands to Mauritius is paused while the government talks with the United States. The pause follows criticism from US President Trump, who said the deal was a mistake. Critics say the UK is uncertain. The bill was due in Parliament soon, but now the government will delay it until discussions finish, sparking confusion and anger among opposition parties.
