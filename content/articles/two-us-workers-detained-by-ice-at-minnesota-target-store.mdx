ICE agents stopped two U.S. Target workers in Richfield, Minnesota, on Jan 8. They were forced to the ground, shoved into an SUV, and one was later seen crying in another parking lot. Both men were released but said they were injured. State Rep. Michael Howard calls the event a “madness” and says ICE had no warrant. The incident follows the death of Renee Nicole Good and has sparked lawsuits.
