The UK plans to open most of the Chagos Islands' protected marine area to small‑scale fishing as part of the handover to Mauritius. This aims to allow the Chagossian people to earn a living from the sea again. Many conservation groups fear that the move will damage the world‑class reefs and wildlife that have thrived since the area was a no‑take zone.
