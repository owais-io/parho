Former U.S. secretary of state John Kerry said countries must stop relying on oil, especially after the war in Iran made prices sky‑high and showed how fragile economies can be when they depend on a few supply routes. He wants governments to invest in clean energy—wind, solar and especially new small nuclear reactors—to keep power steady and low‑carbon. He believes the security risk is the strongest reason to switch to renewables and nuclear.
