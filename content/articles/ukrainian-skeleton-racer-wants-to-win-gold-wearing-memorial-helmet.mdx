Ukrainian skeleton racer Vladyslav Heraskevych was barred from the 2026 Milano Cortina Games for wearing a helmet that shows the faces of 24 Ukrainian soldiers killed in the war. He says he will appeal, return in 2030, and try to win gold wearing the same helmet. The ban sparked praise, a presidential award, and a $200,000 donation for charity. He also criticises the IOC for allowing Russian athletes to compete.
