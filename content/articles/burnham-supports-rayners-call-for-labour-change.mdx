Andy Burnham, mayor of Greater Manchester, says he agrees with Angela Rayner’s warning that Labour’s current leader, Keir Starmer, is just “going through the motions” and that the party must listen to Rayner. Rayner criticised the government’s immigration plan and urged a shift to the left before local elections. Burnham echoed her concerns, saying the party needs to change faster, and highlighted growing frustration within Labour’s ranks.
