Teachers at Queensland Christian schools were instructed to teach creationist ideas in science, including that baby dinosaurs were on Noah’s Ark. They were sent to a conference run by the US group Answers in Genesis, which denies mainstream science. School leaders say they still follow the Australian curriculum, but critics worry that the schools are slipping past oversight.
