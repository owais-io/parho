China carried out a big two‑day exercise called “Justice Mission 2025” around Taiwan, firing missiles and sending warplanes and ships close to the island. President Trump said he isn’t worried and that he has a good relationship with Chinese President Xi, who hasn’t told him about the drills. Taiwan’s leaders condemned the tests as a threat, while China said they were a normal military drill. The U.S. may get involved if China attacks.
