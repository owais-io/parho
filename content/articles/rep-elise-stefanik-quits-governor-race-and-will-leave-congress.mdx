Elise Stefanik, a New York congresswoman and Trump ally, has dropped her bid to become New York’s governor. She said a long Republican primary in a heavily Democratic state isn’t worth the effort and she will not run for re‑election to the House. The party backs Bruce Blakeman instead. Stefanik will finish her term in Congress and may return to the Senate later.
