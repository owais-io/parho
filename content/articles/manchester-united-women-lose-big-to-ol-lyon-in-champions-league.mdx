Manchester United Women lost badly to OL Lyon, a top Champions League team. Manager Marc Skinner shuffled five players, picking 37‑year‑old striker Rachel Williams and leaving key midfielders on the bench. Lyon took control early and later scored two late goals by Melchie Dumornay. The defeat hurts United's chance to finish in the league's top four and qualify for the Champions League quarter‑finals. Skinner said the changes were needed to balance the squad.
