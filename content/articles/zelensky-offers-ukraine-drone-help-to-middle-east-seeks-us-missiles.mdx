Zelensky says Ukraine will send drone‑defence experts to the Middle East next week, hoping to trade skills for US air‑defence missiles it lacks. Ukraine worries a Middle Eastern war could cut its supply of PAC‑3 ammunition. While in Kyiv, he also met Dutch PM Rob Jetten to discuss joint weapons production. He highlighted Ukraine’s experience countering Iranian‑made Shahed drones used by Russia. Europe is now the world’s largest arms importer, with imports up almost 10% in the last five years.
