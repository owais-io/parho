Leeds United won 3‑1 at Pride Park, knocking Derby County out of the FA Cup. Ben Brereton Diaz gave Derby the lead with a half‑time goal, but Leeds levelled soon after. Ao Tanaka then broke the tie with an early second‑half strike, and James Justin sealed the win with a late winner. The victory gives Leeds a spot in the fourth round and ends Derby’s long wait for a win over their rivals.
