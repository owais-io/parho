The planet is making a huge amount of electronic waste—over 62 million tonnes worldwide and a lot more in Australia. Recycling helps recover valuable materials and keeps dangerous plastics out of landfills. Find local drop‑off points, stores that take batteries and gadgets, or manufacturer trade‑in programs. Before you toss a device, back up your files, do a factory reset or wipe the hard drive, and remove any removable memory. For extra‑sensitive data, use professional data‑destruction services. This keeps your info safe and supports a circular economy.
