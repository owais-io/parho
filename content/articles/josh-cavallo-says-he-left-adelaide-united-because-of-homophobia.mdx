Cavallo, who came out as gay in 2021, says Adelaide United blocked him from playing because of his sexuality. He left the club and now plays for Stamford AFC in England. The club denies the claims, saying selections are based on football. Cavallo notes he was sidelined after injuries, but he believes homophobia was the real reason. He says a fresh start in the UK has helped him recover mentally and return to the game.
