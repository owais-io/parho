Arizona police say 84‑year‑old Nancy Guthrie, mother of Today host Savannah Guthrie, is missing after last being seen at her home in the Catalina Foothills. No suspect has been identified yet, but detectives say evidence points to forced entry and blood at the scene. Savannah has asked fans to pray for her mother’s safe return and has canceled a trip to Milan. Authorities are still looking for anyone who might have seen her.
