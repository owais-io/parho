Ukraine could become an EU member as early as January 2027 if a U.S. plan is approved, but most EU leaders say it’s unrealistic and would need all 27 countries’ approval, with Hungary opposing. Zelenskiy is in Berlin meeting Germany, the U.S., and other European leaders about peace talks and security guarantees. The EU froze €210 bn of Russian assets. Russia sued a European bank and sentenced ICC officials, while Ukraine claims recent gains in the east and Russia attacks Ukrainian ports.
