Renee Good, 37, was shot by an ICE agent in Minneapolis. Her death sparked protests in 50 states, with crowds chanting “fuck ICE.” Three Democratic congresswomen tried to visit the ICE center but were denied. The Trump administration blamed a “left‑wing network” for the shooting. Protesters demanded ICE leave communities, criticized the U.S. government, and call for justice immediately.
