Emma Raducanu was beaten 7‑6(3), 6‑2 by Russia‑born Anastasia Potapova in Melbourne’s second round. Raducanu’s first serve was shaky and her forehand slipped, letting Potapova win a tight first set and then close the match in the second. The loss is Raducanu’s worst first‑week result at a slam since her 2024 comeback. Potapova will now face world No. 1 Aryna Sabalenka.
