In Barrow‑in‑Furness, a new mosque is ready for Ramadan prayers even though the building is still unfinished. The South Lakes Islamic Centre has cleared a large space for nightly services, but the site still lacks lights and heating. The community faces far‑right protests and fears the centre could be targeted, yet local supporters rally to keep the project going. The mosque will officially open in July.
