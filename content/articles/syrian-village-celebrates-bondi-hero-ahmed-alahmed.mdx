Ahmed al‑Ahmed, a 44‑year‑old father who moved from the war‑torn village of Nayrab to Australia, stopped a deadly gunman on Bondi Beach in Sydney. He wrestled the rifle away, saved lives, but was shot five times and is now recovering. The world has praised his bravery, and a GoFundMe has raised over A$2.5 million for his medical bills. Nayrab, still scarred by years of fighting, is proud of its hero and sees hope in his courage.
