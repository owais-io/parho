U.S. small businesses are feeling the strain from Trump’s tariffs, which the Supreme Court just struck down. They could get up to $175 billion back, but the refund process is confusing. Meanwhile, the tariffs have raised costs, disrupted supply chains, and created hiring uncertainty. Many owners face legal and administrative headaches while trying to recover the money they paid.
