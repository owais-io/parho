Amazon has finally brought its new AI‑powered Alexa+ to the UK, hoping to revive interest in its smart‑speakers. The upgrade lets users talk to Alexa in natural speech, remembers family preferences, and can do tasks like turning off lights, setting alarms and ordering food with a single command. It’s free for early adopters, but a £19.99 monthly fee or a Prime subscription may be needed later. Some critics worry about inaccuracies and safety, but Amazon says it has built safeguards.
