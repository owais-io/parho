New documents show that convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein sent emails and texts to William J. Burns, a senior diplomat who later became CIA director. The files detail several planned meetings, two of which actually happened in August 2013, when Epstein was already a convicted felon. Burns said he had no idea about Epstein’s crimes and regretted meeting him. He later cut ties after learning about Epstein’s conviction. No wrongdoing was found on Burns’s part.
