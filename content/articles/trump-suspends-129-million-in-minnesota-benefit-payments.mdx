The Trump administration has halted $129 million in federal aid for Minnesota because it says the state is running a big fraud scam. USDA Secretary Brooke Rollins wrote a letter to Governor Tim Walz and Minneapolis mayor Jacob Frey, demanding proof of how the money is spent. Minnesota’s officials say the cut hurts people who need help and will fight the decision in court. The move follows past investigations and Trump’s harsh statements about Somali residents.
