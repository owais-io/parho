Block, the parent of Square and Cash App, is laying off 4,000 of its 10,000 staff because artificial‑intelligence tools make work faster and cheaper. CEO Jack Dorsey said the company can run better with a smaller team. Shares jumped 20% after the news, showing investors feel the move will help profits. The layoffs echo wider worries that AI is cutting jobs across tech.
