French President Emmanuel Macron denied a report that he warned the United States could abandon Ukraine. Speaking in China, he stressed that the U.S. and Europe must stay united in supporting Kyiv. He said there is no mistrust between the two sides. His comments come while the war in Ukraine continues, Russia is negotiating with India, and U.S. and European leaders discuss sharing NATO defence responsibilities.
