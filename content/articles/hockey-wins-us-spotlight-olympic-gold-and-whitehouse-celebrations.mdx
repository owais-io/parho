The U.S. men’s and women’s hockey teams both beat Canada at the 2026 Winter Olympics, winning gold in close 2‑1 games. The victory sparked a surge of interest in the sport and led President Trump to invite the men to the White House. The team also posed for a State of the Union photo, while AI‑made videos poked fun at Canada. The story shows how sports victories can become political showpieces.
