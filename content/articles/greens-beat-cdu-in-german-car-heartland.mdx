In Baden‑Württemberg, Germany’s most important car‑making state, the Greens beat the Christian Democrats (CDU) in a poll. Cem Özdemir, a former minister and person of Turkish descent, will become the first Turkish‑origin premier. The CDU, led by Friedrich Merz, fell from a double‑digit lead, while the far‑right AfD grew but still missed its goal. The result signals trouble for the ruling coalition ahead of other state elections.
