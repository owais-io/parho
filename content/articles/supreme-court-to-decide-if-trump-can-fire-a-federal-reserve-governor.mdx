President Trump has been trying to force the Federal Reserve to cut rates. He fired a Fed governor, Lisa Cook, over a mortgage‑fraud claim and wants the court to let him keep the move. The Supreme Court is weighing whether the president can remove a Fed official without “cause” and the risks of harming the economy. The decision is expected in June and will shape the Fed’s independence.
