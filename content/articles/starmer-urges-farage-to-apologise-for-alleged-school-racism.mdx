Prime Minister Keir Starmer has urged Nigel Farage to apologise for alleged racist abuse he allegedly committed at Dulwich College. The Guardian reported a former student saying Farage shouted anti‑Jewish slogans and hissing gas‑chamber sounds. Starmer called Farage’s explanations unconvincing and demanded he find the victims and say sorry. Farage has denied intent, issued mixed statements and now says he never said those words. Several others confirm the abuse, while others challenge the accuracy.
