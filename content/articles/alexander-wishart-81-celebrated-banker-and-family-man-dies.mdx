Alexander Wishart, 81, died after a long banking career that began as a junior clerk. He rose to manage Harrods Bank, then led the UK branch of Beirut Riyad Bank, and finished as financial controller at fashion agency Gorland. A devoted husband, he and wife Myra raised funds for leukaemia after their daughter Kelly died, founding Kelly’s Trust. He also did home projects and ran two London marathons. He is survived by wife, children, grandchildren and brothers.
