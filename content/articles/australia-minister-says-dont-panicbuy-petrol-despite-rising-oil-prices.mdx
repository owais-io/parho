Australia’s energy minister Chris Bowen told drivers not to rush to the pump even though oil prices are climbing after a flare‑up with Iran. He said the country has enough petrol, diesel and jet fuel to last about a month, and that refineries will keep delivering until May. Bowen warned that higher global prices could raise local petrol costs, but urged people not to panic‑buy or cause shortages. The treasurer asked regulators to watch for price gouging.
