A former Syracuse player recalls a 1996 protest over campus security’s pepper spray, illustrating how Black students felt unsafe. That memory returns after a Texas state trooper shoved South Carolina star Nyck Harbor during a televised game. The trooper was fired, but the incident raises questions about police treatment of Black athletes. Harbor, a top athlete and volunteer, was unharmed, but the event highlights a broader problem with police training and bias.
