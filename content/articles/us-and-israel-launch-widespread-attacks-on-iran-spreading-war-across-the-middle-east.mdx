On Monday, the United States and Israel intensified air strikes on Iran, hitting cities, missile sites, and oil facilities. The attacks spread to nine countries, including Lebanon, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait. Casualties swelled to over 500 in Iran, with damage to hospitals and infrastructure. Iran responded with drones and missiles at oil tankers and allied bases, threatening the Strait of Hormuz. The conflict raised oil prices and drew European allies to join the campaign.
