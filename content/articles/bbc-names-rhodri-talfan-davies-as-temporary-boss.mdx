The BBC has appointed Rhodri Talfan Davies as its interim director general after Tim Davie resigned following a controversy over editing a Donald Trump speech. Davies will become director general in April and will sit on the BBC board from February. The corporation still needs a permanent replacement and is working with the government on future funding and charter reforms.
