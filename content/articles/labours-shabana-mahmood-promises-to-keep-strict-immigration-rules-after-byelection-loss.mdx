Shabana Mahmood says she will keep Labour’s strict immigration rules even after the Green Party beat Labour in the Gorton and Denton byelection. Unions and left‑leaning MPs want a softer approach, but Mahmood insists the policies aim to stop illegal migration and protect public services. She will argue the changes fit Labour values and will visit a think‑tank next week. She also cites Denmark’s model and says polls show voters back her plans.
