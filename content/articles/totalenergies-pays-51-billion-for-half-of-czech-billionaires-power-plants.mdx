Czech billionaire Daniel Křetínský sold half of his power‑plant business to TotalEnergies for €5.1 billion. In return, Total got a 4.1 % stake in his company, making it a major shareholder. The new joint venture will own plants in the UK, France, the Netherlands and Italy, enough to supply electricity for over 3 million homes. Křetínský wants to stay a long‑term partner and help grow flexible European power.
