England’s rugby coach, Steve Borthwick, is turning to the 2003 World Cup champions for inspiration after two heavy defeats. He set up a dinner with former stars like Martin Johnson and Lewis Moody to help his players learn how to bounce back from setbacks. With scrum‑half Alex Mitchell out for the rest of the tournament, Borthwick is preparing the squad for Italy and France while also addressing issues of intensity and discipline.
