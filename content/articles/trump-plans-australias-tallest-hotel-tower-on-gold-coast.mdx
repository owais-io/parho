The Trump Organization plans to build a 340‑metre, 91‑storey hotel‑resort on Australia’s Gold Coast. The tower, called Trump International Hotel & Tower Gold Coast, would be the country’s tallest building and a 6‑star resort with apartments, retail, and a beach club. A local developer says construction will start this year and cost under A$1.5 billion, for the community.
