England beat Australia 152-98 on Boxing Day 2025, winning a key Ashes match. Their bowlers ripped out the Australians in just 30 overs, a faster collapse than the 2010 game. The series was already decided with the Ashes in England’s hands, so the win was more about pride than a chance to win the series. The match shows England’s improved bowling but also their ongoing struggles on the tour.
