A Los Angeles grand jury is looking into pop singer D4vd, whose real name is David Burke, after a 14‑year‑old girl, Celeste Rivas Hernandez, was found dead in a Tesla linked to him. The car, found in a Hollywood Hills tow yard, contained a decomposed body and severed limbs. Burke, who was on tour, has faced subpoenas and may now have to testify. No cause of death yet, and the singer has canceled shows.
