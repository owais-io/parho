Mikael Robertsson and Olov Lindberg started Flightradar24 as a side page on a flight‑price site. The volcano eruption in 2010 made millions watch live flight data. The service grew after big events like MH370, the COVID crash, and the Queen’s coffin flight. It tracks planes using ~58,000 radio receivers worldwide. Revenue comes from paid subscriptions and ads, and it has over 1.5 million paying users.
