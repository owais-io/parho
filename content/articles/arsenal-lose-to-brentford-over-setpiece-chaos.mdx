Arsenal were beaten by Brentford because they couldn't handle set‑piece chaos, says coach Mikel Arteta. The home side missed key players William Saliba and Kai Havertz, and a late equaliser from Keane Lewis‑Potter forced a 1‑1 draw. Arteta said Arsenal lacked ruthlessness inside the boxes and still need to improve defensively. The loss keeps the gap to Manchester City at four points with 12 games left.
