Resident doctors in England have voted to strike for five days, starting Wednesday, after rejecting a government offer that would add more training places but keep their pay unchanged. Doctors want a 26% pay increase. The health secretary called the strike dangerous, while the doctors’ union says the deal is “too little, too late.” Hospitals are already stretched by a flu wave, and this will be the 14th strike since March 2023.
