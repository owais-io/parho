Sara Duterte, the 47‑year‑old vice‑president, declared she will run for Philippine president in 2028. She criticized President Marcos Jr. for corruption, apologized for her earlier support of him, and highlighted her father Rodrigo Duterte’s upcoming ICC hearing. Duterte also mentioned her own impeachment cases and vowed to tackle rising food costs, improve health care, and fight corruption. Polls suggest she could be a strong contender, though critics point to her past controversies.
