Spain is planning to ban people under 16 from using social media to protect kids from harmful content. Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez announced the plan after Elon Musk blasted him on X, calling him a tyrant and fascist. The move follows similar proposals in Greece, France, and the UK. The law would let parents set limits and could target companies like Meta, TikTok, and Google. Public support is high, with 82% backing a ban for under‑14s.
