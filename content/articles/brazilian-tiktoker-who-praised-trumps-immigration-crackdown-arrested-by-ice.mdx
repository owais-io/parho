A Brazilian influencer, Júnior Pena, who used his TikTok and Instagram to cheer on Trump’s hard‑line immigration policies and claim that migrants were all criminals, was taken into custody by ICE agents in New Jersey. He was booked to the Delaney Hall detention center after missing a court hearing. The move follows a surge in deportations of Brazilians under Trump, prompting criticism from Brazilian communities and former political leaders.
