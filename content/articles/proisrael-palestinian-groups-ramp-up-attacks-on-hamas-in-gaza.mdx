After Israel’s cease‑fire in October, Palestinian groups that back Israel have stepped up raids, assassinations and kidnappings inside Hamas‑run Gaza. The Popular Forces, Strike Force Against Terror and Ashraf al‑Mansi, all stationed in Israeli‑controlled eastern Gaza, have gained firepower and are attacking deep into Hamas territory. Their attacks have added to the violence, forcing Hamas to counter‑strike and complicating a U.N. plan to stabilise Gaza. Meanwhile, Israeli jets continue bombing in the region, killing dozens of civilians.
