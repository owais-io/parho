Marine Le Pen is fighting a court case over a 2023 conviction. If the judges overturn it, she could run again in 2027; if not, her 30‑year‑old deputy Jordan Bardella will take her place. The National Rally is gaining popularity, even beyond its blue‑collar base, and polls show many voters see it as a solution to France’s problems. Mainstream parties now have less than 500 days to respond.
