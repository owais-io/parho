Liverpool coach Arne Slot plans to talk to Mohamed Salah on Friday morning to decide if he’ll play in the Brighton match. Slot says the outcome of that chat will shape next week’s lineup. He also insists he has no reason to want Salah to leave the club, even after the striker was left out after a tough interview. Slot keeps the final selection decision to himself and says he will keep Salah in the squad if they agree.
