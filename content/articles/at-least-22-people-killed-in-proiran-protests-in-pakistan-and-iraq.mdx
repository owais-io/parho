After the death of Iran’s Supreme Leader, protests erupted in Pakistan and Iraq. In Karachi, demonstrators stormed the US consulate, set a small fire, and were shot by security forces, killing 10 people. Similar unrest in Gilgit‑Baltistan and Islamabad left others dead. In Baghdad, protesters tried to storm the US embassy, where tear gas was used. The violence shows how the killing of the Iranian leader sparked pan‑regional anger.
