Gas prices in Australia have jumped to record highs, pushing weekly petrol bills over $73 for a typical household. Meanwhile, the Reserve Bank of Australia is expected to raise its cash rate to 4.1% this week after the deputy governor warned that inflation is too high. A rate hike would make mortgage payments more expensive, adding to the cost of living for many Australians. The dollar is also stronger amid global oil price swings.
