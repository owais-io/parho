On Tuesday, the United States joined a group of European allies to promise Ukraine security guarantees if Russia attacks again. Leaders said the pact includes commitments to defend Kyiv, deploy troops after a peace deal, and set up a monitoring system with drones and satellites. Russia has not accepted the plan and has rejected NATO troops in Ukraine. Ukrainian President Zelenskyy welcomed the pledge, while Russia’s President Putin celebrated his soldiers’ ‘holy mission’.
