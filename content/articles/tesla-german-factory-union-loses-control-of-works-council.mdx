At Tesla's German gigafactory near Berlin, the trade union IG Metall tried to win a majority on the works council but lost to the non‑union Giga United, which won 24 of 37 seats. IG Metall will keep working to protect workers’ rights. The fight has been hot, with lawsuits, accusations of slander and even alleged spying. Tesla’s boss Musk has warned the dispute could affect future investment.
