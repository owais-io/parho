Trump has announced the first U.S. air strikes against Iran, calling the operation ‘Epic Fury’. He says he wants to topple the Iranian government. The move stirs memories of 1979 hostage crisis, the Iran‑Contra scandal, and the 1953 U.S. coup that helped the Shah rise to power. Iran still hates U.S. interference, and protests against its regime continue. Trump’s plan has supporters in the U.S. and ex‑royal circles, but many doubt it will succeed.
