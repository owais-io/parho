House Speaker Mike Johnson said he had spoken to GOP members who made Islam‑bashing remarks, but he refused to call the comments wrong. The remarks came from Tennessee’s Andy Ogles and Florida’s Randy Fine, who posted hateful tweets about Muslims. Democratic leaders, including Hakeem Jeffries, have demanded disciplinary action. Johnson’s silence has drawn criticism from civil‑rights groups and many House Democrats and the House Republican leadership.
