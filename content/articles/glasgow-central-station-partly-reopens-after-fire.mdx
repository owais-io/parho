After a fire in a Victorian building next to Glasgow Central, the station’s main concourse will reopen partially on Wednesday. Passengers can use low‑level platforms and the Hope Street entrance, but the timetable is reduced, including fewer trains to London. Nearly a million journeys were disrupted last week. ScotRail says more services will return when it’s safe, and travelers should check their trips before going.
