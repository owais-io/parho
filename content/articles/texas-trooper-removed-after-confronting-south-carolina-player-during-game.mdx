A Texas trooper was sent home after he walked into the tunnel at Texas A&M’s game and bumped into South Carolina’s star receiver Nyck Harbor and teammate Oscar Adaway. The trooper pointed at them and seemed to scold them. Texas A&M’s safety office said the trooper would be investigated and had been ejected from the stadium. South Carolina coach Shane Beamer said he was happy the trooper was sent home. The incident went viral on social media.
