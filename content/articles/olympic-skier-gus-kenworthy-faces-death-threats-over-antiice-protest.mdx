Gus Kenworthy, the British freestyle skier, said he received death threats after posting a picture of himself urinating “fuck ICE” in the snow. The threats included wishes for him to die or break his neck during the Games. He has joined other athletes in criticizing the U.S. ICE agency, calling it “evil and terrifying.” The British Olympic Association has set up support for his safety and to help him cope with online abuse.
