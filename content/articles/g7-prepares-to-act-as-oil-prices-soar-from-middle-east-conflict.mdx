After strikes on Iranian energy sites, oil prices jumped above $100 a barrel. G7 finance ministers met and said they would watch the situation closely and could release emergency oil reserves if needed, but they haven't yet decided to dump the IEA’s 300‑400 million barrel stash. Higher oil prices pushed gas prices up in Europe and the US.
