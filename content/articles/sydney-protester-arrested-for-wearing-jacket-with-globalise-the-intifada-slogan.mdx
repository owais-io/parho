Three people were arrested during a Sydney protest against the US military move in Venezuela. A 53‑year‑old woman was taken off the street because she wore a jacket with the words “globalise the intifada,” which police say is hateful and banned under new hate‑speech rules. Two men were also arrested. No charges were filed, and the protest was held despite a state ban on public demonstrations after a Bondi beach incident.
