Nigel Farage sells personal video messages through Cameo, earning hundreds of thousands of pounds. In these clips he mixes political praise with internet memes like “Big Chungus” and even slurs such as “Ngubu.” Some supporters use the videos to spread far‑right propaganda. Farage’s team says the clips aren’t political statements, but the content shows how he blends humor, racism, and extremist language to rally his base. The practice raises concerns about politicians profiting from hate‑filled messages.
