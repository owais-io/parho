Ben Stokes told reporters in Sydney that the fifth Ashes Test is a big game for England, even though they’re down 3‑1. He said the match matters more than the series outcome and that the team will try to win a game of cricket for England. Groundsman Adam Lewis explained he’s working hard to give the pitch a good green, even under media pressure. Stokes also said Australia’s seam bowlers are the key to their wins.
