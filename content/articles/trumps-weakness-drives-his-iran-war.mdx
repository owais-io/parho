Trump has been a weak president, with low approval and a shaky economy. Instead of fixing things at home, he launched a war against Iran, hoping to rally support. The administration has given confusing reasons for the attack, and it hasn’t helped his popularity. Inside the party, leaders clash over immigration, spending, and personal scandals. With no solid plan and little public backing, the war is a distraction that could hurt the country further.
