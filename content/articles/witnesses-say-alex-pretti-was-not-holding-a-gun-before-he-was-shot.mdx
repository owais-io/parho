Two witnesses say Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, wasn’t holding a gun when federal agents shot him in Minneapolis. One woman filmed the event and says he only tried to help a woman, while a nearby doctor says he was shot after being tackled and was not attacking anyone. The accounts contradict Trump‑era officials who called him a “gunman.” The ACLU filed a lawsuit over the shooting.
