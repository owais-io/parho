Keith Porter Jr., a 43‑year‑old father from Los Angeles, was shot to death on New Year’s Eve by an off‑duty ICE officer named Brian Palacios. The family says Porter was not a threat and that the officer acted wrongfully. They want local officials to arrest and charge the agent, while ICE says it was self‑defence. The case has sparked protests and worries about ICE’s aggressive tactics across the country.
