Donald Trump said he was postponing a plan to strike Iran’s power plants, sparking a mix of relief and worry in Tehran. Iranians remain nervous because a power outage could leave 90 million people without electricity, water or food. Diplomats in Turkey and Oman are working to ease tensions, but the Iranian government insists any US attack would be a pre‑planned war crime. The Gulf region stays on edge.
