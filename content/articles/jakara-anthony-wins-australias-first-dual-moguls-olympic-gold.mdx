Jakara Anthony, 27, took gold in the new dual moguls event at the Milano Cortina Winter Games, giving Australia its first‑ever Olympic title in that discipline. She beat a field of American skiers, including singles champion Elizabeth Lemley, with a score of 20‑15 in the final. The win marks Anthony’s second Olympic gold, making her the first Australian winter athlete to earn two medals in one Games. Australia’s winter medal haul now stands at three.
