Senator Susan Collins, the Republican from Maine, has announced she will run for a sixth term in the U.S. Senate. The election will be a key battle for Democrats, who want to flip the Senate and stop Trump‑era policies. Collins is known for voting against several of President Trump’s picks and for supporting bipartisan measures like the $1.2 trillion infrastructure bill. She has spent big money to win, but faces a tough Democratic primary and general‑election challenge.
