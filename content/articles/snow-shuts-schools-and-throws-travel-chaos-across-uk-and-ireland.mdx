Heavy snow hit the UK and Ireland, shutting hundreds of schools and cancelling flights and train services. In Scotland, up to 34 cm of snow fell, making roads and rail lines treacherous, and schools in places like Shetland, Aberdeenshire and Moray were closed. Across England, Wales, Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland, yellow weather warnings urged people to stay home or drive very carefully. Airports shut runways and Eurostar stopped services to Brussels.
