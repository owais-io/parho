India’s bid to host the 2036 Olympics is at risk after Bangladesh pulled out of the T20 World Cup, a move that followed a political spat over an IPL player. Tensions rose after a Hindu man’s death sparked clashes in Bangladesh, worsening ties with India. Pakistan may also boycott the tournament. With the IOC cracking down on political interference, India must improve relations with Bangladesh and Pakistan to keep its Olympic hopes alive.
