Moise Kean, who had just come back from a family break, was not meant to play against Cremonese, but an injury forced his inclusion. He entered late, scored a goal in the 85th minute, and helped Fiorentina win 1‑0. The team is still at the bottom of Serie A, struggling with coaching changes, big transfer spend, and stadium renovation delays. The win gives them a tiny lift off the relegation zone.
