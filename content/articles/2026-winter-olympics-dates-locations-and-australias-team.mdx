Australia’s big Winter Games are almost here. The 2026 Olympics start on Feb 7 and finish on Feb 23 in Milan and Verona, with events spread across eight Italian towns. Nine Network and Stan Sport will show live in Australia. Australia sends 53 athletes, hoping for medals in skiing, snowboarding and new sport ski mountaineering. The Games also welcome NHL players back for ice hockey.
