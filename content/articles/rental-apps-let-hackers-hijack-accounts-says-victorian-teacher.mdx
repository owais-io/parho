Michael, a 47‑year‑old Victorian teacher, tried to move and apply for apartments online. While filling in his passport number, someone stole his phone number, accessed his bank and super accounts, and began making transfers. He thinks the leak came from rental‑app data he supplied. The incident highlights how many rent‑tech platforms collect too much personal data and may be insecure, prompting calls for tighter regulation.
