Arsenal beat Wolves 2‑1 at the Emirates after a last‑minute own goal by Wolves defender Yerson Mosquera. The match had been a nightmare for the Gunners, who were slow and passive early on, but they found two chances in the second half. Mosquera’s mistake – a mis‑directed back‑pass that bounced into the net – sealed the win. Arteta was furious about his team’s lack of focus, while Wolves finished the season with a dreadful record.
