Usman Khawaja, 38, is struggling with a back injury and a slow drop in his batting. Australia kept him in the squad for treatment, but selectors are unsure if he can return. They are looking at options like Travis Head, Beau Webster, and Josh Inglis. If the new players perform well, Khawaja may be dropped; if not, he could stay and try to revive his career.
