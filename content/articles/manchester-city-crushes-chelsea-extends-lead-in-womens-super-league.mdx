Manchester City dominated Chelsea in a 5-1 win at the Etihad Stadium, with Brazil star Kerolin scoring a hat‑trick. The match cut City’s advantage to 11 points at the top, making the title almost impossible to catch. Chelsea’s coach, Sonia Bompastor, admitted the team is struggling after a run of poor results. City’s coach, Andrée Jeglertz, praised his side’s performance and confidence.
