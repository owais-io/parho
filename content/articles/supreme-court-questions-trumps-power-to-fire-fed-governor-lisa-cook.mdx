The U.S. Supreme Court is reviewing President Trump’s attempt to remove Federal Reserve governor Lisa Cook. Trump claims Cook lied about her mortgage applications, but no formal investigation or hearing was held. The justices are debating whether the president can fire a Fed official without a clear process. The case will decide how much political influence the White House can have over the Fed’s independent policy decisions.
