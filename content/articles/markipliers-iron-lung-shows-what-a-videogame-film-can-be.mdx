The low‑budget sci‑fi thriller Iron Lung, made and starring YouTube star Mark Fischbach (Markiplier), pulls viewers straight into the feel of playing the game it’s based on. With a lone convict driving a rusty sub through a sea of blood, solving puzzles like a video‑game level, the film feels like a long, narrated playthrough. It’s a clear example of how online gaming content can turn into cinema.
