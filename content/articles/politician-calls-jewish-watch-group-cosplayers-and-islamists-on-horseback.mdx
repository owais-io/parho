Reform UK candidate Chris Parry made offensive remarks on X, calling a Jewish neighborhood watch group “cosplayers” and likening them to “Islamists on horseback” after an arson attack on ambulances run by a Jewish charity. He said the group has no legal authority, sparking backlash from Shomrim’s president who called his comments ignorant. The police are investigating the arson, and Parry defended himself as simply clarifying the difference between community groups and police.
