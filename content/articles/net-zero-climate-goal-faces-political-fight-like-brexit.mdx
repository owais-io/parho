A coalition of Nigel Farage’s Reform party, the Conservatives, business groups and right‑wing media is attacking the UK’s net zero target, saying it worsens the oil crisis and urging more North Sea drilling. While 60 % of voters still support net zero, experts worry that the same tactics used in the Brexit campaign could erode public backing. Labour leaders are reluctant to speak up, so politicians and the government need a united, clear message to keep climate action on the agenda.
