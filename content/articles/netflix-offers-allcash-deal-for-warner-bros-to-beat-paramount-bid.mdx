Netflix has turned its $82.7 bn offer for Warner Bros Discovery into a pure cash deal, keeping the same price of $27.75 per share. The move should speed up the shareholder vote and give WBD investors more certainty. The change comes as Paramount Skydance pushes a $108.4 bn hostile takeover and tries to block Netflix by nominating board candidates and suing for info. Netflix says it’s ready for regulatory approval and its subscriber count is growing.
