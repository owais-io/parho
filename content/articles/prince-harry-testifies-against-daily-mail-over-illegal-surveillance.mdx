Prince Harry took the stand in a London court, saying the Daily Mail and Mail on Sunday illegally gathered his private info from 2001 to 2013. He says the papers listened to his calls, tracked his flights, and wrote about his dating life, making him feel paranoid. This is his third major newspaper case, after a £10m settlement with the Sun and a win against the Mirror. Harry spoke emotionally and asked for an apology and accountability.
