A global happiness report says Instagram, TikTok and X, which push algorithm‑driven scrolling, make young people less happy than WhatsApp or Facebook, which focus on real connections. The study of 17 Latin American countries and the Middle East found that heavy use of the former hurts mental well‑being, while WhatsApp and Facebook are linked to higher life satisfaction. Moderation—under an hour a day—seems best, but most people use more than that. In the UK, happiness is the lowest since 2012.
