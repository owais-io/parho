Wessex Water bosses – chief executive Ruth Jefferson and CFO Andy Pymer – received a combined £50,000 extra pay from their parent company, even though the UK government has banned bonuses for water firms that broke the law. The money came from Wessex Water Ltd and wasn’t listed clearly in the company’s accounts. Politicians and regulators are worried about this lack of transparency, and similar pay scandals have hit other water companies.
