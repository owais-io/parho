After President Trump gave a long, controversial talk in Pennsylvania, a doctor explains that the “Goldwater rule” only stops doctors from diagnosing people without a proper exam. It doesn’t forbid doctors from talking about obvious behaviors shown publicly. The rule’s creator said doctors can responsibly discuss a public figure’s actions if those actions are clear and well‑documented. This lets medical experts share useful insights while staying ethical.
