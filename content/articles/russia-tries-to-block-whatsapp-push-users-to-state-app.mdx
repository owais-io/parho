Russia has tried to block the popular messaging app WhatsApp so that people would switch to its state‑run app, Max. The move is part of Moscow’s plan for a “sovereign internet” that keeps users away from Western tech. WhatsApp’s team said the attempt was a step backward and that they’re working to keep people connected. Critics, including soldiers and activists, say it limits privacy and freedom.
