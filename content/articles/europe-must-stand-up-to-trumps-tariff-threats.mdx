EU leaders must act against President Trump’s tariff threats to Greenland and Denmark, according to a policy expert. He is using economic pressure to force political concessions. The article warns that Europe’s hesitation would weaken the Union and hurt Ukraine. It calls for united, strong counter‑tariffs, activation of a new “trade bazooka”, and a clear rejection of Trump’s demands in Brussels. Standing firm would preserve Europe’s credibility and security.
