A 14‑year‑old girl named Chaya Dadon put herself between two children and gunmen on Bondi beach, covering them with her body during a deadly antisemitic attack. She was shot in the leg but is now recovering in hospital. The attack killed 15 people and injured 41. Her grandfather, Rabbi Lieder, praised her bravery and kindness, saying she always wants to protect others.
