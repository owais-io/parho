Tommy Robinson, a far‑right activist, hosted a Christmas carol concert in London that drew about 1,000 people—a sharp drop from the 110,000 who turned up to his September rally. Protesters from Stand Up to Racism were kept separate. Robinson talked about a pastor who changed his view of the church, handed out hymn sheets, and a woman sold flags and Santa hats. The Church of England warned that the Christian message must remain inclusive, while police stressed keeping the event calm.
