Celtic have named Wilfried Nancy, who coached Columbus Crew, as their permanent manager on a two‑and‑a‑half‑year deal. Martin O’Neill stepped down after a 1‑0 win over Dundee that kept Celtic level with Hearts at the top of the Scottish Premiership. Nancy, who won the MLS Cup in 2023 and the Leagues Cup in 2024, will take over just before a League Cup final against St Mirren. Club leaders back the new coach.
