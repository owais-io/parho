South West Water’s repeated raw sewage spills into Devon and Cornwall’s coastal waters have spurred a growing legal action. The lawsuit, which started with 1,400 people in Exmouth, is now open to residents and businesses in Dawlish, Sidmouth, Teignmouth, Newquay and Penzance. Claimants say the company’s failings caused beach closures and harm to local livelihoods. After a £24m Ofwat fine, South West Water remains silent about the complaint.
