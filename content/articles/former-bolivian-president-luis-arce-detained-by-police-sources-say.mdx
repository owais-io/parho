Sources say former Bolivian president Luis Arce was taken to police headquarters on Wednesday, and he is now under investigation. A police unit claims the move followed a court order, while the vice‑president praised the operation and said Arce would face prison for corruption. Arce is accused of misusing funds from a government Indigenous development program, and a former deputy was arrested last week for similar charges. The case is part of a broader crackdown on past government corruption.
