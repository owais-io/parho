An immigration judge denied a U.S. asylum request for five‑year‑old Liam Conejo Ramos and his family. The decision comes after their brief detention in Minneapolis sparked protests last January. The family plans to appeal the ruling to the Board of Immigration Appeals. They worry about the kids staying in school and fear they may be deported back to Ecuador. The case highlights the Trump administration’s crackdown on families.
