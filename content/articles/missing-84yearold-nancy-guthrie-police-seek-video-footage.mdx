An 84‑year‑old woman, Nancy Guthrie, went missing last month after returning home in Arizona. Police think she was kidnapped and have found a masked man on her doorbell footage. They’re asking anyone in a two‑mile radius to share any video from the past month that shows cars, people or anything unusual. They’re also waiting on DNA results from a pair of gloves found near the house. The FBI offers a $100,000 reward.
