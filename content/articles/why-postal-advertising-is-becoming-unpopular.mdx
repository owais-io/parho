The writer says the decline of letter writing is fine if it means stopping postal advertising, which is costly and harmful to the environment. Printing and sending millions of paper pieces wastes resources, most of which never get read. Charities, for example, send many Christmas appeals to the same people, adding to the waste. Readers are invited to send Guardian letters.
