The Bright Line Watch survey of U.S. political scientists says democracy has slipped to a new, lower baseline after Trump’s return to the White House. Ratings are around 57 on a 100‑point scale—well below the 60‑plus scores of the past decade. The trend looks flat for the next few years, with only small gains expected by 2027. The public’s trust is split sharply by party, and many scholars see Trump’s actions as serious threats to democratic norms.
