January’s football transfer window saw early signings in Germany, huge spending in the Premier League, a surge in youth deals, and a wave of loans. Eintracht Frankfurt moved quickly, adding five players before the winter break. Manchester City spent the most (£82.5 m) on new forwards, while clubs like Liverpool and Tottenham balanced big purchases with loan deals. The market also saw a focus on future talent, with several clubs investing in teenagers, and some big clubs stayed quiet.
