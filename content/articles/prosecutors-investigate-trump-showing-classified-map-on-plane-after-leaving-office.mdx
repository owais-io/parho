Federal prosecutors are checking whether former President Donald Trump showed a classified map to people on a plane after his first term, including his White House chief of staff, Susie Wiles. The memo, dated January 2023, also mentions Trump brandishing a military map at his Bedminster club in New Jersey, criticizing the 2021 withdrawal. The map was not charged separately because it was after the withdrawal and likely had no current defense value. The investigation continues.
