Nigel Farage, the Reform UK leader, has paused his paid‑video service on Cameo after a Guardian investigation uncovered more than 4,300 clips he made. The videos included support for a convicted rioter, extremist slogans, antisemitic remarks and misogynistic jokes. Farage said he was “unavailable” and denied that any data was illegally obtained. He also accused the Guardian of a “hit job” and said the videos shouldn’t be treated as politics.
