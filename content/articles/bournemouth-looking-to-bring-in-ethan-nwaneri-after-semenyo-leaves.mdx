Bournemouth want to fill the spot left by Antoine Semenyo and are keen to bring in Ethan Nwaneri on loan, a young striker who hasn’t played much for Arsenal. The club is also watching other defenders – like Marc Guéhi from Crystal Palace and Oliver Glasner – who could move to fill gaps in other teams. Rumours spread across clubs, but the main focus is Bournemouth’s search for a replacement.
