Sunderland and Newcastle are gearing up for a tense derby after Sunderland’s new boss, Régis Le Bris, brought in big‑spending and a fresh squad. The two clubs, only 12 miles apart, have a long‑standing rivalry that saw Newcastle beat Sunderland 3‑0 in the FA Cup last season. With new signings, a £167 m investment and Eddie Howe’s tactical tweaks, both sides want the win to prove themselves.
