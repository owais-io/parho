U.S. airports are seeing long security lines because the Department of Homeland Security hasn’t been fully funded for this fiscal year. TSA agents are unpaid and some are refusing to work, which slows screening. Democrats want reforms to immigration enforcement and voting rules, while Republicans push the Save America Act to tighten voter registration. Trump says he will refuse to sign any bill until that act passes. The budget deadlock keeps lines long.
