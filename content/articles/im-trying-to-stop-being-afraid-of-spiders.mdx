The writer talks about her lifelong fear of spiders, especially big huntsman spiders that often appear in her home. She shares childhood memories of screaming and spraying insecticide. She’s now trying to change by picturing spiders as friendly, reminding herself they’re harmless, and practicing breathing and staying still instead of running. She admits she probably won’t become a spider‑lover, but hopes to feel normal around them.
