Many migrant workers in India lack official records, making it hard for governments to help them during emergencies. In Odisha, a nonprofit has launched a WhatsApp chatbot called Bandhu that lets workers share their name, home village, age, contact details and current location. The data is kept private and updates each time a worker moves. Families can see where their loved ones are, and authorities can respond faster to disasters and accidents.
