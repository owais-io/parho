The Guardian editorial says that minor animal‑welfare fixes hide the fact that we still keep, feed, and kill billions of animals every year. It argues that the only real way to stop animal suffering is to stop eating meat and seafood. The author shares stories of people who quit after seeing farm cruelty and stresses that prawns can feel pain too. The piece calls for a full switch to vegan food.
