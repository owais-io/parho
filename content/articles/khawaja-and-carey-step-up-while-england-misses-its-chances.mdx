England struggled to win in Adelaide, losing two games in six days. Australia kept their composure, even after losing key players. Usman Khawaja, moved down the order, played his best innings, scoring a half‑century. Alex Carey, usually a keeper, hit a century and steadied the score. Australia stayed ahead thanks to solid batting, while England’s plans faltered and they still need a big turnaround.
