South Africa is talking with Russia to get back 17 men and 2 Botswanans who were lured by a daughter of former president Jacob Zuma into Russia for supposed training. They ended up fighting in Ukraine. The South African government says the matter is urgent and is working with Russian authorities. Families have protested and mothers fear their sons are being mistreated. Zuma’s daughter and others face lawsuits over the recruitment.
