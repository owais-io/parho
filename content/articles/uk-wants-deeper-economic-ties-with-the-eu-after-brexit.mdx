Business Secretary Peter Kyle says the British public are not looking back to the pre‑Brexit era but want to move forward. He said the UK wants deeper economic ties with the EU, highlighted by a new competition‑cooperation deal signed in Brussels. The UK also hopes to join the EU’s “Made in Europe” public‑procurement scheme, which will give priority to European goods. Kyle wants a stronger trade agreement than the one brokered under Boris Johnson and is watching US‑UK trade talks.
