The Guardian editorial explains that the cult novel and film *Trainspotting* is turning into a musical, following a long West End trend of turning books into shows. It lists other examples such as *Paddington* and *One Day*, and notes that new songs will tackle modern addiction to phones and the internet. Musicals are expensive but often draw on familiar stories to stay relevant.
