A UAE royal’s investment firm secretly paid $500 million to buy a 49 % stake in a Trump‑family crypto company called World Liberty Financial. The deal, revealed by the Wall Street Journal, came just before Trump’s 2025 inauguration and made the president and his family richer. The story got little media coverage, but it shows how Trump’s business ties with Gulf leaders could influence U.S. policy and raise questions about conflict of interest.
