A software glitch at the understaffed IRS has left about $51 million of donations to state‑level political groups unreported. The problem shows up in the filing system for 527 organisations—tax‑exempt political groups that coordinate spending in races like governors and attorneys general. With a new election deadline approaching, the missing data could hide how money is flowing into key state contests.
