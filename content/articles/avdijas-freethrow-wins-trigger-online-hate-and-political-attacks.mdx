Deni Avdija has been scoring a lot of points with his free throws, which has earned him praise in basketball but also criticism on the internet. Fans call him a “flopper” and many link his play to his background as an Israeli soldier. Because of the Gaza conflict, people are using Avdija as a stand‑in for Israel’s actions, making his career a target for political debate.
