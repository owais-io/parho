Anna Murdoch‑Mann, who helped build Rupert Murdoch’s News Corp, passed away at her Palm Beach home at 81. The former media tycoon’s ex‑wife had been a key figure behind the company’s growth and left the board after their 1998 split. She also was a novelist and charity champion, working with children’s hospitals and earning a papal damehood. Her life, marriage and battles inspired the TV drama Succession.
