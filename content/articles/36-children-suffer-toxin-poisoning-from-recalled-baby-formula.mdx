36 kids got sick after eating recalled baby formula. The UK Health Security Agency says a toxin called cereulide was found in some Nestlé and Danone batches. Symptoms include vomiting and stomach pain that start within minutes and usually clear up in a day. Parents are told to check for and throw away any recalled products, switch to other formula, and call the GP if symptoms appear.
