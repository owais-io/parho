Angela Rayner, Labour’s deputy, has called on Prime Minister Keir Starmer to keep his promise to cap ground rents for leaseholders. The pledge clashes with ministers who fear a cap will scare investors, including pension funds. MPs are pressing for a draft bill, which Treasury officials delayed. Rayner says the cap is needed to stop unfair costs for homeowners and urges the government to act quickly.
