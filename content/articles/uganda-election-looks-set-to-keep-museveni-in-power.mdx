Uganda is preparing for a presidential election that looks set to keep 81‑year‑old Yoweri Museveni in office for a seventh term. His main rival, singer‑turned‑politician Bobi Wine, has faced police and army crackdowns, teargas, arrests and even a death. The government shut down the internet, banned rights groups and used new laws to silence opposition. Many fear the vote could spark fresh violence and deepen political divides.
