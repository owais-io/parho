Israel has launched new air attacks on Tehran and Beirut while Iran is hitting U.S. military sites in the Gulf. The fighting is in its fifth day, with many civilians dead or injured. Iran’s leader, Ayatollah Khamenei, was killed in Israeli strikes, and clerics are choosing a new Supreme Leader. U.S. and Israel say the strikes have damaged Iran’s missiles and air defenses, but the war’s final goal is unclear.
