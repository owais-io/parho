Floyd Mayweather and Manny Pacquiao are scheduled to fight again on September 19 in Las Vegas. The rematch will be shown live on Netflix, letting fans around the world watch from home. Both fighters are in their late 40s – Mayweather is 49, Pacquiao 47 – and neither are at their peak form. The match will still bring in millions of dollars for the two champions and adds to Netflix’s growing sports portfolio.
