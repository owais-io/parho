Wes Streeting, Labour’s health secretary, said he doesn’t want Keir Starmer challenged after the May elections. He wants stability, especially with the Iran conflict, and says any leadership fight could hurt the party. Streeting may run only if forced, but he stresses the public could see it as chaos. He also defended releasing WhatsApp messages and talked about NHS and data contracts.
