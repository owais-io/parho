Miles Cross, 33, was jailed for 14 years after he sold a deadly chemical to people who wanted to kill themselves. He sent the substance to four strangers he met on an anonymous suicide forum. Two of them used it and died. The case, the first of its kind in the UK, involved a big police investigation that shut down the forum and tracked Cross’s online activity. Police hope the sentence will warn others and protect vulnerable people.
