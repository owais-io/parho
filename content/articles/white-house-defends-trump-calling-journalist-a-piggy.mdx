The White House defended Donald Trump after he called a Bloomberg reporter a “piggy” on Air Force One. Press secretary Karoline Leavitt said Trump’s bluntness and daily access to the press are valuable, while ignoring the journalist’s question about the Epstein files. The remark sparked backlash from reporters, CNN’s Jake Tapper, former Fox anchor Gretchen Carlson, and the Society of Professional Journalists, who condemned the insult as part of a pattern of hostility toward women reporters.
