German leader Friedrich Merz said the war in Ukraine will only stop when Russia is financially or militarily worn out. He made the comment at the Munich Security Conference, where leaders from France, Britain, the U.S., and others talked about peace talks. Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, said elections could happen after a short cease‑fire. Britain pledged more weapons, and China said it might send humanitarian aid. Talks are also set for Geneva.
