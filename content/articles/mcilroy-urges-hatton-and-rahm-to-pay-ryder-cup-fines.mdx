Rory McIlroy has challenged golfers Tyrrell Hatton and Jon Rahm to settle the multi‑million‑pound fines that the DP World Tour has imposed because they joined LIV Golf. He says the money is essential for the Ryder Cup, where Europe’s chances could be jeopardised if the fines stay unpaid. McIlroy also warned that uniting the PGA, DP World and LIV tours seems impossible right now.
