Starmer’s allies have launched a “Stop Andy Burnham” drive to block the Greater Manchester mayor from standing in a new by‑election after MP Andrew Gwynne resigns. The Labour NEC is full of Starmer supporters who fear Burnham could use the seat to challenge the leader. Burnham’s return would be costly and politically risky, so the party is planning to keep him out of the selection process.
