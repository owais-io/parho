Elon Musk’s SpaceX is buying AI start‑up xAI in a $1.25 trillion all‑share deal, a move that has excited fans but alarmed minority shareholders. They worry the price is inflated, that the buy‑out is really a bailout for the loss‑making xAI, and that investors in SpaceX will now own a company that also includes the controversial X social‑media platform. The deal could dilute the rocket firm’s focus.
