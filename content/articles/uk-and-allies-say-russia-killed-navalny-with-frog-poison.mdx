Five countries – the UK, France, Germany, Sweden and the Netherlands – say the Russian state used a poison from South‑American dart frogs to kill opposition leader Alexei Navalny while he was in a Siberian prison. Lab tests found the toxin epibatidine in his body. The UK will report the case to the chemical‑weapons treaty body. Russia’s past poisonings, like the Skripal attack, add weight to the claims.
