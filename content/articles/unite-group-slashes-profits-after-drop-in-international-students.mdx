Unite Group, the UK's largest student housing provider, lowered its profit forecast again after a sharp fall in international student numbers. With only 68% of its beds booked for next year, the company cut rents in places like Nottingham, Leicester, and Sheffield and is selling or pausing new projects. It plans to focus on cities with high‑paying universities, sell properties, and cut costs to stay afloat.
