A 43‑year‑old TikTok star, known as Tea Tyme, was arrested for the death of a 59‑year‑old man who was hit by her car in Zion, Illinois. She was livestreaming her drive when the crash happened and later posted a video admitting she hit someone. Authorities charged her with reckless homicide. The victim’s family said they were relieved to see justice move forward.
