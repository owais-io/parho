Former Doctor Who star Noel Clarke was arrested on Tuesday over allegations of attempted rape, sexual exposure and touching that allegedly happened in 2007. Police say he is also linked to a 2013 voyeurism case. He was questioned by detectives and released on bail while the Metropolitan Police investigate further. The case follows a 2021 Guardian story that Clarke successfully fought in court.
