UK households are feeling very worried about their money, with a recent survey showing consumer confidence at its lowest in two years. Most people say they are adding to their debt faster than before, especially young adults aged 18‑24. Lower savings and less willingness to make big purchases signal a slow economy. The gloomy mood is worsened by bad weather and a shortage of credit.
