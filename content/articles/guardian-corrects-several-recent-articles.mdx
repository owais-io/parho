The Guardian issued a corrections notice fixing mistakes in several pieces. It clarified that Tony Benn and Roy Jenkins were wrongly cited as candidates to replace Jim Callaghan, when it was actually Harold Wilson. It also corrected that Sam Tarry was not barred from running again and that Paul Sweeney is a Scottish Labour MSP, not an MP. Other errata covered gas‑price comments, mining waste, and a cancelled Morrissey concert. Readers can send complaints to guardian.readers@theguardian.com.
