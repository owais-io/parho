James Trafford said he didn’t expect Manchester City to sign Gianluigi Donnarumma, who quickly became Pep Guardiola’s first‑choice keeper. Trafford played the first three league games this season but has since been limited to cup and European matches. He’s signed a five‑year deal but is unsure if he’ll stay beyond the summer. Trafford says he’ll keep working hard and see what happens next.
