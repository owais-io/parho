Australia’s leading fast bowler Pat Cummins, who missed the first Ashes Test because of a back injury, may be cleared to play in the second test at the Gabba. He’s been training well, and coach Andrew McDonald and captain Steve Smith say he’s close to being ready. If he returns, Australia might drop spinner Nathan Lyon to use an all‑seam attack. England’s Ben Stokes says they’ll still aim to win.
