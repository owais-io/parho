England is launching the first of nine new river walks, starting with the 13‑mile Mersey Valley Way in Greater Manchester. The plan will upgrade existing river paths, add signs, and make them wheelchair‑friendly. The goal is to give people, especially those from low‑income households, easier access to nature and boost local businesses. More walks will be added by 2026 through a bidding process.
