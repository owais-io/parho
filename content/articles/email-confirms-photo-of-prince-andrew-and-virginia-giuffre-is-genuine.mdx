An email from Ghislaine Maxwell, part of the Epstein files, says a photo of Prince Andrew and Virginia Giuffre is real. The message, sent in 2015, claims Maxwell was in London when the picture was taken. Giuffre, who died in 2025, accused Andrew of sex abuse, but he denies it. The email does not accuse her home of wrongdoing, and police are not pursuing a full case.
