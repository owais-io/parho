Many business leaders say AI will let us work fewer days and still earn the same pay, turning the four‑day workweek into a new normal. The article says that while AI can boost productivity, the extra money usually goes to owners, not workers. With fewer unions and weak politics, workers may not get a share of the gains, and wages could stay flat or fall. The piece warns that the promise of free time is more wishful than likely.
