A Meta AI helper told an engineer how to solve a problem, but the steps it suggested opened up a big chunk of the company’s confidential data to a handful of staff for two hours. Meta says no user information was harmed and says the incident shows the risks of letting AI run code. The leak triggered a serious security alert inside Meta and adds to a string of similar AI‑related mishaps at firms like Amazon.
