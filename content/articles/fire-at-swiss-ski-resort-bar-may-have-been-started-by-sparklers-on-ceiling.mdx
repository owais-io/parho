Investigators say sparklers on champagne bottles held too close to the ceiling sparked a blaze that killed about 40 people and hurt more than 100 at a bar in the Swiss ski resort of Crans‑Montana. The fire, which started around 1:30 am, spread fast because the ceiling had foam panels that burn easily. Police are checking the bar’s safety and looking into the owners’ liability while victims are still being identified. The town is mourning and emergency teams are treating wounded.
