The U.S. Department of Justice has turned into President Trump’s political tool. Ex‑prosecutors say it now launches investigations against rivals like Fed Chair Jerome Powell and Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz, while FBI raids a Georgia election office to prove Trump’s false fraud claims. Courts have thrown out many cases, and dozens of senior prosecutors have quit, saying the DOJ no longer serves fair justice.
