Nottingham Forest is trying to sign Crystal Palace striker Jean‑Philippe Mateta with a £35m offer. The French forward wants to leave Palace, which wants a big package and is also chasing a replacement. Forest, who signed Lorenzo Lucca on loan last week, are 17th in the Premier League. Other clubs like Juventus and Aston Villa are also interested, but Palace says they will not sell without a big deal.
