A court decided that Tower Hamlets Mayor Lutfur Rahman cannot cancel the low‑traffic neighbourhoods in Bethnal Green, Shoreditch and Columbia Road. The schemes were part of a Transport for London plan. Residents, schools and the NHS supported the restrictions, saying they keep streets safer and help people walk and cycle. The judge said the mayor had no power to remove the plans. Tower Hamlets council plans to appeal.
