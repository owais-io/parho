Erika Brooke, 95, died after a long life of sewing and caring in Britain. Born in Czechoslovakia, she fled the war and the Russians, then found work as a seamstress in Bournemouth. She married, had two daughters, and later worked for Poole General Hospital, eventually becoming the sewing room supervisor. Erika’s story shows resilience and a love for the country that welcomed her.
