Victoria’s Liberal leader Jess Wilson announced a plan to expand the city’s inner‑area zoning, allowing taller buildings in places like Collingwood and Fitzroy, and to speed up outer‑suburb development. Premier Jacinta Allan’s Labor government wants to build 300,000 homes in 50 city zones by 2051, focusing on transit‑linked sites. The debate mixes politics and planning, as each side uses housing to win young voters.
