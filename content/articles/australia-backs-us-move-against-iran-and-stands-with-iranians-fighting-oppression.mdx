Australia says it supports US action to stop Iran from getting nuclear weapons and warns of possible retaliation. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese condemned Iran’s regime for attacks on Australians and called it oppressive. The government updated travel alerts for Israel, Lebanon and Iran, urging people to leave if safe. It also activated a crisis centre for Australians abroad. Opposition leaders echoed the criticism, stressing Israel’s right to defend itself.
