In July 2020, President Trump ordered the US out of the World Health Organization, citing mistrust. Biden reversed it in January 2021, but Trump pulled the country out again in January 2025. The move is the second time a major power has left the WHO. The article explains why the WHO is independent, how the US has long funded it, and how the withdrawal hurts global health cooperation.
