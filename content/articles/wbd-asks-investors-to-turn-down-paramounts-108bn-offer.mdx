Warner Bros Discovery (WBD) told its shareholders to reject Paramount’s $108 bn cash takeover bid, calling it too low and risky. WBD has already agreed to sell its studios, HBO and streaming service to Netflix for $82.7 bn, a deal seen as more secure and valuable. Paramount claims it has all the money it needs and will push the offer to shareholders, but regulators and WBD’s board doubt its backing and warn of extra costs.
