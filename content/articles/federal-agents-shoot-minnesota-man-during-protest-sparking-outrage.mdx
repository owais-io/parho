A 37‑year‑old Minneapolis nurse, Alex Pretti, was shot dead by federal immigration officers while filming a protest. Video shows he was unarmed and being pepper‑sprayed, but officials claim he had a gun. The incident sparked protests and the state governor asked the federal agents to leave. A judge ordered the evidence preserved, and local police say the investigation is blocked.
