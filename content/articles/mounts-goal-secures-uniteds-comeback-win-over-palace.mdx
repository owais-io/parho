United beat Crystal Palace 2‑1 at the Emirates, ending Palace’s long winning streak there and United’s five‑year wait for a home victory. Palace first led with a penalty that was retaken and scored by Jean‑Philippe Mateta. United turned it around in the second half, with Joshua Zirkzee scoring first and Mason Mount equalising. The win lifts United up to seventh place and boosts manager Ruben Amorim’s confidence.
