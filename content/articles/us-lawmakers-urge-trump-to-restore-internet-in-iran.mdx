A bipartisan group of US lawmakers has written to President Donald Trump, asking him to let the government help Iranian citizens get online again. Iran’s government has blocked the internet to crush protests that have killed thousands. The lawmakers want the State Department to work with a nonprofit that can bypass censorship. They say the crisis needs urgent action, even before a new law passes, and that the U.S. should push for a safer, free internet.
