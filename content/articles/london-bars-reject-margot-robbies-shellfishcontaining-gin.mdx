Margot Robbie’s gin, Papa Salt, was rejected by many London bars because it contains oyster shells, which trigger a shellfish allergen warning. The warning forces venues to train staff to ask guests about shellfish allergies, and some say the risk isn’t worth it. Robbie will remove the shells and launch an oyster‑free version in the UK by the end of 2026. The bottles still list Australian botanicals, but the allergen issue remains a sticking point.
