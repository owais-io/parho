Liverpool were beaten badly by PSV, sparking worry about coach Arne Slot’s future. Slot said he feels secure because club bosses back him, and that questions are normal when results are poor. He wants to turn things around quickly. A Liverpool midfielder, Curtis Jones, shared his frustration and said the team must change fast. The club’s slump is its worst since the 1950s.
