New studies suggest GLP‑1 weight‑loss injections not only cut food cravings but also lower the risk of drug addiction in US veterans. The medicines may help treat substance abuse, but critics worry about cost, side effects and moralising obesity as a choice. Experts argue both obesity and addiction are medical conditions, so the drugs should be viewed as legitimate treatments, not just a quick fix.
