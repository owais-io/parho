Donald Trump ordered U.S. forces to carry out airstrikes on Islamic State targets in Nigeria on Christmas Day, saying it was a response to the militants killing Christians. It was the first U.S. attack in Nigeria, carried out with Nigerian troops under Africa Command. Trump had earlier warned Nigeria that Christian killings were a threat, sparking controversy. Nigerian officials denied framing the violence as religious persecution.
