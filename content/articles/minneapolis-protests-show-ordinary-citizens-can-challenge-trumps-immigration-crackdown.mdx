In recent weeks, people in Minneapolis have taken to the streets to fight back against the Trump administration’s tough immigration tactics. The protests forced the removal of the federal immigration chief from the city, and many locals helped each other by providing food, medical care and legal support. The demonstrations show that ordinary citizens can stand up to powerful officials, and that community solidarity can shape the future of U.S. immigration policy.
