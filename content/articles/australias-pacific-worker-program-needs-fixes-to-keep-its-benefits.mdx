The Palm scheme brings Pacific workers to Australia, helping them, local businesses, and Australia’s diplomatic standing against China. Workers face abuse, low pay, and bad housing, while short‑term jobs decline and long‑term visas dominate. A new report offers 10 ways to improve the scheme—easier job changes, simpler rules, health coverage, and cutting cheaper backpacker programs. Fixing these issues will keep the program a win for everyone.
