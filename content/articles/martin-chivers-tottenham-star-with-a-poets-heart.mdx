Martin Chivers joined Tottenham in 1968 after a big transfer from Southampton. A knee injury kept him out for almost a year, but he later formed a strong partnership with Alan Gilzean. He scored 174 goals, won two League Cups, and helped Spurs win the UEFA Cup in 1972. After leaving in 1976 he played in Switzerland and later Norway, then returned as a club ambassador. His talent and calm personality made him a Spurs legend.
