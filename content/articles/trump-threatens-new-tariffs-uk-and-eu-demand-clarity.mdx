Donald Trump says he could raise U.S. tariffs to 15% on imports, worrying the UK and EU. They want clear rules on how the new rates affect last year’s trade deals. Businesses fear extra costs. U.S. stocks fell after the news. Customs will stop collecting emergency‑power tariffs next week, adding uncertainty. This uncertainty could hit exporters and consumers, as extra duties may raise prices. Officials in both sides are meeting to resolve the confusion.
