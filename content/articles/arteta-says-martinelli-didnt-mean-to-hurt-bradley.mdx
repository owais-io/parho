Arsenal boss Mikel Arteta defended his 24‑year‑old forward Gabriel Martinelli after the young player was almost sent off for trying to help an injured teammate, Conor Bradley, get off the pitch. Arteta said Martinelli probably didn’t realise what he was doing. Liverpool coach Arne Slot echoed the support, noting that time‑wasting tactics make players nervous. Bradley will get a knee scan, and Arsenal will take the draw as they battle for top spot.
