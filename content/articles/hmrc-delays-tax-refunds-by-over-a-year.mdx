People are waiting more than a year for the UK tax authority (HMRC) to return money they overpaid on tax and National Insurance. A Guardian investigation found refunds that used to take a few weeks are now taking up to ten months. Workers, pensioners and self‑employed people are struggling while HMRC says it will investigate and invest £500 m in digital services to speed things up.
