Japan seized a Chinese fishing boat near Nagasaki and arrested its captain after the vessel ignored an inspection order. The move is the first time Japan has taken a Chinese boat since 2022 and is part of a crackdown on illegal fishing. The incident comes amid rising tensions with China over Taiwan and disputed islands, adding fuel to an already strained relationship.
