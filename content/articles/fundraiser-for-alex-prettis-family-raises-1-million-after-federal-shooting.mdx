After federal immigration officers shot nurse Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, an online fundraiser has collected over $1 million for his family. The GoFundMe page, launched by organizer Keith Edwards, quickly surpassed its $20 k goal and now has more than 29 000 donors. The money goes straight to Pretti’s parents. His death, captured on video, sparked protests and criticism of the Trump administration’s immigration crackdown.
