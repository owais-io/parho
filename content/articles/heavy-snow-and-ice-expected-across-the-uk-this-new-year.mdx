A big cold wave is coming to the UK, bringing heavy snow and ice to most regions during the New Year. Friday will start with snow in Scotland and sleet in England, with up to 40 cm of snow possible in northern Scotland. Temperatures will dip below freezing at midnight. The storm could shut roads, delay flights, and power some rural areas. Health officials warn that the cold could hurt older people and those with health problems.
