Manchester United fan Frank Ilett promised not to cut his hair until the team won five games in a row. The promise went viral, earning him followers, sponsorships and media attention. When United kept losing, Ilett's hair grew, and the story turned into a symbol of how sports fans now chase online attention instead of just the game. The article questions if football is still about winning or just about generating content.
