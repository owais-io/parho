Federica Brignone, the Italian skier from Cortina, won her second gold medal in just three days at the Winter Olympics. She took the women's Super‑G and then the giant slalom, beating the field by more than a second in a race where the others were very close. Brignone recovered from a serious knee injury last year and is now back on top, while U.S. star Mikaela Shiffrin, who is still healing from a crash, finished 11th.
