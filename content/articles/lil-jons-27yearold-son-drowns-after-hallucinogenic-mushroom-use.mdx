Nathan Smith, 27, the son of rapper Lil Jon, died after falling into a pond north of Atlanta while under the influence of hallucinogenic mushrooms. Georgia police found his body in early February, and an autopsy confirmed drowning caused by psilocybin use. The death was ruled accidental. Lil Jon shared the sad news and praised his son’s kindness and talent.
