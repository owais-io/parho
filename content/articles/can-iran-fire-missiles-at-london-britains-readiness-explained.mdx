Israel’s army said Iran can launch 4,000‑km missiles that could hit London after Iran fired at a US‑UK base on the Chagos Islands. Iran denies it. The UK has let the US use its bases for limited defensive strikes and says NATO’s missile‑defence system keeps Britain safe. London officials say there is no evidence Iran plans to target Europe, but they are ready to defend against any attacks.
