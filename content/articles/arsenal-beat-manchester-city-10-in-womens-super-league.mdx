Arsenal clinched a 1‑0 win over Manchester City in the Women’s Super League, with Olivia Smith scoring the only goal early in the game. The Gunners dominated the midfield, thanks to Kim Little and Mariona Caldentey, and praised the crowd’s energy. City struggled to break the lead and managed only a few shots. Arsenal’s win keeps their unbeaten home run intact.
