Trump at the White House said UK warships are “toys” and that NATO hasn't helped in US‑Israel strikes on Iran. He laughed off the UK's offer of aircraft carriers, saying the US didn't need them. Britain has moved HMS Dragon to the eastern Mediterranean after an Iranian missile hit a UK base. UK PM Keir Starmer rejected Trump’s insults and later warned of a two‑front war with Russia at a Finnish summit.
