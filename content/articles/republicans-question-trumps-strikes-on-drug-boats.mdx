Trump’s defense secretary Hegseth said the navy destroyed a suspected Venezuelan drug boat, but some Republicans are upset after a second strike that many say killed survivors. Hegseth denied ordering the second hit, and the White House says it was legal. Senators from both parties are asking for video footage and a briefing, while some want to hold officials accountable.
