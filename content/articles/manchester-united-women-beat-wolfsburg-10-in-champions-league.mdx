The match was tight, but United edged out Wolfsburg 1‑0 thanks to an early goal. United’s players held possession from the start, while Wolfsburg had several chances that hit the post or were off‑side. Riviere suffered an ankle injury after a tackle. United changed a few starters – Awujo, Rolfö, and Malard – and the win keeps them on track for the quarter‑finals.
