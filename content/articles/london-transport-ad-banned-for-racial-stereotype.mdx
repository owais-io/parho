London’s transport authority ran a Facebook video that showed a black teen harassing a white girl. The Advertising Standards Authority said the clip reinforced a harmful stereotype that black men are violent. TfL said the full film had a mixed cast and showed different hate crimes. The ASA banned the short ad, telling TfL to avoid racial stereotypes in future ads.
