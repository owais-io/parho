South Africa’s president, Cyril Ramaphosa, said President Trump’s decision to let white Afrikaners apply for refugee status in the U.S. is racist and uninformed. He criticised Trump for false claims about a “white genocide” in South Africa and for ignoring the country’s apartheid past. Trump, meanwhile, is targeting South Africans with misinformation about land and has cut the U.S. refugee program for war victims.
