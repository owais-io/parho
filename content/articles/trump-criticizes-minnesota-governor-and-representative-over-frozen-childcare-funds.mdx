Donald Trump slammed Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz and U.S. Rep. Ilhan Omar after the Biden‑era agency paused federal money for state‑run child‑care programs. Trump blamed Somali immigrants for fraud, calling Omar “an ungrateful loser,” and said the freeze was a political hit. Walz said the move hurts working families and is a partisan ploy. The dispute stems from alleged fraud in Somali‑run daycares, though the real amount is far lower than Trump claims.
