The article is written by George Newhouse, a former mayor of Waverley council, about the recent attack on the Jewish community in Bondi. He shares his personal ties to the area and says the killings shocked everyone. Newhouse stresses that no Jew should feel unsafe in Australia because of politics or religion. He urges all Australians to stand together, speak out against hate, and give the Jewish community the same protection and respect as everyone else.
