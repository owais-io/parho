New documents show how Ghislaine Maxwell helped Jeffrey Epstein groom teenage girls. The files describe her using friendly, playful language to control victims, telling them not to be afraid and pushing them into sexual acts. She also told girls how to please Epstein. The evidence comes from court testimonies and interviews. Maxwell is serving a 20‑year sentence and has faced criticism for downplaying ties with former President Trump. The case highlights serious abuse of minors.
