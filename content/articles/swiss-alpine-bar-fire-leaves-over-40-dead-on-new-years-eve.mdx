A New Year’s Eve party at the Le Constellation bar in Crans‑Montana turned deadly when sparklers set off a fire in the low ceiling. The blaze spread fast, trapping many people in a crowded basement. More than 40 people died and dozens were badly burned. Firefighters had to smash windows to get in, and ambulances rushed injured victims to hospitals. The Swiss flag flew at half‑mast for five days as the nation mourned the tragedy.
