Over the past few days Iran has stopped almost all oil and gas ships from passing through the Strait of Hormuz. Drone attacks hit four tankers and commercial traffic fell 80%. While the US Navy has taken out many of Iran’s small warships, Iran threatens to burn any ship that tries to cross. Because of the danger, insurers are canceling coverage and oil prices have jumped. Brent crude is up 15%.
