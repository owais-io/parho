Palantir sued the Swiss magazine Republik after a joint investigation by journalists showed the company had tried and failed to sell software to the Swiss government. The article claimed Palantir’s attempts were aggressive, which the company says is false. Palantir wants a “right of reply” in court, while Republik and its journalist partners argue they followed journalistic standards. Critics say the suit is intimidation and threatens press freedom.
