After losing 3‑2 to young star Charlie Manby, 20‑year‑old Cameron Menzies smashed a drinks table in fury, bleeding on the stage. He was escorted out by the PDC chief and later apologized. Menzies has a history of mental‑health struggles—his father had heart surgery last year and his uncle died recently. The incident highlights how the pressure, fan heckling and a fast‑paced game can affect players, and reminds fans of mental‑health helplines.
