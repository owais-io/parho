ICE chartered the Gulfstream jet of Florida developer Gil Dezer, a long‑time Trump supporter, to fly eight Palestinian men—many shackled and wearing prison clothes—to Tel Aviv. After refuelling stops in New Jersey, Ireland and Bulgaria, the men were handed over to Israeli forces and taken to a West Bank checkpoint. The flights cost up to half a million dollars and have drawn criticism for bypassing normal deportation procedures.
