Everton’s manager David Moyes praised the team’s strong away results, noting they’ve won more road games than in recent years. He believes the new stadium’s feel is affecting home performance and wants the club to translate that positivity into the Hill Dickinson Stadium. Moyes also set a higher goal: achieving European qualification and restoring Everton’s reputation on the big stage.
