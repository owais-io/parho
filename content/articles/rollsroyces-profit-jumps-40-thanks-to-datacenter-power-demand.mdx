Rolls‑Royce made 40% more profit last year, mainly because more data centres need power to run AI. The company’s chief executive, Tufan Erginbilgiç, has cut costs and secured better deals, lifting earnings in both its engine and power‑systems divisions. It will also buy back up to £9 bn of shares over three years. Shares jumped 7%, sending the FTSE 100 to a new record.
