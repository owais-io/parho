Sean Bell, a One Nation senator elected to represent New South Wales, has been living in Queensland for the first four months in office, according to parliamentary expense records. He spent over $6,000 on chauffeured rides in Brisbane and almost $5,000 on flights between Brisbane, Canberra and Sydney. Bell also used travel funds to visit regional NSW towns. His stay in Queensland has drawn criticism, especially since senators are expected to live in the state they represent.
