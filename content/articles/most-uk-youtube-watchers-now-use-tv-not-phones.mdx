New research shows UK households now watch more YouTube on TVs than on phones, tablets or laptops. Over half of all home Wi‑Fi YouTube streams happen on the TV, and the trend spans all age groups, with younger people catching up last year. Live TV still makes up almost half of TV viewing. Regulators warn the platform’s growing dominance, and the BBC will start making shows just for YouTube.
