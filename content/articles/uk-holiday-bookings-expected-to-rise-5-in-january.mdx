UK holidaymakers are planning more trips in January, with travel firms predicting a 5% jump in package bookings compared to last year – about 200,000 extra trips. The first weekend after Christmas, called Sunshine Saturday, will see the most bookings. Travelers are favouring quieter spots like Gozo and longer trips, and many are securing holidays for summer 2026 and even 2027 ahead of time. Families love short, quality breaks.
