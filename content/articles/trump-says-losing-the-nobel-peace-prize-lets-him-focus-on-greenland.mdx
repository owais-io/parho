Trump wrote to Norway’s prime minister that not getting the Nobel Peace Prize means he no longer feels bound to “think purely of peace,” and that’s why he’s threatening to seize Greenland. He has even threatened a 10% tariff on goods from several European countries until the US can take the Arctic island. The Norwegian PM clarified the prize isn’t awarded by Norway, and European leaders have condemned Trump’s threats as blackmail.
