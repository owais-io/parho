He says sorry for keeping a friendship with Jeffrey Epstein after Epstein was convicted in 2008. The Labour peer was removed from a U.S. ambassador role because of this link. In a BBC interview he tried to distance himself, but later admitted he was wrong and had no knowledge of Epstein’s crimes. He apologises to the victims and says his continued association was a “terrible mistake” and “misplaced loyalty.”
