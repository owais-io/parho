Heather Jones, an Alabama woman, settled a misdemeanor case after she said a Catholic priest, Robert Sullivan, gave her money in exchange for sex when she was 17. The priest quit his post in November. Jones agreed to two years’ probation for allegedly practicing law without a license. She says the legal move was a tactic to silence her, and she now feels she has closed the chapter on the abuse.
