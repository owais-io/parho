Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta says Kai Havertz should be back soon after a knee injury. The German striker has been out since August and is close to training again. Arteta believes Havertz will add a new spark to the attack. Meanwhile the team is juggling other injuries, like Martinelli and Hincapié, and still has new forwards Viktor Gyökeres and Gabriel Jesus. Arsenal stay top of the table and have kept a clean disciplinary record.
