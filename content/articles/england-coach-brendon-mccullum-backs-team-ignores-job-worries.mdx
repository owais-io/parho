England coach Brendon McCullum says he isn’t worried about losing his job if England keeps losing. He keeps the same batting group for the third Ashes test at Adelaide and believes the players can win. He’s only looking at bowling changes, hoping fast bowler Josh Tongue will start and giving Jofra Archer a chance to bowl hard. McCullum says the team must stay focused and confident to turn the series around.
