Senator Bernie Sanders and Representative Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez introduced a bill to halt the construction of new AI data centers. The move comes as energy costs climb, water shortages worsen, and communities worry about the environmental and economic impact of these massive facilities. The proposal would give lawmakers time to create federal rules to protect workers, curb energy use, and keep power bills affordable. Many local governments and advocacy groups support a pause, and polls show growing support for tighter regulation.
