Wayve, a British startup, drove a fully autonomous electric Ford Mustang through London’s busy streets, showing that self‑driving taxis could navigate the city’s narrow roads and unpredictable pedestrians. The UK plans to approve robotaxis by the end of next year, with Wayve, Waymo and Baidu already testing fleets. The article also discusses how the technology may change the taxi industry and affect drivers and passengers in London.
