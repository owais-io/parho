New Jersey Governor Mikie Sherrill has issued an order that keeps Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) officers out of most state-owned buildings unless they have a court warrant. She also created an online portal so residents can upload videos and photos of ICE activity. The governor said the move is to protect people’s rights and stop ICE abuse. The state attorney’s office will review the reports but isn’t promising specific actions.
