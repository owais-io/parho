Nottingham Forest beat Wolves 1-0 thanks to a late header by Igor Jesus. The win came after a five‑minute VAR delay that overturned an earlier goal, leaving manager Sean Dyche frustrated. Wolves are struggling, winless since April, and the match was a low‑key affair with few chances. Forest’s captain was pleased with Jesus, who is still learning the game. Dyche said fans deserve faster decisions, noting another delay in the second half.
