In 2025, Americans used playful and clever tactics to push back against the Trump administration. They bought tiny ice scrapers at Home Depot, wore inflatable frogs outside ICE offices, and camped outside hotels where agents stayed. Protesters also targeted Tesla, canceled Disney+ subscriptions, handed out plastic whistles in Chicago, bang pots in DC, held pop‑up block parties, and ran ads encouraging ICE workers to quit. These actions kept the protest scene lively and public.
