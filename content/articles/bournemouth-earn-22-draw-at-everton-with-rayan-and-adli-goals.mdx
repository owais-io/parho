Everton lost their home advantage as Bournemouth won 2‑2. Brazilian Rayan and winger Adli scored for the hosts, while Bournemouth also hit back after a foul gave them a penalty that Rayan missed. Everton defender Jake O’Brien was sent off for a collision, ending the match in chaos. The result leaves Everton still chasing Europe but shows their struggles at Hill Dickinson Stadium.
