Police are still looking for 37‑year‑old Julian Ingram after he allegedly killed a pregnant woman, her boyfriend and her aunt in Lake Cargelligo, New South Wales. The lone survivor says Ingram laughed while shooting. An unconfirmed sighting near Mount Hope has led police to search several properties. Officers warn residents to stay safe and call if they see suspicious activity. Ingram was on bail for earlier domestic‑violence charges.
