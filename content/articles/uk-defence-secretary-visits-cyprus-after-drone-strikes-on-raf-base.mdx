John Healey flew to Cyprus to ease tensions after a low‑flying drone, linked to pro‑Iranian forces, struck the RAF Akrotiri base. The attack caused no injuries but angered Cypriot officials, who demanded better warning. Britain is sending a destroyer and counter‑drone helicopters, while France and Greece have deployed support. Families near the base have been evacuated and will stay away for a while.
