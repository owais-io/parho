Quake Brutalist Jam 3 was a six‑week community event where players and modders built over 70 new maps for the classic shooter Quake, all with a brutalist style. The jam added a brand‑new set of weapons and enemies, and even created a huge, story‑driven map that can take hours to finish. It brought together hobbyists and industry pros, keeping the old‑school 3D maze design alive while giving newcomers a chance to try their hand at level building.
