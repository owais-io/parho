New Zealand knocked South Africa out of the T20 World Cup with a 9‑wicket win in the semi‑final. Finn Allen scored an unbeaten 100 off just 33 balls – the fastest in the tournament – while the openers racked up 84 runs in the power‑play. South Africa, who had won all their earlier matches, struggled against the fast bowlers and fell 169‑run chase. The win was a surprise and a major upset.
