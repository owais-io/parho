Russian forces bombed the Ukrainian port of Odesa twice within 24 hours, damaging docks and a ship but no casualties were reported. Earlier attacks hit energy sites, causing fires and blackouts for thousands, including in Kyiv. A Russian general died when a bomb exploded under his car, an incident Russia says may be a Ukrainian hit. Meanwhile, Trump says talks to end the war are “OK,” while Ukraine’s president says peace proposals are solid but still unfinished.
