Nazar Daletskyi, a Ukrainian soldier, was declared dead in May 2023 after a fatal convoy incident. His mother, Nataliia, mourned him for almost three years. Then he turned up alive, released in a prisoner exchange, and their emotional phone call went viral. Nazar is now in a Ukrainian rehab centre, still healing, while Nataliia plans to welcome him home and keep hope alive.
