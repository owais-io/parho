Matt Goodwin, a former university researcher who studied the British National Party, is now running as a Reform Party candidate in a local election. The Guardian traces his shift from academic to outspoken far‑right activist. He has made controversial claims about immigration, minorities and democracy, and has linked himself to other right‑wing figures. Critics say his comments are hateful and he has a history of conflict. The story shows how an academic can become a political firebrand.
