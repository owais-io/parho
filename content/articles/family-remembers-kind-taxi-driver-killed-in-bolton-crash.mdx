Masrob Ali, a 54‑year‑old taxi driver from Bolton, died in a head‑on crash that also killed three teenagers in a Seat Leon that was driving at high speed. The accident happened early Sunday morning. Ali’s family says he was never at fault and describes him as the gentlest, hardworking man who loved his wife and four children. Police are still investigating the collision and are asking anyone who saw the car before the crash to come forward.
