The latest report from the UK Covid‑19 inquiry says the NHS almost collapsed during the pandemic, thanks to huge staff shortages and low capacity. NHS workers were pushed beyond their limits, and patients missed timely care. Bereaved families say the inquiry finally confirmed that the NHS was overwhelmed, contrary to government claims. The report includes 10 recommendations, from boosting capacity to better protecting staff, and calls for urgent action before the next crisis.
