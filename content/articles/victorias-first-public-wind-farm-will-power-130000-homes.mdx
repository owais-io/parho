Victoria’s State Electricity Commission has bought a 33‑turbine Delburn wind farm for $650 million. Construction starts in early 2026 and the 205‑MW plant should be fully running by 2028, enough power for 130,000 homes. It’s Victoria’s first publicly owned wind farm, showing a shift away from coal and boosting the state’s 2035 renewable target. The project will create about 300 jobs and run for 35 years.
