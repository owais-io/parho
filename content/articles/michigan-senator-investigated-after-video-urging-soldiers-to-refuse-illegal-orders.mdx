Michigan Senator Elissa Slotkin says a federal probe is underway after she and five other lawmakers posted a 90‑second video telling U.S. troops to refuse illegal orders. Trump called the clip seditious and threatened the senators, sparking death‑threats and a swatting incident. Slotkin says the investigation is part of an authoritarian backlash. The Pentagon and defense secretary have also criticized the video and are looking into the lawmakers.
