Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth was found by a DoD report to have shared secret information in a Signal chat that could have put U.S. troops at risk. The chat, which included high‑profile officials, was exposed when a reporter was mistakenly added. Hegseth declined an Inspector General interview and said he only shared harmless data. Meanwhile, Congress is watching the administration’s drug‑boat strikes in the Caribbean, with Republicans questioning the justification and the timing of the attacks.
