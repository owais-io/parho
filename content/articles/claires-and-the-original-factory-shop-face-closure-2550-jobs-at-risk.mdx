Claire's and The Original Factory Shop are on the brink of administration, threatening 2,550 jobs. Modella Capital, the private‑equity owner, says the UK high street is in a hard spot with falling holiday shoppers and rising costs. Both chains have been hit by weak consumer confidence, government fiscal policy and inflation. The move follows similar admin moves by other retailers like LK Bennett. The future of these stores and the jobs they hold remains uncertain.
