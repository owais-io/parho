New research shows deepfake scams are no longer rare tricks; they're cheap, easy to make and being used on a huge scale. Scammers now target people with fake videos of politicians, doctors, and even CEOs to lure money. In just the last year, a Singapore company lost half a million dollars to a fake video call, and UK consumers have paid about £9.4bn in fraud. Experts warn that as the tech improves, more attacks will happen.
