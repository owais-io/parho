Megan Davies, 67, has died. She spent years as Cardiff’s branch secretary for the Society of Telecom Executives, now part of Prospect, and was a passionate socialist who marched in anti‑war and anti‑racist protests. A lover of folk music, she sang in choirs and at local clubs, and she enjoyed craft beer, rugby, Spurs and cricket. Megan’s warm community spirit touched many. She is survived by her partner, sister, and brother.
