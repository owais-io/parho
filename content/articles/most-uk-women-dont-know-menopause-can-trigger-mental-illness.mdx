A new poll shows only 28% of UK women realise menopause can spark a fresh mental illness. The Royal College of Psychiatrists has issued a statement to raise awareness. The report says menopause can double the risk of bipolar and raise depression chances by 30%. Women, especially Black women, report feeling uninformed and misdiagnosed. The college urges better training, workplace policies and government action to support women.
