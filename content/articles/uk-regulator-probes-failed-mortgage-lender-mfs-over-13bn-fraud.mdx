The FCA has opened an investigation into Market Financial Solutions (MFS), a mortgage lender that collapsed after borrowing £1.3 bn from banks and hedge funds. MFS founder Paresh Raja had his assets frozen in London and Dubai. Creditors allege the firm lent money to customers linked to Raja, used double pledging, and may have committed fraud. Banks such as Barclays and Santander are seeking repayment.
