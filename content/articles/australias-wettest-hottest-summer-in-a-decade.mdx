Over the last two months, southeast Australia broke multiple heat and rain records. January saw several towns hit nearly 50 °C, and by February they received as much rain in a single week as the whole of last year. Places like Swan Hill, Horsham, Ceduna and Wudinna recorded their hottest January and then their wettest February. The heavy rains caused floods and the monsoon system left much of the country drenched.
