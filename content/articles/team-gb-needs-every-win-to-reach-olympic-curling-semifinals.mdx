Team GB must win every game on Wednesday to reach the curling semi‑finals at the Beijing Winter Olympics. They played three matches – women vs. USA, men vs. USA, and women vs. Japan – and secured all three wins. A dramatic hit‑and‑roll by Becky Morrison turned a close game into a victory. The men won 9‑2, the women 9‑3, and now they face Italy while hoping Norway or Italy lose their match.
