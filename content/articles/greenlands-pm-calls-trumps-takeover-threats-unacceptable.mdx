After Trump said the U.S. needs Greenland, Greenland’s Prime Minister Jens‑Frederik Nielsen told him to stop thinking about taking over the island. He said the comments were unacceptable and that Greenland, which is part of Denmark, will stay independent. The EU and Denmark back Greenland’s sovereignty and warn the U.S. not to threaten the island. Greenlanders are urged to be ready, but the leaders say talk must go through official channels.
