Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK will not get drawn into the wider Middle East war, even though the US wants Britain to send ships to keep shipping lanes open. He said the decision is tough but based on national interest and a calm assessment. Britain may send ships or drones but will avoid a wider conflict. He also pledged money to help households hit by rising oil prices and wants to boost renewables.
