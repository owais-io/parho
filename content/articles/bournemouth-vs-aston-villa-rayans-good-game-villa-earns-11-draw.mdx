Bournemouth hosted Aston Villa and the game finished 1‑1. Villa’s young forward Morgan Rogers opened the score after a fine finish. Bournemouth’s Rayan Ribeiro equalised with a solo effort, but the match had many shots for the home side and the Villa keeper had a shaky night. Both managers praised their teams but noted that Villa’s title chances are gone, while they’re fine in the Champions League.
