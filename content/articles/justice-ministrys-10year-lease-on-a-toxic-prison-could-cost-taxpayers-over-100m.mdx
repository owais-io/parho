The Ministry of Justice signed a 10‑year lease on HMP Dartmoor, a prison with dangerous radon gas levels, in 2022. Parliament’s spending watchdog says the deal will cost the UK more than £100m, with £4m a year in rent and extra £68m for repairs. The lease locked the government in until 2033, even though the prison was closed in 2024 after health risks were known.
