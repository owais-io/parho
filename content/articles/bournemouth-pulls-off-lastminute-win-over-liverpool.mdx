In a dramatic finish, Bournemouth scored a winner just before the final whistle. With Liverpool already two goals down, defender James Hill delivered a long throw in stoppage time. Liverpool’s goalkeeper slipped, and Amine Adli tucked the ball into the net from a tight angle, sealing a 2‑1 victory. The loss extends Liverpool’s winless run to five matches, and the result could drop them as low as eighth in the Premier League.
