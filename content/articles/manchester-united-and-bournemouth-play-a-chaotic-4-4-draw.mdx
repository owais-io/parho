United and Bournemouth smashed 8 goals in a chaotic 4‑4 tie. Bruno Fernandes and Matheus Cunha gave United early leads, but Bournemouth fought back with goals from Eli Junior Kroupi, Evanilson and a late strike that tied the game. United’s defense was shaky, with several mistakes and questionable tackles. Manager Ruben Amorim said the team can improve without adding new defenders, but the result shows the defense still needs work.
