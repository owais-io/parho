Water companies in England and Wales are sending bailiffs to tens of thousands of homes each year for unpaid bills that are under £1,000. The House of Commons committee found that companies like South West Water, Southern Water and Yorkshire Water use bailiffs most, with some sending them over 6,000 times in a single year. While a few firms say they avoid vulnerable customers, critics argue the practice is harsh and unfair, especially when the companies themselves owe huge debts.
