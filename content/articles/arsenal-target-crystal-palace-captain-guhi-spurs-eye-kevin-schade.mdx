Arsenal are eyeing Crystal Palace captain Marc Guéhi to strengthen their defence. Tottenham are after 19‑year‑old left‑back Lucas Souza, and are also considering former Genoa centre‑back Radu Dragusin for a move back to Italy. Kevin Schade from Brentford is linked to Spurs, which would reunite him with coach Thomas Frank. Other clubs are also watching players like Evan Ferguson, Jørgen Larsen, and Nathan Patterson.
