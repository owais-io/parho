The FCC’s new chair, Brendan Carr, told Senate leaders that the agency is not independent, a claim that led the FCC to remove the word from its website. Carr, a Trump supporter, has clashed with TV networks over political content and faces accusations of using his role to silence critics. Senators warned that he could push the FCC to curb free speech. The remarks come amid Trump’s push to tighten control over federal agencies.
