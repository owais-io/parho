Parents are being warned that many cheap Labubus dolls on the market are fake and unsafe. The Border Force seized 260,000 counterfeit copies last year, most of which didn't meet safety rules. The fake toys can contain choking hazards, toxic chemicals and faulty wiring. Law enforcement has shut down shops and jailed people selling them. The message: buy from trusted sellers, avoid deals that look too good to be true, and keep kids safe.
