John Herdman, former coach of Canada, is now head coach of Indonesia’s national football team. His goal is to help Indonesia qualify for the 2030 World Cup, ending a decades‑long absence. Indonesia has struggled historically due to politics, corruption and structure, but recent qualifiers show improvement. Herdman’s record with Canada’s women and men shows ability to build team unity and raise standards. The first test will be the 2026 Asian Cup, and he will work with football chief Erick Thohir.
