After Valentine’s Day, many feel the pressure to show love online, but dating apps can make it harder. Users are tired of swiping, leading to fewer subscriptions and layoffs at big apps like Bumble. New apps add tags that promise better matches, yet the reality is still ghosting and disappointment. The writer shares personal wins and losses, ending with a reminder that meeting in person can still work.
