We visited an Amazon warehouse in Georgia. The tour showed many robots moving shelves and pallets. But visitors couldn’t easily use a bathroom – the staff said you’d have to wait for a guide. Workers often report having to use water bottles for breaks. Amazon says AI will create jobs, but the company has cut many staff. Workers worry about robots replacing them.
