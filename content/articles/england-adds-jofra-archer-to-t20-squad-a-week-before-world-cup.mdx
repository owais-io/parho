England’s fast bowler Jofra Archer was left out of the squad for the Sri Lanka tour after a side strain, but he has now been added to the T20 team just a week before the World Cup starts. Archer is fully recovered and has joined the squad in Colombo for training. His inclusion gives the side more bowling options and valuable match practice before England’s opening World Cup game against Nepal.
