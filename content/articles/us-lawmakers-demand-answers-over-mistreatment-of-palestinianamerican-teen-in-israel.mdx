US lawmakers are demanding answers about what happened to Mohammed Ibrahim, a 15‑year‑old Palestinian‑American who spent nine months in Israeli detention. They say he was beaten, denied food and medical care, and that other teens suffered similar abuse. The letter to Secretary of State Marco Rubio asks if the State Department has spoken to him, if Israel is investigating the case, and if anyone is being held responsible. Lawmakers want a thorough probe and accountability.
