President Zelensky said the US has set a new deadline of June for a peace deal between Ukraine and Russia. He warned that Washington will push both sides to meet the date. Earlier deadlines in August and December failed to bring peace. Talks in Abu Dhabi last week produced no breakthrough, but Ukraine said they were constructive. The next meeting may take place in the US, possibly in Miami, and could tie to US midterm elections.
