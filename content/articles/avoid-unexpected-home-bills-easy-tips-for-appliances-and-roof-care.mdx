Keep your home running smoothly and save money by giving appliances a little TLC. Descale kettles, clean dryer lint, top‑up dishwasher salt, and defrost a freezer once a year. Flush drains with hot water and use strainers to stop clogs. Check your roof and gutters twice a year, fix cracked tiles, and keep a gutter brush handy. Service your boiler yearly, test smoke and CO alarms monthly, and set aside a small emergency fund.
