Banjo Beale, a TV interior designer, and his husband Ro are renovating a derelict mansion on Ulva, a tiny Scottish island, into a boutique hotel. They bought the island as part of a community buy‑out, aiming to bring back people and businesses. The hotel will open in summer, with Ro cooking and a part of profits staying on the island. The project shows how new residents can revive remote places.
