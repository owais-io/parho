A former techno DJ, Jose Zamora, was sentenced to almost five years in prison after running a £40m scam that sold counterfeit aircraft parts to airlines around the world. The fake parts were installed on planes from American, Ethiopian, Delta and Ryanair, causing safety alerts and grounded flights. The fraud, uncovered in 2023, cost airlines over £39m and exposed a home‑based operation that forged certificates and records.
