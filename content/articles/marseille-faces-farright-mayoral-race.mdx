Marseille’s next mayor could be a far‑right politician. National Rally’s Franck Allisio is tied with the current socialist mayor, Benoît Payan, in polls. The city’s voters worry about crime, drug gangs, and policing, and Allisio promises more police and cameras. The election on 15 March is watched across France, as a win would signal the far‑right’s growth in a once‑progressive city.
