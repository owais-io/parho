After a man was shot by police in Bridgeport, an ambulance that arrived at the scene was first taken by a white officer who was part of the chase. He used the ambulance even though he didn’t need medical help. The ambulance only left for the victim 14 minutes later, and the victim, Dyshan Best, died in the hospital. A state probe says the shooting was justified but questions the delay. The officer is on leave while investigations continue.
