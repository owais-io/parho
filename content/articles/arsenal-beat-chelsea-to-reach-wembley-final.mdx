Arsenal’s win over Chelsea in the Carabao Cup semi‑final sends them to Wembley for a final against Manchester City or Newcastle. Manager Mikel Arteta said the 4‑2 aggregate victory gives the team a much‑needed boost and “vitamins” to keep going. With a six‑point lead in the Premier League, Arsenal are chasing an unprecedented quadruple. Arteta and players say the win fuels their confidence and keeps their hopes high.
