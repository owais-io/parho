After a two‑year search, police found the remains of Belgian backpacker Celine Cremer in Tasmania’s Tarkine rainforest. She vanished in June 2023 while hiking near Philosopher Falls. Searchers discovered her phone, then bones, teeth and a car key in the riverbed and surrounding area. Forensic tests are confirming the remains are hers, and investigators suspect she got lost, dropped her phone, and may have drowned in the Arthur River. The case is still under investigation.
