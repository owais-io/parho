Labour MP Joani Reid has resigned the party whip after her husband, David Taylor, was arrested on suspicion of spying for China. In February, Taylor’s company gave Reid £2,400 for media training. Reid says she has no ties to his business and denies any wrongdoing. The case is still under investigation, and she says she has not been questioned by police.
