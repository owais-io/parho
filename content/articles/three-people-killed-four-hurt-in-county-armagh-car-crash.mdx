On Saturday night, a three‑vehicle crash on Armagh Road near Moy killed three people and hurt four. A red BMW, grey Volkswagen and white Audi collided, killing a 31‑year‑old man, a 48‑year‑old man and a 23‑year‑old woman. Ambulances, firefighters and hazardous‑area teams rushed to the scene, and the road remains closed while police investigate. Officials and local politicians expressed deep sympathy for the families.
