Eight‑year‑old Australian girl Chloe Jeffries died when a snowmobile rolled in Japan’s Hakuba Valley. She was on a family holiday when the vehicle overturned near an uphill curve, trapping her under the machine. The tour company halted all snowmobile and snowshoe tours and is reviewing safety. Australia’s foreign office is offering consular support, and the incident marks the fourth Australian death on Japanese slopes this year.
