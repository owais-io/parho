A federal judge has ruled that the FBI cannot search the phones, laptops and other devices seized from Washington Post reporter Hannah Natanson. The judge said the government must protect possible classified info, but also must respect her First Amendment rights and keep her sources safe. The decision means the government will review the material itself before any search, a win for the reporter and press freedom.
