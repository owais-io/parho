A 2023 incident on Scotland’s Isle of Lewis saw 55 pilot whales die after stranding on a beach. Scientists say the whales followed a sick female struggling to give birth, and their tight social bonds pulled the pod into shallow water. The bay’s shape and fine sand made it hard for them to find deeper water, leading to a mass death. Researchers note that similar strandings have grown in size and frequency, but human noise is not blamed.
