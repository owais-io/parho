A company called Zeuz Global, registered in London, is run by Colombian men who are being sued by the U.S. for hiring former Colombian soldiers to fight for Sudan’s brutal Rapid Support Forces. The firm’s address is a flat on Creighton Road, near Tottenham. The Colombian fighters helped the RSF kill thousands, including women and children, in Sudan’s city of El Fasher. The UK is tightening checks on company directors to stop such illegal recruiting.
