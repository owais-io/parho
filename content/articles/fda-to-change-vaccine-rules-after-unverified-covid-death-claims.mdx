The FDA says it will change vaccine rules after people said COVID shots might have caused deaths, but the evidence is unconfirmed. A senior FDA staffer claimed 10 kids died after the vaccine, but no data backs it. Lawmakers and former FDA chiefs want the agency to share proof. The agency says it will look at all ages and give more rules for vaccines, especially for pregnant people and kids. Details are still pending.
