The UK Home Office started a TikTok page showing clips of people being deported and arrested, set to loud music. Critics say it turns real suffering into eye‑catching videos that make people scared. Human‑rights groups want the government to stop this and instead share hopeful stories. The Office says it wants to fight misinformation and warn people about illegal border crossings, sparking debate.
