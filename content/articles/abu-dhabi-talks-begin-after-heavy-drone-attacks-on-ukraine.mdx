Senior Ukrainian and Russian officials are meeting in Abu Dhabi for a second round of talks arranged by former U.S. President Trump. The meeting comes after a wave of drone attacks that killed at least two people in Ukraine’s Dnipropetrovsk region and injured several others in Kyiv and Zaporizhzhia. Trump says the war can end soon, but Moscow and Kyiv remain skeptical. The talks aim to prevent further strikes, but a breakthrough seems unlikely.
