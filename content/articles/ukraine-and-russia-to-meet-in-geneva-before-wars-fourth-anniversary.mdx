This week, Ukrainian and Russian leaders will hold a second round of talks in Geneva, Switzerland. The talks, led by US diplomats from the Trump administration, aim to cover a wide range of issues, from ceasefire plans to territorial claims. Russia’s side, headed by adviser Vladimir Medinsky, pushes for maximalist demands, while Ukraine refuses to give up any Donbas territory. Most observers think a breakthrough is unlikely, but the talks could set the stage for future moves.
