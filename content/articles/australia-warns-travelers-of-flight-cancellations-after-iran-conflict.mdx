Australia says travelers should expect major disruptions after attacks on Iran. Airspace over Qatar, Dubai and Abu Dhabi is closed, and airlines like Emirates, Etihad and Qatar Airways have stopped flights. Around 115,000 Australians are in the region, 11,000 of them on these carriers. The government will give travel updates and consular help, but can’t arrange repatriation flights yet. Some passengers are sleeping at Sydney Airport as airlines work to reschedule.
