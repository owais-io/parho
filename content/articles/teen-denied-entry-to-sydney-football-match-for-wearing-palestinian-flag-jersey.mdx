A 15‑year‑old girl in Sydney was stopped at the stadium gate because she was wearing a football shirt that displayed the Palestinian flag. Security asked her to take it off, and she did before entering the match. The incident sparked criticism, with the A‑League saying it was reviewing the policy. Fans said other country flags were allowed, raising questions about consistency.
