Russia is expected to be able to keep fighting Ukraine through 2026, a major military think‑tank says. The study notes Russia has spent over 7 % of its GDP on defence in 2025, double the US and triple the UK, and has built up a large war economy. But its army is losing recruits and casualties, and new missile and drone attacks threaten Europe. The report urges NATO to boost missile‑defence budgets and encourages Europe to invest more in its own military.
