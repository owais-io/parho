Prince Harry and six other famous people are suing the Daily Mail publisher for illegally gathering their personal data. Lawyers claim the paper used private investigators, tapped calls, and accessed medical records without permission. The case started in high court, and the claimants say the tactics made them feel unsafe and harmed their relationships. The trial could cost up to £38 million and may last up to ten weeks.
