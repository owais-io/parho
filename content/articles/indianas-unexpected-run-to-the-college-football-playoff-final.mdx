Indiana’s football team had an unexpected season, finishing 11‑2 and earning a spot in the College Football Playoff. Led by coach Curt Cignetti and quarterback Fernando Mendoza, the Hoosiers beat Ohio State, Alabama, and Oregon. Their strong defense and balanced offense earned them a place in the championship game against Miami. The story also touches on campus politics and protests, highlighting a broader backdrop of social tension.
