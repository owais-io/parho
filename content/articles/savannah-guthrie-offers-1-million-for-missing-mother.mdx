Savannah Guthrie, host of NBC's Today show, has offered a $1 million reward for clues that could bring her 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy, back home after she vanished on Feb. 1. The host posted the offer on Instagram and urged anyone with information to call 1‑800‑CALL‑FBI anonymously. Guthrie’s family also donated $500 000 to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children. Investigators have found blood and DNA near Nancy’s Tucson home, but no new leads yet.
