Jordan Stolz, a 21‑year‑old speed skater from Wisconsin, is the favourite to win multiple gold medals at the Milan‑Cortina Winter Olympics. He’s already a seven‑time world champion and is expected to win the 500m, 1000m, and 1500m races, and possibly a fourth gold in the mass‑start. His dominance could make him the most talked‑about athlete at the Games, turning the sport into a global story.
