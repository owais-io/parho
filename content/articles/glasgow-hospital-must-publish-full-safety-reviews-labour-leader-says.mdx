Glasgow hospital officials face pressure to release complete patient‑safety reviews for every ward after a six‑year inquiry uncovered deadly infections linked to the building’s design. Labour’s Anas Sarwar, speaking with families of victims, demanded the government publish all reviews and called for a corporate‑manslaughter probe that could reach ministers. He said earlier warnings were ignored and the rush to open the new campus put patients at risk.
