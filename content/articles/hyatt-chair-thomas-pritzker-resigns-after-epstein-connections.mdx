Thomas Pritzker, the 75‑year‑old who ran Hyatt for 19 years, resigned as executive chair after documents showed he kept in touch with Jeffrey Epstein and Ghislaine Maxwell for years. Pritzker said he made a mistake and regrets the association. The board named Mark Hoplamazian, Hyatt’s current CEO, as his successor. His resignation follows similar exits by other executives linked to Epstein.
