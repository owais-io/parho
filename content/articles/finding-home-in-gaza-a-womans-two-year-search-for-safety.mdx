Nour AbuShammala, 26, has moved in and out of her Gaza home six times since war began, even though it’s bomb‑damaged and lacks water or power. She hopes the fighting stops, but fears it could return. Nour wants to study international law, write a book, and get her career back. She’s waiting for a border crossing to open so she can study in Jordan. UN experts say Israel’s actions in Gaza could be genocide, a claim Israel denies.
