Donald Trump said Israeli President Isaac Herzog told him that former Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu would soon get a pardon, but Herzog’s office says the claim is false. Netanyahu is on trial for corruption, and Trump has pushed for a pardon through a letter to the president. Herzog said the president has no special powers to cancel the trial and any pardon must follow legal steps. Many Israelis were upset that Trump tried to intervene in their justice system.
