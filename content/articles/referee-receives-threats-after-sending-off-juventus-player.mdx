After a controversial decision to send off a Juventus defender, referee Federico La Penna got dozens of death threats online. Police told him to stay home for safety. Fans and Juventus officials said the send‑off cost the team three points. La Penna has filed a complaint, and the referees’ association may suspend him for the wrong call. The incident highlights growing violence against football officials.
