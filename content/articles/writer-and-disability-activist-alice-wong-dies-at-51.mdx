Alice Wong, a writer who had muscular dystrophy, died at 51 after an infection. She used a powered wheelchair and wrote a memoir called *Year of the Tiger* that shared her struggles and fight against discrimination. Wong started the Disability Visibility Project to give disabled people a voice and won a 2024 MacArthur “genius” grant. Friends say she inspired many to speak up for themselves and live freely.
