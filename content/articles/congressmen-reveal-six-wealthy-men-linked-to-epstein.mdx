Democratic congressman Ro Khanna and Republican Thomas Massie demanded that the Justice Department unredact the names of six wealthy men tied to Jeffrey Epstein. The men, including Victoria’s Secret founder Leslie Wexner and Dubai billionaire Sultan Bin Sulayem, were hidden in the 3 million‑page file that was released during the Trump era. The pair accuse the DOJ of a cover‑up and call for full disclosure, while the scandal pushes lawmakers to seek stronger sexual‑offense laws.
