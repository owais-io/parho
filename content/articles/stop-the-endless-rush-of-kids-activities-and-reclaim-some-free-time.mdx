Many parents juggle school and a marathon of sports, music, and Scouts for their kids, turning the week into a packed schedule. While these activities build confidence and skills, the constant rush can feel exhausting. The author points out that when the term ends, a break comes that lets families slow down, spend time together, and breathe. The message is to enjoy the quiet holidays as a welcome pause, not a loss.
