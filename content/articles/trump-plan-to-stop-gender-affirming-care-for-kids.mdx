Trump officials want to stop hospitals from giving puberty blockers, hormones or surgeries to children. They plan to use Medicare and Medicaid rules so kids can’t get paid for this care. The agency also warned companies selling breast‑binders. It cut grants to the American Academy of Pediatrics and is changing how gender dysphoria is treated under disability law. Doctors, lawmakers and LGBTQ groups say it harms kids and must be stopped.
