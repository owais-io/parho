Arne Slot admitted Liverpool are not in the title race and that the first half of the season has felt like a constant battle. The Reds are 12 points behind Arsenal and are still trying to find their rhythm, even though they’ve gone eight games unbeaten and regained Champions League places. Slot praised the defence but said the team struggles to create chances. He also spoke about a set‑piece coach leaving the club.
