New South Wales premier Chris Minns said the state will keep funding the Sydney Biennale, even after Jewish groups slammed a DJ’s antisemitic remarks at the opening night. The artist linked Israel to Jeffrey Epstein and chanted “from the river to the sea,” sparking accusations of hate speech. Minns called the words “horrid rhetoric” and warned the Biennale must reassure Jewish audiences that the festival is safe and inclusive.
