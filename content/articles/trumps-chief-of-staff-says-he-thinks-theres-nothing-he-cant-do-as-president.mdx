Susie Wiles, Trump’s chief of staff, told Vanity Fair he believes he’s “unstoppable” and that he would retaliate against opponents if the chance arose. She described him as having an “alcoholic personality” because of his high‑energy style. Wiles also warned Trump about pardoning January 6 rioters and said he often plans to settle scores quickly. The article shows her frustration with his unpredictable decisions and criticism of other officials.
