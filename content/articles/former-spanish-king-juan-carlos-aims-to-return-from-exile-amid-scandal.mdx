After years of scandals and a fallout with Spain, former king Juan Carlos is now trying to come back to Madrid. He left in 2020 for Abu Dhabi after a hunting‑trip controversy and tax issues, and a 2023 book gave him some popularity. New releases of documents about the 1981 coup show he helped stop it, but critics say more evidence is hidden. He hopes to be welcomed as a hero, though many Spaniards remain sceptical.
