Chris Madel, the top Republican in the Minnesota governor race, quit the campaign after accusing federal immigration agents of overreach. He said ICE’s Operation Metro Surge was unconstitutional and put citizens of color at risk, citing the killings of Alex Pretti and Renee Good. Madel said the crackdown hurts Republicans and creates chaos for local police who have no choice but to follow state orders. His exit comes as some Minnesotans distance themselves from Trump‑era immigration policies.
