The Olympic Games in Bormio added skimo, a mix of skiing and mountain climbing. In the first race, Russian athlete Nikita Filippov, competing as a neutral, took silver behind Spain’s Oriol Cardona Coll. The win sparked celebration in Russia but raised questions about neutral status. Athletes noted the event’s intense physical demands and the unpredictable deep snow that slowed some competitors.
