Czech President Petr Pavel told Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy that Prague will supply L‑159 fighter jets that can shoot down drones, and may also provide radar systems. Ukraine’s air‑defence has run low on missiles, and Zelenskyy has asked allies for more help. The country will meet US officials in Miami to discuss security guarantees and post‑war plans. A poll shows most Ukrainians want strong guarantees before trading troops.
