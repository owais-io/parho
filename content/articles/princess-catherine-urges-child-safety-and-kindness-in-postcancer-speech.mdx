Princess Catherine used her first speech after cancer to say children need safe, loving homes and that caring for others matters. She talked to big employers about kindness, while the government threatened to cut money for families with children whose asylum claims failed. The piece says Britain’s politics are getting harsher, putting the royal family in a tough spot: they want to promote compassion but must stay non‑partisan. It warns that future hard‑line policies could clash with the monarchy’s values.
