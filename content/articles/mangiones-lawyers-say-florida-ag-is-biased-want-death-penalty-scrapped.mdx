Lawyers for Luigi Mangione, a 27‑year‑old accused of killing UnitedHealthcare CEO, want the death penalty dropped. They say Florida Attorney General Pam Bondi is biased because she once worked for a lobbying firm that represents UnitedHealth Group, the parent company. Mangione’s team claims Bondi’s ties could influence the case, so they ask the court to dismiss the death penalty, two federal charges, and some evidence. A hearing is set for Jan. 9.
