Mallory Duncan is a Black professional skier, sax‑player, filmmaker and entrepreneur from the Tahoe area. Growing up, he balanced school, music and backcountry skiing, seeing jazz and ski moves as similar improvisations. After missing an Olympic shot, he focused on creativity, launched his own agency and signed a sponsor during the pandemic boom in backcountry skiing. His film, *Blackcountry Journal*, blends jazz and downhill action to show that Black people belong on the slopes.
