Barry, a 52‑year‑old obese Irishman, was challenged by a friend to run the London Landmarks Half‑Marathon. He’s using a training app, Strava and Instagram to track progress and raise money for Great Ormond Street. Despite pain, weight gain and new running gear, he’s logged over 10 km and is raising £25,000 for children and inspiring others.
