After the Home Office stopped refugees from bringing children and partners to the UK, a charity called Safe Passage International is suing in the high court. Judges said the ban breaks the duty to protect children and may breach the Equality Act. The case will be heard later this year, while the government plans stricter rules for families to join asylum seekers.
