A UK Home Office deportation flight for an Egyptian man was cancelled after he swallowed a lithium vape battery. The man, who has a history of disruptive behaviour, was in detention when given a vape. He was treated in hospital and sent back to detention. The private flight, planned to fly to Albania and then Egypt, was halted, and officials are investigating. The Home Office uses expensive private planes for such cases.
