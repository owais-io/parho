Tottenham Hotspur has sacked head coach Thomas Frank after a home defeat to Newcastle, ending his tenure that began in the summer. The club said results forced a change, noting Frank leaves with the team just five points above the relegation zone. Spurs are now hunting a replacement, with names like Roberto De Zerbi and former Brighton boss under consideration.
