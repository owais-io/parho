Donald Trump fired Kristi Noem as the secretary of homeland security on Truth Social while she was giving a briefing. Noem had been blamed for chaos at DHS, including mismanaged ICE operations, the killings of detainees, and a scandal over a personal contract. Trump promised a new role for her but named Markwayne Mullin, a known hard‑liner, as his pick. The change is unlikely to alter the brutal policies and marks Trump’s first cabinet firing.
