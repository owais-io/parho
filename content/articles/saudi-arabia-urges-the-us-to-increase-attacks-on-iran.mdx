Saudi Arabia wants the US to intensify its attacks on Iran, a source said. Riyadh has stayed out of the war so far but will act if Iran keeps striking its oil plants or if Houthi rebels join in. The kingdom wants to keep its own oil routes safe while staying neutral, but it says it’s ready to step up if the situation worsens.
