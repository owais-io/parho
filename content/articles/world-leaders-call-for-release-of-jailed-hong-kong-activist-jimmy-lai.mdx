Jimmy Lai, a 78‑year‑old former Hong Kong newspaper owner, was found guilty on national‑security charges and is now facing life in prison. Governments and rights groups around the world have criticized the verdict, saying it shows how Hong Kong is curbing press freedom. Lai’s health has worsened in jail, and his family and supporters are calling on the UK and other countries to push for his release.
