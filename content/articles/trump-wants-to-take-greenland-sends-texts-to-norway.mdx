Trump sent text messages to Norway’s prime minister saying he wants full control of Greenland. He claims Denmark can’t protect the island from Russia or China and that the US should own it for security. Trump also warned he would use tariffs against Norway and blamed the country for not giving him the Nobel Peace Prize. European leaders and activists protested, with Danish caps saying “Make America Go Away.” The story shows Trump’s push for Arctic dominance.
