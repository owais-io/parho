Anthropic, a company that says it builds safe AI, has refused the U.S. Department of Defense’s demand that its chatbot Claude be cleared for mass surveillance and autonomous weapons. The Pentagon called the firm a “supply‑chain risk,” telling other businesses to cut ties. The move has sparked a public spat, boosted Anthropic’s profile, and reignited the debate over how AI should be used in war.
