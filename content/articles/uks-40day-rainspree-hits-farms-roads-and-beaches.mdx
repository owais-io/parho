For the first 40 days of the year, many parts of the UK – especially Devon, Cornwall, Worcestershire and the Midlands – have been drenched. The constant rain has flooded homes, swollen rivers, damaged roads, and left farmers with soaked fields and lost crops. Horticulturists are struggling to grow new trees, while beaches face sewage overflows. The Met Office warns more wet weather is likely to come.
