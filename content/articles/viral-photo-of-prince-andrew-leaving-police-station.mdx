After Prince Andrew was arrested, he was released from a Norfolk police station after more than ten hours of silence. A Reuters photographer, Phil Noble, captured a striking nighttime photo of him slumped in a car’s back seat, which hit front pages worldwide. Noble chased the case from Manchester to Norfolk, waiting hours in the dark. The shot was hard to get, needing skill, timing, and a bit of luck.
