A former British Transport Police officer was fired after a tribunal found he used hateful language toward Roma, Gypsies and Travellers in WhatsApp chats. The officer, who helped respond to the 2017 London Bridge attack, sent slurs like “dags” and “pikey” linking the community to crime. The panel said the remarks were deliberately discriminatory, even though he claimed they were jokes. He is not seen as racist, but his words were unacceptable.
