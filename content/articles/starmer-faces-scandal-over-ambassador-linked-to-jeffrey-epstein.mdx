Keir Starmer, Britain's Labour leader, is in trouble after appointing former minister Peter Mandelson as ambassador to Washington. Mandelson has ties to Jeffrey Epstein, a convicted abuser, and the appointment was kept secret, sparking outrage among Labour MPs. The scandal threatens Starmer’s leadership and fuels doubts about the integrity of Westminster. Starmer could fire aides or delay investigations, but the damage to his reputation may be hard to repair.
