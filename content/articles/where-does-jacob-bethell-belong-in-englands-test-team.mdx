Jacob Bethell has been batting at No. 3 for England, but his future in that spot is still unclear. He showed solid technique and calm defence in the latest Ashes test, but hasn’t hit a Test century yet. Some critics say he’s been given too much pressure, while supporters argue he needs more experience. England’s coaches are still deciding whether to keep him at No. 3 or try him elsewhere.
