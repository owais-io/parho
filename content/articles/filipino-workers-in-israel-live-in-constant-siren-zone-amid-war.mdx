The war between Israel and Iran has turned Tel Aviv into a daily siren zone. Filipino migrant workers, drawn by higher wages, are living in constant danger. Some have been killed, others face stress and fear. Many wonder whether to stay or return home, but airports are closed and their jobs are still paid. Their families back home depend on their wages, so the choice is tough.
