DJs can earn millions for a few hours of playing, but the author says they’re basically just good at selecting and playing songs that already exist. He thinks the hype around DJs makes them look like real artists, even though they’re just working a set. He respects people who love music, but he sees the DJ cult as a weird way people want a star in club nights. In short, the author thinks DJing is more hobby than true job.
