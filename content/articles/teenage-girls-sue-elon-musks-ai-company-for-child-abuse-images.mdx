Three teenage girls, two still minors, sued Elon Musk’s AI firm xAI, claiming its Grok image generator used their pictures to create and share child sexual abuse images. The girls found AI‑altered nude photos of them on Discord and other apps. Police later uncovered child sexual abuse material made with xAI’s tech. The lawsuit accuses xAI of profiting from the illegal content and failing to stop it, while Musk denies the claims.
