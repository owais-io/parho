Lewis Hamilton, now driving for Ferrari, says the new car feels like part of him and he’s in a good mental place. But his first year at the team finished with no podium and a sixth‑place finish in the drivers’ standings, behind teammate Charles Leclerc. He talks about rebuilding over the winter, staying committed to a long‑term project, and mentions the FIA’s rule change on Mercedes’ engine loophole.
