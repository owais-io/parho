Starmer has been careful about how the UK reacts to the US raid that seized Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro. While he says Maduro was not legitimate, he still backs international law and keeps comments short to avoid angering Trump. Inside the government, some worry that not condemning the move could set a dangerous precedent. Starmer may wait for a stronger signal before taking a tougher line.
