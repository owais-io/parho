US Immigration and Customs Enforcement used a private jet owned by Trump donor Gil Dezer to fly Palestinians from the US to Israel. The jet refueled twice at Shannon Airport in County Clare, Ireland, without needing Irish permission. Some passengers were handcuffed the whole trip. Irish politicians called the stops “reprehensible” and said the country should stop being used to help carry out deportations.
