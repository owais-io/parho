Victoria’s transport department is behind on its plan to let passengers pay for trips with a phone, card or watch. A dispute with the tech firm Conduent has pushed the launch back 18 months and added $136 million to costs. The auditor report says the rollout will finish for all riders by 2028, but it could face more delays. Meanwhile, a few lines have begun testing the new technology.
