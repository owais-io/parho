Two fans in Germany, who support Latvian hockey, raised a Greenland flag during the US‑Denmark game at the Winter Olympics. They said they were showing European unity and backing Denmark, since Greenland is part of the Danish kingdom. The gesture drew attention amid recent US‑Denmark tensions over Greenland. Though the teams played fiercely, the flag added a political message to an otherwise sports‑only match.
