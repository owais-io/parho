Emma Raducanu says she won’t look for a new coach before the US Open season. She’s working with former pro Alexis Canter to regain form after injuries and a viral illness. Raducanu is also a new Uniqlo ambassador and is excited to return to Wimbledon. She acknowledges online abuse but stresses resilience as key to bouncing back from setbacks in 2024.
