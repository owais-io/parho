After a tough election that left no side with a majority, Denmark’s Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen handed in her resignation, triggering the start of coalition talks. The king will meet party leaders to choose a “royal investigator” who will try to build a new government. With the moderate party holding the balance of power, the process could take several days. Meanwhile, small Ukrainian drones landed in Estonia and Latvia.
