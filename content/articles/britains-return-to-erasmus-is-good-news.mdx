The UK will re‑join the EU’s Erasmus+ student‑exchange programme in 2027, opening up free or cheap study trips across Europe. The programme, which ran before Brexit, let students spend weeks in other countries, learn new cultures, and build lasting friendships. It also helps teachers and schools join projects like eTwinning. The British Council and other groups say this will boost Britain’s global skills and bring fresh ideas back home.
