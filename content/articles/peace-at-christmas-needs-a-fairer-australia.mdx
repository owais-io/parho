The article says Christmas peace can’t come from blaming or dividing people. Instead, we must tackle the real problems that create inequality – like rising costs, unequal services, and social exclusion. Politicians often use fear to win votes, which deepens resentment. The author shows examples of people from faiths helping each other at festivals and vigils, proving that goodwill can exist when we work together. To make peace, we need a fairer Australia where everyone feels welcome and can thrive.
