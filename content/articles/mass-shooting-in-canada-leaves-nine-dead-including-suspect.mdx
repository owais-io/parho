A tragic shooting at a small school in Tumbler Ridge, British Columbia, left nine people dead, including the 18‑year‑old suspect and six children. The gunman fired at the Tumbler Ridge Secondary School, killing a teacher and five students, then died by suicide. He had previously killed a mother and step‑brother at a nearby house. Police say he acted alone. The attack is the deadliest school shooting in Canada in decades.
