Taricco, the ex‑Tottenham defender who was coaching Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors in South Korea, was fined and banned after a match‑time gesture was called a racist slanted‑eye sign. He said he was only covering his eyes to point out a handball. The K League upheld the decision, and he will leave Jeonbuk at season’s end. Fans and the club say the punishment is unfair, but the league says it was justified.
