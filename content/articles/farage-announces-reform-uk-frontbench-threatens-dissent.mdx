Nigel Farage has named the first members of Reform UK's frontbench team, bringing in former Conservatives like Robert Jenrick and Suella Braverman. He says the new lineup proves the party isn’t just his and vows to handle any disloyalty harshly. The appointees will lead key areas such as Treasury, education, and business, while Farage keeps the final say on policy.
