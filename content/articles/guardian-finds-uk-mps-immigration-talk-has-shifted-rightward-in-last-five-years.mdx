The Guardian teamed up with UCL to build a custom AI tool that reads UK MPs’ speeches and marks how they talk about immigration. By labeling over 22,000 speech snippets as positive, negative or neutral, the model can spot a clear right‑leaning trend in immigration language during the last five years. The study covers 1925‑2025, analysing about 238,000 immigration‑related fragments across all parties.
