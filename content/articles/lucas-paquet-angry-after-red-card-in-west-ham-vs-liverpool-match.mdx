Lucas Paquetá was sent off after two yellow cards for dissent in West Ham’s 2‑0 loss to Liverpool. The Brazilian, who spent two years under an FA spot‑fixing probe, exploded on social media, saying the FA didn’t give him psychological support. He apologized to fans and teammates, blaming the red card on the pressure he’s faced. West Ham’s coach said playing with ten hurt the team, and Liverpool scored soon after the dismissal.
