Pop stars like Elton John, Dua Lipa and Paul McCartney led a campaign to stop AI companies using artists’ work without permission. In a government survey, 95 % of over 10,000 respondents said copyright should be stronger and that all AI use should need a licence. Only 3 % supported the government’s idea that artists must opt‑out. Ministers dropped that plan and will update the law by March 2026.
