A Swedish research group, V‑Dem, says the United States has slipped back into autocracy faster than countries like Hungary and Turkey. They say Trump’s one‑year reign concentrated power in the presidency, weakened Congress and the courts, and lowered freedom of speech and civil rights. The report puts the U.S. at its lowest democratic score since 1965 and warns that the trend is global, with 41% of the world now under weakening democracies.
