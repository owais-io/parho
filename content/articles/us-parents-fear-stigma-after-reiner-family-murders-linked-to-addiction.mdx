When the Reiner family murders were reported, with their son Nick suspected, it highlighted addiction. Parents who have seen their own kids struggle with drugs say they worry people will focus on violence rather than the common problems of addiction. Many Americans are touched by addiction; it can affect anyone. The article shares experiences of parents who fear stigma, wonder about recovery, and stress that most addicts do not become violent. Recovery is possible but requires support and boundaries.
