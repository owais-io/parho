Dylan Lopez Contreras, a 21‑year‑old Venezuelan student at a Bronx school, was freed from an ICE detention center after ten months in confinement. His arrest last May sparked national anger over federal immigration tactics. While in jail, Dylan wrote about the loneliness, loss of his phone and missing home. Senators, including Chuck Schumer, pushed for his release. He now returns home with a GPS tag and hopes for a fresh start.
