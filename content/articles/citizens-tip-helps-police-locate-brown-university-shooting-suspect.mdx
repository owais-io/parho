A man known only as “John” posted details on Reddit about an odd encounter with the Brown University shooter. Police followed his clues and identified the suspect, Claudio Neves Valente, who also killed an MIT professor. John met the suspect in a bathroom, saw him with a gray Nissan, and later gave the police the information. The mayor thanked him, and he may earn a $50,000 FBI reward. The suspect later died by suicide in New Hampshire.
