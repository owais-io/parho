Heavy snow across Europe left Amsterdam’s Schiphol grounded about 800 flights, leaving roughly 1,000 passengers overnight in makeshift dorms as staff cleared runways. In Paris, 140 flights were cancelled and city buses were shut down, while the storm also hit Spain, the Balkans, Poland and Hungary, closing schools and blocking roads. Officials warned people to stay home and avoid travel; five people died in road crashes in France. Staff at Schiphol set up beds with pillows and blankets for stranded passengers.
