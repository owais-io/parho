England will remove the paywall that hides most land‑ownership records, making it easier to see who owns what. The change comes with a new land‑use plan that balances food production, housing, energy and climate goals. The plan will map green‑space gaps, guide builders away from flood zones, and promote carbon‑sink projects like forests and peatlands. It won’t force owners to give up land, but aims for greener, fairer use.
