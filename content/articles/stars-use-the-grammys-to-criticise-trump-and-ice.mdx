At the 68th Grammys, musicians like Billie Eilish and Bad Bunny used the stage to denounce Trump’s policies and the ICE immigration agency, saying that people should keep fighting and that love is stronger than hate. The article looks at how celebrity words can bring attention to big social issues, but also notes that many viewers think such statements are just surface‑level politics. Trump responded angrily on social media, calling the awards “worst.”
