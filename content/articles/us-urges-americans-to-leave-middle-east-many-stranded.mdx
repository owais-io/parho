The U.S. State Department told Americans in 14 Middle‑East countries to leave immediately because fighting with Iran has escalated. Major airlines have cut flights, leaving thousands stuck. The U.S. says it is arranging military and charter planes, but many people have not yet heard of a rescue plan. Some Americans are staying safe at embassies or shelters while the government works to get them home.
