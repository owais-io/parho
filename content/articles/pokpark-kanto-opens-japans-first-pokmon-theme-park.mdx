Japan has opened its first permanent Pokémon theme park, PokéPark Kanto, on February 5. The 26,000‑square‑meter park sits in the quiet suburb of Inagi, just 30 minutes from central Tokyo, and is a walkable forest with more than 600 life‑size Pokémon statues. Tickets are sold via a lottery, and the park is already sold out for three months. Visitors explore themed habitats, play Pokémon‑themed games, and watch a live Pokémon battle.
