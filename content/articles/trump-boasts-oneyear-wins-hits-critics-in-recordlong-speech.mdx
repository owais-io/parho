Trump gave his first State of the Union after returning to the White House, and it lasted over two hours, the longest ever. He bragged about a one‑year record, said the economy was booming, and blamed immigrants for a Minneapolis death—claims that experts say are wrong. Surveys show only 39% of voters view him positively, and data shows job growth slowed and energy bills rose. The speech was full of exaggerated, misleading statements.
