FIFA is raising World Cup prize money by 50%. The 2026 champions will take home $50 million, while the runners‑up get $33 million and other teams get $9 million each. Every nation is guaranteed at least $10.5 million, including a $1.5 million preparation grant. Ticket prices are still a hot topic—prices start at around $200, but FIFA has added a small discount pool for loyal fans.
