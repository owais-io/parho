During a speech in Pennsylvania, former President Donald Trump spread false statements about everyday costs, immigration, and the economy. He said prices had dropped, but inflation was rising. Trump falsely claimed that Thanksgiving turkey costs were 33 % lower and that all new jobs went to immigrants. He attacked Rep. Ilhan Omar and described Somalia as a “shithole.” Trump also bragged about stopping inflation and saving lives from boat strikes, but evidence was lacking. His remarks were widely debunked.
