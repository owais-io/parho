The UK and the world are building more ways to save surplus wind and solar power so the grid stays steady when the sun hides or the wind dies. Batteries, pumped‑hydro and new tricks like heat‑storage or AI‑guided systems are all part of the mix. By 2030 the UK wants 27GW of batteries, and other storage types are being tested worldwide.
