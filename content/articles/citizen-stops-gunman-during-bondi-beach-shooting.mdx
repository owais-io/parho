A bystander on Bondi Beach stopped one of two gunmen by wrestling the shotgun away from him during a mass shooting that killed 12 people at a Hanukah event. Video shows the man leaping onto the shooter, taking the gun, and putting it in a tree. At least 29 were injured and police call it a terrorist attack. Leaders praised the everyday Australians' courage.
