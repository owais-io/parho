Leicester owner Aiyawatt Srivaddhanaprabha says the club is in trouble but he cares deeply. He watches games from Thailand and feels fans’ pain, comparing it to looking after his son. After the manager was fired, Leicester sits 14th in the Championship and risks dropping to League One. He hopes to reach the play‑offs and notes financial rule issues, but he has no plans to sell.
