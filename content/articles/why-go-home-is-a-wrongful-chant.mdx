Hugh Muir writes that the chant “go home” is pointless because people’s identities aren’t tied to one place. He shows his own DNA shows 15 different roots and that many of us have mixed heritage. The letter includes other readers’ stories of being born in one country and living in many. It argues that racism focuses only on skin colour, not the real, complex idea of “home.”
