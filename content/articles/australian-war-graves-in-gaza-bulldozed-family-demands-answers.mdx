Wilma Spence’s father, an Australian WWII soldier, was buried in Gaza’s war cemetery. Soldiers’ graves, including hers, were bulldozed by Israel’s army, destroying the headstones. Wilma says the Israeli military blamed “terrorist tunnels,” but families doubt it. She has asked the Australian government for help finding the remains and to push Israel to protect the graves. The Australian defence and veterans’ departments say they’re monitoring the damage and will repair it when safe.
