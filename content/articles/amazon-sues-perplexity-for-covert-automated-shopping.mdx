Amazon has sued Perplexity AI over its browser‑based shopping assistant, claiming the startup secretly accesses users’ accounts and disguises AI activity as human browsing. The suit raises questions about who owns and is responsible for autonomous AI agents. Perplexity, a $20bn startup, has faced accusations of scraping copyrighted text and copying other companies’ work. Meanwhile, AI has flooded music charts and podcasts, and even sparked a state‑linked cyberattack.
