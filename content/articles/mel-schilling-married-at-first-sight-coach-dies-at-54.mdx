Mel Schilling, the Australian coach on “Married at First Sight”, passed away at 54 after a battle with advanced cancer. She had fought the disease for months, completing 16 chemotherapy rounds while filming. Mel was loved by her husband, daughter, and fans, and she earned praise from TV colleagues and contestants for her kindness. Tributes poured in from celebrities, the show’s network and supporters worldwide.
