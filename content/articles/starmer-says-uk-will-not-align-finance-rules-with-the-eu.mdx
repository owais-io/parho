Prime Minister Keir Starmer has said the UK will not bring its financial services rules into the EU framework, a move that eases worries for London’s City. While the government will keep looking for ways to work with Brussels when it helps the economy, finance won’t be part of any future alignment. The decision follows concerns that tightening EU rules could hurt London’s markets and make the city less competitive.
