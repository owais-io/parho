CBS News dropped a 60 Minutes episode about the Cecot prison in El Salvador, where many U.S. deportees say they were abused. The network said it needed more reporting, but the correspondent says the decision was political. CBS chief Bari Weiss said the story will air when ready. Critics on social media accuse CBS of censorship and say the Trump‑era deportations were covered up. The cancellation sparked outcry from reporters and readers who fear the network is being silenced.
