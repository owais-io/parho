Storms from atmospheric rivers will dump up to 8 inches of rain and over a foot of snow on the Pacific Northwest this week. Washington and Oregon are under flood watch, especially the Cascades where saturated soils raise the risk of mudslides. The same weather pattern is also delivering heavy rain to Iraq and Iran, easing a long drought.
