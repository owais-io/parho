Percy Fender was a brilliant Surrey captain who never got to lead England because a corrupt cricket official blackmailed him in 1924. A secret audio clip, found 100 years later, shows the offer of shares instead of a salary. Fender refused and never captained the 1924‑25 Ashes tour. He later became a journalist and explained the Bodyline tactic that hurt Australia.
