A 30‑year‑old Iranian boxer, Mohammad Vafaei, has been in prison for five years after joining 2019 protests. His appeal for a new trial was turned down by Iran’s top court on December 15, and he may soon be executed. Human‑rights groups say the death sentence was unfair, and many athletes, including Olympic medallists, have begged the government to stop the execution. Iran has executed more than 1,500 people this year.
