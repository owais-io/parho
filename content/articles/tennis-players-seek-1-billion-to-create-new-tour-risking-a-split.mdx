Players’ lobby group, led by Novak Djokovic’s former partner, is suing the ATP, WTA and four Grand Slam tournaments. They want $1 billion to launch a new Pinnacle Tour that would pay top players more, give equal prize money, and have a shorter calendar. If the funding comes through, the tour could split from the existing ATP/WTA system, pushing tennis into a potential civil war.
