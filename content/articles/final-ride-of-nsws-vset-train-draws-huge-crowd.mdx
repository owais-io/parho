Thousands of train fans packed a platform 150 km from Sydney for the V‑Set’s last trip. The old electric train, built in 1968, chugged up the Blue Mountains to Central Station before being retired. Supporters wore retro outfits, shouted cheers, and celebrated the train’s comfy purple seats. Newer Mariyung trains with air‑conditioning but no reversible seats drew mixed reviews. The event highlighted nostalgia and the shift to modern rail.
