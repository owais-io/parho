Lindsey Vonn has flown back to the U.S. to continue treatment after she broke her leg during the Winter Olympic downhill. She’s still unable to stand and has been in a hospital bed for more than a week. Vonn thanked the Italian team for their care and said she’ll need another surgery here. She’s also recovering from an earlier knee tear and remains proud of her Olympic effort.
