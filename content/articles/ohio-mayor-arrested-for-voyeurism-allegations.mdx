A 48‑year‑old mayor of Butler, Ohio, was arrested after a teen who lived with him said a hidden camera caught him smelling her underwear. Police say the footage shows him smelling several pairs and touching his groin. He pleaded not guilty, was released on a $10,000 bond, and now must wear a GPS tracker and cannot contact the girl. The case is still pending.
