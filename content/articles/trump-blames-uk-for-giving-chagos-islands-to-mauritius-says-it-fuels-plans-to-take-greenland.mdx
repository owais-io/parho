At the World Economic Forum in Davos, former President Donald Trump tweeted that the UK’s move to hand the Chagos Islands over to Mauritius is part of the reason he wants to seize Greenland. He called the UK’s decision “stupid” and said taking Greenland is essential for national security. Trump posted these remarks on Truth Social, but the claim remains unverified.
