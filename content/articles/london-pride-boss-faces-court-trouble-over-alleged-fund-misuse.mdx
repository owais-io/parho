The chief executive of London Pride, Christopher Joell‑Deshields, was taken to court on Tuesday. He is accused of contempt for not handing over the organisation’s property and for not giving a sworn statement. He denies the claims but admitted the missing paperwork. He’s already been suspended over allegations that he spent sponsor money on luxury items. An internal probe is still ongoing. Pride London could face fines or even jail if the judge finds he disobeyed the court order.
