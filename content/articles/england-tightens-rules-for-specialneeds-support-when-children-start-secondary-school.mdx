England is set to review special‑needs support when children move into secondary school. The new rules will make it harder to qualify for an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP), keeping them for only the most serious cases. Schools will get budgets to meet lower‑tier needs and new plans for kids with autism or ADHD. The change aims to cut rising costs and improve outcomes.
