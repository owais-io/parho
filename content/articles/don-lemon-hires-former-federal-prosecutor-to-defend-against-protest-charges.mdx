Don Lemon has hired former U.S. prosecutor Joseph Thompson, who quit after a dispute over how the Justice Department handled immigration killings in Minnesota, to defend him in a case accusing him of blocking church protestors. Thompson left the U.S. attorney’s office after a disagreement about investigating the death of immigrant Renee Good, shot by ICE. Lemon faces conspiracy and civil‑rights charges for a 18 January St. Paul protest.
