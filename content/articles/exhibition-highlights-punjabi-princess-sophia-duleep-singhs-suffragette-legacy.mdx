An exhibition at Kensington Palace opens to tell the story of Sophia Duleep Singh, a Punjabi princess who became a pioneering suffragette. The show shows how she, her sisters, mother and Queen Victoria shaped her life. Visitors can see letters, banners and personal items that reveal her fight for women’s rights, her court battles over taxes, and her sister’s help to Jewish refugees. It links colonial history with the fight for equality.
