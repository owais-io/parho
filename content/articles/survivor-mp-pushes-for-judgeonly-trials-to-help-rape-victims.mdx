Natalie Fleet, a Labour MP who was raped as a teenager, wants her party to support judge‑only trials. She says the current court system delays rape cases and lets drug dealers use long jury trials to stay out of prison. She backs David Lammy’s plan to have judges alone in most criminal cases and wants to cut the backlog. The proposal has hit pushback from lawyers and many Labour MPs.
