In a Sunday match, Celtic defeated Dutch side Utrecht 3‑1, earning a spot in the Europa League playoff round. Goals came from Kieran Tierney, Arne Engels (penalty), and Auston Trusty. An own goal and a penalty helped seal the win. Manager Martin O’Neill praised his team’s resilience and said they still need to improve to progress further. The win lifts confidence after a shaky start to the campaign.
