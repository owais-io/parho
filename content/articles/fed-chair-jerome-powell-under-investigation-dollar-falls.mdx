The Department of Justice has opened a criminal probe into Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell over a $2.5 billion renovation of the Fed’s headquarters and his testimony to Congress. The news sent U.S. markets lower, the dollar fell, and gold hit a new high. Powell said the investigation was a political attack and vowed to keep the Fed independent. The story also touched on UK hospitality rate talks and other stock moves.
