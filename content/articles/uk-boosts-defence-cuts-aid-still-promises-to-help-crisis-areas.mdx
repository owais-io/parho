UK boosts defence spending while trimming overseas aid for the next three years. Facing wars in Ukraine, Gaza, Sudan, and Lebanon, the government says the new aid cuts will be smarter—targeting crisis hotspots, linking help to peace, using science and private finance, and staying strong on women’s rights. The plan aims to keep Britain’s values and global responsibilities intact.
