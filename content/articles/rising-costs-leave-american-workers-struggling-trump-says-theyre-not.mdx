Across the country, many workers are feeling the pinch of higher food, gas, and utility bills while their pay stays flat or even dips. Trump’s rallies boast that inflation is down and wages are rising, but data shows wages for the lowest earners are barely keeping up with prices. Meanwhile, new tariffs, cuts to health and food aid, and potential wage reductions add to the strain, driving more families toward debt.
