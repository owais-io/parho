Drivers in Sydney’s M4 tunnel were trapped for up to four hours after engineers worried the sprayed‑on concrete roof could collapse. The tunnel was closed westbound early Thursday, then one lane reopened by evening. Transurban, who runs the private tunnel, said they found large bulges and are working to fix them. The NSW government said it’s a private‑operator failure and is negotiating refunds. They will refund tolls to affected drivers.
