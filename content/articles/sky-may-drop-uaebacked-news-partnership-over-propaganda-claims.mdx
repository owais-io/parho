Sky, the British TV group, is considering ending its partnership with Sky News Arabia, a channel run by a UAE company. The move follows accusations that the channel spread propaganda and downplayed the violence the Sudanese RSF forces committed. Sudan banned the station, and a UN report called the RSF’s actions genocide. Sky says talks are commercial and not about news content.
