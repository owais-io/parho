England’s cricket tour hit a PR snag when a security guard, Colin Rhooms, shouted at and pushed a Channel Seven camera operator while the team was heading to Adelaide for the next Ashes Test. The incident, shown on TV, drew criticism and a defense from the broadcaster. Despite the row, England’s players remained friendly with fans and media, but the clash highlighted tensions between teams and press during the series.
