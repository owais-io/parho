Sydney Sweeney launched a new lingerie brand called “Syrn” and tried to get attention by climbing the Hollywood sign and hanging bras around it. She says the line is for all body types and wants women to feel comfortable in everyday life. The stunt was controversial because climbing the sign is not allowed, and the Los Angeles Chamber of Commerce was unhappy. Despite the backlash, Sweeney hopes the stunt will boost sales and raise awareness for her new product.
