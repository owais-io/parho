New England Patriots wide‑receiver Stefon Diggs said he is innocent of felony assault and strangulation claims that came after a fight with his personal chef over unpaid work. The court hearing was delayed until after the Super Bowl so Diggs could play. The next pre‑trial hearing is set for April 1. The Patriots say they stand by him, while the chef said she was forced to choke and smacked.
