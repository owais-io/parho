Aston Villa beat Manchester United 2‑1 in a Manchester derby, with Morgan Rogers scoring both goals. The win gives Villa their 10th consecutive victory and keeps them within a point of the league leaders. United’s Matheus Cunha pulled one back but failed to find the net again. The match highlighted Villa’s strong form under Unai Emery and their run of seven league wins, while United struggled with injuries.
