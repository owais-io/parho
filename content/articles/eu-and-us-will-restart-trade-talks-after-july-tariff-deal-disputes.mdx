The EU and US will restart trade talks next week after a two‑month break. Brussels leaders will meet to fix problems from a July tariff deal meant to stop a 50% tariff threat. Key issues: 50% steel and aluminium duties, extra tariffs on products with steel parts, a 15% tax on most EU goods in the US, and duties on wine and spirits. The US wants the EU to speed up the deal, which isn’t yet legally binding.
