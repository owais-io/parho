EU lawmakers have decided to stop using meat‑related names on plant‑based foods. They will ban 31 words such as bacon, steak, and chicken, but will keep terms like veggie burger and meat‑free sausage. The move is part of a broader effort to support farmers. Some farmers welcome the rule, while others think it limits innovation. Consumer groups say the ban could confuse shoppers and limit healthy choices.
