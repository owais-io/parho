President Trump will give his State of the Union speech tonight, but the country remains split. People dislike his handling of the economy, tariffs and immigration, and many think the nation is not stronger than under President Biden. A recent Supreme Court ruling hit his tariff plans, and protests over aggressive ICE raids in cities like Minneapolis are hurting his popularity. Trump also faces criticism over foreign moves toward Iran and a slow release of the Jeffrey Epstein files.
