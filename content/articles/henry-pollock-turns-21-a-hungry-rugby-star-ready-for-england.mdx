Henry Pollock, now 21, is celebrating his birthday while he’s already making waves in English rugby. With a handful of Premiership starts, a try‑scoring debut for England and a world‑breakthrough nomination, the young back‑row is a force to watch. He’s known for his drive, confident attitude and flashy celebrations, but coaches say he keeps his focus on improving. Pollock aims to win a club trophy, get a England start and prove himself in South Africa.
