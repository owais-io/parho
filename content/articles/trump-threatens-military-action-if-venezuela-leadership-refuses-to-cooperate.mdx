After Nicolás Maduro was removed, President Trump said the U.S. might use force again if Venezuela’s new leaders don’t follow his demands. He also mentioned possible attacks on Colombia and Mexico over drug trafficking. Trump said the U.S. would keep a 15,000‑strong force near Venezuela and could block oil ships. World leaders warned the U.S. actions risk regional peace, while Venezuelan people fear another raid.
