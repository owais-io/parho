Someone was recruited on Telegram to write fake Google reviews for hotels and other businesses in exchange for cryptocurrency. Over nine days he was told he could earn up to £800 a day, but the operation was a scam. Fake identities and large Telegram channels were used, and while the reviews never reached the companies, the scam also tried to launder money and trick the writer into paying for fake bonuses.
