In the UK, the job market is slowing. A BDO survey shows hiring intentions are at their lowest in 15 years, and unemployment rose to a five‑year high of 5.2 %. Companies say costs and uncertainty keep them from hiring new staff. Hiring for permanent and temporary roles is still dropping, though some signs of stabilisation appear. The services sector is the only part seeing growth.
