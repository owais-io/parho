Two capybaras from Marwell Zoo in Hampshire slipped out of their temporary enclosure and one, Samba, vanished. The zoo launched a search using dogs, thermal drones, and volunteers, and locals spotted her near a pub and along the River Itchen. Although she’s safe and no predators threaten her, the zoo wants to reunite her with her sister Tango. The hunt has drawn national attention and online memes.
