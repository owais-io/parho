President Trump says the US could take control of Venezuela after seizing President Maduro and his wife. He warned interim president Delcy Rodríguez that if she doesn’t meet U.S. demands, a new military intervention could happen. Republicans, including Secretary of State Marco Rubio, have questioned the claims, and Democrats slammed the raid as illegal. Protests erupted in U.S. cities, while the move sparked backlash from other world leaders.
