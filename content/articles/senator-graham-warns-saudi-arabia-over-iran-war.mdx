Senator Lindsey Graham, who pushed the U.S. into an airstrike campaign against Iran, has taken to Twitter to tell Saudi Arabia that if it won’t join U.S. operations there will be consequences. He says the Saudi embassy is being evacuated after Iranian attacks on Saudi soil and that Americans are dying in a war he helped start. The U.S. has a huge arms deal with Riyadh but no formal mutual‑defence treaty yet.
