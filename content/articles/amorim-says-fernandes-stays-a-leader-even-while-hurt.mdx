Man Utd manager Ruben Amorim says Bruno Fernandes is a great leader, even while he’s hurt. Fernandes has a hamstring injury and could miss up to three weeks, but he still visits training and keeps talking to teammates. Amorim also mentioned other injuries – Mason Mount, Kobbie Mainoo and more – and how many players will be available for the Wolves match. United’s recent 2‑1 loss to Aston Villa and win over Newcastle show they’re fighting to stay near the top.
