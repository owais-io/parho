Labour came third in the Gorton and Denton by-election, a result that has led its leaders to call for a rethink of strategy. The Greens won the seat, showing a shift in local politics, while the Conservatives lost their deposit and saw their support collapse. Commentators highlight how the result points to a deeper crisis within Labour and a growing challenge from smaller parties.
