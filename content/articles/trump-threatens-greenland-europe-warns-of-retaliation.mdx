President Trump has said the U.S. wants to take control of Greenland and threatened Denmark. He also wrote to Norway’s prime minister saying he no longer cares only about peace. European leaders – including the UK, Germany, France, Norway and Denmark – have warned that they will act together and put counter‑tariffs in place. They call Trump’s letter a blackmail attempt and will meet at the EU summit to decide how to respond.
