A 13‑year‑old boy was caught driving his family’s VW campervan at 70 mph on a busy dual‑carriageway in Dorset. He had no licence or insurance. A magistrate gave him six penalty points that will stay on his future licence, a 12‑month conditional discharge, and ordered his parents to pay court costs. The boy said he was sorry and promised not to repeat the offence.
