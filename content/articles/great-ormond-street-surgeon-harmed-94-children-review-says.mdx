An independent review at Great Ormond Street Hospital found that almost 100 children were harmed by a surgeon who performed limb‑lengthening operations. Out of 789 kids treated between 2017‑2022, 94 suffered injury, ranging from severe to mild. The hospital has announced big changes to its orthopaedic service and NHS England is launching another probe into how the hospital handled the issue. Families are seeking justice and better safeguards.
