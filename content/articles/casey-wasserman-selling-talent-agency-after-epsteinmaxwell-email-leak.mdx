Hollywood agent Casey Wasserman is putting his talent firm up for sale after emails showing old chats with Ghislaine Maxwell, linked to Jeffrey Epstein, were released. Several big artists, like Chappell Roan and Abby Wambach, quit the agency, saying the connections hurt their trust. Wasserman says he will step back and work on the 2028 Los Angeles Olympics, while a deputy runs the day‑to‑day business.
