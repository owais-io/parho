Molly Burkett, who died at 93, was a celebrated writer, wildlife rehabilitator and tireless campaigner. For more than 60 years she ran a private rescue centre that cared for injured birds and mammals, helped after the Torrey Canyon oil spill, supplied ravens to the Tower of London and advised the government on wildlife laws. She wrote over 120 books, fought for equal pay in schools and against nuclear waste dumping, and was awarded an MBE in 2008.
