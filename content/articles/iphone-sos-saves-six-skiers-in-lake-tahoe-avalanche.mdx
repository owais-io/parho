A deadly avalanche near Lake Tahoe on Tuesday left 15 people in danger, killing at least eight. Six skiers survived by using the iPhone 14's emergency SOS, which sends a satellite text to 911 even without signal. First responders tracked the survivors and found several bodies. One person is still missing. Apple’s feature, free for two years on new iPhones, can connect to satellites and share location and medical info.
