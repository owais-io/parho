The Guardian issued a correction notice, fixing several errors. First, the £700 million figure for an acoustic fish‑deterrent at Hinkley Point C actually covers all fish‑protection costs, not just that system. Second, a TV show set was wrongly listed as Oxfordshire; it’s actually Birmingham. The notice also lists other small changes, such as a piece on the FDA and a piece on taxonomy. Readers can send complaints to the Guardian.
