Zohair Rajabi and about 34 other families in the Batn al‑Hawa neighbourhood are being forced out by Israeli police. Right‑wing groups, especially Ateret Cohanim, are using a 1970 law to reclaim old Jewish homes and expand settlements. The Gaza war has accelerated the evictions, and many Palestinians fear losing their homes and lives. The Israeli government wants to keep Jerusalem majority‑Jewish, while families are fighting but may soon have to leave.
