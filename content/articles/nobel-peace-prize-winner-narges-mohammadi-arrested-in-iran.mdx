Narges Mohammadi, the 2023 Nobel Peace Prize winner, was taken into custody by Iranian police in Mashhad after speaking at a memorial for a fellow human‑rights lawyer, Khosro Alikordi, who died last week. The arrest happened a day after she was briefly released from prison on medical grounds. Her family says she has serious health problems and fears she may be sent back to jail. The move comes amid a crackdown on activists protesting Alikordi’s death.
