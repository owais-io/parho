New budget changes will raise taxes for many savers and investors. The government will cut the cash ISA limit from £20,000 to £12,000 next year, keeping the overall limit the same and giving older people an exemption. Pensions using salary sacrifice will hit a £2,000 cap, which could lower take‑home pay. Income tax on savings interest and rental or dividend income will rise by about 2 percentage points, adding more tax for basic‑rate earners.
