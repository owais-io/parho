Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI, told reporters in India that the power needed to train AI is similar to the energy humans use to learn over 20 years. He said the public is right to worry about AI’s energy use and called for fast switch to nuclear, wind or solar. Critics say the data‑center boom is unsustainable, that water claims are false, and that Altman’s comparison is dismissive.
