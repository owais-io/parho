An emergency U.N. meeting condemned the U.S. for bomb‑ing Venezuela and capturing President Nicolás Maduro, with 12 countries—including Brazil, China and Russia—calling the attack a serious breach of sovereignty. Maduro was also arraigned in New York on drug‑related charges. In Washington, lawmakers accused the Trump team of redirecting thousands of federal officers to ICE, while Democrats demanded a full inventory of those moves. Trump’s repeated calls to seize Greenland raised alarms among NATO allies.
