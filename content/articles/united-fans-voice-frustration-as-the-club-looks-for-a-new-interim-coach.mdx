United fans at Turf Moor held up a flag that read “Jim can’t fix this” and shouted for a new interim coach, showing how upset they are with the club’s endless managerial changes. The crowd sang old United songs while hoping for a stable future. Supporters discussed former managers like Solskjær, Carrick and Amorim, and voiced worry that the club is in a mess and needs a clear plan. Fans still believe in the club and want the people back.
