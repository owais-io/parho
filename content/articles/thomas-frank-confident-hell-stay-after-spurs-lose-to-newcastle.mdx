Thomas Frank says he will stay after Spurs lost to Newcastle, even with boos and a squad hit by injuries. He claims the board backs him and that problems go beyond his role. Fans chanted former manager Mauricio Pochettino and shouted for a sacking, but Frank points to injury lists and the need for resilience. He is confident he will keep his job before the Arsenal derby.
