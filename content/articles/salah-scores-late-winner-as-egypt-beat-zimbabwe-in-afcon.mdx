In a group game of Africa's Cup of Nations, Egypt beat Zimbabwe 2-1. After a 1-0 loss in the first half, a goal from Omar Marmoush leveled the score. With minutes left, Mohamed Salah spun into the box and scored a left‑foot half‑volley to seal the win. The Egyptian side had been struggling because of Salah’s limited playing time at Liverpool, but the goal lifted the crowd in Agadir.
