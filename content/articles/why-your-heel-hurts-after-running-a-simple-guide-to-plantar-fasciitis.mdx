Plantar fasciitis is a common heel pain that can hit anyone who suddenly ramps up their activity, stands a lot, or carries extra weight. It happens when the thick band of tissue under your foot gets inflamed or torn. Most people feel better with rest, gentle stretching, and strengthening exercises. If pain lasts more than a week, see a doctor for physical therapy or other treatments.
