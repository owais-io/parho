Kristi Noem, secretary of homeland security, announced that every federal agent in Minneapolis must start wearing body cameras right away, and that the program will roll out across the country as money allows. Republican senator Ron Johnson said he has no problem with the cameras, a point Democrats want as part of funding the agency. Democrats, who have blocked new money for ICE, also demand limits on roving patrols, judicial warrants for home entries, and stricter conduct rules.
