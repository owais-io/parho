The Boddington bushfire in Western Australia was downgraded to a watch level after burning over 4,000 hectares, and residents can now return home. An evacuation centre was closed, though 18 people were still inside last night. Meanwhile, Queensland and the Northern Territory are facing a monsoon‑driven flood watch, with up to 500 mm of rain expected in some Gulf Coast areas, threatening roads and isolation. Temperatures will stay hot in most parts of the country.
