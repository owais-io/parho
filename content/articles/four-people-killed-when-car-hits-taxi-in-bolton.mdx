Four people died when a car hit a taxi on Wigan Road in Bolton early Sunday. Three teenagers (18‑19) and a taxi driver in his 50s were killed, and five others were hurt. The crash happened at about 12:45 a.m. A Seat Leon struck a Citroën C4 Picasso taxi. Firefighters, police and ambulance crews responded quickly. Police are asking anyone with footage or information to come forward.
