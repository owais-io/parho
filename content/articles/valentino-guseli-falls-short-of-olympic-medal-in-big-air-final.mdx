Valentino Guseli tried to win Australia’s first Winter Olympics medal in the big‑air snowboarding final, but he fell twice and finished 10th. He landed a spectacular jump in qualifying, but in the medal round he slipped on two landings, losing points. Japan’s Kira Kimura won gold. Guseli said he had no regrets and still hopes to medal in halfpipe later.
