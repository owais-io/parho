England’s cricket team has kept floundering after a bad Ashes tour, and the blame falls on a mix of poor leadership and a risky style called Bazball. Coach Brendon McCullum, manager Rob Key and captain Harry Brook all face criticism for not fixing the team’s shaky preparation and for keeping a high‑paying, high‑risk contract. Fans, funding, and the public’s interest are slipping, making the future of the side uncertain.
