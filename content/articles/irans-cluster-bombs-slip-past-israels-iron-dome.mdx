Iran has been firing cluster bombs at Israel, sending dozens of small bomblets that make the country’s Iron Dome harder to stop. Since the war began in late February, at least 19 missiles with these warheads have hit Israeli cities, killing nine and wounding many more. The bombs break apart mid‑air, so intercepting them is almost impossible. Israel’s defence systems are strained, and unexploded bomblets now pose a danger to civilians.
