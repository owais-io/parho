The Giants are looking for a new head coach after firing Brian Daboll. They are close to hiring John Harbaugh, who spent 18 seasons with the Baltimore Ravens and won a Super Bowl. Talks are still happening, but the Giants see him as the right fit because of his experience and success. If he joins, he'll bring stability and a winning mindset to the franchise.
