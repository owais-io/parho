EU environment chief Jessika Roswall says countries should turn their borders into natural barriers. She points to Poland and Finland, which have planted trees and wetlands to make it hard for tanks and trucks to cross. Roswall also says protecting water and fixing pollution is part of national security. She wants the EU to tackle dangerous chemicals and keep high environmental standards even after Brexit. Nature could be a win‑win for safety and biodiversity.
