Rob Key explained why England’s Ashes tour collapsed. He said the prep was poor, the New Zealand warm‑up didn’t mimic Perth, and the team had been over‑prepared in the wrong way. He avoided naming people responsible and used vague, passive wording, admitting only that he would look into issues. Key defended the head coach and said players weren’t ready for the conditions, but offered no concrete fixes.
