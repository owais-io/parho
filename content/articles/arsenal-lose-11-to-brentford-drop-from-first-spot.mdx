Arsenal failed to keep their lead after a 1‑1 draw with Brentford. Noni Madueke put Arsenal ahead, but Keane Lewis‑Potter headed an equaliser from a long throw. The game was tight, with Arsenal missing key players like Saliba and Havertz. Brentford’s strong set‑piece play and steady defence helped them secure the point, leaving Arsenal in second place. The result keeps the title race tight, with Liverpool now just one point behind Arsenal.
