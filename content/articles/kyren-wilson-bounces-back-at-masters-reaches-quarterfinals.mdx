World champion Kyren Wilson is back in form after a rough start to the season. He beat Chinese player Si Jiahui 6‑2 in the Masters first round to reach the quarter‑finals, even after his cue broke earlier. Wilson posted two century breaks and thanked fans for their support. He will next face the winner of Robertson vs. Wakelin, while John Higgins takes on Barry Hawkins.
