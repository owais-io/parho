On Valentine’s Day, the iconic Lovers’ Arch at Sant’Andrea collapsed into the Adriatic Sea after a strong storm. Rough seas, heavy rain and winds from a Mediterranean cyclone weakened the rock over days. The loss is a big blow to tourism in the Salento region and highlights how rising sea temperatures are making such storms more common.
