Online schooling is becoming the norm for many junior tennis players who need time to travel and train. A study said remote learning can hurt kids’ mental health, but parents of elite athletes argue the flexibility helps keep them on schedule. Schools like ICL Academy and Dwight Global report huge enrollment jumps, offering in‑person meet‑ups for tournaments. While some kids thrive online, others miss face‑to‑face learning, prompting schools to consider blended models.
