British woman Lindsay Foreman was given a 10‑year jail term in Iran on spying charges. She and her husband Craig were arrested while traveling in Iran and later put in court. Foreman said she has tried to be patient, wrote letters, and even went on a hunger strike to protest an unfair trial. The UK government has called the sentence unfair, but no quick release has happened yet.
