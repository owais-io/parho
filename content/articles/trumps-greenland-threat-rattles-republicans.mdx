Trump said he might take Greenland from Denmark, a move that alarmed many of his own party. Some Republicans have begun to speak out against him, calling his threats reckless and saying they worry about his influence. Critics say the threat hurts the US’s NATO allies and could damage Trump’s chances in the 2024 election. The incident shows growing divisions inside the GOP and hints at trouble for Trump’s future leadership.
