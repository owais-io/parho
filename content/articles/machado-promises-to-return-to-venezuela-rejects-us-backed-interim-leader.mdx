María Corina Machado says she will go back to Venezuela soon and will not support the interim president the U.S. has backed. She thanked President Trump for helping remove Nicolás Maduro but says the U.S. is now backing Maduro’s former vice‑president, Delcy Rodríguez. Machado accuses Rodríguez of corruption and torture and says free elections will win her and her allies. The country is still under a state of emergency after Maduro’s arrest.
