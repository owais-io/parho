The Conservatives said they had mistakenly linked Suella Braverman’s move to Reform UK with her mental health, apologizing for the claim. MPs, peers and mental‑health groups slammed the comment as cruel and wrong. The party sent a corrected message and removed the mental‑health reference. Braverman’s new party said the statement was false and harmful, and other politicians called the Tories’ remarks disgraceful.
