After Hamas’s Oct. 7 attack, Israel’s war spread across the Middle East, sparking Iran‑US clashes and Gulf unrest. The article says all these crises stem from Israel’s occupation of Palestinian land. It shows how US and Israeli policies ignored Palestinians while trying to isolate Iran, and how the conflict now pulls Gulf states and the West into costly military and economic ties. It argues that only a free Palestine can end the violence and global fallout.
