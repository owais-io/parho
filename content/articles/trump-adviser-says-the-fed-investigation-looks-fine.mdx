Kevin Hassett, Trump’s top economic adviser, said there was no evidence of wrongdoing in the Justice Department’s probe of Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell over the bank’s expensive headquarters renovation. Hassett defended Powell’s honesty and called the investigation a political attack. Trump praised Hassett and said he would keep him on his team. Other Fed leaders condemned the probe, warning it could harm the bank’s independence.
