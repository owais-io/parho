Trump’s former Fox News host Pete Hegseth, appointed as a top defense adviser, has drawn criticism for his unprofessional behaviour, poor qualifications, and personal scandals. The Guardian columnist says he is ill‑suited for national‑security duties, pointing to his frequent outbursts at the Pentagon, questions about his knowledge of Iran, and past alcohol problems. Most Americans dislike him, and experts worry his loyalty to Trump may trump competence.
