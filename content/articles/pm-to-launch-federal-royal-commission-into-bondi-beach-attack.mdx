Prime Minister Anthony Albanese is expected to confirm a federal royal commission on Thursday that will investigate the Bondi beach terror attack and antisemitism. The decision follows strong pressure from families of victims, Jewish community groups and politicians, who say the previous narrow inquiry was too slow and limited. Former High Court judge Virginia Bell is slated to chair the probe, aiming for a thorough review and quicker answers about police and intelligence.
