A crowd of 571 Weston‑super‑Mare fans travelled to Cleethorpes to watch their mixed‑background team take on Grimsby Town. The match was a tense fight, with Weston pulling level twice but ultimately losing 3‑2 thanks to a late header by Grimsby’s Kieran Green. Although the defeat is a blow, the club’s biggest ever cup run still brought pride and about £150,000 in revenue. Grimsby will now move on to the next round.
