Australia is facing a fuel price shock that has made people think about changing their travel habits. The government has loosened fuel rules and released extra reserves to ease shortages. Experts say the fastest fix is electric cars and better public transport. Short‑term steps include more buses, cheaper train tickets, and encouraging car‑pooling. In the long term, Australia needs to switch to locally produced electricity and electric vehicles.
