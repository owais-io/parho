Ketamine use in the UK is rising fast, especially among teens, and hospitals are struggling to treat the bladder damage it causes. The drug, once used in medicine, is now popular as a party drug, with patients reporting severe urinary problems. Urologists say clinics are overwhelmed, and many patients are too young to get the care they need. Experts call for school‑based warnings and stronger support to help those who abuse ketamine.
