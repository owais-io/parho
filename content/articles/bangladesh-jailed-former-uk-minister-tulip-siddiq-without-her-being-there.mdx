In Bangladesh, former UK minister Tulip Siddiq was found guilty of corruption and given a two‑year prison term, even though she never appeared in court. The case ties her to her aunt, former Bangladeshi prime minister Sheikh Hasina, who was recently sentenced to death for crimes against humanity. Siddiq says the trial was illegal, she never got notice or a lawyer, and UK lawyers say her basic rights were denied.
