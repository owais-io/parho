Marco Rubio told lawmakers that President Trump ordered U.S. airstrikes on Iran because Israel was set to strike Iran, and that the U.S. feared retaliation against American troops. Congress reacted split‑apart – Republicans defended the move, while Democrats said it was an unnecessary war with unclear goals. A war‑powers resolution could force Trump to end hostilities, but Republicans expect it to fail.
