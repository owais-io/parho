FIFA has decided to cut Ronaldo’s three‑match ban, letting him play after just one year of probation. He was originally suspended for a red card during a friendly against Ireland, which he missed the first game of Portugal’s upcoming tournament. The change means Ronaldo can join his team for the next matches, sparking debate about fairness as other players received different treatment for similar offences.
