Lindsey Vonn, 41, has surprised everyone by returning to the world’s toughest downhill races after six years away. She won several World Cup races and was a strong favourite for the Milano‑Cortina Olympics. But last week she tore her ACL in a crash. Despite the injury, she is training hard and hopes to win a medal, which would make her the oldest Olympic skier to win a medal. The risk is huge.
