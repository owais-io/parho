A roof repair is starting on All Saints, a closed church in Bristol that may hold the remains of slave‑owner Edward Colston. After the roof work, the building could be de‑consecrated, turned into a community space, and Colston’s body taken out. The diocese says the repairs are legally required for safety, but local church groups say money should go to active churches that need maintenance.
