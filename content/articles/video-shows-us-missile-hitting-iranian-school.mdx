A video shows a US Tomahawk missile hitting an Iranian naval base next to a primary school in Minab, killing over 168 people, most of them children. The footage, verified by investigative groups and satellite images, proves the strike was by the US, not Iran. President Trump denied it, but experts say the weapon used is only found in the US arsenal. UNESCO and Human Rights Watch call it a war‑crime violation.
