Making Christmas ads is hard, especially after this year’s crises. Marketers try to lift spirits and boost sales. John Lewis made a tear‑jerking ad about a family that feels time slipping away. Marks & Spencer’s spot shows a traffic jam that turns into a party. Tesco’s message is that Christmas is messy, not perfect. Sainsbury’s added a silly giant to a classic scene. The article wonders if people still watch ads at all.
