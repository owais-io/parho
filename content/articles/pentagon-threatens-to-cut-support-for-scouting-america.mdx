The Pentagon says it will cut support for Scouting America unless the organization returns to its old “core values” of faith and patriotism. Trump‑era officials blame the scouting group’s move to welcome girls and LGBTQ members and its focus on diversity, equity and inclusion. The agency threatened to pull funding for the 20,000‑person summer jamboree and other military aid. Scouting America has not yet responded.
