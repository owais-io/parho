Australian veterans, led by the RSL president, have condemned former U.S. President Donald Trump's claim that Australian troops stayed back from Afghanistan's frontlines. Trump said on Fox News that Australian forces were not fully committed. The RSL says the statement is “unfathomable” and demands an apology, citing the 47 Australians who died and 40,000 who served. The comment has also drawn criticism from the UK and calls for clearer future cooperation.
