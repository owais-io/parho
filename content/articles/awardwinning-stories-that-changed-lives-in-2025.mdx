The Guardian celebrated its reporters who collected a haul of prizes this year, from the Amnesty Award to the World Press Photo. It highlighted teamwork—editors, designers, IT, photographers and the people who share their stories—all working together to produce powerful journalism. Highlights include a child bride saved by reader donations, investigations into migrant abuse in Tunisia, salt‑water flooding in Bangladesh, and attacks on Ukrainian maternity hospitals. The piece thanks readers for keeping the work alive.
