A US judge said Meta does not have a monopoly in social networking, so it can keep Instagram and WhatsApp. The case, filed by the Federal Trade Commission, could have forced Meta to sell those apps. The judge pointed to new rivals like TikTok and said the market has changed a lot. Meta’s win means the company avoids a big breakup and keeps its biggest money‑making platforms.
