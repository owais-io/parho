Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri were unhappy with McLaren’s decision not to pit them during a safety car at the Qatar Grand Prix, which let Max Verstappen win and cut Norris’s lead. The mistake left the championship fight between Verstappen and Norris tight, with only a 12‑point gap. McLaren will review the call before the final race in Abu Dhabi. Despite the setback, the drivers say they’ll keep fighting.
