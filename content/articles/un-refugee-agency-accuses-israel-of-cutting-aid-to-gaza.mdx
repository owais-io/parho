UN Refugee Agency chief Natalie Boucly says Israel is breaking law by blocking aid to Gaza. She warns that only about half of the needed trucks are getting through, leaving a stockpile that could feed Gaza for three months stuck outside in Jordan and Egypt. The UN says Israel must allow all aid, but has cut ties with the agency over security claims. Boucly urges the EU and US to press Israel to open the gates and keep children safe.
