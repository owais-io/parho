Campaign groups are urging UK technology officials to require data‑centre developers to prove that their new AI‑focused sites won’t add to the country’s carbon emissions or water problems. With 140 projects on the table that could need 50GW of power – more than Britain’s current peak demand – groups fear huge climate damage. The letter, signed by NGOs, calls for clear rules and renewable‑energy guarantees to keep the net‑zero goal on track.
