Russia’s president Vladimir Putin flew to Delhi to see Prime Minister Narendra Modi, a rare trip after Russia faced sanctions and isolation over the Ukraine war. The leaders will discuss buying Russian weapons, oil and boosting trade, while India keeps its options open between the U.S., Russia and China. India also worries about growing Chinese power along its borders. The visit signals Russia’s hope to return to normal relations, but its impact on Ukraine will stay limited.
