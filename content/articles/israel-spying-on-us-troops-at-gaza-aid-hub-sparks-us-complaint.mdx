Israel is reportedly recording US soldiers and other allies at the new Civil‑Military Coordination Center in southern Israel, which coordinates Gaza aid. The US base commander asked the Israeli side to stop the recordings, but Israel says the talks are unclassified and normal. Allies say the centre also limits food and medicine into Gaza and keeps Palestinians out of planning. The situation has raised worries about spying, aid restrictions and a lack of Palestinian input.
