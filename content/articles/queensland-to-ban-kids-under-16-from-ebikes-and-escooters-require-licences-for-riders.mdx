A Queensland parliamentary inquiry, finished ahead of schedule, says kids under 16 should not ride e‑bikes or e‑scooters and anyone who does must have a learner licence. The plan follows rising injury reports and a child’s death in an e‑bike crash. The inquiry held 17 hearings and received over 1,200 public submissions. It also suggested speed limits, helmet use and separate bike paths. The government has three months to respond.
