Hillary Clinton will testify before the House Oversight Committee about her ties to Jeffrey Epstein and his partner Ghislaine Maxwell, after Republicans threatened to hold her in contempt. The closed‑door hearing will be recorded for later release, mirroring the setup from her 1998 grand jury appearance. Bill Clinton will answer questions the next day, denying wrongdoing. The Clintons want a public hearing, calling the process political, while Republicans see the probe as a way to shift focus from Trump’s alleged links to Epstein.
