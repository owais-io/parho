Former UK Home Secretary Sajid Javid has asked Nigel Farage to say sorry for racist remarks Farage allegedly made as a student. The request follows a Guardian probe that gathered accusations from over 30 classmates, including Jewish and black students, who say Farage bullied them with hate language. Farage denies the claims, calling them “made‑up fantasies.” Javid says an apology is the first step toward proving he’s changed.
