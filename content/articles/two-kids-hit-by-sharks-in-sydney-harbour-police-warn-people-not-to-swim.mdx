In Sydney Harbour, a 12‑year‑old boy was bitten by a shark while jumping off a rock and is now in critical condition after police and paramedics worked quickly to stop bleeding and perform CPR. The next day an 11‑year‑old was bitten at Dee Why, but he was unharmed. Officials say the sharks were likely bull sharks and warned people not to swim in the murky, brackish water after heavy rain. Beaches nearby were closed.
