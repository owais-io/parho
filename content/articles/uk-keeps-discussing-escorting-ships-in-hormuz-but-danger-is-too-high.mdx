Britain says it is still talking with the US and allies about sending warships to escort merchant ships through the Strait of Hormuz. But Iran's missiles and drones make the area too risky for a safe escort right now. Iran keeps attacking tankers and blocking the passage, pushing oil prices high. The UK has sent planners to coordinate with the US but hasn't committed a warship yet. A multinational effort is needed, but the US remains focused on bombing.
