Three wealthy Australians—Dick Smith, Graham Marr, and Richard Barnes—signed an open letter urging the government to tax the richest people more heavily. They argue that extreme wealth robs democracy, fuels inequality and harms the climate. The letter proposes a 5‑10% wealth tax that could bring in billions for social services. Most other billionaires declined to sign, citing pride or different priorities.
