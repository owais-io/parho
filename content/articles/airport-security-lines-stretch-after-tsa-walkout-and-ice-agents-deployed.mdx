TSA agents haven’t been paid and many have quit, causing long security lines at U.S. airports. President Trump sent ICE agents to help, but their presence had little effect on the chaos. Passengers were stuck for hours, missing flights and dealing with overcrowded terminals. The move was meant to ease crowds, but the real problem is the shortage of TSA staff.
