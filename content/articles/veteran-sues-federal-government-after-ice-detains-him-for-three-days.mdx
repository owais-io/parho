George Retes, a 26‑year‑old U.S. Army veteran, was taken by ICE agents while driving to work in Ventura County, California, and held for three days in a Los Angeles detention center without a charge, lawyer, or any explanation. He’s suing the federal government, arguing the detention was unconstitutional. The case, supported by the Institute for Justice, highlights growing concerns about how federal agents treat veterans and the difficulty of filing lawsuits against federal officials.
