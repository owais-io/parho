MP Alex Sobel talks about how poverty in Leeds cuts people's lives by up to 14 years. In his area, the Hyde Park neighbourhood is 14 years younger than nearby Far Headingley, just a half‑hour walk apart. Child and fuel poverty, bad air and poor homes all contribute. He says health isn’t just about hospitals – all government policies must help people live longer and healthier.
