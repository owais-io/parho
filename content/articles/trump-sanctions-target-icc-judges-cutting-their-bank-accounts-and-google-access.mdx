Donald Trump’s U.S. sanctions hit judges at the International Criminal Court, treating them like terrorists. Canadian judge Kimberly Prost lost credit cards, Amazon and Google accounts, making everyday tasks hard. The U.S. also sanctioned 11 officials, including the chief prosecutor. Judges say the moves try to silence the court, but they refuse to let it affect their work. Many other judges and even their families face similar restrictions.
