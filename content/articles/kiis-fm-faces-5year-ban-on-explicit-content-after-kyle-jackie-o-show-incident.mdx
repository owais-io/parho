ACMA has slapped a five‑year licence condition on KIIS FM in Sydney and Melbourne, saying the Kyle & Jackie O breakfast show can’t air strong sexual content. ARN Media terminated Jackie Henderson’s contract after a February on‑air clash with host Kyle Sandilands, who is accused of serious misconduct. The ban follows breaches like leaked staff‑urinating audio and explicit game segments. Sandilands must show how he will fix the breach or face contract termination.
