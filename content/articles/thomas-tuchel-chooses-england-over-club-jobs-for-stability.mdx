Thomas Tuchel has decided to stay on England’s bench, extending his contract until Euro 2028 instead of chasing big club roles. He likes the stability, fewer owners to please, and less constant media pressure that come with national team duties. Club jobs feel like a “wheel” of constant change, while managing England lets him focus on football without transfer headaches.
