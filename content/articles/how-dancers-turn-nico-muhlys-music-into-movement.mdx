Nico Muhly explains how he writes music and how dancers use their own counting to move it. He talks about the difference between the way a score shows rhythm and the way dancers feel it. Muhly has worked with choreographers who change the beat and add pauses, making the same music feel different. He shows how music and dance create a shared story and why rhythm matters for both performers.
