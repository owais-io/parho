Cecilia Giménez, who became famous for a badly restored 19th‑century mural that turned into the infamous “Monkey Christ,” passed away at 94. Her accidental work sparked internet memes, drew crowds to the church in Borja, Spain, and even generated income for the village and a local care home through tourism and eBay sales. An opera based on her story premiered in 2023, and mayor Eduardo Arilla praised her generosity.
