California Gov. Gavin Newsom says TikTok is hiding videos that criticize Donald Trump. He has started a review of the app’s moderation rules, asking the state Justice Department to see if it violates California law. TikTok claims the problem was a technical outage, not censorship. The dispute follows the app’s new U.S.‑owned joint venture, created to keep U.S. data safe.
