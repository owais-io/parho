Spanish writer Arturo Pérez‑Reverte, a member of the Royal Spanish Academy, slammed the body in a newspaper column. He says it ignores professional writers and lets internet commentators, YouTubers and influencers dictate language rules. Pérez‑Reverte warns that accepting every new trend without debate erodes what is considered correct Spanish. The academy has not yet responded, but officials say they will review his complaints and discuss changes in an upcoming meeting.
