The World Cup in Goa was a shocker, with big names falling to surprise players. 26‑year‑old Chinese grandmaster Wei Yi knocked out top Indian star Arjun Erigaisi in a long 79‑move game. Young talents from Uzbekistan, like 19‑year‑old Javokhir Sindarov, also reached the semi‑finals. These outsiders are eyeing spots in the 2026 Candidates tournament, while big‑name players were upset. The event shows a new generation rising.
