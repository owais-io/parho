NASA will send the crew back to Earth earlier than planned because an astronaut fell ill on the International Space Station. The first medical evacuation in 25 years will happen no earlier than 5 p.m. on Wednesday, Jan. 14, with a landing expected in California on Thursday morning. The crew is still stable, but the health issue is undisclosed. The decision was made after talks with medical experts. The station will be shut down by 2031.
