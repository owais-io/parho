Since the UK left the EU, farm exports to Europe have fallen almost 40%. A 37‑percent drop in sales of everything from beef to cheddar has been recorded, with poultry losing the most at 38%. The National Farmers’ Union says easing trade barriers won’t instantly bring back EU markets – it will need years, effort and focus. UK and EU leaders are now setting up regular talks to smooth out the new trading rules and keep farmers protected.
