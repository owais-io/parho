Bill Browder, a former hedge‑fund boss turned activist, says the best way to hurt Russia’s war machine is to stop refineries in India, China and Turkey from buying its oil. He has been lobbying European leaders to use frozen Russian assets to support Ukraine, but Belgium blocks the move. Browder believes cutting oil sales will cut Putin’s funding and help end the conflict.
