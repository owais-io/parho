Monzo’s yearly “Year in Monzo” report, meant to be a light‑hearted look back at how customers spent money, has sparked anger after a customer was told she “fast fooded” and “spent more than most on Just Eat”. Fiona Taylor, who has a history of eating disorder and chronic fatigue, said the comments were humiliating and personal. She filed a complaint with the UK ombudsman. Monzo denied wrongdoing, offered £20, and the case is now back to a senior ombudsman.
