OpenAI CEO Sam Altman told staff that his company has no say over how the Pentagon uses its AI tools for military operations. The admission comes after the U.S. military signed a deal with OpenAI to replace Anthropic’s Claude chatbot, sparking backlash. Employees and rivals worry that the tech could be used for autonomous weapons or surveillance. Altman apologized for rushing the deal and stressed the company wants to keep its AI lawful.
