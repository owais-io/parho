The new archbishop defends a £100m plan called Project Spire that tackles the Church of England’s historic links to transatlantic slavery. Some MPs say the money is wasteful; others argue it’s a moral duty. The fund will invest in projects that help Black‑led businesses and research the church’s past, while also giving grants to support community cohesion and keep parish work strong.
