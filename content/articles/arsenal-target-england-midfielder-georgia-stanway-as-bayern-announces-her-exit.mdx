Arsenal are keen to sign England midfielder Georgia Stanway after Bayern says she'll leave when her contract ends. The 27‑year‑old could move for free, and Arsenal, managed by René Slegers, are seen as the front runners. Stanway has won two European titles with England and three German league titles with Bayern since 2022. Bayern praised her commitment before she announced she wants a new challenge.
