At the Milan and Cortina Winter Games, several US athletes said they felt uneasy about representing their country because of Trump’s policies. Trump called out skier Hunter Hess on social media, saying he shouldn’t have tried out for the team. The athletes, who want to show America’s diversity and compassion, got angry comments from politicians and influencers, and even threats. The US Olympic committee said it was monitoring online abuse and helping athletes stay safe.
