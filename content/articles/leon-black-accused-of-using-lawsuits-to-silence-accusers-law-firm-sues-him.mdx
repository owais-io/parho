A law firm that helped several women accuse Leon Black of sexual misconduct says he used big lawsuits to silence them. Black, a billionaire who co‑founded Apollo Global, denies any wrongdoing. The firm claims his lawsuits were frivolous and hurt its reputation. Black’s lawyers call the claim a sham. The case is about whether wealthy people can weaponise the courts to block complaints.
