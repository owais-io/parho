Police blew up KK Park in Myanmar, a scam hub that once trapped thousands of workers. The raid drew attention, but scams are still booming across Southeast Asia, making up about $44 bn a year—about 40 % of the region’s formal economy. Scammers use AI, deepfakes, and fake investment sites to steal hundreds of thousands of dollars from victims worldwide. The industry is so big it’s linked to politicians and armed groups, making it a “scam state.”
