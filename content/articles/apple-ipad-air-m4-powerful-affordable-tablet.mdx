Apple’s new iPad Air M4 packs a much faster chip, 12 GB RAM and a fast Wi‑Fi 7 chip, making it a laptop‑like tablet. It comes in two sizes – 11‑in and 13‑in – and costs £599 or £799. The screen is bright, the battery lasts 9 hours of heavy use, and it runs iPadOS with powerful multitasking. It can replace a laptop with a keyboard case, and its Apple Pencil support turns it into a great drawing tool.
