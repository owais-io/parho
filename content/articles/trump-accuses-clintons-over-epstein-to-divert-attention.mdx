Trump’s administration has been pushing the Clintons over their ties to Jeffrey Epstein, while critics say the move is a distraction from Trump’s own connection. The Justice Department released photos of the former president with Epstein, and a congressional committee sent subpoenas to Bill and Hillary Clinton for testimony. The Clintons refused to comply, sparking calls for contempt. Trump’s critics claim the attacks are a way to deflect attention from his own ties to the disgraced financier.
