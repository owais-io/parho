In the Super League’s opening match, York stunned defending champions Hull KR. The part‑time side, last seen in the top tier in the 1980s, capitalised on a key moment when Hull’s Mikey Lewis was sin‑binned for a tackle. York scored and levelled the game, then won with a drop‑goal. The win shows the new 14‑team league can thrive in North Yorkshire.
