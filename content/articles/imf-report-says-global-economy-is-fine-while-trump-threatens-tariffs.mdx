The International Monetary Fund released a new outlook that keeps saying the world is stable, even as former President Trump threatens huge tariffs on European goods and even Greenland. The report mentions that American import costs are already higher than before, and that AI could help growth but also risk a recession. Trump’s moves, his withdrawal from global bodies, and rising tensions are left out of the calm report.
