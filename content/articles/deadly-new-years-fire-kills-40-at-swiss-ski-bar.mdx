A New Year's party at a bar in the Swiss ski town Crans‑Montana turned into a deadly fire, killing about 40 people and injuring over a hundred. The blaze started in the basement at around 1:30 a.m., spreading fast and trapping many young patrons. Firefighters and ambulances rushed in, but the cause remains unknown and police say no arson or attack is suspected.
