Cisco CEO Chuck Robbins said the AI boom will create big winners but also cause many companies to fail and jobs to disappear, especially in customer‑service roles, but urged workers to embrace the change. Meanwhile the US dollar fell to a four‑year low after President Trump downplayed its decline, leading traders to sell the currency. Gold reached record highs. Investors expect the Fed to keep rates unchanged.
