After his longest State of the Union, President Trump faced sharp criticism from Democrats who say he hasn’t protected American citizens. Senate leader Chuck Schumer and Rep. Ilhan Omar called his call to stand over immigration a sign of failure, while Trump responded by insulting the congresswomen. The address also sparked protests, an arrest of a guest, and heated partisan exchanges over policy and accusations of abuse.
