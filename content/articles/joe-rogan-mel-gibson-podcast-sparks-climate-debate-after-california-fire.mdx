Joe Rogan and actor Mel Gibson talked on a podcast in January, criticizing climate science while Gibson’s California house burned in a wildfire. The interview mixed false claims about science with real climate‑change facts, blaming California officials for not spending enough on fire prevention. They also spread misinformation about COVID, medicines, and wildfires. The piece shows how polarised 2025 is, with rich people able to rebuild while many poorer residents face lasting damage.
