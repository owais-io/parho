After saying the club was “throwing him under the bus”, Liverpool decided to give Mohamed Salah a short break. He will miss the Champions League game against Inter Milan and could also skip the next Premier League match versus Brighton. Liverpool said no punishment is needed, and Salah will also leave for the Africa Cup of Nations with Egypt. The club hopes the pause will help the team’s form.
