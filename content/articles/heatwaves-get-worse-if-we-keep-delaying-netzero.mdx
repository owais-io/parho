Scientists found that pushing the global net‑zero target past 2050 will make heatwaves hotter, longer and more frequent—especially near the equator. Even after net‑zero is reached, extreme heatwaves will linger for centuries. The study warns that waiting too long worsens the problem, but also says we can start planning for stronger heatwaves for communities and businesses now.
