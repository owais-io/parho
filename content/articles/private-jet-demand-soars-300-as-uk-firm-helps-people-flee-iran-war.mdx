Since the Iran war began, people in the Middle East are trying to get out. Demand for private jets has jumped about 300 %. A UK firm, Air Charter Service, is handling many requests from governments, wealthy travellers and even stranded Britons. With flights cancelled, governments are using Oman's airports to ferry people home, but delays and tight air lanes make the situation tough.
