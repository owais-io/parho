Kentucky Republican Thomas Massie was called a “lowlife” by Donald Trump on Christmas for co‑authoring a law that forced the release of all Jeffrey Epstein files. Massie responded on X, asking supporters to back him for his 2026 race against Trump‑backed challenger Ed Gallrein. Within hours, he raised nearly $3,000 from more than 40 donors. The incident shows how Massie is using Trump’s attack to rally funds and stand against the former president.
