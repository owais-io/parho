Iran told the United States not to let Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu interfere with talks about Iran’s nuclear program. Netanyahu is planning a quick meeting with President Trump to push for limits on Iran’s missiles and to pressure the country to curb its support for proxy groups and improve human rights. Tehran wants the U.S. to focus on nuclear issues alone and reject Israel’s demands. Iranian officials warned the U.S. to keep its own negotiating agenda.
