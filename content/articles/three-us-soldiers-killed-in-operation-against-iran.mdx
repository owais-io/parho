The US Central Command said three American soldiers died and five were seriously wounded during strikes on Iran. These are the first confirmed deaths after the US began the campaign on Saturday. The attacks, carried out with Israel, target Iranian officials and have sparked fears of a wider war. The news has sparked protests in the US, with people demanding an end to the conflict.
