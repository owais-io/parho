A huge winter storm left more than 40 people dead and knocked out power in nearly 300,000 homes. Schools closed and flights were cancelled. People shared what it was like: one woman slept in her car because the power was out, another found ice in her toilets when a house got too cold, and a mayor was seen shovelling snow for a neighbor. Some even skidded on slippery roads. The storm showed how tough winter can be.
