Real Madrid lost 3-2 to Albacete in the Copa del Rey, ending the first week of new manager Álvaro Arbeloa's reign. The game saw the side start with many absences – key stars like Mbappé and Bellingham were left out – and a foggy pitch that made play difficult. Albacete’s Javi Villar and Jefté Betancor scored, while Arbeloa said he would keep the same team for future games, hoping the experience will help the club.
