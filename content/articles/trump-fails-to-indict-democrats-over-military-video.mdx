The Trump administration tried to charge six Democratic senators and representatives for a 90‑second clip that urged U.S. troops to refuse illegal orders. A Washington grand jury said no indictment would be filed, calling the effort a “weaponization” of the justice system. Senators Mark Kelly and Elissa Slotkin said the rejection was a win for democracy and free speech, and warned Trump’s tactics aim to silence critics.
