Jutta Leerdam won the women’s 1000m at the Milan Winter Games, beating her rival and setting a new Olympic record of 1 minute 12.31 seconds. The Dutch skater’s win gave the Netherlands its first medals of the Games, with a crowd of mostly orange‑clad Dutch cheering in the 7,000‑seat arena. Leerdam’s fiancé, boxer‑influencer Jake Paul, was seen crying in the stands. The victory is a highlight of Leerdam’s record‑setting season and a moment of personal triumph.
