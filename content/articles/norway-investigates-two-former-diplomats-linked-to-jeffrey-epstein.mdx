Norway's police are looking into former ambassador Mona Juul and her husband Terje Rød‑Larsen over possible corruption linked to Jeffrey Epstein. Documents show the couple visited Epstein's island with their kids and may have received a $10 million inheritance. They are cooperating with investigators, but lawyers say the charges are unfounded. The case has shocked Norway, where many officials were tied to Epstein’s network.
