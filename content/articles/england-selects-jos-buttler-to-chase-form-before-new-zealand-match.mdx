England’s T20 World Cup squad may not include all of its best players, but coach Tim Southee has confirmed that Jos Buttler will play against New Zealand. The former captain’s form has dipped – his best score is just 26 in six games – but the coaching staff believe he can snap the slump soon. New Zealand’s coach also expects a comeback, and both teams know the match will decide who tops the group.
