The UK is unclear about what it means that the US is now in charge of Venezuela. Chief Secretary Darren Jones said the UK will wait to see if the US strike broke international law and wants a quick, peaceful power shift that reflects Venezuelans’ wishes. He said the UK had no part in the plan and will not judge its success. The UK’s prime minister said he wants facts before deciding on consequences.
