Senator John Fetterman, a Democrat from Pennsylvania, voted to move Republican Senator Markwayne Mullin forward as the next homeland security secretary. Democrats slammed the move, calling it a betrayal of their party and of Pennsylvanian voters. Fetterman’s vote comes after he has taken several pro‑Republican stances, and he has low approval among Democrats but high among Republicans, with 73% of Republicans approving of him and only 22% of Democrats doing so.
