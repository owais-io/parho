Gloria Allred, a long‑time women’s rights lawyer, said that the U.K.’s quick arrest of Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor won’t lead to real justice for the girls and women abused by Jeffrey Epstein. She pointed out that U.S. police and courts often ignore rape and child‑abuse cases involving powerful men, while prioritizing financial crimes. Allred criticised the U.S. Justice Department for its lack of accountability and said many key documents remain hidden.
