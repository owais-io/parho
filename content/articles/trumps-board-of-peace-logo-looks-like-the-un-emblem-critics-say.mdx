Trump’s new “Board of Peace” launched at Davos has a logo that looks like the United Nations flag but in gold and focused on North America. He said it will help end conflicts and will be chaired by him. European leaders say they won’t join, fearing it could replace the UN. The board would cost $1 billion to join. Trump’s gold‑obsessed branding runs from the Oval Office to phones and visas.
