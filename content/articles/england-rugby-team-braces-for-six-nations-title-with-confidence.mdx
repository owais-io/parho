England rugby team is gearing up for the Six Nations championship with a new sense of confidence and swagger. Coach Steve Borthwick has urged the side to play for a grand slam, especially in their big game against Wales. Centre Tommy Freeman talks about a mindset shift that helped stop last‑minute losses and now the players are focused on winning every match, not just being good. That’s the plan as they chase their first title since 2020.
