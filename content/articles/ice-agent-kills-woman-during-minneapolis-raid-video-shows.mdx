During a large ICE raid in Minneapolis, a federal agent shot a 37‑year‑old woman, Renee Good, in the face as she was in a car. Video shows the agent firing three times while the SUV tried to back away. The Department of Homeland Security said the driver tried to run the officer over, but the footage shows no injuries. The incident sparked protests, city officials demanded ICE leave, and state leaders ordered an investigation.
