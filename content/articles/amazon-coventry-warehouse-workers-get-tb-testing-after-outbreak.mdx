Amazon's Coventry warehouse is testing workers for tuberculosis after several cases were found last year. The UK health agency started a screening program, and 10 employees tested positive for latent TB. Amazon is working with the NHS to treat affected staff and offer tests to those nearby. The union wants a temporary closure until infection controls are in place, and the city reminds people to seek free NHS care if they have symptoms.
