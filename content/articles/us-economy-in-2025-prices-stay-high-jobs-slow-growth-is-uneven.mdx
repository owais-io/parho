Trump says the US economy is booming, with jobs jumping and prices falling. But official data show a mixed picture for 2025. Inflation stays high, and the Fed has kept interest rates near record highs to fight it, while Trump wants lower rates. Job growth has stalled and unemployment has risen, especially after a long government shutdown. GDP has bounced back after a weak start, but the future looks uncertain.
