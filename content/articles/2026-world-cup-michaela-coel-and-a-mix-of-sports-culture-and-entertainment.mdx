The Guardian’s 2026 preview covers a wide range of events that fans of sport, culture and entertainment can look forward to. The FIFA World Cup will feature 48 teams, including Ghana and England, with more African and Caribbean teams on the rise. Caribbean festivals like Antigua’s carnival and book fairs in Somaliland and Jamaica will celebrate heritage. The Obama presidential library will open in Chicago. New books, films starring Michaela Coel and a Bad Bunny tour round out the lineup.
