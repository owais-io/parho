Australia won the 2024 Ashes after a tricky schedule that favored them. England's fast, high‑risk batting (Bazball) let the Aussies take control. Key moments included Harry Brook dropping Usman Khawaja, Jamie Smith missing Head, Alex Carey’s sharp fielding and the run‑out of Ben Stokes by Josh Inglis. Travis Head scored big for Australia, and the team’s disciplined play on fast pitches sealed the series. Overall, the Australian conditions and mistakes cost England.
