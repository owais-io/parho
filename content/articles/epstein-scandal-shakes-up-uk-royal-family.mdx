It focuses on Prince Harry’s (now Prince Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor) refusal to cooperate with investigations, his strained relationship with King Charles and Prince William, and the internal divisions within the monarchy. The piece also notes King Charles’s cancer and the need for a major reform of the institution before the next generation can take over.
