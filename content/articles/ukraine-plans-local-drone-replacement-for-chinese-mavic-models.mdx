Ukraine’s defence minister announced plans to produce a domestic drone like the Chinese DJI Mavic, aiming to cut foreign dependence. The new model will have a longer range. Meanwhile, Russian strikes cut power for over a million households, damaged nuclear‑related sites, and killed civilians in cities such as Kryvyi Rih. Peace talks continue, with U.S. and Ukrainian officials pressing Moscow for stronger sanctions, while a Trump‑Putin meeting in Davos offered a glimmer of a deal.
