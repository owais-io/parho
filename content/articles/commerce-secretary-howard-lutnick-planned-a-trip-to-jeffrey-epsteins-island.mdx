Howard Lutnick, Trump’s commerce secretary, was shown by new DOJ files to have arranged a trip to Jeffrey Epstein’s private island in 2012. Emails show Lutnick invited Epstein and his circle to a Caribbean cruise and later to a holiday dinner on the island. He also sent Epstein an invitation to a Hillary Clinton fundraiser in 2015. Lutnick denied spending time with Epstein and said he never worked with him.
