US military leaders told lawmakers that they probably can’t destroy every Iranian drone that’s being fired at U.S. targets. The drones are cheap, one‑way attack planes that fly low and slow, making them harder to hit. Instead of trying to shoot all of them, the U.S. is focusing on taking out the launch sites. The briefing also highlighted the high cost of interceptors and the large amount of weapons the U.S. still has in reserve.
