Catherine, the Princess of Wales, posted a short video on her 44th birthday to thank nature for helping her heal after cancer. It was the last of her four “Mother Nature” clips that show different seasons. She talks about winter bringing stillness, patience, and a chance to reflect. She also says that being close to nature helps people feel calmer and healthier. After her cancer treatment she has been slowly returning to royal duties and supporting NHS staff.
