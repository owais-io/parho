Donald Trump has repeatedly criticized Somali Americans, especially in Minnesota, linking them to his anti‑immigration agenda. His harsh comments have frightened Somali families, who worry about ICE raids and discrimination in schools. The Somali diaspora—strongly connected worldwide—has rallied together, sharing resources and offering support. While the U.S. and Europe see a rise in right‑wing hostility, Minnesota’s community centers and volunteers show that solidarity can still prevail.
