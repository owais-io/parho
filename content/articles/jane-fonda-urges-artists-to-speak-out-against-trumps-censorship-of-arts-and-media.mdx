Jane Fonda led a spirited rally outside Washington’s Kennedy Center, calling on artists and everyday people to refuse silence as Trump’s administration clamps down on books, arts funding and media. She warned that planned shutdowns and budget cuts signal rising authoritarianism. Journalists, musicians and writers joined her to urge the public to stand up for free speech and protect cultural expression.
