Macron visited Cyprus after Iranian drones hit the island. He said Europe must protect Cyprus and that France and other EU countries will send air‑defence systems and warships. The attacks also prompted plans to reopen the busy Hormuz waterway, which has been blocked by the war. Greece, Italy, Spain, the Netherlands and the UK are all helping to guard the island. The move shows Europe standing together against the Iranian strike.
