Great Western Railway will begin passenger service with its new battery‑powered train on a short West Ealing‑Greenford route this Saturday. The train can recharge in just 3½ minutes at West Ealing using a 2,000‑kW charger that only lights up when the train is overhead. It holds up to 273 passengers and could help replace diesel services where installing overhead wires is too costly or difficult.
