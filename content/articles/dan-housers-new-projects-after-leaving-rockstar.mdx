Dan Houser, who helped make GTA and Red Dead, left Rockstar in 2019. He tried film and TV but then started Absurd Ventures in Santa Monica. The studio released a podcast called A Better Paradise and is now turning it into an open‑world game. Other projects include a comedy‑style universe called Absurdaverse and a story game based on the comic American Caper. Houser says he still wants strong, single‑player adventures and is not worried about the rise of live‑service games.
