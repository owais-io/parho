Toyah Cordingley was stabbed to death on a Cairns beach in 2018 while walking her dog. Her attacker, Rajwinder Singh, was extradited from India and is now given a life sentence with no chance of parole for 25 years. The community has spent seven years demanding justice, printing bumper stickers and holding vigils that kept the case in public view. The sentencing brings relief and a sense of closure to Cairns residents.
