Next has posted £1.16 bn pre‑tax profit for the year. The extra £15 m in fuel and freight costs from the Middle East conflict is tiny and can be offset by savings elsewhere, CEO Simon Wolfson said. He added that Next might even add £8 m to last year’s profit by default. Prices will only rise if costs stay high. Shares jumped 5 %.
