Michael Dickson, a 30‑year‑old Australian punter for the Seattle Seahawks, helped his team win the Super Bowl and became only the second Aussie ever to win the trophy. In the 29‑13 victory over the Patriots, Dickson made seven punts, averaging nearly 48 yards, with a key kick that put New England on its own line. He celebrated by talking about craving chips with chicken salt back home. His performance earned praise from commentators and made him the NFL’s highest‑paid punter.
