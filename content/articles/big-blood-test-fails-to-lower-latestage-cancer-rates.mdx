A large study in the UK tested the Galleri blood test, which claims to spot 50 types of cancer early. The trial, involving 142,000 people, showed the test didn’t cut the number of late‑stage cancers. While it found more early cancers, it didn't save lives or reduce deaths. The company’s share price fell, and investors are wary. For now, there’s no proven blood test that improves survival.
