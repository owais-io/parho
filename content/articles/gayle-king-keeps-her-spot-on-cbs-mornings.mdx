CBS News has just signed a new contract with Gayle King, ending months of speculation about her leaving the morning show. King will stay on CBS Mornings as co‑host, but she says she’s open to other roles within the network. The move keeps the show’s main anchor and supports plans to refresh the program after recent co‑host changes. King’s new deal keeps her in the company’s long‑term future.
