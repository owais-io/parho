Zelensky said the next US‑Russia‑Ukraine talks will only happen once the Middle East is stable, especially after US‑Israel strikes on Iran. He warned that Russia used Iranian‑supplied drones. Ukraine wants strong security guarantees from the US and Europe. Geneva talks made little progress; Russia may accept guarantees but no summit yet. Russia keeps fighting in Ukraine, taking settlements and damaging a gas plant, and it condemns the US‑Israel campaign.
