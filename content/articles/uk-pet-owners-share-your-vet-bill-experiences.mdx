The UK's Competition and Markets Authority has told vets to cap prescription costs at £21 and plans a price‑comparison site. It says pet‑owner satisfaction with vet bills is low and competition among vets is weak. Vets must now inform clients that medicines can be cheaper online and prescriptions could save money. The watchdog is asking UK pet owners to share their bill experiences.
