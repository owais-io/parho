Michael Carrick says his friend Ole Gunnar Solskjaer backed him when he became Manchester United’s interim coach. Solskjaer, who was also considered for the role, was close to Carrick from their time together in 2006‑07 and when Solskjaer was a coach. Carrick says Solskjaer wishes the team well and is ready to help if needed. Carrick also talked about how he plans to play the next games.
