The Justice Department filed federal charges against Anthony James Kazmierczak, 55, who sprayed a syringe of apple‑cider‑vinegar mix on Representative Ilhan Omar during a Minneapolis town‑hall. FBI tests confirm the liquid stained her clothes. Kazmierczak is accused of assaulting and intimidating Omar, a crime punishable by up to a year in prison. He was previously arrested for a third‑degree assault. The incident comes amid ongoing threats against Omar and criticism of former President Trump’s remarks.
