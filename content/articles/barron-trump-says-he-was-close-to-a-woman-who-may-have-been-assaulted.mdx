The court heard that Barron Trump, the youngest son of former U.S. President Donald Trump, said he was “very close” to a woman who claimed she was being beaten by a man. He saw a video call where the man punched her and called the police. The woman is on trial for assault and rape by a man named Matvei Rumiantsev, who denies the charges. Barron also asked friends in the U.S. to call the police.
