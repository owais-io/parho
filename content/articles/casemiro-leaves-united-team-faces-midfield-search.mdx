Casemiro will leave Manchester United after the 2023‑24 season, and the club now faces a hard task finding a replacement. The 34‑year‑old defensive midfielder has been key for United, but his wage bill and the team's midfield structure need updating. United may look to sign young players such as Carlos Baleba, Elliot Anderson, or Adam Wharton, and possibly bring back Rúben Neves. New manager will decide the right profile.
