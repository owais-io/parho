Eline van der Velden built the AI actress Tilly Norwood to spark debate about robots in movies. After the first short film, Hollywood and actors unions condemned the idea, and she received many death threats. Van der Velden says she expected criticism but was shocked by the hate. She sees benefits for actors, plans a drama series, and wants to use AI positively.
