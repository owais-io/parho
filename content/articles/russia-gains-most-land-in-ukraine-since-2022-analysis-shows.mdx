Russia has taken the biggest chunk of Ukrainian land since the war started in 2022, grabbing 5,600 km² last year, a new study says. Kyiv is getting ready for a security meeting with allies as Moscow keeps striking towns. President Volodymyr Zelenskyy named new top advisers, including spy chief Kyrylo Budanov, and plans to replace the defence minister. The Russians also bombed Kharkiv, killing a child, while Ukraine moved over 3,000 children out of fighting zones.
