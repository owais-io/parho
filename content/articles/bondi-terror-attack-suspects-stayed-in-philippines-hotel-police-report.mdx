The claim comes as Australian leaders blame the Prime Minister for the 15 deaths. Meanwhile, a 87‑year‑old Holocaust survivor’s grandson praised a Bondi survivor for her bravery. The story also touches on lobbying concerns around an AUKUS‑related group.
