England coach Steve Borthwick has swapped his two front‑row players just before the Six Nations match in Edinburgh. He wants fresh energy to counter Scotland’s hard‑hit style. The change follows a tough loss to Wales, and Borthwick is looking for a win on Scottish soil to boost England’s title hopes. Scotland will try to start fast and keep the game physical. Fans expect a tense, high‑pressure encounter.
