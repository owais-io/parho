Cabinet minister Chris Bowen said the government clarified rules for MPs’ travel expenses before the federal election, not expanding them. He said the changes were meant to help staff book trips safely. Critics point out that several ministers, including Don Farrell and Anika Wells, used tax‑funded travel for personal events. The Prime Minister has asked an independent watchdog to review the perks, and ministers have referred their claims for audit.
