Rachel Reeves gave a calm, low‑key spring forecast that many small and medium businesses felt did not address their real worries. The forecast added no new relief for rising costs, especially the upcoming 1 April business rates change and high energy prices. Owners from a Nottingham brewery, a London metal‑tray factory, a defence tech firm, a department store and a construction recruiter all said they still face high inflation, wage and rate burdens with little clear support from the government.
