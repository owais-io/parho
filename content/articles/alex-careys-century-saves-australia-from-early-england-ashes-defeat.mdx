On opening day at Adelaide Oval, keeper Alex Carey scored 106 runs, giving Australia a boost after England’s early dominance. England's bowlers struggled; Jofra Archer took 3 for 29, while England lost key wickets. Carey's steady batting lifted the team to 326 for 8. The day also saw emotional moments after a nearby terror attack and changes in the Australian lineup, but Carey's play turned the match around.
