Greg Blewett, a former Adelaide cricketer, says the modern Adelaide Oval is a big help for England’s hopes. The ground changed a lot since 2013, adding a big stadium but keeping historic trees and arches. England has struggled on day‑games here, but Blewett thinks the new pitch is easier to play on. He also praised local stars like Travis Head and said a day match could give England a better chance to win.
