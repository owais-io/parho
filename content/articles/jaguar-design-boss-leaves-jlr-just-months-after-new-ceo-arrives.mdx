Gerry McGovern, who spent two decades designing Jaguar and Range Rover models, left JLR's chief creative role just months after new CEO PB Balaji took over. The departure comes after a cyber‑attack that shut the company’s factories in August. Industry experts say it signals a shift in Tata’s control. McGovern’s bold designs, like the pink electric Jaguar, may face changes before they hit production.
