Dayton Webber, a 27‑year‑old who became the first quadruple‑amputee to play professional cornhole, has been arrested on murder charges after allegedly shooting a passenger in his car in Maryland. The incident happened at night, Webber shot the 27‑year‑old passenger, then drove away with the body. He was later caught in Virginia and taken back to Maryland. Police say the victim and passengers all knew each other, but no motive is yet known.
