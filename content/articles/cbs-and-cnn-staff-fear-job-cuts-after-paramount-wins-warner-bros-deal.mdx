Netflix dropped its $83 billion bid for Warner Bros Discovery, leaving Paramount Skydance as the new owner. Staff at CBS News and CNN worry the merger will trim jobs and let politically‑aligned leaders change programming. Concerns include possible censorship, a role for Bari Weiss, and pressure from Trump‑friendly executives. Regulators still need to approve the deal, so the future is uncertain.
