UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer says US intelligence agencies support the Chagos Islands deal, even though President Trump has publicly opposed it. The agreement gives Mauritius control over the islands but keeps a long‑term UK lease on the military base at Diego Garcia, which hosts U.S. forces. Trump linked the handover to his desire for Greenland, calling the move a “stupid” act of weakness. The UK says the deal is finalized and will not be reversed.
