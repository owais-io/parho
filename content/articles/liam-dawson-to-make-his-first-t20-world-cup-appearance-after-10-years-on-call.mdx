Liam Dawson, who has been an England squad member for a decade, is finally set to debut at the 2024 T20 World Cup. The left‑arm spinner has taken 12 wickets at 19.25 in recent games and has been a key player for Hampshire. He will face Nepal on Feb. 8 and help England on the spin‑friendly pitches of India. His long wait ends as he joins the team in a big role.
