After Liverpool’s 6‑goal loss to Leeds, star striker Mohamed Salah gave a long, angry rant to reporters. He blamed being left on the bench, said the team’s coach was losing control, and made a lot of headlines. The outburst shocked journalists, caused a mess in the press room, and raised worries that the controversy could hurt the club’s chances in upcoming European matches and possibly lead to a change of coach.
