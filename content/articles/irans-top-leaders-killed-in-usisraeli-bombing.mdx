US and Israel launched a heavy bombing campaign that killed Iran’s top leaders, including its Supreme Leader and key military heads. With its leadership shattered, Iran’s ability to fight back is weakened. The country now relies on a shrinking stockpile of drones and missiles to hit US and Israeli targets and to disrupt shipping near the Gulf of Oman. Most attacks have been ineffective, and Iran’s larger plans look uncertain.
