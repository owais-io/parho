Police in Tuncurry, NSW, chased a 41‑year‑old man who burst into a house armed with a knife and chainsaw and stabbed a woman. The intruder fled in a white 4WD, crashed on Forster Bridge, and later tried to break into another car. Officers used Tasers, but the man ran toward a police officer and was shot dead. The woman was hospitalized in serious but stable condition.
