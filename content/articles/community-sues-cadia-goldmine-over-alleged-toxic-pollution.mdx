Residents near Orange, New South Wales, have filed a class action against Cadia goldmine, claiming the mine releases a “toxic trifecta” of air, land, and water pollution. They seek compensation for falling property values and an order to stop further contamination. The lawsuit follows years of complaints about dust, heavy metals, and PFAS in local water, and the mine’s past fines for air‑quality violations.
