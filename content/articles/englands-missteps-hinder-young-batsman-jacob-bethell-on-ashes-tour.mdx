England’s approach to the Ashes, with quick, aggressive batting and a short‑order lineup, has backfired. In Perth the team lost a winning position, and in Melbourne they were beaten again. Young fast bowler Jacob Bethell was pushed into the No.3 spot too early, causing frustration. The article also praises players like Josh Tongue and highlights problems with team management and off‑field distractions that hurt England’s performance.
