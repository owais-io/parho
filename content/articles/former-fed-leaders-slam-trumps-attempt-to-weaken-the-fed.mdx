Trump’s Justice Department is investigating Fed Chair Jerome Powell over a Senate hearing about office renovations. Fed bosses Alan Greenspan, Ben Bernanke and Janet Yellen warned that attacking the central bank could hurt the economy and spark inflation. They say the Fed must stay independent from politics. Trump says he knows nothing about the probe and wants to keep the Fed free to set rates based on data. A move that could trigger 1970s‑style price rises.
