On Monday evening, Heaton Park synagogue in Manchester lit its Hanukah menorah while remembering the 15 Australians killed at Sydney’s Bondi Beach. Police surrounded the car‑park as the community reflected on a recent 2023 antisemitic shooting that killed two locals. Despite grief, the service stayed joyful, with music and dancing. Mayor Andy Burnham pledged more protection. Similar vigils in London united Jews worldwide against rising hate.
