A 37‑year‑old nurse, Alex Pretti, was shot by federal agents in Minneapolis during protests over Trump’s immigration crackdown. NBA star Tyrese Haliburton and WNBA MVP Breanna Stewart, among others, posted messages calling the killing “murder.” The incident follows the death of another citizen, Renee Good, and has sparked outcry from athletes, teams, and community leaders who urge calm and a review of ICE policies.
