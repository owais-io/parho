Resident doctors in England went on strike after talks with the government failed. They want higher pay and more training places to become specialists. The NHS is already strained by an early winter flu wave, so patients feel the impact. Politicians say the strike is reckless, but doctors say their wages have fallen in real terms. The dispute could last until 2026 if no deal is reached.
