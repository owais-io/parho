Parents are being urged to stop using GPS trackers on their children. A group of over 70 health professionals says that constant monitoring causes anxiety and keeps kids from learning independence. Tracking can feel like a big invasion of privacy and shows children that the world is unsafe. The article shares a mom’s own experience with an AirTag and how her son refused to be tracked. Experts say freedom helps kids grow.
