Nearly 60 % of people in Wales don’t know how the new closed‑list voting system works, a recent poll says. They also miss which powers belong to the Welsh government versus Westminster – most don’t realise health and education are devolved. The confusion comes partly from most Welsh voters getting news from UK outlets that don’t label stories by nation. The gap could hurt how voters decide in May’s Senedd election.
