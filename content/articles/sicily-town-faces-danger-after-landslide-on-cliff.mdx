A storm hit Niscemi, a hilltop town in Sicily, and caused a 4‑km long landslide that has left houses perched on a cliff edge. About 1,500 residents were moved to safety, and the road to the coast is shut. The mayor says the danger is serious but no one was hurt. Sicily’s government has declared a state of emergency and will spend money to help rebuild.
