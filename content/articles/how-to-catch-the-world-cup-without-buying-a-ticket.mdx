The World Cup can still feel huge even if you can’t buy a seat. Leander Schaerlaeckens explains how U.S. fans can enjoy the 2026 event by attending free fan festivals, watch‑parties, open training sessions, and friendly matches held all over the country. These events let people share the excitement without the steep price of a stadium ticket, keeping the big tournament local and inclusive.
