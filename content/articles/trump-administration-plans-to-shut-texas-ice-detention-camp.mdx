The Trump administration is moving to close Camp East Montana, an ICE detention camp at Fort Bliss, Texas. The move comes after three deaths, a measles outbreak and reports of harsh treatment and beatings. The camp, opened in July, can hold 5,000 but now has about 1,500 inmates. ICE is shifting to new warehouse‑style facilities, and officials say the closure is part of a new detention model.
