Donald Trump spent a day in Florida meeting Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu at Mar‑a‑Lago. He cheered Netanyahu, said he would receive the Israel Prize, and warned Hamas that it would face “hell to pay” if it didn’t disarm. Trump also slammed Federal Reserve chair Jerome Powell, calling him a “fool” and said he might sue him for incompetence. The visit happened amid talks to move a Gaza peace plan forward.
