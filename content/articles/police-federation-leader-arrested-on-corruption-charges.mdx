The chief executive of the Police Federation of England and Wales, Mukund Krishna, was arrested by City of London police on suspicion of corruption and fraud. Three other national board members were also taken into custody. The investigation looks into alleged financial wrongdoing within the union that represents 130,000 officers. Police say the case is complex and they are seeking further information.
