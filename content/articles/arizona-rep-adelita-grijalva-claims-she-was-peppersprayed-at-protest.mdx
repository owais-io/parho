Arizona Representative Adelita Grijalva says federal ICE agents sprayed her with pepper spray during a protest outside a Tucson Mexican restaurant. She was standing nearby while the agents confronted protesters blocking a raid. Grijalva, who had come to watch, says she was asked to leave and then sprayed. The Department of Homeland Security says she was not targeted. The incident sparked criticism from lawmakers and calls for an investigation.
