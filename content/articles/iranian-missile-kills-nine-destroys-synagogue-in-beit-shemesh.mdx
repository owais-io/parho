An Iranian missile hit Beit Shemesh, killing nine people, including four teenagers, and blowing up a synagogue. The blast also damaged a bomb shelter where 30 residents were inside; two died and 28 survived. The attack is the deadliest Israel has suffered since the June war with Iran. It shows how older shelters can be hit and the danger to civilians when missiles slip through air defenses.
