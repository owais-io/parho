England’s Rugby World Cup squad is in flux as coach Steve Borthwick finalises his list for 2027. Core players such as captain Maro Itoje and Oliver Chessum stay, while Tom Willis is ruled out for France. Injuries and shaky performances have pushed players like George Ford into doubt. The squad also mixes veterans with new names, and key spots like scrum‑half and hooker still face uncertainty. New hopefuls like Joe Heyes get chances, while veteran Jamie George faces retirement.
