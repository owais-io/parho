At the Munich Security Conference, Danish Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen told reporters that she thinks former U.S. President Donald Trump still wants to control Greenland, even though he recently backed down from threats to seize the island. Frederiksen said the desire remains, noting that earlier talks eased the situation but tension between the U.S. and Europe over the Arctic territory is still high.
