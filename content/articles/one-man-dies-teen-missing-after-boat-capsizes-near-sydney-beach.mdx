A boat overturned in heavy waves near Palm Beach, a beach north of Sydney, on Wednesday. Surf Life Saving rescued a 40‑year‑old man, who later died in the hospital, and a second man was pulled from the rocks and is in stable condition. A 14‑year‑old boy who was on the boat is still missing. Police and Marine Rescue are searching with helicopters, but rough seas make the task hard.
