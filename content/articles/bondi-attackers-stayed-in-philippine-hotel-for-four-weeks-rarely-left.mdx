Police say the father and son who carried out the Bondi beach attack stayed in a Davao City hotel for the whole month they were in the Philippines, only coming out for short trips. They didn’t meet other guests or receive visitors, and no signs of training or weapons were found. Philippine officials reject claims the pair trained there, and say the stay was just a visit, not a terrorist training trip.
