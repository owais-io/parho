Prince Andrew was arrested over claims he misused his role as a trade envoy, sparking fresh outrage. The news hit King Charles just as he was attending London Fashion Week, showing the strain on the royal household. The scandal has raised questions about the crown’s money, privacy and popularity. Polls say many want the monarchy to change or even end. The case could reshape the future of the royal family.
