Leicester City and Liverpool are fighting to avoid the Women’s Super League relegation play‑off. The league’s top is a no‑contest, but the race for the last spot is tense. Leicester sit 12th with no wins, while Liverpool, just above them, have a chance to climb. A win could keep both clubs in the top tier, with next season’s expansion giving a lifeline. Matchups against big teams like Manchester City and Arsenal make the finish dramatic.
