Russia launched a big missile and drone attack on Ukraine, killing six people and injuring 15. The strike hit the Kyiv region first, damaging power plants, houses, schools and shops, and then struck Sumy, Kharkiv, Dnipro and Mykolaiv. Ukraine said 430 drones and 68 missiles were fired, most of them shot down. Poland scrambled jets, Moldova warned of a drone crossing its border, and Kyiv urges allies for more air‑defence.
