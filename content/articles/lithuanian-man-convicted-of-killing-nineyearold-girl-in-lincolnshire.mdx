A 26‑year‑old Lithuanian man, Deividas Skebas, was found guilty of murdering a nine‑year‑old girl in Lincolnshire in 2022. He stabbed the child while she was playing outside her mother’s shop. The jury, after more than seven hours of deliberation, reached a guilty verdict 11‑to‑one. Skebas, who had schizophrenia, pleaded that his mental illness should lead to a manslaughter conviction, but the court rejected that. He will be sentenced in February.
