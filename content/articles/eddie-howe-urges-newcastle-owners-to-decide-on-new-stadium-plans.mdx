Eddie Howe is calling on Newcastle’s new Saudi owners to decide whether to rebuild St James’ Park or build a new stadium and to start a new training complex for all teams. He says a clear plan would help the club, especially the academy. Though he’s almost certain he won’t see the projects finished while he’s manager, he wants the decisions made right and not rushed. He’s proud of recent trophies but sees the lack of clarity as a problem.
