Jonny Woo, a London drag artist and writer, vents about the “small plates” trend that keeps popping up in restaurants. He says ordering dozens of tiny bites is pricey, awkward, and feels like a leftover Covid habit. Instead of a single, satisfying meal, diners pay twice as much to piece together a full dinner. Woo prefers classic, generous portions and is ready to stop eating those tiny, expensive bites. He’s also promoting his upcoming Un‑Royal Variety show.
