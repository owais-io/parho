Brendan Rodgers has been appointed head coach of Saudi club Al‑Qadsiah, where the club wants to become one of the league’s top five teams. He comes from Celtic, where money worries were a problem, but Al‑Qadsiah has recently spent €65 m on striker Mateo Retegui and will soon move into a new 46,000‑seat stadium. The club sits fifth, 10 points behind leaders Al‑Nassr, and faces pressure to win trophies after a string of short‑term British hires.
