HMS Dragon, a Type‑45 destroyer, is stuck in Portsmouth dock after a scheduled repair. Getting it ready for a Cyprus mission took longer than usual because it had to be refloated, re‑armed, and supplied with food, fuel and missiles. The work usually takes six weeks, but the navy rushed it to about five days, still missing the expected launch time. The delay highlights resource limits and maintenance schedules in the Royal Navy.
