Senator Richard Blumenthal has asked a Senate panel to investigate former Homeland Security secretary Kristi Noem for possibly lying to Congress about how her adviser Corey Lewandowski helped approve DHS contracts. Blumenthal says evidence shows Lewandowski signed off on contracts and the department treated his signature as a green light, including a $250,000 media contract that favored Trump allies. Noem denied any role, but Blumenthal will push for documents and whistleblowers.
