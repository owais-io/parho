England must focus on smart captaincy, better use of bowlers like Brydon Carse and Josh Tongue, and stronger batting from players such as Zak Crawley. They need to set tricky field placements, choose the right ball early, and play aggressive yet controlled shots. Bringing new talent like Matthew Potts to the bowling attack could surprise Australia. If England mixes tactics, they can win two Ashes Tests back‑to‑back in Sydney.
