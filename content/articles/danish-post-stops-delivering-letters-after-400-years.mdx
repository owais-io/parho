Danish post will stop delivering letters on Dec 30, ending a 400‑year tradition. PostNord will cut 1,500 jobs and remove red postboxes, citing digitalisation and a steep drop in letter use. From Jan 1, the private firm Dao will handle letters – people can drop them at a Dao shop or pay online for postage. Most Danes send very few letters, but younger folks send more, keeping the service alive.
