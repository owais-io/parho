A recent test shows high levels of the banned cancer‑linked chemical Pfos in 25 sites across Cumbria and Lancashire. The dangerous “forever chemicals” can stay in the environment for centuries and are linked to serious health problems. A local business that used a private well was told the water was unsafe but got no help from the council. The government has a new action plan, but critics say it doesn’t go far enough.
