Two tiny burrowing owls accidentally boarded a Miami‑to‑Spain cruise ship. They hid in the ship’s green areas, where crew found them early on. After being captured, the owls were taken to a Spanish wildlife center and kept in quarantine. The Fish and Wildlife Foundation will pay for their flight back to Florida. Once cleared, they’ll be released into a nearby urban park to return to their home range.
