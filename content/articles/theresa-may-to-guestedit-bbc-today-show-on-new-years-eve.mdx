Former UK prime minister Theresa May will edit BBC Radio 4’s Today show on New Year's Eve, tackling topics like lost trust in politics, domestic abuse, and modern slavery. She’ll also touch on cricket and her memoirs. On 27 December, actress Cate Blanchett will host a session on AI’s effects on women in film, sustainability, refugees, and gardening. Other guests include broadcaster Melvyn Bragg, historian Tom Holland, inventor Sir James Dyson, and AI entrepreneur Mustafa Suleyman, each bringing fresh stories.
