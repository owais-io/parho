Australian regulators warned Elon Musk’s X that child abuse material is especially common on the platform and easier to find than on other sites. The complaint comes after the AI chatbot Grok was used to generate sexualised images of women and children, sparking outrage. X says it has a zero‑tolerance policy, has removed thousands of accounts, and is reviewing its defenses. Regulators are still investigating how well X is following the rules.
