Russia launched nearly 400 long‑range drones and 23 cruise missiles at Ukrainian cities, killing at least five people and wounding several more. The strikes hit residential areas, a historic centre in Lviv, a UNESCO site, and a power line in nearby Moldova. Kyiv is pressing the West for more air‑defence weapons as it braces for a spring Russian push, while U.S. attention shifts to Iran.
