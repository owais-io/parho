Dr. Peter Attia, a well‑known longevity expert, quit his CBS News contributor role after it was revealed he had exchanged emails with convicted sex trafficker Jeffrey Epstein. The messages, released during a federal investigation, show a friendly but inappropriate relationship. Attia apologized, said he had no involvement in any crimes, and also stepped down as chief science officer at a protein‑bar company. CBS was criticized for moving too slowly to remove him.
