Cambodia’s Prince Group, a casino‑and‑real‑estate giant, is accused of running huge scam rings and using its wealth to gain political power. The U.S. has sanctioned the group and seized billions, while Britain and Singapore have frozen assets. Cambodia handed the founder to China amid international pressure. The crackdown has freed thousands of workers but raises questions about corruption and the government’s role in the scam industry.
