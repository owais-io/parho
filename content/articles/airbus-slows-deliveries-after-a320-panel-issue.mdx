Airbus announced it will deliver only about 790 of its A320 family planes this year, down from 820, after finding a problem with the metal panels on the front of the aircraft. The issue means hundreds of jets must be inspected, causing airlines to cancel and delay flights. While the panels don’t pose a safety risk, the slowdown keeps Airbus on track to meet its profit goal.
