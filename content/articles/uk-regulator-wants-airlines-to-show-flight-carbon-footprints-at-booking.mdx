The Civil Aviation Authority wants airlines and travel sites to tell UK passengers how much CO₂ a flight will produce. The new rules, starting after April 2027, will require airlines to give clear, comparable numbers based on aircraft type, seat class and fuel use. By making this info visible when booking, passengers can compare flights and choose greener options, helping the aviation sector hit its net‑zero goal.
