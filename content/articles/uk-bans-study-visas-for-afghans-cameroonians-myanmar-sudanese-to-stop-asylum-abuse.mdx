Britain’s Home Office has put an emergency brake on study visas for citizens from Afghanistan, Cameroon, Myanmar and Sudan, and work visas for Afghans, after critics say many are using the system to seek asylum. The move comes after data showed 39 % of the 100,000 2025 asylum claims came via legal routes, especially from those four countries. New rules will limit refugee status to 30 months and encourage returns to safer homes.
