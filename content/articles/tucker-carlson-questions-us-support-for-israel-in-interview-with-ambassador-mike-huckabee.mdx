On Friday, Tucker Carlson filmed a long interview with US ambassador to Israel, Mike Huckabee, at Ben Gurion airport. Carlson asked tough questions about why America helps Israel, and whether Israel should claim more land based on biblical stories. Huckabee defended US aid and Israel’s right to its territory. The debate shows a growing split inside the Republican party, with younger conservatives growing more skeptical of Israel while older Christians stay supportive.
