Jake Paul, a 28‑year‑old YouTuber who turned pro in 2020, will face Olympic‑gold medalist and former heavyweight champion Anthony Joshua in a highly‑publicized match in Miami. The bout has sparked safety concerns because Paul is much lighter and less experienced, with doctors and regulators questioning his wellbeing. Despite this, the fight has drawn a nine‑figure Netflix deal and will pay each fighter at least $50 million. Fans are watching a clash between a social‑media star and a traditional boxing icon.
