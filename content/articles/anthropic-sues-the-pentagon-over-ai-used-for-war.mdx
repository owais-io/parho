Anthropic, an AI company, is fighting the U.S. Department of Defense after the Pentagon banned it from federal projects. The company says the ban violates its first‑amendment rights and its own safety rules, which keep its AI from being used for mass spying or fully autonomous weapons. This showdown highlights how tech firms are increasingly working with the military, a trend that started after protests over Google’s Project Maven and has grown since Trump’s defense push.
