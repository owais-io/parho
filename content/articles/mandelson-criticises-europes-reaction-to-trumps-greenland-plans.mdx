Peter Mandelson, who used to be the UK ambassador to Washington, says European leaders are overreacting to President Trump’s interest in Greenland. He calls their response “histrionic” and urges them to use real military and financial power instead of just talking. Mandelson also said Trump’s actions in Venezuela showed he can act fast, and that Europe needs to step up to stay relevant. He thinks Europe should stop waiting and start acting.
