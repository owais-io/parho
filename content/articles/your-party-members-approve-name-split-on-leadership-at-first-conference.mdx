At its first conference, members of the new left‑wing party Your Party voted 37 % to keep the name. The meeting was split: members chose a shared leadership team instead of a single boss, easing a showdown between Jeremy Corbyn and Zarah Sultana. The conference also approved allowing members to join other groups and debated the party’s future direction. Behind the scenes, old fights over expulsions and money still stir tension.
