American chess grandmaster Daniel Naroditsky died in October 2025 after a toxicology test found methamphetamine, amphetamine, and kratom compounds in his system. The death may have been an overdose or suicide, police say. Naroditsky’s friends discovered him unconscious, and his death has shocked the global chess community. FIDE is reviewing possible sanctions against Kramnik for his comments. A dispute with former world champ Vladimir Kramnik over cheating allegations added strain.
