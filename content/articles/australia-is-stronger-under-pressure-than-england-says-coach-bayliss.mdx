Trevor Bayliss, former England coach, watched the Ashes test in Sydney and said the Aussie side is more robust and better at handling pressure than England. He thinks the series was disappointing but still believes Australia can win. Bayliss attributes the difference to Australia’s finals‑style system and its players’ mental toughness. He also notes England may win in 2027 as Australia ages, keeping his focus on coaching rather than blame.
