Estée Lauder is suing the British perfume designer Jo Malone because she used her own name on a line of perfumes sold by the fashion chain Zara. After selling her brand to Estée Lauder in 1999, Malone was barred from using her name for fragrance marketing. In 2019 she launched a Jo Loves perfume collection with Zara that carried her name, which Estée Lauder says violates the original agreement and threatens the brand’s value.
