Ilya Remeslo, once a top Kremlin supporter who helped silence critics, shocked Russia by posting a 90‑k Telegram message demanding Vladimir Putin resign and face trial as a war criminal. He blamed the Ukraine war for Russia’s economic collapse and accused Putin of corrupt rule. The move sparked debate about its authenticity and may trigger a harsh Kremlin response.
