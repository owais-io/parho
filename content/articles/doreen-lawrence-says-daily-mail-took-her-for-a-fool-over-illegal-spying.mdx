Doreen Lawrence, mother of Stephen Lawrence, told a London court that the Daily Mail publisher used illegal tactics to gather her information, including phone hacking, monitoring, and payments to police. She says she was “taken for a fool” and is one of seven people suing Associated Newspapers. The publisher denies the claims, saying the evidence is false. The case focuses on five articles from 1997‑2007, and the trial is still ongoing.
