The former disco club that hosted stars like Grace Jones, Mick Jagger and Bob Marley is back. In 2027, the historic Le Palace will reopen after a new owner and a team of architects and designers restore its 1970s vibe and heritage. The venue will host concerts for up to 1,400 people and bring the club’s legendary spirit back to Paris.
