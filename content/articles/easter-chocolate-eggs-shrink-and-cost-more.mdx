This Easter, chocolate eggs are getting smaller and pricier. Big brands like Galaxy, M&M’s and Maltesers are raising prices by over 40% while cutting weight. For example, an Asda Galaxy egg costs £5.97 now but weighs only 210 g, down from 252 g last year. Rising cocoa costs and supply issues force manufacturers to pass on the price to shoppers. Buyers should check price per 100 g to compare.
