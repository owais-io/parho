Charities are receiving less money because people are spending more on living costs. The Charities Aid Foundation says total UK donations fell from £15.4bn to £14bn last year. Big donors are shrinking and many small donors are cutting back. Even wealthy people say affordability or lack of interest stops them from giving. The government wants charities to help local renewal, but there’s no quick fix. A better economy and new outreach could help.
