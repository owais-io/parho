Meanwhile, Ukraine talks with the U.S. and Europe for a peace plan.   The EU has kept its sanctions against Russia for six more months until July 31, 2026.   A Russian general was killed in a car bomb in Moscow; investigators think Ukraine may be behind it.   Sumy has been quiet until now, but the war continues in many places.
