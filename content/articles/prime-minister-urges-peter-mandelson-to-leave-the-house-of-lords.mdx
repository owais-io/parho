Keir Starmer says Peter Mandelson should quit the House of Lords and urges the upper chamber to change rules so peers can be removed. He says Mandelson helped Jeffrey Epstein get inside information and may have passed a sensitive tax plan to him. The government will investigate Mandelson’s links with Epstein and his time as business secretary. Mandelson has already left the Labour Party and resigned as ambassador to the US.
