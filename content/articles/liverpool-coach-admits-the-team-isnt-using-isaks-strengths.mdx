Arne Slot, Liverpool’s new boss, said his side isn’t making the most of new striker Alexander Isak’s abilities. Isak scored his first league goal but has struggled to find space and touches. Slot blames the missed pre‑season and the team’s style for the slow start, noting they need to create more chances and get Isak into the final third. He compares him to Haaland, saying the right touches matter more than quantity.
