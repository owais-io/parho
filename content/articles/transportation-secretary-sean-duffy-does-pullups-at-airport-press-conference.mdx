Sean Duffy, the U.S. transportation secretary, held a press conference at Reagan National Airport where he did pull‑ups on camera to promote his plan to improve air travel. He unveiled a $1 billion grant to turn airports into wellness spaces with treadmills, bikes and other fitness options. Duffy also launched a civility campaign urging better passenger behavior and better dressing. Critics say the focus on workouts and fashion distracts from bigger problems like bridge repairs and a shortage of air‑traffic controllers.
