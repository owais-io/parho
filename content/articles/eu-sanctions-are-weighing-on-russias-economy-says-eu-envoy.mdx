EU officials say the sanctions imposed on Russia since 2022 are starting to hurt its economy. The envoy says Russia's oil revenue has fallen, inflation is high, and interest rates are soaring. While Russia keeps buying oil and using drones, the EU has blocked many trade routes and flagged Russian tankers. China remains a major loophole, but overall the sanctions are tightening the financial squeeze.
