Australia’s foreign minister Penny Wong told Israel that Australia supports Lebanon’s sovereignty and will not accept any Israeli occupation of southern Lebanon. She warned Israel’s planned “buffer” against Hezbollah could expand conflict and urged Australians in Lebanon to leave if it becomes unsafe. Australia also pledged extra aid for Lebanese civilians and criticised Israeli moves toward a new border and tightened control over the West Bank.
