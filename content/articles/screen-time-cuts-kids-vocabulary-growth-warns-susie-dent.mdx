Susie Dent says kids are losing words as they spend more time on screens than reading. She warns that a growing gap in vocabulary could hurt learning. Dent suggests parents read, talk and play word games with children. She’s even teaming with a snack brand to add fun words to lunchboxes. The goal is to spark curiosity and keep language sharp while still letting kids use technology wisely.
