Donald Trump said he would not impose new tariffs on eight European countries, sparking a quick jump in European stocks. The FTSE 100, Germany’s DAX, France’s CAC, and the pan‑European Stoxx 600 all rose, and investors welcomed the relief. Analysts called it the return of the “Taco trade,” and US markets also bounced back after the tariff threat was withdrawn.
