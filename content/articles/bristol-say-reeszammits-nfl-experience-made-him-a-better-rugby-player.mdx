Bristol's rugby director Pat Lam says that the 24‑year‑old Louis Rees‑Zammit got stronger and more dangerous after trying American football with the Kansas City Chiefs and Jacksonville Jaguars. The player spent 18 months out of rugby but now has more power and better tackling. Lam sees him best at full‑back, where he can kick and finish fast runs. Bristol have won five straight games and will play Sale next.
