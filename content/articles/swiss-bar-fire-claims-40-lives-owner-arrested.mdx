On New Year's Eve, a fire broke out at the Le Constellation bar in Crans‑Montana, killing 40 people, mostly teenagers and young adults, and injuring 116. The blaze started when sparklers on champagne bottles hit ceiling foam. Swiss authorities arrested the bar’s owners, who bought it in 2015, for manslaughter by negligence. No safety inspections had taken place since 2019, and families are demanding a thorough investigation.
