On Thursday, Australians are invited to do kind deeds to remember the 15 people who died in last month’s Bondi terror attack. The Jewish tradition of a mitzvah, a good act, has been spread to everyone. People will help feed families, light candles, gather at the Sydney Opera House and other places for prayers and speeches from leaders. It’s a way to show solidarity and keep hope alive.
