More than 200 artists, writers, actors, musicians and other famous people have written a letter asking the world to free Palestinian leader Marwan Barghouti. He has been in prison for 23 years after a trial many say was unfair. The letter says his release could help bring peace and a new Palestinian state. It also warns Israel may push laws that could bring a death penalty to him. UN passed a resolution about Gaza.
