At the World Economic Forum in Davos, Nigel Farage said the world would be safer if the US took control of Greenland. He cited the Arctic’s changing climate, Russian and Chinese naval activity and his belief in strong nation‑states. Farage also defended his party’s rise in the UK as a reaction to Britain’s perceived moral and economic decline. He remains critical of Davos and globalist agendas.
