Joy Adeboye, 23, in Abuja gets abusive messages and feels unsafe. Without money for a therapist, she turns to a WhatsApp bot called Chat Kemi, which gives quick advice and helps her find help. In Nigeria, doctors and therapists are scarce and mental‑health care is too expensive for most. Many people now use AI chatbots to get instant support, but privacy and regulation worries remain.
