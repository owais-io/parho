Lucy Letby’s parents have slammed a new Netflix documentary, saying it shows private footage of their home where Letby was arrested. They say watching the film would be too painful and call it a “complete invasion of privacy.” The documentary, due Feb. 4, includes never‑before‑released video of Letby being taken from her bedroom. Letby is serving life terms for killing seven babies. Police and Cheshire constabulary have also weighed in.
