The writer recounts how his cricket article was copied without permission, then explains how the internet culture of screenshots, memes, and AI that scrape content has turned theft into a everyday habit. He argues that this new wave of digital stealing is just a modern extension of an age‑old human behavior fueled by power imbalances. The piece urges better protection for ideas and a rethink of inequality that lets theft thrive.
