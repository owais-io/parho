Illinois Lieutenant Governor Juliana Stratton, a progressive candidate, beat US Representative Raja Krishnamoorthi and former congressman Robin Kelly to win the Democratic primary for the open Senate seat. With nearly 90 % of votes counted, she led by more than six points. Stratton’s victory was helped by fresh money from Governor Pritzker and a strong focus on immigration, Medicare for all, and a $25‑per‑hour minimum wage. The win means she will almost certainly secure the seat in November.
