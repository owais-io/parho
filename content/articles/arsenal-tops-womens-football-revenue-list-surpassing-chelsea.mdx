Deloitte’s latest report shows that Arsenal’s women’s team earned the most money in Europe for the first time, pulling ahead of Chelsea by a slim margin. Arsenal made €25.6m, largely from match‑day sales at the Emirates Stadium, where they draw an average of 35,000 fans. Chelsea earned the most from sponsorships, but Arsenal’s overall revenue now leads the Women’s Super League.
