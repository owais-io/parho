England's World Cup‑winning winger Abby Dow has shocked the rugby world by announcing her retirement to pursue her engineering career. The 28‑year‑old, who scored 50 tries and earned 59 caps, left her club Trailfinders in June and hasn't signed with a new team. She thanked coaches, family and fans for their support, and England coach John Mitchell praised her talent and wished her success in her new field.
