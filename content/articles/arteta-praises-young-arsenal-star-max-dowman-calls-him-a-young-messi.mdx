Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta praised 15‑year‑old Max Dowman after he committed to a future contract, comparing him to a young Lionel Messi. Dowman became Arsenal’s youngest ever player in the Champions League and was seen as a top prospect. Arteta said Dowman’s calm, charismatic play and early achievements are unmatched. He also called for larger squad sizes to protect players’ mental health.
