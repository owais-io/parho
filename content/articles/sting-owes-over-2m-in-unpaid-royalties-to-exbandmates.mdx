Sting is facing a UK court case over unpaid streaming royalties owed to former Police members Stewart Copeland and Andy Summers. The dispute centers on whether the 15% arranger fee agreed in 1977 applies to money made from online streams. Both sides claim the fee is due, with the band’s biggest hit, “Every Breath You Take,” streamed billions of times. The case could reshape how royalties are split in the streaming era.
