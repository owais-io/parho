Aston Martin revealed that both drivers, Fernando Alonso and Lance Stroll, will likely have to stop early in the opening Australian Grand Prix because the car’s vibration is too high and could cause permanent nerve damage. Team boss Adrian Newey said the vibration comes from the Honda engine and is making parts fail, while drivers can only do about 25 and 15 laps safely. The team is still trying to fix the problem before the next race.
