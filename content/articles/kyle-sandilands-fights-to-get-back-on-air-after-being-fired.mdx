Radio host Kyle Sandilands has sued the station that fired him, saying the termination was illegal and that he deserves the money he would have earned until 2034. He wants a quick court decision so he can return to the Kyle and Jackie O Show and support his family. The station argues he was overpaid and that his return is unlikely, and the case will be heard in June.
