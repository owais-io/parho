Dean Brennan, the manager of Barnet, has received a nine‑match ban after the FA found he made sexist comments to referee Kirsty Dowle. He also faces a £2,000 fine and must attend a mandatory education programme. This is the first time a football manager has been punished for gender‑based abuse toward a match official. The ban follows an incident in September when Brennan was sent off for dissent.
