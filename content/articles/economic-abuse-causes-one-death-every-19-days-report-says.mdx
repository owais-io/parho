Survivors’ charity Sea studied 454 domestic homicide reviews and found that economic abuse—like cutting off money or forcing debt—caused one death by homicide or suicide every 19 days. More than half of the cases had financial control by an ex or current partner. The report says agencies often miss these signs, leaving victims trapped. It urges better training and guidance to spot and stop economic abuse.
