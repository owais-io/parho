During the holiday shopping rush, many U.S. consumers are avoiding big retailers that support the Trump administration’s policies. A coalition called “We Ain’t Buying It” urged people to skip Target, Home Depot, and Amazon for Black Friday. More than 40,000 people signed a pledge to buy responsibly. Small‑business sites got record traffic, and other boycotts target companies that back immigration raids or sell products tied to the administration.
