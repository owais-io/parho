Khaleed Oladipo, a 20‑year‑old De Montfort University cybersecurity student from London, was stabbed in Leicester’s city centre and died in hospital. Police say an 18‑year‑old has been arrested on suspicion of murder. A post‑mortem found a chest wound caused the death. Oladipo’s family and the university mourn the loss and are receiving support. The incident caused road closures after a reported street altercation.
