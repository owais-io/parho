Trump has threatened not to sign any other law until the Save America Act passes, a bill that would require voters to show proof of citizenship and strict photo ID at every step. It would also allow the government to scrub voter rolls and could cost tens of millions of people more trouble voting. Most experts say the bill can’t win Senate votes, so it’s likely to fail, but its ideas could still fuel Trump’s claims about fraud.
