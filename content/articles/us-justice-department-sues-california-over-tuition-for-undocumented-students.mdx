The U.S. Justice Department sued California, saying the state’s policy lets undocumented college students pay lower in‑state tuition and receive scholarships, which the DOJ claims is illegal and unfair to U.S. citizens. The lawsuit names the state, its top officials, and the University of California and California State University systems. Trump’s administration has taken similar action in other states, arguing that federal law bars such benefits for people without legal status.
