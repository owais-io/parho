Thunderstorms in south‑east Queensland hit hard with big hail, strong winds and heavy rain. The Bureau of Meteorology warned that hail up to 8 cm fell in places like Tamrookum and Mount Tamborine. As the storms moved north, more weather fronts were expected. Energex said over 138,000 customers lost power and about 300 power lines were down. Residents posted pictures of giant hail and some felt a heatwave on top of the storm.
