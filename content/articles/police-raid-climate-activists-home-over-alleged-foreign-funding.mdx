Police searched the house of Harjeet Singh, a climate activist, claiming he used foreign money to push for a fossil‑fuel treaty that could hurt India’s energy security. The Enforcement Directorate says he and his wife ran a front company to funnel money from international NGOs. They found excess whiskey and investigated his trips to Pakistan and Bangladesh. Singh was arrested but bailed, and he says the charges are false.
