Oklahoma Senator Markwayne Mullin was nominated to lead the Department of Homeland Security. During his confirmation hearing he apologized for a comment about a US citizen killed by immigration agents and pledged to avoid mistakes made by former head Kristi Noem. Mullin still supports Trump’s strict immigration policy and urged Democrats to lift the department’s funding freeze. Republicans back his appointment, while Democrats raise concerns over his past remarks and ICE practices.
