Andy Burnham, the Manchester mayor, says he was misled by Downing Street about whether he could stand in a Gorton and Denton byelection. He claims sources told him the NEC would refuse his application, but he was not told that. Labour’s NEC blocked his bid, sparking a civil war inside the party. The fight pits Burnham against Reform UK’s candidate, with Labour leaders calling for unity.
