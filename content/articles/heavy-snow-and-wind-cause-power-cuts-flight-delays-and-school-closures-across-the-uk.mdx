Storm Goretti hit the UK with almost 100‑mph winds, sending thousands of homes into darkness, especially in the south‑west. Snow fell as deep as 27 cm in Scotland and 15 cm in Wales, and a yellow warning covered most of the country for ice and snow. Many flights and trains were cancelled, and over 250 schools shut. As the snow melts, the risk of flooding will rise in some areas.
