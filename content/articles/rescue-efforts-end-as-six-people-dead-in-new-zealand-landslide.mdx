After a mudslide hit a holiday park in Mount Maunganui, New Zealand, police stopped searching for survivors. They found the remains of six people, including a 15‑year‑old, buried under a mountain of dirt. The search could take days because the ground is unstable. Families and the public are grieving, and the police are now moving to a recovery operation, while the weather may slow work.
