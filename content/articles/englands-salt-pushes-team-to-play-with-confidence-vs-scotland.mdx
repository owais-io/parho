England’s captain Phil Salt wants his side to bring full confidence to their T20 World Cup game against Scotland. He says England’s recent losses should motivate them, not scare them, and that the team needs to play “with personality” and stay true to themselves. Salt also shares his love of the Indian ground where he’s had big scores. Scotland’s coach Brad Wheal says the team will play bravely and give it their all.
