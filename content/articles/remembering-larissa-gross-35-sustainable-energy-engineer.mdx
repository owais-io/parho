Larissa Gross, a 35‑year‑old engineer who worked on sustainable energy, died from breast cancer. Born in Canada, she moved to the UK, where she led projects at the climate think‑tank E3G and helped shape energy transition plans. Outside work, she was known for her dry humour, loyalty, and a surprising talent for building campfires. Friends celebrated her life with a “Chemo Club” and a birthday party, and she was survived by her husband, parents and siblings.
