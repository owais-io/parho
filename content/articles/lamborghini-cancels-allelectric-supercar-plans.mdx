Lamborghini decided to stop making all‑electric cars. The company will focus on plug‑in hybrids instead because its wealthy customers aren’t interested in fully electric models. The CEO said EVs would be costly and would lose the engine’s “fun” sound. The brand still sells hybrids like the Revuelto and the Urus SUV. Lamborghini wants hybrids to be the only option by 2030.
