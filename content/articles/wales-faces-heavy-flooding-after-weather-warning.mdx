Met Office warned that heavy rain and strong winds could cause big floods in south and mid Wales. Forecasts call for 20‑40 mm of rain across the region, with some hills getting up to 100 mm, and a risk of landslides. Eleven flood warnings and 51 alerts have been issued, and people are urged to stay away from swollen rivers and not drive through floodwater. The wet autumn has raised the flooding risk, even as some areas still face drought.
