Verstappen took first place at the Las Vegas Grand Prix, giving him another win and keeping his title hopes alive. Lando Norris finished second, with teammate Oscar Piastri fourth, both earning points but also being investigated for possible skid‑block wear violations that could lead to disqualification. The race was a clear win for Red Bull, but Verstappen still trails Norris by 42 points, so he will need a strong finish in Qatar to stay in the championship hunt.
