A big snowstorm has put a blizzard warning on NYC, New Jersey, and parts of the east coast. Weather officials expect 13 to 20 inches of snow and strong winds that could make travel dangerous. City officials are asking residents, especially the homeless, to stay indoors and off the roads. The storm could also cause flooding and power outages.
