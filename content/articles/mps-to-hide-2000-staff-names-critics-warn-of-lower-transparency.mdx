Parliament wants to remove the names of about 2,000 MPs’ staff from a public register, replacing them with job titles. The move, driven by safety worries raised in private talks with staff unions, would make it harder for voters to see who works for each MP and spot potential conflicts of interest. Critics say the change erodes transparency and could create new loopholes for lobbying and foreign influence.
