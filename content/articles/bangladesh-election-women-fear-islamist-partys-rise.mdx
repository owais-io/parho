Bangladesh’s big election after the former leader was overthrown has drawn worries from women. A powerful Islamist group, Jamaat e‑Islam, is gaining support and wants to bring sharia law. The party has no female candidates and its leaders say women can’t run. Women protest, fear a rollback of rights, and worry the election will hurt their freedoms and safety for their future.
