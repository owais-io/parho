The Guardian piece says that England fans won’t want to spend the huge amount needed for 2026 World Cup tickets – up to £5,000 for a final seat. FIFA says fans need to pay the full price in advance, and if a team loses they get a refund after 60 days. The writer has visited 14 tournaments, but finds the price too high and calls for the FA to push FIFA to lower costs.
