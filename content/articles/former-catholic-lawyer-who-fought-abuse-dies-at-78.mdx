Ray Mouton, a Louisiana attorney, first defended a priest accused of child rape. When he discovered the diocese was covering up other abusive priests, he wrote a warning to bishops and became a key source for journalists. His work helped spark investigations and huge settlements. He spent his later years in France, wrote a novel, and kept following the case until his death.
