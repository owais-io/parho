Iranians in the UK are saying they feel unsafe because of growing tensions linked to the Iran conflict. Video clips show people acting aggressively and pressuring shop owners to display Iran’s old flag, causing fear in London’s Little Tehran area. The Metropolitan Police and local councils have met with community leaders and increased patrols. The story highlights how international events spill over into local safety concerns.
