The Kennedy Center has lost its arts reputation after Trump made himself chair. He hired Richard Grenell, who has no arts experience. Artists and shows, like the opera and Hamilton, left. Ticket sales fell. The Washington National Opera is quitting, and the centre’s future looks bleak with political fights over its name. The National Symphony Orchestra is also feeling the pressure, and Washington’s cultural scene is in crisis.
