Donald Trump has warned the U.S. will take strong action if Iran begins executing protestors, as the death toll tops 2,500. He cited the case of 26‑year‑old Erfan Soltani, who may be hanged. Iran has arrested over 18,000 people, cut internet and raided homes for satellite dishes. The U.S. has urged citizens to leave. Iran’s TV confirmed many martyrs, and its UN office said U.S. tactics will fail. Critics say the regime uses swift trials and confessions to silence dissent.
