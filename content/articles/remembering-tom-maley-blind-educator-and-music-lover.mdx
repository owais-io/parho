Tom Maley, who died at 84, was a blind teacher, computer programmer and passionate pianist. He lost his sight as a child but never let it stop him. He taught history in Scotland, worked on braille standards, and later became a piano teacher, giving concerts with local orchestras. His humor, stories and love of music made everyone around him happy. He is survived by his wife, son and granddaughters.
