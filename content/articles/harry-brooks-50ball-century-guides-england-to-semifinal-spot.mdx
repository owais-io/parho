England won a T20 World Cup match against Pakistan and booked a spot in the semi‑finals, thanks to Harry Brook’s explosive 50‑ball century. Brook was promoted to bat at number three and scored 107 runs in just 50 balls, turning a shaky start into a confident chase of 165. The bowlers also performed well, keeping Pakistan from building a big lead. The win means England will face the highest‑ranked teams next, while Pakistan misses the chance to advance.
