After US‑Israeli airstrikes on Iran, oil prices jumped to a year‑high, with Brent crude at $82 a barrel. Fears that the Strait of Hormuz could close spooked markets, sending European indices down 2‑3% and hurting airlines. Defence shares rose, and gold climbed as investors sought safety, while OPEC+ planned a modest output boost amid shipping delays in the Middle East.
