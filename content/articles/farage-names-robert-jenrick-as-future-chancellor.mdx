Nigel Farage announced that former housing secretary Robert Jenrick will be his next chancellor in Reform UK. The move surprises many, as many expected former party leader Richard Tice to get the job. Tice will now handle business, energy and industry. Former chair Zia Yusuf will take on home affairs, including immigration. The reshuffle shows Farage trying to spread power and prove the party is not a one‑man band.
