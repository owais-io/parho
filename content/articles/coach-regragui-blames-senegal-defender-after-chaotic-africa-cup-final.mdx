The Morocco coach, Walid Regragui, blasted Senegal defender Pape Thiaw after a chaotic Africa Cup final. After a disputed goal, Senegal walked off the pitch, then returned when a penalty was awarded. Senegal won in extra‑time, but Morocco lost. Regragui called the walk‑off and Thiaw’s behaviour “shameful” and said it hurt African football. The post‑match press conference turned tense, with journalists walking out.
