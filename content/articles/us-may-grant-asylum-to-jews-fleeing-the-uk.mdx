Trump’s lawyer, Robert Garson, says he’s spoken to the US State Department about offering British Jews who fear antisemitism asylum in the United States. He cites a Manchester synagogue attack, backlash after Hamas’s 2023 Gaza assault, and a 2025 survey that found 35 % of UK Jews feel unsafe. Garson blames the UK government and says the US could help, even as a Biden‑era plan limits refugees to 7,500 next year.
