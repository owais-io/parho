Steve Lamb, a founding member of Warwick’s German studies department and a beloved teacher, died at 79. He studied German in Birmingham, joined Warwick in 1973, and worked on the critical edition of playwright Ernst Toller's works. From 2008 to 2015 he was senior tutor, improving student support after the 2005 student survey. A passionate cricketer, he bowled over 5,900 overs. He is survived by his sons and brother.
