England were beaten by West Indies in the T20 World Cup, falling short of the target of 197. The team lost 166, with seven balls left. Their captain said they were too cautious and missed chances. West Indies built a strong score with key runs from Sherfane Rutherford and Jason Holder. England must win their next games against Scotland and Italy to reach the Super Eights.
