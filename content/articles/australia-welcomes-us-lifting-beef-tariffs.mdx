U.S. President Donald Trump has removed tariffs on beef and other food items that Australia exports to the U.S., a move the Australian government welcomed. Trump said U.S. demand and supply influenced the decision. Australian beef exports hit about $4 billion a year, and the removal may not change demand much right away, but it improves trade relations and reduces costs for U.S. consumers.
