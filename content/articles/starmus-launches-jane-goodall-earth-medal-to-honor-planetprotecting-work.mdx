Sir Brian May, the Queen guitarist, is launching the Starmus Jane Goodall Earth medal. The award will honour people who work to protect animals, people and the planet. It’s named after primatologist Jane Goodall, who died this year. The first winners will be announced at the Starmus festival in October. May said the medal will encourage more people to act against climate change and misinformation. By giving a public prize, the festival hopes to inspire action and show that small everyday choices can make a difference. The medal will have several categories and aims to highlight sustainable projects.
