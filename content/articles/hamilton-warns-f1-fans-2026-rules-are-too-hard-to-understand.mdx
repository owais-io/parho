Lewis Hamilton has told F1 fans that the new 2026 power‑unit rules will be hard to follow, after spinning his Ferrari during Bahrain’s second pre‑season test. He said the mix of combustion and electric power, plus energy‑recovery tricks like “lift and coast,” makes the sport “ridiculously complex.” Other drivers, like Lando Norris, share his worries, and the test saw Max Verstappen fastest, and the new rules will change how drivers race.
