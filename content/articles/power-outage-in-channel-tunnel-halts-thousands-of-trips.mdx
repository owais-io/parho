On Christmas Eve a power failure in the Channel Tunnel left thousands of passengers and vehicle drivers stranded. Eurostar and shuttle trains were halted for hours, and engineers had to fix the fault, leaving only one track in use. Many journeys were canceled, and travellers were told to rebook or wait. Passengers could get refunds, vouchers or hotel compensation. The outage caused long queues and frustration at both ends of the tunnel.
