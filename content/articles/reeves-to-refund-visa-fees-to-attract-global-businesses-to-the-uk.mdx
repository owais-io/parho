UK Chancellor Rachel Reeves will announce at Davos that the government will refund part of the visa fees for certain foreign companies. The move is meant to pull “trailblazer” firms to the UK by making it cheaper to bring skilled staff and speeding up sponsorship approvals. It comes as the UK fights new US tariff threats and seeks to keep investment flowing, while also facing criticism over rising business rates that could hurt hotels and other firms.
