A federal judge has stopped the Trump administration from deporting Mohsen Mahdawi, a 34‑year‑old Columbia student who speaks out about Palestinian rights. Mahdawi was held by ICE after a citizenship interview, but a judge said the government didn’t prove he was a threat. He’s a green‑card holder, never charged, and was released after a court case. The ruling underscores court oversight of immigration detentions.
