Kent county council has been slammed for hiring political assistants that cost taxpayers thousands of pounds. The new assistant, Michael Hadwen, has controversial views on immigration and Russia that sparked outcry. The move was approved by a 45‑to‑26 vote, despite opposition from the Liberal Democrats who say the council is already overspending. Critics say the council is spending public money on politics rather than cutting waste.
