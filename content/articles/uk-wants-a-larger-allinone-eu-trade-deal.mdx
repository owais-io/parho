UK trade minister Chris Bryant said the UK needs a bigger, all‑in‑one deal with the EU, not a series of small agreements. He called for a Swiss‑style framework that would align rules for things like medical devices and chemicals, and push through issues such as youth mobility, safety standards, and student tuition fees. The talks are stalled on a few points, but both sides want a faster, clearer partnership.
