British holidaymakers were terrified on the first flights out of Abu Dhabi after fighting broke out between Iran and Gulf neighbours. Passengers saw missiles and heard explosions, while the UK began evacuation plans for about 300,000 Britons in the region. A few flights managed to leave, but many others were grounded, leaving thousands stranded. UAE authorities warned people to stay away from windows and the airline helped evacuate those who could.
