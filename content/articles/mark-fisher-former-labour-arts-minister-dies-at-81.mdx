Mark Fisher, a lifelong Labour MP, died at 81. He served as arts minister for just over a year before being dropped, but spent 27 years representing Stoke‑on‑Trent Central. A passionate advocate for the arts, he helped shape national cultural policy and pushed early freedom‑of‑information bills. He was also a poet and writer. Fisher came from a politically mixed family and was known for his upbeat, service‑oriented style in politics.
