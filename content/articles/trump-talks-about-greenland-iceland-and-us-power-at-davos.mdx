During his talk at the World Economic Forum in Davos, former President Donald Trump said he wants Greenland and mentioned Iceland by mistake. He claimed he would not use force, but talked about America’s power and that other countries depend on the U.S. He criticized Canada and used a slur about Somali Americans. The speech mixed his usual gaffes with comments about military strength and the U.S.’s global role.
