A 51‑year‑old man charged with a 2003 rape in Manchester says he doesn’t know how his DNA ended up on the victim’s vest and bra. Police linked his DNA after fresh tests. He also admitted browsing the internet for information on wrongful‑conviction cases, including Andrew Malkinson, and said he was very promiscuous at the time of the attack. The jury heard he denies being the attacker and the judge clarified the legal definition of rape. The trial is still underway.
