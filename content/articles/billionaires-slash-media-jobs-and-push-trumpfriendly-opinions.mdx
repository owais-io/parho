Jeff Bezos spent $75 m on a Melania Trump film, while the Washington Post plans to cut up to 200 staff, mostly foreign reporters, and lean into opinion pieces that support Trump‑friendly ideas. CBS News, owned by Larry Ellison, hired Bari Weiss, who has added more opinion writers and will cut newsroom jobs. The moves show tech owners turning media into propaganda that favors right‑wing views and cuts unbiased reporting.
