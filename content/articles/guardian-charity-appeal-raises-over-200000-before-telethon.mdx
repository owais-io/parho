The Guardian’s Hope charity appeal has pulled in more than £200,000 before its big fundraising telethon. It supports five local charities that work to bring people together, fight racism, and build community pride. Readers can call journalists on the telethon to donate. The money will fund projects like youth clubs, community centres, and refugee welcome schemes. The Guardian thanks supporters for helping keep hope alive.
