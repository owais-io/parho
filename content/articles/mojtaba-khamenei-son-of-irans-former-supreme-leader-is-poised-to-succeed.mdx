Iran is looking at Mojtaba Khamenei, the 54‑year‑old son of the late Supreme Leader, to become the next head of state. The IRGC and many hard‑liners think he is the best fit, and the Assembly of Experts—who pick the leader—are close to deciding. No official announcement yet, but Israel has warned he could be targeted. Reformers fear the move will keep Iran’s tough stance against the West.
