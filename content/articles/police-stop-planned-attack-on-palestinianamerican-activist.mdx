The FBI and NYPD uncovered and stopped a plan to kill Nerdeen Kiswani, a New York‑based Palestinian‑American activist. A 26‑year‑old man, Alexander Heifler, was arrested for making Molotov cocktails and trying to threaten her. Police used an undercover officer to track the threat online and in person. The plot was linked to far‑right pro‑Israel groups. Kiswani thanked supporters and said she will keep speaking out for her safety.
