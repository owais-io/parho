Bari Weiss, CBS’s new editor‑in‑chief, announced 19 paid contributors to revamp the network. The lineup blends wellness experts, longevity bloggers, a cosmologist, a former Marine‑turned‑writer, a chef, a conservative historian, a neuroscientist YouTuber, a Black conservative commentator, a wellness entrepreneur, and a climate‑skeptic. Some names stir controversy, raising questions about the network’s future direction and political balance.
