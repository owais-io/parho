During a chaotic match at Stamford Bridge, Chelsea lost their composure and drew 2-2 with Bournemouth. Long throw‑ins from Bournemouth winger Antoine Semenyo gave David Brooks and Justin Kluivert quick goals, while Chelsea’s defense was wild. Fans shouted at Chelsea’s manager Enzo Maresca after he removed injured Cole Palmer in the second half. The result leaves Chelsea looking bad, dropping points from winning positions, and worried about an upcoming game against Manchester City without midfielder Moisés Caicedo.
