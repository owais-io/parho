Hollie Davidson will become the first woman to referee a men’s Six Nations rugby game, breaking a long‑standing gender barrier. She’s faced sexist insults, abuse and doubt since 2015, but gained support from teammates, a coach and a sports psychologist. After leaving a banking job for full‑time refereeing, she has handled high‑profile matches and learned to stand firm against criticism. Her milestone shows progress for women in rugby.
