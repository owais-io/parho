US CIA director John Ratcliffe flew to Caracas to meet Delcy Rodríguez, Maduro’s acting president, as María Corina Machado, a top opposition figure, announced she will run for and win the presidency. Machado gave her Nobel Peace Prize medal to Donald Trump, hoping to win his support. Trump and US officials have backed Rodríguez instead, seeing Machado as too weak to control the military. The visit shows the US is still trying to shape Venezuela’s political future.
