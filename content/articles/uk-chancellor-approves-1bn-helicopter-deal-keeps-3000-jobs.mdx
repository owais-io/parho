UK Chancellor Rachel Reeves will approve a £1 bn contract with Leonardo to build new RAF helicopters in Yeovil, keeping 3,000 jobs safe. Leonardo was the only bidder after Lockheed Martin and Airbus dropped out, and the deal replaces the old Puma fleet. The decision came after a last‑minute Treasury reversal, so the MoD could move forward. The contract is a win for UK defence, jobs, and local industry.
