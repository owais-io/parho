A 65‑year‑old Jordanian named Mohammad Odeh Saleh was arrested in the Philippines for overstaying his visa. Saleh owns a coffee shop that the men who carried out Australia’s deadliest Bondi beach attack stayed at for a month while they were in Mindanao. Police say he has no direct link to the killings, but they are keeping him under watch. His visa expired in March 2025, and he was found in Pagadian City.
