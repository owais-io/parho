In the final Ashes Test in Sydney, rain cut the game in half, stopping play at 211 for three. Joe Root and Harry Brook made the headlines, scoring an unbeaten 154 together – Root 72 and Brook 78 – giving England a solid lead. Australia had no spinner in their attack, which made it harder for the English bowlers. The match was abandoned after 45 overs, leaving fans disappointed but pleased with the English batting performance.
