The UK, together with Sweden, France, Germany and the Netherlands, says the death of Russian opposition leader Alexei Navalny was caused by a frog toxin, pointing to Moscow. They are considering new sanctions and have reported the case to the chemical‑weapon body. Russia denies the claim, calling it nonsense, while the US backs the findings but did not join the statement. The UK says it will keep pressuring Russia and support Ukraine.
