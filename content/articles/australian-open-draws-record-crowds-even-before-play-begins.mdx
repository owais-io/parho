The Australian Open opened to a record 29,000 fans, almost four times last year, thanks to free kids tickets and new events. Melbourne Park added a 15‑day opening week with charity matches, music shows and the One Point Slam where pros play celebrities. Ticket prices rise to A$20 for ground passes, A$65 for the main draw. Tennis Australia says the extra activities will help grow the event and bring more people to the courts.
