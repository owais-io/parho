Storms brought heavy rain and flooding to Washington and Oregon, causing rivers to overflow and roads to close. Fire crews rescued people from cars and flooded woods using kayaks. Meteorologists warned that rivers could rise even more later this week. An arctic blast left parts of Alaska with very cold winds, while another storm sent snow, freezing rain and high winds to the Midwest and the Dakotas. Many schools shut or switched to online classes.
