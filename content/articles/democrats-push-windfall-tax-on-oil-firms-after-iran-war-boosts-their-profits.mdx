Democrats and environmental groups want a windfall tax on oil companies after the Iran war sent fuel prices over $100 a barrel. Big firms like ExxonMobil and Chevron are making billions as gas costs rise to $3.70 a gallon. The tax would use extra profits to help ordinary Americans, with support from groups like the Sierra Club. Oil lobby and some politicians oppose it, but the idea has popularity.
