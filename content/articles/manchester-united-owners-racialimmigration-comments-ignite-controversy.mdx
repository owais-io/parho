Sir Jim Ratcliffe, who owns Manchester United, said Britain’s population has risen by 12 million since 2020 and used the word “colonised” to describe migrants, sparking anger. He is accused of spreading false numbers and hateful language. The club and Manchester city officials have distanced themselves from his comments, warning that football should not be used to push political agendas.
