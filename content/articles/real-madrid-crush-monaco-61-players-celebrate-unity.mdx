Real Madrid won 6‑1 against Monaco in the Champions League, with Kylian Mbappé scoring twice and Vinícius Jr. and Jude Bellingham adding goals. An own goal from Monaco’s Thilo Kehrer helped the win. After a recent coaching change and fan unrest, the players celebrated together, showing unity and a fresh start for the club. The result marks a hopeful turnaround for Real Madrid in the competition.
