German Chancellor Friedrich Merz met President Trump in Washington and warned that Ukraine should not have to give up more territory to Russia. He said the US must keep supporting Ukraine, push hard on Moscow, and help secure a lasting peace deal that includes Europe. Trump said ending the war remains a top priority. The meeting highlighted the need for strong U.S. support and pressure on Russia to protect Ukraine’s borders.
