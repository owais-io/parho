Donald Trump took to Truth Social to attack a New York Times journalist, saying she is “ugly inside and out.” The tweet came after the paper reported on his age and energy levels. Trump has a record of hurling insults at female reporters, calling one a “piggy” on Air Force One and calling another “terrible.” The Times defended its reporters and said the comments don’t affect their work.
