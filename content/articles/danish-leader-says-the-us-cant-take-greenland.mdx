Denmark’s prime minister, Mette Frederiksen, told President Trump to stop saying the U.S. needs Greenland. Trump has repeatedly claimed the territory is essential for national security and even posted a map of Greenland in the U.S. flag. Denmark rejects the idea, saying Greenland is part of the Danish kingdom and not for sale. The debate comes amid growing tensions over Arctic strategy and U.S. military interest.
