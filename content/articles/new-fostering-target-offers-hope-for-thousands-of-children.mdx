Author shares two decades of fostering, describing how children leave traumatic homes and find comfort in a new family. They talk about the joy of children returning home or being adopted, but also the pain when kids are moved between counties or put in residential care. The government wants to add 10,000 new foster placements, inspired by a Ukraine scheme, and the writer hopes strong support will make it happen.
