Thirty‑four people who went to school with Nigel Farage say he used racist and antisemitic language on them when they were teens. They claim he called them “paki,” told them to “go back home,” and made gas‑hissing jokes about Jews. Farage has denied the claims, saying he was just “bantering,” and has not apologized. The accusations are still stirring debate among politicians and the public.
