With the January transfer window almost over, Tottenham is chasing free‑agent Raheem Sterling, who hasn't played competitively for eight months. Seven top clubs – including Tottenham, Napoli, Juventus, Bayern Munich and others – want him. Tottenham boss Thomas Frank sees him as the missing piece to finish the big‑six lineup. The buzz also covers other players, but Sterling’s move would be the headline of this month’s football gossip.
