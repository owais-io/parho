Thirty monks from around the world walked more than 2,300 miles from Texas to Washington, D.C., stopping at the Lincoln Memorial to spread a message of peace, love and compassion. The walk drew crowds, praise from leaders and a few injuries when a truck hit a convoy. Politicians and civilians alike cheered, holding signs of hope and unity as the monks ended their journey near Annapolis.
