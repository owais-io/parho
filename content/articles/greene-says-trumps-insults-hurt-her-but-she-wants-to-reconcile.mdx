Georgia congresswoman Marjorie Taylor Greene said on CNN that Trump’s recent insults calling her a traitor were hurtful, but she hopes they can move past it. She warned that threats against her have risen after Trump pulled his support. Greene also wants all documents about Jeffrey Epstein released, saying the public deserves transparency. She called for less political fighting and said forgiveness is part of her faith.
