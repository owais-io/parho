The Home Office reported only 255 terrorism-related arrests in 2025, a 2% rise from last year. A closer look shows the real figure is 3,034, up 1,114% from 2024. Most of the new arrests were for protests linked to Palestine Action, which the police have excluded because of ongoing court cases. The data is misleading, wrongly labeling many people, including older adults, as terrorists.
