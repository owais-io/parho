Constitution Hill, a 9‑year‑old gelding who won the Champion Hurdle in 2023, will miss next year’s race. Trainer Nicky Henderson said that after a schooling session, the horse’s recent falls and safety concerns mean it’s best to skip the jump event. Instead, he’ll focus on flat racing, following a strong debut at Southwell, with future goals like the Melbourne Cup, Ebor Handicap and Irish St Leger.
