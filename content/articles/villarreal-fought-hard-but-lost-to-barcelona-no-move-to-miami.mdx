Villarreal played at home against Barcelona and lost 2-0. The match was full of chances for Villarreal but they couldn't finish them. Manager Marcelino was disappointed after a player was sent off and after a goal from Lamine Yamal. Barcelona kept control and won their seventh straight game, staying close to the top of La Liga. The story also joked that the match would be better if it were moved to Miami.
