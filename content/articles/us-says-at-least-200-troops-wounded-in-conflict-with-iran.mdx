The U.S. military says about 200 soldiers have been hurt fighting Iran, most injuries are minor and 180 have gone back to work. Ten are seriously wounded. In total 13 U.S. troops have died, including a plane crash in Iraq, a drone strike in Kuwait, and an attack on a Saudi airbase. The war also claims over 1,300 Iranian and many civilians' lives.
