Two prisoners in a hunger strike were taken to hospital after 50 and 42 days of refusing food. The men, linked to Palestine Action, are in Surrey and London prisons. Families and MPs say the prison has delayed medical help and poor communication. Jeremy Corbyn and other MPs have written letters asking the government to intervene and meet the prisoners' lawyers. The government says prisoners have full medical access but critics say it’s not enough.
