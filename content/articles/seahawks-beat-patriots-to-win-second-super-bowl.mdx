The Seattle Seahawks defeated the New England Patriots 29‑13 to claim their second Super Bowl. Kenneth Walker III rushed for 135 yards and was named MVP. Seattle’s defense held the Patriots to just 13 points, while Sam Darnold guided the offense with a 202‑yard passing game and a touchdown. The game was a defensive battle, with Seattle’s field‑goal kicks and a late touchdown sealing the win.
