Washington Governor Bob Ferguson declared a state emergency after a powerful rainstorm turned rivers into flooding, causing mudslides and road damage. He called for federal aid and has the National Guard ready to help. Rivers like the Snohomish are rising close to record levels, threatening homes and farms. Residents are told to stay off flooded roads and stay alert for further weather updates.
