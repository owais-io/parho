Emily Gregory, a Democrat, beat Republican Jon Maples in Florida’s 87th state house district, a seat that includes Donald Trump’s Mar‑a‑Lago. The Associated Press called the race after Gregory led by over two points. Trump had endorsed Maples but voted by mail himself. The result shows voters moving away from Trump‑aligned Republicans, and Democrats say it signals vulnerability even in Trump‑strong areas.
