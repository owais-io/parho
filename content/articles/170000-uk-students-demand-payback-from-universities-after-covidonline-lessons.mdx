More than 170,000 students from 36 UK universities are sending letters to start legal claims. They say they paid for in‑person teaching, but during COVID campuses closed and courses went online. Students want money back – lawyers say each could claim about £5,000. The case follows a recent settlement at UCL and could cost universities millions if it spreads and affect many more.
