Pentagon chief Pete Hegseth told reporters that Tuesday would be the most intense day of U.S. attacks on Iran yet. He accused Iran of firing missiles from schools and hospitals, blaming the country for civilian casualties, while claiming U.S. forces are avoiding such deaths. Hegseth highlighted that more than 5,000 Iranian targets have been hit and that U.S. drones and fighters are intercepting Iranian attack drones. Trump also weighed in, saying negotiations with Iran would only happen under U.S. terms.
