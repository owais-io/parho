Indigenous protestors blocked the door of the Cop30 climate conference to demand a meeting with Brazil’s president, but were instead heard by the conference president. About 50 Munduruku tribe members, joined by green activists, faced police and soldiers before moving to a side building for talks. The Belém summit saw many protests and rallies, all pushing for an action plan called the Belém Action Mechanism, while the conference extended talks to work on finance, trade, emissions and transparency.
