Britain was slow to move its forces after the U.S. ramped up plans to hit Iran. While the US sent carriers and asked to use British bases, the UK kept deployments low and did not want to be part of a first strike. The UK allowed the U.S. to use a base. It had to evacuate staff and send a navy ship to Cyprus. Its navy has no ships in the Middle East, worrying allies and 300,000 Britons living there.
