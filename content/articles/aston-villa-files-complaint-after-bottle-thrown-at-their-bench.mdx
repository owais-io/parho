Aston Villa filed a complaint after a water bottle was thrown at their bench when the game ended. The bottle, which splashed the away dugout, may have come from Chelsea players or the crowd, and Chelsea said it will help investigate. The incident didn’t spoil Villa’s joy after a 2-1 comeback win at Stamford Bridge, their 11th straight victory. Manager Unai Emery praised his team’s comeback but stayed quiet about the title race.
