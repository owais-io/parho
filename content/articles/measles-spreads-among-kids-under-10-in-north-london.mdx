A measles outbreak is now affecting children under 10 in north‑east London, mostly those who haven’t had the MMRV vaccine. About 34 confirmed cases were reported between Jan 1 and Feb 9, and some kids have been hospitalised. The outbreak shows how badly low vaccination rates are spreading the disease. Local health officials have set up temporary vaccine centres in schools and are urging parents to get their children fully vaccinated before the Easter holidays.
