Leeds used a new lineup, but three goals from fringe players Wilfried Gnonto, Ao Tanaka and James Justin turned the game around. After Derby took a 1‑0 lead, the hosts scored in the 40th minute to level and then went ahead, sealing a 3‑1 win. The result puts Leeds 8 points above the drop zone and keeps an FA Cup dream alive, despite being seen as outsiders.
