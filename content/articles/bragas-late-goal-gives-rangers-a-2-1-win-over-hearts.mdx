Rangers beat Hearts 2-1 at Tynecastle, thanks to a late strike by Cláudio Braga. The win cuts Rangers’ points gap to Celtic to just four, while Hearts stay off the top spot after a tough game that saw a red card for Hearts’ defender Auston Trusty. Celtic missed chances to widen their lead, and the match ended with Rangers tightening their chase of the league title.
