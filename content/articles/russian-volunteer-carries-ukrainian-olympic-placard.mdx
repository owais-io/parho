A Russian architect living in Milan, Anastasia Kucherova, volunteered to carry Ukraine’s placard at the 2026 Winter Olympics opening ceremony. She did so in a hooded coat and sunglasses, choosing the Ukrainian team in a moment of resistance. She says the gesture reminds the world that the war continues and shows that not all Russians support it. Her act also highlighted the shared connection between Russians and Ukrainians.
