A 52‑year‑old Costa Rican man, Randall Gamboa Esquivel, was sent back to his home country after spending nearly a year in U.S. ICE detention. He arrived in a vegetative state and died 25 days later. His family says he was healthy when he left, but inside detention he developed severe health problems. They want to know why he got sick, why he was kept quiet, and why the U.S. didn’t notify them.
