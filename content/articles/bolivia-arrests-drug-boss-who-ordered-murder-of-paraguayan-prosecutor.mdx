Bolivia arrests 34‑year‑old drug trafficker Sebastián Marset, who is accused of moving tonnes of cocaine from South America to Europe and ordering the murder of a Paraguayan prosecutor on a Colombian beach. The man, wanted by the US for money‑laundering, is now being extradited to Washington. His arrest signals renewed cooperation between Bolivia and the United States after nearly two decades of strained ties.
