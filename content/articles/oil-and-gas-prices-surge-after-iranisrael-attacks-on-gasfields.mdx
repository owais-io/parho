Oil and gas prices jumped sharply after a new wave of attacks by Israel on Iran’s gas fields and Iran’s retaliation against gas facilities in Qatar. Brent crude rose about 8% to $116 a barrel, while European gas prices climbed as high as €68 per megawatt hour. The price surge rattled stock markets worldwide, and household energy bills in the UK could rise sharply. Experts warn that a prolonged Middle East conflict could push oil to $150 a barrel.
