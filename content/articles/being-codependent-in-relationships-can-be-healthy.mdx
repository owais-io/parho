Many people think codependency is always bad, but therapist Nedra Glover Tawwab says it can be healthy. In her new book, she explains that we learn a lot about ourselves and others through relationships, not by staying alone. The key is balanced support and clear boundaries. If you feel safe, respected and can grow, the relationship is likely healthy. It shows you can be both independent and connected.
