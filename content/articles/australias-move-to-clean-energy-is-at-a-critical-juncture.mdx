The Australian government is at a turning point. A secret climate‑security report shows how oil wars can hurt the nation. Prices and supply worries have pushed people to stock up on fuel, but the real solution is clean power. The government has launched huge support for solar, wind, batteries and electric cars, but some subsidies may be cut. The future depends on faster adoption of renewables.
