Britain’s baby numbers are falling and the country is getting older. Politicians are worried about the loss of new life, but their ideas to make families bigger could hurt women. Reform UK wants taxes to push people to have children and remove support for those who don’t. The article points out the real obstacles—lack of jobs, childcare, homes—and says real help would come from better maternity services and less pressure on mothers.
