The piece says the Trump team treats the conflict with Iran like a video game, posting memes and clips of Hollywood heroes to make it feel like entertainment. Real war uses AI drones, no boots on the ground, and little cost for the U.S. The author argues this detached view lets politicians ignore the suffering of millions and lets social‑media platforms profit from fake or exaggerated content. It calls for remembering the real human cost.
