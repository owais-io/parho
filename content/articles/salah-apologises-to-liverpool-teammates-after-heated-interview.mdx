Liverpool striker Mohamed Salah said sorry to his teammates after a heated interview that criticised the club and manager Arne Slot. Curtis Jones told Sky Sports that Salah told the squad he was sorry if his comments hurt anyone and that he still feels like the same player. Slot has kept quiet to avoid distractions, but Liverpool have won five games since dropping Salah to the bench.
