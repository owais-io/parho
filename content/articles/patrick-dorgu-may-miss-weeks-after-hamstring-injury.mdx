Patrick Dorgu has suffered a hamstring injury during United’s 3‑2 win over Arsenal and may be out for many weeks or even months. He’s been a key scorer for the club, netting three goals in his last seven games. The injury comes as United prepare to face Fulham, while fan group 1958 plans a protest against the club’s owners, the Glazers and chief executive Jim Ratcliffe. The boycott highlights growing fan frustration with the club’s management.
