European leaders gathered at Downing Street to support Ukraine and discuss how to use frozen Russian assets for a loan to Kyiv. They said they made “positive progress” but no final deal yet, and some countries, like Belgium, still oppose the plan. The talks also touched on a U.S. peace proposal that many see as favoring Russia. Ukraine’s president said more support is needed and that he won’t give up land for peace.
