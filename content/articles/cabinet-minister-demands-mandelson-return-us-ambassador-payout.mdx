Pat McFadden, the UK welfare secretary, says former ambassador Peter Mandelson should hand back about £55,000 he received after quitting his US post. The money was paid when he left in September, amid growing scrutiny of his ties to convicted sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein. The Foreign Office is reviewing the payment. Meanwhile, pressure mounts on Prime Minister Keir Starmer to step down or fire staff.
