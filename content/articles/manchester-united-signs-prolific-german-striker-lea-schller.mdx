Manchester United has signed German forward Lea Schüller, who has scored more than ten league goals every season in Germany’s top women's league. The 28‑year‑old netted 54 times in 82 international matches and is known for her speed, two‑footed play, heading ability and strength. She’ll stay with United until 2029, and the club hopes her finish will complement the team’s crosses and boost their goal‑scoring.
