UK ministers have opened a consultation to decide whether to ban people under 16 from using social media. The move comes after pressure from parents, teachers and a mother whose teenage daughter was murdered. The government will look at age limits, how to enforce them, stopping data sharing and limiting addictive features. A bill could put the ban in place within a year, while the UK also plans to make schools phone‑free.
