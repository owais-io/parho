China is keeping a close eye on the United States after the White House postponed President Trump’s visit to Beijing because of the war in the Middle East. The delay could change how the US deals with China on trade and might affect the 2026 mid‑term elections. China thinks a weaker Trump could help it secure a trade deal, but a defeat could also trigger a tougher stance from Republicans. Both sides want to avoid a new trade war.
