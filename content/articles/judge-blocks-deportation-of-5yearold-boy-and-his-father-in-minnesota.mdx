A U.S. judge in Minnesota said the 5‑year‑old boy Liam and his dad cannot be sent to another state while their asylum case is still being argued. The decision follows a viral arrest that sparked anger over ICE’s crackdown on immigrants. The family says they entered the U.S. legally and have an active asylum claim. DHS said the boy was taken for his safety, but school officials said the family tried to keep him home.
