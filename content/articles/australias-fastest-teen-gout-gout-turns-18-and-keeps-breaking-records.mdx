Gout Gout, Australia's quickest sprinter, turned 18 this month after a year of record‑setting races. He won his first national title, reached a world championship semi‑final, and ran under 10 seconds in the 100m and under 20 in the 200m (though some times were wind‑assisted). Outside athletics he finished school, bought a house for his family, and plans a gap year before university to focus on training. He’s eyeing the 2026 Commonwealth Games and the 2025 World Junior Championships.
