A low‑pressure system has brought a big cold front across western and central Europe, producing heavy snow in the Alps and record gusts in Croatia. Snow totals up to 1 metre are expected in the Swiss Alps, while Croatia’s northern region faces blizzard conditions and 70‑mph winds. Meanwhile, Cyclone Narelle, a category 4 storm, has smashed into Queensland, circled the northern coast, and is now heading toward Western Australia, threatening Perth with strong winds.
