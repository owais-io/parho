The FTSE 100, the main UK share index, jumped above 10,000 points on the first day of 2026, a first for the market. It has grown 21.5% last year, helped by firms like Rolls‑Royce and Burberry rising about 4%. The jump signals confidence in Britain’s economy and lifted Europe’s Stoxx 600 to a record high. Investors are upbeat, with 53% of UK retail traders expecting the bull market to continue into 2026.
