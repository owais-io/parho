Trump’s comments on the UK’s plan to hand over the Chagos Islands to Mauritius have swung from support to sharp criticism and back again. The deal keeps a 99‑year lease on the island of Diego Garcia, home to a key U.S. military base. In February 2025 Trump backed the agreement, but in early 2026 he denounced it on Truth Social, calling it a mistake. Later he said he still backs the deal if it protects U.S. interests.
