At a press conference with Israel’s prime minister, Trump called Fed chair Jerome Powell a “fool” and said he might fire him. He spread false claims about the cost of a Federal Reserve building renovation and threatened a lawsuit for “gross incompetence.” Trump also bragged about the U.S. economy and talked about the White House ballroom, saying it would cost less. He said he will name a new Fed chair in January.
