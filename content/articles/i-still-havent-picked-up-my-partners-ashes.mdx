In 2020 my partner died, and I still haven’t picked up his ashes from the funeral home. The pandemic made paperwork hard, and grief left me overwhelmed. The article explains how many Australians face the same dilemma—deciding where to scatter or keep ashes when no clear instructions exist. The writer shares her own experience, her daughter’s university study of death, and how finally seeing her dad’s ashes in her office gave her the courage to act.
