Arsenal's title hopes were dented when they lost 3‑2 to Manchester United. Manager Mikel Arteta blamed a series of errors, especially a bad back‑pass by Martín Zubimendi that gave United a goal‑equaliser. United then scored two more from long range, with Patrick Dorgu and Matheus Cunha. Arteta said the mistakes created anxiety after recent goalless draws and that the team needs mental strength on matchdays.
