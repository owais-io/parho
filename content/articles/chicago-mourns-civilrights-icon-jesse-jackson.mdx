Chicago mourns the death of civil‑rights icon Jesse Jackson, who passed away at 84. On Friday his body lay in repose at the Rainbow/Push headquarters, where crowds gathered to pay respects. The next day he travelled to South Carolina for his final funeral. Friends, family, and supporters—including a 17‑year‑old who met him as a child—shared memories of his activism and kindness.
