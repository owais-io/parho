Xabi Alonso began the 2023‑24 season at Real Madrid with a 13‑win streak but soon ran into trouble. Players refused his stricter schedule and long tactical talks. A bad run, ending in a 5‑goal Super Cup defeat, led the club to part ways with him by mutual agreement. Former player Álvaro Arbeloa was named caretaker, while stars like Mbappé and Bellingham publicly praised the decision.
