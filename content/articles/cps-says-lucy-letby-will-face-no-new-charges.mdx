CPS said there is not enough evidence to bring fresh charges against former neonatal nurse Lucy Letby, who is already serving life sentences for the murder of 7 babies and the attempted murder of 7 others in 2016. The prosecutor looked at 11 possible new offences but found the evidence did not meet the required standard. Letby and her lawyers say she is innocent, but police say the decision does not change their ongoing investigation of hospital staff.
