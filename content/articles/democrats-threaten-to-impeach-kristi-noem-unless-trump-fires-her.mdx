House Democrats have warned President Trump that they will start impeachment proceedings against Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem unless she is removed. The demand follows the fatal shootings of two U.S. citizens by federal agents in Minneapolis. Trump defended Noem, saying she’s doing a good job, but Republican senators Lisa Murkowski and Thom Tillis also urged her resignation.
