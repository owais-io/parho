Boland said it felt awkward to bowl with Alex Carey standing at the stumps, but the tactic has helped Australia take wickets in the Ashes. Coach Andrew McDonald suggested it after seeing English batsmen try to move around the crease. Though it was a mental adjustment for Boland, the move kept England on the back foot and gave Australia a useful weapon. Carey also helped with 267 runs.
