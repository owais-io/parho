Chelsea won 3‑2 at Cardiff after a dramatic match. Buonanotte scored the opener, but Cardiff’s David Turnbull levelled with a diving header. In the final minutes, Pedro Neto and then Garnacho added two quick goals to secure the win. The game kept fans on edge and gave coach Mauricio Maresca a chance to rally his side, who celebrated the victory and the support from the crowd.
