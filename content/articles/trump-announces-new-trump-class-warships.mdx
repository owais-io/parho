Donald Trump said the U.S. Navy will build a new type of warship called the Trump‑class. He plans to start with two ships and then build up to 20‑25 over time. Trump says the vessels will be huge, fast and super‑powerful, with the latest weapons. He also wants the navy to ship these ships domestically to keep America a top shipbuilder. The move comes amid other naming controversies in the Trump era.
