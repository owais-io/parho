During the Sunderland vs Newcastle derby, striker Nick Woltemade accidentally headed a cross into his own net, giving Sunderland a 1‑0 win. The own goal stunned Newcastle and earned Woltemade a sarcastic ovation from the Sunderland fans. The match was Sunderland’s first Premier League game in eight years and followed the recent death of club legend Gary Rowell. Despite a tough season, Sunderland showed they can still win big on their own turf.
