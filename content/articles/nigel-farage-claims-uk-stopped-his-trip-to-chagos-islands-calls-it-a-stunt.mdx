Nigel Farage says the UK stopped him from going to the Chagos Islands for a humanitarian mission, calling it a 'MAGA' stunt. He flew to the Maldives on a private jet, claimed the government blocked his trip, and posted a video on X. Critics say he wasted time and money, and the trip was unnecessary as the UK didn’t know he was traveling. The Chagos Islands remain restricted territory.
