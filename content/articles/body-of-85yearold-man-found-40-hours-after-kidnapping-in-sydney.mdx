Police say the body of 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian was found about 40 hours after he was taken from his home in North Ryde. The remains were discovered near a golf club in Pitt Town. Investigators link a burnt Toyota Corolla and evidence found in a car in Westmead to the case. Baghsarian was a victim of mistaken identity. Family members say they are in shock and ask anyone with footage to help.
