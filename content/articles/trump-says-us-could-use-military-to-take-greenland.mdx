The White House said the U.S. could use the military to seize Greenland, saying it's a top national security goal. President Trump and his team are exploring all options. European allies—France, Germany, the UK, Denmark—told Washington to respect Greenland's sovereignty. The comment comes as the U.S. looks to strengthen Arctic security, but it raises questions about the rights of Greenland's people.
