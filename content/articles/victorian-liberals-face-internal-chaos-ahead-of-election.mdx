The Victorian Liberal Party will run the upcoming state election with new leader Jess Wilson, who aims to reconnect with voters. But internal disputes threaten its chances. Star MP Sam Groth resigned after infighting, and members Moira Deeming and Ann‑Marie Hermans are battling pre‑selection fights and legal issues. A Supreme Court case over a $1.5 million loan to a former leader adds further turmoil, risking extra damage as the party tries to unite for the vote.
