Magistrate Magali Lafourcade says two U.S. advisers met her in May to try to stop France from banning Marine Le Pen from running for office. She reported it to the foreign ministry right away, worried the meeting could sway public debate. Le Pen was found guilty of a fake‑jobs scam and banned for five years. The U.S. advisers said the trial was political and wanted U.S. money for Le Pen’s defense. France says no interference happened.
