The Trump team has bounced between many explanations for the recent strike on Iran—claims of protests, nuclear threats, missile programs, and an alleged imminent attack. New evidence shows that Israeli plans may have pushed the U.S. into action, and top lawmakers are furious that the administration misled the public. With no clear reason settled, the war’s justification remains shaky.
