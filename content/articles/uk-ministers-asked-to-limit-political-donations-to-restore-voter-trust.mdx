19 civil groups have written to UK ministers, urging a cap on political donations to rebuild voter confidence ahead of a new elections bill that will let 16‑ and 17‑year‑olds vote. They also want the bill to ban crypto donations, tighten loopholes, and raise fines for illegal funding. The letter asks for automatic voter registration and protection of the Electoral Commission’s independence. The call comes after a large crypto donation to Reform UK and growing concerns about money’s origins.
