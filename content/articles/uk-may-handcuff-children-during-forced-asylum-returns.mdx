UK Home Office is testing a scheme that could handcuff children during forced returns. A pilot targets 150 families with refused asylum claims, offering up to £40,000 to leave within seven days. If families refuse, they face enforced removal. The proposal includes handcuffing kids who resist boarding a plane. Critics say this is cruel, while the government says it’s a last‑resort measure.
