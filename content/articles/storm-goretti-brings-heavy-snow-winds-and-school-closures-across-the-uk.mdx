Storm Goretti is set to hit England, Wales and Scotland with deep snow, icy roads and strong winds. Schools in many areas, from Oxfordshire to Aberdeenshire, will stay shut until Wednesday, and some northern Scottish pupils may get extra holiday time. Roads may be blocked, and power cuts are possible in remote villages. The Met Office keeps a yellow warning in place, meaning travel could be delayed but most people can still go about their day.
