UK Parliament is set to debate a new rule that would make police treat every suicide where domestic abuse is suspected as a possible homicide. The Liberal Democrats propose the change to ensure better evidence collection and justice for victims. Supporters say the current system lets many abuse‑related deaths slip through the cracks. The amendment faces a tough vote, but it could change how the justice system handles these cases.
