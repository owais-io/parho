Pochettino told reporters that every World Cup match must feel as important as a final. He pointed out that the US already played Australia and Paraguay in friendlies, but those games aren’t the same as the tournament’s high‑stakes matches. Drawing from his own 2002 experience, he urges the team to bring passion and focus, treating the first game—and every subsequent one—like a final.
