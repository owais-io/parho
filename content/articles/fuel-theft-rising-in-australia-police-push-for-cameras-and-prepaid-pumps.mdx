Australia’s police say fuel theft is on the rise as prices climb after Iran’s strike. The state police in South Australia and Western Australia have seen more reports of diesel and petrol being stolen from trucks, farms and service stations. They urge retailers to install prepaid pumps and CCTV to stop the thefts. Police also tell farmers to secure storage and keep detailed records.
