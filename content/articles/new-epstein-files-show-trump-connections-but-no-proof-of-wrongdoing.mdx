The Justice Department has released 3 million pages of Jeffrey Epstein emails. They reveal that Trump and some of his close allies were mentioned or had brief interactions, but nothing proves the former president did anything illegal. The files include emails where Epstein mocked Trump and notes about visits to Epstein’s home. Despite the lack of evidence, the release has sparked debate over why the government kept most pages hidden.
