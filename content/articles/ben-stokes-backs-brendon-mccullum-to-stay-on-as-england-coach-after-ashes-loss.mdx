Ben Stokes says he and coach Brendon McCullum are the right pair to move England forward, even after the recent Ashes defeat. He plans to sit down with McCullum during the off‑season to review how the team can improve. England’s board is cautious about big changes, so a review will look at coaching staff, training, and team culture. The upcoming T20 World Cup may also affect the decision.
