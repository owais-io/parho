Nick Reiner, the son of famed director Rob Reiner, pleaded not guilty to two first‑degree murder charges after his parents were found dead in their Los Angeles home. A new public‑defender took his case after his former lawyer withdrew. He is held without bail and may face the death penalty, though the district attorney has not decided yet. Reiner’s past struggles with addiction and mental illness are noted, and a full autopsy is still pending.
