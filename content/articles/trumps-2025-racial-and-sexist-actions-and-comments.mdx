After being re‑elected, President Trump used executive orders to roll back diversity programs, stop gender‑affirming care for minors and cut funding for Smithsonian exhibits on race. He also changed Denali’s name back to Mount McKinley and led a large deportation campaign that tore families apart. Throughout 2025, he repeatedly made racist, xenophobic and sexist statements about minorities, immigrants, and journalists, sparking widespread backlash.
