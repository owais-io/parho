UK shoppers are set to spend £3.8 billion on Boxing Day – a 2 % rise from last year. Most of the growth will come online, but high‑street shops are also seeing a 1.5 % bump. Despite a weaker Black Friday, people are still hunting bargains, especially for home goods and furniture. Sales on Christmas Eve and 27 December are also strong, making the whole holiday period a busy time for retailers.
