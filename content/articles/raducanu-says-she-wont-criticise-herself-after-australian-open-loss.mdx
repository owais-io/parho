Emma Raducanu lost her second‑round match at the Australian Open to Anastasia Potapova, 7‑6, 6‑2. She said she won’t be too harsh on herself because her training was hit by injuries and the wind made it hard to play. Raducanu had only started full practice a few days before the tournament. She hopes to regroup in Cluj, Romania, and get back to her best game.
