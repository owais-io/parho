Veteran reporter Bob Woodward said he is “crushed” by the Post’s decision to fire more than 300 staff, including its sports, local news and audio teams. He warned that the cuts could hurt readers and the paper’s future, stressing the need to keep strong journalism alive. The layoffs came after owner Jeff Bezos cut political endorsements and re‑shaped the paper’s opinion side, sparking concerns about its long‑term survival.
