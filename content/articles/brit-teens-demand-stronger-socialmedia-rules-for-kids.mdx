In the UK, lawmakers are debating banning people under 15 from social‑media sites, a move backed by a majority of teens who feel the platforms are harmful. Parents, teachers, and some politicians argue tighter limits could protect young people from online dangers and mental‑health issues. However, studies show the link between screen time and anxiety is unclear, so experts say any ban should be part of a wider plan to support children’s wellbeing.
