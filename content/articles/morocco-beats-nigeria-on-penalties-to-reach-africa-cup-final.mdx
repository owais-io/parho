Morocco won a penalty shootout against Nigeria, 4‑2, after a 0‑0 draw in extra time, earning a spot in the Africa Cup of Nations final. Goalkeeper Yassine Bounou made two saves, while Moroccan striker Youssef En Nesyri scored the decisive penalty. The win marks Morocco's first final appearance since 2004. They will play Senegal in the final, while Nigeria will contest a third‑place match against Egypt.
