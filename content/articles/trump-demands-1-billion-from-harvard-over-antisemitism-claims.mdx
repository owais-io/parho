Trump wants $1 billion in damages from Harvard because he says the university is antisemitic. He dropped an earlier $200 million demand, but the fight has moved to court. The White House has threatened research funding cuts and sued Harvard, while the university sued back. A judge stopped the funding cuts, and a settlement is still in play. Side claims the other is trying to control academic freedom; Harvard says it will keep its independence. The dispute could hit student visas.
