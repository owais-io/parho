Virginia's new Democratic governor, Abigail Spanberger, responded to President Trump’s State of the Union speech by saying he’s not delivering on the cost of living and safety for ordinary people. She blamed Trump’s tariffs, tax cuts for the wealthy and aggressive immigration actions on farmers, small businesses and families. Spanberger highlighted the need for affordable housing, energy help and health‑insurance support while calling out the president’s authoritarian tactics.
