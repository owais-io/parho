Ford said England must not let their win over New Zealand be a one‑off. The team beat the All Blacks 33‑19 at Twickenham, their 10th straight win, and moved up to third in the world rankings. Ford scored 13 points, with two drop‑goals that turned the game around. He urged better preparation and attack, and coach Borthwick said the side still has room to grow. Next up are Argentina and Wales.
