A writer points out that while dog owners usually carry bags to clean up after their pets, horse riders often leave piles of manure on trails. He describes a rider who left a huge mound of horse poop, leaving him frustrated. The letter calls for horse owners to feel the same social pressure to clean up, hoping for cleaner public paths and a cleaner countryside.
