Australian Police found more than 28 kg of cocaine hidden behind a television on a luxury bus in South Australia. Border Force officers discovered the drugs after checking a roll‑on/roll‑off vessel at Outer Harbor. AFP detectives seized the packages and are now investigating. The cocaine is worth millions on the street and could lead to many illegal deals. Police say they will stop organised crime from bringing drugs into the country.
