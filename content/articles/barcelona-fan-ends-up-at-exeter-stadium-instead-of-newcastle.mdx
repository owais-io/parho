A Barcelona supporter mistakenly walked into Exeter City’s small stadium thinking he was at Newcastle United’s big ground. Staff helped him and gave him a seat for a lower‑league game instead of the Champions League match he wanted. The fan looked very upset, didn’t speak much English, and was later tracked down by a company for a publicity stunt. The story has gone viral online.
