Danny Ryan proposed a leadership change at the Ethereum Foundation to take advantage of political shifts, especially a Trump‑led crypto‑friendly government, and push Ethereum into Wall Street. The community split between libertarian “cypherpunks” and pragmatic investors. The debate sparked a new leadership team with co‑executives from both camps. Ether’s price stays flat, while projects like Etherealize aim to speed institutional adoption.
