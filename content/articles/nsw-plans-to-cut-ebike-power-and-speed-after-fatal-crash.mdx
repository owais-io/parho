New South Wales is reviewing rules for electric bikes after a rider died in a collision with a garbage truck. The state could cut the maximum power of legal e‑bikes from 500 watts to 250 watts and reduce top speeds from 50–60 km/h to 25–30 km/h. Police urged parents to buy only legal models for kids and are cracking down on modified, high‑powered bikes. The government plans to act soon, but no charges have been filed.
