World Cup manager Mauricio Pochettino criticized US player Tim Weah after Weah said high ticket prices could keep fans out of the 2026 Games. Pochettino said players should stick to football and not comment on politics or prices. Weah had been speaking about ticket affordability, a topic that is very much about soccer and fans. The clash shows how US soccer leadership still resists players’ voices on social issues.
