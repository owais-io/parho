The U.S. military’s top official said Scouting America will change its rules so that only a child’s birth sex matters for membership. The Pentagon wants the organization to drop its diversity, equity and inclusion focus and return to a “boy‑scout” style. Scouting America says it will adjust its programs to meet a federal order while keeping its new name and co‑educational policy. The move has sparked debate over military influence on a youth group.
