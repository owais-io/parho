Amazon’s new film about Melania Trump opened in theaters and was hit by a wave of Republican supporters, especially older women. Even though critics gave it low marks, the movie made $7 million in its first weekend and became the top‑grossing documentary of the year. Fans praised the behind‑the‑scenes look at her life, while critics said the film was bland and overly promotional.
