Trump’s recent comments about Greenland show how the melting Arctic is turning into a new battlefield. As ice disappears, shipping lanes, fishing grounds and hidden minerals become valuable. The US wants Greenland for military bases and the rare‑earth resources that could help it compete with China. The move reflects wider fears that climate change will spark disputes over land, water and resources worldwide.
