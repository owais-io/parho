David Connolly, the new Northern Territory administrator, has issued a public apology for social media posts that many call racist. Aboriginal leaders and more than 20 local MPs want to boycott his swearing‑in because they say he is “toxic” and unfit to represent them. The territory’s government and Prime Minister Albanese back his appointment, but protests are expected outside the ceremony. The governor‑general praised his apology and will still carry out the oath.
