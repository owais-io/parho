Guardian issued corrections for several articles, most notably a piece about Gaza’s death toll. The correction says the Max Planck study didn’t examine indirect deaths and that life‑expectancy drops mentioned apply only to direct deaths. It also notes the study was published in Lancet Global Health, not the general Lancet. Other stories were also amended, and readers can send feedback to Guardian’s readers’ editor.
