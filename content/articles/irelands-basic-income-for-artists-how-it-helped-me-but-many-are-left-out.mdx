I got lucky when the Irish government ran a test to give a basic income to 2,000 artists. For three years I received €325 a week with no conditions other than a survey, which let me focus on writing and even start a family. The scheme showed that supporting artists pays back money to society, but the new permanent plan still limits who can benefit. Many artists worry the program will keep them in the same cycle of uncertainty.
