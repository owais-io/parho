UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer announced a set of measures aimed at keeping children safer on the internet. He wants a new minimum age for social media, backed by fast‑acting powers that let the government impose a ban quickly if the consultation recommends it. The plan also aims to stop endless scrolling for under‑16s, limit the use of VPNs, extend the Online Safety Act to AI chatbots, and force platforms to keep a child’s data after death for investigation.
