The World Cup 2026 draw will take place on Friday, 5 December at the Kennedy Center in Washington, D.C., starting at noon local time. Four pots of 12 teams each – with the three host nations and top‑ranked teams in Pot 1 – will be used to create 12 groups. Six spots still need playoffs, so some teams are in Pot 4. The full match schedule will be released on Saturday, 6 December, and the tournament runs from 11 June to 19 July.
