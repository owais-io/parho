Parliament’s foreign‑affairs committee said the UK’s attempt to reset ties with the EU is missing direction, definition and drive. They said the last summit improved the political relationship but the government has no clear priorities, timelines or milestones. Only the Erasmus student scheme has been agreed so far, while the EU now wants a £1.7bn contribution for a security fund. The report urges a white paper and faster progress.
