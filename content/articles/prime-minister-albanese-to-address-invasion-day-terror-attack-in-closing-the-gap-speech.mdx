Prime Minister Anthony Albanese will speak in Parliament’s annual Closing the Gap speech about a failed terror attack at a 26‑January Invasion Day rally in Perth. The attack was racially motivated and left many Indigenous people feeling unsafe. Albanese pledges extra money – $144 m for community health, $299 m for remote jobs, $218 m for domestic‑violence programmes, and cheaper essentials for 225 remote stores – to help meet the few targets that are on track.
