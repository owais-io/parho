Carlos Alcaraz has become the youngest male player ever to win a career‑grand‑slam, beating Novak Djokovic at the Australian Open. The 22‑year‑old talked about people who doubted him and about his recent coaching split. He says the win proves his hard work and his decision to change coaches was right. Alcaraz keeps proving he can stay on top of tennis, even as rivals like Jannik Sinner challenge him.
