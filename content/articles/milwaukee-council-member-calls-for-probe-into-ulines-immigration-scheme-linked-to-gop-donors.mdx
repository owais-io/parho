A Milwaukee city council member is urging an investigation into Uline, a $8 billion office‑supplies firm owned by major Republican donors. He says the company used a “shuttle program” to bring Mexican workers to U.S. warehouses on visas meant for training, subjecting them to long hours, injuries and punishment for speaking up. The move highlights potential legal violations and raises questions about big donors’ ties to policy.
