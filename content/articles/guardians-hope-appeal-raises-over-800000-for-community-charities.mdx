Guardian's Hope Appeal has raised more than £800,000, bringing money to five charities that work against racism, hate, and division. The campaign, now in its final days, is collecting funds for Citizens UK, Linking Network, Locality, Hope Unlimited, and Who is Your Neighbour?. The money will help projects that build trust, offer youth clubs, food banks, arts, and community events. Readers say they value local groups that strengthen solidarity and keep communities safe.
