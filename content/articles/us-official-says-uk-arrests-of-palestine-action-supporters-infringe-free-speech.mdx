Sarah Rogers, the U.S. undersecretary for public diplomacy, said arresting people who support the banned group Palestine Action in Britain amounts to censorship. She warned that stopping the speech could hurt more than help. The UK outlawed Palestine Action in July 2025 after a vandalism incident. Since then over 2,000 people have been jailed for simply showing signs or saying they back the group. The U.S. sees this as a free‑speech violation.
