Domino’s boss quit after saying pizza sales in the UK were topping out. Pizza shops are closing, and fried‑chicken places like KFC and Popeyes are attracting young customers. While pizza still gets steady take‑away orders, it faces tough competition from supermarkets and delivery apps. Big chains are staying strong, but small operators struggle. Overall pizza sales are still rising, showing the dish isn’t dying yet.
