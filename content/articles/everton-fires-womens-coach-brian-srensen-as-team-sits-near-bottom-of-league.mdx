Everton’s women’s team has fired coach Brian Sørensen after a poor start to the season. The club is ninth in the Women’s Super League and has only won one home game, putting them close to relegation. Even after a small victory, the club said a change was needed. Assistant coaches also left, and academy coach Scott Phelan will lead the team for the rest of the season.
