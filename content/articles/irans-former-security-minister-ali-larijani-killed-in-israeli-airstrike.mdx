Ali Larijani, a long‑time Iranian security chief and former Revolutionary Guard commander, was shot down by an Israeli strike in 2024. He had warned that the West viewed Iran as a threat, especially over its nuclear program, and had tried to negotiate with Europe and the US. A pragmatic figure, he also played a key role in suppressing protests and clashed with President Ahmadinejad before stepping down.
