Harrods' warehouse staff who work through a temporary agency say they didn't get the full holiday pay they deserve. The agency, which supplies workers to the luxury retailer, apparently underpaid thousands of pounds in holiday pay. Now the Guardian is asking other temp workers at Harrods to share their stories and confirm if they’ve also been shorted on holiday pay. If you’ve had a similar issue, contact the reporters through the form.
