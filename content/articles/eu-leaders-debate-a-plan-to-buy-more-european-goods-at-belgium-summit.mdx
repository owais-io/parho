EU leaders gathered at a Belgian castle to discuss how to make Europe more competitive. The main issue was a plan called “Buy European”, which would favor European-made goods, especially in clean tech and defence. France pushes it, while the Nordics and Germany worry it adds too much regulation and hurts trade. The meeting also covered deregulation, single‑market rules and linking Europe’s financial markets.
