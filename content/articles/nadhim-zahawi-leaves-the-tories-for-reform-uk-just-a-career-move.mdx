Zahawi’s switch from the Conservative cabinet to Reform UK is seen as a simple opportunistic move, not a shocking political event. He claims the Tories mismanage the state, but the author says such defections hurt the Conservatives and may not help Reform UK win voters who left the Tories for Labour. The piece warns that party loyalty is hard to regain and that these switches could signal a wider problem.
