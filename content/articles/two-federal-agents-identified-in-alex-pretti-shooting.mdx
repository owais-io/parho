Minneapolis police shot Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, in a confrontation with federal agents. ProPublica documents now name the two officers: Jesus Ochoa, a border‑patrol agent, and Raymundo Gutierrez, a Customs and Border Protection officer. Both were on leave and are under investigation. The shooting sparked protests and demands for a transparent inquiry, as the agents were part of a masked enforcement operation that has already killed another protester.
