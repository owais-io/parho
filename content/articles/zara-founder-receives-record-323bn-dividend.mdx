Inditex, the company that owns Zara and other fashion brands, will pay its biggest shareholder, founder Amancio Ortega, a record €3.23 billion dividend this year. The payout is split in two halves, paid in May and November. Ortega, who still owns 59% of the company, will use the money to buy property, as he has done before. Inditex’s sales rose 3 % to €39.9 billion and profit up 6 %.
