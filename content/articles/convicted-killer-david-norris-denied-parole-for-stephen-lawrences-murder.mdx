David Norris, who helped kill 18‑year‑old Stephen Lawrence in 1993, was denied parole. Prison officials say he still behaves like a racist, abuses other inmates, and keeps a knife. He once claimed he had changed, but evidence shows he continues to use hate language. Norris also refuses to name the other men who helped kill Lawrence, prompting the victim’s family to keep demanding full justice.
