After an Iran‑linked drone crashed into a British airbase, Cypriot citizens took to the streets, demanding the UK bases be shut down. They claim the bases threaten their safety and could drag Cyprus into the wider Iran conflict. The government says the attack shows the bases need stronger protection and will keep negotiating with Britain.
