The Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) has removed Professor Jacob George, its chief medical and scientific officer, from any role in the Pathways trial, which studies puberty blockers for transgender children. The decision follows revelations that George posted gender‑critical comments on social media before taking office, raising concerns he might influence the study. The trial, meant to recruit 226 youngsters, was already paused for safety reasons. The MHRA stresses it is focused on participant safety.
