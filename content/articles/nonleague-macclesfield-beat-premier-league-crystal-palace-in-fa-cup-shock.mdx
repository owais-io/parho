Macclesfield, a sixth‑tier club, pulled off a historic FA Cup shock by beating Crystal Palace, the Premier League champions, 2‑0. The match, played in front of 5,348 fans, was Macclesfield’s first win over top‑flight opposition. Captain Paul Dawson opened the scoring with a header, and Isaac Buckley‑Ricketts added a second. Palace’s star players struggled, and the win has been called the biggest giant‑killing in FA Cup history.
