In the UK, police say child sexual abuse is rising and they arrest about 1,000 paedophile suspects every month. Tech has made it easier for offenders to find and share illegal images, especially on mainstream social media. The National Crime Agency warns that algorithms can push these images to people already interested. Tech firms can help by removing known abuse content, but many say current safeguards are still lacking.
