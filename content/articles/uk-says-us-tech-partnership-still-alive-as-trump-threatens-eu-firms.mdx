The UK says a $40bn tech partnership with the US hasn’t been killed, despite talks stalling. Prime Minister says they are still in talks about tech cooperation. The US paused the deal over the UK’s digital tax and food rules. Meanwhile Trump threatens fines for EU tech firms. The EU says its rules are fair. The US has threatened to use all tools against EU companies if restrictions continue.
