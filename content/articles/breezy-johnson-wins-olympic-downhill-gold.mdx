Breezy Johnson, the American alpine skier who has battled injuries and a suspension, finally took Olympic gold in the women’s downhill at the Milano Cortina Games. She beat Germany’s Emma Aicher by just 0.04 seconds, the smallest margin in the event’s Olympic history. The win came after a dramatic crash that took down legend Lindsey Vonn. Johnson’s victory shows how perseverance and hard work can pay off, making her the second U.S. woman to win this dangerous race.
