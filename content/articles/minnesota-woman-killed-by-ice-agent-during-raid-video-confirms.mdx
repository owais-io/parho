In Minneapolis, a 37‑year‑old woman named Renee Good was shot to death by an ICE agent during a federal raid. A video released by a reporter shows the agent firing, and the footage contradicts ICE’s claim that Good tried to run them over. The incident happened less than a mile from the George Floyd murder site, sparking protests. The city’s mayor and other officials blasted ICE and called for the agents to leave the city.
