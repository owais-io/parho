Nigel Farage spent two nights in Dubai, meeting UAE ministers and speaking with British expats to try to raise money for his Reform UK party. He talked at a GB News event and said the trip was mainly about getting support and learning from the UAE's low crime and tax policies. While he praised the country and mentioned discussions with a minister, he said no donation deals were announced yet.
