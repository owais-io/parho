AI stocks have made the richest US tech leaders even richer. Over the past year, the top ten tech billionaires’ fortunes grew from about $1.9 trillion to nearly $2.5 trillion—a gain of more than $500 billion. Elon Musk jumped to $645 billion, while Nvidia’s CEO Jensen Huang’s net worth rose to $159 billion. Google co‑founders, Amazon’s Jeff Bezos and others also saw big gains. The boom shows how AI fuels high stock prices, but experts warn markets could wobble if AI hype fades.
