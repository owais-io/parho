After Russia invaded Ukraine, the world loudly said it was wrong. But when the U.S. and Israel started striking Iran, many leaders stayed quiet. The attacks have killed over a thousand civilians, including a girls’ school that lost 175 people, and the U.S. says it will give “no quarter.” The piece argues that the West is being double‑sided, ignoring war crimes while praising Ukraine. It stresses that international law must still be respected.
