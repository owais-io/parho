Leon, the healthy‑fast‑food chain, is putting its restaurants into administration and will shut some outlets. The move comes after a recent buy‑back by co‑founder John Vincent and a drop in sales due to more people working from home. The company already closed 10 shops, will drop its Roast Rewards club, and is looking for other businesses to take over leases. Vincent says the aim is to rebuild and return to profit, helping staff find new roles.
