Two men, 47 and 45, were arrested last week over a suspected arson attack on four ambulances belonging to a Jewish charity in north London. They were released on bail until April while police keep looking for other suspects. The Metropolitan Police carried out searches at several London addresses and say the attack was a hate crime. Officers ask anyone with information to come forward.
