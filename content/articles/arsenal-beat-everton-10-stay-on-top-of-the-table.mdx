Arsenal beat Everton 1‑0 after Viktor Gyökeres scored a penalty in the first half. The win kept Arsenal in the league’s Christmas number one spot. Manager Mikel Arteta celebrated the win but said the team still needs better performances. Everton was missing key players and struggled, with a few controversial moments, including a handball that earned a penalty. Arsenal’s win shows they’re ready for the title race.
