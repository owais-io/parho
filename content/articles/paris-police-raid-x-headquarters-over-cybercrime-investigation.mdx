Paris police searched Elon Musk’s X headquarters in France and called both Musk and former boss Linda Yaccarino in for questioning. Investigators are looking at possible cyber‑crime, including the platform’s algorithms, the new AI tool Grok, and the spread of illegal content like child‑abuse images and deepfakes. The raid comes amid a wave of European moves to tighten social‑media rules, with Spain and Greece hinting at bans for under‑16 users. X says the raid is politically motivated and denies wrongdoing.
