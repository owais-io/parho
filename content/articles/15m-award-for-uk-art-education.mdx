The Freelands Foundation has launched a new £1.5m award programme to support visual art education across the UK. Each year three charities will receive £100,000 to fund projects that show innovative teaching and real impact, even after years of cutbacks. Winners will work with the foundation on a case‑study film to share best practice. Applications open Jan 28 and close Mar 24, with winners announced Nov 1.
