Britain will re‑enter the EU’s Erasmus student exchange plan, letting UK students study abroad in Europe without extra fees from 2027. The deal covers vocational, sports and university exchanges, and EU students at UK schools will pay capped domestic fees. It shows the UK government’s push to get closer to the EU, boosting ties and opening new opportunities for young people.
