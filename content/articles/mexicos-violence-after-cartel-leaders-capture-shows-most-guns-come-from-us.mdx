Mexico captured cartel boss El Mencho, sparking violent clashes that killed 62 people. Police seized big guns—Barrett rifles, rockets—most of which came from the US. Mexico has strict gun rules, but the US sells too many guns. Smuggling happens by many tiny dealers, making it hard to stop. US officials seized only a few percent of weapons; lawmakers want tighter gun laws to cut the flow.
