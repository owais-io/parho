After losing at home to Manchester United, Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta called a team meeting to cool down the pressure and remind players to play with enjoyment. He said the team’s reaction was great and that they will focus on the next four months with courage, conviction, and fun. Arteta believes the players can handle the pressure and aim to win the Premier League title.
