A record 1,000 people under 30 in the UK earned more than £1m in 2023, an 11% rise from 2022, HMRC data shows. They brought in over £3bn, averaging about £3m each. The jump is driven by influencers, athletes, musicians and high‑pay tech jobs. Under‑30s now make up 3% of all million‑payers, but their total is still smaller than older earners. Experts warn many will spend their money quickly.
