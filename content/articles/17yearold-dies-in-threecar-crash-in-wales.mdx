A 17‑year‑old girl, Demi Edmunds, died after a three‑car collision on the A4042 in Cwmbran, Wales, on Thursday afternoon. She was the only pedestrian hit and was pronounced dead at the scene. Her brother praised her as “funny, kind and caring” in a tribute. The family asked for privacy, and police are still looking for witnesses or footage to help investigate.
