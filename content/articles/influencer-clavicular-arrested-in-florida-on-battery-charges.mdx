The popular influencer Clavicular, real name Braden Eric Peters, was taken into custody in Florida on battery charges. Police say he and his girlfriend started a fight that left a 19‑year‑old woman injured at a rental property in Kissimmee. Both were booked in jail, and investigators say he posted the incident online. The arrest came after a viral video showed him firing a gun at an alligator, sparking online outrage.
