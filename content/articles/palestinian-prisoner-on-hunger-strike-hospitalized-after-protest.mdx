A 20‑year‑old Palestinian woman in a hunger strike at HMP Bronzefield was taken to hospital after protesters outside the jail demanded help. She has been refusing food for 46 days, has severe pain, and was denied an ambulance earlier. Fellow prisoners are also on hunger strikes. The group wants bail, an end to the Palestine Action ban, and better communication. Politicians have asked for meetings, but officials declined to comment.
