Green Party leader Zack Polanski said a future government would stop measuring success by GDP growth and instead look at people’s health, community life and fairness. He wants new policies to cut housing costs, support energy bills, and tackle climate and inequality. Polanski called for a wealth tax, rent controls, and renationalising utilities. He said borrowing should fund investment, not just growth, and that the party would rethink money‑making rules.
