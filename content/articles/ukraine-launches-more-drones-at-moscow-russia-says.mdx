Russia says Ukraine has flown drones into Moscow every day of 2026, up from earlier sporadic attacks. The ministry logged 57 drones shot down on Sunday, part of 437 over Russia so far. Ukraine claims these long‑range attacks hit logistics and energy sites to hurt Russia’s war effort. The raids forced Moscow’s airports to close, causing flight delays. Two people died in border raids, and Russian‑led strikes also killed civilians in Kyiv and Kharkiv.
