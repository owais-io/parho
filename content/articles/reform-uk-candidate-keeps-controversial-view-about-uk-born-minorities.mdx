Matthew Goodwin, chosen by Reform UK for the Gorton and Denton byelection, refuses to back down from saying that people born in the UK from minority ethnic backgrounds might not be “British.” The claim has drawn fire from Labour, the Greens and the public, who say it fuels division. Goodwin’s stance, along with his comments on Islam and British identity, has put the Reform party in a spotlight as it battles to win a historically Labour seat.
