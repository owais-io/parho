Manchester United publicly reaffirmed its commitment to equality and diversity after co‑owner Jim Ratcliffe made controversial remarks about immigration. The club’s statement, which avoided naming Ratcliffe, highlighted the team’s inclusive values and its historic “All Red All Equal” campaign. Football bodies and fan groups condemned Ratcliffe’s comments, calling them divisive. The FA is investigating whether his words damaged the game’s reputation.
