A woman who spent 25 years enslaved by Amanda Wixon was rescued in 2021. The carer who now looks after her said the victim was skinny, scared, and refused hugs because of fear. After being freed, the woman attended doctors, therapy, and college, and has even taken holidays. Wixon was sentenced to 13 years in jail. The carer urges a wider inquiry into why the abuse went unnoticed.
