Thailand fired airstrikes at Cambodia after a Thai soldier was killed in a clash on the disputed border. Both sides blame each other for breaking a Trump‑brokered ceasefire, and soldiers and civilians have been injured. Thailand has ordered 385,000 residents to leave the border region, while Cambodia moved about 1,100 families to shelters. Leaders in Thailand, Cambodia, Malaysia and ASEAN leaders all urged calm, but the conflict shows the ceasefire remains fragile.
