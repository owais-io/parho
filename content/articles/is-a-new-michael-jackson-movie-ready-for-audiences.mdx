A new film about Michael Jackson is set to hit theaters in April, and the trailer already drew record‑setting views. Fans are excited, but some worry the legacy is still tangled with abuse allegations. The movie will feature his nephew Jaafar Jackson and aim to show his rise from the Jackson Five to global superstar. Industry experts say a biopic can keep his music alive for younger generations, even if the story is complicated.
