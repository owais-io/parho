UK authors push back against AI copyright changes. At this week’s London Book Fair, writers put “Human Authored” stickers on books to protest tech giants using their work to train AI. The Society of Authors says most writers feel their texts are used without permission or pay. They want clear laws, fair payment, and transparency so books keep real human voices. Non‑fiction sales fell, but stories about abuse and work life still sell well.
