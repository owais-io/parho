The Ravens have let go of head coach John Harbaugh, who led the team for 18 seasons. Harbaugh, the franchise’s most successful coach, guided Baltimore to a Super Bowl win in 2012 and made the playoffs in 12 of his 18 years. His final season ended with an 8‑9 record and a late loss to the Steelers. Despite a recent contract extension, the team said the decision was “incredibly difficult.”
