Major League Soccer (MLS) has reopened a probe into Philadelphia Union sporting director Ernst Tanner after a Guardian article added new evidence. The Union put Tanner on administrative leave. He faces accusations of racist, sexist and homophobic remarks and inappropriate physical contact with staff, some dating back years. Tanner denies everything and says he will cooperate. MLS says it will keep the investigation open while the team heads into playoffs.
