A survey of nearly 11,000 teens in England and Wales shows that about 1 in 9 have been asked by criminals to deal with drugs, weapons or money. More than a quarter of those teens said they agreed. About 42% were offered money and 27% drugs or alcohol. Nearly 22% faced threats, 18% were blackmailed and 18% were told they owed money. Teens pulled into crime were far more likely to suffer or cause serious violence.
