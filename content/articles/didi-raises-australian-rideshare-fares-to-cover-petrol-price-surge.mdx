DiDi has added a 5‑cent-per‑kilometre surcharge to rides in Australia to cover the steep rise in petrol prices caused by the Middle East conflict. The extra fee will be passed straight to drivers. Uber, DoorDash, and Australia Post are also looking at similar charges, while airlines like Qantas and Cathay Pacific have increased fuel surcharges. Small businesses and the RBA are monitoring the impact, and some firms say they won’t change prices.
