Pauline Hanson, an Australian politician, has taken advantage of the government’s small‑scale renewable energy scheme to install solar panels on her Queensland home. She says she supports rooftop solar for households but calls for ending subsidies for large‑scale renewables. Her move has drawn criticism from fellow MPs, who argue she is hypocritical for using the rebate while attacking the subsidies that fund the scheme.
