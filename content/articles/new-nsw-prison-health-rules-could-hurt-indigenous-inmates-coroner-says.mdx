Gregory Merriman, a Yuin man, died in a NSW prison after being sprayed with tear gas. The coroner says he had a hidden heart problem that should have been caught in routine health checks, which were delayed by new rules. The changes could let more Indigenous inmates miss vital screenings, risking serious health problems. Over‑representation of Aboriginal people in custody remains a major issue.
