Two Greenlandic siblings, Ukaleq and Sondre Slettemark, raced in the Winter Olympics for Denmark, but they carried and waved Greenland’s flag to show pride in their homeland. Their appearance sparked media attention and support from Greenland’s leaders, who see the event as a chance to raise awareness of Greenland’s culture and its future independence. Trump’s comments about Greenland added urgency to the family’s mission to represent their island on the world stage.
