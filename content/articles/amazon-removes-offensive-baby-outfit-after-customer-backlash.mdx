A baby outfit from KG London with the phrase “Santa’s favourite ho” was found on Amazon for kids from newborn to five years old. Parents and shoppers were shocked by the sexualized slur on a children’s garment. Amazon took the listing down on both the UK and US sites, saying it broke their content rules. The company said it has tools to spot offensive items, but if one slips through they’ll delete it and improve the filters.
