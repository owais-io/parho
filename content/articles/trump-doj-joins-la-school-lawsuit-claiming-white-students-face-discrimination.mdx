Los Angeles schools are in the middle of a new lawsuit. A conservative group says the district’s rule that gives extra help to schools with mostly non‑white students is reverse discrimination against white kids. The Trump‑era Department of Justice says the rule unfairly separates students by race. The district says the program is meant to close achievement gaps for students of color.
