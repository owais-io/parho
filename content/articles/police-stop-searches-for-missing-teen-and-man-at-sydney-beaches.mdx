Police stopped searching for a 16‑year‑old boy who was in a boat that capsized off Palm Beach on New Year’s Eve, and for a 22‑year‑old man named Sushan Khadka who disappeared at Coogee on New Year’s Day. The boy’s boat also carried a 47‑year‑old man who died in the water. Despite land and sea searches, Khadka hasn’t been found. The pause follows a series of beach drownings around Sydney, with six people already dead this holiday season.
