Simon Armitage was asked to write a poem for World Cancer Day. He spent time with 17 people from Yorkshire Cancer Research, including patients, families, and scientists. The poem, called “The Campaign,” uses the image of a dragon to represent cancer and ends on a positive note, encouraging people to keep fighting. It also marks the charity’s 100‑year anniversary. The piece aims to inspire hope and remind us that poetry can comfort during tough times.
