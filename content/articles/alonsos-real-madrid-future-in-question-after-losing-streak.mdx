Xabi Alonso, the 44‑year‑old coach of Real Madrid, has struggled in his first 14 games. After a heavy loss to Atlético and a string of three wins in nine matches, the team has drawn against weaker sides and lost to Celta Vigo, putting Alonso’s job at risk. Fans shouted disapproval, but some players, like Bellingham, still back him. However, Vinícius Junior has warned he won’t renew his contract if Alonso stays.
