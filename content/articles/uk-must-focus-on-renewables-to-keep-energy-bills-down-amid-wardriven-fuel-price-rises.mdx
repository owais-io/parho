Experts warn that wars in the Middle East are driving up fossil‑fuel prices and hurting UK households with higher energy bills. They say the country needs to stop relying on imported oil and gas and instead speed up the switch to renewables and nuclear power. The UK has already lost billions in the last energy shock, and new oil‑field plans will do little to lower costs. The government must focus on clean energy for security and savings.
