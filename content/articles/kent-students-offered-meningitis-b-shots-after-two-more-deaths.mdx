A meningitis B outbreak at the University of Kent has claimed two more lives, prompting the UK Health Security Agency to offer targeted shots to students in Canterbury halls. Pharmacies have run out of the vaccine, sparking calls for a national NHS catch‑up programme for anyone born before 2015. Antibiotic prophylaxis is being distributed, and the club where many cases were linked has closed. Health officials urge anyone exposed to seek treatment.
