Iraq’s national security adviser asked Australia to send back about 13 Australians who were recently moved from Syria to Iraqi prisons as suspected ISIS fighters. The request came amid growing instability in the region and after Iraq received over 5,700 suspected fighters from Syria. Australia says it is not repatriating such individuals and that any return would depend on legal and security reviews.
