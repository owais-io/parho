Australia produces a lot of plastic waste each year. While some states ban plastic food containers, there are no laws stopping people from bringing reusable containers for takeaway. A volunteer‑run website, BYO Containers, lists stores that welcome reusable cups and boxes. The article offers simple tips: use sturdy Tupperware, avoid single‑use plastic, ask politely before ordering, and start with easy foods like sushi or sandwiches. It also explains how workplaces can share reusable containers through services like Returnr.
