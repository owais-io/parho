Todd and Angela James flew from Canada to Australia to visit K'gari (Fraser Island) and hold a traditional ceremony for their daughter, Piper, who went missing and is believed to have drowned. While the ceremony gives them closure, the island is also in the middle of a controversial dingo cull. The Queensland government says the dogs pose a safety risk, but experts warn the cull could threaten the island's endangered dingo population and its cultural significance.
