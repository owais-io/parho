Alex de Minaur says he’s working hard to win his first Australian Open men’s title, the first Aussie to do it in 50 years. He’s a fast, focused player who has 10 titles and is ranked world No 7, but he’s never made a grand‑slam final. After a tough loss in the ATP Finals, he learned to let results slide and focus on his game. He’ll rest over Christmas, then train for the Australian Open.
