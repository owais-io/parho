A federal report says two Customs and Border Patrol officers fired guns at Alex Pretti, a U.S. citizen, during a Minnesota raid that left him dead. The agents were trying to take Pretti and a woman into custody, using pepper spray and tear gas. The incident sparked protests and criticism of the Trump administration’s immigration raids. President Trump said the raid is part of a big investigation and defended ICE’s actions.
