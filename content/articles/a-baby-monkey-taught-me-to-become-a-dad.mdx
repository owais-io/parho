He quit selling expensive Paris apartments and moved to Guinea, where he lived with 66 chimpanzees. There he learned to let go of anger and formed a close bond with a baby mangabey named Elio, who looked like a child. Caring for Elio made him feel like a father, taught him patience, and sparked his fight against poaching. He now feels purposeful, leaving the luxury job behind.
