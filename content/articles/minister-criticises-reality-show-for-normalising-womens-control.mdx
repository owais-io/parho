Australian minister Tanya Plibersek slammed the popular reality show *Married at First Sight* after a contestant said he wanted a woman to be obedient like a dog. She said the clip normalises coercive control and spreads misogyny, putting kids at risk. Plibersek urged parents to keep children away from the show, which draws more than 2 million viewers. Nine Entertainment declined to comment, while producers say the host confronted the contestant.
