Donald Trump is suing the BBC for defamation after the broadcaster aired a documentary that edited his January 6 speech. The BBC wants the case thrown out, arguing the Florida court doesn’t have jurisdiction. Trump’s claim could require the BBC to release sensitive information about his assets, because his lawsuit demands proof of damage to his business and brand. Trump has historically resisted sharing his tax records, adding extra complexity to the fight.
