Meta is shutting down about 500,000 accounts of users under 16 in Australia as the government’s social‑media ban takes effect on Dec. 10. The company began deleting accounts on Dec. 4 and is also blocking new sign‑ups for under‑aged users. People can download their data before the cutoff. The law could fine platforms $49.5 million if they keep under‑16 accounts. Most social‑media sites say they’ll comply.
