Yorkshire Water was fined £733,333 after three sewage spills into Pools Brook in 2018–2019. The leaks killed fish, injured insects, and polluted more than 600 metres of the stream. Causes were a burst sewer pipe, a wet‑wipe blockage, and a joint failure. The company says it has already spent £2.5 m moving the pipes and plans bigger investment to stop future leaks.
