Labour’s chair, Anna Turley, told Sky News that Keir Starmer will still be the prime minister by next Christmas, even if the party looks set to lose badly in upcoming elections. Turley said Starmer has the support of the cabinet and that the party is focused on delivering on promises, especially for NHS and public services. The statement comes as many backbenchers think a leadership challenge could happen after the May election results.
