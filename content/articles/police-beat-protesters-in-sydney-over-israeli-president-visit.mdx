Thousands protested in Sydney against Israeli President Isaac Herzog, demanding he be sent to an international court. Police blocked the planned march and used force, pepper spray and arrests. A man was punched several times, a 69‑year‑old woman broke four vertebrae, and several other protesters were injured or charged. The police commissioner apologized, while the state premier defended the officers and declined an independent inquiry.
