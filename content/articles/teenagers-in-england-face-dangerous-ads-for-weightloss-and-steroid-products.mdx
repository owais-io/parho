Researchers found that more than 40% of English teens have seen ads for prescription weight‑loss drugs, 27% for skin‑whitening creams, and 24% for steroids on social media, gaming, and apps. Many of these products are illegal for people under 18. The study says these ads hurt kids' self‑esteem and can cause health problems. Experts call for stricter rules, a ban on ads to under‑16s, and tighter enforcement of age limits.
