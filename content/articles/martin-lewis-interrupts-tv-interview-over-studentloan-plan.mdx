Martin Lewis, a personal‑finance adviser, barged onto a Good Morning Britain set to challenge the Conservative student‑loan plan. He argued that cutting interest rates would only help those who earn a lot and urged instead a higher repayment threshold. Kemi Badenoch defended the plan, saying it would benefit all. The incident highlighted growing criticism that the current student‑loan system unfairly hurts middle‑income graduates.
