Radio host Kyle Sandilands has issued an apology to his long‑time co‑host Jackie ‘O’ Henderson after their popular breakfast show was suddenly taken off the air. He says he was told by the network to stay quiet while they terminated Henderson’s contract, which she says she never resigned from. Sandilands claims he has not breached his contract and wants the show back on air.
