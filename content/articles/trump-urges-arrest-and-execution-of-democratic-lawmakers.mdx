Trump posted on Truth Social saying Democrats should be arrested and even killed for what he called “seditious behavior.” He repeated the threat that they should be punished by death. The remarks came after the lawmakers posted a video encouraging military personnel to refuse illegal orders. Democratic leaders condemned the posts, calling them dangerous and demanding Trump remove them. The White House later said Trump didn’t actually want executions.
