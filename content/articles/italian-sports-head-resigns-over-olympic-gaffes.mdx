Paolo Petrecca, the head of Rai’s sports department, stepped down after a string of embarrassing blunders during the Winter Olympics opening ceremony. He mis‑named the venue, mixed up celebrities, and made off‑hand comments about athletes from Spain and China. Journalists protested his mistakes, dropping their bylines and threatening a strike. Critics say his appointment reflected political favoritism, and his resignation signals the broadcaster’s growing use as a government mouthpiece.
