Argentina’s coach Felipe Contepomi blasted England’s Tom Curry after the World Cup match, saying Curry performed a reckless tackle on Juan Cruz Mallía that broke his knee and later shoved Contepomi in the tunnel. Mallía had to leave with an ACL injury, forcing Argentina to finish with 14 men. Contepomi called the behaviour bullying, while England’s manager Steve Borthwick defended Curry’s character. The incident sparked debate about on‑field conduct and referee oversight.
