Jena Lisa Jones, who was abused by Jeffrey Epstein when she was 14, voted for Donald Trump in 2024 because he promised to release Epstein’s files. After Trump won, the Justice Department didn’t hand over the documents, and the survivor now worries that the justice she sought will never arrive. She continues to speak out, despite threats, and urges lawmakers to fully release the records.
