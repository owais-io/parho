On Sunday, President Trump spoke at a White House holiday reception to honor two people killed and nine wounded in a shooting at Brown University. He acknowledged the victims and said tragedies can happen. The attack brought the U.S.’s mass‑shooting count to at least 389 this year. Lawmakers are debating whether the federal government should add stricter gun‑control laws. Police say a person of interest, in their 20s, is in custody, but they haven’t confirmed any link to the university.
