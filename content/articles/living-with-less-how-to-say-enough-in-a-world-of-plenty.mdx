Jodi Wilson talks about selling most of her belongings, living in a caravan with her kids, and then buying a small house in Tasmania. She explains “enoughness” – choosing what you really need and letting go of the rest. The article shows how buying new things gives a quick dopamine hit but adds mental clutter. Wilson suggests simple habits like reusing clothes, buying second‑hand, eating what’s in the fridge, and taking breaks to keep life simple and peaceful.
