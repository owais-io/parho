Scotland won a match at Hampden Park that earned them a spot in the World Cup finals for the first time in 28 years. Fans erupted in joy, and players celebrated with dramatic goals from Kenny McLean and Lawrence Shankland. Coach Steve Clarke laughed and promised a relaxing break before the tournament. Captain Andy Robertson was emotional, remembering a missing teammate. The win shows Scotland’s hope and excitement for the big stage.
