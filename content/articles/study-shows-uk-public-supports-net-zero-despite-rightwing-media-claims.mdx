Researchers found that most Britons actually back net‑zero plans, but right‑wing media paint a picture of a massive public backlash. The study shows that MPs think voters are against clean‑energy projects, yet polls say about 40 % support net zero, while only 24 % oppose. Media coverage is twice as negative as public attitudes, creating a false sense that the climate agenda is unpopular. Policymakers should focus on making clean energy simple and affordable.
