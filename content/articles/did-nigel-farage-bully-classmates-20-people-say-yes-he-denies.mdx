Nigel Farage, the leader of Reform UK, denies ever saying racist or antisemitic remarks while he was a teen. Yet 20 former classmates from Dulwich College claim he shouted Nazi slogans, called others “Paki”, “Wog”, “Jude”, and even sang a “gas them all” chant. Teachers witnessed some of the abuse. The Guardian reports these accounts, but Farage’s spokesperson says the allegations are a smear with no evidence. The truth remains contested.
