Russian drones and missiles hit Ukraine, killing two people and setting a hospital on fire. The attacks happened before a Paris meeting where Ukraine’s allies will try to negotiate a peace plan. Ukraine’s leaders say the strikes caused power cuts and a freezing night, forcing people to leave homes. Ukrainian officials also mentioned other attacks on the city of Kharkiv and a U.S. business. Meanwhile, Ukrainian President Zelenskyy is reshuffling his government.
