In Minneapolis, U.S. Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem first denied that ICE agents used pepper spray on protestors. After a judge ordered the agents stop using chemical irritants, and after seeing video footage, Noem reversed her statement and said the department only used chemicals when there was violence. Tensions rose as protesters, some blocked a church, faced federal investigations and accusations of civil‑rights violations.
