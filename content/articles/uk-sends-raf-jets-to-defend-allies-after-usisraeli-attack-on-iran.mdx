In response to a U.S. and Israeli bombing campaign against Iran, the UK says its RAF fighters are on standby in the Middle East. While Britain did not join the strikes, jets are positioned from Qatar and Cyprus to shoot down drones and missiles that might hit allied bases. The UK also warns citizens in Gulf countries to stay safe and says it wants to avoid a wider conflict, urging negotiations.
