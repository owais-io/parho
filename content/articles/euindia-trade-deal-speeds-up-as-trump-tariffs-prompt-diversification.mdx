The European Union and India are moving fast to finalize a large trade agreement after Trump’s U.S. tariffs pushed Europe to look elsewhere. The deal will cut Indian duties on EU cars from 110% to 10%, lower taxes on machinery, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals, and open India’s huge market to more European goods. It is seen as a major boost for EU jobs and a sign Europe is diversifying its trade partners.
