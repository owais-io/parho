Casey Wasserman, the powerful LA entertainment mogul who runs the 2028 Olympic Games, is in hot water after emails with Ghislaine Maxwell, Jeffrey Epstein’s co‑convict, surfaced. The 23‑year‑old, sexual message exchange has led top artists to break ties with his agency and pushed city officials to call for his resignation. Wasserman says the chats happened long before Maxwell’s crimes were known, but critics say his past links hurt his credibility and the Games’ safety.
