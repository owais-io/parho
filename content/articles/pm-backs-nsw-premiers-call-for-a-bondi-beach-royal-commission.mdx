Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said he will back New South Wales Premier Chris Minns’ request for a state‑level royal commission to investigate the Bondi beach shooting that killed 15 people. Minns wants an independent inquiry alongside criminal proceedings. Albanese also pledged support for any action the state takes and will attend a memorial. The opposition wants a national commission, while the state plans to crack down on hate speech and symbols.
