Hollywood actors Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney, who own Wrexham AFC, sold a share of the club to Apollo Sports Capital, a U.S. private‑equity firm, just weeks after the club received £14 m in government grants. The deal will help pay for upgrades to the Racecourse Ground stadium and may boost the club’s valuation to as much as £350 m. Reynolds and McElhenney will still own most of the club.
