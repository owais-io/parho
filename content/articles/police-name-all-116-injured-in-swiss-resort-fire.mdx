A fire in the Le Constellation bar in Crans‑Montana, Switzerland, on New Year’s Eve left 40 people dead and 116 injured. Police have now identified all the wounded and the deceased, most of whom were under 20 years old. The blaze started in the basement when sparklers near the ceiling caught fire. Authorities are investigating the bar owners for negligence, and foreign victims’ bodies are being sent home. The town will hold a memorial.
