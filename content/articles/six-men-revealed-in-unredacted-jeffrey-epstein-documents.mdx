Congressman Ro Khanna told Congress that six men, including billionaire Leslie Wexner and Dubai businessman Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem, have been named in the previously redacted Jeffrey Epstein files. Khanna said the names came out after a brief review with fellow lawmaker Thomas Massie, and that none of the men are charged with a crime. Most of the files remain hidden, and the revelations do not prove guilt.
