Tommy Freeman, a talented Northampton Saints player, says the key to his success is simply playing rugby without overthinking. Now 24, he has grown from an uncertain international rookie to a key England back, starting all three British & Irish Lions Tests in Australia. Freeman talks about his Six Nations preparation, his love for club‑style play, and how he stays relaxed even under big pressure. He stresses that instinct and focus beat constant analysis on the field.
