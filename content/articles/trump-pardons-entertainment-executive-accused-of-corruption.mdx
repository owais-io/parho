Donald Trump quietly gave a pardon to Tim Leiweke, a sports and entertainment executive who was accused of rigging a university arena contract in Texas. Leiweke had been indicted and pleaded not guilty, with a trial scheduled for next year. Trump’s decision was the fifth pardon he granted this week, and officials from the Justice Department didn’t explain why the case was dropped. The justice department said Leiweke’s actions hurt competition and taxpayers.
