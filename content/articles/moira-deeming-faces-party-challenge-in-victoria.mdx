Victorian Liberal upper‑house members Moira Deeming and Ann‑Marie Hermans are up for party pre‑selection. In Deeming’s western seat, former journalist Dinesh Gourisetty is expected to run against her. Hermans in the south‑east may face candidates like Nathan Conroy or Manju Hanumantharayappa. Nominations close Jan. 14, with voting in March before the Nov. election. Both MPs say they will fight the challenges, while supporters point to Deeming’s recent legal win and her strong grassroots backing.
