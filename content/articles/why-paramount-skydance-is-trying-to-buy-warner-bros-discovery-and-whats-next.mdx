Netflix just said it will buy Warner Bros Discovery’s studio and HBO for $82.7 billion, but Paramount Skydance has launched a higher $108.4 billion hostile bid to take over the whole company – everything from movies to CNN. Paramount’s offer pays more per share and covers the whole business, while Netflix only wants the studio and streaming arm. The deal faces regulatory scrutiny, political debate and a Jan. 8 deadline for Paramount’s proposal. Warner’s board will decide which offer to accept.
