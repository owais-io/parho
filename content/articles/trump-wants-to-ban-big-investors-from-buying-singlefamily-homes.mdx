Trump says his administration will stop big institutional investors from buying single‑family homes to help bring down prices and make housing more affordable. He plans to ask Congress to make it law and will discuss more housing plans at the Davos World Economic Forum. Trump blames high inflation, saying it keeps home costs out of reach, especially for younger buyers, and faults former President Biden for the price rise.
