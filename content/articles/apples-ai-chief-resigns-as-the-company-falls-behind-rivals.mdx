Apple announced that its AI leader, John Giannandrea, will leave the company after seven years. He is being replaced by Amar Subramanya, a former Microsoft and Google AI executive. Apple has been slow to launch new AI features, especially the upgraded Siri voice assistant, which has been postponed multiple times. The company hopes Subramanya’s experience will help speed up its AI plans and close the gap with rivals like Google.
