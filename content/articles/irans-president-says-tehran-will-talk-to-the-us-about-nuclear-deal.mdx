Iran’s president told his foreign minister to push for fair talks with the United States about the nuclear program, just as U.S. forces gather in the region for a possible strike. Trump claims talks are happening, while officials plan meetings in Istanbul with Iranian, Arab and Muslim delegates. Meanwhile, Iran warns it could attack Israel and fears a regime‑change strike could spark a wider war.
