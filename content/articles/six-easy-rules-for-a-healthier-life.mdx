Dr. Ezekiel Emanuel says the health craze is too complicated. In his book, Eat Your Ice Cream, he offers six simple rules. Instead of extreme workouts, just get regular movement and keep it consistent. Eat meals with friends, limit screens before bed, and turn hobbies into part of a healthy life. Stop stressing about processed foods and rigid restrictions; choose real foods and enjoy treats in moderation. Keep health habits simple and joyful, not a full‑time job.
