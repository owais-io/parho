U.S. President Donald Trump publicly mocked Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro, claiming Maduro copies his signature dance moves. Trump made the comment after the U.S. raid that seized Maduro and his wife. He also accused Maduro of violence and torture. Maduro, who faces U.S. charges, has been a controversial figure in Venezuela. Trump’s remarks added to Maduro’s humiliation as he faces legal proceedings in New York.
