Three top jobs at Number 10 are opening up, and the UK’s new Prime Minister, Keir Starmer, is picking the people who will shape his government. His chief of staff, Morgan McSweeney, and his director of communications, Tim Allan, are leaving, and a new cabinet secretary will replace Chris Wormald. Candidates include Antonia Romeo and Minouche Shafik, while MPs want a united team that follows Starmer’s vision.
