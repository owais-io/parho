Jersey, a small tax‑haven island, let Russian billionaire Roman Abramovich move his wealth there before Russia invaded Ukraine. The island gave him residency and let companies shift $7 billion in 2017 and 2021. Britain checked him but didn’t block it. After the war started, Jersey froze the assets and launched a money‑laundering probe. Abramovich fought the freeze in court, but the courts rejected his appeals, and the case is still ongoing.
