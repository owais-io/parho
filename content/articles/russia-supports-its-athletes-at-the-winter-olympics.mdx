Russia’s athletes are back in the spotlight at the Winter Games in Italy after years of competing as neutrals. 18‑year‑old figure skater Adeliia Petrosian, in fifth place, could win a medal. Russian officials say the country now supports its Olympians, with press secretary Dmitry Peskov and sports minister Mikhail Degtyarev pledging to return the Russian flag and anthem to the Games. Some athletes still complain about neutral status, but officials view the change as national pride.
