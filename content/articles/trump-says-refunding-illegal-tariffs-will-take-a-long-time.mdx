The Supreme Court recently struck down many U.S. tariffs, leading companies like FedEx to demand refunds. President Trump said refunding these illegal tariffs will take a long time, saying it could cost hundreds of billions. The Justice Department said it’s not asking the Court to rehear the case. Meanwhile, a temporary 10% tariff on many imports will last 150 days.
