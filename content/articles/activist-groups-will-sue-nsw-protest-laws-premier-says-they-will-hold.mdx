New South Wales Premier Chris Minns says he’s confident new protest‑ban laws will stand up in court, but three activist groups – the Palestine Action Group, Jews Against the Occupation 48 and the Blak Caucus – plan to sue. The laws could stop protests for up to three months and let police move them. The groups argue the rules infringe free speech, and they’re backed by other civil‑rights and climate groups. Minns says the laws have been fully vetted.
