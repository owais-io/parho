A secretly recorded call shows the U.S. envoy to Ukraine, Steve Witkoff, giving Russia advice on how to handle Donald Trump and on peace plans that favor Russian gains. Many U.S. representatives, especially Republicans, are furious and want Witkoff fired, calling him a traitor. Trump defended Witkoff, saying he’s just negotiating, while the leak has stirred debate over U.S. officials possibly helping an adversary.
