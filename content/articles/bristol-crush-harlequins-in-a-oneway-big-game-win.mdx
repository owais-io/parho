In the Big Game at Twickenham, Bristol ran the ball and scored six tries to beat Harlequins by a huge margin. Their fast, precise play left Harlequins scrambling, and the home side never found the back of the net. Bristol’s forward line opened gaps, while Harlequins’ passes slipped and the defense kept them shut out. The win shows why Bristol is a team to watch in the Premiership.
