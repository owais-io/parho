Everton were hit with two red cards – Michael Keane for pulling a defender’s hair and Jack Grealish for dissent – as Wolves levelled the score with an 18‑year‑old Mané goal. The game finished 1‑1. Wolves’ manager Rob Edwards praised his side’s second‑half comeback, while Everton boss David Moyes blasted the referee’s decision. Despite playing with nine men in the final minutes, Wolves stayed unbeaten for the third time.
