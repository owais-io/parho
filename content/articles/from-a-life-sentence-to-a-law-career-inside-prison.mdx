Ruth Kamande, a 30‑year‑old woman serving a life sentence in Nairobi’s Lang’ata maximum‑security women's prison, turned a death penalty into a legal career from inside the walls. After a 2018 murder conviction, she studied law through a prison‑based program, graduating top of her class in 2024. Now she helps other inmates win cases, drafts legal arguments, and pushes for prison reform, using her knowledge to protect prisoners’ rights and seek parole.
