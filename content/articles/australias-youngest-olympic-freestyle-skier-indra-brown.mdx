Indra Brown, 16, is Australia’s youngest freestyle skier heading to the Winter Olympics in Milan. She won three World Cup medals in her first season and is ranked No. 1 in the women’s World Cup. Brown loves the feeling of flying in the halfpipe and wants to do her best, not chase a medal. Her family, especially her mother and younger brother, support her journey ahead of the Games.
