Vancouver Rise clinched the inaugural Northern Super League title, beating AFC Toronto 2-1 at BMO Field in front of 12,429 fans. The season proved a success for Canada’s first pro women’s league, built by former internationals and offering high wages (average C$75,000). It attracted 275,000 tickets, made C$30m in revenue, and drew millions of viewers. The league plans to add teams and aim for international competitions.
