Jilly Cooper’s memorial last week was a unique tribute, featuring five horses that stood in a field as a symbol of her love for animals. Royal guests, including the queen, attended, and actor Rupert Everett sang “I Vow to Thee, My Country” in the cathedral. The service also recalled her habit of sending Valentine cards, reminding attendees of her personal touch and the warmth she brought to those around her.
