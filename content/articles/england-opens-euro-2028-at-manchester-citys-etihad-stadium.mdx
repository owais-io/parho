England’s first Euro 2028 game will be at Manchester’s Etihad Stadium on 10 June, if they qualify. They’ll then play two group matches at Wembley. If they finish first in Group B, the last‑16 game is at Newcastle’s St James’ Park; if second, at Everton’s Hill Dixon. The tournament uses nine venues across the UK and Ireland and will sell a record 3 million tickets.
