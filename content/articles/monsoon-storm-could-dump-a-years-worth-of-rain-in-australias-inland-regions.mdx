Scientists warn that a lingering monsoon low could pour the amount of rain Australia gets in a year into parts of New South Wales, Queensland and South Australia in just a few days. Heavy, locally intense showers are expected in remote towns like Marree and Birdsville, threatening flash floods and road closures. While the rain will fill dams and water tanks, it also risks damaging tracks and isolating communities.
