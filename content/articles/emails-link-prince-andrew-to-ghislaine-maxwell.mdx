New emails released by the US Justice Department show that a person who signs off as “The Invisible Man” – probably Prince Andrew – wrote to Ghislaine Maxwell in 2001‑2002. The letters talk about Balmoral, a lost valet, and plans for a trip to Peru. The messages hint that Andrew was looking for “inappropriate friends” and that he was involved in the same circles as Epstein. He denies any wrongdoing.
