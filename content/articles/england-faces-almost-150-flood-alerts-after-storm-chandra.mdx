England faces almost 150 flood alerts after Storm Chandra. The Met Office warns of heavy rain that could cause road, home and business flooding, power cuts and travel delays. 147 homes in Somerset and Dorset are already flooded and a major incident is underway. Local councils urge residents to stay safe, use help services and follow advice on when it is safe to return.
