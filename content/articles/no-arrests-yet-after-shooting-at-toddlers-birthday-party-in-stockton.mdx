A mass shooting at a toddler’s birthday party in Stockton, California, killed four people and hurt 17 others. Police have not arrested anyone and no suspects have been named. Investigators are still gathering evidence—over 50 shell casings and about five guns were found—and it could take months to learn who did it or why. City leaders are demanding answers and more support for victims while pointing out gaps in violence‑prevention funding.
