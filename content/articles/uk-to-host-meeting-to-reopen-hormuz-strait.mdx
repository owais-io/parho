Britain has offered to host a security meeting in London or Portsmouth to plan reopening the Hormuz Strait, a key oil route shut after Iran threatened attacks. More than 30 countries, including the UAE, France, Germany and Canada, have agreed to work together. The summit will bring leaders together to discuss minesweeping drones and how to keep ships safe. Britain urges a quick end to the conflict to stop rising oil and gas costs hurting consumers.
