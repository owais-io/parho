Coach Mauricio Pochettino is still looking for a new star striker for the US men’s national team before the World Cup. With the squad deadline coming up, five players are in the running: Arsenal‑trained Balogun, PSV’s Pepi, Derby’s Agyemang, Coventry’s Wright and Norwich’s Sargent. Each has had recent injuries or club struggles, but the coach will need to decide quickly so the team can lock in its final lineup.
