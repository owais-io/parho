On February 23, a snowstorm turned Washington Square Park into a snowball arena for teens. When the balls became large, hard chunks, officers were struck and injured. Police unions decried the attack and called for arrests. Mayor Zohran Mamdani stayed calm, calling it just a “kids throwing snowballs” joke. His relaxed tone clashes with a hard‑line NYPD, sparking a fresh mayor‑police feud.
