A Manchester toddler named Jude Owens became the youngest person to hit two trick shots in snooker, setting two Guinness World Records. He did a pool bank shot at 2 years, 302 days and a double pot a few weeks earlier at 2 years, 261 days. Jude’s dad noticed his talent early and helps him reach the table with a stool. He’s proud of his boy’s skill and ambition.
