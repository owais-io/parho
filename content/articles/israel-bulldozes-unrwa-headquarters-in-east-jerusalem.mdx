Israeli forces destroyed the UN agency for Palestinian refugees (UNRWA) headquarters in East Jerusalem and fired tear gas at a UN vocational school in the West Bank. Israel says UNRWA helps Hamas, a claim the agency denies. The move follows a law that banned UNRWA from operating in Israel. UNRWA officials say the demolition violates international law and harms aid to millions of refugees. The attack is part of a wider crackdown on humanitarian groups.
