Aisha Chughtai, a Minneapolis city councilor, says the city’s biggest threat is ICE after federal agents shot nurse Alex Pretti and killed another resident. She describes tear‑gas, arrests, and fear in her neighborhood, which has a large immigrant community. Chughtai calls on state and local officials to hold the agents accountable, declare an emergency, and stop evictions so people can feel safe at home. She urges residents to keep standing up in the streets.
