Sean Dyche took over Forest when they were 18th with only five points. Since then they’re 5th in the Premier League, earning 13 points from eight games. He fixed the chaotic defence, kept clean sheets, and made the team fitter and more aggressive. Dyche used a 4‑2‑3‑1 to give wingers a role and built teamwork and confidence. His clear plans and good communication have lifted the club far from relegation danger.
