Liverpool won 5‑1 at Anfield against Newcastle, after a slow start. The visitors took the early lead when Anthony Gordon scored, but Liverpool rallied. Hugo Ekitiké netted two goals, Florian Wirtz added one, and Ibrahima Konaté sealed the match. The result shows Liverpool’s growing consistency under coach Arne Slot, while Newcastle’s fast counter‑attacks couldn’t keep up.
