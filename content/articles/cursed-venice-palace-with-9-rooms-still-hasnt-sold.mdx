The Ca’ Dario palace on Venice’s Grand Canal has nine bedrooms, eight bathrooms and a history of strange deaths. The building is listed for €20m (£17.4m) and has struggled to find a buyer, partly because of its reputation as a cursed place. Christie’s and Engel & Völkers are now trying to sell it again after a fresh renovation, but local legends of murder, suicide and misfortune still scare potential buyers.
