British police have stepped up security around synagogues and Jewish venues after a 12‑dead antisemitic attack on Bondi Beach in Australia. In London and Scotland patrols are increased for Hanukkah celebrations, though officials say there is no direct threat. The Prime Minister, King Charles and community leaders all condemned the violence and pledged support. Jewish groups say more must be done to tackle rising hate and protect their members.
