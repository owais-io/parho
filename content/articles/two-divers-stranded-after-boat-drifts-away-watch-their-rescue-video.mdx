Two divers were out near Mindarie, north of Perth, when their boat slipped from the anchor and drifted away. They surfaced to find no boat, feared it had sunk, and swam toward a distant boat. After about an hour, rescuers found them and their missing boat 2 km out. The divers returned, fixed the anchor, and went back to dive. He shared the footage to remind people to double‑check anchoring and always have someone on board.
