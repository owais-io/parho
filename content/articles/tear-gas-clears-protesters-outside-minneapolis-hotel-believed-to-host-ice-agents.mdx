On Sunday night in Minneapolis, a crowd gathered outside a Home2 Suites hotel, believing ICE agents were staying there. Protesters shouted, sprayed graffiti, and tried to break windows. Minneapolis police and state officers surrounded the crowd. To clear the area, officers released tear gas. The incident follows two earlier killings of Minnesota residents by federal agents. The governor has asked the White House to pull the agents out of the city.
