Judge Sunshine Sykes in California says the Trump White House is terrorizing immigrants and breaking the law. She said the administration denied many people the chance for bond hearings and kept them in jail illegally. The judge ordered DHS to give detainees notice of bond rights and a phone to call a lawyer. She also pointed out the government’s violence against its own citizens, citing two American deaths in Minnesota. The White House blamed the courts.
