The special primary in Georgia’s 14th district pits Trump‑backed Republican Clay Fuller against Democrat‑turned‑cattle‑owner Shawn Harris. It could show whether Trump’s influence can keep the seat Republican or give Democrats a foothold in a deep‑red area. Fuller's campaign raised over a million dollars, while Harris has out‑raised him more than four times. The district is rated R+19, but Democrats have done well in recent years. The top two will head to a runoff on April 7.
