Australia’s new private firm, Secure Journeys, took over immigration detention centres in 2025. Workers and the union say the company keeps too few staff, making the facilities unsafe. The number of serious incidents has risen, and staff morale is low. The government is aware and has asked the company to fix staffing. Critics argue the private prison model risks safety for both workers and detainees.
