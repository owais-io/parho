California state leaders and police have said there's no real danger from Iran after the FBI sent a warning about a possible drone attack. The FBI’s message was based on unverified info. The White House and FBI later confirmed the threat was not credible. Los Angeles officials raised security for events like the Oscars, but said no specific danger to the city exists. California is staying prepared.
