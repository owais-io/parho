Cheshire police said they were right after former MP David Davis criticised the Letby investigation. Davis urged the Crown Prosecution Service to review the case, saying the force focused too much on Letby and ignored lessons from past miscarriages of justice. Police called the critics spreading misinformation. Letby was convicted of killing seven babies, but many experts now say she was wrongfully convicted, and her lawyer has asked the Criminal Cases Review Commission to send the case back to court.
