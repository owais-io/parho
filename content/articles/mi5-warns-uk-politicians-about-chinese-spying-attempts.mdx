MI5 has warned UK MPs and peers that two people linked to Chinese spies are using LinkedIn to try and recruit politicians and others for secret information. They present themselves as headhunters and offer consulting jobs, hoping to get inside knowledge. The alert was sent to the Speaker and Lords leaders, who shared it with everyone. Some MPs already cut ties with the recruiters. The move shows how serious Britain is about foreign interference.
