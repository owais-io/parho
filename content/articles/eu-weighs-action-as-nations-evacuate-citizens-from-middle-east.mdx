The European Union held an emergency online meeting of its foreign ministers to discuss the growing crisis in the Middle East. Top diplomat Kaja Kallas warned that violence could spread and stressed keeping the Strait of Hormuz open. Meanwhile, many EU countries are preparing to evacuate thousands of their citizens who are stuck in or near the conflict zone. The EU may coordinate these evacuation efforts and keep an eye on the situation.
