Arsenal beat Brighton 2‑1 at the Emirates. Ødegaard opened the score with a clinical finish, and a Rutter own goal gave a second. Brighton pushed back but their only chance was saved by David Raya. Despite injuries and a tense second half, Arteta said his side handled the pressure and is back on top of the Premier League table. The win keeps Arsenal in the hunt for the title.
