Starting Monday, Australian adults will need to prove they’re over 18 to visit porn sites, search for adult content, download 18+ apps, or use certain AI chatbots. The new rules let companies pick methods such as photo IDs, credit‑card checks, or parental confirmation, but they must keep personal data to a minimum. If sites don’t comply, they could face fines up to $49.5 million.
