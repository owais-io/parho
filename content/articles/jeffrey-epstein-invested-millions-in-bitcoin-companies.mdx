Documents from the U.S. Justice Department show former financier Jeffrey Epstein helped fund Bitcoin’s early days, putting $3 million into Coinbase and sending money to Blockstream. He also donated to MIT’s Digital Currency Initiative, which helped build Bitcoin technology. Crypto firms say the ties mean little, and investors call him a “skeptical” backer who left early. Most say the revelations won’t change how people use crypto.
