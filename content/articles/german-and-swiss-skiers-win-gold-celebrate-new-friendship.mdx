During the 14th day of the Milano‑Cortina Winter Games, German skier Daniela Maier and Swiss rival Fanny Smith turned a 2022 medal fight into a friendship and a gold‑silver finish. Maier took her first Olympic gold in ski cross, while Smith added a silver. The article also notes China’s Wang Xindi’s narrow aerials win, Norway’s record 17 golds, and a controversy over IOC member Kirsty Coventry’s reaction to FIFA’s Infantino at a Trump‑hosted peace meeting.
