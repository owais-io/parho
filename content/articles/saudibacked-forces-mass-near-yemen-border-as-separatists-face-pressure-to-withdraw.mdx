Saudi‑backed troops, up to 20,000, are massing on the Yemen‑Saudi border as Yemen's Southern Transitional Council (STC) pushes to pull back after taking control of big oil‑rich areas. The STC, backed by the UAE, wants a split into north and south. Saudi Arabia warns of possible airstrikes. The UN says renewed fighting could spread to the Red Sea and beyond. Yemen has lost millions of people in the civil war.
