Students and activists across the U.S. are calling for a national strike on Friday to protest ICE’s aggressive enforcement and recent shootings of residents. They plan to shut down work, schools and shopping to pressure the Trump‑era agency into reform. The protest follows a Minnesota shutdown last week and involves student groups, unions, and celebrities. Many businesses, including Target, have closed, and supporters are gathering in city halls, courts and on the streets.
