The Strait of Hormuz, a key route for oil and fertilizer, has almost stopped trading because of fighting between Iran and Israel. Only a few ships are still crossing, many are waiting in port. Iran has offered a “safe corridor” that ships can use if they pay, but safety is still uncertain. Nations like the UK, UAE and others are working to open the waterway again, but it may take months.
