UK investors are nervous about artificial intelligence tools that could replace human advice. Shares of wealth‑management companies like St James’s Place, Quilter, and AJ Bell fell 5‑13% after Altruist Corp announced a tax‑planning AI. Price‑comparison sites MoneySuperMarket and Go Compare also slid, as new chat‑bot services from Insurify and others promise instant insurance quotes. The downturn shows how AI is reshaping finance, insurance and comparison‑portal businesses.
