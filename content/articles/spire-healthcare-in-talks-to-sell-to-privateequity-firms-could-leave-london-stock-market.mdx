Spire Healthcare, the UK's largest private hospital chain, is in early talks to sell to private‑equity firms Bridgepoint and Triton. If sold, it could leave the London Stock Exchange. Shares jumped 18% after the news. The company runs 38 hospitals and 50+ clinics, and about 30% of its revenue comes from NHS contracts, which are under pressure. Investors say Spire’s shares are undervalued, while the firm is exploring ways to grow and help cut NHS waiting lists.
