Crystal Palace beat Shelbourne 2-0 in Dublin. Uche scored his first goal for the club and Eddie Nketiah added another. The English side, missing some key players, dominated the game and moved up in the Conference League standings. Coach Oliver Glasner said he wants more from Uche. Fans were calm, no clashes, and the match was played at Shamrock Rovers' stadium because Shelbourne's ground doesn’t meet UEFA rules. Palace look forward to next week.
