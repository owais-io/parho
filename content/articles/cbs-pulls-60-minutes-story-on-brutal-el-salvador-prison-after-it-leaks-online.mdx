CBS pulled a 60 Minutes segment that exposed torture and awful conditions in an El Salvador prison. The almost 14‑minute story was meant to air Sunday, but CBS said it needed more reporting. The footage then appeared on a Canadian streaming app and was shared on social media, prompting criticism from CBS staff and politicians. A senator posted the clip, and the incident adds to ongoing tension between 60 Minutes and the Trump administration.
