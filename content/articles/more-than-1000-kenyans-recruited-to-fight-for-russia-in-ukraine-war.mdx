Kenyan officials say more than 1,000 Kenyan men have been recruited to fight for Russia in the Ukraine war. The report says shady recruiters and some Kenyan government staff helped get visas and transport to Russia. The recruits were promised high pay and Russian citizenship. Many are now on the front line or held as prisoners of war. Russia denies any involvement. Kenya’s foreign minister will meet Russian officials soon.
