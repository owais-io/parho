Snapchat’s parent company, Snap Inc., reached a settlement in a California lawsuit that accuses it of causing social‑media addiction. The case also involves Meta, TikTok, and YouTube, but only Snap has settled. The judge said app design can harm users, not just the content. The settlement means Snap won’t face a jury trial on January 27, though the other firms still go to court. The lawsuit could lead to big changes for tech companies.
