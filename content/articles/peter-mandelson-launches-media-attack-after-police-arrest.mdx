Peter Mandelson was taken into police custody in London over alleged misconduct. He immediately launched a media attack, hired a defamation lawyer, and told friends he was a victim of a political plot. The police say they were acting on a tip, while Mandelson claims no evidence and that he was unfairly targeted. Experts say his aggressive PR may hurt his reputation and alienate the public.
