Victoria hit record‑setting heat, with some towns reaching almost 49°C—higher than the 2009 Black Saturday record. The hot spell has trapped extreme temperatures across the north‑west, while the state fires have spread, burning at emergency level. Fire crews battle several major blazes, and officials urge people to stay inside, limit travel, and watch for heat‑stroke symptoms. The heatwave is expected to last several more days.
