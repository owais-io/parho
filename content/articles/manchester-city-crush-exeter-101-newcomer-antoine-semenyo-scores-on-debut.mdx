In a one‑sided FA Cup match, Manchester City demolished League One side Exeter City 10‑1. The game began with a quick 17‑year‑old right‑back Ryan McAidoo scoring, and the new £62.5m striker Antoine Semenyo hit his first goal for City in the 52nd minute, before being substituted in a wave of applause. Three more debutants – Rico Lewis, Nico O’Reilly, and Tijjani Reijnders – added goals, while City’s midfield dominated the match. Exeter’s lone reply came from a 19‑year‑old George Birch.
