Fulton County District Attorney Fani Willis took the floor at a Georgia Senate committee to explain why she prosecuted former President Donald Trump. Senators questioned her romance with special prosecutor Nathan Wade, asking if they got money from the relationship and if they worked with the Jan‑6 congressional committee. Willis shot back that the questions were unfair and that she would not quit. The committee can change law but can’t punish her.
