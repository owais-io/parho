Nana Darkoa Sekyiamah writes about how African customs can help people feel free about sex today. In her book she talks to a Tanzanian sex teacher, Auntie Zai, who runs kitchen parties that give women advice on seduction, marriage and body care. She also shares her own trip to Ghana, where she saw a girls’ initiation called dipo that teaches community and body confidence. The author wants new rites that help girls learn about their bodies, pleasure and identity.
