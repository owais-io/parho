After Jeffrey Epstein was freed from house arrest in 2010, Prince Andrew sent him several emails offering to meet at Buckingham Palace. The messages show Andrew proposing dinner and privacy, and even introducing Epstein to a Russian woman he liked. These emails were released by the U.S. Justice Department, and they have fueled the loss of Andrew’s royal titles and the ongoing scrutiny of his ties to Epstein.
