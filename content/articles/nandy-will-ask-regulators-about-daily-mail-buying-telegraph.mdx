UK Culture Secretary Lisa Nandy said she will ask Ofcom and the Competition and Markets Authority to investigate a £500m takeover of the Telegraph by Daily Mail owner DMGT. The deal would merge several right‑leaning newspapers, raising concerns about media diversity. Nandy hasn’t decided yet but will issue a public‑interest intervention notice that starts a 40‑day probe into whether the merger reduces press ownership plurality.
