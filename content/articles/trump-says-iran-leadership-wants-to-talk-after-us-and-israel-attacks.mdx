Trump said the US and Israel have launched attacks on Iran, killing dozens of leaders, and that Iran now wants to talk. He calls the strikes a “four‑week” campaign that is already ahead of schedule. Trump said the strikes were justified to stop Iran’s long‑standing violence and that the Iranian government should have negotiated earlier. He said he’s open to talks, but remains uncertain about when they will happen.
