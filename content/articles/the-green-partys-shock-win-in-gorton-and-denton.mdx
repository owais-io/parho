In the south‑east Manchester seat of Gorton and Denton, Green candidate Hannah Spencer won by a huge margin, taking a seat that had been a Labour stronghold for decades. The victory shows how the Greens’ focus on the cost‑of‑living crisis, a relatable local candidate and a massive volunteer push can flip even safe seats. The win also highlights voters’ anger at Labour and Reform and a desire for hope instead of fear.
