A 60‑year‑old British tourist is one of 20 people charged in Dubai for posting a video of an Iranian missile attack on the UAE. UAE cybercrime law says sharing such footage can disturb public security, and the law can hit anyone who reposts or comments on it. The tourist deleted the clip after police asked, but still faces up to two years in jail, a fine, and deportation. The case shows how strict UAE rules now limit sharing footage online.
