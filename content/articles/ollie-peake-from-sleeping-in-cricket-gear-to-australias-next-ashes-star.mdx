Ollie Peake, now 19, is Australia's new cricket hope. He grew up sleeping in his gear, and his father was a former captain. He’s captain of the U‑19 team, already played in the Sheffield Shield and helped Australia win a World Cup. With an unbeaten run in Namibia and Zimbabwe, he’s eyed for the 2027 Ashes. Peake stays focused on his state team and long‑term goals.
