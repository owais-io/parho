Cabinet Office officials have been accused of hiding papers that show how the government handled Prince Andrew's travel expenses while he was a trade envoy. The files, which also cover the death of Princess Diana and a bungled telegram to the Queen Mother, were pulled from release as a “mistake,” the Office said. Critics say the move was driven by the royal palace and that no special “royal” exemption should exist.
