Honduran President Xiomara Castro says a coup is happening because the U.S., especially former President Trump, is trying to influence the vote. Trump has backed Nasry “Tito” Asfura, the top contender, and even pardoned a former Honduran president who is now free. Castro says the election is full of threats, manipulation and possible fraud, and she wants international bodies to look into it. Asfura leads with about 40 % of votes, while Salvador Nasralla is close behind.
