After a federal officer killed Renee Good, a local brass band called Brass Solidarity gathered at the site to play music, turning the area into a place of hope. The band, formed after George Floyd’s death, performs civil‑rights songs at protests against police and ICE actions. Their music gives community strength, invites all musicians, and spreads messages of love and resilience through powerful performances.
