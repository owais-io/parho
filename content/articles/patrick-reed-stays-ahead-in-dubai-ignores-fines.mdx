Patrick Reed, a former Masters champion, is leading the Dubai Desert Classic even though the DP World Tour fines him for playing on the rival LIV circuit. He joked that making money on the tour in 2026 will be tough, but he says he'll keep playing. After a 66 in the second round, he's nine under and one ahead of Andy Sullivan, and he recalls a past clash with Rory McIlroy in Dubai.
