In Sudan, two drone attacks on civilian sites killed 28 people, including an infant, and injured many more. One strike hit a market in Saraf Omra, North Darfur, setting a parked oil truck and part of the market on fire. Another hit a civilian truck on a highway in North Kordofan. It’s unclear whether the army or the RSF sent the drones, but the violence is part of a war that has displaced millions and spurred fears of famine.
