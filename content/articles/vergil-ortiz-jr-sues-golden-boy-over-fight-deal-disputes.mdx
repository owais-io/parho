Unbeaten boxer Vergil Ortiz Jr has filed a federal lawsuit against Golden Boy Promotions, saying the company broke their contract and blocked a major fight with Jaron Ennis. Ortiz claims he can quit the deal because Golden Boy’s broadcast deal with Dazn ended, but the promoter says negotiations are still ongoing. The suit seeks to confirm Ortiz’s release, damages, and a clear end to the partnership.
