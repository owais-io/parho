After winning the Nobel Peace Prize, María Corina Machado said Maduro will inevitably leave power, and that the transition should be peaceful, not a civil war. She says the US has put a bounty on him and is boosting pressure, while some Latin American leaders say they’ll help. Machado believes most of the police and army will follow the new government. Experts say the country is united and a sudden break is unlikely to cause chaos.
