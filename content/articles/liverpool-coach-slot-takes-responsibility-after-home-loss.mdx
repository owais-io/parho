Liverpool lost 3-0 to Nottingham Forest at Anfield, their sixth home defeat in seven games. Coach Arne Slot said he will look at himself and make changes, saying he won’t make excuses. He noted one goal can change the game and that his tactical tweaks didn’t help. Slot said the club’s quality means there is a way out of the slump, and he will keep adjusting to improve results.
