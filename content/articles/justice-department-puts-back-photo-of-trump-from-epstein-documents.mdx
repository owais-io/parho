The Justice Department had taken down a picture that showed Donald Trump from the released Jeffrey Epstein files because it might expose victims. After checking, officials said no victims were in the photo, so they reposted it unchanged. Critics said the removal was political. The move is part of a larger debate over how much of the Epstein documents should be public while protecting survivors.
