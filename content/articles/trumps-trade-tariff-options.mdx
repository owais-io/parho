Trump has put a 10% tariff on many imports but can use other tools. Section 122 lets him raise tariffs to 15% for 150 days without Congress. Section 301 allows unlimited tariffs for four years on unfair trade partners, but needs a formal probe. Section 201 lets him shield U.S. industry with tariffs or quotas if imports harm them. Section 232 targets sectors like steel and cars. The Supreme Court blocked a wide‑ranging emergency tariff, but Trump still has these options.
