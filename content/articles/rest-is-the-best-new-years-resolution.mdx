Most people think a busy year is good, but a culture that praises hard work makes us tired and less productive. Experts say the solution is rest – sleep, walks, quiet time, talking, creative hobbies, or just unplugging. They outline seven kinds of rest: physical, mental, emotional, social, sensory, creative, spiritual. Practical tips include scheduled breaks, setting boundaries, and getting support from friends and family. Rest helps us stay energized and more effective.
