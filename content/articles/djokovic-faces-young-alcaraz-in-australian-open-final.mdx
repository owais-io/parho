Novak Djokovic and 22‑year‑old Carlos Alcaraz are set to battle in the Australian Open final. Djokovic, 38, wants to keep his energy after long five‑set wins and hopes he can match Alcaraz’s speed and stamina. Alcaraz, who came back from a fifth‑set deficit to reach the final, also worries about recovery. Either player could win a historic milestone: Djokovic would break his own record for most titles, Alcaraz would become the youngest man to win all four majors.
