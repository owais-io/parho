Aston Martin will miss part of its three‑day pre‑season test in Barcelona. The team won’t run until Thursday, leaving drivers Fernando Alonso and Lance Stroll with only two days on the track. The delay may signal problems with their new car, the AMR26, designed by Adrian Newey. The test is a first‑season “shakedown” before the main races. Other teams have already started their laps.
