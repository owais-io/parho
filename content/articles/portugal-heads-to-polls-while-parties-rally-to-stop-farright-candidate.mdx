Portugal’s voters head to the polls on Sunday for a second‑round presidential vote. The moderate left‑wing candidate, António José Seguro, leads in polls and is backed by many conservative politicians who want to stop far‑right challenger André Ventura. Storm damage and a state of emergency have caused some to call for a delay, but the election will go ahead as scheduled. Experts say the united support may not change the outcome.
