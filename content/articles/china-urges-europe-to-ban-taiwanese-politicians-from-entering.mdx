China has asked European governments to stop Taiwanese politicians from entering the EU, saying their own border rules mean Taiwan officials could threaten China‑EU relations. Beijing sent written warnings to several countries after Taiwanese leaders visited Europe, saying such visits could cross a “red line.” EU states said visa rules are theirs and did not change them. Taiwan said the visits were normal and China had no right to intervene. Beijing is trying to keep Taiwan isolated.
