In the FA Cup fourth‑round draw, non‑league side Macclesfield will host Premier League team Brentford after beating Crystal Palace in the third round. The draw also pairs Aston Villa with Newcastle United and creates other notable ties, like Liverpool vs Barnsley and Wrexham vs Ipswich. Matches run from Feb. 13‑16, and the event shows the magic of giant‑killings and under‑dog stories in the cup.
