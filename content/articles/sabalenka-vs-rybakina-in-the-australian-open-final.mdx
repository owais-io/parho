Sabalenka, who has made six Australian Open finals in a row, will face Kazakh player Elena Rybakina this Saturday. Sabalenka has not dropped a set and is the top hard‑court player of her generation. Rybakina knocked out world No. 2 Iga Swiatek and No. 6 Jessica Pegula to reach the final. The match could decide whether Sabalenka becomes a legendary champion.
