Jeffrey Epstein, a known sexual‑offender, used emails to track the 2018 confirmation of Supreme Court nominee Brett Kavanaugh. He warned that Christine Blasey Ford’s accusations could derail the hearing and urged Republicans to oppose Ford. Epstein even criticized the choice of a female prosecutor and suggested a list of questions for her. While he had ties to former investigator Kenneth Starr, there’s no proof Kavanaugh knew him.
