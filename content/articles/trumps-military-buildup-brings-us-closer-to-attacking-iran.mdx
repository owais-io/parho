Trump’s military buildup in the Middle East has pushed the U.S. closer to a possible attack on Iran, raising oil prices and pressuring Tehran into talks. Iran wants to pause uranium enrichment for up to five years, while U.S. officials fear a deal could let Iran keep some nuclear and missile rights. Negotiations stall as Trump, his aides and the U.S. administration weigh the risks of war versus a new agreement.
