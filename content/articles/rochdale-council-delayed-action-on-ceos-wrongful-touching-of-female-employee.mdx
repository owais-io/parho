Rochdale Council faced criticism for waiting more than five weeks after learning about a disciplinary report that found its chief executive, James Binks, had inappropriately touched a junior female employee while at Manchester Council. The council only suspended Binks after a Guardian article exposed the issue. They received a redacted report on 24 October but only acted after the story was published. The MP praised the suspension and called for zero tolerance of such conduct.
