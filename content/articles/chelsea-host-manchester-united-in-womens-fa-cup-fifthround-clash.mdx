Chelsea will host Manchester United in the fifth round of the Women’s FA Cup, a rematch of last season’s final where Chelsea won 3‑0. The tie is part of a full weekend of all‑WSL fixtures, with Liverpool vs Everton, Brighton vs West Ham, and London City Lionesses vs Tottenham also in play. Lower‑league Chatham Town will face Birmingham City, while Oxford United hosts Charlton. Chelsea will look to defend their title, while United aim for a third straight final.
