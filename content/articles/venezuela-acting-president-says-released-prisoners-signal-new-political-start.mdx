Venezuela's acting president, Delcy Rodríguez, says freeing political prisoners shows the country is opening up for a new political moment and vows more releases. She accuses NGOs of lying and says only hate and violence will be stopped. Critics and human‑rights groups say the numbers are exaggerated and that people still face searches, arrests, and fear. The US and Trump have also weighed in on the situation.
