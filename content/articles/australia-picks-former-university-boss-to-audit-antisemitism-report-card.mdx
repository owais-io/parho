Australia’s antisemitism envoy Jillian Segal ran out of firms to pick for a university report card. She hand‑picked former university chief Greg Craven, a lawyer who has criticized Australian universities. He got a $232,466 contract until June 2027. Critics say he is biased because he has attacked universities before. The report will score campuses on how they handle antisemitism, protests and flags. The project has sparked controversy.
