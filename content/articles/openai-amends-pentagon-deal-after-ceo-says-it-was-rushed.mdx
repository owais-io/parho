OpenAI has changed a rushed deal with the Pentagon after its boss, Sam Altman, said the contract looked sloppy. The agreement, made after the US dropped Anthropic, raised worries that AI could help with domestic spying. Altman says OpenAI will forbid surveillance and weapon use, but critics say the deal was still risky. Employees from OpenAI and Google have signed a letter demanding the government back away from such projects.
