A letter by Prof Helen Colley Manchester urges the Guardian to show more stories of resistance instead of focusing mainly on bad news. She cites Antonio Gramsci’s idea that while we must recognise problems, we should stay hopeful. She argues mainstream media overload us with negativity, making people feel helpless, and that stories of resistance are often ignored. Highlighting these stories would give readers hope and encourage active resistance.
