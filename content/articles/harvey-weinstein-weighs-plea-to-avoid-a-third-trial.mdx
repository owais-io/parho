Harvey Weinstein, the former Hollywood mogul, is mulling a guilty plea in a New York case that could stop a third trial. He was already found guilty of raping one woman and cleared of assaulting another, with a third charge left undecided. Weinstein says jurors intimidated each other, but a judge said this didn’t affect the verdict. He’s in Rikers, says his health is failing, and his lawyer says he’s weighing the plea.
