A gunfight at a hostel near Pretoria left at least 11 people dead, including a 3‑year‑old child, and more than a dozen injured. Police say the attackers may have been connected to an illegal bar inside the building that sells homemade alcohol. They are now looking for three suspects. The incident adds to South Africa’s high murder rate, where many killings are linked to illegal drinking spots and organised crime.
