A French anesthetist known as “Dr Death” was sentenced to life in prison for poisoning 30 patients and killing 12 over nearly ten years. He worked in private clinics in Besançon, tampering with medicine to cause heart attacks. Prosecutors said he targeted colleagues’ patients to ruin their reputations and satisfy a need for power. Victims ranged from 4 to 89 years old, and survivors still suffer long‑term effects.
