Swiss skier Franjo von Allmen captured a historic triple gold in alpine events at the Winter Olympics, becoming only the third man ever to win all three major downhill races. He won the downhill, team combined, and super‑G, taking the podium to a 1:25.32 finish. Other highlights include the close miss by France’s Nils Allègre and Australia’s Jakara Anthony's narrow defeat in moguls.
