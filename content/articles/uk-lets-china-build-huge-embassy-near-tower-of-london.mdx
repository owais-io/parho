The UK has approved a plan for China to build a big embassy near the Tower of London. Spy chiefs said security risks can be managed, but local residents plan to challenge the decision in court. The site would be the biggest in Europe, raising fears it could become a spy hub and a target for protests. Politicians on all sides are divided, and the move comes ahead of Prime Minister Starmer’s trip to China.
