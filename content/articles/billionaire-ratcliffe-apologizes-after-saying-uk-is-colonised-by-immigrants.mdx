Billionaire Jim Ratcliffe said the UK is being colonised by immigrants, citing old data that sparked anger from politicians, union leaders, football fans and anti‑racism groups. He quickly apologised, saying his choice of words was offensive. Manchester United issued a statement supporting diversity and inclusion. The Football Association is looking into whether his remarks hurt the sport, while critics accuse him of hypocrisy after moving to Monaco to avoid tax.
