Trump asked the UK, China, France, Japan, South Korea and other allies to send warships to the Strait of Hormuz to keep it open. Most countries said they are still considering it and have not committed. Germany’s foreign minister called a plan to protect ships from Yemen attacks ineffective and warned that NATO could suffer if allies fail to help. Trump also said the U.S. will not seek a deal to end the Iran conflict.
