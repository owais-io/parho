Joe Root cheered for Harry Brook and Brendon McCullum after England beat Sri Lanka in a one‑day match. Root said Brook had apologized for a nightclub incident and is ready to lead the team. He praised McCullum, calling him one of the best coaches he’s worked with and credited him with improving many players’ games. Root also talked about reflecting on the recent Ashes loss and looking forward to future matches.
