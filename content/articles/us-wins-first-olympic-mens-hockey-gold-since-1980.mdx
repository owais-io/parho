At the Milano‑Cortina Winter Olympics, the U.S. men’s ice‑hockey team won its first gold since 1980, beating Canada in overtime. Jack Hughes scored the decisive goal after a high‑stick hit knocked out his front teeth. Canada missed star Sidney Crosby, and the game was played on the 1980 Miracle on Ice anniversary. Other highlights: Swedish curling champion Anna Hasselborg took gold, China’s Eileen Gu won half‑pipe, and Norway topped the medal table. Italy finished as host.
