Older Hollywood actors like Harrison Ford and Meryl Streep are finding fresh parts on television instead of the big screen. Series such as *Shrinking* give them roles that match their age, letting them explore complex characters without the pressure of blockbuster budgets. This shift has become easier now because film budgets are tight and TV offers more varied drama. Though men still get more roles, the trend signals a fresh chapter for seasoned performers.
