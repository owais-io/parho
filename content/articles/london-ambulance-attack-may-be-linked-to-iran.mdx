Four ambulances belonging to a Jewish charity were set on fire in London early Monday. Police say the attack could be part of a wider pattern of attacks on Jewish sites across Europe, many of which have been linked to Iran. The Metropolitan Police chief warned that Iran poses a “rolling threat” to the UK. Experts say Iran uses low‑tech attacks to send a message, but investigators are still looking for proof.
