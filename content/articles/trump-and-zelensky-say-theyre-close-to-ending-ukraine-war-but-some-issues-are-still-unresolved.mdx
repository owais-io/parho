President Trump and Ukraine’s President Zelensky said they’re almost finished negotiating a peace deal, but some hard points—especially over the Donbas region—are still unsettled. Both leaders say the U.S. will back Europe in final talks, and EU and UK officials praised the progress. France plans a Paris meeting to set concrete security guarantees for Ukraine. Meanwhile, Russia fired bombs in eastern Ukraine, killing one person and injuring three. The talks are expected to finish in the coming weeks.
