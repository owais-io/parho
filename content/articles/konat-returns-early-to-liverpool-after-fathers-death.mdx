Liverpool defender Ibrahima Konaté was planning to miss the match against Newcastle because his father had died, but coach Arne Slot said he called the club early to help with the defensive problems. Konaté returned from leave, trained twice, scored his first Premier League goal and helped Liverpool win 4-1. Slot praised his emotional performance and the team’s support. The team celebrated in the dressing room, showing solidarity during a difficult time.
