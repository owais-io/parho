Two women, Joanna and Laura, were handcuffed for 36‑48 hours while giving birth in UK prisons, a shocking breach of rights. The incident sparked a call for an independent probe. Law firms argue the investigation must be open, allow victims to speak, and hold staff accountable. The scandal highlights systemic failures and the urgent need to protect pregnant prisoners’ dignity and safety.
