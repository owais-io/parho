A shooting at Bondi beach in Sydney left 12 dead and 29 injured, and the government called it a terrorist attack. Jewish leaders around the world said they were shocked and standing with Australia. The Australian Prime Minister pledged support and said hate has no place here. Israel’s president and leaders in New Zealand, Britain, and Europe also sent messages of solidarity, warning that antisemitism is rising and must be stopped.
