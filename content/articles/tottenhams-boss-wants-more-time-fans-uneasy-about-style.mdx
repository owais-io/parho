Tottenham boss Thomas Frank says the club needs more time to improve after a 3-0 defeat. He blames the heavy Champions League schedule for tiring players, which hurts league form. Fans are worried about the team's bland, slow play and missing big wins. Frank stresses that there is no quick fix, and Spurs must grow in style and consistency if they want trophies and European success.
