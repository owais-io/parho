An Australian writer was lured into a fake publishing company that used AI‑made staff pictures and virtual offices in Australia, the UK and New Zealand. The scam sites mimicked real publishers, took money from hopeful authors and promised instant publication. The Australian National Anti‑Scam Centre is investigating, and other countries have similar sites. The case shows how scammers use AI to build fake teams and testimonials, warning writers to check a publisher’s real identity before paying.
