In Wiltshire’s farms, a cold afternoon turned dark when criminals set dogs on hares, a cruel sport that’s grown 20% in a year. Police say the gangs bet on how long the chase lasts and even livestream the hunts. Farmers are building concrete‑filled troughs and extra fencing, but the attackers still break in and sometimes cause serious injuries. With 30 arrests this year and stronger law‑enforcement, authorities are trying to stop the crime.
