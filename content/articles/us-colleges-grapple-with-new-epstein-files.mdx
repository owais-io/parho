The latest wave of Jeffrey Epstein documents has hit US universities hard. Faculty and students at schools like Barnard, Columbia, UCLA, Bard, Harvard, and others have protested, calling for investigations, rescinding donations, and sometimes faculty removal. Some institutions have fired or disciplined staff, while others are launching independent reviews. The fallout shows how a single name can raise doubts about donors, trustees and university leadership.
