Skeleton athlete Katie Uhlaender, who has competed in five Winter Olympics, says Canadian coach Joe Cecchini pulled four Canadian riders from a Lake Placid race to reduce the field and cut the points available. She won the race but the lower points left her too low to qualify for the next Olympics. Uhlaender claims this was a deliberate trick to block American competitors. Several other coaches have warned the IOC, while Canada says the withdrawals were for athlete welfare.
