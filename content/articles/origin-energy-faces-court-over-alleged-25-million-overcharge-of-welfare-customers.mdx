Origin Energy is being sued for overcharging about $2.5 million from more than 3,400 former welfare‑recipient customers using the Centrepay system. The Australian Energy Regulator says the company broke the law more than 77,000 times between December 2019 and March 2025. Origin has apologized, said it had reported the issue in 2021 and is working on refunds. The regulator wants fines, compensation and a new compliance program. Other energy firms have faced similar penalties.
