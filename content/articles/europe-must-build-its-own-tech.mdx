Europe is heavily dependent on US tech—from cloud services to defence gear—making it vulnerable when sanctions cut off access for judges, soldiers, and everyday users. EU leaders are starting to shift to open‑source tools and create shared payment and communication systems, but national borders and fragmented markets still hinder progress. Building a unified digital platform could protect Europe’s security and economy.
