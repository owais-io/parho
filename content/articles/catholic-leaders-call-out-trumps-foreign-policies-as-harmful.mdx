Three top U.S. Catholic archbishops—Blase Cupich, Robert McElroy and Joseph Tobin—warn that President Trump’s actions on Greenland, the military raid on Venezuela and cuts to humanitarian aid threaten global peace and human dignity. They urge a foreign policy that values life, religious freedom, and diplomatic solutions, condemning war as a last resort. The bishops say these moves could lead to suffering worldwide.
