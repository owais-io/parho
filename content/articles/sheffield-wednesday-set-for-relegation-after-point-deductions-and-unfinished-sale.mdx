Sheffield Wednesday is on the brink of dropping from the Championship to League One because they lost 12 points after going into administration and another 6 points for late wage payments. Their poor results and a stalled takeover by a new consortium have left the club in a mess. The owners are still negotiating a deal, but delays could mean bankruptcy and a fight to keep the team alive.
