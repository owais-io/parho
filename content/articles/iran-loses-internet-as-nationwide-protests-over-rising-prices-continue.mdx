Iran's internet was shut off last night as protests about high food and medicine costs spread to all 31 provinces. Authorities say at least 45 people, including children, have been killed and more than 2,000 arrested. Prices for groceries have jumped 70% and medicine 50% since last year. The president urges calm, but police have used force and security forces have warned against support for ex‑royal figures. The protests are the biggest in three years.
