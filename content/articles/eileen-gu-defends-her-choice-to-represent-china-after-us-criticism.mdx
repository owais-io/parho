Eileen Gu, 22‑year‑old American‑born Chinese freeskier, has won five Olympic and world medals. U.S. Vice‑President JD Vance suggested American athletes should represent the U.S., prompting Gu to respond. She said she’s not offended, thanked him, and explained that athletes often choose different countries and that her success isn’t about politics. She wants people to focus on the Olympic spirit. Gu also praised other athletes who compete for different nations and urged viewers to celebrate sportsmanship.
