New U.S. Justice Department papers say Jes Staley, former Barclays CEO, and Larry Summers, ex‑Treasury secretary, were named as executors of Jeffrey Epstein’s estate in earlier drafts of his will. Neither name appears in the final 2019 version. Staley has already been barred from the UK banking sector after an investigation into his close ties with Epstein, while Summers said he had no idea he was named. The files spark fresh questions about their relationships with the convicted sex‑offender.
