Trump criticized the UK for taking too long to let US forces use its airbases for Iran strikes, calling the delay disappointing. He also slammed Prime Minister Keir Starmer over the Chagos Islands deal with Mauritius, saying it weakened the legal footing of the US‑UK airbase on Diego Garcia. Trump noted the UK had not allowed use of Diego Garcia amid legal doubts, though it later said it could change after Iranian attacks hit a UK Cyprus base.
