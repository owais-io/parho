The Service Employees International Union (SEIU) has urged Minnesotans to skip work, shopping and school on Friday to protest federal immigration agents’ activities in the state, including the fatal shooting of Renee Good. The union is calling for a nationwide ‘economic blackout,’ with planned actions in cities such as Los Angeles, New York, and Seattle. SEIU says the money spent on ICE cuts public services and endangers workers. Faith leaders and small businesses in Minnesota are also backing the boycott.
