Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni was declared winner of the national election, securing more than 70 % of the vote. His opponent, musician‑turned‑politician Bobi Wine, denied the results, claiming fraud, kidnapping of poll staff, and voter‑ID machine failures. The government shut down the internet and raided Wine’s home. The vote went through mostly quietly, though police clashes left casualties, and critics say the process was unfair.
