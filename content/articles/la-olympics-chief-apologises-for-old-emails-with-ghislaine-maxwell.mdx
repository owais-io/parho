Casey Wasserman, who runs the LA 2028 Olympic Games, said he “deeply regrets” a handful of 2003 emails he exchanged with Ghislaine Maxwell. The messages, which include odd remarks about massages and private encounters, were released in a new set of government documents tied to Jeffrey Epstein. Wasserman stressed that he had no link to Epstein, had only been on a brief trip with the Clinton Foundation, and expressed sincere remorse.
