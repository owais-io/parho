A Lockheed Martin C‑130 transport plane crashed just after taking off from Puerto Leguízamo in southern Colombia’s Amazon region. The aircraft was carrying 11 air‑force crew and 114 soldiers. Rescue teams found 48 injured people, but no survivors or deaths have been confirmed yet. President Petro called it a “horrific accident” and said the army fleet needs urgent renewal. The cause of the crash is still unknown.
