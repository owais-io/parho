A coalition of Australian and Palestinian legal groups has requested the Australian Federal Police to investigate and arrest Doron Almog, a retired Israeli general, who is traveling with Israeli President Isaac Herzog. Almog has faced former arrest warrants for alleged war crimes in Gaza in 2002. The groups say he should be held for his past actions and for his role in the Jewish Agency. The AFP has opened an inquiry.
