Claudia Winkleman, known for hosting *Strictly Come Dancing* and the hit *Celebrity Traitors*, will start her own BBC chat show this spring. The BBC is investing heavily to launch it with big-name guests. Winkleman is grateful for the chance and jokes she’ll still be “awful” on air. The new show will air in the spring and run alongside the already popular *Graham Norton Show*, keeping the BBC’s talk‑show lineup strong.
