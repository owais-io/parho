Families in Norfolk worry that their loved ones’ graves may be eaten by the sea. Three churchyards in Happisburgh, Trimingham and Mundesley face erosion. The council gave options from doing nothing to moving bodies, but no decision has been made. Families want safe graves or stronger sea walls. The lack of a plan is causing frustration and fear, especially after a nearby village disappeared into the sea.
