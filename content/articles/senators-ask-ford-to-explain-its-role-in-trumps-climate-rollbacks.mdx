A Senate committee led by Sheldon Whitehouse is probing whether Ford Motor Company helped push President Trump to roll back the 2009 climate‑endangerment rule. Whitehouse’s letter to CEO Jim Farley cites Trump’s comments at a Ford plant, where the president claimed Farley urged him to ditch environmental rules. Ford has mixed signals – backing some emission cuts while opposing EPA limits. The investigation follows the EPA’s plan to rescind the key climate finding.
