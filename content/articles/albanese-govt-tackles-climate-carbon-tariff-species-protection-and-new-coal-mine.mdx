Australia’s new Labor government has made a series of environmental announcements. A review says it’s time to add a carbon tariff on imports like cement to stop ‘leakage’ of pollution abroad. The environment minister kept the Maugean skate as endangered and backed the salmon‑farming industry, even while adding 34 new species to the protected list. Meanwhile, the government approved a new coal mine expansion, promising some safeguards for wildlife.
