Jon Ruben, 76, a retired vet who ran a Christian summer camp, was jailed for almost 24 years after he laced sweets with tranquilizers to drug and sexually assault boys under 13. He pleaded guilty to multiple child‑sexual‑abuse, cruelty and drug charges. The judge said he would also serve a licence period, bringing his total sentence to over 31 years. Police continue to investigate his alleged abuse over more than 20 years.
