The US Southern Command says it hit a drug smuggling boat in the Caribbean on Friday, killing three suspected smugglers. It is the second deadly strike this week, after a similar attack in the Pacific earlier in the month that killed two more. The attacks raise legal questions, with critics calling them extrajudicial killings and saying the US gives no way to hold people accountable.
