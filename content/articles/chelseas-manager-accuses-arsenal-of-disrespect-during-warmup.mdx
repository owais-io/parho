After a heated exchange before the Carabao Cup semi‑final second leg, Chelsea’s Liam Rosenior accused Arsenal of disrespecting their warm‑up. Cameras caught Rosenior shouting at an Arsenal staff member for crossing into Chelsea’s half of the pitch. He said teams should keep their own half during warm‑ups and that the incident upset his players. Arsenal won 4‑2 on aggregate and will reach the final.
