England’s Six Nations hopes collapsed when they lost 42‑21 to Ireland, leaving the team in a “zombie” state of poor defence, bad kicking and unclear tactics. Fans worry the squad could also falter against Italy and miss a chance to beat the Springboks. The article says England’s record is weak, with only one grand slam in 23 years, and suggests the coaching staff may need change. Quick fixes are needed.
