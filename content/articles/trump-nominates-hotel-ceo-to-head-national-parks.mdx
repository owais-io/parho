Trump has named Scott Socha, a longtime executive at Delaware North—a hotel and food company that runs services in several national parks—to head the National Park Service. Socha has never worked in government or conservation, and critics say his company has tried to trademark park names and chase profit. The nomination comes as the service lost a quarter of its staff in a federal staffing cut and faces pressure to erase sensitive history.
