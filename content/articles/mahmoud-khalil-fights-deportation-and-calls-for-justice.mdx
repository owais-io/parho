Mahmoud Khalil, a Palestinian student at Columbia, has spent over 100 days in ICE detention while fighting a deportation order. He met with progressive lawmakers in Washington to highlight abuses and seek support. A recent court ruling could block people from challenging their detention until after removal proceedings are complete, threatening many immigrants’ rights. Public opinion has turned against ICE, and Khalil hopes to warn others about unfair treatment.
