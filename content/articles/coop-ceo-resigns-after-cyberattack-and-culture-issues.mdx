The Co‑op Group announced that chief executive Shirine Khoury‑Haq will step down on March 29 after a tough year that included a £107 m cyber‑attack and claims of a “toxic” workplace culture. Interim boss Kate Allum will take over while the board searches for a permanent replacement. The group saw a £125 m loss, dropping sales by 2.3 % and facing rising costs.
