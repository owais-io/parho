Vice‑President JD Vance and wife Usha announced on social media that Usha is pregnant with their fourth child, a baby boy due in late July. It’s the first time a second lady has been pregnant while in office. The Vances already have three kids—Ewan, Vivek and Mirabel. They thanked military doctors and staff for their support, and the news follows other political families sharing baby announcements.
