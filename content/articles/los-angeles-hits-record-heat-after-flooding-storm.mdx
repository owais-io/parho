After a week of heavy rain that flooded parts of Los Angeles, the city is now breaking February temperature records. Downtown reached 91°F (33°C), the hottest 27 Feb on record, and the National Weather Service warns of heat‑illness risk. Residents are told to stay hydrated, limit afternoon outdoor time, and keep pets and kids out of cars. The spike follows a massive winter storm that caused floods, wild‑fire evacuation alerts, and a fatal Tahoe avalanche.
