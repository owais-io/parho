The Fallout show on Prime Video has surprised fans with a strong first season, showing the post‑apocalyptic world through three different characters. It mixes real sets and puppets for scary monsters, giving it a more authentic feel. Creators, who are also gamers, praised the show’s success and are excited for the second season, set in New Vegas, which will come out one episode at a time.
