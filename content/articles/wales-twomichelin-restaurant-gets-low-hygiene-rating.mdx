Ynyshir, a fine‑dining spot near Machynlleth in Wales, earned two Michelin stars but was given only one star for hygiene in a recent Food Standards Agency inspection. The rating highlighted problems with raw and aged fish, paperwork mistakes and unfamiliar safety rules. Chef Gareth Ward said he’s not ashamed, only disappointed, and has added extra cleaning stations and hired a compliance firm. The restaurant wants a re‑inspection to improve its score.
