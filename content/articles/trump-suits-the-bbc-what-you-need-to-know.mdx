Donald Trump has threatened to sue the BBC after a Panorama episode that used edited clips of his January‑6 speech. The BBC says it will not back down and has apologized for the editing mistake. Trump’s lawsuit is part of a pattern of legal attacks on media he views as hostile, which could chill journalism. The UK government is urged to support the BBC, an important part of Britain’s democracy, against these intimidation tactics.
