A Melbourne law professor, Dr Eric Descheemaeker, sued the university after an email he sent to the dean, in which he said the school was becoming an “ideological re‑education camp” and that Indigenous activists wanted to “destroy” it, was leaked. The university tried to fire him, claiming it was because of his political views. The case was scheduled for court, but the parties settled, ending the dispute in November. The university hasn't confirmed if Descheemaeker still works there.
