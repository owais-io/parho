US hockey star Brady Tkachuk says he never said the hateful words that a viral AI video from the White House’s TikTok account shows him saying. The clip, which has more than 10 million views, appeared after the U.S. men’s team won Olympic gold over Canada. Tkachuk says the voice and lips are not his and the video is fake. The incident also followed a Trump call that sparked jokes about the women’s team.
