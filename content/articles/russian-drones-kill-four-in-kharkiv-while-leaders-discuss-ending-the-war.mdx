A Russian drone attack hit Kharkiv, killing four people and injuring 17. The strike caused fires in residential and infrastructure buildings. At the same time, U.S., Ukrainian and European officials were in Geneva talking about a plan to end the war. They said progress was made but kept details vague. The U.S. plan has been adjusted by Europeans to allow a larger Ukrainian army. Both sides remain cautious about final terms.
