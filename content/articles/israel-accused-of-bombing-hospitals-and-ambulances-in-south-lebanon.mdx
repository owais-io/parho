Since the fighting began on March 2, Lebanese health workers say Israel has been hitting hospitals, ambulances and first‑aid stations in south Lebanon, using so‑called double‑tap strikes that hit the same spot twice. At least 128 medical sites have been struck, killing 40 workers and wounding 107. The attacks target the Islamic Health Association, Red Cross and other civilian sites, and have forced medics to take extra precautions. The move could be a war crime, says Amnesty.
