Storms dumped up to 12 inches of rain on the California mountain town of Wrightwood, causing mudslides that buried cars and homes up to their windows. The county issued an evacuation warning, and one person was hurt, while three people died in the wider region from flooding and related accidents. Power was knocked out, but local volunteers rescued pets and helped neighbors. Firefighters and police are on alert as rain continues.
