A 115‑page report by retired and active FBI agents says new director Kash Patel lacks the skills to run the bureau. The report, based on 24 insider accounts, claims managers are too scared to act without orders, turning the FBI into a “rudderless ship.” It also highlights Patel’s controversial past remarks, questionable decisions, and a culture of fear that hurts morale. Some praise his tougher immigration stance, but allies warn his actions could damage international cooperation. The White House denies plans to fire him.
