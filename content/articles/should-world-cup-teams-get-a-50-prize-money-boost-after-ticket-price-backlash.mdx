FIFA has announced a 50% boost in World Cup prize money, but ticket prices have more than doubled, sparking fan anger. Supporters’ leaders say the extra money could be used to lower prices, offer free tickets to travelling fans, and give discounts for disabled supporters. The piece argues that FIFA’s reputation would improve if it acted on these ideas, and questions whether prize money should go to national federations or make the event more affordable.
