Hatton and McIlroy met in Dubai and said there’s no tension over the LIV‑Golf issue. The two chatted about wine rather than penalties. Hatton mentioned his weight gain and plans to slim down. They both played well: Hatton shot two under 70, McIlroy 73. Other Ryder Cup players like Molinari and Fleetwood also posted solid scores. Team captain Donald had to pull out because of illness.
