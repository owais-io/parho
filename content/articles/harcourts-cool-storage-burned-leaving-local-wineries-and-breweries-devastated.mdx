The Harcourt Cooperative Cool Stores, a key refrigerated hub for local wineries and breweries, was destroyed by bushfires. Producers like winemaker Gilles Lapalus and breweries such as Love Shack and Shedshaker lost stock, equipment, and even historic wines. Insurance coverage is unclear, adding uncertainty. The community responded with a fundraiser at Boomtown Winery, raising over $130,000 to help those affected. Trevor Peeler, the store’s former manager, hopes to rebuild and support the industry’s recovery.
