During a Hanukah celebration at Bondi Beach, a shooting erupted, killing a woman and injuring several. The author, a mother, escaped with cuts and a shattered nose, and her husband rescued their daughter. She recounts the chaos, her injuries, and the community’s quick response. She insists she is not a hero, just a parent doing what any mother would do, and reflects on safety and kindness.
