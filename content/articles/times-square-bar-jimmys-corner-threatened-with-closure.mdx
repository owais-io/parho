Jimmy’s Corner is a 1971 dive bar in Times Square that has kept the same cheap drinks and old‑school vibe for decades. The landlord, Durst Organization, told the owner he must leave, so the family sued. The bar’s son, Adam, says his dad would be hurt. Patrons fear losing a historic, friendly spot. If it closes, he plans to open a new bar elsewhere for the next generation.
