The article explains how to handle a gift you don’t want without hurting the giver. It says to smile, say “thank you” and act excited, then quietly hide or return the item later. Avoid telling the person you already have it or that it’s useless. You can keep it in a “gift purgatory” box until you can exchange it or give it away. The goal is to keep the holiday spirit positive and polite.
