U.S. forces seized a Venezuelan oil tanker off the coast, sparking a sharp backlash. President Trump said the move was to stop the tanker from carrying sanctioned oil. Venezuela’s government called it an act of international piracy and accused the U.S. of stealing its natural resources. The Venezuelan opposition praised the seizure, seeing it as a blow against Maduro’s regime.
