The Guardian sifted through more than a million documents released after Jeffrey Epstein’s arrest. It found hundreds of thousands of emails showing how he kept in touch with top figures in business, finance and the arts. The emails include the former Harvard economist Larry Summers, film director Woody Allen, former Barclays chief Jes Staley, and others. These emails show he was a connector among elites, but nothing in them proves he broke the law.
