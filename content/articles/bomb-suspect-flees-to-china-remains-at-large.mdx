Alen Zheng, a U.S. citizen, is accused of placing an undetonated bomb in the visitors centre of MacDill Air Force base in Florida. The device sat hidden for about a week before officials found it and linked it to a 911 call he made. After the bomb was discovered, Alen and his sister sold a car and fled to China. While the sister was arrested in the U.S., Alen remains at large and may face extradition talks.
