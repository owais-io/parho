At the Milano‑Cortina Winter Games, Swiss men swept almost every men’s skiing event, winning four of five races. Franjo von Allmen took gold in the downhill, slalom, and team combined, while Tanguy Nef also claimed a win. Loïc Meillard added a slalom title, and Lucas Pinheiro Braathen from Brazil snagged the only gold that didn’t go to Switzerland. The Swiss team took eight medals, the most of any nation.
