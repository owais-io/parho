After leaving Amazon, Jeff Bezos is now CEO of a new AI startup called Project Prometheus. The company will build AI for engineering and manufacturing. It already raised $6.2 billion and has 100 employees, many from OpenAI, DeepMind and Meta. He co‑runs it with Vik Bajaj, a tech exec from Google X. Details about the company’s plans and location are still unclear.
