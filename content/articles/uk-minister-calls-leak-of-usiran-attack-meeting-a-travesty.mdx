David Lammy says a leak from a secret meeting about the US‑Israel attack on Iran was a travesty. He wants an investigation and says the leak endangers British lives. The meeting debated whether the UK would let the US use its bases to hit Iranian missile sites. Some ministers opposed it, but the final decision was to allow the US strikes after Iran fired back. The UK says it will defend allies but not take offensive action.
