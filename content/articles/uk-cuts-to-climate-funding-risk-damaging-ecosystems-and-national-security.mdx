UK spy chiefs warned that losing rainforests, reefs and glaciers could hurt the country’s food supplies and spark conflicts overseas. Yet the government has cut its £11.6 bn climate‑finance pledge, slashing key nature‑protecting programs. Other countries like the US, Germany and New Zealand are also trimming aid. The cuts mean less money for projects that keep ecosystems healthy—important for national security and the planet.
