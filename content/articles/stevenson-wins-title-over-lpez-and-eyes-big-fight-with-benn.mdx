Stevenson kept his calm and outboxed Teófimo López in a 12‑round showdown at Madison Square Garden, becoming a four‑division world champion. He credits his win to thorough film study, a sharp jab, and disciplined training. After the decision, he opened the door to a potential fight with British boxer Conor Benn, saying the next big match will depend on money and legacy.
