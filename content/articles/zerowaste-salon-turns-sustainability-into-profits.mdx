In La Mesa, California, Scisters Salon & Apothecary has cut its waste by 99% by switching to refillable glass containers, composting hair clippings, and offering sugaring instead of waxing. The owners even created their own clean‑product line after discovering health risks in commercial hair supplies. During COVID‑19 they sold refills from the parking lot to stay afloat, proving eco‑friendly can boost a salon’s bottom line. They now teach other salons to copy the model.
