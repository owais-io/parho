Water has been missing in Iran for months. Dams are nearly empty and only 1 mm of rain fell in Tehran this year. Farmers, mosques, and the media are all hoping for clouds, and even planes are spraying silver iodide to try to make it rain. Some religious leaders blame God or society, while politicians warn that the capital might have to evacuate if it stays dry.
