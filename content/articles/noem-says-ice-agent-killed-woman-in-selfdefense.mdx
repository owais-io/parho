Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem says an ICE officer in Minneapolis shot 37‑year‑old Renee Good in self‑defense after Good allegedly chased the agents with her car. Local officials and witnesses dispute the claim, pointing to video that shows Good driving away. Minneapolis’ mayor and state police say the story is false and the state police won’t investigate. Protests against ICE erupted nationwide, and Noem says she will send more agents if needed.
