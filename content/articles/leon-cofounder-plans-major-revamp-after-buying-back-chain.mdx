John Vincent, the co‑founder of the fast‑food chain Leon, has bought the business back for about £30–£50m after it struggled under Asda. He plans to close some outlets, focus on London stores, and improve the menu by dropping less popular items and re‑introducing favourites. Vincent wants Leon to be a niche, high‑quality brand, with a new “founder’s mindset”, training staff, and aiming to grow again to 100 outlets in four years.
