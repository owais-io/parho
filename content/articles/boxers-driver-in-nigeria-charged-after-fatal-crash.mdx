Nigerian police have charged Anthony Joshua’s driver with dangerous driving after a crash that killed two people. The 46‑year‑old driver is also accused of driving without a licence and careless driving. He was driving the boxer, his trainer, and a strength coach on a Lagos‑Ibadan highway when a tyre burst. The SUV hit a stalled lorry, killing the trainer and coach. Joshua survived with minor injuries.
