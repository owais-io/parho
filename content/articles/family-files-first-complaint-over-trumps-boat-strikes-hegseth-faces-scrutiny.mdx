A US airstrike on an alleged drug boat killed survivors of a first hit, sparking anger from both parties in Washington. Lawmakers are asking whether the double‑tap attack could be a war crime. The Pentagon blamed Admiral Frank Bradley, who ran the mission under defense secretary Pete Hegseth, who defended him. Meanwhile, Trump warned that any country he thinks makes drugs for the US could face military action. A Colombian family filed the first official complaint against the strikes.
