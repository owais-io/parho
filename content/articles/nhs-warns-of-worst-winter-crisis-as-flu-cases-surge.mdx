NHS braces for its worst winter crisis as flu cases rise, with hospitals already treating record numbers of patients. Over 2,600 flu patients are admitted daily, many in intensive care. Doctors plan a strike, but may back down if pay offers improve. Health officials urge people to get flu shots, wear masks, and keep distance, especially for vulnerable groups, to ease the strain.
