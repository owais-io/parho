Formula One chief Stefano Domenicali asked everyone to stay calm after drivers, especially Max Verstappen and Lewis Hamilton, complained about the sport’s biggest rule changes yet. The new regulations mix combustion and electric power, forcing drivers to manage energy and lift off the throttle. Domenicali said the series will monitor the season, adjust if needed, and keep racing exciting.
