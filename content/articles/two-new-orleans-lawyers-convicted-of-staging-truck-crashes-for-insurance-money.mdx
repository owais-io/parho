A federal jury in New Orleans found attorneys Jason Giles and former stuntwoman Vanessa Motta guilty of a fraud scheme that paid people to crash into tractor‑trailers and then file lawsuits for large settlements. The trial also convicted a third defendant of making false statements. The case, part of “Operation Sideswipe,” highlighted how staged crashes can raise state insurance costs and led to a July sentencing date.
