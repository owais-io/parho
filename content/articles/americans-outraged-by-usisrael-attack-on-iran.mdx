After a U.S. and Israel strike on Iran killed civilians and some U.S. service members, many Americans expressed disapproval. Retired doctors, business owners, and veterans warned about civilian harm, lack of a clear strategy, and the risk of dragging U.S. youth into a lost war. Some noted domestic problems like poverty and healthcare that should be priorities instead of foreign intervention. Trump’s earlier anti‑war stance was highlighted.
