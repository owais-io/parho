CBS News is under pressure because its parent company, Paramount Skydance, led by David Ellison (Larry Ellison’s son), wants to buy Warner Brothers Discovery, which owns CNN. To get the deal approved, they’re trying to curry favor with the Trump administration. This shift has sparked moves that limit editorial independence: shows are being altered, reporters are leaving, and the network’s reporting on ICE is being twisted. The story warns that big‑business deals can threaten unbiased news.
