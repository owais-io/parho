The Society of Authors has created a “Human Authored” logo that writers can put on their books to show they were written by people, not AI. The move comes after a wave of AI‑generated titles and a lack of government rules forcing tech companies to label machine output. Supporters include Mary Beard and Malorie Blackman, and the scheme launched at the London Book Fair. A survey found 82 % of members want it.
