At the Munich Security Conference, Estonia’s EU foreign policy chief Kaja Kallas said the U.S. claim that Europe is on the brink of “civilisational erasure” is wrong and is just trendy euro‑bashing. She highlighted Europe’s values like press freedom and mentioned that many non‑Europeans, even in Canada, want to join the EU. Kallas also warned that the U.S. cannot solve the Ukraine war without European support.
