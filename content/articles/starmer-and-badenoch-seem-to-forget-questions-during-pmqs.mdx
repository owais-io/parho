During a recent session of Prime Minister’s Questions, opposition leader Keir Starmer and Home Secretary Kemi Badenoch appeared to struggle with memory lapses, skipping questions and repeating each other. The Guardian piece jokes that the pair are “forgotting” everything from Peter Mandelson to the Iran war, turning the session into a chaotic, almost meaningless exchange. The satire lampoons parliamentary debate and the politicians’ confusion.
