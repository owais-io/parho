The Australian Open saw mixed fortunes. Daria Kasatkina and Kimberly Birrell lost early, ending hopes of top Australian women. Daria Maya Joint also fell. But six Australian women reach the second round, the most since 1992. Maddison Inglis beat Birrell in a long match, while others progressed. Men’s side: Dane Sweeny and James Duckworth advance, with Monfils making his final appearance.
