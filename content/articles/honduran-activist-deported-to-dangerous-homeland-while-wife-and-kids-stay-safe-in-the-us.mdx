Oscar, a Honduran environmental activist, was deported by ICE to his home country even though his wife and children were granted asylum in the United States. After being held in a Louisiana detention center, he was sent back to Honduras where he fears for his life because of threats linked to his activism. The family had been targeted for opposing a big solar project, and now Oscar must hide while hoping to reunite with his family later.
