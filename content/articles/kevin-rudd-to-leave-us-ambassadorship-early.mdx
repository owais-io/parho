Kevin Rudd, ex‑Australian prime minister, will quit his role as ambassador to the United States on March 31, a year early. He is leaving to head the Asia Society think‑tank and its China research centre. Rudd secured the US‑Australia submarine deal, helped release Julian Assange, and won big investment deals on minerals and AI. His outspoken criticism of former U.S. president Donald Trump has made him controversial. Australia will name a new ambassador soon.
