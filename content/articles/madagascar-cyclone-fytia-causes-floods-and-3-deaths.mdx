Tropical Cyclone Fytia hit Madagascar over the weekend, bringing heavy rain and strong winds. About 150 mm of rain in some places triggered flooding and landslides. The storm caused three deaths and left nearly 28,000 people in need of help. More than 40,000 homes could be flooded, and many schools and roads were shut. Sailors were warned to stay in port while the cyclone weakened. Officials urged residents to stay alert and follow local evacuation orders.
