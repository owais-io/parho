Jeffrey Epstein’s thousands of emails reveal a private club of powerful men who met for dinners and seminars, while women were rarely invited and mostly used as secretaries, hosts, or sex workers. The messages expose a patriarchal network that praises men, flirts with women, and trades favors to stay connected.
