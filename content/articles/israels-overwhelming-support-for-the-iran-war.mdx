Israel is the only country where the vast majority of people back the war against Iran. Polls show more than 90% of Jewish Israelis support the fighting, while the U.S. public is largely opposed. The war has hit everyday life: missile alerts, school closures, and bomb shelters are now normal. The support also boosts Prime Minister Netanyahu’s reputation for security, but he still struggles in upcoming elections and worries about damaging ties with the U.S.
