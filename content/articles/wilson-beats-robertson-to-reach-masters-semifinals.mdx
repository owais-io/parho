Kyren Wilson won a 6‑5 match against Neil Robertson to reach the Masters semi‑finals. After taking a 4‑1 lead and making two century breaks, Robertson fought back, winning four frames and taking a 5‑4 lead. Wilson levelled the match with a 111‑break in the 10th frame and then won the final frame to clinch the win. In the other draw, Wu Yize dominated Xiao Guodong 6‑0 to secure his own spot in the semis.
