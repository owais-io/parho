Keir Starmer has chosen Antonia Romeo as the UK’s top civil servant, making her the first woman in the post. Romeo, a long‑time reformer who led major departments like the Home Office and International Trade, has been praised for her drive but faced past bullying accusations that were cleared. Despite criticism from former officials, the government says her experience will help tackle cost‑of‑living pressures.
