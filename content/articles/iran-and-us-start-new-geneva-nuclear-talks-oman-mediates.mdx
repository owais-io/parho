Iran and the United States have begun a new round of nuclear talks in Geneva, with Oman acting as mediator and the International Atomic Energy Agency’s head observing the talks. Iran wants the US to stop allowing it to enrich uranium and hopes for sanctions relief, while the US wants Iran to limit enrichment to below 5% and give full inspections. The talks aim to prevent further conflict and reduce tensions.
