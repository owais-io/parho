After a goalless 0-0 game against bottom‑placed Wolves, Nottingham Forest owner Evangelos Marinakis fired manager Sean Dyche. Fans were unhappy with the team's poor finishing – 35 shots, little life on the pitch – and shouted at Dyche after full‑time. The decision made Forest the club with the most coaches this season. Dyche said he had tried to fix problems left by previous managers, but the club chose change.
