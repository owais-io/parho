Tottenham are 14th in the Premier League, winning only two of 14 games and dropping out of both domestic cups, but they beat Eintracht Frankfurt 2‑0 in the Champions League to secure a place in the last‑16. Midfielder João Palhinha says the win shows the team can compete, but the focus must stay on fixing their league form. He blames injuries and missed chances, not the manager, and urges players to step up.
