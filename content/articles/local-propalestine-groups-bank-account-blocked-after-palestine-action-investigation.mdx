A local UK group that supports Palestinians had its bank account frozen after police began investigating a banned group called Palestine Action. The group, which runs peaceful protests and vigils, says it has no links to Palestine Action and has never donated to it. The bank and police didn’t explain why the account was blocked. Similar freezes have hit other peaceful organisations, sparking anger and calls for the bans to be reviewed.
