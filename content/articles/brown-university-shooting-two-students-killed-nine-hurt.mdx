Two people died and nine were injured in a shooting at Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island. The attack happened in a classroom in the Barus + Holley building during final exams. A suspect in black is still missing. The city ordered a shelter‑in‑place and urged residents to stay inside until it ends. Police released footage of a possible shooter, but the status of the suspect is still unclear.
