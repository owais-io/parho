A 21‑year‑old activist who spent 18 months in jail was cleared of burglary and violent disorder after a jury found her not guilty. She says the verdict shows the public won’t punish people for supporting Palestinian rights. The court saw evidence that the raid on an Israeli defence firm was to stop weapons used in a genocide. She remains hopeful but still struggles with prison trauma.
