Nottingham attack mother Emma Webber is calling for the full truth to be uncovered before the statutory inquiry into the 2023 attacks. She wants the NHS, police, and other agencies that may have failed to act to be held accountable. The inquiry will look at how the attacker, Valdo Calocane, was treated by health services and why he was free to carry out the killings. Families say the investigation should expose systemic neglect and lead to real change.
