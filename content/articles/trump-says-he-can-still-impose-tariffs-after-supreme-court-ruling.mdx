After the Supreme Court declared many of his tariffs illegal, President Trump blasted the court and claimed he still has the right to impose new duties. He’s used a different law to slap a 10% tax on imports, which lasts 150 days, and is thinking about raising it to 15%. Trump’s moves keep U.S. trade disputes tense with China, Mexico and other partners.
