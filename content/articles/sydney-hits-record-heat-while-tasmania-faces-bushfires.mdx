Sydney’s central business district reached 37.8 °C, its highest in almost two years, as a summer heatwave sweeps the east coast. Western suburbs even climbed above 40 °C. At the same time, firefighters in Tasmania and New South Wales are battling large bushfires that have damaged homes, power lines and roadways. Heat‑ and fire‑danger warnings are in place, and some areas may face power cuts for weeks.
