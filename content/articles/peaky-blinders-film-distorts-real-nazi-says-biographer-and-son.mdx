The new Peaky Blinders movie, The Immortal Man, shows a Nazi named John Beckett as a ruthless villain. But Francis Beckett, who wrote a biography of his father and is also his son, says the real John Beckett was a different, less dangerous man. Beckett says the film, along with other WWII movies, invents heroic myths that mislead people and can fuel modern far‑right ideas.
