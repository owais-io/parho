Back on the Map is a Sunderland charity that is turning trouble‑ridden streets into safe, welcoming neighbourhoods. It buys and repairs homes, runs community centres and gardens, and even owns shops that offer food and workshops. Residents get training and support for benefits, and tenants help keep the area tidy. By building pride in local parks, shops and friendships, the charity fights the far‑right’s divisive message and makes the town safer.
