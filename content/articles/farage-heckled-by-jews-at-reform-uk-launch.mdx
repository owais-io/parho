Nigel Farage spoke at the launch of Reform Jewish Alliance, a group for Reform UK, but Jewish activists stormed the stage and shouted accusations. They said the party is using the group to push policies that would target other minorities and that Farage has a history of antisemitic remarks. The hecklers, from Na’amod, also criticized allegations from Dulwich College classmates. Farage denied the claims and called them politically motivated. The event highlighted growing tensions within the British Jewish community.
