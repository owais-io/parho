Gina Rinehart’s mining firm, Hancock Prospecting, donated almost $900,000 to the right‑wing group Advance during the 2024‑25 election period. It also gave $105,000 to the Victorian Liberal party and $99,000 to the Western Australian Liberals. The 2025 federal election saw record spending: Liberals and Nationals spent about $215 m, Labor $160 m, yet Labor won. New donation rules will apply next election in 2028.
