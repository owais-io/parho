A new study by the Center for American Progress says President Trump’s Environmental Protection Agency is cutting back rules that protect air, water and children’s health, even though his “Make America Healthy Again” campaign promises to improve public health. The EPA has weakened pollution limits, let factories skip clean‑air rules and delayed methane deadlines. The report warns that these rollbacks could expose kids to more toxins and worsen diseases like asthma, cancer and obesity.
