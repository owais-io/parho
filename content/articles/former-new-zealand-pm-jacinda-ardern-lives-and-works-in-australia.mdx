After stepping down, former New Zealand prime minister Jacinda Ardern has settled in Australia with her family. She says she’s working there, which gives her more time back home. The move comes amid a wave of New Zealanders leaving for higher wages and easier work permits in Australia. Ardern’s move follows her resignation in 2023 and her shift to academia and global causes. Her move reflects a wider trend of leaders relocating abroad after their terms.
