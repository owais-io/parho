On Sunday afternoon, a 13‑year‑old boy was attacked by a shark while swimming near Nielsen Park in Sydney’s east. He suffered serious leg injuries and was taken to the Sydney Children’s Hospital in critical condition. Police and paramedics first treated him on a water‑police boat and at Rose Bay wharf, and he was later air‑lifted to the hospital. The attack happened outside a damaged shark‑net enclosure that was repaired last month. Sydney’s beaches have seen several recent shark incidents.
