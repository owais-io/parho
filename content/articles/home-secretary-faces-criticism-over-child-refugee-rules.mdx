Labour peer Alf Dubs and several refugee charities say Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood is blocking children who are refugees abroad from joining family in the UK. He says the government is treating them like a political weapon and refusing to open safe routes for unaccompanied children. Mahmood argues the UK needs stricter border controls and will create new legal paths for asylum seekers once the system is “re‑ordered”. Dubs wants laws that let children reunite with relatives.
