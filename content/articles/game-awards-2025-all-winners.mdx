The Game Awards 2025 announced winners in 30+ categories. Game of the Year, Game Direction, Narrative, Art Direction, Score & Music all went to “Expedition 33.” Battlefield 6 won Best Audio Design. Silent Hill f got Best Performance. “Doom: The Dark Ages” won Best Action. “Despelote Dispatch” was Best Debut Indie, “Blue Prince” Best Independent Game, and “Wuthering Waves” Best Mobile. The event also honored content creators and esports, celebrating a mix of mainstream hits and indie gems.
