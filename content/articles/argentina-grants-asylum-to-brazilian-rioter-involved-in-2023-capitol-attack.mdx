Argentina has granted asylum to Joel Borges Correa, a 47‑year‑old Brazilian who was convicted for taking part in the 2023 pro‑Bolsonaro riots that stormed Brazil’s congress and presidential palace. The decision follows Brazil’s request to extradite 61 former rioters. Argentina’s court said Correa faced political persecution and would be at risk if returned. The ruling could affect Brazil’s October election, giving the opposition a point to question the legitimacy of the 2023 riots.
