Iran has warned it will destroy desalination plants in Gulf countries—Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, and Oman—if the U.S. follows Trump’s threat to hit Iran’s power grid. These plants are the main source of drinking water and cooling for power and industry in a desert region that gets almost no rain. A strike would cut water for weeks, hurt hospitals and factories, and could spark panic and unrest.
