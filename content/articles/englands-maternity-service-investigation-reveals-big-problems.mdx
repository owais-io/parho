A new investigation into England’s maternity services has found serious issues, like staff shortages, racism, and poor care that puts women and babies at risk. The probe, led by Lady Amos, follows past scandals where babies died or were harmed. Findings show that black women and those from poorer areas are hit hardest, and many hospitals still need big improvements. The final report will come this spring with safety changes.
