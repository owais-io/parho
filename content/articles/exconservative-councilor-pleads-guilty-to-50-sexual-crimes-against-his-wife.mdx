Philip Young, a former Swindon Conservative councilor, admitted in Winchester court to 50 sexual offences over 14 years, including 11 rapes, 11 drug‑induced assaults, 14 voyeurism cases and 14 sexual assaults. He was remanded in custody; five other men were released on bail while police hunt for an additional suspect. A trial is scheduled for 5 October.
