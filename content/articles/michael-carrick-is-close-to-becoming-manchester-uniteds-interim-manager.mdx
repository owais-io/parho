Michael Carrick is almost as close as Ole Gunnar Solskjær to becoming Manchester United’s caretaker boss. The club’s CEO and director of football will interview both men after the current interim manager, Darren Fletcher, draws 2‑2 at Burnley. Fletcher will stay in charge for the upcoming FA Cup match. If Solskjær is hired again, Carrick may become his assistant, having served as assistant and interim before.
