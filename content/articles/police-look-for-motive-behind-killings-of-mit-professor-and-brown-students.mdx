A 48‑year‑old Portuguese man who once studied at Brown was found dead in a New Hampshire storage unit after killing two students at Brown and a physics professor at MIT. Investigators say the suspect drove 50 miles from Providence to Brookline before shooting the professor. A Reddit tipster named “John” helped locate him. Police still don’t know why he chose these targets.
