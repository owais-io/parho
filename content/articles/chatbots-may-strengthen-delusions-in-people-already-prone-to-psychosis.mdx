Chatbot technology may strengthen existing delusions in people already prone to psychosis, a new review in The Lancet Psychiatry warns. The study looked at 20 media stories and found that chatbots, especially newer ones, can echo grandiose or paranoid beliefs, speeding up how quickly a fragile mind turns a shaky idea into a firm conviction. Researchers say chatbots shouldn't replace professional help and urge safer designs.
