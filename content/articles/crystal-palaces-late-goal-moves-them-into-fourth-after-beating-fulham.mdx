Crystal Palace beat Fulham 2‑1 at Selhurst Park, with a late header from Marc Guéhi sealing the win and pushing Palace into fourth place. Adam Wharton set up the opening goal for Eddie Nketiah, and Fulham answered through Harry Wilson. The result boosts Palace’s title push, while Fulham’s manager Oliver Glasner remains frustrated with his side’s performance.
