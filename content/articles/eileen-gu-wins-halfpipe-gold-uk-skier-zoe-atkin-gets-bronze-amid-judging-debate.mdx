Eileen Gu won gold in the women's halfpipe at the Winter Olympics, but the event sparked a debate after British skier Zoe Atkin received bronze. Some in the UK team felt Atkin's higher jump should have earned her silver. The judges awarded Gu 94.75, Atkin 92.5, and Chinese skier Li Fanghui 93.0. Gu celebrated her win, while Atkin praised her own achievement and the overall competition.
