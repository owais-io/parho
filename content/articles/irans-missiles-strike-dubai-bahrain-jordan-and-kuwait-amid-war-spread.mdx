Iran fired missiles at the Fairmont hotel in Dubai, setting it ablaze, while drones hit Bahrain, Kuwait and Jordan. The attacks struck a US naval base, an airport and hotels, causing injuries and damage. Iran’s strikes followed US and Israeli bombs, expanding the conflict beyond Israel and Iran. Residents feared airspace closures and possible Israeli retaliation, leading to panic and precautionary stock‑ups.
