Health officials in England spotted a new mpox strain that blends parts of the severe and mild versions of the virus. The virus came from a traveler who had recently been in Asia. Scientists say the mix isn’t surprising and that mpox can keep evolving, so they’re monitoring it closely. Most mpox cases are mild, but people who can get vaccinated are urged to do so.
