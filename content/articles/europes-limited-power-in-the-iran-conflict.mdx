After Donald Trump ordered a strike on Iran, European leaders found their options limited. The EU has tried to keep international law on the table, but Trump’s unilateral move and threats at allies like Spain show Europe can’t force his hand. The piece argues the EU must grow its own defence powers—even nuclear—and become more independent if it wants real influence. Rising oil prices underscore the urgency.
