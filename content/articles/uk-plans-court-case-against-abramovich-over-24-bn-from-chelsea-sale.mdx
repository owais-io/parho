The UK government is preparing to take Roman Abramovich to court after he missed the March 17 deadline to hand over £2.4 bn from selling Chelsea FC. Officials insist the money must fund humanitarian aid in Ukraine, but there’s a dispute over whether it can be spent elsewhere. They’ve warned Abramovich’s lawyers and will back a foundation that will use the funds once released. Aid groups fear the delays harm Ukraine.
