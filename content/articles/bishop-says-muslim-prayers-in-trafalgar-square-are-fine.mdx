Bishop Arun Arora argues that the open Muslim iftar in Trafalgar Square is not a threat. He compares it to yearly Christian remembrance services that happen in public parks and notes that other faith leaders join them. Arora says the right‑wing politicians' criticism is Islamophobic and hurtful. He calls on Christians to welcome all faiths, citing the church’s duty to protect free worship. He warns that such comments could fuel anti‑Muslim violence.
