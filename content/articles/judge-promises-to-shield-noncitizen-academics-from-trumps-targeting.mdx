Judge William Young says he will protect non‑citizen academics who sued the Trump administration. He calls Trump an authoritarian and will issue an order to stop the government from changing their immigration status as retaliation. The judge demands the administration prove it has legitimate reasons before deporting anyone. He criticized Trump for hurting free speech and targeting pro‑Palestinian scholars like Mahmoud Khalil and Rümeysa Öztürk.
