Bobi Wine, Uganda’s top opposition leader, has been on the run for almost three weeks after the ruling president’s party won the election. He says police and the army raided his home, and he escaped, leaving his wife behind. While hiding, Wine has posted on social media calling the results wrong and taunting the army chief, Muhoozi Kainerugaba, who has threatened him. The feud has raised fears for Wine’s safety.
