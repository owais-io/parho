Former EU foreign‑policy chief Federica Mogherini, now running the College of Europe, and two other officials are under investigation for fraud and corruption. Belgian police raided the College, the EU foreign‑service headquarters, and Mogherini’s home. The case focuses on a contract to run a diplomatic training academy that the College won, with claims of bribery, conflict of interest and leaked confidential info. All three are free and presumed innocent, and the college will cooperate with the probe.
