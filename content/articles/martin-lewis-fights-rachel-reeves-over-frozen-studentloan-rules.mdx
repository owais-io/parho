Martin Lewis, founder of MoneySavingExpert.com, is upset with Chancellor Rachel Reeves for freezing the salary threshold that triggers student‑loan repayments. The change keeps the plan‑2 repayment level at £29,385 for years, so many graduates will pay more and sooner. Lewis says it breaches the loan contract and urges people to write to MPs. Reeves defends the move as fair and necessary for sustainability. The debate could affect voters.
