Martin Ødegaard turned back on injuries and helped Arsenal beat Aston Villa 2‑1. He created two goals, including a through‑ball for Zubimendi and a cross for Trossard. Arsenal had a shaky start after Gabriel Magalhães opened the scoring, but Ødegaard’s energy and vision steadied the team. The Norwegian now shows more touches and shots than anyone else, and his captaincy is solid. Arsenal stay a point ahead of City.
