A Myanmar army plane bombed the Mrauk U General Hospital in Rakhine state, killing at least 31 people and wounding more than 60. The attack happened as the junta pushes through an offensive before a disputed election. The Arakan Army, which controls most of the state, said the strike hit the hospital directly. The UN and U.S. called the attack a possible war crime and urged the military to stop hitting civilians.
