Alexander Zverev was furious when his opponent, Carlos Alcaraz, received a medical timeout for a cramp during their Australian Open semi‑final. Zverev called the decision “bullshit” but said he didn’t want it to eclipse the match. Alcaraz, who cramp‑stricken on the court, finished 6‑4, 7‑6(5), 6‑7(3), 6‑7(4), 7‑5 in five hours and 27 minutes. The timeout was allowed by the tournament’s medical staff, even though cramps aren’t normally covered.
