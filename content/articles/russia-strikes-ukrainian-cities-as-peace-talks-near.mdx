Russia has hit several Ukrainian cities with drones and missiles, killing civilians and damaging homes, just before a new round of peace talks. President Zelenskyy condemned the attacks and warned of more retaliation. The U.S. is working on diplomatic talks but will play a smaller role than last week. Meanwhile, Ukraine faces harsh winter and energy shortages, while the U.S. urges stronger sanctions on Russian “shadow fleet” oil shipments.
