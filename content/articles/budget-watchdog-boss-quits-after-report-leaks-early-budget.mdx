The chief of the Office for Budget Responsibility, Richard Hughes, has resigned after an internal investigation found a major security failure that let the government’s budget documents leak early. The leak caused chaos for Treasury Secretary Rachel Reeves and raised questions about how sensitive information is protected. The government says the resignation should calm tensions, but critics say it shows a bigger problem with how the budget is handled.
