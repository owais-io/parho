Aden Durde, a 46‑year‑old coach from London, will be the first British coach to work on an NFL sideline at the Super Bowl. He helped Seattle’s defense jump from 30th to 6th place and lowered opponents’ points. Durde’s path went from playing in the UK to coaching in NFL Europe, then with the Cowboys and Falcons. He now looks forward to more international talent in the league.
