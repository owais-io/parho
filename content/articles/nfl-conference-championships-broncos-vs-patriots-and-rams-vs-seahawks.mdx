Broncos face Patriots, Rams face Seahawks in NFL conference championships. Patriots must limit edge rushers and rely on Stefon Diggs, while Broncos need to protect rookie QB Stidham and run the ball. Analysts favor the Patriots. Rams count on running back Kyren Williams to wear down Seattle, but the Seahawks are missing key runner Zach Charbonnet and lean on passing. Prediction: Seahawks win over Rams. Both games are tight, but the Patriots are tipped to win.
