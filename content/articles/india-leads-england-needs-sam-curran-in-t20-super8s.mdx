India is the clear favourite for the T20 World Cup Super‑8s, but England’s shaky batting means they’ll need Sam Curran to spark things. Pakistan did fine against Namibia but fell apart against India. Sri Lanka surprised by beating Australia, led by Pathum Nissanka’s fast century. New Zealand enjoy home ground advantage, while Zimbabwe’s fast bowlers and West Indies’ batting keep them in the mix. South Africa have mixed results and will face India in a tough match.
