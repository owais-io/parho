International Women’s Day (IWD) is more than a sales push. On March 8, companies spam emails offering “women‑friendly” gifts, but the day also sparks deeper thought. A university sermon tried to read the Bible from a feminist angle, spotlighting unnamed female figures. The piece links IWD to rising misogyny, Christian nationalism, and extreme right ideas that push women into subservient roles. It reminds us that gender issues touch everyone.
