Romantic novels are finally getting the recognition they deserve. Next year the British Book Awards will honour romance, a first after years of being called silly. Famous writers Joanna Trollope, Sophie Kinsella and Dame Jilly Cooper – who all passed away recently – sold millions and made films and TV shows from their books. Fans love romance for quick escape, and sales have surged thanks to social media and online book clubs. Classic authors like Jane Austen still inspire the genre.
