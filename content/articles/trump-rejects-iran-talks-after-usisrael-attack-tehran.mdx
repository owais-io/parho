On Tuesday, the United States and Israel launched a new wave of airstrikes on Iran, targeting command centers, missile sites and senior cleric buildings. President Trump said he had rejected Iran’s offer to restart talks. Iran responded with dozens of missiles and drones aimed at Israeli and US sites, and at energy infrastructure across the Gulf. The fighting has killed hundreds, damaged oil refineries, raised global oil prices, and spread to Lebanon, where Israel sent troops to fight Hezbollah.
