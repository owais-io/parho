Former UK prime minister Gordon Brown calls for an international court that would try those who attack children in war. He cites the 2023 missile strike that killed 168 schoolgirls in Iran and argues schools should be protected like hospitals under law. Brown says current rules are weak and the new court would focus on bombed schools, abducted pupils, and child slavery, holding leaders accountable.
