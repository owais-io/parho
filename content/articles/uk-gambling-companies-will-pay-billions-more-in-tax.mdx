UK tax will hike duties on online gambling, adding about £1.1 bn a year by 2029. The rise doubles the remote‑gaming tax to 40 % and boosts sports‑bet duties to 25 % for online wagers, while bingo tax is removed. Shares of firms like Evoke fell 18 %, and companies warn of job cuts and lower profits. The government says the extra money will help curb addiction and fight the illegal gambling market.
