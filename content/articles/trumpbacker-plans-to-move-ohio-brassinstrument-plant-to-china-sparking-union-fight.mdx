John Paulson, a billionaire who backed Donald Trump, wants to move a 150‑job brass‑instrument factory in Eastlake, Ohio, to China by June 2026. Conn Selmer says the shift will boost competitiveness, but workers and the UAW union say it’s a betrayal and will hurt the town. The plant’s closure could cost the community money and jobs, and the union is fighting to keep it open. Despite Paulson’s public praise for American manufacturing, workers feel betrayed.
