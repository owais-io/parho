Florida’s wildlife commission said 52 black bears were killed in a December hunting season it says helps keep the population. The hunt used traps, bows and dogs, and the commission said it followed scientific data. Many Florida residents and wildlife groups say the hunt is cruel and based on bad science. They were upset by photos of hunters with dead bears and say the hunt will hurt Florida’s future. The commission says the hunt is safe and needed for conservation.
