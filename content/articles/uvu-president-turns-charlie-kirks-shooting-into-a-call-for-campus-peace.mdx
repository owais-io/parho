Astrid Tuminez, the first woman president of Utah Valley University, was stunned by the livestream of a Charlie Kirk shooting. Drawing on a life that took her from poverty in the Philippines to a PhD at MIT, she keeps the campus open to all voices. After Kirk’s death, she launched “Better Selves for a Better America,” a dialogue‑and‑mediation program. Her goal: a campus culture that values peace, not politics.
