Women in tech and finance are at higher risk of losing their jobs to AI and automation. A City of London report shows that mid‑career women are being overlooked by automated CV checks that ignore caregiving gaps, and that around 119,000 clerical roles could vanish in the next decade. Reskilling could save companies £757m in redundancy costs and keep talent pipelines strong. Employers and unions call for training investment to close the widening digital talent gap.
