Jofra Archer showed up to the Gabba with a pillow, which critics say made England look lazy in the Ashes series. After England lost the first Test, the team’s slow fielding and lack of fresh bowlers left them struggling. Archer, who was supposed to bowl with a fresh ball, didn’t take any wickets and spent the day resting. The image and England’s performance suggest the side is not pulling hard enough against Australia.
