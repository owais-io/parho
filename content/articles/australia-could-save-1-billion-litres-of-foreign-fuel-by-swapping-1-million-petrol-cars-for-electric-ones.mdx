If Australia swapped one million petrol cars for electric vehicles, it could cut foreign fuel use by more than 1 billion litres a year, experts say. Right now only 2 % of the 20 million car fleet is electric. Rising fuel prices from Middle‑East tensions are making EVs more attractive, and sales are already climbing. More EVs would reduce oil price shocks and help the country hit net‑zero goals.
