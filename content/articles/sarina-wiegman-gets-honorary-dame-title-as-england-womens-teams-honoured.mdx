Sarina Wiegman has been given an honorary dame title after leading England’s women’s football team, the Lionesses, to two European titles. The honours list also celebrates England’s women’s rugby team, the Red Roses, who won the World Cup. Several players, coaches and officials from both sports received OBEs, MBEs and other awards. The government praised the historic achievements and highlighted how these women have inspired the country.
