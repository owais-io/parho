Kemi Badenoch says a national investigation into grooming gangs must look at ethnicity, religion and culture. She criticised the government for slow progress and said survivors need support. Opposition wants a two‑year deadline and wants police, councils and the government examined. The government’s plans have stalled, with chair candidates dropping out and victims leaving the panel. The inquiry still has not started.
