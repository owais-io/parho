New victims advocate Cindy Butts says delayed justice after disasters like Grenfell and Hillsborough is a shame and a stain on society. She wants to stop families from acting as their own investigators, give them support, and make sure the truth comes out quickly. Since starting, she has set up help for the Manchester synagogue attack victims. She will push for better resources, more independent reports, and stronger laws to hold officials accountable.
