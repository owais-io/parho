Trump says he's extending his defamation case against the New York Times after a Siena poll showed his approval rating at 40%. He accuses the paper of publishing false results and demands they reveal how the poll was calculated. The Times defended its rigorous methodology. The lawsuit follows a previous $15 billion claim that was dismissed last year, with Trump insisting the Times must be held accountable for “fake news.”
