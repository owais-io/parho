Phil Brickell, the MP for Bolton West, says the Office for Students has been too slow in investigating fraud, bullying and mismanagement at the University of Greater Manchester. He says the regulator has ignored whistleblowers and media reports for almost a year, leaving about 11,000 students and staff confused and worried. Brickell has written angrily to the education secretary, demanding the regulator explain the delay and act quickly.
