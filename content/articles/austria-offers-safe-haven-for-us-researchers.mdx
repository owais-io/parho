After the Trump administration cut science funding and threatened researchers, many U.S. academics are leaving for safer places. Wali Malik, a robotics expert, moved to Vienna, joining Aithyra, a new life‑sciences lab that uses AI. Austria’s ministry wants 50 U.S. scientists in a year, offering fast‑track hiring and fellowships. The country’s “scientific asylum” has drawn scholars from Yale, Toronto, and France, turning it into a brain‑gain hub.
