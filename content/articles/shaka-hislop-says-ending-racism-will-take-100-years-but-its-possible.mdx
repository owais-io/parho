Shaka Hislop, former Newcastle goalkeeper, says breaking racism will be a long fight, but progress is happening. A scary incident at a petrol station in 1995 spurred him to join anti‑racism work with Show Racism the Red Card, teaching kids and football fans. Now a cancer survivor and analyst, he urges patience, noting 100 years to erase racism, yet hope remains.
