Donald Trump hosted a New Year's Eve bash at his Mar‑a‑Lago club, where he auctioned a freshly painted portrait of Jesus for $2.75 million. He said the proceeds would split between St. Jude’s Children’s Hospital and the local sheriff’s department. Trump called the artist a world‑class talent and wished for “peace on Earth.” Guests included former adviser Rudy Giuliani, Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, and other high‑profile figures. Meanwhile, Trump posted controversial comments on social media.
