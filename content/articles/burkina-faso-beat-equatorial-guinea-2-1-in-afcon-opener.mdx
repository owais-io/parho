Burkina Faso won their first Africa Cup of Nations match against Equatorial Guinea 2‑1. They scored early with a headed goal from Tapsoba after a cross by Irie, and sealed the win in stoppage time when Minoungou netted the second. Equatorial Guinea played with 10 men at times, struggled to keep the ball, and received a red card. Burkina dominated possession and showed they’re ready to push for the semi‑finals.
