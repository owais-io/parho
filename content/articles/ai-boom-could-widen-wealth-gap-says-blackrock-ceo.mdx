BlackRock chief Larry Fink says the rapid rise of artificial intelligence could make the gap between rich and poor bigger. He points out that only a few big companies and investors stand to profit from AI, and that could leave many people behind. Fink says we need to open the stock market to more people so they can share the gains. He warns that AI could make wealth more concentrated.
