Former Northern Ireland U‑21 footballer Amrit Bansal‑McNulty says he was racially abused by Crawley manager John Yems and that Queens Park Rangers and Crawley Town didn’t protect him. He’s suing the clubs for £11.1 million (about £6 million after tax) for lost career chances and personal injury. A tribunal hearing is underway, while both clubs deny wrongdoing and Yems has faced a long FA ban for discrimination.
