Iran has seen huge protests over high prices and a falling currency, sparking a violent crackdown by security forces. The government has shut down the internet for over a month, making it hard to know what’s happening. At least 500 people, mostly protesters, have died, and more than 10,000 were arrested. Trump says Iran’s leader wants to negotiate, but he’s also weighing a possible military strike. Sanctions and diplomatic tensions add to the chaos.
