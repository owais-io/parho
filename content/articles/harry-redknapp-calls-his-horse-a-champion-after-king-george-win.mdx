Harry Redknapp, the owner of The Jukebox Man, proudly announced his horse’s win in the King George VI Chase, calling it a “Champions League” moment. The race was a dramatic photo‑finish, with Jukebox Man beating favourites like Jango Baie and Gaelic Warrior. Redknapp praised the horse’s spirit and plans to target the Cheltenham Gold Cup next, celebrating the excitement of the steeplechase.
