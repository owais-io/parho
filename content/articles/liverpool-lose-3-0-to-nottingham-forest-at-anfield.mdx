Nottingham Forest beat Liverpool 3-0 at Anfield for the second time this season. Murillo, Savona and Gibbs‑White scored, giving Forest their biggest win at the stadium. Liverpool struggled all day, missing chances, losing set‑piece work and defensive lapses. Midfielder Alexander Isak was taken off early. The defeat marks Liverpool's eighth loss in eleven games and drops them into the bottom half of the Premier League table.
