Storm Bram is making life dangerous in parts of the UK. Heavy rain is flooding streets and rail lines, and wind gusts up to 80‑90 mph could tear roofs or send debris. Train services from London to Wales and in Scotland are delayed or cancelled, and buses are running instead in some areas. In Northern Ireland, a yellow warning keeps people on alert for strong winds and possible power cuts. Stay safe, check transport updates and keep supplies handy.
