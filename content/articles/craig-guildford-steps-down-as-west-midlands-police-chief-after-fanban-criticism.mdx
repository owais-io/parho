West Midlands Police Chief Craig Guildford has resigned after a report said his force misused evidence and an AI tool to ban Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a Birmingham match. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood told MPs she no longer trusts him, forcing his exit. The case highlights how politicians can pressure police leaders and raises questions about whether police should stay local or be overseen by the central government.
