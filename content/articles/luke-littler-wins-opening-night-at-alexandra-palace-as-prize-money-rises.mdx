Luke Littler beat Darius Labanauskas 3‑0 on the opening night of the darts championship at Alexandra Palace, the first of a 128‑player event. The tournament has doubled prize money to £1 million, a huge change that can change a player’s career. While big‑money winners like Littler and newcomer Arno Merk can earn £25,000 or more, many pros still struggle with smaller weekly tournaments. The event is growing fast but keeps its old‑school feel.
