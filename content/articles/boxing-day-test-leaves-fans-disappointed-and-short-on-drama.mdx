During the Boxing Day Test, a huge crowd turned to disappointment as the match ended in a messy, wicket‑heavy flurry. England and Australia both struggled on a tough pitch, and the game finished early with little drama. Fans who had planned their holidays and spent money on tickets felt let down, and local vendors and broadcasters lost revenue. The match’s poor timing and lack of excitement made it a flop for everyone.
