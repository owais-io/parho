Liverpool earned a 1-1 draw at Anfield against Burnley when Marcus Edwards scored the equaliser. The match saw Liverpool create many chances but struggled to finish. Fans booed as the team failed to win at home, and manager Arne Slot admitted frustration. Burnley’s defender Bashir Humphreys made crucial saves, while Liverpool’s key players missed opportunities. The game highlighted Liverpool’s ongoing struggles to secure victories.
