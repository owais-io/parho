Families of Australian soldiers whose graves were in the Gaza War Cemetery say Israeli bulldozers destroyed a corner of the site, flattening graves like that of Patrick Cotterill. They demand the cemetery be restored and safe‑passage for officials to inspect the damage. The Australian government, the RSL and the Commonwealth War Graves Commission have all asked Israel to repair the graves, while the IDF claims the work was defensive. The conflict in Gaza continues to threaten the site.
