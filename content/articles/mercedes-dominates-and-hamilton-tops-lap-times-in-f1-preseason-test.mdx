F1’s first pre‑season test in Barcelona went off without a hitch. Mercedes ran almost flawlessly, covering a huge distance and showing their new engine is reliable. Lewis Hamilton of Ferrari took the fastest lap of the week, giving his team a boost. Other teams – Red Bull, Audi and Aston Martin – had quieter runs, with some struggling to get the new engines up to speed. The test was a good sign for the new season.
