Labour minister Josh Simons is under fire after he falsely accused journalists of ties to Russian intelligence. A Guardian investigation found no hack or evidence of Russian links, just a mistaken claim. The scandal has sparked pressure from politicians across parties to sack or make him resign, as his conduct is seen as a breach of ministerial ethics and trust.
