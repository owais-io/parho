The Guardian has issued a list of corrections for several stories it published earlier this month. Highlights include changing the quiche to free‑range eggs, clarifying that most Warhammer models use only a few pieces, fixing the spelling of the University of Exeter’s student paper to Exeposé, and correcting other errors about a power broker, a chess review, and a vaccine mix‑up. Readers can send complaints or questions to the Guardian’s editor.
