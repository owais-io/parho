Gregory Bovino, a top U.S. border‑patrol officer, was pulled from his Minneapolis post after a wave of backlash over a shooting that killed Alex Pretti. Bovino had made false claims that Pretti was a threat, and his videos and speeches were criticized as aggressive and militaristic. The White House ordered his removal, and the Department of Homeland Security cut his social‑media access, marking a sharp fall for the once‑celebrated agent.
