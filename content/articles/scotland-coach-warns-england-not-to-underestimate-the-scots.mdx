Scottish coach Gregor Townsend is warning England not to underestimate the Scots as they face them in the Calcutta Cup match at Murrayfield. He has shuffled the lineup, keeping most of the back three but dropping two forwards, and says the crowd can help drive a win. With England on a long unbeaten run, Scotland wants to reclaim the cup and prove they can beat the hosts.
