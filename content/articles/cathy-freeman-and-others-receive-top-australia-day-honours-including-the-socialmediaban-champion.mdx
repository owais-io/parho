On Australia Day, the country celebrated 949 Australians with awards for service to sport, science, arts and public life. Olympic star Cathy Freeman was made a Companion of the Order of Australia for her athletic achievements and community work. Julie Inman Grant, who helped pass the country’s first social‑media ban, received a Public Service Medal. Other honorees included skateboarder Renton Millar, quantum physicist Michelle Simmons, and rock legend Rob Hirst.
