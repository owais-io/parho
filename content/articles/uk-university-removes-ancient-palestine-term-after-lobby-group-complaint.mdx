A UK university changed its course materials after a pro‑Israel lobbying group complained that the phrase “ancient Palestine” was historically inaccurate and could hurt Jewish students. The Open University said the term was academically correct but agreed to remove it and add a note. Critics say the move shows political pressure on universities and threatens free speech and Palestinian history.
