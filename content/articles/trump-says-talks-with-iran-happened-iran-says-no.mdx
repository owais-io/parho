President Trump says there were talks between the U.S. and Iran, claiming the two sides reached major points of agreement. Iran says no talks have happened since U.S. airstrikes began. Trump extended his 48‑hour deadline to 53 hours and warned of attacks if Iran didn't open the Strait of Hormuz. Israel also launched strikes but promised to spare energy sites. Pakistan and other Gulf states say they are passing messages, and a meeting may take place in Islamabad.
