Russia has fired missiles and drones at Ukraine’s capital Kyiv and the city of Kharkiv, killing one person and injuring many. The attacks happened as Russian and Ukrainian leaders, with U.S. officials, meet in Abu Dhabi for a high‑level peace discussion. Ukraine’s energy and heating systems are suffering because of the attacks, leaving many without power during a harsh winter. The talks focus on military demands, with Russia insisting Ukraine return to Donbas first.
