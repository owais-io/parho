England lost 4‑1 in the Ashes, sparking talk about who stays in charge. Ben Stokes said he fully backs Brendon McCullum as head coach, even though the team was beaten badly in Sydney. McCullum admits he needs to improve but will not let anyone dictate his methods. The ECB has started a review of England’s preparation and play style. Stokes vows to stay captain and help the side rebound.
