Armed police and security forces flooded Iran’s universities to stop a fourth day of student protests against Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. On some campuses, students fought with Basij militiamen and pick‑up trucks parked outside the University of Tehran had machine‑guns. Students who had been involved in earlier protests were barred from entering and classes were closed. The unrest is happening while Iran’s nuclear talks with the U.S. continue. Human rights groups say more than 7,000 people died in January protests.
