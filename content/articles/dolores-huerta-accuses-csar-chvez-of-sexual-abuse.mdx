Dolores Huerta, the UFW co‑founder, has said she was sexually abused by César Chávez in the 1960s. She released a statement on Medium after a New York Times investigation, claiming Chávez forced her to have sex twice—once when she was 36 and earlier in a hotel room. Huerta explains she stayed silent for 60 years to protect the farm‑worker movement, adding her story to others’ allegations.
