Cory Bernardi, a One Nation candidate, posted two Facebook videos in February and March that mocked Kaurna language signs at Adelaide University and a hospital. He dismissed the greetings as meaningless and claimed using Aboriginal words was erasing history. Kaurna elders and politicians called the videos disrespectful and harmful. The South Australian government urged unity, while opposition leaders debated whether to support One Nation, sparking a broader debate over Indigenous naming rights.
