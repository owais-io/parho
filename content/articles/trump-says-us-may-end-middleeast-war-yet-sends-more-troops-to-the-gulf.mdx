President Trump said the U.S. might “wind down” fighting in the Middle East, but the government is simultaneously moving three amphibious ships and about 2,500 marines into the Gulf. Trump also talked about possibly blocking Iran’s Kharg Island, a key oil hub. Meanwhile, Iran has fired drones and missiles, and U.S. and Israeli forces keep striking Iranian ships and facilities, keeping tensions high and oil prices up.
