The Guardian apologised for a series of mistakes in recent pieces. A photo of singer‑producer Hassan Matthews was wrongly placed in an article about rapper Dot Rotten’s death. An editorial also mis‑named the AI report it cited, referring to the 2026 International AI Safety Report instead of the 2025 Statement on Inclusive and Sustainable Artificial Intelligence. Other corrections covered a krill‑harvesting claim, a Ukraine briefing, and a California motel incident.
