North Korea finished a new street of houses in Pyongyang for families of soldiers who died fighting for Russia in Ukraine. The project, called Saeppyol Street, was unveiled by Kim Jong‑un and his daughter, and the leader said it would give families a place to live and show pride in their sons. The move is part of a propaganda push to keep people loyal and justify the large number of North Korean troops sent to the war.
