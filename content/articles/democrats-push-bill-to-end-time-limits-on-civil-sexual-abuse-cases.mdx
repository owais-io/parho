In Congress, Democrats have unveiled a new bill called Virginia’s Law, which would eliminate the time limits that currently prevent people from suing for civil sexual abuse. The bill also stops offenders from escaping justice by moving cases to other states. The push comes after the release of huge amounts of Epstein documents and was joined by survivors, including Virginia Giuffre, and her family. Senators and House members emphasize the need to give victims a chance to be heard.
