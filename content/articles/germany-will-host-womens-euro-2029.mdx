Germany has been chosen to host the 2029 Women’s European Championship after winning a landslide vote from UEFA’s committee. The German bid promised to sell a record one‑million tickets and beats bids from Poland and a Denmark‑Sweden partnership. Eight German cities will host matches, with the biggest stadium being Munich’s Allianz Arena. The move follows England’s record‑attended 2022 final. Meanwhile, England has named Gavin Step its new women’s technical director.
