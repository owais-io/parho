A few weeks before Jeffrey Epstein died in jail, his lawyers and federal prosecutors in Manhattan met to talk about whether he could cooperate with the case. Documents show they discussed a possible resolution, but the lawyers didn’t propose any concrete plan. Epstein had been charged with sex‑trafficking minors and died by suicide after being found unconscious in his cell.
