**  
In Colorado, an 18‑year‑old named Max Osterman died after buying fentanyl‑laced pain pills from a Snapchat dealer. Police said Snapchat and Meta kept them waiting for months, even after several legal orders, and never gave them Max’s messages. The delays hurt cases of child sexual abuse and drug‑related deaths. Law‑enforcement officers say tech firms play legal games, while experts call for faster rules and new laws to force quicker help.
