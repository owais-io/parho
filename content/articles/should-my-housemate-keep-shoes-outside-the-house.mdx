Zara and Maya have lived together for over a year, but their views on shoes are at odds. Zara, from a culture that leaves shoes outside, thinks Maya should always remove them inside to keep the floors clean. Maya says she forgets sometimes and that shoes are part of her routine. The story shows how different backgrounds can clash over simple house rules and the need for compromise.
