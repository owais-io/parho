Elena Rybakina beat world No.1 Aryna Sabalenka to win the Australian Open, her second Grand Slam after Wimbledon. She says hard work and a supportive team kept her confident. The victory follows her coach’s suspension being lifted last year. Sabalenka, frustrated by the loss, praised Rybakina’s play but said she fought well. Rybakina hopes the title drives her through the rest of the season.
