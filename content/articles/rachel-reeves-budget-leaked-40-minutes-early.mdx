On a Wednesday, the Office for Budget Responsibility accidentally posted Rachel Reeves’ budget forecast online 40 minutes before she was supposed to speak. The slip sent markets reacting – the pound rose, bond yields fell. Reeves was shocked, and OBR said it was a mistake and launched an investigation. Critics say the leak shows a pattern of budget leaks, and some MPs even called it a criminal act.
