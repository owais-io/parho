A year ago, the Trump team stopped U.S. money that helped feed and educate 70,000 children in Timor‑Leste. The Care Australia program “Hatutan” can now help only about 12,000 kids, and staff were let go. The cuts also halted many U.S. programmes worldwide, leading to thousands of deaths from disease and hunger. Australia is trying to fill the gaps, but the damage is still big.
