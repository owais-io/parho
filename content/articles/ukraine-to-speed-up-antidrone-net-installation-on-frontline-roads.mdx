Ukraine is rushing to put anti‑drone nets on 4,000 km of frontline roads by year‑end, spending an extra 1.6 bn hryvnias ($37 m). They’ve doubled coverage speed from 5 km a day in January to 12 km in February and plan 20 km a day in March. The move comes after Russian drones hit supply lines, hospitals and civilian traffic. Meanwhile Ukraine is preparing for Geneva talks and has opened a drone‑making plant in Britain.
