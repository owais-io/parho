Guardiola said he couldn’t enjoy City’s 5‑4 win over Fulham because the game almost turned into a loss. City led 5‑1 after 54 minutes, then Fulham scored three, with Samuel Chukwueze netting twice. City stayed in front and won. Guardiola talked about the emotional roller‑coaster, praised striker Erling Haaland for becoming the fastest to 100 Premier League goals, and said the team needs to defend better.
