After the US and Israel attacked Iran, the UK sent its only destroyer, HMS Dragon, to Cyprus. The ship had to be rushed out of dry‑dock and was only ready after several days, showing how thin the UK’s navy really is. The government has cut ship numbers and defence spending, so the country is not prepared for sudden conflict. Prime Minister Starmer said Britain would not join a wider war, keeping the military at low levels.
