Russia says Ukraine fired 91 drones at President Putin’s home, claiming they were shot down by air defence. Ukraine denies it, calling the story false and blaming Russia for trying to damage talks with the U.S. President. President Trump appeared to believe the claim, while most experts doubt it and say there is no proof. The incident comes as Russia tries to influence U.S. opinion during a fragile peace push.
