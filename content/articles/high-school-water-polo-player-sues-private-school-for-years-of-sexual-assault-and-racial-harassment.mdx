An 18‑year‑old Black water‑polo player, Aidan Romain, has sued Harvard‑Westlake School in Los Angeles. He says teammates sexually assaulted him and used racist slurs from 2022 to early 2024. Romain claims the school didn’t report or stop the abuse and even retaliated against him. The suit names the school’s president, the water‑polo coach, a former teammate and the school itself. The case shines a light on abuse in elite sports programs.
