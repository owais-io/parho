Meghan says the Daily Mail crossed ethical lines by reporting from her estranged father’s hospital bed after his surgery. She claims reporters broadcasted their interactions and made it hard for her to reach him. The paper denies wrongdoing, saying the journalist had the father's consent and was a friend. The dispute comes as Prince Harry plans to sue the publisher over alleged illegal information gathering.
