New Zealand has said it would support the UK’s decision to strip Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor of his place in the line to the throne after his arrest over alleged misconduct. The move follows a similar statement from Australia. The UK may pass a law to remove him, which would require agreement from all Commonwealth realms where the monarch is head of state.
