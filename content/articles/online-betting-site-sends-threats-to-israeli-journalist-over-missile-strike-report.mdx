An Israeli reporter, Emanuel Fabian, was harassed by users of the prediction market Polymarket after he published a brief story about a missile strike near Jerusalem. Gamblers who had placed a huge bet on whether Iran would attack Israel demanded he change the article, threatening him and his family. The incident highlights how online betting platforms are now influencing war reporting and can create dangerous pressure on journalists.
