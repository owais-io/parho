The conflict between Israel, the U.S., and Iran has pushed up oil, gas, and even flight and grocery prices in Europe. Fuel prices are rising, heating oil costs are up by more than a third, and flights to Asia are now expensive and hard to book. The conflict also threatens grain and nut supplies and could raise insurance costs for ships. Europe watches closely as prices climb.
