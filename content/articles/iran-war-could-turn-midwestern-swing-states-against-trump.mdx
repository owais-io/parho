Trump promised to bring peace to the Middle East, but the U.S. has launched several wars, including a costly conflict with Iran that has driven up gas prices. In swing counties in Michigan, Wisconsin, and Pennsylvania, many voters—especially independents—are unhappy with the war and its cost. While most Republicans back Trump’s actions, the growing discontent could hurt his reelection bid in the coming elections.
