Angela Rayner’s tax investigation by HMRC is expected to be cleared before the May local elections, says her supporters. With the tax case finished and money from her memoir and talks, she could step back into politics. Rayner has spoken out against Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s plans and warned the Labour party is at risk. Some think a leadership challenge could happen if Starmer’s position weakens, but Rayner hasn’t confirmed she will run.
