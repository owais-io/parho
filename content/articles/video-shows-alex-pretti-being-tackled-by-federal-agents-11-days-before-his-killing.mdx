Video shows Alex Pretti being tackled by federal agents 11 days before his killing. Footage from January 13 in Minneapolis shows officers grabbing him, using tear gas and pepper balls, while he protests. Pretti had a concealed‑weapon permit but never fired a gun. His family says he posed no danger. The clip is part of wider protests against ICE’s crackdown in the city.
