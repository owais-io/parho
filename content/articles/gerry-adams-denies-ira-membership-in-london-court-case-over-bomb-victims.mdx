Gerry Adams, former Sinn Féin leader, is suing three victims of IRA bombings in a London civil court. The men want the judge to find that Adams was a senior IRA member and can be held personally liable. Adams has denied ever joining the IRA, saying his statements are true. Witnesses say he did, but he refuses to admit it. The case is still ongoing.
