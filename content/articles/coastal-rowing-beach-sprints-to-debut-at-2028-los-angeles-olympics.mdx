A new rowing event called Beach Sprints will be added to the 2028 Los Angeles Olympics. Runners sprint from the sand, jump into boats, race around a buoy and sprint back. It mixes speed, strength and a beach‑party vibe. The sport is growing in the UK, with coastal clubs and athletes training for the Games. It offers a lively, spectator‑friendly alternative to traditional rowing.
