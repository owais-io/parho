Elena Rybakina outplayed second‑seed Iga Swiatek in the Australian Open quarter‑final, winning 7‑5, 6‑1. The 26‑year‑old Kazakh used her powerful serve and aggressive groundstrokes to keep Swiatek under pressure the whole match. The win moves Rybakina into the semi‑finals, where she will face sixth‑seed Jessica Pegula. Swiatek said she will work on her serve for future tournaments.
