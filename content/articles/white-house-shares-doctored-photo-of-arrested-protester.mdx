The White House posted a photo that was digitally altered to make a woman who was arrested during an ICE protest look like she was crying and to change her skin tone. The original picture from the police showed her calm and handcuffed. A Guardian check matched the images and proved the changes. The White House defended the post, while critics say it spreads false information.
