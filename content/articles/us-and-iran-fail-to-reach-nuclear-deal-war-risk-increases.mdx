U.S. and Iran ended nuclear talks without a deal, raising the risk of U.S. military action. Both sides claimed some progress, but key issues like uranium enrichment were unresolved. The U.S. team, led by Trump’s envoy, was disappointed with Iran's proposals, while Iran said it would keep in touch. Oman mediators said talks would restart next week, but tensions remain high.
