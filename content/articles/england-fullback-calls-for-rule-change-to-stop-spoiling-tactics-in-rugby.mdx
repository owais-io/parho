England rugby full‑back Freddie Steward says current rules let players spoil high kicks, making it hard to catch the ball. He wants a change that bans one‑handed “flapping” and forces jumpers to use both hands. Steward says the new law makes the air a tougher, more physical place, increasing injury risk. Some fans like the rule, while others think it keeps the contest fair.
