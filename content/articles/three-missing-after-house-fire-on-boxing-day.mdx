On Boxing Day early morning, a house on Brimscombe Hill near Stroud caught fire. Police say one person escaped and was taken to hospital. Three others, thought to be inside, are still missing. Emergency crews closed the road and are searching. The cause of the fire is still being investigated. Residents are asked to stay away and anyone with info can call 101.
