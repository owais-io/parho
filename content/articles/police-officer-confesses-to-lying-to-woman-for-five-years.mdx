A former undercover police officer named Mark Jenner has admitted that he deceived a woman named Alison and kept a secret relationship with her for five years while spying on left‑wing activists. He used the romance to appear trustworthy and gather information. Jenner also lied to his own wife and told no one the truth. He gave evidence in a government inquiry that is examining how undercover officers operated and treated people.
