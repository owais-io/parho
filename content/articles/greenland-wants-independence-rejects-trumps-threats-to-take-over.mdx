Greenland politicians from all five parties said Greenlanders want to stay independent and decide their own future, rejecting both Denmark and the United States. They issued a joint statement after President Donald Trump warned he would “take” the island if needed for national security. A 2025 poll shows 85 % of Greenlanders oppose American annexation, while only about 7 % of Americans support a military takeover. Denmark’s prime minister has called Trump’s threats unacceptable.
