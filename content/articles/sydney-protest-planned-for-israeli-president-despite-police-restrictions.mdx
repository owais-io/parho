Palestine Action Group wants to march in Sydney against Israeli President Isaac Herzog, but NSW police have extended protest restrictions after the Bondi terror attack. The ban stops organisers from getting the usual approval and could lead to arrests. The law, which allows 14‑day extensions up to 90 days, is now being challenged in court. Protesters call for police cooperation and stress their right to peaceful demonstration.
