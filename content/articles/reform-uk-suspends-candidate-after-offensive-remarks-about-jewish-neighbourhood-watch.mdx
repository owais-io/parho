Reform UK has dropped Chris Parry from its Hampshire mayoral race after he compared the Jewish neighbourhood watch group Shomrim to “cosplayers” and “Islamists on horseback”. Parry’s remarks came after an arson attack on a Jewish charity ambulance service and followed earlier offensive comments he made online. The party is investigating him and will replace him before the May elections.
