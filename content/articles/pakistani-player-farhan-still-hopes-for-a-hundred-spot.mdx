Pakistani batsman Farhan hopes to earn a spot in England’s Hundred league, even though the four teams owned by Indian franchises have said they won’t pick Pakistani players. He’s one of 63 Pakistanis on a 710‑player longlist, but selection is outside his control. The ECB warned against discrimination, and Farhan says any team that wants him will get him for the upcoming season.
