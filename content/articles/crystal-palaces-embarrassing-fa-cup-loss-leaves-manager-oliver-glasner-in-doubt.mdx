Crystal Palace lost badly to Macclesfield in the FA Cup, a team from the sixth tier. Manager Oliver Glasner was stunned and said he had no explanation. The defeat has added to a nine‑game winless streak, shaking the squad’s confidence. Injuries, lack of depth and financial worries make it hard for the club to keep Glasner. Fans hope the team can fix these problems and avoid another shocking loss.
