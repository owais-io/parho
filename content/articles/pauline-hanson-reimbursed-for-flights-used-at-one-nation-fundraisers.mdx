Pauline Hanson has used taxpayer money to pay for thousands of flights and car rides that took her and her daughter to One Nation fundraising events across Australia. The Guardian found that her expenses were listed as parliamentary travel, even though the trips were mainly for political campaigning. The costs included trips to Adelaide, Brisbane, Sydney, Perth and Tasmania. Parliament rules say travel must be for official work, not fundraising.
