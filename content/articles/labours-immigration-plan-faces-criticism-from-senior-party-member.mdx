Alf Dubs, a senior Labour peer and former refugee, says the party's new hard‑line immigration plan is a mistake after Labour lost a safe seat to the Greens. He wants the government to adopt a kinder approach, especially for child refugees and family reunions. The new scheme would make people who enter illegally wait 20 years for permanent leave. The Greens say Labour is copying far‑right rhetoric.
