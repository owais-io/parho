Snow and ice across Europe killed six people and caused many road crashes. In France, three died on roads and two more were killed in Paris. A woman in Sarajevo died after a tree fell on her. Flights from Paris and Amsterdam were canceled while crews cleared snow. Schools closed in the UK, and storms caused flooding and fallen trees in Italy and the Balkans.
