Larry Summers, who ran Harvard from 2001 to 2006, will stop teaching and leave his post at the Kennedy School this academic year. He said he was stepping down while the university looks into documents linked to Jeffrey Epstein that were just released by the government. Emails show Summers and Epstein were friends for years. The move follows other questions about Summers’ ties to the convicted sex‑trafficker.
