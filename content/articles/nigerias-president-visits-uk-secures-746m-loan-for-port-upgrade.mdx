Nigeria’s president visited the UK for a state dinner, marking the first visit in 37 years. King Charles acknowledged the colonial past but said it should help shape a future. The UK will loan Nigeria £746m to upgrade two ports and supply 120,000 tonnes of steel to support a British steel company. The deal also boosts fintech and education ties. Nigeria wants to grow beyond oil and become a regional trade hub, but citizens worry about debt and fair benefits.
