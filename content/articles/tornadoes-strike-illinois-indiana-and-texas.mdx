A bad storm on Tuesday caused several tornadoes in eastern Illinois, Indiana, and central Texas. At least two people died in Indiana and many homes were destroyed. The National Weather Service warned that more storms, with heavy rain, hail and strong winds, could hit up to Michigan on Wednesday. First responders are clearing damaged homes and helping people, while officials say the worst of the weather may come later in the week.
