On Jan. 9, a wave of Russian attacks cut power in about 70 % of Kyiv, especially on the left‑bank side. Residents had to survive in freezing darkness, with no heat, water or cooking power. Families huddled in shelters and warming tents, while some left the city. Energy companies say the goal is to force people out. Similar outages hit Odesa, Dnipro and Zaporizhzhia, leaving many in a cold, power‑starved crisis.
