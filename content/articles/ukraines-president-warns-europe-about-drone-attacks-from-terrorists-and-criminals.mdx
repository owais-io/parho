Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, told British lawmakers that Europe must get ready for drone attacks from terrorists, criminals and lone operators. He explained that cheap, commercial drones can now be weaponised and that Russia, using Iranian‑made Shahed drones, is already striking Ukraine’s infrastructure. Zelenskyy said the same technology could be used by non‑state groups across Europe, so he urged allies to strengthen air‑defence and share Ukrainian support.
