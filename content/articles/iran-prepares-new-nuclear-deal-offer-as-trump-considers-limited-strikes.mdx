Iran’s foreign minister says a new draft for a nuclear deal will be ready in a few days, while President Trump is weighing limited strikes on Iran. The US has moved warships to the Middle East and has talked about targeting Iranian leaders. Iran’s nuclear program has expanded since Trump ended the 2015 deal, and the country says a military solution can’t stop its enrichment. Trump also threatened strikes after Iran’s crackdown on protests, sparking UN worries about escalating tensions.
