Steve Clarke, Scotland’s manager, wants to confirm whether Newcastle United midfielder Harvey Barnes is ready to switch his national team from England to Scotland before the country’s March friendly games. Barnes has played one match for England but said he’s open to Scotland. Clarke says the squad needs committed players and wants to keep team harmony. He also mentioned staying on as coach after the World Cup, pending talks with the FA.
