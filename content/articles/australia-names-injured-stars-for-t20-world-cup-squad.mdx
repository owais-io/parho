Australia announced a 15‑player squad for the February T20 World Cup in India and Sri Lanka. Star fast bowlers Pat Cummins, Josh Hazlewood and power‑hitter Tim David are in, even though they’re nursing injuries. The unexpected pick is left‑arm spinner Matthew Kuhnemann, who has played only four T20Is. New faces include Xavier Bartlett, Matt Short and Cooper Connolly, while Josh Inglis is the lone wicket‑keeper. Australia will start the tournament against Ireland in Colombo.
