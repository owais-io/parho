In Sydney, police pepper‑sprayed thousands who were gathered to protest Israeli President Isaac Herzog’s visit. The protest was held in a restricted area after a new law banned marches there. Police said they were protecting Herzog, but a state MP said officers were over‑reacting, and a Greens MP claimed she was punched. The incident drew criticism that letting people march might have avoided the clash.
