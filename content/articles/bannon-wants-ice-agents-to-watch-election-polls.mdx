Steve Bannon, a former White House adviser, wants federal immigration agents to watch polling places in the November midterms to stop “undocumented people” from voting. Election officials fear the move could intimidate voters and interfere with democracy. Trump has also pushed for federal control of elections and laws that would require proof of citizenship. Courts have blocked much of Trump’s executive order, and the Justice Department is fighting to keep voter rolls private.
