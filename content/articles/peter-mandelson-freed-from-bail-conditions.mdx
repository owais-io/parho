Police dropped all bail conditions for former cabinet minister Peter Mandelson, saying he’s not a flight risk, but the investigation into alleged misconduct continues. He was arrested after a tip that he might flee to the British Virgin Islands and was suspected of leaking Downing Street emails to Jeffrey Epstein. Mandelson denies wrongdoing. The case follows documents showing his long contact with Epstein and has led to resignations within the government.
