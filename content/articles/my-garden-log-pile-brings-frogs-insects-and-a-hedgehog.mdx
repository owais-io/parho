In a simple garden log pile, the writer explains how dead wood attracts frogs, insects, and even a hedgehog. She collects logs from neighbours and trees, builds a log heap that supports diverse fungi, beetles, spiders, and more. While adding a hedgehog box, she carefully moves frogs and other creatures, keeping the pile healthy and inviting for wildlife. This creates a small ecosystem right in her backyard.
