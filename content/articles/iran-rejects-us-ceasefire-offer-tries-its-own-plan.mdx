Iran says it will not accept the U.S. cease‑fire proposal it received via Pakistan. Instead, Tehran has its own five‑point plan that demands an end to fighting, protection of its officials, no new war against it, reparations, and Iranian control over the Hormuz Strait. The U.S. and other countries keep talking, but talks are stalled. Meanwhile Israel keeps striking Iranian targets and Iran fires rockets at Israel and the Gulf.
