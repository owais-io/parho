Australia’s state coroner, Teresa O’Sullivan, said a psychiatrist didn’t spot a relapse in Joel Cauchi before he killed six people in a Bondi Junction mall in 2024. The 837‑page report names the doctor, Andrea Boros‑Lavack, as having missed warning signs and let him go back to his GP. The findings call for reforms in New South Wales’ mental‑health system, better housing for the homeless with mental illness, and stronger emergency‑service coordination.
