Reyna, who hasn't played much for Borussia Mönchengladbach, was called up for a friendly in Philadelphia. He started, scored a header in the first five minutes, and set up the winner to give the US a 2‑1 victory over Paraguay. The win gives coach Mauricio Pochettino a promising option ahead of World Cup preparations, and shows the team can adapt to new defensive and attacking roles.
