Former Premier League referee Chris Foy talks about the growing rule that handballs giving away penalty chances are only punished if they are deliberate. He explains how he and VAR kept a high bar, citing matches where he did not award penalties for Harry Maguire or Patrick Dorgu, but did so for Jaydee Canvot and Yerson Mosquera. Foy says this consistency shows referees are using a mature, clear approach.
