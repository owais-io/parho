After stepping down, 83‑year‑old Joe Biden has mostly stayed out of the spotlight. He’s been busy writing a bestselling memoir, dealing with prostate cancer and trying to fund a presidential library, but his public appearances are rare. Meanwhile, former President Trump constantly attacks Biden in speeches and on social media, painting him as a weak leader. Many Democrats see Biden’s fade‑out as a sign his legacy is slipping, but Trump keeps using the former president to stir debate.
