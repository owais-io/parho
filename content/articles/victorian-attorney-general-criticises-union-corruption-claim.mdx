Victoria's attorney general, Sonya Kilkenny, blasted integrity expert Geoffrey Watson after he said CFMEU union corruption cost taxpayers $15 billion. Watson said the figure was a rough estimate based on expert opinions, but the report's chapters were removed for lack of evidence. Kilkenny called the claim reckless, while police minister Anthony Carbines also denounced Watson. Watson replied that officials were attacking him instead of addressing real corruption.
