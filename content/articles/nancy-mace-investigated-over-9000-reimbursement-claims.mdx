Nancy Mace, a Republican congresswoman, is under a House ethics probe after a watchdog group said she may have claimed more than $9,000 in housing subsidies that were not hers. The report says she asked for the full amount even though she owns only 28% of the D.C. house. Mace refused to talk to the investigators and says the findings are flawed. The case could hurt her bid for South Carolina governor.
