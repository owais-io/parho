Greenland's new prime minister, Jens‑Frederik Nielsen, surprised the world when he firmly rejected U.S. President Donald Trump’s claim that Greenland could be taken by force. The 34‑year‑old, a former badminton champion, won a coalition government after a surprise election win. He chose Denmark, NATO and the EU over the U.S., and his stance has earned praise from Denmark, Germany and France. Locals see him as a strong, calm leader under pressure.
