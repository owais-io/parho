Ahmedabad has been chosen to host the 2030 Commonwealth Games and has promised it will not repeat the problems that plagued Delhi in 2010. Organisers say they already have most venues ready, budgets in place, and will feature 15‑17 sports, more than the 10 planned for next summer’s Glasgow Games. They want to show India can run a smooth, large‑scale event and celebrate the Commonwealth’s 100‑year anniversary.
