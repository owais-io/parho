The BBC has split World Cup presenting between Kelly Cates, Gabby Logan and Mark Chapman, each sharing the Match of the Day role. There’s no clear favourite for the final, and the network still hasn’t chosen who will host it. Gary Lineker left after an antisemitic incident, so the hosts now share the workload. BBC podcasts and new digital plans have boosted viewership, while covering a 48‑team tournament from Salford strains costs.
