Tom Steyer, a wealthy environmental activist who ran for president in 2020, announced he will seek California’s governor seat. He says he wants to fix the state’s housing crisis and make utilities cheaper, and will push for corporate taxes and free preschool and community college. He joins a field that includes former congresswoman Katie Porter and former California officials. Steyer is a longtime donor to Democrats and has spent millions on climate and social causes.
