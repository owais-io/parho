Mexico’s Sinaloa cartel war is still raging, and Arizona guns are a major reason. Data shows 62 % of the guns Mexico seized in 2024 that were bought in the US within a year come from Arizona, making it the biggest source after Texas. Because Mexican law restricts gun sales, cartels buy weapons across the border, often using American “straw purchasers.” Mexico is suing Arizona gun stores, and the president has asked the U.S. to help stop the flow.
