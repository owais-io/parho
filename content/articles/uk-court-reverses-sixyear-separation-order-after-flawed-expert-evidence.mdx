A UK mother who had been cut off from her children for almost six years because of faulty court evidence has finally been reunited with her son. The judge overturned earlier harsh orders based on an unregulated psychologist’s testimony, which had removed the kids from the mother. New guidance now requires such experts to be regulated, and the court is working to change the rules.
