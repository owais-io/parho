Providence police posted a new video of the suspect in the Brown University shooting, asking residents to spot details like posture and gait. The masked man was seen walking the area before the attack. Police say he's “casing the area.” They’re still chasing him, with 200 actionable tips and a $50,000 FBI reward. Local officials say the investigation is going well but urge patience while they collect any footage from the last week.
