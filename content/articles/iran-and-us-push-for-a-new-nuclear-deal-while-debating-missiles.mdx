Iran and the United States are gearing up for talks in Geneva, with Iran demanding it can enrich uranium to about 5% purity, keep its missile program open, and get a lift on sanctions. U.S. officials like Marco Rubio and special envoy Steve Witkoff have offered these terms, but President Trump has shifted positions, warning that Iran could build weapons and threatening war. Both sides are hoping a deal will avoid conflict and ease economic pressure on Iran.
