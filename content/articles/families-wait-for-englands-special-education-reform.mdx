England’s special education system is in crisis, and parents are desperate for help. The government is set to announce a big overhaul, but families worry it could cut support they rely on, like EHCPs and specialist schools. Stories of children with autism, ADHD, and other needs show long waitlists, limited places, and financial pressure on councils. Mothers hope reforms will finally bring the resources and flexibility they need.
