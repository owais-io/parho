Luke Humphries, the 2024 world champion, lost 4‑2 to Gabriel Clemens in the first round. Clemens, who had struggled for three years, came back from 3‑0 down to level 3‑3 and then won the deciding set. He averaged 115.6 in the final set, becoming the first German to hit a three‑figure average at a world final. The upset shows how quickly form can change and sets the stage for a tense tournament.
