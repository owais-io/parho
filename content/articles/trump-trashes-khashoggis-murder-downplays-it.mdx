Trump dismissed the killing of journalist Jamal Khashoggi during a press conference with Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman. He said “things happen” and blamed the victim, contradicting U.S. intelligence that linked the prince to the murder. His comments show a new low in respect for the truth and journalists. The CPJ says such attacks on reporters make the world less safe and let killers get away with it.
