After her husband died, a woman struggled to get solar‑energy payments from ScottishPower. The company kept the money in his account, asked for endless paperwork, and lost her documents. Emails were sent to the deceased and staff failed to coordinate. Only after weeks of complaints did ScottishPower finally pay the money, but no interest, and offered low compensation. Finally they agreed to £300, which she accepted.
