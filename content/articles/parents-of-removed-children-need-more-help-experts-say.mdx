Parents whose babies are taken into care should get trauma‑aware help, a child‑safety review says. After 2023 baby Victoria Marten’s death, experts say missing support for her parents may have worsened the cycle of abuse. The report urges better engagement with families, coordinated help after a child’s removal, and new rules to track high‑risk parents. It also calls for the state to keep sex offenders in the loop about new partners and pregnancies.
