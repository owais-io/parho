Blackburn Rovers are in turmoil. Fans are unhappy with owner Venky’s and many are boycotting games and refusing to buy merchandise. Manager Valérien Ismaël has only one win in 15 matches, with a 1‑0 loss to Hull keeping the team in the relegation zone. The club is in financial trouble, with kit supplier Macron suing them, and the manager is hoping to sign new players before the deadline. Fans and players are all worried about a League One drop.
