President Trump has asked European allies to send warships to guard the Strait of Hormuz after Iran blocked the waterway. Countries like France, the UK, Australia and Japan say they won't deploy ships, and the EU is only planning a small naval mission. The blockade has pushed oil prices above $104 a barrel and caused Japan to release emergency fuel reserves. Trump says the fight will end soon but has not set a timeline.
