The Philadelphia International Airport set a new Guinness record by building a 1,200‑foot long line of cheesesteaks. More than 100 staff and volunteers made the sandwiches using nearly 1,000 pounds of steak and 225 pounds of cheese sauce. After the record was confirmed, the sandwiches were given to airport workers, especially TSA staff who are still waiting for pay. The event highlighted local pride and community spirit.
