Senegal beat Morocco in a chaotic African Cup of Nations final. A controversial penalty and off‑side call sparked a 15‑minute delay. Senegal’s coach, Pape Thiaw, walked out with most players, but striker Sadio Mané coaxed them back. The match ended 2‑1 for Senegal thanks to a last‑minute goal by Papa Gueye. FIFA and CAF said the incident will be investigated, but no serious punishments were announced yet.
