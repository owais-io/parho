Grace Bell, who has a rare condition that left her without a womb, received a transplanted uterus from a donor who had died. In December, she gave birth to a healthy baby boy, Hugo, at Queen Charlotte’s Hospital in London. It’s the first UK birth using a womb from a deceased donor and the 25th‑30th worldwide. Bell says the donation is a miracle and thanks the donor’s family for their generosity.
