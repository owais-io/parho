In the first quarter, Manchester United earned a £13 m operating profit, up from a £7 m loss a year earlier. That comes after new owner Sir Jim Ratcliffe cut costs, including 450 staff, and ended some perks. But debt hit a record £1.29 bn. Revenue slipped to £140 m and cash on hand fell to £80 m. The club is seeking a new kit sponsor to replace the one that ended.
