Trump may order airstrikes on Iran if his envoys, Jared Kushner and Steve Witkoff, think Tehran is holding out on a nuclear deal. The president is waiting for Iran’s latest proposal before deciding. He has said he’ll consider limited strikes, and if that fails, a bigger attack to change the regime. Iran has threatened retaliation. Meanwhile, Trump’s new 10% global tariff has started, and Mexico’s military killed the drug‑cartel boss “El Mencho.”
