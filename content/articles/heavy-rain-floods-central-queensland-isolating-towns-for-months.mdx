Central Queensland is flooded after the ex‑tropical cyclone Koji dumped more than a year’s rain in a week. Towns such as Clermont, Eungella and Dysart are cut off by rising waters and landslides. Residents are trapped and may need helicopter supplies. Local leaders say roads are damaged and the military might help with drops. The state keeps flood warnings active and watches for more rain.
