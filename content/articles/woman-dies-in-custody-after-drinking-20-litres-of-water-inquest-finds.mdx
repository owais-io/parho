A 47‑year‑old woman, Tammy Shipley, died in a New South Wales women’s prison after drinking at least 20 litres of water in 12 hours. CCTV showed 67 cups of water, and police never warned staff of her serious mental health problems. Guards only saw her collapse an hour and a half later. The coroner said the death was due to water intoxication and is looking at how prisons flag people with mental illness.
