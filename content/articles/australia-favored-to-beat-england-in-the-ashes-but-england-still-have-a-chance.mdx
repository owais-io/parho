The upcoming Ashes in Australia is shaping up to be a tight contest. The Australian team, although missing some key bowlers, has a solid lineup and home‑ground advantage, giving them the edge. England has talented players like Joe Root and Ben Stokes, but they face injury worries and rely on a few fast bowlers. Both sides will play well, but Australia’s experience at home could tip the balance.
