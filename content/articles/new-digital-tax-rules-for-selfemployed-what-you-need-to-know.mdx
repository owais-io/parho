HMRC is introducing Making Tax Digital, a new online system that will require self‑employed people and landlords earning above certain thresholds to record and submit their tax data every quarter. The thresholds start at £50,000 next month and will fall to £30,000 and £20,000 by 2028. Users must choose software, log every income and expense, and file quarterly updates by the end of each quarter. HMRC will send letters, but anyone meeting the limits must comply unless exempt.
