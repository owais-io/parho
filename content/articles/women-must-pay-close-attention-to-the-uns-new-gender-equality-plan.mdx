In Afghanistan, a judge told a woman seeking divorce that “a few beatings won’t kill you,” showing how the Taliban’s laws punish women. In the West, the Heritage Foundation promotes policies that push women back into early motherhood and away from careers. The UN now plans to merge UN Women and UNFPA, a move that could cut funding for gender equality and empower anti‑women governments. Feminists fear this will keep a male‑dominated world, while the UN chief warns that gender equality remains a fight.
