A deadly avalanche in California’s Sierra Nevada killed nine skiers, and authorities have now recovered all their bodies. The search lasted five days, with help from the state police, National Guard, utility company and volunteers. Six survivors were rescued earlier, but the rest were buried by the slide. The incident is the deadliest since 1981, and investigators are looking into possible negligence.
