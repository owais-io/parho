An Irish man named Seamus Culleton has spent five months in U.S. ICE detention even though he has a legal work permit and no criminal record. He married a U.S. citizen and runs a plastering business in Boston. ICE arrested him during a random sweep, preventing him from attending a green‑card interview. He says he never signed deportation papers and the jail conditions are harsh. Irish officials are pressing for his release.
