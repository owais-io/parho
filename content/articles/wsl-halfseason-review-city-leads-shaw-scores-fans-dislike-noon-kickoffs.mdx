Manchester City are topping the WSL, beating rivals and scoring plenty of goals. Khadija Shaw is the top scorer, and new defender Jade Rose is doing a great job. Tottenham’s new boss Martin Ho has helped them improve, but Arsenal are falling behind because of old players, injuries and unhappy teammates. Fans are unhappy with games starting at noon, which makes it hard to watch. In the second half people hope for fewer big injuries and better match times.
