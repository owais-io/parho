Jamaican rugby is building a bright future with a growing club in the UK that has 500 members and volunteers who coach across England. They’re creating pathways for players of Jamaican heritage and already have a youth squad that could compete in 2028. The programme depends on volunteers and personal funding, and hopes World Rugby will support camps in Jamaica. The goal is to reach the 2031 Rugby World Cup.
