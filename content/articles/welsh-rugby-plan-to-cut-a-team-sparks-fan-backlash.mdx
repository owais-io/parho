WRU chair Richard Collier‑Keywood says he has fan backing to shut down one of Wales’ four pro rugby teams, but fan groups say otherwise. The union wants to close the Ospreys to save money as part of a £28 million five‑year fix. Supporters say 90% oppose the move. WRU faces debt and a stalled professional deal, and the chair may soon face a no‑confidence vote. The debate highlights financial strain on Welsh rugby.
