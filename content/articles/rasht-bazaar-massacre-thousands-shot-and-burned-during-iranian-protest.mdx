In January, protests in Iran were crushed with force. In the city of Rasht, a huge crowd gathered at the old bazaar. Government forces opened fire, set the market on fire, and shot people trying to escape. Witnesses say kids, women and the elderly were killed. Hospitals filled with burn and gunshot victims. The event is seen by human‑rights groups as a massacre and a crime against humanity.
