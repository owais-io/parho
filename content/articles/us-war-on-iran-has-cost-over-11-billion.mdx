The U.S. has spent more than $11 billion in the first week of strikes on Iran, but that money could have helped U.S. schools, paid student loans, and eased homelessness. Meanwhile, over 2,500 people, including children, have been killed in Iran and Lebanon, and hundreds of thousands are displaced. The war’s cost keeps rising, and the future of the conflict is uncertain.
