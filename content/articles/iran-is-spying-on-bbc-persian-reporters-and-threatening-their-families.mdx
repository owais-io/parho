Exiled Iranian journalists working for the BBC Persian say Iran’s security forces are watching their every move and threatening their relatives back home. They report families are being interrogated, assets frozen, and even parents detained, all aimed at silencing their reporting on protests. The journalists, who work in the UK, have had to take extra safety measures and some have quit because of the pressure. The situation worsened after Iran shut down the internet during recent protests.
