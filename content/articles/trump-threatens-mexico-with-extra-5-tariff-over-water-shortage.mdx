Trump says Mexico must give 200,000 acre‑feet of water by Dec. 31 or he will add a 5 % tariff on Mexican imports. He claims Mexico violated a 1944 treaty that shares Rio Grande water with the U.S. The water shortfall is hurting Texas farms, causing crop losses and closing a sugar mill. Mexico says drought and misuse of water are the cause. Other U.S. lawmakers want to withhold aid unless Mexico supplies water, while Trump rolled out a $12 billion farm‑aid package.
