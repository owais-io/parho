On Saturday, an ICE officer shot and killed 37‑year‑old nurse Alex Pretti in Minneapolis. Video shows he was unarmed, sparking anger across the political spectrum. President Obama’s family called the death a tragedy and urged a full investigation. Many Democrats want ICE to leave Minnesota and are threatening to withhold DHS funding. Republicans argue the shooting was justified, while a federal judge blocked the administration from tampering with evidence.
