Former school boss at Dulwich College says he takes allegations of teenage racism by Nigel Farage seriously and that such behaviour is incompatible with the school's values. He explains that the college hasn't commented publicly on individual claims to protect its reputation, but has strong equality and anti‑bullying policies. Farage denies the accusations, calling the comments uninformed. The debate has drawn criticism from Reform members, who call the claims a smear.
