Newcastle United beat Tottenham Hotspur 2‑1 at the Emirates, thanks to a late winner from Jacob Ramsey. The win was the third in a row for Newcastle but the result was a blow to Spurs, who have only won twice in their last 17 league games. Manager Thomas Frank faced angry boos from Spurs fans, and the defeat adds to the club’s struggle to escape the relegation zone.
