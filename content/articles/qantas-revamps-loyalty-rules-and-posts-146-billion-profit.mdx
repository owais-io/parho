Qantas is changing its frequent‑flyer rules to keep customers on board. New rules let members keep unused status credits, earn credits by spending normally, and set higher thresholds for gold and platinum tiers. The airline also rewards long‑term loyalty with a lifetime status option. While doing this, Qantas posted a record pre‑tax profit of $1.46 billion and lifted loyalty‑business revenue by 19 %. The CEO also raised dividends.
