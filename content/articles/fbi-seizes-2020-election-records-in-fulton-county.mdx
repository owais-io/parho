The FBI executed a search warrant at Fulton County’s election office to seize records from the 2020 vote, including ballots and tabulation data. The first warrant was found to be legally flawed, but the FBI got a corrected one. County officials, led by commissioner Mo Ivory, objected and are exploring legal options to protect the chain of custody. Democrats worry the raid could set a precedent for future elections.
