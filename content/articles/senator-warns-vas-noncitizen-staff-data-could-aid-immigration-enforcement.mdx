Senator Adam Schiff has written to the VA and Homeland Security, saying the department’s plan to collect data on all non‑citizen employees and affiliates looks like a covert way to target people for immigration checks. He says the move could scare VA staff and waste time that’s already scarce because the VA is struggling with staff shortages. The VA calls the review a routine update, but many lawmakers and veterans groups say it raises serious privacy and fairness concerns.
