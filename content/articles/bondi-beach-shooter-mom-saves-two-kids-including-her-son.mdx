During a gun attack on Bondi beach, a woman named Jessica Rozen ran for her 3‑year‑old son, then lay on top of another child to protect him. She survived with minor cuts. The shooting was part of a violent incident that killed at least 15 people and left 27 wounded. Ordinary Australians, like Ahmed al‑Ahmed, also stepped in to stop gunmen. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese praised the community’s courage and called the responders Australia's best.
