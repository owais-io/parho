A drone struck the UK’s RAF Akrotiri base in Cyprus, causing only minor damage and no injuries. Residents nearby were told to stay inside until further notice, and non‑essential staff were asked to leave. The attack happened after Britain let U.S. forces use its bases to hit Iranian missile sites. Britain says the base is only defending itself and is not taking part in the strikes. Cyprus says it is not involved.
