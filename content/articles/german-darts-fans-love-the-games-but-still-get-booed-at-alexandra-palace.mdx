At Alexandra Palace, German fans are filling the seats, buying tickets in record numbers. They’re friendly, enjoy a good night out, and love the game. But on stage they still get heckled by English crowds. The piece shows how darts exploded in Germany in the last decade, how fans and players are getting to know each other, and how the rivalry between the two countries is mostly good‑natured.
