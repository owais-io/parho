New Mexico officials have started a search of Jeffrey Epstein's ranch, Zorro Ranch, after a Guardian report showed it had not been investigated by federal agents. The state, led by Attorney General Raúl Torrez, is reopening a 2019 case paused while New York prosecutors focused on the main Epstein investigation. The search, led by state police and the sheriff, may help confirm survivors’ claims, though evidence is likely limited due to the ranch's age. Residents are urged to stay away.
