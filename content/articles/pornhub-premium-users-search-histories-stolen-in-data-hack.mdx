A group called ShinyHunters hacked data from Pornhub’s analytics partner Mixpanel, revealing search histories, viewing habits, email addresses and locations of over 200 million records of premium users. The hack didn’t expose passwords or payment info. Pornhub said it stopped using Mixpanel in 2021, so the data were old. The hackers threatened to release the data for bitcoin. No evidence the data has appeared online yet.
