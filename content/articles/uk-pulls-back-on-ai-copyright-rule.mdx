The UK government has stopped its plan that would let AI companies use copyrighted songs, movies, books, and art without asking creators. Tech secretary Liz Kendall said the proposal was too risky after talking to artists, writers, unions, and AI firms. Big names like Elton John and Dua Lipa said the move protects creators. The government will still review how AI can use creative content and may introduce licensing rules.
