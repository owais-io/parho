The UK cancelled the first 2026 flight that would have taken asylum seekers from the UK to France under its “one in, one out” policy. Ten detainees had tickets for a Wednesday morning flight, but the tickets were withdrawn without explanation. A report by 80 detainees raised concerns about unfair detention and the cost of charter flights. The Home Office said it would not comment on operations.
