The Justice Department missed the 19 Dec 2025 deadline to hand over all records on Jeffrey Epstein, a law that Congress passed to make those documents public. They’ve posted only about 12,000 files – a tiny fraction of the 2 million documents the agency says it’s reviewing. Survivors, lawmakers and legal experts are pushing for a court‑appointed special master to force full disclosure and stop further delays.
