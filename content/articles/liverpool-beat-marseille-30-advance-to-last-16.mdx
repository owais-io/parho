Liverpool beat Marseille 3‑0 in the European competition, sending them into the last 16. A free‑kick from Dominik Szoboszlai, an own goal caused by Jeremie Frimpong, and a stoppage‑time strike from substitute Cody Gakpo sealed the win. Mohamed Salah returned to the starting lineup after the Africa Cup of Nations. Liverpool’s confident play and solid defense helped them finish 9th win in 11 European away games.
