Phones today automatically brighten, sharpen, and smooth pictures without you turning on any settings. The writer shows raw shots that look muted next to the phone’s edited version that looks much brighter and crisper. He explains that companies use machine‑learning to guess what we like and then add that polish. While it makes photos prettier, it can hide the real look of a moment and limit how we express ourselves. Some people still prefer unedited, film‑style photos.
