Prime Minister Keir Starmer announced that leaseholders in England and Wales will have their ground rent capped at £250 a year. The government will also ban new leasehold flats and let existing leaseholders switch to commonhold. The move aims to cut costs for homeowners and give them more control over their buildings. It is part of a draft bill that also removes the threat of forfeiture for low debts.
