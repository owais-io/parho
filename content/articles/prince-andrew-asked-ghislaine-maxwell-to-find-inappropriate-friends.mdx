New emails from 2001‑2002 show Prince Andrew (labeled “A”) asking Ghislaine Maxwell to arrange meetings with “inappropriate friends” and to provide “fun, friendly” girls for him. The documents also reveal FBI interest in questioning Andrew about ties to another sex‑offender, Peter Nygard, and include mentions of Donald Trump. The release adds fresh detail to the long‑running investigation into Andrew’s relationship with Epstein.
