Jessie Diggins started the Milan Olympic skiathlon with a crash that knocked her out of the lead. The fall left her battling to climb back and she finished eighth, 2 minutes behind the gold. Sweden’s Frida Karlsson won the race in a strong, fast finish. The event was tough on wet, slushy snow and marked Diggins’ final Olympic appearance before she retires.
