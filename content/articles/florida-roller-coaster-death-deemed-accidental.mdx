A 32‑year‑old man died on a high‑speed roller coaster at Universal’s new Epic Universe park. Officials say the death was accidental. The man suffered serious head and body injuries, including a broken nose and thigh bone, after the ride stopped. Safety checks found no negligence. Witnesses said the lap bar was tight and the rider was thrown forward. The incident followed a similar case that led to new safety laws.
