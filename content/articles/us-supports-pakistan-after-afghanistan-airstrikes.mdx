The United States said it backs Pakistan’s right to defend itself after Pakistan carried out airstrikes on cities in Afghanistan. The Taliban in Kabul said it is ready to talk, while Pakistan claims Afghan forces attacked its troops and that the Taliban shelters militants. The UK and EU called for a quick halt to fighting and urged both sides to negotiate. US officials are monitoring the escalating tensions closely.
