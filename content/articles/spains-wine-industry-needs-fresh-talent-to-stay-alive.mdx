Spain’s wine sector is struggling because most growers are older. A study says 39% are 51‑65 and 35% are over 65, while only 9% are under 40. To keep producing wine, the industry must hire about 22,600 younger workers. The problem is linked to young people leaving rural areas for cities. Modern tech and a focus on sustainability could make the job more attractive.
