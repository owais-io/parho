In Nepal’s most watched election, 35‑year‑old Balen, a popular rapper who became Kathmandu’s mayor, is running against the veteran communist leader KP Oli for the Jhapa‑5 seat and the prime minister’s job. Balen’s rise followed a brutal youth protest that toppled Oli’s government, and his supporters hope he can bring fresh jobs, fight corruption, and end the old political cycle.
