Mia Brookes, 19‑year‑old British snowboarder, reached the Big Air final at Livigno and finished third. Team GB’s mixed curling pair, Bruce Mouat and Jen Dodds, play a semi‑final that could secure a medal, while freestyle skier Kirsty Muir is a favourite for gold. With two athletes in medal positions, the country hopes for a “Magic Monday” – three medals in one day – but experts say it’s unlikely. Still, confidence keeps the dream alive.
