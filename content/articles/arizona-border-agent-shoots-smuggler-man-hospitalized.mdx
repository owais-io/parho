A border patrol agent chased a smuggler in Arizona, firing shots at the suspect and a helicopter. The smuggler, Patrick Schlegel, is in critical condition but stable after being taken to a trauma centre. The agent was unharmed. Schlegel was convicted in 2023 for smuggling and firearms offenses and escaped from a prison facility in 2024. He is now wanted for assaulting a federal officer.
