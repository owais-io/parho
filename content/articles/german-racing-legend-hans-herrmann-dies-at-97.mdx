German racing legend Hans Herrmann, who won Le Mans in 1970 after promising his wife he'd quit if he won, died aged 97. He had 19 Formula One starts, two Mille Miglia class wins, and a career full of daring drives and close calls. After racing he ran a parts shop and remained active in motorsport circles.
