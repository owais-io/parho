Simon Jenkins warns that the UK should keep its distance from the escalating conflict between Israel and Iran. He argues that the US and Israel are pushing for a larger war, but Britain’s security is not at risk. Jenkins criticises Prime Minister Keir Starmer for sending military planners to Washington and for making Britain a front‑line partner. He calls for the bombing to stop and warns of repeating past mistakes that led to costly wars.
