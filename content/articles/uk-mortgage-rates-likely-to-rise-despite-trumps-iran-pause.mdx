Homeowners are finding fewer mortgage choices and rates are climbing this week. Even though President Trump paused attacks on Iranian power plants, investors think the Bank of England will raise rates several times before year’s end, pushing the average two‑year fixed rate to 5.43%, the highest since February 2025. Lenders are pulling products and the market feels pressure from possible inflation after U.S.‑Israel tensions.
