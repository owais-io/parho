The U.S. is set to defend its Olympic figure‑skating team title in Milan. With a deep roster, the squad includes world champions like Ilia Malinin and Alysa Liu, the “Blade Angels,” and veteran pair Madison Chock & Evan Bates. Their nickname shows strong camaraderie. The team isn’t just about winning medals; it represents a new generation that balances technical push, artistic expression, and cultural outreach. Together, they could shift skating into mainstream sports.
