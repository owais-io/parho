Ahmed al‑Ahmed, 44, a Sydney shop owner, fought gunman Sajid Akram on Bondi Beach during a Hanukkah event shooting. He wrestled the attacker, took the gun, and stopped more deaths. Ahmed was hit five times, had surgery and is recovering. The incident was praised by the prime minister and other leaders, and a fundraiser raised $2.5 million. He says he feels sad for victims but proud of his actions.
