In the UK, big indoor grower Thanet Earth says a new rise in electricity standing charges will add £900,000 a year to its bill, raising tomato production costs by 5%. Growers warn they’ll have to raise prices or cut output, hurting consumers and food security. The move follows government‑led energy support for heavy‑industry sectors that food producers miss. Growers are urging the government to intervene.
