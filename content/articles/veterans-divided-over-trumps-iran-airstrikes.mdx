Veterans are split on President Trump’s decision to launch airstrikes against Iran. Some say the attacks echo past mistakes and risk more lives, while others support them as a defense against a hostile regime. The debate highlights veterans’ personal trauma, the legality of military action, and differing opinions among lawmakers and veteran groups. Congress, the American Legion, and former soldiers voice concerns over casualties, unclear goals, and whether the strikes serve national interests.
