Britain’s central bank has warned that AI stock prices may tumble because they’re valued too high. The BoE’s latest report also says global risks like cyber‑attacks and market shocks are still a threat. On the upside, its biggest banks have passed new stress tests and the Bank plans to lower capital rules for high‑street lenders. Housing prices rose a bit in November, and pension funds are diversifying away from U.S. stocks.
