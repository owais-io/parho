Lily Allen and actor David Harbour just agreed to sell their Brooklyn townhouse, which they listed for $8 million last October, for $7 million. The four‑storey brownstone, known for its quirky vibe and the couple’s break‑up, was recently featured in a Guardian piece that humorously dubbed it a “haunted house.” The sale highlights how quickly celebrity homes can change hands for large sums.
