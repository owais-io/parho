Ukraine and Russia met in Abu Dhabi and agreed to exchange 157 prisoners, a concrete result even though there was no real breakthrough in ending the war. The talks were the first serious meeting between senior officials in months and were helped by the U.S. and UAE. Both sides said they would keep talking, but Russia still wants Ukraine to give up the entire Donbas, which Kyiv rejects. The conflict remains.
