Climate advisers say the UK can reach net‑zero emissions by 2050 for about £4 billion a year, less than the cost of the recent oil price spikes. The switch to renewables, electric cars and heat pumps would cut future fuel bills, improve air quality and save £2‑£4 for every pound spent. The report also warns that politicians who want to keep fossil fuels would expose families to higher prices.
