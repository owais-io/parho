British households are buying more solar panels and heat pumps after energy prices spiked because of the war in Iran. Octopus Energy says sales jumped 54% this month and people are choosing bigger roof systems. Heat pump and electric‑vehicle charger sales are also up. The government wants new homes to have solar from 2028, and plug‑in solar kits will soon be easier to buy. Experts say the shift will help keep bills low when oil prices rise.
