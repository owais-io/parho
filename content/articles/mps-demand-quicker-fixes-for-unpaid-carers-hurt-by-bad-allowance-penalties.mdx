MPs are asking the Department for Work and Pensions to speed up the process of fixing thousands of unpaid carers who were wrongly told to repay huge debts. The issue started when the DWP’s carer’s allowance system wrongly penalised people who had part‑time jobs. The committee says the system’s design is out of date and unfair, and that many carers suffered health and financial problems.
