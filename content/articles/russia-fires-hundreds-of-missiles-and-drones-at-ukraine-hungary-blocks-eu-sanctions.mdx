Russia fired nearly 300 drones and 50 missiles at Ukraine, damaging homes and infrastructure, on the eve of the fourth anniversary of the invasion. President Zelenskyy urged allies to strengthen air defenses. Meanwhile, Hungary says it will block EU sanctions unless Ukraine resumes Russian oil deliveries, and Slovakia threatens to cut power. Despite talks, new bombings and casualties keep mounting, while world leaders call for peace.
