Six skiers have been found after an avalanche at Castle Peak in California, but 10 are still missing from a group of 16. The incident happened amid heavy snow that has closed roads and prompted avalanche warnings across the Sierra Nevada. Local officials, including Governor Newsom, are leading a rescue operation, while the sheriff’s office urges caution for back‑country skiing.
