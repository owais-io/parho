Josh Tongue took five wickets for 45 runs on day one of the fourth Ashes Test at the MCG, helping England lead by 46 runs after they dismissed Australia for 152. The pitch was moving and spitting, making batting hard. Tongue praised his bowlers for hitting the right lengths and said his aim was to put pressure on the Australians. He also thanked his family and loved the Ashes atmosphere.
