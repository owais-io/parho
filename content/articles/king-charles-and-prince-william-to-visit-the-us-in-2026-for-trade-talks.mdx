King Charles and Prince William are each set to travel to the United States in 2026. Their trips are part of a plan to revive a big tech‑investment deal that the U.S. paused because Britain didn’t lower other trade barriers. The king’s visit could come in April, while the prince will be there during the World Cup in July. The goal is to smooth out disagreements over digital taxes and farm access, and keep UK exports tariff‑free.
