Former Labour minister Peter Mandelson was arrested in London and then released on bail while police investigate claims he misused his position. The case centers on his friendship with convicted child‑abuser Jeffrey Epstein and allegations he leaked confidential emails and sensitive information. The arrest has sparked political backlash, with Prime Minister Keir Starmer facing criticism for appointing Mandelson to the U.S. embassy. Other investigations into related figures are also underway.
