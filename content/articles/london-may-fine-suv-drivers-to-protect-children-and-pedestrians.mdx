London officials are planning to charge SUV drivers because large cars are dangerous to pedestrians, especially children. Transport for London (TfL) wants to study the risks and may add extra fees or tighter speed limits for big vehicles. Research shows SUVs are 77% more likely to kill a child in a crash. The mayor says the plan will help make streets safer and reduce deaths.
