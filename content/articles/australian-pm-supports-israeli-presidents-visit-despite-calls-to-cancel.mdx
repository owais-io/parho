Prime Minister Anthony Albanese backs the Australian government's decision to invite Israeli President Isaac Herzog to visit in February, even after a deadly Bondi terror attack. Several federal MPs, including Sophie Scamps, and pro‑Palestinian groups say the visit could stir division and call for Herzog to be cancelled. Security will be tight in Sydney, while protests are planned across the country to protest Israel’s war in Gaza.
