Austrian snowboarder Benjamin Karl won gold in the men’s parallel giant slalom at the Milano‑Cortina Games, his second Olympic win. After crossing the finish line, he ripped off his shirt and flexed like Austrian legend Hermann Maier, a planned tribute that lit up the crowd. Karl’s victory added a fourth Olympic medal to his collection and marked the end of his career, as he announced retirement. Norway also won a speed‑skating gold, and other athletes celebrated successes throughout the Games.
