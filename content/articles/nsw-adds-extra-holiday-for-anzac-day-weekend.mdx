New South Wales will give its residents an extra public holiday in 2026 and 2027 when Anzac Day lands on a weekend. The move, announced by Premier Chris Minns, lets people enjoy a long weekend, though small businesses warn about extra costs. Victoria, Queensland and other states said they won’t add a holiday. The decision may become permanent after reviewing its social and economic effects.
