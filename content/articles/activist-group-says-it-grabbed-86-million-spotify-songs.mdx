Anna’s Archive, an activist group, claims it copied 86 million Spotify tracks and their metadata. Spotify has shut down the accounts that did it and says the music hasn’t been released yet. The group wants to share the files on torrents as a “preservation archive”, hoping to keep music safe. Critics warn the files could feed AI training, while Spotify is tightening safeguards. The move adds to a wider debate over copyright and AI use in the UK.
