Sport England banned its X account because the platform promotes misogynistic content, especially after attacks on England women's football players. Chair Chris Boardman said X's AI tool, Grok, spread sexist posts, harming the safety of sports participants. The decision was part of a broader move to protect women and girls in sport. Boardman also criticized Elon Musk’s leadership, calling X divisive and irresponsible. Ofcom is investigating X for sexualised images, and new laws will target non‑consensual content.
