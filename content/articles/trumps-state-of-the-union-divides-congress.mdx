In a record‑length State of the Union, President Trump said the economy is booming, inflation is under control, and the border is secure. Republicans applauded these claims, calling the country a golden age. Democrats blasted the address as full of lies, noting tariffs raise costs for families and that his promises are false. The speech sparked heated debate and angry exchanges in Congress.
