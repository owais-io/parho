England have already qualified for next summer’s World Cup and will play Albania in their final match. Coach Thomas Tuchel has warned against red cards, especially after a recent loss by Portugal’s Ronaldo. Harry Kane is close to Pelé’s record with eight goals in this qualifying run. Norway and Portugal have also secured spots, while Scotland’s chances depend on a draw against Denmark. Manchester City lead the Women’s Super League.
