Meta ignored reports about an Australian influencer with 800,000 followers who promoted an illegal offshore crypto‑gambling site called Rainbet. Despite the Australian regulator warning that influencers could be fined up to $2.4 million, Meta said the posts didn’t break its rules. The influencer’s videos still appear on Instagram, with links to the casino hidden in her bio. The regulator says illegal gambling ads are illegal and can cost huge fines.
