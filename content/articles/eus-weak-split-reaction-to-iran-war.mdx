The EU’s reaction to the war in Iran has been slow and split. Commission president Ursula von der Leyen urged a “realistic” approach, but France and Germany disagreed. France says Brussels is overstepping, while Germany says the EU should not lecture allies. European leaders have been criticized for being stunned and sidelined, and some say the EU is not doing enough to uphold international law.
