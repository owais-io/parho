After Tehran’s move in the Gaza war, Iran-backed militias are still striking Israeli and U.S. targets, but they have not gone all‑in on a full war. Hezbollah, Iraqi Shia groups and Yemen’s Houthis have fired missiles, drones and rockets, yet keep their attacks limited, perhaps waiting for the conflict to grow or for Tehran’s orders to break. U.S. forces, a carrier battle group and Red Sea shipping lanes remain in danger, while Iraq and Jordan face ongoing attacks.
