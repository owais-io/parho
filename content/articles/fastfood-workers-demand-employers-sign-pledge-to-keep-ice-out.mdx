Fast‑food workers in California are asking their bosses to sign a pledge that says they’ll keep ICE agents out of private areas of the restaurant. The union says the pledge should let staff hide in cars, keep a protected space, and stop officers from entering without a warrant. Some McDonald’s employees walked out after managers refused to agree. The push comes after several ICE raids that scared workers and hurt sales.
