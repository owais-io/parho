New rules from the UK Home Office now require British dual nationals to show a British passport or proof of entitlement when flying back to the UK. The change has left several teenagers stranded abroad – a 16‑year‑old in Denmark, a 19‑year‑old in Madrid and an 18‑year‑old in Mumbai all were denied boarding because they had no passport. Families say the government failed to warn them and the short notice has caused missed school, cancelled visits and travel chaos.
