US officials decided to keep the nuclear talks with Iran in Oman after a last‑minute push from Arab states, keeping the negotiations alive. The meeting will happen in Muscat on Friday, with U.S. forces positioned in the region as a warning. Iran insists talks must focus only on its nuclear program, while Washington wants to address missiles, terrorism and human rights. Uncertainty remains over the final agenda and outcomes.
