Starmer is convening a top‑level emergency meeting to see how the UK can help families with higher prices caused by the US‑Israel‑Iran conflict. He wants all government tools—bank policy, energy support and anti‑profiteering measures—to ease the cost‑of‑living hit. The meeting will also look at fuel shortages and keeping the Strait of Hormuz open, while Starmer talks with the U.S. to calm tensions.
