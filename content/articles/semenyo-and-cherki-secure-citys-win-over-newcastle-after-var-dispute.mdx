City beat Newcastle in the Carabao Cup semi‑final after a VAR controversy. New‑comer Antoine Semenyo scored first, then a second goal was taken away. Rayan Cherki finished the game in stoppage time. Newcastle’s manager Eddie Howe was angry, and the club’s owners announced a new £200m training ground nearby. The result leaves City ahead and Newcastle with doubts for the next leg.
