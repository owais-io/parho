A NASA probe from 2012, weighing about 1,300 pounds, will re‑enter Earth’s atmosphere Tuesday evening. Most of it will burn up, but some pieces might survive and fall to the ground. The Space Force says the odds of anyone being hit are about 1 in 4,200, or practically zero for a single person. NASA and the Space Force will keep monitoring and update predictions. Rarely has falling space junk injured someone.
