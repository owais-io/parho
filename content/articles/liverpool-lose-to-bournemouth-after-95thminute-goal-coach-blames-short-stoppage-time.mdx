Arne Slot’s Liverpool lost 3‑2 to Bournemouth when Amine Adli scored a 95‑minute winner. Slot said his side ran out of energy after playing back‑to‑back away matches, including a Champions League game. He complained the referee gave only four minutes of stoppage time, which he thinks was too short. The loss was Liverpool’s first since November and highlighted fatigue and timing issues.
