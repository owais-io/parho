One GWR train is being tested with new Wi‑Fi tech from Formula One. It switches between 5G towers and low‑orbit satellites to give passengers super fast internet, even on long journeys. In a recent run from London to Newbury, speeds hit over 120 Mbps and video calls worked smoothly. If the pilot succeeds, GWR could offer fast Wi‑Fi on all its trains by 2030, helping commuters stay connected and productive.
