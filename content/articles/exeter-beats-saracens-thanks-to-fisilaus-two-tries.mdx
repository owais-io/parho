In a hard‑fought match at Twickenham, Exeter pulled off a dramatic win over Saracens. The team had trailed 24‑13, but Greg Fisilau scored two quick tries to level the score. Henry Slade then kicked a late penalty to give Exeter a bonus point, finishing 22‑20. Saracens had more tries, but missed a late penalty and had a player sent off. Exeter’s coach said the team is building a strong side.
