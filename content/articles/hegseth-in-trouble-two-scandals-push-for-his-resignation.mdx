Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth is in crisis. A report says he shared secret details about Yemen airstrikes on a public messaging app, and lawmakers say he ordered a deadly follow‑up strike that killed survivors of a prior attack in the Caribbean. Democrats and some Republicans want him gone, but President Trump still backs him. The scandals have sparked anger over safety, secrecy and the nation’s drug‑war tactics.
