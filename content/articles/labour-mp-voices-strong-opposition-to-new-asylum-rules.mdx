Tony Vaughan, the new Labour MP for Folkestone, used to post pro‑government Instagram videos but has now taken a stand against his party’s new asylum policy. He says deporting refugees is wrong and that the government should focus on fair immigration controls and proper integration. Vaughan, a former immigration lawyer, worries about stripping assets from asylum seekers and turning permanent status into a temporary one. He’s facing criticism from his own party but believes the policy could deepen division in his coastal community.
