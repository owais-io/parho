Boxing legends and the Nigerian government send messages of support after a car crash near Lagos that killed Anthony Joshua’s trainer and coach, while the boxer was badly hurt. The 36‑year‑old is in hospital in Lagos, reportedly stable. Tyson Fury, Oleksandr Usyk, Jake Paul and President Tinubu all posted condolences, urging Joshua to stay strong and wishing a speedy recovery.
