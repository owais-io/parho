Russian strikes on Ukraine on Saturday killed six people and injured ten, including children, hitting an apartment block in Kharkiv and a site in Dnipro. The attacks triggered a nationwide air alert, and rescuers are searching for survivors under the rubble. Poland rushed fighter jets to defend its airspace. The U.S. is reviewing sanctions on Russia while deploying a drone‑fighting system to the Middle East, and Ukraine is offering its drone‑defence expertise to partners.
