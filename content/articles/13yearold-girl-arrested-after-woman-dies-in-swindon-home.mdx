Police in Swindon arrested a 13‑year‑old schoolgirl after a woman in her 50s was found dead in her house on Baydon Close. Officers arrived at the scene around 7 p.m. Friday and discovered the woman wasn’t breathing. The girl was taken into custody for questioning while police investigate the circumstances. Authorities say no other suspects are being searched for and are urging the public not to speculate.
