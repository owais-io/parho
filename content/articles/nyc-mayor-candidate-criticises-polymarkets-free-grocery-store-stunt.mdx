Zohran Mamdani, a candidate for New York City mayor, had promised city‑run grocery stores to lower food prices. When Polymarket, a crypto prediction market, announced a free pop‑up grocery shop, Mamdani quickly replied with a sarcastic comment from a satirical site. Polymarket said it would open a temporary “free grocery store” and had donated $1 million to the Food Bank. Mamdani’s plan would cost about $60 million a year and is aimed at cutting grocery inflation.
