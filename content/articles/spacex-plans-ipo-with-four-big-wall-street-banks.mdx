SpaceX is preparing to go public, lining up four major Wall Street banks—Bank of America, JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, and Morgan Stanley—to lead the deal. The company aims to raise about $25 billion, valuing it at roughly $800 billion, a jump from last year’s $400 billion estimate. Musk’s aerospace firm makes money from reusable rockets, satellite launches and its Starlink internet service. The IPO comes amid a surge of high‑value tech listings in the U.S.
