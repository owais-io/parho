Chile's rugby boss Pablo Lemoine says the 2027 World Cup gives smaller nations like Chile a real chance to win a game and reach the next round. He talks about the new four‑team pools and hopes to beat debutant Hong Kong, but he knows beating Australia or New Zealand is very hard. Lemoine also points out that Chile needs more money and better internet to train, and that rugby is struggling even in big rugby nations.
