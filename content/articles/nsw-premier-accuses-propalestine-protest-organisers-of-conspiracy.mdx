The New South Wales premier, Chris Minns, said that the group that organised pro‑Palestine protests is full of communists and that they pushed police into violent clashes. The comment came after a protest in February over Israeli president Isaac Herzog’s visit, where police were accused of brutality. Several lawmakers want an inquiry into police conduct and are calling for a review. The premier denied he was responsible for the incidents and has faced backlash from the Muslim community.
