Italy’s Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni is preparing to auction 270 gifts she received from foreign leaders, worth around €800,000. The sale will take place before Christmas at the Bertolami Fine Art house, with all proceeds going to charity. Items include a chainsaw‑wielding statuette from Argentine President Javier Milei, and python‑skin shoes with gold heels from Saudi Arabia, among more usual gifts such as scarves, tea sets and a ceramic bowl from former US President Joe Biden.
