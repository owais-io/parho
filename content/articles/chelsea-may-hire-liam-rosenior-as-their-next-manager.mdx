Chelsea’s board is eyeing Liam Rosenior, a 41‑year‑old former player who has coached youth, worked as an assistant, and managed teams in England and France. His time at Derby, Hull City and now Strasbourg shows he can adapt and use a flexible, high‑press system that fits Chelsea. He understands the club’s multi‑club ownership and has learned from big names like Wayne Rooney and Sir Alex Ferguson. If hired, he could be the first Black permanent manager in the Premier League.
