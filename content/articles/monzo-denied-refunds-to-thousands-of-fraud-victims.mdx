Monzo has denied refunds to more than 1,000 fraud and scam victims, with the Financial Ombudsman Service finding the bank wrongly rejected 34% of 3,372 complaints last year. It tops other UK banks in wrong rejections, just behind NatWest and HSBC. Fraud is the most common crime in Britain, and a new rule now forces banks to reimburse most APP fraud cases. Victims say they feel ignored and want fast, fair payouts.
