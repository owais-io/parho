After Israeli air strikes hit Lebanon, Hezbollah fired rockets at Israel, sparking a new war. Residents fled cities, 52 people died and 29,000 were displaced. Lebanese families, exhausted by past conflicts, feel betrayed by Hezbollah’s actions. The government banned Hezbollah from military roles and ordered arrests for those launching rockets. Citizens shelter in basements and streets, fearing bombings, while the city watches for drones and jets.
