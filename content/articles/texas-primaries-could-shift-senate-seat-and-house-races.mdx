Texas voters on Tuesday will decide who gets the state's Senate seat and several key House seats. Republicans are fighting over the seat, with 4‑term Senator John Cornyn, state attorney general Ken Paxton, and congressman Wesley Hunt. Democrats are picking between James Talarico and progressive Jasmine Crockett. The race is expensive, with over $122 m in ads, and shows how Trump’s supporters are still powerful in Texas.
