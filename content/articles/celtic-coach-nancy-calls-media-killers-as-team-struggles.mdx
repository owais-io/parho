Celtic's new coach Wilfried Nancy says media and fans are “killing” him because of the team's poor start. He points to a lack of pre‑season and the pressure before the big Old Firm match. Celtic have lost five of seven games, and many fans want a new coach. Nancy defends his three‑man defence, but the board is unsure whether to keep investing in him. Fans are angry over the board and the former coach’s exit.
