A luxury cruise ship, the Coral Adventurer, ran aground near Papua New Guinea last weekend. No one was hurt, but the incident caused chaos for some travelers. One passenger said the situation was dangerous and will never board again, while others said it was a minor scare and they enjoyed the trip. The ship was pulled free, investigated, and its owners offered refunds and credits after cancelling the voyage.
