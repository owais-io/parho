A Chicago trans man named Lio Cundiff dove into Lake Michigan at Belmont Harbor after a gust blew a baby’s stroller into the water. He stayed in the cold water for about four minutes, keeping the stroller and baby from sinking. Two helpers pulled them onto shore. Both were taken to hospital; the baby is fine. A GoFundMe raised $54,000 to cover his costs. The story sparked media attention and a conversation about trans rights.
