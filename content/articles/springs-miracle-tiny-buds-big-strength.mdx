After a tough winter filled with loss and health challenges, the author turned to the nearby garden for comfort. Watching spring bloom—especially the dramatic bud burst on trees—fills her with awe and a sense of resilience. She likens the pressure inside a newly unfurled leaf to that of a car tire, reminding us that even small things can hold great power. It’s a quiet reflection on nature’s healing.
