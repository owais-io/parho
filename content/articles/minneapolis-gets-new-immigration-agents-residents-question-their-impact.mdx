Federal immigration agents have been sent into Minneapolis and St. Paul after ICE officer Renee Good was shot last week. Residents say the presence of agents changes how they move around town. The ACLU has sued the Trump administration over alleged racial profiling and unlawful arrests in Minnesota. People in the Twin Cities are watching closely, asking how the lawsuit and agents affect their daily lives and safety.
