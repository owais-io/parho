Trump’s administration is moving many of the U.S. Department of Education’s jobs to other federal agencies, a step many Democrats say cuts funding for schools. The department said it will team up with the Labor, Interior, Health and Human Services, and State departments to reduce paperwork and focus on students. Critics warn the cuts hurt special‑needs students and low‑income families. Governors and lawmakers vow to protect education funding.
