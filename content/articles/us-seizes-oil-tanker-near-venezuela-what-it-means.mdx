The US Coast Guard took a big oil tanker named Skipper off Venezuela's coast. The ship had been blocked by US sanctions for moving oil for Iran. The move is part of a wider US push to stop illegal oil trade and pressure Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro. Venezuela says the seizure is piracy, while the US says the oil will be taken legally. The raid could make it harder for Venezuela to sell oil abroad.
