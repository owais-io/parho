Sonia Bompastor, Chelsea’s head coach, has signed a new deal that keeps her with the club until 2030. The 45‑year‑old won a domestic treble and reached the Champions League semi‑finals last season, but Chelsea are now 12 points behind league leaders Manchester City. Despite recent defeats, the club and Bompastor stay confident. She says the team will bounce back and focus on staying competitive on all fronts.
