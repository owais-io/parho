Dan Elkayam, a 27‑year‑old French man, was shot during a Jewish celebration at Bondi Beach. His friend Jesse Singer spoke at the Sydney funeral, recalling beach football and Dan’s zest for life. The community pledged to honor him with acts of kindness and offered support to those grieving. The memorial underlined unity and the call to live meaningfully.
