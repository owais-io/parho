Russian drones and missiles hit Kharkiv, cutting power to 80 % of the city as temperatures drop to ‑14 °C. NATO chief Mark Rutte warned Ukraine faces its worst winter in a decade and urged EU lawmakers to be flexible with funding. He also praised US‑made weapons support and noted Ukraine wants NATO membership, but some members oppose it now. The report also mentioned Russian gains and North Korean involvement.
