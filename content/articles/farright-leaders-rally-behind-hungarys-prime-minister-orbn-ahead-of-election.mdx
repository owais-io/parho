At a Budapest meeting, leaders from France, the Netherlands, Italy, Spain and the US praised Viktor Orbán, Hungary’s prime minister, as an example of strong leadership. They said he keeps Hungary independent from the EU and fights for its people. Orbán faces a tough election, with rivals who want closer ties to Brussels and Ukraine. Polls suggest he could lose, but his supporters hope he will win to keep Hungary “self‑determined.”
