After the Iran conflict spiked oil prices, EU leaders push the US to enforce the G7 price cap on Russian crude. They warn that easing sanctions would help Russia. France will inspect petrol stations, Italy plans to tax profiteering firms, Germany and Austria refuse to lift sanctions, while Hungary and Croatia set fuel price limits. Smaller states also look at temporary price cuts.
