A fresh cold front is headed into the southeastern U.S., bringing record‑low temperatures, power outages, and a chance of snow to places like Florida and South Carolina. Millions are still without power, and the worst‑hit states are Tennessee and Mississippi. The cold could harm Florida’s crops, and NASA will delay its Artemis‑3 test launch. Officials warn people to stay warm and check on vulnerable neighbors in the coming days.
