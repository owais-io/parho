Manchester City broke a four‑game winless run by beating Wolves 3‑1 at home. Manager Pep Guardiola left star striker Erling Haaland and playmaker Phil Foden out, bringing in others like Marmoush and Bernardo Silva. A VAR review of a Mosquera handball was dismissed by the referee, Farai Hallam, a first‑time top‑flight official, sparking Guardiola’s frustration. The win is a relief for City after a rough patch, while Wolves stay far from safety.
