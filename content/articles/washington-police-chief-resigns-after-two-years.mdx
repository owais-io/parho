Pamela Smith, the first Black woman to lead Washington, DC’s police, is stepping down on Dec. 31 after less than two years in charge. She says she wants more family time, not because of Trump’s federal takeover of the department. Mayor Bowser praised her for cutting crime and restoring safety. The city is still handling federal control, a crime‑stats investigation, and will soon need a new chief.
