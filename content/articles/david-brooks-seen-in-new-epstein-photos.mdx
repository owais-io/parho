David Brooks was found in a group photo from a 2011 dinner with Jeffrey Epstein, released by a House committee. He was sitting next to Google co‑founder Sergey Brin. The Times said Brooks had no further ties to Epstein. The photos come after Brooks wrote a column dismissing the Epstein saga. His appearance with Epstein, though brief, has drawn criticism for not being disclosed earlier. Other figures in the photos include Bill Gates and Woody Allen.
