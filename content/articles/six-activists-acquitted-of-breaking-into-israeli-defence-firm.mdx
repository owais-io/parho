Six activists from the Palestinian advocacy group Palestine Action were found not guilty of aggravated burglary after a court heard that they had broken into Elbit Systems’ factory in Filton, near Bristol, on 6 August. The activists were accused of bringing sledgehammers and threatening violence, but a jury found no evidence of violent disorder or criminal damage. The judge ended the case after 36 hours of deliberation, leaving the defendants free to continue their protests.
