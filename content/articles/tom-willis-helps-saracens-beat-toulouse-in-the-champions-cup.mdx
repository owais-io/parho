Saracens beat Toulouse 21-10 in the Champions Cup thanks to a strong first half from Rotimi Segun and Tom Willis, who scored two tries. The match saw a friendly clash between the Willis brothers, adding a family twist. The win lifts Saracens’ chances of reaching the last‑16 and gives the team confidence after a tough season. Toulouse struggled to keep up in wet conditions, and Saracens now face Glasgow Warriors for a knockout spot.
