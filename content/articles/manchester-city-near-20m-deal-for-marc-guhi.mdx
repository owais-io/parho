Manchester City is about to sign centre‑back Marc Guéhi from Crystal Palace for £20 million, just before his contract ends. Palace manager Oliver Glasner said the deal is almost final and Guéhi will miss the upcoming match against Sunderland. City need a defender because key players like Rúben Dias and John Stones are injured, and the move could help keep their defence strong.
