A new Guardian investigation found that the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) chartered a private jet owned by Florida property magnate Gil Dezer, a long‑time friend of Donald Trump, to fly Palestinian men from Arizona to Tel Aviv. The men were then handed over to Israeli authorities and sent back to the West Bank. Officials say the flights were part of the Trump administration’s push for mass deportations, but ICE has not commented on the cost or details.
