Jad Salamah, a Year 12 student at Condell Park High, was denied entry to his graduation ceremony in September 2024 after refusing to remove a keffiyeh that bore the Palestinian flag. He sued the New South Wales education department for racial discrimination, and the parties later settled confidentially. The department said it values cultural diversity, while anti‑racism groups say the case shows ongoing anti‑Palestinian bias in Australian schools.
