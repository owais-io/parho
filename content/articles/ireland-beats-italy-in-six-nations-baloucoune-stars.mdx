In a rain‑slick match in Dublin, Ireland beat Italy 10‑5 to keep their Six Nations hopes alive. The game was hard‑hit and physical, but a brilliant try by Robert Baloucoune lifted the hosts, while Jack Conan added a second early in the second half. Italy fought back, but couldn't score a bonus point. Ireland remains in contention for the top spot and will aim for a stronger finish in the next match against Wales.
