Chloe Kim, the 25‑year‑old snowboarder who won gold in 2018 and 2022, hurt her shoulder while training in Switzerland. She fell from the halfpipe, dislocating the joint and now needs an MRI to see how bad it is. With the Winter Games less than a month away, she’s unsure if she can compete, but says she has no major pain and hopes to recover quickly. The injury could end her bid for a historic third straight gold medal.
