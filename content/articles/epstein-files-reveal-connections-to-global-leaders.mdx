A fresh batch of about 3 million documents about Jeffrey Epstein has just been made public. Inside, lawyers‑style emails show the convicted sex‑offender chatting with people like Norway’s Crown Princess, wellness guru Deepak Chopra, former UK ambassador Peter Mandelson, ex‑Israeli prime minister Ehud Barak, and Indian billionaire Anil Ambani. The papers also give a bizarre rulebook that the billionaire told his Florida staff to follow. The files don’t prove anyone did anything illegal, but they reveal how Epstein’s network spread worldwide.
