The letters argue that social media can be both harmful and helpful.  Some writers point to the toxicity of big sites and say they should be banned, while others highlight how new platforms like Bluesky, UpScrolled, and Mastodon let people share useful ideas without ads.  They say the quality of a site depends on the people who run it, and that users can choose to quit or push for better rules.
