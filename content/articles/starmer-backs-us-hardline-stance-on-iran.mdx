Keir Starmer said the UK supports the US effort to stop Iran from getting nuclear weapons and criticised the government’s crackdown on protests. He didn’t comment on possible US strikes but voiced support for Trump’s hard‑line tone. The comments were made during a China visit, where he met allies and urged action against the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps. The EU has labelled the IRGC a terrorist group, and the UK plans new law to proscribe it.
