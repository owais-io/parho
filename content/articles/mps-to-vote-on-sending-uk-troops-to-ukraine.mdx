Starmer told MPs that the UK will only send soldiers to Ukraine after a peace deal and that Parliament must debate and vote on it first. He said the government would give details later, including how many troops, but no statement is needed until the decision is made. Conservatives asked why he didn’t give a full speech, and Starmer said the plan follows existing military rules. The move would start after a ceasefire.
