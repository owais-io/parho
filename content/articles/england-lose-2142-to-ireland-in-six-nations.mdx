Ireland beat England 42‑21 at Twickenham in a Six Nations match. They dominated from the start, with key tries from Jamison Gibson‑Park, Caelan Doris and Stuart McCloskey. England struggled with inaccuracy and missed chances, and several players underperformed. Ireland’s defence was solid and their midfield kept the ball moving. The win gives Ireland a strong position in the championship and a memorable comeback.
