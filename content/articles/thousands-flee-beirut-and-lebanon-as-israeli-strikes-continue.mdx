Israeli forces launched strikes on Hezbollah targets across Lebanon after the group fired missiles and drones at Israel, killing 31 people and injuring 149 in Beirut’s southern suburbs. The army urged residents of about 50 villages in eastern and southern Lebanon to leave, sparking mass exodus. Meanwhile, the U.S. claims many Iranian leaders have been hit, oil prices jumped, and flights have been grounded.
