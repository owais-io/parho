About 75 people gathered at Cleveland Heights’ community center to watch the U.S. women’s ice‑hockey team win gold at the Winter Olympics. The crowd cheered when Laila Edwards, a hometown player, scored the winning goal in overtime. Residents felt proud, shared stories, and celebrated the city’s first Black woman on the team. The event sparked town‑wide support and plans for a parade when her college season ends.
