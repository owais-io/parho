Many fresh graduates in the UK are having a tough time finding jobs. Leah Savage, a marketing grad, has applied to 100 jobs and only had two interviews, saying it feels hopeless. Young people say employers want experience they can't get yet, and AI tools sift out CVs because of formatting. Unemployment for those under 25 is over 15%, and many are on benefits or living with parents.
