Richard Moth, the new Archbishop of Westminster, said the Catholic Church has let vulnerable people, like refugees and abuse victims, down. He urges the church to listen to those hurt and protect all its members. Moth also criticised government plans to keep asylum seekers in hotels and called for better care. He said the church has a chance to attract new believers, especially young adults, after COVID.
