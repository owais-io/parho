Former Townsville mayor Troy Thompson said he has epilepsy to promote an electromagnetic bracelet tied to his partner’s business. Queensland’s Crime and Corruption Commission found no proof he was diagnosed, just that he had seizures and took medication. Thompson has already been caught lying about cancer, his military record and university degrees. No criminal charges yet, but the false epilepsy claim was part of a larger scandal that hurt his political career.
