About 40 people died and 119 were injured in a bar fire in the Swiss ski resort Crans‑Montana. The blaze started when sparklers set bottles on fire. Many badly burned survivors are in Swiss burn wards and have been flown to hospitals in France, Germany, Belgium and elsewhere. Identifying the dead is hard, so doctors are using DNA and dental records. Families and governments are working together to find missing victims.
