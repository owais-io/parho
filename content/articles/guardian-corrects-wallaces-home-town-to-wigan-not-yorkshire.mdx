The Guardian is issuing a set of corrections about its recent stories. The most notable fix is about the famous cartoon character Wallace, who is actually from Wigan in Lancashire, not Yorkshire, as a previous article claimed. The publisher also amended several other items – from a measles outbreak in London to a book review and a biography – to fix errors and clarify facts. Readers can send feedback to the editorial team.
