A high‑speed train crash near Adamuz, Spain, killed at least 39 people and injured dozens. Two trains collided – a private Iryo service heading north to Madrid and a Renfe train going south to Huelva. The crash happened just after 8 p.m. on a straight stretch of track, leaving cars twisted and debris spread far from the spot. Rescue teams are still searching for missing passengers, and investigators have not yet identified the exact cause.
