Guardian readers have already raised more than £500,000 for a Christmas charity drive called Hope. The money goes to five community groups that work to unite people, fight hate, and support local projects such as food kitchens, youth clubs and neighborhood rebuilds. Stories of a Jewish‑Muslim food kitchen and a post‑riot neighborhood makeover have inspired many donors, who say giving spreads hope and kindness in a time of rising bigotry.
