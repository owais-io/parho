Australia’s Winter Olympics have surprised fans. Steven Bradbury captured the first Australian gold in 2002 when the other skaters crashed. Zali Steggall earned a bronze in alpine skiing in 1998, proving Aussies could compete on snow. Alisa Camplin won aerials gold in 2002 and Lydia Lassila took bronze in 2010. Torah Bright topped it with a 2010 snowboard half‑pipe gold. These five medals still fuel hope for future Games.
