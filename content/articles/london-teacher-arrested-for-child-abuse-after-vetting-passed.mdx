Vincent Chan, 45, worked as a teaching assistant and nursery worker in north London. He was arrested in June 2024 after police found thousands of indecent images and videos of children. Chan has admitted 56 offences, including sexual assaults and voyeurism, against toddlers and girls as young as two. His case shows how safeguarding checks failed to stop him, and parents are demanding accountability.
