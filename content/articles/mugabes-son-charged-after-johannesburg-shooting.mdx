Bellarmine Chatunga Mugabe, 28, the son of former Zimbabwean president Robert Mugabe, has been charged with attempted murder after a 23‑year‑old was shot in the back in Johannesburg on Feb. 19. He also faces charges of obstruction, illegal firearms, immigration violations and theft. The case is still under investigation, the gun has not been found, and the victim remains hospitalized. Mugabe’s lawyer seeks bail at the next hearing.
