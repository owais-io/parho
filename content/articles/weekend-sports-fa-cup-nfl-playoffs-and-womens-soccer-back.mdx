This weekend starts with the FA Cup third‑round games, where lower‑league Macclesfield play Crystal Palace and several Premier League teams face each other, including Tottenham vs Aston Villa and Newcastle vs Bournemouth. The Women’s Super League is back after the winter break, with Arsenal hosting Manchester United and West Ham visiting Chelsea. Meanwhile, the NFL playoffs kick off, featuring the Jaguars against the Bills and the Eagles vs 49ers.
