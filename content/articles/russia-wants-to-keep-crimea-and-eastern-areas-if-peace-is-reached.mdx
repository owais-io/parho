Russia is asking for control over Crimea, Sevastopol, and parts of eastern Ukraine such as Donetsk and Luhansk if peace talks go forward. They say those areas must stay under Russian rule to protect their interests. Ukraine and the West see this as a threat to its borders. The demand is part of Russia’s new peace plan, but it would give Russia more territory.
