England won their second ODI against Sri Lanka, ending an 11‑game losing streak at home. Joe Root steadied the chase, scoring 75 before getting out, while bowler Adil Rashid took 2 wickets for 34 runs on a turning pitch. England’s batting lineup, especially the partnership between Root and Harry Brook, put the game in control, and the team secured a five‑wicket victory to level the series.
