More people in the UK are now talking about a possible civil war. The idea started in fringe online groups but has spread to newspapers, social media and even some MPs. A professor from King's College and other right‑wing voices claim that Britain could split into three sides—Muslim communities, anti‑government white Britons and the state—leading to violence and even ethnic cleansing. Though some experts say the country is prepared to handle conflict, polls show that up to a third of Britons think war could happen in the next decade.
