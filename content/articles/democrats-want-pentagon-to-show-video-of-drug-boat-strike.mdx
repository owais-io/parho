U.S. Democrats are asking the Pentagon to release footage of a 2‑September drone strike on a drug‑smuggling boat in the Caribbean. The attack killed 11 people, and a follow‑up strike killed two more who were reportedly clinging to wreckage. Critics say the shots were illegal and could be war crimes. The Pentagon says the operation was lawful, while the president says it could be released after review.
