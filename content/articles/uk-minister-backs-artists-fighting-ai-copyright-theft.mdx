UK technology secretary Liz Kendall says she understands artists want to be paid when AI companies use their work. She wants to reset the debate and find a solution that works for creators and tech firms. Artists like Paul McCartney, Elton John, and others have protested AI use of copyrighted material. The government is working on new rules and will publish reports by the end of this year and March 2026.
