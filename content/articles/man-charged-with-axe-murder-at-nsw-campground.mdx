A 26‑year‑old man, Ahwen Kohn, has been charged with murder after an axe attack at a remote New South Wales campground. The incident happened in the Little Styx River area near Ebor, where Kohn and two friends were camping. A 33‑year‑old man died from head injuries, and a 25‑year‑old woman was also hurt. Police arrested Kohn after the friend drove him to help. He faces up to life in prison.
