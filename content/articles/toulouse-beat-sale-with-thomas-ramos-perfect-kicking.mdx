Toulouse won easily at home, beating Sale 47‑16 in a European rugby pool match. Thomas Ramos scored 27 points – a try and 11 of 11 conversions – showing why he’s the world’s top kicker. Sale were weakened, missing 13 players, and Toulouse had injuries but still pushed hard. Both teams clinched a spot in the last‑16 knockout stage, with Toulouse moving up to second place in their pool.
