Australia’s measles cases jumped last year, partly because kids aren’t getting vaccinated enough and many people travelled abroad for the holidays. Clusters of infections were found after a traveller spread the virus in shopping malls and cinemas on Boxing Day, leading NSW, SA, WA and Victoria to issue alerts. Doctors are telling people to check if they have two MMR shots – free boosters are available for those born after 1966.
