Several Republican lawmakers are upset about a U.S. Justice Department investigation into Federal Reserve chair Jerome Powell. Senator Thom Tillis says he will block every Fed appointment until the probe is finished, while other senators and congressmen call for the Fed to stay independent. The DOJ subpoena followed Powell’s testimony on the costs of a new Fed building. Powell insists he follows the law, but Trump wants lower rates.
