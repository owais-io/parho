Hundreds of tonnes of illegal rubbish have piled up 10 metres high in a field near the River Cherwell in Oxfordshire. The local MP says removing it could cost more than the council’s whole annual budget. The Environment Agency has limited resources and has closed the site for six months while it investigates. Organised crime is suspected, and the pile threatens toxic runoff into the river, endangering wildlife and local communities.
