Minnesota officials say the FBI won’t give them evidence about the January shooting that killed Alex Pretti, a VA nurse who was shot by Customs and Border Protection officers during a federal immigration crackdown. The state agency says it can’t investigate properly without the info. Governor Tim Walz wants an independent probe, while the FBI has also blocked evidence in other similar cases. The refusal has angered officials and raised calls for a fair investigation.
