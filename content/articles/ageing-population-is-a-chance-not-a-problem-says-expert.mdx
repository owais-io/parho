Ageing is not a crisis – it's a chance. Prof Sarah Harper says many older adults are healthy, educated and ready to work. She urges new jobs, flexible hours and retraining, and better childcare to help younger generations. Fertility rates are low everywhere, but that can be managed by supporting women and improving work, housing and gender equality. The key is to value older workers and link pensions to contributions.
