John Harbaugh has been signed to a five‑year contract as the New York Giants’ new head coach, ending a brief period on the market after being let go by the Baltimore Ravens. The deal could be worth up to $100 million. Harbaugh said he’s proud to join the historic franchise and is excited to work with the team and owners. He’ll start guiding the Giants in 2026.
