Parents of 16‑year‑old Murray Dowey are suing Meta, the company behind Instagram, after he killed himself in December 2023. The parents say he was tricked into sending private photos to a gang that used them to extort money, a crime that Meta’s design made easier. They claim the platform failed to protect teens and misled users about safety. Meta has added new safety tools, but the Doweys say nothing has changed.
