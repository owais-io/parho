England’s cricket captain, Harry Brook, has admitted he lied about the night he was punched by a nightclub bouncer in Wellington. He said teammates were there, but he tried to hide it. The incident is under investigation, and Brook and two other players have been fined. He apologized and said he will learn from the mistake. Brook remains England’s captain and will lead the team at the T20 World Cup.
