Trump has warned he will raise tariffs on European goods if Greenland isn’t handed over to the U.S. German and UK industry leaders say the extra taxes would cost billions and hurt jobs. EU leaders are meeting in Brussels to decide on a joint response, while manufacturers scramble to ship products before the 1 February deadline. The move risks a full trade war between the EU and the U.S.
