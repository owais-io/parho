With a win over Brentford, Chelsea won their first Premier League game in six matches. Manager Liam Rosenior praised the players’ grit after a tense match. João Pedro opened the scoring with a clever finish, and later Cole Palmer converted a penalty to seal the 2‑1 win. Brentford missed chances and had a player sent off. The result boosts confidence and keeps Chelsea close to Manchester United in the standings.
