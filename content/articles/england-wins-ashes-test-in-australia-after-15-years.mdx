England won the Boxing Day Test in Melbourne, chasing 175 to beat Australia by four wickets. It was England's first win in Australia in almost 15 years and ended their 18‑match losing streak on Australian soil. Ben Stokes and Joe Root helped chase the target, while England's bowlers took Australia out twice on a difficult pitch. The victory keeps England alive in the Ashes series, moving them to a 3‑1 lead.
