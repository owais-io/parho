Musk said the Trump‑era job‑cutting program he led, called Doge, was only a little successful and he would never do it again. He explained that it laid off more than 200,000 workers, saved some money (though the figures are unverified), and caused chaos in government. Musk added he’d rather focus on Tesla, SpaceX, and X.
