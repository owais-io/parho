A Russian official warned that Moscow’s navy would step in to protect Russian‑linked vessels from what he calls “western piracy.” He said blockades are illegal and would be broken. The comments come as Russia’s shadow fleet of tankers tries to dodge sanctions, while the EU, US and UK have already targeted many of those ships but struggle to seize them physically. The warning adds pressure to ongoing Ukraine talks.
