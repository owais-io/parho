Laura Wittmann, a Uline customer‑service worker, sent a company‑wide email and resigned because she could no longer work for a firm run by the Uihleins, the U.S. election’s biggest donors to Trump‑aligned Republicans. She criticized their money, their political influence, and their support of policies she sees as harmful. The letter, shared on Reddit, called on other staff to stand up, and Uline accepted her resignation immediately.
