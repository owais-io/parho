A 10‑year‑old girl from Minnesota was freed from an ICE detention center in Texas after a month. She and her mother were taken by federal agents on Jan. 6 and were the first of five children from the same school district to be held. A judge blocked their removal, and the family’s asylum case is still pending. The move follows a planned reduction of ICE staff in Minnesota.
