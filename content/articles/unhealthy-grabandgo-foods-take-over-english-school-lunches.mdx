Pupils in England are eating pizza, sausage rolls, and paninis because grab‑and‑go food is easier to pick up on the move. A Bite Back report says 60 % of students buy these items at least once a week, and the trend could raise obesity and hurt concentration. The charity and chefs like Jamie Oliver want stricter rules, better monitoring, and healthier menu choices.
