Peter Mandelson, who was fired as Britain’s ambassador to the United States, may soon be asked to hand over text and email records from his personal phone. The Cabinet Office is digging into how he got the post, after MPs raised concerns about his ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The investigation will compile messages from anyone who talked to Mandelson and send the data to parliament and police for review.
