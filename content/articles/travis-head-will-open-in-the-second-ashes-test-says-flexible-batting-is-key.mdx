Travis Head has arrived in Brisbane to get ready for the second Ashes Test and says he’ll bat again at the top of the order. He believes teams that can put batters in any spot will win, echoing captain Pat Cummins’ view that the batting order is “overrated” and that players can be used in many ways. Head says he’s not worried about the pink ball or lights; he’ll simply adapt to the wicket and play the game.
