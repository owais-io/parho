Anthropic and OpenAI are fighting over whether their chatbots should show ads. Anthropic launched ads that poke fun at OpenAI, while OpenAI says ads will be separate, clear and not affect answers. OpenAI wants to keep AI free for everyone, but plans revenue from ads. Anthropic argues ads hurt the chatbot’s helpfulness and privacy. The clash shows how companies balance money, user trust and free access.
