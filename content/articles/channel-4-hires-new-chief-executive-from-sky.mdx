Channel 4 has named Priya Dogra, who used to run Sky’s advertising business, as its new chief executive. She will start in the new year after the previous boss left for a live‑event company. Dogra’s move comes as Channel 4 faces a possible takeover of ITV by Comcast, which would create a huge TV‑ad monopoly. The broadcaster says it will fight any deal that threatens its future.
