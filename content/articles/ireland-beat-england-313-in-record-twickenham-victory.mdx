Ireland stunned England with a 31‑3 win at Twickenham, setting a new venue record. The Irish team dominated from the start, pulling ahead to a 22‑0 lead before lunch. England struggled with turnovers, line‑out mistakes and two yellow cards, while Ireland’s defence stayed solid and their backs kept scoring. Ireland now keeps the Six Nations title on the line.
