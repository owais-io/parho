Former CIA chief Leon Panetta says U.S. President Donald Trump is the main reason the Iran war started after a strike on Iran’s supreme leader. Panetta warns the conflict is dragging on, raising U.S. oil prices, and that Trump’s unclear goals and poor teamwork with allies could lead to a bigger fight—perhaps by opening the Hormuz waterway to keep oil flowing. The former defence secretary says Trump must stop “magical thinking” and face facts.
