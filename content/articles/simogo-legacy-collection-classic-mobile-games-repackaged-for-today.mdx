Simogo was started by two Swedes, Simon Flesser and Magnus Gardebäck, who left a studio to make fun iPhone games. Their first seven titles— from simple arcade fun like *Kosmo Spin* to the spooky adventure *Year Walk*—were all released between 2010 and 2015. Because iOS keeps changing, the studio re‑packaged the games for PC, Switch, and other devices, so they still work today. The collection costs £13.49 and lets you enjoy these charming, imaginative games anytime.
