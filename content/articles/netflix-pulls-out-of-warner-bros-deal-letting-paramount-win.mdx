Netflix decided not to raise its $82.7 billion offer for Warner Bros Discovery after Paramount Skydance lifted its bid to $31 per share. The streaming giant said the deal was no longer worth it and let Paramount win the fight. Paramount is now expected to buy Warner Bros, including CNN, but the merger will face antitrust scrutiny. Stock prices jumped for Netflix and rose for Paramount.
