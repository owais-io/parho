Airstrikes are pounding Iran’s northern border with Iraq, and the US and Israel say they’re ready to give Kurdish fighters air support if they move into Iraq. Baloch militants are also crossing into Iran from Pakistan, and experts warn that supporting ethnic armed groups could spark a civil war if Tehran falls. The US says it can’t shoot every Iranian drone, so it’s focusing on destroying drone launch sites.
