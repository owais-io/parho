The writer spends a sunny morning walking along the coast near Gwbert, Wales. He describes cliffs, gulls, and a fishing boat, then climbs a hill to see a small church tucked in the hillside. The view includes the beach at Mwnt and the surrounding hills. He notes the birds, sheep, and the occasional chough. It’s a peaceful, picturesque spot that invites repeated visits.
