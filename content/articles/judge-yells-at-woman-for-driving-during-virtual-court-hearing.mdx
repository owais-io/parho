Kimberly Carroll was seen driving a car while joining a Detroit court hearing on Zoom. Judge Michael McNally called her out, asking if she was really the driver and told her she couldn't be driving. Carroll insisted she was a passenger, but the video showed she was in the driver's seat. She later apologized, admitting she made a mistake, and the incident went viral online.
