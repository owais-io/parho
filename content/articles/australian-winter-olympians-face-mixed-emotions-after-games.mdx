After the Winter Olympics in Italy, Australian athletes are dealing with a mix of pride, gratitude, anger, and disappointment. Snowboard star Scotty James, who almost won gold, says his emotions run deep after the narrow miss. Former Olympians and new team members talk about how the Games change them, and the next season and future competitions will shape their paths. Many athletes are already planning their return or next goals.
