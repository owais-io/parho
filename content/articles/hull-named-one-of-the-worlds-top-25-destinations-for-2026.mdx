Hull has been named one of the world’s top 25 places to visit in 2026, thanks to a £70 million makeover that’s turning the city into a tourist hotspot. The project will bring back the famous Arctic Corsair trawler, open the historic Spurn Lightship, restore Queen’s Gardens and reopen the Maritime Museum. With a polar bear exhibit and a new aquarium, Hull aims to surprise visitors and boost its reputation.
