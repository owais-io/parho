Hamas has begun a secret vote to choose a new leader after heavy losses in Gaza. Members across Gaza, the West Bank, prisons and the diaspora are voting for a 50‑member council that will pick the politburo and interim boss. The main candidates are Khalil al‑Hayya, a hard‑liner linked to Iran, and Khaled Meshaal, who favors dialogue with Israel. The choice will shape Hamas’s future and the possibility of peace in Gaza.
