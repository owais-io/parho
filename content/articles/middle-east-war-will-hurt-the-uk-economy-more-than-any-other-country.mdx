The OECD says the Middle East war will hurt the UK economy more than any other industrialised country, cutting growth to 0.7% for 2026 from 1.2% because of higher inflation and costly energy imports. France, Germany and Italy will see smaller hits of about 0.2%. Oil prices have doubled after US‑Israel attacks on Iran, raising business costs. The UK plans to use AI, stronger EU ties and more local powers to strengthen its economy.
