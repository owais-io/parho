Iran’s new foreign minister Abbas Araghchi is heading to Geneva to talk with U.S. negotiators about the nuclear deal. Araghchi is a veteran diplomat who prefers patient, step‑by‑step bargaining, unlike the more direct U.S. envoy. He has worked closely with Iran’s top leaders and has a long history of dealing with the nuclear issue. Talks may involve easing Iran’s enrichment, re‑opening IAEA sites, and easing U.S. sanctions.
