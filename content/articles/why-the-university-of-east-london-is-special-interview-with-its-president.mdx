The University of East London (UEL) has flipped a risky, debt‑heavy past into a thriving, debt‑free institution that values social inclusion and career readiness. Since 2018, UEL has grown, improved graduate outcomes by 25 % in five years, and added new student‑started businesses. Its president says the university’s vision keeps everyone involved and rewards staff. UEL is also building health‑care hubs and wants researchers who make real‑world impact.
