In a surprising by‑election, Green candidate Hannah Spencer won with 40% of the vote, outpacing Reform’s 28% and Labour’s 25%. Spencer, a former plumber and plasterer, gave a modest victory speech that highlighted her working‑class roots and local community. Reform’s Matt Goodwin angrily blamed the Greens for cheating, while Labour’s Angeliki Stogia was left humiliated. The result marks a historic win for the Greens.
