An illegal weight‑loss drug operation was raided in Northampton, seizing thousands of unlicensed pens that may contain the unapproved drug retatrutide. No one has been arrested yet. A Guardian investigation points to entrepreneur Fasial Tariq, who runs Wholesale Supplements Ltd. Tariq also backed a controversial crypto venture, Paradox Coin, and his high‑end car rental business. The case shows regulators are catching up slowly with online drug sales that can be dangerous.
