President Trump gave a long talk about the U.S. strike on Iran’s leader, saying the fight could go 4‑5 weeks but might last longer. He blamed Iran’s nuclear and missile plans and said the war aims to destroy those programs and stop Iranian support for militants. Trump also bragged about a new White House ballroom, which many found tone‑deaf. Critics point out doubts about his claims and the war’s cost.
