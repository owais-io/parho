Australia is looking at offering help to Gulf countries hit by Iran’s missile attacks, but it says it won’t send troops into a war. Foreign Minister Penny Wong told ABC that Australia will give defensive support, like air‑defence or logistics, while keeping its forces out of offensive actions. The government has helped many Australians leave the region and has launched Operation Beech to bring them home.
