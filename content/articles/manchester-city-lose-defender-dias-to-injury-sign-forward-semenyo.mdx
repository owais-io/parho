Manchester City have a big injury problem after defender Rúben Dias is out for up to six weeks with a hamstring injury. The club is now looking to strengthen its back line, even eyeing Crystal Palace captain Marc Guéhi. Meanwhile, they’re close to signing forward Antoine Semenyo, paying almost £65m to meet his release clause, with a medical scheduled. City will have only 13 fit senior players for their next game.
