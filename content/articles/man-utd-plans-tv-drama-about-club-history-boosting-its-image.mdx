Manchester United is looking to make a TV series that tells the story of the club, from the Munich disaster to recent successes. The show, said to be similar to The Crown, would be produced by Lionsgate and could bring in a few million pounds. The club is also hoping to use the drama to improve its image after a costly, poorly received post‑season tour in Asia last summer.
