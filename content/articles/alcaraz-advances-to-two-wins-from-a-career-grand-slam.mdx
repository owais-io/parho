Alcaraz edged out Australian Alex de Minaur in a straight‑sets win at the Australian Open, moving just two matches away from the first career Grand Slam. De Minaur had impressed earlier by beating former top‑10 players, but Alcaraz’s speed, power, and focus turned the match in his favor. Alcaraz talked about staying concentrated and said his hard work is paying off.
