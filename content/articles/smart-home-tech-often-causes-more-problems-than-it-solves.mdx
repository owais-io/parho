Many people are frustrated with smart gadgets that don’t work as promised. A popular doorbell only sends a phone alert, so deliveries are missed when no one’s home. Other issues include lockouts, lights that fail, and even hacked cameras. Repair crews struggle because few are trained on these devices, and some companies stop supporting older models. Some experts suggest a mix of smart and old‑school tools for safety and privacy.
