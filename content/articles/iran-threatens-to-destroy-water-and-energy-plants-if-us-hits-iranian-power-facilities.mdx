Iran says it will destroy water and energy plants across the Middle East if the U.S. follows through on President Trump’s threat to destroy Iranian power plants. The U.S. has given Iran 48 hours to open the Strait of Hormuz, a vital oil route. Meanwhile, Iran has launched missiles at Israeli cities and threatens to hit U.S. and Israeli infrastructure, raising fears of a wider war and severe civilian harm.
