High court papers about a £4 million claim over a Picasso painting have shown the secret offshore holdings of businessman Sasan Ghandehari, who paid Nigel Farage’s £50 000 Davos trip. The case pits a British‑Virgin‑Islands firm against Christie’s, which says Ghandehari never paid for the artwork. Court filings reveal Ghandehari’s $10 billion family trust and his wife’s ownership, raising questions about money that helped fund Farage’s campaign.
