Trump is pushing a new voting bill that would require proof of citizenship and strict voter‑ID rules, ban most mail ballots, and add restrictions on transgender participation in sports. He has threatened not to sign any other legislation until Congress approves the act. The bill faces a 60‑vote hurdle in the Senate and critics say it would disenfranchise millions, especially young voters and people of color.
