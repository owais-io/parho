Around 200,000 university students who studied during the Covid‑19 pandemic are now part of a large legal claim asking for money back because they didn’t get the in‑person teaching and campus facilities they paid for. The move follows University College London’s £21m settlement with 6,500 former students. Lawyers say the case is under consumer law, and they’ve already sent letters to 36 universities. The outcome could hit the whole sector.
