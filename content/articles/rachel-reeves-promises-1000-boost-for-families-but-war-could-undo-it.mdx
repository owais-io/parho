Rachel Reeves says people will be over £1,000 a year better off by the next election, but the war in the Middle East and rising energy prices could change that. The Office for Budget Responsibility expects disposable income to grow only 0.6‑0.9% a year, and inflation to stay near 2%. Mortgage rates are falling but may rise again, while energy bills could jump if gas prices stay high. Jobs are still shaky and the economy may slow.
