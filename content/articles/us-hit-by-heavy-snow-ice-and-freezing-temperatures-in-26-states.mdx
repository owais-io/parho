The United States is being hit by a powerful winter storm that has brought heavy snow, freezing rain, and very cold temperatures to 26 states. Snow fell across the south and moved north, causing travel chaos and 800,000+ homes without power. Ice covered roads and trees, even dropping power lines. The cold hit as low as –20 °C in Texas, and some trees “exploded” as sap froze inside. At least seven people died.
