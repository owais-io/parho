María Corina Machado, a leading Venezuelan opposition figure, will release a 120‑page book called The Freedom Manifesto in the U.S. The book explains her plan for rebuilding Venezuela's democracy after removing President Maduro, covering rights like freedom, security, and fair voting. Its release was delayed to add updates following recent political changes. The book will come in English and Spanish, with a print run of 50,000 copies.
