In Tumbler Ridge, a school shooting killed eight people, many children. The tragedy shocked Canada. People from all over flew to the small town to help. Volunteers brought food, therapy animals, counselors, and supplies. The Prime Minister and other leaders visited. A retired paramedic, pastor and police chaplain now stays to support residents as they cope with grief. The town is coming together, showing hope and solidarity amid the pain.
