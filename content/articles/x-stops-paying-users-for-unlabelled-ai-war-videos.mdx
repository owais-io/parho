X will stop paying users who post AI‑made war videos without a label. The platform says anyone who uploads such footage will lose creator revenue for 90 days and a repeat offence will mean a permanent ban. The rule comes after the Iran conflict saw fake battle clips spread across X, Instagram and Facebook, tricking millions into thinking they were real. Experts warn that cheap AI can fuel war misinformation and that people are even asking bots to confirm fakes.
