On a weekday in Minab, Iran, a missile strike turned a school into rubble and killed about 168 people, most of them girls. The UN says the attack must be investigated. Some blame Iran, others point to U.S. Tomahawk missiles found by the BBC. The bombing follows other school attacks in Iran and Gaza, highlighting how children are losing their right to safe learning.
