A 445‑page report by the National Anti‑Corruption Commission found two senior officials guilty of corrupt conduct in the illegal robodebt scheme, but it won’t lead to criminal charges. Victims, including the mother of Rhys Cauzzo who took his own life, say the findings are a huge disappointment. They want full accountability, a national apology, and laws that prevent the scheme from happening again.
