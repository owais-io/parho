Leonardo Helicopters in Yeovil is waiting for the UK Ministry of Defence to sign a new £1 billion helicopter contract. If the deal isn’t sealed before the end of March, the plant could shut down, putting about 3,000 jobs in jeopardy. Workers and the union are worried about a delay, while Leonardo has warned it may pull investments in Britain if the order is not confirmed. The MoD says defence spending is rising, but the plan is still being finalised.
