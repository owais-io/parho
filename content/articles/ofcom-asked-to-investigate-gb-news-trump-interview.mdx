Britain's media regulator, Ofcom, is being asked to look into a GB News interview with former U.S. President Donald Trump. Viewers say the show let Trump spread false claims about climate change, London policing, and sharia law without questioning them. Critics, including climate experts and political groups, argue the network broke impartiality rules. Ofcom has received many complaints but hasn't decided whether to launch an investigation.
