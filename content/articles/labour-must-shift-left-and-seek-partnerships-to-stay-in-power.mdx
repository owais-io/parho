Labour is in trouble and may lose the next election if it stays the same. The party should move further left, focus on its core voters and stop trying to please both right‑wing and left‑wing people at once. It could also seek a partnership with the Liberal Democrats, Greens or other parties, giving it a better chance of winning seats. The party must also give its activists more voice, as many feel ignored.
