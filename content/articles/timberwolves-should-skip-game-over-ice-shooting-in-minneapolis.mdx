An ICE officer shot and killed a woman in Minneapolis during a raid, sparking outrage and a mayoral call for ICE to leave the city. The Minnesota Timberwolves have a game against the Cleveland Cavaliers the next night, and the article argues they should refuse to play until the incident is investigated. It cites the 2020 NBA boycott after Jacob Blake’s death as a precedent, suggesting a boycott would force change.
