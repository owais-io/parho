Trump has asked the defense department and other agencies to start pulling out and making public all government documents that talk about aliens, extraterrestrial life, and UFOs. He said this after former President Obama joked about aliens on a podcast. The move comes amid growing public interest sparked by leaked Navy videos and a 2022 congressional hearing on UFOs. The Pentagon has promised more transparency, though most military UFO reports remain unexplained and often turn out to be drones.
