At 2 a.m. on Saturday, Caracas woke to explosions and helicopters. The US claimed it raided the capital and captured President Nicolás Maduro and his wife, taking them to a warship. Trump told Fox News it was a quick operation, but Venezuelan officials said the raid was a mistake. Residents heard smoke and sirens, and analysts warn that the regime may stay in power. People are unsure what comes next.
