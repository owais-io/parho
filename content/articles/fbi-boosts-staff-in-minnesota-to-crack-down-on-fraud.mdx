The FBI has sent more agents and investigators to Minnesota to take down big fraud cases that have stolen money from federal programs. It follows a $250 million scheme that led to 78 charges and 57 convictions. Federal prosecutors say up to $9 billion may have been stolen, largely linked to the state’s Somali community. The move comes after Trump’s attacks on Somali immigrants and after Representative Ilhan Omar was criticized for ties to alleged fraudsters.
