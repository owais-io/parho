Over 300 earthquakes were recorded in the UK this year, the British Geological Survey says. The biggest felt events were two near Loch Lyon in Perth and Kinross on Oct 20, with magnitudes 3.7 and 3.6. People felt the tremors even 37 miles away. Other hot spots were Scotland’s western Highlands, southern Wales and northern England. The BGS monitors the ground with 80 stations and urges continued study of small quakes.
