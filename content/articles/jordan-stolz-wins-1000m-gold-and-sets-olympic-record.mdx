American speed skater Jordan Stolz won the men’s 1000 metres at the Winter Olympics, setting a new Olympic record of 1:06.28. After a 15‑minute delay for a reskate by Dutch skater Joep Wennemars, Stolz’s finish left the Dutch crowd stunned. The win puts him on track to chase up to four gold medals this Games, ending a 16‑year U.S. medal drought.
