Israel’s president, Isaac Herzog, is set to arrive in Australia next week. The government welcomes him, saying the trip can help heal after the Bondi massacre, but many people, including some Jewish groups, say he should be barred because of his comments about the Gaza war. Palestinian activists plan mass protests and have asked police to investigate him for alleged incitement. The Australian police are reviewing the complaints.
