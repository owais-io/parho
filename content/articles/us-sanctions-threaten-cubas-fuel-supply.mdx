Trump’s new tariffs on countries that sell oil to Cuba are pushing the island toward a fuel crisis. Only one shipment has arrived this year, and Cuba could run out of gasoline and diesel in weeks. Mexico cancelled its planned delivery, and Venezuela, Russia and Algeria have stopped sending oil. The shortages are leaving everyday people waiting in line, affecting transport, electricity and hospitals.
