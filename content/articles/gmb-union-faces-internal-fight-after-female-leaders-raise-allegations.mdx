The GMB union is in turmoil after two senior female staff say they were bullied, harassed and unfairly fired. The first was dismissed for alleged racist remarks, the second is on sick leave after raising complaints. Both claim the firings were retaliation for their whistle‑blowing. GMB says the dismissals were justified and that the claims were unsubstantiated. The dispute comes as the union’s general secretary, Gary Smith, faces a May re‑election.
