Lawyers for Palestinian prisoners in the UK say the men and women refusing food could die if MP David Lammy doesn’t meet them. Two strikers have been on hunger strike for 45 days, and their health is worsening. The prisoners want to end a ban on their group, have better communication, and get medical help. Many MPs have asked Lammy for a meeting, but he has not replied.
