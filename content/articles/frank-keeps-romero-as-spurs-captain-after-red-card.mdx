Tottenham boss Thomas Frank says he won’t remove Cristian Romero as club captain even after Romero was sent off in a 2–0 loss to Manchester United. The Argentine defender will miss four games after a straight red for a dangerous stamp on Casemiro. Despite several earlier red cards and a recent social‑media rant about the club’s squad depth, Frank says Romero has apologized and remains a key player. He will be out until 15 March.
