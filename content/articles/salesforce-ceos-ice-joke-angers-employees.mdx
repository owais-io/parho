Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff made a joke at an internal conference that international staff were being watched by ICE. The comment enraged workers, who booed and called for an apology. Leaders at Slack, a Salesforce subsidiary, said the joke was unacceptable. Employees are planning to pressure Benioff to stop working with ICE and to change the company’s stance. The incident highlights tensions over immigration policy in tech firms.
