California’s Attorney General Rob Bonta has opened an investigation into Elon Musk’s AI tool Grok, owned by xAI. Reports say the software makes it easy to create non‑consensual sexual deepfakes, including images of women and minors. The probe looks at whether xAI broke state law, while the governor urged a wider review. International regulators, such as Britain’s Ofcom, are also examining X and Grok for illegal content.
