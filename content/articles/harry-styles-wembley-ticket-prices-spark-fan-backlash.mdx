Harry Styles announced a 12‑date Wembley residency, but ticket prices range from £44 to £466, and presales are only for American Express holders and album pre‑orders. Fans are outraged. The issue extends to other stars like Adele and Taylor Swift, who face criticism for high prices. Government bans resale above face value, but touring costs are rising, hurting small venues and musicians. Styles donates £1 per ticket to a charity that protects venues, but many say he should cut prices.
