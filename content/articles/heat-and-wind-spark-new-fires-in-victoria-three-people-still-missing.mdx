A sudden shift of strong north‑westerly winds, combined with record‑high temperatures, has sparked fresh bushfires across Victoria. The fires have burned many homes, especially in the town of Ruffy, and left over 70,000 houses without power. Three people—a man, a woman and a child—went missing in the Longwood area. State officials are offering emergency relief and urging residents to stay safe.
