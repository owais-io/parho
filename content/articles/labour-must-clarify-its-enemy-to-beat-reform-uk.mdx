Labour’s leadership is split while the party faces a new rival, Reform UK. Prime Minister Keir Starmer has warned that Reform’s policies could tear Britain apart, but some party officials blur the line between Reform and the Conservatives. The party needs a clear, unified attack on Reform, backed by polling, to win back voters and protect its base. Without a decisive message, Labour risks losing ground to rivals.
