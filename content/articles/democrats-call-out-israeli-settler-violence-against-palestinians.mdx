U.S. Democratic lawmakers are publicly condemning violent attacks by Israeli settlers on Palestinian civilians in the West Bank. Politicians like Ritchie Torres and Daniel Goldman urge the Israeli government to prosecute attackers and warn that such violence hurts Israel’s global standing. The criticism comes amid a sharp rise in public support for Palestinians among Democrats, a shift that could influence upcoming elections and Aipac‑backed campaigns.
