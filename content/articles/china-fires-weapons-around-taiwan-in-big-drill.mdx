China launched a big military drill around Taiwan, sending ships, planes and missiles to simulate a blockade of the island’s ports. The exercise, called Justice Mission 2025, warned Taiwan that Beijing wants the island back under its control. Taiwan’s defence forces said they would respond, and the U.S. has approved a large weapons package for the island. The drill raised tensions with Japan and shows China’s growing military power.
