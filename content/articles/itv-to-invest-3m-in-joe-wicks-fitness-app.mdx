ITV has agreed to put up to £3 million into the Body Coach fitness app created by fitness star Joe Wicks. In return, the app will run adverts on ITV’s TV channels and its streaming service ITVX, with a new commercial airing on January 1. The deal lets Wicks bring his workout and healthy‑food plans into millions of homes, while ITV gets fresh content to keep viewers engaged. It comes as ITV faces pressure from larger media groups.
