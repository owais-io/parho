A group of seven bishops, three rabbis and an imam has asked the UK government to rethink plans to scrap the right to permanent refugee status. They warn that cutting the 5‑year protection period and forcing families to leave could hurt integration and social cohesion. The leaders say the changes need careful review and better support for refugees who may feel unsafe returning home.
