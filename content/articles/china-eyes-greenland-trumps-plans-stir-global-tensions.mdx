China wants to boost its presence in Greenland, but it faces pushback from the US, Denmark and NATO. Trump’s plan to take control of the island has made the issue even more tense. Beijing sees the move as a chance to weaken the US‑led alliance and expand trade routes like the Polar Silk Road. Still, Chinese investment in Greenland has stalled because of political opposition and security concerns.
