A BBC review found 87 flights that carried Jeffrey Epstein and his passengers into or out of UK airports from the early 1990s to 2018. Some of those passengers were British women who say they were abused by Epstein. Only a few of the flights happened after he was convicted in 2008. UK police say they haven’t found new evidence to reopen an investigation, but they’ll look if new information comes up.
