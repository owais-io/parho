President Trump said he'll lower US tariffs on Indian goods if India stops buying Russian oil. He claims the two leaders reached a trade deal, promising India will cut tariffs and buy more U.S. energy and tech. The move is meant to pressure Russia and support Ukraine. India is already cutting Russian oil imports, and the U.S. hopes the tariff cuts will boost U.S. exports.
