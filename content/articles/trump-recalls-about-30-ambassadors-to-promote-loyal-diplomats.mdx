Trump’s team quietly pulled almost 30 ambassadors and senior diplomats from overseas posts. They plan to replace them with people the president thinks will support his policies. Career diplomats say this is unusual and could make the foreign service more political. The move affects many countries, especially in Africa and the Middle East. Critics worry it hurts U.S. influence and treats career staff unfairly.
