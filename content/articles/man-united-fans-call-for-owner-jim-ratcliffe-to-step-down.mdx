The 1958 fans group wants to oust Manchester United owner Jim Ratcliffe, calling him an “incompetent clown.” They plan a protest at the Feb. 1 match against Fulham and call for a vote of no confidence in the club’s ownership, including the Glazers. The group also criticises the club’s executives after coach Ruben Amorim was sacked, saying the club is failing on and off the pitch. With about 100,000 members, they say the club’s heritage must be protected.
