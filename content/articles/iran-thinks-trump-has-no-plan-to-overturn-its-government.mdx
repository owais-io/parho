Iran still wants to push hard in talks with the U.S., even though it has been hit by missile attacks, sanctions and a hit to its currency. Tehran’s diplomats act confident that President Trump won’t attack the country, so they keep demanding tough conditions. Inside Iran, protests have turned violent, but some activists say the U.S. should not strike. Others worry that a foreign attack would hurt a peaceful transition. The situation is tense.
