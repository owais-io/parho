The House Oversight Committee, led by Republican chair James Comer, wants to file a contempt of Congress case against former First Lady Hillary Clinton because she and her husband Bill declined to testify about their ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The committee will vote next week, and if approved, the matter could go to the Justice Department for possible indictment. Clinton’s team calls the subpoenas illegal, but GOP members see it as a way to hold the Clintons accountable.
