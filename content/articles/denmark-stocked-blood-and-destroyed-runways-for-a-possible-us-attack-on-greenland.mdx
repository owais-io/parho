In January, Denmark prepared for a potential US invasion of Greenland. Danish troops flew to the island carrying explosives to blow up runways in Nuuk and Kangerlussuaq and brought blood bags to treat wounded soldiers. The move came after Donald Trump threatened to seize Greenland, prompting Denmark to seek help from European allies and deploy a mix of NATO troops. Official Danish and Greenlandic leaders did not comment.
