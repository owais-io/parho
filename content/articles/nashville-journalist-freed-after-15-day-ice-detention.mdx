Estefany Rodríguez, a 35‑year‑old journalist from Nashville, was held by ICE for 15 days after being arrested in Nashville, then moved to jail in Alabama and a detention center in Louisiana. She said the arrest was without a warrant and she was mistreated, isolated, and sprayed with cleaning fluid. Lawyers finally secured her release on a $10,000 bond. The case has sparked concern among press‑freedom and immigration groups.
