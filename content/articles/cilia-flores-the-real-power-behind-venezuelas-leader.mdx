Cilia Flores, the wife of Venezuela’s president Nicolás Maduro, is seen as the real power behind the scenes. She has been called the “brains” while Maduro is the “brawn.” Flores helped build the regime, ran the national assembly, and appointed judges. U.S. prosecutors now accuse her of big‑scale drug trafficking and bribery. She was seized by U.S. forces and faces charges of narco‑terrorism and drug trafficking.
