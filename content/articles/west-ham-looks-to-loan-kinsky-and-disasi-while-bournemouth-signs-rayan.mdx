West Ham is preparing to bring in a new goalkeeper and defender to help them avoid relegation. They sent Igor Julio back to Brighton to free up loan slots and are eyeing Tottenham's Antonin Kinsky and Chelsea's Axel Disasi. Meanwhile, Bournemouth closed a deal for 19‑year‑old Brazilian Rayan from Vasco da Gama. The article also mentions other moves like West Ham signing Adama Traoré and Villa signing Tammy Abraham.
