Trump talks to Putin and says the US will lift some oil sanctions to help ease the price rise caused by the Hormuz blockade and the US‑Israeli war with Iran. The move could let Russia ship more oil, which might make it harder to punish Moscow for Ukraine. The US also plans naval escorts to keep tankers safe and has a temporary waiver for India.
