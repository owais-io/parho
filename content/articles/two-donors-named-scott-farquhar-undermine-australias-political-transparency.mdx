Australia stopped publishing donor addresses in 2025, making it harder to know who is giving money to parties. In a recent donation list, a billionaire tech founder and a local businessman both named Scott Farquhar appeared, but without addresses only their names show. This confusion shows how the new rules make it tough for journalists to check who funds politics, hurting transparency.
