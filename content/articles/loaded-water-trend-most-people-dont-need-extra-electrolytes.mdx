Influencers are turning plain water into “loaded” drinks with salt, magnesium, and glitter, claiming they’re essential for health. Experts say that for everyday folks, the food we eat already supplies enough electrolytes, and plain water keeps us hydrated. Only athletes or people losing a lot of salt through sweat need special drinks. To check hydration, look at the colour of your urine.
