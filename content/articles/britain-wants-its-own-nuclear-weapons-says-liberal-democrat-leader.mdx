At the Liberal Democrats spring conference, leader Ed Davey will argue that Britain should build and run its own nuclear weapons, instead of depending on the United States. He says the current Trident system relies on US‑made missiles and maintenance, making the UK’s deterrent vulnerable to American politics. Davey warns that if the US backs down, Britain’s security could be at risk, and he calls for billions of pounds to invest in a sovereign nuclear programme.
