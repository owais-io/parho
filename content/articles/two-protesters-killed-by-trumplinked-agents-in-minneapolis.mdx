In Minneapolis, federal agents tied to Trump’s immigration policy shot two protestors, Renee Good and Alex Pretti, who posed no danger. Video shows the killings were unnecessary, yet Trump officials defended the agents and blamed the victims. The White House has not opened a criminal inquiry, instead launching a shallow use‑of‑force review. The article calls for higher accountability, criticizing Trump’s leadership for allowing lawlessness in raids.
