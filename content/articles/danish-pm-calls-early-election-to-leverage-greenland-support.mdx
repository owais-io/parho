Denmark’s prime minister, Mette Frederiksen, has announced an early vote to take advantage of a boost she received after U.S. President Trump threatened to press into Greenland. The move comes before the law forces her to set a date by the end of October. Polls now show her Social Democrats are ahead after last year’s local defeats, and she also announced a new wealth tax to fund schools.
