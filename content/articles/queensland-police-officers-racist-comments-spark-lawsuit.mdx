A Punjabi rideshare driver in Queensland, Australia, says police officers used racist language and ignored his requests for an interpreter and a lawyer. He was charged with indecent acts for a year, but the case was dropped in 2024. The driver is suing for nearly $500,000, claiming the police ignored evidence, used a body‑camera recording, and harassed him because of his race. He says the incident ruined his life.
