United coach Ruben Amorim is open to letting 20‑year‑old midfielder Kobbie Mainoo go on loan. Mainoo has rarely started for Manchester United, with his only league start in a cup loss to Grimsby. Amorim said he’d welcome a conversation about a temporary move and wants players happy, even if it means sending them away. He’s also frustrated by the club’s under‑performance but sees the loan as a way to help Mainoo progress.
