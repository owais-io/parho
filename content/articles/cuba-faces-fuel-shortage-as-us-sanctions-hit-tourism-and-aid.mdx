Cuba is running out of fuel because of US oil sanctions. Airlines from Canada, Russia, and others have stopped flying in, and even the World Food Programme is struggling to deliver food. Diplomats say the situation could get worse in weeks, especially in cities where electricity and water are scarce. Some locals are making charcoal stoves to cook, while others hope the government can find a way out.
