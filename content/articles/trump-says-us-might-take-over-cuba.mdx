Trump told a Texas crowd that the U.S. might carry out a “friendly takeover” of Cuba. He said Cuba is in trouble and is talking with the U.S., but gave no details. The comments come amid growing tensions after the U.S. captured Venezuela’s president Maduro and cut ties with Cuba. Cuban exiles in Miami support the idea, but many Cubans fear a repeat of past U.S. domination.
