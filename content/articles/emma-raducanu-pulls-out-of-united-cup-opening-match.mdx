Emma Raducanu had to sit out her first United Cup match in Perth after pulling out an hour before play. She was dealing with a foot injury that’s been bothering her since last season. Her spot was taken by Katie Swan, who lost to Naomi Osaka. Raducanu stayed on the sidelines, cheering the British team. The event also saw other players dealing with injuries and illnesses, but the British team kept fighting.
