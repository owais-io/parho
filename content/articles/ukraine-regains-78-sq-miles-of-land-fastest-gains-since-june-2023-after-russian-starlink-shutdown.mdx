Last week, Ukrainian forces pulled back 201 sq km (78 sq miles) from Russia, the biggest quick win since a June 2023 counter‑offensive. Analysts say the hit to Russian Starlink satellites caused communication problems for the enemy. Meanwhile, Ukrainian police arrested a former energy minister over a bribery case that has rattled the government. Trump urged Ukraine to negotiate quickly ahead of talks in Geneva, and Poland temporarily closed airports due to Russian air strikes.
