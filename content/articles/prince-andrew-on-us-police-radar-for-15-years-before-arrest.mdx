U.S. law‑enforcement officials had been watching Prince Andrew for almost 15 years, long before he was arrested in the U.K. Documents show he was named in a 2011 FBI probe into Jeffrey Epstein, and that prosecutors tried to interview him about alleged abuse claims. The prince denied any wrongdoing, and a Justice Department memo said he offered no help. Despite his public promises, he never cooperated.
