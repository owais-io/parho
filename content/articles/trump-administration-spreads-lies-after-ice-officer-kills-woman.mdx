ICE officer shot and killed 37‑year‑old mother of three in Minneapolis. Trump officials quickly painted her as a violent terrorist and claimed she tried to run the officer over. No evidence supports those claims, and videos show the officer was not in danger. The administration’s false statements were spread on social media and TV, while state leaders called the attacks out of line. An investigation is still ongoing.
