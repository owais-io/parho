Newcastle manager Eddie Howe is furious after a 2‑2 draw with Chelsea. He says a penalty should have been awarded when Trevoh Chalobah forced a defender into Newcastle’s goalkeeper, but the referee and VAR ruled no foul. Howe blames officials for the loss, even though Newcastle had already dropped 13 points from winning chances this season. Chelsea’s coach says a draw is fine after a late comeback. Newcastle had led 2‑0 before Chelsea pulled back.
