Washington and Kyiv have sharpened a new peace proposal that would see Ukraine pull back some troops and create a demilitarised zone in the east, while Russia would withdraw forces from several regions. Ukraine also drops its push for NATO membership in exchange for security guarantees. Moscow wants Ukraine to give up about 5,000 sq km of Donbas and to renounce NATO ambitions. The plan is sent to President Putin, but Russia’s final stance is still pending.
