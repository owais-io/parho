The famous 867‑5309 phone number from Tommy Tutone’s 1980s hit is now being used to help people with cancer. Cancer Support Community (CSC) and Gilda’s Club have set up a free helpline at 1‑CSC‑867‑5309. When callers dial the number they reach trained volunteers who offer information, emotional support and resources for patients and caregivers. The singer Tommy Heath supports the move, saying the number can bring hope and smiles to those facing cancer.
