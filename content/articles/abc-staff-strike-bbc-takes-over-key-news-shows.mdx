ABC staff walk out for 24 hours, the first strike in 20 years, over a pay offer that unions say is too low and fears of AI replacing journalists. During the walk‑out, BBC World Service will fill ABC’s big news slots, like 7.30 and AM, and other radio shows will be swapped for pre‑recorded BBC programmes. Music stations such as Triple J will play only music. The strike pits the MEAA and CPSU against ABC management.
