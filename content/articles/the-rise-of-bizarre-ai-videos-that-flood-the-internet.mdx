AI slop is the low‑quality, bizarre content that floods social media to get views. In 2024‑25, memes like “shrimp Jesus” and AI‑made cartoons of cats stole the spotlight. Creators use free tools to make silly videos, hoping to earn money, but only a few succeed. The trend shows how platform algorithms chase clicks and how a few people can profit from cheap, viral content. Some creators have made a living, but many face takedowns.
