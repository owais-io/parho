An Israeli air strike on a health centre in Burj Qalaouiyah, southern Lebanon, burned the building and killed 12 doctors, nurses and paramedics. This brings the total number of healthcare workers killed by Israel in the last 12 days to 31. The attack, part of the ongoing Israel‑Hezbollah war that began in March, has drawn criticism from the WHO and human‑rights groups, who say attacks on medical staff are war crimes.
