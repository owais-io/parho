A sudden rise in measles cases across the UK has shaken the country, with more than 3,600 people infected in 2024, mainly kids under ten. The disease returned after the pandemic lowered vaccine coverage, dropping below the 95% level needed for herd immunity. Hospitals are treating severe cases, including a fatality, and health officials warn that missing the two‑dose MMR vaccine can be deadly. Parents should check their status and catch up if needed.
