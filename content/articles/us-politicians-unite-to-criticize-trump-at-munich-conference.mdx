At the Munich Security Conference, a few Democrats and a handful of Republicans openly attacked former President Donald Trump. They criticized his unpredictable moves and his attacks on allies like Canada and Greenland. The politicians argued that sticking to clear rules is better than letting Trump’s unpredictability decide. The bipartisan criticism showed that even some Republicans are willing to speak out against Trump’s policies. The debate highlighted growing frustration with Trump’s approach to foreign policy.
