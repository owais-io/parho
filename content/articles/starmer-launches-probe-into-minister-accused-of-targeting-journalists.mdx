Prime Minister Keir Starmer has started a formal inquiry into Cabinet Office minister Josh Simons, who is accused of falsely linking journalists to Russian propaganda and of telling intelligence services that a reporter had ties to a former Jeremy Corbyn adviser. Starmer has asked independent adviser Laurie Magnus to review Simons' conduct. The probe follows Simons’ pressure on security officials about a Sunday Times story and his leak of the investigation’s details. Politicians call for his dismissal, while some defend him.
