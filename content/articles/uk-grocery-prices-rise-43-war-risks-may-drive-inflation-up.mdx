UK grocery inflation jumped to 4.3% last month, reversing a four‑month decline, and economists warn that a Middle East war could push prices higher and hurt growth. Energy costs are already climbing, raising fuel and gas bills. While chocolate prices hit a nine‑year high, online shopping grew to 13% of total sales, with Ocado and Lidl leading growth. Retailers saw mixed results: Tesco and Sainsbury’s gained share, Asda lost sales.
