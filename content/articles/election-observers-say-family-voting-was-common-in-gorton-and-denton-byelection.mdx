Election observers from Democracy Volunteers said that in the Gorton and Denton byelection a lot of people were seen helping family members vote together, something that breaks the secret‑ballot rule. They found this happening in 68 % of the polling stations and affecting about 12 % of voters. The observers also noted low signage and some ID‑checking problems. Parties called for an investigation, while the returning officer said the observers waited until polls were closed before raising concerns.
