Nicky Henderson says he’s putting all his attention on Southwell for Constitution Hill’s first flat race after the horse fell badly in recent hurdles. Despite a recent crash that halted his hurdling plans, Henderson plans a “jump‑flat‑jump” week with jumping guru Yogi Breisner. The plan is to see how the horse handles the flat before deciding on the big Champion Hurdle.
