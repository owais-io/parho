Peter Kyle, the UK business secretary, said the government will take stakes in fast‑growing businesses to speed up economic growth. Speaking at Davos, he explained the British Business Bank can buy equity, citing a recent £25 m investment in Octopus’s software spin‑off Kraken. Kyle hopes this “activist” move will create jobs, boost wealth, and attract talent, even amid global uncertainty.
