Trevor McKibbin rescued Dezi Freeman from a bushfire in 2020, only to see him turn into a fugitive after a tragic shooting in Victoria’s Porepunkah. Three months later, police still don’t know if Freeman is alive, dead, or hiding. Local residents, a reward, and a new inquest are all part of the confusion. Misinformation and conspiracy theories keep piling on, while the community tries to heal from the event.
