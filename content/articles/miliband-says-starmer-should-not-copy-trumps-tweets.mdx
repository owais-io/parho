Energy Secretary Ed Miliband told the BBC that Prime Minister Keir Starmer should not copy President Trump’s tweets. Miliband said Britain would be in a worse position if it did so, especially while Starmer deals with a growing diplomatic crisis over Greenland and possible U.S. tariffs. Starmer isn’t going to Davos, and European leaders are condemning Trump’s threats, while Britain defends its calm approach.
