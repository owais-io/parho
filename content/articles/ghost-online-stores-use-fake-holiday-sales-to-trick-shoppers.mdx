Ghost online shops lure Aussie shoppers with fake Christmas and Black‑Friday deals. The ACCC says these sites pretend to be local brands but often ship cheap overseas copies or nothing at all. Examples include a makeup site using fake news headlines and AI‑generated pictures. Meta and Shopify are being urged to stop the ads. Consumers should double‑check sites and look for real business numbers before buying.
