At a meeting with all 64,000 Lloyds staff, the bank’s boss said he was opening an investigation after the bank used employee bank‑account details to argue pay increases. Staff were upset because the data showed their salaries and savings, and the bank had shown this to unions. The regulator is looking into a possible breach, and one union said it could sue if it finds the bank broke privacy rules.
