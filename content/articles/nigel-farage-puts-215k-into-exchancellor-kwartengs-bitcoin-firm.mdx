Nigel Farage has put £215,000 into Stack BTC, a bitcoin firm run by former Chancellor Kwasi Kwarteng. The move shows Reform UK’s growing ties with the crypto world. Farage wants people to pay taxes in crypto and could create a sovereign wealth fund of digital assets if he wins power. The company now owns 21 bitcoins worth over £1 m, and its shares jumped 67% after the announcement.
