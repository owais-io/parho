The Justice Department released a large set of documents about Jeffrey Epstein’s investigations, but most are heavily redacted. The visible files show photos of Epstein with celebrities like Michael Jackson and politicians like Bill Clinton, plus evidence such as computers. This is the first of many releases the department plans. Democrats say the Trump administration hasn't fully complied with the Epstein Files Transparency Act, which demands all unclassified records be released by December 19.
