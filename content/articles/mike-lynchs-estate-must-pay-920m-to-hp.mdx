The estate of late tech mogul Mike Lynch has been told by a London court to pay £920 million to Hewlett‑Packard (HP). The money covers compensation, costs and interest over HP’s 2011 purchase of Lynch’s software firm Autonomy, which HP later valued much lower. The estate, worth about £500 million, could go bankrupt. HP said the case is almost settled; Lynch’s family will appeal.
