European Commission chief Ursula von der Leyen angrily told U.S. President Donald Trump that the U.S. cannot interfere with elections in European countries. She said elections belong to voters, not foreign powers. The comment comes as Trump pushes a U.S. peace plan that would withdraw Ukraine’s troops from the Donbas, a move Zelenskyy says needs guarantees that Russia won’t take over. Europe is also debating how to use frozen Russian assets to support Ukraine.
