A new report from the Munich Security Conference says Europe must become more assertive and less dependent on the US after the Trump administration showed a shift toward authoritarianism. Polls in the report show Europeans are ready to act without US leadership. The study criticises Trump’s “demolition” tactics and calls for bold decision‑making and stronger defence ties. It also notes growing concern that US democracy is in decline.
