Five Ukrainian men tell how the war has reshaped their lives. One, a tailor, spent three years in Russian captivity and now fights for former prisoners. Another, a builder, lost a leg but returned to the front, saying the war made him stronger. A former gamer lost fingers but keeps fighting. An Afghan‑Ukrainian lawyer lost an eye and now helps wounded soldiers. A DJ turned soldier says war has changed his view on women and confidence.
