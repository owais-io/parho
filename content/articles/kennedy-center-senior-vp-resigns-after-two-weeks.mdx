Kevin Couch was hired as senior vice‑president of artistic programming at the Kennedy Center, but he quit less than two weeks later. He gave no explanation. The resignation comes amid a wider crisis after President Trump reshaped the center’s board, causing artists to cancel shows and sparking a fight over the venue’s name. Ticket sales have dropped sharply, adding pressure on the struggling institution.
