Wrexham AFC just got an £18m grant from the Welsh government to upgrade its stadium, and the TV‑star owners have sold a minority share to Apollo Sports Capital. The deal has raised questions about public money going to a privately owned club, but the new investor is unrelated to the former Apollo founder’s ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The move shows how TV, money and local jobs are linked in modern football.
