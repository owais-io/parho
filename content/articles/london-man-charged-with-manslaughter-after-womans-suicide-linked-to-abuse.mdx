A 45‑year‑old man in southeast London has been charged with manslaughter after a woman named Gillian Morand died by suicide in 2020. Police say she had been abused for years, and the abuse may have led to her death. The case is rare because it connects domestic abuse to suicide. Prosecutors say they have enough evidence to bring the case to court.
