Former Espanyol keeper Joan García, now playing for Barcelona, had a stunning debut in the Barcelona derby. He kept a clean sheet, making a string of crucial saves that helped Barça win 2-0. The match was tense, with fans and media hurling criticism because he switched sides. Despite the pressure, García stayed calm and performed like a superstar. His €25m move from Espanyol to Barcelona was a major change for both clubs.
