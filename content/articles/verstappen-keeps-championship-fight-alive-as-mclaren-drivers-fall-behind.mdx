In Qatar, Max Verstappen won the Grand Prix and pulled back into the title race. Red Bull’s upgrade let him finish ahead of McLaren’s Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri, who were held up by a strategy mistake: the team chose not to pit during a safety‑car break like everyone else. Now all three drivers are just 16 points apart heading into the final race in Abu Dhabi, making the championship a close fight.
