Everton beat Aston Villa 1‑0 at Villa Park. Thierno Barry scored after a mix‑up between Emiliano Martínez and a defender. Villa had a shaky start, losing their captain John McGinn early. Everton’s defence stayed solid, and the win gives them a boost after recent poor results and keeps the club’s hopes alive. Manager Mike Moyes praised Barry and the team for stepping up.
