MIT is grieving after the tragic shooting death of Nuno Loureiro, the director of its plasma science and fusion center. He was shot at his home in Brookline and died at the hospital. Police are still investigating, and no suspect has been named. Loureiro, a 47‑year‑old Portuguese scientist, had a distinguished career and won a presidential science award. The university released a letter offering support and mental‑health resources to the community.
