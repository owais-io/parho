Afghans who helped the U.S. now fear deportation after Trump’s new rules. 25‑year‑old pilot Ali fled Kabul in 2021, now living in Boise, Idaho, working as an Uber driver while studying to become a commercial pilot. Trump’s administration has paused Afghan visas and green‑card approvals, sparking fear among U.S. Afghans that they could be detained or deported. NGOs and former U.S. soldiers urge the rules be reversed, saying the U.S. broke its promise to Afghan allies.
