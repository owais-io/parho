A content creator on Instagram urges Gen Z to ditch phones and live like the 1990s, citing the freedom of no‑cell‑phones and chance encounters. A 2023 survey says 60 % of Gen Z adults wish they could return to a pre‑plugged era, and psychologists say this “offline nostalgia” can make life richer. The columnist admits she never lived in the 90s and thinks the decade is now a romantic, imagined construct for young people.
