Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey spoke at a press conference after the shooting of Alex Pretti by federal agents. He demanded that the federal presence in the city stop, calling it an invasion that hurts trust and safety. Frey highlighted peaceful protests by residents and said the operation was damaging American values. He urged President Trump to end the federal operation and restore calm in Minneapolis.
