Australia has just made it illegal for people under 16 to use social media. Platforms are already asking for age checks. The Guardian is asking readers to tell how the ban is affecting them. They want stories about account deactivations, mistaken removals, or other problems. People can submit their experiences through an online form, and parents must fill it for kids.
