Nigel Farage has announced Reform UK's first four frontbenchers. Robert Jenrick will handle finance, praising previous budgets and supporting strategic industries like steel and cars. Suella Braverman will focus on education, saying schools need stronger discipline and will cut the equality department. Richard Tice will run business, trade and energy, pushing for cheap oil and gas and dropping net‑zero targets. Zia Yusuf will oversee home affairs and immigration, pledging swift deportations and a tougher stance on Islam.
