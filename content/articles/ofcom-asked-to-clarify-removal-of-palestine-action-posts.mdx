Human rights groups and academics ask Ofcom to explain what a recent ruling that declared the ban on the Palestinian protest group Palestine Action unlawful means for social‑media sites. The Metropolitan Police say they will no longer arrest people who support the group at protests. Signatories, including Amnesty International, want to know if platforms must still delete Palestine‑related posts, how new terrorist‑content rules apply, and if posts can be restored if the ban is lifted. Ofcom has yet to respond.
