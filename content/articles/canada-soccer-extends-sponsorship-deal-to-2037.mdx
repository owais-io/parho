Canada Soccer has signed a new 11‑year contract with its commercial partner, Canadian Soccer Media & Entertainment, that runs through 2037, with an option to extend another five years. The deal comes after years of conflict, player strikes and a $40 million lawsuit over low revenue. It now gives the federation more money, a better revenue‑sharing plan and promises live TV for all international games, with equal exposure for men’s and women’s teams.
