Eugenie and Beatrice joined King Charles and Queen Camilla for the 11 a.m. Christmas Day church service at Sandringham. Their dad, Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor, who lost his royal titles last year, was not there; he’s staying at the Royal Lodge near Windsor. The sisters, with their husbands, walked behind the king and queen, and the whole family, from Sandringham House to St Mary Magdalene church. The event followed a tough year for the royals.
