A customer had a gas leak from a new kitchen hob installed by Wickes, which put her life at risk. An emergency engineer found the leak, but the replacement hob was never installed. Other customers experienced long delays, poor communication, and substandard work, with the company offering minimal compensation. The story urges customers to seek help from the Furniture and Home Improvements Ombudsman or take legal action.
