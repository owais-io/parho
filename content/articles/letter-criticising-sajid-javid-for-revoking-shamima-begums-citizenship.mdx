A Guardian reader writes a scathing letter to the editor, calling out former Home Secretary Sajid Javid for stripping British citizenship from Shamima Begum. The letter also touches on Javid’s own troubled youth, citing his time in jail for stealing from slot machines and beatings by his father, and criticises the government's decision as unjust and driven by “spurious national security” concerns. The piece is part of a collection of personal letters covering a range of unrelated topics.
