Congress passed a bill in January that kept money for museums and cultural agencies Trump wanted to cut. The Smithsonian, National Endowment for the Humanities, National Endowment for the Arts and other groups get funding. Supporters of the Institute of American Indian Arts (IAIA) campaigned hard, sending letters and speaking to lawmakers. The IAIA will receive $13.5 million for 2026, helping keep its programs running.
