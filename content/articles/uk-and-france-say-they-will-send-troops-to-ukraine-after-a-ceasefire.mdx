After a Paris summit, Britain and France said they are ready to put troops on Ukrainian soil if a ceasefire happens. The deal, signed with Ukraine’s president and backed by the US, aims to create military hubs far behind the front line to reassure defenders. Russia has said it would block such a move. The plan also includes monitoring the ceasefire and supporting Ukraine’s army, but the size of the force is unclear.
