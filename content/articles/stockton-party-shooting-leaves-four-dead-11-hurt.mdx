A fatal shooting at a toddler’s birthday party in Stockton, California, killed four people – three children and a family member – and wounded 11 others. Police say it was a targeted attack, but the suspect is still unknown. Residents are being urged to come forward with any information. The incident has shocked a city already battling high gun‑violence rates, and local officials are calling for more community vigilance and support for families affected.
