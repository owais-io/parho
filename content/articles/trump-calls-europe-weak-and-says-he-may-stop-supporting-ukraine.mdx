Donald Trump said in a recent interview that Europe is weak and losing its identity because of immigration. He also suggested Ukraine should hand over land to Russia and said he might pull US troops out. Trump called European leaders “stupid” and criticised their politics. European heads of state and the EU rejected his comments, calling them offensive and a threat to the US‑EU alliance. They urge Washington to respect Europe’s independence.
