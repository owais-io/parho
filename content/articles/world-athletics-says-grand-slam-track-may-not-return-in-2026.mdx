The Grand Slam Track (GST) league, led by former sprinter Michael Johnson, just filed for bankruptcy and still owes athletes and creditors huge sums. World Athletics, led by Sebastian Coe, warned that GST might not be allowed to come back in 2026 even if it pays its debts. Coe stressed the sport needs a solid, sustainable financial model and said it will guard the event calendar carefully.
