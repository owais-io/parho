Texas Congressman Tony Gonzales has ended his run for another term after saying he had a relationship with a former staff member who later died by suicide. He will finish his current term. The House Ethics Committee is investigating his conduct. Party leaders urged him to step down. The scandal has also drawn calls for disciplinary action and potential expulsion from Congress.
