England’s national rugby team suffered a heavy defeat at Murrayfield, with coach Steve Borthwick’s side exposed for poor discipline, missed tackles, and bad decision‑making. Scotland’s fast start and strong ball handling left England scrambling, while key players like Ollie Lawrence and Ben White were left out. Borthwick faces questions about selection and strategy ahead of Ireland’s visit, as the team looks to rebuild confidence after this embarrassing loss.
