The $14 billion Metro Tunnel opened in Melbourne, moving several suburban lines into a new tunnel to promise faster, more frequent trains. Early reports show power faults, platform confusion and overcrowding, especially at Caulfield where passengers are stuck transferring. Some new stations feel unfinished, and a few have not yet opened. Despite the glitches, travel times have cut in half and commuters generally find the service reliable.
