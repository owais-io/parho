Iran has named a new supreme leader, Mojtaba Khamenei, even though he was badly hurt in an Israeli attack. The government says he can still give orders, and the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps says they can run the war without him. There are no photos or videos of him, and some people doubt he is alive or healthy. The army’s structure is strong enough that the war can continue on autopilot.
