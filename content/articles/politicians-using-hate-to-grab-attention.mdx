Politicians are using hurtful language to get media coverage and win support. The article explains how “vice‑signalling” – saying things that break social taboos – has become a trick for leaders like Trump and Farage. It shows how racist, sexist or anti‑immigrant remarks can create a following, erode trust in mainstream politics, and make hate feel normal. The piece warns that this strategy fuels division and undermines democracy.
