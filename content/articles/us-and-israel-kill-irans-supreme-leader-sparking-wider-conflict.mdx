US and Israel targeted and killed Iran’s supreme leader, Ayatollah Khamenei, in a missile and drone strike. The attack killed dozens of Iranian officials and caused a wave of Iranian missiles and drones to hit Israel and Gulf states. Casualties grew, schools and civilian areas were hit, and the world has warned it could lead to a wider war. The incident has shaken global stability and drawn strong diplomatic reactions.
