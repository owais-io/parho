Victoria's Labor party is quietly debating whether to replace Premier Jacinta Allan. Polls show her approval at a record low, and some members of the party’s left wing want a new leader. Allan has denied the rumours, saying she has the support of a united caucus. A leadership spill would be a big event, but most party members say it’s not likely to happen soon.
