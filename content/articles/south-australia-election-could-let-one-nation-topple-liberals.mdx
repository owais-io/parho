South Australia’s upcoming state election is turning into a showdown for the Liberal party, which is losing ground to Labor and the rising One Nation. Polls show Labor ahead, One Nation beating Liberals in many areas. The Liberals have put One Nation above Labor on voting cards, hoping to snatch seats, but the move could backfire. The result will tell if One Nation can become a major force in the state.
