Project Play says 22 children died in the last two years crossing the Channel because of a “catastrophic failure” by the UK and France. The report describes teargas attacks, evictions, and police slashing of dinghies in northern France. It also notes the UK has paid £473 million for border security but doesn’t explain how the money is spent. The group calls for a public inquiry and safer routes for asylum seekers.
