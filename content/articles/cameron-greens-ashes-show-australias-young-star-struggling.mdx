Cameron Green, a promising all‑rounder for Australia, has mixed results in the recent Ashes. His aggressive, Bazball‑style batting has led to flashy scores but also rash dismissals, and he has taken only a few wickets. Though he’s praised as a future star and can bowl fast, his average and consistency lag behind expectations. With injuries and position changes, the team is hoping he can regain his form.
