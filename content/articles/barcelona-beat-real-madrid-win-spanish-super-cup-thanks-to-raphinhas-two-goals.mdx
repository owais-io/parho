Barcelona won the Spanish Super Cup by defeating Real Madrid 3-2. Brazilian winger Raphinha scored twice and helped with a pass that led to Lewandowski's goal. Real Madrid missed chances and could not find a winner. The match was chaotic, but Barcelona dominated, with Pedri, Vinícius and others contributing. The victory keeps coach Xabi Alonso in his job and gives Barcelona a bright start to the season.
