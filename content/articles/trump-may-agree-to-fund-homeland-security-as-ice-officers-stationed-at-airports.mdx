President Trump has said he might agree to fund the Department of Homeland Security if lawmakers also pass his Save America Act, which tightens voting rules. Meanwhile, ICE officers are stationed at several U.S. airports as a test run for possible use at polling places later this year, a suggestion made by Steve Bannon. The Senate confirmed Markwayne Mullin as DHS secretary, and the Supreme Court is reviewing cases that could limit mail‑in voting and asylum processing.
