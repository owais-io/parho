England missed five catches in the second day‑night Ashes Test at the Gabba, letting Australia take a 44‑run lead. Australian bowlers, especially Brydon Carse, got key wickets, while England’s batters like Joe Root were hit by loose fielding. Australia’s total went from 291/3 to 378/6. The dropped chances left England frustrated and behind, despite earlier successes. England had hoped to build on their earlier century, but the missed chances cost them the game.
