Arsenal beat Chelsea 2‑0 in the WSL, with Beth Mead scoring and Mariona Caldentey adding another. The win is Arsenal’s first at Stamford Bridge and their first away win over Chelsea in eight years, moving them a point behind Manchester City. Chelsea coach Sonia Bompastor said the title race is probably finished, but her side will keep fighting. The result keeps Arsenal in Champions League contention.
