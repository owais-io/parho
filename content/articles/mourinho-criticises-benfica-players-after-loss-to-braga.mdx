Mourinho was furious after Benfica lost 3‑1 to Braga in the League Cup semi‑final. He said the squad would stay at the Seixal training base, not sleep, and keep training until the next match. He accused the players of not listening to his talks and warned they need to focus if they face Porto next week. The loss pushes Benfica further behind leaders Porto in the league.
