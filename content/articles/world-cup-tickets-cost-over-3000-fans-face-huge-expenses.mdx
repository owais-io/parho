The cheapest ticket for the World Cup final will cost about £3,120, and a full ticket package can run more than £10,000 when you add flights and hotels. Fan groups and football support organisations say this is unfair and are urging FIFA to stop selling tickets at these high prices. They point out that even the cheapest seats are over £100 and many families can’t afford the trip.
