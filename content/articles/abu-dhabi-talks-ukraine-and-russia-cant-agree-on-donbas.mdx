Ukrainian and Russian officials are meeting in Abu Dhabi, the second round of talks set up by former U.S. President Trump. The talks are led by U.S. diplomats and include top Ukrainian and Russian negotiators. Key issue: land. Ukraine wants to keep the Donbas region, Russia wants it all. Both sides say they’re ready to talk but hold firm. If talks fail, the war could go on.
