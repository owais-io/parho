The writer looks back at his first Oscars in 2016 and feels the ceremony has lost its sparkle. He notes the #OscarsSoWhite push for diversity, the Academy’s slow membership changes, and the film industry’s bigger problems like studio mergers, empty theatres, and AI. While wars and political turmoil keep the world distracted, the Oscars still show movies matter and help people see themselves.
