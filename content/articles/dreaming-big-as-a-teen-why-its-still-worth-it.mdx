Adrian Chiles talks about how it feels tough for teens to dream about careers in media, where jobs are scarce. He shares a story of a 16‑year‑old who wanted to be a drama teacher, not a star, and he admires that focus. He also remembers actor Michael Sheen, who turned his football talent into acting after a scout passed. Chiles points out that even though the odds are slim, the people who chase big dreams make our culture richer.
