Como drew 0‑0 at Atalanta, showing their strong possession but still struggling to win. Coach Cesc Fàbregas says the team has a clear style that relies on ball‑control and technical skill. The club has spent over €200m on players, but they need to finish chances better. Young talents like Nico Paz and Martin Baturina get chances, yet missed penalties and a red card keep the team from top results.
