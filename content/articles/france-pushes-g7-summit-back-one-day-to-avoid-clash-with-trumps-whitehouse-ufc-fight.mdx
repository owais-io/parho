France moved the G7 leaders’ meeting from 14‑16 June to 15‑17 June to sidestep a clash with a big UFC event Trump plans for the White House South Lawn on the 14th. The fight will host about 5,000 guests and is part of Trump’s 250‑th‑anniversary celebrations. France said the change came after talks with other G7 members, not as a direct response to the fight.
