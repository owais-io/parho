Antony Catalano, co‑owner of the Australian Community Media group, has been put on leave after a court charged him over an alleged incident involving a woman at an apartment. The company’s CEO told staff the move was temporary until the case is resolved, while the media group remains committed to a safe workplace. Catalano faces the Melbourne Magistrates’ Court, was released on bail, and has yet to comment.
