Breezy Johnson, a top American downhill skier, is back in the World Cup after a 14‑month doping ban and a string of injuries. She finished 13th at Beaver Creek, proving she can still race at home. Johnson says she’s learning to manage her nerves and keep chasing her goal of winning a gold medal at the upcoming Milan‑Cortina Olympics.
