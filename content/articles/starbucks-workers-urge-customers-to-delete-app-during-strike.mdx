Starbucks baristas are on strike, demanding better wages, predictable hours and an end to union‑busting. The union, Starbucks Workers United, has been striking for nearly three months and now asks customers to delete the Starbucks app to show support. Around 11,000 workers across 576 U.S. stores are involved, with 1,000 on strike at about 50 locations. Starbucks says only a small fraction of stores are affected, while the union says the fight matters beyond coffee.
