Russia’s central bank says it will sue Euroclear, which holds about €210bn of Russia’s frozen money, for 18 trillion roubles (around $230bn). The EU wants to use those assets to give Ukraine a loan, but Moscow calls it theft and warns it could target European investors in Russia. Euroclear has declined to comment. Russia may try to enforce its claim in friendly countries, while EU officials work to block such legal moves and explore other ways to fund Ukraine.
