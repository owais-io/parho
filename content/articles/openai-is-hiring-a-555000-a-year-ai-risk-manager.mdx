OpenAI is looking for a new head of preparedness to help stop AI from harming people and the world. The role, paid $555,000 a year, will track new AI abilities and plan how to keep them safe, from cyber‑attacks to mental‑health harm. The job will be tough, with no set start date, and the winner will get company stock too. The move comes as other tech leaders warn that powerful AIs could become dangerous.
