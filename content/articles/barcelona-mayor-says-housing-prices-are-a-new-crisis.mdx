Barcelona’s mayor says the rising cost of housing is a crisis that needs a EU response. He and 16 other city leaders are pushing the EU to create an affordable‑housing fund, similar to COVID aid, to channel €300 bn a year into new homes. House prices have jumped 48% and rents 22% since 2010, and 1 in 10 people spend a large share of their income on rent. The mayors warn the crisis could fuel populists and threaten social stability.
