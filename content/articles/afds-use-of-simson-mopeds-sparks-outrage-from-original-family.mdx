The Jewish‑descendant family that owned the Simson moped company in Germany says the far‑right party AfD’s use of the bike is a mock‑ery of their history. AfD leaders, especially Björn Höcke, ride the mopeds at rallies to show “freedom” and “individuality”. The family, who fled Nazi Germany, has asked the party to stop using the name, calling it an insult. AfD is labeled extremist by German security services.
