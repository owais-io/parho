Victoria’s bushfires have left more than 500 buildings, including 90 homes, destroyed and burned 404,000 hectares. While 12 major fires still burn, emergency warnings have been lifted for the first time in a week, though officials warn conditions could get worse next week. The state and federal governments have put $10 million into clean‑up, and are working to reopen roads and help farmers who lost livestock, and plan to keep communities safe.
