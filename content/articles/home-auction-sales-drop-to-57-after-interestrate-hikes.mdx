Australia’s home auction clearance rate fell to 57%, the lowest this year. The dip follows two interest‑rate hikes that made borrowing costlier, pushing more sellers to list homes and reducing buyers, especially first‑time buyers. Sydney and other cities saw notably low success at auction, while resellers still made record profits from earlier price rises.
