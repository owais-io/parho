Some say the moves hurt ordinary people, spoil U.S. relationships, and signal a dangerous, aggressive foreign policy. Others praise the show of strength and want U.S. influence over valuable lands. Many feel the costs at home are ignored, democracy is tested, and the country’s reputation is damaged.
