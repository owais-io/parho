Adelaide’s heatwave has trapped renters like Melissa Fisher, who can only cool herself in a bedroom with a cheap air‑con unit. Extreme temperatures across Australia, combined with poor insulation in public housing, make living conditions unbearable. Renters are urging governments to enforce minimum energy standards and improve insulation. While state and federal bodies claim investment, reforms are stalled and advocacy groups push for better, healthier homes.
