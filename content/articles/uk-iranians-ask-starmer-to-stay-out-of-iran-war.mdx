A group of Iranian ex‑political prisoners and over 100 Iranians living in the UK wrote a letter to Prime Minister Keir Starmer. They warn that attacking Iran will only strengthen Tehran’s regime and entrench authoritarian rule. They urge the UK to adopt a pro‑democracy approach, give prisoners internet access, and avoid supporting Israel or US attacks. The letter contrasts with those backing ex‑king Reza Pahlavi, who want regime change through force.
