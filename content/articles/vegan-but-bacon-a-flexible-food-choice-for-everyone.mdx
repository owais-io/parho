A plant‑based creator coined the phrase “Vegan but Bacon” to show that eating mostly plant food but allowing occasional animal items can help people make progress without feeling pressured. The idea encourages small, realistic steps instead of strict all‑or‑nothing rules, making diet changes easier and less stressful. It’s become popular on social media as a gentle, harm‑reduction approach to eating.
