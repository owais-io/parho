BBC is re‑examining its charter and funding. The licence fee, which still funds the broadcaster, may no longer reach everyone who watches BBC shows. Only 80% of UK households pay, while 94% use BBC services. The BBC says a subscription model would hurt low‑income viewers and cut local programming, and that ads could harm its independence. It wants a simpler fee system and stronger protection from government control.
