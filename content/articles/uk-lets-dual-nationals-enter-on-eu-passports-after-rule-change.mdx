Britain’s Home Office has reversed a rule that forced dual nationals to show a British passport when returning after gaining UK citizenship via the EU settlement scheme. The new policy lets them use their EU passport or ID card instead. Thousands were caught out by the original rule, and critics say the update was hidden and came too late. The change still leaves some groups unchanged.
