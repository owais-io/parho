Police in Melbourne are investigating a claim that a white ute shouted Nazi slogans at school‑age Jewish boys on Glen Eira Road. The Prime Minister called it a hate attack that has no place in Australia. Victims escaped unharmed. Police say two people are wanted and are looking for video evidence. Leaders from the Jewish community and state officials are demanding the perpetrators be arrested and the anti‑hate laws strengthened.
