In a tense Champions League match, Newcastle was leading but lost 1‑1 after Alejandro Grimaldo hit a late free‑kick equaliser for Bayer Leverkusen. The goal came after Newcastle struggled to defend corners and set pieces, and their goalkeeper’s error helped Leverkusen score. Newcastle manager Eddie Howe admitted the team’s defensive lapses and said he must fix set‑piece problems before the next game.
