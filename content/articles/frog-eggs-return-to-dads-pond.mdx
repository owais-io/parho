After 10 years, frog eggs appeared again in the pond my father and I dug 28 years ago. Each spring the pond had turned from a frog home to a newt stronghold as newts ate tadpoles and other little pond critters. Now the newts are thin, so frogs are starting to breed again. The pond shows how one species can make others disappear, then reappear.
