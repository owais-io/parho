Sir Tim Smit, who started the Eden Project in Cornwall 25 years ago, says people now care more about nature and the climate crisis is getting urgent. He thinks political extremes will fade when people see a hopeful future. Smit talks about energy independence, the problems of water privatization, and how fungi could help clean up pollution. The Eden Project has attracted millions, helped Cornwall’s economy, and plans to expand in the UK and China.
