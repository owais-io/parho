Trump’s praise for leaders like Hungary’s Viktor Orbán and El Salvador’s Nayib Bukele shows how the U.S. can slide into authoritarianism. Opposition leaders from Hungary, Turkey and El Salvador warn that power‑grabbers move fast and many private actors back them. They say democracy survives when people fight for everyday issues—jobs, schools, health—rather than just slogans. The message: stay alert, listen to the public, and act together.
