US Senator Chris Murphy warned that if President Trump tries to annex Greenland, NATO would collapse. Trump is pushing for a military plan to take over the Danish territory, but many military leaders say it’s illegal. Danish Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen and EU defence officials say the move would spark a war with Europe and force NATO allies to step in. Murphy also criticised Trump for focusing on Greenland while ignoring U.S. families’ problems.
