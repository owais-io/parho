Emerald Fennell’s new movie of *Wuthering Heights* just opened in the UK, starring Margot Robbie and Timothée Chalamet. American critics liked the film, calling it colorful and tragic, while British papers were much harsher, calling it flat and bland. The week also saw the kidnapping of NBC anchor Savannah Guthrie’s mother, the UK princess Princess Eugenie visiting an art fair in Qatar, English Heritage’s new “bonding benches” at landmarks, and a London bus tour of historic bridges.
