Cyclone Narelle is expected to hit far north Queensland on Friday morning. The storm strengthened to category‑five over the Coral Sea and will arrive as a category‑four, with wind speeds above 225 km/h. Residents could face strong winds, heavy rain, storm surges and flash flooding. Queensland Premier David Crisafulli has urged people to take the warning seriously, warning of widespread damage. The Bureau of Meteorology will keep the map updated.
