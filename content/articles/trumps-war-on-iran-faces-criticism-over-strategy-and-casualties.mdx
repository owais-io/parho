U.S. President Donald Trump has joined Israel in attacking Iran, sparking criticism at home. Critics say the campaign lacks clear goals and could last months. Over 200 civilians have died in Iran so far. Trump said the operation would last four to five weeks but could go on longer. Australia’s defence minister confirmed Australian troops in the UAE are safe after an Iranian drone strike, and only a few evacuation flights have left the region.
