Max Verstappen won in Qatar, cutting his points gap to race leader Lando Norris to just 12. With Red Bull’s new car upgrades, the Dutch driver hopes to finish Abu Dhabi strong for a fifth world title. McLaren made costly strategy mistakes that gave Verstappen the lead, and Norris still needs a podium to win the championship. Verstappen says he’ll push hard and use every advantage, aiming to keep the pressure off.
