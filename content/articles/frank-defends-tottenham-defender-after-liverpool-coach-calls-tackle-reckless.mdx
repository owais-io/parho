Liverpool manager Arne Slot slammed Tottenham’s Micky van de Ven for a “reckless” tackle that broke Alexander Isak’s leg. Tottenham boss Thomas Frank disagreed, saying the move was a normal defender’s reaction and that van de Ven had apologised to the Swedish striker. Slot was also angry that the referee didn’t issue a card. Isak will miss months after surgery, and Liverpool are still waiting for him to recover.
