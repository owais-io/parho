The UK government is considering major changes to how people can live and become citizens. Many community leaders and faith groups say the proposals could hurt integration and make life harder for over a million people. They argue that the rules should help people feel stable and welcome, not make status uncertain. The letter asks ministers to pause, listen, and revise the plans to keep fairness and inclusion at the centre.
