Habib Diarra, a 22‑year‑old Senegal international, chose Sunderland over big clubs because the club’s coach and family‑like atmosphere suited him. He grew up in France, captained Strasbourg, and won an Africa Cup medal. After a groin injury he is ready to help Sunderland hit 40 points and aim for Europe. Diarra praises the team’s unity and says he feels at home here.
