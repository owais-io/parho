Keyvan Moghissi was a 98‑year‑old heart surgeon from Tehran who spent most of his career in Britain. He studied medicine in Switzerland, then moved to the UK, where he built a leading heart‑surgery centre in Hull and helped start the European Thoracic Surgery Club. He pioneered heart bypass and other techniques, loved music, and was known for his kindness. He retired in 1994 and passed away in 2024, leaving a legacy of saved lives. Survived by his son and grandchildren.
