US ambassador Bill White accused Belgium of antisemitism over its probe of three Jewish men who performed circumcisions without medical training. He called the investigation “ridiculous” and threatened to bar a Belgian socialist politician from visiting the U.S. Belgium’s foreign minister slammed the comments as uncalled‑for and reminded White of diplomatic rules. The incident has escalated into a diplomatic row between the two countries.
