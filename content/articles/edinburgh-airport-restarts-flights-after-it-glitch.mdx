Flights at Edinburgh Airport are back on track after a computer glitch in the air traffic control system caused a halt earlier this week. The delay left some planes waiting on the tarmac for up to two hours and forced a few flights to land in Dublin instead. EasyJet and Ryanair were among the airlines hit. Passengers were told to check with their airlines for updates as the airport worked to fix the problem.
