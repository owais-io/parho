Avocado use in the U.S. has exploded, especially for Super Bowl guac. One medium fruit is packed with healthy fat, fiber, and vitamins that can lower bad cholesterol, boost gut health, and may help people feel full. Some studies even link regular avocado eating to better heart health and smarter thinking. But growing the crop in Mexico has caused forest loss, though new certification may curb deforestation.
