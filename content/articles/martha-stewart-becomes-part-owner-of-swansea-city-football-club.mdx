Martha Stewart, the famous lifestyle star, has joined rapper Snoop Dogg and footballer Luka Modric as a minority owner of Welsh club Swansea City. The club plays in England’s second division. The owners said the investment size is not disclosed, and it isn’t expected to change transfer plans this winter. The move highlights a growing trend of US celebrities buying stakes in lower‑tier UK clubs. Other US owners include Tom Brady at Birmingham City and Ryan Reynolds at Wrexham.
