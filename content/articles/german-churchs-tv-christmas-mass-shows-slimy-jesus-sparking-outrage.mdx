A German Catholic diocese aired a Christmas Eve mass that featured an adult woman wrapped in sticky rice paper to represent newborn Jesus. Critics called the scene “slimy Jesus” and said it was disrespectful. Right‑wing media and many viewers blasted it. The diocese apologized, saying it hurt people’s faith and that future TV services will be carefully planned. They also admitted that some traditional parts were shortened for the broadcast.
