The author, a novelist, has just bought a gaming company and talks about his game, Zombies, Run!, which turns a workout into a zombie‑chase story. He says AI can help with simple text, but real stories need human creativity to make us feel connected. He urges games to focus on quality storytelling, not just long word counts, and to keep human collaboration at the heart of game design.
