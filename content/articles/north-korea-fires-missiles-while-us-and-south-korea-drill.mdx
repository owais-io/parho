North Korea fired more than ten ballistic missiles into the sea near its east coast on Saturday. The launches coincided with large U.S. and South Korean joint drills that the allies say are defensive. North Korea calls the exercises rehearsals for war. Meanwhile, former U.S. President Donald Trump has spoken about a possible meeting with Kim Jong‑un, a suggestion that has drawn little response from Pyongyang. The region remains tense amid sanctions and stalled talks.
