Former UK prime minister Gordon Brown has told the cabinet secretary to launch a full inquiry into Peter Mandelson, a former business secretary, after it emerged that Mandelson sent a confidential email about the government’s financial strategy to Jeffrey Epstein in 2009. The email, detailing potential asset sales and policy ideas, was forwarded to Epstein, who was jailed for sexual offences. Brown says the leak happened during the global financial crisis and wants the findings published quickly.
