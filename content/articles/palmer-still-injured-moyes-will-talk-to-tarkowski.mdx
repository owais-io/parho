Chelsea striker Cole Palmer still has a groin injury and can’t play full matches, raising doubts about his World Cup spot. His coach says the problem may be managed without surgery, but training has been mixed. Everton boss David Moyes will talk to defender James Tarkowski after a VAR incident in their last game. Arsenal’s Gabriel Jesus is staying, and Tottenham’s Dominic Solanke hopes to return soon.
