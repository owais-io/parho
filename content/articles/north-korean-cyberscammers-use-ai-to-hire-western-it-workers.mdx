North Korean hackers use AI tools to forge fake identities and alter voices, getting hired as IT staff in Western companies. Microsoft calls the groups Jasper Sleet and Coral Sleet, who generate realistic names, emails, photos, and even deep‑fake videos for interviews. After employment, they send wages back to North Korea and may threaten to leak data. Companies are urged to use video or in‑person interviews to spot AI deception.
