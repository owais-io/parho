Saint Paul’s new mayor, a Hmong refugee who grew up in Laos, is confronting frequent ICE raids in her city. After a fatal shooting of a Minneapolis resident by an ICE officer, she says federal enforcement is targeting her community. She’s led programs to help residents record ICE activity and recover seized cars, and the city has joined a lawsuit against the surge. The mayor says the raids show a new, racially‑charged immigration push from the federal government.
