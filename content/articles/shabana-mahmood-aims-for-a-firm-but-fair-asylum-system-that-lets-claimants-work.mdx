Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood has said she wants a new asylum system that balances security with fairness. She plans to let up to 21,000 people who have waited a year for their claims start working, and will clear the country’s overcrowded asylum hotels. The government will also cut support for claimants who break the law or can afford to live on their own. Some Labour MPs worry the changes could hurt integration and cost the UK.
