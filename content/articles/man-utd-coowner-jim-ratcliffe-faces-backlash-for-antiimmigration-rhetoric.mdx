Man Utd co‑owner Jim Ratcliffe sparked controversy after he made anti‑immigration comments. He blamed immigrants for “handouts” and used far‑right language, leading to criticism from politicians, the mayor, fan groups and anti‑racism groups. Ratcliffe’s own immigrant background and tax‑avoidance record were highlighted, raising accusations of hypocrisy. He later issued a “non‑apology” saying he only meant to discuss managed immigration, but the backlash continues.
