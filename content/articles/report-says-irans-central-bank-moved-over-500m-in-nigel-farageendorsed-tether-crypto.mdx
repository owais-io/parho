Elliptic, a crypto‑tracking firm, says Iran’s central bank has moved more than $507 million in Tether stablecoins. The move, linked to the UK politician Nigel Farage who champions Tether, shows Iran using crypto to dodge global banking restrictions and support its currency. Farage has pushed for the UK to welcome digital money, while Tether says it follows sanctions rules. The findings raise questions about the crypto’s role in Iran’s economy.
