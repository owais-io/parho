A two‑year‑old red fox snuck aboard a cargo ship heading from Southampton, England, to New York. After a 3,400‑mile journey, port officers spotted the animal and coordinated with wildlife agencies. The Bronx Zoo took custody of the healthy, 5‑kg fox the next day. It is being examined and will receive a permanent home after further health checks. The zoo is feeding it a diet of produce, protein, and biscuits.
