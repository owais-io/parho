Benedetto “Nitto” Santapaola, a feared Sicilian mafia boss linked to many murders and the 1992 bombing that killed anti‑mafia prosecutor Giovanni Falcone, died in a Milan prison where he was serving life sentences. The 87‑year‑old was known for ordering the killing of journalist Giuseppe Fava and for running the Catania clan from inside jail. His death may end a long reign of violence in southern Italy.
