During his 2024 campaign, Trump said he would end wars, not start them. After winning the election, he launched airstrikes on Yemen, Syria, Iran, and other countries. His biggest move was a large attack on Iran, claiming it was an imminent threat. He built this case on old accusations and ignored polls showing most Americans opposed war. The move mirrors the Bush‑era Iraq invasion, raising doubts about Trump’s promises.
