New York Mayor‑elect Zohran Mamdani says Donald Trump is a fascist and a threat to democracy, even after they met warmly at the White House. The meeting was surprisingly friendly; Trump praised Mamdani’s work and they talked about housing, food prices and cost‑of‑living. Mamdani keeps police commissioner Jessica Tisch, who has cut crime. While the two agree on some issues, they still clash over police numbers and politics.
