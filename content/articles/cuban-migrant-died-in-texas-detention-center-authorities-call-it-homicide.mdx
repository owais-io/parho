Geraldo Lunas Campos, 55, died in a Texas immigration detention center called Camp East Montana. An autopsy says he died from asphyxiation caused by neck and torso compression while being restrained by guards. Witnesses say he was handcuffed and held down by five officers, with one squeezing his neck until he went unconscious. DHS first said he died from medical distress, later saying he tried to kill himself. The report now calls it a homicide. Lawyers want witnesses to testify.
