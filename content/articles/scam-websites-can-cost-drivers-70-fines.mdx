Scam websites copy official toll sites and trick drivers into paying extra, leaving them with large fines. Thousands have been duped on fake sites for the Dartford Crossing, London’s ULEZ, and Mersey Gateway. Victims only realize the fraud when a penalty notice arrives. Experts urge motorists to pay through gov.uk, verify the website, and use chargeback or report the scam if they’ve already paid.
