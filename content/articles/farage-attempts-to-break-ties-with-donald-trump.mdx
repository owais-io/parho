Farage has been trying to break his long‑time friendship with Donald Trump, but the effort is still shaky. The former UK Independence Party leader has spent the last decade promoting Trump, and the move to distance himself has been met with skepticism. With a weak foreign‑policy track record and no clear defence posts in his shadow cabinet, many see the attempt as a sign he’s losing influence.
