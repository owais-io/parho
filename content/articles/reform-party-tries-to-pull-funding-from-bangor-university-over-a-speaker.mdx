The article says that Bangor University's debating society said no to a Reform UK MP and an influencer who want to speak about migrants and free speech. Reform is pressuring universities to let them speak or risk losing £30 million of state money. The piece argues the move could hurt small universities, shows that most students still value free speech, and warns that politics could use money to silence dissent.
