The Dublin racing festival kicked off with a big win for Brighterdaysahead, who beat the odds‑favoured Lossiemouth in the Irish Champion Hurdle. The two March‑2019 foals were in their first Grade‑One clash, and Brighterdaysahead ran confidently while Lossiemouth struggled. The result reshuffled betting for next month’s Cheltenham Festival, putting Brighterdaysahead as a top contender and pushing Lossiemouth up the price list. Both trainers eye the Champion Hurdle.
