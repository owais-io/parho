Liverpool’s new £125m striker, Alexander Isak, hurt his left leg while scoring Liverpool’s first goal against Tottenham. He left the game in pain and the club is waiting for an MRI to see if he broke the bone. Coach Arne Slot says it could mean months out, and the team will have fewer attacking options. Liverpool may look to sign new forwards while hoping Isak returns soon.
