A heavy snowstorm left New York streets covered for more than a week. Critics, especially from the right‑wing New York Post, say Mayor Zohran Mamdani is responsible for the slow clearing. The Post and several social‑media users accuse him of mismanaging snow removal and of failing to keep streets safe. Mamdani says the cold front, not the mayor, kept the snow from melting and that sanitation crews are working hard.
