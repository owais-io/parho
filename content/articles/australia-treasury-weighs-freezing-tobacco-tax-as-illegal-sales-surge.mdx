Australia Treasury is modelling how cigarette prices affect demand as illegal cigarette sales grow. Experts are calling for a freeze on the high tobacco tax to stop the black market from draining the budget. Illegal packs sell for $10‑$15, half of all smokes, while legal packs cost over $40. Freezing the tax could lower prices, reduce crime and keep health gains.
