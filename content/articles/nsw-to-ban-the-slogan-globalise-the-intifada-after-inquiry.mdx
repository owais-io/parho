The chair of a New South Wales inquiry says his report will recommend banning the slogan “globalise the intifada,” a phrase used by pro‑Palestine supporters. Jewish groups also want “from the river to the sea” and “death to the IDF” outlawed. The inquiry closed to public input, sparking criticism that it was rushed and lacked transparency. Opponents warn that banning specific slogans could face legal challenges. The final report is due by the end of January.
