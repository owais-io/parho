Labour MPs and other politicians want the UK government to halt new deals with US data company Palantir because they say the contracts lack transparency. Palantir has secured over £500 million in NHS and defence deals, and its founder is linked to former UK ministers and controversial figures. Calls to pause the company’s AI services in policing and healthcare have grown after blocked FOI requests and questions about conflicts of interest.
