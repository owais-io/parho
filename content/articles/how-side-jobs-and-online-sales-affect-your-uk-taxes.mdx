Since 2024, UK sites like Vinted, eBay and Airbnb must send HMRC data if you sell over 30 items or earn more than £1,700 a year. Profits from trading, rent or freelance work count as income, but you have a £1,000 trading allowance. If you earn over £1,000 from extra work, you must register for self‑assessment, keep records, and can deduct expenses. HMRC letters usually ask for proof; respond quickly and, if you owe, negotiate a payment plan.
