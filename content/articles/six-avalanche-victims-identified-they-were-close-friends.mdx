A deadly avalanche at Castle Peak in the Sierra Nevada this week killed eight people and left one missing. Six of the dead have now been identified – Carrie Atkin, Liz Clabaugh, Danielle Keatley, Kate Morse, Caroline Sekar and Kate Vitt. They were all close friends who often skied together. Families are grieving, saying the women loved the mountains and supported their friends and neighbors. Their loved ones say the tragedy will be remembered forever.
