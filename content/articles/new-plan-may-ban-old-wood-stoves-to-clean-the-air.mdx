England’s new environmental plan will tighten air‑pollution limits and could ban older wood stoves in many places. It aims to cut fine‑particle pollution to EU levels (10 µg/m³) by 2030 and to WHO levels by 2040. The plan will spend £500 m on landscape projects, targeting 250,000 ha of wildlife habitat by 2030. It also promises every home will be within a 15‑minute walk of green space.
