More musicians have cancelled performances at Washington’s Kennedy Center after the board renamed it to include Trump. Jazz group The Cookers pulled out of a New Year’s Eve show, citing the center’s new name and the politics it represents. Other artists, like a folk singer and a dance company, also withdrew, while the center’s president threatened a lawsuit. The cancellations spark a debate over art, politics, and freedom of expression.
