James Hurrell shocked top‑ranked Stephen Bunting, winning 4‑3 in a tense PDC World Championship match. The 41‑year‑old, normally ranked 63, dominated the set play and outscored Bunting in averages. Bunting, despite strong finishes, fell short, ending a frustrating season. Hurrell, who returned to darts after a life‑saving surgery, now looks confident ahead of his next quarter‑final, and the win could boost his world standing.
