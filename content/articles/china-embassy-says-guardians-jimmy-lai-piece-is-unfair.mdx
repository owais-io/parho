China’s embassy in London says the Guardian article that called Jimmy Lai a democracy champion is wrong. The embassy claims Lai actually helped plan protests that hurt Hong Kong and that he is a Chinese citizen. It says Hong Kong is fully part of China, and the 2020 National Security Law has brought stability and protected residents’ rights. The embassy urges the Guardian to respect China’s sovereignty and be objective.
