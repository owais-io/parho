The article warns that shouting at small businesses on social media can be harmful. It explains how one bad day—sick staff, a kitchen mistake, or personal struggles—can ruin a shop’s reputation when shared online. Small owners lack the money to fight a viral attack, unlike big corporations. Instead, readers are urged to share praise or give private feedback, not launch public attacks.
