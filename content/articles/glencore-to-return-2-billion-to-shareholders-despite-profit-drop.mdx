Glencore has announced it will return $2 billion (£1.47 billion) to shareholders even though its 2023 profit fell 6 % to $13.5 billion. The decision comes after talks to merge with Rio Tinto collapsed. The company said it will keep investing in copper, aiming for more than 1 million tonnes a year by 2028. Glencore also highlighted a $4 billion stake in Bunge that it considers surplus capital for shareholders.
