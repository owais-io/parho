Mexican teens are posting TikTok videos pretending to be agents of the 1970s DFS, the country’s brutal secret police. They use AI to dress in old‑school suits, pose with luxury cars, and brag about “impunity” and wealth. The trend has sparked criticism because the DFS is remembered for torture, kidnappings and even drug‑related killings. Some users counter the videos with clips that remind people of the agency’s violent past, warning against glorifying it.
