Brazil’s environment minister Marina Silva urges all nations to face the challenge of dropping fossil fuels. She says a roadmap would help, but it should be voluntary and decided by each country. The plan could start at COP30 in Brazil, where countries discuss how to cut fossil‑fuel use. While some oil‑rich states resist, Brazil hopes to build a fair process that lets everyone move toward cleaner energy.
