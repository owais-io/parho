An alleged abuse victim, known only as N, says the soon‑to‑be archbishop of Canterbury, Sarah Mullally, is unfit for the job after the Church of England chose not to act on a complaint against her. N claims Mullally mishandled a 2019 abuse claim and even leaked details, which led to his suicide attempts. The decision to let Archbishop York Stephen Cottrell, who has his own complaints, review the case has sparked criticism, and N plans to appeal.
