Melania Trump presented her 2025 inaugural gown at the Smithsonian National Museum of American History, adding a second dress to the first‑ladies collection—a rare honor. She talked about the silk dress, its designer, and her documentary on its creation. Trump praised the museum even as her husband had earlier criticized it, underscoring the lasting link between presidential fashion and American history.
