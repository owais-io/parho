Trent Alexander‑Arnold may leave Liverpool for Manchester City. Liverpool say they won’t sign a new left‑back after a poor season and are letting the defender go, while City want to fill their right‑back vacancy. Meanwhile, other clubs are moving players: Flamengo may send Lucas Paquetá back to Brazil, Vasco da Gama’s Rayan is headed to Bournemouth, and Chelsea are hunting young centre‑backs from France and Spain.
