Trump fired Kristi Noem, the head of Homeland Security, after a year of controversial policies. FEMA workers said her leadership hurt the agency’s ability to respond to natural disasters. She wanted to cut FEMA’s size, cut funding, and personally approve big spending, which delayed aid during floods and left some states without help. Staff celebrated her removal, hoping the new boss will restore FEMA’s capacity.
