My teenage daughter started believing in God and wears a silver cross every day. I, a non‑religious mom, am proud and supportive. Research shows teens feel spirituality strongly because their brains separate feeling from thinking. I can help by staying open, encouraging her to question sources, and modeling tolerance. This healthy exploration lets her find identity and meaning while keeping our relationship strong.
