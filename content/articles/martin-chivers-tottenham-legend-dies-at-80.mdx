Martin Chivers was a top striker for Tottenham in the 1970s, scoring 174 goals for the club and helping them win the 1971 League Cup and the 1972 European Cup. He also played for England, scoring 13 goals in 24 matches, but missed out on a World Cup. After football he ran a pub and stayed active with Spurs events. He died aged 80.
