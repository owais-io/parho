Kathy Ruemmler, Goldman Sachs’ chief legal officer and former White House counsel, has quit after emails revealed she called the convicted sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein “Uncle Jeffrey” and received luxury gifts from him. She also helped him with legal strategy while he was still on trial for sex crimes. The firm said she’d “regret ever knowing him,” and her boss said he respected her decision.
