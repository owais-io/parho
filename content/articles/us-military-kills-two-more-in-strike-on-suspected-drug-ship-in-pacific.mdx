The US military says it killed two men on a suspected drug‑smuggling boat in international waters of the eastern Pacific. It’s the 30th naval strike on such vessels and the death toll for smugglers rises to at least 107. The Pentagon posted a video and said the strike was ordered by Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth. Trump mentioned an unreported hit in Venezuela, claiming it would stop a huge drug network.
