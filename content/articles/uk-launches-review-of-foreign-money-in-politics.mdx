The UK government will investigate how money from hostile states like Russia and China might be influencing British politics after a former Reform UK politician, Nathan Gill, was jailed for taking bribes. The review will look at current campaign‑finance laws, how well they catch foreign money, and whether the Electoral Commission can enforce the rules. Findings will help shape future elections and new democracy‑protecting laws.
