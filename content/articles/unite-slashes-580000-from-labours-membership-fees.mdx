Unite, one of the UK’s biggest trade unions, will cut the money it pays to the Labour Party by 40%, reducing donations by £580,000. The move follows a dispute over a Birmingham bin strike, where Unite workers opposed a Labour‑run council. The union says Labour is not listening to workers and plans to review its affiliation at next year’s rules conference.
