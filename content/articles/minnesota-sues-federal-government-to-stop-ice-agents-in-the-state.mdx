The Minnesota attorney general filed a lawsuit against the federal government to halt the surge of ICE agents after a fatal shooting and reports of excessive force. The suit claims the agents are making warrantless arrests, targeting churches, schools and businesses, and lack proper training. Minnesota wants a court order to end the federal presence, while similar complaints are also being raised in Illinois. Local officials say the influx hurts the Twin Cities.
