Jamie Dimon, head of JP Morgan, warned at the World Economic Forum that AI could spark civil unrest if rolled out too quickly. He urged governments and businesses to help displaced workers with training and support, citing truck drivers as an example. Dimon said companies will cut staff as AI expands but society must plan slow, retraining programmes to avoid large job losses.
