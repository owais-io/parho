Western Australia police are looking into anonymous flyers sent to homes in Perth that falsely accuse LGBTQ+ people of crimes like paedophilia. The flyers even feature photos of Pride WA board member Gregory Helleren with false allegations. Police have received several complaints and are investigating. The incident has sparked demands for stronger anti‑vilification laws, as current state laws do not fully protect sexual or gender minorities from hate crimes.
