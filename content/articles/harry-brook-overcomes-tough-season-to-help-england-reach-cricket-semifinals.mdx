Harry Brook says the past few months were the hardest of his life, after a rough Ashes tour and a nightclub bouncer fight that kept him in the headlines. Despite that, he scored a brilliant century in the last ODI against Pakistan, putting England into the World Cup semi‑finals. The win, and his new batting spot at No. 3, have lifted his confidence and earned praise from teammates and rivals alike.
