Readers wrote letters for and against Labour leader Keir Starmer after he named former minister Peter Mandelson as U.S. ambassador. Some praised Starmer’s integrity and skill, while others say the move shows poor judgment and a lack of vision. The letters discuss public dislike, policy worries, and how the appointment might affect the country’s future direction.
