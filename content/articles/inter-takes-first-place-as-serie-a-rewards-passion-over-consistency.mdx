After beating Genoa 2‑1, Inter Milan moved to the top of Serie A, with coach Cristian Chivu saying the team is doing well despite criticism. Inter’s strong start, winning 11 games and never drawing, has put them ahead while rivals like Milan and Napoli struggle with injuries and shaky results. The story also notes Fiorentina’s trouble fighting relegation, and how clubs are balancing domestic and European challenges.
