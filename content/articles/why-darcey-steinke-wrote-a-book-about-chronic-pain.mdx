Darcey Steinke had a painful herniated disc that made everyday life hard. With limited medicine and no clear cure, she wrote a memoir called This Is the Door. She interviewed about 80 people to learn how pain changes work, feelings, and faith. The book shows how pain can spark empathy, artistic creativity, and new spiritual ideas while also forcing us to face mortality deeply.
