Eve Muirhead, Britain’s head of the Olympic team, says the 53 athletes heading to the Milano‑Cortina Games could win up to eight medals – the most Britain has ever won in one Winter Games. UK Sport has put £25.5m into winter sports and athletes are already winning world events. New tech like aerodynamic helmets and skinsuits are being tested, but the real challenge will be handling pressure and small margins that decide races.
