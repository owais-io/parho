Severe Cyclone Mitchell is heading toward the Pilbara coast, staying at Category 3 intensity. Authorities have issued emergency warnings from Karratha to Onslow and opened evacuation centers. The storm will make landfall between Exmouth and Onslow early Monday, bringing high winds, heavy rain, and possible flooding. Residents are told to stay indoors, ports are closed, and roads may be blocked as rivers rise. The cyclone’s moisture could also spark thunderstorms across inland Australia.
