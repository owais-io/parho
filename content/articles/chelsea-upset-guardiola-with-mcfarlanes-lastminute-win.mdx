Chelsea’s new interim boss, Calum McFarlane, got a big surprise when Enzo Fernández scored an equaliser in the 94th minute, keeping Chelsea in the Premier League. The win was a shock to Pep Guardiola, who had to watch his City side lose points. McFarlane’s team played a tight 4‑3‑3, kept City from scoring early, and used quick counter‑attacks to win the game. This result keeps Chelsea close to the top of the table.
