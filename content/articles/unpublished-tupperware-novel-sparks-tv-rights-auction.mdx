A new book called Wet Ink, written by 33‑year‑old Abigail Avis, tells the story of a 1960s London housewife who sells Tupperware parties while secretly writing erotic stories. Though it won’t hit shelves until 2027, six big TV producers already fought over the rights to make it a series. Kudos, part of Banijay, won the deal. The craze shows how many writers, especially women, are drawing fierce competition from film and streaming studios.
