Stefon Diggs, the Patriots’ top receiver, is accused of strangulation and assault after an incident on Dec. 2. A judge is deciding whether to keep police reports sealed before his arraignment on Jan. 23. Diggs’s lawyer wants the documents hidden to avoid publicity and is negotiating a settlement with the victim. The Patriots say he denies the claims and will cooperate. The case could affect his start before the AFC title game.
