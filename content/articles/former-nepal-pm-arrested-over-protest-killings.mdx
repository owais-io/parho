In Kathmandu, former prime minister KP Sharma Oli was taken into custody early Saturday after a police report linked him and former home‑affairs minister Ramesh Lekhak to the deaths of 19 youths during 2023 protests that toppled his government. New PM Balendra Shah, an ex‑rapper, said the arrests were about justice, not revenge. Oli’s lawyer calls the detention illegal. The move follows a commission recommending up to 10 years in prison.
