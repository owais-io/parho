Iran’s latest protests have turned deadly, with over 2,000 killed as security forces fire on crowds. The regime shut down the internet, making it hard to know the exact toll, and has shown photos of morgues to intimidate citizens. Protesters from all social classes and every province demanded better living conditions and an end to political oppression. While U.S. President Trump urged them to keep fighting, no concrete help has come. International attention has faded, leaving many victims unseen.
