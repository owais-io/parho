Sir Tim Berners‑Lee, the man who invented the web, says its original free‑and‑open purpose is being lost to profit, big tech and social media addiction. He is leading a movement to give users control over their data with a new system called Solid, which lets people keep their own digital “pods.” He also worries about AI’s unchecked growth and supports child‑safe phones over bans on popular sites.
