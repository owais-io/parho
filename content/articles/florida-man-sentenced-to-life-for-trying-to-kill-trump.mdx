Ryan Routh, a Florida man, was sentenced to life in prison after he tried to shoot Donald Trump at a golf course in September 2024. Judge Aileen Cannon also added a 7‑year sentence for a gun charge. Routh had caused chaos in court, even attempting to stab himself after the jury found him guilty. He did not show remorse, and prosecutors said he must spend the rest of his life behind bars.
