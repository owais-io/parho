A federal agent shot a man in the leg during a raid in north Minneapolis, sparking large protests. The incident follows the death of Renee Good last week, heightening city tensions. Police and city officials called on people to leave the area, while the mayor condemned ICE actions but urged calm. With thousands of ICE officers in the state, Minnesota’s governor urged residents to record any misconduct.
