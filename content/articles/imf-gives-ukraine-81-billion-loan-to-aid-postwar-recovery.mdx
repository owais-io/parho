The IMF has approved an $8.1 billion, four‑year loan for Ukraine, with $1.5 billion paid immediately. The money will fix the country’s balance‑of‑payments problems, boost rebuilding after the war, and help Ukraine pursue EU membership. Kyiv and Washington met to discuss reconstruction, planning a trilateral meeting with Russia in March. Meanwhile, drones have briefly breached nearby airspace.
