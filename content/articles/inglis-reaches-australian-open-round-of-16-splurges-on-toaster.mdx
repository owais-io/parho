Maddison Inglis made it to the Australian Open round of 16 before losing 6‑0, 6‑3 to world No. 2 Iga Swiatek. The Aussie player celebrated her run by treating herself to a fancy toaster, a small luxury from her $480,000 prize money. Inglis said she was proud of her effort and will use the money to support her tennis and buy other household items. Swiatek continues her quest for a seventh grand slam.
