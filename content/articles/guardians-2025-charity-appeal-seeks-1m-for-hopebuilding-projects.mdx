Guardian’s 2025 appeal, centred on hope, is close to its £1m goal, with over £900k already donated. The money supports five charities that use projects to bring communities together and reduce hate. Projects include the Linking Network, which pairs schools from different backgrounds; Citizens UK, which trains organisers; Salaam Shalom Kitchen, a Muslim‑Jewish food project; 174 Trust’s Circle of Change; and Who Is Your Neighbour?, which runs tough‑talk sessions. All aim to build empathy, unity and a better future.
