Boris (69) and Sofia (61) Gurman, a 34‑year‑married couple, died trying to stop a gunman at Bondi beach. Dashcam footage shows them tackling shooter Sajid Akram on Campbell Parade. They were part of a Hanukah gathering when the attack began. Their family said they were kind, hardworking, and self‑less. The incident is the worst mass shooting in Australia since 1996.
