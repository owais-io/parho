Democratic lawmakers attacked VA Secretary Doug Collins on a hearing for repeating Trump‑era claims that a VA nurse, Alex Pretti, was a “terrorist” when she was shot by immigration officers in Minneapolis. Collins refused to answer questions and the debate turned into a fight over VA plans to cut staff, bring in private insurers and possibly lose doctors and nurses. Democrats also raised worries about deporting veterans and the real impact on benefits.
