People in the US and Iran keep trying to stop a war over Iran’s nuclear plans, but Iran wants to enrich uranium for national pride. It began in the 1970s and is now tied to politics and a sense of prestige. Iran says it’s a right under the NPT, while the US has imposed heavy sanctions. The deal talks have stalled, and Iran sees enrichment as a way to stay powerful and defend itself.
