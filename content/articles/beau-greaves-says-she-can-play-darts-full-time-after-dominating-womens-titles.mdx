Beau Greaves grew from a shy schoolgirl into a darts superstar who has won the Lakeside women’s world title and racked up 86 straight wins on the PDC Women’s Series. Now 21, she’s beaten top male players, earned a tour card for 2026‑27, and says she can do darts full‑time. She wants to move out, play more against men, and keep breaking gender barriers in the sport.
