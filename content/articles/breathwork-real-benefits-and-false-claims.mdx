Breathwork is a popular wellness trend that promises stress relief and better health. Some techniques, like deep belly breathing and Wim Hof’s method, have proven benefits such as lowering stress hormones and reducing inflammation. Many courses charge hundreds of dollars and make grand claims that lack solid evidence. Experts say breathing is free, and risky or exaggerated practices should be done under medical supervision. The science behind breathwork is still growing, so it’s wise to separate fact from hype.
