Jeremy Corbyn has won the leadership of Your Party and plans to cooperate with the Green Party to push a united left‑wing agenda. He wants to heal divisions, especially among pro‑Gaza and Muslim members who felt alienated by former co‑leader Zarah Sultana. The new leadership aims to tackle cost‑of‑living, housing and climate crises while standing against racism and far‑right politics.
