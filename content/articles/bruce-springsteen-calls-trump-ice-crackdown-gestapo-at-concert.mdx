At a New Jersey concert, Bruce Springsteen slammed the Trump administration’s surge of ICE officers, calling their tactics “Gestapo.” He dedicated the song “The Promised Land” to Renee Good, a woman killed by an ICE officer in Minnesota, and urged the crowd to stand up against armed federal troops and unlawful arrests. Trump fired back, calling Springsteen overrated and a “pushy, obnoxious jerk.”
