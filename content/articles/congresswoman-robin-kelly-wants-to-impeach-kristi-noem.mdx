Congresswoman Robin Kelly announced three impeachment articles against DHS Secretary Kristi Noem, saying she obstructed oversight, acted unconstitutionally, and abused her office. This follows the killing of Renee Good. The article argues that impeachment can remind people that government officials must be held accountable, especially in a political climate that often tolerates misconduct. It urges Democrats to signal that actions have consequences and that this step could slow down the rapid cycle of scandals.
