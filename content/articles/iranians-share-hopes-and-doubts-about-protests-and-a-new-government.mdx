Many Iranians say protests after Mahsa Amini’s death haven’t changed anything and may never do. Fashion designers, store owners, a sports coach, an unemployed worker, a gold trader and others talk about jobs gone, money losing value, and families suffering. While most dream of freedom, most are skeptical that the Islamic Republic will fall soon and worry that even a new leader won’t fix deep problems.
