US forces attacked a boat in the eastern Pacific that was suspected of moving drugs. Two people were killed. The strike was the first since President Trump ordered the capture of Venezuela’s president, Nicolás Maduro, earlier this month. The U.S. has hit more than 30 smuggling boats since September, killing over 100. It is also targeting oil tankers linked to Venezuela. A video shows the boat exploding and the search for any survivors continues.
