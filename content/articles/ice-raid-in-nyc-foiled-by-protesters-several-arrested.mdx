Federal immigration agents tried to raid a parking garage in Manhattan’s Chinatown, but about 200 local protesters stopped them. They blocked the trucks, shouted, and in the melee some hurled trash cans and planters. A masked driver sprayed a chemical irritant. Police arrested several agitators. City officials said the raid was cruel and the NYPD’s help was illegal. The mayor‑elect condemned the action and promised to protect immigrants.
