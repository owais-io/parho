A former Canberra high‑school student is feared to have been executed in Iran, sparking worry among his classmates and the Australian government. Canberra officials have taken the case to Tehran, urging the country to end the death penalty and halt violent crackdowns on protests. The ACT government has offered wellbeing support to affected students, while Australia maintains a strong opposition to executions in any circumstance.
