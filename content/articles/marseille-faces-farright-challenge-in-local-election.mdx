In March, Marseille voters decide if the far‑right National Rally can win the city council, a test for France’s political mood. The RN focuses on crime and policing, while the left says it is anti‑immigration and racist. Many residents worry about safety and poverty, and the election could shape the 2027 presidential race. The result matters for national politics in the near future.
