Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said that striking Russia’s energy sites is a legitimate military target because the money that powers the war comes from that sector. He added that Russia’s own drone attacks on Odesa, Kharkiv and other cities have caused deaths and fires. Meanwhile, security forces arrested a man in Dubai linked to a shoot‑up of a Russian officer. The US set a June deadline for peace talks, and Ukraine is sanctioning foreign suppliers of drone parts.
