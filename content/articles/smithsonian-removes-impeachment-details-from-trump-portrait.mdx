The Smithsonian’s National Portrait Gallery in Washington, D.C., has taken down the plaque that listed Donald Trump’s two impeachments and the Jan. 6 Capitol attack. The new plaque only notes his years in office. Trump had earlier fired the museum’s director, a move the board rejected. The removal is part of Trump’s broader effort to change how his presidency is remembered.
