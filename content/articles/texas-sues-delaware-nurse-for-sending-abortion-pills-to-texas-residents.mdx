Texas has filed a lawsuit against Delaware nurse practitioner Debra Lynch, who runs the abortion‑pill delivery service Her Safe Harbor. The state says Lynch shipped pills to Texans, breaking Texas’s abortion ban, and wants the court to stop her from providing abortions there. The case highlights the clash between “shield laws” that protect out‑of‑state providers and Texas’s strict anti‑abortion rules. Lynch has said she will keep mailing pills.
