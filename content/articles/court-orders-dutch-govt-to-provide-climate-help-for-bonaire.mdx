A Dutch court said the Netherlands treated people on the Caribbean island of Bonaire unfairly by not helping them deal with climate change. The judge said the government must create a climate‑adaptation plan for the island and set clear, binding targets to cut carbon emissions. The case was brought by Bonaire residents and Greenpeace. The ruling cites human‑rights law and could inspire more climate justice actions.
