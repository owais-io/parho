The FA is struggling to find opponents for England’s final warm‑up games because the 2026 World Cup starts late. Coach Thomas Tuchel wants friendlies close to the tournament kickoff, but FIFA rules forbid matches in the last five days before a team’s first game. The FA is eyeing teams like New Zealand or Costa Rica, but only clubs playing early in the opening round can face England on 10 June. The plan remains unsettled.
