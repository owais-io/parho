Brighton and West Ham played a tight match at the Amex Stadium. Jarrod Bowen opened the scoring for West Ham in the first half. Brighton struggled in the first 45 minutes, but Georginio Rutter headed a late equaliser to keep them in the game. The weather was wet and windy, making play hard. The result was a 1‑1 draw that left Brighton still fighting to avoid relegation.
