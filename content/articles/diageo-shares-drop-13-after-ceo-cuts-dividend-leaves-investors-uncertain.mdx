Diageo’s shares fell 13% after the new chief executive, Sir Dave Lewis, cut the dividend by half and offered little clarity on how the company will boost profits. The spirits giant also lowered its annual outlook and the market has been weak, especially in the US and China. Investors want concrete plans for pricing and cost cuts, but Lewis’s comments have been vague, making the turnaround appear uncertain.
