Alex, a Sri Lankan who fled civil war in 2009, travelled by boat to Australia and was wrongly accused of smuggling. He spent five years in jail, then almost a decade in detention while his protection visa kept getting denied. His mental health fell; he tried to kill himself before. In September 2024 he set himself on fire at Perth’s Kings Park and died, leaving his family behind.
