Aston Villa beat Leeds 2-1 after Morgan Rogers scored twice, including a spectacular free‑kick. The win lifts Villa into Champions League qualification and leaves Leeds still in the relegation zone. Leeds had an early goal from Lukas Nmecha, but Villa’s midfield and new signings, Donyell Malen and Ian Maatsen, took control in the second half. Coach Daniel Farke says Leeds will improve, while Villa’s performance shows a growing squad depth.
