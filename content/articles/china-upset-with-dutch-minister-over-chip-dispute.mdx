China is “extremely disappointed” with Dutch minister Vincent Karremans after he spoke about a chip dispute that hurt the car industry. The Netherlands had taken control of the Chinese‑owned chip maker Nexperia, prompting China to ban its chips worldwide. China blames the Dutch for the chaos. A Dutch delegation will visit Beijing next week to try to resolve the conflict.
