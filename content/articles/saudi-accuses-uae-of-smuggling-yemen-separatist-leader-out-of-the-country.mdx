Saudi Arabia says the United Arab Emirates helped Aidarous al‑Zubaidi, a southern Yemen separatist leader, escape after he missed talks in Riyadh. Saudi officials say he left Aden, went to an Emirati‑owned port in Somaliland, then flew to Somalia before ending up at Abu Dhabi. The move worsens a spat between Riyadh and Abu Dhabi over the south‑Yemen rebellion, which has already strained their alliance against the Houthi rebels.
