UPS Flight 2976 crashed near Louisville, Kentucky, on Nov. 4, killing four pilots and hitting nearby businesses. The blast killed 15 people in total, now including Alain Rodriguez Colina, who was badly hurt at a scrapyard and died on Christmas. Kentucky’s governor and Louisville’s mayor confirmed the loss. The crash caused the FAA to ground all MD‑11 cargo planes, and UPS faces lawsuits over using older aircraft.
