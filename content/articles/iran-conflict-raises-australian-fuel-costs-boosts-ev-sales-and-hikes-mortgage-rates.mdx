The clash near Iran’s Strait of Hormuz is sending oil prices soaring, which hurts Australia in several ways. Higher fuel costs are pushing more people to buy electric cars. Banks are raising interest rates, so mortgage payments climb. Shipping, flights and food are getting pricier, and fertilizer is at record highs. Even helium for MRI machines is in danger, meaning hospitals could face shortages.
