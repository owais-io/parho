Jakob Ingebrigtsen, Norway’s top distance runner, shares a day in his home gym and farmhouse. The interview shows how he trains, recovers from an Achilles injury, and stays focused on winning despite past setbacks, including a court case with his father. He talks about his obsession with success, his love of cars, and his family life with his wife and new daughter. He plans to keep training hard for future titles.
