London traders were nervous before Rachel Reeves’s budget, but an accidental early release of the government’s forecast changed things. The leaked numbers sent traders scrambling to read the 200‑page OBR report, causing quick moves in bond yields and the pound. Yields fell from 4.5% to about 4.4% and the pound gained about 0.5%. The market saw a small relief, with no major surprise for investors today.
