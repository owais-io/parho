The White House said the U.S. would be lucky if Donald Trump stayed on for a third term, even though most experts say he can't run again under the Constitution. The comments came after Trump talked about the idea with lawyer Alan Dershowitz, who is writing a book on the topic. Trump’s team says his first year was a success, but his approval ratings are low. Many supporters still buy Trump 2028 gear and want to see him return.
