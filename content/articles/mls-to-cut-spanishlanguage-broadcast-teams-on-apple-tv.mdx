MLS will cut the number of Spanish‑language commentary teams on Apple TV by about half for the 2026 season. The league will keep only its best seven pairings, leaving other games with a single announcer. Some Spanish‑language shows will be dropped, and English coverage will also shrink. MLS plans to produce about half of matches from London, using IMG, while keeping on‑site reporters for key games. The decision follows data that Spanish‑speaking viewers prefer live games over studio shows.
