Three smart logicians each wear a hat with a positive whole number on it. Everyone sees the other two hats, and the numbers are public knowledge. One of the numbers equals the sum of the other two. After a round of statements where each says they don’t yet know their own number, the first logician suddenly figures it out. The puzzle asks what number he must have.
