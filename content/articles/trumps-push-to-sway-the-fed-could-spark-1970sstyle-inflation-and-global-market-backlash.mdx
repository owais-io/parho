Trump is pressuring Fed chair Jerome Powell to lower interest rates. Economists warn this could lead to runaway inflation like the 1970s and cause global investors to pull money out of the US. The DOJ has opened a criminal probe into Powell over building repairs, and Trump has threatened to fire him. Fed independence is crucial for keeping prices stable. If rates are cut too fast, inflation could rise again, hurting low‑income households.
