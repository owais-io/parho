Two ex‑hackers spoke to sixth‑form students in Manchester, sharing how they fell into cybercrime through online gaming. They warned about real‑world risks like doxing and swatting, and explained that gaming can teach useful hacking skills. The speakers are now ethical hackers, working with a company that turns gamers into cybersecurity testers. Their talk aims to inspire students to channel their talents into protecting businesses instead of breaking the law.
