Boris (69) and Sofia (61) were a Sydney couple who were shot to death while trying to stop a gunman at Bondi on Sunday. Their funeral in Woollahra was full of family, friends and officials. The rabbi called them heroes and said they earned a special place in heaven. Mourners remembered their kindness, courage, and the love they showed their son Alex. The event drew politicians and the Jewish community, making it a moving tribute.
