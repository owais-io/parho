At Davos, IMF chief Kristalina Georgieva warned that AI will change 60 % of jobs in rich countries, boosting pay for some but cutting entry‑level roles and squeezing the middle class. ECB president Christine Lagarde added that widening wealth gaps risk economic trouble and that cooperation on AI, taxes and trade is vital. Both leaders urged governments to prepare for a future where AI, trade tensions and geopolitics make the job market more fragile.
