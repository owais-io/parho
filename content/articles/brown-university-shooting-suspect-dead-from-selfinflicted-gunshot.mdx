A man named Claudio Neves Valente, who was suspected of killing two people and injuring nine at Brown University and then killing a MIT professor, was found dead from a self‑inflicted gunshot in a New Hampshire storage unit. Police say he acted alone. The White House responded by suspending the green‑card lottery, blaming it for letting him enter the US. He was a former Brown student and a Portuguese national. The incident has raised questions about immigration and campus safety.
