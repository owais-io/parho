Georgia senators are shocked that Tulsi Gabbard, the president’s director of national intelligence, was seen at an FBI raid on a Fulton County election warehouse. The FBI was taking 2020 election records. Gabbard’s presence has raised questions because she’s linked to election‑conspiracy theories and her job is normally overseas. Lawmakers want an explanation and are calling for an intelligence‑committee review, while others say she was there for election security.
