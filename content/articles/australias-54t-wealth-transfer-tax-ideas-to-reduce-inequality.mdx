Australia is set to hand over $5.4 trillion in wealth over the next 20 years, and economists warn it could make inequality worse. Experts suggest new taxes on inheritance, pensions and property could help. Ideas include taxing inherited money, capping superannuation bonuses, and even taxing family homes or changing property taxes. The goal is to spread wealth more fairly and support a stronger economy.
