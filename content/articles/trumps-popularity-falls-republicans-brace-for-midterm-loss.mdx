After a busy year, Trump’s approval has dropped to 36 %, with many voters blaming him for high prices and weak immigration policy. Republicans fear a mid‑term blow‑out because the party that holds the White House often loses seats. Trump’s executive orders, tax cuts, tariffs and attacks on institutions have sparked lawsuits and court defeats, while Democrats rally on affordability and crime. Several top GOP senators have already said they won’t run again, pointing to a shaky future for the party.
