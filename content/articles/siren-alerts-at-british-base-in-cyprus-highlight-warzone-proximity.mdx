At RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus, sirens blared twice today after a ballistic missile launched from Iran was suspected of heading toward the base. The alerts forced soldiers and nearby civilians to seek cover, and the noise has driven many locals to leave the area. A drone, likely fired by Hezbollah, also damaged an U‑2 hangar, prompting new air‑defence equipment to arrive. The UK is boosting security while Cypriot residents protest the high alert.
