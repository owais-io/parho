The US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agency will help with security during the Milan‑Cortina Winter Games. Many Italians, outraged by ICE’s tough US immigration crackdown, have launched petitions and called on the government to stop the agents’ involvement. Local officials, an EU lawmaker, and the mayor of Milan have voiced opposition, while the US says ICE will only support risk assessment, not enforce immigration law.
