California skater Alysa Liu surprised everyone by winning gold in the women's figure skating event at the Winter Olympics, ending a 24‑year drought for U.S. women. After quitting in 2022, she returned to training and delivered a clean performance that beat Japan’s Kaori Sakamoto and Ami Nakai. Liu landed all seven triple jumps and scored 226.79, becoming the first American woman to win individual Olympic gold since 2002.
