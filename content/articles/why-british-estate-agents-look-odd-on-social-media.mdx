Emma Beddington explains that she loves glamorous property shows but gets fed the opposite on her feed. When British estate agents appear, they’re dull, presenting ordinary homes in gloomy settings. She feels the algorithm mixes these bland listings with aspirational content, and she wants her feed to show more exciting, high‑end property stories—full of drama, luxury, and visual flair.
