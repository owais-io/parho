An illegal waste pile up to 6 m high sits in a field next to the River Cherwell, Oxfordshire. The mound is thought to contain rubbish from local councils, schools and other authorities, raising concerns of corruption. The Environment Agency is investigating but has not cleared the site, warning that the waste is leaking into the river. MPs and local groups are demanding urgent action to protect the river and flood‑plain.
