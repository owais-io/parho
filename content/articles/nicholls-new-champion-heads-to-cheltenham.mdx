Paul Nicholls’ stable is back on the winning track. Five‑year‑old No Drama This End claimed the Grade One Challow Hurdle, beating rivals with a strong finish. Nicholls says the horse matches past stars like Denman and plans to race him at Cheltenham, possibly switching him to chasing after this season. His other horse, Minella Yoga, also won and will head to the festival. These victories lift Nicholls’ confidence and hint at future success.
