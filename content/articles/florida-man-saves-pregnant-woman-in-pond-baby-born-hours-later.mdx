A man named Logan Hayes jumped into a cold Florida pond on Feb 6 to rescue a 34‑week pregnant woman whose car was sinking. He helped her escape and swam her to shore. She was taken to a hospital where doctors performed an emergency C‑section. The baby, a girl named Ivory, was born just hours after the rescue. The incident shows how quick action can save lives.
