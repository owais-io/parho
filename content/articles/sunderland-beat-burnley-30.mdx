Sunderland won 3‑0 at Burnley. Goals came from Mukiele, Brobbey and a quick counter‑attack. The victory keeps Sunderland in the middle of the table, close to the 40‑point mark, while Burnley stay in the relegation zone and remain winless in the league. Manager Scott Parker said the team still has to improve, and Sunderland’s coach praised the squad’s adaptation and future chances.
