Iain Peters, 77, was abused by a former teacher who later hosted the BBC kids show Jackanory. He kept the abuse hidden for about 50 years, using climbing and mountaineering as a way to escape trauma and stay alive. After finally speaking out in 2015, the abuser was jailed. Peters wrote a book, The Corridor, won a mountain‑writing award, and urges other survivors to come forward without shame.
