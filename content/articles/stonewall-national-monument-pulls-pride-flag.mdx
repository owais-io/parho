Trump officials removed the large pride flag from the Stonewall National Monument in New York after a memo said only U.S. and agency flags could fly. Stonewall marks the 1969 riots that sparked the LGBTQ+ rights movement. State leaders, lawmakers and the local LGBTQ+ community blasted the move and plan protests, saying the flag must return. The NPS says the monument still tells its historic story.
