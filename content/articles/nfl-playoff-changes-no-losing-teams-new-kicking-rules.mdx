The article argues that the NFL should stop sending teams with losing records to the playoffs, keeping division winners with winning records at home. It calls for a clean playoff expansion to 16 teams, giving byes to the top two seeds and reseeding each round. It also critiques the new kickoff rules that make long kicks too easy, suggesting a ‘kicking zone’ or narrower uprights to add risk. These changes would keep the playoffs competitive and exciting.
