Energy Minister Chris Bowen said Australians should stop buying jerry cans in a panic as fuel prices climb after a surge in global oil prices linked to the Middle East war. He said supply to Australia is on track and that deliveries will continue into April. Bowen criticized retailers for price‑gouging and urged people to be calm, noting that panic buying is causing the problem. The government is working with fuel suppliers and regulators to keep prices stable.
