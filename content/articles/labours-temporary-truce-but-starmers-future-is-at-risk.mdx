Keir Starmer faces a challenge from inside Labour after Scottish leader Anas Sarwar urged him to resign. MPs are holding off a leadership contest, giving Starmer a short reprieve, but the party’s recent poor performance in a by‑election has made the mood uneasy. Starmer’s allies plan to shore up his position with a positive spring statement and April cost‑of‑living cuts, but the next general election will test him.
