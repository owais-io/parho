Governor Josh Shapiro announced he will seek another term as Pennsylvania’s governor, seeing the 2024 election as a step toward a potential 2028 presidential bid. He’s pushed for more school funding and protected abortion rights, but progressives criticize his cautious stance on climate and education. Shapiro is a strong critic of Trump and a vocal supporter of Israel, drawing both praise and backlash.
