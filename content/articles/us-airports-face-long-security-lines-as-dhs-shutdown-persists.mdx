Travelers across the US are stuck in long airport security lines because the Department of Homeland Security is shut down. TSA staff have gone weeks without pay, so there aren’t enough people to screen passengers. Airports like JFK and George Bush International have reported wait times up to two hours, and flights are delayed or cancelled. The problem comes as the country prepares for a busy spring travel season and a 4% rise in passengers.
