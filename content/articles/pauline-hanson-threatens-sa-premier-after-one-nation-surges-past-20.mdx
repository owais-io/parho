After the South Australian election, Pauline Hanson celebrated One Nation’s jump to over 20% of the vote. Speaking to cheering supporters, she warned Premier Peter Malinauskas that she would leave ‘landmines’—political threats that could explode. The night was festive, with fans in orange and selfies with Hanson. She said the result had put major parties on edge and promised tough policies, especially on immigration, for the future and the next year.
