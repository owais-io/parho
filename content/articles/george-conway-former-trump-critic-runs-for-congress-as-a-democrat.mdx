George Conway, a former Republican who spent years fighting Donald Trump, has announced he will run for Congress in New York’s 12th district as a Democrat. He helped start the anti‑Trump group Lincoln Project and is now challenging a crowded field for the seat of retiring Jerry Nadler. Conway, who was married to Kellyanne Conway and is now divorced, says he will fight corruption and protect democracy.
