Harlequins senior coach Jason Gilmore said he admires England back George Furbank and could bring him to the club. Furbank is out of contract next summer and has been in talks with Harlequins. Gilmore won’t discuss details, only that any good player fits the squad. He also previewed the Saturday match against Bristol Bears and the double‑header at Twickenham.
