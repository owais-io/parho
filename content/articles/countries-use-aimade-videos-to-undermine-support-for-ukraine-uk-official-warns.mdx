UK foreign secretary Yvette Cooper will warn that countries are flooding social media with AI‑made videos to weaken support for Ukraine. She says the campaign, led by Russian agencies using deepfakes and fake documents, is part of a broader disinformation network called Doppelgänger. Cooper will urge other nations to help Britain fight this “information warfare” and highlight how false stories also stir social divisions. The speech comes amid the Ukraine war’s tense talks.
