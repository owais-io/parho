At the World Economic Forum in Davos, former U.S. President Donald Trump said Canada owes the United States for its help and should be grateful, calling out Prime Minister Mark Carney’s criticism of U.S. policy. Trump also warned that he would seize Greenland for a missile‑defence project, and claimed the U.S. would protect Canada. Canadian officials condemned the remarks and said Canada is looking to diversify trade beyond the U.S., seeking partners like China and India.
