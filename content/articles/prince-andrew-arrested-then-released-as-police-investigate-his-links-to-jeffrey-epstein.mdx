Britain’s police arrested former Prince Andrew, a 60‑year‑old member of the royal family, last night for allegedly sharing confidential information with Jeffrey Epstein. He was taken to a police station in Norfolk, questioned for almost 12 hours, and then released while the investigation continues. The case is the first arrest of a living royal in modern Britain. Law‑enforcement agencies are looking into his possible misconduct in public office.
