New FBI documents reveal that from 2002 to 2005 Epstein used a network of friends, including Ghislaine Maxwell, to find and groom under‑age girls for sex, even hiring victims to bring more. The papers describe how Maxwell asked for young women to work at her Palm Beach home, how Epstein’s circle ran out of girls, and that victims were told to recruit others. They also hint at the sharing of child‑porn images and links to other suspects.
