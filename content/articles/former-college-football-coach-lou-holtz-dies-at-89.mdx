Lou Holtz, the celebrated coach who led Notre Dame to a national title and turned around programs at Arkansas, NC State, and others, passed away at 89. After a long coaching career, he also worked as a TV commentator. Holtz was known for his faith, family values, and belief in people. He was honored with the Presidential Medal of Freedom and is remembered for his positive spirit.
