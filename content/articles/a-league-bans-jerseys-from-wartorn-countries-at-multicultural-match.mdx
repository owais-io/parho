The A‑League has ruled that fans cannot wear jerseys from countries currently in major wars for this week’s multicultural match. Western Sydney Wanderers posted a list of banned and allowed clothing, citing league and venue rules that ban “offensive or inflammatory” items. Critics say the ban stifles political expression and targets groups like Palestinians, after a teenager was turned away for a Palestinian flag shirt. Fans and civil‑rights groups have slammed the policy as unfair and exclusionary.
