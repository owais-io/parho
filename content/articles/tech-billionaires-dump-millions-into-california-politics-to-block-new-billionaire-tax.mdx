California’s upcoming governor race has seen tech billionaires pour millions into campaigns, all hoping to stop a proposed billionaire tax that would fund social programs. Google co‑founder Sergey Brin and former CEO Eric Schmidt have donated big sums to both sides, while other Silicon Valley leaders like Tony Xu, Reid Hoffman and Patrick Collison are backing Republican Steve Hilton and Democrat Matt Mahan. The battle over the tax is heating up as signatures are still being collected.
