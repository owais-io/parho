Dana Briggs, a 70‑year‑old Air Force veteran, was taken to a hospital after ICE agents knocked him down during a protest in Illinois. Although the Justice Department dropped all assault charges against him, Briggs says he may file a civil suit over how he was treated. His case, which got national attention, is one of several involving veterans who were injured by federal agents during protests. The incidents show that protesting can still result in serious harm.
