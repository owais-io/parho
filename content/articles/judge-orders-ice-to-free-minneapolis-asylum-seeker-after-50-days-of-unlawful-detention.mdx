A federal judge in Minnesota said ICE had no legal reason to keep Elvis Joel TE, an Ecuadorian asylum seeker, in custody. He had been held for 50 days after a warrantless raid that also took his 2‑year‑old daughter to Texas. The judge ordered the girl released and the father released by Sunday. The case shows how ICE’s aggressive tactics can violate the law and harm families.
