Sarah Porter was running the brutal 108‑mile Montane Winter Spine Challenger South when she received death threats because of her Afghan women’s charity. Organisers, after a risk assessment and police input, decided it was safest to remove her from the course and disabled her tracking device. Porter was disappointed but said she would keep running and her fundraising page is still open.
