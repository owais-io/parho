The article talks about how Britons say “thank you” too much. The writer, who grew up in India, explains that this habit can feel pointless and even rude. She points out annoying British thank‑yous, like “thanks in advance” or the clipped “thank you” in meetings. She also notes that 64 million short thank‑you emails a day could add to carbon emissions. The piece ends with a call to speak less.
