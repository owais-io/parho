With the 2026 Formula One season starting, teams worry that Mercedes and Red Bull have used loopholes in the new engine rules to gain a big performance edge. The teams say their engines can increase compression during a race, giving a 0.3‑0.4 second advantage on the track while still meeting the official limits. Ferrari, Audi, Honda and others are writing to the FIA and will debate the issue at a pre‑season meeting.
