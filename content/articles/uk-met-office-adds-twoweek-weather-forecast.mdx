The UK Met Office will add a 14‑day weather forecast to its website and app, using a new probabilistic method that shows chances of rain, wind and heat. New research says people can read and use these numbers to plan safely. The move follows AI advances from Nvidia and DeepMind, and a new partnership with the BBC for weather updates.
