On Saturday night in Tamoun, a Palestinian village, Israeli border police opened fire on a family returning from a Ramadan shopping trip. They killed mother Waad, father Ali, and their five‑ and seven‑year‑old sons, shooting them in the head and face. Two boys survived with shrapnel wounds. No arrests were made. Palestinian officials called the attack an extrajudicial execution and part of a broader campaign of violence in the West Bank.
