Amera, a 22‑year‑old Yazidi girl from Sinjar, was kidnapped by ISIS in 2014. While held with her family, she secretly wrote letters hoping her missing brother, Ali, would find them. After escaping in 2015, she moved to Australia, studied law, and published a book about her captivity. Now she campaigns for an international probe to locate missing Yazidis, especially her brother.
