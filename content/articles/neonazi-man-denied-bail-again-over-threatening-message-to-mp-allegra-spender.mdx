A 30‑year‑old neo‑Nazi member, Joel Davis, was again denied bail after police charged him with sending a menacing message aimed at MP Allegra Spender. The case followed a protest where he spoke from a megaphone and shouted anti‑multicultural slogans. His lawyer said he now has a newborn son and the conditions in jail are harsh, but a magistrate rejected the request, citing a strong case against him.
