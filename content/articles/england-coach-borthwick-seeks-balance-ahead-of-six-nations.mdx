England’s rugby coach Steve Borthwick spent a day with the Red Arrows to get ideas on how to pick and run a team that can win the Six Nations. He wants to keep his squad sharp, give younger players a chance and still rely on experienced men. With injuries to key players and a lot of competition for places, Borthwick is juggling who will start against Wales and how best to use his squad for the coming tournament.
