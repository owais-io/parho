Three days after launching a new operation, ICE has taken more than 100 people into custody in Maine, mainly in Portland and Lewiston. The arrests target African asylum seekers from countries like Somalia, the DRC and Angola, and include some with no criminal record. State leaders, including Gov. Mills, have complained about secrecy and the impact on businesses and schools, while local groups are helping families find detained relatives.
