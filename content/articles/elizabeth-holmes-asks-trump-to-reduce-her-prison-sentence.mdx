Elizabeth Holmes, the former Theranos CEO, has asked former President Donald Trump to reduce her 11‑year prison sentence after being convicted of fraud. Her request, filed last year, is still pending and the White House has not yet responded. Holmes once claimed her startup could run many blood tests from a single drop, but was found guilty of defrauding investors and patients. The case highlights corporate fraud and legal accountability.
