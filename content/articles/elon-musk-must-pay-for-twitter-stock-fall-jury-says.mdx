A California jury found Elon Musk caused Twitter’s stock to drop so investors could buy it at a cheaper price. Investors sued after Musk posted criticism of bots and fake accounts, then bought Twitter for $54.20 a share. The court ruled his comments likely moved the market, and he may owe investors billions in damages. Musk says he didn’t intend to hurt the stock and plans to appeal.
