Snow falls, turns to mud, and then a white egret appears on the lake. The author describes watching the bird hunt fish, marveling at its clean white feathers. Later, a snowdrop bud shows early spring. A jackdaw with missing feathers turns out to be leucistic, a harmless genetic variant. The writer also mentions future rain and the ongoing task of drying laundry.
