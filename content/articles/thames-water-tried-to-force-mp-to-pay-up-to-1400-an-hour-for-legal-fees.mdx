Thames Water attempted to make Liberal Democrat MP Charlie Maynard cover its legal costs, up to £1,400 an hour, after he argued for public interest and sought government control of the company. The UK Supreme Court rejected the claim. Thames Water, already facing £17 bn of debt and lender pressure, said it wanted to deter future appeals. Maynard said the move was retaliation.
