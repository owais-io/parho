Ruben Amorim, United’s manager, says he still doesn’t know if Amad Diallo, Bryan Mbeumo and Noussair Mazraoui will play in the home game against Bournemouth because they have been called up for the Africa Cup of Nations. The match is on Monday, the FIFA day clubs must release players. Amorim is waiting on national‑team decisions and is preparing backup plans. Other United injuries also affect the squad.
