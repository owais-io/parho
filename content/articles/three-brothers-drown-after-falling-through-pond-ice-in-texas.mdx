A private pond near Bonham, Texas, turned deadly when three brothers—aged six, eight and nine—fell through its thin ice. The youngest tried to ice‑skate, slipped, and the other brothers dove in to help. The mother, Cheyenne Hangaman, raced in but the ice broke, and the boys could not be saved. The incident highlights the dangers of unsupervised ice play. Local authorities are investigating the cause.
