A guest at Queensland’s Paradise Resort was rushed to hospital after drinking a bottle of hand sanitizer that was found to contain dangerous levels of methanol. The product, supplied by the state’s largest privately owned hotel group, was recalled after the poisoning incident. No other hospital cases have been reported yet. Residents are told to stop using the sanitizer and discard it safely.
