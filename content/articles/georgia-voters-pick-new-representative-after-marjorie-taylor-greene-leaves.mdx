Georgia voters are choosing a new congressman for the district that once had Marjorie Taylor Greene. A crowded primary will send the top two candidates to a runoff on April 7. The main contenders are Republican Clay Fuller, who has Donald Trump’s endorsement, and Democrat Shawn Harris, a retired army general who raised more money. The race shows how Trump still influences the state’s conservative voters.
