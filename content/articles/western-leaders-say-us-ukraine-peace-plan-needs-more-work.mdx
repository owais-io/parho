Western leaders at the G20 meeting in South Africa said the US’s plan to end the war in Ukraine is a good start but still needs more work. The plan, which was leaked this week, accepts some of Russia’s demands, like giving up parts of eastern Ukraine and limiting its army. European leaders want Ukraine’s borders to stay unchanged and said they will talk more in Switzerland. France’s President Macron also warned that the G20 must find common ground.
