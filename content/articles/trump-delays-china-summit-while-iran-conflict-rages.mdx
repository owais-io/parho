President Trump asked China to postpone his March 31 summit in Beijing because the war with Iran is still ongoing. He and Vice‑President JD Vance appeared together for the first time in weeks, with Trump saying the fight would likely finish soon. Trump urged China and allies to keep the Strait of Hormuz open and send ships, while Vance denied trying to split the administration.
