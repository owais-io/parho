A big fire next to Glasgow Central Station caused part of the old building to collapse, so the high‑level tracks and waiting area will stay shut for the rest of the week. Low‑level services will resume Wednesday. No one was hurt, but many nearby shops lost stock and are looking for help. The government is urged to offer quick support to those businesses.
