Prime Minister Keir Starmer has named Antonia Romeo as the UK’s top civil servant, but former colleagues say she bullied people while working in New York. In 2017, they lodged a formal complaint. An investigation found she had a case to answer, but the Cabinet Office cleared her. The old complaint has resurfaced, and critics call the appointment poor judgment. Romeo says she is proud and ready to lead the civil service.
