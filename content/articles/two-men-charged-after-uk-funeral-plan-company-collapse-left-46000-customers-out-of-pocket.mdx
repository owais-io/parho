The Serious Fraud Office has charged Richard Wells and Neil Debenham for running a pre‑paid funeral plan company that shut down in 2022. Safe Hands let people save money for their own funerals, but when it failed it could not pay customers. About 46,000 people lost their saved money and now have to figure out how to pay for their own funerals. The men will appear in court on Feb. 5.
