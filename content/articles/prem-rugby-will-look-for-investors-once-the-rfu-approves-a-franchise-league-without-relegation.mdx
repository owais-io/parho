Prem Rugby is ready to raise money if the Rugby Football Union approves its plan to make the top division a franchise league with no relegation. The club sold a 27% stake to CVC for £200 m and now seeks more cash after three clubs went bankrupt. Raine Group and Deloitte are reviewing finances, and the league wants to centralise commercial operations and may expand to 12 teams.
