House Democrats released new photos and videos from Jeffrey Epstein’s Caribbean island. The footage shows rooms like a bedroom, an office, and a dentist’s chair, plus a chalkboard with words like “deception” and “power.” Taken in 2020 by U.S. Virgin Islands authorities, the images are part of a push to keep pressure on the Trump administration before the Justice Department must release more files by December. Congress also asked the U.S. Attorney General for an update.
