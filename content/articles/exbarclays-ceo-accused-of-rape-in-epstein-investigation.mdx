US prosecutors looked at claims that former Barclays boss Jes Staley raped a woman during a massage in a Jeffrey Epstein case. The allegations were in an 86‑page memo that was unsealed last week. Staley has never been charged and says he denied any wrongdoing. No evidence shows prosecutors decided to pursue the case, and the claims remain unproven, but they have not been investigated.
