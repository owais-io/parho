Experts say you don't need fancy bins or endless money to tidy up. Start by getting rid of stuff you don't use, tackling one drawer at a time. Keep a rule: whenever you buy something new, consider ditching an old item. Give every room a clear purpose and put every item in a specific spot that’s labeled. Spend just a few minutes each day keeping things in order, and you'll feel lighter and more organized.
