After a 2-2 draw with Bournemouth, Chelsea manager Enzo Maresca refused to give a press conference, saying he was unwell. The club suspected he was planning to leave, especially after he tried to use interest from other clubs as leverage. His tactics, injury management and poor results have frustrated fans and the board. With only one win in seven games, Chelsea have already decided he will be dismissed.
