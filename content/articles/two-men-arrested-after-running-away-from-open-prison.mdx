Two men were caught after they ran away from an open prison on New Year’s Day. The men, 35‑year‑old Matthew Armstrong and 40‑year‑old Daniel Washbourne, slipped out of HMP Leyhill between 5 and 8 pm on Jan 1. Armstrong, a convicted murderer, was taken near a train station before noon, while Washbourne was found in Bristol with injuries around 1.30 am. Police also suspect Armstrong of a robbery that night. An investigation will start soon.
