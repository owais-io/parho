Iran’s top clerical council has decided on a new supreme leader, but the name hasn't been made public yet. The decision comes after the previous leader, Ali Khamenei, was killed in a joint U.S.–Israeli strike. Israeli forces say they will target anyone who takes on Khamenei’s role. Early reports suggest the late leader’s son, Mojtaba, may be in the running, but no final choice has been confirmed.
