Senator Amy Klobuchar said she will run for governor of Minnesota after the current governor, Tim Walz, decided not to run again. Klobuchar’s video said the state needs leaders who will fight against ICE and stop fraud in federal programs. Walz left after clashes with the Trump administration over federal agents in Minneapolis. The race will focus on crime, immigration and misuse of public money. Her campaign will also target corruption and protect Minnesota values.
