Hospitals in England are seeing a sharp rise in violence against NHS workers, with around 285 attacks reported each day in 2024-25. Nurses and doctors are being threatened, assaulted, and even subjected to racist acts. This violence hurts staff morale, slows care, and may keep new recruits away. The article urges health ministers to give clear rules and support to protect frontline staff and keep hospitals safe.
