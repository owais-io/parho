Iran’s airspace, shut for a few hours because of protests, is open again. The government said 26‑year‑old protester Erfan Soltani has not been sentenced to death and will face prison if convicted. Thousands are still marching in Tehran, and the U.S. and other countries are watching closely. Social media is flooded with fake videos of the protests, adding confusion.
