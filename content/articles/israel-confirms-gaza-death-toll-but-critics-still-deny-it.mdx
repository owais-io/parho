Israel’s military finally said it accepts the Gaza health ministry’s death toll—over 70,000 people since Oct. 7. For two years, Israeli allies and US officials kept dismissing the figures as inflated, even as independent checks confirmed their accuracy. New data shows most of the dead were civilians, yet critics shift focus to the civilian‑to‑militant ratio, keeping the denial alive.
