Canadian billionaire Stephen Smith has bought a 26.9% stake in the Economist Group, the company that publishes the weekly magazine. The deal is the third big change in the paper’s 183‑year ownership record. Smith’s family company owns financial businesses and a proxy‑advisory group. The purchase won’t change the paper’s editorial independence, and the deal still needs approval from trustees and the board. The Economist made £48m profit last year.
