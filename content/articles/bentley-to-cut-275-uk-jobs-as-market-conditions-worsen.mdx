Bentley is slashing 275 positions in the UK, about 6% of its 4,600 staff, because sales are weak and profits fell 42% last year. The luxury carmaker is also delaying its electric‑vehicle launch and will keep selling fossil‑fuel cars until 2035. The cuts are part of a wider effort to stay competitive amid US tariffs, weak Chinese demand and shifting parent‑company strategies.
