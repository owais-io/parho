Donald Glover, known as Childish Gambino, said he had a stroke during his 2024 world tour. After a painful night in Louisiana, he was hospitalized in Houston and told he’d had a stroke, needing two heart surgeries. He cancelled the rest of the tour, including shows in the US, UK, Europe and Australia. Glover revealed the news at a Los Angeles festival and mentioned he’d broken a foot earlier. He’s now retiring the Childish Gambino name.
