President Trump’s choice to back Israel in an attack on Iran has shocked the world and split conservative media. Big outlets owned by Rupert Murdoch cheer the strike as necessary, while many right‑wing pundits—especially younger conservatives—call it a betrayal of the anti‑war, “America‑first” message. Some criticize the war’s cost and risk of sparking a civil conflict, while fringe voices mix anti‑semite rhetoric.
