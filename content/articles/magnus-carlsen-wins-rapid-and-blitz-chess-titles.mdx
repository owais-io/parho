In Doha, Norway's Magnus Carlsen took home both the world rapid and blitz championships, earning €70,000 for each title. Despite a few blunders—losing a rook in one rapid game and spilling pieces in blitz—he finished strong with 10.5/13 in rapid and a 2.5‑1.5 win over Uzbekistan's Abdusattorov in blitz. Carlsen said he’ll focus on a handful of classical events in 2026 and praised his end‑game skill.
