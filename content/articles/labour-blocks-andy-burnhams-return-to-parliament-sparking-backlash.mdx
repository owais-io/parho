The Labour Party has barred former Manchester MP Andy Burnham from standing again, a move critics say favors internal rivals over a popular figure. The decision has sparked anger from party members, especially in Greater Manchester, who fear a mass exodus. Some letters suggest other candidates, while a few note unrelated issues like football and climate. The controversy highlights divisions within Labour's leadership and the broader debate over party direction.
