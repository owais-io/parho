Ofwat has slapped Welsh Water (Dŵr Cymru) with a £44.7 million fine after finding serious breaches in how the company runs and maintains its wastewater network. The regulator said the firm caused excessive sewage spills, had poor oversight and lacked proper upgrade plans. Welsh Water apologises, pledges a major transformation program and tighter monitoring, while bills for customers are already set to rise further.
