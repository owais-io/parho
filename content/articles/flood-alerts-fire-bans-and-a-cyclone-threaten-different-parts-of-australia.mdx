Australia’s north is flooding from heavy rain, with flash floods and high rivers threatening communities and cattle. Emergency crews helped tourists and residents. In South Australia, hot weather has triggered fire bans and several fires. A weak cyclone, Hayley, is heading for Western Australia, expected to bring heavy rain, strong winds and a storm surge along the coast. The rest of the country sees mild temperatures.
