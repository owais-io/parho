The UK's anti‑slavery watchdog, led by Eleanor Lyons, released a report saying sites that list sexual services can help exploit women. The study looked at 12 websites, interviewed 12 survivors and found that most ads show signs of trafficking, like shared phone numbers, drug references, or “new to the area” tags. The watchdog asks for stronger laws, better safety checks and more help for survivors.
