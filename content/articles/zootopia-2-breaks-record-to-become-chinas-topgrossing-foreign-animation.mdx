Zootopia 2, Disney’s new animal‑cop adventure, just broke every record for a foreign animated movie in China. In its first week it earned about 2 billion yuan (≈£213 m), beating even Avengers: Endgame on a single day. The film is doing better in China than in the U.S. Even though China usually prefers local movies, the sequel’s feel‑good story and long‑running promotion have won over Chinese audiences. It shows that a good story can still beat local competition.
