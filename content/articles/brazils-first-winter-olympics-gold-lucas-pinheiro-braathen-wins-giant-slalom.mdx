Lucas Pinheiro Braathen, a 25‑year‑old skier born in Norway but raised in Brazil, won Brazil’s first gold medal at the Winter Olympics by beating Swiss champion Marco Odermatt in the giant slalom in Bormio. The win made headlines across Brazil, with President Lula calling it a sign that Brazilian sport has no limits. Pinheiro Braathen, who once wanted to be a footballer, now carries the country’s flag on the snowy slopes.
