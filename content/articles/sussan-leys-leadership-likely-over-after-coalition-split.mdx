After a second split in just eight months, the Liberal Party is looking to replace Sussan Ley. The break came when she fired three Nationals senators who opposed the new hate‑speech laws, sparking a walk‑out of the frontbench. Senior MPs say Ley’s job is finished and a spill could happen soon. Candidates like Angus Taylor and Andrew Hastie are front‑and‑center, while the party struggles to keep unity.
