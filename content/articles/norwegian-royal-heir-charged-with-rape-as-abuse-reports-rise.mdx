In Norway, Marius Borg Høiby, the 28‑year‑old son of the crown princess, is set to face a February trial on 32 charges, including rape and illegal filming of women. After the case broke, women’s health centers saw more people seeking help for abuse, saying the trial lowered the barrier to report violence. The scandal has also sparked debate about the royal family’s popularity and support in an otherwise calm country.
