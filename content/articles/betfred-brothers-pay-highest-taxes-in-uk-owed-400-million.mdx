Betfred brothers Fred and Peter Done are the biggest taxpayers in the UK, paying a total of £400 million in tax – half of that comes from gambling duty. The list also names other heavy contributors such as the Wetherspoon pub chain and popular singers like Harry Styles. It highlights that many wealthy residents are moving abroad to lower‑tax places, while the top 1% of earners still give about a quarter of all UK income tax.
