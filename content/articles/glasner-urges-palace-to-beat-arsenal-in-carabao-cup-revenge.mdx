Oliver Glasner is pushing Crystal Palace to win the Carabao Cup quarter‑final against Arsenal, even as the team is juggling four competitions. After a heavy loss to Leeds, he plans to use his best squad, though many players are tired from European games. Arsenal’s manager, Mikel Arteta, also faces a packed schedule, but both sides want to secure a win. The match gives Palace a chance to avenge their earlier defeat to Arsenal and keep their cup hopes alive.
