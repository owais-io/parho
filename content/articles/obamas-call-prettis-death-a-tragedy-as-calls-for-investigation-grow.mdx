The 37‑year‑old nurse Alex Pretti was shot to death by federal immigration officers in Minneapolis. Video shows officers removed his handgun, which he wasn't holding, before shooting him. President and First Lady Obama called the death a heartbreaking tragedy and urged a full investigation. Trump officials deny wrongdoing and the administration is under pressure to explain the incident. A federal judge will hear arguments about stopping the controversial immigration crackdown in Minnesota.
