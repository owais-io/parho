Coco Gauff won her opening match at the Australian Open, beating Uzbekistan’s Kamilla Rakhimova 6‑2, 6‑3 on Rod Laver Arena. She struggled with a weak serve, making a few double‑faults, but used her strong defense and smart shot‑selection to dominate the baseline. Gauff said she has learned to keep pressure off herself in early rounds. She’s also been working with a new coach to fix her serve and will next face Olga Danilovic.
