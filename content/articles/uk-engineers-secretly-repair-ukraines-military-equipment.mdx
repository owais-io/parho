British engineers are working in a hidden Ukrainian workshop to fix donated weapons, including British howitzers, Soviet tanks, and Swedish artillery. Contractors BAE Systems and AMS team up with Ukrainian technicians—many of whom previously worked at destroyed factories—to repair up to 30 vehicles. This effort is part of the “coalition of the willing” to rebuild Ukraine’s army without sending UK troops, showing how Western help might look after a peace deal.
