Pep Lijnders, who spent almost ten years with Jürgen Klopp at Liverpool, has joined Pep Guardiola’s coaching team at Manchester City as an assistant. Klopp jokingly threatened to take the job himself if Lijnders declined, and the former Liverpool coach said the trust and vision Guardiola offered made the switch easier. Lijnders is proud to join one of the world's biggest clubs and looks forward to competing against his old team again.
