Robin Ince, the comedian who co‑hosted the BBC Radio 4 science podcast The Infinite Monkey Cage for 16 years, quit after BBC bosses said his public opinions were “problematic.” He said his support for the trans community, criticism of Donald Trump, and other views conflicted with the BBC’s impartiality rules, so he chose to leave instead of staying silent. The BBC thanked him, while his exit follows broader criticism of the broadcaster’s handling of political and social topics.
