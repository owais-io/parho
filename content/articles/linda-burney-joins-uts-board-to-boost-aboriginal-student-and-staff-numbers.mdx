Linda Burney, a former federal MP and Indigenous minister, has joined the University of Technology Sydney’s board to help increase Aboriginal students and staff. She brings decades of experience in education and Indigenous policy. UTS faces a restructuring backlash and low Indigenous representation – only 2.2% staff and 1.1% students. Burney aims to raise equity, expand the curriculum, and bring fresh governance to support the university’s future.
