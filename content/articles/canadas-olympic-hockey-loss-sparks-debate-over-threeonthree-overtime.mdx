Canada lost both the men’s and women’s hockey finals to the United States in Olympic three‑on‑three golden‑goal overtime. Many say the format is rushed, TV‑driven, and unfair, turning a hard‑fought match into a quick shoot‑out. Critics argue the rule changes the game too much and suggest longer full‑skate periods instead of a single goal in a small‑player format.
