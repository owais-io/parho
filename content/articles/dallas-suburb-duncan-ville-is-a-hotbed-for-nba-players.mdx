From the suburbs of Dallas to the NBA, Duncan Ville has become a key source of future stars. Its high school, a champ, has sent six pros in five years and the area’s gym, Duncanville Fieldhouse, trains countless prospects. Nearby schools like Faith Family Academy keep adding top‑ranked players. The city’s tight network—public schools, charter programs, AAU teams—lets talent stay local, grow, and move straight to the pros. Dallas is now a top route to the NBA.
