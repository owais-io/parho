The Somerset Levels are facing heavy rain from storms like Chandra, forcing pumps to work hard to keep water from flooding homes. Residents such as Julian Taylor are moving belongings upstairs, fearing evacuation. Local builders are building embankments to protect properties. The Environment Agency and government are criticized for slow responses, while the Flooding on the Levels Action Group calls for quicker pump deployment and upgraded facilities. Climate change is driving more intense rainfall, and many residents remain worried.
