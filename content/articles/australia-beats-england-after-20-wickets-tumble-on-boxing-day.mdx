At the Melbourne Cricket Ground, a record crowd saw 20 wickets tumble on Boxing Day. England collapsed, losing 10 wickets on the first day and only scoring 110. Australia’s bowlers, especially Michael Neser and Josh Tongue, took a lot of wickets, while the Aussies’ batsmen built a steady lead. The win gives Australia a comfortable advantage in the Ashes series.
