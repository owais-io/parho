At the Beijing Olympics, France’s Laurence Fournier Beaudry and Guillaume Cizeron won ice‑dance gold, beating U.S. champions Madison Chock and Evan Bates who took silver. The French pair’s victory is shadowed by controversies: Fournier Beaudry’s boyfriend was accused of sexual assault, and Cizeron has been accused of abusive behavior by a former teammate. A Netflix documentary and a recent memoir have brought the scandals to light, sparking debate in the skating world.
