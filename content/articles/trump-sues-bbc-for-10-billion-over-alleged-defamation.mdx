Donald Trump has sued the BBC for $10 billion after a Panorama documentary supposedly misquoted him. The BBC says the edit was a mistake, not defamation, and is preparing to defend itself. At the same time, the UK government is reviewing the broadcaster’s funding model as licence‑fee numbers decline. The lawsuit could hurt the BBC’s reputation and spark questions about media freedom and UK‑US relations.
