Jonas Vingegaard, the two‑time Tour de France winner, fell during a training ride near Málaga after an amateur cyclist tried to stay close. He was hurt but not seriously, and the team urged fans to keep a safe distance. The incident highlighted how many riders get close to fans on the road. Vingegaard should recover in time for the UAE Tour and will debut at the Giro d’Italia.
