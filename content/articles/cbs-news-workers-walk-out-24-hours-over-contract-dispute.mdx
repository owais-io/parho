Workers at CBS News 24/7 walked out for a full day after their contract expired and management refused to agree on a new deal. About 60 employees, represented by the Writers Guild of America East, staged rallies in New York and San Francisco. They say the proposed terms are worse than the old contract, demanding fair wages and job protections. CBS says negotiations continue in good faith.
