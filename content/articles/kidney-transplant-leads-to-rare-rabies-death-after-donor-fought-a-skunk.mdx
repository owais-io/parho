An 80‑year‑old man in Michigan died of rabies after receiving a kidney from another man who had been scratched by a skunk while defending a kitten. The donor never showed symptoms, but the virus was later found in his kidney. The CDC said it was a very rare case of transplant‑transmitted rabies. Other patients who received parts from the same donor were given shots and stayed healthy.
