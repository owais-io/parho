The Metropolitan Police have arrested two men, aged 45 and 47, over an arson attack that set fire to four ambulances run by a Jewish charity in Golders Green. The attack, treated as an antisemitic hate crime, happened near a synagogue and left the vehicles burning. Police say at least three people were involved and are still searching for others. They urge anyone with info to come forward.
