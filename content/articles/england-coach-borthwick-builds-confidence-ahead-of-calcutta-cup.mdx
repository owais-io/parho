England’s rugby coach Steve Borthwick is feeling optimistic about the upcoming Calcutta Cup match in Scotland. After a string of recent wins, he says the team now sees away games as chances, not fears. He highlights strong performances, low penalty counts at home, and the need for quick, aggressive play to cut off Scotland’s attack. Borthwick urges his players to stay intense, move the ball, and keep pressure high.
