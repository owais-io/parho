City won 5-4 at St James’ Park, but star striker Erling Haaland had to exit at halftime with a minor injury. The win keeps them within three points of Arsenal in the Premier League title race. Pep Guardiola praised the team’s performance but didn’t want to celebrate too early, saying they need to stay consistent in the remaining games. Despite the loss, City’s goal difference stays close to Arsenal’s.
