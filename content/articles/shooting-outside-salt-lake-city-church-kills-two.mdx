On Wednesday, a gunman opened fire in the parking lot of a Mormon church in Salt Lake City, killing two adults and injuring several others. The shooting happened while a funeral was taking place inside. Police say it was not a targeted attack on the religion. No suspect has been captured, and investigators are still searching for the shooter. The incident shocked the local community, with many people rushing to help.
