Enzo Maresca is on the verge of leaving Chelsea after a fallout with the club’s board. Fans booed him after a 2‑2 draw with Bournemouth, and he has made cryptic comments that upset senior staff. With the team winning only once in seven games, the board is ready to discuss a new coach by Thursday. Maresca’s contract runs until 2029, but he may want to leave without a payout. Chelsea may turn to another manager, but it is uncertain.
