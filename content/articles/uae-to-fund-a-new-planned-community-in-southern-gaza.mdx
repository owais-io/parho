The UAE is ready to build the first planned city in Gaza’s southern city of Rafah, a project that could cost billions. It will give residents water, schools, and hospitals, but only after they undergo biometric checks and security vetting. Israeli officials have approved the plans, and the US has a stake in the project. Critics warn it could push Palestinians out of the area and increase surveillance.
