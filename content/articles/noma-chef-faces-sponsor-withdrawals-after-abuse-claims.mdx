After the New York Times revealed that Noma’s head chef René Redzepi had abused staff, major sponsors pulled out of his Los Angeles pop‑up. American Express and Blackbird ended their partnerships and will refund ticket holders. Redzepi apologized, said he had done therapy, and the restaurant says it has improved its workplace. The event sold out, but the support was cancelled amid the abuse allegations.
