Guardiola said City are breathing down Arsenal's neck after beating Liverpool 3‑1 at Anfield. He warned they need to improve to close the six‑point gap. City won against Liverpool, with Silva and Haaland scoring, ending a 17‑year Anfield drought. Guardiola said a few more points won’t be enough; they must win at home and hope Arsenal slip, while also criticizing referee decisions.
