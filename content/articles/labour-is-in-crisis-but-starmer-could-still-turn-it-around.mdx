Labour is struggling, and many say its leader Keir Starmer is losing the party’s left wing and failing to win back voters. Critics say he should be more inclusive, stop pushing the left away, and focus on fighting right‑wing parties like Reform and the Greens. The party also needs to address issues that keep Muslim voters away and consider proportional voting. Starmer is stuck, but a calm, flexible approach could help him rebuild support.
