Dean Hamilton, a 47‑year‑old from Somerset, pleaded guilty at Winchester crown court to raping and assaulting Joanne Young. He admitted one count of rape, one assault by penetration and two sexual assault charges. Young had already suffered years of drug‑rapes at the hands of her husband, former Tory councillor Philip Young, who also pleaded guilty to nearly 50 offences. Four other men face trial for similar crimes, and police are seeking a fifth suspect.
