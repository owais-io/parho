The European Court of Human Rights has asked the UK to explain why it took away Shamima Begum’s citizenship in 2019. Begum, who left home at 15 to join ISIS, was stripped of her British passport on national‑security grounds. The court wonders whether the UK considered her a trafficking victim before revoking her citizenship. Begum is now stateless, and the move has sparked criticism from UK politicians who say she poses a threat.
