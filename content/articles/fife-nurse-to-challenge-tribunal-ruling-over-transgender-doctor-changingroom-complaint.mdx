Sandie Peggie, a 30‑year‑old nurse from Fife, was suspended after she said she didn't want to share a women's changing room with a transgender doctor. An employment tribunal said she had been harassed, but it dismissed her claims of discrimination and victimisation. Peggie will appeal the decision. The tribunal also had to correct some quotes that were wrong, raising doubts about its accuracy.
