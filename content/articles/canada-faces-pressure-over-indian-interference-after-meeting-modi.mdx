After a trade‑focused visit to New Delhi, Canadian finance minister Mark Carney is being asked if he still thinks India poses a threat to Canada. The meeting came amid allegations that India helped kill Sikh activist Hardeep Singh Nijjar and that Indian officials are spying on Canadians. Canada wants a free‑trade deal with India and hopes trade could hit $70 bn by 2030, but officials say security concerns remain.
