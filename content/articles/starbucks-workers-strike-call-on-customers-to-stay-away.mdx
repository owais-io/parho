Hundreds of Starbucks workers and supporters in Brooklyn’s Clinton Hill picketed the café on Red Cup Day, urging customers to avoid the store. Over 1,000 baristas in more than 40 U.S. cities walked off the job to demand better pay, staffing, and benefits after contract talks stalled. The protest drew strong community backing, with many people turning away from the café and booing those who still entered.
