Bruce Lehrmann’s appeal in the defamation case was thrown out by the federal court, which confirmed that he likely raped Brittany Higgins and knew she didn’t consent. The judges found all his arguments unfair and said the media coverage was reasonable. Lehrmann now owes more money and his lawyers were paid pro‑bono. He plans to fight further, but the High Court is still far away.
