Police in Blackpool arrested a 16‑year‑old boy after they discovered items that could be explosives in a house on Gloucester Avenue. The discovery forced several nearby homes to be evacuated as a safety precaution. Bomb‑disposal experts and fire and ambulance teams helped inspect the materials. Officers warned residents to stay away from the area and said the emergency response could last into New Year’s Eve. The police are working to clear the scene as quickly as possible.
