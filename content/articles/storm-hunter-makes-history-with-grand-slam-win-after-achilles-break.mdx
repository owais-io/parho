Australian tennis star Storm Hunter beat Spain’s Jessica Bouzas Maneiro in her first round match at the Australian Open, proving no one has ever come back from an Achilles tear in tennis. After 18 months of recovery, she used data, expert advice and a step‑by‑step plan to return to top‑level play. Hunter’s win shows that even a career‑threatening injury can be overcome with hard work and determination.
