Trump mocked Biden in Pennsylvania, calling him sleepy, while Trump himself has been seen dozing at meetings. Both men, now 79, face health worries and criticism about the economy. Trump promised a strong economy but voters are concerned about rising prices. Critics see the two presidents as similar, both using old‑age jokes and dealing with a cost‑of‑living crisis. The debate shows that age and economic challenges are common to both, and the public questions whether they can still lead.
