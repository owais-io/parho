In a thrilling African Nations Cup match, Egypt won 3‑2 against Ivory Coast, sparking excitement after years of middling performances. The win felt like a nod to Egypt’s golden era when they won three straight titles. Key moments included Salah’s sharp goal and a clever counter‑attack that stunned the Ivorian side. Coach Hossam Hassan’s tactical shift to a 4‑3‑1‑2 surprised opponents and gave the team fresh energy. The victory sets up a semi‑final against Senegal, adding another chapter to Egypt’s football story.
