Elon Musk asked his followers on X if he should buy Ryanair, the biggest low‑cost airline in Europe, after the boss, Michael O’Leary, rejected his offer to put Starlink internet on the planes. Musk and O’Leary traded insults, with Musk calling the CEO “idiot” and the airline mocking him. Most people in Musk’s poll said yes, but the stock market didn’t cheer, and Ryanair shares fell about 1%.
