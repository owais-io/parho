Kyle Sandilands says he was fired from his $100 million Kiis FM contract unfairly. He argues the show was meant to be loud and provocative, so the on‑air fight with co‑host Jackie “O” Henderson shouldn’t count as serious misconduct. ARN Media fired him for alleged breach, but Sandilands wants the court to cancel the termination, keep the money he would earn until 2034, and award damages. ARN says it will defend the case.
