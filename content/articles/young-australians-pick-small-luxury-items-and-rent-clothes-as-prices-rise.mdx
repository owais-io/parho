Young Australians on a tight budget are picking out small luxury items, like mini wine bottles and rental clothes, to satisfy their wants without overspending. They trade cheaper basics for premium products and experiences, and many prefer second‑hand or subscription wardrobes. Stores are meeting this trend with 187ml wine and 250ml beer cans. While some use credit to keep up, experts warn of rising debt and that these little luxuries might feel like an escape from high living costs.
