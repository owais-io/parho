Bondi Beach was hit by a shooting during a Jewish festival, killing at least 16 people. A beachgoer named Ahmed al‑Ahmed stopped one attacker by taking the gun away, saving many lives. Newspapers worldwide highlighted this heroism with headlines like “Bondi hero” and “Horror and heroism.” Some papers added political comments, and U.S. religious leaders discussed how to protect their communities. The event shocked Australia and raised questions about safety.
