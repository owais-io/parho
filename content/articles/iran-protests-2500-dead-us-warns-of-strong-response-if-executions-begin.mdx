More than 2,500 people, mostly protesters, have been killed in Iran’s protests. The U.S. Human Rights group counted 2,571 deaths, including 12 children and 147 security staff. Iran’s internet has been shut down, but some phone calls and Starlink satellite internet are working. U.S. President Trump warned Iran that he would take strong action if the regime carries out executions. International leaders call for calm and condemn the violence.
