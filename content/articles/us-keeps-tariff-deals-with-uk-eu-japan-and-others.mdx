After a Supreme Court ruling that said many of President Trump’s tariffs were illegal, he announced a new 15% tariff on all imports. Trade rep Jamieson Greer said the U.S. would still honor deals it had already made with the UK, EU, Japan, Switzerland and about 20 other countries. Britain and the EU want the agreements kept and worry a new tariff could raise rates on goods like wine and cars. Business leaders hope the U.S. will keep the deals.
