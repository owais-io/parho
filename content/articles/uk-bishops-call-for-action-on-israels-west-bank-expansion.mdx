Three leading Church of England bishops wrote to the Guardian saying the UK is letting Israel extend its control over the West Bank. They note the government has not replied to the International Court of Justice’s ruling that Israel’s occupation is illegal. After visiting Palestinian Christian villages, the bishops heard residents say they can’t stay or have no choice but to leave. They urge the UK and the world to protect Palestinians and stop supporting Israel’s illegal actions.
