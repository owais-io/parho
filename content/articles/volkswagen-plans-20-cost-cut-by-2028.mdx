Volkswagen is planning a big cost‑cutting drive to trim its expenses by 20 % by 2028. The plan could mean closing plants and reducing the workforce, and it is part of a wider reshuffle of the company’s brands and factories. The move is aimed at keeping profits stable amid rising competition from Chinese cars and higher production costs. The company has already saved a few billion euros, but details on the new cuts are still unclear.
