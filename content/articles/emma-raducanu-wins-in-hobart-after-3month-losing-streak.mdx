Emma Raducanu finally won a match at the Hobart International, beating Camila Osorio 6‑3, 7‑6. It’s her first victory since September, after a foot injury kept her from playing most of the season. The match was paused by rain, but Raducanu returned stronger, winning the final set tie‑break. She says the win feels special and will help her regain confidence before facing Magdalena Frech next.
