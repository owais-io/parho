Police found forensic clues in a burnt Toyota Corolla that linked it to 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian and a derelict property in Dural, 11 days after he was kidnapped. The car, believed to have been stolen from Victoria, was seen in Glenorie the day after the abduction. Authorities are combing bushland around Westmead and Dural, urging anyone with footage to help locate Baghsarian, who has not been found.
