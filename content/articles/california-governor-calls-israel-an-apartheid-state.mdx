California governor Gavin Newsom said Israel is an apartheid state during a book promotion in Los Angeles. He criticized Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and the U.S. war with Iran. Newsom highlighted public opinion shifts, with many Americans now supporting Palestinians over Israelis, especially among Democrats. He called the U.S. partnership with Israel a mistake, adding that Netanyahu’s hard‑line actions hurt U.S. interests. His remarks come as midterm elections loom.
