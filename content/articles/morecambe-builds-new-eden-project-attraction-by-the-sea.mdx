Morecambe in Lancashire is starting construction on a new Eden Project attraction that will sit by the sea. The plan includes two domes – a plant house and a digital theatre – and a free community garden moved from the Chelsea Flower Show. The project aims to bring jobs, tourism and a net‑zero eco‑boost to a town struggling with high poverty. Young locals helped design the garden and hope the attraction will spark real change.
