BTS’s biggest tour ever is set to visit 34 cities worldwide, and economists say it could bring tens of billions into the U.S. economy. Fans—known as Army—are ready to travel, spend on tickets, hotels and local food, just like Taylor Swift’s fans. Small businesses and local tourism will see a surge as concert‑goers become tourists, boosting hotels, restaurants and even Korean‑style pop‑up shops.
