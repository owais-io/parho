A Reform UK council leader, Ian Cooper, is being investigated after posts that many say are racist and hateful. He allegedly called London mayor Sadiq Khan a “narcissistic Pakistani”, told a black lawyer to “F'd off back to Nigeria”, and warned that migrants were trying to “colonise the UK”. Reform UK has suspended two other MPs for offensive messages, and the party says it is conducting an urgent internal review.
