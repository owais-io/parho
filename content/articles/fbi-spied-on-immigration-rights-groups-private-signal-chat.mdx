The FBI secretly accessed a private Signal chat used by immigration‑rights activists who monitor U.S. immigration courts in New York City. The group’s messages were described as extremist by the FBI, even though the activists claim they are simply watching court hearings and filming officials for accountability. How the agency got into the encrypted chat is unclear, and many say the surveillance violates basic civil‑rights and free‑speech protections.
