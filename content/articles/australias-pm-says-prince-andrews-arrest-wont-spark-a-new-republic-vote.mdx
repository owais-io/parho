Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said Prince Andrew (Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor) has fallen from grace after his arrest over alleged misconduct. He added that the case won’t trigger another Australian republic referendum, as he already ruled out a vote in his first term. Albanese praised the monarchy but says Australia still needs a head of state. He said no Australian inquiry is underway, and the cost of living remains a priority.
