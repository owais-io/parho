Channel 5 says its new drama about former BBC presenter Huw Edwards is needed to raise awareness of grooming and online safety. Starring Martin Clunes, the film follows allegations that Edwards paid a 17‑year‑old for explicit images and sent child‑pornographic pictures to another man. The alleged victim, who works with the producers, speaks at the end to encourage others who feel silenced. Channel 5 says the timing is right to help people understand grooming.
