The article is written by Iranian activist Nasrin Parvaz, a survivor of imprisonment and torture. She laments the U.S. and Israeli bombs striking Iran and how some Iranians, including monarchist supporters of the former Shah, cheer the attack. Parvaz says the regime uses the war to silence protests and that the world should not stay silent. She calls on Western nations to stop the bombing and support Iranian people’s right to free speech.
