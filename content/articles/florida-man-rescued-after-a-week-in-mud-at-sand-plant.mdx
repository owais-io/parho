A Florida man named Andrew Giddens, missing since Valentine’s Day, was found after more than a week stuck in shoulder‑deep mud near a Vulcan Materials sand plant. Deputies spotted his abandoned car, learned he’d been missing for days without food or water, and sent a rescue team. It took about three hours to free him with ladders, ropes and backboards, and he was air‑lifted to a hospital in critical condition. Authorities said no charges would be filed because of his mental health.
