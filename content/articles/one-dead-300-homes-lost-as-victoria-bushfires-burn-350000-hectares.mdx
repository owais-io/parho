Victoria’s worst bushfire heatwave has killed one person, destroyed over 300 homes and buildings, and scorched 350,000 hectares of bushland. Firefighters are still battling 32 active blazes while residents are urged to leave or shelter. The prime minister announced $19.5 million in recovery funds. Air quality is poor, and tap water in some towns is unsafe. The fire is part of a growing trend of extreme weather from climate change.
