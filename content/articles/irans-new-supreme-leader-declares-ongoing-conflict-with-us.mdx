After the death of former Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, his son Mojtaba was announced as the new top commander. In a TV‑read message, he said Iran would keep the Strait of Hormuz shut and keep striking U.S. bases in the region. He demanded U.S. pay for attacks and warned of retaliation if refused. The speech also praised Iranian allies and called on Gulf neighbors to cut ties with Washington.
