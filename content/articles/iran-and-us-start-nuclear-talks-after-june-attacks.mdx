After last June's Israeli‑led strikes on Iranian sites, Washington and Tehran held indirect talks in Muscat, Oman. The meeting focused only on Iran’s nuclear program, with both sides saying they will not threaten each other. Iran wants guarantees that the talks will not be used for regime change, while the US wants wider security issues addressed. Both sides agree to keep the door open for further talks, but deep mistrust remains.
