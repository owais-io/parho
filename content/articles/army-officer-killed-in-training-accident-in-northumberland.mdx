A 25‑year‑old British army officer, Captain Philip Gilbert Muldowney, died during a live‑fire training exercise at Otterburn Training Area in Northumberland. The incident is under investigation. Muldowney, a Royal Artillery fire‑support commander, joined the army in 2020 and had served in Estonia, completed a commando course, and was praised for his enthusiasm and leadership. Fellow soldiers and commanders paid tribute, calling him a rising star and a joy to work with.
