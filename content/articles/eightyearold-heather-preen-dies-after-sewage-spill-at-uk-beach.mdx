In 1999, eight‑year‑old Heather Preen died after drinking raw sewage at a UK beach. Her mother, Julie Maughan, now leads a Channel 4 drama called “Dirty Business” that shows how private water companies routinely dump untreated sewage into rivers and seas. The film highlights the 3.6 million hours of dumping in 2024 and urges stronger treatment and public ownership to protect people’s health.
