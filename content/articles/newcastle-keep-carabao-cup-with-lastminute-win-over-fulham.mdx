Newcastle United stayed on top of the Carabao Cup after a 1‑0 win at St James’ Park. The game started with a quick goal from Yoane Wissa, but Sasa Lukic levelled for Fulham shortly after. In the dying minutes, 19‑year‑old Lewis Miley popped up from the bench to score the decisive goal, giving the Magpies their first trophy in 56 years. Manager Eddie Howe praised his squad’s resilience and looked forward to the semi‑finals.
