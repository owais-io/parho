Olympic medals in the 2026 Winter Games are breaking during celebrations. Athletes like Breezy Johnson, Ebba Andersson and Justus Strelow have seen their medals crack or fall off after winning. Organisers say the problem may be the medals’ break‑away cord, which is supposed to release if pulled too hard. A formal investigation is starting to find the cause and to stop further damage.
