Louisiana pastor Milton Otto Martin III was sentenced to 7 years in prison for molesting a teenage girl. He was found guilty of indecent behavior with a minor, not the earlier statutory rape charge. The survivor testified that the abuse still hurts her. The judge chose the maximum penalty and will require Martin to register as a sex offender. The case follows a wider church‑abuse scandal.
