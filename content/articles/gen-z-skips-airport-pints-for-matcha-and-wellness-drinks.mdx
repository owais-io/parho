The new trend? Gen Z travellers are ditching pre‑flight pints for matcha, gut‑health shots and smoothies at UK airports. TikTok influencers are driving a 165 % jump in matcha sales, while super‑smoothies jumped 650 %. Even face‑mask sales on planes up 399 %. Younger passengers also lean on AI tools like ChatGPT to plan trips, and they favour city breaks over party destinations. Meanwhile, the usual party spots like the Canary Islands are losing steam.
