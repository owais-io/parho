A gunman opened fire at Brown University on Saturday, killing two students and seriously wounding nine others. Police say the suspect, a male in his 30s wearing black and possibly a camouflage mask, is still on the loose. Officers have searched the campus and nearby homes, but the search is hampered by holiday crowds. Local and federal agencies are working together to find the shooter and keep the community safe.
