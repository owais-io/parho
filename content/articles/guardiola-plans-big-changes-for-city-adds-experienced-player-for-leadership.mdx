Guardiola is revamping Manchester City’s squad for a Carabao Cup clash with Brentford, making 10 lineup changes and adding an experienced player to give on‑field leadership. He’ll start young keeper James Trafford, while many others miss out because of injuries or international duties. Guardiola says the focus is still on the upcoming West Ham game, but the new set‑up aims to avoid the earlier defeat against Bayer Leverkusen.
