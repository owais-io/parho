Charlotte Niddam, 15, from Britain, is missing after a deadly fire at a bar in the Swiss ski town of Crans‑Montana. The blaze, sparked by sparklers, killed around 40 people and injured more than 100. She had recently moved back to France with her family and often worked as a babysitter in the resort. Her school and family are pleading for her safe return and spreading the word online.
