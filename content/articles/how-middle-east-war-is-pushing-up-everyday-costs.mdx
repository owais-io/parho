The war in the Middle East is shaking global shipping and oil prices, which is now pushing up fuel, transport and mortgage costs worldwide. This ripple effect is raising the prices of everyday goods and services. Many people notice higher bills, delayed deliveries, and shortages. The Guardian asks readers to share whether they’ve felt these price hikes, delivery problems, or jet‑fuel surcharges on holiday bookings.
