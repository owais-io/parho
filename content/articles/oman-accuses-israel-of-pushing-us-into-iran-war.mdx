Oman’s foreign minister Badr Albusaidi says the US lost control of its foreign policy and blames Israel for pushing Trump’s administration into war with Iran, even after talks in Geneva showed a deal was near. He calls the strikes illegal and a catastrophe, criticising both sides and urging a return to negotiations. Oman is one of few Gulf states openly condemning the US for acting as Israel’s proxy.
