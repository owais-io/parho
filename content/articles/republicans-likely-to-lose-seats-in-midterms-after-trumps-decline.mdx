After Trump’s 2024 win, many voters grew tired of him. Republicans let him run the show and now, polls show Democrats leading by up to 14 points. Voter turnout for Republicans fell sharply, and most people dislike Trump. In the 2025 elections the Democrats won big, taking control of state legislatures and gaining many seats. In the next midterms, Republicans are likely to lose a lot of seats and maybe the Senate.
