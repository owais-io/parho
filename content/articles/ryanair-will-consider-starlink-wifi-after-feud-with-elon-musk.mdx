Ryanair’s finance boss said the airline might try Starlink satellite Wi‑Fi on its planes in the future, even after a public spat with Elon Musk. The airline’s chief executive said adding the antennas would drag fuel and cost more money, so they’re still evaluating options. Ryanair hopes passengers will pay for Wi‑Fi on short‑haul flights, but it’s still far from launching the service.
