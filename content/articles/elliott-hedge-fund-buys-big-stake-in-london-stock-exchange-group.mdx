Elliott Management, a hedge fund that pushes companies to improve, has quietly taken a large stake in the London Stock Exchange Group (LSEG). The fund is working with LSEG to boost earnings, push for a share buy‑back, and close the gap with rivals. LSEG’s stock jumped 6% at the opening but closed down 1%. Investors worry AI could hit its data‑business, contributing to a 35% share decline in the last year. Elliott has also targeted BP and other firms.
