Lee Child, the author of the popular Jack Reacher series, says that UK schools should teach thrillers to make reading more exciting for children. He spoke at a prison in Doncaster, where he runs literacy sessions that help inmates read and write. Child believes better reading habits can lower crime and keep communities safer. He’s working with MPs and the justice minister to spread the idea nationwide.
