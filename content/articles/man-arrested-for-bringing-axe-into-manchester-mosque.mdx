A 55‑year‑old man named Darren Connor was taken into custody after police said he walked into a Manchester mosque with an axe in his bag. He is charged with possessing an offensive weapon and with drugs. The mosque’s chair said the incident happened during Ramadan prayers, and police are stepping up patrols around places of worship. The other man was only charged with a separate offence.
