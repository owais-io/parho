US intelligence says Iran could hit oil tankers with drones or missiles far more seriously than by laying mines. The Trump team struck 16 Iranian mine‑laying ships, but experts warn a single missile or drone could sink a tanker, even with U.S. navy escorts. Iran’s forces have shut down the Strait of Hormuz, raising oil prices and prompting U.S. officials to plan for a return of regular traffic in a few weeks.
