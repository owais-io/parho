A 15‑year‑old from Melbourne, Ezra Sholl, has a severe disability that keeps him mostly at home. He uses Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok and even messages football players to stay connected. The government plans to ban kids under 16 from social media, saying it could harm their health. Ezra says the ban would cut off his only link to friends, support groups and entertainment. He urges the government to regulate instead of remove, so kids who need online access aren’t left isolated.
