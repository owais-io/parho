Labour lost its safe seat in the Gorton and Denton by‑election to the Greens, who won by 4,402 votes ahead of Reform. Deputy leader Lucy Powell says the loss came because many white working‑class voters switched to the Greens, driven by tactical votes to keep Reform out and a protest against Labour’s performance. The party must rethink its strategy and give voters clear reasons to return.
