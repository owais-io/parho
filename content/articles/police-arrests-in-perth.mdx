Police in Perth stopped a party at a house on May 2. Three teenagers were arrested: a 17‑year‑old, a 15‑year‑old, and a 13‑year‑old. The 17‑year‑old was taken to a police station for an assault charge. The 15‑year‑old was charged with a different offence. The 13‑year‑old was kept for a short time and will be released later. One of the teens is also facing an assault charge.
