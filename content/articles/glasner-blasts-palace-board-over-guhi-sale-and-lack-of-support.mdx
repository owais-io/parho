Oliver Glasner, Crystal Palace’s manager, blasted the club’s board after a 2‑1 loss to Sunderland. He says they’ve abandoned his squad, especially after selling captain Marc Guéhi to Manchester City just before a game. Glasner left out substitutions, felt the team had no support, and warned the club could face relegation if changes aren’t made. He’s set to leave at season’s end.
