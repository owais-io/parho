Eleven Australian women stuck in a Syrian detention camp have told reporters that they would allow their children to leave on their own, hoping the kids could get back to Australia. One mother, Zahra Ahmad, pleaded that her children must be saved from the camp’s fear. Some women said they wouldn’t want their children taken away, but the group’s main concern is getting any safe way for the kids home.
