Trump told the nation he would strike Iran after a failed diplomatic round in Geneva, where Iran agreed to limit nuclear work. He urges Iranians to overthrow the regime, hoping they’ll become his ground force. The move risks a wider Middle‑East war, could shut the Strait of Hormuz, and may leave Iran in chaos, not peace, with global oil markets affected. No one knows how the conflict will play out.
