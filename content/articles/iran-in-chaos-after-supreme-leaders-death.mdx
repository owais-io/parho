The death of Iran’s Supreme Leader, Ali Khamenei, has left the country in a dangerous mix of mourning and unrest. Many senior officials have already been killed or targeted, weakening the regime’s security forces. At the same time, the UAE and other Gulf states have been hit by missiles and drones, tightening regional pressure. Protesters want change, but the ruling elite are scrambling to keep order and name a new leader, raising fears of civil breakdown.
