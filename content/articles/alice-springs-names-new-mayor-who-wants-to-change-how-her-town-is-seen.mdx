Asta Hill, a former criminal‑lawyer and human‑rights advocate, became Alice Springs’ first Greens‑backed mayor. She grew up in the town, works in a family home and now has two kids. Hill says the city’s media image—full of crime and panic—doesn’t match reality. She wants to give young people a safer, welcoming space and focus on community safety, rights and real solutions, like fixing childcare shortages.
