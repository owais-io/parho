Bekhal Mahmod, Banaz's sister, has lived under a new identity for 20 years after her sister was killed for trying to leave an arranged marriage. She is campaigning for Banaz’s law, a UK bill that would define honour‑based abuse, help police spot it, and punish offenders. The government is considering the proposals, but Bekhal fears anti‑immigration talk may silence other victims.
