Democratic senators have filed new war‑powers resolutions that would force the U.S. to pull out of its military actions in Iran unless Congress gives permission. They also want public hearings of defense and state officials to explain the war. Republicans blocked similar measures, and polls show most Americans dislike the conflict. Democrats argue the war raises costs for families and needs an open debate.
