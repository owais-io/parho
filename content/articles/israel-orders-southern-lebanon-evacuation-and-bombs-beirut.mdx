Israel told residents within 25 miles of the Lebanese border to move north, calling it a safety step. That same day, it carried out air strikes on central Beirut and southern Lebanon, hitting buildings and homes. The attacks follow a surge of rockets and drones from Hezbollah, which claimed to have worked with Iran. The fighting has already killed over 600 people and displaced nearly a million.
