White House chief of staff Susie Wiles said in a Vanity Fair interview that many people in President Trump’s cabinet are “crazy” and that Trump himself is not good at governing. She also called some cabinet members and advisers untrustworthy. Meanwhile the United States is seeing a slump in jobs, higher unemployment, rising prices and a drop in people’s approval of Trump. Many voters are worried about the economy and feel the administration is failing.
