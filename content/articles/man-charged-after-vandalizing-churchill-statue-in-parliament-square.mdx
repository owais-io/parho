A 38‑year‑old man, Caspar San Giorgio, was arrested and charged with criminal damage after he spray‑painted Winston Churchill’s statue outside Parliament with the words “Zionist war criminal” and other anti‑Israel slogans. The Metropolitan Police took him in early Saturday after the attack. The statue was cleaned and protected, and government officials called the act “abhorrent.” The statue has been vandalised before during protests, but this is the latest incident.
