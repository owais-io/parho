A long‑standing border fight between Thailand and Cambodia has flared up again, killing civilians and soldiers. The dispute centers on an old Hindu temple, Preah Vihear, that both countries claim. Earlier this year the two sides signed a ceasefire backed by Trump, but fighting broke out after a landmine incident and accusations of violations. The clash left 7 civilians dead in Cambodia, 3 soldiers dead in Thailand, and many wounded, showing how fragile the peace remains.
