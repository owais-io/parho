On the second‑to‑last day of the Ashes, England’s 22‑year‑old Jacob Bethell scored a brilliant 142 not out, the team’s first Test century. He built a 302‑for‑8 total, giving England a 119‑run lead over Australia, who were 183 behind in their second innings. Bethell’s steady, spin‑focused innings gave England hope amid a series where Australia already dominated 4‑1. His debut made him a surprising but essential player.
