On a quiet morning, a 75‑year‑old boat owner in Shropshire woke up to a 50‑metre hole that swallowed his canal boat and others. He quickly jumped off and rang the alarm, saving his neighbours. Firefighters and police declared a major incident, helped 10 people and put in barriers to stop water from flooding nearby. No one was hurt, and the canal is being checked for the cause.
