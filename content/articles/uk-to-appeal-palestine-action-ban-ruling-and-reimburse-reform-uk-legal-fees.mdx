UK officials say they will pay Reform UK £100,000 to cover legal costs after the firm challenged local election procedures. At the same time, the government plans to appeal a recent three‑judge decision that ruled the ban on the anti‑Israel group Palestine Action unlawful. The move comes after the election bodies reversed a previous cancellation of local elections. Both actions show the government balancing legal challenges and political pressures.
