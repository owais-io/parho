Tropical Cyclone Narelle is getting stronger as it heads toward Western Australia’s Shark Bay. The storm, already a powerful Category 3, may become Category 4 by Friday. Residents of the small town of Denham, which has no full hospital, are evacuating, and tourists are urged to leave before the cyclone passes. The Bureau of Meteorology warns of heavy rain and strong winds that could damage homes and threaten lives.
