UN Secretary‑General António Guterres will speak in London on the 80th anniversary of the UN to say that powerful forces are trying to weaken worldwide cooperation. He warned about huge arms spending, the climate crisis, fake news online, and authoritarian use of algorithms. Guterres urged more multilateralism and law, but avoided naming specific countries. The talk comes as the US has cut UN funding and left the climate deal, threatening the UN’s aid and peace‑keeping work.
