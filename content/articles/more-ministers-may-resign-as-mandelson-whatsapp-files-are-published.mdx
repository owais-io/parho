Keir Starmer may face more resignations when the next batch of Peter Mandelson WhatsApp messages is released. The files contain informal chats that could hurt ministers, civil servants, and aides. Starmer has already apologised for the appointment mishap and says no cover‑up happened. Conservatives claim the messages show a cover‑up and want an investigation. The release will be reviewed by a security committee and may prompt further resignations.
