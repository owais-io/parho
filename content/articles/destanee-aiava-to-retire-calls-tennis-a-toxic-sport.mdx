Australian tennis player Destanee Aiava said she will stop playing professionally at the end of 2026. She blasted the sport, calling it racist, misogynistic, homophobic and hostile. In an angry Instagram post she compared tennis to a “toxic boyfriend” and said she can’t live with constant criticism. She hopes to find a career that makes her happy and free from the tennis world’s toxic culture.
