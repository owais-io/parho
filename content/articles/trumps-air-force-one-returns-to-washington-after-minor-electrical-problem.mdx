Trump’s Air Force One had to return to Washington after a minor electrical fault was found early in flight. The president switched to a smaller C‑32 jet and headed to Davos for the World Economic Forum, but the stop‑over caused a brief delay. The issue was deemed safe, and the older presidential aircraft, in use for nearly 40 years, continues to get sporadic maintenance challenges.
