A fisherman in Gippsland spotted a pink platypus while fishing. The animal isn’t a rare albino – experts say its light colour is just a natural variation. The sighting drew online curiosity, but scientists explain it’s normal for platypus to have a range of colours. The species is near‑threatened, with numbers dropping until recent conservation efforts have helped populations begin to recover. Observers wondered if it was an albino, but scientists clarified it’s just a rare shade.
