Celtic dismissed French coach Wilfried Nancy after just 33 days in charge, having lost six of his first eight games. The 48‑year‑old was replaced by long‑time manager Martin O’Neill, who will act as interim until season’s end. Nancy’s final loss, 3‑1 at Rangers, sparked fan protests against the board. O’Neill said he wishes Nancy luck and will focus on getting the team back to winning ways. Shaun Maloney and other staff return too.
