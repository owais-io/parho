UK factories may lose their edge because energy costs have jumped. A report from the CBI and Energy UK says 40% of firms are cutting investment, and 90% have seen bills rise in five years. Energy prices are up 70% for electricity and 60% for gas after the Ukraine war. If bills stay high, jobs could be lost and factories moved abroad. The report calls for a full review of energy rules and better support for industry.
