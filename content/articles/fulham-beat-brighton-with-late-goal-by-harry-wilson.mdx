In a dramatic Premier League clash, Fulham edged Brighton 1‑0 thanks to Harry Wilson’s last‑minute free‑kick that slipped past Bart Verbruggen. The goal came after a tense game where both teams missed chances and the goalkeepers struggled. Wilson’s strike capped a rare win for Fulham, while Brighton’s manager blamed errors and said the better team sometimes loses. Fans left the stadium disappointed but hopeful.
