Prime Minister Keir Starmer is travelling to China with a business delegation to improve ties. He faces tricky issues: the jailed Hong Kong journalist Jimmy Lai, the treatment of Uyghurs, and China’s pressure over Taiwan. The UK has also agreed to a large Chinese embassy near the Tower of London, despite espionage concerns. Starmer wants investment in British infrastructure, while keeping a firm stance on human rights and security.
