After the U.S. lost the 2024 Ryder Cup, the PGA of America wants Tiger Woods to be the next captain. Woods hasn’t played since the Open and is also keeping the door open to play the Masters. He’s juggling PGA Tour board duties and a recent back surgery, so he’s still weighing whether he can devote the time to lead the team. Europe’s likely to keep Luke Donald as captain.
