Gregory Bovino, the former US Border Patrol commander, said he’s retiring and that he would have stopped more illegal immigrants if he had the chance. In a New York Times interview, he blamed the Trump administration for not doing enough and didn’t apologize for the fatal shootings of two U.S. citizens in Minneapolis. He also criticized the justice system and praised Trump. The comments have reignited debate about aggressive immigration enforcement and the agency’s tactics.
