Luke Davis, 31, was sentenced to 4½ years in prison after he stopped his HIV medication and failed to tell a woman he was infected. The victim found out she had HIV in 2021 and said it feels like a “life sentence.” A judge said Davis acted selfishly and ignored advice to use condoms and disclose his status. Police found he had many other partners, but no more victims have been named.
