Israeli President Isaac Herzog came to Bondi Beach to show support after the 15‑person massacre. He carried stones from Jerusalem to leave at the site and spoke about antisemitism being a global problem. Herzog also warned Australians about rising hate and defended Israel’s war in Gaza, saying the protests in Sydney were unfair. The visit was meant to comfort families and signal that the world remembers the attack.
