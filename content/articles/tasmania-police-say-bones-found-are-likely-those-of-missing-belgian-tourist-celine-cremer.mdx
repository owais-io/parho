Police in Tasmania say bones found near Philosopher Falls are probably those of missing Belgian backpacker Celine Cremer, who vanished near Cradle Mountain in June 2023. After a 12‑month search by police, state emergency services and volunteers, a privately funded team uncovered her phone and later bones, teeth, a car key and clothes. The findings give families some closure, while a coroner will confirm the identification.
