Judge Charles Breyer decided that the federal government cannot keep the National Guard in Los Angeles. The Trump administration had put about 4,000 troops there to deal with protests, but California’s governor said it was illegal. The court ordered most of the troops back to the state’s control, ending the federal takeover. The decision means the troops won’t stay until the end of February.
