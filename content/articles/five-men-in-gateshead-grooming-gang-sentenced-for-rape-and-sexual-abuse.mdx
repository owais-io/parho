Five men in a grooming gang were convicted of raping and sexually assaulting girls in Saltwell Park, Gateshead. They used alcohol, cocaine, and blackmail to lure victims. After a lengthy court case, they were sentenced to 18 months to 14 years in prison. The victims, aged 13‑16, showed bravery during the investigation. Police started the inquiry after complaints from two girls in 2019.
