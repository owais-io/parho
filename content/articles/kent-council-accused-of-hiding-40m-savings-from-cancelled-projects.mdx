The council says it saved £40m by cancelling two green projects that never existed. An MP requested documents; the council delayed and gave vague answers. She says the council is hiding evidence and may have broken FOI rules. The council says there's no business case, the projects were only ideas. They promise to send info soon. The council has had internal turmoil and some councillors suspended.
