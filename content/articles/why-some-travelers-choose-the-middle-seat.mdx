Some people now pick the middle seat on flights, even though it feels awkward. They say it lets them see the window and use both arm‑rests, and they see it as a chance to practice humility or to connect with strangers. TikTok star James Cashen says chatting with fellow passengers makes the flight less stressful. Psychologist Gillian Sandstrom suggests asking simple questions about the flight or destination to spark conversation.
