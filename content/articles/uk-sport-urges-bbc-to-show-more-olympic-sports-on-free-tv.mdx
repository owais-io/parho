UK Sport chair Nick Webborn has asked the BBC to broadcast more Olympic and Paralympic events between the Games. A recent survey found that 69 % of Brits want more free‑to‑air coverage, and 66 % want more Paralympic sports shown. Webborn has spoken with BBC sports directors and said the talks are positive, but no detailed plan has been set yet. He hopes increased coverage will keep fans excited ahead of the 2026 Winter Games.
