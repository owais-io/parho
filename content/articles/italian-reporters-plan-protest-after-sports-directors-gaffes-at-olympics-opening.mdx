Sports reporters at Italy’s state TV (Rai) will protest after their sports director, Paolo Petrecca, made a series of embarrassing mistakes during the Milano Cortina Winter Olympics opening ceremony. He pointed out the wrong stadium, confused an actress for Mariah Carey, and mixed up Olympic and Italian officials. The union CDR said the coverage hurt the channel’s reputation and that journalists will withhold bylines and strike for three days. Critics claim the gaffes show government influence on the broadcaster.
