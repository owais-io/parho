Sheffield Wednesday have been punished again, losing six more points for not paying players and staff. The club was already hit with a 12‑point penalty in October after the former owner was sent to administration. The new deduction puts them at minus 10 points, at the bottom of the Championship. They have only one win this season, and talks about an appeal are still underway.
