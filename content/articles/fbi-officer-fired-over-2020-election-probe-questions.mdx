Paul W Brown, the FBI's Atlanta chief, was reportedly removed after he challenged a Justice Department search of Fulton County election records tied to the 2020 election. Brown said the raid was based on unproven voter‑fraud claims from former President Trump. The raid seized 700 boxes of documents, while Trump’s aides were also at the office. Democrats say the action is political retribution and the county's elections were fair.
