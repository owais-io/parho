Declan Conlon, a long‑time eel fisherman, is asking a Belfast court to force the Irish government to stop the severe pollution choking Lough Neagh. The lake, once a thriving ecosystem, is now covered in green algae from farm runoff and bad wastewater. The case seeks real action to clean the water, protect wildlife, and restore the lake for future generations.
