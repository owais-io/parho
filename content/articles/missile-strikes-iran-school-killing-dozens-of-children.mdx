A missile hit Shajareh Tayyebeh school in Minab, Iran, on a Saturday morning while classes were in session.  The blast destroyed the building, killing up to 168 people—mostly girls aged 7‑12—and injuring many more.  The school sat near an IRGC compound but was a civilian school.  Iran, UNESCO and the US said they would investigate, while misinformation spread online.
