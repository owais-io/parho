Manchester United beat Fulham 3-2 thanks to a stoppage‑time header from Ben Sesko. Kevin Keegan had earlier equalised, but the Slovene’s winner came after a flurry of goals and a tense VAR debate. United’s captain Bruno Fernandes and midfielder Casemiro also helped score, while Fulham’s Jiménez earned a late penalty. The win marks United’s third straight victory under interim manager Michael Carrick.
