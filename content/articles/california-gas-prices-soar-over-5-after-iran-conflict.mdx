Because of the US‑Iran conflict, gas in California has jumped to over $5 a gallon, the highest in the U.S. AAA says the average is $5.20, up 55 cents from last week. Nationwide prices also rose, but not as much. The spike is due to higher oil costs and disruptions in shipping through the Gulf of Oman. Drivers and businesses are feeling the impact.
