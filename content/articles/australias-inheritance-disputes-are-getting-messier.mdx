Australia is seeing more fights over family wills as a huge $5.4 trillion of assets will pass to the next generation. Rising house prices, more blended families, and cheaper will kits make disputes harder. Lawyers report a jump from 104 to 735 mediation cases in just a few years. Money, grief and grudges mix, so families often end up damaged and angry, even after a will is made.
