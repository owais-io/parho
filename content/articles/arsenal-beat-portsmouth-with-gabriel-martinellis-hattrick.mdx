Arsenal beat Portsmouth 3‑1 in the FA Cup, with Gabriel Martinelli scoring a hat‑trick of headers from corners. His goals ended a long streak of Portsmouth trying to defeat Arsenal, who had lost 23 times before. The win sends Arsenal into the fourth round, keeping their chance of a quadruple alive. Martinelli’s performance also ended a fight with a Portsmouth defender that had upset fans.
