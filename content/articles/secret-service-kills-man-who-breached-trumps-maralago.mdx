US Secret Service agents killed an armed intruder who breached the perimeter of Donald Trump’s Mar‑a‑Lago club in Palm Beach. The 20‑something white male was holding a shotgun and a gasoline can when agents ordered him to drop them; he raised the gun, and the agents fired, killing him. Trump and his wife were not at the property, and the incident happened while he was at the White House.
