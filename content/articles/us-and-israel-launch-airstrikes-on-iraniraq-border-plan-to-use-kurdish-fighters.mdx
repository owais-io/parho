US and Israeli airstrikes hit Iranian border posts with Iraq. Washington says it can back Kurdish fighters if they cross into Iran, while Israel says its jets are degrading Iranian targets. Iran warns against separatists and has struck back at Kurdish groups in Iraq. Kurdish parties say they’re ready, and some US officials have talked to them. The moves could spark civil war and alarm neighbors like Turkey, Iraq and Syria.
