I thought I was immune to scams, but a text from my bank about points that were about to expire tricked me into clicking a fake link. The page looked real, so I gave my login details and paid $2.99. Later, I saw $500 missing from my account. I reported it, and my bank refunded me and replaced my cards. The lesson? Even tech‑savvy people can fall for scams—always double‑check.
