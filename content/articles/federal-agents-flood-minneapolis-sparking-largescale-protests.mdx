After ICE officer killed 37‑year‑old Renee Good in Minneapolis, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security sent more than 2,000 federal agents to the city, sparking door‑to‑door raids. Protesters in Minneapolis and other U.S. cities—Los Angeles, Salt Lake City, San Francisco—shouted, honked, and used drums to block agents. The mayor said most protests were peaceful but schools went remote for safety. Trump officials and the DHS offered different explanations for the shooting.
