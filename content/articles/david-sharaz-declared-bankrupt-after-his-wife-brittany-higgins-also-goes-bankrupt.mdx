David Sharaz, a former journalist, was made bankrupt by a Perth court after his wife, Brittany Higgins, was declared bankrupt a few days earlier. The bankruptcies stem from a defamation case that Linda Reynolds, a former Liberal senator, won against the couple. Reynolds is now seeking more than $500,000 in damages and legal costs, and the court ordered Sharaz’s assets seized and a trustee appointed to manage his finances.
