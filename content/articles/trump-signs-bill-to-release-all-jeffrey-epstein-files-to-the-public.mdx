Trump just signed a bill that forces the Justice Department to turn over all the documents it has about Jeffrey Epstein, the convicted sex offender who died in 2019. The department has 30 days to hand over files on Epstein’s investigation, his associates, flight logs, and the circumstances of his death, while redacting victims’ names. Republicans finally moved the bill after Democrats pressed for the release. Trump had earlier said he wouldn’t release the papers, but now agrees.
