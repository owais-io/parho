Reform UK candidate Chris Parry, running for mayor, made a series of racist tweets. He told Labour MP David Lammy to “go home to the Caribbean” and said other minority politicians, including Anas Sarwar, Naz Shah and Sadiq Khan, were not loyal to Britain. Reform UK has not yet condemned him, and its deputy leader has suggested his remarks are part of normal political debate. Labour says the party must act quickly.
