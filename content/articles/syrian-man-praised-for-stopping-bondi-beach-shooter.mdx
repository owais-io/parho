Ahmed al‑Ahmed, a 44‑year‑old shopkeeper from the Syrian village of al‑Nayrab, was in Bondi on Sunday when a terror attack broke out at a Hanukah party. He quietly crept up, grabbed a rifle from a gunman and threw it to the ground, saving lives. He was shot twice and is in hospital. The incident made him a hero in both Sydney and his hometown, and a GoFundMe raised $2.3 million for his care.
