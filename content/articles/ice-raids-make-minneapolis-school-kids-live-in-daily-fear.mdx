ICE raids in Minneapolis have turned school life into daily fear, with students hiding during visits, teachers scrambling for remote lessons, and parents worried about their families being taken. The federal agency’s presence has shut schools, split class attendance, and left kids with panic attacks and trauma. Educators are organizing protests and support networks, while children and teachers alike struggle to keep normalcy amid constant threat.
