US officials have banned visas for Europeans who criticize online safety laws in the EU and UK. The move targets former EU commissioner Thierry Breton and U.K.-based anti‑hate group leader Imran Ahmed. Republicans call the Digital Services Act and Online Safety Act anti‑free‑speech. Elon Musk slammed the anti‑hate organization, and U.S. lawmakers are also pushing back on the UK’s rules. These bans are part of a wider free‑speech showdown.
