The Trump administration has frozen more than $10 billion in childcare and family aid for California, Colorado, Illinois, Minnesota and New York. It says the states have let fraud happen, but it hasn’t given any proof or clear plan. Democrats say the move is political and unfair. The five governors are furious, saying they have no guidance and that the decision hurts families and children. The freeze also follows other accusations that the administration targets immigrants and political opponents.
