A new survey of 80 journalists shows that people from ethnic minority backgrounds in UK television news still struggle to reach top roles. Many feel they are treated as “diversity hires” and face resentment from white colleagues who think they’re being pushed back. About 63 % report workplace racism and 70 % say there are few chances to progress. The report warns that diversity programs risk becoming symbolic if not truly integrated.
