France will follow Australia and ban social media for children under 15 starting the 2026 school year. A draft bill also bans mobile phones in high schools and will be reviewed by the Conseil d’État before being debated in parliament early next year. The move is driven by worries about screen time, bullying, sleep loss and mental‑health problems. France hopes the law will align with EU rules.
