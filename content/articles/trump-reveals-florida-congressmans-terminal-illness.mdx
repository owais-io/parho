Trump talked about a Florida Republican congressman, Neal Dunn, who has a terminal heart condition. He said the doctor had put the diagnosis on record and that the congressman was getting surgery. The speaker, Mike Johnson, had to clarify that the information was not public. Dunn announced he would not run for re‑election and was retiring. Republicans worry the small majority could lose seats in the next election.
