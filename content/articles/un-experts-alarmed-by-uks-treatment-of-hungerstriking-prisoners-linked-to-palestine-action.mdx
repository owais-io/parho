UN experts say the UK is not protecting the health of eight prisoners from Palestine Action who are on hunger strike while waiting for trial. They are being denied quick medical help and some have been sent to hospital many times. The UN says the state must give emergency care and respect human‑rights rules. Families and supporters are asking the justice secretary to meet them and explain what will happen. The case shows a risk of cruel treatment in custody.
