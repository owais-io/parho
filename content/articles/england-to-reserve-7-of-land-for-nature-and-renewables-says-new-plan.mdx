England’s new land‑use plan says about 7 % of the country’s land – roughly the size of three Cornwall counties – must be set aside for nature, forests and renewable energy to hit climate goals. The government says there will still be enough land for farms and homes. The plan maps where housing, farms and green projects should go, gives incentives for turning farmland into forests or wetlands, and pushes for nature protection near cities.
