Chelsea had 44 % more injuries from June to October than the previous season, many during the Club World Cup. An insurance report says injuries cost Europe’s top five leagues almost £3 billion in five years. Chelsea’s injuries cost them £17 million by October, almost a quarter of their £80 million prize money. The findings echo worries that packed schedules harm young players and club finances.
