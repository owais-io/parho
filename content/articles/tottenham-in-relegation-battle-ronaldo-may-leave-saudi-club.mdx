Tottenham’s season has slipped into danger, with only five points separating them from the bottom three after a loss to Newcastle. Coach Thomas Frank was sacked, and the club’s poor 17‑game run keeps them on track for about 38 points – the same as last year when they stayed up. Ronaldo’s strike at Al‑Nassr and possible exit reflects shifting ownership plans. Atletico Madrid’s Diego Simeone faces a possible final season as the team oscillates between success and setbacks.
