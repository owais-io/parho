England’s T20 World Cup plans are delayed because two spinners, Adil Rashid and Rehan Ahmed, still need visas from India. The delays could keep them from playing warm‑up games in Sri Lanka, hurting squad preparation. The England and Wales Cricket Board has asked the UK government to speed up the process, while the players are in South Africa and Australia. Even the venue for a Bangladesh match is uncertain due to security worries.
