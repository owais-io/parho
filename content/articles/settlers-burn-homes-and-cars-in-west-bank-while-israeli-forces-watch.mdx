Across the West Bank, settlers set fire to houses and cars, beat residents, and throw stones at vehicles in at least a dozen villages. The attacks happened overnight and continued into the night, even though Israeli troops and police were present. This violence is part of a wider surge of settler attacks that have left more than 1,000 Palestinians injured or killed since October 2023. Human‑rights groups say the government lets these acts go unchecked.
