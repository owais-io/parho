A coalition of immigration groups, lawyers and U.S. citizens has sued Secretary of State Marco Rubio and the State Department to overturn a new order that stopped immigrant visa approvals from 75 countries. They say the freeze is unfair, based on false welfare‑claims, and violates the law, harming families, doctors and people with medical needs. The lawsuit argues the policy lacks legal authority and is discriminatory.
