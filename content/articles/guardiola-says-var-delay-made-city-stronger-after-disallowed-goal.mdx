Manchester City won 2‑0 over Newcastle in the first leg of their Carabao Cup semi‑final, but the game was marred by a long VAR delay that ruled out Antoine Semenyo’s second goal. Guardiola, while happy with the win, blasted the delay, saying it made his players angry and helped them fight harder. Rayan Cherki’s 98th‑minute goal sealed the lead, and Newcastle coach Eddie Howe said the delay was too long and could hurt his team in the second leg.
