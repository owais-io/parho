France has moved a small group of its soldiers to Greenland as part of a NATO plan to show the U.S. that the Arctic region is taken seriously. President Trump said Greenland was very important for U.S. security, which sparked a rush of military activity. Russia warned it was “seriously concerned” about NATO’s presence. Denmark, Greenland, and other allies are already sending troops and planes, hoping to reassure the U.S. and deter Russia and China.
