Antonia Romeo, a seasoned civil servant known for her outgoing style and focus on equality, is back on the shortlist to become the UK cabinet secretary after the previous choice left the job. She has held senior roles at the Home Office, Ministry of Justice and overseas, and is praised for her people‑first leadership. Some traditional civil servants worry about her public self‑promotion, but many say she could revitalize the service.
