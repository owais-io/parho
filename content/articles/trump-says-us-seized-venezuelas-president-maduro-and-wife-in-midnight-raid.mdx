Trump says US forces stole Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and his wife Cilia Flores during a midnight attack in Caracas. He praised the operation and said the couple will face US courts. No one has confirmed where they are, and many countries, including Colombia, the UN and Cuba, condemned the raid as illegal. Venezuelan officials are unsure who runs the country now, and the US has been attacking Venezuela for months with sanctions and strikes.
