Manchester United Women won 3‑0 against Atlético Madrid in the first leg of the Champions League playoff, with goals from Elisabeth Terland, Melvine Malard and Julia Zigiotti. The win puts them into the quarter‑finals, where they'll face Bayern Munich. United’s manager, Marc Skinner, praised the team's defence and finishing. Atlético, now with a new coach and a back‑three system, struggled early and lost momentum.
