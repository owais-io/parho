Matt Weston became the first British man to win a gold medal in skeleton at the Winter Olympics in Cortina. He set four track records and finished 3 min 43.33 sec, almost a full second ahead of Germany’s Axel Jungk. The win makes Britain the most successful nation in the sport. Weston, a former taekwondo world‑second and rugby player, says his training and focus helped him achieve this moment.
