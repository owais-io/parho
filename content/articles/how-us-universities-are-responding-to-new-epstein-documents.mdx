After a flood of new Jeffrey Epstein documents, several U.S. colleges and universities have taken steps against faculty or staff named in the files. Some schools put professors under review, shut down research centers, canceled conferences, and issued statements. Students and staff have launched petitions, open letters, and forums to voice their concerns and ask their schools what actions are being taken and how they feel about the campus environment.
