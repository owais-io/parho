Mexican security forces killed the infamous drug boss “El Mencho” in Jalisco, sparking chaotic roadblocks and gunfire across eight states. The raid, backed by U.S. intelligence, killed him and several associates, but left several officers dead or wounded. The violence forced airlines to cancel flights, shut schools, and urged citizens to stay home. The event shows the cartel’s reach and the ongoing U.S.-Mexico fight against drug trafficking.
