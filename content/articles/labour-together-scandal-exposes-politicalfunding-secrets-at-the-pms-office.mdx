Labour Together, a group that helped shape Keir Starmer’s rise, hid £730,000 of political donations and hired a PR firm to attack journalists who exposed the secret. The firm, APCO, paid to dig up negative material, even suggesting foreign leaks, and shared the findings with security services. Starmer’s ministers, including the one who ordered the work, face scrutiny. The government is opening an inquiry, but questions about the PM’s knowledge remain.
