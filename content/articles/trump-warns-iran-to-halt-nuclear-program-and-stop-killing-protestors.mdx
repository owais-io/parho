Trump told reporters that Iran must end its nuclear program and stop killing protestors before U.S. warships sail to the region. He said the U.S. navy is ready to use force if Tehran does not comply. The president also highlighted Iran’s violent crackdown on demonstrators. Meanwhile, Iran’s foreign minister is in Turkey discussing a possible deal, but talks have stalled. Trump’s warnings aim to pressure Tehran into change.
