Border patrol agents shot a man and his wife after a chase over an alleged undocumented driver near a Portland hospital. The incident followed the killing of a woman by an ICE officer in Minneapolis, stirring anger in Portland. Police say the victims are alive but injured. City officials want a local investigation and to halt ICE operations, while protesters demanded the agency leave the city. The FBI is now leading the case.
