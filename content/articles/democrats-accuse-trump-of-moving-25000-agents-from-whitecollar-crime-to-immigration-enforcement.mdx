Senator Elizabeth Warren and other Democrats wrote a letter to federal inspectors asking for an investigation. They say President Trump has moved over 25,000 law‑enforcement workers from fraud, money‑laundering and tax‑evasion cases into Immigration and Customs Enforcement. The shift has slowed or stopped many financial‑crime investigations, leaving victims and ordinary citizens exposed. The lawmakers want the inspectors to reveal how many staff were reassigned and how many cases were affected.
