Thames Water, the UK's biggest water company, said it made £386 m in profit for the first half of the year after letting customers pay 31 % more in April. The company has £17 bn of debt and worries it could be taken over by the government if it can’t agree a deal with its lenders. It also faces big fines for sewage leaks and is still negotiating a second rescue package.
