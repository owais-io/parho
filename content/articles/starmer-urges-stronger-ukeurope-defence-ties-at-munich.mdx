At the Munich Security Conference, UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK must work closer with Europe on defence procurement. He wants the UK back in the EU’s Security Action programme and a new European Defence Mechanism that lets all European democracies share costs. Starmer cites Russia as a long‑term threat pushing Europe to act together. He stresses Britain and Europe need each other for security, while NATO and the US partnership stay strong.
