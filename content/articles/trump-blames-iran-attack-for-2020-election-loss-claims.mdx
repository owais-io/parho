Donald Trump posted on Truth Social that the U.S. strike on Iran was a response to Iran’s alleged interference in the 2020 election, saying the country tried to stop him from winning. He also echoed earlier posts linking Venezuela to election fraud. Trump has no proof that Iran caused his loss; while Iran has been accused of hacking campaigns, no evidence shows it tipped the election. The White House has not responded.
