Two priceless altar cards from Napoleon III’s crypt were stolen in 2014 and were thought lost. Antiques dealer Paul Gostelow spotted similar Napoleonic designs on the BBC show Lovejoy, realised he owned two of the three missing cards, and called the police. The cards were returned to St Michael’s Abbey in Hampshire in poor condition. Authorities hope the third card will also be found.
