Colin Gray, 54, was found guilty of second‑degree murder and involuntary manslaughter for giving his 14‑year‑old son a rifle. Colt Gray shot four people, killing two students and two teachers, on September 4 at Apalachee High School. The jury deliberated only a few hours and convicted Gray on more than 20 charges. He faces up to 30 years in prison, and his lawyers plan to appeal.
