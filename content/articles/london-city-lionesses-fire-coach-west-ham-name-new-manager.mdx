London City Lionesses fired coach Jocelyn Prêcheur after a season that saw them promoted and finish sixth in the Women’s Super League. The club says it wants a new direction to reach the top. Meanwhile, West Ham replaced manager Rehanne Skinner with former Juventus boss Rita Guarino, who will start before Christmas. Both clubs are looking for fresh leadership to boost their futures. They hope the changes will help them climb higher in the standings.
