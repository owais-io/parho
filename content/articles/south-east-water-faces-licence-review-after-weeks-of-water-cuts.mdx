South East Water might lose its licence after almost a week of water outages hit homes in Kent and Sussex. Environment Secretary Emma Reynolds has asked Ofwat to check the company’s licence, saying residents are suffering and that the water company has failed. If the licence is revoked, the firm would be put into special administration until a buyer is found. South East Water says it’s fixing leaks and wants a turnaround plan.
