Restaurant owners across the U.S. are being harassed and threatened after publicly supporting immigrant rights. In New York, a bar owner had a pro‑immigrant sign vandalised and received hate messages. Other owners report losing customers and getting fake bad reviews after posting solidarity posts. With many restaurant workers being immigrants, these attacks hurt businesses, lower sales, and create fear for owners and staff.
