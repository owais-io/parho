Channel 4’s drama *Dirty Business* tells the tragic story of an eight‑year‑old girl who died in 1999 after drinking raw sewage. The film shows how Britain’s water companies, now owned by private funds, have missed huge debts and left rivers raw‑sewage polluted. Activists fight to bring Thames Water into public hands, but the government still backs private investors. The show urges the industry to be nationalised to protect health and the environment.
