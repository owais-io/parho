Coco Gauff broke her racket after a heavy loss at the Australian Open, hoping to hide the act in a quiet corridor. But cameras everywhere recorded it, sparking a debate about players’ privacy. Many top players, like Djokovic and Swiatek, say the constant filming feels like a zoo. The WTA said players deserve private space, while Tennis Australia pledged to listen and improve conditions.
