In Indiana, identical twins Nick and Nathan keep their friendship strong even though one is a Democratic city council member and the other supports Trump. A video Nick posted to say people shouldn’t confuse him with his brother went viral, sparking jokes and even threats. The brothers say they avoid political fights, stay kind, and listen to each other. They believe keeping civility and family bonds matters more than winning arguments.
