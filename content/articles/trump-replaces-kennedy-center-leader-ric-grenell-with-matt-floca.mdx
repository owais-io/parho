Donald Trump announced that Ric Grenell, the former foreign‑policy adviser who had overseen controversial changes at the Kennedy Center, would step down. He named Matt Floca, the centre’s operations VP, as Grenell’s replacement. The move comes amid a planned two‑year renovation and backlash from artists who cancelled shows after Grenell pushed Trump’s name onto the building. Floca, who joined in January 2024, has a construction‑management background.
