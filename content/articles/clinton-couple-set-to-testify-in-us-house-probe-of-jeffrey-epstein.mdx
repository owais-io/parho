House investigators have subpoenaed former President Bill Clinton and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton to give closed‑door depositions about their ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The Clintons say they’re being targeted to distract attention from Donald Trump’s friendship with Epstein. They’ll testify in Chappaqua, New York, after refusing to appear on Capitol Hill. Critics claim the hearings are partisan, while the Clintons highlight past political scrutiny and their past testimony.
