Week 14 of the NFL is heating up. The Baltimore Ravens and Pittsburgh Steelers, both 6‑6, face each other in a title‑decider that could decide the AFC North. A win lifts the Ravens’ playoff chances to 81% (vs 36% if they lose) and the Steelers to 61% (vs 16% if they lose). The Jaguars (8‑4) host the Colts (8‑4), while the Bills (8‑4) play the Bengals (4‑8). Other games include the Falcons vs Seahawks, Browns vs Titans and more.
