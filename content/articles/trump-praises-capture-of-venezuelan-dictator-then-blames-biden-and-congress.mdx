At Mar‑a‑Lago, Trump celebrated the night raid that ousted Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro, praising U.S. forces. Then he switched to complaints: he blasted former President Biden, criticized Congress for leaking, and said he’d run Venezuela if he weren’t president. Trump also mentioned a pardon he gave to ex‑Honduran president Juan Orlando Hernández and complained about his own health. The piece shows his mix of praise for military action and venting about politics.
