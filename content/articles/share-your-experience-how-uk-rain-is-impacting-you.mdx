For the past month, the UK has been hit by steady rain and floods. Farmers, builders, sports teams, wildlife, roads and homes all feel the damage. In Devon, Cornwall and Worcestershire people have seen rain every day for 40 days, while Northern Ireland had its wettest January in 149 years and Wales already received 39% of its February rainfall. The Guardian wants to hear how this weather has changed your life, but reminds everyone to keep safety first.
