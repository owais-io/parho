Salah, the star of Liverpool, is a huge icon in Egypt, so his words after a 3‑3 draw sparked a media frenzy. Egyptian newspapers, TV hosts and even food‑TikTokers rushed to defend him, while UK outlets slammed him. Most Egyptians feel Salah deserves a free‑wheeling voice because he’s helped the national team win titles and bring pride. As the Africa Cup of Nations approaches, fans promise to stick by him no matter what.
