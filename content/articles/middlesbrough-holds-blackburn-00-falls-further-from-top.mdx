Middlesbrough played Blackburn to a goalless draw on Boxing Day, leaving them still in second place but eight points behind champions Coventry. New manager Kim Hellberg tried a tight 4‑2‑2‑2 lineup that lacked wing play, and the team struggled to finish chances. Blackburn’s midfielder Todd Cantwell was key, and the hosts could have taken the lead but missed chances. Boro will need to widen their attack for their next game against Hull.
