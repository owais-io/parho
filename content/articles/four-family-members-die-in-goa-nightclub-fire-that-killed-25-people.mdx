Twenty‑five people died in a nightclub fire in Goa, including four sisters and their husband who were on their first holiday there. The blaze started just before midnight at Birch by Romeo Lane, a bar and club that had no proper exit in its basement. Most guests escaped, but 20 kitchen staff suffocated. The owners and staff were arrested for ignoring safety rules. Authorities are still investigating whether fireworks or faulty wiring sparked the fire.
