Peter Mandelson, the former UK ambassador to Washington, was dismissed in 2019 after emails showed he supported Jeffrey Epstein. New emails released by a U.S. House committee reveal he still talked to Epstein in 2016, long after Epstein was charged. The messages include a birthday note and a comment about Donald Trump. The findings reignite questions about why the UK appointed Mandelson despite his past ties, causing criticism of the government.
