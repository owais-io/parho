Ukraine is facing its toughest winter yet as the war drags on. Soldiers and civilians alike feel worn out, with power outages, drone attacks and damaged towns. While U.S. President Donald Trump pushes for peace terms that many Ukrainians fear, life continues in cities: markets, bars and even champagne tastings stay open. Residents of places like Vovchansk and Staryi Saltiv share stories of loss, but also hope that peace will finally come.
