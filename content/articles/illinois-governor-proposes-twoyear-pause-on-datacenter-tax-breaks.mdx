Illinois Gov. J.B. Pritzker has announced that for the next two years the state will stop giving tax breaks to new data‑center projects. He says the huge power needs of AI data centers hurt local communities and raise electricity bills. He also wants grid operator PJM to make developers pay for the extra power costs so consumers aren’t hit with higher rates. The move comes amid growing debate over how fast the tech industry can grow while keeping costs low.
