Cuba has slammed the United States for seizing the Skipper oil tanker off Venezuela, calling it piracy and maritime terrorism. The tanker, carrying almost 2 million barrels of Venezuelan crude, was on a course to Galveston, Texas, after briefly stopping off Cuba’s Matanzas port. Cuban officials say the move is part of U.S. pressure on Venezuela and harms Cuba’s interests.
