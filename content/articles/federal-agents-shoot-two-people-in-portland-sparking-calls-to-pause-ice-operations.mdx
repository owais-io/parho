U.S. border‑patrol agents shot two people outside a Portland hospital after stopping a car they said was linked to an undocumented immigrant. One man was hit in the leg, the other in the chest. The agents said they fired when the driver tried to run them over. Portland’s mayor wants ICE operations paused while an investigation is done. The incident comes after other recent shootings involving federal agents.
