ICE agents in Oregon used a custom app called Elite to pick neighborhoods and people to arrest. They had daily arrest quotas, admitting to trying to hit set numbers. The info came from a court hearing in a lawsuit that stopped warrantless arrests. The app’s data can be wrong, and the quotas may lead to unlawful, racially biased detentions. A judge ruled that ICE must stop detaining people without warrants.
