Aldrich Ames was a CIA officer who secretly sold U.S. secrets to the Russians. He gave the KGB names of more than 30 spies, and about ten of those spies were killed. Ames also leaked information that helped the Soviets run their own spies against the U.S. He was caught when the FBI traced his bank deposits, sentenced to life in prison, and died there at age 84.
