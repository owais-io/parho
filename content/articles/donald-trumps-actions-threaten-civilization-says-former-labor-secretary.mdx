Reich says Trump’s actions – from a 2021 coup attempt to recent moves against Venezuela and threats to Cuba, Colombia, and Greenland – weaken law and endanger civilization. He argues civilization protects the weak from the strong; rising inequality lets the powerful exploit the weak. Big tech, oil, and AI concentrate power further. Trump’s unchecked presidency, backed by Congress and the Supreme Court, adds risk. Reich warns this unchecked power could lead to war and damage global stability.
