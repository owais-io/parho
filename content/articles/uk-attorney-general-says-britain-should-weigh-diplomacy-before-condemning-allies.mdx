UK’s top lawyer, Richard Hermer, said Britain should weigh diplomacy before calling out allies for breaking international law. He spoke after the UK was criticised for not condemning a US attack on Venezuela. Hermer said the UK must follow rules but also use diplomatic tools. He defended Labour’s focus on rights at home and warned that a Reform government could hurt the rule of law. He urged Britain to stay committed to international law and cooperation.
