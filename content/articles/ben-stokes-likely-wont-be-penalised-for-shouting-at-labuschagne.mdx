During a tense match in Sydney, England captain Ben Stokes got into an argument with Australian batsman Marnus Labuschagne. Stokes shouted a curse word and put his arm around Labuschagne, but no official action was taken. The ICC rules say this could be a mild offence, but match officials decided not to punish him. The incident didn’t change the game’s outcome, and England continued to fight on.
