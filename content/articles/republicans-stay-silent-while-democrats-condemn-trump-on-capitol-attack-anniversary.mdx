On the fifth anniversary of the January 6 attack, most Republicans stayed quiet while Democrats used the day to call Trump a threat to democracy and blame Republicans for letting him escape punishment. Trump had pardoned about 1,500 people involved in the attack, and Democrats said this shows he is protected. A small group of former rioters marched near the Capitol, and Democrats held an unofficial hearing to discuss the event.
