San Francisco has sued ten food giants, arguing that ultra‑processed foods are hurting public health and that the city is paying for the treatment costs. The suit names brands like Coca‑Cola, Pepsi, Kraft, Nestlé and others, accusing them of marketing unhealthy products as “healthy” and of using harmful additives. The city wants damages and says the companies knew the foods could cause disease but kept selling them.
