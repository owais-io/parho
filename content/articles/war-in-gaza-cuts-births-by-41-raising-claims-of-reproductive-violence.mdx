Israel's war in Gaza has dropped births by 41% and caused many maternal deaths, miscarriages, and newborn problems, according to two reports. The studies say the fighting, bombings, and destruction of hospitals and an IVF centre have stripped mothers of care and even harmed their ability to have children. The UN and health experts say this could be a form of reproductive violence and possibly genocide. Israel denies the claims.
