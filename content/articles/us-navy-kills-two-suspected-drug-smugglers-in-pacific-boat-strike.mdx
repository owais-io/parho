The US Southern Command said a strike on a boat in the eastern Pacific killed two suspected drug smugglers. The attack was ordered by the new commander, Gen. Francis L. Donovan, who took over after the previous commander stepped down over policy disagreements. The strike is part of a broader campaign against drug trafficking and Venezuelan oil smuggling, which also saw US forces board a tanker in the Indian Ocean.
