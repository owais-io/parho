Sam Kerr, captain of Australia’s women’s soccer team, and her girlfriend, US midfielder Kristie Mewis, will get married on New Year’s Eve in Perth. The ceremony will be held at a private home with about 120 guests, and the couple will write their own vows. They’ve been engaged for two‑and‑a‑half years, have a son together, and will delay their honeymoon because of football schedules.
