A 4‑year‑old boy named Gus Lamont has gone missing from his family’s sheep station in September. Police now say his disappearance is a major crime, not just a wandering incident. They’ve named a suspect inside the household, but clarified it isn’t a parent. The boy’s grandmothers, Shannon and Josie Murray, said they’re devastated and want Gus reunited with his parents.
