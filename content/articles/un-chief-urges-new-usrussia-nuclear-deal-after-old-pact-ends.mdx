The UN’s top diplomat, António Guterres, said it’s a “grave moment” that the US‑Russia nuclear treaty, New Start, has expired. He urged Washington and Moscow to sign a new agreement quickly, warning that without limits the world could face an arms race and a higher risk of nuclear use. The two countries hold more than 80 % of the world’s nuclear weapons, so any break in control could threaten global security.
