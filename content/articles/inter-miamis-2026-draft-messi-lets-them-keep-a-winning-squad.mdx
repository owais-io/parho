Messi’s arrival changes the game: his fame attracts teammates who would otherwise leave or ask for big paychecks. Miami signed goalkeepers, defenders, and strikers for less money than rivals would pay. The club’s new roster shows how Messi lets them keep top talent while staying within league rules.
