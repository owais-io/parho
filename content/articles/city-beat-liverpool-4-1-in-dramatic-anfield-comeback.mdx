City were behind after a goal from Liverpool’s Dominik Szoboszlai, but the visitors staged a dramatic comeback at Anfield. Bernardo Silva, Erling Haaland and Matheus Nunes all scored, with Haaland earning a penalty that helped swing the match. City won 4‑1, keeping their title hopes alive. Despite a late red card for Szoboszlai, the game showcased City’s resilience and tactical superiority.
