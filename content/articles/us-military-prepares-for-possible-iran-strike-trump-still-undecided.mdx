The U.S. armed forces have moved ready for a strike on Iran, with aircraft carriers and warships already in the Middle East. President Trump has not yet decided whether to use force, hoping diplomatic talks in Geneva can reach a deal. Military officials say the fleet will be fully deployed by mid‑March, and Israel is also preparing to join a potential attack. Russia warns the world of the danger of escalating tensions.
