Marco Rubio, the U.S. Secretary of State, gave a 22‑minute speech at Munich’s security conference that celebrated a return to empire. He praised America’s historic overseas power, ignored the dark legacy of colonisation, and urged European allies to drop guilt and support the U.S. Many German and other European leaders applauded him. The speech also mentioned plans to claim Greenland and hinted at military force against Denmark, showing a push for a more aggressive U.S. foreign policy.
