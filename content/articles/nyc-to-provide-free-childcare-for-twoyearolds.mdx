New York Governor Kathy Hochul and Mayor Zohran Mamdani announced a plan to give free childcare to two‑year‑old children in the city. The program will start in “high‑need areas” and aim to cover about 2,000 kids this fall, expanding gradually. The plan builds on existing free preschool for ages three and four, but questions remain about long‑term funding and rollout details.
