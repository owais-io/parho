A French magistrate has asked two French‑Israeli men to appear in court for “complicity in genocide” after they reportedly helped block humanitarian aid to Gaza. The summonses were issued in July and don’t require arrest, but the case is the first time a country has treated aid blockages as a genocide crime. The complaint was filed by Palestinian rights groups, and other activists are also under investigation.
