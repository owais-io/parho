The U.S. offered Ukraine NATO‑style security guarantees in talks in Berlin, showing progress toward ending Russia’s war, but a deal on territory still hasn't been reached. President Zelenskyy asked for more sanctions and weapons if Russia rejects the talks. European leaders see the talks as a big step, hoping for a ceasefire around Christmas. Meanwhile, Ukraine hit a Russian submarine, and EU leaders are considering using frozen Russian assets to fund Ukraine.
