Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg took the stand in a Los Angeles trial that is testing whether social‑media companies designed their platforms to harm young users. He said the firm has made some progress on checking ages, but still admits children often lie about how old they are. Lawyers argued that addictive design worsens teens’ depression and suicidal thoughts. The case could force big tech to change how it protects kids.
