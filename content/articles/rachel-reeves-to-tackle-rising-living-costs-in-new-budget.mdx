Rachel Reeves will deliver a new budget that aims to help people cope with high prices while raising taxes to close a £20bn gap. She plans to freeze income‑tax limits for two more years, cut generous salary‑sacrifice schemes, add a surcharge on the most expensive homes, and introduce a pay‑per‑mile tax on electric cars. The plan has drawn criticism from both sides of politics, with some calling for a wealth tax and others warning it will hurt working people.
