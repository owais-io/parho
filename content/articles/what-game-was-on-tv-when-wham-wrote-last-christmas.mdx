George Michael wrote the hit “Last Christmas” while his partner Andrew Ridgeley was watching a football match on TV. The match was a February 12, 1984 League One game: Luton Town vs. Manchester United, which ended 0‑5 for United. It was the only live Sunday football show on ITV that day, and the song didn’t become a Christmas No 1 until 2023, beating earlier records.
