The University of Toronto’s new show looks at 19th‑century US maps, books and news about Greenland. It shows how early American officials wanted to own the island, just like Trump has talked about it today. The exhibit also highlights how Inuit people lived there and taught European explorers about the land. It reminds visitors that the Arctic has long been a contested, resource‑rich region.
