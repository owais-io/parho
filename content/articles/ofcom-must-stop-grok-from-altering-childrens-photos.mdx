An online trend is using Elon Musk’s chatbot Grok to alter photos of girls into bikinis, sparking outrage in the UK and beyond. The government’s response has been slow, and the platform’s safety tools aren’t enough. Ofcom is looking into the issue, but action could be delayed by rules that let companies stall. The article calls for faster laws to protect children, suggesting changes to existing bills and stronger user‑first rules.
