Texas Governor Greg Abbott said Turning Point USA and its affiliate Club America will open clubs in more than 500 high schools to promote free‑speech values and counter left‑leaning ideas. The groups, founded by the late Charlie Kirk, face criticism for controversial views on LGBTQ+ and other minorities. Critics worry the clubs could push a political agenda in schools. Abbott said the state will support the clubs and will oppose any school that blocks them.
