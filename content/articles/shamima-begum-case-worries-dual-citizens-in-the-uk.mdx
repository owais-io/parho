Peter Walker, a dual Australian‑British citizen, writes that stripping Shamima Begum of her British citizenship could set a dangerous precedent for other dual nationals. He worries that right‑wing politics, led by parties like Reform UK and Nigel Farage, could push for mass deportations and erode naturalised citizens’ rights. Comparing the threat to Nazi Germany’s loss of rights, he urges lawmakers to act before a future Reform government puts dual nationals at risk.
