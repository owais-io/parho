An Australian mother, Amanda, asked Snapchat to delete her 14‑year‑old son’s account, but the platform said it wouldn’t act because the profile listed the user as 25 years old. Snapchat only locks accounts when it can confirm a user is under 16, to avoid false takedowns. After Amanda supplied ID, Snapchat finally shut the account, but many parents feel the burden of proving age falls on them.
