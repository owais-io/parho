Kristen Galvan, a 15‑year‑old Texas girl who vanished after being trafficked by a Houston gang, has been confirmed dead. Her remains were found under a bridge in Missouri City in 2020, but DNA testing only matched her to the body in 2025. Kristen was groomed on Instagram, forced into sex work, and later beaten and buried. Her mother, Robyn Cory, has been fighting for justice and awareness for over five years.
