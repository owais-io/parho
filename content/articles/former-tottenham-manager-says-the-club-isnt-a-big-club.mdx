Former Tottenham manager Ange Postecoglou says the club isn’t a big club because they keep wage budgets low and can’t sign top talent. He calls the “Spursy” tag real and says the team needs risk‑taking to move from mid‑table to Champions League. Tottenham just sacked Thomas Frank after a poor run, adding to uncertainty. The former coach also recalls winning the Europa League last season but now sees the club stuck in a “Spursy” mentality that holds them back.
