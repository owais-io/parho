Tottenham striker Kolo Muani had a scary tyre blowout in his Ferrari on the motorway, but he still made it to the Champions League match in Frankfurt. He scored a volley to give Spurs a 2‑1 win over Eintracht Frankfurt. Tottenham dominated the game, with Solanke adding another goal, and manager Thomas Frank praised the team's performance. Despite the car mishap, Muani proved he could still deliver.
