Milan is set to host the Winter Olympics, and the opening ceremony will take place at the famous San Siro stadium. The event brings excitement but also worries about high costs, security, and political tensions. Ticket prices are high, so many locals won’t be able to attend, but free fan zones and TV screens let people join the celebrations. The city is also preparing for tight security and the presence of international officials.
