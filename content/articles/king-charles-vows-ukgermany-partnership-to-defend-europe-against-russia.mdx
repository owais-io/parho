King Charles welcomed German President Frank‑Walter Steinmeier at Windsor Castle and said the UK and Germany will work together to defend Europe and support Ukraine against Russian aggression. The visit highlights the new Kensington treaty, which promises closer cooperation on migration, defence, trade and education. The king praised the renewed friendship between the two countries, noting that past wounds have been healed and their shared values will guide future security and prosperity.
