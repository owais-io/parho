Coffee chain Caffè Nero will open up to 30 UK stores and 50‑70 overseas this year, but warns that coffee prices will rise because of war, higher staff costs, and climate impacts. The company bought Compass Coffee and other brands, boosting sales but also debt. CEO Gerry Ford says they’ll grow steadily, not too fast, but cup prices may stay high as costs stay up.
