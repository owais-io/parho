Trump signed an executive order that protects companies making glyphosate herbicides, like Roundup, and the phosphorus used to make them. The order says these chemicals are vital for national security and food supply, and it shields producers from lawsuits. Critics say it ignores health risks linked to cancer and pushes the chemical industry. Farmers and activists argue it endangers public health.
