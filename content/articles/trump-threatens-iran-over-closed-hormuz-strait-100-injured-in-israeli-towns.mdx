Japan is looking into clearing mines from the Strait of Hormuz if a ceasefire ends the US‑Israel war, says foreign minister Toshimitsu Motegi. The strait, which supplies most of Japan’s oil, has been blocked by Iran. Trump has threatened to attack Iranian power plants if the strait stays closed for 48 hours. Meanwhile, Iran launched missiles that injured about 100 people in southern Israel. The region is also facing drone and missile attacks on Saudi Arabia, UAE and British bases.
