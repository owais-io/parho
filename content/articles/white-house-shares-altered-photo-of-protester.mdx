The White House posted a doctored picture of Nekima Levy Armstrong, who was arrested during an ICE protest. The image shows her crying and with darker skin, which experts say is fake. A White House spokesperson said the picture was real, but the Guardian found it was digitally changed. The post is part of a series of AI‑generated posts the White House has made, raising concerns about misinformation.
