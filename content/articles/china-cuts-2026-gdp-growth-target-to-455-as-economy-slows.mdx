China announced its 2026 GDP growth goal is only 4.5‑5%, the lowest in decades. The move signals a shift from heavy exports to a focus on high‑quality growth, tech and reforms. The plan also aims for 5.5% urban unemployment and 12 million new city jobs. In the five‑year plan, China will boost consumption, innovation and cut carbon intensity by 17% by 2030, while raising defence spending 7%.
