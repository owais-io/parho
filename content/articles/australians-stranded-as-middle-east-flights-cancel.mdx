Australia’s foreign minister Penny Wong warned that the US‑Israel‑Iran conflict is causing big flight disruptions. About 115,000 Australians are in the region, and 11,000 rely on airlines that have suspended flights. Formula One teams bound for Melbourne had to change plans, and fighting has spread into Lebanon with heavy airstrikes. The story also mentions a Senate censure of Pauline Hanson, a housing‑market scare, a crocodile sighting, a lunar eclipse, and a memoir by Christina Applegate about living with MS.
