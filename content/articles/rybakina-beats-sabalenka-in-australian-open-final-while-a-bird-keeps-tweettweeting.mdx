The Australian Open women’s final at Melbourne Park saw Elena Rybakina defeat world No 1 Aryna Sabalenka to win her first major title. The match was interrupted by a myna bird perched in the roof, whose chirps added a quirky backdrop to the tense contest. Rybakina held her nerve after losing the second set, broke back in the third, and won in straight sets, drawing cheers from a quiet crowd.
