An Italian woman who broke her ankle during a Zoom meeting at home won a court case that now classifies home‑working injuries as work accidents. The university employee was denied help from Inail, the national injury fund, so she sued with her union. A Padua judge ordered the fund to pay her back benefits and medical costs, setting a new precedent for remote‑work workers.
