Washington Post workers are worried that their boss, Jeff Bezos, might cut staff, especially in foreign and local news. They've sent three letters and are planning a protest, but Bezos has not responded. Employees say the cuts would hurt the paper’s coverage and local reporting. The union and reporters want Bezos to protect the newsroom, but his silence has made everyone uneasy.
