Justice Department pulled a picture of Donald Trump from a release of Jeffrey Epstein documents, then put it back after checking it didn’t endanger victims. The removal sparked calls from Democrats that the department was protecting Trump, a former friend of Epstein. Officials said 16 images were taken down at the request of victim‑rights groups and that investigations are still underway. The incident shows worries that the DOJ may have mishandled sensitive materials.
