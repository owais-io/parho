Keir Starmer has said the UK will not hit the US with counter‑tariffs after President Trump threatened a 10‑25% duty on British goods. He wants calm talks, says a trade war would hurt businesses and families, and the government is ready to help firms if the US follows through. Some politicians want retaliation, but Starmer stresses keeping alliances strong and working on shared security.
