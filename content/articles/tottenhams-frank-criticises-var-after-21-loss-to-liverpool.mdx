Tottenham manager Thomas Frank was furious after a 2‑1 defeat to Liverpool. He complained that VAR wrongly upgraded a yellow card to a red for Xavi Simons, calling it too harsh and unfair, and said the three‑game ban was excessive. Frank also said VAR missed a push by Liverpool’s Hugo Ekitiké on Tottenham’s Cristian Romero, which helped Liverpool score. Despite the anger, he praised his players’ effort.
