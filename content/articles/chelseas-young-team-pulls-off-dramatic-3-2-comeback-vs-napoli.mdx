Chelsea’s young squad fought back to beat Napoli 3‑2 in the Champions League, sparking joy for coach Liam Rosenior. After falling behind 2‑1 at halftime, João Pedro scored twice and Cole Palmer made a huge impact from the bench, securing a 3‑2 win at Stadio Diego Armando Maradona. The victory keeps Chelsea in the knockout stage and means they avoid a two‑legged playoff next month. Rosenior praised the squad’s resilience and hope for future success.
