Chinese football is in turmoil as the Chinese FA imposes life bans, point deductions, and fines on 13 clubs for corruption, hurting the 2026 Super League. Fans are frustrated, yet hope grows after the U‑23 team reached the Asian Cup final, showing youth investment pays off. Meanwhile, provincial amateur leagues have sprung up after professional clubs folded, keeping football alive at the local level.
