Police went to a man's house on the NSW Central Coast to check on his welfare after hearing he might need help. The 49‑year‑old grabbed a screwdriver and other sharp items, and the officers tried using stun guns, pepper spray and tactical teams, but he resisted for about six hours. He set fire to an aerosol can, tried to stab a policeman, and was then shot. He died on scene, and a police officer was hurt. Investigations will follow.
