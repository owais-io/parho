In Minneapolis and Saint Paul, families are staying inside their houses for weeks because federal agents have been raiding the area. One man, José, hasn’t left his home for 29 days while his wife and kids wait after his arrest. Another couple’s husband, Chris, was taken to a Texas detention center and has only just been released. The fear of being taken has turned many homes into “jails.”
