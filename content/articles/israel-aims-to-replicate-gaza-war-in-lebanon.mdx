Israel is preparing a full‑scale ground attack on southern Lebanon, repeating the tactics it used in Gaza. Politicians and commentators are calling for mass evacuations, the destruction of villages, and even the annexation of territory. The plan includes targeting civilian infrastructure, dropping leaflets warning residents to leave, and using white‑phosphorus attacks. The international community has largely stayed silent, but human‑rights groups warn that the move could lead to genocide. Sanctions and stronger condemnation are urged to stop the escalation.
