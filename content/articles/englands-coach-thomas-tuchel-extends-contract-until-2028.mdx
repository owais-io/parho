Thomas Tuchel has signed a new deal that keeps him as England’s head coach through the Euro 2028 tournament, regardless of how the team does at the World Cup this summer. The FA wants stability and wants to keep him focused on the national team instead of club offers. Tuchel joined in January 2025, led England to an unbeaten qualifying run, and only lost a friendly to Senegal.
