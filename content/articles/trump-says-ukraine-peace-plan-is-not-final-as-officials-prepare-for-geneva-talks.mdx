Trump said his peace plan is not final after Ukraine officials slammed it as a bad deal like Munich. The plan wants Ukraine to give up land, cut its army, and drop long‑range weapons, with no EU or NATO help. Ukrainian leader Zelenskyy warned the country faces a hard choice: keep its land or lose US support. Officials will meet in Geneva on Sunday to discuss the plan.
