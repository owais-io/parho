Joani Reid, a Labour MP for East Kilbride and Strathaven, has temporarily stepped away from the party whip while an investigation looks into her husband David Taylor’s arrest for suspected China spying. Reid says she is not under police scrutiny and is cooperating with the Labour inquiry. The case also triggered police raids on other homes tied to alleged espionage, raising broader security concerns.
