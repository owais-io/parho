Harry Brook, England’s 27‑year‑old middle‑order batter, is seen as a generational talent but hasn’t yet produced a match‑winning innings. In the current Ashes series, England are struggling, and a big score from Brook could change the game. He admits he needs to “rein it in” and learn to handle pressure, especially against strong Australian bowlers. The article stresses that a solid performance from him is crucial for England’s hopes.
