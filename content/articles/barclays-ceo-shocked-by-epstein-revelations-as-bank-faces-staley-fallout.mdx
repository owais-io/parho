Barclays’ chief executive, CS Venkatakrishnan, expressed deep shock and disappointment over the disturbing details in the newly released Jeffrey Epstein files. While he praised the victims, he avoided commenting on allegations against former boss Jes Staley, who left the bank amid earlier investigations. Barclays is also battling a U.S. class‑action suit claiming it misled investors about Staley’s ties to Epstein, even as the bank reports record profits and plans to return billions to shareholders.
