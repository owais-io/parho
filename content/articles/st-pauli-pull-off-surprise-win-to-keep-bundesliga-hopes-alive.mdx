St Pauli were almost out of the Bundesliga after a long losing streak, but they shocked Stuttgart 3‑2 at home. Manolis Saliakas scored their first goal, Danel Sinani hit a penalty, and Jamie Leweling sealed the game with a late strike. The win moves St Pauli back into the fight to stay up, just two points behind rivals. The club, with the lowest wages in the league, relies on strong fan support and smart budgeting.
