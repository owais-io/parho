Spain’s Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez is refusing to let the United States use Spanish air bases and threatens a trade embargo if the US backs Israel’s air strikes on Iran. He says Spain will not help war that goes against its values. The move is both a stand against war and a way to gain domestic support ahead of elections. Sánchez also keeps an eye on EU and China ties.
