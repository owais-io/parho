Joseph Earp explains how his love for learning and his new habit of daily running have changed his life. He enjoys digging into random topics, and the surprise of not knowing keeps him curious. Running pushes his physical limits, but each time he feels he can't go on, he pushes a bit further and realizes his body and mind are stronger than he thought. He argues that embracing uncertainty, not claiming full knowledge, keeps the world open.
