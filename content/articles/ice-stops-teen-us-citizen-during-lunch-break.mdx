In McMinnville, Oregon, U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement stopped a 17‑year‑old American student while he was in a car during lunch. Officers smashed a window and told him to get out even after he said he was a U.S. citizen. He was taken to a Portland ICE center and let back home that evening. His family says the agents may try to charge him. Local schools and advocacy groups say several U.S. citizens were detained this week.
