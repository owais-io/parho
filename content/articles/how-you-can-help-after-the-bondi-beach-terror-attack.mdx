A terrorist attack on Bondi Beach killed 16 and left dozens injured. Hospitals are short of blood, especially O‑negative, and are asking anyone to donate. Call 13 14 95 or visit a Lifeblood centre to book an appointment. The state has an online condolence book and a memorial at Bondi Pavilion, while vigils for the Jewish community are planned. You can also support victims through verified GoFundMe pages. Helplines are available for emotional help.
