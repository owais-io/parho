A new video from January 13 shows Alex Pretti being grabbed and taken to the ground by federal officers during protests in Minneapolis. The footage contradicts claims that he was brandishing a gun; he is seen holding a phone and a gun that he never pulls. The clip was released just 11 days before officers fatally shot Pretti on January 24, raising questions about the circumstances of his death.
