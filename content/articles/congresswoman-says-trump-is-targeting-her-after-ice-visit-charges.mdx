LaMonica McIver, a New Jersey congresswoman, was charged with interfering in an arrest at a Newark ICE detention center after a routine oversight visit. She says the case is a political hit by the Trump administration and that she was simply doing her job. The charges could bring up to 17 years in prison, but she has pleaded not guilty and is fighting them. A judge denied her request to drop some charges, and the case is still pending.
