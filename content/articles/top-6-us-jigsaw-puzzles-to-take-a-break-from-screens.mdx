After testing more than 80 puzzles, the guide names Ravensburger’s 1,000‑piece set as the overall winner, thanks to sturdy, thick pieces and sharp images. For fewer pieces, Buffalo Puzzles offer affordable, artist‑designed 200‑400‑piece options. Kids will love EeBoo’s colorful, eco‑friendly 100‑piece dinosaur puzzle. Custom photos look best with Ravensburger’s higher‑price line. The 5,000‑piece Space Odyssey is great for big‑table challenges, while Nautilus’s wooden puzzles impress with heavy, quality pieces. Prices range from $10 to $120.
