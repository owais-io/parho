Baseball fans are buzzing about a possible salary cap that could change the sport forever. Owners want a cap to balance the big money spent by teams like the Dodgers and Mets, while players fear it would limit their earnings. The debate echoes past strikes and legal battles, and could even lead to a league shutdown. At its heart, the question is whether a cap will make teams fairer or just hurt the game’s financial future.
