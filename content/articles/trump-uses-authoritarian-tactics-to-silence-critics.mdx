Senator Elissa Slotkin says former President Donald Trump is using authoritarian tactics to silence critics. Slotkin and other Democrats were investigated after a video urged military members to refuse illegal orders, and Trump threatened investigations, arrest and even death. The senator claims the president’s actions chill free speech and mimic tactics used by dictators. Slotkin’s comments highlight a growing concern about political intimidation in the US.
