UK and US doctors and nurses say Israeli officials have stopped them from entering Gaza after they talked to the press about the war. About half a dozen medics have been denied entry, even though international law says aid must flow freely. The blocks come after the doctors voiced concerns or criticized the conflict. Israeli security agencies say they’re following rules, but many say the real reason is the doctors’ outspoken voices.
