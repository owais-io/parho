The Caf general secretary, Veron Mosengo‑Omba, ignored a recommendation that Gabon football president Pierre‑Alain Mounguengui should be barred from Caf’s executive committee because of allegations he covered up sexual abuse of young players. Despite evidence, Mosengo‑Omba dismissed the suggestion, claiming it was a conflict of interest and wanted Mounguengui’s support. The move has raised concerns about corruption and the safety of young athletes.
