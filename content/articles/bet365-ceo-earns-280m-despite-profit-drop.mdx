Bet365 owner Denise Coates took home more than £280 million in 2025, even though the company’s pre‑tax profit fell from £627 million to £349 million. Her group made £4 billion in sales, up from £3.7 billion, and she earned £104 million in salary plus a huge dividend. Bet365 is growing in the US and South America, has left China, and is still a big taxpayer in the UK.
