A 19‑year‑old Canadian backpacker, Piper James, was found dead on K'gari (Fraser Island) after a dingo attack. Coroners say she died from drowning with injuries caused by the dingoes, and no other people were involved. Her parents travelled to the island, held a ceremony, and voiced that killing the dingoes would hurt the native canids she loved. Queensland officials culled 10 dingoes, sparking ecological concerns.
