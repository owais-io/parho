Rabbi David Mason of UK charity HIAS+JCORE says his organisation will keep helping refugees even as far‑right groups attack Jewish groups with antisemitic rumours. He spoke at a Refugee Shabbat event in London, where 60 synagogues plan to host talks and meals for asylum seekers. Mason warns that anti‑refugee rhetoric and policies could hurt communities, and he urges the government to adopt kinder laws.
