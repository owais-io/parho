Binance, the world's biggest crypto exchange, is suing the Wall Street Journal for defamation after the paper said it let a network that supports Iran‑backed terror groups keep trading on its platform. The US government is also looking into Binance for possible sanctions violations. Binance says it was investigating those transactions, froze accounts, and reported the activity to authorities. The lawsuit follows a federal investigation and a huge fine.
