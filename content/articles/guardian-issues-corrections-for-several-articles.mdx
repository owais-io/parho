The Guardian today published a list of corrections to fix errors in several recent stories. They corrected a mistaken photo of Prince Andrew, a misspelt council leader’s name, and other small mistakes like mis‑reporting a woman’s title. Readers can send complaints to Guardian email or write to the Readers’ editor. This move underscores the newspaper’s commitment to accuracy.
