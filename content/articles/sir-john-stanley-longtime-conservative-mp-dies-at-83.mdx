Sir John Stanley, who died at 83, was a long‑time Conservative MP for Tonbridge and Malling, serving from 1974 to 2015. He held several ministerial roles, including housing and defence, and was known for his support of selling council homes. Stanley was involved in controversies such as the Belgrano incident, the civil‑service leak of Clive Ponting, and mishaps while at the Ministry of Defence. He was knighted in 1990 and is survived by his wife and children.
