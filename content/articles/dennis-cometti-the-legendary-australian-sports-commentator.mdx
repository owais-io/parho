Dennis Cometti was a celebrated Australian sports commentator famous for his wit and precise calling. Although he started in Western Australian football, he grew to become a national icon, covering AFL grand finals, cricket matches, and Olympic events. Cometti’s style blended humor, timing, and deep preparation—he kept detailed notes but spoke naturally. He was known for calm professionalism and memorable moments. Though he has passed away, his voice and craft continue to inspire broadcasters and fans alike.
