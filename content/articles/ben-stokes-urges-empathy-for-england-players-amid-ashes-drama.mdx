Ben Stokes is asking fans and the media to be kinder to his England teammates as the Ashes series looks doomed. England are down 3‑0 and were accused of partying like a “stag do” during a break in Noosa, with footage of Ben Duckett drunk. Stokes has made squad changes, started an internal review, and said he cares most about his players’ wellbeing, sharing his own past pressure.
