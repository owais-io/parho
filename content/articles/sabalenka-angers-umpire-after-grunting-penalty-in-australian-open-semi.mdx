Sabalenka beat Ukrainian Elina Svitolina 6‑2, 6‑3 in the Australian Open semi‑final, reaching her fourth straight final. During the match the Belarusian let out a loud grunt, and umpire Louise Engzell awarded a point to Svitolina. Sabalenka complained that the call was wrong and said it pushed her to play more aggressively. She now heads to a final against Elena Rybakina.
