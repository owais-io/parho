The Africa Cup of Nations is set to run from December 21 to January 18, a time that clashes with the European club season and gives teams only a few days to prepare. Coaches from Angola, Benin and other countries have voiced their anger at the short notice, blaming FIFA for the timing. Morocco, the host nation, is eager to win its first Afcon since 1976 and keep its fans happy.
