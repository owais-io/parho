After a new deal lets TikTok stay in the U.S., many users are deleting the app. The agreement set up a U.S.‑based company that owns most of TikTok, but users still face changes to rules, technical glitches, and worries about privacy and censorship. As a result, some creators and everyday users are unsure whether they’ll keep using the app or quit.
