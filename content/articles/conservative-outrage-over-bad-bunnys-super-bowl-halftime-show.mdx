Conservative media stars like Megyn Kelly and Trump slammed Bad Bunny’s Super Bowl halftime show for singing in Spanish and calling it un‑American. They said it threatened U.S. culture and that only English music should be played. The right even staged an “All‑American” country‑music show to protest. The author argues the reaction is angry and ignores that Bad Bunny’s set was joyful and inclusive. He points out the divide over what “America” should mean.
