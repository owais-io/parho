A new hepatitis‑B study in Guinea‑Bissau that only vaccinated half of newborns has sparked alarm. Danish researchers, linked to US vaccine critic RFK Jr, ran the trial. Health groups and the WHO say it lacks proper ethics and informed consent. The trial is now paused while experts review it, but the controversy could influence future US research funding and vaccine policy.
