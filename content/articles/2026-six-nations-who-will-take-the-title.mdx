Experts give their guesses for the 2026 Six Nations, with most betting on France or England to win. France’s star Antoine Dupont and French wing Louis Bielle‑Biarrey are seen as key to victory, while England’s depth and consistency are praised. Some writers think Italy or Scotland could surprise, and the top try‑scorer title might go to Bielle‑Biarrey again. The debate shows no clear favorite.
