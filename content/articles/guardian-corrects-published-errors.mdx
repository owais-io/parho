The Guardian has issued a notice correcting several mistakes in recent stories. It clarifies that the charity Supporting Wounded Veterans—not Cambridge researchers—raised money for a new MDMA therapy trial for veterans. It also corrects a mix‑up involving Pokémon’s Pikachu and its executive. Other errors are fixed, including a mis‑named British artist and a false claim about modern slavery in Britain. Readers can submit further complaints to the Guardian’s editorial office.
