Arsenal Women delivered a 4‑0 win over Belgian side OH Leuven in the first leg of their Champions League playoff, sending them one goal away from a quarter‑final tie with Chelsea. Frida Maanum, Olivia Smith, Alessia Russo and a late cameo by Kim Little (who made her 400th appearance) all scored or assisted. The victory came after a 1‑0 win over Manchester City and shows the squad’s depth as they rest key players for the next match.
