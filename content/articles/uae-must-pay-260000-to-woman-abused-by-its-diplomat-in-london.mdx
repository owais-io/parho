The UK High Court ordered the United Arab Emirates to pay £260,000 to a Filipino woman who was forced into modern slavery by a UAE diplomat in London. She was locked in the family's flat for 89 days, worked long hours without pay or breaks, and suffered abuse. The judge awarded damages for false imprisonment, PTSD, and punitive penalties, holding the UAE accountable for the diplomat's abuse.
