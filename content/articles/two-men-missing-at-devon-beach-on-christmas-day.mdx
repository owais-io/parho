On Christmas Day, two men aged in their 40s and 60s went missing in the water off Budleigh Salterton, Devon. Rescue teams from the Coastguard, RNLI, police and ambulance searched the beach and sea from 10 a.m. until 5 p.m. While several swimmers were rescued, the men were not found. Weather warnings of strong winds and waves made swimming dangerous, and officials urged people not to go out on Boxing Day.
