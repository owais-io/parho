Philip Pacey, who worked as a tutor‑librarian at St Albans School of Art in 1970, has died. He guided students with a relaxed, conversational style, introduced them to new writers, and oversaw a rich slide and book collection. He also hosted evening gatherings for poetry and music, leaving a lasting impact on those who studied under him.
