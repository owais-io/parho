A commuter train near Barcelona ran into a collapsed retaining wall, killing the driver and injuring 37 people, four seriously. Firefighters and ambulances rushed to the scene. Rain may have caused the wall to collapse. The accident follows a high‑speed collision in Andalucía that killed at least 42 people. Rescue teams are still checking the track for more victims.
