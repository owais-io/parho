France, Germany and the UK asked Iran to end its attacks on US bases and instead negotiate a peaceful solution. The leaders said they are not part of the strikes but are working with the US, Israel and regional partners to keep the area safe. They called for a UN meeting and for Iran to let its people decide their future. The message is that more fighting would hurt everyone.
