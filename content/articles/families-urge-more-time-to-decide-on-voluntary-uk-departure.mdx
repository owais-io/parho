Families who were told to leave the UK within a week are asking the Home Office for more time. 150 families with refused asylum claims have a 7‑day deadline to opt for a voluntary return, offered up to £10,000 per person. The Home Office warns that those who refuse may be forcibly removed, even with children handcuffed. The families fear forced deportation and are pleading for a longer decision window.
