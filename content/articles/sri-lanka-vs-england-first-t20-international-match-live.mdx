England and Sri Lanka are about to start their first T20 international match in Kandy. The game will begin after the toss, with England’s captain Harry Brook ready to use the fast bowler Jofra Archer, who returned earlier than expected. The match is a warm‑up for England ahead of the T20 World Cup and follows a poor Ashes series. Weather in Kandy may be wet, but the game should go ahead.
