After a Florida‑registered speedboat fired on Cuban border guards, four exiles were killed and six others injured. Cuba’s president said the country would defend itself firmly, warning against terrorist and mercenary attacks. The incident could spark new tensions with Washington, but diplomats are talking to calm things. U.S. officials say they had no role, while Cuban authorities claim the boat carried weapons and that its crew were exiles planning an infiltration.
