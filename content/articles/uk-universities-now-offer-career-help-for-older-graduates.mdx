Many UK universities are letting people who finished their degrees in their 40s and 50s use the campus careers office for free, even years after they graduate. Gil Hamilton, who had never applied for a new job in 18 years, found a CV coach and interview prep at Lancaster University and landed a new job. Career advisers say older graduates often miss out on skills and need a trusted source of support. The move is becoming common, with over 40 % of universities in England and Wales now offering this lifelong access.
