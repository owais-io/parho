A fire started at about 3 a.m. Saturday at the Beth Israel Congregation synagogue in Jackson, Mississippi, and was later found to be arson. No one was hurt. The blaze damaged the building, especially the library, and destroyed or damaged several Torah scrolls. Police, with help from the FBI, have arrested a suspect. The synagogue and the community say they will rebuild and stand together.
