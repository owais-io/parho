A huge crowd of 93,442 people watched the Boxing Day Test at Melbourne’s MCG, the biggest cricket crowd the venue has ever seen. The day was cold and windy, but fans in bright costumes and quirky outfits were still in high spirits. Cricket Australia is looking ahead to possible pink‑ball night tests and plans to enlarge the stadium so more people can come. It was a lively celebration of a long‑standing cricket tradition.
