Anna Bilonozhenko, a 43‑year‑old Ukrainian who escaped war, says being hit by a car during Liverpool FC’s trophy parade was the most traumatic event in her life. She broke her knee and spent nine days in hospital, still dealing with pain and psychological shock. The driver, Paul Doyle, was sentenced to over 21 years. Anna, who moved to the UK with her daughter, hopes to recover and regain safety.
