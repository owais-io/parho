Steve Bannon, the former Trump adviser, sent hundreds of texts to Jeffrey Epstein over almost a year, offering legal and media advice to protect Epstein’s reputation. The messages show Bannon guiding Epstein on how to respond to the press, plan a film, and even use the fallout to push his own right‑wing agenda and boost Donald Trump. The texts were released by a House committee. Bannon has not commented on the exchanges.
