Scotland lost 3-2 to Greece, but a 1-1 draw between Denmark and Belarus keeps their World Cup hopes alive. Greece scored early, Scotland fought back with two goals, but couldn't finish the game. The draw in Copenhagen means Scotland still has a chance to finish top of the group. Coach Steve Clarke said the team showed spirit but needs more confidence, especially in defence.
