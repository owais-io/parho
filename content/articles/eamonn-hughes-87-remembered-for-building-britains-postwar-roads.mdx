Eamonn Hughes, an Irishman who grew up in Galway, spent 45 years working on big construction projects in the UK. He started as a labourer in Leeds and later helped build parts of Leeds United’s Elland Road stadium and many roads. He married Angela, had five children, and retired early to care for her after she died. Eamonn was a quiet, dependable man who fixed things around the house well into his 80s. He is survived by his family.
