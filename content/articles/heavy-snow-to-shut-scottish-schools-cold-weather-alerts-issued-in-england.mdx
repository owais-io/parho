Heavy snow will hit northern Scotland, shutting schools in the Shetlands and delaying start times in Aberdeen. Drivers are told to plan ahead and keep winter gear in their cars. Flights from Inverness and Aberdeen were cancelled. Yellow warnings for snow and ice cover much of the UK, and health alerts have been issued for England, warning that cold can be dangerous for older people and those with health problems.
