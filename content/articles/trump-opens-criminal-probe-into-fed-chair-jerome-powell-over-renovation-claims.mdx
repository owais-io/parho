The Justice Department has started a criminal probe into Fed Chair Jerome Powell and the central bank over a renovation project in Washington. Trump has long accused the Fed of misusing money and pushed for lower interest rates, but Powell says he was honest with Congress. The investigation looks at whether Powell lied about the renovation. It follows Trump’s campaign to limit the Fed’s independence, sparking market jitters and a surge in gold and silver.
