On the opening day of the Olympic team skating event in Milan, US pair Madison Chock and Evan Bates set a high bar with a 91‑point performance, giving the U.S. an early lead. Their score pushed the team to the top of the standings, with Japan close behind. The U.S. team also had solid results from singles and pairs, keeping them in first place. Meanwhile, U.S. officials, including the vice‑president, were in town to watch the ceremony and matches.
