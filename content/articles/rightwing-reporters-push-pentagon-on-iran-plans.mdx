During a March briefing, Pentagon officials answered questions from a mix of right‑leaning and mainstream reporters about the U.S. operation against Iran. The press line up had more conservative outlets like One America News and LindellTV, but they also asked serious questions about the need for a new war, the role of Israel, and an exit plan. Some former mainstream journalists praised the shift toward tougher questioning, while others felt the new media still lagged behind in challenge.
