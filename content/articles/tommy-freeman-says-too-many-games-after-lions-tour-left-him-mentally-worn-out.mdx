The 24‑year‑old England centre Tommy Freeman admitted that playing 34 matches last season—19 for Northampton, nine for England and six for the Lions—pushed his mental health to the limit. Although he got five weeks off after the tour, he still felt anxious and tired. Coaches at Northampton and England adjusted his training to help him recover. Freeman plans to keep playing, hoping to boost England’s chances against Ireland.
