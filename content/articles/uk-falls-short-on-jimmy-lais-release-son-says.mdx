Sebastien Lai, the son of jailed Hong Kong journalist Jimmy Lai, told a UK parliamentary hearing that the government missed a chance to push for his father's release during Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s visit to China. He criticised the focus on a new Chinese embassy instead of human‑rights demands and warned that his father’s health is declining in solitary confinement, risking death in prison.
