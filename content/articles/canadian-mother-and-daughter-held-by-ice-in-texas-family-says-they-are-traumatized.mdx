A Canadian mother and her 7‑year‑old daughter with autism have been held by US ICE in Texas for almost a week. The family says the experience is traumatic and they were moved to a hard‑to‑live‑in detention center. Their documents are valid, but officials are forcing them to consider leaving the US. A congressman and lawyers are fighting to get them released.
