Patrick Reed has returned to the PGA Tour from the Saudi‑backed LIV circuit, a move that signals the failure of the Saudi experiment in golf. The $6 billion‑plus tour has struggled to attract top talent and compete with established tours. Reed’s comeback shows that players prefer the traditional structure and that the Saudi money cannot sustain a lasting rival. The LIV future looks bleak.
