UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer will speak at Downing Street about U.S. President Donald Trump’s threat to hit NATO allies, including the UK, with tariffs over a disputed Greenland deal. Starmer says the UK won’t be bullied and will work with European partners to counter the move. He has already called leaders in Washington, Brussels, and Denmark to coordinate a response. The story shows tensions between the UK, US and EU over trade and security.
