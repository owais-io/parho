Scottish SNP leader John Swinney says the UK is on the brink of a major change. He believes nationalist parties in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland are close to winning elections that could break up the union. Swinney highlighted strong support for independence, praised Glasgow’s unity after a fire, and promised money for recovery, childcare, and first‑home help. He also criticised US and Israel’s actions in Iran.
