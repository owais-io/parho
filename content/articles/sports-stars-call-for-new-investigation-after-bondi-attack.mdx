More than 60 athletes signed an open letter asking Prime Minister Anthony Albanese to set up a federal royal commission to look into antisemitism, radicalisation and the Bondi attack that killed 15 people. They say a national inquiry is needed to stop extremism and protect communities. Albanese has said a national commission would take too long and instead wants a review of security agencies by former ASIS chief Dennis Richardson. The athletes insist the issue is bigger than politics.
