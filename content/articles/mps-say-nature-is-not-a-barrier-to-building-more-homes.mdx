MPs have said nature is not stopping the UK from building more homes. Their report says the real obstacle is a lack of skilled people in ecology and planning, not the environment itself. It also criticises a new planning bill that lets developers pay a fund instead of protecting local wildlife. The report warns that the bill could break the law’s goal to stop nature from getting worse by 2030.
