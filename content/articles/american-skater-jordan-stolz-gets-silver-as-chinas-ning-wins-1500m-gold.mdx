During the Beijing Winter Games, 21‑year‑old U.S. speed skater Jordan Stolz aimed for a third gold in the 1500m, but finished with silver in 1:42.75. China’s Ning Zhongyan took gold in a new Olympic record of 1:41.98. Stolz had already won gold in the 500m and 1000m earlier. Ning’s win is China’s first speed‑skating medal at these Games. Stolz said he’s proud but disappointed.
