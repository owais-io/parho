Pinterest said it has laid off about 700 staff—roughly 15% of its workforce. Two engineers wrote a program that sniffed out who had been let go, then shared the names with others. The company called the action a breach of privacy and fired the engineers. The move comes as Pinterest, and other tech firms, shift more resources to AI while cutting jobs.
