Three Australians died after police used so‑called “less lethal” weapons like bean‑bag rounds and tasers. Inquests and court cases are now looking into how the shots were fired and whether officers followed proper rules. Critics say the weapons can still kill, yet police say they’re safer than guns. Calls for better training, clearer rules, and more oversight grow as families fight for answers.
