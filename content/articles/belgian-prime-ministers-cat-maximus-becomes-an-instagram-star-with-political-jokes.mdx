Maximus, the Belgian prime minister’s grey rescue cat, has become an Instagram hit with 142,000 followers. Since his arrival in the office, the cat’s posts carry playful comments about politics, hinting at his boss’s frustrations and boosting his warm image. The account, run lightly by De Wever’s team, shows Maximus in everyday scenes with thought bubbles about budget talks, strikes and cabinet drama. Fans laugh, while some see it as a political tactic.
