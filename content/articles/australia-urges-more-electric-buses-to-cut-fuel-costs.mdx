Fuel prices have climbed past $3 per litre, and Australia’s transport groups say it’s time to replace diesel buses with electric ones. Only 1% of Australian buses are electric, compared to 80% in China. Electric buses are cheaper to run, cleaner, and don’t depend on foreign oil. States are already planning fleets for 2040, and some new depots already have charging stations. The shift could make travel safer, quieter, and cheaper for everyone.
