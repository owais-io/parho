President Trump has announced that the U.S. should acquire Greenland, a Danish territory and NATO member. The plan has triggered a strong response from Greenland, Denmark, and several European allies, who warn it could damage NATO ties. White House officials say they’re exploring options, including a possible military move, but a poll shows only 7% of Americans support seizing the island. Trump’s comments come amid broader tensions over Venezuela and other U.S. policy moves.
