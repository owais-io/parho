House Democrats released a new batch of photos from Jeffrey Epstein’s estate, showing people like Donald Trump, Bill Clinton, Prince Andrew, and several celebrities. The 19 images first dropped, plus 70 added later, are part of a larger set of almost 100,000 pictures the House oversight committee is studying. Democrats say the photos raise questions about Epstein’s ties to powerful figures, while the White House calls the release political and unverified. More photos are expected soon.
