Florida’s public universities are facing new state rules that limit how teachers can talk about race, gender, and inequality. Many sociology professors are choosing not to change their lessons and say they’re keeping a full education. They say the vague rules chill discussion and threaten academic freedom. The move has sparked protests and worries that teachers might leave Florida or face legal trouble.
