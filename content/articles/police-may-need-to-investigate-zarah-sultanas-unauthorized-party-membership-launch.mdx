Zarah Sultana launched a new Your Party membership site without permission, which could be serious crime. The Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) said the launch might need police action, and the group that runs Your Party was told to seek help from the police. Jeremy Corbyn’s group warned followers to ignore the site. The ICO said it wouldn't intervene further, and Sultana said the issue is closed. Your Party declined to comment.
