A 23‑year‑old student, Quentin Deranque, died after a violent attack during a protest in Lyon. Police say he was hit by at least six masked people and are looking for suspects. The attack happened near a conference attended by a left‑wing MP. The case has sparked heated debate in France ahead of local elections, with the left party and the government blaming each other.
