Keir Starmer says the best way to stop the Middle‑East conflict is a negotiated settlement with Iran, not more fighting. He has refused to let the UK help US and Israel with their initial attacks and has sent extra fighter jets and anti‑missile helicopters to Cyprus to protect British people there. Starmer’s stance has sparked criticism from some politicians, but most Britons back a defensive approach.
