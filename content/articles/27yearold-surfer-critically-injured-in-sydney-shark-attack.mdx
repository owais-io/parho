Police and ambulance crews rushed to Manly Beach after a 27‑year‑old surfer from Wollongong was bitten by a shark. The attack left him with severe leg injuries and a blood loss that required CPR and 13 units of blood. He’s in a critical but stable condition. This is the third shark attack in Sydney in two days, adding to fears after a fatal attack last month. Officials advise people to stay out of the water until conditions improve.
