Iran is in Geneva negotiating its nuclear program with the U.S. The talks hinge on three U.S. demands: allow Iran to enrich uranium, let Tehran dilute its highly enriched stockpile, and not touch Iran’s missile programme. Trump’s position is uncertain; he has warned about Iranian missiles and said Iran won’t give up nuclear weapons. The meetings also touch on other U.S. policy issues, including fraud investigations in Minnesota and media coverage.
