Four Democratic primaries in Illinois were hit by a $13.7 million spend from Aipac‑linked Super PACs that hide their Israel ties. Donors who normally give to Aipac also backed three Chicago candidates, raising questions about coordination. The money has backfired as voters in urban, educated districts are turning away from Aipac‑aligned candidates, preferring progressive rivals who oppose the Gaza war. Aipac’s strategy may be losing support in the 2026 midterms.
