Nationwide said UK house prices fell 0.4% in December and were just 0.6% higher than a year earlier, the slowest growth since early 2024. The market still feels “resilient” with mortgage approvals near pre‑Covid levels, and Nationwide expects 2–4% price growth next year. Meanwhile, Lidl’s Christmas sales in the UK exceeded £1bn in the four weeks to Christmas Eve, drawing 51 million shoppers and a 70% jump in seasonal produce.
