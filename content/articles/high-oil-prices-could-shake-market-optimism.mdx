The FTSE 100 hit its first 10,000‑point mark in early 2025 but fell back after Iran’s strike on Qatar’s big gas plant. Investors still think oil will fall, but if prices stay above $100 a barrel, inflation could rise and banks might hike rates. Big oil stocks like Shell and BP benefit, while fund managers largely expect cheaper fuel by year’s end.
