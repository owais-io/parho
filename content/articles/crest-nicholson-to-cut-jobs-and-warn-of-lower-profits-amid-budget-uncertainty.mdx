Crest Nicholson, a British housebuilder, says it will close one office and cut about 50 jobs because sales have been weak and the government’s upcoming budget keeps investors on edge. The company now expects profit for the year to be on the lower end of its £28‑38 million forecast, and it warns that the market will stay tough for a while. Other builders like Taylor Wimpey are also feeling the pinch as buyers pause.
