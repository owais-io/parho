UK officials claimed billions of pounds of new AI investment, but a Guardian investigation found most of the money is already spent on existing data centers or not yet started. Politicians love big figures and use them to look successful, while the media repeat them without checking. This trick keeps people misled, erodes trust, and distracts from real economic policy. The article urges a shift from flashy announcements to real, long‑term plans.
