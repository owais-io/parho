At the University of East London, diversity and inclusion aren’t just policy words – they guide everything. Vision 2028 ties hiring, promotion and student support to fairness, aiming to match staff to the student body’s makeup. With half its professors from ethnic minority backgrounds and programmes tracking gaps, UEL keeps leaders accountable through regular reports and mentoring. Students and staff feel welcomed and valued, making the campus a model of inclusive culture.
