Luke Littler, just 18, has already won his first world title and is chasing more trophies. He’s quick, confident, and not obsessed with money like Phil Taylor, but he wants to set a new record of wins. Littler’s success has inspired young players worldwide, and the sport is expanding fast—new big events in Saudi Arabia and a move to a larger venue in London. The future looks bright for him and darts.
