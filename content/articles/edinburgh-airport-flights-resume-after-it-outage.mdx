Edinburgh Airport had to shut down its operations Friday morning because an IT glitch in its air‑traffic control system stopped all flights. Flights started again around 10:40 am, but many planes were delayed, some had to wait two hours, and a few inbound flights were rerouted to other airports. British Airways, EasyJet, Ryanair and transatlantic carriers like Delta were affected. Passengers were told to check with airlines, and the airport thanked everyone for their patience.
