Florida Congressman Maxwell Frost was punched by a man at a Sundance film festival party in Utah. The attacker said Trump would deport him and shouted racist remarks before being arrested. Frost was unharmed and thanked security and police. The suspect, Christian Young, faces burglary and assault charges, and Democrats called the incident a shocking act of hate and raised concerns about safety at events.
