Next week, Chancellor Rachel Reeves will announce extra help for UK households that rely on heating oil, whose prices have surged after the Middle East conflict. The support will come from the Crisis and Resilience Fund, giving local councils money to aid low‑income and vulnerable families, especially in rural areas and Northern Ireland where oil is the main heat source. Reeves also plans to curb unfair price hikes by petrol retailers and has asked the CMA to monitor the market.
