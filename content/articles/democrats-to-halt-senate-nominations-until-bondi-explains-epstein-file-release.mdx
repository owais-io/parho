Two Democratic senators, Jeff Merkley and Ben Ray Luján, said they'll stop civilian nominations until Attorney General Pam Bondi gives a briefing on the release of Jeffrey Epstein files. They claim the Trump administration hasn't shared information as required by the Epstein Files Transparency Act, which mandates all unclassified documents be released by December 19. Bondi hasn't provided the requested briefing, so the senators will block nominations until the issue is resolved.
