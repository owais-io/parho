A woman named Heather Danae Lewis was mistakenly accused of joining an anti‑ICE protest at a Minnesota church. Prosecutors dropped the case after learning she was actually in a supermarket parking lot at the time. She was wrongly linked to the protest because of cellphone data and a resemblance to someone on video. The charges can’t be brought again, ending the stress for her and her family.
