Jesse Jackson appeared on Sesame Street in 1972, leading young children in the chant “I am somebody.” The clip shows kids of all races repeating his words, a simple but powerful reminder that everyone deserves dignity. After Jackson’s death, the moment went viral, sparking new discussions about diversity on TV. The scene captures the spirit of the civil‑rights era and still inspires people to affirm their worth.
