Nuala McGovern shares her personal journey through miscarriage and IVF, showing how society struggles to talk about infertility. She explains why common phrases—like “I’m sure it will happen”—can feel hurtful, and stresses the need for empathy and realistic language. With stats that 1 in 7 couples face fertility problems and IVF success rates of only 25%, McGovern urges listeners to use gentle words and listen more, so people feel supported instead of judged.
