In a rain‑slick game in Rome, Italy won 27‑15 against Scotland, their second win in three years. Italy’s players were better prepared, handled the wet conditions well, and scored through clever plays and solid defense. Scotland struggled with errors and a weak set‑piece, causing them to lose their opening game. The loss puts pressure on Scotland’s coach to improve before facing England next week.
