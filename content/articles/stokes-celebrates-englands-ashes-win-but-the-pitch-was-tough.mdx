Ben Stokes celebrated England’s first Ashes win after 18 Tests without a victory. He hugged captain Joe Root and said it felt great to finish on a high. The team chased 175 on a hard, bowler‑friendly pitch that had 36 wickets in two days. Stokes praised bowlers Josh Tongue and Jacob Bethell. The pitch may trigger a review because it was too tough for a two‑day match.
