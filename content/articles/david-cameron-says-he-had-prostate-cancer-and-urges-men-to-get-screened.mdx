Former UK prime minister David Cameron has revealed he was treated for prostate cancer and is calling for a screening program to catch the disease early. He had a high PSA test, a biopsy that confirmed cancer, and received focused electric‑pulse therapy. Cameron says men should talk more about their health and wants screening to help spot cancer before it spreads. Prostate cancer is the most common cancer in UK men, but no routine screening exists yet.
