Michael Akadiri, a comedian and doctor, explains why he thinks public displays of affection on morning commutes are bad. He loves love and sees dating shows as fun, but thinks hugging or kissing in a bus or train at 8 a.m. is disruptive and should be punished. He says people should keep romance for evenings and weekends, not early‑morning rides, and urges commuters to stay professional.
