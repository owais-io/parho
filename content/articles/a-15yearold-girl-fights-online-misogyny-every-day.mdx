She is a 15‑year‑old girl who tells how Instagram and TikTok bombard her with hateful, sexist comments. Men call her a “thot” or “bitch”, judge her body, and judge her past sexual choices. The constant harassment makes her feel ugly, scared and angry. She says it damages her self‑esteem and worries about future relationships. She wonders if a ban on under‑16s could help stop this hate.
