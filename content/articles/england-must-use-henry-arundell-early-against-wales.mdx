England are strong favourites for the Six Nations, thanks to an 11‑game win streak. Their coach, Steve Borthwick, expects a win over Wales but warns it won’t be a huge score. He says the team should give young centre Henry Arundell the ball early to beat Wales’ kicking style. The squad is deep, but injuries could bite. Borthwick wants the team ready before the Scotland match.
