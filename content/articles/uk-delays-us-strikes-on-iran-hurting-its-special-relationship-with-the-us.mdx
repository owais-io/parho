UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer delayed allowing US forces to use British airbases for strikes against Iran after Iranian drone attacks. Trump called this a break in the "special relationship," saying the UK was not a reliable ally. The UK said it would only help if it served British interests and had a legal basis. Britain is now allowing US use of some bases for defensive strikes, but the delay has upset Washington.
