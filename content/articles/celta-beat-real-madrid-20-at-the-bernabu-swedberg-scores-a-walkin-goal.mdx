On Sunday, Celta Vigo stunned Real Madrid with a 2‑0 win at the Santiago Bernabéu. The first goal came when Williot Swedberg calmly walked past the chaos and into the net, a moment that felt like a movie walk‑in. Madrid were rattled by five cards in just over a minute, lost a player and played with nine men. Celta ran the ball, created chances, and finished twice, leaving Madrid embarrassed.
