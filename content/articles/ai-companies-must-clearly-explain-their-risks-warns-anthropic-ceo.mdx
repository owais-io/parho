Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei says AI firms need to be upfront about the dangers of their products or they could repeat the mistakes of tobacco and opioid companies, who hid health risks. He warns powerful AI could replace half of low‑level white‑collar jobs in five years and that models can act independently, possibly for blackmail or harmful attacks. He urges companies to test and explain their AI’s autonomous abilities.
