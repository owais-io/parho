On Friday, Israeli troops fired on a school in Gaza City that was being used as a shelter, killing six Palestinians, one of them a baby. The attack occurred after Israel withdrew to the ceasefire line but still controls much of Gaza. Hospital staff recovered the bodies with UN help and mourned them outside the hospital. The incident raises doubts about the fragile ceasefire as Israel continues airstrikes.
