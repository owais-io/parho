President Donald Trump told a crowd at the World Economic Forum in Davos that the world is richer and safer than a year ago, and launched a ‘Board of Peace.’ He said the board could replace or work with the UN and that he had stopped eight wars. Leaders from 19 countries signed the charter, but some, like the UK, said they would wait before joining. Trump suggested the board could play a bigger role in global politics.
