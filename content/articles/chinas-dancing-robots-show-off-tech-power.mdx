At China’s biggest TV event, a fleet of humanoid robots performed flips, kicks and choreographed dances. The spectacle highlights China’s advances in robotics and is used to impress viewers and signal national tech prowess. Experts say the robots still need work before they’re useful in factories, but the display shows China’s focus on moving from cheap assembly to high‑tech manufacturing.
