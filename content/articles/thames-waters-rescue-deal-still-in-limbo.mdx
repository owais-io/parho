After two years of shareholders calling Thames Water “uninvestible,” the company is still negotiating a rescue plan with its creditors and regulator Ofwat. New figures add £3.35 bn in fresh equity and boost debt to £3.25 bn, with an extra £3.3 bn line of credit for 2028. Creditors want 30 % write‑downs and more equity, but key details—like customer payouts, performance targets and future sale profits—remain vague, keeping the deal’s outcome uncertain.
