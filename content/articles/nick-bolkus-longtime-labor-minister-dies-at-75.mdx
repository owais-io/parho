Nick Bolkus, a long‑time Labor senator and minister, passed away at 75. He helped shape Australia’s multicultural policies, served in the Hawke and Keating governments, and was the first Greek Australian cabinet minister. He was known for letting many Chinese citizens stay after the 1989 Tiananmen protests. Premier Malinauskas, Prime Minister Albanese, and colleagues praised his dedication and mentorship. He is survived by his wife Mary, daughter Aria, daughter Mikayla, and son Nick.
