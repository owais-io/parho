Matt Weston and Tabitha Stoecker became the first British duo to win a gold medal at the Winter Olympics, taking home gold in the brand‑new mixed‑team skeleton race. Their combined time of 1 minute 59.36 seconds beat the German pair by 0.17 seconds. The event pairs a country’s fastest man and woman together, racing back‑to‑back. Weston, a skeleton champion, and Stoecker, his friend and teammate, celebrated a historic victory for Great Britain.
