Former South Korean President Yoon Suk Yeol was found guilty of trying to impose martial law in December 2024 and got a life‑with‑labour sentence. Many citizens and political groups think the punishment is too soft, especially after past leaders received death sentences. They fear Yoon might be pardoned later and want tougher limits on future pardons. Yoon says he still supports his plan and will keep fighting.
