After a year in the hospital with a feeding tube, I struggled to get back into daily life. I started posting simple outfit videos on TikTok to get myself dressed and feel connected again. By sharing my style—and my body honestly—I built a supportive community of almost 4,500 people. My videos highlight fashion for older, plus‑size women, showing that confidence can come from just putting clothes on. They keep me and others feeling seen and powerful.
