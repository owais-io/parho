Crypto billionaire Christopher Harborne, who has given over £12 million to Reform UK, told the Guardian he no longer wants a partnership between Reform and the Conservatives before the next election. He said he supports regulating cryptocurrency in the UK. Harborne’s large donations, including a recent £3 million, have put Reform ahead of other parties in fundraising, raising questions about limits on political gifts, especially from crypto. He also holds stakes in Tether and Bitfinex.
