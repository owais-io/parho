Toads in the UK are disappearing. Road deaths, loss of ponds, pollution, construction and warmer winters hurt them. Volunteers help by picking up toads on busy roads, saving millions of frogs. You can join a patrol or create a pond or shelter in your garden. Avoid pesticides and leave leaf litter for winter. Small actions make a big difference in keeping these amphibians alive.
