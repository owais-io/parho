Donald Trump has blasted UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer for not backing U.S. airstrikes on Iran. He said the U.K. isn’t needed, even though the U.S. is using British bases in Fairford and Diego Garcia to launch operations. The UK had just allowed four U.S. bombers to land at an RAF base and is readying its carrier HMS Prince of Wales for potential deployment. Trump’s comments highlight tension in the U.S.–U.K. “special relationship.”
