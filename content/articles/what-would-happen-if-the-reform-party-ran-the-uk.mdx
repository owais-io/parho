The article looks at how the newly‑popular Reform Party, led by Nigel Farage, has won many local seats but is already facing chaos, resignations and disagreements. It follows Green councillor Sam Jones in Warwickshire, who says the party still lacks money, keeps making costly staff hires, and wants to cut free bus passes for kids. The story shows that while the party promises quick fixes, running a council is messy and harder than it looks.
