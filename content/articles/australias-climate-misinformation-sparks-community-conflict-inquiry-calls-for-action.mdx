Australia’s Senate inquiry said climate‑related fake news is causing fights in communities, delaying renewable projects and weakening policy. The report urged the government to hold tech firms accountable for harmful messages, boost media‑literacy lessons, fund research into misinformation and its health impacts, and track hidden digital influence. It called for Australia to sign a UN pledge against climate fake news. The inquiry exposed a “denial machine” of conservative groups and warned that AI could worsen the problem.
