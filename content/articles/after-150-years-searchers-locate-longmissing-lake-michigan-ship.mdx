Seventy‑year‑old diver Paul Ehorn has finally found the wreck of the 1872 passenger steamer Lac La Belle in Lake Michigan, 150 years after it sank. After nearly 60 years of searching, he used side‑scan sonar to pinpoint the ship 20 miles off Wisconsin’s coast. The discovery adds to thousands of hidden Great Lakes wrecks that hunters fear are being damaged by invasive mussels. Ehorn will showcase the find at Wisconsin’s 2026 Ghost Ships Festival.
