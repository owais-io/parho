More than 200 flood alerts were active across the UK on Sunday after the Met Office issued a yellow rain warning for Monday. The forecast predicts 10‑15 mm of rain widely, up to 20‑30 mm in some spots, with strong south‑southeast winds. Flooding is already occurring in parts of England hit by Storm Chandra. The Environment Agency issued 96 warnings and 219 alerts. Roads, homes and businesses may flood, and traffic could be difficult on Monday.
