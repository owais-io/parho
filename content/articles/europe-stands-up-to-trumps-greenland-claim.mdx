Trump has demanded that Denmark give Greenland to the United States, sparking anger across Europe. EU leaders are considering tough tariffs on U.S. goods and the “anti‑coercion” weapon that can block American companies in the EU market. France pushes for hard action, while Germany favours dialogue. The debate shows Europe may finally stop appeasing Trump and use its economic power to protect its interests.
