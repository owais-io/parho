German officials say no doctors were prosecuted for giving Covid‑mask or vaccine exemptions, contradicting claims by US health spokesman Robert F. Kennedy Jr. The German health minister says prosecutions only happened for fraud or forgery, and patients can freely choose their treatment. Former minister Karl Lauterbach told Kennedy to focus on US issues. Kennedy has a history of spreading vaccine misinformation and conspiracies.
