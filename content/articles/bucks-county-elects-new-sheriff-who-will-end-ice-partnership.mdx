In Bucks County, a swing county in Pennsylvania, Democrat Danny Ceisler beat Republican Sheriff Frederick Harran in the sheriff election. Ceisler promised to end the county’s 287(g) agreement with ICE that lets local officers enforce federal immigration law. The move follows criticism from immigrant groups, the ACLU, and a lawsuit. The win is part of a wider Democratic surge in the county, with other local offices flipping to Democrats.
