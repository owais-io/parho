Liverpool are keen to bring Mohamed Salah back into the squad for their Champions League clash against Marseille next week. Salah will return after finishing Egypt’s Africa Cup of Nations on Saturday. Manager Arne Slot says he’s happy to have the forward back and hopes he can play in France, especially with right‑winger Alexander Isak injured. Liverpool have no rush to buy new defenders but will act if injuries worsen.
