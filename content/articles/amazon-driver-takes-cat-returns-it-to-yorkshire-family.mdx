A family in West Yorkshire was upset when an Amazon delivery driver took their cat, Nora, after a failed package delivery. The cat was filmed being carried away, but was later returned safely. The family, who has had to treat Nora for a heart condition, is relieved. Police are still investigating. Amazon said it wasn’t directly responsible but is looking into the incident. The family criticized Amazon’s response.
