Nottingham Forest won 4‑0 against Hungary’s Ferencváros, with Igor Jesus scoring twice and James McAtee sealing the game from the spot. The victory secures a place in the Europa League playoffs, where they will face either Fenerbahçe or Panathinaikos, chosen on Friday in Nyon, Switzerland. Owner Evangelos Marinakis will also be watching the draw, giving the club a much‑needed lift after a tough trip to Portugal.
