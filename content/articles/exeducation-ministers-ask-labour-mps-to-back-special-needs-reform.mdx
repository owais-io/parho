Five former UK education secretaries – Blunkett, Morris, Clarke, Kelly and Johnson – have written to Labour MPs urging them to support a major overhaul of the special educational needs (SEND) system. They say it’s a rare chance to fix a broken system and give children more help, especially in mainstream schools. Some MPs worry about new rules for EHCPs and legal appeals, but most welcome the change.
