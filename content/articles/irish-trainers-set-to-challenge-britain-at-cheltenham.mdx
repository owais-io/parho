At next month’s Cheltenham festival, Irish horse trainers are ready to compete with Britain’s best. Leaders like Gordon Elliott and Willie Mullins have dozens of runners, and Ireland won 20 races last year. Their strong teams, backed by smaller stables, are set to make a big impact. British owners and bettors should watch Irish entries closely, as the country’s deep talent could change the race outcomes.
