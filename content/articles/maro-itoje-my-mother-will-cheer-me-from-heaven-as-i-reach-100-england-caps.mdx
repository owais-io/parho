Maro Itoje said his mother will be cheering for him from heaven as he reaches his 100th England cap. He missed the training camp because of her death and now hopes to win the match against Ireland to keep the Six Nations title alive. He talks about the mix of pride and sadness, and how the team must focus to bounce back after Scotland’s loss. He also mentioned teammate Henry Pollock’s energy.
