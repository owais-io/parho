The Philadelphia Phillies are about to sign hitter Kyle Schwarber to a five‑year, $150 million deal, keeping the home‑run king in Philly after a strong 2025 season. Schwarber has been a key player for the team and has drawn interest from several clubs, but Philadelphia moved quickly to lock him in. Meanwhile, Los Angeles Dodgers signed reliever Edwin Díaz to a three‑year, $69 million contract, taking the top free‑agent closer out of the Mets. This could stir more moves in the free‑agent market.
