Last Christmas, thousands of backpackers crowded Coogee Beach in Sydney, throwing away over 20 tonnes of rubbish. The cleanup took a whole night and into Boxing Day, with workers using rakes, hoses and more to clear litter, broken glass and even urination spots. The Waverley council had warned people not to come, citing construction on Bronte Park and crowd‑control fears. Officials say they will keep beaches clean and may add fences or other rules.
