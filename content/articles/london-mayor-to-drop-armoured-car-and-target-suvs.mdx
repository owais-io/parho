Sadiq Khan, London’s mayor, said he’d push the Metropolitan Police to stop using his large armoured car and switch to smaller vehicles. He wants to tackle the surge of big SUVs because they’re more likely to kill children and cause congestion, parking problems, and traffic delays. TfL will research SUV definitions, add extra charges, cut speed limits and tweak parking rules. This is part of a 43‑measure safety plan to reduce road deaths.
