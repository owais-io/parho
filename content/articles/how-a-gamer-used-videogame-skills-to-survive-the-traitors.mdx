Jade Scott, a gamer, used experience from strategy and social deduction games to navigate the reality‑TV show The Traitors. She played Minecraft, CS:GO, Dota, Project Winter and Dale & Dawson. On the show she stayed quiet, used observation, and defended herself in meetings. Her gaming helped her think through accusations and build arguments. After the show she reflected on how it helped her PhD viva defense and moved on to other games.
