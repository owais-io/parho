Donald Trump welcomed Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu to Mar‑a‑Lago for a lunch meeting. They talked about Gaza, saying Hamas must disarm or face big consequences, and that Israel might hit Iran’s missile sites if it keeps building weapons. Trump said he and Netanyahu disagreed on the West Bank but will resolve it later. He also claimed a pardon for Netanyahu was coming and that he would receive an Israeli prize. The meeting mixed politics and bragging.
