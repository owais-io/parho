Europe’s birth rates are falling below the level needed to keep the population steady, meaning the continent could shrink in a few generations unless more people move in. Monbiot points out that Trump’s claim immigrants will destroy Europe is a racist scare‑tactic. He says we need migrants to keep societies alive and that history shows mixing cultures benefits everyone. The real solution is better sustainability, not blaming newcomers.
