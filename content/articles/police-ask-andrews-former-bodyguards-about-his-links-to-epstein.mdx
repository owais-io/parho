The Met police are asking people who once protected Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor to share any clues they may have about his ties to Jeffrey Epstein. Investigators are looking at whether London airports helped move trafficked people and whether the prince’s bodyguards ignored his visits to Epstein’s island. No new sexual‑offence reports have come in yet, and no wrongdoing by the officers has been found so far.
