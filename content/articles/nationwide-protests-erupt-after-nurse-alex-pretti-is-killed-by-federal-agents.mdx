After a Minneapolis nurse, Alex Pretti, was shot dead by federal agents, people across the country staged protests. From Minneapolis to New York, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Boston and Providence, crowds shouted against ICE and demanded justice. The demonstrations followed another death, that of Renee Good, and highlighted complaints that federal officers used excessive force and misrepresented Pretti’s weapon status. Protesters faced cold weather and called for the abolition of ICE.
