Jake Paul, the YouTuber turned boxer, fought champion Anthony Joshua in a $138 million event that was as much a media stunt as a sports match. The bout, streamed by Netflix, drew cheap tickets and huge online attention, with Paul getting knocked out in the final round. The fight shows how boxing can turn fame into profit, but also highlights problems: cheap entertainment, celebrity fighters, and a sport that still promises hope to the poor.
