Norwegian biathlete Sturla Holm Lægreid apologized publicly for cheating after winning a bronze medal. His former girlfriend said it is hard to forgive, even after his public apology. She was upset that the story appeared on a day when biathlon celebrated a teammate’s death. Lægreid tried to explain he had made a mistake and wants to focus on the Olympics. He promised no more comments.
