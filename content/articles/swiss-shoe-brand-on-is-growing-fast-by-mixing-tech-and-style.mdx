On, a Swiss shoe company, uses clever tech like a “flying fibre” spray to make light, cushioned running shoes. It partners with stars such as Roger Federer, Zendaya, and FKA Twigs, and has opened new stores in London. The brand’s high‑end shoes start at about £120, and it’s selling millions of pairs worldwide. On aims to build more local factories to cut shipping and carbon, while keeping its shoes stylish and affordable.
