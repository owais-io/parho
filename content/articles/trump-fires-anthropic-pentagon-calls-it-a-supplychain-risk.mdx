Trump bragged about cutting ties with AI firm Anthropic, saying he fired them like dogs. That same day, the Pentagon officially named Anthropic a “supply‑chain risk,” banning all government use of its tech. Meanwhile, talks between the defense department and the company have restarted, and the military already uses Anthropic’s Claude chatbot for intelligence. The move could hurt Anthropic’s $60 billion funding, but Microsoft says it can keep working with the company on non‑defense projects.
