Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick admitted he once ate lunch with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein on Epstein’s private island in 2012. He told the Senate committee while lawmakers from both parties are demanding he step down. Lutnick says the meeting was brief and he has no ongoing ties to Epstein. Trump’s team still backs him, but critics see the association as a risk.
