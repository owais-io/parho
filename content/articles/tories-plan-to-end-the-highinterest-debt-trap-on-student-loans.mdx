Kemi Badenoch announced the Conservative Party will remove the real‑interest rates that make plan 2 student loans grow faster than repayments, a move aimed at easing the financial burden on graduates. Labour ministers instead focus on maintenance grants and are not tackling the high interest rates. The debate has highlighted worries about the fairness of the current system and the value of some university courses.
