On Saturday, Sydney’s Mardi Gras parade will attract about 350,000 people as 170 floats and 10,000 marchers light up Oxford Street. After the parade, pop icon Grace Jones will perform in the Opera House forecourt and Bad Bunny will play at Engie Stadium to 40,000 fans. The city will run an all‑night metro service, extend venue hours, and police will keep the event safe while Ramadan markets draw 50,000 visitors.
