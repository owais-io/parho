Casper Ruud was beaten by Ben Shelton 6‑4, 6‑3, 6‑4 in the Australian Open fourth round. The Norwegian, who has been hoping to go home early, will leave if his wife goes into labour. Ruud said he’d fly back to Norway the moment she needs help. Shelton’s strong serve gave him the edge, while other top players like Jannik Sinner and Novak Djokovic are also moving ahead in the tournament.
