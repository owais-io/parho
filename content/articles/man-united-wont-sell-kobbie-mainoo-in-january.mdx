Manchester United says it will not sell midfielder Kobbie Mainoo in January because they believe he can develop into a key player. Coach Ruben Amorim, who hasn't started him this season, is willing to loan him out but wants him to earn a spot through training. The club is on a winning streak, losing only once in nine games, and owner Jim Ratcliffe has given Amorim a three‑year window to prove himself.
