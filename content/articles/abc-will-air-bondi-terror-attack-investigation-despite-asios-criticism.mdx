ABC is set to show a Four Corners investigation of the Bondi beach terror attack, even though Australia’s intelligence agency, ASIO, says the episode contains major mistakes. ASIO claims the ABC’s stories about the shooters’ ties to Islamic State are false and warns it may take action. The program examines how the shooters were radicalised, intelligence failures, and links to a terror cell.
