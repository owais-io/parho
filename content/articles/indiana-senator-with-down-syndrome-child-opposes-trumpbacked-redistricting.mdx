Michael Bohacek, a state senator who has a daughter with Down syndrome, said he would vote against Indiana’s congressional map changes that favor Republicans. He was prompted to act after President Trump used a slur against a political opponent. Bohacek wants to stand up for people with disabilities and resist Trump’s pressure. Some GOP lawmakers in Indiana have already received threats, and the redistricting plan will be debated in December.
