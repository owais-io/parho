Judge Christina Snyder halted California’s rule that would have barred ICE officers from wearing masks. The law still requires federal agents to show their badge and agency name. California was first to try a mask ban after high‑profile ICE raids. The Department of Justice said the law unfairly targeted the federal government. The mask ban is suspended, but the identification rule remains in effect.
