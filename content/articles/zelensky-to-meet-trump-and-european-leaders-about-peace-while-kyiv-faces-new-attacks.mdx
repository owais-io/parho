Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelensky, is heading to Florida to talk about peace with U.S. President Donald Trump and European leaders, including EU chief Ursula von der Leyen. They want to finish a 20‑point plan that covers security guarantees, rebuilding, and land disputes. The talks come after fresh missile strikes on Kyiv that injured 11 people and caused fires. Russia and Ukraine are still debating the terms, while Moscow warns its own side is ready to negotiate.
