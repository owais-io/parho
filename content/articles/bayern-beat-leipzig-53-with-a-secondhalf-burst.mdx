Bayern Munich turned a 1‑0 deficit into a 5‑3 win over RB Leipzig with a thunder‑clap second half. After a quiet first half, Bayern’s forwards—Gnabry, Olise and Musiala—scored five goals, with Gnabry’s 100th club goal and Olise’s hat‑trick turning the tide. Manager Vincent Kompany praised the team’s relentless pressing, and the game showed Bayern’s ability to shut down opponents after the break. Their performance left Leipzig scrambling and highlighted Bayern’s dominance in the league.
