Trump announced the U.S. had carried out attacks on Iran, joined by Israel, after intelligence showed a chance to target the country's leaders. The strikes hit Tehran and reportedly killed Supreme Leader Khamenei and several top officials. The move followed stalled talks over Iran's nuclear program and missile buildup. Trump said Iran refused to discuss its missiles, while Israel had been tracking Khamenei's movements. The attacks drew criticism from Oman and raised concerns about regional stability.
