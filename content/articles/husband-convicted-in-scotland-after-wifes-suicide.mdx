Husband convicted after his wife, who had suffered abuse for 18 months, jumped from a motorway bridge to end her life. Lee Milne was found guilty of culpable homicide and domestic abuse in a Glasgow court, a first of its kind in Scotland. The case highlighted ongoing violence, previous assault convictions, and the impact on families. He faces sentencing on 10 April, marking a landmark legal step against domestic violence.
