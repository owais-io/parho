Plymouth Argyle has been in turmoil for two years, changing managers five times and dropping to League One. Their 1‑0 win over Rotherham on Saturday lifted the pressure of a second straight relegation and gave fans a break. Tom Cleverley, the new manager, is focused on steady results and building a solid defence. Owner Simon Hallett still aims for a top‑six Championship spot, but the club’s finances are tight and supporters remain on edge.
