Trump said he wants a future deal for Greenland, promising to drop tariffs on eight European countries. He described a framework that could give the U.S. sovereignty over small parts of Greenland for military bases. Danish officials said they’re not ready for that and Danish parliament members said NATO control is “out of the question.” The deal is still vague, and Denmark’s approval is unclear.
