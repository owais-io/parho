President Trump is preparing for a possible air strike on Iran while also hosting a first‑time meeting of his new “Board of Peace” in Washington. The Board, made up of 45 mostly authoritarian countries, is set to oversee Gaza’s reconstruction after a $5 bn pledge, but critics say it replaces the UN and excludes Palestinians. Meanwhile, the US has sent new fighters and a carrier to the Middle East, and Russia‑Iran drills have heightened tensions.
