Trump’s threat to slap 25% tariffs on eight European countries over a Greenland deal has sent investors scrambling. Gold jumped 1.6% to $4,666 an ounce, topping an all‑time high of $4,689, while silver climbed to $94.08 before settling at $93.15. European stock indices fell, hitting drops in France, Germany, Spain and the FTSE 100. Carmakers and tech shares on European exchanges slipped, and the dollar weakened against the Swiss franc and yen.
