Senegal won 3‑0 against Botswana in Group D at the 2025 Africa Cup of Nations. Nicolas Jackson scored twice, giving Senegal an early lead and a second goal before the hour. Cherif Ndiaye sealed the win with a late strike. The 2021 champions showed their strength and look favourites for the title. The match was played in Tangier and Senegal’s victory puts them on track to top the group.
