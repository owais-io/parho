After Iran shut off its internet, police and soldiers fired on protest crowds in many cities. Doctors and witnesses say thousands were killed, with estimates up to 35,000. Official counts say about 3,100, but doctors claim the real number may be 10 times higher. Many medics were arrested for helping injured protesters, and the government is refusing to explain what happened.
