David Moyes says Everton winger Jack Grealish will miss the rest of the season after a fractured foot that likely needs surgery. The injury rules him out of the club’s campaign and cuts his chances of a World Cup call‑up. Moyes added that Everton could buy Grealish for £50 m this summer, but the cost and his recovery are uncertain later.
