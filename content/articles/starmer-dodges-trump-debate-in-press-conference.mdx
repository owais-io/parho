UK Labour leader Keir Starmer held a press conference to sidestep criticism over US President Donald Trump’s remarks about the Iran war. He emphasised Britain would stay out of the fighting, protect citizens and keep oil supplies flowing, but kept details vague. The meeting also revealed Starmer’s growing fatigue from juggling war, domestic issues and leadership challenges. He was asked about former minister Peter Mandelson, giving confusing answers. The story shows Starmer struggling to balance diplomacy, policy and party politics.
