An Afghan asylum seeker, 23, was found guilty of raping and abducting a 12‑year‑old girl and filming the act. He was sentenced to 15 years in prison, plus an extra 12 months on licence. The case sparked anti‑asylum protests in Nuneaton, where some demonstrators wore Nazi‑style shirts. The judge said the victim still suffers trauma and that the defendant knew the girl was under 16. The defendant, who fled Afghanistan in 2022, had applied for asylum in the UK.
