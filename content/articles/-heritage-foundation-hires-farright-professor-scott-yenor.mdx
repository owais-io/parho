**  
The Heritage Foundation, already criticized for defending antisemitic views, has appointed Scott Yenor—known for anti‑feminist, homophobic, and Christian‑nationalist remarks—as director of its Kenneth Simon Center. Yenor is linked to secret male‑only groups and has promoted radical ideas about family and sexuality. The move sparked backlash from conservatives and watchdog groups, who say it signals a shift toward extremist positions. Heritage is facing renewed criticism for ignoring the strong objections.
