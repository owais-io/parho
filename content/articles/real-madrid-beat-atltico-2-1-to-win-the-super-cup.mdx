In a tense match in Jeddah, Real Madrid held off Atlético Madrid 2‑1. Fede Valverde opened the scoring early, and Rodrygo added a second after a late burst from the Spanish side. Atlético had several chances and fought hard, but Real’s defense and goalkeeper Courtois kept the advantage. With the win, Real advance to a Sunday showdown with Barcelona.
