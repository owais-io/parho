A bailiff boarded a Ryanair flight at Linz airport because the airline didn’t pay a passenger €890 in legal costs and compensation for a 13‑hour delay. A seizure sticker was put on the cabin, giving the court control over the plane. The aircraft still flew to London, but the court can auction it if Ryanair fails to pay by the deadline.
