The Bureau of Meteorology (BoM) said its new public website cost $96.5 million—much more than the $4.1 million it had originally quoted. The federal environment minister is unhappy and wants the BoM’s chief executive to explain the extra spending and how future budgets will be handled. The additional money covered building, testing, and securing the site’s complex data systems. BoM has made updates to improve usability after launch criticism.
