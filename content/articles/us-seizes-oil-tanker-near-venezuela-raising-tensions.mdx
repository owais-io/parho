US forces boarded a crude‑oil tanker off Venezuela’s coast to seize it, showing a video on social media. The tanker was carrying oil that the US says is linked to sanctions on Venezuela and Iran. The move is part of President Trump’s pressure on Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro, who has urged his people to stand up against the U.S. The incident has made the already‑tense U.S.–Venezuela relationship even more volatile.
