Tarryn Allarakhia and Haji Mnoga met on a National League pitch in 2023 and have since become friends and teammates in the Tanzania national team. Both play club football in England – Rochdale and Salford – and are heading to Morocco for the Africa Cup of Nations. Tanzania is still chasing its first win in the tournament. The pair hope to bring experience and a bit of history to the side as they face Nigeria, Tunisia and Uganda.
