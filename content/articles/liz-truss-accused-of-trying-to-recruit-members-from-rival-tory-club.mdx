Liz Truss is being criticised for walking into the luxury 5 Hertford Street club and asking members to join her new club at Leconfield House, which would cost a hefty £500,000. 5 Hertford members pay under £2,000 a year and enjoy top‑tier perks. Truss’s club aims to be a “strategic nexus” for business and politics, but many insiders feel the move is disrespectful and unlikely to win new members.
