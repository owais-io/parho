Because of President Trump’s trade wars and the Iran attack, global markets are shaky. That has pushed up oil prices and made the UK property market uncertain. As a result, construction projects worth more than £100 million have fallen by over a third in early 2024. Builders are worried, councils face lower tax income, and some developers want to cut affordable homes. If the private sector keeps leading, the UK may struggle to meet housing and growth targets.
