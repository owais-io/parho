The US has announced it will carry out more airstrikes against Islamic State camps in north‑western Nigeria, saying the moves will help stop attacks on Christians. Nigerian officials say the strikes are part of a joint effort with the US and that they have intelligence and cooperation. No deaths were reported in the latest hit, but villagers describe fear and confusion. The fighting is part of a wider pattern of violence that has left many Nigerians hurt.
