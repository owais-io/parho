Haitians living in Springfield, Ohio, are scared of an upcoming ICE raid and unsure about their legal status. A judge recently blocked the Trump administration from ending the Temporary Protected Status that keeps them in the U.S., but many still worry about deportation. Local businesses and restaurants have closed, and some families are leaving for Latin America. City officials say they’re monitoring the situation, but residents fear loss of jobs and community ties.
