A senior Australian defence official warned that if the Aukus partnership with the US and UK fails, Australia could end up with no submarines. He said there is no “Plan B” and that the government is committed to Aukus, which promises eight nuclear‑powered subs. Critics point to slow US shipbuilding and unclear guarantees. The debate highlights Australia’s struggle to replace its aging Collins class submarines.
