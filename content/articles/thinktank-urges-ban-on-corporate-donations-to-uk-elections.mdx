Thinktank CenTax says UK law on corporate donations still lets foreign money influence elections. Their study found over 4,000 companies gave £293 m to parties, many owned by people who can’t vote in Britain. The new bill’s checks are too weak and can be dodged. CenTax calls for an outright ban or stronger rules, plus mandatory registration and disclosure of company owners. UK ministers say the changes will keep democracy safe.
