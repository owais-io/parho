Guardian is correcting a handful of recent articles. It notes Waymo’s self‑driving tech isn’t fully finished yet, a mistaken club name in an England squad list, and a few other small errors in stories about politics, sports, and the Middle East. The corrections also cover a misnamed player and a misstatement about a nuclear facility incident. The notice gives the right information and invites readers to send complaints to the editors.
