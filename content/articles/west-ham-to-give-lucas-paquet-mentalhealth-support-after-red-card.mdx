West Ham will provide psychological help to midfielder Lucas Paquetá after he was sent off for dissent in the Liverpool game. Paquetá has been under a lot of pressure, still dealing with a two‑year FA spot‑fixing investigation and the recent red card. The club says it won’t excuse his behaviour but wants to help him stay focused. Meanwhile, they’re watching his form, as the team’s survival chances depend on his performance.
