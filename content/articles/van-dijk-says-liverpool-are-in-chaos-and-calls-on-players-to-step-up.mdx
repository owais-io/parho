Van Dijk was furious after Liverpool lost 3‑0 to Nottingham Forest, saying the team is in chaos and the manager is let down. He blamed the players for not taking responsibility and for conceding many set‑piece goals. The Dutch captain also criticised the lack of form from new signings and the team’s poor consistency. He vows to rally the squad for their next game at West Ham.
