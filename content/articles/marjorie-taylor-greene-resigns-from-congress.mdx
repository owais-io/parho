Marjorie Taylor Greene announced she’s quitting the U.S. House, sparking surprise from many and a grin from President Trump, who praised the move. Her resignation comes as the GOP faces deeper splits over Trump‑style politics and recent controversies. Greene said she left because of her self‑respect, family concerns and the pressure of a hostile primary she’d face if she stayed. Some think she might even run for president later, but she has denied those rumors.
