The federal government has drafted a bill that will criminalise the promotion or incitement of hatred with intent, carrying up to five years in prison. It also expands visa cancellation rules for people linked to hate or extremist groups, creates a new hate‑group list, and tightens gun ownership checks. A national buy‑back scheme will be introduced to remove certain firearms from the public.
