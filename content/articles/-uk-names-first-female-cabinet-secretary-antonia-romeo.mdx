** Prime Minister Keir Starmer has announced that Dame Antonia Romeo will become the UK’s new cabinet secretary, the first woman to hold the post in the country’s 100‑year history. Romeo has spent nearly a decade leading major departments such as the Home Office, Ministry of Justice and International Trade. The appointment was fast‑tracked after the previous secretary was dismissed, sparking debate over the selection process and her past management style.
