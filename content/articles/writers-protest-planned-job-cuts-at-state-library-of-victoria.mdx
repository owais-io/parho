Author Helen Garner and more than 200 writers signed an open letter to the State Library of Victoria, saying the library is being turned into a “party hub” and that planned job cuts and a shift to digital tourist projects would hurt its public role. The proposal would cut 39 staff, halve reference librarians from 25 to 10 and outsource IT. A union petition has 2,260 signatures. The library says it has consulted staff, while the government says the board is responsible.
