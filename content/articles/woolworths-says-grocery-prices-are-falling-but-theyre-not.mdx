Woolworths claims grocery prices have fallen for eight quarters, but most shoppers find everyday items are more expensive. The chain uses a method that averages the price of goods actually bought, so when customers switch to cheaper products the overall average drops. The Australian Bureau of Statistics, which uses Woolworths data, shows food prices rose 3.4% last year. Critics say the claim is misleading and masks rising costs.
