Damien Egan, a Labour MP from Bristol who supports Israel, was set to speak at a local school but the visit was called off after a pro‑Palestine group, the National Education Union and parents pushed back. The school said a protest was planned outside and cancelled the talk to keep students safe. The MP hasn’t spoken yet, and it’s unclear if the visit will happen later.
