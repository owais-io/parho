In Shotton Colliery, a 10‑year‑old memorial Christmas tree was cut down after its lights were switched on. Police arrested a 26‑year‑old who is now in custody and released a 23‑year‑old on suspicion. The tree commemorated WWI soldiers and was a big community event. Villagers and the church called the act “mindless vandalism” and hoped the culprits would be punished. Police are still looking for witnesses and CCTV footage.
