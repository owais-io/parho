In a small NSW town, the local council voted to stop flying the Aboriginal flag in its chambers and only display the Australian flag, saying it would show unity. The move left Aboriginal elders and residents angry, saying it erases their history and culture. They’re concerned the council’s new protocol also limits welcome‑to‑country ceremonies. The decision will be reviewed after public comments.
