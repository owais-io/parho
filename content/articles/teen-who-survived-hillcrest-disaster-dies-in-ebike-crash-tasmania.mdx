Lucas Reid, a 15‑year‑old from Devonport who had survived the 2021 Hillcrest jumping‑castle tragedy that killed six kids, was killed on New Year’s Eve when he lost control of his e‑bike and crashed into a pole. The accident happened at Don Heads Road, where a passer‑by helped but he died on the spot. Officials and the GWS Giants club praised his resilience, and police are investigating the crash.
