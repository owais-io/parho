Australian 19‑year‑old Maya Joint was upset in straight sets by 15‑year‑old Czech Tereza Valentová. Joint struggled with serve and unforced errors, losing 6‑4, 6‑4 in 92 minutes. She said she’ll learn from the loss and come back stronger next year. In the same tournament, wildcard Taylah Preston won her first Grand Slam match, beating Zhang Shuai 6‑3, 2‑6, 6‑3.
