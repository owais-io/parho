Newport County boss Christian Fuchs, a former Leicester player, is determined to keep his club in the Football League. He says he’s stubborn and will grab any chance to develop talent. Fuchs learned from managers like Tuchel and Rodgers and now uses data to improve the team. Newport have only three wins this season and have struggled at home, but he wants to build a stronger fortress. He retired just three years ago and still trains with the squad.
