Jannik Sinner beat Ben Shelton in the quarter‑final and will now play Novak Djokovic in the semi‑final at the Australian Open. Sinner says Djokovic is a very tough opponent and that he needs to play his best. He’s on a 20‑match win streak, has a 9‑1 record against Shelton, and has never lost to Djokovic since 2023. The match will be a real test of his game.
