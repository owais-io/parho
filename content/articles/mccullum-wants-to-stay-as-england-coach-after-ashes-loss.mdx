Brendon McCullum says he will keep coaching England even though Australia won the Ashes. Replacing him would cost the board a lot, so the decision is up to senior officials. The England board is nervous about a change because the team has lost many games this year. McCullum focuses on mindset and supporting players, hoping England can bounce back in the next two Tests and prepare for the World Cup.
