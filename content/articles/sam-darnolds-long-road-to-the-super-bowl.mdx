Sam Darnold has bounced between NFL teams after a rough rookie year with the Jets, stints with the Panthers, Vikings, and finally the Seahawks. Despite many setbacks, he improved in Seattle, leading them to the Super Bowl. The story follows his comeback journey, showing how he kept fighting, found a supportive coach in Kyle Shanahan, and earned a chance to play in the biggest game. It’s a tale of resilience and second chances.
