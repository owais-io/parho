Kemi Badenoch sent a letter to Conservative MPs urging them to stop any moves that could weaken the party from inside. She highlighted recent defections – including Robert Jenrick, Nadhim Zahawi and Andrew Rosindell – and said they don’t signal a shift to the centre, but rather a problem with character and loyalty. Badenoch called for staff and MPs to act in line with the party’s values and warned against internal sabotage.
