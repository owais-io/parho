Eli Katoa, a Melbourne Storm rugby‑league player, had emergency brain surgery after a bleed during a Tonga game. He spent more than two weeks in hospital and is now home, on the road to recovery. Katoa says he hopes to play again next season and thanks his partner and Storm fans for their support. The club will get salary‑cap relief, but his return is still uncertain.
