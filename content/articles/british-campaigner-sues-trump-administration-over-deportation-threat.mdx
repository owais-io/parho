Imran Ahmed, head of a UK anti‑hate group, was warned the U.S. could deport him for his work against online hate. He sued Trump allies, claiming the threat violates his U.S. rights. A judge granted a temporary restraining order blocking any arrest. The case highlights U.S. actions against European critics of big tech. The UK says it supports efforts to keep the internet safe.
