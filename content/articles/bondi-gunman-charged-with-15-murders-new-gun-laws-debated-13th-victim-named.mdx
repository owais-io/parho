The Bondi gunman Naveed Akram faces 59 charges, including 15 murders, after a shooting that killed 15 people. NSW’s parliament was recalled to tighten gun laws, and the premier wants rules to curb protests tied to terrorism. Political leaders called for responsibility and anti‑antisemitism screening. The 13th victim, Boris Tetleroyd, was identified, and 21 people remain in hospital, one police officer losing sight in one eye.
