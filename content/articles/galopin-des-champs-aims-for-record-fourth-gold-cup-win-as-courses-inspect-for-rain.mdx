Rain and weather worries have prompted safety inspections at Leopardstown and Sandown before Saturday's big racing weekend. The courses are deemed fit but could get more rain overnight. The highlight is Willie Mullins' Galopin Des Champs, who has chased three Gold Cup wins and is favoured to try for a record fourth. Other races will feature top hurdlers and chasers, but Galopin's performance will draw most attention.
