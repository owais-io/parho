Subsidies that lowered health‑insurance costs under the Affordable Care Act expired, pushing premiums above $1,500 for many people. Congress is trying to bring the subsidies back, but the deal has hit a familiar roadblock: abortion coverage. Republicans want stricter limits on abortion in ACA plans, while Democrats say that’s unacceptable. The Senate hasn’t acted yet, and some states have extended open enrollment to try to help people catch up.
