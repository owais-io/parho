A new survey shows that 73% of UK teens and young adults are nervous about their careers and the economy. Many fear AI will hurt job chances, with 59% concerned. About three‑in‑five feel unprepared for work, 64% think entry‑level roles are insecure, and a quarter lack the right qualifications or experience. Local job shortages hit 31%, while 12.5% are NEET. The UK ranks low among OECD youth job prospects.
