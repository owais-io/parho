A drone struck the RAF Akrotiri base in Cyprus, causing little damage. Around the same time, the UK said it would let the U.S. use British bases to defend against Iranian missile threats. The decision sparked sharp reactions from UK politicians, with critics calling it a mistake and supporters saying it protects British citizens abroad. The move shows Britain’s shifting stance on Middle‑East tensions.
