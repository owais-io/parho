People in Australia are drinking less cow milk and more oat, soy and almond milk. Researchers say plant milks give lower greenhouse‑gas emissions than dairy. Oat milk uses the least water, while almond milk uses the most. Processing and packaging also add to the impact, especially for plant milks. There isn’t a single best choice, so mixing different milks can help lower the overall environmental cost.
