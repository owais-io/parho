Readers told us their top 2025 games. The Guardian shared a long list of fan‑picked titles, from the cinematic Death Stranding 2 to the new VR hit Ghost Town. Fans praised everything from the storytelling of Lies of P to the multiplayer fun of Split Fiction. Other highlights were Ghost of Yōtei, Mechabellum, Monster Train 2, Arc Raiders and the nostalgic Chicory. The article thanks readers and invites them to keep sharing ideas for future editions.
