Four years after Russia’s invasion, the war has cost both sides huge losses. Around 415,000 Russians and 600,000 Ukrainians are thought to have died, and millions of Ukrainians are displaced. Despite relentless fighting and heavy bombings, Ukraine’s people and government keep resisting. Talks with Moscow have stalled, and Europe must keep supporting Ukraine with aid and sanctions to help it survive and eventually end the conflict.
