A police officer in Sydney said he didn’t have time to turn on his body‑worn camera before he shot a 41‑year‑old man who was having a psychotic episode. The man was stabbed with kitchen knives and was fired on three times in his driveway. The incident is the second of four deaths in 2023 after police interactions with vulnerable people, sparking calls for better mental‑health policing and camera use.
