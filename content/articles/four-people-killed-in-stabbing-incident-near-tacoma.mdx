Four people were killed when a 32‑year‑old man attacked others on a street in Wauna, a small town west of Tacoma, after violating a protection order. The sheriff’s office says all four victims and the suspect died. The attack happened at a house while officers were responding to a call about the man’s no‑contact order, which was invalid because it hadn’t been served. Investigators are looking into the incident.
