The French energy firm Engie has agreed to buy UK Power Networks, which runs power lines across London, southeast, and east England, for £10.5 billion. The deal gives Engie a big step toward its goal of becoming a top energy‑transition company. UKPN serves 8.5 million customers on 192,000 km of cables. The purchase comes as the UK upgrades its grid to support green energy, with planned spending of £22 billion by 2028.
