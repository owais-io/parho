A 23‑year‑old far‑right activist, Quentin Deranque, was killed after a clash in Lyon. Eleven suspects, including aides to Jean‑Luc Mélenchon’s LFI party, were arrested. The case has shocked France, hurting LFI’s reputation and showing its ties to the now‑banned anti‑fascist group Jeune Garde. The far‑right party, RN, is using the tragedy to push itself into the mainstream, while the left is split over whether to distance itself from Mélenchon.
