Blackpool hospital’s leaked RCP report found a culture of bullying, harassment and racial discrimination that hurt patient safety. Staff said they were overworked, sent to see patients without supervision, and felt afraid to speak up. Several employees have been jailed for drug theft, assault and other crimes. The trust delayed sharing the report, but has since hired new doctors and added training, though investigations continue.
