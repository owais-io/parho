On the fourth anniversary of Russia’s invasion, UN Secretary‑General António Guterres urged an immediate ceasefire, saying the war stains humanity. The General Assembly passed a resolution backing Ukraine’s borders and condemning Russian attacks, but the US and China abstained. Guterres said more concrete steps were needed. Ukrainian leaders and NATO allies called for an unconditional ceasefire, while U.S. senators drafted a bipartisan resolution to support Ukraine.
