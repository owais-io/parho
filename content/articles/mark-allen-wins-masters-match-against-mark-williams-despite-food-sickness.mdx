Mark Allen fought through food poisoning to beat Mark Williams 6‑2, winning five straight frames and earning a spot in the Masters quarter‑finals. The 2018 champion said he had been ill for a week but decided to give his best. Zhao Xintong also won 6‑2 against Gary Wilson, stringing together four back‑to‑back centuries to reach the quarter‑finals. These wins set the stage for the upcoming clashes.
