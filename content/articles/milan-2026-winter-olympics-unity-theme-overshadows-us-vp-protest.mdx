At Milan’s 2026 Winter Olympics opening, the theme was harmony. The show mixed Italian culture, dance, and famous singers. But the presence of US Vice‑President J.D. Vance and ICE officials sparked protests. Fans booed while the crowd cheered the US team. IOC president Kirsty Coventry highlighted the idea of “ubuntu” – we rise together. The ceremony finished with President Mattarella declaring the Games open.
