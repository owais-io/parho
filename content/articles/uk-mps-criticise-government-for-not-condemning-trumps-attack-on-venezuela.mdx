UK MPs from Labour, Conservative and Liberal Democrat parties criticised ministers for not condemning Trump’s attack on Venezuela. They want the foreign secretary and prime minister to call it illegal and stand up for international law. The government has stayed quiet, saying it will wait to decide if the move breaks the law. MPs fear this silence could embolden other countries like China and Russia.
