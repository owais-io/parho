A former undercover police officer, Carlo Soracchi, is accused of telling anti‑fascist activists to set fire to a charity shop linked to the far right. The activists say they rejected the idea and never acted on it. The Spycops inquiry is now looking into whether Soracchi fabricated reports and tried to provoke violent actions.
