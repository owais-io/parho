Ontario’s Premier Doug Ford told reporters that whisky lovers should buy Crown Royal now because he intends to ban the brand from the province. Ford’s move comes after Diageo, the owner of Crown Royal, announced it would shut its Ontario plant and shift production to the U.S. He’s also said he’ll keep an eye on other Diageo brands that sell well in Ontario.
