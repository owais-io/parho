Darick Moore, a 24‑year‑old from Clay County, was arrested after police say he shot and killed six people, including his father, brother, uncle, a local pastor, and a 7‑year‑old girl. He also stole a truck used to travel between the homes. The case may go to capital murder and could bring the death penalty. Police are still investigating why he did it, and he will appear in court next week.
