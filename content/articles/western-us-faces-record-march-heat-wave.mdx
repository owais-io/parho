California, Nevada and Arizona are bracing for the most intense March heat on record. The National Weather Service has issued a heat advisory for the Bay Area and central coast, warning temperatures could hit 90°F and set new monthly highs. Las Vegas will see 95–100°F, while some low‑elevation areas could reach 103°F. The heat threatens ski resorts, reduces snowpack, and raises health risks, so officials urge people to stay hydrated and indoors.
