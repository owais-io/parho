Three years after the Premier League opened a massive disciplinary investigation into Manchester City, no verdict has been announced. The case, covering over 130 charges about financial misreporting and cooperation, finished hearings in December 2024 but remains pending. Fans, clubs, and the media are left in the dark, fueling speculation and frustration. The league’s silence and the delay have sparked doubts about fairness and the speed of its justice system.
