Labour peer Julie Elliott has urged Treasury chief Rachel Reeves to put the north of England at the heart of the UK’s net‑zero strategy. Research shows clean‑energy work makes up a larger share of the north’s economy than the national average, with Sunderland leading. Labour fears Reform UK could dismantle the sector if it wins local elections, hurting jobs. The government expects the clean‑energy industry to double to 800,000 jobs by 2030, with 140,000 in the north.
