The Six Nations starts Thursday, with France back after a long break and Ireland in a weaker lineup. France’s star player, Antoine Dupont, is rejoining after injury, giving the hosts a strong attack. Ireland has lost key players and will rely on new faces, like the Prendergast brothers, to fill gaps. The match also kicks off the new Solidarity Trophy, adding extra stakes to an already dramatic opening.
