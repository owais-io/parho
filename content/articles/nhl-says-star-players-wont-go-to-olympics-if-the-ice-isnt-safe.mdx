Because the main rink in Milan is delayed, the 2026 Winter Olympics ice court won’t be ready until after the new year. NHL leaders are worried the smaller, wider rink and poor ice quality could harm players. Commissioner Gary Bettman and deputy Bill Daly have warned they won’t send top stars unless the ice is safe. The IOC insists everything will be ready on time, but the NHL remains doubtful.
