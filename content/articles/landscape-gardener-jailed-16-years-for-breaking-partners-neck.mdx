A landscape gardener was sentenced to 16 years in prison after he broke his partner’s neck, leaving her paralyzed. The court found him guilty of serious assault after a 27‑minute jury decision. The victim, a teacher, now has a full spinal injury and needs constant care. She described a cycle of abuse, while his defense said he didn’t intend to cause severe harm.
