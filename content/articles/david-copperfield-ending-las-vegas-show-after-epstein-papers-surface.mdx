David Copperfield has announced he will stop performing his long‑running show at MGM Grand in Las Vegas next month. The decision comes after new documents from the Epstein investigation were released, showing FBI emails that linked Copperfield to the late sex‑offender. Copperfield says the move is unrelated to those files, and his lawyers insist he was never close to Epstein. MGM will refund future tickets.
