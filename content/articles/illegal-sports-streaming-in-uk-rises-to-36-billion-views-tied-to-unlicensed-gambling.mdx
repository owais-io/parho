UK sports fans are watching 3.6 billion illegal streams, double the number from three years ago, a new report says. Most of these streams carry ads for unlicensed bookmakers, linking piracy with a £379 million surge in illegal betting. While regulators have given £26 million to fight the black market, the crackdown is still behind the pace. The problem is hitting Premier League clubs and the national gambling industry, as crime keeps finding ways to profit from stolen content.
