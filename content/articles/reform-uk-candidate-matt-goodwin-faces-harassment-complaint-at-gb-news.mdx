Matt Goodwin, the Reform UK candidate for the Gorton and Denton by‑election, was accused by a former GB News staffer of making inappropriate, sexually harassing remarks. The complaint, raised in 2025, led to an internal investigation that found the claims credible but concluded no formal discipline was needed. Goodwin apologized, the matter was called a “minor workplace issue,” and he remains on the ballot.
