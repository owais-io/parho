Adrian Chiles spotted a bright billboard near Cardiff City stadium that read “WALE’S LARGEST VAPE SHOP”. He was shocked to see the apostrophe after the final S – a typo that should not be there. He filmed it, posted the photo, and joked the mistake might be a clever marketing trick. The article shows how a small typo can grab people’s attention.
