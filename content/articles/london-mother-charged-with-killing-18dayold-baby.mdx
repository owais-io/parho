A 43‑year‑old mother was arrested in Westminster after her 18‑day‑old baby was found dead in a flat on Horseferry Road. The infant fell from a height, was taken to a hospital and died. Police say the case is still under investigation. The mother has been charged with murder and is being held in custody, with a court date set for Monday.
