Tech leaders like Sundar Pichai (Google), Sam Altman (OpenAI) and Dario Amodei (Anthropic) are in Delhi for an AI summit hosted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The meeting aims to make India a hub for AI in the global south, with ministers from Kenya, Indonesia, Egypt and others attending. Modi wants AI to help farming, water and health, but critics worry it could boost surveillance and widen inequality. Safety experts also warn about the risk of powerful AI.
