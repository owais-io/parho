Argentina marks 50 years since a 1976 coup that killed thousands. Traditionally, people remember the victims and fight for justice. President Javier Milei has started to deny the scale of the abuse, praise the military and threatens to pardon former officers. His actions have sparked protests and alarm from human‑rights groups. But many still defend the memory and push back.
