England won the T20 World Cup group game against Scotland, winning by five wickets. Tom Banton hit a 63 off 41 balls to lift the side, while Harry Brook’s team struggled at times but held on. Scotland started well but lost momentum as England’s bowlers tightened up. Scotland’s top scorer Richie Berrington made 49, but they could not chase the target. England now heads to the next round.
