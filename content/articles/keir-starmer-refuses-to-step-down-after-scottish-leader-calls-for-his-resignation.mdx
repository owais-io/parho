Labour Party leader Keir Starmer has refused to quit after Scottish Labour chief Anas Sarwar urged him to resign. Starmer told MPs he would fight any challenge and gained backing from most cabinet members. The same day, his top adviser and communications chief left the government. The move shows Starmer still holds power, but internal tensions and a potential future leadership contest remain.
