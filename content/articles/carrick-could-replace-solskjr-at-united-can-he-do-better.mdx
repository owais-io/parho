After a long spell of managerial changes, Manchester United may look to keep interim boss Michael Carrick in charge. Carrick gave the team a quick, attacking win over City, showing he can inspire a new style. But United have struggled to stay on top, and past managers like Solskjær have only had mixed success. Deciding whether Carrick can deliver the stability and results the club needs is still up in the air.
