Wales lost 52‑26 to New Zealand, extending their losing streak to 34 games. New coach Steve Tandy saw a small glimpse of future hope as Tom Rogers became the first Welsh player to score three tries against the All Blacks. New Zealand’s tries came from line‑outs, mauls and a sprinting finish, while Wales replied with a couple of quick tries of their own. The match was a tough lesson but gave the Welsh a reason to smile.
