Barry‑Murphy, now the head coach at Cardiff, once ran Manchester City’s U‑21 team and learned a lot from Pep Guardiola. He says players should keep playing, but too much tiki‑taka would upset Cardiff fans. After leaving City, he joined Cardiff to try his own style. Growing up in Cork, he values education and has a close family. He balances football coaching with his life at home, keeping players motivated.
