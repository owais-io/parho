The Kennedy Center board agreed to shut the venue for two years to carry out a $257‑million renovation. The move, driven by President Trump’s vision, also involves renaming the center and changing its leadership. The closure has prompted protests and cancelled shows, including a Hamilton run. The decision marks a sharp shift in the center’s direction, turning it into a flagship for Washington’s cultural agenda.
