After the U.S. won the 2026 World Cup spot, coach Mauricio Pochettino is already thinking ahead to 2030. He wants to create a core group of players that can stay together and grow. Key names include Pulisic, McKennie, and Adams, who will be in their early thirties by 2030. Younger talents like Balogun and Tessmann could fill gaps. Success will depend on players staying fit, improving, and keeping the ambition to play another World Cup.
