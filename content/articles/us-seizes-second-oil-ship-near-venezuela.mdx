On Saturday, US forces seized a Panama‑flagged oil tanker called Centuries east of Barbados. It’s the second ship the US has intercepted near Venezuela, following a December 10 seizure. The action is part of a blockade aimed at stopping Venezuelan oil that may fund drug trafficking. The vessel wasn’t on the official sanctions list, indicating a possible escalation. Venezuela calls the seizure piracy, while US officials say they’ll keep blocking illegal oil shipments.
