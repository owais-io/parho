South Korea’s foreign ministry protested a banner at the Russian embassy in Seoul that says “victory will be ours,” a message linked to Russia’s war in Ukraine. The banner remains, raising tensions. Meanwhile, Russia continues air strikes on Ukraine, killing civilians, while Ukraine blames Russia for attacks in Lviv and Belgorod. The EU’s next sanctions package is blocked by Hungary, and Putin says nuclear development is now top priority.
