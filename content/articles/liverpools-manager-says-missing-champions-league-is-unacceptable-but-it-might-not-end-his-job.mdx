Liverpool manager Arne Slot said it would be unacceptable if the club failed to qualify for the Champions League this season. He also said that not making the competition might not automatically end his job, as the club looks at many factors. Liverpool are currently sixth in the Premier League, only a few points from a Champions League spot. Slot acknowledges the season has been tough, with a low win rate, but he remains optimistic.
