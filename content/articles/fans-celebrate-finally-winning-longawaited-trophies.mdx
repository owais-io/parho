Fans worldwide celebrated long‑awaited wins. Newcastle United won the Carabao Cup after 56 years, Crystal Palace claimed the FA Cup for the first time in 120 years, Tottenham lifted the Europa League after 17 years, and PSG won the Champions League 39 years after debut. India’s women beat South Africa to win the Cricket World Cup for the first time. These victories lifted spirits and proved patience pays off.
