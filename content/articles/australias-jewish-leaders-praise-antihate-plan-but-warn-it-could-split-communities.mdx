Australia’s top Jewish group says the government’s new anti‑antisemitism plan is an important first step, but some warn it could be used as a political tool that splits communities. The plan will adopt 13 recommendations from an anti‑hate report, including the IHRA definition of antisemitism. Critics fear the language could blur legitimate criticism of Israel with hate speech, and that tougher rules could curb protests and free expression.
