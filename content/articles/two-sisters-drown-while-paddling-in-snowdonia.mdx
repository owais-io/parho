Two sisters, both students at the University of Chester, drowned while paddling fully clothed in a natural pool in Snowdonia. Neither could swim, and they fell on a slippery rock near a waterfall. Rescue teams reached the water, but both were pronounced dead at the scene. An inquest ruled the deaths accidental, urging people to be careful around such pools.
