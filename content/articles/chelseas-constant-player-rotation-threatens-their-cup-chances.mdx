Chelsea’s manager Enzo Maresca has been rotating his lineup so much that the team is struggling to stay consistent. After a loss to Atalanta and shaky games against big clubs, the Blues now have only two group‑stage matches left in the European Cup to win. If they fail, they’ll face a tough playoff. Fans and pundits blame the frequent changes for Chelsea’s lack of form and growing uncertainty about their future in the competition.
