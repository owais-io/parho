The U.S. Justice Department is investigating Minnesota Governor Tim Walz and Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey, accusing them of trying to block the Trump‑era ICE crackdown. The probe follows an ICE officer’s shooting of a woman that sparked protests. Officials claim the subpoenas are a political hit‑job, while Trump has urged investigations against critics.
