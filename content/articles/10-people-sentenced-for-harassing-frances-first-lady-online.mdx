A Paris court handed down sentences to 10 people who posted or shared false claims that Brigitte Macron was a man. The defendants, ranging from teachers to business owners, received punishments such as compulsory courses, suspended prison terms, and bans from certain social‑media sites. The case follows a U.S. defamation lawsuit against a podcaster who repeated the false story. Brigitte Macron said she will keep fighting the attacks and wants to help teens stand up to bullying.
