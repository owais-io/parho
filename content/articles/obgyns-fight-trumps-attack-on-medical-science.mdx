The American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) is standing up to defend medical facts as the Trump administration spreads false claims about pregnancy drugs, vaccines and abortion. New president Steven Fleischman urges members to oppose misleading statements, lobby against Medicaid cuts that would hurt pregnant people, and fight the One Big Beautiful Bill that could leave 10 million uninsured. ACOG also refuses federal funds that could limit its research and pushes clear science to patients.
