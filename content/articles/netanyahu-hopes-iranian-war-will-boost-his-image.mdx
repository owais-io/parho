After Israel started bombing Iran, many Israelis still support the war and want the U.S. to keep attacking. Prime Minister Netanyahu thinks destroying the Iranian regime will make people see him in a better light, especially before the 2025 elections. But polls show his popularity hasn't risen much. Critics worry he may use the war for personal gain, and the U.S. public is uneasy about another conflict. The outcome could affect Israel’s future politics and its ties with America.
