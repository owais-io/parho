Diego Villavicencio, a Florida resident, has been charged with sending violent threats to U.S. President Donald Trump, California congressman Eric Swalwell, and possibly Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell. A federal indictment lists messages from September 2025 to January 2026 that say he will kidnap, kill, and shoot these officials. Villavicencio allegedly replied to a tweet from Swalwell. The case will go to trial in May in Tallahassee, as lawmakers warn that threats to politicians have risen sharply.
