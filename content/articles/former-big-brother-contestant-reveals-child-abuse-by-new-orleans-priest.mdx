Renny Martyn, 71, former Big Brother contestant, testified in federal court about being molested at age six by a priest at a Louisiana school. She spoke during a hearing on the New Orleans archdiocese’s bankruptcy case, which is trying to pay about $230 million to survivors. Martyn said the abuse left lasting scars and she finally feels liberated by speaking out. The case is part of a larger clergy abuse settlement.
