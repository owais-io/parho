A Queensland hospital is looking into a privacy breach after anti‑abortion activist Joanna Howe posted a video that included a picture of a 16‑week‑old foetus she said was taken inside the hospital’s Butterfly Room. Howe also shared detailed medical records that could identify patients. Hospital officials say they are investigating, and medical experts warn that claims of babies being “born alive” after abortions are misleading. The post has sparked anger and calls for stricter privacy rules.
