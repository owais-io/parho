In Bavaria, a group of 50 sheep slipped away from a herd of 500 and wandered into a Penny supermarket. The animals explored the aisles, causing a small mess in the drinks section but didn’t eat any food. Customers and staff calmly let them out after about 20 minutes. The incident was filmed and shared online, turning the sheep into viral celebrities. The owner said the animals were fine and plans to sponsor their feed for a year.
