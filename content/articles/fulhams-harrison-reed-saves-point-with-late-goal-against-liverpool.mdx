Liverpool were leading 2-1 with just minutes left, but Fulham’s substitute Harrison Reed fired a long‑range strike into the top corner to make it 1-1. The goal came after a long ball back to Reed, who had only played a handful of games that season. Liverpool’s defence, missing key players, failed to stop the shot. The match ended in a draw, hurting Liverpool’s title hopes.
