In Lyon, 11 people were arrested after a far‑right activist, Quentin Deranque, was killed during a protest. Among the detainees is a parliamentary aide from France’s left‑wing LFI party. Authorities say Deranque was beaten by a group of six and later died from brain injuries. The incident has sparked national outrage, prompted bomb‑threat evacuations at LFI headquarters, and raised fears ahead of upcoming elections.
