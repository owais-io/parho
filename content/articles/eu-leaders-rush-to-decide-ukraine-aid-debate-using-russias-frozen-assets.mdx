EU leaders are hurrying to agree on a €90bn loan for Ukraine using Russia’s frozen assets, but Belgium is against it. Poland’s leader warned it’s a choice between money now or more blood later. Russia’s central bank will sue banks that use its assets. Some EU states want to borrow money instead. The summit must finish by Friday and a US‑Russia peace talk is scheduled in Miami.
