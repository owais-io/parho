Bangladesh's new interim government is facing violent mobs. After the killing of opposition leader Sharif Osman Hadi, angry crowds attacked the offices of the Daily Star and Prothom Alo. Firefighters were fired at, and the military had to rescue reporters. Since 2024, 184 people have died in mob attacks, up from 51 in 2023. Journalists feel unsafe and worry the country is sliding back into violence.
