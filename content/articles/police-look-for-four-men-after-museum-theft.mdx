Police are asking anyone who saw or knows four men for help after about 600 valuable items were stolen from a Bristol museum’s storage site in the middle of the night. The loot included medals, jewelry, art, and natural‑history objects. Detectives described the men by clothes and build and want anyone who saw the men or the items online to call 101. A similar burglary at Exeter also stole watches and a gun.
