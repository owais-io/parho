Philadelphia is dealing with a new drug called medetomidine, a veterinary sedative that is now mixed with fentanyl on the streets. It causes very painful withdrawal and organ damage that Narcan can’t fix. Sunshine House, a local harm‑reduction center, has reversed thousands of overdoses, but hospitals are overwhelmed with patients needing ICU care. City leaders are debating safe‑supply programs while police and treatment centers struggle to keep up.
