Trump says a 15‑point plan could stop fighting with Iran, but experts think he’s just repeating an old idea. The same plan was offered last year and Iran rejected it. No new talks or proof of a fresh deal exist. While the U.S. pauses planned strikes, the plan’s demands are hard for Iran to accept, so the chances of a quick deal are low.
