West Ham beat QPR 1‑0 in the FA Cup third round, with Argentine striker Taty Castellanos scoring the only goal in extra time. The win, Nuno Espírito Santo’s first since November, gives the club a morale boost before a tough Premier League season that could end in relegation. Fans enjoyed the cup game and praised the players, while the coach said a goal changes everything and the team can step forward. More signings will be needed next season.
