Global central banks signed a statement backing Fed chair Jerome Powell as he faces threats from President Trump and a US justice investigation. They say bank independence is vital. Powell says the DOJ is targeting him for high‑cost renovations at the Fed. Trump denies knowing about the probe. Big names like Jamie Dimon and former Fed chairs back Powell. The move worries lawmakers and could affect future Fed leadership.
