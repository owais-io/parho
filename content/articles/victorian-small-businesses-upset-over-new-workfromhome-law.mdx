Victoria’s government has moved forward with a law that gives all workers the right to work from home two days a week, but the rule will also apply to small businesses. Many small‑biz owners say the extra paperwork will hurt them and could even make some move their operations elsewhere. The plan was part of the Labor Party’s election promise and is meant to help workers, especially mothers, stay in the workforce.
