Britain lost 2‑1 to Greece in the United Cup. Emma Raducanu struggled after a foot injury, dropping her match 6‑3, 3‑6, 6‑1 to Maria Sakkari. She said she was tired but proud of fighting through three sets. The men’s No. 5, Billy Harris, also lost to Stefanos Tsitsipas, but a mixed‑doubles pair won the final match. The Britons will regroup after the defeat.
