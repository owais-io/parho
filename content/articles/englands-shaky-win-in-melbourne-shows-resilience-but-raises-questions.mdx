England beat Australia by four wickets in the fourth Test at Melbourne, but the game was marred by a poor pitch and heavy criticism from Australian cricket.  England’s bowlers, especially Josh Tongue and Brydon Carse, played key roles, taking most of the wickets.  Although the win was celebrated, many felt the match conditions made it less convincing, sparking debate about the game's fairness.
