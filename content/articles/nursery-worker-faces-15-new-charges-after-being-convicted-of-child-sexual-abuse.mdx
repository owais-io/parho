Vincent Chan, 45, was found guilty of 26 child‑sexual‑abuse crimes at a London nursery. Police now charge him with 15 more offences, including indecent‑image and public‑decency cases involving nine girls. The new charges cover incidents that happened elsewhere, not just at the closed nursery. He remains in custody while investigators review the additional crimes, which date back several years and span multiple locations in London.
