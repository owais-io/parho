Australia’s 2023 sports season was a blockbuster. Athletics star Josh Gout shot to world fame after his World Championships semi‑final, while rookie Formula One driver Oscar Piastri battled teammate Lando Norris. In football, Jordan Mailata helped the Eagles win the Super Bowl. Australians also excelled in basketball (Alanna Smith, Dyson Daniels), surfing (Molly Picklum), high‑jump (Nicola Olyslagers), and women’s football with Arsenal’s Champions League win. AFL, AFLW, and NRL stories added more drama.
