On March 28, thousands will gather across the U.S. for the No Kings 3 protest, led by groups like Indivisible and the ACLU. Organizers say they want about 9 million people, up from 7 million last time, to stand up against ICE and the Trump administration. They plan a non‑violent, safe march, with training and local partners to keep it peaceful. The goal is to pressure officials to change the agency’s hard‑line tactics.
