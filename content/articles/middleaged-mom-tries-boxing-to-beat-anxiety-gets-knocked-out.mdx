Anna, a 50‑something mother who lived with constant anxiety, decided boxing might help. She trained hard, entered a local fight night, and was surprisingly knocked out in the first round. Despite the embarrassment, the experience pushed her out of her comfort zone, gave her a new sense of courage, and she says she’d do it again.
