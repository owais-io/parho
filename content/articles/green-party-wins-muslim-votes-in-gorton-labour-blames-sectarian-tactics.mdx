After the local election, the Green Party won key Muslim votes in Gorton and Denton, surprising Labour. Labour, Reform and Conservatives say the Greens used targeted messaging, even showing pictures of India’s prime minister Modi and a campaign video in Urdu, to win these voters. They also point to a report about “family voting” as evidence of manipulation. The Greens deny any wrongdoing and say their win shows unity, not division.
