An 18th‑century potato‑shaped flask at the Nairn Museum is sparking a viral Instagram trend. The writer thinks potatoes are underappreciated and should be celebrated all year. They joke about making a ceramic replica and suggest a TikTok “potato bed” trend, where a sheet and pillows mimic a potato’s skin. The piece encourages people to think of potatoes as more than food, inspiring creative, cozy holiday ideas.
