The Australian government says it cannot reveal whether Australian sailors or officers were aboard the US submarine that torpedoed an Iranian warship in the Indian Ocean, killing at least 87 people. About 50 Australians serve on US submarines as part of the Aukus partnership, and two may have been on the vessel that struck the Iranian ship. Senators have asked for clarity, but officials cite operational secrecy.
