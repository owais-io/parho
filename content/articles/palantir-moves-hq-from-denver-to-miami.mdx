Palantir has shifted its main office from Denver to Miami, citing Florida’s business‑friendly atmosphere. The data‑analysis firm, once based in Palo Alto and later Denver, moved after criticism over its work with U.S. immigration enforcement. The change follows co‑founder Peter Thiel’s Miami office and follows protests in Colorado over surveillance tools. While the company reports strong growth, it faces political backlash in both states.
