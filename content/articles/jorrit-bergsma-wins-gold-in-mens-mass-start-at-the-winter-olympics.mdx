Jorrit Bergsma, the 40‑year‑old Dutch speed‑skater, took gold in the men’s mass start at the Milano Cortina Winter Games. It’s his second medal of the Games and first Olympic gold since 2014. Bergsma worked his way from the back of the pack to the front, staying out of trouble until the final laps. He beat Denmark’s Viktor Thorup and Italy’s Andrea Giovannini, while U.S. star Jordan Stolz missed the podium.
