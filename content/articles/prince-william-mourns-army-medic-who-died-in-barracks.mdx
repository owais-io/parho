Prince William paid tribute to 25‑year‑old army medic Lucy Wilde, who was found dead in her Warminster barracks. The Ministry of Defence says the death is under investigation. William said he was saddened by Wilde’s courage and compassion, and he sent condolences to her family. Wilde had served the Welsh Guards, later joined the Royal Yorkshire Regiment, and was known for helping others and handling a serious vehicle crash in the Falklands.
