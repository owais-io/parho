Santos, an Australian gas company, beat a lawsuit that said it lied about being “clean” and its net‑zero plans. The court said the company’s claims were not illegal and ordered the plaintiff, a shareholder‑advocacy group, to pay Santos’ legal costs. Santos says it keeps updating its climate plan and welcomes the win, while the activists say the case was a blow to holding firms accountable.
