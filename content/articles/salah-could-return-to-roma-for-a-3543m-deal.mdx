Salah is on the move. Reports say Liverpool could send the Egyptian star back to Roma, where he played before, for a summer deal costing about £35‑£43m. Chelsea has dropped out, giving Liverpool a clearer path. Other buzz: Conor Gallagher may join a Premier League side, and United could sell Bruno Fernandes to fund new signings. Clubs like Arsenal, West Ham, and Newcastle are also eyeing various players.
