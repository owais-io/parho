More than 2,000 trafficked and unaccompanied children disappeared from UK care last year. A report found 864 of 2,335 trafficked kids and 1,501 of 11,999 lone asylum‑seeker children were missing. The charities say the system fails these vulnerable youngsters, especially those without legal status. They call for better safeguarding, safe homes and clearer data. The government says it will overhaul child‑care rules to fix gaps.
