EU leaders said they will help Cyprus when it talks to Britain about the future of the two UK military bases on the island. The bases, part of a colonial legacy, have raised security worries, especially after Iranian‑backed drones attacked a runway last month. Cyprus wants a clear discussion about whether the bases stay, while the EU’s president in Cyprus says the EU will stand by it.
