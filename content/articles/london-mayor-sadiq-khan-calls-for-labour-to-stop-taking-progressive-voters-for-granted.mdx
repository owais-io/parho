London Mayor Sadiq Khan said Labour must stop taking progressive voters for granted after the Gorton and Denton byelection where the party lost a long‑held seat to the Greens. He criticised Prime Minister Keir Starmer for calling Green supporters “extreme” and warned that Labour’s current strategy risks losing left‑leaning voters to Reform UK. Khan urged the party to rethink its approach before the May elections.
