Norwich City’s new boss, Philippe Clement, knows the team has a tough job ahead after a 4‑1 loss to Birmingham. The club has lost 12 games and won only three, so fans are furious about the “Moneyball” recruiting plan and the lack of fitness. Clement says the players must own their fitness, but the club’s young‑player strategy is still being tested. He vows to make the squad the fittest in the Championship.
