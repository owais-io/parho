Every night in downtown Los Angeles, huge black‑and‑white portraits of local residents flash on buildings with the question “Am I Next?” The project, led by the California Community Foundation, LA Plaza Cultura y Artes and the Japanese American National Museum, shows stories of people—both citizens and immigrants—taken by ICE. It invites anyone to submit their photo and reminds viewers that if anyone can be detained, no one is truly safe.
