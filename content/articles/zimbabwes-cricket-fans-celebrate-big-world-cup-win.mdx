Zimbabwe’s cricket team has made a surprising comeback at the T20 World Cup, beating big teams like Australia and Sri Lanka. Fans from all parts of the country are cheering louder and singing in their own languages, showing the sport is now for everyone. Players such as Blessing Muzarabani and Brian Bennett are performing strongly, while the captain, Sikandar Raza, says the team is working hard to keep the momentum. The win gives hope that Zimbabwe’s cricket future can stay bright.
