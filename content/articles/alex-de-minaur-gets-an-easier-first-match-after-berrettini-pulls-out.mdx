At the start of the Australian Open, Alex de Minaur’s first‑round opponent, former world No. 6 Matteo Berrettini, withdrew because of abdominal pain. That means de Minaur now plays Mackenzie McDonald instead of a tougher fight. The win‑draw gives the Aussie a smoother start. Fans still watched other matches, like Australian Talia Gibson beating Anna Blinkova, while the event attracted nearly 218,000 spectators.
