Trump says the federal government should take control of voting machines in at least 15 states before the 2026 mid‑terms. His remarks follow the FBI’s raid on Fulton County and an investigation by the director of national intelligence into Puerto Rico’s voting machines. The move could spark court battles, slow early voting, delay results, and raise fears of a power grab that would hurt voters, especially Democrats.
