On Friday, Minnesota unions, churches, and local businesses shut down work, school, and shops to protest ICE officers after the death of Renee Good. The “Day of Truth & Freedom” calls for ICE to leave the state, accountability for the officer, and a halt to federal ICE funding. City council and unions support the march, and many childcare centers closed in solidarity. Despite cold weather, residents joined the protest.
