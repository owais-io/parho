Arsenal’s new signing Eberechi Eze scored a hat‑trick against Tottenham, giving the Gunners a 3‑1 win and putting them six points clear at the top of the Premier League. Manager Mikel Arteta praised Eze for adding an “aura” to the team, saying his talent and drive help everyone. Eze, who almost moved to Spurs but was bought by Arsenal for £67.5m, was grateful for the moment, while Tottenham’s coach apologised for the poor performance.
