Russia’s army moved the slowest in Ukraine in almost two years in February, covering only 123 square kilometres. The slowdown happened after Elon Musk cut Starlink internet access to Russian troops. Ukrainian forces made several small gains, especially near Dnipropetrovsk, while Russia kept pushing east toward Kramatorsk and Sloviansk. A Ukrainian drone hit a Russian oil terminal, damaging it and killing five. Talks on peace, EU membership and oil flows are still stalled.
