A 56‑year‑old American named Douglas Ruch claimed he had terminal cancer and raised money on GoFundMe to travel and volunteer. A New Zealand journalist accused him of running multiple scams. After a Guardian investigation, Ruch died in Auckland on Dec. 18, just a day after the accusations were published. He had raised more than $230,000 for his tour, but most people now say he was a conman. His family was silent.
