Sheffield United won 3-0 over Sheffield Wednesday at Hillsborough, with Tyrese Campbell scoring twice. It was the first time an away team has won a derby by three goals in 131 years. United are still at the bottom of the Championship, but the win is a bright spot in a tough season. Wednesday, led by Henrik Pedersen, remain in the relegation zone and are looking for a new owner to turn things around.
