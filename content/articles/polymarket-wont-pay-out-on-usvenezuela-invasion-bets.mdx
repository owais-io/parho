Polymarket, a betting site where people wager on future events, told users it wouldn’t honor millions of dollars’ worth of bets that the US would invade Venezuela by January 31. The site said that the capture of President Maduro didn’t count as an “invasion.” Traders who had placed big bets on the event are upset, saying the definition is being twisted. The controversy comes as the platform gains popularity and has ties to Trump family members.
