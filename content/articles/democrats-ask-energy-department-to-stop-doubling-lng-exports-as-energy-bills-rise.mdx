Senators Elizabeth Warren, Bernie Sanders and others have written to Energy Secretary Chris Wright, urging the U.S. government to scrap plans to double liquefied natural gas (LNG) exports. They say the export boom has cut supply for home heating and electricity, driving up prices for American families. The lawmakers point out that the fossil‑fuel industry, which has heavily donated to the president, benefits from the exports and request a written response about cutting consumer costs.
