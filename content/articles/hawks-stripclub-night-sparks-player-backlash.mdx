San Antonio center Luke Kornet criticized the Atlanta Hawks for a planned theme night at strip club Magic City, saying the event could objectify women and reflect poorly on the NBA. The Hawks were promoting the night with music from TI and selling club merch, while calling Magic City a cultural icon. Kornet, a devout Catholic, wrote on Medium that the partnership was inappropriate for fans of all ages.
