Tesco put up signs in Welsh instead of Cornish at its Helston store in Cornwall. Local councillors were surprised but praised the idea of bilingual signs. They asked the company to switch to the right language, as Cornwall’s Cornish language has just gained legal protection. Tesco apologized, removed the Welsh signs, and plans to replace them with Cornish ones soon. The move follows Cornwall's recent recognition as a protected minority language.
