Australians love tough weather sports, but ice hockey is just starting to catch on. Since 2010 the O’Brien Icehouse Academy now serves over 1,200 students a year, and beer‑league play has exploded. The sport gains visibility from the Winter Olympics, NHL games on Disney+ and pop‑culture hits like Heated Rivalry. Melbourne’s women’s league now draws sold‑out crowds, and the close‑knit community at the three city rinks keeps new fans coming back.
