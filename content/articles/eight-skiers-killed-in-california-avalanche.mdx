An avalanche near Castle Peak in California’s Sierra Nevada on Tuesday killed eight skiers, with one still missing. Rescue teams found six survivors and recovered five bodies, but weather kept others out of reach. Heavy snow, strong winds, and a recent storm created dangerous conditions. Authorities urged people to stay away from the Sierras while search and recovery continues to help families.
