Heavy rain across New Zealand’s North Island caused a massive landslide at a Mount Maunganui campground. Emergency crews found people trapped, and authorities say a child is among those missing. Helicopters and ground teams are searching, while the government has declared a state of emergency in five regions. Prime Minister Luxon thanked rescuers, and officials warn that another bout of intense rain could worsen flooding and landslides.
