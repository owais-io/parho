Harry Kane, England’s star striker, has been hit with a flood of online jokes and memes after a string of big goals. Fans mock his interview style and even claim he didn’t appear in a recent match, despite him scoring 21 league goals this season. Meanwhile, Bayern Munich are talking about extending his contract, while Barcelona’s interest is heightened by a £57 m release clause.
