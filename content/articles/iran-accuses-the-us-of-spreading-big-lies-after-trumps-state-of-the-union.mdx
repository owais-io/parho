After Donald Trump’s State of the Union speech, Iran’s officials blasted the U.S. for “big lies” about Iran’s missile program, nuclear ambitions, and the death toll from protests. They warned that if the U.S. keeps attacking Iran with misinformation, the country will retaliate. The accusation comes just before a Geneva‑mediated nuclear talk with U.S. envoys, raising tensions while the U.S. has increased its military presence in the Middle East.
