Overnight gunfire and explosions shook Niamey’s main airport. Residents saw bright streaks and heard loud blasts, and some planes were hit. The military ruler, General Abdourahmane Tchiani, blamed France, Benin and Côte d’Ivoire—although no proof was given—and promised retaliation. Experts think the attack was likely carried out by jihadists. Heavy security now surrounds the airport as the junta shifts its focus away from western allies toward Russia.
