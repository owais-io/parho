Trump gave a 20‑minute Christmas‑themed address in the White House, using a blue suit and a Diet Coke. He blamed Biden for high prices, claimed inflation was the worst in 48 years, and promised a $1,776 “warrior dividend” to service members. The speech was heavy on criticism of immigration and Democrats, and it hinted at Trump’s frustration as recent polls show only a third of Americans approve of his handling of the economy.
