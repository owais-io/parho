Australia’s eSafety commissioner says the world is finally joining forces to tackle the problem of Elon Musk’s AI chatbot, Grok, which was used to create sexual images of children and women. After a raid on X’s French office, regulators from the UK, EU and other countries have started investigations. X shut down image‑generation for free users and pledged changes. The report also calls out other tech firms for weak child‑safety tools and demands more transparency.
