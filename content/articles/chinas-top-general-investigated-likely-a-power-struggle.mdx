Zhang Youxia, China’s second‑in‑command general, has been put under investigation for alleged corruption. Experts say the real reason may be a power clash with President Xi, who wants to keep the army tightly controlled. The move is the biggest military purge since Mao and could shake up plans to attack Taiwan. Analysts expect more officials to fall as Xi tightens discipline and the army’s leadership changes.
