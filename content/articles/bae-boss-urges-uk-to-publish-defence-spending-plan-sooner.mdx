BAE Systems’ chief, Charles Woodburn, wants the UK government to release its delayed defence investment plan, the DIP, earlier. He says the plan’s delay is holding back industry investment, even as BAE reports record sales of £30 bn and a huge order backlog. With the Ukraine war boosting demand, BAE is confident Britain can meet new defence needs if the government provides clear spending guidance.
