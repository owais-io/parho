Two colonial‑era statues in Melbourne’s Flagstaff Gardens were smashed and painted with anti‑settlement slogans overnight. The Pioneer Monument, a sandstone obelisk built in 1871, was broken and marked “land back” and “death to Australia.” A nearby Separation Memorial also received red paint. Police say a vehicle was used to tear the monuments apart and are investigating. City leaders called the acts disgraceful and urged people to help catch the vandals.
