Voice notes are a lifeline for new parents, letting them keep in touch with friends even when schedules are tight. The writer describes how she and her friends exchange quick audio messages while juggling nappies, work, and lockdown restrictions. Texts feel impersonal; voice captures warmth and helps soothe babies. Some friends dislike hearing their own voice, but for many, audio keeps friendships strong during the hectic early months.
