Europe’s Commission wants to force governments to buy more EU‑made, low‑carbon products. The new Industrial Accelerator Act would make public money spend on 70% EU parts for electric cars and require 50% EU jobs for big foreign clean‑tech investments. The goal is to raise EU manufacturing to 20% of GDP by 2035 and beat China’s dominance. The plan could face pushback from the US, India and others.
