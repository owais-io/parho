A 9‑year‑old boy in New South Wales was photographed on a man’s lap while the vehicle was traveling the Great Western Highway at about 1:35 a.m. Police say the adult driver was very tired and claimed he was in control of the car. The boy was not wearing a seatbelt. The driver now faces four charges, including reckless driving, and will appear in court in April.
