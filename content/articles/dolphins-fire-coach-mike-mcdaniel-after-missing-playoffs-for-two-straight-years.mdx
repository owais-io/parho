Miami’s owner, Stephen Ross, decided to part ways with head coach Mike McDaniel after the team missed the playoffs in 2023 and 2024. Ross said the club needs big changes and praised McDaniel’s creativity and effort. The Dolphins struggled last season, losing to Pittsburgh before the season ended, and the team’s final game was marred by Tua Tagovailoa being benched. McDaniel, a former 49ers coordinator, had taken the Dolphins to the playoffs twice but was let go after consecutive first‑round exits.
