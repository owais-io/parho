Home Depot in Los Angeles has installed high‑pitch noise machines in its parking lot, which critics say are meant to push away day‑labor workers. Advocates say the sound causes headaches and nausea, and they want the devices removed. The store has also been a frequent site for ICE raids, and local groups want Home Depot to speak out against those actions. The company says the noise devices are only for safety and not linked to immigration enforcement.
