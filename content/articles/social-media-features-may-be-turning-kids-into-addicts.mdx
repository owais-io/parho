A Los Angeles court heard a big case where lawyers say Meta and Google’s endless scrolling and autoplay videos are making children addicted. The trial looked at how these features keep users scrolling, watching, and checking notifications nonstop, giving quick dopamine hits. Experts debated whether this is real addiction or just habit. The verdict could change how tech companies design their platforms.
