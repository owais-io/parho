Andy Burnham, the Greater Manchester mayor, said Westminster insiders can't lie after Labour’s national executive committee blocked his run in the Gorton & Denton byelection. He accused Downing Street sources of spreading false messages and warned politicians paid by the public shouldn't lie. Burnham plans to campaign in the byelection and call out untruths, while thanking Prime Minister Rishi Sunak for supporting the Hillsborough law. He said he will not be bitter.
