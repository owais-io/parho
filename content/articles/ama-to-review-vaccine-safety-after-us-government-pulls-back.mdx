After the U.S. health agency stopped recommending routine vaccines, the American Medical Association teamed up with the Vaccine Integrity Project to review the safety and effectiveness of flu, Covid‑19, and RSV shots. They’ll meet monthly, bring in other experts, and create a science‑based review process. The move fills a gap left by the collapsed Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices and government inaction.
