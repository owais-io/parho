Pupils in London are being urged on TikTok and Snapchat to join violent “school wars”, prompting police and schools to act. The Metropolitan Police have asked platforms to block accounts that spread these messages and have warned kids that fighting, carrying weapons, and planning violence can lead to arrests and long‑term trouble. School leaders sent letters to parents, and the police are monitoring the situation closely. Some students think the posts are jokes, but authorities stress the seriousness.
