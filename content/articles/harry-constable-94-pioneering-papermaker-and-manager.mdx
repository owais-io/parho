Harry Constable, a 94‑year‑old pioneer in the paper industry, led modern changes at Chartham mill in the 1970s and 80s. Starting as a lab assistant, he rose to managing director, championing new techniques like photo‑base papers and adopting Total Quality Management so all staff saw the company’s purpose. He also cared for safety, hiring laid‑off workers, and served as a magistrate. He is survived by his wife’s family and his legacy of inclusive leadership.
