Unison boss Andrea Egan says Labour must let local members pick their Gorton candidate fairly. The by‑election happened after MP Andrew Gwynne quit. Andy Burnham, Greater Manchester mayor, could run, but leaders close to Prime Minister Keir Starmer want to stop him, fearing he might challenge for the top spot. Egan warns against any manipulation and wants a clean, democratic process.
