A Chicago deli teamed with a local dispensary to give away pastrami sandwiches topped with cannabis‑infused mustard on National Hot Pastrami Day. The stunt, part of a marketing experiment, shows how cannabis businesses are looking for new ways to stand out amid a tough market. Chefs are still testing how to infuse food without ruining flavor, and experts warn that profit margins and regulations make selling infused foods hard. The idea could grow into packaged mustard or other infused condiments.
