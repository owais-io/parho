Crans‑Montana, a Swiss ski resort where a bar fire killed 40 on New Year’s Day, has been chosen to host the Alpine skiing events at the 2038 Winter Olympics. The city is part of Switzerland’s bid, which has special backing from the International Olympic Committee, meaning no other country can challenge it while the bid is in place. The venue will help avoid costly new construction. Other Swiss towns will host different sports.
