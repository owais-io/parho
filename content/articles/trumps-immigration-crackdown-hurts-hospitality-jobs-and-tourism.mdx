The largest U.S. hospitality union says Trump’s strict immigration rules have cut 98,000 jobs and hurt tourism, losing $1.2 billion in revenue. Workers fear detention even if they’re legally hired, and several airports and hotels report staff quitting or being scared to work. Small businesses in Minneapolis lost up to $81 million in January, and restaurants across the country are closing or slowing openings.
