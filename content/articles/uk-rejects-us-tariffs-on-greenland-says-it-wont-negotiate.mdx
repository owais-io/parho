Culture Secretary Lisa Nandy said the UK’s position on Greenland is “non‑negotiable” and it will not accept U.S. tariffs that target the UK and other NATO allies. Trump wants the UK to buy Greenland, a move the UK says belongs only to Greenlanders and Denmark. UK leaders and opposition alike condemn the tariffs, warning they will hurt businesses and undermine cooperation.
