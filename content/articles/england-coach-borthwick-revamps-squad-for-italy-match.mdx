England coach Steve Borthwick is overhauling the squad for the Six Nations game against Italy. Fin Smith is likely to start at fly‑half, replacing George Ford who struggled against Ireland. Borthwick may shuffle the centres and backs, moving Tommy Freeman to centre and picking Ben Spencer or Jack van Poortvliet at number 9. With injuries to Alex Mitchell, Ollie Lawrence and Ollie Chessum, the back‑row is also in flux. The team wants to refresh a squad that hasn’t met expectations.
