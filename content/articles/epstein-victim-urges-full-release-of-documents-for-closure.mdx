Marina Lacerda, a 37‑year‑old who was abused by Jeffrey Epstein as a teenager, is calling for the full release of his files. She said the documents will give her and other survivors closure and help bring other men to justice. Lacerda revealed how she was groomed, paid to massage Epstein and later pressured to recruit other girls. Trump’s comments and partisan politics have delayed the release.
