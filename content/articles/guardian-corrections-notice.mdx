The Guardian has issued a correction notice for several of its recent articles. A Turkish farms piece was wrongly credited to a photographer, the farmer’s name was misspelled, and the repair cost was under‑stated. A trivia question about a Norwegian skier’s medal count was inaccurate after a win, and a few other stories were amended. Readers can send complaints to the Guardian’s editor.
