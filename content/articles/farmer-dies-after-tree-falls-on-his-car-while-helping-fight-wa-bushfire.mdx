A 34‑year‑old farmer was killed when a tree fell on his car while he was driving home after helping fight a bushfire in southern Western Australia. The incident happened near Mindarabin on Old Ongerup Road. The man was part of a local volunteer fire‑response team. This is the second WA bushfire death in weeks. Weather warnings say heatwaves and high winds are still a risk across the state.
