Three‑hundred‑year‑old Keelmen’s Hospital in Newcastle, once a shelter for river workers and later a chilly student hostel, is getting £4.6 million from the National Lottery Heritage Fund. The Tyne & Wear Building Preservation Trust, with Newcastle City Council, will turn the Grade II* listed building into 20 affordable homes, ready for residents by Christmas 2027. The project shows how old, derelict buildings can be saved and reused.
