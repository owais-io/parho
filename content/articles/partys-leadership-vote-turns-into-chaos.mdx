The party’s leadership election was a mess. A livestream that was supposed to show the results went silent, leaving members scrambling for updates online. Chaos erupted as two factions fought over votes and a few ballots were thrown out. In the end, Jeremy Corbyn and Zarah Sultana were named leaders, but the process highlighted deep divisions and a lack of organisation.
