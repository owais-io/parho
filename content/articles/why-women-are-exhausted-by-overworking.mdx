Many women feel a so‑called “competence hangover” – a tired, irritable exhaustion that comes from always doing more at work and home. Studies show women take on extra tasks, get rewarded less, and are judged harshly when they finish quickly. This constant pressure, plus domestic duties, leads to burnout. The article suggests women need to shift their mindset and seek help so they don’t collapse.
