Over the weekend, the US carried out a surprise airstrike on Venezuela, arresting President Nicolás Maduro and his wife. President Trump said the move was to seize control of Venezuela’s oil assets for American companies and to replace Maduro with an interim leader who would cooperate with Washington. The action drew criticism from Latin American leaders, the EU, and major global powers, and left the future of Venezuela’s government uncertain.
