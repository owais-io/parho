The article shares the writer’s experience of seeing a viral Instagram reel of a dancing raccoon that turned out to be an AI‑generated clip. After a friend told her it was fake, she questions how to tell others when they’re fooled by deep‑fake content. She argues we need a collective way to warn people without spoiling their fun, because AI could be used to scam or replace jobs. The piece ends with a warning about the growing danger of AI.
