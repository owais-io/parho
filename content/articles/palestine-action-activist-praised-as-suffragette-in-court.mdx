Charlotte Head, a 29‑year‑old Palestine Action activist, is on trial in London for allegedly breaking into an Israeli defence company’s UK site. Her lawyer praised her as a “remarkable woman” and likened her to suffragettes, arguing that the protest was not violent. The prosecution says the defendants caused serious damage and a police officer was injured. The judge told jurors to keep emotions aside as the case proceeds.
