Larry Ellison, co‑founder of Oracle, has pledged more than $40 billion to back Paramount’s $108 billion offer to buy Warner Bros Discovery (WBD). Paramount’s bid was rejected by WBD’s board, which had already chosen an earlier $82.7 billion sale of its studios and HBO to Netflix. The guarantee is meant to reassure investors that Paramount has enough money. WBD shares rose 2.8%, while Paramount’s jumped 7% after the news.
