During a House judiciary hearing, Attorney General Pam Bondi attacked Democrats, calling them a “washed‑up loser lawyer” and refusing to answer questions about how the Justice Department handled the massive Jeffrey Epstein file releases. She avoided discussing the redactions, denied apologizing to victims, and kept making political attacks on President Trump. The session highlighted tension over how the files were handled and the department’s credibility.
