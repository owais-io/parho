Germany’s foreign minister has urged France to raise its defence budget, saying the country must match its promises for European sovereignty. He pointed out that EU members agreed to hit 5 % of GDP in defence by 2035, but France is still far behind. The call comes amid growing tensions between Berlin and Paris over defence policy, nuclear strategy and the future of European security.
