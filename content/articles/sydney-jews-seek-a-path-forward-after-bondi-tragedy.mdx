After a brutal shooting at Bondi Beach that killed at least 15 people, Sydney’s Jewish community is grappling with grief and safety fears. Rabbis cancel services, pause Hanukah celebrations, and worry how to explain the violence to kids. They urge Australians to stand in support, plan public rallies, and keep faith alive. Despite the terror, many still light Hanukah candles and hold onto hope.
