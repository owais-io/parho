House Democrats have threatened to impeach Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem after the fatal shooting of Alex Pretti and the death of Renee Good by federal agents. Trump defended Noem, but several Republican senators are now demanding she resign or be removed. The party split comes as a DHS‑funding bill that includes ICE money faces a vote, and Noem will testify before a Senate committee on March 3. The calls add pressure to an already tense border‑policy debate.
