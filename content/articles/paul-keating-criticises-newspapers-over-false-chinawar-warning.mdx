Former Australian prime minister Paul Keating has slammed the Sydney Morning Herald and the Age for a 2020 front‑page story that warned Australia could go to war with China in three years. He says the claim was false, calls the reporters “maladroit” and criticises the editors for publishing the piece. The story has been called exaggerated and racially charged, and Keating wants better journalism standards.
