Workers in Minnesota are pressuring major companies—like Target, Home Depot, and Hilton—to stop cooperating with ICE after the deaths of Renee Good and nurse Alex Pretti. Labor unions organized a “Day of Truth & Freedom” that called for a week‑long economic blackout. Thousands of workers signed letters asking firms to bar ICE from job sites and to speak out. Companies have largely stayed silent, while ICE raids cost taxpayers millions and disrupt local jobs.
