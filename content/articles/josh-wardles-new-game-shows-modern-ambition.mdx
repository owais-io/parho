Josh Wardle, the creator of Wordle, just launched a new online puzzle called Parseword. The Guardian piece uses his move to talk about what drives people today. It argues that instead of resting on a hit, Wardle is chasing more fame and money, like actor Timothée Chalamet’s own public push for greatness. The article mixes praise and criticism of this endless hunger for success.
