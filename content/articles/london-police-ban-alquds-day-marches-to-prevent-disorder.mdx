London police have stopped the traditional al‑Quds Day march, saying it could spark violence between supporters and counter‑protesters. Instead the rally will stay in a two‑hour block on the south bank of the Thames. Police will arrest anyone who chants for an ‘intifada’ or waves flags linked to banned groups. The ban covers the protest and any counter‑demonstrations for a month.
