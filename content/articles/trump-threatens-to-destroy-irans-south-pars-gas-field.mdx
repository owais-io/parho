After Israel hit Iran’s South Pars gasfield, Iran launched new attacks on Qatari LNG plants. President Trump threatened to blow up the entire South Pars field in retaliation. Oil and gas prices jumped, and stock markets fell. The UAE, Qatar, and the EU said the strikes on energy sites were dangerous and urged a halt. The conflict keeps the Strait of Hormuz at risk and worries about global energy supply.
