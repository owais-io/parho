In a Thai‑Cambodian border town, Thai troops took over a huge compound that had been used by scammers to trick people worldwide. The site was built to look like bank branches and police offices, complete with fake uniforms and training rooms. Workers used scripts to lure victims into fake investments, romance and “law‑enforcement” calls. The raid shows how large the Asian online‑fraud industry is and the efforts to shut it down.
