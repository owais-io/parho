Israeli police raided a Christmas party in Haifa and arrested a Palestinian man in a Santa suit, along with a DJ and a street vendor. The police said the Santa resisted and struck an officer, but a rights group claimed the force used was excessive and the raid lacked legal authority. The incident occurs amid restricted Christmas celebrations for Palestinians, with events also held in Bethlehem and a small Christian community in Gaza.
