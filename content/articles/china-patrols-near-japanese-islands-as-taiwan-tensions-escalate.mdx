China sent coast guard ships through the waters of the Senkaku/Diaoyu islands, which Japan controls but China also claims, saying it was protecting its rights. The move comes after Japan’s prime minister warned that any Chinese attack on Taiwan could trigger a military response, angering Beijing. China has increased its military presence around Taiwan and warned its citizens against traveling to Japan, affecting many Chinese students studying there.
