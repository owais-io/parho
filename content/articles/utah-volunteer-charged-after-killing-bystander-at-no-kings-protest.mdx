In June, a Salt Lake City protest drew a 43‑year‑old volunteer named Matthew Alder who fired three shots. He aimed at a man with a rifle, Arturo Gamboa, but killed a bystander, Arthur Ah Loo, who was filming. Alder is now charged with manslaughter. The district attorney says he used lethal force recklessly. Gamboa, whose gun was unloaded, will not be charged. Alder faces up to 15 years in prison and a $10,000 fine.
