Google’s AI‑generated health summaries often leave out the big safety warnings that the info could be wrong. The Guardian found that Google only shows the disclaimer after you click a “Show more” button and it appears in a tiny, light font at the bottom. Experts say this can trick users into trusting inaccurate medical advice, raising serious health risks. Google has removed some but not all such summaries.
