UK pet insurance premiums have dipped a bit even as vet bills climb, thanks to competition among insurers. The average yearly cost for lifetime coverage fell 1.6% last year, with biggest savings for pets aged 4‑6. Most owners still skip coverage—about one‑third say they don't have insurance, and 46% think it’s too pricey. Moneyfacts has highlighted top insurers such as ManyPets and Napo.
