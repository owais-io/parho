A review into how Manchester Arena bomber Hashem Abedi attacked prison officers has urged the UK to adopt a US‑style system that rewards good behaviour and punishes bad. The report, written by Jonathan Hall, looks at Abedi’s 2017 bombing and his 2024 attack on staff at HMP Frankland, where he used boiling oil and homemade knives. Parliament wants the findings published soon, and new body‑armour and Taser programmes have been rolled out for staff.
