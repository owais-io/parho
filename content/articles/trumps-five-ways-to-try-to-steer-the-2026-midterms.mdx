Trump has said Democrats are cheating in elections and is looking for ways to stop them. He wants states to tighten rules on mail‑in voting and voter ID, and he’s hinted at a possible executive order. GOP state lawmakers are drafting such restrictions, and Trump’s allies say federal agents could even watch polling places. All these moves aim to make it harder for Democrats to win the 2026 midterms.
