US officials met with NATO allies in Kyiv and said they expect President Zelenskyy to agree to a peace deal soon. They warned that if Ukraine delays, any future deal could be even harsher, forcing the country to give up more territory and accept amnesty for war crimes. Some European diplomats were shocked by the details, saying the U.S. kept allies in the dark and that Russia had a big say in drafting the plan. The US wants a deal signed by Thanksgiving.
