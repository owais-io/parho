England dominated Wales in the Six Nations, winning by a huge margin as Henry Arundell scored a hat‑trick of tries. The Welsh side was sent to the sin bin twice in the first quarter, dropping to 13 men, and struggled to keep up. England’s fast attack and solid defence left Wales with almost no chance to recover. Coach Steve Borthwick was upset the team didn’t finish even harder.
