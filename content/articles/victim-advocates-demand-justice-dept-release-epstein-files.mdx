One month after a federal law deadline, the Justice Department has not released all Jeffrey Epstein investigative files. Victim advocates, including lawyers for dozens of survivors, are furious, saying the delay shows officials cover up powerful men’s crimes. They call for a special master to enforce the law and ensure all documents are made public. The issue remains a major breach of trust for survivors.
