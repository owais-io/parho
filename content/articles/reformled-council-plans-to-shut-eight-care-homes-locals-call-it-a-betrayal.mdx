Derbyshire County Council, run by Reform UK, said it will close eight care homes after a sale fell through. The decision has upset residents, staff, and local MPs, who fear job losses and poorer care. Labour MP Linsey Farnsworth and union leaders have urged a rethink. The council promises to help residents find new homes, but the closure comes just before Christmas.
