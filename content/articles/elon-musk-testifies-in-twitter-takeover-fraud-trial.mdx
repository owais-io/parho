Elon Musk was asked to explain himself in a lawsuit from Twitter investors who say he lied about buying the social network. They claim he deliberately attacked the company on Twitter, causing its stock to fall so he could pay less for it. Musk says he didn’t intend to hurt the share price and that his tweets had mixed effects. The case is in San Francisco and may last 2‑3 weeks.
