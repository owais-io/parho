Delcy Rodríguez has stepped into Venezuela’s leadership after Nicolás Maduro was ousted. She says she will open the economy, invite foreign oil companies, and improve ties with the U.S. and China. Some people think this is a clever spin, because she helped run Maduro’s regime and oversees the security agency. Experts say the plan could bring investment but may keep the government’s tight control over politics.
