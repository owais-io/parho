Liam Ramos, a 5‑year‑old from Minnesota, was taken by ICE while walking home from school. He and his father were sent to a Texas detention center that holds many children. Liam looked sad and was given bad food. Two Texas congressmen visited, and a federal judge ruled the detention unconstitutional, freeing Liam and his dad. The case has sparked outrage over the treatment of children in U.S. immigration custody.
