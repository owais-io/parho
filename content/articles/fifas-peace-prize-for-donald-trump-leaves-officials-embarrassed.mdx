The FIFA World Cup committee awarded the organization’s inaugural peace prize to former U.S. President Donald Trump during the draw in Washington, D.C. FIFA boss Gianni Infantino presented the award, sparking surprise and embarrassment among officials over the unclear selection process and the political implications. The gesture comes as the World Cup heads toward 2026, with FIFA cautious about any controversy.
