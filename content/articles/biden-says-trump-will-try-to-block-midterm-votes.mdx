In a speech in South Carolina, former President Joe Biden warned that Donald Trump will try to “steal” the upcoming midterm elections by putting up voting barriers. Biden urged voters to turn out, saying that power still lies with the people. He also criticized Trump for not supporting families of victims of recent tragedies and for his harsh immigration crackdown.
