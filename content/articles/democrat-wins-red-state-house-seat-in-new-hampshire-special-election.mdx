A Democrat, Bobbi Boudman, won a special election for a New Hampshire state house seat, flipping a district that Donald Trump carried in 2024. The win comes after she lost the seat twice before; it was the third attempt. More than 4,000 voters turned out, and Boudman got about 52% of the vote. The result adds to a growing list of Democratic gains that could signal a mid‑term wave. Republicans still control the state legislature.
