BT is appointing Katie Milligan as the new boss of Openreach, its network arm that is building full‑fibre broadband across the UK. The move comes after Clive Selley stepped down, having led the £12 billion rollout to 25 million homes. Milligan will decide whether to push the network to 30 million homes by 2030. The change is part of a wider reshuffle by CEO Allison Kirkby as the company battles competition and customer losses.
