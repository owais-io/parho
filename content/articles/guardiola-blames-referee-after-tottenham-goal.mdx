After Tottenham’s 2‑2 draw at the Emirates, Manchester City manager Pep Guardiola slammed the referee for allowing a controversial goal by Dominic Solanke. The goal, which came after Solanke’s back‑hand strike on Marc Guéhi, was later credited and helped Spurs level the score. City’s loss of a lead dropped them to six points behind Arsenal in the Premier League title race. Guardiola has also questioned other recent decisions and Tottenham manager Thomas Frank gave limited sympathy.
