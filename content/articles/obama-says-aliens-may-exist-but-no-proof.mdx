Barack Obama said on a podcast that aliens might exist, sparking headlines. He clarified that he hasn’t seen any proof of contact. He explained that the universe is huge, so while life could be out there, the chances of aliens visiting Earth are very low. He also denied that the government hides extraterrestrials at Area 51, dismissing the popular conspiracy. The comments caused a media buzz before he clarified.
