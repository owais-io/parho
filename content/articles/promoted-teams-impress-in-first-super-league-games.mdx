The Super League has added four new teams, raising the total to 14. In its early rounds, three of the promoted clubs—Bradford, Toulouse and York—have already beaten top‑tier opponents. Bradford defeated Toulouse 18‑6, while York knocked out world‑champion Hull KR and Hull FC. These surprising victories suggest the new teams can compete, raising questions about whether 14 is the right number and how future TV deals might change the league’s size.
