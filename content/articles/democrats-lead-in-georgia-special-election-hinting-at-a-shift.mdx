In a special election for a Georgia seat vacated by former Rep. Marjorie Taylor Greene, Democrat Shawn Harris won the first round with 37% of the vote, ahead of Republican Clay Fuller. The race was tight and turnout was low—more than half of voters from last year. Some precincts saw a jump in Democratic votes, and voters talked about the Iran war, gas prices, Trump and immigration. The result may give clues for the 2024 November election.
