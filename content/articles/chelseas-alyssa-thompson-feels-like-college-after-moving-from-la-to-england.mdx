21‑year‑old American forward Alyssa Thompson left LA for Chelsea in September. She talks about it feeling like college, being away from family, learning independence. She has already scored for Chelsea, including a Champions League goal, and is a regular starter under coach Sonia Bompastor. The move has broadened her game, exposing her to different styles and world‑class teammates. She looks forward to key derby matches and hopes to win trophies.
