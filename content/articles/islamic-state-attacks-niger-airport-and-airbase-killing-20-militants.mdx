Islamic State militants attacked Niamey’s main airport and a nearby air base on Thursday night, killing 20 attackers and arresting 11. They came on motorcycles, used drones and heavy weapons, and struck aircraft belonging to Ivorian and Togolese airlines. Niger’s military said the blast also set fire to ammunition. The attack comes amid a series of jihadist strikes in the Sahel and after a coup that left Niger under a military junta.
