European newspapers say Prince Andrew’s arrest marks a major turning point for the British royal family. They say the monarchy is under serious threat and must show it can work with the law. Many papers call for the king to act firmly and for justice to be served, like for any citizen. Some warn the royal house could be damaged forever if the scandal is not handled correctly.
