Morgan Rogers has turned Aston Villa into a title‑contending team. In the past two months the 23‑year‑old striker has scored four goals, giving Villa 10 straight wins and a spot only a few points behind Arsenal. His clever finishes against big clubs like Manchester United and West Ham have made him a fan favourite and a potential England pick. Villa’s record is shaky, but Rogers’ form keeps the dream alive for a surprise title challenge.
