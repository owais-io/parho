New emails from Woody Allen’s wife, Soon‑Yi Previn, that were part of a batch of documents released about Jeffrey Epstein, show her telling the convicted sex offender that the #MeToo movement had gone too far. She also called an underage girl involved in a sexting scandal “despicable and disgusting” and criticized journalist Ronan Farrow’s coverage. The files reveal Previn’s close ties to Allen, their friendship with Epstein, and the long‑standing controversy over Allen’s relationship with her.
