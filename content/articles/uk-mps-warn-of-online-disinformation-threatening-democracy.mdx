A senior Labour MP, Emily Thornberry, said the UK is constantly hit by online disinformation that undermines democracy. She highlighted Iranian bot farms pushing Scottish nationalism, biased algorithms painting London as unsafe, and even U.S. politicians spreading lies. Thornberry urged tech firms like X, Meta and TikTok to explain how foreign false messages spread. The committee is asking social media companies to act and start real talks to stop the spread of lies.
