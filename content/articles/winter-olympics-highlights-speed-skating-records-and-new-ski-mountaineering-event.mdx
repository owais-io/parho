In the latest Winter Games, China’s Ning Zhongyan set a new Olympic record in men’s 1500m speed skating, beating the previous mark and earning gold. For the first time ever, ski mountaineering debuted; Switzerland’s Marianne Fatton won the opening race and Spain’s Oriol Cardona Coll captured the men’s sprint, marking Spain’s first Olympic gold in 54 years. The US and Canada battled in women’s ice hockey, with the US taking gold in a dramatic overtime win.
