Howel Jones was a 96‑year‑old teacher, Methodist preacher and long‑time local politician. Born in Liverpool, he taught in schools across England and Kenya, leading several primary schools. From 1982 he served 21 years as a Liberal Democrat councillor, was mayor of Clitheroe and Ribble Valley, and chaired the local party. After losing his first wife, he married his second partner, returning to Clitheroe. He left a legacy of faith, service and love for Liverpool FC.
