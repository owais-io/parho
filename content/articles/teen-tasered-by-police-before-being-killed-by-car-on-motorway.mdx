An 18‑year‑old named Logan Smith was taken out of an ambulance on the M5 motorway in Somerset when paramedics thought he was a danger. Police tried to talk to him, then used a Taser, which knocked him to the ground. While he lay there, a car hit him and he died on the spot. Police and the Independent Office for Police Conduct are investigating the use of the Taser and the whole incident.
