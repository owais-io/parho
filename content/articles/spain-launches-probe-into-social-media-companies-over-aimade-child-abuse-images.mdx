Spain has ordered prosecutors to look into X, Meta and TikTok for allegedly letting their AI create and share child‑abuse images. Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez says the move is to protect kids’ rights and stop social‑media giants from being immune. The decision follows an expert report on deepfakes and sexual content and comes as the EU also probes X’s AI chatbot, Grok. While the platforms say they remove such material, the government plans further rules, including a ban for users under 16.
