Gisèle Pelicot, a 73‑year‑old woman who survived 51 rapes committed by her ex‑husband and other men while she was drugged, is speaking out on France 5. She says shame hurts victims and urges them to fight it together. In her first TV interview she shares how the police showed her the evidence and how she chose to go public with the case. Her upcoming book and her story give hope to other survivors.
