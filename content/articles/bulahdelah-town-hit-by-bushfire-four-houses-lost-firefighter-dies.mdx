In Bulahdelah, a New South Wales town, a raging bushfire burned 4,000 hectares and destroyed four homes, including two on Emu Creek Road. A veteran firefighter died when a falling tree struck him. Local residents, like Garry Morgan, fought to save their houses with help from helicopters and volunteers. The fire threatened the Pacific Highway, forcing road closures. Firefighters now focus on the nearby town of Nerong as weather keeps the blaze alive.
