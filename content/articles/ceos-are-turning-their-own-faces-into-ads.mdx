Recently, several CEOs have put themselves front and centre in marketing. McDonald’s chief tried a new burger in a video that got mocked. Ring’s founder appeared in a Super‑Bowl ad that backfired. FIFA president Gianni Infantino put his own picture on official merchandise. The trend shows CEOs trying to be relatable, but critics say it’s self‑promotion that can backfire. Some say it reflects a shift towards personal branding, but the backlash shows audiences don’t always respond well.
