Vanuatu, a tiny Pacific island, is pushing a UN climate resolution that follows a 2023 court ruling saying countries could owe reparations for climate damage. The US, led by the former Trump administration, wants the draft cut and has pushed out the part that would track climate losses. Vanuatu has trimmed the wording but still hopes the weaker version will pass. The move shows the split between climate‑sensitive islands and fossil‑fuel powers.
