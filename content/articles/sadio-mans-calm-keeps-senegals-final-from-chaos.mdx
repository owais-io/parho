During Senegal’s Africa Cup of Nations final, a last‑minute penalty sparked a walk‑out and a near pitch invasion. The team’s captain, Sadio Mané, stayed on the sidelines, talked to his teammates and then to angry fans, urging them to stay calm. His leadership kept the match going and helped Senegal finish the tournament on a positive note, showing football’s power to bring people together.
