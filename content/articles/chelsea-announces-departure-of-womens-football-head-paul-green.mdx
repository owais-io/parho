Chelsea has announced the departure of Paul Green, the club’s long‑time head of women’s football. Green joined in 2013 and worked closely with Emma Hayes to build a championship‑winning team, winning 19 trophies. He helped recruit new manager Sonia Bompastor, who won an unbeaten treble. Fans and players praised his impact, but the club said his role had been limited by new directors. Chelsea thanked him for over a decade of service.
