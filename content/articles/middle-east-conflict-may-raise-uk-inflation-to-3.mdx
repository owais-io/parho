The Office for Budget Responsibility says the war between the US and Israel, and fighting with Iran, could push UK inflation up to 3% by year‑end. Higher oil and gas prices are driving up fuel costs. The chancellor keeps a close eye on prices but says the government has limited room to cut bills. If inflation rises, the Bank of England might delay rate cuts.
