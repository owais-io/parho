Bill Clinton’s spokesperson says the White House is trying to blame him after Congress released old photos of Clinton with Jeffrey Epstein and Ghislaine Maxwell. The statement claims Clinton cut ties with Epstein before his crimes were known and that the pictures are irrelevant. White House officials made snarky posts about the photos, while Trump has kept suggesting Clinton was involved with Epstein. Both Clintons are set to testify before a House committee soon.
