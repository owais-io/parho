At the World Economic Forum in Davos, former U.S. President Donald Trump said he would not use the U.S. military to take control of Greenland, a move that could have rattled NATO. Though he rolled back tariffs on several European countries and talked about a “Gaza board of peace,” his talks were vague and his tone aggressive. European leaders warned that Trump’s unpredictable actions could still hurt their security and trust.
