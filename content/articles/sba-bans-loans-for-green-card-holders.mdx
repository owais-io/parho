Small Business Administration (SBA) now says only U.S. citizens can get its loan guarantees. Green‑card holders and other legal residents are barred. The move, part of new administrator Kelly Loeffler’s America‑First push, surprised owners like restaurant owner Aneesa Waheed. Critics say it hurts immigrant‑owned businesses and local economies. The change means many small‑business owners will need to find harder, costlier private funding.
