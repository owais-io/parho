Peter Mandelson, a former business secretary, is linked to U.S. tech firm Palantir, which has big UK contracts, including a £241m defence deal and a controversial NHS data platform. Campaigners say he may have leaked sensitive information to Jeffrey Epstein and helped secure Palantir contracts. MPs and politicians are urging the Cabinet Office to reveal his role and call for an independent inquiry, raising concerns about data privacy and reliance on foreign tech.
