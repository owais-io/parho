The piece talks about how some people in the UK, like Nigel Farage, have shouted “go home” to black and immigrant politicians. The writer, whose parents came from Jamaica and Africa, shows that many people have mixed roots. He points out new studies that say many minority citizens risk losing their British status. He urges us to rethink what “home” means and to talk more openly about belonging.
