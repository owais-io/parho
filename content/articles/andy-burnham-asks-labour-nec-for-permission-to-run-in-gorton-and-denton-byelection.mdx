Andy Burnham, former Manchester mayor, has written to Labour’s National Executive Committee asking permission to stand in the upcoming Gorton and Denton by‑election. He says the move protects the party’s best interests and defends the city’s values against divisive politics. Burnham will run a unifying, positive campaign and support national Labour policies. He has asked the NEC to decide before he makes further public comments.
