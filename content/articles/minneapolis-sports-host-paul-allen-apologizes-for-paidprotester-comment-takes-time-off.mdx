Paul Allen, a longtime sports commentator for Minneapolis station KFAN, said sorry after joking that people protesting the Trump‑era immigration crackdown were paid. The comment came after two ICE officer shootings that sparked city protests. Viewers and critics called for his resignation, and Allen said he would take a few days off. He explained the show stays away from politics and wanted to give listeners a break from heavy topics.
