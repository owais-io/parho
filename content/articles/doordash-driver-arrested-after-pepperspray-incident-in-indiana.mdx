A 29‑year‑old DoorDash driver in Indiana was arrested after a customer saw her spray pepper spray on a food order in front of a doorbell camera. The woman said she was trying to kill a spider, but officials said spiders would be inactive in the cold. DoorDash removed her account. The incident caused the couple to vomit and report the attack. Authorities are pressing felony charges.
