Liverpool won 6‑1 at Anfield against Qarabag in the Champions League, sending them straight to the last‑16 round. Mohamed Salah scored his first goal for the club since November, while Alexis Mac Allister, Florian Wirtz, Hugo Ekitiké and Federico Chiesa also found the net. The win was a relief after a rough Premier League season and helps manager Arne Slot keep the team in the top‑eight of Europe.
