Robin Birley, owner of London private clubs, plans a new Palm Beach venue to compete with Trump’s Mar‑a‑Lago. He’s secured a property and is working with designers to create a flashy American feel. The club will host wealthy Brits, right‑wing politicians, and celebrities, and will link his London clubs to U.S. locations. The move comes as many British elites move to Florida, driving up property prices and creating demand for exclusive clubs.
