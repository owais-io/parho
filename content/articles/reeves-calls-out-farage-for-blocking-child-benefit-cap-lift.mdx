Reeves says Farage’s opposition to raising the child benefit cap is racist and will keep many children poor. The chancellor, who will bring the bill to parliament on Thursday, is frustrated that the change has been delayed. Farage says the policy would mainly help foreign‑born families and wants to keep the limit. Reeves argues everyone deserves support, regardless of birthplace or skin colour.
