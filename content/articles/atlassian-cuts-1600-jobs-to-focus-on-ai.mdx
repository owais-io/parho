Software company Atlassian has laid off roughly 1,600 workers, about 10% of its staff, as it restructures to invest more in artificial intelligence. Most cuts are in research and development and engineering roles across the world. The co‑founder said the move is needed for the company’s future but apologised for the impact. Workers will receive pay packages and support, while a union says they were told too late.
