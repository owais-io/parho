Health regulators say it is dangerous to buy weight‑loss shots such as Wegovy and Mounjaro from unverified online sellers. Because the NHS can’t supply enough of them, a black market has grown, and the MHRA has seized £250k of fake pens. Counterfeit products can be contaminated or wrong doses, putting people at risk. Doctors urge patients to get prescription drugs only from licensed pharmacies.
