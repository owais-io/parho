Healthy life expectancy in Britain has dropped three years for women and two for men in just three years. Experts blame government cuts and poor handling of the pandemic. While Sweden’s numbers keep rising, the UK is now five years behind. If the government doesn’t act, older workers may leave the job market sooner. Academics say extending healthy life is key to keeping people working longer.
