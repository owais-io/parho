Venus Williams says she married actor Andrea Preti in a Palm Beach wedding last weekend. They met at Milan Fashion Week, got engaged in January, and had a small ceremony in Italy earlier this year. The Florida celebration included family, friends, a courthouse signing and a big party. The 45‑year‑old tennis star, a seven‑time Grand Slam champion, will keep playing into 2025 while staying active in fashion and culture.
