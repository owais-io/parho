US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) supervisor Andres Wilkinson has been charged with helping an undocumented woman live in the United States. Authorities say he gave her a home, credit cards and a car while she stayed in Laredo, Texas. They also say he knew she was in the country illegally and helped her move past border checkpoints. Wilkinson has not yet pleaded and the case is still pending.
