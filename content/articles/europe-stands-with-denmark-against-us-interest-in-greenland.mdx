European leaders – the UK, France, Germany and others – have joined forces to defend Greenland’s right to decide its future after a Trump aide hinted the US might try to take control. They said only Denmark and Greenland can decide what happens. Meanwhile, the US still talks about buying or using force. The leaders also promised new security help for Ukraine. Denmark is holding emergency talks, and the EU is watching the situation closely.
