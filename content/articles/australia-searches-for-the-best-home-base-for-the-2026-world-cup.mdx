With the 2026 World Cup coming up, the Socceroos are picking a “home away from home” in North America. They’re weighing hotel comfort, training facilities, travel time and local climate. While big cities like Southern California are tempting, a quieter spot would let the squad rest better. Other teams are doing the same hunt, and the Australian coach keeps eye on tiny details like pillows and coffee to keep the players happy.
