In Queensland, the house where a 2022 police shooting happened is now for sale at $190,000. Aidan Train, the executor of the killers’ estate, wants to sell it before anyone else can buy it. The police union had tried to buy it to stop it from falling into the hands of conspiracy theorists, but no deal was reached. The building is in poor shape and still holds items left by the killers. Possible buyers include the union or documentary makers.
