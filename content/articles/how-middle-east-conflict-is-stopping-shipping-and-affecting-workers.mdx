The fighting in the Middle East is shutting down the Strait of Hormuz, a key waterway that connects the Persian Gulf to the Gulf of Oman. Because of the strikes, many ships are stuck or rerouted, and port workers face more danger and uncertainty. Even big cruise liners with thousands of tourists are anchored near ports, keeping passengers on board. Workers and crews are calling for help to see how the crisis changes their daily jobs.
