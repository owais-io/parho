Trump has ordered a full blockade of all oil tankers that are under U.S. sanctions from entering or leaving Venezuela, saying the move will stop oil that funds drug trafficking and other crimes. The U.S. has already seized a tanker carrying about 2 million barrels of Venezuelan crude and has carried out military strikes near Venezuela, killing dozens. The step heightens tensions between the U.S. and Maduro’s regime.
