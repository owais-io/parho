In the Guardian column, Paul Daley warns that Australia should not follow the Trump‑led US and Israel in airstrikes against Iran. He argues the attacks are illegal, cause civilian deaths, and could pull Australia into a dangerous conflict. Daley calls for Australia to protect its independence, avoid military involvement, and question the US‑Israeli alliance. The column also highlights that joining the US could risk Australian troops and shift focus from regional security to global power struggles.
