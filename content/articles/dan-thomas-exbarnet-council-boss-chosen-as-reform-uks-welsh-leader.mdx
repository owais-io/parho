Nigel Farage announced Dan Thomas, former leader of Barnet council, as the new head of Reform UK in Wales ahead of Senedd elections. Thomas left the council in 2022, joined the party last year, and now returns to his Welsh home to fight Labour’s hold on the region. Farage praised Thomas’s experience and love for Wales, giving him full policy control. The party also has two Welsh members after recent defections.
