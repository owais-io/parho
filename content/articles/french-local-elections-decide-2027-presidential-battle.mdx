France is holding a second‑round vote in local elections that many see as a preview of the 2027 presidential race. Big cities like Paris and Marseille are still undecided, and the far‑right National Rally hopes to win key mayorships. In Paris, Socialist Emmanuel Grégoire faces a split right; in Marseille, the far‑right RN is close to the centre‑left incumbent. Alliances between parties will shape the outcome.
