European leaders have agreed to lend Ukraine €90bn (about $105bn) to cover budget gaps while the war with Russia continues. The loan will be interest‑free for two years and is backed by the EU budget, but it won’t come from frozen Russian assets. Ukrainian President Zelenskyy thanked the EU and said the money will help defend the country. In the U.S., President Trump urged Ukraine to finish peace talks quickly while diplomats in Miami plan new talks with Russia.
