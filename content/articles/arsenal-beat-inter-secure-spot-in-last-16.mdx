Arsenal kept their winning streak, beating Inter 3‑0 in the group stage and earning a top seed for the last‑16. Brazil striker Gabriel Jesus hit a brace, including a 19th‑season goal from a corner. The win gives Arsenal a comfortable lead in the Premier League and keeps their cup hopes alive. Jesus said it was a dream, and coach Mikel Arteta praised the squad’s confidence.
