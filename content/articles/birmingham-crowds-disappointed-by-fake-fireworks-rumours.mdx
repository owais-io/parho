Hundreds of people in Birmingham went to Centenary Square on New Year’s Eve expecting fireworks, but nothing happened because the display was a hoax spread online. Police said there was no city‑organised fireworks event and warned that repeating rumours each year can create safety problems and traffic jams. The city has faced the same issue last year, and similar fake event stories have upset visitors across the UK.
