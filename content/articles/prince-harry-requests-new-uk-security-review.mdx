Prince Harry has asked the UK Home Secretary, Shabana Mahmood, to review the security he gets when he visits Britain. He lost automatic police protection when he stepped down as a working royal and his court case was rejected. Harry says he and his family don’t feel safe in the UK. The royal security committee will re‑evaluate his threat level and report back next month. The government says its protection system is tight but won’t give more details.
