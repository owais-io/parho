An Australian tourist died in a Balinese hospital because the Australian consulate failed to issue an emergency passport needed for a medical transfer. Jake, his son, tried to get help and emails went unanswered. The consulate’s complaint was ignored for over two years, leaving the family with lasting trauma. The article also notes Australia’s $5.4 trillion wealth transfer to younger generations and police search bias, but the main focus is the consulate failure.
