Trump’s latest threats to hit Iran with military strikes come as the country’s crackdown on protesters has already killed thousands and silenced doctors. While he urges Iranians to keep protesting, he also warns of a “massive Armada” that could cause even more bloodshed. The article argues that strikes would hurt ordinary people, not help them, and that the best move is to revive diplomatic talks, support civil society, and keep the military out of the way.
