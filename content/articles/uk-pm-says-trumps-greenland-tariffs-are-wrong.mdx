Starmer says the US’s plan to slap a 10% tariff on the UK and other NATO allies for deploying troops to Greenland is wrong. Trump warned the levies would rise to 25% if a purchase deal fails. The UK insists Greenland is Danish territory and that its future should be decided by its people. Other politicians back the PM, calling the move a threat that could hurt businesses and undermine cooperation.
