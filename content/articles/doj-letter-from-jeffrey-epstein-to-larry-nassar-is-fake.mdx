On Tuesday, the Department of Justice said a letter that seemed to come from Jeffrey Epstein to former gymnastics doctor Larry Nassar was fake. The letter, dated August 2019 and found in prison mail, claimed Epstein and Nassar shared a love of young women and even mentioned President Trump. Investigators said the document was not genuine. The DOJ added that just because a paper is released by the DOJ doesn’t mean its claims are true.
