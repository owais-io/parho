A March heatwave has hit the western United States, breaking records across California, Nevada, Arizona and even northern California. Temperatures are 25‑35 degrees above normal, with places like Palm Springs, Phoenix and Los Angeles seeing highs over 100°F. Heat alerts stay in effect until Sunday, and national parks are warning visitors to stay hydrated and avoid hiking during peak heat. The spike shows extreme winter heat could continue into the weekend.
