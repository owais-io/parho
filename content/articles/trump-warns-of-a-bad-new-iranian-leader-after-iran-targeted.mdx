Trump said that if the US‑Israeli strike on Iran’s top leader is successful, the biggest risk is a new ruler who could be as bad as the old one. He praised the military effort but cautioned that Iran might still be dangerous and that protests could be risky. Trump also criticized Spain and the UK for not fully cooperating with US military plans and threatened trade cuts with Spain.
