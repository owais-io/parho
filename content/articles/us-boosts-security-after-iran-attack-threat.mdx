After the U.S. and Israel bombed Iran, U.S. officials stepped up security because they fear Iranian retaliation. Police in major cities added patrols around schools and places of worship, and the FBI and DHS have put counter‑terrorism teams on high alert. Experts warn that Iran could use direct attacks, cyberattacks or inspire lone‑wolf attacks. Some say the FBI has lost staff, but overall the U.S. remains prepared. No one should panic, just stay alert.
