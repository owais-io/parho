Because a strong flu strain is hitting the UK early this year, NHS leaders say people with cold or flu symptoms should wear masks in public, especially on buses and trains. They want to mimic Covid rules to stop people from passing the virus. The government says masks are optional, and encourages flu shots, which it has boosted for the winter. Some politicians think masks hurt social interaction and oppose mandates. Hospitals are at record flu patient levels.
