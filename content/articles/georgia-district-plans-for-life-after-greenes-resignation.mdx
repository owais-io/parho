Marjorie Taylor Greene said she will quit her seat in January. Residents in Ringgold and nearby towns are thinking about what happens next. People care more about everyday problems—housing costs, taxes, jobs—than political battles. Some locals feel the GOP is split between loud outsiders and moderate voters. They hope a calm, practical candidate will step forward. The change gives Democrats a chance in a usually red area.
