Tom Pickup, a Reform UK councillor on Lancashire County Council, was suspended after a WhatsApp group he joined shared hateful and extremist posts, including calls for genocide and violence. Pickup says his own remarks were taken out of context and that he didn’t know about the most extreme messages. The party is investigating; he remains on council as an independent while other councillors have urged his resignation.
