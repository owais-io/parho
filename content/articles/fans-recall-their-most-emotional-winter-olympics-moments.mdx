Readers shared their favorite Winter Olympics memories, from the first moguls race in 1992 to Shaun White’s 2018 gold. One fan remembers watching Edgar Grospiron jump over bumps in France, while another recalls Franz Klammer’s reckless downhill run in 1976. A former office worker talks about the perfect 6.0 by Torvill and Dean in 1984, and a snowboarder says Shaun White’s win made him cry. These stories highlight the power of sport to move people.
