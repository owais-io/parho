The French striker Ousmane Dembélé was named FIFA's best male player in 2025 after winning the Champions League with Paris Saint‑Germain, while Spanish midfielder Aitana Bonmatí claimed the women's title for the third year running as Barcelona reached a final and Spain were Euro runners‑up. England's coach Sarina Wiegman and Chelsea goalkeeper Hannah Hampton took the coaching and women's goalkeeper awards, respectively. The ceremony also highlighted the best XI line‑ups from top clubs.
