Two top‑tier women’s teams, Manchester United and Tottenham, face each other again after a dramatic 3‑3 draw last week. Fans say the game feels like a derby even though the clubs are far apart. Past clashes, a red‑card incident and a new manager on the Tottenham side have fed the tension. The next match will decide who goes into the league cup semi‑finals.
