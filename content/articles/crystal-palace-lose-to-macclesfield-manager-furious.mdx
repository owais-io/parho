Crystal Palace lost 2‑1 to sixth‑tier Macclesfield in the FA Cup. Manager Oliver Glasner was furious, saying the team showed no quality and didn't create chances. Palace became the first Premier League club to lose to non‑league opposition since 1909. Macclesfield’s win was emotional, as they dedicated it to a 21‑year‑old player who died in a car crash. Glasner blamed the squad, not the artificial pitch.
