On Sunday night, a 21‑year‑old man named Austin Tucker Martin, who had a shotgun, tried to get inside Donald Trump's Mar‑a‑Lago resort in Florida. Secret Service agents and a sheriff’s deputy confronted him inside the secured perimeter and shot him. Martin had come from North Carolina and had recently become obsessed with the Jeffrey Epstein files. Trump was not at the property and has not yet commented on the incident.
