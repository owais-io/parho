Global Counsel, a consulting firm co‑founded by former British politician Peter Mandelson, has gone into administration after clients pulled out following revelations that Mandelson had ties to convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. Barclays, Tesco, the Premier League and others have ended contracts, even after the firm tried to cut ties and sold Mandelson’s shares. The administrators will run the company and likely lay off many staff. The move follows similar fallout for co‑founder Benjamin Wegg‑Prosser.
