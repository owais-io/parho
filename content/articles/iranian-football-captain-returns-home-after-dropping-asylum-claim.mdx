The captain of Iran’s women’s national football team, Zahra Ghanbari, has turned back on an asylum offer and left Australia to return to Iran. She was the fifth player in the squad to change her mind after the Asian Cup. Australian officials say the decision was made freely, but Iranian officials and some players’ families reportedly pressured them. The move has drawn criticism and concern over the players’ safety back home.
