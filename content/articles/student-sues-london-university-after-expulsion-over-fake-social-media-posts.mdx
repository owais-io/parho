A former student, Sarah Mbabazi, is suing Richmond American University London for £98,000 after she was expelled in 2024 for alleged hate‑speech tweets. She claims she was impersonated by a fake account and that the university did not investigate properly. The Office of the Independent Adjudicator found the case weak and ordered a review. A new panel cleared her of misconduct, but she wants full damages, an apology, and a court case continues.
