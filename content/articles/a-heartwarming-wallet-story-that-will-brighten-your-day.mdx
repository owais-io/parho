While on a train to London, the author lost his wallet and felt embarrassed. A stranger named Natalya found it, took it to the Guardian office, and returned it with all items intact. Another journalist later had a similar experience. These stories show that everyday kindness still exists, even though the news often focuses on bad things. The author argues that we should remember these good moments and not let pessimism dominate our view.
