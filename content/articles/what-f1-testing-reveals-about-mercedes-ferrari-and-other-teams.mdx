During pre‑season testing, Mercedes showed reliability and handling, hinting they may be faster than they let on. Ferrari’s engines give them quick starts, making them hard to beat at the line. Red Bull’s energy system lets them keep power on straights, but some drivers still worry about the new rules. Aston Martin struggled with gearboxes, while new team Cadillac managed to run enough laps to stay hopeful. Midfield teams like Haas and Alpine are improving, still have big work ahead.
