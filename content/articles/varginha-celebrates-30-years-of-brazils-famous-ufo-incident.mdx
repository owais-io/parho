In 1996 the town of Varginha, Brazil, reported a strange creature that killed animals and scared locals. Some say it was an alien; others claim it was a hoax. A new documentary shows witnesses were paid, and a police report says the sightings were a mix of coincidence and mass panic. Despite the controversy, the town now sells alien souvenirs and attracts 200,000 tourists each year, turning the story into a local business.
