Victim Svetlana Pozhidaeva says Bard College president Leon Botstein, who met Epstein many times—including on his private island—helped make the sex‑trafficker look respectable. Emails show Botstein flew with Epstein in 2012 and spent time with him, while also fundraising. Bard’s board has hired WilmerHale to review Botstein’s contacts and any money linked to Epstein. The investigation also looks at his 50‑year tenure and the college’s handling of sexual‑harassment cases.
