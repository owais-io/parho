After centuries of trading, London's historic Smithfield meat market and Billingsgate fish market will move to a new site in the Royal Docks in east London. The City of London Corporation plans to build a new complex on a 10‑hectare brown‑field site owned by the Greater London Authority. The move should create about 2,200 jobs and keep traders in the capital while boosting local spending. Traders will be supported to find new premises, and apprenticeship programs will continue.
