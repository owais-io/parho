Senator Sarah Henderson has publicly attacked the ABC over its live analysis of the U.S. and Israel’s airstrikes on Iran. She demanded the ABC’s editor be removed and called for an independent review. The ABC’s ombudsman said the reporting was fair and met standards. Meanwhile, the broadcaster is facing a possible 24‑hour strike by its union, adding tension to the dispute.
