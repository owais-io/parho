Since Feb. 25, 2026, British citizens with a second passport must show a valid UK passport or a special certificate to fly, take a ferry, or use a train to enter the UK. Many dual‑nationals were caught off guard and could be stranded overseas. Liberal Democrat MP Will Forster is demanding a “grace period” to help those who don’t yet have a passport, and airlines are warning they’ll need government approval to let people board.
