Israel announced a new ground operation in southern Lebanon, ordering tens of thousands of displaced residents to leave their homes. The move has raised alarms that Israel might keep the area occupied, similar to past wars. Up to 850 people have died and about a million civilians have been displaced. Some villages were told they could stay, but many fear forced, permanent exile. European leaders warned the offensive could lead to a prolonged conflict.
