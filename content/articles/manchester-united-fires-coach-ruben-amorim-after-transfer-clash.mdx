Manchester United ended Ruben Amorim’s 14‑month stint as head coach after a clash with the club’s hierarchy over transfer plans. Amorim had wanted a larger squad and was unhappy that director Jason Wilcox was controlling signings. The club said a new coach would help them climb the table from sixth place. With big money spent and little progress, the board decided a change was needed. Amorim will leave after the season.
