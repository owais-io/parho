During a Pentagon press conference, Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth bragged that the U.S. is winning the fight against Iran and can keep fighting as long as needed. He said the U.S. will soon control Iranian skies. Meanwhile, Congress is set to vote on a Democratic war‑powers bill that would force President Trump to ask for approval before any new attacks. Five Republicans and some Democrats will need to back the bill.
