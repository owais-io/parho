Trump has called Netflix a “political hack” over Susan Rice, demanding she leave the board or face consequences, even as the company fights to buy Warner Bros Discovery. The move comes as Netflix and Paramount Skydance race to outbid each other for the media giant, raising concerns about a new streaming monopoly. Trump’s remarks come after he previously said he wouldn’t intervene in the deal, but his new threats aim to influence the outcome.
