Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has called a cabinet meeting to tackle a fuel supply crisis triggered by the war in Iran, warning that old economic reforms won’t fix the problem. He also said no Australian Defence Force personnel were hurt when an Iranian missile hit a UAE base. Meanwhile, authorities warn of a potentially record‑size tropical cyclone hitting Queensland’s coast, adding to the day’s worries.
