Police in Canada warned Sikh activist Moninder Singh that his family was at risk because of threats that may come from India. This comes just before Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney visits India for trade talks. Singh says he and his wife and children have been threatened, and he believes the danger comes from India’s campaign against Sikh activists. The Canadian government says it has stopped the threat, but many fear the visit could silence critics.
