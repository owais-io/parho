Ben Stokes says he will keep leading England even after a 3‑0 loss in the Ashes. He calls the tour a disappointment and blames the weak preparation. Coach Brendon McCullum finally admits the training plan was wrong and takes responsibility. England’s fast bowlers were sloppy, and many players, like Nathan Lyon and Pat Cummins, are injured or uncertain to play. The team now faces a tough road ahead.
