Massive monsoon rains and tropical storms flooded parts of Indonesia, Thailand, Sri Lanka, Malaysia and Vietnam. In Sumatra, floods have killed over 1,100 people, while Thailand’s Hat Yai saw 176 deaths. Villagers like Aminah Ali and Busra Ishak clambered onto rooftops, fought against 3‑meter‑high currents, and were rescued after days of waiting. The floods tore homes, cut roads, left many with no food or clean water, and exposed weak warning systems.
