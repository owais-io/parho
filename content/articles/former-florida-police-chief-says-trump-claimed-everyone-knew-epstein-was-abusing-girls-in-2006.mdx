Former Palm Beach police chief Michael Reiter says he spoke to Trump in 2006, and Trump told him that “everyone has known he’s been doing this” about Jeffrey Epstein. Reiter’s claim appears in the Justice Department’s release of 3 million Epstein files and clashes with Trump’s later comments that he had no idea about Epstein’s abuse. The story comes amid criticism that Trump downplayed the Epstein scandal and the Justice Department has yet to confirm the exchange.
