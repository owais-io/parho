Trump said he would send ICE agents to U.S. airports if Congress refuses to fund the Department of Homeland Security. The move comes as the TSA is short‑staffed after a shutdown, with many workers quitting or sick‑out because they haven’t been paid. ICE officers are not trained for airport security, so the plan could worsen travel delays. Trump’s threat is part of a larger budget fight that’s left many airports scrambling.
