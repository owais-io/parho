Telco Services Australia, a Perth‑based call‑centre company, won a big $90 million contract with Centrelink but reported no corporate tax for two years, even though it earned over $185 million. The company says related‑party payments wiped out profits, so nothing was owed. No illegal activity has been proven, but the complex group structure hides how much tax is really paid. The story highlights how many Australian government agencies depend on outsourced call centres.
