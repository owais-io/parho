Serbian President Aleksandar Vučić said he would punish those involved after a planned Trump‑owned tower in Belgrade was halted. Protesters and a prosecutor stopped the $500 million project, and a minister was charged with abuse of office. Vučić vowed to hold the people responsible. The move follows backlash over the Trump family’s business ties, a heritage‑site dispute, and Serbia’s energy troubles.
