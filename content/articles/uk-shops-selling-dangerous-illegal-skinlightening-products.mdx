Trading standards officers warn that skin‑lightening creams sold in butchers, small grocery stores and online are often illegal and contain dangerous ingredients like hydroquinone, mercury or strong steroids. These products can cause skin damage, infections and even organ harm. The law requires clear labelling and UK manufacturers. Consumers are urged to avoid unlabelled kits, not use them on children, and report suspicious items. Enforcement actions include fines and court cases.
