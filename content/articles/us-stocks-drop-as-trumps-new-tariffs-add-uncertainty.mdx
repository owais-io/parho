U.S. stocks fell sharply on Monday after President Trump announced new tariffs on all countries, even though the Supreme Court said his earlier tariffs were illegal. The move added uncertainty for investors and pushed major indexes down. A poll shows most Americans, including many Democrats, support the court ruling and dislike the tariff plan. The decision comes as the U.S. heads into midterm elections and investors worry about future trade deals.
