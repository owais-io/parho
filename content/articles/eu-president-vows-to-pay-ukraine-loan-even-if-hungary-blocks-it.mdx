European Commission president Ursula von der Leyen said the EU will find a way to give Ukraine a €90 billion loan even though Hungary’s Prime Minister Viktor Orbán is blocking it. The loan is meant to help Kyiv fight Russia. EU leaders could not convince Orbán to lift the ban at a Brussels summit, so the EU is looking for other routes to release the money. Meanwhile, talks between Kyiv, Washington and Moscow are on pause.
