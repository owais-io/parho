After the Taliban took control of Afghanistan in 2021, Pakistan thought the new rulers would keep the country friendly and stop its use as a base for militants. That didn’t happen. Taliban and the Pakistani TTP now fight each other, and Pakistan’s soldiers have been hit by attacks that cross the border. Islamabad’s leaders said patience ran out and they started airstrikes, turning the region into a battlefield. Both sides want talks, but the relationship is tense.
