Pope Leo has urged that airplanes be used for peace, not war. He said aerial bombings should be banned, calling them a return to the horrors of the 20th century. The pope spoke during a meeting with ITA Airways staff and later called for a ceasefire during his Angelus prayer, urging the world to stop suffering from conflicts like the US‑Iran tensions. He wants prayers and peace to end hostilities.
