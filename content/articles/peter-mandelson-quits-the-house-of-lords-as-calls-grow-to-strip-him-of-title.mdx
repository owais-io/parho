Peter Mandelson has stepped down from the House of Lords, but critics say he should lose his title because of past misconduct. Starmer wants the government to strip him of the honor, arguing that his appointments came from patronage rather than merit. The article also points to wider corruption in the Lords, where many peers have been paid by businesses and foreign governments.
