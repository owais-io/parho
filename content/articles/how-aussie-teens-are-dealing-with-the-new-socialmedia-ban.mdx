Australia’s new law stops kids under 16 from creating accounts on major social‑media sites. The ban has sparked similar moves in the UK, France, and other European countries. Over 4.7 million accounts were flagged and shut, but many teens simply create new profiles with fake birthdays or switch to messaging apps. Some say the ban is a hassle, others report less screen time and more real‑world interaction.
