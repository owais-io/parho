American bobsledder Elana Meyers Taylor finally won gold in the monobob event at the Beijing Winter Olympics, her fifth Games. She has earned medals at every Olympics she has attended, but injuries and her role as a mother to two deaf children with Down syndrome made the journey tough. The win shows her determination and also makes her a role model for disabled families and women in sport. She hopes her story inspires others.
