Australian police say they have received crime reports over comments made by Pauline Hanson about Muslims. The police aren’t sure yet if a criminal investigation will start, but they will speak later. Hanson’s words, which many see as hateful, have prompted the mayor of Canterbury Bankstown to call for charges. The comments have also sparked threats to mosques and criticism from other politicians.
