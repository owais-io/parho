Two small sailing boats carrying food and medicine that disappeared on their way from Mexico to Cuba were found safe by the US Coast Guard. The boats were part of a humanitarian convoy and were expected in Havana in a few days. Cuban leader Miguel Díaz‑Canel expressed deep concern and said authorities were searching for the nine people aboard. The rescue message arrived late Friday, ending the mystery.
