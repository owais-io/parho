The International Energy Agency says the war in Iran has triggered an oil crisis worse than the 1970s shocks, with the Strait of Hormuz closed and losing 11 million barrels of oil per day. Australian fuel stations are running short of petrol and diesel, while ABC staff are set to strike for the first time in 20 years, likely causing news disruptions.
