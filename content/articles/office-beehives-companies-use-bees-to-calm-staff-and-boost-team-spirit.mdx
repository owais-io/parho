More UK firms are putting bee hives on office roofs, courtyards and car parks to give workers a nature break. Watching bees eases nerves, sparks teamwork and shows a green image. Some businesses stream hive cameras into break rooms, while others bring hives to staff lunches. The trend improves mental health, but experts warn that too many hives could hurt local wildlife.
