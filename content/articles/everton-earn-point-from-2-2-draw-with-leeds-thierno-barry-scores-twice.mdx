Everton held a 2‑2 draw with Leeds at Goodison Park. After a strong first half for the visitors, Everton’s manager David Moyes changed his lineup, giving the team a boost. French striker Thierno Barry, who had struggled to score earlier in the season, netted twice – once from a cross and again with a sharp finish. The game ended in a point for each side, with both teams looking to improve.
