Novak Djokovic said he won’t give up in his Australian Open semi‑final against Jannik Sinner, even though he’s the heavy underdog. The 38‑year‑old has reached five straight grand slam semi‑finals but hasn’t won a set since the third round this year, and his body has been strained before. Sinner has beaten him at the last three majors and dominates the tour. Djokovic is focused on conserving energy to reach the final.
