After ICE agents shot three people, including a woman named Renee Good, across Minneapolis and Portland, thousands of people are marching across the country. Protesters in cities from New York to Tucson, Phoenix, and Durham want ICE to leave communities and demand justice for the victims. Groups like Indivisible, ACLU, and labor unions are coordinating events and calling on lawmakers to act.
