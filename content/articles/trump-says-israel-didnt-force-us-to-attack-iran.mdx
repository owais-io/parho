Donald Trump said the U.S. hit Iran on its own, not because Israel pushed him. The president rebuffed criticism from Senate Democrats after Secretary of State Marco Rubio implied the strike was a pre‑emptive move against Iranian retaliation for Israeli attacks. Trump said he feared Iranians would attack first, so the U.S. had to act. He also left open the possibility of sending troops, adding uncertainty to the conflict.
