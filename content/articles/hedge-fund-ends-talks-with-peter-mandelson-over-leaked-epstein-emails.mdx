A London hedge fund, Rokos Capital Management, ended talks with former UK business secretary Peter Mandelson after emails showed he may have leaked sensitive government data to Jeffrey Epstein. The revelations, linking Mandelson to Epstein and showing he sent crisis‑related info to the convicted sex offender, sparked a police investigation. RCM cut ties instantly, citing the scandal, and Mandelson stepped down from the House of Lords.
