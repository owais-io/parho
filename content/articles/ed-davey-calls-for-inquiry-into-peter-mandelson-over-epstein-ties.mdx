Lib Democrat leader Ed Davey said on Radio 4 that a public inquiry is needed into former Labour minister Peter Mandelson, who may have leaked government data to Jeffrey Epstein. Davey wants a bill to strip Mandelson of his peerage and believes the case for an investigation is overwhelming. The article also recounts Mandelson’s own interview, where he denies wrongdoing and calls himself a “naive” ally of Epstein. Many politicians want to remove his title and hold him accountable.
