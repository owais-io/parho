A six‑year‑old girl was the sole survivor of a fatal train collision near Adamuz in southern Spain, where 42 people died, including her parents, brother, and cousin. The crash happened when a high‑speed train from Málaga crashed into a train coming from Madrid. The little girl was found walking on the tracks with a minor head wound. Authorities are still recovering bodies, and the country is mourning the loss.
