A Costa Rican family says their brother, Randall Gamboa, was deported from the United States in a vegetative state and later died. He had been in Texas detention, then flown home on an air ambulance. The family describes him as severely ill, with skin ulcers and a bad odor, and believes he was mistreated. U.S. and Costa Rican officials have not answered questions about his health or treatment, and the cause of death is still unclear.
