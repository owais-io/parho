Guardiola told Erling Haaland not to push himself to score more goals, saying he’s still young and improving. After the penalty that sealed a 2-1 win over Liverpool – Haaland’s third goal in 10 games – the striker said he needs to get better but won’t blame fatigue. Guardiola praised Haaland’s calm, composure and his vital role for City in the league and Champions League.
