The Pope has chosen Bishop Richard Moth, 67, to lead the Roman Catholic Church in England and Wales. He will replace Cardinal Vincent Nichols as archbishop of Westminster. Moth, who ran the Arundel and Brighton diocese since 2015, said he wants to get to know the priests and people of Westminster and keep building on strong foundations. He’s also active on social justice, assisted‑dying debates, and refugee support. He’ll take the post on Feb. 14.
