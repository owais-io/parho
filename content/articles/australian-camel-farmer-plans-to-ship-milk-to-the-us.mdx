Australian farmer Paul Martin runs a camel dairy in Queensland and plans to ship 60,000 litres of milk to the US this year. Camel milk is a health trend, allergy‑friendly and popular with Somali and Arab communities. The crop could grow into a new market and help manage Australia’s feral camel population. Somali‑Australian Ahmed Selat visits the farm, linking cultural ties and new food ideas.
