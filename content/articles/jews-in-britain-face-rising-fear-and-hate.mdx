In 2025 Britain recorded 3,700 antisemitic incidents, the second‑highest ever, sparking fear in Jewish communities. Schools now rely on heavy security, and attacks on the Hatzola charity’s ambulances have made headlines. The piece calls on police and the government to step up protection and urges ordinary Britons to stand with Jews, reminding readers that anti‑Semitic hate threatens the whole country.
