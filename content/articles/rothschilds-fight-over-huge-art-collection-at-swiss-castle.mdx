Two senior members of the Rothschild family are fighting over a huge collection of art, furniture and historic items kept at the Château de Pregny in Switzerland. 93‑year‑old Nadine, widow of Edmond, says she inherited the pieces and wants to open a Geneva museum. Her daughter‑in‑law, Ariane, argues the collection should stay together in the castle. Both have won and lost court cases, and the battle is still on.
