The U.S. is pressuring Thailand to stay committed to a ceasefire with Cambodia, saying trade talks could be paused if the country backs out. Thailand has paused the deal after accusing Cambodia of new landmines that injured a soldier. Since then, there have been casualties along the border. Trump mentioned the “threat of tariffs” to keep the truce alive, but Thailand says trade talks will continue separately.
