In Hopkinstown near Pontypridd, a community group raised over £70,000 to buy Capel Rhondda, the chapel where the hymn Cwm Rhondda was first performed. The Grade‑II listed former Baptist chapel will become a community space for choirs and Welsh‑language groups. The Welsh government added a £10,000 grant for repairs, showing how locals can protect historic sites.
