Donald Trump is pushing the Save America Act, a strict voter‑ID law that would require proof of citizenship and limit mail‑in ballots. He said he will refuse to sign any bill until Congress passes it, warning that the law could shut out millions of voters, including married women with changed names. The proposal also adds a ban on transgender transition surgeries for minors. Critics say the act would undermine voting rights.
