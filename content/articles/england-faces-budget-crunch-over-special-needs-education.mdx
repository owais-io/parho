England’s special‑needs schools are running out of money. A report from the Institute for Fiscal Studies says spending on children with SEND will double by 2028, squeezing funds for regular schools. Ministers must decide whether to slow SEND spending, cut mainstream budgets, or raise extra money. The government plans a new white paper to expand SEND support in mainstream schools, but the system still falls short of families’ needs.
