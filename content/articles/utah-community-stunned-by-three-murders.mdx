A 22‑year‑old man named Ivan Miller was arrested in Utah for three brutal murders that shocked a small town. The victims were 34‑year‑old Natalie Graves, 65‑year‑old Linda Dewey and 86‑year‑old Margaret Oldroyd. The killings happened near a trail in Capitol Reef National Park and spread across Utah, Colorado and New Mexico. Police found the suspects’ cars and weapons, then caught Miller near one of the cars. Residents are mourning and adding pink ribbons to honor the victims.
