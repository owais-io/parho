The writer realized they were tricked by AI‑generated posts and worries about older family members—often called “WhatsApp aunties”—getting fake videos and news. These elder relatives can spread misinformation, especially in diaspora communities, where they have a lot of influence. Instead of shouting “it’s fake,” the author suggests a gentle approach: first show interest, then point out odd signs like glitches or missing shadows. This keeps conversations respectful and helps keep the community’s info safe.
