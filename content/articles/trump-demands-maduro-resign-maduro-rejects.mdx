Trump said he had an urgent phone call with Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro and gave him a clear demand: resign now or leave the country. Maduro refused, demanding global amnesty and keeping control of the armed forces. The U.S. has also sent a navy to Venezuela’s coast and has been pressuring the country for months. While Trump offers safe passage, many think the threats are just rhetoric.
