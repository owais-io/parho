Pedro Sánchez has taken a stand against Donald Trump over Spain's refusal to let US troops use its bases to strike Iran. In a short televised address, the Spanish prime minister rejected Trump's trade threat and said Spain would not support the war. While France and Germany stayed neutral, Spain’s move shows it is ready to oppose the US. Other European leaders, like Italy and Denmark, have avoided speaking out, keeping their own positions.
