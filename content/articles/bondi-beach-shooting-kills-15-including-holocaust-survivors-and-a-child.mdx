A gunman opened fire on Bondi Beach on Sunday, killing 15 people and injuring 38. The victims included Holocaust survivors, volunteers, faith leaders, and a child, many of whom were at a Hanukkah celebration. It’s Australia’s deadliest mass shooting since 1996. Police say the victims’ ages ranged from 10 to 87. Support hotlines are listed for those feeling scared.
