French prosecutors want the appeal court to keep Marine Le Pen’s five‑year ban on running for public office. They also ask for a four‑year prison term, with three years suspended and one year at home under an electronic bracelet, plus a €100,000 fine. Le Pen was found guilty of stealing €4.8 million from EU staff‑pay funds. If the ban stays, she can’t run in 2027.
