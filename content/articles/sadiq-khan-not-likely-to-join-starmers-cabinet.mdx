Sadiq Khan’s allies say the rumours that he could be made a peer and join Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s cabinet are just speculation. They say a decision on a peerage would come from Downing Street, and they ruled out using Khan to boost the new government’s talent pool. Khan could still sit in the Lords while staying mayor, but he has not yet decided whether he wants a fourth term.
