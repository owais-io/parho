In New South Wales, Premier Chris Minns said the police commissioner will decide when to activate new protest‑ban powers that can last up to three months after a terror attack. The laws also tighten gun rules, cap how many guns people can own, and plan a buy‑back scheme. Protest groups say they’ll sue the laws. Minns visited a mosque to stress unity and warned of extra police presence over Christmas.
