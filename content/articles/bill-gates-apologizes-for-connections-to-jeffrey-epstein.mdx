Bill Gates apologized to his foundation staff after learning about his ties to Jeffrey Epstein. He said it was a big mistake to spend time with the convicted sex offender and that he never took part in any of Epstein’s crimes. Gates admitted to two affairs, one with a Russian bridge player and another with a physicist. He warned that his association hurt the foundation’s reputation and promised to do better.
