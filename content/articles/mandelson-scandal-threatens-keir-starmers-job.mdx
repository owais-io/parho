Peter Mandelson, a former Labour minister, is being removed from the House of Lords after new evidence links him to Jeffrey Epstein. He was recently named the UK’s ambassador to Washington, a move that has shocked many in the government. Labour leader Keir Starmer and his chief of staff, Morgan McSweeney, face criticism for the choice. Opposition parties want documents showing whether Starmer knew about the links before appointing Mandelson, raising doubts about his judgment.
