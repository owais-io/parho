Trump says the rapid rise of data‑center power use could push up electricity costs and hurt his party’s chances in November. He and Microsoft agreed the tech giant will pay full taxes and no discounts, and he wants the grid operator to hold a reliability auction. OpenAI is also promising to pay its own way. The move comes amid a global push to manage data‑center energy use.
