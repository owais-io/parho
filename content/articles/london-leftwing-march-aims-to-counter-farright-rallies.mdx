London’s big protest on Saturday will bring thousands of left‑wing groups, unions and famous musicians to march toward Trafalgar Square. Organisers say it’s a counter‑demonstration to the growing far‑right rallies that have spread racist slogans. The march unites a wide range of parties—Labour, Green, SNP, Lib Dems—and cultural figures like Paloma Faith and actors. It hopes to show that progressive forces can organise together against hate.
