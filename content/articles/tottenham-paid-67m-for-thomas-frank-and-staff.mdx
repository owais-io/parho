Tottenham paid 6.7 million pounds to bring former Brentford coach Thomas Frank and his team after they were let go. The money appears in Brentford’s latest accounts. Spurs sacked Frank after eight months, having lost many games and only just above relegation. The fee will cost Spurs more when they fire him now, while Brentford shows big profits and higher player costs.
