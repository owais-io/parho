A survey of 3,000 U.S. employees shows almost a quarter didn’t take a holiday last year, and 82% avoided using their paid time off. Experts say the fear comes from worrying that AI and automation might make them replaceable. The piece argues that managers should let staff use their leave, that unlimited PTO can boost morale, and that tech can actually help workers enjoy more breaks.
