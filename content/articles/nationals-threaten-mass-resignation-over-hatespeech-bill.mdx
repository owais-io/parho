Three Nationals senators crossed the floor to oppose a Labor hate‑speech bill, breaking shadow‑cabinet rules. Their leader, David Littleproud, wrote to shadow leader Sussan Ley threatening that all Nationals frontbenchers would quit if the resignations were accepted. Ley insists cabinet solidarity is non‑negotiable. A virtual crisis meeting is set for 6 pm to decide. The move could topple Ley’s leadership and split the Coalition, putting the government’s new laws at risk.
