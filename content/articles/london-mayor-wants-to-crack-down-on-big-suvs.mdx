Super‑sized SUVs are now common in London and other cities, but they’re big, unsafe and polluting. The article explains that children are far more likely to be killed by an SUV, and that these cars use up more parking space and more fuel. London Mayor Sadiq Khan wants to put higher parking fees and extra charges on SUV owners to protect people and cut pollution. He believes that bold steps can win public support.
