Kirsten Shaw, who runs the post office at Rothera Research Station in Antarctica, asked Royal Mail for a new postbox. Royal Mail sent a small red “lamp” box with the King Charles III logo. The postbox will let station staff collect and send letters to family. Mail comes to Antarctica only a few times a year via ship or plane, and costs just 87p to send to the UK.
