Manchester City have won five games in a row, beating Crystal Palace 3-0 in the FA Cup and winning against Real Madrid earlier. The win puts them just two points behind league leaders Arsenal. Manager Pep Guardiola praised the team's fighting spirit and their defence, especially captain Rúben Dias. He said the club is improving after tough losses to Newcastle and Bayer Leverkusen. Palace's boss said City were better in the box.
