In the FA Cup’s fourth round, Manchester City beat Salford 2‑0, Burnley edged Mansfield 2‑1, and Norwich defeated West Brom 3‑1. Other key results: Southampton lost to Leicester, and Aston Villa faced Newcastle. The day saw plenty of drama, including a late goal by Norwich’s Ben Slimane and a free‑kick winner for City. The wins move each club closer to the next round.
