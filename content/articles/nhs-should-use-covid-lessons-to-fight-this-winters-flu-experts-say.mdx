Flu cases are climbing and doctors are on strike, so the NHS is under pressure. Experts say the pandemic taught us useful tricks that can help this winter. They urge more free flu shots, easier ways to get vaccinated, better sick pay so people can stay home, and better ventilation in offices and schools. If we do these things, hospitals won’t be swamped and people will stay healthier.
