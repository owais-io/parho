The Melbourne Cricket Ground now hosts a new Shane Warne exhibit called “Treasures of a Legend.” A team from the Rest Is History podcast got a behind‑the‑scenes tour. They saw Warne’s collection of hats, helmets, bats, stumps, boots and the famous ball that changed his career. The museum worked with Warne’s family, who added voice‑over guides. Fans get to see the real objects that made the legend.
