I rode a crowded bus on St Patrick's Day and was offered a seat by an older man, accepted it to sit with a friend, and later two other men offered seats. I declined each time to keep from “letting the old man in,” reflecting on aging, self‑image, and staying active. The piece mixes humor with a personal view on age and bus manners.
