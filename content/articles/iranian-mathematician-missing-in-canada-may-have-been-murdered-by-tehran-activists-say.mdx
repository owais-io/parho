Police in Canada say an Iranian mathematician and activist, Masood Masjoody, was likely murdered after he disappeared in February. He had spoken out against Iran’s government and the former shah’s family. Activists fear the case shows Tehran’s effort to silence critics abroad. The RCMP has not found a body and is still searching. They say the investigation is ongoing and no suspects are known yet.
