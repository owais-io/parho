Walid Khalidi, a Palestinian scholar and centenarian, passed away on March 8, 2026. He proved that about 750,000 Palestinians fled or were expelled during the 1947‑48 war, challenging the Israeli claim that they left by order of Arab leaders. Khalidi wrote two key books, co‑founded the Institute of Palestine Studies, taught at universities in Beirut, Harvard and Princeton, and helped push for a two‑state solution. His work shaped the modern Palestinian story and global understanding of the conflict.
