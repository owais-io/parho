A brutal police raid on 28 October 2025 in Rio's Complexo da Penha favela left 122 dead, including five officers. The operation, called Operation Containment, aimed to catch the boss of the Red Command gang, Edgar Alves de Andrade, nicknamed “the Bear.” None of the killed were on the arrest list, and one victim had no ties to crime. The raid was condemned as a massacre, and the gang boss remains at large.
