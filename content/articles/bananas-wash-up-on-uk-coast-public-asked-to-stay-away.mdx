When 16 containers fell off the Baltic Klipper near Portsmouth, bananas, plantains and avocados drifted onto West Sussex beaches. The Coastguard and local councils urged people to stay away while they clear the wrecked containers, as the containers are hazardous and the fruit may be spoiled. The Receiver of Wreck didn’t need to be notified because the goods are perishable. Supermarkets said there’s no emergency, but the public should not take the fruit.
