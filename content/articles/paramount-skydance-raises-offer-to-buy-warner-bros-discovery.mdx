Paramount Skydance lifted its bid to buy Warner Bros Discovery, raising the $30‑per‑share offer to a higher price. The move comes as Warner’s board set a deadline for offers and Netflix is also eyeing the deal. Warner and Paramount declined to comment, while Netflix didn’t respond. If Warner accepts, it would trigger a $2.8 billion breakup fee for Netflix. Trump’s recent remarks and Netflix’s CEO said the deal stays business‑only.
