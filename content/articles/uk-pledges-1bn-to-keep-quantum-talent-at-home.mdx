The UK government says it will stop quantum scientists and startups from moving abroad and has promised £1bn to help build a quantum computer. Technology Secretary Liz Kendall explained that Britain wants to keep its talent in fields now dominated by the U.S., especially AI. The funding will help companies design quantum machines that could speed up research in medicine, finance and energy. Quantum computers use special bits called qubits that can hold many states at once.
