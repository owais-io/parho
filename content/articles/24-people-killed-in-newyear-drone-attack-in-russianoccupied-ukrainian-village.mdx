A Ukrainian drone strike during New Year celebrations in a Russian‑occupied village in Kherson killed 24 people and injured about 50. Russian officials say the drones hit a café and hotel, claiming one had incendiary chemicals. Kyiv says it only targets Russian military sites. Meanwhile, Russia claims Ukraine tried to hit President Putin’s home but denies proof. Both sides are in tense negotiations, with peace talks gaining momentum.
