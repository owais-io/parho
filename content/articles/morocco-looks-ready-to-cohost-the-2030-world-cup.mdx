The Africa Cup of Nations this year shows Morocco has the stadiums, pitches, hotels and trains it needs for the 2030 World Cup. Six cities could host matches, and a new 115,000‑seat stadium is under construction. Transport is good but some smaller stadiums could crowd out fans. On the pitch, African teams are getting stronger – Morocco even beat Spain and Portugal in the last World Cup. So Morocco looks ready for 2030.
