Spain says it will not let the United States use its military bases to help fight Iran, rejecting a claim from the White House. The Spanish defence minister and foreign minister both denied that Madrid has changed its position, calling the comments false. While France has temporarily allowed US aircraft on its bases, Spain is deploying a frigate to protect Cyprus and is holding a naval group in the Mediterranean. EU leaders are also discussing the situation.
