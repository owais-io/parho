England’s 12‑game winning streak snapped when Ireland beat them at Twickenham. The Irish team showed calm, experience, and strong defense, while England made many errors, missed penalties, and received two yellow cards. The loss is a blow to coach Steve Borthwick, who had been building a strong squad, and raises doubts about England’s future plans.
