Australia has been asked to help Middle‑East countries hit by Iran’s missile and drone attacks. The government says it will not send ground troops, but could use its AWACS Wedgetail aircraft for surveillance and the NASAMS missile system to defend against short‑ and medium‑range threats. Ministers are debating the details, while Greens warn against deeper involvement and call for caution.
