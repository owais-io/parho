Porsche announced more layoffs after a costly reversal of its electric car strategy and a slump in China sales. New CEO Michael Leiters said the company will streamline management and cut bureaucracy, with more details coming in autumn. Porsche’s profit fell sharply, and a €3.9 bn write‑down marked the cost of changing course. The company will focus on profitable combustion models and delay new electric vehicles.
