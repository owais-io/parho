Chelsea’s new manager, Enzo Maresca, didn’t explain why he said he’d had the club’s “worst 48 hours,” which has caused a lot of speculation. He avoided naming anyone, only said owners are important, and refused to say whether he’s talking to the club’s directors. His comments have made the press and fans wonder if he’s unhappy. With Chelsea still fighting for titles, his comments could put his position in jeopardy.
