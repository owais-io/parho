Sky has announced a UK version of the long‑running American sketch show Saturday Night Live. While some, like John Oliver, call it a bad idea, the format could fit Britain if it keeps the quick‑turnover, weekly‑written structure and a big‑name monologue. Past US attempts at British shows have failed, and UK viewers lack the nostalgia that keeps the original alive, so the new series will need fresh material to win over audiences.
