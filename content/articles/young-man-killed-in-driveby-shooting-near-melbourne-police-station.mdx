A young man was shot and died outside a police station in Melbourne’s Fitzroy after midnight. Police say it was a targeted drive‑by: a silver SUV fired two shots at two men walking past the station. The victims fled, and the gunman escaped. No arrests yet. Investigators are looking for the SUV and want witnesses to call CrimeStoppers to help catch the suspect.
