Ukraine hit two Russian factories that build and repair military transport planes about 800 km from the border, damaging hangars and some aircraft and cutting Russia’s ability to keep planes. Other news: a pro‑Putin social media star has publicly criticized the president; Spanish police arrested people helping a Russian businessman dodge sanctions; the UK is preparing a court case over money raised by billionaire Abramovich. Russia says a Ukrainian drone hit an LNG tanker and it captured a village in Donetsk.
