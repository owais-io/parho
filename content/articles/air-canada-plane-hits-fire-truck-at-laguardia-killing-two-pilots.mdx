An Air Canada Express plane was landing at New York’s LaGuardia Airport when it hit a fire truck that was responding to another emergency. Both pilots on the CRJ‑900 were killed and nine people were injured. The crash closed the airport for hours, causing flight delays and cancellations. Investigators are looking into air‑traffic‑control decisions and whether staffing levels were a factor.
