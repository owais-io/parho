Trump let India buy Russian oil for 30 days, but sanctions have been strangling Russia’s economy. The move could lower oil prices and help Russia earn hard‑currency, hurting the sanctions effort. Russia is already cutting investments and hiding debt, and the US is seizing rogue tankers and blocking car exports. With oil prices high and a domestic cost‑of‑living crisis, Trump’s decision is seen as risky for US policy.
