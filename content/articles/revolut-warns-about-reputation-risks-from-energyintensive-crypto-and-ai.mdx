Revolut, which recently became a full UK bank, announced a 57% jump in profits and a 46% rise in revenue, hitting a £1.7bn pre‑tax profit. It also added 16 million customers, bringing its global user base to 68 million. The company warned that its support for crypto mining and AI data centres—both power‑hungry—could hurt its reputation as energy prices climb. Revolut plans to expand services, but must balance growth with environmental concerns.
