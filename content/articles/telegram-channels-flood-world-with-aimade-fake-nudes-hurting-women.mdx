Millions of people are using Telegram to share AI‑made fake nudes, spreading sexual abuse worldwide. At least 150 secret channels offer paid services that turn photos into erotic videos or images. Even after Telegram says it removes illegal content, hundreds of thousands of deepfakes keep surfacing. The rise is fueled by cheap apps and a lack of laws protecting women, especially in poorer countries. Victims suffer mental health problems, job loss and social isolation.
