Trump’s attacks on Iran have put the U.S. and Israel into a new conflict that could let China gain a strategic advantage. Beijing says it wants peace, but the fighting uses U.S. weapons made with minerals China controls, such as gallium. With the U.S. focused abroad, China can keep its oil supplies from Iran and Venezuela and may use its mineral dominance to influence future U.S. defense deals. The move could boost China’s position before the next U.S. election.
