Dan Bongino, the FBI’s number‑two official, has said he will resign in January. He thanked former President Trump and others in a brief social‑media post, and he’s not expected to return to the FBI’s headquarters this month. Bongino, a former Secret Service agent and podcaster, has been a controversial figure, making many unverified claims while he worked for the bureau.
