Giannis Antetokounmpo has joined Kalshi, a prediction‑market app that lets people bet on anything from sports to politics. Fans and commentators argue the deal creates a conflict of interest, because the NBA allows players to promote betting companies but forbids them from betting on league events. His move follows other athletes who endorse sportsbooks, raising questions about trust in the game and the rise of sports‑gambling apps.
