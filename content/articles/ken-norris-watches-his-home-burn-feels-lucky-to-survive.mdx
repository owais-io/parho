Ken Norris watched a bushfire destroy most of his Longwood East property while staying in his house. He and his wife, Sharon, hurried with four dogs and two goats to the Mornington Peninsula, leaving behind outbuildings, an orchard and a vineyard. The fire reached their block in about an hour. While the house remained, they saw buildings go up in flames and later had to be helped out by neighbors. They are grateful they survived and still have their home.
