After a busy African Cup of Nations break, Sunderland faced a weakened Brighton side. The home team struggled to score, but Sunderland kept a tight defence and held them to a 1‑1 draw. With key Brighton players absent, the visitors were unsettled. Sunderland still remain in the hunt for Champions League places, while Brighton have lost four games in a row and are looking for a revival.
