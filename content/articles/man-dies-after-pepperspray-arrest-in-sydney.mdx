A 52‑year‑old man died after police used pepper spray during an arrest at a Homebush home in Sydney’s west. Officers were called at 8:30 a.m. over a domestic‑violence complaint. The man had a medical episode, was treated on the spot, and then taken to hospital, where he passed away that evening. Police are still investigating, and the case will be reviewed by a coroner and the Law Enforcement Conduct Commission.
