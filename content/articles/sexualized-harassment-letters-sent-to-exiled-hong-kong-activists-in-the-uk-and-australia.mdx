Exiled Hong Kong activists have received disturbing letters with deep‑fake sexual images. Neighbors of activist Carmen Lau in the UK were mailed fake pictures of her posing as a sex worker, while former legislator Ted Hui and his wife in Australia got a poster advertising his wife as a “lonely housewife”. These attacks are the first time the Chinese authorities’ wanted list has been targeted with sexualized content. Police are investigating, but the sender remains unknown. The incidents highlight rising transnational harassment of activists.
