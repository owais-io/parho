Australians will pay $31 for a one‑hour high‑speed train trip between Newcastle and Sydney starting in 2039, the government says. The whole project, which will add a line to Parramatta and Western Sydney Airport by 2043, will cost about $93 billion. The rail could create 99,000 jobs, add 160,000 homes and cut travel time to an hour, but critics worry it’s very expensive.
