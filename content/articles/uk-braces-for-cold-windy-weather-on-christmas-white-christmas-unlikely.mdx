The UK’s weather office says expect cold, windy days around Christmas. A yellow alert warns of low temperatures that could affect vulnerable people and cause extra trips to the doctor. Wind gusts up to 55‑65 mph may disrupt roads, power and bring big waves to coastlines. Snow looks unlikely, and the Met says a white Christmas is highly unlikely. A few flakes fell in Jersey, though.
