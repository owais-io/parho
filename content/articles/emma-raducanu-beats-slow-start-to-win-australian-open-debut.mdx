Emma Raducanu won her opening Australian Open match after a shaky start. She was down 1‑3, 15‑40 against Thailand’s Mananchaya Sawangkaew, but then rallied and won 6‑4, 6‑1. The 23‑year‑old said practice was tough because she had only one day to adjust after a late flight. She praised her late‑night practice and said the win gives her confidence as she prepares for a tough next‑round opponent, possibly Sabalenka.
