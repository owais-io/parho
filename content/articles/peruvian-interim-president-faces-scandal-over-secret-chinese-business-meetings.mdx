Peruvian interim President José Jerí is under investigation after videos show him meeting a Chinese businessman in a restaurant and a closed shop in Lima’s Chinatown. He says he was targeted by a smear campaign and denies wrongdoing, even saying he won’t resign. Prosecutors are looking into possible ties to illegal timber trading and Chinese investment. The scandal, dubbed “Chifagate,” comes as the U.S. and China compete for influence in Latin America.
