Tottenham has gone through many managers, and now it needs patience again. The club's last good spell under Mauricio Pochettino showed that giving a coach time can pay off. With the team struggling in the league but still in Europe, owners are eyeing options like Roberto De Zerbi, John Heitinga or even a return for Pochettino. Fans want stability, but the club’s future depends on choosing the right person.
