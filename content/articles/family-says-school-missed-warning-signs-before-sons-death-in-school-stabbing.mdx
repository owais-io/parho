Harvey's family says the school missed red‑flag warnings before the 15‑year‑old was stabbed to death. A review by former headteacher and inspector found bad record‑keeping, gaps in leadership and failures to share behaviour reports. The family wants the full report published and schools to install knife‑arch barriers. They also push for clearer safeguarding rules so no other child faces a similar tragedy.
