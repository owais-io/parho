Arsenal and Liverpool met at the Emirates for a Women’s Super League match. Arsenal’s Olivia Smith scored early, giving the Gunners a 1‑0 lead. Liverpool’s Beata Olsson answered with an equaliser, making history as the first player to score four goals in her first five WSL starts. Both sides had strong starts but missed chances. The game finished 1‑1, leaving Arsenal still chasing the title while Liverpool hoped for their first win.
