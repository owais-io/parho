A Russian LNG tanker, damaged by a suspected drone strike near Malta, has been drifting in the Mediterranean for two weeks. The ship, carrying around 90 tonnes of heavy oil and possibly natural gas, is now in Libyan waters, raising fears of a major oil or gas spill. European countries warned the European Commission of the danger, but no leaks have been detected yet. Russia says the attack came from Ukrainian drones launched from Libya.
