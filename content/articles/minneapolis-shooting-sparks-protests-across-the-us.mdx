On Wednesday, an ICE officer shot and killed a woman during a large enforcement operation in Minneapolis. The incident sparked protests in the city and across the US, with many demanding ICE leave the state. Governor Tim Walz said he would investigate and could call in the National Guard, while Mayor Jacob Frey urged the federal agency to go home. The shooting is part of a broader crackdown that has already caused several deaths.
