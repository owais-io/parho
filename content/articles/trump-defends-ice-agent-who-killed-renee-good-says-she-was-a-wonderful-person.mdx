Trump defended the ICE agent who shot 37‑year‑old Renee Good in Minneapolis, calling her a “wonderful person” but saying her “tough” actions justified the shooting. He said the agent was in self‑defense, but videos show Good was turning away. The mayor called the kill a reckless use of power. The administration is boosting enforcement in Minnesota, especially against Somali Americans, and will not investigate the incident.
