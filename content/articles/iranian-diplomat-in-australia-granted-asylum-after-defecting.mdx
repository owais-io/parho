Mohammad Pournajaf, who ran Iran’s embassy in Canberra, fled the country and asked for protection in Australia. He was granted asylum in 2023 after living there since 2018. His move came amid a broader crackdown on Iranian officials in the country, including the expulsion of other diplomats and the granting of visas to a group of Iranian women’s football players. The case highlights growing tension between Iran and Australia.
