Roger Federer has flown back to Melbourne for the 2026 Australian Open, where he will headline the opening ceremony with an exhibition doubles match featuring former stars Agassi, Rafter and Hewitt. The veteran praised the fierce rivalry between young Spanish star Carlos Alcaraz and Italy’s Jannik Sinner, calling their games “great” and hoping they stay injury‑free. Alcaraz opens against Adam Walton, Sinner faces Hugo Gaston, while Djokovic, Swiatek and Sabalenka are also set for big early‑round battles.
