Liberal MP Melissa McIntosh wrote to the ABC’s managing director, demanding an investigation into a satirical show by Tony Armstrong that aired on the public broadcaster. She says the program, which tackled Indigenous racism, may have breached ABC rules and could harm viewers, especially children. The complaint cites a song performed by school kids that shows Indigenous children in prison. The Greens and some civil‑rights groups defended the show, while the ABC said it protected child actors.
