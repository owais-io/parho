Australia temporarily relaxed diesel quality rules to keep fuel flowing after many gas stations ran out. The government lowered the required flashpoint temperature from 61.5°C to 60.5°C, allowing diesel from the US, Canada and Europe to be used. This follows a 60‑day petrol rule change that added extra supply. Asian fuel imports are slowing, and state leaders want better coordination.
