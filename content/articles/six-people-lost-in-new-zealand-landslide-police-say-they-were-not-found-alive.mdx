A landslide at a holiday park in Mount Maunganui buried six people, including two students, a teacher, and a Swedish tourist. Police said the search has moved to recovery because the victims were unlikely to be found alive. Families mourned the loss, and a community vigil was held with the Prime Minister in attendance. The incident comes after a week of storms that caused other landslides and flooding.
