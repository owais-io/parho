At COP30 in Brazil, the UK showed that even small emitters matter. Though the UK accounts for about 1% of global emissions, it backed a plan for a 99% global cut and invested heavily in renewables and nuclear. The summit saw 83 countries and many businesses agree on a clean‑energy roadmap, while Brazil will push a plan to stop deforestation by 2030. Despite the US leaving the Paris deal, most nations chose cooperation, proving teamwork matters for climate progress.
