Cheltenham’s Trials Day shows what could happen at the festival. Trainer Harry Redknapp is hoping his new runner, Taurus Bay, can win the Grade‑Two Novice Hurdle and move into the big races, while his top horse, The Jukebox Man, looks set for the Gold Cup. Ben Pauling says Taurus Bay is smart and may handle the test. Other hot names include Sir Gino and Act of Innocence, who are favorites for the International Hurdle.
