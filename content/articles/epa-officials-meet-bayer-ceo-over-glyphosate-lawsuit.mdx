In June, EPA officials sat down with Bayer’s CEO, Bill Anderson, to discuss the company’s battle over its glyphosate weed killer, Roundup. The meeting happened just before the Trump‑era government pushed the case to the Supreme Court. Critics say the talks give big pesticide firms too much sway over regulators. Afterward, the administration backed Bayer with court filings and a production‑act move.
