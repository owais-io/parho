On Monday, Cristiano Ronaldo didn’t show up for his Al‑Nassr match, sparking rumors that he’s on strike over club investment. The Saudi league, which has attracted big-name players, is now questioning how much influence these stars have on team decisions. While Al‑Nassr beat Al‑Ahli and stayed near the top, the league’s future might hinge on whether it can control superstar behaviour and maintain fair competition.
