Manchester City beat West Ham 4‑0, with Erling Haaland scoring twice and Tijjani Reijnders adding a goal. City’s win came after Arsenal beat Everton to stay ahead for Christmas Day. Pep Guardiola praised the team’s progress but said they still need to improve, especially defensively. West Ham’s form has been poor, losing their seventh straight game. City looks to keep pushing for titles while West Ham fights to stay in the top flight.
