UK and French governments are being criticised for not stopping anti‑migrant activists who travel from the UK to northern France to harass migrants. A group called Raise the Colours, describing itself as a “border control force,” has attacked boats and tried to destroy dinghies, and some members were arrested by French police. French NGOs say the governments haven’t acted enough and call for stronger measures.
