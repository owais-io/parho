Experts say the US‑Israel war with Iran could trigger a surge of terrorist attacks on American soil. Recent shootings and truck‑ram attacks in the U.S. have raised alarms, and analysts point to Iran’s history of plots and “sleeper cells.” U.S. security agencies appear underprepared, and some political voices suggest the threat could be used to advance Trump’s agenda. The risk is real, though evidence linking attacks directly to Iran is limited.
