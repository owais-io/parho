Airlines in Europe say the sharp rise in jet fuel prices caused by the war in the Middle East will push up ticket costs. While some have hedged fuel costs, they can’t keep the extra money from reaching passengers forever. The carriers plan more flights to Asia to bypass disrupted Gulf hubs and urge people to book early. They also ask the EU to lower green‑tax burdens and delay the 2030 sustainable‑fuel rule.
