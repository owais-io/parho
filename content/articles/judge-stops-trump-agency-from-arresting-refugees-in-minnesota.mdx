A federal judge in Minnesota has halted the Trump‑era plan to arrest and detain refugees who already live in the state. The ruling came after more than 100 refugees were taken into custody, some even flown to Texas and then released without help. The judge ordered all detainees back to Minnesota and gave the administration five days to free those in Texas. The decision stops the “Operation Parris” re‑vetting effort while a lawsuit continues.
