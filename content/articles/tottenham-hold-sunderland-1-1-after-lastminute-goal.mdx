Tottenham drew 1-1 with Sunderland after a late goal from Brian Brobbey. Manager Thomas Frank blamed the missing attacking options – the sale of Brennan Johnson, the early injury to Mohammed Kudus and the suspension of Xavi Simons – for the team's lack of firepower. Spurs controlled the first half but could not finish, while Sunderland showed good tactics and an important equaliser. Frank said the club still needs sharper forwards.
