A Washington grand jury decided not to indict six Democratic lawmakers—Elissa Slotkin, Mark Kelly, Jason Crow, Chris Deluzio, Maggie Goodlander, and Chrissy Houlahan—after they released a video telling soldiers they could refuse unlawful commands. President Trump blasted the clip, calling it “seditious,” and the defense secretary censured Mark Kelly. The lawmakers say the video was protected speech and accuse the administration of weaponising the justice system.
