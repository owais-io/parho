The CIA has posted a new Mandarin‑language video on its YouTube channel, part of a series aimed at recruiting Chinese soldiers who are unhappy with the army’s recent corruption purges. The clip follows a fictional officer who leaves the PLA after questioning leadership and tells viewers that the CIA is protecting their future. Although China blocks YouTube, users can bypass the firewall. Beijing has warned that the videos encourage infiltration and says it will take action against “anti‑China” forces.
