Britain says it might help the U.S. hit Iran’s underground missile launch sites if it helps protect allies' air‑defence stocks. US bombers will soon fly from UK bases to strike Iran’s “missile cities.” The UK’s navy ship, HMS Dragon, is being readied to support operations in Cyprus. While the UK hasn’t fully joined the campaign, Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK would allow limited missions to destroy Iranian missile stores or launchers.
