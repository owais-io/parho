Bad Bunny’s Spanish halftime show at the Super Bowl has become a flashpoint for politics. Trump supporters argue the act is a cultural attack, while the star uses his music to celebrate Latino pride amid U.S. immigration crackdowns. The NFL says the show will promote diversity, and many Americans, especially young and Hispanic voters, back it. The performance sparks debate about identity, language, and the future of American sports.
