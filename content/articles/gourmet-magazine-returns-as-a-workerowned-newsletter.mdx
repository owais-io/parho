After 16 years, a group of five former journalists are bringing back Gourmet magazine as a worker‑owned newsletter. They’ll publish at least two issues a week and aim to keep the classic, detailed recipes and essays that made the original famous. The new team, led by Amiel Stanek, wants to focus on serious cooking lovers, not mass‑appeal quick meals, and hopes to keep the magazine’s high standards while staying independent and flexible.
