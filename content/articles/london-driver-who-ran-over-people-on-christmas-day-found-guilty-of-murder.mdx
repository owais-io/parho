Anthony Gilheaney, 31, drove a Mercedes across central London on Christmas Day 2024 while drunk, hitting people on footpaths and streets. He killed 25‑year‑old Aiden Chapman and seriously injured at least four others, including a couple leaving midnight mass. The jury found him guilty of murder, attempted murder and other charges. He has a mandatory life sentence and will serve a minimum term before parole can be considered. He had previous dangerous‑driving convictions.
