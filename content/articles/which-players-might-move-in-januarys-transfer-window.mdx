A lot of Premier League and European clubs are eyeing several players for the January transfer window. Bournemouth’s striker Antoine Semenyo could be snapped up by Manchester City thanks to a release clause, while Crystal Palace captain Marc Guéhi is in line for a summer free‑transfer. Other targets include Tottenham’s Brennan Johnson, Portugal’s Rúben Neves, Barcelona’s keeper Ter Stegen, and Saudi striker Ivan Toney. Young talents like Turkey’s Kenan Yildiz and Dutch star Kees Smit also face moves.
