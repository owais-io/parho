Trump’s administration has agreed with several Republican‑led states to shut down Biden’s student‑loan repayment program, the Save plan, which had helped over 7 million borrowers lower their monthly payments. The education department will stop new enrollments, deny pending applications, and move current borrowers into other repayment options. Republicans say the program was illegal and overstepped the law; supporters warn it will leave many borrowers with higher, less affordable payments.
