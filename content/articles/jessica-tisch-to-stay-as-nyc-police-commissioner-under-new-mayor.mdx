Mayor‑elect Zohran Mamdani said he’ll keep Jessica Tisch as police commissioner, citing her record of cutting crime and removing guns. Tisch, who took the post in November, has already confirmed she’ll stay on. Both officials promise to work together on safety and policing reforms. State leaders also back the decision. Mamdani had earlier called for police cuts but now says he isn’t. He’ll take office on Jan. 1.
