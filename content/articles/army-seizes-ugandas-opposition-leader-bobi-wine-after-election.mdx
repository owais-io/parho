After Uganda’s election, where President Museveni won a large majority, opposition leader Bobi Wine was reportedly taken from his house by an army helicopter. His supporters say the soldiers fired on his campaign team, killing 10 people. Wine, who has accused the election of fraud, called for resistance. The incident shows a violent crackdown on opposition as Museveni’s rule continues.
