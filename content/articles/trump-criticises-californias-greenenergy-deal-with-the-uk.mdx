Trump slammed a new green‑energy agreement between the UK and California’s governor, calling Newsom a “loser” and saying the deal was inappropriate. The UK and California signed a memorandum to boost clean‑energy jobs, share expertise on biodiversity and climate resilience, and discuss AI and wildfire lessons. Trump warned the partnership could hurt UK‑US ties, while Newsom used his European trip to meet leaders and push climate action.
