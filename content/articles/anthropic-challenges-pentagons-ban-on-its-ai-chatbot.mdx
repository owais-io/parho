Anthropic has sued the Department of Defense to stop a ban that keeps the U.S. military and contractors from using its Claude chatbot. The company says the ban is a punishment for its safety stance and threatens its business. In court, a judge will decide if the government’s move, called a “supply‑chain risk,” is legal. The case could change how the Pentagon uses AI.
