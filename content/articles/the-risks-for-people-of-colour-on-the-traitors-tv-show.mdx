The Guardian column talks about how the new format of the reality show “The Traitors,” which adds a secret traitor, has drawn huge public interest. It argues that the show may not be fair to people of colour, pointing out early cast exits that look like unconscious bias. The writer notes that the series feels fun and unpolitical, but warns that non‑white contestants could face extra challenges.
