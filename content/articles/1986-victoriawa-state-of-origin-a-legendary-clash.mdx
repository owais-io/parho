The 1986 State of Origin match between Victoria and Western Australia is still remembered as one of the greatest Australian rules football games. With nearly 40,000 fans in a packed Subiaco Oval, the game saw a dramatic late snap by Gary Buckenara that gave WA a three‑point win. The match was full of lead changes, powerful plays and a wild crowd, marking a peak moment that experts say will never be repeated.
