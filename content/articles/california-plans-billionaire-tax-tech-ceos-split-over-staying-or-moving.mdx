California is planning a one‑time 5% tax on anyone worth more than $1 billion to fund education, food and health programs. Nvidia’s Jensen Huang says he’ll stay and pay it, while other tech giants – Larry Page, Peter Thiel, David Sacks – have announced moves to tax‑friendly states like Florida and Texas. The proposal still needs signatures and the governor’s sign‑off to go on the November ballot, sparking debate over keeping the state’s richest people.
