Their move comes after a tense back‑and‑forth with Committee chair James Comer, who threatened contempt charges if they refused. The Clintons had said the subpoenas were invalid and political, but now will give sworn testimony. Meanwhile, new DOJ files keep the Epstein case alive and Democrats accuse a cover‑up in the United States.
