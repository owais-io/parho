People often set goals in January, but most drop them by February. Winter’s long nights and cold weather make us sleepy, sluggish, and stressed. A psychologist at Swansea University says that as daylight returns in spring, stress drops and motivation rises. She advises waiting until March to start new habits, choosing gentle, realistic goals to give your body time to recover.
