China’s top car maker, BYD, unveiled batteries that can drive 600 miles and add 250 miles in just five minutes, but that needs megawatt chargers. China plans thousands of such stations in two years. Britain’s split, privately‑run power network can’t handle these loads without huge upgrades. The piece highlights the need for state‑led grid planning to keep up with fast EV charging.
