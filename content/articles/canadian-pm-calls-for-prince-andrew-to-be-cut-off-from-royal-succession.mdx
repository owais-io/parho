Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney has said Prince Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor should be removed from the royal succession after he was arrested for suspected misconduct. Although Andrew gave up his titles in 2022, he still sits eighth in line to the throne and is a counsellor of state. Carney wants the UK Parliament and Commonwealth countries to act, and Australia and New Zealand already back the move. Andrew denies wrongdoing and has not been charged.
