Crystal Palace beat Wolves 2‑0 thanks to goals from Daniel Muñoz and Yeremy Pino. Pino’s second, a fine shot from the edge of the box, sealed the win and pushed Palace into fourth place. Wolves remain winless and sit at the bottom of the Premier League, their only team with no victories in the top seven tiers of English football. This was Rob Edwards’ first full game as Wolves’ permanent manager.
