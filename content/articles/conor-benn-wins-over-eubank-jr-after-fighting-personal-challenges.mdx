Conor Benn said he had struggled with doubts and past drug‑test controversy, but he finished a 12‑round, one‑sided win over Chris Eubank Jr at Tottenham. The fight earned him £8 million and a sense of closure to a three‑year feud. Benn plans to drop to welterweight and chase a WBC title like his dad. Eubank admitted the fight was tough and that he needs to heal before his next steps.
