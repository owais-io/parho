New South Wales Premier Chris Minns is calling for tighter gun rules after the Bondi beach shooting. He wants limits on how many guns a licence holder can own, bans on certain shotguns and belt‑fed mags, and stronger police power to stop licences for people who pose a risk. Minns is also reviewing whether hunting can still be a reason to own a gun. He had previously supported a hunting‑rights bill but is now turning back after the attack.
