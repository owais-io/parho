The EU Commission plans to delay parts of the AI Act, relax data‑privacy rules, and simplify cookie consent, sparking fierce criticism. Supporters say the changes will cut administrative costs and help start‑ups grow, while privacy groups claim they undermine people’s data protection. Tech giants welcome the easing but say more reform is needed. Critics worry Brussels is shrinking safeguards under the guise of simplification.
