Seven dogs that vanished in China were found after a video showed them walking more than 17km back to their village. The footage, posted on March 15, sparked an online frenzy, gathering over 230 million views. Residents and volunteers helped track the dogs through snow and cold weather. After four days of searching, owners were reunited with the pack, led by a corgi named Dapang. The incident sparked debate over dog safety and online rumors.
