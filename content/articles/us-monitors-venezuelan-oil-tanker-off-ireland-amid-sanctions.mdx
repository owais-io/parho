US watches a Venezuelan oil tanker 250 miles off Ireland as it tries to dodge a US blockade. The ship, which has changed its name and flag, began in Iran, picked up oil in Venezuela, and then headed for Russia. US P‑8 aircraft from the UK and Iceland are patrolling the area. Washington has imposed a quarantine on Venezuelan oil to pressure President Maduro, while the Venezuelan government calls the move piracy.
