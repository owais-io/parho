Bath beat Saracens 35-30 after a comeback. Henry Arundell scored two tries, including a late winner, and made key defensive moves. Bath trailed 14-3 but used several substitutions to turn the game. Saracens had early advantage with Owen Farrell and Max Malins, but an injury to Tom Willis hurt them. Arundell's speed and tackles impressed Bath coach, and his performance may catch England coach's eye. Bath looks set to win the Premiership title.
