Prime minister Anthony Albanese called Grace Tame’s life “difficult” in a one‑word remark, sparking outrage. Tame replied on social media, calling him condescending and claiming he’s echoing former PM Scott Morrison. The exchange drew criticism from politicians and sparked debate over gendered language and the cost of speaking out. The incident has fueled a broader debate on how women who speak up are labeled.
