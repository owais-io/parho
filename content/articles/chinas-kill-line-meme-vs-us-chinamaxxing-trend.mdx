Chinese netizens have turned the U.S. into a “kill line” meme, claiming it’s a dangerous place where the middle class falls. At the same time, U.S. teens are posting “Chinamaxxing” videos that show them enjoying Chinese customs. The memes show China’s critics use America’s problems to distract from domestic troubles, while the government promotes the trend to improve its image and ease social tension.
