Ali Larijani, Iran’s top security chief, was killed in an overnight Israeli strike, confirmed by Iran’s National Security Council. His death, along with his son and bodyguards, marks the loss of the country’s senior political figure in the current conflict. Iran’s army chief vowed retaliation, while Israel’s defense ministry said it had also struck other senior Iranian leaders. The attack highlights Israel’s continued air raids on Tehran and the escalating Iran‑Israel war.
