The board hires WilmerHale to review all communications between President Leon Botstein and Jeffrey Epstein, including money given to the college and a 2012 trip to Epstein’s island. The review will also suggest new rules for fundraising and donor checks. Botstein says he first met Epstein in 2011 after receiving a $75,000 gift and that he attended the island trip for fundraising reasons. The investigation responds to fresh evidence released by the Justice Department.
