President Trump told reporters that the peace talks between Ukraine and Russia are “closer than ever,” after meeting with Ukrainian President Zelenskyy and having a long phone call with Russian President Putin. He said a draft deal is about 95 % ready, but still needs to sort out hard points over territory and how the war ends. He warns the talks could still collapse, leaving the war for years.
