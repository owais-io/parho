People in demanding jobs feel they must always work hard, but that pressure can burn them out. The article shares stories of a new mum, a senior NHS manager and a busy parent who all feel trapped by ‘hustle culture’. It explains how our own drive to be productive can become a problem when it stops letting us rest or delegate. The authors suggest changing our mindset, testing small breaks, and accepting that rest matters for its own sake.
