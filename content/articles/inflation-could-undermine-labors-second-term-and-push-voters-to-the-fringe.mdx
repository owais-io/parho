Rising inflation is the biggest worry for Australian families and threatens Labor’s plans for its second term. Prices jumped to 3.8% this year, above the 2.4% rate before the election. The Reserve Bank will likely raise interest rates again, hurting those on mortgages and raising power bills. The surge in living costs is also boosting support for fringe parties such as One Nation.
