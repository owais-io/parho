Imran Khan, the former Pakistani prime minister, is locked in solitary confinement at Adiala prison and has been denied medical care for a serious eye problem called a central retinal vein occlusion. His party says the delay could cause permanent blindness. Lawyers, family and PTI leaders have tried to visit him but faced tear gas and water cannons. A court order for visits is not being followed.
