Hamdan Ballal, one of the Oscar‑winning No Other Land documentary’s co‑directors, says his home in the West Bank’s Masafer Yatta area was raided by Israeli soldiers and settlers. His brother was pushed to the ground, had his neck hit, and had to go to hospital. Ballal’s family was detained for hours. The attack comes after Israel opened a land registry that lets Israelis claim West Bank property, sparking global backlash.
