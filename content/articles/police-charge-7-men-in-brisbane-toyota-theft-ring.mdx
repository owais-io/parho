Queensland police say a foreign crime ring stole about 60 Toyota cars, including Land Cruisers and Prados, from Brisbane between Oct and Dec. They packed the vehicles in shipping containers and sent them to the UAE. Police found 15 cars and seized 7 shipping containers. Seven men were charged with more than 300 offences and will appear in court in January. Police say the group is no longer a threat.
