Donald Trump promised a US hospital ship for Greenland, saying locals weren’t getting enough care. Greenland’s prime minister replied, “no thanks,” explaining that the territory already has free, taxpayer‑funded health services and six hospitals for fewer than 60,000 people. Denmark’s leaders defended their system, and the move highlighted Trump’s push to acquire Greenland, which has strained ties with Denmark and other NATO allies.
