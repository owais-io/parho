Newly released court documents show that Ghislaine Maxwell used manipulative psychological tricks to lure vulnerable teenage girls into Jeffrey Epstein’s abusive circle. The filings, part of a grand jury case, reveal how Maxwell convinced girls to trust her, offering money or friendship, then bringing them into Epstein’s orbit. She is now trying to reduce a 20‑year sentence, while the case has drawn attention during Donald Trump’s presidency. The documents also raise questions about how the DOJ handled Epstein’s papers.
