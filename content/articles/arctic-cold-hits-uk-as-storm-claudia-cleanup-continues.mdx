Arctic cold is coming to the UK as Storm Claudia’s flooding damage still feels like a crisis. The storm left big floods in Wales and England, with record‑high rivers and many homes flooded. The government still has 41 flood warnings in England and strong warnings in Wales. Temperatures have dropped to -7 °C in Scotland, and the Met Office warns of snow, ice and frosts. A cold‑weather alert covers the Midlands and northern England, and firefighters are stretched thin.
