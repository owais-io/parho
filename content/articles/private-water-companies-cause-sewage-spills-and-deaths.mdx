In the UK, private water firms have been blamed for thousands of sewage spills that pollute beaches and rivers, leading to illness and even death. The Guardian’s investigation follows Sarah Lambert’s life‑threatening infection after a sewage leak and Channel 4’s docudrama exposing decades of under‑investment. Experts argue the system should be nationalised, but the government keeps saying it would cost too much. Public support for change is at 82 %.
