In Minnesota, ICE agents have been raiding homes and forcibly removing people, sparking outrage. The agency’s aggressive tactics culminated in the shooting death of 37‑year‑old Renee Good, a U.S. citizen. Her murder highlights how Trump’s immigration drive has turned law‑enforcement into a political weapon, using fear to push for mass deportations and targeting communities based on race and ideology. Many Minnesotans feel the force is invading their everyday life.
