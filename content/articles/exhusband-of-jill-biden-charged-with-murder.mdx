William Stevenson, 77, who once married Jill Biden in 1970, was arrested and charged with first‑degree murder after his wife, Linda Stevenson, was found dead in their Delaware home. Police responded to a domestic dispute, discovered Linda unresponsive, and she was pronounced dead after attempts to revive her failed. An autopsy will determine the cause of death. Stevenson is held at Howard Young Correctional Institution without bail. The Biden office hasn't replied to inquiries.
