The 24‑year‑old Bondi attacker survived a shoot‑out with police and faces 59 charges, including 15 murders and a terrorist act. Investigators say the attack may have been inspired by ISIS. He, along with his father who was killed by police, shot at a Hanukkah gathering, killing 15 people, including a 10‑year‑old child, and leaving 17 injured. He won’t be released on bail and will go to court again on 8 April.
