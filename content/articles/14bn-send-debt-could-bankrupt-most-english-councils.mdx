Councils in England are almost bankrupt because of the cost of special educational needs (Send). If the government doesn’t change the system, 80 % of councils could go out of business within two years. The debt could reach £14 billion. Some councils are already borrowing money and even thinking about raising council tax beyond limits. The government will soon publish a white paper to reform Send, but the plan is still unclear.
