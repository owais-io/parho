Tonga's newly elected Prime Minister, Lord Fatafehi Fakafānua, welcomed a February partnership with the United States that aims to research deep‑sea minerals. He said Tonga will approach the work cautiously and only under international rules. However, environmental groups and many Tongans worry that mining could harm the ocean and the country’s fishing‑based livelihoods. Critics call for more public debate and even a referendum before any mining starts.
