Tesla has been given a licence by the UK regulator Ofgem to supply electricity to homes and businesses across England, Scotland and Wales. The licence lets the company sell power, but not gas, so customers must have a separate gas contract. Tesla plans to bring its Texas model, which uses a virtual power plant to let Powerwall owners sell battery power back to the grid, to Britain. Tesla’s car sales in the UK have fallen; political controversies hurt its reputation.
