Hull KR, once battling financial trouble and low crowds, stunned the rugby world by beating Brisbane Broncos 30-20 after a tense comeback. The Rovers dominated most of the game, with Mikey Lewis’s kicks setting up several tries, but Brisbane fought back in the final quarter. Despite a late scare, Hull KR held on to win the World Club Challenge, marking the club’s first title.
