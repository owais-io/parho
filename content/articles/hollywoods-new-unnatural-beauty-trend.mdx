Hollywood once promoted healthy, polished looks. Today a new trend mixes drug‑induced weight loss and heavy cosmetic surgery, producing thin, gaunt faces that feel fake. Celebrities like Jim Carrey spark criticism, with comments calling them sick or unnatural. Fans no longer idolise the look; they feel uneasy. The result threatens acting realism and Hollywood’s cultural influence, as stars no longer appear like relatable people.
