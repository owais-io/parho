A 29‑year‑old Venezuelan migrant, Joswar Torres, was released after seven months in ICE custody in Washington state. His detention sparked a protest of nearly 2,000 people in Spokane that led to 30 arrests and a federal judge ruling that DHS had violated his constitutional rights. The case drew national attention and saw federal prosecutors charge several demonstrators, including a former city council president, while a DOJ prosecutor resigned over the indictments.
