Lanlan Yang, a 23‑year‑old Chinese woman in Sydney, denies two new charges for a July crash that injured a Mercedes driver. She already pleaded not guilty to four earlier accusations, including refusing a breath test and dangerous driving. Yang admitted she broke her bail by not updating her address. The case will be heard again in March. The story has drawn a lot of media attention because of her wealth and high‑profile lifestyle.
