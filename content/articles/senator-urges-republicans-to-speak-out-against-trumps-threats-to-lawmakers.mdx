Senator Mark Kelly has called on Republicans to publicly denounce former President Donald Trump’s death‑threatening comments toward six Democratic lawmakers who said military personnel can refuse illegal orders. Trump wrote that the lawmakers should be arrested and even “punishable by death.” The six Democrats made a video telling soldiers they can refuse unlawful orders. Republicans have said nothing, while Democrats and the Vice‑President condemned the remarks.
