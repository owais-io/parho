Judge Gregory Carro has set the state murder trial of Luigi Mangione for 8 June, moving it ahead of the federal case scheduled for September. Mangione is charged with the shooting death of United HealthCare CEO Brian Thompson. The judge said he was frustrated that the federal judge had set a later date. Mangione’s defense says they won’t be ready for June.
