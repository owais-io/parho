The U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) awarded a $250,000 public‑relations contract to American Made Media Company, a firm run by former Trump campaign staff. The job was awarded in just 31 hours, and the contract notice required the firm to support Trump‑style policies. The quick, partisan process has raised concerns about fairness and transparency in federal spending.
