A batch of photos released by the U.S. Justice Department shows former Prince Andrew hosting Jeffrey Epstein and Ghislaine Maxwell at royal homes like Sandringham and Balmoral. The pictures, taken in 2000, depict the prince welcoming the couple to exclusive events such as Ascot and a Balmoral cabin. Biographers describe Andrew as a “useful idiot” who let Epstein use his royal ties for business. Andrew denies any misconduct and has been asked to testify in U.S. hearings about Epstein.
