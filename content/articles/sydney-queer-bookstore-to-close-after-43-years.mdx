The Bookshop Darlinghurst on Oxford Street, a favourite for Sydney’s LGBTQ community, will shut its doors on Christmas Eve after 43 years. Owner Noel Lee, who has worked there for 24 years, has seen the shop become a safe space for readers, friends, and a quiet meeting place through tough times like the AIDS crisis. Now financial pressures and delays in a new location force its closure, leaving loyal customers sad.
