Brommapojkarna, a small Stockholm club, has built a reputation for spotting and training talent from as young as eight. With over 400 youth teams playing four times a week, the club has produced players who now play for Brighton, Arsenal and other top clubs. The academy is key to its finances and success, and the club proudly promotes former students into its first team. The focus on early training and strong scouting keeps the club thriving.
