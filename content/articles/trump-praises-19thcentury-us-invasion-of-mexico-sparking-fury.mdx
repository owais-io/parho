Trump posted a message celebrating the 1846‑1848 war that took half of Mexico’s land. Mexican President Claudia Sheinbaum condemned it as a threat to Mexico’s sovereignty. Critics, including former ambassador Arturo Sarukhan, said the comment shows a willingness to intervene militarily. Trump’s administration has already sent troops to Venezuela and threatened action in Colombia and Cuba, raising fears that Mexico could face a new US invasion.
