A pair of House bills that would ban gender‑affirming care for kids just passed. One would make giving this care a felony, the other would stop Medicaid from covering it. Doctors, nurses and activists say the laws will hurt trans youth, who already have high suicide rates. They point to research that shows proper care lowers mental‑health problems. Rep. Sarah McBride and others slammed the bills as government overreach and dangerous.
