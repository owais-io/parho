The UK’s Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) has stopped almost 20 million illegal erectile‑dysfunction pills in five years. Most of the seized drugs had no active medicine, wrong doses or harmful ingredients. Criminals use the shame around the condition to sell fake pills online. The MHRA shut down over 1,500 websites and removed 1,200 social‑media posts. Doctors warn that buying from unverified sites can harm health, especially for people with heart problems.
