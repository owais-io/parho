An investigation into Mossbourne Victoria Park Academy found that teachers often shouted at students, put them in corridors, and used harsh punishments that humiliated pupils. The report says this climate of fear hurts especially vulnerable children, including those with special needs, and raises mental‑health concerns. Parents and the Department for Education want the school to change its culture or face leadership replacement. The trust says it is reviewing the findings.
