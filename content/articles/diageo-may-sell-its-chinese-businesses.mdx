Diageo, the company that makes Guinness and Johnnie Walker, is looking at selling its Chinese assets. The move comes after sales there have fallen and the new boss, Dave Lewis, wants to simplify the company’s portfolio. Diageo is reviewing its stake in Shanghai‑listed Sichuan Swellfun, a Baiju distributor, with banks Goldman Sachs and UBS. The company has also sold other parts, like a stake in East African Breweries, to focus on core brands.
