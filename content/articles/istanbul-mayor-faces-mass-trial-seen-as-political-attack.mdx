A big corruption trial has begun against 400 people, including jailed Istanbul mayor Ekrem İmamoğlu. Critics say the case is a political hit aimed at stopping him from running for president. He entered the court to cheers, but the judge denied him a chance to speak. Human rights groups call the trial unfair, and if convicted he could face over 1,900 years in prison.
