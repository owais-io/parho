The piece highlights six iconic Winter Olympic moments that captured hearts worldwide. In 2010, Sidney Crosby’s last‑second goal gave Canada a gold‑medal win. Six years earlier, snowboarder Lindsey Jacobellis earned her first gold after years of heartbreak. In 1998, Austrian skier Hermann Maier crashed but returned to win gold. The 1984 Sarajevo ice‑dance routine of Torvill and Dean earned a perfect score. 1980 speed skater Eric Heiden swept five golds. Finally, 2022’s British curler Eve Muirhead captured Scotland’s only gold.
