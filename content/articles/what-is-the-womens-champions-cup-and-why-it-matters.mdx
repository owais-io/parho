The Women’s Champions Cup is a club tournament for the best women’s teams from each continent. It starts in London and ends at the Emirates Stadium, offering £1.68 million in prize money and a trophy. FIFA launched it to give clubs international play while the World Cup is delayed. Four teams – Arsenal, Gotham FC, Corinthians and AS Far – will battle for a spot in the final, drawing interest but also debate over dates and sponsorship.
