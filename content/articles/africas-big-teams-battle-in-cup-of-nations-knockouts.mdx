The Cup of Nations knockout stage is full of familiar African powers. Morocco, the 2022 World Cup semi‑finalist, are hosting and look like favorites but feel pressure. South Africa beat Cameroon, while Senegal defeated Sudan with a strong attack, but their midfield may falter. Mali won against Tunisia, showing how a team can pull off a surprise. Nigeria and Egypt keep pushing, but overall the draw is predictable, with only a few shocks and a lot of quality.
