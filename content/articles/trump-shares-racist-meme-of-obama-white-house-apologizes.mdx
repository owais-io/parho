On Tuesday, President Trump celebrated Black History Month, but a few days later he posted a meme that turned Barack and Michelle Obama into ape‑like figures. The video was removed quickly, and a White House staffer said it was posted by mistake. Senators and representatives, both Republican and Democrat, condemned the image as hateful. The incident shows how even brief social media posts can spark national backlash.
