US and Israel carried out a joint air‑strike campaign against Iran, hitting military sites and the compound of Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. Iran fired back, striking Israeli and US bases in Gulf states and even a school in the south‑east, killing dozens. The US president called the operation “massive” and warned of a missile‑industry wipe‑out. World leaders urged a return to talks, while Britain and other allies defended the strikes as defensive.
