EU wants European students in the UK to pay the same tuition as UK students, about £9,500 a year, but British officials say this would cost universities £140m a year. The disagreement has stalled talks ahead of a Brussels summit where Prime Minister Keir Starmer plans to launch a new EU deal. The dispute could damage Starmer’s plan to reset relations with the EU.
