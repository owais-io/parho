Learning Resources, a Chicago‑area toy maker that sells educational games and classroom tools, is suing President Trump over tariffs it says are illegal. The company will pay about $14 million this year and wants that money back. It has moved production to other countries and slowed new product launches because of unpredictable rates. The case could reach the Supreme Court, which may decide whether the tariffs are lawful.
