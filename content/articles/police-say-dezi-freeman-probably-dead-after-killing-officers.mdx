Police suspect Dezi Freeman died a few hours after he shot two officers in Porepunkah, Victoria. A huge search is underway in the rugged high‑country near Mount Buffalo – the largest manhunt in state history. Officers heard a gunshot in the bush and are looking for his body, using drones, cadaver dogs and foot teams. A $1 million reward is offered for information. While no proof of life exists, police think he may have died.
