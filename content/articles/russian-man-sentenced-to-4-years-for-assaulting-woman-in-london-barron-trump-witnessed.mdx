Matvei Rumiantsev, a 23‑year‑old Russian, was jailed for four years after violently attacking a woman in London. The assault was caught on a FaceTime call that Barron Trump, the president’s youngest son, saw. Trump called the police after seeing the attack. Rumiantsev also tried to convince the victim to drop her case, adding to his charges. The judge noted he showed no remorse.
