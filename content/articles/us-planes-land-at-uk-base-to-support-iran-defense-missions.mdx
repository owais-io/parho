Two US cargo planes landed at RAF Fairford in Gloucestershire, bringing weapons and spare parts for U.S. bombers that have been deployed to support defensive operations against Iran. One plane came from Glasgow’s Prestwick airport after bad weather at Fairford, the other from New Jersey’s McGuire airbase. The arrivals follow the UK’s decision to let U.S. forces use its bases after a drone was shot down over the country.
