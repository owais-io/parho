Madonna spent a weekend in Margate, a seaside town turned art hotspot. She praised the creative energy, especially her 25‑year friendship with artist Tracey Emin and the TKE Studios residency that brings young artists to the town. Madonna also highlighted a tiny Italian restaurant she loves. The story shows how artists and celebrities have revived Margate’s culture, even as some locals feel squeezed by rising costs.
