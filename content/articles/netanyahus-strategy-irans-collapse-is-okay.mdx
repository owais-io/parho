Israeli leaders, especially Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, see a dangerous Iran as justification for holding territory. Former PM Yitzhak Rabin warned of Iran’s nuclear threat and pushed for peace with neighbours, but Netanyahu rejected that, citing security. After Hamas’s 2023 attack, Israel expanded military action, targeting Iran and its allies, while seeking support from the US and Gulf states. The war could spiral into wider conflict. The article urges Israel to follow Rabin’s peace plan instead.
