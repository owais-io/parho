Bithumb, South Korea’s second‑largest crypto platform, mistakenly credited customers with 620,000 bitcoins (worth about $40bn) instead of Korean won during a giveaway. It fixed most of the error, but 13bn won ($9m) worth of bitcoin was still lost after users sold or withdrew the coins. The exchange is negotiating returns from customers and faces a government investigation into the incident.
