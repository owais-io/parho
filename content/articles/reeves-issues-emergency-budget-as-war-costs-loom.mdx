UK Chancellor Rachel Reeves told Parliament that the economy is in danger because of the war driven by the US. She announced emergency measures, including support for children and families, and plans to stop price hikes. Reeves also said the government would look at oil drilling in the North Sea and more nuclear power by the 2030s. She warned that the war could lead to a financial collapse and said she was still figuring out the damage.
