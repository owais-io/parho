ABC workers at the national broadcaster walked off the job for 24 hours, forcing the network to air BBC news and repeats. The boss, Hugh Marks, called the strike a “bit of a game,” which unions said was offensive. Staff demand higher pay and better conditions, and argue the offer is below inflation. Marks says future smaller actions are likely and denies job‑security issues. The station returned to normal after the strike ended.
