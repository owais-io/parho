Trump is blocking visas for foreign fact‑checkers and content moderators, part of a plan to stop people who fight online misinformation from coming to the U.S. The move follows his lawsuits against media and criticism of social media companies that removed him. Critics say the policy is unfair, unconstitutional, and keeps those who help keep the internet safe out. It shows Trump wants to control the narrative he shares with the country.
