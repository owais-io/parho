Washington and Tehran will meet again in Geneva this week, as the US thinks Iran is ready to reduce its highly enriched uranium and prove it wants no nuclear weapons. The talks arrive amid rising student protests in Tehran and Mashhad, with clashes against the Basij militia. Iran’s foreign minister says a quick deal is possible but warns the US could attack. Trump’s team has moved from backing protesters to focusing on nuclear talks, while the UN faces a stalled human‑rights inquiry.
