Bard College faculty have asked the board to create a plan to transition out of the presidency of Leon Botstein after emails revealed his ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The board hired WilmerHale to investigate their communications, and faculty are calling for an independent review of donor vetting and conflict‑of‑interest policies. While some pushed for an immediate resignation, the faculty settled on a “transition plan” that keeps them involved in the next steps and supports victims.
