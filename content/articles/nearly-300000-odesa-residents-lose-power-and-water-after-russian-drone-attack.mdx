Russia's overnight drone attack hit Odesa, cutting power and water for almost 300,000 residents. One person was hurt, and children were among the injured. The strike was part of a larger effort to cripple Ukraine's energy system during winter. Ukrainian officials said drones and missiles targeted key facilities, leaving many without heat and electricity. The attacks continue as the country battles to keep its energy grid running.
