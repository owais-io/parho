The U.S. Department of Homeland Security is opening an internal probe into Gregory Bovino, a senior U.S. Border Patrol officer, after a New York Times report said he made anti‑Jewish remarks to a federal prosecutor. The alleged comments, including mocking Shabbat and using the phrase “chosen people,” were made during a January phone call. A CBP investigator is reviewing the claim, and a Minnesota prosecutor is already looking into possible criminal charges against federal agents.
