Far‑right activist and U.S. Senate hopeful Jake Lang was arrested in Minnesota after he posted a video of himself kicking down an anti‑ICE ice sculpture on the state capitol steps. The artwork, made by veterans’ group Common Defense, originally read “Prosecute ICE” and Lang changed it to “Pro ICE.” Authorities charged him with felony property damage and he was taken into custody the same day.
