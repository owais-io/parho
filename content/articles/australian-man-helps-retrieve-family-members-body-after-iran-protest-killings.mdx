Payam, an Australian who left Iran at 17, helped retrieve the body of his relative Eli, who was shot during protests near Tehran. The family had to sift through 700 bodies before finding her. Authorities offered a choice: pay $9,000 or sign a form saying the death was by civilians. The story shows the trauma, fear for loved ones, and the toll of Iran’s crackdown.
