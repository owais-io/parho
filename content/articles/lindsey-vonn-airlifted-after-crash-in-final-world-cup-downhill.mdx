Lindsey Vonn, a 41‑year‑old American skier, crashed during the last World Cup downhill race before the Milano‑Cortina Olympics. She hit a jump, slid into the safety netting, and hurt her left knee. Medical teams checked her, then she was flown to a hospital on a helicopter. The race was stopped, and several other skiers fell in the same bad weather. Vonn is still a top contender for the Olympic downhill.
