Chelsea beat Burnley 1‑0 at Turf Moor. Pedro Neto scored after a quick pass from Enzo Fernández, who set the play. The win keeps Chelsea three points behind the leaders, after four clean‑sheet victories. Burnley had chances but finished with no goal, and a potential penalty was not awarded. The result gives Chelsea confidence before big matches against Barcelona and Arsenal.
