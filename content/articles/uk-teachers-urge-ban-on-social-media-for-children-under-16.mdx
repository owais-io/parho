The Teachers’ Union NASUWT says kids under 16 should not be allowed to use social media because it harms their mental health and focus. Teachers report more violence and bad behaviour in class that they link to social media. An 89% poll of union members backs a statutory ban. The government says it supports schools limiting phones and has laws to protect under‑18s, but is not yet banning under‑16s.
