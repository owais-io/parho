In the bright spring light, ravens fly, chaffinches sing, and a bumblebee buzzes over hedges. A woodpecker drums on a beech tree, while butterflies break out of hibernation to dance in freshly tilled soil. A weathered tree stump reminds the author of an old poem about time, and the rhythm of planting potatoes and planting flowers shows how nature and human work blend. The piece celebrates simple, disciplined moments of nature.
