In 1862, formerly enslaved residents on Hilton Head Island created Mitchelville, the first self‑governed town for freed people. A new documentary, “The Spirit We Move With,” tells their story and the ongoing legacy of the Gullah Geechee community. It follows families, food, and land, highlighting their fight to keep traditions and property amid development. Premiering at Mitchelville Freedom Park, the film will travel to festivals, sharing a history of resilience and future hope.
