Just Eat has added a new AI voice assistant to its UK app that talks with customers about what they want to eat and then gives personalized suggestions. The chatbot can understand casual or vague requests, speaks many languages, and can be set to different accents. It aims to cut down the “choice overload” of thousands of take‑away options, and also helps people with mobility or visual impairments. The feature will roll out internationally later this year.
