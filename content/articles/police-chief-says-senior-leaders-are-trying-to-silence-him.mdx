Inspector Andy George, head of the National Black Police Association, says senior police chiefs and the Home Secretary are pushing to get rid of him. He claims he’s facing unfair investigations and a campaign to stop him from speaking out about racism and inequality in the police. Despite being re‑elected by his colleagues, he’s being told to return to regular duties and has launched a discrimination claim.
