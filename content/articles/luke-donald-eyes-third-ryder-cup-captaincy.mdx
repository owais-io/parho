Luke Donald is in Dubai talking to European Tour officials about becoming Europe’s Ryder Cup captain for a third time. After Europe won in 2022 and 2024, he could set a record by winning three consecutive Cups. He’s weighing the role against his family life and the political fallout of players like Jon Rahm and Tyrrell Hatton, who are fighting sanctions for playing in LIV Golf. The next Ryder Cup is in Ireland in 2027.
