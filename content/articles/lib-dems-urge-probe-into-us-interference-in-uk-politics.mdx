Lib Democrats want the UK to investigate foreign interference from the US as well as Russia. They say Donald Trump's policy supports far‑right parties in Europe and uses racist language about migration. The party claims the US is actively trying to influence UK politics. They urge the independent review to cover US interference to protect British democracy. The call comes after protests and a boycott of a US state banquet.
