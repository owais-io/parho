Brooklyn Beckham has stopped appearing in family photos and Instagram posts, even blocking the Beckhams on the platform. The rift started after his wife, Nicola Peltz, didn’t wear a Victoria Beckham dress at their wedding and the couple renewed vows three years later. With Brooklyn spending Christmas with the Peltzes, the feud shows how a public family dispute can affect everyone’s holiday feelings, beyond the spotlight.
