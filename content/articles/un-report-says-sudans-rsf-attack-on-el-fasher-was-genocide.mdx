A UN fact‑finding team says the Rapid Support Forces’ siege of El Fasher last year was genocide. They found the army killed, raped and starved thousands of the Zaghawa and Fur people and staged mass killings for 18 months. 320 witnesses were interviewed and 25 videos were checked. The US has now slapped sanctions on three RSF commanders. The report adds new weight to calls for accountability.
