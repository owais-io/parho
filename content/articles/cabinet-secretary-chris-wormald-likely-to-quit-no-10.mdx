In a flurry of resignations at Downing Street, Chris Wormald, the cabinet secretary, is negotiating an exit after only a year in the role. He was seen as a safe choice, but critics say he hasn’t pushed civil‑service reforms hard enough. His departure would be the third top staff exit in days, adding to the turmoil after the prime minister’s chief of staff and communications director quit. Starmer is trying to regroup the government.
