Bath dominated Newcastle at Kingston Park, winning 58‑13 and moving to the top of the Premiership table. They rested many key players but still scored eight tries, including a surprise switch that sent all eight substitutes into the game at once. Newcastle, still adjusting after a new owner takeover, suffered its 19th straight league loss. The match showed Bath’s strength and Newcastle’s ongoing struggles to find success.
