A man climbed onto the balcony of Iran’s embassy in London and pulled down the Iranian flag, swapping it with the old pre‑Islamic revolution lion‑and‑sun flag. The protest drew up to 1,000 people and police arrested two for trespassing and assault. The incident occurred while thousands worldwide protest Iran’s government, which has killed many demonstrators and shut down the internet. UK leaders urge restraint and support free‑speech rights.
