Trump has launched a new visa that lets wealthy foreigners buy a “golden card” for $1 million, promising quick U.S. residency. Applicants must also pay a $15,000 fee, pass a background check, and meet a 1 % yearly maintenance fee. Critics say it lets rich people pay their way into a country that traditionally welcomes hardworking immigrants. The plan comes amid a tough anti‑illegal‑immigration push, sparking debate over the U.S.’s open‑door reputation.
