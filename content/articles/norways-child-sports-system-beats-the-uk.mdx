Norway puts kids first when it comes to sport. They teach children to play, not just win. If a team is ahead, a player is taken out so everyone gets a chance. Coaches focus on enjoyment, not pressure, and teams avoid shouting or tears. The UK’s sports clubs are often small and costly, making it hard for kids to play. Norway’s bigger clubs let kids join and learn for free.
