After the federal immigration officer Gregory Bovino left Minneapolis, residents still fear raids and danger. Even though President Trump said the situation would calm, ICE keeps coming. People in neighborhoods watch ICE vans, schools run online for scared students, and families stay home for weeks. Local officials say nothing has changed and want ICE gone. Trump’s comments and attacks on politicians add to the tension.
