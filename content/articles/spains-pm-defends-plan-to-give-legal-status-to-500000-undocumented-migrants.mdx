Spain’s Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez released a short video to explain and defend his government’s new decree that will legalise 500,000 undocumented migrants and asylum seekers. He says the move offers dignity, community and justice for people already living and working in Spain. Critics claim it’s too far, but Sánchez points to the support from many citizens, churches and NGOs. The plan reflects Spain’s more open stance compared to other European leaders.
