Many UK households spend as much as £1,200 a year on subscriptions, but a Nationwide survey found 19% of people pay for services they never use. Cancelling or merging these “zombie” subscriptions could save up to £400 a year. Experts say start by listing all recurring payments, checking when you last used each, and look for free trials or family plans. New rules will soon make companies reveal and help cancel subscriptions.
