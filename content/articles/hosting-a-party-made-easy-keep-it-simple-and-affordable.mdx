Americans rarely host or attend parties, and many feel it’s hard. Experts say the trick is to keep things simple: invite a few friends, pick a comfortable venue, and use a short menu. Candlelight, music, and a small beer‑wine bar create a cozy vibe. Send invitations weeks ahead and give guests a warm tone. Focus on welcoming people, not perfection, and let the evening flow.
