Seven West Media’s revenue dropped $7m after two short Ashes Tests in Perth and Melbourne. The quick games meant fewer advertising slots and lower ticket sales, hurting the broadcaster’s earnings. Cricket Australia also lost money from the truncated matches, affecting player revenue shares. The broadcaster and the league hope to recover in the next summer’s season, though they expect a profit in the following season.
