Families of residents at William Blake House in Northamptonshire are calling for a formal investigation after the charity faced a £1.6 million debt, including unpaid taxes and a £1 million payment to a trustee’s company. The charity has spent over £800 k on strategy fees and £240 k on consultancy for a venture that never launched, raising serious governance concerns. With residents’ care at risk, the Charity Commission and local council are already probing the situation.
