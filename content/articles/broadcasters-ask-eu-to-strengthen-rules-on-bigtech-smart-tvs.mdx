Broadcasters from across Europe, including Disney and Warner Bros, wrote to the EU’s antitrust chief asking it to treat Google, Amazon, Apple and Samsung as gatekeepers for smart‑TV operating systems. They say the companies’ recommendation and search tools can steer viewers toward some shows and away from others, giving them unfair power. The letter comes as Brussels eyes stricter enforcement of its Digital Markets Act amid rising US‑EU tech tensions.
