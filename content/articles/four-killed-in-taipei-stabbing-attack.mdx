A 27‑year‑old man from Taoyuan attacked people in Taipei’s main train station and nearby shopping area, using smoke grenades and a knife. The attack happened on a busy Friday afternoon and ended when police chased him; he fell from a building and died. Four people were killed and several others hurt. Authorities say he had a criminal record and had missed military duty. Taiwan’s leaders promised stronger security and said no leniency will be shown.
