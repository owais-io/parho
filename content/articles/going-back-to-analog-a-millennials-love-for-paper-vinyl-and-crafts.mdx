I’ve been filling my bag with paper, crosswords and vinyl, not just my phone and charger. The digital world feels like a constant drain, so I’m turning back to analog stuff that feels real and calming. Trends show a boom in analog hobbies, and I’ve found that hand‑written notes, old records and scrapbooking help me stay present and reduce screen time. It’s a simple, sensory way to enjoy life.
