After a rocky relationship, former president Donald Trump has sued JPMorgan Chase, saying the bank shut his accounts for political reasons after the January 6 riots. The lawsuit follows years of tension between Trump and bank chief Jamie Dimon, who had once been a close ally and advisor. Dimon had criticized Trump’s policies, and the bank closed accounts deemed risky. Trump claims the action was unfair, while JPMorgan says the claim is baseless.
