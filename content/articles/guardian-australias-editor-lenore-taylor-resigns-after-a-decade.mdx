Lenore Taylor, who led Guardian Australia for ten years, has resigned as editor‑in‑chief. Under her, the site grew from a small start‑up to the country’s fourth‑most‑read news website, winning 12 Walkley awards and expanding into podcasts, video and social media. She built a team of 140 reporters and earned praise for bold reporting on politics, the environment and Indigenous issues. The Guardian will appoint a new editor while she reflects on her next challenge.
