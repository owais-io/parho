A British soldier died on Tuesday in Ukraine after an accident while watching a Ukrainian weapons test far from the front. It is the first time a serving UK military person has been killed in the country since Russia’s invasion. The Ministry of Defence said the family was notified and the death was not caused by enemy fire. Prime minister and defence secretary offered deep sympathy. The UK still keeps a small team in Ukraine to support the country.
