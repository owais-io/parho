Arsenal won a 2‑1 Champions League match against Kairat, with Kai Havertz scoring both goals. The German forward returned to start after a year out injured, helping the Gunners keep a flawless 8‑game winning streak in the league. The victory also keeps Arsenal’s hopes of a quadruple alive. Coach Arteta praised Havertz’s impact and introduced two young players, but a late Kairat goal still kept the match tense.
