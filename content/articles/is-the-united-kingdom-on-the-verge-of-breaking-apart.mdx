The Guardian article warns that Britain might split. Experts say when parties that keep the UK together weaken, the country can break apart quickly. The Conservatives and Labour are losing support, and English politicians are increasingly looking only at England, not the whole UK. After leaving the EU, English nationalism has turned against the union. The article calls for a new, inclusive vision for England that keeps the UK united.
