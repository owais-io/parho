Child sexual abuse online jumped 26% last year, with over 51,000 offences reported in England and Wales, according to police data. Half of the cases involved children sharing pictures with each other, and Snapchat was the most used platform. Police chief Becky Riggs says tech firms should use AI to stop indecent images from being posted. She also wants a nationwide study to understand the full extent of the problem.
