The US‑Russia New START treaty, which limits nuclear warheads, ends this week, raising alarm that a fresh arms race could start. Leaders like former President Trump and Russia’s officials say they may negotiate a new deal, but experts warn the treaty’s loss would undo decades of stability and could damage the global non‑proliferation system. The collapse could also revive old tensions in the Ukraine‑NATO region.
