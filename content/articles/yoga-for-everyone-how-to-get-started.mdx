Many people think yoga is only for fit, skinny, white folks, but experts say it’s for anyone. Yoga started in India 5,000 years ago to unite body and mind. Today it’s a mix of poses, breathing and meditation. Beginners can try free YouTube videos or a local class, wearing comfy clothes and a simple mat or towel. The key is to find a style that feels safe, calm and fun.
