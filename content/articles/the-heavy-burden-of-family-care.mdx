Many people feel trapped caring for parents or relatives who didn’t care for them. The article points out that without enough paid carers, hospitals or homes, those left alone would be unsafe. It argues that carers should be free to decide how much they help and that more funding is needed. Practical tips include using external caregivers, visiting instead of keeping relatives at home, and urging men to share duties.
