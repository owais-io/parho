Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, was shot dead by federal immigration officers in Minneapolis. Video shows no gun in his hands. The NRA and other pro‑gun groups want a full investigation and say the shooting was not justified. They argue the Second Amendment protects lawful gun owners, even during protests. State officials and the Minnesota Gun Owners Caucus also call for a transparent inquiry.
