Ya’akub Vijandre, a 38‑year‑old Muslim photojournalist, was arrested by ICE in October after a handful of social‑media posts. He says the government is attacking his faith and is worried about his family’s safety. While held in a Georgia detention centre, he reports guards treat detainees poorly. His lawyers argue the posts are protected speech and are being targeted because of his religion. The case highlights rising Islamophobia in immigration policy.
