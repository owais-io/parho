A licensed gun dealer in Arizona, Laurence Gray, faces federal charges for trying to sell several rifles, a machine gun and pistols to the Jalisco New Generation cartel and the Sinaloa cartel—both designated as terrorist groups. The indictment says Gray offered high‑caliber weapons that could help the cartels fight the Mexican government. Mexican authorities say most guns seized in 2024 that came from the U.S. were sourced from Arizona. The ATF is investigating the case.
