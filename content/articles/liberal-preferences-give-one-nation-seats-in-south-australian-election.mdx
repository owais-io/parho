Labor won a landslide in South Australia, but the Liberal party gave preference to One Nation, giving the right‑wing party seats in parliament. Analysts say the preference deal will secure at least two upper‑house seats for One Nation and likely more in the lower house as vote counts finish. The move gives One Nation official status, while Labor stays in government.
