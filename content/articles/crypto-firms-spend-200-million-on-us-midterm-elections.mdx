The cryptocurrency industry is pouring about $200 million into the 2024 US midterm races, with most of the money going to Illinois and a handful of other key states. Super‑Pac Fairshake alone has over $193 million in cash and has already spent $32 million on candidates that support crypto-friendly laws while attacking opponents. The aim is to secure the Clarity Act and keep Congress welcoming to digital assets.
