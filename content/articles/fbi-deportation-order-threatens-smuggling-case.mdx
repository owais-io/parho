FBI investigators are trying to bring a Belarusian woman, Yana Leonova, into the US to face charges for smuggling aviation parts to Russia. But immigration officials have ordered her detention and deportation, which has thrown the case into chaos. A judge described the situation as “Kafkaesque.” Leonova is accused of fraud, conspiracy and money‑laundering, and of moving $2 million worth of sensitive equipment through an Armenian company. She was extradited from France and is now in a Washington, DC jail.
