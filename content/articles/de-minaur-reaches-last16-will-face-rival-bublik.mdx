Alex de Minaur beat Frances Tiafoe in a tight three‑set win to reach the last‑16 of the Australian Open. He now faces Alexander Bublik, who has beaten him twice in big matches and has risen into the top 10. De Minaur has made the last‑16 five years in a row and hopes to overcome his nemesis. Bublik is known for his show‑tune style and recent surge in the rankings.
