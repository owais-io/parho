David Farley, a 69‑year‑old farm businessman, was picked as One Nation’s candidate for the Farrer by‑election. He compared former prime minister Julia Gillard to a “non‑productive old cow” in a joking remark at a conference about a new abattoir plant. The comment sparked outrage from women’s groups, a Liberal shadow minister and even party leaders, turning the race into a test for One Nation.
