England’s fast bowler Mark Wood joked about driving 2,500 miles from Perth to Brisbane to make up time after a heavy defeat. The England team collapsed in the first Test, letting Australia take a 1‑0 lead. Wood said the squad feels “shell‑shocked” and is regrouping. The side has an 11‑day break before the night‑time Test in Brisbane and is hoping its strong bowlers can bounce back.
