Berlin’s mayor, Kai Wegner, is being asked to quit after it was revealed he spent an hour playing tennis while the city was in a blackout caused by a fire that cut power to thousands. He said he was working nonstop, but later admitted he took a break on a tennis court. The incident, dubbed “tennis‑gate,” sparked criticism from opposition parties who say he didn’t take the crisis seriously. Wegner faces a political backlash ahead of the upcoming regional election.
