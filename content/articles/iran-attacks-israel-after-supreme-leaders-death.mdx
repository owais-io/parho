After the death of Iran’s Supreme Leader, the country fired missiles and drones at Israel and Gulf cities. Israel struck back by hitting Iranian targets in Tehran. U.S. President Trump warned of stronger retaliation. Several top Iranian military figures were reported killed, and at least 133 civilians died from U.S.-Israel strikes. Protests erupted across Iran and abroad, while the UN plans a meeting to discuss the situation.
