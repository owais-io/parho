Spanish police seized nearly 10 tonnes of cocaine hidden inside a cargo of salt on a merchant ship off the Canary Islands. The vessel, thought to have left Brazil, was boarded by special forces and international police partners. Thirteen people were arrested, and the ship was towed to Santa Cruz de Tenerife. This haul is the biggest drug catch at sea for Spain in over 20 years.
