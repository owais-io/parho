Police in Johannesburg’s Mulbarton suburb raided a street and arrested 12 Ethiopian men, who were part of a larger smuggling operation. The men were charged with illegal entry and kidnapping after a driver tried to flee. The case highlights a dangerous route that many Ethiopians take to reach South Africa, hoping for jobs and a better life. Smugglers charge high fees, lock migrants in poor conditions, and the journey often ends in violence or death.
