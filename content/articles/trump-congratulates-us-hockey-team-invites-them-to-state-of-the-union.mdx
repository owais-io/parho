After the U.S. men’s hockey team won gold in the 2026 Winter Olympics, President Trump phoned the players from a locker‑room video call. He praised their “unbelievable” performance and asked them to attend the State of the Union address this week as guests of honor. The team is considering the invitation, while the women’s gold medalists can’t join due to earlier commitments.
