After almost three years playing at the Olympic stadium, Barcelona came back to their home ground, Camp Nou, in front of 45,000 fans. The team won 4‑0, with young Lamine Yamal and Ferran Torres scoring, and the match felt like a celebration of both the old club and a new start. The stadium is still under renovation, but the return gave supporters a familiar, emotional moment and a glimpse of future plans.
