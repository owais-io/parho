A five‑year‑old Minneapolis boy, Liam Ramos, was taken with his dad by ICE and sent to a Texas detention center. His mother started a GoFundMe campaign to pay legal fees, and the community has donated over $110,000. The family says they were wrongly detained, while ICE claims the child was “abandoned.” The case highlights growing tensions over immigration enforcement in the U.S.
