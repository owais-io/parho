In Iran, a sharp drop in the currency and soaring prices has made life hard for many. Traders, students and women have shut shops and schools to protest. The demonstrations have spread from Tehran to other cities, with chants like “Death to the dictator” and “Woman, life, freedom.” The government says it will talk, but many feel the talks are fake. The protests are the biggest since 2022.
