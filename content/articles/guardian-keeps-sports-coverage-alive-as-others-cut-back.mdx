Guardian is keeping sports coverage alive even as other newspapers cut sports sections. They’re expanding coverage of women’s football, the Winter Olympics, and big events like the World Cup. The paper hires new reporters, starts a sports business column, and uses live blogs and newsletters. It also looks at how sports relate to politics, gender, and racism. The goal is to show sport can change the world.
