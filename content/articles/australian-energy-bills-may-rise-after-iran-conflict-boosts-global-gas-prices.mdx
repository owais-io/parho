U.S. and Israeli missile strikes on Iran could cause a surge in global gas prices, as Qatar’s LNG export halted and wholesale prices jumped 50% in Europe and 40% in Asia. Australian gas prices have already tripled over the past decade, and last year’s energy shock lifted household bills by 27% and industrial gas by 46%. The government plans a gas reservation scheme, but experts warn domestic prices could still rise.
