Last January’s wildfires in Los Angeles county destroyed about 16,000 homes and killed 31 people. Three families share how they’re trying to recover. The Hostetter family lost their Altadena house, moved five times, and is fighting paperwork to rebuild. In Pacific Palisades, Jenny Weigle‑Bonds relied on friends’ help after no official evacuation alerts reached her. Architect Steven Lewis watches neighbors rebuild with fire‑safe design, but costs and slow progress test patience.
