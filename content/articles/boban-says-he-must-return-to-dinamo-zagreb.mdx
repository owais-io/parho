Zvonimir Boban has returned to Dinamo Zagreb as president, saying he would betray his values if he didn’t. After leaving UEFA last year, he wants to rebuild the club with a new youth academy and better training. He also talks about his time at FIFA and why he resigned, criticising politics in football. Boban aims to make Dinamo a top club, using a model inspired by Barcelona’s youth system.
