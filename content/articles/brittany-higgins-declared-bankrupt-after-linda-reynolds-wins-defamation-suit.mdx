Brittany Higgins, a former political staffer, was ordered into bankruptcy after Linda Reynolds won a defamation lawsuit against her. A court judge froze Higgins’s assets, giving a trustee control to collect the $315,000 in damages, over $1 million in legal costs and any other debts. Reynolds wants to find out how Higgins spent her $2.4 million settlement and recover money owed. The bankruptcy will last about five years and could involve selling property or assets.
