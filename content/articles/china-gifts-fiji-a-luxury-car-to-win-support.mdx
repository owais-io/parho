China recently gave Fiji’s president a black Hongqi luxury car as part of its ‘prestige diplomacy’. The move signals that China is ready to spend money to build ties and get Pacific leaders to back the One China policy. Other powers – Australia, the US, Japan – also give vehicles to island governments, turning cars into moving billboards that show which country is a key partner.
