FBI can monitor citizens on a huge scale even without AI. The agency buys data from commercial brokers that collect people’s location, browsing and other details. This practice lets the FBI avoid needing a warrant. Privacy groups say it breaches the Fourth Amendment. The debate is part of a larger fight over whether AI firms should help the government track Americans.
