Scotland’s Holyrood election campaign has kicked off with parties holding launches across the country. The SNP, led by John Swinney, pushes for a historic majority that would trigger another independence vote, while Labour’s Anas Sarwar criticises Reform UK’s influence. Reform UK is less active after several candidates quit. Polls show Reform and Labour tied for second, but the SNP remains in the lead, sparking fierce debate over Scotland’s future.
