Australian novelist Craig Silvey was arrested this week on charges of possessing and distributing child‑exploitation material. Because of the case, his books have been taken off school curricula in Victoria and Western Australia, and a stage play based on his children's book *Runt* has been put on hold. Publishers and theatre companies are pausing promotion, and education departments are asking schools to stop using his works while the investigation continues.
