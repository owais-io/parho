Blue tits are back in my garden, but many garden birds are now facing hidden dangers. Research shows a third of UK gardeners use pesticides, and house sparrow numbers drop 40 % where the slug poison metaldehyde is used. Pesticides also end up in pet fur, which birds use for nesting. To help birds, cut pesticide use, plant native shrubs and flowers that give food all year, and skip supplement feeding.
