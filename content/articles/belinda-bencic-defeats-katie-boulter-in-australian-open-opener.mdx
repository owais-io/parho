Belinda Bencic beat Britain’s Katie Boulter 6‑0, 7‑5 in her Australian Open opening match. Bencic, who has never won a set in a season before, is now 6‑0 and looks like a title contender. Boulter, ranked 113, said she will keep working hard and play more matches to rebuild her ranking. Other first‑round highlights include Naomi Osaka winning 6‑3, 3‑6, 6‑4 over Antonia Ruzic and Elena Rybakina beating Kaja Juvan.
