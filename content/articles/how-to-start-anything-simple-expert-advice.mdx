This Guardian article gives quick, expert‑backed pointers for getting started with a wide range of activities—from reading and cooking to running, weightlifting, therapy, and dating. It breaks each habit into easy steps: pick a small goal, practice regularly, and use tools or classes if needed. The guide also covers keeping a dream journal, using sunscreen, budgeting money, and walking instead of driving to cut your carbon footprint. Follow these simple tips to turn new ideas into lasting habits.
