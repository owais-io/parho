The NBA is partnering with the UAE, sponsoring games and a tournament that use UAE money. The UAE is believed to arm Sudan’s Rapid Support Forces, which carry out mass killings and sexual violence. Critics say the NBA is helping the UAE hide its role in these crimes. While the league says it follows US guidance, activists and refugees call out the deal as sportswashing and supporting genocide.
