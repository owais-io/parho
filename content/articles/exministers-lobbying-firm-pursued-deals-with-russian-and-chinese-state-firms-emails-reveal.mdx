After leaving his role as business secretary, Peter Mandelson started the lobbying firm Global Counsel with Ben Weg Prosser. Emails show they chased contracts with Russian and Chinese state companies such as Rusnano and China International Capital Corp. The documents also reveal meetings with Jeffrey Epstein, the convicted sex offender, to discuss business. The fallout has embarrassed the UK government, with the prime minister facing scrutiny for appointing Mandelson as US ambassador. Global Counsel says it cut ties with the scandal.
