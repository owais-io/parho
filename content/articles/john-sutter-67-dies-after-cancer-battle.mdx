John Sutter, a 67‑year‑old teacher and musician, died from oesophageal cancer. A specialist in teaching English to adult learners, he taught across Europe, helped launch national online courses, and ran the learning charity Learning Unlimited. He was known for blending theory and practice, inspiring teachers, and promoting fairness for migrants learning English. Outside the classroom, he played guitar in the reggae band Mighty Sounds. He leaves his partner Samantha, two daughters, a stepson and a sister.
