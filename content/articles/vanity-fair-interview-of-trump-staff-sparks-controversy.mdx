The Guardian article says Vanity Fair interviewed White House chief of staff Susie Wiles and published dramatic photos of Trump’s top aides. Critics say the piece uncovers uncomfortable truths, while Trump supporters call it a biased hit piece and claim the photos were doctored. Wiles and some officials say the images are true, but their comments were taken out of context. The story has split readers into two camps over how the team is portrayed.
