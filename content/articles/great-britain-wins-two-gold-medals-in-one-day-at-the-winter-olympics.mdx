Great Britain captured two gold medals in one day at the Winter Olympics. Charlotte Bankes and Huw Nightingale won the mixed team snowboard cross, Britain's first gold on snow in 102 years. Later, Tabitha Stoecker and Matt Weston took gold in the skeleton mixed team event, making Britain the first champions of that discipline. The day also sparked a curling rule debate over a double‑touch call.
