Joe Root had a strong tour of Sri Lanka, scoring big in Tests and ODIs and earning applause from fans. Despite his form, England’s T20 World Cup squad didn’t include him. Coach and teammates say the format needs fast, explosive hitters, and the current T20 line‑up already features top players like Jos Buttler and Ben Stokes. Root accepted the decision and will watch the tournament from the sidelines.
