The article explains why many professional women are less eager to chase higher roles. A recent survey found that over half feel less motivated to aim for promotions than two years ago. Factors include a gender “ambition gap,” workplace bias, a smaller pay gap, higher burnout rates, extra emotional work, and few female role models. These pressures combine to make promotion feel less rewarding and more stressful for women.
