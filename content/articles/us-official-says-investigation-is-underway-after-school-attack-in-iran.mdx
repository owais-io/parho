US Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth said the U.S. is investigating the deadly strike on a girls’ school in southern Iran that killed at least 165 students and injured many more. Iranian officials blame the attack, while the U.N. and U.S. officials urge a thorough inquiry. The strike happened amid the first U.S. and Israel attacks on Iran, leading to widespread casualties and a tense regional backlash.
