Ahmed al‑Ahmed, a Sydney shopkeeper who fought off attackers at a Bondi beach incident, is doing better after three surgeries for five gunshot wounds. He may leave hospital soon and focus on rest and family. He has received visits from politicians, religious leaders, and supporters. A GoFundMe raised $2.5 million to help his recovery. He may soon be honored for his bravery.
