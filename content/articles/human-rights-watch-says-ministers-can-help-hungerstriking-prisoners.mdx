Human Rights Watch wrote to the UK attorney general saying ministers can help stop the hunger strike by prisoners linked to the Palestine Action group. The letter argues that the government could direct prosecutors not to oppose bail requests, even though officials say they can’t intervene in court matters. The strikers, including Heba Muraisi, have been in jail over 400 days and risk serious health problems. The government denied the claim.
