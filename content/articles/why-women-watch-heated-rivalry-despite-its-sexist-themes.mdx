Zoe Williams explains why women keep watching the hockey drama “Heated Rivalry” even though it shows sexist ideas. The show follows two male leads in a romance that feels idealised. Women in the article feel admiration and discomfort – they enjoy the men’s looks and the love story, but also notice the show treats men as objects and shows patriarchal obstacles. The column calls out sexism and notes the drama has a larger cultural meaning.
