Researchers found that UK households discard about 168 million light‑up Christmas items and cheap tech gifts each year, worth £1.7 bn in spending. The study surveyed 4,000 adults and estimated 39 million fairy‑light sets, 28 million garlands and wreaths, 23 million light‑up figures, and 16 million pre‑lit trees were thrown away. Many of these batteries can spark fires; over 1,200 bin fires were recorded in 2023‑24. Experts urge recycling or donating, not tossing.
