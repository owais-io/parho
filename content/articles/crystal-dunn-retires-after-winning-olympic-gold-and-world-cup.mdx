Crystal Dunn has retired from professional soccer after a decorated career that includes Olympic gold, a World Cup win, and 160 appearances for the US women’s national team. She won three NWSL championships, earned MVP honors, and played in midfield, forward and defense. Dunn says she leaves the game with peace and thanks her teammates, coaches and family. She looks forward to a new chapter.
