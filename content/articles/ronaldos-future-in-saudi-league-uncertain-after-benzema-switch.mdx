Karim Benzema’s move to Al‑Hilal has made people question whether 40‑year‑old Cristiano Ronaldo will stay at Saudi club Al‑Nassr. Ronaldo was absent from a recent league win, and he’s upset with Saudi Arabia’s Public Investment Fund, which owns stakes in both clubs, after a rival signed his former Real Madrid teammate. The article also touches on other transfer chatter, including moves for Crystal Palace, Arsenal, Tottenham and Manchester United, and rumours about a new manager for Manchester City.
