A German tourist tried to get $100,000 from Los Tacos No 1 in Times Square after he said the salsa was too spicy and caused stomach and mouth problems. The federal judge said he didn’t prove the restaurant was negligent – he could have asked about the sauce himself. The case was thrown out, and the tourist has dropped other lawsuits he filed during the same trip.
