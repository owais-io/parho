Tottenham manager Thomas Frank says the club feels "cursed" by injuries. Lucas Bergvall, 19, hurt his ankle in a Champions League win over Borussia Dortmund and may be sidelined for several months. Spurs already have 19 injuries this season, second‑most in the Premier League, and have lost 625 injury days. Frank added that other players like Mohammed Kudus and Richarlison are also injured, but some teammates remain fit for the Burnley match.
