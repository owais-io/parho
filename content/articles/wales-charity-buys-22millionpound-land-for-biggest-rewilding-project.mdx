A Welsh charity called Tir Natur has bought 480 hectares of land in Ceredigion for £2.2 million. The aim is to create Wales’s largest rewilding area by restoring rivers, peat and woodland, and using natural grazing animals to help the soil heal. The project hopes to bring back species such as red squirrels and beavers. Farmers have concerns about land management, and the charity says it will work closely with local farmers.
