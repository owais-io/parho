A 13‑year‑old girl named Madison died last month in Manchester, and her family is grieving. Her parents posted a heartfelt tribute on Christmas Eve, praising her art and love. Police say they are still looking into the circumstances. A 14‑year‑old boy was briefly arrested on harassment charges linked to the case but has since been released on bail. Authorities want anyone with details to come forward.
