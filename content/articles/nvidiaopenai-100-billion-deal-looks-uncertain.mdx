Last week, people said Nvidia might invest $100 billion in OpenAI, but the deal may be gone. Nvidia’s boss said it was non‑binding and not final, and OpenAI said it was looking for other chip partners. The news sent Nvidia’s shares down 10% and raised worries among investors, including Oracle, which had a big cloud contract with OpenAI. Analysts say the size of the deal is too big for the companies’ real needs.
