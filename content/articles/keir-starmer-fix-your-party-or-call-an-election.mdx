Lib Dem leader Ed Davey says Labour’s leadership has been riddled with back‑and‑forth drama. He urges Prime Minister Keir Starmer to stop fighting within the party and actually run the government. If the party can’t settle its quarrels, he says the country should vote for a new election. Davey warns that a weak Labour government could pave the way for a far‑right win and stresses that people need real, effective policy now.
