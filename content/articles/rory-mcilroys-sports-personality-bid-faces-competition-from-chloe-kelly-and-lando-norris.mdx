Rory McIlroy had an amazing 2025, winning the Masters and the Ryder Cup. Now he’s aiming for the Sports Personality of the Year award, but golf rarely wins—only Dai Rees and Nick Faldo have. His main rivals are England striker Chloe Kelly, who won the Euro 2025 shoot‑out, and F1 champion Lando Norris. The award mixes skill and popularity, so the outcome could surprise fans, and McIlroy plans to attend the ceremony in Manchester to try to win over voters.
