Australian storm‑trooper Cyclone Narelle, a Category 5 cyclone, swept across Cape York in Queensland. Residents at Archer River, Lockhart River and Coen braced by staying in shipping containers and other sturdy buildings. Winds reached 190‑270 km/h, uprooting trees and knocking over power lines. Despite the power, damage was less than feared; no injuries were reported and most homes stayed intact. Local leaders praised the community’s preparedness and quick recovery.
