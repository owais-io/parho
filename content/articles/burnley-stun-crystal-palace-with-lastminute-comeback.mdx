Burnley pulled off a shock 3‑2 win at Crystal Palace, turning a 2‑0 deficit into a victory in the last seven minutes before halftime. Goals from Mejbri, Anthony and an own goal by Jefferson Lerma gave the visitors hope of staying in the Premier League. The win keeps Burnley's slim survival chances alive, while Palace still face a tough fight to avoid relegation.
