A Utah man, Abdul Raouf Afridi, was arrested after he tried to shoot Imam Shuaib Din outside the imam’s home. The incident happened on a Sunday night during Ramadan. Police say the gunman fired at the imam as he left the mosque, but the shots missed. Afridi faces 12 charges, including assault and gun‑related offenses. Authorities are still looking into why he targeted Din and whether the act was a hate crime.
