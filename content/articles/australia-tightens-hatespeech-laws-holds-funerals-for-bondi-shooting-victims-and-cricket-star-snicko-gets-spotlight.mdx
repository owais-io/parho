Australia’s Labor government is tightening hate‑speech laws, adding power to cancel visas for people who spread hate after the Bondi beach shooting. Prime Minister Albanese backs a plan to fight antisemitism, while opposition leader Sussan Ley pushes for urgent new law. The tragedy left 17 injured, 14 named victims, including 10‑year‑old Matilda. Flags returned to Bondi, the site reopened, and mourners held a moving funeral. Meanwhile, cricket star Snicko is again in the Ashes spotlight.
