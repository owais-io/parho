Australia will take on India in a women's T20 international in Sydney, with Sophie Molineux captaining her first match after former skipper Alyssa Healy retires. The Aussie squad drops vice‑captain Tahlia McGrath and fast bowler Megan Schutt, while spinner‑batter Nicola Carey returns. India’s line‑up features world‑champion Harmanpreet Kaur, Shafali Verma and Smriti Mandhana. Weather looks fine, but rain may delay the toss. The game marks a new chapter for Aussie women’s cricket.
