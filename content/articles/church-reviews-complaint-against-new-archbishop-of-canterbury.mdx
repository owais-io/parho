Church of England is looking into a complaint about the soon‑to‑be archbishop of Canterbury, Dame Sarah Mullally. The claim says she mishandled an abuse report from 2014 that was later filed in 2019. The complaint was supposedly lost because of paperwork mistakes, and Mullally was never told about it. The case will be reviewed by Archbishop Stephen Cottrell, who can decide if Mullally should face sanctions. Mullally says she wants better safety rules.
