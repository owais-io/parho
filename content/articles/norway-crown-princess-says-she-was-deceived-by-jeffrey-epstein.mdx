Norway’s Crown Princess Mette‑Marit says she was misled by Jeffrey Epstein, who she met in 2011. She claims she didn’t know he was a convicted sex offender, even after learning about his past. The Crown Princess apologized, said she regrets the contact, and wants to help victims. Norway’s parliament has set up a probe into links between the country’s foreign office and Epstein. The story also ties in her son’s legal troubles.
