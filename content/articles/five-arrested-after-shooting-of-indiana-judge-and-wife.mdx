Five people have been arrested after a gunman attacked Indiana Judge Steven Meyer and his wife at their home. Three suspects—Raylen Ferguson, Thomas Moss and Blake Smith—face attempted‑murder and conspiracy charges. Two other people, Amanda Milsap and Zenada Greer, are accused of bribery and helping the crime. Police say the motive is still unclear. The judge and his wife were injured but are recovering, and the case has drawn support from the community.
