German chancellor Friedrich Merz and Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said they support Donald Trump’s claim that Russian President Vladimir Putin agreed to stop attacks on Ukraine’s power grid for a week. The ceasefire, however, has not been confirmed by Russia, and attacks on civilian infrastructure continue. The leaders hope the U.S. can enforce the pause while Ukraine faces severe cold and power shortages. Meanwhile, Russia denies the truce.
