Progressive members of Congress announced they will refuse to fund the Department of Homeland Security until the agency adopts major reforms. The move follows the death of a Minneapolis woman shot by an ICE officer during a federal operation that targeted the Somali community. The progressives want ICE to stop wearing masks, require warrants for arrests, and end use of private detention centers. Their stance could delay the funding bill and trigger a partial government shutdown.
