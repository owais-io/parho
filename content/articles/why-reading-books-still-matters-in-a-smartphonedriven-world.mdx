The UK’s National Year of Reading celebrates the joy of books, but experts warn that the rise of smartphones and constant digital noise are making it hard for people to sit down with a book. A new report links childhood reading with better school and life outcomes, yet the distraction of screens threatens the pleasure and benefits of reading. The article asks whether books can still be fun and useful when our attention is pulled elsewhere.
