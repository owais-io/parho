The house that George Harrison bought in Letchmore Heath was once a training school for nurses at St Bartholomew’s Hospital in London. Young trainees spent months learning basic skills on each other, then moved to wards for real patient care. The article also mentions the village’s film history with ‘The Village of the Damned.’ It shows how the house has shifted from medical training to a celebrity home.
