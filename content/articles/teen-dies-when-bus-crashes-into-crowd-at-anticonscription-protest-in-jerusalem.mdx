An 18‑year‑old boy was killed when a bus drove into a crowd at an ultra‑Orthodox protest in Jerusalem. Police arrested the driver and say he was hit after rioters blocked the road. The incident happened while thousands were demanding that the army stop conscripting religious students. The move has split Israel’s politics, with ultra‑Orthodox parties opposing the draft and the government wanting more recruits.
