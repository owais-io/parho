George Clarke, a TV architect and Grenfell supporter, says he won’t use products from Arconic, Kingspan or Celotex because the Grenfell inquiry found those firms acted with systematic dishonesty. He urges architects, builders, landlords and homeowners to avoid them too, calling it a moral choice while the companies deny wrongdoing and still make big profits. The call comes as the industry faces possible debarment and slow legal proceedings.
