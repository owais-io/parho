The Los Angeles Clippers, once a steady playoff team, are now losing 14 of 16 games. Their new owner and former star Chris Paul, who came back for a final season, was told to leave. He only played 14 minutes a game and had tensions with coaches. The team’s record is the worst in the West, and the loss of Paul feels like the end of a hopeful reunion.
