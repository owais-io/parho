President Trump says the U.S. could decide on a nuclear deal with Iran within about ten days. Meanwhile, the U.S. is sending more warships and fighter jets to the Middle East, including two aircraft carriers. Experts say this shows the U.S. is ready to launch air attacks on Iranian sites if Trump chooses to. The move comes after talks in Geneva and after Israel’s bombing of Iranian facilities last year.
