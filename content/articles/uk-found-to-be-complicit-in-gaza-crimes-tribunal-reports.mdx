An unofficial tribunal led by former Labour leader Jeremy Corbyn says the UK helped Israel commit crimes in Gaza and failed to prevent genocide. The report urges Britain to stop arms sales, cut intelligence sharing, and review trade with Israel. It calls for a public inquiry and full transparency. The findings may be used in upcoming local elections, where Labour faces pressure from the Greens and Your Party to address Palestinian issues.
