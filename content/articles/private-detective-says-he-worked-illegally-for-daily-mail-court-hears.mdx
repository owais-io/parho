Daniel Portley‑Hanks, a 79‑year‑old private investigator, says he gathered personal data for the Daily Mail and Mail on Sunday for about £1 million, claiming it was legal at the time. He now believes the work was unlawful in the UK. The case, brought by Prince Harry, Elton John and others, argues that the newspaper used him to collect private info. The Daily Mail denies wrongdoing and calls the claims false. The court is reviewing evidence.
