Todd Snider, the beloved alt‑country singer‑songwriter known for songs like “Alright Guy,” died at 59 after being hospitalized with pneumonia. His record label and fans shared his passing on social media. Snider was a key figure in Nashville’s East Nashville scene, and his music highlighted a gentle, honest side. Health problems and a recent incident led to tour cancellations, and the music community mourns his loss.
