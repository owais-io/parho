A US senator proposed a bill that would strip dual citizens like the author of their American passport if they also hold another nationality. The writer says the U.S. is forcing Europe to choose between full cooperation or breaking ties, even supporting far‑right groups that want to weaken the EU. He argues Europe is the only major power still backing the rule of law. He refuses to give up his French citizenship and believes Europe needs a stronger, united identity.
