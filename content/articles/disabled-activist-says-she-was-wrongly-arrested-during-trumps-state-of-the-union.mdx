Aliya Rahman, a 43‑year‑old disabled software engineer, says she was arrested while standing at the State of the Union because she didn’t follow Capitol rules, not because she was protesting. She had been taken by ICE from her car in Minneapolis and now claims law‑enforcement targeted her. Capitol police say she was demonstrating. Rahman has legal aid and says the arrest was retaliation.
