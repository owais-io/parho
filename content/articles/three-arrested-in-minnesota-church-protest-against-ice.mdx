Three people were arrested after a protest at a church in St. Paul, Minnesota. The demonstration was against ICE, the U.S. immigration agency, and included the church’s pastor being accused of working for ICE. National leaders, including Trump and the White House, praised the arrests, while former CNN anchor Don Lemon livestreamed the disturbance. The arrests were part of a broader backlash over attacks on places of worship.
