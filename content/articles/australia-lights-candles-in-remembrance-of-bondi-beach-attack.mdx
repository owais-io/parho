On Sunday, gunmen opened fire on a Hanukkah celebration at Bondi Beach, killing 15 people, including a 10‑year‑old girl and an 87‑year‑old Holocaust survivor. A father and his 24‑year‑old son carried the guns; the father was shot by police and the son is in hospital. Australians lit candles nationwide to honor the victims, while Prime Minister Albanese announced stricter gun‑control laws. The tragedy also highlights a surge in antisemitic attacks across the country.
