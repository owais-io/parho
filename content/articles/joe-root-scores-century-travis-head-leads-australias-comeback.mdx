Joe Root made 160 in Sydney, giving England a solid 384 total in the Ashes Test. It was his first century in Australia and his 41st Test hundred, a big moment after past failures in the city. Australia replied with Travis Head's 91 not out, cutting England's lead to about 218. England lost wickets and struggled to defend the target, leaving the match still in doubt.
