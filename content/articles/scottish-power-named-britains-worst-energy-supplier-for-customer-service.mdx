A Which? survey of nearly 12,000 customers put Scottish Power at the bottom of the UK energy‑supplier rankings for customer satisfaction. British Gas and EDF Energy were right behind it. Other low‑scoring firms include So Energy, Ovo Energy and E.ON Next. Scottish Power says the survey doesn’t match its own service records and disputes the findings, but the ranking highlights growing customer complaints across the sector.
