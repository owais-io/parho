Trump says the U.S. will build a new class of battleships called “Trump‑class,” which will be larger, faster, and far more powerful than any ship built before. The plan starts with two vessels and could grow to 20–25. Naming the ships after him breaks the long tradition of naming U.S. battleships after states, reflecting Trump’s aim to leave a lasting legacy and signal strength to rivals.
