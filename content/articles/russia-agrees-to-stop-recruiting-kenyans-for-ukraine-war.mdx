Russia has agreed to stop recruiting Kenyan citizens to fight in Ukraine, Kenyan foreign minister Musalia Mudavadi said after talks in Moscow. About 1,000 Kenyans are believed to be fighting for Russia, and some, like long‑distance runner Evans Kibet, say they were tricked into signing contracts. The deal follows accusations that Moscow has been enlisting foreign nationals from many African countries to bolster its forces in the war.
