New documents show that Jeffrey Epstein’s 10,000‑acre ranch outside Santa Fe was used for alleged sex abuse of teenage girls, yet federal agents never searched the property after his arrest. Survivors describe repeated abuse there. New Mexico officials opened an investigation, paused it when the FBI stepped in, and are now pushing for a truth commission. The ranch has since been sold and may become a memorial for victims.
