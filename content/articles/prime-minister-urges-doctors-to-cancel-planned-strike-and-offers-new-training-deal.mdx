Keir Starmer says resident doctors should not strike next week because the NHS is under heavy strain from a severe flu outbreak. He has offered a deal that includes a pay rise, 4,000 extra training spots and coverage of exam costs. The government is also willing to postpone any strike until after Christmas if the doctors accept the offer.
