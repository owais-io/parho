An Israeli air strike on Thursday killed Alireza Tangsiri, a senior commander of Iran’s Revolutionary Guard navy. Tangsiri had led Iran’s maritime forces since 2018 and pushed new weapons like cruise missiles, drones and fast boats that threaten shipping in the Strait of Hormuz. He warned the U.S. about attacking Iran’s oil hub, Kharg, and said the strike would make the region safer. His death also took the head of the IRGC Navy intelligence.
