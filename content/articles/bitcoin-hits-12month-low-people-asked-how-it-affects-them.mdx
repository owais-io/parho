Bitcoin slid to $63,000 last Thursday, less than half of its October 2025 peak, and it’s not the only crypto losing value. Ether has dropped more than 30% this year, and the whole market has shed about $2 trillion since early October. The crash has hurt stocks of companies that own bitcoin, adding pressure to the broader market. Investors are now looking at gold for safety and wondering how the fall affects their money.
