New Mayor Zohran Mamdani started his job by acting on his campaign promises. He set up two teams to fast‑track new housing and cut red‑paper rules. He ordered homeless shelters to meet safety standards and called for ending solitary in jails. He also signed a free‑childcare plan for two‑year‑olds with the governor. Even a small repair on the Williamsburg Bridge got done. Critics still shout, but Mamdani is already making progress.
