Robert Rhodes, 52, was found guilty of killing his wife Dawn after a child gave new evidence. He had been acquitted in 2017, but a new trial in 2024 used the child's testimony that Rhodes planned the murder and forced the child to lie. The court also found him guilty of harming the child and lying in earlier cases. He faces sentencing in January.
