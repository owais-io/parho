A London music club said sorry after an antisemitic image appeared on stage at a Primal Scream concert. The screen showed a Star of David mixed with a swastika during a song, along with political pictures and Gaza footage. The club said it had no idea it was shown, condemned antisemitism and promised safety. Jewish groups called for an urgent investigation. The band and police were asked for comment.
