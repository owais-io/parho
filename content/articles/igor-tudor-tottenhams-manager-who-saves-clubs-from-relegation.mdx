Igor Tudor, known as a “ferryman” for taking struggling football teams to safety, has joined Tottenham Hotspur for the rest of the season. He’s saved clubs like Udinese, Lazio, and Juventus from relegation or poor finishes by turning results quickly. Tudor favours a fast, pressing style with a back‑three, which can suit Spurs’ injured squad. Though he rarely stays long, he is praised for steady, short‑term improvement.
