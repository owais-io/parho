Rain and the highest tides of the year caused flooding in California’s Bay Area, shutting roads and forcing rescue teams to help people stuck in cars. Water reached over 3 feet in some places, and the storm is expected to keep coming for a few more days. The same weather also hit Southern California, flooding streets, causing mudslides and canceling flights. Residents are advised to stay safe and avoid driving through flooded areas.
