In 2025, London recorded 97 homicides – the lowest in more than a decade, down from 153 in 2019. Killings of people under 25 dropped to just 18. Police say smarter, targeted work and youth diversion programs, with a 90 % success rate, have helped. London’s rate is lower than most European and many U.S. cities, but there are still concerns about race and community trust.
