Paul Quinn, 51, was accused of raping and beating a woman in 2003, a crime that led to Andrew Malkinson’s wrongful 17‑year imprisonment. In 2022, Quinn’s DNA matched evidence on the victim’s clothes, and he was arrested. A court found Quinn had been doing many online searches about the Malkinson case after it resurfaced, especially after new DNA evidence linked him. He denies the charges.
