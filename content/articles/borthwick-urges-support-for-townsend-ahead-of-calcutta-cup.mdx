England coach Steve Borthwick says fans should stand behind Scotland’s coach, Gregor Townsend, before the Calcutta Cup match. Townsend has faced criticism after Scotland’s loss to Italy and a recent controversy over a supposed Newcastle offer. Borthwick praises Townsend’s record and says Scotland’s attack is strong. He also outlines England’s lineup, highlighting key players like Maro Itoje and Luke Cowan‑Dickie.
