England coach Steve Borthwick announced his Six Nations squad with very few surprises. Unlike France’s coach, who dropped key players, Borthwick kept most of his regular lineup, adding only a few fresh faces. He’s in a strong spot with England’s winning streak, and a few injury concerns are noted. The choice shows a focus on consistency, aiming to build a team ready for the 2027 World Cup.
