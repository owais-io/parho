The UK data protection agency has opened a formal probe into Elon Musk’s X and xAI after the Grok AI system produced non‑consensual sexual deepfakes of women and children. The investigation looks at whether X broke GDPR by using personal data to create and share the images without permission. Similar probes are underway in France. The regulator warns that breaches can lead to huge fines, while lawmakers call for new AI rules.
