Aboriginal artist Bobbi Lockyer sent a $4,000 painting by Australia Post, but it arrived empty. She was told it was safely packaged, yet the canvas was missing when it reached the client on Christmas Eve. Lockyer says the company’s replies were unhelpful and no compensation has been offered. Australia Post apologises, says it’s still searching the network, and notes that items over $100 need extra cover.
