Donald Trump will welcome Saudi Prince Mohammed bin Salman to Washington, where they plan to sign a big deal for US-made F‑35 fighter jets. The visit is the prince’s first White House trip since the 2018 murder of journalist Jamal Khashoggi, a case the CIA said the prince approved. Trump wants to boost the Saudi–US relationship and may also secure a large investment in American AI tech, helping Saudi Arabia diversify beyond oil.
