Congress has written a letter to the Secretary of Homeland Security and ICE director, asking that 47‑year‑old Rodney Taylor, a double amputee, be released from a Georgia detention center. He was taken in after a burglary conviction, and now suffers from deteriorating health, unfit prosthetics, moldy showers and no meal help. Lawmakers cite Guardian reports of dehumanising conditions and call for his release for medical reasons.
