OpenAI’s video app Sora will soon let users add their faces to 20‑second clips with Marvel, Pixar, Star Wars, and Disney characters. Disney is investing $1 billion and will license about 200 icons like Iron Man and R2‑D2. The partnership gives fans creative freedom, but it doesn’t use real actors’ likenesses. Disney says it may even feature popular fan clips on Disney+.
