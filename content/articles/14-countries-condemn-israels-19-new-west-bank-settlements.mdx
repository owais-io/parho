Fourteen countries, including the UK, Canada and Germany, have denounced Israel’s approval of 19 new settlements in the West Bank, saying it breaks international law and could spark unrest. The move pushes the total to about 210 settlements, up from 141 in 2022. The governments urge Israel to reverse the decision and support a two‑state peace plan, stressing the settlements are illegal.
