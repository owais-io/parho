Inside Labour, the soft‑left wing is waiting for the right moment to launch a leadership bid. Andy Burnham, once a favourite, is now sidelined, while Health Secretary Wes Streeting could be ready to run in May if seats are lost. Most MPs are cautious; a confidence vote like the one used against Jeremy Corbyn is being discussed, but Starmer’s base seems solid. Candidates like Angela Rayner and Shabana Mahmood are also watching the clock.
