Paul Nurse, the Royal Society’s president, defended the society’s decision not to remove Elon Musk from its list of fellows, saying only fraud or serious research mistakes warrant expulsion. Musk’s election in 2008 is now under fire because of his controversial actions and an AI tool that can remove clothing from photos. Some scientists agree with Nurse, while others want to expel Musk for harming science’s reputation. The debate shows the society’s rules are being questioned.
