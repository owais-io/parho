After a U.S. air strike on Iranian facilities, Tehran fired a wave of missiles at Israel and several U.S. military bases in the Middle East. Iran says it is defending itself and has warned that any base supporting Israel will be targeted. The attacks hit airbases in Qatar, Kuwait, the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Iraq and Bahrain. The move has drawn criticism from Gulf countries and could spark a wider war.
