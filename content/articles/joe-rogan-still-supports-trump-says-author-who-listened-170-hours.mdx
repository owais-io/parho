The author, who has listened to more than 170 hours of Joe Rogan’s show, argues that Rogan still backs Trump despite criticism. He points out Rogan’s repeated comments on ICE, immigration, and the “great replacement” theory, showing how the podcaster supports Trump’s agenda. The piece says Rogan hasn’t changed his view of Trump, even after incidents like the Epstein files or ICE protests. It stresses the need to see the full context of Rogan’s talks.
