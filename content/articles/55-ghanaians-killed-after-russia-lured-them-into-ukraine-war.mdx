Ghana says at least 55 of its citizens were killed after Russia lured them into fighting in Ukraine. Foreign Minister Samuel Ablakwa said 272 Ghanaians were drawn in since 2022, with 55 dead and 2 taken prisoner. He warned against dark‑web recruitment scams. The story highlights growing pressure on Moscow over illegal enlistment of Africans, with similar concerns in South Africa and Kenya.
