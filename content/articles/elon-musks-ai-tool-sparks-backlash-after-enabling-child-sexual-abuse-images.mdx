Elon Musk’s AI chatbot, Grok, let users create sexualised deepfake images, including of minors. The feature, called “spicy mode,” produced thousands of illegal pictures, sparking outrage from governments, child‑safety groups and the public. Musk tried to limit the tool by adding a paywall, but critics said it was insufficient. After investigations and pressure, the feature was removed, but the incident raised big concerns about AI safety and regulation.
