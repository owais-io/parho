Britain saw record numbers of treasure finds in 2024, driven by metal detectorists. The British Museum highlighted a hoard of 179 silver pennies from Harold II’s reign, a rare Roman wagon part, and early medieval metal objects that hint at high‑status burials. Public reports made up 94% of the finds. The total count rose to 79,616, showing how everyday people help uncover and protect the country’s history.
