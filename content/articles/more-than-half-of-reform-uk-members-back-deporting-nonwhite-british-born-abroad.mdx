An anti‑racism group found that more than half of Reform UK members think non‑white British citizens born abroad should be forced to leave. The poll, run by Survation, shows 54% in favour of deportation and 22% for those whose parents were born in the UK. Nigel Farage’s party is trying to win middle‑ground voters, but right‑wing rivals like Rupert Lowe and Tommy Robinson are pushing for stricter borders. The findings raise concerns about growing racial nationalism in the UK.
