EQT, a Swedish investor, will take a 42% stake in Yorkshire Water’s parent, helping to pay a £600m loan and support plans to clean up sewage spills. The deal comes as Yorkshire Water faces fines for repeated sewage leaks and a scandal over £1.3m bonus paid to its CEO. New funding may ease scrutiny, but bills for customers could rise about 6% to £636 next year.
