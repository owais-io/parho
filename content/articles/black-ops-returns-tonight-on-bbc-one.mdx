Tonight on BBC One, the popular sitcom Black Ops makes a comeback. Two former police support officers, Gbemisola Ikumelo and Hammed Animashaun, now play young MI5 agents, Dom and Kay. The show is full of laughs as Kay deals with a nervous colleague who’s eager to work with a Black man, while Dom falls for a higher‑up agent. Viewers can expect silly antics, light‑hearted romance and plenty of fun.
