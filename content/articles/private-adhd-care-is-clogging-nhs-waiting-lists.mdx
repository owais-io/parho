Private ADHD care is clogging NHS waiting lists as patients return after private assessments stall. The problem stems from unregulated private clinics that don’t always follow NICE guidelines and lack qualified staff, forcing GPs to pull patients back. Families face huge medication costs, long waits and treatment gaps. NHS trusts are overwhelmed, and the government admits it can’t handle the surge in ADHD referrals.
