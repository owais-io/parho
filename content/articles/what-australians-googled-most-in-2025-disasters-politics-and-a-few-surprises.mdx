Australians’ biggest Google searches in 2025 were driven by a big cyclone, the death of activist Charlie Kirk, and the national election. Belle Gibson, the wellness‑scammer, also made the top ten. People searched for election info, safety tips, and sports highlights, plus odd topics like a Grammys outfit and how to make ice‑cream grow in a garden. Classic trends such as DIY fixes, Easter recipes, and “how to talk to women” stayed popular too.
