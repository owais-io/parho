During the four weeks to December 28, shoppers spent £13.8 billion on groceries, a 3.8 % rise. Aldi and Lidl combined captured 16.8 % of the market – the biggest share ever – while Asda and Co‑op slipped. Lidl grew fastest, almost catching Morrisons, and Aldi edged close to Asda. Online sales rose 7.5 %, with Ocado up 15 %. Premium own‑label items also jumped 9 %. Tesco remains the biggest grocer with 28.7 % share.
