Trump said the U.S. doesn’t need NATO after allies rejected his plan to reopen the Strait of Hormuz, which Iran has closed. He called the move a foolish mistake and blamed countries like the UK, Japan, Australia and South Korea for not helping. The strait is vital for global oil shipments, and Iran’s blockade has pushed prices above $100 a barrel. Trump hinted at possible military action against Iran.
