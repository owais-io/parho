Trump’s flight to Davos was postponed when Air Force One had a minor electrical fault, causing him to switch planes and lose about three hours. He’s still set to speak in the afternoon, where he’ll focus on the Greenland dispute and criticize European trade policies. Meanwhile, officials like Scott Bessent used the press conference to attack France, Germany and even California’s governor, while the World Economic Forum’s schedule readies for Trump’s controversial remarks.
