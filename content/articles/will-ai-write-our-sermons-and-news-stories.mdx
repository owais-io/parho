AI is entering many jobs, from the church to Hollywood, the Pentagon, and newsrooms. Pope Leo XIV says AI can’t give a true sermon, urging priests to write their own. Writers’ unions fear job loss, while some editors see AI as a drafting tool that editors polish. The debate is wide, and experts insist human oversight is key, even if AI takes over routine tasks.
