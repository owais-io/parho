Baker Mayfield took to social media to criticize former Cleveland Browns coach Kevin Stefanski after Stefanski’s move to the Atlanta Falcons. Mayfield said Stefanski treated him like “garbage” and called him “failed” in an interview, noting he still hasn’t heard from him after being shipped to Carolina in 2022. With Tampa Bay, Mayfield will face the Falcons twice a year, making the comments more personal. Mayfield has had a rocky NFL career, including a Pro Bowl stint with the Buccaneers.
