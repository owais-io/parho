Trump’s threat to impose new tariffs on eight EU countries that supported Greenland troops has sparked a strong response from EU leaders. France’s Emmanuel Macron and other officials have warned the U.S. will not tolerate intimidation. The European Parliament is set to pause the trade deal signed in Scotland, and EU leaders say the deal can’t be ratified under these conditions. The UK already has a limited trade deal in place.
