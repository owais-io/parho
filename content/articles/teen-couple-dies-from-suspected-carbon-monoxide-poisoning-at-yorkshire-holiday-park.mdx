A 15‑year‑old girl and a 17‑year‑old boy were found dead in a rental at Little Eden holiday park, Bridlington, after suspected carbon monoxide poisoning. Police have arrested a 42‑year‑old man on gross negligence manslaughter and extended detention of a 33‑year‑old suspect. Families are grieving and an online fundraiser raised over £16,000 in two days. The park remains cordoned while authorities investigate.
