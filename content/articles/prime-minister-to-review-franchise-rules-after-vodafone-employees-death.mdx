Prime Minister Keir Starmer promised to review franchising laws after a Vodafone employee’s death sparked claims that the telecom giant pressured franchisees. A court case by 62 former Vodafone stores says the company enriched itself unfairly. Starmer will study the case outcome to decide if new laws are needed to protect franchisees from abuse. Vodafone denies the allegations and says it has resolved the dispute.
