A Pakistani man in Australia, Naveed Akram, was mistakenly shown as a shooter in the Bondi Beach attack. His photo went viral, making him feel terrified and deeply upset. He told police the posts were wrong, but they advised him to shut down his social accounts. Akram filmed a video to prove he wasn’t the attacker, but the fake posts kept spreading. He’s still scared to leave home and worries for his safety.
