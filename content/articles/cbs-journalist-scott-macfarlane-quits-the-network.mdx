Scott MacFarlane, a CBS News correspondent known for covering the January 6 Capitol attack, announced he was leaving the network. He said he wants more independence and hasn’t lined up another job. MacFarlane felt CBS didn’t give Jan 6 the attention he believed it deserved, and his resignation followed criticism of the network’s editorial direction under Bari Weiss. Colleagues called him a diligent journalist, and some critics of Weiss also noted his departure.
