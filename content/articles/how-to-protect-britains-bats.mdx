Bats are often disliked, but they’re important and fascinating. The Bat Conservation Trust explains why they need protection and shows simple ways to help. Reduce garden lights, keep cats indoors, plant flowers that attract insects, and build or buy a bat box. If you find a wounded bat, follow safety steps and call the helpline. Get involved in local monitoring and spread the word to keep UK bats thriving.
