In Florida, parents who are undocumented are signing wills and guardianship papers because they fear being taken by ICE and left with their children. After violent raids in Minneapolis and other cities, families worry about sudden detention and deportation. They’re also moving money into trusts, saving documents online, and choosing trusted friends as “point persons” for their kids. The fear is that they could disappear and their children would be left alone and in danger.
