An Irish tourist, 35, was found drowned at Whitehaven Beach near the Great Barrier Reef. That’s one of five deaths that happened in a month of bad surf on Australia’s east coast. A woman was swept out at Sydney’s Maroubra beach early on New Year’s Day, and another woman died at Dunbogan beach near Port Macquarie. A man disappeared at Coogee, and a boy was missing after a boat capsized. Surf‑life‑savers warn everyone to stay out of the water.
