A 42‑year‑old man, Alireza Askari, was jailed for at least 26 years after he strangled his ex‑wife, Paria Veisi, in their former home in Penylan, Cardiff. Police found her body buried under the garden’s patio slabs. Askari tried to hide evidence with his aunt, bought a knife set and chemicals, and even texted a girlfriend in Iran about the murder. The judge said he killed her because she wanted a divorce.
