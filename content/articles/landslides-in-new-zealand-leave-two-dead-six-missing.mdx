Heavy rain triggered two landslides in New Zealand's North Island. One hit a house, killing two. Another hit a campsite, crushing cars and a building, killing two more and leaving at least six missing, including teenagers. Police are still searching. The government says it will support families and investigate why no evacuation happened. Residents are warned about unstable ground and told to stay away from risky spots.
