Police in Canberra pulled down art posters that showed Donald Trump, Benjamin Netanyahu, Elon Musk, JD Vance and Nigel Farage in Nazi uniforms from a music venue. The posters were examined under new hate‑symbol laws that came after the Bondi shooting. Investigators said the displays didn’t break the law, so no charges will be filed. The posters were returned, but the owner wants an apology and says the laws are rushed and flawed.
