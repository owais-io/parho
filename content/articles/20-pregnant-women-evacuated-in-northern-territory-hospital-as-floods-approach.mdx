Queensland and the Northern Territory are in a flood alert as heavy rains from several tropical lows threaten rivers and low‑lying towns. The Katherine hospital was evacuated, taking 21 patients and up to 20 pregnant women, and an emergency unit was set up nearby. Roads and the Stuart Highway closed, and local councils warned residents to stock supplies, have plans and move to higher ground.
