Jeffrey Epstein used promises of college scholarships to lure women into his abuse ring. Rina Oh, a 21‑year‑old art student, says he told her he’d help her get into School of Visual Arts and give her a scholarship. When she refused his demands, he withdrew the offer. Several other survivors have told lawmakers that Epstein arranged or paid for tuition at NYU, Columbia, Harvard and MIT to keep them indebted. New documents show how he used these promises to control and exploit students.
