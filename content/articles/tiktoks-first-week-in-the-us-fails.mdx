After TikTok was sold to U.S. investors, the app faced outages and backlash. New owners changed the privacy policy, raising privacy worries. A snowstorm knocked out Oracle data centres, causing users to lose videos or get no views. Celebrities and politicians claimed TikTok was censoring anti‑ICE content, sparking media attention. The company blamed weather, but trust eroded, and a new rival gained popularity.
