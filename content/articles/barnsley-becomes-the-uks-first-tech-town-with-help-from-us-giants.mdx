Barnsley, a town in South Yorkshire, is being branded the UK’s first ‘tech town’. The government is bringing in Microsoft, Google, Cisco and Adobe to help the council use AI in schools, hospitals and businesses. Residents will get free AI training, while the local hospital will test AI for check‑ins and the college will try AI to boost learning. Some locals worry about the impact of AI and the role of the big tech firms.
