Seattle and San Francisco play a Saturday night showdown for the top NFC spot. Both teams are on six‑game winning streaks and a win would give them a bye week; a loss forces a wildcard trip. Seattle’s defense has allowed the fewest points in the league and the 49ers’ offense is powerful, but their quarterback needs help after losing a key tackle. The game is about who can shut the other down and keep their streak alive.
