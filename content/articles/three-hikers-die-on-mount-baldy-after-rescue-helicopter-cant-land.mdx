Three hikers died on Mount Baldy after high winds stopped rescue teams from landing. They were found on Devil’s Backbone trail. A call earlier in the day said a 19‑year‑old had fallen 500 feet. Helicopters couldn't land, and a medic later found all three dead. Their names weren’t released. Two other hikers were saved that same day, one hurt after a 100‑foot fall. The cause of death is still unknown.
