An Oxfam board fight erupted over the exit of chief executive Halima Begum. An independent review of her conduct, citing bullying and poor leadership, led some trustees to call for her departure, but others say the board never agreed. Begum says she resigned, not sacked, and claims the media is attacking her reputation. Her lawyers say she was targeted by a witch‑hunt, and investigations are still underway.
