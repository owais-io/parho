Brighton's iconic Palace Pier is being sold after years of falling visitor numbers and rising costs. The 126‑year‑old pier, owned by Brighton Pier Group, saw a 4% dip in sales and profits fell from £1.7 m to £300 k last year. The group hired Knight Frank to find buyers in the UK and abroad, hoping to return money to shareholders while keeping the pier profitable.
