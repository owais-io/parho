The FBI raided the Fulton County election office in Georgia last month after a referral from a White House lawyer named Kurt Olsen. Olsen had been working to overturn the 2020 presidential results and pushed the FBI to act on false claims that the election was stolen. The raid was driven by debunked allegations from Trump‑loyal election deniers and raises worries that Trump might try to influence this year’s mid‑term elections.
