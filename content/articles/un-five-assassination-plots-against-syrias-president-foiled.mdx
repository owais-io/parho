The UN report says five separate plots to kill Syria’s president or his top ministers were stopped last year. Two attempts targeted President Bashar al‑Assad in Aleppo and Daraa, and the group behind them was linked to an Islamic State front. Intelligence from a neighbour helped Syria's security forces foil the plans. The report notes IS is ramping up recruitment, using front groups, and aims to destabilise the government.
