Gregory Bovino, the border patrol commander who led Trump‑era enforcement in Minneapolis, is leaving the city after the fatal shooting of Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse killed by a U.S. agent. The federal administration is reshuffling its immigration team there, appointing Tom Homan to oversee operations and scaling back the presence. Pretti’s family accuses the administration of lying about their son.
