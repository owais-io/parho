The NSW government has decided to end the exemptions that let over 670 clubs and pubs keep poker machines open after 4 am. From 31 March, 673 venues must close by 4 am, though they can appeal the rule. The change follows research showing many people gamble and get harmed after midnight, an audit report, and pressure from parties like the Greens. The aim is to cut gambling‑related harm and big money losses.
