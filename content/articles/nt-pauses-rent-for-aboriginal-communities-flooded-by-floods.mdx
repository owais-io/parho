The NT government has frozen rent for public housing tenants in flooded Aboriginal communities in the Big Rivers region. The pause is automatic and doesn’t require applications. Floods caused record water levels in Daly and Katherine rivers, leaving many residents displaced. Officials say they’re focused on safety, repair, and food security. Critics argue relief has been uneven, especially outside Katherine, and call for broader support. The pause is part of wider emergency funding for the region.
