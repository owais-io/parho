Coach Liam Rosenior blames himself for Chelsea’s 3‑2 defeat to Arsenal, even though goalkeeper Robert Sánchez made two key errors. He praises Sánchez’s earlier save and says mistakes belong to the team, not just the goalkeeper. Despite injuries and suspensions, Chelsea lost 3‑2, with Arsenal leading 3‑1 before a late double by Alejandro Garnacho. The match was the first leg of the Carabao Cup semi‑final.
