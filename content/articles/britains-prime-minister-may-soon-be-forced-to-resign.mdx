Keir Starmer’s time as Britain’s prime minister looks shaky. His chief of staff quit after revelations that Starmer had hired Peter Mandelson, a former ambassador linked to the Epstein scandal. MPs, party bosses and the press are calling for a change, but the cabinet has defended him so far. Without a clear replacement, Starmer may have to step down before the next Scottish or Welsh elections add pressure.
