Tesla avoided a 30‑day ban in California by ditching the word “autopilot” from its ads and instead calling its full‑self‑driving system “supervised.” State regulators said the change stops misleading drivers. The move comes after a judge found Tesla had misled consumers about its cars’ ability to drive themselves. Meanwhile, Tesla is shifting focus to robotaxis and robots amid a drop in electric‑vehicle sales.
