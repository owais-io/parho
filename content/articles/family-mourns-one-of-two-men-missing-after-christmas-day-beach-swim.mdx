On Christmas Day, two men went missing while swimming in Budleigh Salterton's sea. One of them, 45‑year‑old Matthew Upham, has been lost; his family posted a heartfelt tribute on Instagram, saying he was deeply loved. Rescue teams – coastguard, RNLI, police – searched the water until 5 p.m. but found no trace. The search stopped; police say investigations continue and warn people against swimming on Boxing Day.
