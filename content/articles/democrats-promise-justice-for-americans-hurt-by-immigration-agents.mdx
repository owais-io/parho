On Tuesday, Democratic lawmakers apologized and pledged to hold immigration officers accountable after hearing harrowing stories from families who were hurt or killed by federal agents. Victims, like the family of Renee Good, who was shot by ICE in Minneapolis, shared their pain. The hearing had no Republican presence, and officials called for investigations and legal action against the agents involved. The session highlighted how these incidents have deeply affected communities across the country.
