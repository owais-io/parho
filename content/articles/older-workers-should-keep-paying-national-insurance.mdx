Simon Jenkins says people in their 60s and 70s can stay healthy and productive by working longer. The letter writer notes that when you reach the retirement age you stop paying national insurance, even if you keep working. They propose the rule be changed: anyone who works should continue to pay national insurance, regardless of age. The letter also points out ageism in many areas and that older people are still active.
