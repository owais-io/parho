The Premier League broke the transfer‑spending record this summer, spending more than £3bn, and now has 41% of the Guardian’s 2025 Top 100 male footballers – up from 33% last year. The league’s money has helped it dominate the list, though English clubs are less dominant in European finals. Ousmane Dembélé topped the ranking, beating Lamine Yamal. Spain and Brazil still lead in national‑team players. 2026 will bring even more football excitement.
