ICE plans to spend about $38 billion to buy and turn warehouses into new immigration detention centers across the U.S. The plan includes 16 regional sites that can hold 1,000–1,500 people each and eight larger centers that could house 7,000–10,000 detainees. The new centers will keep people detained for a few weeks before deportation, amid rising ICE hiring and arrests.
