The Trump team has taken steps that put people who are legally in the U.S. at risk. It paused refugee resettlement, cut refugee limits, and shut down temporary protected status for about a million people. The agency also hit DACA holders, asylum seekers, and people with visas for work or study, threatening arrests, detentions, and deportations. Courts are fighting these moves, but many still face uncertainty.
