At the Bafta awards, John Davidson, who has Tourette’s syndrome, shouted the N‑word while actors were presenting. The BBC didn’t cut the clip before airing, so it was shown on TV and online. The incident sparked backlash from celebrities and a Tourette’s charity, which said the slur was a symptom, not a choice. The article explains what Tourette’s is, how rare swearing tics are, and the challenges people face.
