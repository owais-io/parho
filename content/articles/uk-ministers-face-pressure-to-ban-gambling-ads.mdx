New polls show 70% of Brits want tougher limits on gambling ads. MPs and charity groups are urging the government to ban ads that reach kids on TV and social media. Gambling firms spend about £2bn a year on ads and have already agreed to cut on‑air slots after 9 p.m. The industry lobby says spending is lower, but the government says more work is needed to protect vulnerable people. No legislation is set yet, but pressure is mounting.
