UK retailers are set to trim staff hours and cut jobs because wage bills and other costs are rising. A BRC survey shows 61% of finance leaders will reduce working hours or cut overtime, 55% plan to cut head‑office roles and 42% will cut in‑store jobs. Rising tech, online rivals, and weaker consumer spending are adding pressure. The move could hurt young people who rely on part‑time retail work.
