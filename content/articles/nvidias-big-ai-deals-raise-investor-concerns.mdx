Nvidia, the chipmaker that powers AI tools like ChatGPT, has made huge investments—$125bn in deals this year, including $10bn a year to OpenAI for ten years. Critics say the company is using a form of vendor financing, lending money to customers so they buy Nvidia chips. The risky moves have led some investors to compare it to past corporate collapses. If AI growth stalls, Nvidia could lose money and its stock could fall.
