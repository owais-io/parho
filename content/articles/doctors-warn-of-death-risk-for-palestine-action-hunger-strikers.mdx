Doctors say that Palestinian Action activists who are on hunger strike in UK prisons face serious health risks and may even die. Past hunger strikes, like the IRA strike in 1981, show that long fasts can be deadly. The experts say that prison staff need clear guidelines for caring for strikers beyond the first few days, and that the government should act before more activists die. They call for urgent medical oversight and better support.
