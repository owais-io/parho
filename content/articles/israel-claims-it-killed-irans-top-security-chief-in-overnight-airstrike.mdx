Israel says it killed Iran’s top security chief Ali Larijani during overnight airstrikes, a claim the Iranian government has not yet confirmed. The Israeli defence ministry also said it hit a Basij commander and other senior figures. The move could shift power inside Iran’s leadership during a tense war. Europe has turned down US calls to send warships to the Strait of Hormuz, while oil prices jump after Iran’s attacks.
