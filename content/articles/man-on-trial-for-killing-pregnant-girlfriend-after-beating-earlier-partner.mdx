Stephen McCullagh, 36, faces charges of murdering his pregnant girlfriend, Natalie McNally, in Northern Ireland. Prosecutors say he pre‑planned the attack, using a pre‑recorded video game stream as an alibi while he travelled 17 miles to her home. Earlier, he allegedly recorded counseling sessions and physically abused a former partner. The case is in court, and a 12‑person jury will decide over the next few weeks.
