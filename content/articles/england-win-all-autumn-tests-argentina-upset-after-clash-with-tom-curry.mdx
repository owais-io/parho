England completed a perfect autumn by beating South Africa, Fiji, Argentina and Australia, winning all four tests at Twickenham. The win gave them 17 tries and a new 11‑test streak. The game against Argentina finished 17‑3, with England’s George Ford and Max Ojomoh scoring key points. After the match Argentina coach Felipe Contepomi accused England’s Tom Curry of shoving and making a reckless tackle that injured Juan Cruz Mallía, sparking a heated post‑match clash.
