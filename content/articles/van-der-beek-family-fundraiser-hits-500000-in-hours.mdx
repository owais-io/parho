Actor James Van Der Beek, famous for Dawson’s Creek, died at 48 after a battle with bowel cancer. His wife, Kimberly, and six kids launched a GoFundMe to cover medical bills and keep the family stable. The fundraiser set a $500,000 goal, but within hours raised close to that amount, prompting an increase to $700,000 and later $1 million. Fans, former castmates, and celebrities have donated, showing widespread support.
