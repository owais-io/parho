California Republicans have gathered enough signatures to place a voter‑ID law on the November ballot. The measure would require voters to show ID at the polls and would force election officials to confirm they are U.S. citizens. Led by Assemblyman Carl DeMaio and funded by donor Julie Luckey, the effort follows a national GOP push for tighter voting rules, while opponents warn it could block legal voters and cost the state hundreds of millions a year.
