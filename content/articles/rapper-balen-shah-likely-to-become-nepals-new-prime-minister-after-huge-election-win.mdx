Balen Shah, a popular rapper turned politician, is set to become Nepal’s prime minister after his Rastriya Swatantra Party won a record‑breaking landslide. The party secured 122 of 165 direct seats, giving it a clear majority. Shah’s rise came after a wave of youth protests that toppled the old government, and voters now expect him to tackle corruption, rebuild trust, and steer Nepal’s foreign relations.
