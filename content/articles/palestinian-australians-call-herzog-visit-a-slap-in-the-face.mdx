A visit by Israeli president Isaac Herzog has sparked protests from Palestinian Australians who feel it glorifies a government accused of genocide. After a deadly anti‑Jewish attack on Bondi beach, Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese welcomed Herzog to promote unity, but many Palestinians say the visit ignores the suffering of their families in Gaza. They are calling for protests, weapon‑export bans and a boycott of Israel.
