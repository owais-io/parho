Australian duo Olivia Gadecki and John Peers beat France's Kristina Mladenovic and Manuel Guinard to win the Australian Open mixed doubles title again. They played a 93‑minute match that ended 4‑6, 6‑3, 10‑8 in a match tiebreak. Gadecki made key points in the tiebreak, while Peers hit a winner to secure the match. This is the first team to defend the trophy since 1989, and they earned almost $200,000.
