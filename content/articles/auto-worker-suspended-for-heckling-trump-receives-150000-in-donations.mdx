At a Ford plant in Michigan, worker TJ Sabula shouted insults at President Donald Trump during a tour and was then suspended. A GoFundMe for his lost wages hit $150,000, with donations from fans, the band Dropkick Murphys and others. The United Auto Workers union says Sabula’s rights are protected and will fight the suspension. Trump’s team says the president reacted “appropriately.” The fundraiser helps keep the worker’s family afloat while the case is pending.
