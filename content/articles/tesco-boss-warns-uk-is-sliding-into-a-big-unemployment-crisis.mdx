Tesco UK boss Ashwin Prasad says the country is slipping into a serious unemployment problem. Unemployment is at a four‑year high of 5.1% and over 9 million people are not working or looking for work. He blamed government policy, rising benefits costs and business taxes. Prasad urged the government and companies to act fast to get more people into jobs, especially young workers.
