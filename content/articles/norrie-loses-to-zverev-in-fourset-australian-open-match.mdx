Cameron Norrie tried to play aggressively from the start, but German star Alexander Zverev’s stronger serve and speed beat him 7‑5, 4‑6, 6‑3, 6‑1 at the Australian Open. The match showed Norrie’s solid baseline game, but he couldn’t overcome Zverev’s power in a four‑set fight. It was another loss for the Briton against top players, though he still showed grit and good form.
