Trump said the path to peace in Ukraine is still unclear after talks with Putin that didn’t break a deadlock. U.S. envoys, along with Jared Kushner, will meet Ukrainian negotiator Rustem Umerov in Florida. The main sticking point is four occupied regions; Ukraine won’t give up the land it lost. While Russia pushes on the front, Ukraine faces a corruption scandal. The U.S. and EU keep supporting Ukraine, and the UN called for the return of children taken by Russia.
