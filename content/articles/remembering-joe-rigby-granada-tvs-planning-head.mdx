Joe Rigby, a former Granada TV executive, died at 87. Born in Birmingham, he left school early and joined the army’s radio service, which sparked his media career. He rose through Granada’s ranks, becoming head of programme planning and hiring future TV stars like Andy Harries and David Liddiment. Rigby championed Granada’s northern identity and later lived in Spain before returning to the UK, where he was active in his church. He is survived by his wife, children, and grandchildren.
