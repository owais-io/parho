A Nigerian cargo plane had to land unexpectedly in Bobo Dioulasso, Burkina Faso. Eleven Nigerian soldiers were on the aircraft, and now there’s confusion about where they are. Burkina said the troops were freed and could return home, but Nigerian officials say the situation isn’t settled yet. The Nigerian embassy is working with Burkina authorities to get the soldiers back. The incident added to tensions after Nigeria helped stop a coup in nearby Benin.
