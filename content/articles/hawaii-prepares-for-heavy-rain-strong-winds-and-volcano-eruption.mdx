Hawaii is bracing for a big Kona storm that will bring heavy rain, strong winds, and possible flooding across the islands. The governor has issued an emergency proclamation and closed parks, urging residents to stay safe on roads and plan evacuation routes and shelters. A flood watch remains until Saturday. Meanwhile, Kilauea volcano erupted, sending ash and lava fountains that closed nearby roads.
