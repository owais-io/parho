Harlequins smashed the Stormers 63‑10 at the Stoop, with Nick David scoring three tries in a single match. The win puts the Quins into the Champions Cup last‑16 stage. Despite a rough patch, the team showed strong teamwork and confidence. Stormers had used a weakened squad, making the victory easier for Harlequins. The next challenge will be a home game against La Rochelle.
