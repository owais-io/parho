A campaign group says the UK Lords must act to protect women who were criminalised for abortions. In 2023 the Commons stopped criminalising women who sought abortions outside the law, but many still have records that hurt jobs and insurance. Lords are to add amendments that end police investigations and pardon past convictions. The story shares Becca’s case, showing how the record blocks her future, and urges the Lords to pass the bill.
