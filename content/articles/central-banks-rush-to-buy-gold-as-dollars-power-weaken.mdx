Central banks around the world are buying large amounts of gold because they’re worried the U.S. dollar isn’t as reliable as it used to be. The price of gold has hit record highs, so countries are moving their gold back into their own vaults and cutting their stock of dollars. This shift is driven by political tension, especially the war in Ukraine and Trump’s policy changes, making gold the safest backup.
