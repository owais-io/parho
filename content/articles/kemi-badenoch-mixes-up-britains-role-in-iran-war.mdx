Britain’s interest in the Iran conflict is unclear as opposition MP Kemi Badenoch mixes up her stance. She first urged Britain to join the US‑Israeli campaign, then said she only wanted British forces to hit targets inside Iran, confusing the public. Her flip‑flop shows the right‑wing party’s lack of clear thinking on foreign policy and its tendency to follow the US instead of its own interests.
