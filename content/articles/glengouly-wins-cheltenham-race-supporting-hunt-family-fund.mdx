Glengouly, a 33‑to‑1 outsider, won the December Gold Cup at Cheltenham. The race backs the Hunt Family Fund, created by BBC commentator John Hunt and his daughter after the murder of Hunt’s wife and daughters, to fight violence against women. The winners’ owners donated £10,000 of the prize money to the cause. Faye Bramley, a new trainer, celebrated her first Cheltenham win with just her fourth runner.
