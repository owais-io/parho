Since taking over Real Sociedad on Dec. 20, American coach Pellegrino Matarazzo has turned the team around, moving them from 16th to 8th in La Liga. In six league games he won four, including a shock 3‑1 win over Barcelona. He’s the first U.S. manager in Spain’s top division, brings a maths‑based tactical mind, and has quickly learned Spanish to connect with players.
