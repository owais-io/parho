David Howman, former WADA chief, told a conference that many elite athletes still cheat with drugs and that the current anti‑doping system is not strong enough. He said that education programs help but don’t stop the biggest rule‑breakers. He wants more aggressive testing, better transparency, and a focus on actually catching cheats instead of just following the rules. The Athletics Integrity Unit has been good but still needs to step up.
