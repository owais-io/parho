Pat McFadden, a senior UK minister, said the UK is not obliged to meet every request from US President Trump, who wants allies to send ships to the Strait of Hormuz to counter Iran. Instead, the UK will consider sending minesweeping drones and focus on defensive actions that fit international law. McFadden stressed that the UK‑US friendship remains strong, even if they disagree on specific actions.
