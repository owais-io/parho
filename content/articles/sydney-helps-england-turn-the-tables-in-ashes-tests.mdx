In Sydney, England has often surprised Australia, turning the tide of Ashes series. From a 1894 win after a follow‑on to 1954’s 3‑1 series win with Frank Tyson, and a 1987 comeback that started the Australian dynasty. In 2003, England’s strong play in Sydney helped build confidence that led to the 2005 series win. Sydney’s pitches and moments give England hope for future Ashes.
