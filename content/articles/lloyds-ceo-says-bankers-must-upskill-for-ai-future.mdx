Lloyds boss Charlie Nunn said banks need to train staff for AI. He said AI will change how customers use banking, and the bank will hire people with new skills. Nunn said some jobs may be cut but new roles will appear. He highlighted a £50m AI boost last year and expected it to double. He added that Lloyds made a 12% profit rise, boosting dividends and a buyback.
