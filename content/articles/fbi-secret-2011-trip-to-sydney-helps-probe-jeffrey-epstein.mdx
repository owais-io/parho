The FBI secretly flew to Sydney in 2011 to interview a woman thought to be Virginia Giuffre, the high‑profile Epstein accuser. Australian police helped deliver a victim letter and the agents collected photos of Giuffre with Prince Andrew and Ghislaine Maxwell. Documents also show Epstein emailing Australian women about jobs and hiring a personal assistant. The files reveal how Australia was linked to the U.S. probe.
