Deirdre “Cookie” Sibly, 67, from Port Lincoln, was sailing from Reunion Island to South Africa on the yacht Acteon with friend Pascal Mahe. In the Mozambique Channel they sent a distress call, but the yacht was later found adrift with two bodies on board. Australian authorities are helping her family, who may need to travel to Africa to identify the remains. The incident is being investigated by Australian, French, South African and Mauritius officials.
