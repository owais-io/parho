Scientists say 2025 is likely to become the UK's hottest year ever, based on current temperature trends. The Met Office tracks temperatures above the 2022 record, but a cold winter will still influence the final tally. If confirmed, 2025 would be only the second year since records began with an average above 10°C. The trend shows record heat every few years, highlighting rapid climate change.
