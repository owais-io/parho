King Charles used his Christmas speech to call for calm and togetherness after a year of growing division. He reminded everyone of the teamwork and courage of people in World War II, and said those qualities should guide us now. The king praised those who defended others in recent attacks, urged kindness to neighbours, and suggested a holiday pause from screens to strengthen family, faith and community. He also highlighted the spiritual journey in the Christmas story.
