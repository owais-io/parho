Leicester City were hit with a six‑point penalty after an independent panel found the club broke Premier League financial rules over the last three seasons. The drop puts them just above the relegation line in the Championship, tied on points with West Brom and Blackburn. The club says the punishment is too harsh and is planning an appeal. They also have a new interim coach and are looking for a technical director and chief executive.
