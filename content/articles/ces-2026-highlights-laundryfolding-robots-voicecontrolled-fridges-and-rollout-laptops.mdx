This week’s CES in Las Vegas unveiled a wave of gadgets that could soon live in our homes. Several robots—like LG’s CLOiD and Switchbot’s Onero H1—promise to fold laundry, cook and clean. New robot vacuums can even climb stairs, while Samsung’s Family Hub fridge opens its doors by voice. Tech makers also showed a 16‑inch laptop that unrolls into a 24‑inch screen, and Samsung introduced a three‑fold phone and a revived BlackBerry with a real keyboard.
