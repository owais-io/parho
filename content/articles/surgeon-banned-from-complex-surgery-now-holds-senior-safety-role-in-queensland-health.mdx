A cardiothoracic surgeon who was once barred from performing major heart and lung operations in New South Wales has just been hired as the first surgical lead for Queensland Health’s safety unit. Dr Michael Byrom’s past restrictions were lifted after he finished extra training, and Queensland Health says it did a full background check. The state premier has asked an investigation into the appointment, saying it wants to make sure the move is safe.
