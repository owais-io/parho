Three Democratic senators—Warren, Van Hollen and Blumenthal—have written to Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta and data‑center operators asking if the huge power use of their facilities is pushing up household electricity costs. They want companies to share the cost, explain future plans, and show how they keep bills from rising. The senators also raised concerns about environmental impacts and secret contracts that hide the true cost of power.
