Trump warned Republican lawmakers that any of them who helped roll back his tariffs on Canada would face serious consequences in the next election. Despite the threat, six Republicans joined Democrats in a mostly symbolic vote to reject the national emergency that gave the president the power to impose the tariffs. The move will go to the Senate, but Trump would still need to sign to reverse the tariffs.
