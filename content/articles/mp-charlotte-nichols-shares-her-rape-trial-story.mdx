Charlotte Nichols, Labour MP for Warrington North, finally spoke out in Parliament about the rape she suffered in 2021. After more than 1,000 days waiting, the man who assaulted her was acquitted. Nichols details the abuse, the spread of intimate photos, and her own mental health struggle. She now pushes for specialist rape courts and a trauma‑informed justice system, receiving £11,000 in compensation from CICA.
