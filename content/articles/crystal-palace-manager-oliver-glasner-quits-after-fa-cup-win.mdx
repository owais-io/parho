After winning the FA Cup, Crystal Palace boss Oliver Glasner has confirmed he will leave the club when the season ends. He said the decision was long‑planned and not due to lack of money. Palace are also losing key players like Marc Guéhi and have had a tough week after a defeat by non‑league Macclesfield. The club will need a new manager and more signings to stay competitive.
