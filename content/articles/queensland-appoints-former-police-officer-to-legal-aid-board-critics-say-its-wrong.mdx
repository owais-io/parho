Queensland’s government added a former police sergeant to the Legal Aid board. Many, including a lawyer who represented the family of a man who died in custody, say the man had a bad record. He was linked to investigations that caused the death and was later found to have made dishonest statements. Critics say putting him on the board hurts trust, especially for Indigenous people who rely on legal aid.
