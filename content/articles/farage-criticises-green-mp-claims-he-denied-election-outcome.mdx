Nigel Farage, leader of the Reform Party, criticised the newly elected Green MP Hannah Spencer after the Manchester by‑election. He said the Reform candidate won among British‑born voters and accused the Green party of ‘family voting’. Spencer called his remarks racist and compared him to Donald Trump for denying election results. Reform plans to limit voting rights to British citizens, dropping Commonwealth voters, while the Green MP defended her win as fair and inclusive.
