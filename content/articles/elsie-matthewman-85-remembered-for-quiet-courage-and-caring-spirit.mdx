Elsie Matthewman (85) was a devoted mother, wife, and community helper. After raising her family, she helped parents of babies who had trouble sleeping by co‑founding Cry‑sis, a charity that now offers nationwide support. Born in Sheffield during WWII, she worked in a local factory, loved dancing, and was known for her red lipstick. Even after her husband's death, she stayed active, using tech and traveling with friends.
