Prince Harry and Meghan Markle are fighting back against claims made by author Tom Bower in his new book. Bower says the Queen and the couple were threatened and that Meghan was a danger to Harry. The couple say the book is baseless and written by a “deranged” writer who loves drama. They want readers to ignore the book and focus on facts.
