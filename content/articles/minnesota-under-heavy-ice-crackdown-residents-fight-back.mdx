A resident in Minnesota reports how the Trump administration’s aggressive ICE raids have turned the state into a battlefield. Two U.S. citizens were killed by federal agents, and thousands of people—many without crimes—were detained or deported. The community has rallied, setting up mutual aid, protests, and support for those affected. Residents describe fear, anger, and a determination to resist what they see as politically motivated violence.
