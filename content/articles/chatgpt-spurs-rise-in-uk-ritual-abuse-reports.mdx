UK experts say ChatGPT is helping people find help for what they call ‘satanic’ ritual abuse, and that has led to more reports. Police and charities note that these cases—often involving families, traffickers or online gangs—are still under‑reported. New training and specialist groups are being set up to spot and investigate such abuse. The rise shows the role of AI in connecting survivors to support.
