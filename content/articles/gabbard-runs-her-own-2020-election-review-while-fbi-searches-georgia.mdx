Tulsi Gabbard, the director of national intelligence, is conducting her own review of the 2020 election after President Trump asked her to. She has been briefing Trump and the White House on possible voting‑machine weaknesses and foreign interference. Last week Gabbard was at an FBI raid in Fulton County, Georgia, where agents seized election data. Democrats and intelligence officials criticized her presence, saying the FBI investigation is separate from her review.
