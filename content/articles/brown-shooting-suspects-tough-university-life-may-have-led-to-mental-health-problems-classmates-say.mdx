Investigators are looking into why a Brown University shooting killed two students and an MIT professor. Former classmates of the suspected gunman, Cláudio Valente, and one victim, Nuno Loureiro, say that the tough, competitive environment at Portugal’s University of Lisbon may have damaged Valente’s mental health. Valente, a top student, left Brown after a few months and later returned to Portugal. Classmates warn that high pressure and lack of support in academia can lead to tragedy.
