Former England striker and TV pundit Eni Aluko has taken a strong jab at fellow commentator Ian Wright, saying he and other men are grabbing the limited broadcasting slots for women’s football. She claims that men are blocking her and her teammate from high‑profile shows, even though they have more caps than Wright. Aluko’s comments have sparked backlash, with many calling her attacks unfair and self‑serving.
