After flying in a Blue Origin craft that included pop star Katy Perry and others, Vietnamese‑American astronaut Amanda Nguyen faced an unexpected wave of online harassment. She said the attacks were sexist and made her feel depressed, even for weeks. Nguyen, who is also a survivor‑rights activist, said the negativity almost broke her. She has since gotten support and feels the weight of the backlash easing.
