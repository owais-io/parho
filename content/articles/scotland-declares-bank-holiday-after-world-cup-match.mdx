Scotland’s football team will play in the 2026 World Cup for the first time since 1998. The first minister, John Swinney, said that Monday 15 June will be a national bank holiday so that everyone can celebrate, no matter if Scotland wins or loses. The holiday will give public‑sector workers a day off, but other employers can decide. The idea lets fans celebrate the team’s historic achievement and boosts tourism and business.
