The letter says Labour’s poll slump is because its leader, Keir Starmer, isn’t clear on ideas and keeps changing plans. The writer thinks the party needs a new, more practical leader who can set a clear mission, unite with other left parties, and act against the far‑right. He also says Labour must address its stance on Gaza and Israel to win back voters, especially Muslim voters.
