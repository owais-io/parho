The Guardian discovered that firms selling unlicensed weight‑loss drugs such as retatrutide are posting glowing reviews on Trustpilot, earning 4‑5 star ratings. These drugs are still experimental, not approved in the UK, and can cause serious health issues. Researchers say regulators need stronger safeguards, and Trustpilot has begun blocking these companies. The story shows how easily dangerous medicines can be bought online.
