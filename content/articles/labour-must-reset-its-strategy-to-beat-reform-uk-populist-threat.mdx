Former Labour ad strategist Sir Chris Powell says the party needs a full reset to beat the threat from populist Reform UK. He warns that Prime Minister Keir Starmer lacks a clear plan and that without new messaging, branding, policies and better voter outreach, Reform UK could win voters’ trust and threaten democracy. Labour’s low poll numbers and internal discontent make a fresh strategy urgent.
