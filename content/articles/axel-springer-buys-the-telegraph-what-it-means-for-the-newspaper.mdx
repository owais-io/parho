The Telegraph, Britain's historic newspaper, has been bought by German media giant Axel Springer. Staff welcomed the deal, hoping for a fresh start. The takeover followed a long fight and shows how big news brands still attract foreign buyers. Springer aims to boost digital, AI and keep the paper's conservative tone. The move may affect UK media and political influence.
