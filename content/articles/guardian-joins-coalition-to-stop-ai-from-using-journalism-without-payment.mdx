The Guardian has joined a group of UK media outlets that wants AI companies to pay for using news articles. The group, called Spur, includes the BBC, FT, Sky News and Telegraph. They say AI chatbots are trained on many articles without permission or payment, hurting journalists’ income. The coalition wants a global licence system that lets AI companies use content only if they pay and respect copyright, and it will create tools to protect intellectual property.
