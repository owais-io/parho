Journalist Lucia Osborne‑Crowley travelled from London to interview survivors of the Jeffrey Epstein‑Ghislaine Maxwell scandal. While writing her book “The Lasting Harm,” she faced threats, stalking and hostile emails from people tied to Maxwell’s circle. The article covers her past abuse, the pain of covering a case where powerful men used influence to groom victims, and survivors’ push for more transparency and justice.
