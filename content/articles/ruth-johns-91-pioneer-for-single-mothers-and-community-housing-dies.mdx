Ruth Johns, who died at 91, was a journalist and social activist who helped single mothers find homes. In the 1960s she started a playgroup at her house, which grew into the Family First Trust – a community housing scheme that bought and converted houses for young mothers. She also wrote books about her work, led national community groups, and campaigned for local voices. Ruth was survived by her children, grandchildren and great‑grandchildren.
