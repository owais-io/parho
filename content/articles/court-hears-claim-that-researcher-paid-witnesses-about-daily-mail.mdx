Researcher Graham Johnson, who has studied press wrongdoing, says he paid private detectives and former journalists for their testimony about illegal activities at the Daily Mail publisher. He claims he never paid for evidence used in court, but has spent more than £100,000 on contacts and writers. The payments came from a Max Mosley estate company and a loan from former MP Evan Harris. The publisher denies all accusations.
