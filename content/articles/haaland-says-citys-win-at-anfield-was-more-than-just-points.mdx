Erling Haaland told fans that Manchester City’s 2‑1 victory over Liverpool at Anfield was more than just three points. The win, his first at the stadium, lifted morale after a poor run and showed the team’s fighting spirit. He said the result sent a message to rivals, especially Arsenal, and impressed supporters with a late penalty. City look to build confidence ahead of upcoming games.
