A 90‑year‑old woman was left without her original phone number for three months after BT mishandled a transfer. The mistake kept her disconnected from family, doctors, and a vital personal‑alarm service. After repeated calls, BT finally restored the number and offered compensation. The case shows how slow telecom processes can endanger elderly customers and highlights the importance of quick, reliable customer support.
