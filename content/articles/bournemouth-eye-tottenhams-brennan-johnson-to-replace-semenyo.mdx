Bournemouth need a new winger because Antoine Semenyo is about to leave for Manchester City. City are ready to activate his release clause, so Bournemouth is looking for a replacement. The club is considering Tottenham’s Brennan Johnson, who hasn't played much this season but scored a lot last year and hit a winning goal in the Europa League final. Other teams like Crystal Palace also want Johnson, and Tottenham may sell or loan him.
