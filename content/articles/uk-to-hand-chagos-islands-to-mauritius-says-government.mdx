The UK will hand the Chagos Islands back to Mauritius, even though US President Trump called it stupid. The islands include Diego Garcia, where a big U.S. military base sits. The UK says it will keep the base running for 99 years under a lease. Trump’s comments add pressure, but the government insists the deal is legal and backed by the U.S. The plan faces opposition in the UK Parliament.
