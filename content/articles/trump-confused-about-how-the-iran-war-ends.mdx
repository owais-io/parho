Trump gave a mixed‑up press conference about the US fight in Iran. He said the war was “very complete” yet also “the beginning,” and talked about a possible drawdown but didn’t give clear next steps. He tried to calm markets by hinting at easing sanctions, but also mentioned Iran’s supposed use of Tomahawk missiles. The comments left reporters and Democrats puzzled, calling his plan incoherent.
