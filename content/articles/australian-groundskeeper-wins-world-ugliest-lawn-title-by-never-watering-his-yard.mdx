Jarno Coone, a groundskeeper in Kyneton, Victoria, let his front yard grow wild without watering it. His untamed lawn was named the World’s Ugliest Lawn in 2025. The contest, started in Sweden to promote water saving, rewards the most neglected lawns. Coone, who also manages 1,100 acres of school grounds, enjoys the wildlife that thrives in his overgrown yard and sees the prize as a reminder to live with nature.
