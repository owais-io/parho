Chelsea manager Liam Rosenior has made it clear that Cole Palmer is happy and will not leave for Manchester United. He said the striker loves playing at the Blues and there’s no need to worry about a move. Chelsea still need a win against Napoli in their final Champions League group game to keep a top‑eight spot. The club is also looking to let Raheem Sterling go before the window closes.
