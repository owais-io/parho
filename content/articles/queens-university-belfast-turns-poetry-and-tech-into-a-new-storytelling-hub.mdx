Queen’s University Belfast has opened two new creative hubs – the Seamus Heaney Centre for poetry and writing, and MediaLab, a tech lab that makes games, films and virtual reality. The centres let writers, poets, actors and tech experts work side‑by‑side. Students take workshops, masterclasses and get hands‑on experience with motion‑capture and LED‑backdrop filming. The partnership shows how old‑school storytelling can mix with modern tech to create fresh, immersive stories.
