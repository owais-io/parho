A Russian tanker loaded 730,000 barrels of oil in March and is on its way to Cuba, which is facing power cuts because of a US blockade. Trump says he will take over Cuba and has stopped oil shipments from Mexico and Venezuela. The ship, under sanctions, is expected to unload at Matanzas on March 23. Another vessel carrying diesel is also sailing near Cuba.
