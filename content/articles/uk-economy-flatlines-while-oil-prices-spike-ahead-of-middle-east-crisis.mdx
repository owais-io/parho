The latest UK data shows no growth in GDP for January, with the economy basically flatlining. Services grew a little, but construction and job‑related services fell sharply. A cyber‑attack helped lift manufacturing, but that’s a one‑off. Oil prices are climbing again because of tensions in the Middle East, which will push up fuel and utility costs. The Bank of England is expected to keep interest rates steady, so the government’s plans to boost growth face doubt.
