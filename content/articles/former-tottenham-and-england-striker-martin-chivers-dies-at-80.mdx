Former Tottenham and England striker Martin Chivers has died at 80. The 1946‑born forward played for Southampton, Tottenham and a few other clubs, winning the League Cup and UEFA Cup with Spurs and scoring 13 goals in 24 England games. He is remembered for smooth play and strong aerial ability. Tottenham, Southampton and former teammates, including Steve Archibald, paid tribute and the club will wear black armbands.
