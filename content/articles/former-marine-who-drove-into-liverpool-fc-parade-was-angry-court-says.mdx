A former Royal Marine named Paul Doyle drove his Ford Galaxy into a Liverpool FC victory parade, hitting over 130 people, many needing hospital care. Court witnesses saw the car’s dash‑cam footage of him screaming and speeding through the crowd. The attacker later pleaded guilty to 31 charges. He is expected to receive more than 10 years in prison. Victims suffered serious injuries and lasting trauma, while a police officer stopped the car by grabbing the gear.
