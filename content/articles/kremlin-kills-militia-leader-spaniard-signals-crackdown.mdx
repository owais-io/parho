Russian militia leader Stanislav Orlov, known as Spaniard, died under mysterious circumstances. Rumors say he was shot by Moscow security forces in Crimea, not in Ukraine. His funeral in Moscow’s Cathedral was a big event, but no official death cause was given. The killing shows the Kremlin is cracking down on rogue nationalist groups after the Wagner mutiny. The state wants to warn others not to act independently.
