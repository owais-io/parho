Peter Mandelson’s files released by the government show he fought for a bigger pay‑out after being named UK ambassador to Washington. He asked for £547,000 instead of the £75,000 already offered, claiming the job ended his career. The papers also confirm that Starmer was aware of Mandelson’s past links to Jeffrey Epstein and that the vetting process was rushed and had gaps. No 10 has asked police to keep part of the records.
