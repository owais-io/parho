The article says that online harassment of women, especially journalists, is increasing and is linked to real‑world violence. A UN Women report surveyed women in 119 countries and found online abuse is rising, and that offline attacks have more than doubled for female journalists over five years. AI tools like deepfakes make it easier to spread false attacks. The cycle of online and offline violence fuels fear and impunity. Action is needed.
