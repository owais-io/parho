Nottingham Forest’s fans were furious after the team lost badly in Braga, missing a striker and making many errors. The loss, a shock for a club that had high hopes under manager Sean Dyche, highlighted squad fatigue and lack of depth. Dyche blamed the players and said he wants more than “OK” results, stressing the tight schedule and the need for a stronger attack.
