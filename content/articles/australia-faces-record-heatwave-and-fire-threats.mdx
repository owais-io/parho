South Australia is in a brutal heatwave, with Adelaide reaching 42 °C and some towns near 50 °C. In Victoria, officials warn that tomorrow could see record heat, and people are urged to stay indoors and stay hydrated. Fires are burning, forcing evacuations of over 1,100 homes near the Otways, and fire bans aim to stop new outbreaks. Health officials advise watching for heat‑stroke symptoms and staying safe.
