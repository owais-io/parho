Manchester City beat Real Madrid at the Bernabéu, turning a 1‑0 deficit into a 2‑1 win thanks to Nico O’Reilly’s header and Erling Haaland’s penalty. The game started badly for City, with a first‑minute mistake and a penalty that was overturned by VAR. Manager Pep Guardiola said the team lost control in the early stages and that their young players need more experience, but praised their fighting spirit.
