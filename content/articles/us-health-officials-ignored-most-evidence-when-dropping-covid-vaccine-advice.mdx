Internal memos released after a lawsuit show that U.S. health officials almost ignored all the research when they decided to stop recommending COVID‑19 vaccines for pregnant people and children. The memos cited only a handful of studies and missed evidence that shows the vaccines are safe and help protect against severe illness. Critics say the decision was driven by politics, not science, and it puts mothers and infants at higher risk.
