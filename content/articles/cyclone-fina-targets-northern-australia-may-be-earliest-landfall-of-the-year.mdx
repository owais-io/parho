Cyclone Fina could be the first storm in Australia to hit the coast in November, aiming for the Northern Territory. The storm, about 370 km north‑east of Darwin, might reach the coast Friday or Saturday and could intensify to a category‑two. Experts say it could match the record set by Cyclone Ines in 1973. Climate change is making storms stronger and faster, so keep an eye on updates.
