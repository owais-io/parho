Last week a Minneapolis Star Tribune photographer captured a border‑patrol agent spraying pepper‑spray at a protester. An antifa group, Pacific Antifascist Research Collective, identified the agent, Tyler Gramlin, and put up flyers calling him a “kidnapper/terrorist.” The group is part of a larger effort to name ICE officers, who many say act like secret police. The move comes amid growing criticism of ICE’s treatment of detainees and calls to end mask‑wearing.
