At a Virginia liquor shop, a raccoon broke in, smashed bottles, and drank the alcohol. It fell asleep in the bathroom, was found by a staff member, and animal control said it was clearly drunk. The raccoon had no injuries, slept a few hours, and was released back into the wild. Raccoons are becoming more urban and adaptable, and similar stories of animals drinking alcohol have popped up around the world.
