France’s health ministry will mail letters to 29‑year‑olds telling them it’s time to start a family. The column says the plan is unnecessary and unfair, especially to women, and it turns people into pawns in a political fight over birth rates, pensions and immigration. The author argues that France should focus on better support for parents, not on nagging young adults.
