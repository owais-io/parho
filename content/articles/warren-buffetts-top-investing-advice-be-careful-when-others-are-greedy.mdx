Warren Buffett, who will retire at the end of 2025, has long shared his investing ideas in letters to Berkshire shareholders. He says the best way to grow wealth is to buy good businesses at reasonable prices, usually with cash, and keep a mix of whole companies and shares of great ones. Buffett warns to be cautious when others are greedy and to avoid risky derivatives. He values the cash cushion from insurance, trusts strong managers, and has a succession plan.
