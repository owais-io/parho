Howard Lutnick, the US commerce secretary, said he will sit for a House panel investigating Jeffrey Epstein’s criminal network. He has been asked to explain his 2012 trip to Epstein’s island and a photo that appeared and disappeared online. Democrats said they could subpoena him if he refuses. Lutnick says he did not do anything wrong, and no charges have been filed.
