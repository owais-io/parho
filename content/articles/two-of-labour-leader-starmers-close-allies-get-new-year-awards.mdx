Two of Keir Starmer’s close allies, former development minister Anneliese Dodds and former shadow health secretary Jonathan Ashworth, have been honoured in the new year list. Dodds was made a dame, Ashworth a CBE, recognising their political work and charity efforts. Dodds resigned last year over aid cuts, while Ashworth lost his seat but still led the Labour Together think‑tank. Other awards went to former Black Rod Sarah Clarke, ex‑MP Adam Ingram, and Chris Ruane.
