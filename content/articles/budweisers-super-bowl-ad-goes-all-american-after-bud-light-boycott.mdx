Budweiser’s new Super Bowl ad, “American Icons,” replaces political themes with pure American symbols. A Clydesdale horse and a baby bird grow up together, turning into a bald eagle that soars over the Lincoln Memorial while the music of Lynyrd Skynyrd plays. The campaign follows a boycott of Bud Light that hurt sales and pushed Modelo ahead in the market. By leaning on animal friendship, nostalgic music and patriotic imagery, Budweiser hopes to win back fans.
