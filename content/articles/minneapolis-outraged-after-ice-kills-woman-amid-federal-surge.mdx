ICE agents have moved into Minneapolis in large numbers, and a 37‑year‑old American citizen, Renee Good, was shot and killed by an ICE agent. The shooting sparked angry protests and fear in the city. Local activists and volunteers keep watch, record ICE movements, and help people who get taken. The incident has raised concerns about safety, jobs, and whether the federal surge will stay.
