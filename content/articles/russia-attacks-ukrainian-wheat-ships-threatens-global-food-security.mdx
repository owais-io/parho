Russia launched drones at Ukrainian wheat ships and oil tanks, threatening global food security. Two civilian vessels, Emmakris III and Captain Karam, were hit while loading wheat. Ukraine says these are war crimes. In response, Ukrainian drones hit Russian ports like Tuapse, damaging infrastructure, and even parts of Moscow, causing an injury. President Zelenskyy says the alleged attack on Putin's home is fake. He plans a summit with allies in France.
