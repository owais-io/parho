Reform UK says it will give child rapists life‑in‑prison sentences that can’t be shortened if it wins the next election. The party wants mandatory whole‑life orders for anyone convicted of child rape, especially in grooming gangs. They note the current law lets judges hand out life sentences, but sometimes sentences are too short. The plan also talks about freeing up prison space by deporting foreign criminals. Support hotlines are listed for victims.
