Donald Trump announced he will sue the BBC over a misleading edit of his 2021 Capitol riot speech. Even though the broadcaster apologized, Trump says he still plans to take legal action and may seek $1 billion in damages. The BBC has pulled the offending Panorama episode from repeat broadcasts. Trump said he’d call UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer this weekend to discuss the issue.
