In November 2023, 34‑year‑old Hodan Diiriye took in a 14‑year‑old orphan named Saabirin. Weeks later, the girl died after being strangled. Police found videos that showed abuse. Diiriye was arrested, tried in a rushed court and found guilty of murder. In February 2024 a firing squad executed her. Human‑rights groups say she didn’t get a fair trial and the case shows how women can be treated unfairly in Somalia’s justice system.
