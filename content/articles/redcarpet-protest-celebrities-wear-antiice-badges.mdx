The red carpet is turning into a protest stage, with stars at the Grammys and Golden Globes wearing anti‑ICE pins. The pins, slogans like “ICE out” and “Abolish ICE,” are made by artists and sold on Etsy and TikTok. The trend is part of a wider craft‑activism movement, with knit hats, T‑shirts and nail art encouraging people to speak up about immigration enforcement. It shows how everyday crafts can spark social change.
