An unknown explosion hit the Le Constellation bar in the ski resort town of Crans‑Montana at 1:30 am on New Year’s Eve. Police say “several dozen” people died and roughly 100 were hurt, many with severe burns. The area is closed, a no‑fly zone is in place, and injured patients are being moved to hospitals across the region. Authorities say the blast was not a terrorist attack.
