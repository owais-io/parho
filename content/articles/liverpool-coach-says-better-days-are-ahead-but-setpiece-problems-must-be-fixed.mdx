Liverpool coach Arne Slot says the team can be better but still has to fix set‑piece problems that caused many losses. After a tough stretch of nine defeats in 12 games, Liverpool have climbed to fifth place. Slot says the team is still below expectations and that luck played a role in recent draws. He believes the players are getting fitter and that a stronger focus on set pieces will help the club improve.
