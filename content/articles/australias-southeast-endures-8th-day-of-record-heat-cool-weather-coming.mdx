For the eighth straight day, parts of south‑eastern Australia have seen scorching temperatures above 40 °C, with some places hitting near‑record highs of 50 °C. The heat is set to fade after a cool change expected over the weekend, bringing a 15‑°C drop in many areas. The warmer spell has sparked bushfires and made the region vulnerable, but a monsoon low should bring relief and some rain.
