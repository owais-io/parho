After rescuing two hikers from England’s highest mountain, a volunteer rescue team has asked them to pay a £130 hotel bill, return two head torches they took, and a hospital crutch left in the team’s vehicle. The men were treated kindly by a local innkeeper, who offered discounted lodging and snacks, but the bill remains unpaid. The team thanks donations that helped cover the cost.
