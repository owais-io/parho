England’s coach Steve Borthwick says injured backline players may be ready for next week’s Calcutta Cup against Scotland. Ollie Lawrence could return from a knee injury, while Fin Smith and Tom Roebuck are also in the mix. Other backs like George Furbank have come back from injury. A loose‑head prop, Fin Baxter, will miss the tournament after surgery, and Ben Curry is still healing. The squad hopes to strengthen its backline for the game.
