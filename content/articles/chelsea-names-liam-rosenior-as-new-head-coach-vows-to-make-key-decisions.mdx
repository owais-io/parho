Rosenior is ready to lead Chelsea. After Enzo Maresca left, the club hired the 41‑year‑old from Strasbourg on a six‑and‑a‑half‑year deal. He says he will make key decisions but will work with the club’s hierarchy. He wants to build a young, energetic squad and hopes fans judge him by results, not his name. Chelsea fans and ownership are still wary, and the club faces a FA charge over a bottle incident.
