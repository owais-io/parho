Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem said Venezuelans who were living in the U.S. under Temporary Protected Status (TPS) can now apply for refugee status. She said the Trump administration’s decision to revoke TPS was a “national interest” move and that the U.S. wants a new Venezuelan leader who will fight crime and keep the country safe. Many Venezuelans in Florida are still fighting to keep their legal status after the revocation.
