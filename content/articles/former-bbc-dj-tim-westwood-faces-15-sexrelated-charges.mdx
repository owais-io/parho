Former BBC DJ Tim Westwood is accused of 15 criminal acts, including rape, sexual assault and indecent assault, that allegedly occurred over 33 years from the 1980s to 2016. The claims involve seven women and places such as BBC studios, hotels and nightclubs. He pleaded not guilty in London court and was granted bail until his trial, set for January 2027. Westwood denies all the allegations.
