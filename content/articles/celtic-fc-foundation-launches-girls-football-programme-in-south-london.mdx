Celtic FC Foundation has set up a new girls’ football program in Brixton, London, even in heavy rain. Four local teams joined coaches from the foundation to give girls from poor backgrounds a chance to play. It’s part of the club’s 125th‑anniversary charity work, which also runs projects like Breaking Barriers for refugees. The program shows Celtic’s long‑standing focus on community and social change.
