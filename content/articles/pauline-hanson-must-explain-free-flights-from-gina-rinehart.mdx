Australian Senator Pauline Hanson has not fully disclosed several free flights she received from mining billionaire Gina Rinehart’s companies. The flights, taken last year for campaign events and meetings, were worth more than the $300 threshold that requires Senate reporting. After the Guardian raised the issue, Hanson updated her register but still didn’t explain why she missed the earlier declaration. The case highlights possible breaches of Senate rules.
