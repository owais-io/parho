At a White House holiday party, President Trump said his domestic‑policy chief, Vince Haley, will focus on building a big arch in Washington, DC—like France’s Arc de Triomphe. He praised Haley’s enthusiasm for the project. Trump didn’t mention the rising cost of living, health‑insurance hikes, or tariffs that worry many Americans. The news was shared on social media, sparking criticism that a monument should not be the top priority.
