A Labor senator from Victoria has publicly urged Prime Minister Albanese to set up a royal commission to investigate the Bondi terror attack and the rise of antisemitism in Australia. This move adds pressure on the government, which has been consulting Jewish groups about a possible inquiry. Some Jewish leaders have expressed concerns over a suggested commissioner, former High Court justice Virginia Bell, and call for a panel with diverse expertise.
