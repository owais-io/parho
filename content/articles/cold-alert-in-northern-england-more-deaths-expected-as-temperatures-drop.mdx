Northern England has an amber cold health alert from 8 pm Sunday to noon Monday. The UK Health Security Agency warns that the low temperatures could raise deaths, especially for people over 65 and those with heart or lung problems. Hospitals and care homes may struggle to keep indoor temperatures at 18 °C, and staff could be short due to travel delays. The Met Office predicts frosts, fog and a brisk wind, making travel hard.
