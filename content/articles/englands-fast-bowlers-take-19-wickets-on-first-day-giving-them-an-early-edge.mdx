England’s fast bowlers took 19 wickets in the first day of an Ashes Test, including Ben Stokes’ five‑for‑23 and Mitchell Starc’s seven‑for‑58. Australia were bowled out for 172 and were 123/9 at stumps. England’s batting was shaky, but the early wickets gave them a big advantage. The day shows England’s quicks are a huge threat while Australia’s top order struggled to cope.
