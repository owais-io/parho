Norway’s kids learn to love sport first, not compete hard early. Schools play ski, families ski together, and every child gets a trophy, no matter how small. The result? 90% of Norwegian children stay active, and the country still tops the Winter Olympics. In the UK, kids drop out when sport stops being fun. A new youth‑sport strategy could bring joy, health and better athletes to Britain.
