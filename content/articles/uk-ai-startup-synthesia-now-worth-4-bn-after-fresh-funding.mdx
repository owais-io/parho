Synthesia, a London‑based AI company that turns real people into realistic video avatars, doubled its value to $4 bn after raising $200 m from Google Ventures. The firm makes avatars for business training, safety videos and corporate messaging, and already works with banks, the NHS and the UN. Though it posted a loss last year, Synthesia plans to hit $200 m in revenue this year and says the new money will help it hire more staff.
