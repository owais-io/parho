Keir Starmer says he prefers calm, private talks with President Trump over loud, public criticism. He wants a steady, practical approach to deal with US‑UK issues, even when Trump fires off attacks on the UK. Some of his ministers support this calm strategy, while others worry about Trump’s unpredictability. Meanwhile, US leaders try to keep the relationship smooth, but the public debate continues.
