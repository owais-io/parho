Israel’s army said that the brother of the man who drove a truck into a Michigan synagogue was a Hezbollah commander who handled weapons for the group. The commander was reportedly killed in an Israeli air strike last week. The attack in Michigan, where the driver later died by suicide, was linked by some to revenge for family deaths in a prior Israeli strike in Lebanon. The incident raises fears of more revenge attacks in the US amid the Middle East war.
