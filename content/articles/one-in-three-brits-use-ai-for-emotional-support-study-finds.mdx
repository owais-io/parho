A new UK study shows about one in three people turn to AI chatbots for emotional help or company. Roughly 10% chat with AI every week, and 4% daily. The report notes positive experiences but also warns after the tragic death of a teen who used ChatGPT for suicidal talks. It highlights how AI can influence opinions and is getting faster, even beating PhD‑level experts in some tasks. Safety measures are improving, but concerns remain.
