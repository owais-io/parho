Donald Trump and Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu launched a bombing campaign against Iran, claiming it was needed to stop a supposed nuclear threat. However, UN rules say force can only be used with Security Council approval or in self‑defence. The attack was therefore illegal. The piece explains why the move is wrong, the lack of evidence for an imminent threat, and how other countries reacted.
