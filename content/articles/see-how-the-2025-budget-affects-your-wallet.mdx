The Guardian offers an easy‑to‑use calculator that shows whether the new budget will make you richer or poorer. Just enter details like your income, household size and which tax brackets you fall into, and the tool adds up changes to income tax, National Insurance, child benefits and more. It tells you your net gain or loss, so you can plan ahead and see how the government’s plans impact you.
