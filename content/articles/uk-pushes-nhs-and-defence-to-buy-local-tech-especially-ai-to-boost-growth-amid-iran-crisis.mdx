The Treasury says the chancellor will push the NHS and the Ministry of Defence to buy British tech, especially AI, to boost growth during the Iran crisis. He says the government wants to be one of the fastest adopters and will create a quick‑buy taskforce outside normal procurement. The plan also includes getting closer to the EU and strengthening key UK regions.
