House Democratic leader Hakeem Jeffries said Trump is trying to steal this year's midterm elections. He warned that Democrats will block any attempts to change election rules or take over voting. Trump has sued states, claimed voter rolls are wrong, and the FBI raided Georgia’s election office. Jeffries said states should run elections safely and that no president can dictate how they happen.
