Trinity Rodman, a star U.S. soccer player, wants to earn more than the NWSL allows. European clubs can pay her over $1 million a year, but the league has a hard salary cap. The NWSL blocked her deal, sparking a union grievance. The league is testing a rule that lets teams spend a bit more on top players. The situation shows U.S. teams struggle to keep talent and compete with Europe.
