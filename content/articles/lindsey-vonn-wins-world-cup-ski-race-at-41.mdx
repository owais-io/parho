Lindsey Vonn, 41, won a World Cup downhill race in San Moritz, becoming the oldest winner in the event’s history. She’s back after five years off and a serious knee operation that required titanium implants. This is her first win in eight years and her 44th career downhill victory. The result puts her among the favorites for the 2026 Winter Olympics downhill race.
