The UK Treasury is drafting rules that will bring cryptocurrency companies under the same supervision as banks and other financial services by 2027. The FCA will set standards for transparency, consumer protection, and anti‑money‑laundering. Crypto exchanges and wallets must register and follow these rules. The plan aims to curb fraud, stop illegal activity, and ban crypto donations to politics.
