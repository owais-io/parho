The opening ceremony of the Milano‑Cortina 2026 Winter Games featured US star Mariah Carey. Organisers said her vocals were pre‑recorded, a common practice for big broadcasts, but didn’t confirm whether the pre‑recording was used. Carey was not paid for her performance. The director stressed the need for precise timing with 1,300 performers and 500 musicians. Meanwhile, the Games faced political headlines, a US vice‑president’s appearance, and a sabotage attack on Italian rail lines that delayed trains.
