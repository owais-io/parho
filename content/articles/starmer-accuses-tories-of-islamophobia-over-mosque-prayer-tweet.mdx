Keir Starmer blasted the Conservative Party after Shadow Justice Secretary Nick Timothy posted a clip of Muslims praying in Trafalgar Square, calling the act “domination.” Starmer said the Tories have a “problem with Muslims” and urged Kemi Badenoch to fire Timothy. The comments reignited debate over Islamophobia in the party. Muslim leaders and former MPs criticised the remarks, saying they make Muslims feel unwelcome and create division in Britain.
