Europe’s new biometric border system, the EES, is causing long queues and delays at airports, especially during summer. Travel groups want the EU to let borders pause or reduce checks to keep travel smooth. Airports report up to three‑hour waits and worries about even longer lines in July and August. Staff shortages and faulty kiosks add to the problem, and some countries have already stopped the system temporarily.
