Corteva, a major U.S. chemical maker, has agreed to pull Enlist Duo from the market. The herbicide mixes Agent Orange, a chemical weapon used in Vietnam, with glyphosate, a controversial weed killer linked to cancer. Environmental groups fought the product for over ten years, saying it harms people, wildlife, and promotes super‑weeds. The decision ends a long legal battle, though the company still sells other pesticides that contain 2,4‑D.
