Emma Raducanu says her first Australian Open match starts at 9 p.m. and she has only one practice session after a late flight. She calls the timing “very difficult” and worries she won’t have enough time to get used to the court. She’ll face Thai player Mananchaya Sawangkaew, who is also making a comeback. Raducanu says she’ll stay focused on her game despite the odd schedule.
