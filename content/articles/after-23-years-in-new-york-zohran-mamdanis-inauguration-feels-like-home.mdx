After living in New York for 23 years, the writer feels a deep connection to the city. She attended a Queens fundraiser for a Muslim women’s shelter and heard about Zohran Mamdani’s election. Mamdani, a young Muslim from Queens, promises rent‑free housing for millions and has become a symbol of hope for many New Yorkers. The author celebrates his inauguration as a milestone that makes the city feel like home.
