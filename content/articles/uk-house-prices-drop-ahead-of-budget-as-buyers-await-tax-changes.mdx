Before the UK budget, house prices are slipping as buyers fear tax changes. Rightmove data shows the average asking price fell 1.8%, or £6,589, to £364,833 in November – the steepest fall for the month in over a decade. About a third of homes cut prices by 7%. Experts say sellers are lowering prices to stay competitive while buyers wait for the budget. Mortgage growth is also expected to slow next year.
