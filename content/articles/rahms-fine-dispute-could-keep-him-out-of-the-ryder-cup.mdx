Jon Rahm, Spain’s star golfer, is in trouble over a huge fine from the European Tour for playing in Saudi-backed LIV Golf events. While his teammate Tyrrell Hatton and seven other players have agreed to pay and keep their tour cards, Rahm will not pay. If he loses the appeal, he could be suspended and miss the 2027 Ryder Cup, putting Europe’s chances in doubt.
