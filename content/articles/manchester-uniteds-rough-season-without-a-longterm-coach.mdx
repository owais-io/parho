Manchester United has a new, temporary manager, Michael Carrick, after months of coaching changes since Sir Alex Ferguson left. The club has been knocked out early from domestic cups, hasn’t played in Europe this year, and fans say the team feels aimless and underperforming. With only 40 games this season and little stability at the top, supporters wonder if this marks the lowest point in the club’s recent history.
