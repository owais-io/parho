Jeffrey Epstein victims are upset that the Justice Department only shared some of the documents it was supposed to release by December 19, under a new law. Lawyers and lawmakers say the DOJ has failed to give all the files and may hide powerful names. Survivors say the limited release shows the government’s slow and dishonest work, but they still hope more documents will come out soon.
