The article details how Cabinet Office minister Josh Simons is under fire for a possible smear campaign. He hired a PR firm to investigate a story about Labour Together, but the firm’s report was never completed. Simons then sent emails to the National Cyber Security Centre, wrongly linking several journalists to a Russian‑linked hack. The story raises questions about his claims, the PR firm’s work and whether he misled the public.
