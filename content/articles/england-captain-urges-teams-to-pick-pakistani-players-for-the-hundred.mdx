England white‑ball captain Harry Brook has urged the four Indian‑owned teams in this year’s Hundred to pick Pakistani players, saying it would be a shame to miss out on the talent and crowds they bring. Brook, the tournament’s highest‑paid player, will not captain his side this summer and is busy preparing England for upcoming Tests and a T20 World Cup match. He’s also hoping injured bowler Jacob Bethell can return.
