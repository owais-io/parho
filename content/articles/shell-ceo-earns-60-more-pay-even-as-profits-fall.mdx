Shell’s new chief executive, Wael Sawan, got a 60 % pay rise, taking his total 2025 package to nearly £14 million. Most of that was a bonus and a share award, even though the company’s profits fell 22 % last year. Pay critics say the increase looks unfair when energy prices could rise again due to Middle‑East tensions. The move has sparked backlash from campaigners and investors.
