Nantucket, a popular summer resort in Massachusetts, found its wastewater testing revealed cocaine levels almost three times higher than the U.S. average. The town started testing last summer to track drug use. Results in September were 50% above national figures, and later spikes in October and December pushed levels to dangerous levels. Officials say the data will help guide outreach and support for residents.
