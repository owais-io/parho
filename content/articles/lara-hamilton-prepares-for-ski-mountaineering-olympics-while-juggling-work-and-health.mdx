Lara Hamilton is set to compete in ski mountaineering at the 2026 Winter Olympics in Italy. She grew up skiing with her dad in New South Wales and later moved to the U.S. for college, where she shifted from back‑country skiing to the faster, lighter ski‑mountaineering style. She works part‑time, DJs and coaches to fund her training, while also managing ankylosing spondylitis, a painful spinal condition. Despite these hurdles, she hopes to put Australia on the world map.
