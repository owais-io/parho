A Guardian reader worries that his critical emails about Donald Trump could cause him to be denied a US visa, because the new visa rules want to see five years of social media activity. He jokes about the absurdity of such a requirement, comparing it to finding bridge players who haven't said “No Trump.” The piece also shows a few other readers sharing unrelated comments, then invites anyone to send their own letters for publication.
