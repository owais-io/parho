Two homeless men had paid for rooms at a Holiday Inn in Manchester but were turned away because staff said the hotel wouldn’t allow them. The cold was –6 °C and a charity had arranged the booking. The men were then helped into a nearby Travelodge that welcomed them and even took dogs. The hotel apologized, said it would improve staff training, and the chain says it welcomes all guests.
