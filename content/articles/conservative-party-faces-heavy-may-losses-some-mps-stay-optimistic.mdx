The Conservatives are bracing for a big defeat on May 7. Kemi Badenoch, the party’s current leader, is seen as safe for herself, but polls show the party has only 17 % support – the lowest in years. MPs are split: some are upbeat about tactical voting and past successes, while others warn of a crushing loss in councils, Scotland and Wales.
