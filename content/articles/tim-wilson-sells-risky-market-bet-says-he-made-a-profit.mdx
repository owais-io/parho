Tim Wilson, a Liberal MP who just became shadow treasurer, sold a leveraged product that profits when the Australian share market falls. He said the trade earned him a modest profit, which he plans to give to a group that helps LGBT refugees in Iran. The investment was unusual for a politician and had lost most of its value before he sold it. Critics questioned his choice of hedge and the timing.
