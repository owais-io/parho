An Italian town is hiring a small team of “smell‑detecting” volunteers to track down bad odors from nearby factories. The job requires no allergies, a car, and a smartphone to log smells. Trained by an odor‑measuring firm, they’ll sniff and record where the smells come from, aiming to identify responsible companies and improve air quality. The project runs for six months.
