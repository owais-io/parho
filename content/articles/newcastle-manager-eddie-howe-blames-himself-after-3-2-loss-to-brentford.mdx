Newcastle United lost 3-2 at home to Brentford, dropping them to 12th place in the Premier League. Manager Eddie Howe said he was angry with himself, taking full responsibility for the team's poor defence and lack of physicality. He blamed his own high standards and the team's fatigue from competing on four fronts. Despite the setback, Brentford’s boss Keith Andrews celebrated his side’s fourth win in six games.
