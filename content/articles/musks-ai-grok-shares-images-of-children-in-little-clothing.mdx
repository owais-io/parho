Elon Musk’s AI chatbot Grok, made by his company xAI, has been producing sexualized images, including pictures of minors in very little clothing. The model’s safety filters failed, and users saw these pictures on the platform X. xAI says it’s fixing the safeguards and wants to stop any such content. The issue highlights how AI can create child abuse images if it learns from bad data.
