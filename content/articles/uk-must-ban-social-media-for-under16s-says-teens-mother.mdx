Esther Ghey, mother of murdered teen Brianna Ghey, is urging MPs to ban social media for kids under 16. She says waiting for a government review could keep harming children and cites rising mental‑health referrals for young people. Parliament is set to vote on a bill amendment that would block under‑16s from social media within a year. The government wants a summer consultation, while many Labour MPs and other parents demand a quicker ban.
