During a Florida appearance, former President Donald Trump said, without proof, that the bombing of an Iranian elementary school might have been carried out by Iran or another party. He pointed to video showing a US Tomahawk missile hitting a nearby naval base, but claimed the attack could be from another nation. Trump also said the US might lift oil sanctions to lower prices and praised the ongoing military operation, despite criticism.
