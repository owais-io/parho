Trump warned Cuba that the U.S. will stop sending oil and money unless the island agrees to a deal. He linked the threat to the U.S. raid that ousted Venezuela’s leader and killed many Cuban and Venezuelan soldiers. Cuba’s president said the country will defend itself. Some U.S. lawmakers praised Trump’s tough stance, while Cuba and Venezuela say they are stable and will continue their own plans.
