In late 2022, Genna Freed found out she had breast cancer right after her mother, Julie Newman, finished her last radiation session. Both women dealt with the shock of a double diagnosis and had to balance treatment with family duties. The story shows how a family history of cancer, especially with a BRCA gene mutation, raises risk and adds emotional strain. It highlights the importance of support when two relatives face cancer at once.
