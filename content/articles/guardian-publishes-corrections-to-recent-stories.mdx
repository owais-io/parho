Guardian issued corrections to several recent stories. It clarified John Lewis Partnership’s cashflow figures and clarified that a 6.9% pay rise only applied to shop‑floor staff, not all partners. Other corrections covered topics ranging from a minister’s investigation of journalists to a proposed law protecting men who pressure women to suicide. The piece also listed ways readers can complain or request further edits.
