Labor MP Ed Husic says it’s a mistake to invite Israeli President Isaac Herzog to Australia after the Bondi terror attack. He worries Herzog’s comments about Gaza and the war make the visit controversial, and he says the trip could hurt social cohesion. Husic supports peaceful protests against Israel’s Gaza bombardment and says the visit will spark protests. He urges the government to reconsider.
