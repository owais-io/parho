After the Gorton and Denton byelection, Reform UK and the Conservatives have asked the Electoral Commission to look into claims of ‘family voting’, where a family member may pressure another to vote a certain way. The allegations come from Democracy Volunteers, who said they saw high levels of this practice. While the groups say the vote was changed, the commission will investigate. The story highlights concerns about election fairness.
