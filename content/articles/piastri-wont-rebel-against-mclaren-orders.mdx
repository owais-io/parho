Oscar Piastri, the young Australian driving for McLaren, says he will never go against his team’s orders even as the 2025 season starts. He believes that breaking ranks would hurt his chances of winning the title. Piastri also said the new rules, which add battery‑powered boosts, make the cars smaller and faster. He keeps a close working relationship with McLaren’s boss, Zak Brown, and says the team will still be in the front.
