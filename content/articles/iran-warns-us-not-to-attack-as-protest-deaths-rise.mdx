About 500‑600 people are reported dead in Iran's protests, and the government says it will shoot anyone who goes out. About 10,000 people have been arrested. Iran has told the U.S. not to strike, even as President Trump talks about military options. Rights groups say the numbers are higher because the Iranian internet is shut down. The crackdown is hardening tensions and could lead to more violence.
