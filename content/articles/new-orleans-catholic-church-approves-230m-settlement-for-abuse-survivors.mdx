After a long lawsuit, a judge approved a $230 million payment to about 600 survivors of sexual abuse by clergy in the New Orleans Catholic archdiocese. The deal also changes how the church handles future abuse claims and protects children. Survivors gave powerful testimonies in court, and the settlement will put money into a trust by the end of December, with payouts starting early 2026.
