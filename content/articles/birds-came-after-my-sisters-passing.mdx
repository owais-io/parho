After her sister Nic died from cancer, a starling fell from a murmuration and she carried it home. A few days later, a robin hovered over her in bed, offering comfort. She sees the birds as gifts: one to bury, one to thank. The visit reminds her to open windows and welcome good things. The experience shows how grief can bring unexpected comfort and a reminder to stay open to life’s small miracles.
