An arctic blast is set to keep temperatures below freezing across the UK for the next week, threatening the racing calendar. Many racecourses—including Wincanton, Haydock, and Lancashire—will perform track inspections at 8 am to check safety. Several meetings have been cancelled or postponed, such as Lingfield’s Monday card, while others, like Musselburgh’s Sunday programme, may run with no inspection. The cold weather will keep racing uncertain until early next week.
