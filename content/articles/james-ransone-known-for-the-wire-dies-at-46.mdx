James Ransone, a 46‑year‑old actor best known for playing Ziggy on *The Wire*, died in Los Angeles by suicide. Fans and fellow actors posted heartfelt messages after the news. Ransone had roles in *Generation Kill* and the horror film *It Chapter Two*. He had spoken publicly about sexual abuse in his youth, which contributed to addiction struggles. His death highlights mental‑health challenges faced by many in the industry.
