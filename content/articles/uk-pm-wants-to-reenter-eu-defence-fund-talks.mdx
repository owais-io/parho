Prime Minister Keir Starmer says the UK should talk again about joining the EU’s €150 bn Safe defence fund. He says Europe needs to spend more on security and that the UK can help build weapons and share technology. Talks stopped in November because the cost was too high. Starmer wants more joint deals and hopes the UK can become a partner in future EU defence projects.
