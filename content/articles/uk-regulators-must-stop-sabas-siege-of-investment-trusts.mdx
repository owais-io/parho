Last year hedge fund Saba Capital tried to take over seven UK investment trusts, including Edinburgh Worldwide. It pushed board changes and kept fighting even after board members won each vote. Edinburgh’s board threatened to sell the trust’s own shares so investors could walk away and avoid Saba’s control, especially over its big SpaceX stake. Regulators are being asked to give trusts protection against such relentless “siege” tactics.
