Tottenham Hotspur have appointed former defender Igor Tudor as caretaker coach until the end of the Premier League season, giving them time to decide who will stay on after the current manager Thomas Frank leaves. Tudor, who left Juventus earlier this year, is expected to steady the club while the team fights against relegation. The decision leaves the door open for Mauricio Pochettino to return after his World Cup coaching duties. Meanwhile, Spurs are dealing with a heavy injury list and a tense atmosphere.
