American skater Amber Glenn said she and Canadian musician Seb McKinnon have resolved a copyright issue after his song played in her Olympic routine. McKinnon, who goes by CLANN, posted on social media to question the use of his track “The Return,” but later congratulated Glenn on her team gold. Both sides call it a misunderstanding and plan to work together. The case shows how complicated music rights are in figure skating.
