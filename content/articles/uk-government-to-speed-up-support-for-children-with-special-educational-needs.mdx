The UK government will overhaul the special educational needs system, raising the threshold for who can get an Education, Health and Care Plan (EHCP). The aim is to cut the time it takes to provide support from months or years to weeks. Education Secretary Bridget Phillipson says the new system will keep legal rights to support but make the process faster and less stressful for families.
