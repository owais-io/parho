The Australian government has let a US private prison firm, Management & Training Corporation (MTC), run offshore and onshore immigration detention centres, including a huge contract on Nauru and another worth $2.3 billion. In the US, MTC has faced complaints about abuse, overcrowding and even a death of a detainee. Critics in Australia say the company’s bad record should stop it from caring for vulnerable migrants, and they want a review of the contracts.
