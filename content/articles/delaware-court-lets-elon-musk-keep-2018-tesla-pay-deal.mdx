The Delaware Supreme Court has allowed Elon Musk to keep the $56 billion pay package from 2018. The court said canceling it would be unfair. Musk could earn up to $139 billion. The decision comes after a lower court said the deal was too big. Musk is worth about $600 billion. The pay is tied to hitting big Tesla goals, like raising the company’s value to $8.5 trillion. Shareholders also approved a new plan that could give Musk $1 trillion in ten years.
