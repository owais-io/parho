Tom Jenkins, a London photojournalist, traveled to Italy for his first Winter Olympics. He covered sports like ski jumping, big air, ice hockey, biathlon, curling, and more, learning new rules and dealing with the challenges of mountain travel. Jenkins captured exciting moments, such as Matt Weston's gold medal run, Lindsey Vonn’s crash, and artistic shots of jumpers silhouetted against floodlights. The story blends fast action with beautiful snowy landscapes, showing the highs and lows of the Games.
