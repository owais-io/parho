Texas billionaire Tom Hicks, who owned the Dallas Stars, Texas Rangers, and a stake in Liverpool FC, died at 79 in Dallas. He won a Stanley Cup with the Stars and took the Rangers to the World Series in 2010. His time at Liverpool was tough and the club was sold in 2010. Friends and fans paid tribute. He is survived by his wife, six children and 14 grandchildren.
