England’s cricket squad took a short holiday in Noosa, Queensland, after a rough start to the Ashes series. Australian tabloid newspapers poked fun at the team, showing pictures of player Ben Stokes in beachwear and using playful headlines. The papers noted England’s two‑match loss and said the players need to win the remaining Tests to recover. A coach said the break might help them reset and try new tactics before the next games.
