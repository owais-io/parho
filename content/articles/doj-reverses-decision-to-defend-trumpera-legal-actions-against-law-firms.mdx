The Justice Department had just said it would no longer defend Trump’s executive orders that targeted law firms for representing clients it disliked. Yesterday, it flipped that stance and said it wants to appeal the court’s ruling that blocked those orders. No reason was given for the sudden change. The firms—such as Susman Godfrey and others—had fought against the orders and celebrated the DOJ’s initial withdrawal.
