BBC chief Tim Davie said the broadcaster would stay on Elon Musk’s platform X to keep reaching young people and fight misinformation. He faced calls to quit after X shifted right and after the Grok AI tool created graphic, illegal images. MPs and officials, including former transport secretary Louise Haigh and Labour leader Keir Starmer, have urged the BBC and other institutions to leave. The BBC also wants more money for its World Service to counter state‑backed propaganda.
