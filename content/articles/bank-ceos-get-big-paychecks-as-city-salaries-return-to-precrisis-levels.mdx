Three UK bank bosses have received huge pay packages, signalling that high salaries in the City are coming back to pre‑crisis levels. NatWest’s chief, Paul Thwaite, earned £6.6 m in 2025, a 33% jump on last year. Citigroup’s CEO Jane Fraser topped $42 m in 2025, while Lloyds’ Charlie Nunn rose 20% to £7.4 m. Bank bonuses climbed, matching record profits and a renewed focus on rewarding performance.
