After the Bondi bombing, the NSW government wants to add the phrase “globalise the intifada” to the list of banned hateful words and make it illegal to display symbols of terrorist groups. Police would also be able to remove face coverings at rallies, limit gun ownership to four firearms, and could temporarily ban protests following a terror event. These changes still need parliamentary approval.
