A passenger bus traveling from Pokhara to Kathmandu veered off a narrow, steep road in Nepal, plunging 200 metres into a valley. The crash killed 19 people, including 17 who died on the spot and 2 more later, and injured 25 others. Among the victims was 24‑year‑old British hiker Dominic Stewart from Exeter, who had just finished a Himalaya trek. Rescue crews struggled to reach survivors due to lack of gear, and Nepal’s authorities are investigating the incident.
