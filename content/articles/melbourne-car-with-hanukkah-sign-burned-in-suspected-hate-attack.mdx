An empty car in St Kilda East, Melbourne, was set on fire early on Christmas morning after a “Happy Chanukah” sign and a Hanukkah candle were displayed on it. Nobody was inside, but people in the nearby house had to leave for safety. Police are looking for a suspect. Jewish community groups say they are on alert and safe, and leaders in Victoria and at the federal level have pledged support.
