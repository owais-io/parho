In Melbourne, 35,000 teachers joined a strike that shut about 500 public schools. They marched from Trades Hall to Parliament House, demanding a 35% pay rise over four years and better conditions. The government offered only an 18.5% increase. The strike left many students without school and parents backing the teachers. Union leaders warned of further action if the deal isn’t reached.
