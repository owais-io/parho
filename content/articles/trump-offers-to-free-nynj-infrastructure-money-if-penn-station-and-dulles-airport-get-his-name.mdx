He froze the funds during a 43‑day shutdown, sparking criticism from Senator Chuck Schumer. Trump’s name‑branding push has already hit places like the Kennedy Center and the U.S. Institute for Peace. He’s also pushing other projects, like Trump Accounts and a government drug‑sale site.
