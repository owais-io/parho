Amnesty International says that people held in Florida’s ‘Alligator Alcatraz’ detention center are being shackled in a metal cage, left outside without water, and exposed to bad conditions. The report also points to other Miami facilities where detainees face long waits, poor food, lack of showers, and sometimes violence from guards. Florida officials deny the allegations, calling them political attacks, but the court and human‑rights groups keep pressing for a review.
