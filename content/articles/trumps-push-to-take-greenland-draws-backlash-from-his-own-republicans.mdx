Donald Trump wants the U.S. to buy or seize Greenland, but many Republicans are opposing. Senators Tillis and Murkowski called his tariff threat on European countries a mistake, saying it could push NATO allies away. They warned that military action in Greenland would hurt U.S. businesses and damage alliances. Trump claims Greenland is needed for national security against Russia and China, but most Americans and GOP lawmakers disagree.
