The WHO reports that at least 13 hospitals and other medical sites in Iran have been struck by attacks from the US and Israel. Four medics were killed and 25 injured. The fighting has also damaged hospitals in Lebanon and caused many people to flee Tehran and Beirut. Because of the fighting, the WHO’s emergency supplies hub in Dubai is closed, blocking aid to 75 countries. Health workers warn the war jeopardises international humanitarian help.
