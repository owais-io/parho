Paul Holdsworth from Kendal writes about how regular public benches help people who need to sit often, improving their freedom to move around. In Windermere, a bench near a butcher’s shop sparked a complaint that it would bother vegetarians, but the author notes it was actually useful for anyone needing a rest, showing how seating design matters.
