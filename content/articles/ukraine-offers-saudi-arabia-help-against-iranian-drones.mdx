Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, told Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman that Kyiv is ready to share its experience fighting Iranian‑made Shahed drones. He said Ukraine’s cheap interceptor drones can be sold in bulk and offered aid to Saudi Arabia. The message comes amid fresh Russian attacks on Ukrainian cities and a Swedish seizure of a grain‑stealing cargo ship. Zelenskyy also urged an international response to Russia’s missile and drone strikes.
