During a Los Angeles trial, Instagram’s chief Adam Mosseri told judges that social‑media addiction isn’t a real medical condition and that the company prioritises user safety. Families and school districts sued Meta, TikTok and YouTube, claiming the apps’ endless‑scroll and other features make teens more depressed and suicidal. The CEO cited internal tests and safety tools, but parents point to leaked memos and failed safeguards that have led to teen harm.
