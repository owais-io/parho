Australia’s households are paying more for fuel and food because the Iran crisis has pushed global energy prices higher. Politicians are now talking about a new tax on the huge profits that gas and LNG companies make when prices climb. The plan would help bring back some money for Australians and curb inflation while encouraging a shift to cleaner energy.
