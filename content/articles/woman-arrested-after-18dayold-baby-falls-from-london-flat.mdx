A 43‑year‑old woman was arrested on suspicion of murder after an 18‑day‑old baby girl fell from a flat on Horseferry Road in Westminster. The baby was taken to a hospital but died. Police say the incident probably happened at home and are asking anyone with information to speak up. The woman is in custody while the investigation continues.
