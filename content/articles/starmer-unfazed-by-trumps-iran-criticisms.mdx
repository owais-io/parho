Prime Minister Keir Starmer dismissed President Trump’s harsh remarks about the UK’s role in Iran. Trump said Starmer was slow to let US forces use British bases, and even called him “not Winston Churchill.” Starmer replied that UK airbases and intelligence sharing are part of the “special relationship” with America and that the UK’s decisions are made in Britain’s interest. British public opinion is largely against the US operation in Iran, so Downing Street feels free to ignore Trump’s comments.
