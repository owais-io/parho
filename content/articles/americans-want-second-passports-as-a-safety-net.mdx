Many U.S. citizens are now trying to get a second passport through family ties. They say the country’s politics, cuts to rights, and job uncertainty make them feel unsafe. Getting citizenship by descent is easier than regular immigration and gives travel freedom and a backup plan. Young adults, parents of children with rights concerns, and people who love their heritage are behind the trend. Applications have jumped 500% since 2023.
