Morocco used the Africa Cup of Nations to showcase itself to the world, but critics say the focus on fancy stadiums left real problems behind. The tournament attracted huge crowds of African diaspora fans in cities like Paris and New York, and highlighted African coaches who led 15 of 24 teams. Fans celebrated historic figures like Congo’s Lumumba and sang popular songs, while some moments of dirty play and complaints about treatment marred the event.
