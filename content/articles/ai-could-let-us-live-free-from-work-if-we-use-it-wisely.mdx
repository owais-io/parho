Elon Musk says AI will soon make most office jobs unnecessary, turning work into an optional activity. If companies become super‑profitable, governments could tax that income and give everyone a universal basic income, letting people quit 9‑to‑5 jobs. The piece argues that freeing us from boring office routines could let us spend more time with family, hobbies, and self‑discovery—provided we embrace the change instead of fearing it.
