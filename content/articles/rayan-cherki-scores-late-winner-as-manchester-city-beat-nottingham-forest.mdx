Manchester City won 2‑1 over Nottingham Forest thanks to a late goal by Rayan Cherki. His goal, set up by a clever assist, extended City’s win streak to eight games, keeping them near the top of the table. Forest’s manager Sean Dyche protested a VAR decision that cost them, and the match also honored late former player John Robertson. City celebrated the win in high spirits.
