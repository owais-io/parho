Julie Inman Grant, the eSafety commissioner, is leading Australia’s first ban on social‑media apps for anyone under 16, starting Dec. 10. She has spent two decades inside tech companies like Microsoft, Adobe and Twitter, learning how their systems can harm children. Now she pushes back against platforms such as X, TikTok and YouTube, saying kids can’t win a fight with invisible algorithms. Grant has faced heavy online harassment, including threats from Elon Musk, but remains driven to protect young Australians.
