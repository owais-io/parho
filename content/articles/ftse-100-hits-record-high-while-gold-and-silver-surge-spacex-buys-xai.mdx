The FTSE 100 climbed to a new record, helped by a rebound in gold and silver prices. Gold is up about 12% from its recent lows and silver about 22%, sparking renewed buying as speculative positions were trimmed. Meanwhile, SpaceX announced it will acquire its AI venture xAI for $250 billion, giving the combined company a $1.25 trillion valuation. The move boosts optimism in markets.
