Zoe looks back at her first Valentine’s Day as a single person in decades. She’s excited and plans to celebrate with married friends, enjoying the humor of seeing their husbands and the surprise that her uncle wants to join. The day feels more about laughter, reconnecting, and embracing being single than missing a romantic partner. She shares the joy and anticipation of spending the holiday with family and friends instead of a date.
