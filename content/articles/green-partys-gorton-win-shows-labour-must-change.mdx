Gorton and Denton’s by‑election saw Green candidate Hannah Spencer defeat Labour, revealing a shift in working‑class voters. The loss raises questions about Labour’s messaging—blaming migrants, failing to energise voters, and missing chances to showcase its work on air quality and jobs. Letters from readers urge Labour to reconnect with progressives, improve communication, and warn that the result could fragment politics while praising the Greens as a viable alternative.
