After weeks of protests and a big rally in Preston, Reform councillors in Lancashire have reversed their earlier plan to shut down five care homes. Instead, they will invest in the facilities, keeping them open for residents and staff. The decision was made after public complaints about the closures and a strong community response. The council still says it hasn’t finished reviewing the consultation, but the homes are saved for now.
