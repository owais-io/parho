US banks are looking at suing the OCC because new crypto licensing rules let fintech and crypto firms operate with less oversight. The BPI, representing big lenders, says this could risk consumers and the financial system. The OCC, led by a former crypto exec, has made it easier for these firms to get national trust charters. Banks worry about weaker supervision and the blurring of what counts as a bank.
