After a 2‑0 win over Middlesbrough, Coventry City stayed at the top of the English Championship, thanks to Haji Wright’s hat‑trick. Wright’s goals came early, putting the match in control before the home side pulled one back. The victory ended Middlesbrough’s six‑game winning run and gave Coventry confidence as they push for automatic promotion. Manager Frank Lampard praised his team’s performance, while Boro coach Kim Hellberg said the race will stay tight for the rest of the season.
