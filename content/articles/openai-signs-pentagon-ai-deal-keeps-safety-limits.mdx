OpenAI has agreed to provide AI services to the Pentagon, but only under strict rules that forbid domestic spying and fully‑autonomous weapons. The contract came after former President Trump ordered federal agencies to stop using competitor Anthropic’s tech. OpenAI says it will keep its safety principles in the deal and hopes the terms could apply to all AI firms. The move shows the company can win a defense contract while staying true to its safety promises.
