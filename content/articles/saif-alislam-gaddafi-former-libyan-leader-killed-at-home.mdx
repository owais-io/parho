Saif al‑Islam Gaddafi, the eldest son of Libya’s former dictator and once a potential political rival, was shot dead on 3 February 2026 by four masked men at his home. He had been an international war‑crime suspect, held in a western Libyan city for years, and in 2021 tried to launch a presidential bid with support from Gaddafi loyalists. His death comes amid tensions between rival factions, and the killers remain unknown.
