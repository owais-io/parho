In March, 18‑year‑old Emir Balat and 19‑year‑old Ibrahim Kayumi were arrested after police said they tried to set off homemade bombs outside NYC Mayor Zohran Mamdani’s Gracie Mansion. The men claimed they were inspired by the Islamic State. The incident came amid far‑right protests and counter‑protests outside the mayor’s home. If convicted, the teens could face decades in prison. The case highlights growing concerns over radicalization in the US.
