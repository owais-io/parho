John Beam, 66, was shot to death at Laney College’s sports complex in Oakland, California. Police called the incident a “targeted” shooting, and a 27‑year‑old man, Cedric Irving Jr, was arrested and faces life in prison. Beam was a revered football coach who guided thousands of students to college and jobs. The community gathered to honor him with candles, flowers, and stories of his impact.
