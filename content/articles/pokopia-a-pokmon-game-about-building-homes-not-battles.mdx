Pokopia lets you play as a Ditto that builds and restores Pokémon habitats instead of battling. You create grass, trees, and homes to attract Pokémon who then appear and help you work the land. The game feels like Animal Crossing with cute, peaceful gameplay, and you can visit friends’ worlds. It shows Pokémon developers exploring new ways to enjoy the franchise beyond fighting.
