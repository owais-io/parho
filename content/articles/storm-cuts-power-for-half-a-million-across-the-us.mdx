On Tuesday, a powerful storm hit the U.S., knocking out power for about half a million homes and businesses from the Midwest to the East Coast. Millions of trees were downed, snow and rain fell, and several tornadoes touched down. Flights were canceled, and cold temperatures and high winds kept many people without electricity and made travel hard. Emergency crews worked overnight to restore power.
