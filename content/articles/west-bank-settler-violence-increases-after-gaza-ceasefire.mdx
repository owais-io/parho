Since the Gaza ceasefire, violence in the West Bank has spiked as Israeli settlers harass Palestinian farmers. The UN recorded more than 260 attacks last month, the most since 2006. Activists say settlers act with impunity. Meanwhile, the UN Security Council approved Donald Trump's Gaza peace plan, while protests grew in Charlotte after federal immigration raids. Trump also reversed his stance on releasing Jeffrey Epstein documents, prompting a House vote.
