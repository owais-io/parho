The US Secretary of State told Middle East embassies to avoid public statements that might inflame tensions or confuse US policy, a move seen as a rebuke of the Israeli ambassador after his controversial remarks on a TV show. The memo came as President Trump’s team pushed for a tough nuclear deal with Iran, fearing comments could harden Tehran’s stance.
