UK firms with offices or supply chains in the Middle East are warned that Iran’s hackers are a growing risk after recent US‑Israeli attacks. The National Cyber Security Centre says the danger is higher for any business with a regional presence, even though the direct threat to the UK remains unchanged. Companies should boost IT monitoring, follow NCSC guidance, and prepare for potential attacks or cyber‑damage.
