Wales captain Dewi Lake urged Cardiff fans to give his side a boost before the Six Nations clash with Scotland. After a heavy loss to France that pushed Wales to a 13‑match losing streak, Lake said the energy of a packed stadium would be the team’s “16th man”. He highlighted defensive work in training and the need for more tries, hoping home support will help Wales turn things around.
