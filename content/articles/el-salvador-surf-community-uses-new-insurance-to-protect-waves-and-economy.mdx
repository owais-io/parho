In El Salvador, surfers and local businesses depend on the wild waves of Oriente Salvaje. Climate change throws big storms that hurt surf visits and hurt the local economy. To help, the community partnered with Save the Waves and an insurance broker to create a parametric insurance policy that pays out when rainfall reaches a high level. The money can help businesses recover and restore the mangroves that keep the waves clean.
