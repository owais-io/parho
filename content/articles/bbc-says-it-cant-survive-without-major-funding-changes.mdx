BBC says it can’t keep running unless it changes its funding. Most UK viewers use the BBC, but fewer than 80 % of households pay the licence fee. Streaming sites blur the rules about when a licence is needed. The broadcaster wants a simpler fee system and is looking to open its iPlayer to other UK public‑service channels. Without reform, it warns the BBC could lose support and fail.
