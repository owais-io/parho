Abel Ortiz spent 38 years in Los Angeles, but after Trump’s hard‑line immigration policies made him feel unsafe, he left the U.S. by car to Mexico. He now runs a salon in Mexico City, enjoys newfound freedom but struggles with grief, language barriers and feeling like a stranger. His story, filmed by the Guardian, shows how fear can push people to leave their long‑time home.
