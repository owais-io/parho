Flu cases in England jumped 55% in one week, putting hospitals, GP surgeries and ambulance services under heavy pressure. The NHS is preparing for a possible worst‑case scenario over the next two weeks. Meanwhile, the British Medical Association plans a strike by junior doctors next week over pay disputes. People are being asked how the outbreak is affecting them and what they think about the strike.
