Trump and Israel claim the war will topple Iran’s brutal regime and stop its threats, but the plan is shaky. Trump keeps changing the goal, while Israel pushes hard to destroy Iranian weapons. History shows air‑only coups rarely succeed and often leave chaos. Iran could double‑down on nuclear weapons and stay strong, while the war gives Russia money and forces Gulf states to rethink their ties to the West. The fight risks big losses for everyone involved.
