Riverside County Sheriff Chad Bianco, who is running for California governor, has taken more than 650,000 ballots from last year’s election to investigate alleged fraud tied to Proposition 50. State officials, including Attorney General Rob Bonta, say the claims are unfounded, noting only a 103‑vote difference between machine and final counts. The seizure has sparked legal battles and raised concerns about undermining trust in California’s election system.
