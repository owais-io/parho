An inquest into Kelly Wilkinson’s 2021 murder heard that police had turned her away from a Southport station on 16 April, telling her to “cool off” and give her estranged husband a break. This was just four days before he stabbed, doused her with petrol and set her on fire. The case shows police missed several chances to intervene, and the coroner will now consider new evidence.
