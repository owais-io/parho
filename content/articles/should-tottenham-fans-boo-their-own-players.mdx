Tottenham fans are arguing whether it’s okay to boo players after mistakes. After a costly error by keeper Guglielmo Vicario, some supporters said they’ll boo him. Manager Thomas Frank said fans should not boo while players are on the pitch, but can do so afterward. Player Pedro Porro said he’d not tolerate disrespect from the stands. The debate shows tension between fans wanting to express frustration and players seeking support.
