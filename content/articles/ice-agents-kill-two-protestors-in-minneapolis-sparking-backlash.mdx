ICE agents killed two protesters in Minneapolis—Renee Good, a U.S. citizen, and Alex Pretti, a nurse—during a protest. Both were shot while trying to leave or help others, not posing a threat. The killings drew anger, led to protests, and made some Republicans call for investigations. The events have highlighted concerns over ICE's tactics and may influence public opinion and future federal funding decisions.
