A Roman Catholic priest in Louisiana was arrested after police received a report that he had touched a child inappropriately multiple times over a year. Deputies received the complaint on December 2, and the priest was booked and jailed on January 16. He was released on a $15,000 bail. The case comes amid a wave of abuse claims that have hurt the local Catholic diocese and cost millions.
