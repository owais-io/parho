Federal district court judges are quietly fighting back against President Trump’s controversial orders. They have blocked or reversed dozens of executive actions, from immigration rules to election laws, and have even threatened officials with contempt to force compliance. The judges face threats, doxxing, and even death threats, yet they keep up the legal battle. Their work shows that the rule of law can still stand, even when the president tries to ignore it.
