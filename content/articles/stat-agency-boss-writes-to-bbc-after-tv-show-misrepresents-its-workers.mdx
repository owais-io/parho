The head of the Office for National Statistics, Darren Tierney, sent a letter to the BBC saying a recent episode of the drama Industry made its field interviewers look like fraudsters. Tierney warned the show could break public trust in the agency’s data‑gathering work, which relies on people feeling safe when interviewers visit homes. He asked the BBC to meet the team and review the depiction.
