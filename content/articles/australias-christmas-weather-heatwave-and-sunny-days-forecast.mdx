Australia’s Christmas Day is set to feel the country’s biggest heatwave yet. Most of the east coast will be hot and sunny, with Queensland reaching 33 °C, New South Wales dry and sunny but a few showers, and Victoria and Tasmania cooler with only light rain. Western Australia and the Northern Territory will have the hottest temperatures, up to 39 °C in Perth and 32 °C in Darwin. NRMA warns that parked cars can heat up quickly, posing a danger to children.
