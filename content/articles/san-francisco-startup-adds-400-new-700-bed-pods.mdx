Brownstone Shared Housing, a San Francisco startup, has bought a six‑storey building to add up to 400 sleeping pods that cost $700 a month. Each pod holds a twin bed and can stack with others, sharing a kitchen, bathroom and workspace. The price is far below the city’s median $3,065 rent, attracting people who can’t afford apartments. After a dismissed eviction lawsuit, the CEO says demand is high and the company will keep expanding.
