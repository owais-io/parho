Yarck, a small Victorian town, was hit by a huge bushfire that started in Longwood. Around 300 homes were lost, 80 houses burned and 350,000 hectares of land were scorched. One person died. Farmer David Rigby, who had water, a generator and hoses, fought the flames and saved most of his house and nearby neighbours. Animal shelters lost hay, no power. Local pubs stayed open. Firefighters and politicians are still clearing roads and helping farmers get aid.
