During the Liverpool FC parade, a driver ran over the crowd. Dan Barr, a former soldier, jumped into the car, forced the door, and pressed the hand‑brake and seat‑belt button to stop the vehicle. He later received a High Sheriff award for his bravery. Barr describes the screaming, the sudden silence, and how everyone tried to get to the car. He says the moment was shocking but he did what he could, not a heroic act.
