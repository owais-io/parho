A recent survey found that 48 % of people in the UK never asked their GP for help when they got ill, mainly because they think it will be hard to get an appointment. About a quarter tried to deal with the sickness on their own or waited for it to disappear. Doctors worry that this could seriously hurt health. The study highlights how many people fear long waits for NHS services like GP, A&E, and ambulance.
