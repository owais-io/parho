Thai villages along the Cambodia border are in chaos again as fighting flares up. A ceasefire that was made by former US President Donald Trump has been broken, forcing more than 500,000 people to leave their homes. Residents say the war could keep going for months, and many doubt that any leader, even Trump, can end it quickly. The conflict has left families homeless and worried about their future.
