An 84‑year‑old mother of Today show anchor Savannah Guthrie, Nancy Guthrie, vanished from her Tucson home in late January. Blood droplets were found on the porch, and a white van was seen nearby. Investigators received ransom emails demanding millions of Bitcoin and asking for proof of life. No suspects are named and the FBI warns that AI could fake proof. Savannah posted pleas on Instagram while authorities continue the search.
