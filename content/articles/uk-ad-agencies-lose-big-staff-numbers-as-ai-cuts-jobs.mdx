The IPA says UK ad agencies lost 14% of staff in 2025, the biggest drop since 2004. Creative firms in London lost over 2,000 jobs, and young workers under 25 fell 19%. Most quit voluntarily, and 24% of agencies plan further cuts. AI tools are replacing routine work, reducing advertised positions and graduate hiring, sparking a crisis in the industry.
