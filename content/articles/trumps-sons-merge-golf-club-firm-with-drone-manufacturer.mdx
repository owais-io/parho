Trump’s sons’ golf club company is merging with drone maker Powerus through a deal that aims to take the drone firm public. The move is part of a broader push by the Trump family into drone tech, a growing Pentagon priority used in places like Ukraine. The merger has sparked ethical questions about the family’s business dealings while Donald Trump is president.
