Joey Porter, a former Steelers linebacker, spoke on a podcast during Super Bowl week and slammed Ben Roethlisberger, calling him “not a good person.” Porter said Roethlisberger and James Harrison broke the team’s “brotherhood” by criticizing coach Mike Tomlin on their own shows. He also accused Roethlisberger of refusing to sign memorabilia for teammates and not being a true captain.
