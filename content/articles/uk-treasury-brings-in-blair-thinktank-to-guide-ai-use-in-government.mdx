UK Treasury has invited Tony Blair’s think‑tank and major tech companies to advise on how to use AI in government services. The meeting included the AI head at the Blair Institute, leaders from IBM and other AI firms, and former Google and Facebook advisors. Ministers want to speed up AI adoption across the public sector, but critics warn the move gives tech giants too much influence. The plan aims to boost efficiency before the next spending review.
