The 75 Hard challenge has made a comeback thanks to Paddy McGuinness, and Emma Beddington, who loves trendy self‑help plans, sees it as an inspiration but decides not to copy it. She’s designing her own series of daily goals—one brain‑boost version, a house‑cleaning version and a gentle, joy‑focused routine. She’s basically saying, “I’ll make my own rules and maybe even sell some biscuits while I’m at it.”
