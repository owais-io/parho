Kevin Rees, a 63‑year‑old retired electrician from Sidcup, joined online anti‑ULEZ groups and built a homemade bomb. On Dec. 6 he lit it, blowing up a ULEZ camera on Willersley Avenue. The blast spread shrapnel 100 m, damaging cars and a child's house, but nobody was hurt. He was convicted of bombing and weapon charges at Woolwich Crown Court. The case shows how online communities can radicalise ordinary people and highlights the debate over ULEZ and social media.
