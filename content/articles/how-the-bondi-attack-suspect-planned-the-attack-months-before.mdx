Police say a father and son who carried out the Bondi beach attack spent months planning it. They filmed a video praising the Islamic State, made pipe bombs and shotguns, and trained with guns in rural New South Wales. They rented an Airbnb in Sydney, traveled to the Philippines, and did a reconnaissance trip to Bondi before the December 14 attack. On the day they loaded weapons and bombs into a car and killed 15 people at a Jewish event.
