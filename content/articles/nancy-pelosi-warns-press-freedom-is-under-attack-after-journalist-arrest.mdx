Pelosi said the United States is facing a new threat to press freedom after the Trump administration arrested a journalist and searched another’s home. She also warned that the Washington Post’s recent mass layoffs are draining newsrooms across the country. Pelosi called the actions a chilling attack on the first amendment, saying a starving press weakens democracy. She urged Congress to protect journalists and support newsroom resources.
