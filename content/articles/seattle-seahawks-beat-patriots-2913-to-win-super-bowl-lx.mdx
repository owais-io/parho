Seattle Seahawks beat New England Patriots 29‑13 to win Super Bowl LX, their second championship. Kenneth Walker III rushed 135 yards and was named MVP. Former starter Sam Darnold threw 202 yards and a touchdown, turning his draft‑bust past into a championship moment. The win avenged an 11‑year‑old loss and cemented the Seahawks’ status as champions. The game also featured a celebrated Bad Bunny halftime show.
