A deadly gun attack on Bondi beach in Sydney killed 15 people and left many wounded. The suspected attackers were Sajid Akram and his son Naveed. Indian police say Sajid’s family in Hyderabad were unaware of any radical plans and had no extremist record. Police plan to charge Naveed, who was injured but has recovered from a coma. The incident is being treated as a terrorist attack on the Jewish community.
