FBI director Kash Patel announced that agents will start learning mixed‑martial‑arts moves from UFC fighters, saying current hand‑to‑hand training isn’t “alpha enough.” The plan includes teaching techniques like Brazilian jiu‑jitsu and ground‑and‑pound, and a UFC “Freedom 250” event will be held at the White House. Critics view the partnership as a strange blend of law‑enforcement and entertainment.
