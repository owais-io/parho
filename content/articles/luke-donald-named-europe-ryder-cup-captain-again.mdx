Luke Donald has been confirmed as Europe's captain for the 2025 Ryder Cup, marking a third term. His stay gives Europe a steady hand after back‑to‑back wins in 2018 and 2022, while the United States faces uncertainty over Tiger Woods’s future captaincy. Donald praised his team's unity and said he is ready for another challenge. The announcement also highlights the ongoing tensions between the PGA and the DP World Tour.
