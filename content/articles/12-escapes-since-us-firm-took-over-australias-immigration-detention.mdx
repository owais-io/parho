A watchdog report says 12 people escaped from Australian immigration centers after a US company, MTC, began running them. The report says there were serious understaffing problems, weaker security and poor planning. One escape involved a high‑risk detainee who was put in a Kia Carnival that had no safety features. He stabbed staff and fled. The government says it is boosting staff, safety checks and oversight.
