Russian forces hit two Ukrainian power plants, plunging the country into blackouts and forcing emergency cuts across most regions. Kyiv asked Poland for help, while Polish airports near the border halted flights as a precaution. President Zelensky urged stronger air‑defence and faster repairs to the damaged grid. Meanwhile, EU leaders plan a wide ban on services that help Russia ship crude oil, aiming to cut its war funding.
