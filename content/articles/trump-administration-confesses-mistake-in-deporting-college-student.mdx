US officials said they made an error when they deported 19‑year‑old Lucía López Belloza from Massachusetts to Honduras. She had been trying to surprise her family in Texas for Thanksgiving. A court had ordered she stay in the U.S. for 72 hours, but ICE ignored the order and sent her abroad. The administration apologized but still argues the case is legal. The student and her lawyer want her returned to the U.S.
