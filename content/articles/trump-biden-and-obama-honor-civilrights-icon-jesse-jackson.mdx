Trump, Biden and Obama, along with other U.S. politicians, posted tributes to civil‑rights icon Jesse Jackson after his death at 84. They praised his long‑standing work for voting rights, equality and social justice, noting how he inspired their own political careers. The comments highlighted Jackson’s influence on movements, his two presidential campaigns and his efforts to unite people across race and class. The messages also thanked Jackson for shaping America’s moral direction.
