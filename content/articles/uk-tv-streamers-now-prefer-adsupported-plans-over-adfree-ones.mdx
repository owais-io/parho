UK TV streamers have switched to cheaper ad‑supported plans, passing ad‑free subscriptions for the first time. The number of ad‑supported users is expected to reach 26.5 million by year‑end, up 7 million from last year. Ad‑free subscribers dropped to 23.1 million. Main streaming services charge about £64 a month, so cheaper plans are attractive. The growing ad market is reaching £1.38 billion this year.
