Alistair Carns, a former Royal Marine and defence adviser, is being talked about as a possible Labour leader. He was elected in 2024 and became the armed‑forces minister in 2025. Labour’s new MPs want fresh faces and say Carns’s military background gives the party a strong image. Some worry he lacks clear views on economic and social issues, but supporters say he could bring new energy to the party.
