Trump gave Iran an extra 10 days to open the Hormuz Strait, claiming talks are progressing. Meanwhile, the U.S. and Israel are still striking Iran, with both sides threatening further attacks. Iran says the U.S. is double‑talking and wants a deal that ends fighting and keeps the Strait open. Pakistan is mediating, hoping to secure a peaceful solution for the region.
