An angel in Rome’s Basilica of St Lawrence was restored in a way that looks like Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni, sparking a probe by the culture ministry and the Diocese of Rome. The repair was needed after water damage, but some say the new face was deliberate. Meloni posted a laughing comment, while politicians and heritage officials warn against art being used for propaganda. A local craftsman says he simply restored the original design, not the PM’s likeness.
