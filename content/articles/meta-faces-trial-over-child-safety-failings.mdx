Meta is under fire in New Mexico after a judge paused a child‑safety case. Lawyers say the company put profit before protecting kids, letting predators use Instagram and Facebook to groom minors and spread child‑abuse material. Court documents show Meta delayed reporting abuse and kept kids in risky situations. Meta says it’s working on safety tools, but critics say it’s too slow and that encryption blocks police from seeing threats.
