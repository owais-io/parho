Kamala Harris says she hasn’t finished with politics and might run for president in 2028. After losing the 2024 election, she has stayed in the public eye, publishing a memoir, speaking at events and backing a Texas Senate candidate. The author criticises Harris for not taking a clear stance on Gaza, for staying close to wealthy donors, and for lacking authenticity. She worries the Democratic Party’s blandness could let Trump or another outsider win.
