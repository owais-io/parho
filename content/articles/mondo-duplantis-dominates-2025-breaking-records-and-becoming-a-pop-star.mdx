In 2025, Swedish pole‑vaulter Mondo Duplantis set four new world records, kept both indoor and outdoor titles, and won every competition he entered. He was named World Athletics’ male athlete of the year and the BBC’s overseas personality. Duplantis also made headlines with a spontaneous kiss of his fiancée that went viral, and he’s now charting a music career with hit singles and a future in sports politics.
