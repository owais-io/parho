Trump has posted over 6,600 times on Truth Social and held 433 press events in a year, producing 2.4 million words. Diplomats worldwide must sift through his nonstop noise to spot real policy hints. They use extra monitoring tools, track his posting times, and even follow Fox News to gauge what he’s hearing. The constant stream of jokes, conspiracies and self‑promotion makes it hard to find useful information.
