Revolut, the popular money‑transfer app, finally got a full UK banking licence after five years of waiting. The regulator cleared it to keep customer deposits, launch current accounts and offer more services like loans and mortgages. The company will start opening new accounts soon and plans to grow into 30 more markets by 2030. The licence marks a big step for Revolut’s goal of becoming a full‑service bank.
