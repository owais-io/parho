A former Brown student and Portuguese national left video clips that show he had been planning a shooting for about six semesters. He killed two students at Brown and later a MIT professor before shooting himself in a New Hampshire storage facility. The videos, translated from Portuguese, reveal he didn’t feel sorry, denied mental illness, and had no clear motive for choosing his victims. The incident happened in December 2023.
