Curling mixed‑double games at the Milano‑Cortina Winter Games saw Norway beat Great Britain, while Canada and Sweden also won. A brief power outage cut the action for four minutes, but play resumed quickly. Meanwhile, Great Britain’s skeleton team faces a helmet ban that could cost them medals; they have appealed to the court of arbitration. The story also notes Italy’s security plans and the presence of ICE agents.
