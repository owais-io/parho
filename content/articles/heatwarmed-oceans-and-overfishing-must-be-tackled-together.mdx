Rising ocean temperatures are steadily killing fish and hurting communities that rely on them. Even when some areas see a quick, temporary fish boost, the overall trend is a decline. Overfishing and warming are feeding each other, so they can’t be fixed separately. Governments need long‑term plans that treat climate and fishing as one problem, not two.
