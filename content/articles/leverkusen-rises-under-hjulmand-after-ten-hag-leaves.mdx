Leverkusen beat Leipzig 4‑1, showing big progress under new coach Kasper Hjulmand after Erik Ten Hag departed. Hjulmand’s 28 points in 13 games and a Champions League win over Manchester City signal a strong rebuild. Despite missing many players for the African Cup of Nations and injuries, Leverkusen kept going, with goals from Martin Terrier, Patrik Schick and teenage star Montrell Culbreath. The win confirms Leverkusen's rise in the Bundesliga.
