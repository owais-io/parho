Arsenal’s title hopes are being threatened by Aston Villa, who beat them twice in a row in 2023 and 2024. Arteta says the club must stop Villa to keep chasing the league crown, especially as City and Liverpool still pose challenges. Villa’s recent winning streak and Arteta’s injury problems make matches tough. Arsenal need to win the remaining games, with key players returning to push for their first title since 2004.
