Andy Burnham could have won the Gorton & Denton byelection, Labour’s deputy leader Lucy Powell said after the Greens took the seat. Powell blamed the party for not letting Burnham run and urged the party to use his popularity in Greater Manchester. She said the result shows the need for a stronger national strategy and hinted Burnham might return to Parliament.
