Last year MPs voted to stop criminalising women who get abortions outside the legal system, a major win for reproductive rights. Now the House of Lords is set to debate amendments to the bill. Pro‑choice and pro‑life proposals are on the table, ranging from keeping telemedicine, deleting the decriminalisation clause, to pardoning past convictions. Police could also decide whether to arrest. The bill should become law soon.
