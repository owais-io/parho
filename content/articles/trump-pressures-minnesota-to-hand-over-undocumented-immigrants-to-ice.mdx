Donald Trump is trying to get Minnesota to cooperate with federal ICE agents. He’s offering to pull ICE teams out of the state if Minnesota will hand over undocumented people in its custody, stop sanctuary policies and let ICE use state prisons. The administration also hinted it might take voter data or investigate state police. The move threatens Minnesota’s independence and could hurt state power to investigate federal actions.
