Prince Andrew has been in the news for years because of photos linking him to Jeffrey Epstein. A 2001 picture shows him with a 17‑year‑old girl who later said he abused her. A 2010 photo shows him walking with Epstein in New York. Emails and a 2023 release of over 3 million Epstein files revealed more pictures of him with women and a photo of him being held by police. In 2022 he settled a civil case but denied wrongdoing.
