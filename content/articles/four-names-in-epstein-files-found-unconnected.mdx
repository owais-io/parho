Representative Ro Khanna read six names linked to the Epstein files on the House floor. The Justice Department said four of those men had no connection to Epstein. They were actually part of a NYPD photo lineup used for investigations. Khanna and colleague Thomas Massie pushed to reveal the names, hoping for transparency. Some names were well‑known billionaires, but the others were ordinary people with past arrests.
