The column compares Starmer to the late‑tenured leader John Major and says Labour can still win the next election, but only if four things happen. First, Starmer must step down. Second, the government has to stop making costly policy mistakes that scare businesses. Third, a new leader must offer a clear plan for jobs, health, and infrastructure. Finally, the economy must start growing again, so people see real improvement in living standards.
