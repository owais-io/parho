Vice‑President JD Vance and Senator Marco Rubio attended the US women’s hockey opener at the 2026 Winter Olympics in Milan. They sat in the crowd, took photos, and the game went smoothly with no protests. Vance’s team had a security presence, but fans and media were more interested in recording the moment. The US team won 5‑1 against the Czech Republic, and the delegation talked about national unity.
