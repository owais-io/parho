A recent NFIB survey shows U.S. small‑business owners are optimistic about 2026, citing lower cost pressures, easing hiring challenges, and rising capital spending. Fiserv’s sales index recorded a December uptick, and a Comerica survey found 80% of owners feel confident and expect revenue growth of about 8%. Intuit’s data shows modest employment growth across most sectors. Still, some groups—farmers, retailers, truckers—face higher costs and regulation worries.
