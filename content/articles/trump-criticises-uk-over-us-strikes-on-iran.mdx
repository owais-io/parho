Donald Trump has publicly slammed UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer for refusing to let the US use British bases for initial strikes on Iran. Trump says the UK is uncooperative and that relations have deteriorated. Starmer counters, saying the UK will only allow defensive use of Diego Garcia and other bases if needed, and that it is protecting British interests. The comments have highlighted tensions between the two allies over the Middle East conflict.
