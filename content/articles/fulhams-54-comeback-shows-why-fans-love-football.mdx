Fulham faced Manchester City at Craven Cottage and were down 5‑1 with less than an hour left. The home crowd rallied, and the team pushed back to 5‑4, keeping the match dramatic until the final whistle. The tense game drew attention to the stadium’s quirky mix of luxury suites and regular fans, and even highlighted Erling Haaland’s milestone of 100 Premier League goals. Fans left feeling the thrill of a true football comeback.
