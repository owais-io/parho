Border patrol chief Gregory Bovino was caught on a phone call making sarcastic jokes about the Jewish faith of Minnesota’s U.S. attorney, Daniel Rosen. He laughed at Rosen’s observing Shabbat and used the phrase “chosen people” mockingly. The call was part of Bovino’s push for tougher action against people he said were blocking immigration enforcement. After the incident, the Trump administration demoted Bovino and sent him out of Minneapolis.
