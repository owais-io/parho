After pro‑democracy activist Jimmy Lai was sentenced to 20 years in prison, Britain has widened its Hong Kong visa scheme. The change lets adult children of BNO holders who were under 18 when Hong Kong returned to China apply on their own, plus their partners and kids. About 26,000 people are expected to move in five years. MPs worry about new rules for permanent residence and English and salary levels.
