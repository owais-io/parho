Cyclone Narelle, a Category 5 storm, is set to hit far north Queensland on Friday morning. It will bring wind gusts up to 315 km/h and heavy rain that could cause flash flooding. Residents in the warning zone have been evacuated, schools are closed, and emergency crews are ready. Narelle is the first Category 5 to hit Cape York since 1899, and warm ocean waters helped it grow so fast.
