Premier David Crisafulli kept Queensland’s Liberal National Party (LNP) united, but MP Nigel Dalton crossed the floor to support a motion ending a gag on abortion debate. The move exposed cracks as the party juggles abortion, gun‑law and youth‑crime issues while One Nation gains ground. The LNP must decide whether to allow dissent or crack down—a choice that could hurt its hold on power.
