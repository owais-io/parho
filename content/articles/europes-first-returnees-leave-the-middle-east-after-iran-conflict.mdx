The first group of Europeans caught up in the Iran conflict has finally made it back home. Italian defence minister Guido Crosetto was one of the officials stranded in Dubai; others, like Czech and Greek ministers, are returning as well. Still, hundreds of thousands of Europeans remain trapped in the Middle East. Governments are working on more flights and evacuation plans while some, like Spain, have taken political stances over the conflict.
