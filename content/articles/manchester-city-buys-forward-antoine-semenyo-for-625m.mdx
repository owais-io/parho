Manchester City has signed 26‑year‑old forward Antoine Semenyo from Bournemouth for £62.5 million, keeping him until 2031. The new striker aims to lift City, who are six points behind Arsenal with 17 games left, into the title race. Semenyo will debut against Exeter in the FA Cup, as Pep Guardiola plans little rotation to win more matches. He scored 10 goals this season and hopes to grow under City’s coaching.
