On a Friday, a federal magistrate judge said no to signing off on possible criminal charges against former CNN anchor Don Lemon over a protest at a Minnesota church. The decision outraged Florida Attorney General Pam Bondi and angered Trump, who wants Lemon arrested. Lemon’s lawyer says the protest was protected speech. The judge’s rare refusal may mean a grand jury will also doubt the case. Two protestors were arrested.
