Heavy floods shut the dirt roads to Birdsville and Bedourie for months, forcing residents to rely on air‑delivered supplies. While essentials like meat and medicine arrive, soft drinks and snacks are scarce, leaving the local store with only two chip flavours. The community keeps spirits up with themed pub nights, and locals have learned to adapt to long isolation periods.
