Reform UK, led by Nigel Farage, is being accused of breaking its pledge to cut council tax after several councils it controls plan big rises. Kent County, the party’s flagship, wants a 3.99% increase, while other councils like Derbyshire and Northamptonshire propose the 5% cap. Opposition councillors say the party promised lower taxes, yet it now raises them, sparking anger and calls for honesty.
