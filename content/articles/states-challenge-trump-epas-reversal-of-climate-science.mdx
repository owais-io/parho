A group of 24 states, plus many cities and counties, sued the Trump‑era Environmental Protection Agency because it cancelled a 2009 rule that said greenhouse gases endanger public health. The lawsuit wants that rule back and aims to stop the EPA from removing limits on car emissions. States say the move will make climate disasters worse and harm people’s health. The court may merge this case with other environmental lawsuits.
