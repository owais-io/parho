London officials have stopped the annual Al‑Quds Day march that usually shows support for Palestinian rights. The Home Secretary said the ban was needed to avoid big clashes and because some past marchers have used flags of the banned group Hezbollah and shouted violent slogans. Police say the protest could cause serious disorder and may involve people linked to Iranian state activity. Organisers say the ban is unfair and a form of Islamophobia.
