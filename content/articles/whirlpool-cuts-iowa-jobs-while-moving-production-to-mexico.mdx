Whirlpool has announced it will cut 341 jobs at its Amana, Iowa plant, after already cutting 250 earlier this year. The company says it is modernising the site but has been shifting many refrigerator lines to a new factory in Mexico. Workers and the union say the cuts hurt small towns and that the company still supports Trump‑era tariffs that don’t protect American jobs.
