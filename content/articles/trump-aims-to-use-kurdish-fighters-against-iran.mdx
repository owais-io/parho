The Kurds, a 30‑40 million‑strong stateless group spread across Turkey, Iraq, Iran, Syria and Armenia, have long fought for autonomy. Trump’s administration is looking to recruit Kurdish fighters, known as peshmerga, to help pressure Iran. The US has previously backed them in Iraq and Syria. Kurdish leaders in Iraq say they will stay neutral. Analysts say the plan could strain Iran’s borders but is unlikely to push deep into the country.
