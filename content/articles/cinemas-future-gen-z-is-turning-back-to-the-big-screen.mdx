Cinema is facing a crisis as long movies and streaming keep audiences away. But young people are turning back to the big screen. Gen Z finds film more influential than social media, and more U.S. teens are going to theaters. The industry worries about long‑running films hurting profits and many UK cinemas risk closing. Still, movies like Saltburn are drawing crowds and filmmakers say the theater experience is irreplaceable.
