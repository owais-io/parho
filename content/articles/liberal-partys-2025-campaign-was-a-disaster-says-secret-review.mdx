An internal Liberal Party review shows why their 2025 election was a historic flop. The report blames leader Peter Dutton’s dour, self‑focused style and a chaotic takeover of campaign decisions that left staff scrambling. A costly nuclear‑power plan, broken staff relationships, and misleading polls also hurt the party. The review notes a lost female vote and warns the party must fix these mistakes for next time.
