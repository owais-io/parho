Authorities are looking into whether criminal negligence caused a deadly avalanche in California’s Lake Tahoe that killed eight skiers and left a ninth missing. The Nevada sheriff’s office and OSHA are involved, and the district attorney will decide on any charges. The avalanche happened while a guide company, Blackbird Mountain Guides, was leading a group that had known about dangerous weather. Recovery is delayed by bad weather.
