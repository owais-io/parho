A new centre called The Forest Glade in Ukraine treats soldiers with mental wounds. After more than three years in Russian captivity following the battle for Mariupol, 25‑year‑old Kyrylo Chuvak spends three weeks there outside Kyiv. The short stay gives him a chance to heal from trauma before he returns to the front line. The centre helps soldiers recover fast enough to keep fighting.
