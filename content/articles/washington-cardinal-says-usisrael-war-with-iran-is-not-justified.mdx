Cardinal Robert McElroy, archbishop of Washington DC, said the US‑Israel strike on Iran is not morally justified. He argues it fails the just‑war test because there was no immediate attack, the goals are unclear, and the harm outweighs any benefit. The Catholic Church, including the pope and the Vatican, has urged dialogue and peace. Parishioners worry about the war’s future and its impact on U.S. soldiers.
