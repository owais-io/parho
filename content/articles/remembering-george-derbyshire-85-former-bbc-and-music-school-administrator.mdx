George Derbyshire, who passed away at 85, spent his career managing finances for the BBC and two top arts schools – London’s Guildhall School of Music and Drama and Hong Kong’s Academy for the Performing Arts. He loved Chinese culture, could speak Cantonese, and later served as a local councillor in Watford. He was survived by his wife, three children, grandchildren, and a sister.
