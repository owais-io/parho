Marina Hyde writes that Andy Burnham, Labour's popular mayor of Greater Manchester, could try to challenge Keir Starmer for the party's leadership. She compares Burnham's political fate to actor Johnny Depp, saying a star can lose allure when he becomes available. Hyde explains that the NEC must approve Burnham's move to MP, and internal Labour politics could block him. Even if he runs, success is far from guaranteed.
