Portland Communications, a UK PR firm, is accused of paying people to edit Wikipedia so that its clients look better. An investigation traced changes from 2016 to 2024, showing the firm hired Web3 Consulting to remove negative information about clients such as Qatar, covering up criticism of the World Cup and other scandals. These edits violate Wikipedia rules and are known as “black‑hat” edits. Portland denies current involvement, but former staff confirm it happened.
