Senegal, the 2022 champions, will face Sudan, who made the tournament despite a war. Mali should beat Tunisia, and Morocco will have an easy game against Tanzania. South Africa will take on Cameroon in a tight match. Egypt should win over Benin, but Benin could surprise. Nigeria’s star forwards should overpower Mozambique. Algeria will play a tactical fight with Congo, and Ivory Coast will try to defend their title against Burkina Faso.
