Morgan McSweeney, former chief of staff to Labour leader Keir Starmer, has quit, but voters are still furious. They blame Starmer’s choice to make former politician Peter Mandelson the U.S. ambassador, despite links to Jeffrey Epstein. The scandal shows a big gap between politicians and ordinary people, and it fuels anger ahead of a key by‑election in Gorton and Denton. Labour faces pressure to prove it cares about everyday life and public trust.
