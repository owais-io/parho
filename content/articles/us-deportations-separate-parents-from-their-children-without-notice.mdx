A new report shows that the Trump administration has been sending parents to Honduras without asking if they have kids or letting them decide whether to bring their children. Many parents were deported within days of detention, leaving toddlers, babies and even children with disabilities in the care of friends or family who might also be at risk of deportation. The study urges lawmakers to protect families and improve child‑care arrangements during deportation.
