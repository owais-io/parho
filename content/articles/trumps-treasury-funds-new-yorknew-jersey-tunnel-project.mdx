The Trump administration finally released the last $127 million owed to the Gateway tunnel project, plus extra money, after a court order. This move lets construction restart next week. The tunnel, part of a $16 bn cross‑Hudson plan, repairs a storm‑damaged tube used by 200,000 commuters and 425 trains daily. New York and New Jersey leaders see this as a big win for workers and travelers.
