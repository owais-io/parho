A Guatemalan man, Francisco Gaspar‑Andrés, died in ICE custody at a Texas army base, the first death at that camp. His wife of 25 years, Lucía Pedro Juan, was deported before she could see him. Critics say the camp has poor conditions and delayed medical care. ICE says detainees get good treatment, but lawmakers and human‑rights groups argue otherwise, calling for investigations and better oversight.
