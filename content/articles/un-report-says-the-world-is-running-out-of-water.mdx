A UN report says the world is in a water crisis, with many rivers, lakes and aquifers over‑used and polluted. 75 % of people live in places that are already water‑insecure, and cities are sinking while water‑conflicts have jumped from 20 to more than 400 in just a decade. The paper calls for cutting water withdrawals, smarter irrigation, and new rules to keep the planet’s water supplies from collapsing.
