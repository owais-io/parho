iRobot, the company that makes the Roomba vacuum, filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy and will be taken over by Picea Robotics, a Chinese supplier. The deal is meant to help the struggling company survive amid supply‑chain issues and cheaper rivals. iRobot said it can keep making and selling Roombas, and the move won’t disrupt its app or customer support. The sale has raised privacy concerns, but the company remains active.
