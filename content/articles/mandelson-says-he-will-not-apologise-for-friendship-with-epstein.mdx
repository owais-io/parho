Peter Mandelson, a Labour peer, said he won’t apologise for staying friends with Jeffrey Epstein, the convicted child sex offender. He claims he never saw anything in Epstein’s life that would make him suspect his crimes. Mandelson was removed as US ambassador after emails showed he defended Epstein. In a BBC interview he said he regrets not protecting victims but he doesn’t feel he should apologise for the friendship.
