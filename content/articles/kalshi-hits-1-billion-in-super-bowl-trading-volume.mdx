Kalshi, an online prediction market, recorded over $1 billion in trading volume during the Super Bowl, a 2,700 % jump from last year. Most money was on Bad Bunny’s opening song and his stage guests. Unlike traditional sportsbooks, Kalshi lets users trade against each other and only earns fees. The company says it protects customers, keeps deposits safe, and has stepped up checks for insider trading. The platform also gained NBA star Giannis Antetokounmpo as a shareholder.
