Prosecutors in Forlì, Italy, are investigating a 27‑year‑old ambulance driver who may have poisoned five elderly patients while transporting them to hospitals and care homes. The deaths happened during or soon after transfers, with patients suffering cardiac arrests. The driver, who works for the Red Cross, has been suspended but not arrested. Authorities are looking into more cases and the investigation could uncover more victims.
