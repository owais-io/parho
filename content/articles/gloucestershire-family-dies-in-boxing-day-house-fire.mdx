A Gloucestershire family lost a mother and two children when a house fire broke out on Boxing Day. The father, a police officer, fought to get them out but flames forced him back. The fire started on the ground floor and spread quickly. Police say it was a tragic accident, not suspicious. One child's body has been recovered, and search continues for the other. Support is offered to the community.
