An 80‑year‑old man was arrested in Salford after he tried to rob a 95‑year‑old woman. He entered her house, tied her hands, demanded cash and a purse, and threatened her. A neighbour confronted him, and he fled. Police quickly found and arrested the suspect, who is now in custody. No one was hurt, and the victim is receiving support. The police said no injuries were reported and are helping the victim.
