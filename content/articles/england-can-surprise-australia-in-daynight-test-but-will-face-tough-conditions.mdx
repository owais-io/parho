England’s next test against Australia will be played under the pink ball at twilight, a format that tests batters’ sight and timing. The team has struggled before, especially in Adelaide, and will need more match practice to adjust to shifting light and pitch. Coach Mark Ramprakash says England’s experience and recent good form give them hope, but they must stay focused, adapt quickly and keep calm in pressure moments.
