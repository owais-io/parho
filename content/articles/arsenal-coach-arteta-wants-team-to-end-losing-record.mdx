Arsenal coach Mikel Arteta says the team is ready to break the habit of starting a new year at the top of the Premier League. With a four‑point cushion over Manchester City, Arsenal travel to Bournemouth next Saturday while City faces Chelsea. Arteta praised the squad’s energy after a 4‑1 win over Aston Villa and urged them to win more games. He also confirmed Declan Rice’s knee injury will keep him out of the next match.
