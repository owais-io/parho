The World Health Organization says fighting in the Middle East is turning into a health disaster. Its regional chief, Dr Hanan Balkhy, urges an immediate end to violence and calls hospitals “safe havens.” She warns attacks on nuclear plants and water‑desalination sites could worsen the crisis, while millions of displaced people lose access to care. Chronic‑illness patients and mothers face higher risks, and mental health may suffer long‑term.
