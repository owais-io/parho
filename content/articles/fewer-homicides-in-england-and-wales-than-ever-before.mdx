In the year to September 2025, England and Wales recorded 499 homicides, a 7% drop from the previous year and the lowest total since records began in 2003. Knife‑related killings fell 23% to 174. Overall knife crime cases slipped 9%, but shoplifting rose 5%. The government added 3,000 prison spots, aims for 14,000, and plans more neighbourhood officers and £2 bn in policing investment.
