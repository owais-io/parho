Tottenham Hotspur are struggling near the bottom of the Premier League, and fans are frustrated with poor results. The club has seen two managers in quick succession: Ange Postecoglou and Thomas Frank. Both have faced injuries, defensive problems, and lackluster tactics, leading to a string of losses. Some supporters think bringing back former managers like Harry Redknapp could restore stability and push Spurs out of danger.
