Trump said Ukraine’s President Zelenskyy hasn’t read the U.S. peace proposal and isn’t ready to sign, even though Zelenskyy’s advisers like it. The deal, drafted by Trump’s team, aims to end the Russia‑Ukraine war but faces questions over security guarantees and occupied territory. Zelenskyy will meet UK, France and Germany in London to discuss the plan, while Russia says parts of it don’t work and has not approved it. The talks remain stalled.
