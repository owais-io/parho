Australia didn’t win the right to host COP31, but it made a deal with Turkey that puts Chris Bowen in charge of the talks. In this role, Bowen can push for more renewables, help Pacific islands go 100% green, and bring a big green trade fair to Adelaide. He’ll also work on green steel and try to bring major investment to Australia while keeping the country and Pacific ahead.
