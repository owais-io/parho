Brisbane’s New Farm Park was hit by a large red imported fire ant nest, forcing a women's football match to be cancelled at the last minute. The ants, an invasive species first spotted in Queensland in 2001, have spread across more than a million hectares and are now breaking containment fences. Experts warn the ants swarm aggressively, sting, and can kill people with allergies. State funding for eradication is running out, and scientists fear the problem could worsen next spring.
