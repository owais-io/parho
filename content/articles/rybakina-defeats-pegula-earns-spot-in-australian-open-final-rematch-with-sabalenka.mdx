Elena Rybakina beat Jessica Pegula 6‑3, 7‑6 in the Australian Open semi‑final. The match lasted 101 minutes and ended with a tense tiebreak in which Rybakina saved two set‑points. Her powerful serve and steadiness helped her win after Pegula pushed back in the second set. Rybakina now faces world No. 1 Aryna Sabalenka in the final, a rematch from last year’s final.
