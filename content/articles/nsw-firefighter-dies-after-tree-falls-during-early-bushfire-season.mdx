A 59‑year‑old firefighter was killed when a tree fell on him during a back burn in New South Wales. The death comes as the state faces dozens of fires that have destroyed homes and threatened communities. State leaders, including the premier, said the incident shows how risky the job is. The fire season is already harsh, with many houses lost and people evacuated.
