After marrying an Australian‑resident husband, Kiran expected to live with him in Brisbane, but he kept her in India, watching her through cameras and ordering her to do chores for his parents. When he finally brought her to Australia on a tourist visa, she had no right to stay permanently, even though her children are Australian citizens. She now faces visa uncertainty and abuse, and is fighting to keep her family together.
