England has taken Will Jacks back into the side for the second Ashes Test in Brisbane, swapping most of its fast bowlers for more spin and a stronger batting line‑up. The move follows the loss of Mark Wood and the need to adapt to day‑night conditions. Captain Ben Stokes said they’ll use a spinner to help bowlers rotate and give the new ball a better start. Australia still haven’t named their squad.
