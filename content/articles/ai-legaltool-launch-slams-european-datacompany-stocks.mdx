Anthropic unveiled a new AI tool that can draft contracts, triage NDAs and automate other legal tasks. The news rattled investors, sending shares of publishing and data firms—like Pearson, Relx, Sage, and the London Stock Exchange—down 8‑14%. The FTSE 100 fell into the red, and experts warned the tech could shrink margins or even replace human legal staff, sparking job‑loss fears.
