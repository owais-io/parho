After Napoli beat Genoa 3-2, coach Daniele De Rossi slammed VAR for the penalty that let Napoli score in injury time. His frustration echoed across Serie A, with Luciano Spalletti also complaining after Juventus had goals overturned. Both coaches say VAR now creates confusion and unfairness, and they call for clearer rules and better refereeing. The debate shows how technology is changing football tactics and the game’s fairness.
