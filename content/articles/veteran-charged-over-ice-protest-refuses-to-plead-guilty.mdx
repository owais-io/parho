A 36‑year‑old U.S. veteran named Bajun Mavalwalla has been arrested on federal conspiracy charges for helping block Immigration and Customs Enforcement agents in a June 2025 protest in Spokane, Washington. He says he never planned the protest and refuses to plead guilty. The case shows a rise in using conspiracy charges against anti‑ICE demonstrators and sparked outrage among fellow veterans and some prosecutors, including a DOJ attorney who resigned. Mavalwalla’s trial is set for May 18.
