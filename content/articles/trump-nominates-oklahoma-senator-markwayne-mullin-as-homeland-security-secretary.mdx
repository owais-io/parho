Donald Trump has chosen Oklahoma U.S. Senator Markwayne Mullin to lead the Department of Homeland Security. Mullin, known for his hard‑line stance on immigration, will replace Kristi Noem after her fallout over a controversial advertising contract. The move gives the administration a vocal supporter of strict border enforcement and mass deportation. Mullin’s background includes a lack of a bachelor’s degree and a past in plumbing and mixed martial arts.
