Pearson, a major exam company, was hit with about £2 million in fines by the UK exams regulator Ofqual for serious problems in three tests. In 2023, about 5 % of students took an online English test from home, letting others cheat. Other breaches involved uneven grading in GCSE English and too‑hard questions for non‑native speakers in A‑level Chinese. Pearson said it will improve its processes to avoid future mistakes.
