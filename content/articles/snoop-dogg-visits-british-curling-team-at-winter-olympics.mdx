Snoop Dogg appeared at the Winter Olympics in Cortina d'Ampezzo and visited the British curling team, Bruce Mouat and Jennifer Dodds. The rapper, an honorary coach for the US squad, asked the pair for a photo and tried sweeping a stone on the ice. While the Brits were unbeaten in their matches, the US team beat Canada to keep their medal hopes alive. Snoop also carried the Olympic flame earlier.
