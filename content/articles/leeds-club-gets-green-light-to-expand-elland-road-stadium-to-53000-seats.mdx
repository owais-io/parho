Leeds United’s plan to enlarge Elland Road stadium has been approved by the city council, boosting capacity from 37,645 to about 53,000. The move follows a long‑awaited permit and includes road upgrades and new parking rules to ease matchday traffic. Construction will start with the West Stand and later the North Stand, keeping the stadium open during the build. The upgrade will also help Leeds qualify to host European games.
