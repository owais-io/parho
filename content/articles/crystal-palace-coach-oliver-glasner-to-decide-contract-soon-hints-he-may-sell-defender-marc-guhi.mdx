Oliver Glasner, Crystal Palace’s coach, says he will decide in the next few weeks whether to stay. He and the club’s chair had talks, but busy fixtures put them on hold. He also said the club could sell defender Marc Guéhi this month if a good offer comes in. While his contract ends this summer, he’s being eyed by Manchester United and other big clubs.
