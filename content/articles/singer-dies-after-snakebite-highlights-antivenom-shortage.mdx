Nigerian singer Ifunanya Nwangene, 26, died in Abuja after a snakebite. Her friends tried to get antivenom, but the hospital ran out, forcing a pharmacy trip that ended too late. The death sparked a debate over Nigeria’s lack of ready antivenom supplies. WHO says the drug is essential, but shortages and training gaps keep many bites fatal.
