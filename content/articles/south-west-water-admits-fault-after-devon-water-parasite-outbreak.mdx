South West Water (SWW) has pleaded guilty to giving people unsafe water after a parasite, Cryptosporidium, spread in Brixham, Devon, in spring 2024. About 150 residents fell ill, many needing hospital care, and the contamination affected around 2,500 homes. The company faces a June sentencing and a fine of hundreds of thousands of pounds. Residents still report health problems, and the case is a reminder that water companies must keep supplies safe.
