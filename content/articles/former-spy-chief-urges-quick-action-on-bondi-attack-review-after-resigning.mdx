Former ASIO boss Dennis Richardson quit the antisemitism inquiry and says the review of the Bondi beach attack must finish soon, not wait until December. He argues that delays in looking at security and intelligence failures hurt the safety of the small Jewish community that was terrorised. Richardson feels his expertise is needed early, and that families want clear answers about why the attack happened.
