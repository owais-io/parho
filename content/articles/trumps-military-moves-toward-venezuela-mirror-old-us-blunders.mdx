Trump has moved a huge US navy force into the Caribbean to pressure Venezuela, saying it will stop drug smuggling. He even seized an oil tanker and said he could invade the country. Critics point out this is like old US “gunboat diplomacy” used by presidents such as McKinley and Roosevelt, which often backfired and hurt the region’s stability. They urge Congress and international groups to stop the risky plan before it leads to war.
