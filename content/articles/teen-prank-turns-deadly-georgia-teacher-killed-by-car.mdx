Jason Hughes, a 40‑year‑old math teacher and coach in Gainesville, Georgia, died after being run over by a car as teens drove away from a toilet‑paper prank on his house. Five teenagers were arrested; the driver, 18, faces first‑degree vehicular homicide, while the others are charged with trespassing and littering. The teens were released on bond, and Hughes’s wife wants the charges dropped, hoping to prevent further harm to the teens.
