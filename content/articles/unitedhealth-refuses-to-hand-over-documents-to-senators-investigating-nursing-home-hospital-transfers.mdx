UnitedHealth is being asked by Senators Ron Wyden and Elizabeth Warren to turn over internal records about a program that pays nursing homes bonuses for keeping residents out of hospitals. The company has said it will continue to cooperate but has not given the requested documents. The senators have warned that if UnitedHealth does not comply, they will use other means to get answers. The investigation follows reports of wrongful deaths and lawsuits over the program.
