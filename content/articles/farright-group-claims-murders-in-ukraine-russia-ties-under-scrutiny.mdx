Ukrainian branch of the banned far‑right Base group says it carried out several killings, including a car‑bomb that killed a Ukrainian border‑service officer in Odesa. The post claims the victim was a “traitor” helping Russia. The group, linked to a U.S. founder accused of spying for Moscow, has also been tied to violent cells in Spain, Belgium and the Netherlands. Ukrainian and European security officials warn it could be part of Russian sabotage.
