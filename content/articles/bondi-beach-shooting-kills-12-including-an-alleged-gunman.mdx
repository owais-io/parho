At Bondi Beach in Sydney, a shooting during a Jewish Hanukah festival killed 12 people, including an alleged gunman. Police say there may be a third shooter and found suspicious devices in a nearby vehicle. The NSW Police Commissioner and Premier called the attack a terrorist act. More than 40 ambulances and police units rushed to the scene, and the Prime Minister urged calm while the investigation continues.
