ByteDance, the owner of TikTok, says it will limit how its new AI video tool, Seedance 2.0, is used after Disney threatened legal action. Disney accused the tool of using its movie characters without permission. The company said it respects copyright and is adding safeguards to stop unauthorized use. Hollywood studios, the Motion Picture Association and actors’ union have also criticized the AI generator for copying copyrighted works.
