French winemakers are in crisis. Bad harvests, heatwaves, high costs, and falling sales have hit the sector hard. They want the government to pay for damaged vines and help turn unsold wine into biofuel. Thousands protested in Béziers, and a quarter of growers might quit, threatening 100,000 jobs. The minister will meet winemakers to discuss a rescue plan before the national wine fair.
