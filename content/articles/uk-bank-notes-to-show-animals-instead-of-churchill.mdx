Bank of England plans to replace historic figures on UK banknotes, like Winston Churchill, with images of wildlife. A July public consultation showed 60% of 44,000 respondents preferred nature over landmarks or historic people. The move has sparked protests from politicians who see it as a loss of national pride, while critics argue it avoids divisive politics. The decision mirrors similar debates in the US about changing the $20 bill.
