A broken website that looked like it was announcing Angela Rayner’s run for Labour leader showed up on a staging server in January and vanished quickly. Rayner’s team says the page was made without her permission and called it a fake or “false flag.” The glitch came while Labour’s leader, Keir Starmer, faces pressure after his chief of staff quit and other controversies. The incident has raised questions about Rayner’s possible future bid and the party’s internal politics.
