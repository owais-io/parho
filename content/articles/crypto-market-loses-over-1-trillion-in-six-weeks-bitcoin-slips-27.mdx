In just six weeks, the global crypto market has shrunk by more than $1 trillion, a quarter of its value. Bitcoin is down 27%, trading around $91,000, its lowest level since April. Investors worry that a bubble in artificial‑intelligence stocks and rising tech valuations could burst, sending markets lower. Gold has also dipped, and expectations of a U.S. interest‑rate cut are fading, adding to the uncertainty.
