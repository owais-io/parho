In a rainy FA Cup match at Hull City’s home ground, Chelsea’s Pedro Neto scored three goals to win 4‑0. Despite a strong first half from Hull and several missed chances by Chelsea, Neto turned the game around with a long‑range opener and two more from the penalty spot. The win puts Chelsea into the next round and boosts new manager Liam Rosenior’s confidence. Hull’s fight for promotion remains a distant goal.
