The UK says that toddlers who spend too much time in front of screens can learn fewer words. New research found that 2‑year‑olds who watch about five hours a day speak 53% of a test’s 34 words, while those with only 44 minutes a day use 65%. Almost all 2‑year‑olds watch TV, and the government will give parents tips in April to keep screen time healthy.
