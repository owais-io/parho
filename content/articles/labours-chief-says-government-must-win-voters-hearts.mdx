Keir Starmer’s chief of staff told ministers that Labour’s government needs to reconnect with voters emotionally. In a cabinet meeting, the Prime Minister urged the team to ignore polling and prepare to face Nigel Farage’s Reform UK. He highlighted three key tools – emotion, empathy and evidence – to rebuild trust. With polls showing the Conservatives ahead and Labour at only 17 %, Starmer wants to focus on the cost‑of‑living agenda and boost MPs’ local work.
