The Rugby Football Union wants to host more concerts at Twickenham to help pay for a £660 m upgrade. But the local council limits concerts to three a year and worries about transport and antisocial behaviour. To win support, the RFU plans to upgrade the rail station, add undercover police and other security, and push more fans onto nearby tube lines. They will meet residents at a drop‑in session and resubmit their plans after the local elections.
