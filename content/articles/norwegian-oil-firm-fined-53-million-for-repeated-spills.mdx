Equinor, Norway’s biggest oil company, is being hit with a £53 million penalty after years of oil and gas leaks at its Mongstad refinery on the North Sea coast. The fine, plus a large confiscation order, comes after investigations found 40 tonnes of gas, including dangerous hydrogen sulphide, were released between 2016‑2021. Equinor says it was not negligent and will fight the case in court.
