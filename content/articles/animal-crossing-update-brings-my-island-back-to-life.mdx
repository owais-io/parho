After a year of lockdown, the author returns to Animal Crossing: New Horizons and finds their island largely the same. The latest update and a £5 Switch 2 upgrade improve graphics and add new features like farming, a museum café, a hotel, and an easy reset tool. The author reconnects with friends, cleans up, and remembers how the game kept them sane during the pandemic. It’s a gentle reminder of the game’s calming, creative charm.
