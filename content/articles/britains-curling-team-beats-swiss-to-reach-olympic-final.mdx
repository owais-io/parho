Britain’s men’s curling team shocked the Olympics by beating the unbeaten Swiss 8‑5 in the semi‑final, thanks to a spectacular shot by Bruce Mouat. The win gave them a place in Saturday’s final against Canada. The match was electric, with Scottish fans cheering with bagpipes and songs. The British women also won their game, but a Swiss victory over the U.S. in a tense extra‑end kept Canada in the finals.
