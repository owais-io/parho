A train on Mexico’s new Interoceanic rail line in Oaxaca derailed, killing 13 people and injuring 98 of the 250 aboard. The crash adds to worries about the fast construction of the line, built to link the Atlantic and Pacific. President Claudia Sheinbaum will visit Oaxaca to find out what happened. Opponents want a full safety audit, while officials say all standards were met.
