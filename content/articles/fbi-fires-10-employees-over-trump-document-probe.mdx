After the FBI subpoenaed the phone records of FBI director Kash Patel and White House chief of staff Susie Wiles, at least 10 FBI staffers were dismissed. The firings come as the agency probes Donald Trump’s handling of classified documents found at Mar‑a‑Lago. Patel says the subpoenas were politically motivated and part of a Biden‑era “government overreach.” The case is tied to former special counsel Jack Smith’s investigation into Trump’s alleged misconduct.
