Joe Kent, the former U.S. counter‑terrorism chief, quit his job saying Trump started an unnecessary war in Iran and that the country posed no real threat. Trump called Kent’s resignation a mistake and said Iran was dangerous. Other conservatives, from Tucker Carlson to Megyn Kelly, voiced doubts. Polls show most independent voters and many young people disapprove of the war, raising worries for Republicans before the 2024 midterms.
