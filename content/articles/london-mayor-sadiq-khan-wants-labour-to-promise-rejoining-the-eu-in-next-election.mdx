Sadiq Khan, the mayor of London, wants the Labour Party to promise that voters will bring the UK back into the EU in the next general election. He says the country needs to join the customs union and single market again and that staying out has hurt London’s economy, prices and culture. Khan points to new studies that say Brexit would have cut growth by about 10 percent.
