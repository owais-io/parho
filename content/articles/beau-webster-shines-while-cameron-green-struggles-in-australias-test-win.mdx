Australia’s Test match saw Beau Webster step out of the shadows, delivering a calm 71‑not‑out and three wickets, turning a sluggish team into a winner. In contrast, fast‑bowler Cameron Green, once hailed as a rising star, struggled with bat and ball. The game shows selectors favored Green, but Webster’s all‑round display proves he deserves more chances. The story also hints at Green’s uncertain future as Australia heads to the next series.
