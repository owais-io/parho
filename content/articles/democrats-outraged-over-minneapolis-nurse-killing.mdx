A 37‑year‑old nurse, Alex Pretti, was shot dead by a Customs and Border Protection officer in Minneapolis. Democrats, including Ilhan Omar, Elizabeth Warren, and Kamala Harris, blasted the killing as an unjust execution and called for accountability. They accused the federal agency and Trump’s administration of endangering residents and demanded changes. Republicans blamed local officials for not protecting federal agents. The incident sparked a heated partisan debate about immigration enforcement in cities.
