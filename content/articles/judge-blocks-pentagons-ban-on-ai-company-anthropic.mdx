A federal judge in California has put a temporary hold on the Pentagon’s plans to ban the AI company Anthropic. The judge said the government’s move was over‑reaching and could hurt Anthropic’s business. The case centers on the company’s refusal to let the military use its Claude model for lethal weapons or mass surveillance. The injunction gives Anthropic a week before a full court hearing.
