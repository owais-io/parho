England beat New Zealand 22‑7 at Twickenham, snapping a 13‑year losing streak. The hosts trailed 12‑0 early but then stormed back with four tries, helped by George Ford’s two drop goals. New Zealand scored quickly at first but suffered a head‑injury and a yellow card, and missed chances in the second half. England’s comeback shows their hard‑work paying off and gives fans a big win.
