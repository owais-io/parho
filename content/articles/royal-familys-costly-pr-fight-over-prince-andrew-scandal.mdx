After the 2020 lawsuit from Virginia Giuffre, the royal family spent millions on legal fees and PR to keep Prince Andrew out of court. The article shows how the family hid the costs and tried to keep the story quiet, even using private money from the late Queen and the Duke of Edinburgh. It criticises the monarchy for not being transparent or helping the victim, and for playing a costly cover‑up game.
