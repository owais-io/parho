For weeks, the US has been hit by record cold that has spread from the Northeast to the South. Florida has seen its first snow, and even iguanas are falling from trees. The heat‑wave‑turned‑freeze has caused power cuts, traffic jams and at least 110 deaths across the country. Authorities urge people to stay warm, and wildlife agencies are collecting the frozen reptiles.
