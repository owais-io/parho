On the Dead Sea, 1,500 Israeli and Palestinian mothers held a peace rally. Three days later, a Hamas attack sparked a war that killed tens of thousands, mainly women and children. Despite grief and criticism, the mothers keep working together. They spoke at a Hague conference, lost friends, and now launch a new group, Mothers’ Call. In 2026 they plan to walk from Rome to Jerusalem, urging leaders to bring lasting peace.
