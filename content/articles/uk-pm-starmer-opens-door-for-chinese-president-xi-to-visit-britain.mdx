UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s recent trip to China has paved the way for Chinese President Xi Jinping to visit Britain in the future. During talks, Starmer said the relationship was no longer in an “ice age” and pushed for more trade, visas and business deals. China agreed to lift a visa ban, cut whisky tariffs and sign new investment deals, while keeping national‑security concerns in mind.
