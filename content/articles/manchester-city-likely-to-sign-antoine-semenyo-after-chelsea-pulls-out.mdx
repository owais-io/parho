Manchester City are the front runners to sign Bournemouth winger Antoine Semenyo, after Chelsea stopped chasing him. Liverpool and Manchester United also want the 25‑year‑old, who has 8 goals and 3 assists this season. His contract has a £65m release clause that kicks in January. City are thought to be his preferred move, but nothing is final yet. Chelsea said they were happy with their current forwards, so they dropped their bid.
