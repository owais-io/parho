St Vincent’s hospital in Sydney says the number of serious injuries from electric bikes (ebikes) has doubled in a year. In 2025 there were 200 cases, up from 103 in 2024 and 45 in 2023. Many riders were young adults, speeding over 25 km/h, often at night and under the influence. The injuries are often severe—broken ribs, lung damage, and even ruptured bowels—leading to surgeries or ICU care.
