Last week, about a thousand Formula One crew members had to change flights because of the Middle East conflict. The cars and gear were already shipped from Bahrain and are waiting in containers on the Albert Park track. Most drivers and engineers will still arrive for the race, but some support staff may miss the start. Teams are also watching the situation in Bahrain and Saudi Arabia, where races are coming up.
