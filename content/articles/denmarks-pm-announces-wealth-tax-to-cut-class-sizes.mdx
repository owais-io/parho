Denmark is gearing up for an early election on March 24. Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen has moved left, announcing a 0.5% wealth tax on assets over 25 million kroner. The money will lower class sizes for 6‑to‑9‑year‑olds from about 26 to 14. The tax also follows a tougher stance on immigration and a growing support after the Greenland dispute, and it aims to strengthen the welfare state.
