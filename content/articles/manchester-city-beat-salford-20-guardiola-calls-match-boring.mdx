Manchester City won 2‑0 against Salford in the Carabao Cup, with defender Marc Guéhi scoring his first goal for the club. Despite a solid first‑half effort, City were slow and struggled to create chances. Guardiola said the game was “boring” and that his team didn’t read the spaces well. The win keeps City in the competition, but the manager admitted the squad needed to be more fluid.
