Dylan Pietsch, a Wallabies winger, keeps his mind clear by painting his teammates' boots before the 2020 Olympics. The 27‑year‑old Wiradjuri artist uses dotting to stay present and fights depression with art, meditation and journaling. He’s opened up about a suicide attempt and his broken jaw, and now talks to schools and Indigenous groups about mental health. He’s also getting married and painting bigger canvases while staying ready for the 2026 World Cup.
