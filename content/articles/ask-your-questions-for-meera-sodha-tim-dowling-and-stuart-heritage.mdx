Guardian Live is hosting a Q&A on Nov 26 at 7 p.m. in London’s Kings Place, with chefs, writers and comedians. Meera Sodha, Tim Dowling and Stuart Heritage will chat about everything from holiday recipes to pop‑culture moments, joined by Nish Kumar and Georgina Lawton. Readers can send in questions about cooking, comedy or culture, and a handful will be featured live. Tickets are available for in‑person or online viewing.
