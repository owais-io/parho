On Wednesday morning, a drone strike hit the upscale neighbourhood of Himbi in Goma, eastern DRC, killing three people, including a French UN aid worker named Karine Buisset. The attack, blamed by rebel spokesman Lawrence Kanyuka on the Congolese government, occurred while M23 rebels control the city. The French president called for respect for humanitarian law. The incident shows how drone warfare is being used more in the ongoing conflict.
