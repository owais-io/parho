U.S. officials, including Jared Kushner and Steve Witkoff, met Ukraine’s top negotiator in Miami for a third straight day. Both sides said real progress depends on Russia showing real commitment to peace. Meanwhile, European leaders are discussing using frozen Russian assets to fund Ukraine, while Russia warns this would be theft. Russia also continues drone attacks on Ukrainian infrastructure and responds to Ukrainian strikes in Russia.
