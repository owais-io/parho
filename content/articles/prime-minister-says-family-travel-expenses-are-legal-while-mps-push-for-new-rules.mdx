Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said his family travel costs follow the rules after spending about $2,800 on tickets and travel for the AFL grand final, State of Origin and the Australian Open. Other ministers also used the allowance for sporting events. Some MPs and opposition leaders say the rules are too generous and should be tightened. A government spokesperson says all disclosures were correct. Critics call for a fresh review of travel entitlements.
