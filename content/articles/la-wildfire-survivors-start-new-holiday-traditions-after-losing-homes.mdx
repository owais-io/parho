After January wildfires, many Los Angeles residents lost their homes and holiday decorations. Michael and Anne Holland, who lost their Altadena house, are now living in a Pasadena apartment and hope to plant a small tree for Christmas. Others, like Jodie Ludwin, had to leave the city for a second home in the UK. Some families kept precious items thanks to neighbors. A first rebuilt home opened, giving hope for the season.
