Aston Villa coach Unai Emery told the media that dreaming is free, but he is focused on tomorrow’s game against Arsenal, which could keep Villa in the Premier League title race. He reflected on last season’s struggles, his wish to win the Champions League, and said Villa learned from a slow start. The team will also miss suspended players Matty Cash and Boubacar Kamara. Emery questioned the league’s schedule, which has Villa playing Arsenal twice before meeting Nottingham Forest.
