After finishing third in the Champions Cup, Gotham FC said the tournament didn’t match their growth plans. They had to pull players back from the off‑season, play in London, and juggle training schedules, hurting their preseason. The club focused on player health and well‑being, while owners stepped up with money and support. Gotham is now talking with league officials about a fairer format that lets teams invest fully.
