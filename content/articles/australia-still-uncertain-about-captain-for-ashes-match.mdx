Australia is still deciding who will lead the team for the Ashes series against England, as captain Pat Cummins may or may not be fit to play. The selectors have had a confusing selection saga, with players like Usman Khawaja being added then dropped. England already chose a T20 fast bowler for the fourth spot. The uncertainty over Cummins leaves fans wondering who will captain the side, but the match will go ahead as planned.
