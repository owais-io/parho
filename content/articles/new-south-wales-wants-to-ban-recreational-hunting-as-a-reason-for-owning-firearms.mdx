After the Bondi massacre, the Greens, independent MPs and gun‑safety groups are asking the NSW government to stop letting people keep guns for “recreational hunting.” They say it would cut the state’s gun licence numbers from over 1.1 million. The plan also includes tighter rules on who can own a gun, how many they can have, and how licences are renewed. The government is preparing a bill that could bring the toughest gun laws to Australia.
