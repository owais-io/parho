New emails show that Jeffrey Epstein and a high‑profile Manhattan lawyer tried to use the legal system to get a woman out of the United States. They discussed revoking her tourist visa, arresting her, and keeping an eye on her movements. The woman was a nuisance to Epstein’s friend, Leon Black, who had legal disputes with her. The plan never succeeded, and the woman was never deported.
