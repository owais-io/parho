FIFA has teamed up with TikTok to turn the World Cup into a social‑media explosion. The deal gives TikTok creators inside access, archive footage, and a chance to share behind‑the‑scenes moments. In return, creators are expected to post tons of content that will keep fans talking on the platform. The move could bring more viewers to FIFA’s own streams, open new advertising revenue, and help FIFA stay in control of the conversation around the tournament.
