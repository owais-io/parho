After a US aide said the U.S. might take Greenland by force, leaders from the UK, France, Germany, Denmark, and other European countries issued a joint statement saying Greenland belongs to its people and that only Denmark and Greenland can decide its future. The leaders also pledged new security help for Ukraine. The U.S. still talks about buying or using force on Greenland, but Denmark says an attack would threaten NATO.
