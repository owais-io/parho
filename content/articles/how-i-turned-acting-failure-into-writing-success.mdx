My first year after school was a mess. I chased acting, moved to the city, but kept missing auditions and ended up working kitchens, bars and even selling potatoes. I applied for unemployment and got a letter from the Department of Social Security offering a writing job. Realizing my love of stories, I switched to studying writing. The hard days became building blocks that helped me find a new career and feel proud.
