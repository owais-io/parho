Arsenal crushed Wigan 4‑0 in the FA Cup, with Eberechi Eze scoring the winner and creating two more chances. The Gunners took a 4‑0 lead early and showed strong attacking play. Injuries to key players like Martin Ødegaard and Kai Havertz worried the club, but Eze’s return gave hope. Wigan struggled, and Arsenal’s win keeps their quadruple dream alive.
