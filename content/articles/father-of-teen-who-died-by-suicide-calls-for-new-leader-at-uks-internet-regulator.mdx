Ian Russell, the father of 14‑year‑old Molly Russell who took her own life after seeing harmful online content, says the UK regulator Ofcom is not doing enough to protect children online. He wants a new head of Ofcom and criticises delays in the Online Safety Act and a stalled probe into a suicide forum that was still reachable in the UK. Russell and other bereaved families are urging the government to impose stricter transparency rules on tech firms.
