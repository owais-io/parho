In 2026, ten tech firms could list on the stock market, each worth over $100 billion. Big names like OpenAI, Anthropic, SpaceX, and Stripe are eyeing IPOs, while crypto exchange Kraken and design tool Canva also show interest. The move comes amid an AI boom, political uncertainty, and high investor appetite. Success or failure of these flotations could signal whether the AI‑era market is a bubble or sustainable growth.
