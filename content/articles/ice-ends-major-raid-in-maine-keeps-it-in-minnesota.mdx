Federal immigration agents stopped a big enforcement push in Maine after about 200 people were detained. Senator Susan Collins said the raid, known as "Operation Catch of the Day," is over and only normal ICE activity will continue. Meanwhile, border czar Tom Homan says the larger operation in Minnesota will stay on. The move follows protests, political pressure, and a failed funding vote. People in Maine feel relief, but concerns remain about future raids.
