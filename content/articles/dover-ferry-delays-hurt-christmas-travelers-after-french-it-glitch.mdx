Thousands of people heading to Dover for Christmas faced delays because of IT glitches in French border control. The port warned passengers not to arrive more than two hours early and to plan extra travel time. Despite long queues on the approach roads, checking in on the ferry went smoothly. Experts said patience and route‑checking are key, and National Highways will cut roadworks to help traffic flow.
