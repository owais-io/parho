Team GB skier Gus Kenworthy made headlines at the Winter Olympics when he wrote the phrase “Fuck ICE” in his own urine on the snow before his half‑pipe run. He used the post to demand that U.S. senators curb the power of Immigration and Customs Enforcement. The protest happened while ICE officers guarded the U.S. vice‑president in Milan, and the IOC said athletes can share views on social media. Kenworthy has a history of speaking out on human‑rights issues.
