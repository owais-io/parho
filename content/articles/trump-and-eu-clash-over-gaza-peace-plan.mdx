At the Munich Security Conference, EU foreign‑policy chief Kaja Kallas and Spain’s minister said Donald Trump’s new “Board of Peace” for Gaza ignores the UN’s original rules and excludes European donors. They warned the board lets Trump control money that could go to his allies. Trump’s top aide said the focus should be on fast aid, weapons removal and ending Gaza’s split. The dispute shows deep tension over how to end the Gaza war.
