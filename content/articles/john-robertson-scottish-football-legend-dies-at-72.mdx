John Robertson, the iconic Scottish footballer and former Nottingham Forest star, died on Christmas morning at 72. He helped Forest rise from the second tier to win two European Cups, scoring key goals in 1979 and 1981. Robertson earned 28 caps for Scotland, including a win over England. After retiring, he coached at several clubs. He is survived by his wife, daughter and many fans.
