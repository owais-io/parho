Modern Milkman, a UK dairy delivery company, is now picking up old electronics and toys while delivering milk and other groceries. For £2.50 you get a bag to drop your broken phones, laptops or toys into, which the company sends to EMR Group for recycling. The move tackles the growing e‑waste problem, expands the business beyond its 100,000‑home network, and could grow into other waste types.
