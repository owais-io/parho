Swing Left, a grassroots Democratic group, is revamping the party’s door‑knocking approach ahead of the 2024 midterms. Rather than chasing only registered voters at the last minute, volunteers now walk around neighborhoods early, chat with anyone they meet, and record open‑ended conversations. AOC and other lawmakers joined a test run in Kingston, NY. The talks are fed into AI to uncover real feelings, hoping to rebuild trust and boost turnout.
