England captain Zak Crawley said his team will still try to win on the final day of the third Test in Adelaide, even though they’re chasing a huge target of 435. He admitted it’s a tough fight and that they’re “staring down the barrel,” but he promised they’ll never give up. Crawley also praised opener Ollie Pope, defended his own comments about “no weak men,” and praised the Australian bowlers for being hard to score against.
