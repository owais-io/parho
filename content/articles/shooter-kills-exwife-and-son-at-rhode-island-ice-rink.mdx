A 56‑year‑old man opened fire at a high‑school hockey game in Pawtucket, Rhode Island, killing his ex‑wife Rhonda Dorgan and their son Aidan. He also wounded the ex‑wife’s parents and a friend. The shooter died after shooting himself. Bystanders helped stop him, and the crowd fled the arena. Police are still investigating. The state governor offered grief support, following a recent university shooting.
