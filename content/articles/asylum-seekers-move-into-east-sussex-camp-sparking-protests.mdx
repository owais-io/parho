The Home Office has begun putting asylum seekers into a former military base in Crowborough, East Sussex. 27 men arrived early Thursday, with plans to bring 500 in the next months. Local residents have already protested, raising money for legal challenges. The move is part of a plan to replace hotel housing with larger facilities, but the town council and nearby communities are pushing back, arguing it could hurt local life and safety.
