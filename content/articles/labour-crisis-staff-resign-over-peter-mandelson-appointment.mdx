Keir Starmer's team has been in turmoil after Peter Mandelson was chosen as Britain's ambassador to Washington, sparking resignations and calls for the prime minister to quit. Morgan McSweeney, his chief of staff, quit, followed by Tim Allan, No 10’s head of communications. Labour leader Anas Sarwar said Starmer should step down to avoid a May election disaster. Some ministers back Starmer, while others condemn the appointment, which was criticised for ties to Jeffrey Epstein.
