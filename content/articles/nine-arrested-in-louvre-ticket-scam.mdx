Nine people, including two Louvre staff and tour guides, were arrested by French police in a €10m ticket‑scam investigation. The scheme reused tickets to bring Chinese groups into the museum, with staff allegedly helping. Authorities seized almost €1m in cash and are probing money‑laundering links. The Louvre is still recovering from a recent jewel heist and water‑leak, and unions are striking over renovations and pay.
