The former chief of the West Midlands police, Craig Guildford, was told to retire after a report found that police intelligence was exaggerated when banning Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a football match. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood said she lost confidence in him. The police commissioner asked the Independent Office for Police Conduct to investigate Guildford’s conduct and any misleading evidence. The watchdog will look into possible misconduct.
