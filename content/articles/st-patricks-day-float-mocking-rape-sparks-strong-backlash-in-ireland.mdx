An Irish St. Patrick’s Day float that staged a sexual‑assault scene and carried the words “Epstein files” drew furious criticism. Rape‑crisis charities, politicians and local councillors called the display a disgrace, saying it made sexual violence look like a joke. Organisers later apologized, stopped a second run and pledged to improve vetting for future parades. The float also showed an Irish flag and a sign saying “Prince Andrew goin to jail.”
