Liverpool has signed 20‑year‑old centre‑back Jérémy Jacquet for £55m. Meanwhile, Chelsea are interested in 18‑year‑old Tylel Tati, who could cost around £26m. Manchester United can’t stop Barcelona’s £26m buy‑out clause for Marcus Rashford, so his future is still unclear. Other moves include a possible loan of Everton’s Tyrique George, Zinchenko’s switch to Ajax, and several players heading to Italy and Germany.
