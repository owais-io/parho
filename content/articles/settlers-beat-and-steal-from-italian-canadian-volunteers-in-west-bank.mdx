Three Italians and one Canadian volunteer were beaten and robbed by Israeli settlers in the West Bank village of Ein al‑Duyuk. The attackers burst into the house at 4:30 a.m., beat them for 15 minutes, and destroyed the home’s solar panels. The volunteers were hospitalized, and the incident follows a rise in settler violence after a new outpost opened nearby. Italy, Canada and the UN condemned the attack and called for action.
