The Guardian’s consumer team tested presents, kitchen gadgets and Christmas trees to find the most sustainable and long‑lasting options. They show you how to choose gifts that won’t break or end up in a recycle bin, how a well‑chosen fake tree can lower emissions, and ways to reduce food waste with reusable crackers and leftovers. They also give future‑proof tips—secondhand shopping, fixing items and avoiding wasteful buys.
