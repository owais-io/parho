Three people were arrested in Spain in a bust of the Base, a worldwide neo‑Nazi terrorist group. Police say the trio trained in weapons and paramilitary tactics and used social media to recruit more. They were found with guns, knives, ammo and racist material. The leader had links to the U.S. founder of Base. The case shows Spain is cracking down on white‑power cells.
