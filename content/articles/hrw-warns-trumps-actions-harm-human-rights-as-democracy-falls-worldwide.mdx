Human Rights Watch says Donald Trump’s last year of office has weakened democracy in the United States and around the world. The report says the planet is slipping into a “democratic recession”, with almost three‑quarters of people living under autocratic leaders. Trump’s policies, according to HRW, attack elections, the courts, free speech and other rights. The group urges democracies like the UK, EU and Canada to unite and defend the rules‑based order.
