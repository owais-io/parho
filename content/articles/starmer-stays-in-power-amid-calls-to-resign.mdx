UK newspapers report that Labour leader Keir Starmer has survived a push from Scottish Labour leader Anas Sarwar to resign after a scandal involving former cabinet minister Peter Mandelson. The scandal forced Starmer’s chief of staff to leave, and some party members blame him. Despite the pressure, the cabinet has largely backed Starmer, and rivals such as Wes Streeting are watching. The story shows growing unrest inside the party.
