England’s winger Immanuel Feyi‑Waboso hurt a hamstring and will miss the rest of the Six Nations, leaving the squad short for the Scotland game. Coach Steve Borthwick also worries about George Furbank and Ellis Genge, who have head and hamstring injuries. England are on a 12‑match winning streak but still need a victory at Scotland’s Murrayfield to finish unbeaten. New players Ollie Lawrence and Fin Smith have joined the squad.
