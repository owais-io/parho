Col Christos Flessas, a 54‑year‑old squadron leader in the Greek air force, was arrested for allegedly passing secret NATO and Greek military information to China. He says he was lured by money and didn’t know he was spying. Greek authorities, tipped by the CIA, are putting him on trial and he could face life in prison. The case shows how China is trying to get inside Europe’s defence systems.
