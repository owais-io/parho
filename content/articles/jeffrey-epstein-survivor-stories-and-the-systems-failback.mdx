New files about Epstein show a web of powerful men who turned a serious abuse case into a political spectacle. Survivors, who have been waiting for justice for years, are frustrated by the slow, piecemeal release of evidence and the way it keeps them in the background. Lawyers say the documents expose a culture of entitlement, but the law has yet to hold most named figures accountable, leaving victims still in pain.
