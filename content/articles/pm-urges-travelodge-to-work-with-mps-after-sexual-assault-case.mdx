Keir Starmer wrote to Travelodge’s chief executive, demanding the hotel cooperate with MPs following a sexual assault that happened when staff gave a keycard to an attacker. MPs want to know why security failed. Travelodge has started an independent review and made quick changes to room‑access rules. Starmer says the review must be fast and focus on preventing future assaults.
