OpenAI will show short ads next to ChatGPT answers for people in the U.S. The first tests start soon, but only adults over 18 and not on sensitive topics. Ads will appear in a simple box above or below replies and will be linked to what the user is chatting about. The move is part of a plan to bring in extra money while keeping subscriptions and a new low‑cost plan.
