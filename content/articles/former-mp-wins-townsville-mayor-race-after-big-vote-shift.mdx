Nick Dametto, a former state MP who quit his party last month, won Townsville’s mayoral by‑election with 61 % of the vote – a huge swing from the 2024 result. The election followed the resignation of former mayor Troy Thompson, who stepped down after accusations of lying about his military service. Dametto’s win was welcomed by the Queensland premier, and his opponent, Ann‑Maree Greaney, has conceded.
