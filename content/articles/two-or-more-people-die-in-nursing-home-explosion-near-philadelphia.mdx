An explosion at Silver Lake healthcare center, a nursing home outside Philadelphia, collapsed part of the building. The blast happened around 2 pm after workers smelled gas. At least two people were killed and five are missing. Rescue crews shut off gas and electricity and tried to pull survivors out. Firefighters, police and emergency teams are still working on the scene, while officials say the cause is unclear and may be a gas leak.
