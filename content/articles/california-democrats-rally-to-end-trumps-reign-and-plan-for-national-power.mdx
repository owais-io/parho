California Democrats met in San Francisco this weekend to rally against President Trump and prepare for the 2024 mid‑term elections. Delegates praised recent state victories and California’s role as a model for the national party. Speakers warned that Trump’s policies hurt California, and they urged unity ahead of the governor’s race. The event also highlighted divisions over tech company influence and the need to fight Trump’s legacy. Democrats aim to shape national debate with progressive ideas.
