An appeals court said the Trump administration can stop Medicaid money from going to Planned Parenthood in 22 states and D.C. The ruling cancels a judge’s order that had blocked the move. Planned Parenthood says this will close many of its centers and hurt people who need reproductive care. The decision is part of a long fight over a law that bars Medicaid payments to abortion‑providing groups.
