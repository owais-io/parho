Rebecca English, the Daily Mail’s royal editor, denies that she hired private investigator Mike Behr to obtain flight and other details about Prince Harry and Chelsy Davy. Emails show Behr requesting “blagged” information, but English insists she never asked for it and that any data came from other sources. The case is part of a broader lawsuit accusing the paper of illegal information gathering.
