Tottenham Hotspur’s boss Thomas Frank kept training as the club’s future hangs in the balance. After a 2‑1 loss to West Ham that left Spurs 14th and fans calling for a sacking, the board has been reluctant to act. Frank welcomed former Ajax coach John Heitinga as a new assistant and told the team to stay focused ahead of their Champions League match against Borussia Dortmund. Fans are angry, but the club remains cautious.
