In Sydney, a gunman killed at least 15 people during a Hanukah menorah lighting organized by the Chabad-Lubavitch group, a global Jewish outreach movement. Chabad runs public lightings to encourage holiday celebration and offers community services worldwide. The group has faced past violence, but this attack was a major blow. Despite the tragedy, many Hanukah events around the world continued, even drawing larger crowds as people showed support.
