A passenger from Bristol to Dublin was stuck on a Ryanair plane for six hours after the flight was diverted to Manchester. Ryanair said the passenger had missed a “phantom” rebooking to Dublin, so no refund or expense payout was offered. After the passenger challenged the claim, Ryanair eventually refunded the ticket and agreed to cover hotel and transport costs, but still denied complimentary drinks and had poor customer‑service communication.
