Jackie O says she was forced out of the Kyle and Jackie O Show, not that she quit. She claims the media spread misinformation after Kyle Sandilands’ on‑air misconduct and plans legal action. The show was pulled following serious misconduct allegations, and Sandilands was suspended. The pair had a $200m contract and faced regulator sanctions for offensive content.
