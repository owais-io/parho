Overnight, the US said it carried out airstrikes on Venezuela and captured President Nicolás Maduro and his wife, flying them out of the country. President Trump posted a photo on Truth Social and claimed the US would run the country until a new leader is chosen. The move follows months of US pressure, including naval blockades and arrests of Venezuelan officials. Venezuela’s military remains in control, and it is unclear what will happen next.
