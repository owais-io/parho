The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission asked the University of Pennsylvania to give it a list of every Jewish faculty, staff, and student. Penn’s faculty and Jewish groups say the request is a threat to privacy and a dangerous echo of history. They sued the EEOC and asked a federal court to stop the subpoena. The university has already shared documents about antisemitism, but refuses to hand over personal data. This move has shocked the campus community.
