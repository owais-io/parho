Cristian Romero, Tottenham’s captain, blasted the club’s squad after the January transfer window, calling it “disgraceful”. He posted on Instagram after the team, weakened by injuries, lost to Manchester City 2‑0 and later drew 2‑2. Tottenham had only two new signings and one sale, leaving the squad thin. Romero’s comments angered manager Thomas Frank, who defended the club’s approach and said they would keep things internal.
