A US refueling plane crashed in western Iraq, killing six service members. The Pentagon named the crew: Maj John Klinner, Capt Ariana Savino, Tech Sgt Ashley Pruitt, Capt Seth Koval, Capt Curtis Angst, and Tech Sgt Tyler Simmons. The crash, not caused by enemy fire, happened during a routine mission and left at least 13 Americans dead in Operation Epic Fury. Families mourn their loved ones while the military honors them as heroes.
