Donald Trump said he only needs his own conscience to stop him, not international law. He talked about trying to control Greenland, the situation with Venezuela and worries about China and Taiwan. Trump downplayed concerns about a Chinese takeover of Taiwan, said Russia's treaty expiry is fine, and said they’d just make a better agreement. He also mentioned protests after an ICE shooting in Minneapolis and strained ties with European allies for the public.
