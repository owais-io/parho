The United States kept its Olympic figure‑skating team title by beating Japan in a close finish. The showdown came after Japan’s skaters made a comeback in the pairs and women’s free programs. In the final men’s free, 21‑year‑old Ilia Malinin landed five big jumps and earned a solid score that put the U.S. ahead. Italy took bronze, while Madison Chock and Evan Bates helped secure the win. It’s the first time the U.S. has defended the title on the ice.
