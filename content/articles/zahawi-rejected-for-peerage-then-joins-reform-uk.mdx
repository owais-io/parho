After being fired as Tory party chair for tax problems, Nadhim Zahawi asked the Conservatives for a peerage but was turned down. A few weeks later he left the Tories and joined Reform UK, the new party led by Nigel Farage. Zahawi says the country is headed for unrest and that only Farage can stop it. His move shows Reform is becoming a home for disgraced politicians.
