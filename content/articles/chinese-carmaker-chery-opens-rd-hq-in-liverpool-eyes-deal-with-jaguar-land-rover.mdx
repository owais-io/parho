Chinese carmaker Chery has announced it will set up a research and development headquarters in Liverpool. The move could open the door for a deal that would let Jaguar Land Rover (JLR) build Chery electric cars in the UK. Chery plans to use the Liverpool office for design, engineering and sales work. UK officials and JLR leaders are already talking about using JLR’s spare factory space in Halewood for production, but no final agreement exists yet.
