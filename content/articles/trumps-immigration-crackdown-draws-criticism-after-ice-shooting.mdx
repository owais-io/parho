In early 2024, ICE officers in Minnesota shot a U.S. citizen, Alex Pretti, and had previously killed nurse Renee Good. Trump’s administration labeled the victims as terrorists, sparking public outrage. Polls show 58% think ICE is too harsh. Trump softened his comments, saying Pretti wasn’t an assassin, but still faces calls to curb ICE. The incident highlights growing anger over Trump’s immigration enforcement.
