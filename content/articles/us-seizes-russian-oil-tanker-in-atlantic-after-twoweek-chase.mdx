The United States boarded and seized a Russian‑flagged oil tanker called Marinera in the Atlantic after a two‑week chase that began in the Caribbean. The move was part of a crackdown on ships that the U.S. says are breaking sanctions. Russia protested, sent a submarine to protect the tanker, and demanded the crew be treated humanely. The U.S. said it will keep targeting sanctioned ships, even those linked to Venezuela.
