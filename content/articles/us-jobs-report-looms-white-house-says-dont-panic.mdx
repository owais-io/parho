Investors are keeping a close eye on next week’s US non‑farm payrolls, which are expected to add about 70,000 jobs in January. The White House, via officials like Kevin Hassett, is urging markets not to overreact and to expect smaller numbers in the future. Meanwhile, beer giant Heineken announced it will cut 5,000‑6,000 jobs worldwide as demand slows and it refocuses on growth.
