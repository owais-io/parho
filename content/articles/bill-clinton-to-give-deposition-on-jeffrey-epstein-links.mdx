Bill Clinton will give a deposition next Friday for a House committee looking into his ties to Jeffrey Epstein. The hearing will be held behind closed doors, with a video released later. Hillary Clinton has already testified and called the probe political theatre. The committee wants to know why Clinton flew on Epstein’s private jet in the 2000s, although he denies any wrongdoing. Neither Clinton has been charged with a crime.
