Ahmad Ali, a 12‑year‑old from Swindon, was stuck in Dubai airport for three nights while his family waited for a flight back to the UK. Emirates staff looked after him and helped keep him safe amid missile strikes that began Saturday. He was reunited with his parents at Heathrow and expressed gratitude for the airline’s care. Other families also endured long delays and unusual sheltering, all reflecting the broader travel chaos caused by the conflict.
