Ueno Zoo in Tokyo is preparing to send its two pandas, Xiao Xiao and Lei Lei, back to China. The animals, loaned since 2021, will leave next week after a farewell visit by thousands of fans. Their departure marks the end of the panda‑diplomacy program between Japan and China, a friendship symbol that has weakened amid disputes over Taiwan and the Senkaku islands. No new pandas are expected to come to Japan for now.
