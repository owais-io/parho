Liverpool coach Arne Slot praised the support from both Liverpool and Wolves fans as they celebrated the late striker Diogo Jota at Anfield. The tribute saw Jota’s young sons as mascots and fans of each club singing each other's songs, showing respect across the rivalry. Wolves players visited the memorial the night before. Liverpool beat Wolves 2‑1, with Florian Wirtz scoring his first goal for the club. The match highlighted shared respect for Jota.
