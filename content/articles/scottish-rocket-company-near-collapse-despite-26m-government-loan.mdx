A British rocket maker called Orbex is close to folding, putting 150 jobs in danger. The firm received £26 million in government loans, but no new funding has come through. Talks with the Treasury‑backed National Wealth Fund failed, and a possible German buyer didn't materialise. The company is still looking for a rescue deal or merger to survive, while the UK government says it will keep supporting the space sector.
