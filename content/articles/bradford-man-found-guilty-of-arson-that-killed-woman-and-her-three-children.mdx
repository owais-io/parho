Sharaz Ali, 40, was found guilty of setting a house on fire in Bradford that killed Bryonie Gawith’s sister and her three children (ages 9, 5 and 22 months). He did it after his former girlfriend, Antonia Gawith, ended their abusive seven‑year relationship. Ali was also convicted of attempted murder of Gawith. He was driven by jealousy, drugs and alcohol. A co‑accused, Calum Sunderland, was found guilty of manslaughter.
