In the Gorton and Denton by‑election, Reform UK’s Matt Goodwin avoided a penalty after a court ruled that the party’s name and address were missing from about 81,000 leaflets by mistake. The judge said the omission was caused by a printing error – a font change that cut off the imprint – and that Goodwin and his agent had acted in good faith and corrected it. No £5,000 fine or disqualification will be imposed.
