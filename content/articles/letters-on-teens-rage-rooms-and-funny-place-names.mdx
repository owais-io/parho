A handful of letters discuss why some people still feel like teenagers even in their seventies, whether rage rooms help us vent, and how towns sometimes name everything after themselves. One writer jokes his future wife warned him he’d always act 17. Another laughs that Manly, a Sydney suburb, names almost everything after itself. Readers also mention odd names like “Ugley Women’s Institute” and a quirky codeword, and are invited to send their own letters.
