AstraZeneca's chief executive, Pascal Soriot, earned a 6.4% pay increase last year, bringing his total earnings to £17.7 million. He earned £1.5 million in salary, £4.3 million in annual bonuses and £11.6 million in long‑term share bonuses. Next year could push his pay to about £19.6 million. The company also cancelled a £450 million vaccine expansion and a £200 million research investment. Its shares jumped 32% last year, with revenues up 8 % and profits up 11 % from strong cancer drug sales.
