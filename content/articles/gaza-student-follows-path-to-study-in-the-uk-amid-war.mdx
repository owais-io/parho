The war in Gaza, which began after an October 2023 attack, has left the region’s education system on the brink of collapse. Amid the violence, some students fled. One of them, 27‑year‑old Aseel Mousa, left Gaza to study in Leicester. She worked as a journalist before the conflict, then earned a student visa, but she now carries guilt for her success while family members and classmates are trapped or dead. The story shows how the conflict forces dreams into exile.
