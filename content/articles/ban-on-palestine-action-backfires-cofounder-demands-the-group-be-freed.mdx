The UK Supreme Court said the ban on Palestine Action was unlawful and too harsh, citing violations of protest and free‑speech rights. The proscription order still stands while the Home Secretary’s lawyers argue for its appeal, leaving over 2,500 members in limbo. Co‑founder Huda Ammori insists the government will fight to keep the ban, but the decision marks a major legal victory for the group and its supporters.
