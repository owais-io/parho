Thousands of seafarers are stuck on a tanker off the coast of Dubai after the Strait of Hormuz was closed amid rising tensions with Iran. With no GPS or reliable internet, the crew can only send brief updates to their owners. They fear attacks from Iran’s Revolutionary Guards, and the situation has pushed oil prices higher. Seafaring unions are urging governments to help bring the crews home and keep them safe.
