Mermelstein is a street photographer from New Jersey who moved to NYC in 1979. He captured a strange image of a man in a suit holding a bottle of Windex on 50th Street. The photo feels mysterious and alive. It was part of his book *Sidewalk*, earned him a European award, and he keeps photographing everyday moments. His work shows the quiet beauty of city life, and he loves color to bring moments to life.
