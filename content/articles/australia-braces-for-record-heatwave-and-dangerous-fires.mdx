Australia is warning of a massive heatwave that could hit record temperatures across Victoria, South Australia and New South Wales. The hot spell will last at least five days, with some spots reaching 48‑49 °C. Fires already burning in Victoria, South Australia and the Wimmera are under extreme fire danger, and many areas face days of 40 °C-plus heat. No rain is expected for two weeks.
