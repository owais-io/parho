On its first day after a brief reopening, only a handful of sick and wounded Palestinians were able to cross the Rafah border into Egypt. Israel and Egypt ran joint security checks that let just 27 people – five patients and 22 others – pass through. Many more, including cancer patients and those needing urgent care, still wait in Egypt or cannot leave at all, while the crossing remains a key, limited lifeline.
