Florida Governor Ron DeSantis spent $1.2 million a day from taxpayers’ money to run the hard‑to‑reach Alligator Alcatraz detention center. He and his team had promised that the federal government would cover the cost, but a federal review says that money may not come. The jail is also under a lawsuit for environmental damage. The case shows that Florida may owe the state more than $600 million.
