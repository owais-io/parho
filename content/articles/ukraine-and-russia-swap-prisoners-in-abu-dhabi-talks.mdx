U.S. officials helped Kyiv and Moscow meet in Abu Dhabi for a second round of talks, where the two sides swapped many prisoners. The meetings didn’t break the conflict, but both sides said they’d keep negotiating. U.S. Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent said new sanctions could be added if talks fail. Meanwhile, Russia’s economy is strained and the city of Belgorod near the border is being hit by shelling.
