Keir Starmer’s former communications chief, Matthew Doyle, has had the Labour whip removed from his new role in the House of Lords after it was revealed he campaigned for a friend, Sean Morton, who was convicted of holding indecent images of children. Doyle apologized for his ties, but the scandal has dented Starmer’s leadership and sparked criticism over how the party handled the situation.
