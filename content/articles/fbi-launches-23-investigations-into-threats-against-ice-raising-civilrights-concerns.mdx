The FBI has begun investigations into threats against ICE in at least 23 parts of the country, based on a memo from former President Trump called NSPM‑7. The memo labels anti‑ICE protests “political violence” and urges law‑enforcement to look for signs like gun stockpiling or online research about ICE agents. Civil‑rights groups warn the language is vague and could put protestors under surveillance. Two recent violent attacks on ICE facilities were cited. The FBI has not responded to questions.
