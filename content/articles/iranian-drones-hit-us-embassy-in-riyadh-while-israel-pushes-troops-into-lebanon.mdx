During a widening Middle East war, Iranian drones struck the U.S. embassy in Riyadh, sparking a small fire and prompting Americans to stay away from the compound. Iran kept hitting U.S. bases in the Gulf and launched rockets at Israel, while Hezbollah fired missiles at northern Israel. Israel began moving soldiers into southern Lebanon to stop Hezbollah attacks. The fighting has spread to at least nine countries and hit oil facilities, pushing up global gas prices.
