An undercover officer for Scotland Yard, who used the alias Simon Wellings, accidentally called an activist and left a recording that caught him talking to special‑branch officers about campaigners. Activists recognised his voice, and he was kicked out of the group Globalise Resistance. He later admitted to filing thousands of reports—some exaggerated—on left‑wing activists. A public inquiry now looks into how 139 spies monitored activists over decades.
