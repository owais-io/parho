The 2028 Los Angeles Games will kick off with the women’s 100m final on day one, a first‑day headline event that will run all three rounds in a single day. Top sprinters like Sha’Carri Richardson and Dina Asher‑Smith welcomed the move, saying it celebrates a century of women in Olympic sprinting. Organisers say the schedule lets many athletes compete in two events, and the opening day will feature the most women’s finals ever.
