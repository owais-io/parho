Telstra and consulting firm Accenture are launching a $700 million joint venture to speed up AI projects. As part of the plan, they’ll cut about 209 jobs, with some moved to India. Affected staff will get help finding new roles or receive transition support. The cuts follow Telstra’s earlier 2,800‑job reduction in its enterprise arm, but customers still see no change. The goal is cost savings and quicker AI delivery.
