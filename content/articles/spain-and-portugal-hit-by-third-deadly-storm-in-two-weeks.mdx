Storm Marta struck Spain and Portugal on Saturday, bringing heavy rain that killed two people. It’s the third fatal storm in the area in just two weeks, following Storm Kristin (five deaths) and Storm Leonardo (one death). Forecasts predict more rain over Spain, Portugal, France, and later in southern Italy, Greece and Turkey. Heavy showers are also expected in South Africa and Lesotho, risking flooding and damage.
