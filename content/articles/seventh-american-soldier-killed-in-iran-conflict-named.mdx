The US has named its seventh soldier killed in the war with Iran: 26‑year‑old Sgt. Benjamin Pennington from Glendale, Kentucky. He died on March 1 after an Iranian strike hit the Prince Sultan airbase in Saudi Arabia, where he was stationed with the Army Space and Missile Defense Command. The attack is still being investigated, and he will be posthumously promoted to staff sergeant. The war began after US‑Israel strikes on Iran.
