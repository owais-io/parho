Venezuela says Trinidad and Tobago helped the US seize an oil tanker near its coast. The tanker carried almost 2 million barrels of Venezuelan oil. Venezuela calls the move piracy and a breach of international law. Trinidad announced it will let US forces use its airports and has a new radar system, which the Venezuelan government says turns the island into a US military base. Venezuela has cut gas talks with Trinidad in protest.
