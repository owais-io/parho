Trump’s Justice Department has opened an investigation into Fed chief Jerome Powell, accusing him of lying to Congress about the Fed building. The move is part of a broader pattern of Trump attacking independent officials to keep full control. Critics say the probe threatens the Fed’s independence and could shake financial markets. The story highlights Trump’s insistence on unchecked power and the potential clash with courts and other institutions.
