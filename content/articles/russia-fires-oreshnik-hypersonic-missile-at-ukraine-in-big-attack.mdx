Russia launched an Oreshnik hypersonic missile at a target in western Ukraine near the EU border in a big overnight attack. Ukraine said the strike hit a place that might be a gas‑storage site and recovered missile pieces. Moscow claims it was revenge for a supposed Ukrainian drone attack on Putin’s home, but Kyiv says the claim is false. Ukraine warns the missile threatens European security and urges partners to press Moscow harder.
