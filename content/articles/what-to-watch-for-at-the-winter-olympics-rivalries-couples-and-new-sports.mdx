NHL players return to the Olympics, sparking heated US‑Canada match‑ups. A Canadian coach was accused of pulling his athletes from a qualifier, while US skater Katie Uhlaender is fighting the decision. Russian athletes will compete as neutrals. Norway’s Johannes Klæbo could make history. Italy hopes for 19 medals, and celebrity skaters Jutta Leerdam and Jake Paul turn heads.
