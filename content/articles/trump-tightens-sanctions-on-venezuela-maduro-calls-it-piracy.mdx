Trump has stepped up pressure on Venezuela by expanding sanctions and threatening to strike land targets. He seized a tanker called Skipper and announced plans to target other oil ships linked to the Venezuelan regime. Maduro called the move piracy and said Venezuela would defend its oil. The US says it will follow legal procedures. Russia backs Maduro, while some Latin American leaders said they could offer asylum and the opposition wants him out.
