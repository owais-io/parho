A bar in the Swiss ski resort Crans‑Montana caught fire on New Year’s Eve, killing 40 people and injuring more than 100. Town officials say the building was never inspected in five years, a mistake that the mayor has publicly apologized for. The bar owners are now under criminal investigation and families of the victims want the council to take responsibility. The case is still being examined by police and prosecutors.
