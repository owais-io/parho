Britain is getting its big aircraft carrier, HMS Prince of Wales, ready for a possible trip to the Middle East. The navy can launch it in just five days if told to, but no final decision has been made. Other ships and jets, like the fighter Typhoons and Wildcat helicopters, are already in the region after U.S. and Israeli airstrikes. The UK is also evacuating thousands of British citizens from the area.
