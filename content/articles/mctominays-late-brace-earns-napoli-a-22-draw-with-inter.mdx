Napoli and Inter finished 2‑2 after a tense Serie A match. Inter led 2‑1 thanks to a Thuram goal and a penalty, but Napoli’s Scott McTominay scored two late goals – a first‑time volley in the 81st minute and a second‑minute strike to level the score. The result keeps Napoli in the title race, while Inter stays a point ahead of their rivals.
