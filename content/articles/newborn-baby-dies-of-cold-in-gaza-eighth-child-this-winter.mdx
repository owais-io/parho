Twenty‑seven‑day‑old baby Aisha Ayesh al‑Agha died in Gaza on Saturday from severe cold. She was the eighth child to die of hypothermia this winter. War‑related fuel shortages and blocked medical supplies have left hospitals in Gaza helpless, while displaced families live in flimsy tents that are vulnerable to storms and wind. Doctors report thousands of miscarriages, premature births and infant deaths since the conflict began, adding to the already high casualty numbers.
