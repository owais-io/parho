France’s star half‑back duo, Antoine Dupont and Matthieu Jalibert, are turning the national team into a dazzling attacking machine. After recovering from injury, Dupont’s clever playmaking and Jalibert’s creative running have helped France win big in the Six Nations, scoring most of the tries. Their partnership is so strong that even tough opponents like Wales and Ireland feel overmatched, and the pair could threaten every rival if they stay healthy.
