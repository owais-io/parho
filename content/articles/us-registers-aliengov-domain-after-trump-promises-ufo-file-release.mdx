The U.S. Cybersecurity Agency added the domains alien.gov and aliens.gov to its official registry, a move that follows President Trump’s pledge to release government files on UFOs. The registration comes amid growing congressional interest and national‑security worries about unidentified aerial phenomena. Politicians from both parties have spoken about UAPs, and experts say the releases may not answer all questions but show the administration’s willingness to be transparent.
