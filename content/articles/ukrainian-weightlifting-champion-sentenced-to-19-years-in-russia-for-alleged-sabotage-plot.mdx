Yulia Lemeshchenko, a former Ukrainian weight‑lifting champion, vanished from her Kharkiv gym in autumn 2023. Months later she was caught in Moscow and charged with spying for Ukraine, blowing up power lines near St Petersburg, and stalking a Russian air‑force commander. Russian prosecutors say she carried out sabotage and planned assassinations, and she was handed a 19‑year sentence. Lemeshchenko denied wrongdoing, saying she only did what she felt was necessary, but her story is still under debate.
