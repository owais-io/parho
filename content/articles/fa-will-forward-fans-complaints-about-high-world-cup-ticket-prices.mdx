England fans are furious because the cheapest 2026 World Cup tickets will cost about $220—ten times more than the $21 promised in the original bid. The FA, learning of the hike only yesterday, will send supporters’ concerns to FIFA. Fans call the prices a scandal and may boycott games. Similar anger is heard from Scotland and Germany, but the government will not intervene.
