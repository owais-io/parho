Jannik Sinner beat Alex de Minaur 7‑5, 6‑2 at the ATP Finals, keeping his indoor winning streak and staying 13‑0 against the Australian. He advanced to a third straight final while Carlos Alcaraz won his match to join him. The final will pit the two year‑end world No 1s against each other. Sinner’s dominant form makes the showdown a must‑watch finish to the season.
