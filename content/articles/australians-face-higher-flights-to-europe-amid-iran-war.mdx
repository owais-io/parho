The Iran‑Israel conflict is making it harder and more expensive for Australians to fly to Europe. Emirates and Qatar Airways have cut services, pushing travelers to use longer, cheaper routes via Singapore or other Asian hubs. Qantas is raising fares to cover rising fuel costs and added stops. Travel insurance rarely covers war‑related problems, and many Australians are looking at safer, cheaper destinations instead of Europe.
