President Trump has been sending mixed messages about the war against Iran. He switches between saying the war is almost over and that it’s just beginning, which confuses both the public and his own military. Trump prefers social‑media bluster to calm speeches, and he blames Iran for a deadly strike even though evidence points to the U.S. being responsible. This lack of clarity risks alienating allies and eroding trust.
