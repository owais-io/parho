Bodø/Glimt stunned Manchester City 3‑1 in the Champions League, giving Norway its first group‑stage win since 1996. Prime Minister Jonas Gahr Støre attended and praised the Norwegian side’s lively play. Meanwhile, Juventus edged Benfica 2‑0 thanks to Weston McKennie’s goal, keeping their knockout hopes alive. Other clubs had mixed results, with Inter, Arsenal and Bayern also in the spotlight. The round highlighted unexpected upsets and key moments across Europe.
