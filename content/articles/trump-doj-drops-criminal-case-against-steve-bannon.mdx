The DOJ, led by Trump‑appointee Jeanine Pirro, has decided to drop the criminal case against former aide Steve Bannon. Bannon was jailed for two contempt‑of‑Congress convictions after refusing to testify about the Jan. 6 investigation. The move, which would prevent the case from being refiled, follows a pattern of the Trump DOJ clearing up cases tied to the Capitol riot. Bannon was released from prison just before the 2024 election.
