An 18‑year‑old named Tristan Roberts was given a life sentence with a minimum of 22½ years for killing his 45‑year‑old mother in north Wales. He planned the attack, bought weapons, and recorded the murder on a dictaphone. He had posted violent, misogynistic messages on Discord, citing the horror film American Psycho. After the body was found, police said he had kept his mother captive for hours before striking her with a hammer.
