North Carolina man Michael Phillips says he has the world’s smallest penis, measuring 0.38 inches when erect. He posted a video on TMZ’s YouTube channel, where he challenged anyone to prove him wrong and used the clip to talk about micropenis, a medical condition that can cause body shame. Phillips says he was diagnosed in 2025 and wants more people to understand the condition and seek help.
