Veteran park ranger Betty Reid Soskin died at 104 after a remarkable career. She joined the National Park Service at 85 and was the oldest active ranger when she retired in 2022 at 100. Soskin spent her life telling the stories of the WWII home front, especially the experiences of women of color. She worked at Rosie the Riveter/WWII Home Front National Historical Park and inspired visitors with her own history.
