Pakistani businessman Asif Merchant, 47, says Iran’s Revolutionary Guard coerced him into a plan to kill Donald Trump, threatening his family. He admits working with a terrorist group and mapping the plot on a napkin. An FBI informant provided the details. He was arrested in July 2024. Prosecutors deny evidence of duress. The trial began amid U.S. and Israeli air strikes on Iran, and Trump hinted at the plot.
