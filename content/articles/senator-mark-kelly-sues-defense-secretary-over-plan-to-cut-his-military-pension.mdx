Senator Mark Kelly, a retired Navy captain and former astronaut, filed a lawsuit against Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth to stop a move that could lower his military retirement rank and cut his pension. The case comes after Hegseth censure Kelly for a video that urged troops to refuse illegal orders, which the senator says was protected free speech. Kelly says the censure threatens veterans' rights and wants the court to void it.
