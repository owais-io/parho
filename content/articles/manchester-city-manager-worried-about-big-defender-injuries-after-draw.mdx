Pep Guardiola is concerned about injuries to key defenders Josko Gvardiol and Rúben Dias after a 1‑1 draw with Chelsea. He will find out on Monday how serious the problems are. With centre‑back John Stones already out and other midfielders sidelined, City face a tough January schedule and a big match against Brighton. Guardiola says the squad will stay strong and look for solutions.
