Judge Ana Reyes has blocked the Trump administration from revoking temporary protected status (TPS) from up to 350,000 Haitians. TPS lets them stay, work, and stay safe in the U.S. while Haiti remains dangerous. Reyes said the move was hateful and likely illegal. She also said the decision has no effect right now. The court says TPS will remain in place until the case is decided. Trump also tried to end TPS for Venezuelans, Ukrainians and others.
