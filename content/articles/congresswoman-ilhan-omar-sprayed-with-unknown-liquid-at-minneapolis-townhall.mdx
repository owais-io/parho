At a Minneapolis town‑hall, Representative Ilhan Omar was sprayed with an unknown liquid by a man holding a syringe. Security stopped the attacker and he was taken to jail on assault charges. Omar kept speaking and said she would not let the attack stop her. The incident follows a wave of threats against her and other lawmakers, sparking calls for stronger safety measures.
