President Trump was upset that Australia decided not to send warships to help keep the Strait of Hormuz open, a key oil route. He called the decision a surprise and criticized allies for not getting involved. Australia said it has provided support it was asked for, and it did not receive a direct request from the US. The clash comes as the Hormuz blockade is pushing up fuel prices in Australia.
