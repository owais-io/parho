A huge batch of Jeffrey Epstein documents released by the U.S. justice department shows that Prince Andrew, nicknamed “The Duke,” and his ex‑wife Sarah Ferguson kept in touch with Epstein right after he finished his house arrest in 2010. The emails talk about a Russian woman, plans to meet at Buckingham Palace, and a dinner in New York. The files also contain photos of Andrew with a woman, though no wrongdoing is proven.
