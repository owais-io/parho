The Guardian has issued a set of corrections for several recent stories. It clarified that Cardinham Woods is run by Forestry England, not the Forestry Commission. It also noted that the capercaillie, said to live only in the Highlands, is also found elsewhere in the UK. Other updates address misstatements in a variety of pieces, and readers can send complaints or requests to the Guardian’s editorial team.
