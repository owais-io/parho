A major reorganisation at Porton Down, the Defence Science and Technology Laboratory, has badly lowered staff morale and slowed critical research. The whistleblower says hundreds of jobs were “deleted” and employees had to re‑apply, creating confusion and a safety worry. Surveys show engagement at a record low, and safety ratings are the worst in the civil service. The Ministry of Defence denies layoffs but says reforms aim to improve safety and keep the lab productive.
