Miami’s real‑estate market is flooding with ultra‑wealthy buyers snapping up luxury homes, pushing prices higher and making it hard for everyday Floridians to afford housing, food, and services. A recent poll shows many residents are considering leaving because of the cost of living. While the influx boosts the economy, it also strains essential workers and fuels a growing affordability crisis.
