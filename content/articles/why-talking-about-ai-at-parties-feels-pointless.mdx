Zoe Williams says that artificial intelligence moves faster than we can discuss it, making chats at a party feel unhelpful. She points out that most people either ignore the real dangers of AI or over‑optimise it, and that even reading up on the topic can be outpaced by AI itself. She argues we need deeper thinking, not just casual talk, because AI changes life in ways we’re still catching up with.
