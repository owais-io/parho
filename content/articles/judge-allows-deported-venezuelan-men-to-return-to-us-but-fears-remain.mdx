A federal judge in Washington said Venezuelan men who were sent to a brutal prison in El Salvador by the Trump team can come back to the U.S. to fight their cases. Luis Muñoz Pinto, 27, is one of them. He says he wants to prove he isn’t part of a gang, but he fears being detained again and facing the same nightmare. The judge also said the U.S. must pay for their travel.
