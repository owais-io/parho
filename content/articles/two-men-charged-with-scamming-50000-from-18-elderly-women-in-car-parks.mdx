Two men have been charged after allegedly scamming 18 elderly women in shopping centre car parks across New South Wales, stealing more than $50,000. Police say the men, aged 37 and 57, pretended to spot a problem with a car and asked the owner to lift the bonnet, then one of them stole items from inside the vehicle. The pair used tourist visas from Romania. Both men are denied bail and face court.
