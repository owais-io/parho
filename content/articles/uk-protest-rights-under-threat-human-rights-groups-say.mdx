Two big reports say England and Wales are making it harder for people to protest. New laws let police stop gatherings for vague reasons, and arrests for carrying simple items like cable ties have happened. The reports point to harsh sentences for climate and pro‑Palestinian demonstrators and call for the laws to be changed or removed. An independent review is planned to balance police powers with the right to protest.
