Leicester City fired manager Martí Cifuentes after dropping to 14th in the Championship. A bad home loss to Oxford United and only one win in five games left the club in turmoil. The owner thanked Cifuentes but said a new coach is needed. Andy King will step in temporarily as Leicester look to change leadership while dealing with fan protests and financial rules.
