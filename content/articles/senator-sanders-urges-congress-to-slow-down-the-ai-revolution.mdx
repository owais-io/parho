Bernie Sanders told Congress that the U.S. is unprepared for the rapid rise of AI and called for a pause in building new AI data centers. Speaking at Stanford with Silicon Valley congressman Ro Khanna, he warned that AI could take millions of jobs and make people overly dependent on machines for emotional support. He also launched a tax idea targeting billionaires and urged the U.S. to put workers’ interests first.
