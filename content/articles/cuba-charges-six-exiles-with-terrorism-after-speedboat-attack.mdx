Cuban prosecutors accuse six U.S.-based exiles of loading a speedboat with weapons and trying to destabilise Havana. The boat was fired upon by the coast guard, killing four people and injuring others. The suspects, including a U.S. citizen, are now in provisional detention. The case heightens already tense U.S.–Cuba relations, with Washington cutting oil shipments and demanding reforms.
