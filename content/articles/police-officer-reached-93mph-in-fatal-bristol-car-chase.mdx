A police officer, Matthew Pike, chased a white Volkswagen in Bristol on 4 November 2021 and reached speeds up to 93mph. The chase ended when the VW crashed into a Honda driven by Dr Keryl Johnson. Johnson was badly hurt and later died. Although the driver of the VW was the main cause, the court said Pike’s dangerous driving also contributed to the death. Pike denies the charges and the trial is ongoing.
