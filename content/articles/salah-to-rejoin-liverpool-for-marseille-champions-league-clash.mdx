Liverpool coach Arne Slot confirmed Mohamed Salah will be back in the squad for the Champions League game against Marseille. Salah trained with the team after the Africa Cup of Nations, and Slot said there are no lingering problems between them. The club has been unbeaten in the last 12 games since dropping Salah, but the forward’s goals are still needed to secure a place in the knockout stages. Liverpool are hoping his return boosts their chances.
