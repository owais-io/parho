In 2023, Donald Huffines, a Texas Trump‑aligned candidate, bought a 10,000‑acre ranch in New Mexico that once belonged to Jeffrey Epstein, the infamous sex‑trafficker. The purchase was made through a private LLC and only revealed after the family told a local paper. Huffines, who is running for Texas comptroller, has not said why he bought the property. The deal raises questions about his links to Epstein and his political agenda.
