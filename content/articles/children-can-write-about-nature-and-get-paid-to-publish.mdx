The Guardian’s Young Country Diary is looking for 8‑to‑14‑year‑old kids to write 200‑250‑word stories about what they see in nature. Parents fill out a form, give permission, and send the article before 2 Feb. Four stories will be published and the kids get paid. Teachers are encouraged to take their class outside. If a story isn’t chosen, another chance opens in March for spring.
