Washington officials say floods can push rats into the sewer system, making them appear in toilets. They advise staying calm, flushing the rat or adding dish soap to the toilet to push it back into the sewer. If the rat is too big, close the lid, call pest control, and use another bathroom if needed. The floods have caused severe damage, especially in older homes with old pipes.
