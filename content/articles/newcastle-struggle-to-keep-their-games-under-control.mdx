Newcastle United are having a hard time keeping their high‑energy style in check. After big wins they drop points, and injuries and a busy schedule are making things worse. Manager Eddie Howe says the squad has good parts, but fatigue and missing key players like Joelinton hurt them. The team must decide what to prioritise with the Carabao Cup semi‑final, Champions League tie and the Premier League.
