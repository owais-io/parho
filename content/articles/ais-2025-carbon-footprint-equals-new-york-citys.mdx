Researchers say the AI boom has produced about the same amount of carbon dioxide in 2025 as the whole of New York City, and its water use tops all bottled‑water consumption worldwide. The study, by Digiconomist founder Alex de Vries‑Gao, also finds AI accounts for over 8 % of global aviation emissions. The report calls on tech firms to disclose their climate impact and pay for the environmental costs, as datacentres grow faster than ever.
