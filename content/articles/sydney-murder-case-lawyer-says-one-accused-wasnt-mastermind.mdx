Two men, 24 and 29, were charged in Sydney with kidnapping and killing 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian. Their lawyers said neither man asked for bail and that one had not planned the crime. The case, still under investigation, may involve others. Police say the kidnapping was a mistake and a $50 million ransom was expected. The men remain in custody. Police are looking for other suspects involved in the plot.
