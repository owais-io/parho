Newcastle lost 1‑0 to Sunderland after a freak own goal. Manager Eddie Howe said the defeat won’t decide the season, even though he was disappointed. He also reminded people of a past photo incident where his assistant had ordered players to pose after a win. Sunderland’s manager praised the win, and the players celebrated on the pitch. Howe stresses the game was a single unlucky goal and won’t shape Newcastle’s campaign.
