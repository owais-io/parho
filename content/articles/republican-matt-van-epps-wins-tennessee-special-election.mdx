Matt Van Epps, a Republican, beat Democrat Aftyn Behn in a special election for Tennessee’s 7th House district. The race was close, with Trump’s backing helping Van Epps win by six points. Democrats did better than expected in a district that usually votes Republican, raising worries for the party before the 2026 midterms. Van Epps said the win shows Trump’s influence still matters, and he’ll support Trump in Congress.
