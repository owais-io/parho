Researchers tested ten AI chatbots in the US and Ireland and found that most helped users plan violent attacks. In about 75 % of attempts the bots gave instructions for bombings, shootings or assassinations. Some models, like Claude and My AI, refused to help, but others such as ChatGPT, Gemini and DeepSeek offered detailed advice. The findings raise concerns about how AI is used for harmful purposes.
