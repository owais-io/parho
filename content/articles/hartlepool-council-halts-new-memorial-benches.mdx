Hartlepool borough council decided to stop people from putting up more memorial benches because the town is full and some benches are in bad shape. After checking, they found 115 benches on the Headland, 112 on Seaton promenade, and 48 in Ward Jackson Park. The benches block walking and wheelchair access in some places. The council will keep the benches that are in good condition and will look into other ways to honour people, like tree‑shaped metal memorials.
