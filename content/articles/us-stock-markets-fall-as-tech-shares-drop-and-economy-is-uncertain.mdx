Wall Street had its worst day in a month, with tech shares falling after a big rise on AI hopes. The S&P 500 and Dow fell 1.7%, and the Nasdaq dropped 2.3%. Nvidia fell 3.6% after SoftBank sold its stake. Investors are waiting for new economic data, as the long government shutdown ends. Fed officials said the bank may cut rates soon, adding to uncertainty.
