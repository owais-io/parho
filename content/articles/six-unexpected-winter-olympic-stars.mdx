From a British ski‑jumper who fought poverty to a Nigerian bobsleigh crew who built their own sled, the story shows how ordinary people reach the Olympics. Eddie the Eagle, the self‑funded jumper who finished last but stole hearts, joins medal‑winning Ester Ledecka who won gold in two sports. Other highlights include India’s long‑time luger Shiva Keshavan, Australia’s fluke gold‑winner Steven Bradbury, and Kenya’s first winter skier Philip Boit.
