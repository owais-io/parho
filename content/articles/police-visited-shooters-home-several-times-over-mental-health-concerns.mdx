Police repeatedly visited the home of Jesse Van Rootselaar, the 18‑year‑old accused of the Tumbler Ridge school shooting that killed six people. Authorities were called because of concerns about the suspect’s mental health and a history of weapons. They had taken guns from the house and later returned them after a licence expired. The incident has raised questions about Canada’s gun‑seizure rules when a person is under mental‑health supervision.
