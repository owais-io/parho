Joe Kent, who ran the National Counterterrorism Center, quit after opposing US airstrikes on Iran. He says the White House sidelined debate, that Israel pushed the attack, and that no credible intelligence warned of a threat. Kent claims he may have leaked classified info, sparking an FBI inquiry. The bureau has declined to comment.
