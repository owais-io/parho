A study of 65 patients in London followed for two years after root‑canal treatment showed big drops in blood sugar and inflammation. The work also found better cholesterol and fat levels, which can lower heart‑disease risk. The researchers say treating tooth infections removes bacteria that get into the blood and hurt overall health. It reminds us that keeping our teeth clean and treating problems early can help protect us from diabetes and heart disease.
