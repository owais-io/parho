A 34‑year‑old man named Valdo Calocane, who stabbed three people in Nottingham in June 2023, tried to give himself up to MI5 in London in May 2021. Police officers met him at the MI5 building but did not ask why he wanted to be arrested, hoping to build rapport. The case was not followed up properly by police or MI5, and his earlier complaints were ignored. He was later jailed in a high‑security hospital.
