After England’s tough Ashes loss and a player’s nightclub scuffle, head coach Brendon McCullum said he’ll stay on if he can truly steer the team. He’s already talked to ECB leaders and is set to coach the white‑ball tour of Sri Lanka, while a review of the Ashes tour looks for changes in the Test setup. McCullum stresses authenticity, wants control over decisions, and will work closely with captain Ben Stokes to rebuild the squad’s culture and performance.
