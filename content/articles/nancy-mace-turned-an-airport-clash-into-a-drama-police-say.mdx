Nancy Mace, a South Carolina congresswoman, was at an airport in Charleston on 30 October when a mix‑up about the color of her car turned into a fight. She shouted at police and TSA staff, using profanity and calling them idiots. A police report says the congresswoman made the situation a spectacle by refusing to follow the airport’s rules. Mace denies the claims, says the report is false, and plans to sue the airport. She’s also running for governor.
