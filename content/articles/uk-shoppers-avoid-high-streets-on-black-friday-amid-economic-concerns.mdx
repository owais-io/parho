Shoppers turned away from UK high streets on Black Friday, with footfall down 2% and 7.2% less than last year. Online sales were mixed, and the cost‑of‑living squeeze is hurting spending. KPMG warns that weak consumer buying and rising unemployment could keep growth slow in 2026, and businesses are feeling the pressure. Hospitality firms worry about higher business rates, and the budget’s relief may not be enough.
