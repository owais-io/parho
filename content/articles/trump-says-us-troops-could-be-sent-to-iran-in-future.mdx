President Trump said the U.S. might send ground troops to Iran if needed, especially to protect nuclear sites he said were vulnerable. He also talked about the U.S. bombing Iran last year and said that sending troops now is not on the table but could happen later. Trump criticized the UK for not joining U.S. strikes on Iran and met with families of soldiers killed in the Middle East.
