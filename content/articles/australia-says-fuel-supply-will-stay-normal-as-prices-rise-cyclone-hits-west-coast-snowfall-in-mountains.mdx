Australia’s government says petrol supply will stay normal even as prices rise. Minister Chris Bowen said no big cuts to fuel sales are needed; the Prime Minister said the cabinet would meet to coordinate. Diesel and petrol prices jumped 10% and 8% last week. Meanwhile, Cyclone Narelle is hitting the Western Australian coast with heavy rain and strong winds. The first big snowfall of the year has also fallen in the Snowy Mountains.
