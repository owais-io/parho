GB Energy boss Jürgen Maier and other renewable heads say boosting North Sea oil and gas could help lower the UK’s rising energy bills, create jobs and raise tax revenue. Maier also backs the government’s ban on new exploration licences and wants to use existing fields for the long haul. The debate is heating up as the government considers licences for the Rosebank and Jackdaw fields, while gas prices soar amid the Middle East conflict.
