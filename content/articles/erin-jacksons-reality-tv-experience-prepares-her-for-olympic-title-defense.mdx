Erin Jackson is a U.S. speed‑skating star who joined the reality show “World’s Toughest Test,” where she faced scary challenges and proved she’s tougher than she thought. She won a gold medal in Beijing 2022 and now heads to the 2026 Milan‑Cortina Games to defend her 500‑meter title. Despite injuries and media buzz, Jackson is focused on training and inspiring future skaters.
