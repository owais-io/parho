Moonpig, the online card shop, says using AI to design cards and help customers write messages has lifted sales by 6.7% to £169 m in the last six months. About half of orders now use AI tools for stickers, photos or handwriting. The company’s new chat bot answers a third of questions. The boost has taken Moonpig from a loss to a £26.6 m pre‑tax profit. CEO Nickyl Raithatha is leaving, and Catherine Faiers will take over.
