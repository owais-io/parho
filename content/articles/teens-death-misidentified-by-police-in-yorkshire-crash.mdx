An 18‑year‑old named Joshua Johnson died in a crash near Rotherham on 13 December, but police first thought the victim was 17‑year‑old Trevor Wynn. Families were mis‑informed and spent hours in hospital with the wrong person. After checks, authorities confirmed Wynn was alive and Johnson had died. Johnson’s family said the mistake was “unimaginable” and asked for privacy. The police have opened an inquiry with the Independent Office for Police Conduct.
