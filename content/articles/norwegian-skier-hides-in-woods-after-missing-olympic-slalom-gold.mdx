Atle Lie McGrath, a 25‑year‑old Norwegian skier, missed out on the men’s slalom gold at the Winter Olympics after a mistake in the final run. He was also grieving his grandfather’s death on the day of the opening ceremony. Feeling overwhelmed, he threw his ski poles, went into the woods for a few hours, and later spoke about needing time for himself. The win went to Switzerland’s Loïc Meillard, while others took silver and bronze.
