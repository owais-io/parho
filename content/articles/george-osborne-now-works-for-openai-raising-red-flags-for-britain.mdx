Former UK chancellor George Osborne has joined OpenAI, leading a team that will work with governments to roll out AI technology. This move shows how big tech firms are hiring ex‑politicians to act like diplomats and shape policy. It blurs the line between business and government, raising worries that AI companies could become quasi‑governmental actors. The article urges more transparency and scrutiny of these tech‑government partnerships.
