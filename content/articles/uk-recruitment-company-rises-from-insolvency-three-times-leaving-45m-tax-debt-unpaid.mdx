An HR agency in the UK has gone bust, been bought back, and gone bust again – three times in four years. The same bosses keep running the new versions, and each time the company leaves a huge tax bill unpaid. HMRC says this “phoenix” style is legal but costs the Treasury about £1bn of lost tax. In total, creditors and the government are still owed £7.6 m, including £4.5 m to the tax office.
