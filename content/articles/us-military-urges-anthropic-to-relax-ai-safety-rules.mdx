Defense officials met with Anthropic’s CEO, demanding the company allow the U.S. military to use its Claude AI model without safety limits. Anthropic has resisted, fearing the model could support surveillance or autonomous weapons. The Pentagon threatened to cancel a big contract and label the company a risk if it doesn't comply. Other AI firms have already agreed to the government’s terms, sparking debate over AI’s role in war.
