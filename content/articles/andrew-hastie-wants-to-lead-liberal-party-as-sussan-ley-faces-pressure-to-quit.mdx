Andrew Hastie, a former army officer, is positioning himself to run against current Liberal Party leader Sussan Ley. Inside the party, many members are urging Ley to resign after recent splits with the Nationals. Hastie, who left the shadow cabinet earlier this year, is seen as a favourite who could win voters moving to One Nation. If a spill occurs, he would need support from MPs across factions.
