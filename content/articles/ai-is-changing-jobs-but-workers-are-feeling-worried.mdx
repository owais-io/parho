AI is quickly automating coding and other tech jobs, turning the workplace into a place of pressure and uncertainty. Tech workers are split between embracing AI and questioning its effects, while leaders shift focus from burnout to change and disruption. The industry also faces a memory‑chip shortage and high‑profile staff exits, showing that money, not safety or worker wellbeing, often drives decisions. These shifts mean the future of work must balance technology gains with real human concerns.
