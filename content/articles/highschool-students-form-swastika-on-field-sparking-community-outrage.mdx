High school students at Branham High in San Jose made a swastika on the football field and posted it online with an anti‑Jewish quote. The image spread on Reddit, sparking hate and anger. Instagram removed the post and the school said it would investigate but not reveal names. The district plans to work with Jewish groups and anti‑hate organisations to prevent future incidents.
