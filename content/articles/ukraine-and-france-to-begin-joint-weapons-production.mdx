Ukraine and France have signed a letter of intent to start building weapons together, moving from buying to producing. The deal will cover large‑scale defence projects and includes new shipments of French systems like Aster missiles, Mirage 2000 jets and SAMP‑T air‑defence units. Ukraine also plans to open export hubs in 2026 to sell its own drones and other weapons across Europe, boosting its war‑time economy and securing long‑term supplies.
