NSW Liberal leader Kellie Sloane has just named a new shadow cabinet, bringing several fresh faces into the opposition. The party still trails the minority Labor government, with only 35 seats to Labor’s 46, and recent polls show a 28% primary vote, threatening a loss of more seats. Sloane’s team will focus on housing, the environment and industrial relations, but the coalition must decide its stance on key issues like climate policy, gun laws and budget plans.
