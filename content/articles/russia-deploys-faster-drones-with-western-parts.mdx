Russia has started firing a new fast drone called the Geran‑5 at Ukraine. The drone can reach 600 km/h and has a Chinese jet engine, but Ukrainian analysts say it also contains parts from Germany, China and the US. Kyiv’s defence forces have shot down two of them, one near Kyiv and another near Dnipro. Russia says it can produce up to 500 drones a day, a huge jump from earlier reports.
