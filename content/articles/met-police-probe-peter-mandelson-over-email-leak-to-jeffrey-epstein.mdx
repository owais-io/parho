Met Police have started an investigation into former business secretary Peter Mandelson, who is accused of sending confidential Downing Street emails and market‑sensitive information to Jeffrey Epstein, the convicted child‑sex‑offender. Documents from the Epstein files show Mandelson forwarded internal discussions after the 2008 financial crash. The revelations sparked a backlash across politics, forced Mandelson to resign from the House of Lords and prompted calls to strip him of his peerage. Police will interview him, search his devices and collect witness statements.
