Sea Shepherd’s Allankay crew headed to Antarctica to film huge krill‑trawling ships that harvest millions of tonnes of krill each year. The NGO says the fishing is harming penguins, whales and the entire food chain. The crew filmed the boats, the smell of the catch, and the sea life around them while urging people to stop the industry. They hope to draw attention and pressure the international commission to protect the ecosystem.
