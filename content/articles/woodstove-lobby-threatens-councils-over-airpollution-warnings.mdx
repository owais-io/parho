Wood‑stove lobbyists have sent legal threats to several UK councils that run public information campaigns warning about the health risks of burning wood in homes. The industry body says the flyers are unfair and breach advertising rules, but the campaigns are backed by science linking wood smoke to heart, lung and cancer problems. The government is consulting on new stove rules, but critics say it misses a chance to ban them in cities.
