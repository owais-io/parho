The Gorton and Denton by‑election in Manchester is a hot‑button race, with Labour, the Greens and Reform UK all fighting for a seat. Leaders Keir Starmer, Zack Polanski and Nigel Farage have sent personal messages urging voters. The contest shows how a few hundred votes can decide a result and signals a shift toward multi‑party politics in a system designed for two parties. The story also touches on national issues like the Chagos Islands deal and asylum figures.
