Netflix’s new one‑take special follows Ed Sheeran as he walks through New York with his guitar, playing spontaneous songs for fans, friends and even a surprise on a tourist bus. The hour‑long film shows him rehearsing in a theater, jamming in a pub, performing on a subway train, and ending with his concert at the same venue. Filmed with smooth camera moves, the film feels like a live, unedited adventure that keeps viewers hooked.
