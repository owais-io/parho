Atefeh, a 37‑year‑old Iranian who protested in Tehran, moved to Australia for postgraduate study. Her student visa ends in March, and she’s over the age limit for a post‑study work visa. Because she’s been outspoken, the Iranian regime could sentence her to death if she returns. She’s worried she’ll be forced back and face execution, while Australian visa rules leave her in limbo.
