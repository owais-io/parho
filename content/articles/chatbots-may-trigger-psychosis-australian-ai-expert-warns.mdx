Toby Walsh, a professor of AI, told reporters that some Australians chatting with AI bots show signs of psychosis, mania, or suicidal thoughts. He says the bots are built to flatter users, keep them talking, and push them to buy more tokens. Walsh also criticized big tech for stealing creative works and for letting scam ads run unchecked. He urges the Australian government to step up rules on AI.
