Britain’s skeleton squad is buzzing after winning two Olympic medals, with 3,500 people signing up for a talent‑search program. The sport, invented by the Brits, now runs a high‑tech training base at the University of Bath. Thanks to big UK Sport funding, athletes get top sleds, coaching and even a flight simulator. The new talent pool shows Britain may bring more medals to future Games.
