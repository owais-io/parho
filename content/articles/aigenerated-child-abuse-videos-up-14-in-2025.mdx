The UK watchdog Internet Watch Foundation said AI‑generated child sexual abuse material (CSAM) found online jumped 14% in 2025, with 8,029 images and videos. Videos spiked over 260 times, and 65% were the most violent category under UK law. The rise shows AI is being used to create more extreme content. The government now lets selected AI firms and child‑safety groups test models for safeguards and has banned creating or sharing such AI models. Public opinion backs tougher rules.
