At a London hotel, Reform UK members and guests celebrated the release of “How to Launder Money,” a book written by former financier George Cottrell and co‑author Lawrence Burke Files. Cottrell, who once served time in U.S. prison for wire fraud, says the book is meant for law‑enforcement and policy makers, not criminals. The event also highlighted Nigel Farage’s attendance and the authors’ call to rethink anti‑money‑laundering rules.
