Iran has been sending its inexpensive Shahed‑136 drones to the Gulf, hitting places like Bahrain, Kuwait, and the UAE. In just two days more than a thousand drones were launched, with the UAE downing about 645 of them. The drones are cheap, noisy and easy to make, and can still cause serious damage to buildings and key facilities. Their use shows Iran’s aim to pressure US‑allied Gulf states.
