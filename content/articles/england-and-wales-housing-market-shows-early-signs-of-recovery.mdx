RICS survey says English and Welsh home sellers are starting to feel better after last year’s slowdown. In January, more agents saw higher sales and higher prices, but buyers are still cautious. Demand fell last month, but the drop is less steep than before. House prices are moving toward a “turning point” after hitting a low last October. The bounce will depend on mortgage rates and economic confidence.
