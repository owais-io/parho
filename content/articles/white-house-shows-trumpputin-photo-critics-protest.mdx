The White House has put a framed picture of former President Trump standing beside Russian President Vladimir Putin on the Palm Room wall. The photo, from their 2023 Alaska summit, has sparked fire‑storms from Democrats, journalists and others who say it glorifies Putin while ignoring the Ukraine war. Trump’s team says the picture marks a historic diplomatic moment. Putin’s envoy posted a thumbs‑up on X. The move highlights how decoration choices can stir political debate.
