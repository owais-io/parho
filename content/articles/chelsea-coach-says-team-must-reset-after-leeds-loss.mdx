Chelsea coach Enzo Maresca admitted his side lost to Leeds and must reset. The defeat dropped them to fourth place, nine points behind league leaders Arsenal. Maresca blamed squad rotation after a tiring week and said Leeds were better on all fronts. He said Chelsea will learn from mistakes and focus on the next game against Bournemouth, keeping his job secure.
