The Mariupol Drama Theatre was destroyed by a Russian airstrike in 2022 while people were hiding in its basement. Now Russian forces say the rebuilt theatre will open again with a Russian fairy‑tale play, but former actors say it’s “dancing on bones” and want a memorial instead. Amnesty and others say the strike was a war crime. Meanwhile, Russian authorities are seizing homes and forcing people to choose citizenship.
