Police in Arizona have taken a suspect into custody while searching for Nancy Guthrie, the 84‑year‑old mother of TV host Savannah Guthrie, who vanished last month. The FBI released new footage of a masked man tampering with a door‑bell camera at the home on the day of her disappearance. No suspect has been named, and authorities are still looking for clues, asking anyone with information to call the FBI.
