UK officials warned Elon Musk’s X must stop showing indecent AI images or risk a ban. Ofcom is speeding up an investigation after many users posted images of women and children that have been altered to look naked or sexually suggestive. X said only paid subscribers could create images now, but critics say that isn’t enough. Ministers are ready to use legal powers to block the site if it doesn’t comply.
