At the PDC World Darts Championship, Dutch player Gian van Veen beat 2024 champion Luke Humphries 5‑1, while England's Luke Littler defeated Krzysztof Ratajski 5‑0. Both wins moved them into the semi‑finals, marking a shift toward a younger generation in the sport. The victories were seen as a sign that the era of Michael van Gerwen and other veterans may be winding down. Fans celebrated the high‑energy play and the bright future of darts.
