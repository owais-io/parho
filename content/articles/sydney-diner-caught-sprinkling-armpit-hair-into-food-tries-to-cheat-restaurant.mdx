A customer in a Sydney restaurant was recorded on CCTV sprinkling armpit hair into his food, apparently to try and get a free meal. After confronting the head chef, he left without paying, having ordered expensive items. The incident highlights bizarre customer behaviour and raises concerns about hygiene and security in eateries. The story also invites other hospitality workers to share their toughest customer experiences.
