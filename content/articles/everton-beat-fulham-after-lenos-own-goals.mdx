Everton beat Fulham 2-1 thanks to two own goals by keeper Bernd Leno. The match started with Fulham ahead after a goal by Vitalii Mykolenko, but in the second half Everton equalised and then won with an own goal from Leno. Fulham’s manager Marco Silva blamed a lack of aggression and the team’s drop in performance after the break. Everton’s comeback shows the importance of set‑piece play.
