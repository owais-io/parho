A 17‑year‑old named Naveed Akram, who was learning to be a bricklayer, was charged with 15 murders and a terrorist act after a deadly attack at Bondi. Police say he had been monitored by ASIO for six months, and his father held multiple guns. The pair had recently traveled to the Philippines, where investigators wonder if that trip raised any red flags. The case is now being investigated by police and intelligence agencies.
