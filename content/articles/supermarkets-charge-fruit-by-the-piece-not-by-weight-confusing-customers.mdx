The Australian consumer regulator says supermarkets are now pricing fresh produce by item instead of by weight, which can trick shoppers into paying more for small fruits and veggies. The regulator wants all stores to show a single unit of measurement so prices are clear. It’s also suing Coles and Woolworths for false discounts. The move has raised worries about hidden costs as grocery prices rise.
