Business secretary Peter Kyle said at a party conference that Nigel Farage is like the former politician Enoch Powell, blaming slow economic growth for the appeal of far‑right parties. Kyle warned that without higher growth, people will turn to disruptive politics and lose trust in democracy. He also said Reform UK is far right, citing recent racist comments from the party’s new student group. The comments echo past fears about Powell’s inflammatory speeches.
