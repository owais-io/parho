After a night of intense air raids, people in Tehran say the city is becoming a war zone similar to Gaza. With the internet cut off, many share scary messages about loud explosions, trembling windows and the risk of dying. Residents feel caught between attacks from Israel and the Iranian government’s own forces, and some are already leaving the capital. The bombardment has left many terrified of civilian deaths.
