Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney will travel to the remote mining town of Tumbler Ridge on Friday to attend a vigil for the six children and a teacher who were killed when a former student opened fire at their high school. The victims include 12‑year‑old Kylie Smith and 13‑year‑old Ezekiel Schofield, and the shooter, Jesse Van Rootselaar, died by suicide. Police say they had visited the suspect’s home before the attack and that the town lacks enough mental‑health services.
