McLaren said sorry after Formula One officials disqualified its two championship hopefuls, Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri, from the Las Vegas Grand Prix. The pair had finished second and fourth, but a post‑race check found their cars’ skid plates were too thin. The ruling dropped them from the title race, putting Max Verstappen closer to the lead. McLaren says the rule breach was accidental and will focus on the last two races.
