In Shotton Colliery, County Durham, a 10‑year‑old Christmas tree that had been erected to honour WWI soldiers was cut down with a chainsaw between 10 and 11 pm, just hours after the lights were switched on at 5 pm. About 80 people had gathered to watch the lighting. Local residents, the parish church and police say it was deliberate vandalism. Authorities are asking anyone who saw it to come forward.
