Amorim, the new Manchester United coach, is unsure about staying because he’s in conflict with the club’s football director, Jason Wilcox. The club said it won’t spend much money this season, making Amorim feel he can’t build the team he wants. He has said he’s the manager, not just a coach, and that he might leave when his contract ends in 18 months. The board hasn’t decided yet.
