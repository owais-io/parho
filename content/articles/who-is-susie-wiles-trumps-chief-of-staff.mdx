Susie Wiles, a former Florida political consultant, became Donald Trump’s chief of staff. In a Vanity Fair interview she described herself as an “alcoholic’s daughter” and criticized Trump’s personality. She’s said she tried to curb his plans, such as pardons and National Guard deployments, but her comments have sparked controversy. The article argues she acts as an enabler, avoiding confrontation to protect her own safety.
