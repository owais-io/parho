In his first week, Tony Dokoupil, the new face of CBS Evening News, has faced a whirlwind of interviews, a touring schedule, and mixed reactions. He landed a tough talk with border czar Tom Homan that drew praise, but his chat with defense secretary Pete Hegseth was criticized for being too soft. A top producer was suddenly fired, raising concerns inside the newsroom. Viewership is below rivals, and social media has been split on his performance.
