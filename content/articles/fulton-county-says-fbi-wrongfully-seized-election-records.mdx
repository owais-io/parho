Fulton County lawyers are suing the FBI, saying the agency wrongly took 700 boxes of 2020 election documents. They argue the FBI’s warrant was based on false claims and that the agency ignored evidence that the election was clean. The case questions whether the FBI acted with bias and whether it must share exculpatory evidence. Trump’s comments and the agency’s past actions add political pressure.
