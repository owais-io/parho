Amazon announced it will eliminate 16,000 jobs around the globe, mainly in the US and some UK positions, following a previous wave of 14,000 cuts three months earlier. The move is part of a cost‑cutting push to streamline operations. A mistaken email earlier this week told some employees they already had lost jobs. Affected workers will be given 90 days to find internal roles. Amazon still plans to hire in key areas.
