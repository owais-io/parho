Acas, the UK’s independent dispute‑resolution service, said it’s ready to step in and help end the long‑running strike by resident doctors over pay and training. But neither the government nor the doctors’ union has accepted its offer yet. The strike continues, with doctors walking out again while the BMA plans a new ballot that could allow fresh strikes. Patients and staff are caught in the crossfire.
