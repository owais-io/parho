Guardiola told reporters he won’t talk to Donnarumma about his four yellow cards in ten Premier League games. He said the 26‑year‑old is “adult enough” to keep his cool. If he gets another warning in the next six matches, Donnarumma could become the first goalkeeper banned for five cautions early in a season. Guardiola praised midfielder Phil Foden and noted winger Rodri will miss a game with a hamstring injury.
