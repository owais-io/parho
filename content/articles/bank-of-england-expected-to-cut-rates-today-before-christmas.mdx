People are betting the Bank of England will lower borrowing costs today, dropping the main rate from 4% to 3.75% just before Christmas. The move would help a cooling economy and falling prices. Other banks in Norway, Sweden and Taiwan keep rates the same. Meanwhile, hotel chain Whitbread shares rose after a new activist investor says the company is undervalued. Currys, an electronics retailer, saw profits jump but warns higher taxes will keep shoppers cautious.
