President Trump told oil companies that Venezuela is open for business and that the U.S. wants to help them extract oil there. He said the arrest of Nicolás Maduro gives firms a chance to rebuild Venezuela’s oil fields. At a White House meeting, leaders from Chevron, ExxonMobil and others said they’d invest to restore the country’s aging oil infrastructure. Trump promised lower energy costs for Americans and some money for Venezuelans.
