Liverpool manager Arne Slot held a press conference where he said he was talking to Mohamed Salah’s agent before the striker was named on the bench, but he avoided giving details. He also talked about the club’s injury problems and who might play against Wolves. Meanwhile, England fans are outraged by FIFA’s World Cup ticket prices, which could cost over $5,000 for a final seat, calling it a “slap in the face” and demanding lower prices.
