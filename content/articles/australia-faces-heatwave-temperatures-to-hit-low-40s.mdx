Australia’s Bureau of Meteorology has issued heatwave warnings for large parts of the country, with temperatures expected to hit low 40s (104‑110 °F). The NSW south coast has an extreme warning, and Sydney, Newcastle and Wollongong could see highs in the high 30s to low 40s. Western Australia and the Northern Territory also have severe warnings, with hot conditions in the Kimberley, Pilbara and NT. Stay cool indoors, use fans or AC, and keep blinds closed.
