A 69‑year‑old woman at a Sydney protest against the Israeli president was hurt when a police officer allegedly pushed her violently, breaking four vertebrae. She said other people were shoved on top of her and feared a stampede. Police say they’re reviewing footage. The protest was part of a big demonstration that faced heavy policing under a “major event” law that limits civil claims. Sydney’s mayor wants an independent probe.
