Dubai has been hit by war. The fighting between the U.S., Israel and Iran has shaken the city’s reputation as a safe, tourist hotspot. Many foreigners, especially teachers and wealthy visitors, have left. Terror alerts and missile strikes damaged airports, hotels and data centers. Businesses shut down and banks evacuated staff. Workers and tourists are fleeing, tourism that once brought $30bn a year is in danger. Dubai’s economy may suffer heavy losses.
