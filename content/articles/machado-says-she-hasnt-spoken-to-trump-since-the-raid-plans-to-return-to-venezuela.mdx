After the U.S. raid that seized Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro, opposition leader María Corina Machado told Fox News she hasn't spoken to President Trump since October 2025. She left Venezuela to receive a Nobel Peace Prize in Norway and says she’ll go back home soon. Trump rejected working with her, saying she lacks support in Venezuela. Machado welcomed the U.S. action as a win for freedom, while the United Nations and several countries condemned the raid as a violation of international law.
