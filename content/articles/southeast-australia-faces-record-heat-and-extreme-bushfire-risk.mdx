A huge heat dome is set to hit South Australia, Victoria, New South Wales and southern Queensland, pushing temperatures over 40 °C for several days. The weather could break all‑time records, especially in Victoria’s Mallee and New South Wales’ Riverina. With no rain expected, the fire danger will rise to catastrophic levels, sparking total fire bans and urging residents to stay cool and heed warnings. The state’s emergency services warn that any new fires could spread quickly.
