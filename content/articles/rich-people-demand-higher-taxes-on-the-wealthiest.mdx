About 400 millionaires and billionaires from 24 countries have written an open letter asking world leaders to raise taxes on the super‑rich. They say extreme wealth buys political power, hurts democracy, and fuels climate and poverty problems. The letter, signed by celebrities and philanthropists, was released during the World Economic Forum in Davos. Polls show most rich people think higher taxes would help public services and reduce inequality.
