US Vice‑President Mike Vance has said that AI‑generated sexual images of women and children on X are unacceptable. British MP David Lammy told the Guardian that Vance, who usually supports AI, voiced concerns about the “hyper‑porn” deepfakes that X’s chatbot Grok can create. The UK is considering banning X to stop such manipulation, while Elon Musk has criticised the UK move.
