Chancellor Rachel Reeves says she will work to keep car costs low. She says the mileage rates set in 2011 are still good but motoring costs have gone up, so she is looking into the issue and may act later. The government is keeping fuel duty low for now, but there may be a hike later. Nigel Farage wants to stop the fuel duty rise. The government could give a package to help families if oil prices rise.
