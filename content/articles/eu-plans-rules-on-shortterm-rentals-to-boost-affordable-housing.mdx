EU will make rules on short‑term rentals, like Airbnb, to stop speculation and help people find affordable homes. The plan, due by 2026, won’t ban holiday lets but will give cities tools to limit how often homes can be rented out. Brussels also wants to ease red tape for builders and boost funds for new homes, aiming to curb soaring rents.
