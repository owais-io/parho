Doug LaMalfa, a Republican U.S. congressman from northern California, died at 65 after emergency surgery in a local hospital. He had served the state’s first congressional district for seven terms, fighting for farmers, water and fire‑recovery programs. His passing narrows the GOP’s House majority. Trump and other Republicans paid tribute, and the congressman was known for his work on forests and water. LaMalfa was also a long‑time farmer and a supporter of Donald Trump.
