Afghanistan says it stopped Pakistani jets from bombing Bagram airbase near Kabul. The move comes after weeks of fighting across the border, with Pakistan saying it is at war with Afghanistan. Afghan troops used anti‑aircraft guns and missiles to repel the attack. Diplomatic talks from Saudi Arabia, Qatar and others have failed to end the clashes. Both sides claim many casualties, but independent verification is lacking.
