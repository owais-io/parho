The war in the Middle East began with an Israeli strike that killed Iran’s top leader, followed by U.S. air attacks. Iran has fought back with missiles and drones, hitting Israel and Gulf states. The U.S. and Israel still lead the war, but Iran’s confidence and actions—like closing the Strait of Hormuz—are pulling the initiative back. Oil prices have spiked, and the U.S. faces pressure to end the conflict quickly.
