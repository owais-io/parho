UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer has been asked to sit on a new “peace board” that President Donald Trump is forming to run Gaza after a ceasefire with Hamas. The board will oversee Gaza’s temporary administration and rebuilding and is led by Trump. Starmer is expected to accept, and talks about the board’s exact members are still happening. The move shows Starmer’s close ties with the U.S., even as Britain pushes for a full ceasefire and humanitarian aid.
