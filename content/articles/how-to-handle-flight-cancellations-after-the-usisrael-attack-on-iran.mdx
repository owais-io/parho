Hundreds of thousands of travelers are stuck because Iran has closed airspace after the US‑Israel attack. Flights to Israel, Iran, Qatar, and other Gulf countries are grounded, and airlines like Emirates and Etihad have cancelled routes. UK citizens abroad should register with the FCDO for updates and expect the government to help with accommodation. UK and EU airlines must offer refunds or new flights, but compensation for war is limited. Travel insurance usually doesn’t cover war.
