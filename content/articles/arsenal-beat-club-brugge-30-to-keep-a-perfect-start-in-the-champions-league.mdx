Arsenal won 3‑0 over Club Brugge in Brussels, giving them a perfect start to their Champions League run. Noni Madueke scored twice, while Gabriel Martinelli added his fifth straight CL goal. The victory means Arsenal only need one point from the next two games to reach the last‑16, thanks to goal difference. It also marked Gabriel Jesus’s return from injury after an ACL tear. Arteta praised the players’ flair and the team’s improved attack.
