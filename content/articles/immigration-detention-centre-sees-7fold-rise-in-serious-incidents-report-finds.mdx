A watchdog report says serious incidents at a Gatwick‑area detention centre have jumped from 4 last year to 28 this year – a seven‑fold rise. The events included protests, people climbing anti‑suicide nets, and the National Tactical Response Group was called in 12 times. The report also highlighted past abuse uncovered by a BBC Panorama investigation, investigations of 22 Serco officers, and concerns that many detainees were held for long periods and treated poorly.
