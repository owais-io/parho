Texas Democrats are battling in a Senate primary that could change the state's political future. One candidate, James Talarico, a former state rep, promotes a calm, Christian‑friendly message to win over former Trump voters and independents. The other, Jasmine Crockett, a congresswoman, uses sharp attacks on Trump and Republicans to rally the base. The race highlights how each strategy could flip a red state.
