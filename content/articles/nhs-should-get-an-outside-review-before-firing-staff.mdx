Dr. Narinder Kapur, a former NHS whistleblower, is urging NHS England to add an outside second opinion before staff can be dismissed. He cites two cases – a nurse who set himself on fire after being wrongly fired, and his own unfair sacking – that show a gap in protecting staff. Kapur says minority staff face more discipline and wants stronger safeguards. NHS England says it is working on protecting whistleblowers.
