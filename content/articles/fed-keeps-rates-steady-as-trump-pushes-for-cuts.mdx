The Fed decided to keep interest rates unchanged after its first meeting of the year, even though President Trump wants cuts. Most Fed members said rates are at a neutral level and will pause further cuts for now. Trump has been loud, calling the Fed chair “stiff” and even launching a criminal probe over office renovations. Powell says the investigation is a “pretext” and reaffirms the Fed’s independence.
