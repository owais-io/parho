A U.S. Department of Defense tweet bragging about its “lethalitymaxxing” went viral. The phrase is part of bizarre slang used by incel communities on sites like 4chan, where men use coded words about looks and misogyny. The post shows how that online language is leaking into government messages. Experts warn this spread normalises hateful speech and makes it harder to leave toxic communities.
