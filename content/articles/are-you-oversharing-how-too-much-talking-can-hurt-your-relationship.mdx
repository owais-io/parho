A psychologist says that too much talking can harm a relationship. Mark Travers says open communication is good, but over‑communication hurts. He lists three warning signs: constantly asking for reassurance instead of clarity, voicing every feeling before you understand it, and chatting about the relationship so much that attraction wanes. Couples should focus on trust and keep important matters for later, not every thought out loud.
