Labour leader Keir Starmer faces a crisis as new documents reveal Peter Mandelson, a senior party figure, may have used his influence to secure deals with US banks and other powerful interests. The revelations have shocked MPs and the public, threatening Starmer’s leadership. Supporters worry that a lack of proper vetting could lead to more scandals. The story could force a leadership change before the next election.
