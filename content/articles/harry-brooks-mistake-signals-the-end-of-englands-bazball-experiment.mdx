England's batting faltered in Adelaide when Harry Brook, after a risky stroke, was dismissed. The loss sparked criticism of the Bazball style, once hailed as high‑energy but now seen as chaotic. With two tests already lost, commentators say the Ashes will be taken by Australia before Christmas. Brook’s mistake highlighted the pressure on England’s new approach and its shaky future.
