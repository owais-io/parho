A father and his 24‑year‑old son are accused of pulling out legally licensed firearms at Bondi beach, killing 15 people and injuring dozens during a Hanukah celebration. The son was taken to hospital after being shot by police, while the father was killed on the scene. Police recovered all six guns, some long‑arms, and are reviewing gun‑licence rules. The duo had been on ASIO’s radar, but no terror threat was confirmed.
