Australia’s new law forces online sites that show porn, extreme violence or self‑harm content to check users’ ages before they can sign up or watch. Because the deadline is this Monday, several porn sites – like RedTube, YouPorn and Tube8 – have started blocking people who visit from an Australian IP address. The regulator warns companies that fail to comply could face fines of up to $49.5 million. The move follows the UK’s earlier attempt and aims to keep kids out of harmful material.
