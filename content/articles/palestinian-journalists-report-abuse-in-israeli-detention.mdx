A CPJ report says almost 60 Palestinian journalists who were held by Israel after the Oct. 7 attack were beaten, starved and sexually abused. The study, based on 59 interviews, names physical torture, rape, forced stress positions and neglect. Many were held without charges under administrative detention. Journalists lost an average of 23.5 kg in weight. Israel’s prison service and army deny the claims.
