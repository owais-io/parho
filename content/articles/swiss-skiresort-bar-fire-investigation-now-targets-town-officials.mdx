A deadly fire at a bar in Crans‑Montana that killed 40 people and injured 116 is sparking a new criminal probe. Prosecutors are questioning a current and a former local council official over possible safety breaches that may have contributed to the blaze, which started after sparklers ignited foam‑lined ceilings. The owners, Jacques and Jessica Moretti, were already suspects for negligence. Authorities aim to clarify who was responsible for the disaster.
