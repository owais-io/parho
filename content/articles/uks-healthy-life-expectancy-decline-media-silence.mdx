New UK data shows a child can expect to stay healthy only until about age 61, after which the next two decades are marred by illness. The article argues that this drop in healthy life years is a political choice tied to austerity, cuts to public services, and growing inequality. It points to Sweden’s opposite trend and calls on UK leaders to make health a priority, not just growth.
