Hospitals in Gaza are running low on basic supplies like gauze, antibiotics and thermometers because airstrikes are still happening. Over 50 people have died and more than 100 were injured in recent attacks. Medics say the health system is stretched thin, with staff and ambulances short. Even though a ceasefire was signed, fighting continues and aid deliveries are still falling short of the huge need.
