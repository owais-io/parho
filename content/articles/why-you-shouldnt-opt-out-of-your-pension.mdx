UK workers are automatically put into workplace pension plans, but some people opt out, losing free money and tax breaks. Staying enrolled lets you grow your savings over time. Even if you’re saving for a house, the long‑term benefit of a pension is huge. Small tips—add a percent when you get a raise, keep paying during maternity or job loss, use a lifetime ISA for deposits, and don’t rush to withdraw before 55—help you build a stronger retirement fund.
