Israeli airstrikes have killed hundreds of Lebanese, including children. Families like the Hamdan family lost all members. Towns like Al‑Nimiriya become ghost towns; people are forced to leave by displacement orders. Aid workers still help, but face the risk of tanks and possible invasion. Bombs hit homes, schools, and even a bridge over the Litani River. The war, sparked by Hezbollah rockets, has escalated beyond previous clashes.
