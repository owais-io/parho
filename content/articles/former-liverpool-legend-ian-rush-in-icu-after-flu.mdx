Ian Rush, the 64‑year‑old former Liverpool and Wales striker, was admitted to the Countess of Chester Hospital last week for breathing problems caused by the flu. He spent two days in intensive care and is still receiving treatment. Liverpool FC is wishing him a quick recovery. Meanwhile, England is seeing a record‑high number of flu cases, with hospitals treating thousands of patients daily.
