Australia’s health minister Mark Butler has warned that the rapid rise of electric bikes and scooters is a real emergency. In New South Wales, 226 ebike injuries in 2024 and 233 in the first seven months of 2025, plus several deaths, have spiked. The government is tightening import rules and giving police powers to seize illegal models, while retailers and teens argue limits won’t stop high‑speed tricks. Schools are now running safety lessons to curb risky rides.
