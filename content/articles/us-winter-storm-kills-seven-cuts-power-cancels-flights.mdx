A massive winter storm across the United States has killed at least seven people, left over a million people without power, and caused more than 10,000 flights to be cancelled. Heavy snow and ice hit everything from the Gulf Coast to New England, with some areas expecting up to 18 inches of snow. State and federal officials declared emergencies and urged people to stock up on supplies.
