Exeter pulled off a close win over Leicester, thanks to an early try from Greg Fisilau and a brilliant solo effort by Ridl that pushed the score to 14‑0. Leicester’s players struggled with missing key men, and a late break by Stephen Varney sealed the victory for Exeter. The game highlighted Exeter’s growing physical edge and set a positive tone for their upcoming matches.
