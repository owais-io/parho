The Crown Prosecution Service (CPS) has drafted guidance that says male circumcision might be treated as child abuse if it is done badly or in unsafe conditions. The move follows several deaths and serious infections linked to the procedure. Religious groups say circumcision is a protected tradition and only harmful when performed poorly. They call for better training and standards, while critics want the law to prevent abuse.
