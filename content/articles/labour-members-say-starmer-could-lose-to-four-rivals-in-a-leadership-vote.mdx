Labour members polled by Survation say four big names could beat Prime Minister Keir Starmer if the party held a leadership vote. Andy Burnham, Angela Rayner, Wes Streeting and former leader Ed Miliband all topped Starmer in the polls – Burnham and Rayner by wide margins. More than 50% of members want a new leader before the next election, and even many of Starmer’s own supporters do. The results show a split inside the party.
