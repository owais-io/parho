After the Trump administration revoked his security clearance in March, lawyer Mark Zaid sued and won a court order. A federal judge said the revocation was political retaliation for representing whistleblowers and ordered his clearance be fully restored. Zaid can now again handle classified cases. The White House had listed many names on a memo, but it no longer applies to him. The White House hasn't responded to questions.
