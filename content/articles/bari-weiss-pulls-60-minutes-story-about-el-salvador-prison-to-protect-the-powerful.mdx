CBS News editor‑in‑chief Bari Weiss stopped a 60 Minutes report that exposed cruel conditions in an El Salvador prison where Trump‑era migrants were sent. Weiss said the piece wasn’t ready and needed more comments from the Trump administration. Critics say this was a censorship move to shield powerful figures. The story had already been approved, edited, and promoted, and it highlights how media can be pressured to stay silent.
