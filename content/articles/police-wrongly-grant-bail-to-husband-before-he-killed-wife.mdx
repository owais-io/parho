Police gave bail to Brian Johnston, the estranged husband of Kelly Wilkinson, even though she was flagged as a high‑risk victim. Days later, on 20 April 2021, he set her house on fire and killed her. An inquest says the bail should have gone through a magistrate, not police. It will review police policies and the officer who made the decision. Johnston has been sentenced to life imprisonment.
