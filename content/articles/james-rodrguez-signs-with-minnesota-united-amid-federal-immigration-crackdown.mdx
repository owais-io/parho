Minnesota United football club has signed Colombian star James Rodríguez. The move comes while a large federal immigration team was stationed in the state, a period that caused worry and even death for some residents. The club says the player will help the team prepare for the World Cup and boost its culture. Rodríguez left Everton and is ready for a new challenge in the MLS.
