Georgia’s Democratic lawmakers wrote to the Justice Department, asking it to explain why Tulsi Gabbard, a former national intelligence director, was at the scene when the FBI seized almost 700 boxes of 2020 election records in Fulton County. The letter, signed by Senator Raphael Warnock and House members Lucy McBath and Nikema Williams, also warns about a possible foreign‑intelligence link and criticises President Trump’s claims that the election was rigged. The lawmakers want a briefing by February 13, 2026.
