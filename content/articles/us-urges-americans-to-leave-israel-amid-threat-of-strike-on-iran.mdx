The U.S. has told Americans in Israel to leave as a possible strike on Iran looms. The State Department warned of travel risks and urged non‑essential workers to depart. Talks over Iran’s nuclear program stalled, and U.S. officials say military action could be a last resort. Meanwhile diplomats are preparing for technical talks in Vienna to verify nuclear activities. These tensions raise concerns for residents and international observers.
