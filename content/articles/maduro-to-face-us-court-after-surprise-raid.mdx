The former Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro was taken by U.S. forces in a surprise raid last weekend. He will appear in a Manhattan federal court on Monday to answer charges of drug trafficking and weapons crimes. Trump praised the operation as a success and said the U.S. will control Venezuela for now. Venezuelan officials say the country is still run by a new acting president. Democrats say the raid violates U.S. law and should be stopped.
