A high‑speed train from Málaga to Madrid derailed near Adamuz in Córdoba, Spain, on Sunday night. The Iryo train hit a Renfe service, causing both trains to tumble off the tracks. At least 39 people have died, and many others were injured. Rescue teams are still working at the site, while officials and the prime minister express condolences and investigate why the crash happened on a straight track.
