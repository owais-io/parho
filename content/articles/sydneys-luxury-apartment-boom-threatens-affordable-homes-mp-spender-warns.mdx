MP Allegra Spender says Sydney’s trend of tearing down affordable flats to build luxury apartments is hurting community trust. She wrote to the housing minister and is urging councils to adopt rules that stop big losses of homes, like the City of Sydney does. Woollahra Council is fighting a proposed luxury tower that would cut 15‑17 affordable units. Spender says the current definition of “affordable” is too high and must change to help workers and families.
