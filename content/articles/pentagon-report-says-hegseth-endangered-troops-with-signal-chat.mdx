The Pentagon’s watchdog found that Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth broke rules by sending classified details about a Yemen airstrike to a Signal chat that also had top officials. The chat was discovered when a reporter joined the group. Hegseth refused to talk, wrote a short note claiming he only shared safe info and could declassify it. Congress and critics are calling for his resignation, while Hegseth defends himself on social media.
