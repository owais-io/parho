Tottenham Hotspur fired coach Thomas Frank after a string of poor results. The board is looking for a new manager and has several options: Roberto De Zerbi, assistant John Heitinga, and former England boss Mauricio Pochettino, who’s busy with the US World Cup squad. They also considered Andoni Iraola and former striker Robbie Keane. A decision is expected before the summer.
