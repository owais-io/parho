Arsenal and Chelsea played a tense match that ended 1‑1. Chelsea got a red card when Moisés Caicedo tackled Mikel Merino too hard, leaving them with ten men. The team still scored early in the second half through Trevoh Chalobah, while Arsenal answered with Merino’s equaliser. Despite being down a player, Chelsea kept pressure, but the game finished in a draw, leaving Arsenal with a point.
