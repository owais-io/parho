Dubai is being hit by Iranian missiles, but many British expatriates are treating it like everyday life. After a bomb at the Fairmont hotel, the government warned against sharing unverified videos and even shut down the internet briefly. Despite fears, most Brits stay, work from home or keep their businesses running, saying it feels normal. The government says safety is still top priority, and the economy will recover soon.
