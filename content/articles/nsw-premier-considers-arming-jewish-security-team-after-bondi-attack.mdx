New South Wales Premier Chris Minns says his government is looking into giving weapons to the Community Security Group (CSG), a Jewish protection team, after the Bondi beach attack that killed 15 people. Police will also bring long‑rifles to big events like New Year’s Eve in Sydney. Minns wants a royal commission to review the attack and improve security, even considering army troops for Jewish sites.
