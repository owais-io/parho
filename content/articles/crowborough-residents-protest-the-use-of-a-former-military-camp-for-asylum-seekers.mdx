Crowborough residents marched to protest the government's plan to house up to 500 male asylum seekers at a former military training camp. Local and far‑right activists joined the march, raising concerns about safety and the loss of a historic site. The Home Office says the move will replace hotels, but many locals fear the camp will bring danger and disrupt community life. A legal challenge is already underway, and police have increased their presence.
