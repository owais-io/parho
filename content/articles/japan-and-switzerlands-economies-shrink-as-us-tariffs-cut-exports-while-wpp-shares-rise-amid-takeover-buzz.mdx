Japan's economy fell 0.4 % in the July‑September quarter, the first drop in six quarters, after U.S. tariffs hit exports. Switzerland also saw a 0.5 % shrinkage, hurt by the same tariffs on chemicals and pharma. In London, advertising giant WPP’s shares jumped because investors think it might be bought by rivals or private‑equity firms. The company’s chairman is set to take on a Heathrow role too.
