The National Trust wants to protect the 55‑metre chalk giant on Cerne Hill in Dorset. It has already secured most of the £2.2 million price for 340 acres of surrounding land, but needs an extra £300,000. Donations will help keep the area safe for wildlife, especially the rare Duke of Burgundy butterfly, and allow more people to explore and study the ancient hill figure. Celebrities and locals are backing the appeal.
