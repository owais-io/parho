Savannah Guthrie’s 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy Guthrie, vanished last weekend in Arizona. Police found her DNA on the porch and suspect a kidnapping. The FBI is on the case and offers a $50,000 reward. The family released a tearful video asking anyone holding Nancy to speak. Law enforcement says she may still be alive, but her lack of medication worries everyone. The case has drawn national attention, including support from President Trump.
