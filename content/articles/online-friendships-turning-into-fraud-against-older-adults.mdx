Lonely seniors are being tricked online by scammers who pose as new friends on social media. After gaining trust, the fraudsters ask for small payments that add up to thousands of pounds. Banks say the average loss is around £3,100. Victims are advised to cut ties immediately, report the scam, and keep records of any messages.
