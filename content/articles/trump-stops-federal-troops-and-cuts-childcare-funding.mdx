On the last day of 2025, Trump pulled federal troops out of Los Angeles, Chicago and Portland. He also froze all state childcare payments, releasing money only when states can prove it’s being used properly. An appeals court let the administration cut Medicaid funding from Planned Parenthood centers in 22 states. A poll says 23 % of Americans think the health system is in crisis, and the stock market hit near‑record highs.
