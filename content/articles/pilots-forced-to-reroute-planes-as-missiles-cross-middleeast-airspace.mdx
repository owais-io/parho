With Iran’s war escalating, missiles have been fired into commercial flight paths. Pilots like Keith Tonkin have had to turn planes around or squeeze into tight corridors, diverting to nearby airports. Airspace over Dubai, Doha and Abu Dhabi shut down, leaving crews with little choice and added stress. Airlines are scrambling to keep passengers safe while managing congested air routes.
