Liverpool won a tense 1‑0 match against Tottenham, who were reduced to nine men after Xavi Simons was sent off. Alexander Isak scored early but was injured after a tackle in the second half and had to leave the pitch. Despite the loss, Tottenham’s fightback boosted their coach Thomas Frank’s morale, while Liverpool remain in fifth place. The game showed Liverpool’s resilience and left Isak sidelined for a while.
