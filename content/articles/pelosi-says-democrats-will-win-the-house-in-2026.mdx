Nancy Pelosi says Democrats will win the House back in the 2026 mid‑term elections and that Hakeem Jeffries will become speaker. She wants the new Democrats to restore congressional power that has been eroded by Republicans and President Trump. Pelosi notes Trump’s low approval and the usual loss of seats for the president’s party in mid‑terms. She will retire after 2027, hoping her final act is to bring the House back under Democratic control.
