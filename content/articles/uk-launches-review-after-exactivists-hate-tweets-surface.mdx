Yvette Cooper has started a review after old tweets from Alaa Abd el‑Fattah, a British‑Egyptian activist, were found that call for the killing of Zionists and other hateful remarks. The government had helped free him from prison in Egypt and welcomed him to the UK, but the revelations sparked calls to deport him or strip him of citizenship. The review will look at how the case was handled and whether the vetting process was adequate.
