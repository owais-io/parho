After the Supreme Court said most of his tariff plan was illegal, President Trump increased the worldwide duty on imports to 15% on Saturday. He said the move was legal and would start right away, even though the exact paperwork isn’t clear. The change hits goods from every country and follows his earlier 10% levy. European leaders warned about uncertainty, and U.S. business groups are demanding refunds.
