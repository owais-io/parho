On Jan. 30 and 31, activists will hold a national strike and protests across 50 states to demand the end of the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agency. The action follows at least eight deaths linked to ICE, including two in Minneapolis. Students at the University of Minnesota will walk out, while the group 50501 plans rallies, vigils, and boycotts of businesses that help ICE. Organizers want Congress to stop funding ICE and stop local police from cooperating.
