Mark Collett, the boss of Britain’s big far‑right group Patriotic Alternative, spoke at a nationalist meeting in St Petersburg, Russia. The event, set up by oligarch Konstantin Malofeyev, brought together right‑wing groups from Europe and Russia. UK MPs say it shows how the Kremlin might use such groups to stir trouble at home. Collett said he had no ties to Russia and was not paid to speak.
