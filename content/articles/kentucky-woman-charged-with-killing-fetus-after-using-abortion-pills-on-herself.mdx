A 35‑year‑old Kentucky woman, Melinda S Spencer, has been arrested on charges of first‑degree fetal homicide, abuse of a corpse and tampering with evidence after she reportedly bought abortion pills online, ended her pregnancy herself and buried the remains in her backyard. Kentucky doesn’t outlaw self‑induced abortions, but the state’s abortion ban means people in states with tighter laws often face criminal scrutiny. Police got involved after clinic staff learned of her pregnancy. No official comment yet.
