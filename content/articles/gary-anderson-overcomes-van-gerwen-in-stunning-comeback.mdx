Gary Anderson, 55, beat Michael van Gerwen 4‑1 in the first round of the British Open, sending the older player into the quarter‑finals for the first time since 2022. Anderson has been picking matches that suit him and enjoying the game again, while van Gerwen has been hampered by poor form and personal troubles. The win shows Anderson’s experience still counts, even as van Gerwen’s sharp edge is fading.
