Ronald Araújo has returned to Barcelona after a 47‑day mental‑health break. He was in the squad for the season‑opening Super Cup and was given the captain’s armband by manager Xabi Alonso. He lifted the trophy after the win over Real Madrid, showing the team’s support. The moment was emotional for Araújo, who had been struggling with criticism and was praised for his courage and resilience.
