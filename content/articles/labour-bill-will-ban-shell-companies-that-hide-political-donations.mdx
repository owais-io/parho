Labour’s new elections bill will close loopholes that let shell companies hide the source of political money. It will require any donor firm to prove it is truly UK‑based, and it will cap gifts from foreign businesses and restrict large donations, including those in cryptocurrency. The bill also adds 16‑year‑olds to the roll, pilots automatic registration, and strengthens rules against intimidation. The aim is to make politics more transparent and stop foreign influence.
