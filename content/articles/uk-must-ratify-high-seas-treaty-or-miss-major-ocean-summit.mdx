UK could be left out of a major oceans summit if Parliament doesn't finish signing the UN high‑seas treaty. The agreement protects marine life beyond national borders and goes into force this weekend. 18 environmental charities wrote to the foreign secretary, calling the slow pace a mistake and urging the bill to pass before the first Ocean Cop in August. 81 countries, including big powers, have already made the treaty law.
