Since the US and Israel began bombings on Feb. 28, Iran’s people have faced heavy losses. At least 175, many children, were killed in attacks on a school and a sports hall. Hospitals, historic sites, and thousands of homes were damaged, while black‑rain storms and toxic fumes hit Tehran. The UN warns of power cuts, medicine shortages, and rising food prices. Trump threatens to destroy Iranian power plants, and Iran says it may shut the Strait of Hormuz.
