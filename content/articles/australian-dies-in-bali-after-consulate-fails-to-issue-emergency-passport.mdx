An Australian man, 69, died in a Bali hospital after a surgery complication left him needing a transfer. His son Jake asked the Australian consulate to issue an emergency passport, but officials said they couldn’t act without his father’s consent. Despite repeated requests, the passport never came and the man died on 7 January. A complaint was ignored for years; the department later apologized and said it had reviewed the case.
