A man in his 50s died after a tree fell on his caravan during Storm Goretti in Cornwall. Emergency services responded to the Mawgan area of Helston. No foul play was suspected. The storm triggered yellow and amber warnings across the UK, bringing heavy wind, snow and ice. Power outages hit the south‑west, West Midlands and East Midlands, and rail services could be disrupted. Weather forecasts call for more snow and ice, so drivers should take extra time and local authorities urge caution.
