South East Water has been hit with a £22.5 million fine by Ofwat after repeated water shortages in Kent and Sussex from 2020‑2023 that left more than 280,000 people without supply. While the cold snap helped, regulators say the company failed to keep its network in good shape. The penalty won’t appear on bills, but experts warn customers may see rises later. The company has fought the decision in court.
