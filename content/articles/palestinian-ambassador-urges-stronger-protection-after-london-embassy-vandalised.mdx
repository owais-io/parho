Masked men waved Israeli flags and stuck stickers on the Palestinian embassy in London. Ambassador Husam Zomlot said the attack shows a lack of protection and asked the UK to give the embassy stronger security. He reminded Britain that diplomatic sites must be protected. The embassy has faced vandalism and threats before. Police said they review security for all embassies, but many, like the Palestinian mission, have minimal police presence.
