The chancellor said the government is looking for ways to soften the shock of the Iran conflict on household bills. She will not repeat the big subsidies of the Truss era, but will aim for targeted help for low‑income families. Reeves wants to stop companies from exploiting the crisis, and will give an update on fuel duty rises. She also plans to push the clean‑energy agenda and negotiate a deal with the EU to cut food prices.
