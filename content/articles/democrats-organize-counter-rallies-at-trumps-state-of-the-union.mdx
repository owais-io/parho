Democrats, including senators and congressmen, refused to attend President Trump’s State of the Union. Instead they staged a “People’s State of the Union” on the National Mall, chanting “Abolish ICE” and demanding release of Epstein files. While Trump spoke, a second event called “State of the Swamp” gathered celebrities at the National Press Club. The rallies drew about 220,000 viewers online and urged voters to fight back in the November elections.
