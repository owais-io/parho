Zubir Ahmed, a Muslim MP, says that online hate has grown since 9/11 and that a new government definition of “anti‑Muslim hostility” could help stop it. He points to a recent transplant video that went viral, the surge in hate‑crime statistics, and criticism from Conservatives who say the law is too broad. Ahmed believes the new definition recognises real racism and could protect Muslims and their families.
