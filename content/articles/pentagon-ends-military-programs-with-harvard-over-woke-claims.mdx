The Pentagon has decided to stop all military training, fellowships, and certificate programs at Harvard University. Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth called Harvard “woke” and accused it of promoting left‑leaning ideas that hurt the armed forces. This move follows a broader Trump‑era push to cut funding to universities that the administration says are anti‑American. Harvard’s leaders reject the accusations and defend academic freedom.
