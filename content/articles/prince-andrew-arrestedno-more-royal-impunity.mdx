Prince Andrew, the king’s brother, was taken into police custody on suspicion of misconduct in public office after a complaint by the anti‑monarchy group Republic.  The arrest follows years of hidden allegations linking him to Jeffrey Epstein and several women who said he abused them.  The case marks a shift: the monarchy can no longer hide behind privilege, and Parliament may now hold the prince accountable.
