Teachers in Victoria are set to strike tomorrow, with about 30,000 staff walking out after a 98% union vote. The protest demands a 35% pay rise, smaller classes and better mental‑health support, while the state government offered only an 18.5% increase plus a 1.5% overtime allowance. The walkout may close up to 500 schools, leaving parents to arrange childcare. Parents are advised to avoid sending children to school, and some schools will only provide supervision for a number of students.
