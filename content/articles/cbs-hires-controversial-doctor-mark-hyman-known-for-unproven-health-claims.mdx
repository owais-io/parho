CBS has added Dr Mark Hyman, a leading voice in “functional medicine,” to its new talent pool. Hyman promotes bold, unverified health tricks—like extreme diets, cold plunges, and unapproved supplements—while also backing vaccine‑skeptic ideas tied to RFK Jr. Critics say his work spreads pseudoscience and could mislead CBS viewers, especially as the network aligns with Trump‑era politics.
