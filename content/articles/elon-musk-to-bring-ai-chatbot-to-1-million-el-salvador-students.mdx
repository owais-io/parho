Elon Musk's AI company, xAI, is partnering with El Salvador’s government to roll out its chatbot Grok in more than 5,000 public schools over the next two years, reaching over 1 million students. President Nayib Bukele, a tech‑savvy leader, says the move will improve math, science and English. Critics point out that Grok has previously spread extremist, antisemitic and conspiratorial messages, raising safety concerns. Similar AI‑in‑schools programs have appeared in Estonia and Colombia.
