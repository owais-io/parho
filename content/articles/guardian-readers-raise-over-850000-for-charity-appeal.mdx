The Guardian’s final days of its Hope appeal have lifted more than £850,000 for five charities that fight racism, hate, and division. The charities—Citizens UK, the Linking Network, Locality, Hope Unlimited Charitable Trust, and Who is Your Neighbour?—work on projects like community support, youth clubs, and food banks. Readers are motivated by stories of local heroes standing up to hate and building stronger, more tolerant neighbourhoods.
