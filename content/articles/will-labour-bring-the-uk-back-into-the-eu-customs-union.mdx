Labour is quietly re‑examining a move to re‑join the EU customs union, a big change that could boost the UK economy. New minister Nick Thomas Symonds got a cabinet seat, showing the party is serious about a closer EU link. Starmer keeps saying he won’t go past his “red lines”, but many ministers think re‑joining could raise GDP by about 2%. The plan is still unconfirmed, but talks are happening behind the scenes.
