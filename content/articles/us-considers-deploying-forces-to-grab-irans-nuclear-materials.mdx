The Trump administration is reportedly thinking about sending special‑forces teams into Iran to grab its stockpile of highly enriched uranium (HEU), which could make nuclear bombs. Experts warn the job would be very risky and hard to pull off, because the material is buried deep in fortified sites. Trump said an operation would only happen after a major blow‑up of Iranian forces, and critics say the plan is still unclear.
