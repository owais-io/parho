Stacey Abrams, former Georgia governor candidate, announced that her 10 Steps campaign has gained new partners and millions of voters. The coalition, made up of groups like Democracy Forward, Indivisible, and Gen‑Z for Change, aims to organize people against Trump‑era policies that threaten democracy. Abrams says the movement must build a strong infrastructure before big actions like strikes. She calls the U.S. a “competitive authoritarian” country and urges everyone to join.
