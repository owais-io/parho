A new book by Nikolay Kononov tells the full story of Pavel Durov, the Russian entrepreneur who created the messaging app Telegram. It traces his rise from a science‑star pupil in St Petersburg to a billionaire tech leader who fights Russian officials and, later, French authorities. Durov is shown as a libertarian vision‑seeker who keeps all power in his own hands while pushing a free‑speech message. The book also covers his scepticism of governments and occasional attraction to conspiracy theories.
