Senators from both parties blasted President Trump after the U.S. seized a Venezuelan oil tanker off its coast. They say the move shows Trump is pushing the country toward war and that the official drug‑interdiction story is a lie. Trump said the tanker was seized for drug trafficking, but critics argue it’s a step toward regime change. The action comes amid a larger build‑up of U.S. forces in the Caribbean.
