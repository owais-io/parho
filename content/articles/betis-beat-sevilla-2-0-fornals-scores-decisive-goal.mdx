In a tense Seville derby, Betis beat Sevilla 2‑0. The match was delayed for 20 minutes after objects were thrown. Betis midfielder Pablo Fornals scored the first goal, making a brilliant run that beat defenders. A second goal from Sergi Altimira sealed the win. Several key players were unavailable, but Betis’s depth helped them secure their first derby win since 2018 under coach Manuel Pellegrini.
