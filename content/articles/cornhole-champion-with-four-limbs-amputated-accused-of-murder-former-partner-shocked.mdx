Former cornhole partner Mike Hoffman says he was shocked to learn his teammate, Dayton Webber, who was born with all four limbs amputated, has been arrested for allegedly shooting a passenger to death. Webber, a championship‑winning professional, was charged after a fight in a Maryland car left a 27‑year‑old dead. Hoffman recalls Webber’s independence and success, but says the case hurts two families. The investigation continues.
