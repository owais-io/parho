French President Emmanuel Macron was seen wearing a pair of high‑end, French‑made aviator sunglasses at a Davos meeting. The bright, gold‑bordered frames, produced by the centuries‑old Maison Henry Jullien, quickly became a hot commodity. When news spread, the company’s website crashed and orders surged worldwide. Macron had bought the glasses himself after a diplomatic gift offer was rejected. The trend highlights France’s luxury industry and the president’s style choices.
