Despite a new law that will stop criminalising abortion, police in England are still arresting women suspected of illegal terminations. Between June last year and January this year, the Metropolitan and Nottinghamshire police reported several arrests, including cases where women were caught in hospitals or homes after miscarriages or early pregnancies. Activists say the police actions are traumatic and unfair, especially when the law’s changes should protect these women. Parliament is still debating the final amendments.
