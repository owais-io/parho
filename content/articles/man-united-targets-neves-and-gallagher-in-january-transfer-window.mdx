Manchester United is looking to add Rúben Neves from Al‑Hilal and Conor Gallagher from Atlético Madrid in January. They also want Tyler Adams from Bournemouth but that might wait until the summer because of an injury. United is fighting Liverpool and Manchester City to sign forward Antoine Semenyo, who prefers Liverpool. The club wants players with Premier League experience and is also eyeing Brighton midfielder Carlos Baleba for summer. Semenyo's release clause is £65m and can be triggered next month.
