Trump says the U.S. is looking at “very strong” options if Iran continues to kill protesters. The Iranian crackdown has killed at least 500 people, with rights groups reporting hundreds more arrested and many deaths. Iran says it wants to negotiate, while Trump hints he could step in if the violence escalates. Iran’s parliament warns Washington against miscalculations, and the country has cut internet access to curb protests.
