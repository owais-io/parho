The FBI has announced a $50,000 reward for anyone who can help catch the man who shot two students at Brown University. The suspect, described as a stocky 5‑ft‑8‑in male wearing dark clothes, is still free. Police have been asking for video and witnesses, but the investigation has been slow, causing frustration among students and local officials. The state and university are urging anyone with information to come forward.
