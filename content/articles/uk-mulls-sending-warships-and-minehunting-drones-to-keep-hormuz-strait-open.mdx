Britain is considering sending warships and special drones that can locate and destroy mines into the Middle East to keep the Strait of Hormuz open. The Strait is a key oil route threatened by Iran, and the U.S. has asked the UK and other countries to help. Energy Secretary Ed Miliband said ministers are exploring every option, but no final decision has been made. The government is also weighing how rising oil prices will affect the UK.
