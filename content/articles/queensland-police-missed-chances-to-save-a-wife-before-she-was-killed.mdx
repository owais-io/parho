An inquest into the murder of 27‑year‑old Kelly Wilkinson revealed that Queensland Police missed two key chances to stop her death. Her estranged husband was wrongly granted bail for rape, and a breach of Wilkinson’s protection order was mistakenly filed as a “street check,” letting the risk slip through the system. The probe will look at police training, policy and resource gaps that left Wilkinson without the help she needed.
