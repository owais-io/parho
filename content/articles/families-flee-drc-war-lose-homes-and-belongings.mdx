Families from the town of Luvungi in eastern DRC fled for 21 miles on foot after bombings started, carrying only what they could. They lost houses, livestock, clothes and even newborns. Thousands now live in Busuma camp in Burundi, facing food shortages and health risks. The survivors say they will not return home, living in fear of disease and with only a few possessions left.
