Britain’s recent energy price shock shows how quick‑fix politics have hurt its future. The country has half of Europe’s tidal power, but governments only give it tiny support, so it stays more expensive than wind. In Scotland tidal farms already produce energy, and with better backing they could match wind costs in five years. Rooftop solar with batteries could keep rural land green while boosting the grid.
