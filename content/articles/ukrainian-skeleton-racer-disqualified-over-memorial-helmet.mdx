Vladyslav Heraskevych, a top Ukrainian skeleton racer, was kicked out of the 2026 Winter Olympics after he refused to remove a helmet that displayed photos of 24 Ukrainian war victims. The International Olympic Committee said the helmet broke its rules and that political statements aren’t allowed during competition. Talks with the IOC failed just before the race, and Heraskevych plans to appeal while the sport community voices outrage.
