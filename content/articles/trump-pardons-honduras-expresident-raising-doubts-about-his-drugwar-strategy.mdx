Trump pardons former Honduran president Juan Orlando Hernández, who was convicted of running a major cocaine smuggling operation. Critics say this undermines his hard‑line drug‑war image and reveals double standards, especially as he continues to target other Latin American leaders like Venezuela's Maduro. Experts argue the pardon is politically motivated and exposes inconsistencies in U.S. drug policy. The move has sparked debate over whether Trump is using drug prosecutions for political gains.
