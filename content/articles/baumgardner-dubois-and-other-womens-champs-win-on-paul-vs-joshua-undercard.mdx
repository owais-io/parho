On Friday night in Miami, four women’s world title fights were on the undercard of Jake Paul vs. Anthony Joshua. Alycia Baumgardner kept her junior‑lightweight titles, defeating Leila Beaudoin by unanimous decision. Caroline Dubois retained her lightweight crown over Camila Panatta. Australia’s Cherneka Johnson won a close decision against Amanda Galle, and Costa Rica’s Yokasta Valle kept her straw‑weight belt after a bloody majority decision. The fights gave women a big stage.
