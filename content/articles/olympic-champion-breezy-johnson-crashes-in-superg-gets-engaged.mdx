Breezy Johnson, who won Olympic gold in downhill, fell during the women’s super‑G when she clipped a gate and hit the safety fence. While she missed a medal, her boyfriend, Connor Watkins, surprised her by proposing near the finish line. The couple, who met on a dating app, said the Olympic setting made the proposal special, and Johnson happily left with both a medal and a ring.
