Germany’s oldest monastic brewery, Weltenburg Abbey, will be sold to the Munich brewer Schneider Weisse. The move comes after a decade of falling sales and the church has had to keep the brewery afloat. The sale will finish in January 2027, keep 21 workers, and stop production at the Bischofshof plant. It’s a way to protect a 1,000‑year brewing tradition while saving jobs.
