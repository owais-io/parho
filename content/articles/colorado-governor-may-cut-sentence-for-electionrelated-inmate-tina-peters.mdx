A lawyer says Colorado Gov. Jared Polis is likely to cut Tina Peters’ nine‑year prison term. Peters, a former county clerk, was convicted for letting an unauthorized person access election equipment and for trying to influence a public office. Trump has asked the governor to pardon her. Democrats and law‑enforcement officials worry a clemency would be unfair and undermine democracy.
