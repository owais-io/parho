A fiancé of a Jamaican‑born U.S. Army veteran says he deserves to stay in the country after being detained in Georgia. The veteran was taken by ICE after a minor arrest, a move that reversed a Biden‑era protection for non‑citizen veterans. His family is fighting deportation, raising money online and hoping lawmakers act. Congress is probing how many veterans are being targeted, as VA data may expose more.
