After gas prices spiked following Russia’s war in Ukraine, Pakistan faced frequent power cuts and rising bills. Falling solar panel costs and generous government incentives made rooftop solar a cheap, reliable alternative. By 2025, solar now supplies about 20 % of the nation’s power, saving roughly $12 bn in oil and gas imports. The shift also eases fuel shortages and reduces inflation, showing renewables can boost energy security.
