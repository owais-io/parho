Eluned Morgan, leader of Welsh Labour, launched the party’s campaign for the upcoming Senedd elections. She said Labour is proud of its patriotic spirit and will focus on fixing NHS wait times, improving schools and boosting the economy. She promised a £4 billion NHS plan, cheaper bus fares, lower energy bills and ending homelessness by 2034. Morgan criticised Plaid Cymru and Reform UK for lacking clear plans and threatened to damage the NHS.
