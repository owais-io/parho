Former U.S. Senator Kyrsten Sinema admitted in court papers that she had a romantic affair with married bodyguard Matthew Ammel. A lawsuit from Ammel’s estranged wife claims the affair broke her marriage, but Sinema says all encounters happened outside North Carolina and asks the case be dismissed. She also denies sending a photo or arranging drug trips, and her campaign paid Ammel more than $100,000 after she left office.
