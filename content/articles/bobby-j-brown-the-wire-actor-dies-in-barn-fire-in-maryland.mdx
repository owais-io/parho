Bobby J. Brown, best known for his role in HBO’s “The Wire,” was killed in a barn fire at his Maryland home on February 24. He had gone inside to try and start a car, the blaze spread quickly, and his wife could not extinguish it in time. Brown was pronounced dead on the scene. His family said the death was accidental, and they mourned his loss.
