A Russian missile struck the Pivdennyi port near Odesa, killing seven and injuring 15. Ukraine said it had also shot down a Russian tanker off Libya with drones. The port is a key export hub and is often targeted. Ukraine is working with Moldova to set up new routes for trucks and passengers. Talks to end the war are happening in Miami, with U.S. and Ukrainian officials negotiating while Putin vows to keep fighting.
