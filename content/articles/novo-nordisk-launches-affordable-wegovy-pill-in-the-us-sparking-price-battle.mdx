Novo Nordisk has just started selling its Wegovy weight‑loss pill in the United States. The pill costs about $149 a month for the lowest dose, much cheaper than the $1,000‑plus monthly price of the injectable version. It’s needle‑free and easier to take, so it should attract many people who want to lose weight. The move starts a price fight with rivals like Eli Lilly, who also sell GLP‑1 injections. The drug is now available at major pharmacies and online.
