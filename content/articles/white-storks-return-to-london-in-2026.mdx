London is bringing white storks back in 2026 as part of a rewilding project. The birds will be released from an aviary at Eastbrookend Country Park, near a new data centre in Dagenham. Alongside storks, beavers will be reintroduced to restore wetlands. The project, funded by the city mayor and local council, will let schools and residents watch the animals, boost pride and encourage kids to care for nature.
