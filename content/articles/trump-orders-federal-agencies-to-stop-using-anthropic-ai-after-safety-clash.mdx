Donald Trump told all U.S. federal agencies to immediately stop using Anthropic’s Claude AI system. The Department of Defense had been trying to get the company to loosen its safety rules so the Pentagon could use the tech for military purposes, but Anthropic refused to drop its guardrails. Trump criticized the company’s stance on social media, demanding it cede control to the U.S. military.
