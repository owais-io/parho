After the US‑Israel attack on Iran, many flights were cancelled, leaving British residents in the Middle East stranded. Families in Bahrain, Dubai, and Saudi Arabia have been living in constant fear, trying to stay safe while waiting for flights to resume. Some have had to sleep on floors, stockpile food, and keep children calm. Others were moved to hotels in Dubai as airports closed, and many hope to return home when airspace opens again.
