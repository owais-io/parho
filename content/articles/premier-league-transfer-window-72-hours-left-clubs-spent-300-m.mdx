With just 72 hours left in the January transfer window, Premier League clubs have already spent around £300 million—second‑largest in a decade. Big spenders like Manchester City and Aston Villa have been active, while clubs such as Arsenal, Liverpool, Manchester United and Newcastle still haven't opened their accounts. Notable moves include Raheem Sterling ending his Chelsea contract, West Ham signing two South Americans, and several players moving to lower‑profile clubs. Fans can expect more surprises as the window closes.
