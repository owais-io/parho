Waitrose has pulled back its Deeside mineral water after the Food Standards Agency warned that some bottles might contain tiny glass pieces. The 750ml bottles are made from a spring near Balmoral Castle. Customers who bought the water should return it to the store or call for a refund. Look for the batch codes on the label to see if yours is affected.
