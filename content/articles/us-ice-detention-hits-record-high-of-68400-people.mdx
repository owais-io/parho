US ICE detention reached a new record of more than 68,400 people as of December 14, 2025. ICE releases data every two weeks, showing the number rose again after a peak earlier that month. Since January, the administration has arrested over 328,000 and deported nearly 327,000 people. Most detainees have no criminal record, even though officials say they target only serious offenders. The Trump administration also cut legal protections for many immigrants.
