Researchers discovered that popular TikTok and Instagram influencers are urging healthy, fit young men to get testosterone tests, even though most of them don't need it. These posts link low testosterone to being a “real man” and sell tests, supplements or drugs. The study says this marketing spreads fear, creates unnecessary medical costs, and may hide real health problems. Experts warn routine testing can be harmful and is usually not needed.
