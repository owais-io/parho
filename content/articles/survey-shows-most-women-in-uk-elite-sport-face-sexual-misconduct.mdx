A recent survey of 260 women in UK elite sport found that 88 % had experienced some form of sexual misconduct in the last five years, including harassment, assault and rape. Five people (2 %) said they were raped. Most perpetrators were men, and only a third felt governing bodies were handling the problem well. The report urges organisations to act quickly to protect women and keep the sport safe.
