Police in Canberra took down five posters from a local bar that showed leaders like Trump, Putin, Netanyahu in Nazi uniforms. The owners called it anti‑fascist art, but police said the images might break new hate‑symbol laws. The bar was closed for a couple of hours, and the posters were later put back with “CENSORED” stickers. The incident sparked debate over art, free speech and the new laws.
