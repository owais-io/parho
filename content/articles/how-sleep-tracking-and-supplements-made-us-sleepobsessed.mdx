A few decades ago, people rarely talked about sleep, but now it's a major obsession. Sleep trackers, apps, podcasts, and even gummies promise perfect rest, and some people worry they’ll get sick if they don’t sleep right. Experts say the idea of needing exactly eight hours is a myth and that our sleep needs vary. If you’re stressed about your sleep, simple tips or a basic app can help you relax instead of over‑analyzing.
