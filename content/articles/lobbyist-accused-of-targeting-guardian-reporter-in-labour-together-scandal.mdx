Tom Harper, a lobbyist hired by the Labour Together thinktank, made a 58‑page report that accused journalists of Russian ties and then began probing Guardian reporter Henry Dyer. The report, which has no evidence, and Harper’s new inquiry have sparked investigations by PR trade bodies and a Whitehall ethics probe. Politicians call for Labour leader Keir Starmer to fire the former Labour Together director, Josh Simons, who is also under scrutiny.
