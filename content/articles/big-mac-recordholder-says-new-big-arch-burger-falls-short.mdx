Donald Gorske, who has eaten more Big Macs than anyone else in history, says McDonald’s new Big Arch burger isn’t as good as the classic Big Mac. The 72‑year‑old finds the Big Arch’s extra meat and white cheddar too heavy for him and prefers the familiar flavor of the Big Mac he has had since 1972. McDonald’s promoted the Big Arch as its biggest burger, but Gorske says it’s not for him.
