Rory McIlroy was crowned BBC’s Sports Personality of the Year at a ceremony that also named the European Ryder Cup team Team of the Year. McIlroy praised golf’s achievements and his own career milestones, saying he still has many major championships ahead and remains injury‑free. Fellow golfer Tommy Fleetwood congratulated him. McIlroy mentioned upcoming trips, including a fight in Miami and a return to Florida. The event marked a high point for golf.
