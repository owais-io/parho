UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Ukrainian specialists will travel to the Gulf to advise on intercepting Iranian drones that are attacking allies in the region. Britain is not joining the US‑Israeli strikes on Iran but will keep defensive operations running. Starmer will pair Ukrainian experts with British advisers to help Gulf partners shoot down the drones. The move comes amid growing tension after Iran’s drone attacks in response to recent strikes.
