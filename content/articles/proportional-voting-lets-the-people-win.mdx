A Guardian letter argues that the UK’s first‑past‑the‑post system forces voters to pick between “good” and “bad” parties, leaving many voices unheard. Proportional representation (PR) would match seats to votes, reduce tactical blocking, and keep extremist parties out of power. Critics worry PR could give tiny parties too much influence, but the writer says PR still keeps democracy fair and balanced.
