A 19‑year‑old Mexican man died at a Florida ICE detention center, making him the youngest person to die in ICE custody during Trump’s presidency. The death, ruled a presumed suicide, is still under investigation. He was arrested for fraud and resisting an officer, and ICE said he had no known mental‑health problems or suicide risk when he was screened. The facility has a history of unsafe conditions, including toxic sprays and carbon monoxide leaks.
