In a Pentagon briefing, former Fox News host Pete Hegseth defended President Trump’s decision to launch a major military operation in Iran. He said the war would be fought on U.S. terms, without “politically correct” rules or a goal of nation‑building, and that the U.S. would stay until it achieved its objectives. Hegseth blamed Iran’s missiles for the attack, but gave no clear exit timeline, hinting the U.S. might keep troops in the region.
