The Guardian has published a set of corrections to earlier articles. It clarifies that the Tumbler Ridge shooting piece wrongly named a victim’s relatives, corrects the year of Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean’s bronze medal to 1994, and fixes several other errors, including a note about a grand jury decision and a mislabelled protest photo. Readers can submit further questions or requests for clarification through the Guardian’s contacts.
