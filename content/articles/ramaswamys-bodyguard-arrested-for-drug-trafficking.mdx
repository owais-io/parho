Republican candidate Vivek Ramaswamy was alarmed when his family’s bodyguard, Justin Salsburey, was arrested on federal drug‑trafficking charges. Authorities found 261 packages of fake OxyContin laced with fentanyl, meth, MDMA and steroids at the couple’s Ohio home. Ramaswamy’s team immediately cut ties with Salsburey, and the wife, a schoolteacher, was put on unpaid leave. Ramaswamy, a biotech entrepreneur, now faces a May primary for Ohio governor.
