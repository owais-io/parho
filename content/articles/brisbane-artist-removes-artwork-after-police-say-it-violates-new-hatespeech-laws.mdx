James Hillier, a Brisbane visual artist, was told by police that some of his Palestine‑supporting pieces could break Queensland’s newly passed hate‑speech laws. He took down three artworks, including one that used the banned phrase “from the river to the sea.” Hillier is now seeking legal advice while hoping to keep speaking out about Palestine without risking jail.
