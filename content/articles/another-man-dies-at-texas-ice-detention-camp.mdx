A second man, Victor Manuel Diaz, 36, died at Camp East Montana in El Paso, Texas, on Jan. 14, after another detainee, Geraldo Lunas Campos, died earlier in the same camp. ICE says Diaz died of a presumed suicide but the cause is still under investigation. Campos’ death is being investigated as a possible homicide after witnesses say guards choked him. Last year ICE lost 32 detainees, the most in two decades, and this year five more have died.
