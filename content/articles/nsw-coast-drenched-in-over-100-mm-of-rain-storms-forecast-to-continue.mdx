New South Wales has dumped more than 100 mm of rain on its south coast, causing flash floods and dozens of people asking for help. Storms are expected to keep falling over the weekend, with heavy showers in Sydney, Illawarra and the Central Coast. Many beaches have been closed, and police warn against surfing. Climate change is making such extreme weather more common.
