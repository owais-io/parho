Viktoriia Roshchyna, a Ukrainian journalist, was taken by Russia in 2022 and held in harsh prisons. In September 2024 she was moved from Taganrog to Sizo‑3 in Kizel, where she became very thin and couldn’t walk or eat. A former Azov soldier, Mykyta Semenov, witnessed her suffering and said she was beaten and on a hunger strike. She died after eight days, with bruises indicating strangulation.
