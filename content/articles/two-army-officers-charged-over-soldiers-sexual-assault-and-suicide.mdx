A former sergeant was jailed for sexually assaulting a 19‑year‑old Royal Artillery gunner, Jaysley Beck, who later committed suicide. Now, the Ministry of Defence has charged two officers—Major James Hook and Colonel Samantha Shepherd—for failing to maintain good order after the assault. The case followed an inquest that highlighted command failures. Beck’s mother says the army still hasn’t delivered on promised changes for young service members.
