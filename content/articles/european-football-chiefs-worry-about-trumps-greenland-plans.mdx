European football leaders met in Budapest to talk about U.S. President Donald Trump’s possible plan to annex Greenland. The leaders are worried because Greenland is part of Denmark and a move could spark a boycott of the World Cup, which the U.S. will host. No firm decision was made, but talks continue, and UEFA may act if the situation escalates. The meeting was informal and linked to a Hungarian football anniversary.
