Fans say the cheapest tickets for next summer’s World Cup final will cost over £3,000, and the most dedicated supporters could spend more than $6,900 to follow their team from the first match to the final. Football Supporters Europe calls this a betrayal and demands FIFA halt ticket sales until prices are fair. The outrage shows how high the cost has risen, especially with travel and accommodation added.
