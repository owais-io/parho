Jan Ainsley, 76, was a university lecturer teaching sociology, politics, education and the arts. She worked in Evesham and Chichester, championing lifelong learning and adding diverse writers and film to the curriculum. After cancer, she helped create a course for women with breast cancer and taught medical students. She later ran the New Park cinema, helped it thrive, and campaigned for a funded NHS. She is survived by husband Geoff, son Jack, step‑daughter Maria, five grandchildren and sister Kate.
