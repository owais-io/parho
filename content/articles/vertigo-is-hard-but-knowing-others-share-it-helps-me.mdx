Rebecca Shaw talks about her battle with benign paroxysmal positional vertigo (BPPV). She explains how tiny ear crystals make her feel like the world is spinning, causing dizziness, nausea and sometimes vomiting. She’s found comfort knowing that famous cricketer Steve Smith and others suffer the same. Shaw also mentions treatments like the Epley maneuver, her hospital visits, and how hearing others' stories makes her feel less alone.
