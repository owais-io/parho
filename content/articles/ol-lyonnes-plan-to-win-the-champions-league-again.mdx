OL Lyonnes, the rebranded name for Lyon’s women’s team, is now owned by Michele Kang and wants to win the Champions League fast. They’re adding new players, a big stadium and a new training centre, and they’re doubling marketing and fan‑experience staff. Attendance is low, so the club is trying fun events and a shared schedule with the men’s team to draw more fans. The plan shows how the club wants to match men’s resources and boost French women’s football.
