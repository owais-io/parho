Brooklyn Beckham’s Instagram roast‑chicken video and his mum’s “like” turned a small family joke into a public feud. The incident highlights a new trend where Gen Z and millennials cut ties with parents, calling it “no‑contact.” Experts say up to 20‑27% of families in the UK and US experience estrangement. Some use tactics like “grey rocking” to stay calm, while support groups form online for those going through the same split.
