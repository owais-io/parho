Iran’s protests began with shopkeepers angry over a plunging rial and rising food prices. They quickly turned into a nationwide march against the theocratic leaders. The government has fired on crowds, killed hundreds, and shut down phones and internet. Protesters use satellite links to share videos of rallies and bodies. Outside Iran, the U.S. and Israel call for regime change and are ready to use sanctions or even military force.
