The article argues that British newsrooms lack diversity, especially Black journalists, and that this shapes what stories get told. A new study shows a backlash against diversity initiatives and that Black reporters are rarely seen in top award lists. The author, a university professor, says that a lack of Black voices leads to biased coverage of events like Brexit and Grenfell. She calls for more Black representation in editorial leadership.
