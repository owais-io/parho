At the 2026 Winter Olympics, the United States beat Canada 3‑2 in overtime to win gold. Center Jack Hughes scored the winning goal after losing two teeth from a hit in the third period. Goalie Connor Hellebuyck made key saves, keeping the Americans alive. The win ends a 12‑year gold drought for U.S. men’s hockey and creates a new Olympic legend for Hughes.
