Kemi Badenoch, a senior UK Conservative, has clarified that she never wanted Britain to join the US‑led strikes on Iran, although she supports the attack. She now says the UK will not be involved but will help locate missile sites. Badenoch also called Donald Trump’s attacks on Prime Minister Keir Starmer “childish”, distancing herself from the unpopular US president. The shift reflects changing views amid public opposition to the campaign.
