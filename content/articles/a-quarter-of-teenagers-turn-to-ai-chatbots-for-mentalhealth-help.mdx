Research shows about a quarter of teenagers in England and Wales who have experienced youth violence are turning to AI chatbots like ChatGPT for help. They say the bots feel like a friend, are available any time, and keep secrets. Many teens feel the NHS and charities are slow or lack empathy. While some worry about safety and regulation, experts say more human support is still needed.
