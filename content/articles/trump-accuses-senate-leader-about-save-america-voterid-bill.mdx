Trump has taken out his anger at Senate Majority Leader John Thune over the Save America Act, a bill that would overhaul U.S. voting rules. Thune says the Senate has no 60 votes to pass it and that the bill’s “talking filibuster” would fail. Trump wants a vote and has added extra provisions—mail‑vote bans, restrictions on gender‑affirming care and trans athletes—that some Republicans reject. The law could block millions from voting and will be tested during the mid‑term election.
