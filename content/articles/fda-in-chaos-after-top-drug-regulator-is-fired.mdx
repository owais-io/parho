After the FDA’s chief drug regulator was suddenly fired, the agency has been thrown into chaos. The director of the drug review center resigned amid accusations of retaliation, and the new top job has seen back‑and‑forth choices. Industry insiders say the turmoil hurts trust and could speed up approvals in a chaotic way. The shake‑up shows deep power struggles that worry the public and the pharma world.
