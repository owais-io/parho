Bernie Sanders says America is slipping and explains how we can reverse that trend. He lists problems: shrinking middle class, rising inequality, low wages, weak schools, healthcare, unaffordable homes, food, bad public transport, and political corruption with a greedy billionaire class. He calls for a grassroots movement that ends corporate influence, guarantees Medicare for All, free college, affordable housing, higher wages, stronger unions, and fair taxes. He uses a NYC campaign example and stresses people can unite to win.
