Europe plans a big loan for Ukraine using Russian money that is frozen in EU banks. Brussels says it will help Ukraine pay for its army and keep the country running. Belgium worries the plan could hurt its banks and create legal problems, so the EU added extra safeguards. EU leaders will decide by month‑end whether to use the frozen money or other EU funds. Ukraine still needs a lot of money to keep fighting the war.
