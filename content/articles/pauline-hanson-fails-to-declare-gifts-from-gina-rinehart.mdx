Pauline Hanson, leader of the One Nation party, has repeatedly failed to declare flights and other gifts from mining magnate Gina Rinehart. The Senate rules require members to report such gifts within 35 days, but Hanson and her staff have delayed or omitted several entries. This oversight raises questions about conflicts of interest and the integrity of her campaign. Politicians and voters are calling for stricter enforcement and clearer rules.
