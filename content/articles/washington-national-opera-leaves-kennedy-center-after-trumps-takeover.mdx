The Washington National Opera has decided to end its long‑standing partnership with the Kennedy Center after Donald Trump became the institution’s chair. Trump’s takeover sparked a wave of cancellations from performers and a drop in ticket sales, and many donors and audience members have boycotted the venue. WNO will become an independent nonprofit and is looking for a new home in Washington, D.C., though no lease is yet signed.
