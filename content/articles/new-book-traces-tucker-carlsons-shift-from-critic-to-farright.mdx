A new book by Jason Zengerle looks at how Tucker Carlson went from a sharp‑witted conservative critic to a far‑right media star. It tracks his career from a 2009 CPAC speech to his rise on Fox News, his close ties with Trump, and his eventual firing in 2023. Zengerle also digs into Carlson’s private life and the shift in his views, raising questions about why he was let go and what’s next.
