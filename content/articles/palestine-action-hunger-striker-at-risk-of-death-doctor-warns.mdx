Umer Khalid, a 22‑year‑old Palestinian activist, has stopped drinking water during a hunger strike that began in November. A doctor says he could die in a few days, especially because he has a muscle‑weakness condition. The strike was part of protests against charges linked to alleged break‑ins. Most of the group ended their strikes after the UK government denied a big arms contract, but Khalid still continues.
