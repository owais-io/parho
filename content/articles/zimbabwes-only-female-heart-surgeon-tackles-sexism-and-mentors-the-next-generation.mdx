Dr Kudzai Kanyepi is Zimbabwe’s sole female heart surgeon. She trained in South Africa while pregnant and now works at Harare’s main hospital. Every day she faces sexism—fewer referrals and constant doubt about her skill. Despite that, she performs surgeries, mentors young doctors, and plans a foundation to help girls from poor backgrounds study medicine and to bring more children access to heart surgery.
