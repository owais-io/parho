On Christmas, Donald Trump slammed Kentucky Representative Thomas Massie, who helped pass a law to release all Jeffrey Epstein documents, calling him a “lowlife.” Massie responded by sharing the insult on X, asking followers to donate to his 2026 campaign. He raised nearly $3,000 in two hours. Trump has already backed a rival for Massie’s seat, deepening the split between the two Republicans. The Justice Department still has more Epstein files to process.
