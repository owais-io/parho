Families across Minnesota, New York, California, Illinois and Colorado are in danger of losing federal childcare help as the Trump administration threatens to freeze billions in aid over alleged fraud. The move would shut classrooms, hurt parents and the workers who run them. State governments sued, winning a temporary halt. The fight shows how politics can endanger child care for families and the workforce.
