Three‑week‑old Panya is a pygmy hippo born at Duisburg zoo in Germany and has become an online hit. His tiny size and adorable looks have made people share pictures and comment, sparking a frenzy similar to last year’s viral hippo Moo Deng. The zoo keeps Panya in a private enclosure with his mother for now, but will open him up to visitors later. Fans are cautioned not to put too much pressure on the little animal.
