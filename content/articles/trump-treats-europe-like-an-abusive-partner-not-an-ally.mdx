European leaders are scrambling to counter Donald Trump, who is pushing Ukraine into a deal that benefits the US and Russia. Trump wants fewer security guarantees for Europe and aims to make the continent weaker and more independent, so it can be easily controlled. The editorial urges Europe to strengthen its own defence and economic power, unite more closely, and adopt a tougher stance against Washington while protecting its values.
