UK MPs worry that freezing the BBC World Service’s budget could let foreign state media fill the gap. The watchdog said the service’s funding is still unclear just before its current deal ends. With trust in BBC stable but rivals rising, MPs fear losing influence and opening the door to propaganda. They call for clearer funding and better governance to keep the service a trusted voice.
