U.S. senator Marco Rubio briefed top lawmakers on Iran just before President Trump’s State of the Union speech, where he warned Tehran would never get nuclear weapons. Rubio said the U.S. had deployed aircraft and warships and was ready for a strike if talks failed. Trump said diplomacy was the goal but threatened “bad things” if Iran didn’t cooperate. Talks are set for Geneva, while U.S. embassy staff in Lebanon are on standby.
