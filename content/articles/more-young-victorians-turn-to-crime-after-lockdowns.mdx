Many children in Victoria have fallen into crime after lockdowns. Dr Adam Deacon, a child psychiatrist, explains that missing school, family troubles, and online influences push kids toward crime. The state has seen a sharp rise in youth offences, prompting tougher laws and new funding for early help. Deacon stresses that understanding each child’s background can stop crime before it starts.
