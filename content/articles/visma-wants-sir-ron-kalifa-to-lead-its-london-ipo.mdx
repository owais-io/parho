Visma, a large European software firm, is preparing a €20 bn IPO that could be held in London. To boost its chances, the company has approached Sir Ron Kalifa – former Worldpay chief and Bank of England director – to become chair of the new company. The listing’s venue is still undecided, with Stockholm seen as a rival after a recent Swedish IPO success. Visma is holding investor talks and has Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley and UBS on its side.
