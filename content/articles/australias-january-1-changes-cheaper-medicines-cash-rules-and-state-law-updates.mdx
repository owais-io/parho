Starting Jan 1, Australians get cheaper PBS drugs (max $25), a 24‑hour nurse‑run 1800 MEDICARE line, and a new online mental‑health chat. Social‑security payments get modest increases, and grocery and petrol shops must accept cash for bills up to $500. Families receive three subsidised childcare days a week. State changes include free transport for kids in Victoria, higher toll levies in NSW, and a child‑sex‑offender register in Queensland.
