The long‑running TV show *Time Team* has rebooted on YouTube and Patreon, drawing a global fan base of 350,000 subscribers and 16,000 monthly patrons. After its 2013 TV cancellation, fans pushed for a revival, and the team now funds digs themselves, including a month‑long project at Orkney’s Ness of Brodgar. With flexible online formats, they keep audiences engaged, plan big events like a 3‑hour Sutton Hoo broadcast, and even aim to add kids’ content.
