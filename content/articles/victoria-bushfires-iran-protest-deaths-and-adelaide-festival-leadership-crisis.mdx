Victoria bushfires have left one dead, scorched homes and businesses, and cost many families and wine producers huge losses, while fires still burn. In Iran, protests have sparked hundreds of deaths, with authorities arresting key activists as the US and Tehran clash over possible intervention. Meanwhile, Adelaide’s Writers’ Week has been rocked by a leadership crisis after board members quit over the removal of Palestinian author Randa Abdel‑Fattah.
