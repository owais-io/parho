Kirsty Muir was close to a medal in the women’s big‑air event at the Winter Olympics, but a fall on her final jump left her in fourth place. Canadian Megan Oldham won gold, and Italian Flora Tabanelli took bronze. Muir’s jump was strong earlier, but a small error pushed her just outside the medals. The race also saw other highlights, like Switzerland’s first men’s slalom win since 1948.
