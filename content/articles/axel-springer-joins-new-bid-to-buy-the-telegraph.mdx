Axel Springer, the owner of Politico, has joined a consortium led by Dovid Efune to bid for The Telegraph. The Daily Mail owner's proposed £500m deal is under regulator review for media plurality concerns. Efune's group says its offer has enough cash, less debt, and no regulatory hurdles. The new bid could push the Daily Mail deal to collapse. Ofcom will study the impact on media diversity. The Telegraph's ownership saga has lasted three years.
