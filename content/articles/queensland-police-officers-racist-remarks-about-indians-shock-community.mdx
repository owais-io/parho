A Queensland police officer was caught on a body‑camera saying “fucking Indians” on Australia Day, sparking outrage among the Indian community. The officer later helped charge an Indian Uber driver with indecent exposure; the case was dismissed after witnesses failed to appear. The driver has sued the police, and the race‑discrimination commissioner says the incident will make Indians less likely to report crimes. Police say the officer faced professional development, but critics call for stronger discipline and independent oversight.
