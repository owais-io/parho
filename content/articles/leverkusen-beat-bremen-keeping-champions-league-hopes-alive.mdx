Leverkusen beat Werder Bremen 1‑0 with Lucas Vázquez’s first goal for the club. It was a relief after a rough start to 2026, with the team having lost three of its first four games. Coach Kasper Hjulmand said the win showed the squad’s experience was key. The result keeps Leverkusen in the hunt for a Champions League spot. The win also helped them stay on track for a top‑24 Champions League spot, potentially leading to a February clash against Villarreal.
