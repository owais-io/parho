Trump’s overnight air attacks on Iran’s military leaders have left Democrats divided. Some call the move illegal and want a quick vote to stop the president from acting alone, while others praise the strike and even celebrate the death of Iran’s top leader. The split shows a deeper rift about how far the party should back Israel and use force in the Middle East.
