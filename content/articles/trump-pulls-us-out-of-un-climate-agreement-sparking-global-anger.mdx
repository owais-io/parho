Donald Trump announced that the United States will leave the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change and several other environmental groups, calling them “against U.S. interests.” The decision has angered world leaders, climate scientists, and environmental groups. They warn it will isolate the U.S., cut its influence on clean‑energy funding, and hurt its ability to compete with China. The move may also need a future president to seek Senate approval to re‑join.
