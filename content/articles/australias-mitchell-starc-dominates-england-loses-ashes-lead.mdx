In the second Test of the Ashes at the Gabba, Australia’s star bowler Mitchell Starc bowled a match‑winning spell, taking key wickets including captain Joe Root and ending England’s hopes of turning the series around. England struggled after a huge first‑innings deficit and were left with a slim chance to bat again. Starc’s performance, along with solid Australian batting, sealed a 2‑0 series win for the hosts.
