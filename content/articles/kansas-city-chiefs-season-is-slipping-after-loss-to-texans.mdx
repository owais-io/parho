The Chiefs dropped to 6‑7 after a 20‑10 defeat by the Texans in Houston. Patrick Mahomes struggled, completing only 42.8% of passes and being intercepted three times. The team’s offense was sluggish and its defense showed gaps, leaving many veteran players looking tired. With the playoffs still possible but a long way off, the Chiefs may need a bigger overhaul to stay competitive.
