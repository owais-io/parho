At dawn on Sunday, an Iranian drone burst into a U.S. Amazon data‑centre in the UAE, sparking a fire and cutting power. A second site was hit, and a third in Bahrain was damaged. Iran says the aim was to expose the military value of such facilities. The attacks halted rides, food delivery, and banking for millions, and cast doubt on the Gulf’s plans to become a global AI hub. Investors worry about protecting the region’s costly data‑centre infrastructure.
