Crystal Palace and Aston Villa finished a 0‑0 draw. Palace were missing key players, including keeper Emiliano Martínez, and had no big chances, while Villa missed a late opportunity to climb the table. After the game, Unai Emery was furious that officials didn’t award a penalty, storming onto the field. Aston Villa boss Oliver Glasner kept his unbeaten record against Emery at seven matches.
