After a 1-0 loss to Everton, Aston Villa boss Unai Emery said his team missed a big chance to move into second place in the Premier League. Villa are seven points behind Arsenal and tied on points with Manchester City. The defeat also came after a player injury and the club has to keep looking at transfer options. They will face Fenerbahce in the Europa League next week.
