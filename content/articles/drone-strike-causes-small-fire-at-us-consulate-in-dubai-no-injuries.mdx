Authorities put out a small fire after a drone struck the parking lot of the US consulate in Dubai. No one was hurt and the blaze was fully extinguished within minutes. The incident followed drone attacks on US embassies in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, where some buildings were damaged. Washington is arranging flights to help Americans leave the region and is working with airlines to increase capacity.
