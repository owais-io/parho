Melissa Leone learned she was Lakota and joined healer Nathan Chasing Horse. She trusted his healing rituals, became part of his Circle, and later his daughter was sexually abused. Chasing Horse now faces 21 charges, including kidnapping and sexual assault of a minor. His trial in Las Vegas highlights how spiritual leaders can manipulate and exploit Native communities, revealing a wider problem of abuse by so‑called medicine men.
