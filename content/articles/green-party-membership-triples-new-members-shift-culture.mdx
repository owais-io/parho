Green Party membership in England and Wales has jumped from 66,000 to 215,000, bringing in many new faces, most of whom came from Labour or Jeremy Corbyn’s Your Party. The influx is changing the party’s tone and sparking debates about policy and culture. While some worry about a takeover, the party’s democratic structure makes that unlikely, and extra funds will help professionalise staff and processes.
