Workers at Rubicon, a Utah landscape firm, filed a federal lawsuit against former Utah Attorney General Sean Reyes, who is a Trump ally, after a raid on their offices claimed they were involved in human trafficking. The company says the charges were false, based on wrong information, and were meant to harm the business for political reasons. The raid caused layoffs, pay cuts, and loss of contracts. The workers are seeking compensation for the damage.
