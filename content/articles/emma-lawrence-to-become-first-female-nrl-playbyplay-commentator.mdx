Emma Lawrence will become the first woman ever to do the play‑by‑play commentary for an NRL game on Triple M radio. The move marks a big step for gender equality in sports broadcasting, a field that has been dominated by men. Lawrence has been a well‑known voice on rugby‑league shows, and her new role will help open doors for more women in the future.
