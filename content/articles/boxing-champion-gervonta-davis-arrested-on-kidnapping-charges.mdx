Boxing champion Gervonta Davis was arrested in Miami after a two‑week search, on charges of battery, false imprisonment and attempted kidnapping tied to a domestic‑violence case last fall. Police say he forced a former girlfriend into a back room at a strip club, beat her, then took her outside. The woman sued, and the case led to an arrest warrant. Davis’s fight career is now in doubt.
