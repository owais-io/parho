Anthony Joshua knocked out YouTuber‑turned‑boxer Jake Paul in a six‑round fight in Miami, showing that real fighters still win. Joshua scored four knockdowns, ending the bout at 1:31 of the sixth round. Paul tried to hold on, but the champion’s power was too much. The match drew 300 million Netflix viewers and sparked debate over the safety of celebrity fights. Joshua’s win keeps his reputation strong and reminds people boxing is still serious.
