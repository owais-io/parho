Claudio Neves Valente, a 48‑year‑old Portuguese ex‑Brown student, was found dead in a New Hampshire storage unit after he shot himself. He is believed to be the man who killed two students and wounded nine at Brown University and later shot MIT physics professor Nuno Loureiro. Police say he acted alone, had changed car plates and was a lawful permanent resident. His death ends the investigation.
