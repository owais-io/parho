During a heavyweight bout at Madison Square Garden, Jarrell Miller’s hairpiece slipped off after a punch, leaving a bald spot for a moment and making the crowd laugh. He tossed the wig into the crowd and kept fighting, ultimately winning by a close split decision. Miller joked about losing hair after using a strange shampoo, and he’s been working to rebuild his career after previous doping suspensions.
