Cecilia Giménez, an 81‑year‑old Spanish woman, was working on a church painting when her unfinished attempt turned into the internet meme “Monkey Christ.” The image spread worldwide, embarrassing her but also turning her quiet town, Borja, into a tourist spot. Visitors buy Monkey Christ‑themed souvenirs, and the money helps pay for local care homes. In the end, Giménez’s kindness and patience made her a true hero, not just a viral joke.
