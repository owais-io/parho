Craig Guildford, chief constable of West Midlands police, stepped down after an inquiry found the ban on Maccabi Tel Aviv fans was based on exaggerated intelligence. The ban was meant to stop fans from attending a Birmingham game, but reports showed the threat was overstated. Home Secretary Shabana Mahmood lost confidence in him and the force’s credibility. Guildford retired, and a deputy will temporarily lead while a new chief is sought.
