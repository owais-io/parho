The University of East London (UEL) welcomes around 40,000 students from more than 160 countries, especially mature and disadvantaged students. Its modern campuses in Docklands, Stratford and USS offer gyms, 24‑hour libraries and green spaces. Staff receive mentoring, flexible hours, wellness workshops and chances to lead projects. UEL also gives students internships, student societies and strong support for social mobility. The university is highly ranked in teaching and student satisfaction.
