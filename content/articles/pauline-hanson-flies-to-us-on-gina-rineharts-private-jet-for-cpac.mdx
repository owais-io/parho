Pauline Hanson and her staff flew on mining magnate Gina Rinehart’s private jet from Australia to Florida. They stayed at Rinehart’s Palm Beach home and joined her at the Trump resort before heading to the Conservative Political Action Conference (CPAC). After the trip, Hanson filed a declaration for the free travel and hospitality, later correcting it. The visit shows Rinehart’s support for Hanson and the broader conservative movement, raising questions about funding and disclosure rules.
