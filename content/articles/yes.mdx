After the Trump speech was edited wrong in the 2024‑pre‑election *Panorama* show, the BBC said it was wrong.   They issued a brief apology on its website.   The BBC also said it would pull the incorrect version from the story and replace it with the full, un‑edited speech.   So, the BBC apologized and fixed the mistake, but it hasn’t offered a big settlement or a full re‑statement that Trump demanded.
