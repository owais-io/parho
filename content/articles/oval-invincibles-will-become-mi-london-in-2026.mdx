The Oval Invincibles, the Hundred’s most successful team, will change their name to MI London next year. The switch comes after the Ambani family’s Reliance group bought 49% of the club. Surrey keeps 51% ownership. The new name will align the team with other MI brands, like Mumbai Indians, MI Cape Town and MI New York. The change will help grow the fan base at home and worldwide.
