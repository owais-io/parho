Veterans are furious after Trump called Democratic lawmakers traitors for a video that told U.S. soldiers not to obey “unlawful” orders. The president’s angry comments were slammed by former military lawyers who say the rhetoric is wrong and could put soldiers at risk. Experts warn that the debate over what orders are illegal is too political and could split the armed forces. They urge the military to stay neutral and follow the law.
