New York Mayor Mamdani surprised Trump in Washington, proposing a 12,000‑unit housing project that could bring the biggest federal investment in New York in 50 years. Trump seemed enthusiastic, but gave no timeline or location. Mamdani also asked the president to help release a student arrested by immigration agents and mentioned other detained students. The mayor posted a photo of himself with Trump holding mock newspaper headlines in the Oval Office.
