Several new Polymarket accounts made bets for a US‑Iran ceasefire that could win almost $820,000 if it happens before March 31. The $70,000 stake was placed by eight wallets created just before the market ticked up, suggesting inside knowledge. Analysts say the split wallets hint at either big traders hiding their moves or real insider trading. Polymarket’s ceasefire odds have jumped from 6 % to 24 % in days, sparking scrutiny.
