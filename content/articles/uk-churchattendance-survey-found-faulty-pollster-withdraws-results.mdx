An online poll by YouGov claimed a jump in church attendance in England and Wales, but the firm later found many fake responses. The study, which fed a Bible Society report that said 12% of adults and 16% of 18‑24‑year‑olds were now attending church, has been pulled. The pollster will redo the research, while the Bible Society still sees a “positive story.” Critics say online panels are prone to fraud and AI, and Humanists UK says there’s no revival.
