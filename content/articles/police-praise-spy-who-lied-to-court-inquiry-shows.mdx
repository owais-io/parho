An inquiry into the UK’s covert police unit, the Special Demonstration Squad (SDS), has shown that senior officers praised an undercover officer, Jim Boyling, for lying about his identity in a court case against environmental activists. The investigation revealed that the police hid the truth from the court for decades, which helped the unit keep its cover and led to some activists’ convictions being overturned. The probe is still looking at how many people were wrongly convicted.
