Trump’s harsh comments about Somali people in Minneapolis have frightened the community, leading to quieter streets and closed shops at the Karmel mall. Immigration officers are on the lookout, and many residents carry passports and stay home to avoid being stopped. Local leaders, including a city mayor and lawmakers, defend the Somali community, saying most are U.S. citizens or legal residents. The situation has also disrupted medical appointments and community gatherings.
