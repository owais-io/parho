New AI‑powered dating apps like Fate ditch the swipe‑thing and use chat‑based interviews instead. An AI called Fate asks questions, then gives you five matches that match your answers, not just how “good” you look. It can also coach you on what to say. Some users say it makes decisions feel more thoughtful, while experts warn people may become too comfortable asking a chatbot for romance advice.
