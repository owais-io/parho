An Austrian climber, Thomas P, is on trial for the death of his girlfriend, Kerstin G, who froze to death while they were climbing Großglockner. He says he is sorry but denies criminal guilt. Prosecutors say he, the more experienced climber, left her near the summit, didn’t turn back in bad weather and didn’t call for help early. If convicted, he faces up to three years in prison. The case may change mountaineering liability law.
