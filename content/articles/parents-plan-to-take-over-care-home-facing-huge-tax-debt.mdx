A group of 22 families whose children live at William Blake House have launched a bid to run the care home themselves after the charity that manages it faces a £1.6 million tax bill and a possible winding‑up order. The families say the board has been secretive, paid a £1 million fee to a company owned by the chair, and put the children at risk. They want to create a not‑for‑profit company to keep the home open and improve its care.
