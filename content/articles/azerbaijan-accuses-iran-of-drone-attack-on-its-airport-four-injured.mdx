Azerbaijan says Iran fired four drones at its Nakhchivan airport, one hit the terminal and injured four people, while another fell near a village school. The Azerbaijani defence ministry demands an apology and criminal accountability, and the president is preparing a response. Iran denies responsibility. The incident could widen regional tensions, affect air‑travel routes, and test the country’s growing ties with Israel and its distance from Russia.
