People buying cheap, modded Amazon Fire Sticks online can end up giving criminals access to their computers. The fake sticks install malware that steals passwords and bank details, costing users an average of £1,700. Police say illegal streaming is a false bargain. If you have one, unplug it, change all passwords, enable two‑factor authentication, run a virus scan, and report any theft to Action Fraud.
