Arc Raiders is a fast‑growing online shooter that drops players into a post‑apocalyptic Earth to loot, fight or trade with others. You can solo or team up, explore ruins, defeat alien robots and collect gear. The game has a friendly chat system, lets you upgrade skills and craft items, and rewards teamwork in huge battles. It’s available on PC, Xbox and PS5, and a headset helps you hear enemies and talk with teammates.
