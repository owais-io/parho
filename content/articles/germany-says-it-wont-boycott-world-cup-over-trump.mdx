Germany’s football federation (DFB) confirmed it will not skip the World Cup, even after some officials suggested a boycott to protest Donald Trump’s actions. The DFB said the tournament is a chance for unity and wants to keep sport separate from politics. While Trump’s policies have worried Europeans, Germany plans to play its matches in the U.S., Canada and Mexico and welcome fans worldwide. The federation is preparing with political, security and business partners for the event.
