Real Madrid faced Manchester City in a high‑stakes match that ended 2‑1. The Spanish side started strong, with Rodrygo scoring first and a win giving hope. But City answered with a corner‑shot goal and a penalty to tie. The final minutes saw Courtois make a double save before the match slipped away. After the loss, the club’s board is still weighing whether to keep coach Xabi Alonso.
