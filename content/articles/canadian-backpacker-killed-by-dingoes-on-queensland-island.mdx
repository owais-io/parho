A 19‑year‑old Canadian backpacker, Piper James, was found dead on K’gari, Queensland, after a pack of dingoes attacked her near a shipwreck. A coroner reports evidence of drowning and dingo bites but the exact cause is still unclear. The incident has sparked talk of culling dingoes, but experts say tourism habits, like feeding the animals, raise the danger for both people and the protected dogs.
