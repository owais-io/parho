Crystal Palace finished their 27th European match a 1‑1 draw with Finnish side KuPS, meaning they will now play a two‑leg playoff at the end of February. The team used a mostly youthful lineup, with a goal from Christantus Uche early on and a late equaliser from Justin Devenny. Palace will face either Bosnian side Zrinjski or Czech team Sigma Olomouc, giving fans a chance to see another country in the first round.
