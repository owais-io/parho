Russia has asked a British diplomat to leave the country after claiming he was a spy. The unnamed envoy has 14 days to depart, says the Russian foreign ministry. The move follows a warning from Russia’s FSB that Britain was running covert officers there. This follows a series of reciprocal expulsions between the two countries since the Ukraine war began. The UK has criticised Moscow’s “intimidation” campaign.
