ICE agents arrested 47‑year‑old Juanita Avila, a green‑card holder, in Cottage Grove, Oregon, without a warrant. The incident sparked a lawsuit that won a federal judge’s order blocking ICE’s warrantless arrests in the state. The case highlighted how ICE used quotas and apps to target people, many of whom are documented. The ruling gives locals hope that such raids can stop.
