In Cairo’s Nile, fishers have switched from catching fish to gathering plastic bottles because pollution has made fishing hard. A social enterprise called Very Nile pays them higher prices for trash, helping them earn more. Fisher Mohammed Sayed now makes more money from plastic than from fish. The program also gives boats, jobs to women, and builds a clinic, turning a polluted river into a source of income.
