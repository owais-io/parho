Trump said UK soldiers had stayed off the front lines in Afghanistan, sparking outrage. A few days later, he posted on Truth Social praising the troops and acknowledging their sacrifices, though he never apologized. UK leaders welcomed the change, calling it a “recognition” of the offense caused. The move was seen as a diplomatic win, but critics say no apology was made.
