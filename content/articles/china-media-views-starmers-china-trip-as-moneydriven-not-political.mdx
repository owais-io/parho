Chinese state media welcomed UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer’s visit to Beijing, describing it as a move driven by money, not politics. They said Britain is putting its economy ahead of stances and ignoring pressure to confront China over Putin. Starmer announced new business deals to help British firms expand in China. China sees the trip as a practical response to global uncertainty and a chance to strengthen trade ties, while the UK seeks growth and independence from U.S. pressure.
