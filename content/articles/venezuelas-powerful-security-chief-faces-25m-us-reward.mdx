Cabello, Venezuela’s interior minister, runs the police and prisons and is the country’s real power behind the scenes. He’s nicknamed “Octopus” and even hosts a TV show. After President Maduro was seized in a US raid, Cabello’s grip is shaky – the US now offers a $25 million reward for his arrest. Some say he’ll side with the U.S. to survive, while others think he’ll stay loyal to Maduro. He faces sanctions, drug‑trolling charges, and a growing threat from the CIA.
