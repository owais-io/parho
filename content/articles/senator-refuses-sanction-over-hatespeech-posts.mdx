The Australian senator Ralph Babet has said he won’t accept a sanction imposed by the Independent Parliamentary Standards Commission for using hate words online. The commission found his posts with the N‑word, homophobic and ableist slurs breached the code of conduct. Babet rejected the punishment and the required training, calling the watchdog “dangerous.” The move has drawn criticism from parties and raised questions about how parliament enforces behaviour rules.
