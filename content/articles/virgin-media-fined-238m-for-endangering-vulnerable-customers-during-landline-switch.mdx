Virgin Media was fined £23.8 million by Ofcom after the company failed to protect thousands of vulnerable people who use telecare alarms when it switched their analogue landlines to digital. The regulator said the switch process missed many telecare customers, leaving their alarms disconnected and putting them at risk. Virgin Media admitted the mistake, self‑reported incidents, and has started extra checks and support for those affected.
