Qatar's royal family has given the U.S. a 13‑year‑old Boeing 747‑8 to serve as the next Air Force One. The aircraft, worth about $400 million, will be modified for presidential security and is scheduled to arrive by summer 2026. Trump welcomed the gift, saying he wouldn't pass up such an offer. Critics worry about the plane’s age, security and Qatar’s motives. Meanwhile, Trump’s current Air Force One had a minor electrical issue and returned to Washington.
