A Croatian journalist has taken the case of former Serbian President Aleksandar Vučić to Milan prosecutors. He claims Vučić helped set up a “sniper safari” during Sarajevo’s 1990s siege, where wealthy foreigners from Italy and other countries paid soldiers to kill civilians for sport. Witnesses say Vučić was at a military post when the attacks happened. The investigation is still ongoing, and Vučić has not yet responded.
