Australia will launch its largest gun buyback in 30 years after the Bondi beach attack, and New South Wales will limit hobby shooters to four guns and push for powers that could temporarily ban protests in certain spots. The government also remembered Bondi victims at a funeral and noted 15 people still in hospital. Meanwhile, cricket star Tom Head scored another century and other headlines touched on heatwaves and other local issues.
