The Albanese government rushed a bill that lets the Home Affairs minister stop temporary visa holders from certain countries from entering Australia for up to six months, if the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister sign off. The move targets groups by country and visa type, aiming to manage risk after wars break out. Critics say it’s unfair and confusing, especially after the government granted visas to an Iranian football team that same day.
