Former Greek finance minister Yanis Varoufakis has been sued in Athens for saying he once took ecstasy. He admitted it on a podcast and was accused of “inciting others to use drugs.” The case could lead to up to six months in jail and a €50,000 fine. Varoufakis claims the suit is a political attack linked to right‑wing influence in Greece. He says the charge is an example of wider fascist trends in Europe.
