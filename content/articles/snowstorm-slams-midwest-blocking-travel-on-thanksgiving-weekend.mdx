A huge snowstorm hit the Midwest on Thanksgiving weekend, dropping more than a foot of snow and strong winds. 53 million people were under alerts, causing a 45‑car pile‑up on I‑78 in Indiana and a Delta plane to skid off the Des Moines runway – no serious injuries. Flights were delayed and cancelled, especially around Chicago’s O’Hare, which set a new November snow record. East‑coast cities may face cold rain that could disrupt travel.
