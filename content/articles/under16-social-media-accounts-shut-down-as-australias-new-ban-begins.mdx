Australia is stopping half a million Facebook and Instagram accounts for users under 16. The move starts this Thursday and will be in effect from Dec 10. The Young Nationals say the ban cuts a vital link for kids in rural areas, and they want it repealed. Other news includes a cricket player taking early wickets for England, a child‑care paedophile charged with new offences, Victoria cutting 1,000 jobs, a heatwave warning, and a plan to computer‑generate disability support plans.
