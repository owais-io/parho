President Donald Trump has asked the United Kingdom to deploy warships to the Hormuz Strait, a vital shipping lane where a fifth of the world's oil passes. He said the UK should help keep the strait safe amid attacks from Iran after U.S. and Israeli strikes. Trump warned that Iran might still threaten ships with drones or missiles, and he criticized UK leader Keir Starmer for not joining the military campaign. The UK has so far refused to send vessels.
