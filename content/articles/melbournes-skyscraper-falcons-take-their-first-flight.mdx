Three young falcons that hatched on a high building in Melbourne’s downtown have flown for the first time after weeks of watching them on a live video. The birds were born in late September and early October on a ledge 150 m up a 35‑storey office block. Viewers followed a 24‑hour livestream and saw the trio take off, one falcon even crash‑landing before successfully flying again. Their parents will keep feeding them until they’re ready to hunt and leave the city.
