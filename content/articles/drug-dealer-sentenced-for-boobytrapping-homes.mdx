A 60‑year‑old man named Ian Clutton was given a seven‑year prison term after he set up Home‑Alone‑style booby traps in three houses to protect his cannabis‑making operation. Police raided the homes after Border Force seized fake guns shipped to his ex‑wife. Traps included fishing wire, a homemade flame‑thrower, and stun guns. The search also found large amounts of cash, drugs, and real weapons, and 130 nearby properties were evacuated for safety.
