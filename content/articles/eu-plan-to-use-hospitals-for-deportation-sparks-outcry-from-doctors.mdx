More than 1,100 European doctors, nurses and other health workers have written to lawmakers to stop new rules that would let hospitals help find and deport people without legal status. They say the plan would turn hospitals into immigration checkpoints, break patient privacy, and scare people from seeking care. The move comes after far‑right parties gained seats in the European parliament and could hurt public health across the EU.
