The IG Metall union wants to take over the works council at Tesla’s factory near Berlin. The council decides on pay and hours. Tesla says the union is only trying to grow IG Metall, while the union claims Tesla’s conditions are bad and needs a deal. Both sides have sued each other, and the Brandenburg state has urged them to negotiate. The fight could decide Tesla’s future in Europe.
