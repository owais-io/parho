The FBI has released a new profile of the man believed to have abducted Nancy Guthrie, an 84‑year‑old Arizona resident. They say he is about 5‑9 feet tall, average build, and was seen with a black backpack. The reward has risen to $100,000. Police are asking anyone with video of the area between January 1 and February 2 to come forward. No suspect has been found yet, but the family and neighbors remain hopeful and concerned.
