Forest beat Spurs 3-0 at the City Ground. Callum Hudson‑Odoi scored twice, and Ibrahim Sangaré finished the game with a goal. Tottenham’s goalkeeper and two defenders made mistakes that let Forest win easily. The loss pushes Spurs further from safety, while Forest gains a five‑point cushion above West Ham. The match was a rough day for Spurs, who could not repeat their earlier Champions League win.
