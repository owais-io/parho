Nick Kyrgios, who just had surgery on his knee and wrist, says he won't play singles at the Australian Open because he isn’t ready for five‑set matches. Instead he will join his friend Thanasi Kokkinakis in doubles. He will still compete in the Kooyong Classic and plans to be back next year. He had a quick loss in his first singles match after a long break. He made this decision after talks with Tennis Australia.
