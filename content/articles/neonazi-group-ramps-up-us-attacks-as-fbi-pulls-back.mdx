The Base, a neo‑Nazi terrorist group, is stepping up its U.S. activity even as the FBI shifts resources away from far‑right investigations. Led by former Pentagon contractor Rinaldo Nazzaro, the group posts videos of armed members and plans attacks on infrastructure. Nazzaro says he wants an armed uprising like al‑Qaida, targeting the U.S. and Europe. Recent arrests in Spain, the U.K. and the Netherlands show the group remains a serious threat.
