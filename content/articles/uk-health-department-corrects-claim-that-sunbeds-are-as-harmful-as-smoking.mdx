The UK health department had to backtrack after saying sunbeds are as dangerous as smoking. Health officials had made the claim, but fact‑checkers said it was wrong. Smoking actually causes about 80 times more cancer cases than sunbeds, according to Cancer Research UK. After pressure from the Office for Statistics Regulation, the department corrected the posts and pledged not to repeat the comparison.
