Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro was arrested by U.S. forces in a raid ordered by President Trump and is now in a Manhattan court. He pleaded not guilty to charges of drug and weapons crimes. The United Nations Security Council met and many countries called the raid an “aggression” that violates international law. Trump says the move stops drug flow and claims he will run Venezuela’s oil, but the world is shocked and worried about future attacks.
