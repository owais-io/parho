Donald Trump has threatened to slap a 10% tariff on eight NATO countries that sent troops to support Greenland. European leaders, especially Denmark, have slammed the move and said they will not give up Greenland. Other leaders warn this could spark a trade war and even threaten NATO’s unity. France is looking at EU tools to fight the pressure, while Britain is unsure if it will retaliate. The situation puts a strain on US‑Europe ties.
