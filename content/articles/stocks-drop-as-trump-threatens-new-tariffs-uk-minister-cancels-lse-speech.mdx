President Trump has warned he will slap new tariffs on goods from eight European countries, starting 10% in February and climbing to 25% in June. The news rattled European markets, sending the FTSE 100 and other indices lower. In London, Finance Minister Rachel Reeves pulled out of a planned LSE talk and will instead speak at the World Economic Forum. EU leaders are eyeing counter‑tariffs while the UK braces for a hit to its car industry.
