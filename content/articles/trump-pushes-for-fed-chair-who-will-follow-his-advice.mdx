Trump says the Federal Reserve should listen to him and is already picking who might replace the current chair, Jerome Powell, who will finish his term in May. He has named former Fed governor Kevin Warsh and economic adviser Kevin Hassett as top choices. Trump wants the new chair to lower interest rates, which the Fed has resisted. The president has publicly criticized the Fed and even tried to fire a member of its rate‑setting committee.
