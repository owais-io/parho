Guardian has issued a series of corrections for several previously published pieces. It says plug‑in hybrid cars use about six litres of fuel per 100 km, not the two litres previously quoted, and that Libya’s coast is the longest in the Mediterranean only among non‑European countries. Other corrections touch on sewage‑crisis figures, smoking addiction facts, hedgehog‑habitat tips, extremism laws, film awards, theatre naming, forest access, football relegation, royal concerns, and personal stories. Readers can send complaints to the Guardian’s editors.
