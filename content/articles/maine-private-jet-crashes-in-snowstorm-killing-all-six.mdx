During a snowstorm, a private Bombardier jet left Bangor, Maine, crashed shortly after takeoff, killing all six people aboard. Victims included Houston attorney Tara Arnold, her husband Kurt Arnold, event planner Shawna Collins, and pilot Jacob Hosmer. The NTSB says the plane went upside down before it caught fire. First responders reached the site within a minute. No further details have been released yet.
