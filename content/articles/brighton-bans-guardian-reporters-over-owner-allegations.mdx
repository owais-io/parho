Brighton & Hove Albion blocked Guardian reporters and photographers from its home games after the paper exposed claims about owner Tony Bloom. The move drew anger from MPs, press groups and fans who see it as a hit on press freedom. Bloom faces a lawsuit over betting allegations, and he denies betting on his club. Critics say the ban sets a bad precedent for sports clubs censoring journalists.
