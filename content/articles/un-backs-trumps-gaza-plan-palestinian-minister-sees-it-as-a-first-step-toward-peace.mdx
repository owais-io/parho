UN passed a resolution supporting Donald Trump’s plan to end the Gaza war. Palestinian foreign minister Yasser Abu Al‑Azh said it is only the first step toward lasting peace, even though Hamas says it would not follow the plan and calls it an international police force. The plan includes a Palestinian police force, an international security force, and a push for Gaza’s disarmament, but details are still missing.
