Police arrested former Prince Andrew, the former Duke of York, after emails showed he sent confidential trade‑envoy reports to Jeffrey Epstein. The case centres on allegations of misconduct in public office, with Andrew released under investigation. Police say the arrest is part of a broader probe, and they will not name the suspect until charges are filed. If convicted, the offence can carry up to life imprisonment, but the law is currently being re‑examined.
