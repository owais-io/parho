This article explains how social media feeds keep us in echo chambers, especially during family gatherings. The author suggests stepping back from arguing about specifics and instead talking about how the internet works. She offers simple tips: keep chats short, share how ads drive content, and encourage people to look at other sources. By understanding these digital forces, we can keep family harmony and broaden our views.
