Lara Croft is back with two new Tomb Raider games. Amazon will publish them. The first, Tomb Raider Catalyst, releases in 2027 and has Croft racing treasure hunters across India’s markets, mountains and ancient sites. The second, Legacy of Atlantis, arrives in 2026 and reboots the 1996 original with modern combat and puzzles. Both games star British actress Alix Wilton Regan as Croft, and Amazon also plans a new TV series starring Sophie Turner.
