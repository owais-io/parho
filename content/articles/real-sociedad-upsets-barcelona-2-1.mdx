Real Sociedad shocked Barcelona by winning 2-1 on Sunday night, ending the champions' 11‑game winning streak. The win was celebrated with the city’s Tamborrada festival, drumbeats and marching bands, giving fans a festive atmosphere. New coach Pellegrino Matarazzo praised the team's spirit and said luck helped but the players played well. This victory gave the club a new sense of confidence, and the coach said the team’s unity and energy were key.
