Florida lawmakers just passed a bill that will rename the Palm Beach International Airport as the President Donald J Trump International Airport. Republicans pushed the change, while Democrats argue the move ignores local voters and should wait until Trump’s service ends. Trump’s organization has trademarked the name, saying no money will be made, but the bill still grants the state overriding control. FAA approval is expected soon.
