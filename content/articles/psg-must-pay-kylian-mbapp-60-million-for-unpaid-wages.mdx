A Paris labour court ordered Paris Saint‑Germain to pay more than €60 million to star striker Kylian Mbappé because he wasn’t paid the wages and bonuses he was owed. The case follows Mbappé’s move to Real Madrid in 2024, and PSG had been seeking €440 million in damages. The club will likely appeal, but the ruling confirms the player’s rights were upheld.
