Australia has won a record six medals at the Winter Olympics, beating its own best from 2022. Since Steven Bradbury’s bronze in 1994, Australians have medaled at every Games, including his 2002 gold. More events and growing women’s participation explain the rise, but the country’s success is still impressive for a land with little snow. Experts point to new training sites, like a water ramp for aerial jumps, as key.
