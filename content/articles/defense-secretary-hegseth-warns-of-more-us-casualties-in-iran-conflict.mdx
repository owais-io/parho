Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth told CBS that the U.S. war with Iran will bring more American casualties, saying the fighting has already cost seven service members and that the war is only at the start. He defended President Trump’s decision to let the U.S. join Israel in attacks and said the air campaign will continue until “strategic objectives” are met. Critics say Hegseth celebrates the violence, and the conflict has already killed hundreds, including civilians in a school strike.
