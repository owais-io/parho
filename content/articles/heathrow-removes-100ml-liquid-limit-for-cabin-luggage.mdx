Heathrow has finally lifted the 100ml limit on liquids in carry‑on bags after installing new CT scanners. Passengers can now keep up to 2 litres of liquids, gels and toiletries, and laptops or tablets, inside their hand luggage. The change only applies to flights out of Heathrow; travelers should verify rules for return flights. The upgrade is expected to cut plastic bag use and speed up security checks.
