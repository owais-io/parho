A suspected cyber‑attack hit France’s national post office, La Poste, and its bank, La Banque Postale. A DDoS attack made their websites and apps unusable, slowing parcel sorting and delivery just before Christmas. Online banking and mobile payments were disrupted, though ATMs and in‑store card machines still worked. La Poste said customer data was safe. The attack follows recent cyber incidents on French government agencies and private firms.
