Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has set up a new fuel‑supply taskforce and told motorists to buy only what they need as the fuel crisis worsens. The Australian Competition & Consumer Commission is probing major companies – Ampol, BP, Mobil and Viva Energy – for possible anti‑competitive price‑gouging linked to shortages triggered by the Iran war. Petra Stock has explored quick‑turn energy options to cut dependence on foreign oil. The Guardian is asking readers how they’re coping.
