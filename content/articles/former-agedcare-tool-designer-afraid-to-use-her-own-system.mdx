Lynda Henderson helped build Australia’s new aged‑care assessment tool but now fears using it. The government added an algorithm that scores answers and assigns care levels, cutting off assessors’ ability to override results. Many users say the scores under‑estimate their needs, leading to less funding. Henderson, who needs more support herself, feels terrified that the system could reduce her care if it misclassifies her.
