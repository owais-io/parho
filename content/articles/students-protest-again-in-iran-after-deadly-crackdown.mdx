After a brutal January crackdown that killed thousands in Iran, students at universities in Tehran and Mashhad are holding campus protests again. They’re chanting anti‑government slogans, even with security forces and Basij volunteers in the streets. Videos show clashes, broken windows, and students refusing to return to classes because the campus feels “empty” after the deaths. The demonstrations aim to keep the movement alive and demand freedom for future generations.
