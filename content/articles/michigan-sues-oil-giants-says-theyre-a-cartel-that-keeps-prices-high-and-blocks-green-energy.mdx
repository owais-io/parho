Michigan has sued the biggest oil companies, saying they formed a cartel that keeps energy prices high and stops clean technology like electric cars. The state claims BP, Shell, Chevron, Exxon Mobil and the oil lobby are hiding facts about climate danger and using legal tactics to block competition. If the case succeeds, it could force oil companies to change how they market and price energy.
