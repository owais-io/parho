A New York hedge fund, Corvex, says Whitbread should review its plans after the UK budget announced higher business taxes. The fund owns 6% of the company and believes its share price underestimates the value of its hotels and properties. Whitbread says it has a clear five‑year plan to grow in the UK and Germany. The move follows warnings that the tax changes could cost the company up to £50 m next year.
