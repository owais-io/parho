Jonathan Gerlach, 34, was arrested in Pennsylvania after police found more than 100 human bones and body parts in his car, home, and storage locker. Investigators say he broke into at least 26 graves at Mount Moriah cemetery in Yeadon, stealing remains that could be hundreds of years old, including the bones of famous people like Betsy Ross. He faces about 575 charges, including grave robbery, trespassing and corpse abuse, and is being held without a $1 million bail.
