The U.S. House will hold a vote this Tuesday to force the Justice Department to hand over files about Jeffrey Epstein, a move that could finally reveal details of the former financier’s links to powerful figures, including former President Donald Trump. Trump, who had promised to release the documents but then opposed the bill, has flipped and says he will sign if it passes. The bill has bipartisan support and could lead to a Senate vote before Trump signs.
