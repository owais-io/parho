New York mayor‑elect Zohran Mamdani has hired former FTC chief Lina Khan for his transition team. Experts say this signals a push against private‑equity firms that buy and merge small businesses to raise prices and lower quality, especially in healthcare and real‑estate. Khan’s past lawsuits and focus on transparency may make these firms more cautious, while the mayor could strengthen public hospitals and demand clearer city contracts.
