Norman Gifford, a left‑arm spin bowler who played 710 first‑class games and took over 2,000 wickets, passed away on 21 January. He won three County Championships with Worcestershire and captained both Worcestershire and Warwickshire. Though he earned only 33 Test caps, his domestic record and leadership earned him a Wisden honour and an MBE. Gifford was known for his friendly, open nature and his nickname, “Apple Norm.”
