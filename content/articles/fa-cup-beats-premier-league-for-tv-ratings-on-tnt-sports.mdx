On TNT Sports, the FA Cup pulls in more viewers than any Premier League match this season. The third‑round game between Brighton and Manchester United averaged 876,000 viewers, reaching 2.1 million people—about 10 % of the TV audience at that time. Even after the FA sold rights to TNT, the Cup’s popularity stays strong, with 22 third‑round ties on TV and nearly five million viewers in total.
