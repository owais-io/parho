The US Treasury Secretary, Scott Bessent, told European leaders in Davos that they should not hit back with tariffs when President Trump threatens a 25% duty on European goods over Greenland. He warned that retaliation would hurt markets and that Europe already holds a lot of US debt. Bessent said European countries should keep their trade deals and wait to see what happens, urging calm after earlier panic.
