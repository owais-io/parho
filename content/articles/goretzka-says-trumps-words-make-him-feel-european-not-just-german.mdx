German midfielder Leon Goretzka said that Donald Trump’s recent threats and criticism of Europe made him feel not only German but European. The Bayern Munich player, who hopes to play in the World Cup, said Trump’s comments reminded him of Europe’s strength on the pitch. Goretzka also talked about being careful when speaking out as a sports star, and mentioned he may look to play abroad after his Bayern contract ends.
