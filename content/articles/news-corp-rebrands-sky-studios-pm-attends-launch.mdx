News Corp’s Holt Street studios have been renamed after the company lost the rights to the Sky News name. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese appeared first for a podcast at Guardian Australia, then joined Sky presenters and News Corp chair Lachlan Murdoch for the new name launch. The rebrand has been kept secret, with six possible trademarks filed and awaiting approval. The story shows the overlap of media, politics and branding.
