FIFA’s president Gianni Infantino hosted the 2026 World Cup draw at Washington’s Kennedy Center, a ceremony that many saw as a big, boring show with a heavy Trump focus. Infantino joked with the crowd, introduced Trump, and gave him a “peace prize.” Critics say the event was over‑the‑top, commercialized, and turned the draw into a political stunt rather than a sports moment.
