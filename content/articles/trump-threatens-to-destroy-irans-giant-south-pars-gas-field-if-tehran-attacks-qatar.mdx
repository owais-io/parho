Donald Trump has threatened to blow up Iran’s giant South Pars gas field if Tehran strikes Qatar after Israeli attacks on the site. The move could spark a new round of violence and drive up oil and gas prices. Analysts warn that attacking the field might harden Iran’s resolve rather than weaken it. The U.S. has already spent billions on the conflict, and the war’s cost is rising.
