The European Parliament has halted a US trade deal after President Trump threatened 10% tariffs on EU goods unless the bloc lets him control Greenland. Because of the threat, the EU may retaliate with tariffs on US products, and talks are ongoing to avoid a trade war. Meanwhile, Parliament also voted to challenge a new trade deal with Latin America, sparking further disputes.
