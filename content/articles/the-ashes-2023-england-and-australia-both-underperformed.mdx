The 2023 Ashes in Australia finished early, with fans streaming to the Sydney Cricket Ground for the trophy presentation. Both England and Australia played poorly, leaving fans disappointed. England's squad seemed unprepared and didn't adapt to Australian conditions, while Australia relied on a few good bowlers and a couple of strong batting performances. The series was seen as mediocre by many, with many feeling the spectacle fell short.
