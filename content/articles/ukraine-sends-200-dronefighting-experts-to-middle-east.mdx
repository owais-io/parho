Ukraine sent 200 anti‑drone experts to the Middle East to help fight Iranian Shahed drones, President Zelensky said to the UK parliament. He warned European nations to prepare for drone attacks as the technology spreads. The EU said Russia could profit from higher energy prices and that Ukraine remains a top security priority. Ukraine is repairing the Druzhba pipeline, while a court case may involve billionaire Roman Abramovich over £2.4 bn. Romania is searching for drone debris after a Russian strike.
