When we face hard talks, we often flip between shouting and staying silent, both hurting relationships. Buddhism says the key is “wise speech” – true, useful, kind, timely, and loving. It asks us to listen, be calm, and speak with care, not to win arguments. Building trust and being honest while protecting feelings helps us stay connected. This approach turns tough chats into chances to grow together.
