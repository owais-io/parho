One Nation, led by Pauline Hanson, has reached its highest-ever poll vote at 17%, half of Labor's 34%. The party's support is mixed because some voters are unsure whether Barnaby Joyce's move to One Nation will help or hurt the party. Many Australians are worried about rising living costs, especially food and energy. Prime Minister Albanese’s approval dropped to 43%.
