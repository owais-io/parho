UK MPs say political donations in cryptocurrency should be banned for now to stop foreign money from messing with elections. The chair of the national security committee wants a temporary stop and tighter checks on donors, including only using FCA‑registered crypto services. He also says the government should set one police lead for political‑finance crimes and review sentences for election offences. The move comes after a party began accepting crypto donations.
