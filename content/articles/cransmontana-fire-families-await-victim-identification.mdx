A deadly fire broke out at the Le Constellation bar in the Swiss ski resort Crans‑Montana on New Year’s Eve. About 40 people died and more than 100 were injured, many of them young. The blaze started when sparklers were placed too close to the ceiling. Families and friends are still waiting for the victims to be identified, some using DNA tests. Police are also checking the building’s foam and other safety details.
