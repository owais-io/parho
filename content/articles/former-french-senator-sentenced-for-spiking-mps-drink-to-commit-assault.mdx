A 68‑year‑old former French senator, Joël Guerriau, was convicted of putting MDMA into the champagne of MP Sandrine Josso in November 2023 and attempting to rape her. The court gave him a four‑year prison sentence, 18 months of which must be served in jail. Josso, a MoDem MP, survived the attack and has become a key voice in France’s fight against drug‑assisted sexual violence. Guerriau has appealed and will await a new trial.
