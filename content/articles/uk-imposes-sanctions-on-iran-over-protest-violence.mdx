The UK said it will launch new sanctions against Iran after protests that killed and arrested hundreds. The measures will hit finance, energy, transport, software and other key sectors. Foreign Secretary Yvette Cooper also summoned the Iranian ambassador to protest the regime’s violence. The move comes as EU leaders promise further action and US President Trump urges Iranians to keep protesting. The UK aims to pressure the regime to stop its crackdown.
