Anthony Joshua beat YouTuber‑turned‑boxer Jake Paul in Miami, stopping him in the sixth round and sending Paul to the hospital with a broken jaw. After the win, Joshua admitted the fight wasn’t his best but said he had met his goal of hurting Paul. He then challenged heavyweight champion Tyson Fury to step into the ring, saying Fury should stop talking and fight if he’s serious. Joshua is now looking toward a 2026 showdown.
