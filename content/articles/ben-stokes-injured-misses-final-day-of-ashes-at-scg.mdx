Ben Stokes hurt his groin during the fourth morning of the Ashes tour and will miss the last day at Sydney's SCG. He was able to bat a bit, but the injury keeps him from bowling, leaving England without their seam‑bowling captain. England’s lead is 119 with eight wickets down, and Jacob Bethell will take on the big bowling job. Stokes will watch from the pavilion hoping for a quick recovery.
