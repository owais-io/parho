A strong storm in Hawaii has caused flash flooding, landslides, and even blizzard‑like snow on high peaks. Roads collapsed and a house washed away. Winds up to 20 mph made driving dangerous and damaged doors. The National Weather Service says the worst is over, but rain continues, and flood warnings still exist for Maui and the Big Island as officials call for cleanup.
