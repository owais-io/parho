The United States and Israel have been striking Iranian targets with airstrikes, a campaign that some say looks a lot like Russia’s fight in Ukraine. Both wars began with vague goals and a claim of defending against a threat, but later leaders pushed for deeper changes. Trump talked about replacing Iran’s leaders, while Putin said he was finishing a “special operation.” The lack of clear limits risks a long‑term conflict, so experts say the U.S. must set realistic, measurable aims.
