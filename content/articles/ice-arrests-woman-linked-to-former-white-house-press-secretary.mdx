Bruna Ferreira, a Brazilian‑born U.S. resident, was seized by ICE in Revere, Massachusetts, while on her way to pick up her son. She is the mother of the nephew of former White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt. Ferreira, who came to the U.S. as a child and has DACA protection, has a past battery arrest and no ID. She’s now in a Louisiana ICE center and facing deportation. A GoFundMe raised over $16,000 to help her fight the charges.
