The BBC has asked a U.S. court to toss out former President Donald Trump’s $10 billion defamation suit, saying the case would unfairly limit the broadcaster’s reporting. Trump’s lawyers claim the BBC’s documentary edited a speech in a misleading way, but the BBC says the film was never shown in the U.S. or Florida, so the court has no authority. The BBC also argues that the suit is baseless and costly, and would chill press freedom.
