Australian rider Josie Baff captured the gold medal in the women's snowboard cross at the Milan‑Cortina Winter Games, giving Australia its second gold in a single Olympics for the first time since 2010. The 23‑year‑old surged ahead in the final, finishing just 0.04 seconds ahead of Czech skier Eva Adamczykova. Baff credited fellow Australian Cooper Woods’ earlier win as motivation. Meanwhile, France’s Quentin Fillon Maillet won gold in the men’s biathlon sprint, and Great Britain saw mixed results.
