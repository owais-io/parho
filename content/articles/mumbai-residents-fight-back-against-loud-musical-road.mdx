Some Mumbai residents are upset about the city’s first musical road on Coastal Road. The asphalt grooves play the song “Jai Ho” when cars drive at 45‑50 mph, meant to encourage safe speeds. But more than 650 families complain that the constant music, playing from dawn to midnight, is noisy and distracting. They want the music turned off, saying it’s a safety distraction and extra noise for their homes.
