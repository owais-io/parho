At the Munich Security Conference, US Representative Alexandria Ocasio‑Cortez blasted President Trump, saying his foreign‑policy moves threaten the transatlantic alliance and could usher in authoritarianism. She accused the administration of pushing the US to withdraw from global affairs, backing Russia, and supporting Israel’s war in Gaza. AOC called for a left‑wing, rules‑based approach and warned that income inequality fuels right‑wing populism. Her remarks came amid a US government shutdown.
