A biotech company has set up two old payphones—one in San Francisco and one in Abilene, Texas—so strangers can call each other across party lines. People from each city connect to a Democrat or Republican on the other end, aiming to spark friendly chats that lower stress and boost happiness. The project, run by Matter Neuroscience, has already recorded a few conversations and plans to share them online in January.
