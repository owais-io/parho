David Moyes is marking the first year he returned to manage Everton. After a tense game against Wolves and a red card for Michael Keane, he's still upset, but he’s learning. The club has had injuries and a bad run of form, but Moyes says the team is moving toward middle of table and he hopes to qualify for Europe. He wants a new stadium and stronger youth sides, and plans to stay as long as club needs him.
