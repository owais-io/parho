The 2024‑25 NBA trade deadline brought a handful of big moves. Washington added Anthony Davis and Trae Young, giving up a few picks and veterans. New York traded for Jose Alvarado to strengthen its bench. Minnesota opted for Ayo Dosunmu over a Giannis deal, while Indiana brought in center Ivica Zubac. Los Angeles Clippers made a few swaps but kept Darius Garland. The Pelicans made the fewest moves and lost key players.
