ARN Media fired popular Australian radio host Kyle Sandilands after a February on‑air argument with co‑host Jackie O, calling it serious misconduct. The station also ended their top‑rated Kyle and Jackie O Show. Sandilands says the $100 million contract was unfairly terminated and he will take legal action. Both hosts have separate 10‑year deals, and regulators have already cited the show for offensive content.
