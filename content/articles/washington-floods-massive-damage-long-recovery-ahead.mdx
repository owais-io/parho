Washington got record floods after over a week of heavy rain. The damage is huge but still unclear. More than 600 rescues happened, and 400,000 people lost power. One person died when driving into a flooded area. Roads like State Route 2 are buried and may stay closed for months. The state is giving money to help with hotels and food while it waits for federal aid.
