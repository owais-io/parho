The Trump administration is drafting a 5,000‑person military base in southern Gaza, covering over 350 acres. The base would be part of an international stabilization force led by a new Board of Peace chaired by Trump and Jared Kushner. Plans include bunkers, watch towers, a weapons range, and barbed‑wire fences. No clear rules or local permission are set, and many countries have not joined the board. The UN approved the force, but details remain vague.
