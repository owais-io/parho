Morning in Cortina, Italy is quiet as the Olympic curling arena opens for mixed‑duo matches. The British pair, Bruce Mouat and Jenn Dodds, already have a winning streak and are looking ahead to the semis. Meanwhile, American skier Lindsey Vonn is racing in her final Olympics after a five‑year break. Fans get to watch both the slow, precise game of curling and the fast, thrilling downhill races all in one town.
