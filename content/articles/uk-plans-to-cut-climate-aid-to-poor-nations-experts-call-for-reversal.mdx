The UK government announced it will slash climate finance for poorer nations by 20%, a move criticized by climate activists who say it hurts vulnerable people. The decision, made despite promises to increase aid, is seen as short‑sighted and dangerous, as climate change impacts those least responsible for it. Experts call for reversal to protect lives and the planet the global community.
