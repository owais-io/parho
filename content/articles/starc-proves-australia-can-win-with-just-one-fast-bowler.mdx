Mitchell Starc took 7 wickets for 58 runs in the first innings of the Perth Test, showing Australia can still beat England even without star bowlers Pat Cummins and Josh Hazlewood. He bowled long, accurate spells at over 140 km/h, breaking key English batsmen and keeping runs to 5.2 per over. Starc’s performance is his best ever, proving a single fast bowler can turn the game. England will face him again soon.
