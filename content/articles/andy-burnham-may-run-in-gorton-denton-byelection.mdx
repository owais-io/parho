After former minister Andrew Gwynne quit, a by‑election is looming in Manchester’s Gorton and Denton seat. The Labour Party worries it could be lost without a high‑profile candidate. Mayor Andy Burnham might stand, but it’s not yet confirmed, and rivals like Reform UK, the Greens and pro‑Gaza groups are eyeing the chance. Local voters, many upset over the controversy, may swing the vote, turning the race into a multi‑party contest.
