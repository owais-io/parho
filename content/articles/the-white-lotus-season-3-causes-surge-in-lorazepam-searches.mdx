After the third season of the TV show 'The White Lotus,' searches for the anti‑anxiety drug lorazepam jumped sharply. Researchers found almost twice as many people Googled the name and how to get it in the 12 weeks after the season aired. The study worries that fans might look online for the drug, which can be dangerous if bought from unlicensed sites or taken without a doctor’s guidance.
