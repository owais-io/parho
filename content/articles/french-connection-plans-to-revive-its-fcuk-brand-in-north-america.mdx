French Connection, the UK clothing line famous for its cheeky FCUK slogan, has hired a North American partner to bring its clothes back to the U.S. and Canada. The new owners, who bought the brand in 2021, will use G‑III Apparel’s network to sell men’s and women’s wear and plan to revive the FCUK logo. Sales dipped 10% to £108 m last year, but profits grew, and the group is cutting bad stores and boosting quality.
