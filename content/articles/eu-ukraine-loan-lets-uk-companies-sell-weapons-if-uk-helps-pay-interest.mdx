The EU has approved a €90 billion loan for Ukraine that could give British defense firms a chance to sell weapons to Kyiv. For this to happen, the UK would need to agree to share in the loan’s interest costs. The deal is part of a broader effort to bring the UK into EU defense talks after last year’s failed Safe programme negotiations.
