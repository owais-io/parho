After Hezbollah fired missiles and drones toward Israel, the Israeli army launched airstrikes on southern Beirut suburbs and other Hezbollah sites in Lebanon. The strikes killed at least 31 people and destroyed buildings, prompting evacuations of 55 villages. Israel said it hit senior Hezbollah commanders, while Lebanon’s government condemned the attacks and warned against further escalation. The incident follows a 2024 war between Israel and Hezbollah that left thousands dead.
