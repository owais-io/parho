A year after a huge wildfire destroyed 7,000 homes in Pacific Palisades, LA Mayor Karen Bass called a newly built model house a “major milestone.” But many residents argue the house isn’t a real replacement for their lost homes—it’s a showcase for developers. They’re still stuck with slow permits, insurance battles, and a long rebuild. The mayor says 340–390 homes are under construction, but locals feel the city’s progress is far behind the damage they endured.
