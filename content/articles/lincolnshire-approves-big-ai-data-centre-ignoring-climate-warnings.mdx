North Lincolnshire council gave the green light to the Elsham Tech Park AI data centre, a plan that could emit about the same CO2 as all UK domestic flights in a year. The council said nearby clean energy would keep emissions low, but critics say the figures are wrong and the project would hurt the climate. The centre could create up to 900 jobs and bring £10 bn in investment, while the developer promises new green spaces and water‑saving designs.
