At Old Dominion University in Norfolk, a man named Mohamed Jalloh opened fire, killing one student and wounding two others. The FBI says the attack was an act of terrorism, noting Jalloh shouted “Allahu Akbar” before shooting. The gunman was stopped and killed by ROTC students in a classroom. The university has closed classes, and authorities are still investigating the motive and details.
