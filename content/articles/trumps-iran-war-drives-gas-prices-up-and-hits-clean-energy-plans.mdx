Trump’s attacks on Iran have made oil prices jump, raising gas prices across the country. The war shows how depending on oil makes the US vulnerable to price shocks and inflation. At the same time, Trump’s policies cut support for renewable energy, electric cars and energy‑saving upgrades, hurting the country’s move toward cleaner power. Experts say the war highlights the risks of fossil fuel dependence and the need for a shift to renewables.
