Coach Thomas Tuchel told England’s players that the team would be fine losing the last World Cup qualifier against Albania, but he wants them to avoid red cards that could hurt the squad. He warned after Cristiano Ronaldo’s possible ban and reminded players to think before fouling. Tuchel is also considering squad changes because of injuries and wants disciplined, connected play. He stresses no needless mistakes.
