Naomi Osaka stunned fans in Melbourne with a jellyfish‑inspired look that she created with Nike and designer Robert Wun. The outfit, with turquoise‑green hues, frills and tentacle‑like details, was inspired by a storybook she read to her daughter. Osaka won her first‑round match and said the style helped her feel confident. It shows how tennis players use fashion to make a statement on court.
