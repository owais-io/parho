Ros Howells, later known as Lady Howells, was a key leader for Caribbean culture in the UK. In 1986 she chaired a committee at the Commonwealth Institute in London, driving a year‑long Caribbean Focus programme that included touring art shows, a music village, a theatre company, and a literary conference. She calmly handled tough moments and was loved by colleagues and friends alike.
