Since the war between Israel and Iran escalated, missiles and drones have been flying over the Gulf. Flights are diverted and shipping routes are closed, especially near the Strait of Hormuz. This disrupts the Gulf’s plans to grow their economies and keep the region safe. Gulf countries have tried to keep peace by talking to Iran and Israel, but the war shows how fragile their security plans are. They need stronger cooperation and better defence to avoid future conflicts.
