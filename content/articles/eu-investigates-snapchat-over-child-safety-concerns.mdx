The European Union has launched a probe into Snapchat after worries that kids are being exposed to grooming and sexual content. Regulators also say four porn sites let minors see adult material. The investigation is part of the Digital Services Act, which aims to protect teens from online harms. Snapchat says it cares about safety, while the EU may order changes or fines. If found guilty, the sites could face fines up to 6% of global revenue.
