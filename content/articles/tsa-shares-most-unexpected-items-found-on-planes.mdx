The top item was a fake pipe bomb made of PVC pipes, wires and paper caught in a Boise passenger’s checked bag. The second‑worst was turtles – one wrapped in a bra, another hidden in a pair of pants. Other odd finds included bullets in a Nesquik box and pills hidden in a shampoo bottle. TSA warns travellers to check the “What Can I Bring” tool before flying.
