Online criminals are using Elon Musk’s Grok AI tool to create illegal pictures of children. A UK watchdog says the tool could bring this kind of child‑abuse material into the mainstream. Reports show girls 11‑13 being depicted topless and in sexual poses. The social‑media app X is full of such images, sparking outrage. A UK committee has stopped using X, and regulators are looking into stronger safeguards.
