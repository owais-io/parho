The U.S. Justice Department has decided to drop the criminal charges against Jan “Jay” Carey, a 55‑year‑old veteran who set a U.S. flag on fire in front of the White House to protest a Trump executive order that aimed to stop flag burning. The decision was made just before a deadline for prosecutors to respond to a lawsuit arguing that the charges violated Carey's First Amendment rights. Carey's lawyer says this is a win for free speech.
