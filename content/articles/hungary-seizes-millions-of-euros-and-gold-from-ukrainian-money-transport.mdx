Hungary stopped a convoy of two armored bank trucks that were carrying about €35 million in cash, $40 million in euros, and 9 kg of gold. Seven Ukrainians on the trip were arrested, and Hungary said it is investigating possible money‑laundering links. Ukrainian officials claim Budapest is using the incident for political gain before Hungary’s next election, while the Ukrainian president has publicly threatened the Hungarian prime minister.
