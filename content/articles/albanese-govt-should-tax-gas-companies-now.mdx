Australia’s rising fuel prices, driven by the war in Ukraine and high global gas prices, have left consumers frustrated. Gas companies are earning huge profits but pay little in taxes. Politicians from several parties are calling for a windfall tax on LNG exports, and the government has been slow to act. If it doesn’t move soon, voters may blame the government for not curbing gas company greed.
