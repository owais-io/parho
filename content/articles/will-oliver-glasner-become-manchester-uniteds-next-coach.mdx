Oliver Glasner, the Crystal Palace coach, is a strong candidate for Manchester United after Ruben Amorim’s dismissal. He is known for his direct style and 3‑4‑2‑1 setup. While other clubs like Tottenham and Bayern have shown interest, he may stay at Palace for another season. His pressing tactics work for smaller teams; it’s uncertain if they fit United’s possession‑heavy style. Fans and managers wonder if he can succeed at a top club.
