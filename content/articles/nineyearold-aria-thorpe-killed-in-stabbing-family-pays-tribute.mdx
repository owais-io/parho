Aria Thorpe, a nine‑year‑old from Weston‑super‑Mare, was stabbed to death on Monday. A 15‑year‑old boy was arrested and now faces a court hearing by video link. He didn’t plead and is expected to go on trial in June next year. Aria’s family, especially her mother’s side, spoke about how bright and happy she was and thanked neighbours for their support. The case is still developing.
