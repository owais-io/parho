A deadly fungal outbreak at Sydney’s Royal Prince Alfred Hospital killed two patients and sickened four more. NSW Health Minister Ryan Park said there was no cover‑up, claiming the government told patients and families but kept the news from the public to avoid unnecessary panic. An expert panel, led by Dr Kerry Chant, guided the response, and the transplant unit was closed and later cleaned and reopened.
