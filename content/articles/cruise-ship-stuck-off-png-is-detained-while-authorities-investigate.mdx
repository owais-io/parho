An Australian cruise ship, the Coral Adventurer, ran aground on a reef near Papua New Guinea’s coast. It is stuck 30km from Lae and has been detained by PNG and Australian authorities because it might be damaged and unsafe. There are no injuries among its 80 passengers and 43 crew. Investigations are underway to find out how the ship got stuck, while tugs try to refloat it. The ship will be inspected once freed.
