Top economists, led by Nobel laureate Joseph Stiglitz, want Sri Lanka to stop paying its debt now. Cyclone Ditwah killed over 600 people and destroyed thousands of homes. Sri Lanka’s debt was cut last year, but repayments were still a huge part of the budget. The experts say the disaster makes it impossible to keep paying. They call for a new deal that eases the load. Climate scientists say the heat made the flooding worse.
