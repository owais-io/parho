Australia is bracing for heavy rain and flooding across central and southern regions, with thunderstorms predicted in all states and territories on Sunday. South Australia, the Northern Territory and Victoria are already drenched, while cities like Sydney and Melbourne may see additional rain. A tropical low has caused record‑setting rainfall in a remote community, and authorities warn of flash flooding, strong winds, and landslides, especially near recent bushfires.
