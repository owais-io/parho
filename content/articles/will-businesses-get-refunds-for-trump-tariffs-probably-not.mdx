The Supreme Court said President Trump had exceeded his power to impose tariffs. Small businesses wonder if they can get the money back, but the answer is uncertain. Trump still has other trade‑law tools he can use, and the administration says refunds are unlikely. Big firms have sued for money back, but the process is slow and expensive. Congress could force refunds, but that seems unlikely right now.
