The Nigerian government freed a final group of 130 schoolchildren who had been kidnapped from St Mary's Catholic school in Niger state. They will meet their families in the capital on Monday after a month of worry. The abduction is part of a larger wave of school attacks in the country, but the kidnappers haven’t been identified. Security forces are stretched across the region, and the incident adds to a growing list of abductions nationwide.
