Linda Reynolds says she will keep fighting legal battles over the Brittany Higgins case, even after the fight has cost her millions. She says courts have cleared her of covering up the rape and found that Bruce Lehrmann was guilty. Reynolds has sued Higgins for damaging posts, won $315,000 in damages, and is now trying to recover the money she spent on court costs. She also criticises politicians and the media for turning the story into a political fight.
