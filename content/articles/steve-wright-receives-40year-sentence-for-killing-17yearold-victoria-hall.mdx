Steve Wright, already serving a whole‑life sentence for murdering five women, was given an extra 40 years at the Old Bailey court for killing 17‑year‑old Victoria Hall in 1999 and for trying to kidnap 22‑year‑old Emily Doherty. He pleaded guilty, and the judge noted how Hall was abducted, murdered, and left in a ditch. The court added the new sentence to his existing convictions, reinforcing his status as one of Britain’s most dangerous offenders.
