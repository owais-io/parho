Chef Sean Sherman, an Oglala Lakota and James Beard award winner, grew up hunting and gathering on the Pine Ridge Reservation. He’s released a new cookbook called *Turtle Island* that blends stories, history, and recipes from Native American communities across North America. The book celebrates traditional foods like bison, corn, beans, and game, and stresses the importance of food sovereignty—helping tribes control their own food. Sherman also runs the restaurant Owamni and a nonprofit that teaches and supplies Indigenous cooking.
