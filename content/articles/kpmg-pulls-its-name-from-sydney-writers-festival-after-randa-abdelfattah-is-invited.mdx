KPMG asked the Sydney Writers’ Festival to remove its name from the event’s website after the festival announced Palestinian academic Randa Abdel‑Fattah as a speaker. The firm said it isn’t a “partner” and that it had received complaints about Abdel‑Fattah’s controversial social‑media posts. The festival keeps the invitation, while the controversy has drawn criticism from Jewish leaders and politicians.
