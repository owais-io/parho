Three Israeli‑American brothers who once closed high‑end real‑estate deals for celebrities are on trial in New York for sex trafficking. Prosecutors say each raped at least ten women, used drugs to incapacitate them, and coordinated attacks at parties. Victims include Kate Whiteman and others, with text messages showing drug plans. The brothers plead not guilty and claim the suits are part of an extortion plot. The case mixes celebrity life, alleged crimes, and a fierce legal battle.
