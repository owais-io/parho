At the Australian Open, several players had to end their matches early because of injuries. Francesca Jones fell on court 15, hurting a muscle and choosing to retire while trailing 6‑2, 3‑2. Men’s top‑seed Félix Auger‑Aliassime and Canadian 21‑year‑old Marina Stakusic also withdrew after cramps. The day’s retirements show how even mild heat can push athletes over the edge. Jones, ranked No. 69, is battling a rare genetic syndrome but remains determined.
