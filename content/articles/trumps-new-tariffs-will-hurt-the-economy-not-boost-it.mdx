After the Supreme Court struck down his first tariff plan, Trump rolled out a new 15 % tariff on many imports. He says it protects American jobs, but experts warn it will raise prices, hurt factories, slow investment and damage trade ties. The move adds uncertainty, cuts hiring and costs families up to $800 a year. Most Americans disapprove of the policy.
