A DHS report to Congress says two U.S. Customs and Border Protection agents fired their guns at Alex Pretti in a Minnesota raid, even though officials had claimed he was brandishing a weapon. The report contradicts earlier statements and adds to backlash over videos showing aggressive agent tactics. President Trump said he might cut the number of agents in the state, while the investigation is still underway.
