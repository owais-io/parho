During the 74th Miss Universe pageant, a series of controversies rattled the event. Judges quit over claims of a secret pre‑selected list, a Mexican contestant was publicly berated, and several participants fell on stage. The organisers blamed misinformation and said no extra jury was created. Despite the drama, Fatima Bosch from Mexico was crowned Miss Universe, ending the week’s turmoil.
