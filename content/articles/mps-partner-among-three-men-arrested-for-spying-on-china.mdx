A man who is the partner of a current Labour MP, plus two other men, have been taken into custody by the Met Police on suspicion of spying for China. The arrests are part of a wider national‑security probe into Chinese interference in UK democracy. Police say there’s no immediate threat to the public. The government has formally protested to China and warned it will take action against anyone who tries to influence UK policy.
