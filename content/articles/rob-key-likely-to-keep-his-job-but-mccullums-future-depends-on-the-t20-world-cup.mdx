England’s cricket boss Rob Key looks set to stay on after the board reviews the Ashes loss. The decision hinges on his handling of the team and any changes he’s willing to make. The real test for coach Brendon McCullum comes with the T20 World Cup next month—his performance there could end his tenure. The board wants to avoid a big shake‑up and keep the team stable.
