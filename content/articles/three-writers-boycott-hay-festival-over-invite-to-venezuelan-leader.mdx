Three Colombian authors have pulled out of the Hay Festival in Cartagena because they disagree with inviting Venezuelan opposition figure María Corina Machado. The writers say Machado backs U.S. pressure and potential military action against Venezuela, and her ties to far‑right leaders make her a controversial figure. They reject giving her a platform, while the festival stresses the importance of free dialogue. Machado’s support for Trump and her stance on Venezuela sparked the boycott.
