After Storm Goretti, thousands of homes in Cornwall still lack power and water. About 1,250 houses are without electricity, and 3,000 homes have no water, mainly around Helston. Schools are closed or opening late. Fallen trees have ripped out water mains, hurting supplies. MPs have asked the government to declare a national emergency, while some nursing homes had to evacuate. The storm has left many vulnerable residents without basic services.
