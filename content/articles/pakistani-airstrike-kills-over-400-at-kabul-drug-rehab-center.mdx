Pakistani planes struck a state‑run drug rehab centre in Kabul, killing more than 400 people and injuring over 260. The hospital, called Omid, collapsed and burned, leaving many bodies under rubble. Afghan rescue teams are still digging out victims. The UN wants an independent probe, while Pakistan says it only hit Taliban‑linked military sites. The strike has drawn criticism from the UN, India and China.
