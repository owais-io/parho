West Ham has verbally agreed to sell Brazil midfielder Lucas Paquetá to Flamengo for a fee between £36 m and £40 m, ending any loan back to the Premier League club. Paquetá, who wants to return home after being cleared of spot‑fixing charges, has missed West Ham’s last three games with a back injury. The sale will give West Ham money to strengthen a squad that is fighting to avoid relegation.
