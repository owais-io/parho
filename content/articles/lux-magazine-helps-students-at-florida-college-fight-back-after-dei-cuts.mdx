In 2023, Florida governor Ron DeSantis cut diversity programs at New College, firing staff and ending gender studies. The campus became more conservative and many students and professors left. In February, the feminist magazine Lux held a talk on campus, bringing in activists to show students they still have allies. Students heard about protests, support groups and learned they’re not alone, sparking hope and plans to organize again.
