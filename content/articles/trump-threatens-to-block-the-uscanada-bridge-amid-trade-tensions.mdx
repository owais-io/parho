Donald Trump has threatened to block the opening of the $4.6 bn Gordie Howe International Bridge that links Windsor and Detroit, as the U.S. prepares to vote on new tariffs on Canada. Trump says Canada has treated the U.S. unfairly and that a new Canada‑China deal will even ban Canadian ice‑hockey. He also says he himself approved the bridge but blames Obama. Critics warn the delay would raise costs, hurt supply chains and jobs in Michigan.
