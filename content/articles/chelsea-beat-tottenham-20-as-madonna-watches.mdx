Chelsea beat Tottenham 2‑0, with Keira Walsh and Alyssa Thompson scoring, ending a run of defeats and keeping their faint hope of winning the Women’s Super League alive. Pop star Madonna watched from the stands, having come to see her daughters play earlier that week. Meanwhile, Liverpool beat Aston Villa 4‑1, West Ham edged Brighton 3‑2 after falling behind, and Everton won 1‑0 against London City Lionesses.
