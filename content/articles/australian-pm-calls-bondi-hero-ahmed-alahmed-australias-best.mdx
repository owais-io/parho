Ahmed al‑Ahmed, a Syrian‑born shopkeeper in Sydney, tackled a gunman at Bondi, saving lives. He’s now in hospital with two gunshot wounds. Prime Minister Anthony Albanese praised him as “the best of our country” and visited him. The community has sent flowers, and a GoFundMe has raised over $2 million to help. Ahmed is expected to receive a top bravery award.
