New emails show that after Jeffrey Epstein was released from prison in 2009, he still showed up at private dinners with tech giants like Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos and Bill Gates. The events were organized by literary agent John Brockman’s Edge network, which funded Epstein heavily. The emails also reveal he was asked to sponsor and attend many of these gatherings, but no public apologies or explanations were made.
