Women in Lesotho’s capital line up each morning hoping for factory jobs, but Trump’s 2025 tariffs have made those opportunities scarce. The country’s garment industry, once a key source of employment, now faces lower wages and many closures. Workers like Moleboheng Matsepe have gone from full‑time sewing to barely earning a few dollars a week. Despite the US offering duty‑free access under Agoa, the tariffs still hurt Lesotho’s economy and its people.
