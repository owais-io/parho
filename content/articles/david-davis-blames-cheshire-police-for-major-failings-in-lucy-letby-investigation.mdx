David Davis said Cheshire police made big mistakes in the investigation of nurse Lucy Letby, saying they ignored guidance and didn't bring in the right experts. He argues the police focused too much on Letby instead of looking at all the facts, calling the case a miscarriage of justice. The police minister says the probe followed the law. Letby was convicted of killing seven babies, but a review body is looking into the case.
