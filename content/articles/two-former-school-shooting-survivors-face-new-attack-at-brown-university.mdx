Brown University is once again the scene of a shooting, this time involving two students who had survived earlier school attacks. Mia Tretta, 21, and Zoe Weissman, 20, were both shot in past incidents. On Saturday, a man opened fire during final exams, killing two and injuring nine. The victims were in their dorms when they heard the gunshots. The attack highlights the ongoing U.S. debate over gun control.
