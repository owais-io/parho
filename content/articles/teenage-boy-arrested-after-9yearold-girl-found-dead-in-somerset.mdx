Police arrested a teenage boy in Weston's Worle after a 9‑year‑old girl was found dead in a home in Lime Close. The incident shocked the town. Police are still investigating and will do a post‑mortem. Residents are being asked to stay calm and report any concerns. Council leaders and local officers say the community will unite and provide support to the family. No evidence of danger to the public.
