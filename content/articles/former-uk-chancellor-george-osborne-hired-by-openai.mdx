Former UK Chancellor George Osborne has joined OpenAI to lead its work with governments worldwide. Based in London, he will head the “OpenAI for Countries” team that helps national governments roll out AI. Osborne already chairs the British Museum, advises Coinbase and hosts a podcast. OpenAI is expanding AI projects in places like Norway, UAE and the UK, and Osborne will grow ties in Argentina, Australia, Germany and South Korea. He joins a company that is navigating legal challenges over ChatGPT.
