A protest group called Everyone Hates Elon hung a photo of Prince Andrew being driven away from a police station on a wall in Paris’s Louvre. The picture shows the former duke in a Range Rover after his arrest for alleged misconduct in public office. The activists added a card saying “He’s Sweating Now.” The stunt highlights Andrew’s ongoing controversy over links to Jeffrey Epstein and his recent suspension from royal duties.
