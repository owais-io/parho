Regional petrol stations are running out because people have been buying fuel in large numbers, spiking demand by up to 238% in some places. The energy minister says Australia still has enough fuel, but supply chains are stretched. An emergency taskforce is working to help farmers and independent stations get fuel. Customers are urged not to panic‑buy, as this can make shortages worse.
