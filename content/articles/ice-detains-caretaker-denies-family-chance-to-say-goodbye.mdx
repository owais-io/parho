In Texas, ICE arrested Maher Tarabishi, who had looked after his son Wael with a rare muscle disease, during a routine visit. Without Maher’s help, Wael’s health worsened, he was hospitalized and died on January 23. The family begged ICE to let Maher attend the funeral, but the request was denied, citing a false claim that he belongs to the PLO. The decision left the family devastated.
