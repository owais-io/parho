Trump’s Justice Department released a batch of Jeffrey Epstein documents that many lawmakers say is incomplete and heavily redacted. Democrats and Republicans want the full files by the deadline set by the new Epstein Transparency Act. The missing documents include a photo of Trump with Epstein and others. Some officials are threatening legal action, and the controversy has sparked accusations that the department is hiding evidence.
