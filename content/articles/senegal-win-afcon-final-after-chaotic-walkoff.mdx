In the African Cup of Nations final, Senegal beat Morocco 2‑0 after a chaotic scene. Two injury‑time penalties caused Morocco to walk off in protest, and riot police were called. The walk‑off left a tense atmosphere, but Senegal eventually won when Pape Gueye scored a late header. The match ended with controversy, and Senegal’s coach may face sanctions for the walk‑off.
