Australian political cartoonist Jon Kudelka died at 53. His wife announced his death in South Hobart. He was celebrated for his sharp, witty cartoons that critiqued politicians and tackled science denial. Over 30 years he produced around 10,000 cartoons, winning several Walkley Awards. Kudelka battled an inoperable brain tumor in 2024 but continued to write and draw until his death. He is remembered for his humor and impact on public debate.
