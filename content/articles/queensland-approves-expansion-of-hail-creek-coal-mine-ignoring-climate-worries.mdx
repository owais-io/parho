Queensland has approved a three‑year extension for the Hail Creek open‑cut coal mine, letting Glencore add 24 million tonnes of coal. The move will clear 600 hectares of important koala habitat and release huge amounts of methane – the biggest source of methane from Australian coal mines. Environmental groups say the state has ignored climate science, and the project is now being reviewed by the federal government.
