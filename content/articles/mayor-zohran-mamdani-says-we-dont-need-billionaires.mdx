Zohran Mamdani, the new mayor of New York City and a democratic socialist, won the race with bold promises. He pledged to protect immigrants, fight Islamophobia, and curb extreme wealth by raising taxes on the richest. Mamdani used humor and viral moments—like riding a Citi Bike—to show his commitment to affordable transit and social justice. His message is a clear challenge to big money and bigotry.
