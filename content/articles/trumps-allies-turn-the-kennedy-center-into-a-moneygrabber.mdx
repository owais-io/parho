A Senate investigation says Trump’s friends took over the Kennedy Center, renamed it after Trump, and turned it into a money‑getting place. Trump ousted the board, installed allies, gave the center free access to big groups like FIFA, and paid for luxury hotels and meals. The investigation says the center is losing millions, paying friends without paying proper rent, and using public money for political favors.
