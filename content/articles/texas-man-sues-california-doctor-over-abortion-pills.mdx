Texas man Jerry Rodriguez filed a lawsuit against California physician Remy Coeytaux, claiming the doctor mailed abortion pills to Rodriguez’s girlfriend in 2024 and 2025, leading to two pregnancies ending. Rodriguez wants $100,000 in damages and wants Coeytaux barred from prescribing abortion drugs in Texas. The suit uses Texas’s new law that lets private citizens sue anyone shipping abortion pills into the state. The case follows Louisiana’s extradition request, but California protects doctors with shield laws.
