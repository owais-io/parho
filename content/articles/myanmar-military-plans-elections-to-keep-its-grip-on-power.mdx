Myanmar’s military is holding elections on Dec. 28 to try to show it still rules. Few opposition parties remain and many areas are barred from voting because they’re in fighting zones. The junta is backed by China but faces criticism from the West and the UN. Critics say the vote is a sham, and people fear arrest for speaking out. The country is still in violent conflict.
