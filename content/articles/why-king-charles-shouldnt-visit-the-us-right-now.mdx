A letter writer argues that King Charles III’s trip to the United States would look like the UK supporting Donald Trump’s controversial policies. He says the king’s role is to stay out of politics, and a visit could be seen as pandering to Trump. The writer believes the king should wait until Trump apologises for his recent slights before considering a visit.
