Governments in Thailand, the Philippines and Vietnam are trying to save energy after Middle East fighting has pushed oil prices up. They are telling people to use less air‑conditioning, cut electricity and even let workers tele‑work. They also offer short‑term fuel subsidies and cash to transport drivers, but the help may only last a few weeks. The high fuel cost is hurting drivers, commuters and businesses.
