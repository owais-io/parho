Bloomberg says it has the audio of two secret calls between Trump adviser Steve Witkoff and Russian officials, showing how Witkoff was trying to win Trump’s favour for Moscow. The recordings were leaked, but no one knows who passed them to the news outlet. Experts guess it could be a Russian, Ukrainian, U.S., or European intelligence service. The leak fuels doubts about Witkoff’s loyalties and how much the Kremlin may be influencing U.S. talks.
