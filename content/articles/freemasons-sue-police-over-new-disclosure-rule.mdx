The Metropolitan Police have announced that any officer or staff member who is a Freemason must now disclose that membership. The move, aimed at improving transparency and public trust, has upset the United Grand Lodge of England, which says the rule is unfair, discriminatory and breaches data‑protection laws. UGLE has written to the police and says it will seek a judicial review. The police say the rule does not stop people from joining Freemasonry.
