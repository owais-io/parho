Lindsey Heaps, the captain of the US women’s soccer squad, is returning to her home state to join Denver’s brand‑new NWSL team, the Denver Summit, when her contract with Lyon ends. The 31‑year‑old has won world titles, an Olympic gold, and club championships in the US and France. She signed a deal that runs through 2029, and is excited to help build a winning club in Colorado.
