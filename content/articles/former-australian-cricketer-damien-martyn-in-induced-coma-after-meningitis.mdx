Former Australian cricketer Damien Martyn is in an induced coma after doctors diagnosed him with meningitis. He fell ill on Boxing Day and was rushed to a Queensland hospital. Friends, teammates and cricket officials are sending prayers and wishes. Martyn, who once captained Western Australia and scored over 4,400 Test runs, had posted excitement for the Ashes before his sickness. He hopes to fight and recover.
