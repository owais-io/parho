The Treasury has opened an inquiry after key budget details were leaked before the autumn budget. Minister James Murray said security checks will be reviewed. The leaks covered big policies, like a tax‑freeze, an electric‑vehicle levy, and a tourist tax. Conservatives say Rachel Reeves is trying to sway opinion, while business leaders say the rumours hurt confidence. The investigation will look for who leaked the data.
