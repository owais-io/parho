Jeremiah Azu surprised everyone by running the 200m at the UK Athletics Indoor Championships, a race usually not held indoors. After winning the 60m title the day before, he clocked a personal best of 20.77 seconds, using the event as extra training. Azu said the competition gives a speed boost that training alone can’t. While other stars like Keely Hodgkinson and Josh Kerr skipped races, Azu’s win added excitement to an otherwise quieter meet.
