Kirsty Coventry, the president of the International Olympic Committee, confirmed that she has not yet spoken to U.S. President Donald Trump about preparations for the 2028 Los Angeles Olympics. She said the IOC stays out of politics and will focus on getting all national committees ready. Coventry also plans to meet U.S. Vice‑President Jan E. Vance before the 2026 Winter Games, stressing the Games must remain a place of unity, not geopolitics.
