US forces boarded the Russian‑flagged tanker Marinera after a two‑week chase that started in the Caribbean and finished in the Atlantic. They also seized the M Sophia, another sanctioned vessel. The move follows President Trump’s “blockade” on oil ships heading to Venezuela. White House says it will keep seizing such vessels, but officials didn’t rule out a reaction from Russia. The action could strain relations with Moscow while talks over Ukraine are still underway.
