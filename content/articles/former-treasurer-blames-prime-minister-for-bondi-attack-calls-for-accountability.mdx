Josh Frydenberg, a former Liberal treasurer, said Prime Minister Anthony Albanese must take personal responsibility for the 15 people killed in the Bondi shooting. The comments come as New South Wales Premier Chris Minns plans tighter protest rules. The opposition wants Parliament to sit early to tackle antisemitism and gun reform. Albanese stresses the need for gun changes, while the coalition pushes for stricter immigration and bans on hate speech.
