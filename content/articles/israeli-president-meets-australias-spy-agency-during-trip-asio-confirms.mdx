Israeli President Isaac Herzog secretly met Australia’s top spy agency during his visit, a move that sparked debate in Canberra. The Australian Security Intelligence Organisation confirmed the meeting, but the government didn’t explain why the visit happened. A Senate inquiry asked the foreign minister for details, and critics say Australians deserve more transparency. The trip came after protests over Israel’s actions in Gaza and a deadly antisemitic attack in Australia.
