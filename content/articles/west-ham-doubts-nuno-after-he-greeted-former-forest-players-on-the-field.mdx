West Ham’s fans and board are losing confidence in manager Nuno Espírito Santo after a string of losses. The club is also upset that Nuno greeted his former Nottingham Forest teammates on the field after a 2‑1 defeat. With only two wins in 16 games, his tactics and man‑management are under fire, and many say he should have been fired after the Wolves loss. The club faces a tough choice before next matches.
