CBS canceled a 60 Minutes piece on a brutal El Salvador prison that held many Venezuelan migrants sent by the Trump administration. The decision sparked anger from reporters, critics and politicians. CBS’s new editor‑in‑chief said the story needed more work, but a senior correspondent said the report was fact‑checked and should air. Critics say the move shows political pressure and highlights tension between journalism and corporate politics.
