Today show host Savannah Guthrie stopped by NBC studios in New York to thank her colleagues and say she will return to the show. Her 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy, vanished in Arizona over a month ago and hasn't been found. The FBI and local police are still searching, but no new clues have emerged. Guthrie has increased the missing‑person reward to $1 million and asks anyone with information to come forward.
