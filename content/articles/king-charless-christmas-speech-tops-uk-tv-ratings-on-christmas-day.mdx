King Charles’s Christmas speech was the most watched TV program on Christmas Day in the UK, drawing almost 7 million viewers across BBC, ITV, Sky News and GB News. The address, recorded in Westminster Abbey, was similar in size to last year’s. Although the speech was top, the final episode of “Gavin & Stacey” still attracted the most viewers overall, with 12.3 million. Other popular shows were “The Scarecrow’s Wedding” and “Strictly Come Dancing.”
