Mind, a UK mental‑health charity, is starting a year‑long review of how artificial intelligence is used in mental‑health care. The move follows a Guardian exposé that showed Google’s AI “Overviews” gave people dangerous medical advice. The commission will bring doctors, patients, policy makers and tech firms together to map risks, test safeguards and help shape stricter rules for AI‑driven health info.
