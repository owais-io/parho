Joanna Ross, a passionate teacher who worked for 15 years at a special school in London, died at 70 after a battle with Alzheimer’s. She was known for her energy, creativity, and commitment to inclusive learning. Joanna helped design a new school building that met students’ physical and cognitive needs, and she was honoured as Teacher of the Year in 2015. She touched many lives with kindness, inspiring students, families, and colleagues alike. She is survived by her sisters, nieces, and nephew.
