American speed skater Jordan Stolz won the men's 500‑meter race at the Milan Cortina Winter Olympics, beating the old Olympic record with a time of 33.77 seconds. He took his second gold of the Games and set the fastest 500‑meter time ever at sea level. The finish was clean: all three skaters beat the old record, and Stolz's margin of 0.11 seconds was the biggest since 1988. With a recent 1000‑meter win, he could chase a third gold.
