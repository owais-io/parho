A wave of AI‑generated videos shows fake Black voices and faces, including Trump‑related images, that spread online. These clips – called digital blackface – use AI tools to mimic Black speech and culture, often with racist jokes. The Trump White House has used doctored photos and deepfakes to stir hate, showing how the state can bend reality with tech. Some tech firms are trying to stop it, but the problem keeps growing.
