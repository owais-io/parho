After England beat Wales at Twickenham, Richmond Council handed out 24 fixed‑penalty notices for public urination. The fines are £150, or £100 if paid early. The Rugby Football Union is also pushing for more concerts at the stadium, but local residents oppose the plan, citing antisocial behaviour. The RFU says it will work with police and council to keep matchdays clean and is running a charm offensive to win neighbourhood support.
