A former prince, Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor, was taken into custody at his Norfolk home on suspicion of misconduct in public office linked to the Jeffrey Epstein case. Police say he may have shared sensitive information while serving as a trade envoy. The King said the law must run its course, while Epstein’s accuser’s family welcomed the arrest. Investigations are still underway.
