Yuli Novak, head of Israeli human‑rights group B’Tselem, says the war in Gaza is genocide—systematic killing, starving and forced displacement of Palestinians. He points out that the violence began long before the Oct. 7 attack and that the US and other Western governments have largely ignored or supported it. The article calls for Israel and the US to be held responsible, warns that letting this happen normalises future atrocities, and urges people worldwide to act.
