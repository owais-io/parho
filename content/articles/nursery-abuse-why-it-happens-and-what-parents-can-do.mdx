Parents are shocked by recent cases where nursery workers sexually abused or neglected children. The problems stem from low pay, short hours, and a shortage of well‑trained staff. Many nurseries have no CCTV or clear records of staff qualifications. The author urges the government to install cameras, keep a register of workers, and give inspectors more power to keep children safe. Urgent action is needed to protect young kids.
