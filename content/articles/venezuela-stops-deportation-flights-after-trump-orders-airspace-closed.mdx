Venezuela has halted all deportation flights after President Trump warned that the country's airspace would be closed. The Venezuelan government called the warning a colonial attack on its sovereignty and said it would not accept foreign orders. Trump’s move is part of a broader U.S. strategy that includes military strikes in the Caribbean and covert operations in Venezuela. Meanwhile, the White House has launched a site targeting media it calls biased.
