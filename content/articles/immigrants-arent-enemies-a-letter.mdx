A young writer replies to Reform UK's spokesperson Zia Yusuf, arguing that immigrants are not enemies but vital parts of British life. They fear politicians might deport friends, spread hate, or dismantle communities. The letter lists immigrants as doctors, nurses, teachers, shopkeepers, and family members, stressing that they help make Britain safer and stronger. The writer calls for seeing immigrants as people, not threats.
