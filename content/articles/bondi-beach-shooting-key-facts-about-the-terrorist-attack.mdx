On Sunday evening, two gunmen fired on a Hanukkah gathering at Bondi Beach in Sydney, killing 16 people and injuring dozens. One shooter was killed by police, the other, a 24‑year‑old, is in critical condition. Authorities say it was a terrorist attack linked to antisemitism. The incident prompted calls for tighter gun laws, and leaders worldwide offered condolences. A volunteer man helped stop one shooter, and many Australians signed up to donate blood.
