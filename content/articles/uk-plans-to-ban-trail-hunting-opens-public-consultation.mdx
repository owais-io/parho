UK officials say they will outlaw trail hunting, a practice that lets dogs follow a pre‑made scent across the countryside. The move follows claims that trail hunting masks illegal fox hunting. The animal‑welfare minister, Sue Hayman, announced the ban and opened a public consultation to decide how it will work. The hunt community, including the Countryside Alliance, warns the ban could hurt rural life and the economy.
