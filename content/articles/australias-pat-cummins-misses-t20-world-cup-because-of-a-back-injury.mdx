Pat Cummins, Australia’s Test captain, had to drop out of the T20 World Cup after a back injury kept him out of most Ashes tests. He explained that the injury needs about four weeks to heal, so he will skip the tournament to rest and avoid risking further damage. Australia will replace him with younger bowlers, while Cummins focuses on long‑term fitness for future Test and ODI games.
