NBC’s Savannah Guthrie is fighting for her 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy, who vanished from a Tucson home a month ago. A $1 million reward has been offered and the FBI moved its investigation to Phoenix, but evidence so far is weak. Guthrie also pledged half a million dollars to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children and stresses how media attention can help find missing people. The case highlights the unequal coverage of missing persons, especially among Indigenous and minority groups.
