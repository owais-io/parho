81 women added to a civil lawsuit against Fort Hood army doctor Blaine McGraw, who’s already facing criminal charges for secretly filming patients. The suit says McGraw repeatedly touched patients inappropriately and secretly recorded them during gynecological exams. It expands beyond the criminal case, adding assault, sexual assault, and battery claims. Victims say he used his phone during exams and failed to document a rape kit. The case could test new Pentagon reforms on sexual misconduct.
