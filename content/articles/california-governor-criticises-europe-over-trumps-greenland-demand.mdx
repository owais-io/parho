California Governor Gavin Newsom called on Europe to stop being complicit in Donald Trump’s demand that he be allowed to buy or annex Greenland. He made the remarks at the World Economic Forum in Davos, criticizing European leaders for playing along and refusing to stand up to Trump. Newsom also joked about selling kneepads for politicians who kneel to Trump. Europe is wary of harming ties with the US, and some leaders have tried to negotiate with Trump.
