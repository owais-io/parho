Trump officials told Congress that special forces pulled Venezuelan President Nicolás Maduro from his home and put him in a New York jail. Republicans say it was a lawful arrest of a drug‑trafficker, while Democrats claim it was an illegal act of war that could upset the country. The move split lawmakers, drew criticism from many countries, and left most Americans unsure about the U.S. role in Venezuela.
