Mandarin ducks were once a fancy, expensive pet kept by wealthy families. They were imported from China and valued for their bright colours and status. Today, there are thousands of pairs in the UK and you can spot them in many gardens and ponds. The male’s vivid green head, purple neck, and big cinnamon‑sail feather make a stunning display while it sings and fluffs its white breast. These birds bring a splash of exotic beauty to everyday scenes.
