UK shoppers are buying more healthy foods like fruit, yoghurt, fish and poultry as they try to hit New Year fitness goals, while grocery price inflation slowed to its lowest since April. Sales of fresh fruit up 6% and cottage cheese jumped 50%. More people are buying own‑label items, which now make up 52% of spend, and functional drinks are up 13%.
