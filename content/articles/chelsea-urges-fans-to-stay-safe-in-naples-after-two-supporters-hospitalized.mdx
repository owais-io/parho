Chelsea warned its supporters to be extra careful in Naples before their Champions League match against Napoli. Two fans were taken to hospital after an incident that left them with non‑life‑threatening injuries, possibly from knives pulled by Napoli ultras. The club said it’s aware of the event and asked fans to follow safety advice. The match is crucial as Chelsea hopes to finish in the top eight and advance to the last 16.
