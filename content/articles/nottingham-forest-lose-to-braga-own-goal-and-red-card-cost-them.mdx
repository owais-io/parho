Nottingham Forest’s first Europa League game in almost 30 years ended in a 1-0 loss to Braga. A key error by captain Ryan Yates caused an own goal, and the match saw a red card for Elliot Anderson after a last‑minute scuffle. Fans were disappointed, especially after the team had only one win in eight games. Forest’s attacking problems were clear, and the loss hurts their European hopes.
