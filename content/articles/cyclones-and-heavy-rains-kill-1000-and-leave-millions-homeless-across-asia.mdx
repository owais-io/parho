A mix of tropical cyclones and monsoon rains has flooded huge parts of Asia, killing more than 1,000 people and displacing hundreds of thousands. In Indonesia, 502 died and 508 are missing, with 300,000 people forced out of homes. Sri Lanka’s Cyclone Ditwah left 355 dead, 366 missing and 1.3 million affected. Thailand, Vietnam and Malaysia also saw heavy flooding, causing dozens of deaths and huge damage. Rescue teams are working to deliver aid and restore order.
