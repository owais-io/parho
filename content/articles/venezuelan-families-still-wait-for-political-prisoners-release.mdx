Families of Venezuelan political prisoners continue to camp outside prisons, demanding their loved ones be freed. Despite a new government saying it will release inmates, only about 250 have come out of 900 detained, leaving many still behind bars. The slow pace shows the new administration is making little real change, keeping the regime’s grip on power and leaving families desperate for closure.
