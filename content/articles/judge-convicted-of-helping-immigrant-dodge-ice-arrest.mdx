A jury in Wisconsin found Judge Hannah Dugan guilty of obstruction for helping a Mexican immigrant avoid ICE, but not guilty of the separate concealment charge. The case highlights Trump’s tough‑on‑immigration push. Dugan may face up to five years in prison and plans to appeal. Prosecutors said she misled federal agents, while her lawyers argued she followed courthouse rules. No sentencing date has been set yet.
