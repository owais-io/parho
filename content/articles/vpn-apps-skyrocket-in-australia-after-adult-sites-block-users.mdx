Australia’s new online‑safety rules require adult websites and other services to check users’ ages, so sites like RedTube and Pornhub blocked Australian visitors. Many Australians turned to VPN apps to appear overseas and keep accessing content. VPNs jumped in the app charts, with Proton and NordVPN rising into the top ten. Regulators warn non‑compliant platforms could face huge fines, while privacy experts caution that free VPNs might sell user data.
