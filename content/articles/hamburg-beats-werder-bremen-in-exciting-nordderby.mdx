Hamburg won a thrilling Nordderby against Werder Bremen, ending a long wait for a derby win. The match went back and forth, with Hamburg’s Albert Sambi Lokonga levelled early, Luka Vuskovic scoring a stylish goal, and Yussuf Poulsen hitting the winner from the bench. Fans cheered loudly, and coach Merlin Polzin praised the team’s energy. This victory gives the club a boost as they aim to stay in the Bundesliga.
