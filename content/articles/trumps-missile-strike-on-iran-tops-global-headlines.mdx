On Sunday, US and Israeli missile attacks on Iran made front pages worldwide as news spread that Iran’s supreme leader, Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, was killed. Papers from New York to London, Paris to Beijing ran headlines and editorials ranging from shock to celebration. Some called the move reckless, others saw it as a chance for change. The stories show how the attack has sparked anger, fear, and calls for Iranian resistance.
