Coco Gauff won her match against Iga Swiatek 6‑4, 6‑2, but the U.S. team lost 2‑1 to Poland in the United Cup. Poland’s victory came when the doubles pair Jan Zielinski and Katarzyna Kawa beat Gauff and Christian Harrison in a tight 7‑6, 7‑6 win. Poland now moves on to the final against Switzerland.
