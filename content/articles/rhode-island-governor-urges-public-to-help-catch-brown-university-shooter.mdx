The governor said law‑enforcement is doing everything possible to catch the person who fired the 9‑mm gun that killed two students and injured nine others at Brown University. Police released new video of a person of interest seen walking alone near campus and asked anyone who saw them to come forward. The FBI offered a $50,000 reward, and the search continues as the city stays on alert.
