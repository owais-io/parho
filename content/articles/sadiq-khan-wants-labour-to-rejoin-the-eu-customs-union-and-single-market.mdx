London Mayor Sadiq Khan has said Labour should promise in its next election to bring Britain back into the EU customs union and single market. He believes this will fix economic, social and cultural damage caused by Brexit, and argues a manifesto commitment would avoid a second referendum. Labour leader Keir Starmer is unlikely to agree, but Khan wants to raise the issue before upcoming local elections.
