BHP tried again to buy Anglo American, but the offer was dropped after Anglo rejected it. The UK miner is already planning a merger with Canadian rival Teck Resources to form a huge copper group. BHP had offered a mix of cash and shares, but decided to stop the chase, saying it can grow on its own. City rules now keep BHP from bidding for another six months.
