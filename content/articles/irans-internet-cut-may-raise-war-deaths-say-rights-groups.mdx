Iran is in the middle of a war with US and Israeli bombings. At the same time, the Iranian government shut down most of the internet, leaving civilians unable to see news, evacuation warnings or call family. Human‑rights groups say the blackout makes the war more deadly because people can’t find safe spots or report injuries. Many schools, hospitals and homes have been hit, with over 1,100 civilians dead, including 180 children.
