On New Year’s Eve, people traveling between Sheffield and Doncaster had to wait because a thief stole signal cables in Conisbrough. The break‑in disrupted CrossCountry, Northern and TransPennine Express trains, causing up to 30‑minute delays for some routes. Network Rail staff are on the scene and the British Transport Police are looking into it. This theft is part of a growing problem; last year more than 100 incidents cut over 100,000 minutes of train time and cost millions of pounds.
