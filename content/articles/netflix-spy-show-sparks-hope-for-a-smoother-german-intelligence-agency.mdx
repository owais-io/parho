Netflix’s new six‑part spy drama “Unfamiliar” follows a former BND couple in Berlin who hack a Russian agent’s taxi to uncover secrets. The show has drawn 20 million viewers worldwide and paints the German spy agency as clumsy and ruled by strict rules. In real life, the BND’s budget rose 26 % and lawmakers are considering new powers like facial‑recognition and cyber‑retaliation. The series may change how people see the BND.
