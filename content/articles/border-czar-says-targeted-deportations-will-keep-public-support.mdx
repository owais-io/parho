Tom Homan, Trump’s new border czar, told NBC that deporting only people who commit serious crimes will keep voters on the president’s side. He pointed out that most ICE arrests are not violent offenders, and warned that mass deportations could erode public trust. Even though he’s moving to soften enforcement in Minneapolis, aggressive actions, including shootings, continue nationwide.
