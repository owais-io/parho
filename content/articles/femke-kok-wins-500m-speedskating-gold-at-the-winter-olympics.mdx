At the Winter Olympics, Dutch skater Femke Kok took the 500‑meter speed‑skating gold. She ran an Olympic‑record 36.49 seconds, beating defending champion Erin Jackson who finished fifth. Kok’s fast start, smooth turns and strong finish gave her the biggest win margin in 54 years. She adds a first Olympic gold to her three world titles. Japan’s Miko Tahagi earned bronze, while Jutta Leerdam came in silver.
