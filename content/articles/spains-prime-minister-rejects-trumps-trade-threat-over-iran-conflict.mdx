President Donald Trump warned Spain that the U.S. would cut all trade if Spain refused to let U.S. forces use Spanish bases for attacks on Iran. Spanish Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez fired back, saying Spain would not help any war and calling the conflict a danger to millions. He urged the U.S., Israel and Iran to stop fighting, defended Spain’s EU trade stance and said the EU will act to protect member interests.
