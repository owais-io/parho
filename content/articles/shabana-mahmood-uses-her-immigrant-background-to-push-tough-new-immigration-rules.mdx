Shabana Mahmood, a Labour MP, has rolled out new hard‑line immigration rules and linked them to her own story as the daughter of immigrants. She argues that too many migrants are tearing the country apart and that stricter controls will protect families. Critics say she mixes personal narrative with policy to silence opposition, and that her new measures will hurt refugees and split families.
