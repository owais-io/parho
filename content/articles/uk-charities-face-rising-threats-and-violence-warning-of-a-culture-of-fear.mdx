A rise in death, rape and other threats has turned many UK charities into targets of violence and hate. The Charity Commission says staff, volunteers and beneficiaries are being harassed online and on the streets, especially those helping women, refugees and minority faith groups. New guidance urges charities to review security and keep records private. The Commission warns that normalising this abuse could erode society’s values.
