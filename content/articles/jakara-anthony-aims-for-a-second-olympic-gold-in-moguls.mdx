Jakara Anthony, the 27‑year‑old Australian freestyle skier, is back in Italy to defend her 2022 Olympic gold in moguls. The course is steeper than usual, making the race tougher. Anthony is ranked world No.1, has won 26 World Cup titles, and has the best chance of becoming the first woman to win back‑to‑back moguls gold. She faces rivals like France’s Perrine Laffont and US stars Jaelin Kauf and Elizabeth Lemley, while the pressure of being a defending champion looms.
