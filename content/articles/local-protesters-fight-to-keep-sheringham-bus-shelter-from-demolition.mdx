In Sheringham, residents camped in a 1950s bus shelter to stop it from being torn down for safety and accessibility reasons. When bailiffs arrived at 4 am to enforce an eviction notice, protesters stood their ground and a standoff ensued. A town councillor and MP said the council’s actions were intimidating. Over 3,000 people signed a petition, and a meeting will decide the shelter’s future.
