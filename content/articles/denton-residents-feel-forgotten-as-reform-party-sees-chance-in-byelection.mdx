Denton, a suburb just 6.5 miles from Manchester, feels left behind. Residents complain about falling high‑street shops, rising council tax, bad transport and litter. The upcoming February by‑election is a chance for the Reform Party to win, especially as Labour and the Greens fight each other in nearby Gorton. Supporters hang turquoise banners and hope a local candidate will bring back services and pride to the town.
