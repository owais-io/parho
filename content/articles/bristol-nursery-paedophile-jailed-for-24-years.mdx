A 30‑year‑old nursery worker in Bristol sexually abused five children, aged two and three, and was sentenced to 24 years in prison. The judge called him “evil” and warned he could be a lifelong risk to kids. Parents had raised concerns months before the abuse was uncovered, but the nursery failed to act. The Partou group says it will improve training, policies and CCTV to protect children.
