The Justice Department has released a large, mostly redacted set of documents about its investigation into Jeffrey Epstein. The files include photos of Epstein with famous people like Michael Jackson, Bill Clinton, and Richard Branson. They also show evidence collected, but many details are hidden to protect victims and active investigations. Democrats say the release still hides too much, while Trump says it shows transparency. The law requires all unclassified files to be made public.
