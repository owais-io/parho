At the UEFA congress in Brussels, FIFA boss Gianni Infantino spoke about the ongoing ban on Russian teams and hinted that the rule might be relaxed for youth squads. He also touched on the US being a suitable co‑host for next summer’s World Cup, the political tension with former President Trump, and the controversial status of places like Greenland. The meeting also raised issues about Israeli football and the future of club competitions.
