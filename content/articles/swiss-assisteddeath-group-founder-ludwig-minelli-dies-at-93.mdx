Swiss assisted‑death group founder Ludwig Minelli died at 93 after choosing assisted death. He started Dignitas in 1998 to help people decide their own end‑of‑life. The organisation says it will keep working in his spirit. More countries are legalising assisted dying, and the UK is debating a new bill. Minelli’s work helped shape Swiss law and influenced a European Court ruling.
