Jordan Shipley, a former Texas football star and NFL receiver, was badly burned on his ranch near Burnet, Texas. He was using a machine that caught fire, and although he freed himself, he suffered third‑degree burns over more than 20% of his body. He was flown to a hospital in Austin and is in critical but stable condition. Shipley will need many surgeries and is being prayed for.
