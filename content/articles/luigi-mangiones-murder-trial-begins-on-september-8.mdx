The federal trial of Luigi Mangione, accused of shooting United HealthCare CEO Brian Thompson, will start with jury selection on September 8. Judge Margaret Garnett announced the date in Manhattan. The case has drawn strong public anger at the for‑profit health‑care industry, and Mangione has a group of supporters in court. A decision on whether the death penalty will be sought will set the next trial date, while his lawyers want to block evidence from a police search.
