Because the US‑Israel strikes on Iran keep going, oil supplies are in doubt. That has pushed gasoline up to almost $4 a gallon in the US. A top oil analyst says the price could hit $3.80‑$3.85 soon, and diesel may rise to about $5 a gallon. The conflict also hurts oil output in the Middle East, raising worries that prices could climb even higher.
