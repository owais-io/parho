Australian online‑safety chief Julie Inman Grant has denied U.S. congressman Jim Jordan’s claim that she pushes content takedowns abroad. She says her job is to protect children in Australia, not to limit American free speech, and she will send a letter to explain her view. Grant also answered questions in Canberra about a Guardian investigation into Roblox, noting the platform is mainly a game but adds social tools that must be watched.
