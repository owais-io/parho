The Reserve Bank of Australia (RBA) raised interest rates again, with the board split 5‑4. Officials say inflation is still too high because demand outstrips supply, not because of higher petrol prices. The move aims to slow consumer and business spending, but many households will feel the pinch. The RBA urges Australians to be careful with their budgets while the government prepares its budget and global tensions linger.
