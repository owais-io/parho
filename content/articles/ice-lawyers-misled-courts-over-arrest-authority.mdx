ICE lawyers told courts that a memo from last year gave them the power to arrest people after immigration hearings. In reality, the memo said nothing about those arrests. The New York Civil Liberties Union is suing ICE, saying the agency has illegally detained thousands of people who were just trying to get legal status. The lawsuit highlights how ICE keeps using wrong evidence to justify detentions.
