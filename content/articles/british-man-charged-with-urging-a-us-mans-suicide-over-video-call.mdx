A 21‑year‑old man from West Yorkshire, Dylan Phelan, is accused of encouraging a 21‑year‑old man in Louisiana to kill himself during a video call on 30 October 2024. The Crown Prosecution Service says there is enough evidence to bring the case, which is part of a public‑interest prosecution. Phelan also faces separate child‑image charges, but those are unrelated to the suicide. He will appear in Leeds court on 11 February. Family of the victim are being offered support.
