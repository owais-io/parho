Australia’s east coast power prices are expected to fall from July, thanks to more wind, solar and batteries. The regulator proposes a 1.3%‑10.1% drop for homes and up to 21.2% for small businesses, saving households over $200 and businesses over $1,300 in some regions. A new “solar sharer” plan offers free mid‑day power. Consumers are urged to shop for better deals as prices stabilize.
