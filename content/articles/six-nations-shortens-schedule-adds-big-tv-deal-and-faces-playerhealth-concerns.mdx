This year’s Six Nations tournament is shorter, cutting a rest week and tightening the calendar. Coaches worry about player fatigue as teams must play five big games in six weekends. A new £90m ITV deal will show matches on free‑air TV, helping rugby reach more fans. Talks about a global calendar could move the Six Nations forward, and balancing tradition with modern demands will decide rugby’s future.
