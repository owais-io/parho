UK Labour MP Rushanara Ali says governments must ban political donations in cryptocurrency to stop foreign interference. She will push for an amendment to the elections bill that closes loopholes letting donors hide identities with crypto wallets, split big gifts, and use privacy coins. Other MPs and groups back the move, noting that countries like Ireland, Brazil, and US states already outlaw such donations. The ban could curb foreign money in UK politics.
