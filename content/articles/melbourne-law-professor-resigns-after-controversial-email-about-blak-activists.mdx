Dr Eric Descheemaeker, a law professor at the University of Melbourne, said the school was being controlled by “Blak” activists and was heading toward destruction. After an email he sent in 2023 was leaked, the university tried to dismiss him, claiming misconduct. Descheemaeker sued, arguing it was a political‑opinion protest. The dispute was settled, and he agreed to leave the university for other opportunities.
