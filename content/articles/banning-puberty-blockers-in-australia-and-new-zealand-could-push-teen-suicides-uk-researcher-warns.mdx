A UK sociologist who studied over 100 transgender teens after the UK banned puberty blockers says similar bans in Australia and New Zealand could lead to many suicides. She points out that blocking blockers makes teens feel extreme anxiety, stress and fear. The bans were based on a poorly‑reviewed study, and experts say the drugs are safe and reversible. Many doctors and parents are now worried about the mental health of young trans people who can’t get treatment.
