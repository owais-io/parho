20‑year‑old Alysa Liu took a break from skating after the 2022 Olympics, focusing on school, hiking, and mental health. When she returned, she set no score goals—just a chance to express herself on the ice. In Milan, she nailed clean jumps, topped the podium, and earned the U.S.’s first Olympic figure‑skating gold in 24 years. Her story shows how letting go of pressure can lead to real triumph.
