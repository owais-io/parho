A Guardian report says a group called the 4 Day Week Foundation is asking the government to try a four‑day school week in England and Wales. Many teachers and parents think it would help teachers’ health and make learning easier for some children, like those with autism. But people worry about running the extra day, paying for it, and keeping lessons on track, especially for kids who need extra support.
