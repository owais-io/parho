Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth said there is no set time to end the U.S. war on Iran. He didn’t give a plan for stopping the attacks, saying President Trump will decide when to stop. The Pentagon has already hit more than 7,000 targets in Iran and is ready for a bigger strike today. It also asked Congress for extra money to keep fighting, while the war has pushed oil prices up.
