Manchester United’s new coach, Ruben Amorim, has faced a lot of criticism, but the article says the real decisions are made by the club’s owners and board. Sir Jim Ratcliffe, who owns the team, wants a back‑four and is pushing data‑driven tactics. Experienced former player Jason Wilcox is a senior adviser, but he is only following the board’s direction. The piece argues that managers now mostly present ideas, while executives decide what happens.
