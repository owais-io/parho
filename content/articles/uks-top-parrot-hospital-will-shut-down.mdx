A Swindon‑area exotic animal hospital that treats parrots, birds and other pets is closing after its owner, Vets Now, decided the business isn’t profitable. The clinic, known as Great Western Exotics, was founded by vet Dr Neil Forbes and has been a specialist centre for complex exotic‑animal care, even offering a residency program for avian vets. Owners and staff worry that no other place can replace the services, and the CMA is looking into vet‑practice takeovers.
