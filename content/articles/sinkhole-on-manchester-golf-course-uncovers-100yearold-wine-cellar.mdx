A groundsman at Davyhulme Park Golf Club in Greater Manchester dug out a small hole on the 13th tee and discovered a hidden brick cellar that had been sealed for over a century. Inside, he found dozens of empty, blackened wine bottles and a doorway that led back to a ruined 12th‑century hall. The club plans to protect the bottles and decide how best to display the historic cellar.
