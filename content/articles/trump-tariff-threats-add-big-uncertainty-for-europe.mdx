Trump is threatening to raise tariffs on imports from the EU and UK. That would make businesses nervous and slow down new investment. It could also push up prices and hurt the US economy. European leaders are worried because the uncertainty makes planning hard, especially as France and Germany face budgets and growth issues. The move could also shake US markets, even if investors stay calm for now.
