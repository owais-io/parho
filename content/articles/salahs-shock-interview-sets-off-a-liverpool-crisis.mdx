Mohamed Salah’s angry interview after the Leeds game revealed deep problems at Liverpool. He accused the club of letting him down, blamed manager Arne Slot, and hinted he might leave for Saudi Arabia. The comments upset teammates, the coaching staff, and the club’s owners. Liverpool now face a tough decision about whether to keep Slot or move on, while dealing with a costly contract and a losing season.
