In the U.S., many Iranians live in fear for loved ones back home as Iran’s government violently cracks down on protests. They report constant anxiety, internet outages, and reports of arrests and killings. Many say words and sanctions are not enough and urge the world to take stronger action—whether through sanctions, diplomatic pressure, or even military intervention—to protect civilians and end the regime’s violence.
