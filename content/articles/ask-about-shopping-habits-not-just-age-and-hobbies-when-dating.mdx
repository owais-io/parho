When dating, the article says it’s better to talk about everyday habits like how you shop, what you do at the airport, or how you react to a bad movie, rather than just age or hobbies. A study from the dating app Plenty of Fish found people now skip small‑talk and jump straight to important questions. These practical details can show if you’ll get along long‑term, but you’ll never know everything until you’re together.
