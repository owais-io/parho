Trump wants the US to take Greenland, a Danish territory, sparking anger in the House and Senate. Several Republican leaders—Senators Tillis, Bacon, McConnell—have openly warned the president that annexing the island would alienate NATO allies, hurt his legacy, and upset voters who oppose the move. They argue Greenland is a sovereign ally, not a strategic prize, and that the president should abandon the idea.
