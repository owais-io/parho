Hilary Knight helped the U.S. women's ice‑hockey team win Olympic gold, even though she was playing with a torn medial collateral ligament in her knee. She said the injury made walking and playing difficult, but she kept going thanks to a strong support team. Knight tied the game against Canada in the last minutes, and the U.S. sealed the win in overtime. She’s excited about the future, but unsure if she’ll return for the 2030 Games.
