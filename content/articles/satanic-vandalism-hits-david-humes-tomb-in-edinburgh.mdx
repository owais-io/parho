A group of vandals painted occult symbols on the tomb of philosopher David Hume and other graves at Edinburgh’s Old Calton cemetery. A tour guide discovered drawings of a naked woman with a blood‑stained knife and a baby’s noose, plus red tape with strange writing. The council said there is no CCTV and the damage was temporary, but the incident has added to a history of nightly vandalism and fights at the cemetery.
