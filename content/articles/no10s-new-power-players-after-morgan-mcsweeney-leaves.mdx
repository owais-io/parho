After Morgan McSweeney quit as prime minister’s chief of staff, two new acting chiefs – Vidhya Alakeson and Jill Cuthbertson – step in. Alakeson has a think‑tank background and a talent for business outreach, while Cuthbertson is a logistics expert who helped keep Starmer’s campaign running smoothly. Other key hires include Darren Jones as chief secretary, Amy Richards as political director, and Harvey Redgrave leading policy. Together they are reshaping Starmer’s inner circle.
