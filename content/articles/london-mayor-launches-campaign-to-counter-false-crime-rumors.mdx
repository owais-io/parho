London’s mayor Susan Langley has started a push to stop false stories that the city is full of crime. The campaign will work with banks, fund managers and other firms to spread facts about London’s safety, talent and business climate. While data shows crime rates are low, some foreign bankers and online posts have raised worries. No concrete source of the rumours has been found, but officials say it feels like an active disinformation effort.
