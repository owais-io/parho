Anthony Joshua was released from a Nigerian hospital after a car crash that killed two of his close teammates. The 36‑year‑old, who had just beaten Jake Paul, suffered minor injuries and was kept for observation. He and his mother attended the funeral of the deceased members. The accident highlights Nigeria’s high road‑accident death toll and has sparked worries about safety.
