Police in New South Wales are offering a $250,000 reward for anyone who can help locate Julian Ingram, the man suspected of killing his pregnant former partner, her boyfriend and her aunt in Lake Cargelligo. The 37‑year‑old has been missing for almost two months, and police have searched huge areas looking for his car and any clues. They say he may have support from someone in the community and urge anyone with information to come forward.
