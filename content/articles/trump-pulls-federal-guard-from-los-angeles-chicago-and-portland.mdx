Trump decided not to put federal troops in Los Angeles, Chicago and Portland. The change came after a California judge put the National Guard back under Governor Gavin Newsom’s control and after the Supreme Court said the White House could not deploy the guard in Illinois. Trump said he might bring them back later, but for now the troops are returning to their families. The move ends a controversial push to use guard troops in protest‑heavy cities.
