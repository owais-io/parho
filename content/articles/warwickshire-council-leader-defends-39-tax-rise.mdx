Warwickshire County Council leader George Finch, 19, says a 3.89% council tax increase is needed to keep the council financially viable. Senior officers warned a smaller rise would threaten stability, but Finch argues the council must balance the budget. He cites rising costs and national funding gaps, and promises savings through AI and efficiency programs. The move faces scrutiny after Reform UK’s pledge to cut taxes and waste.
