Irish Prime Minister Micheál Martin is getting ready to visit Washington for St. Patrick’s Day, but the trip is more complex than usual. With President Trump’s unpredictable style and Irish concerns about corporate tax, immigration, and U.S. actions in the Middle East, Martin must balance friendly gestures—like giving shamrocks—with addressing Irish voters’ demands for a firmer stance. The visit could also feature a golf outing, but many fear it might backfire.
