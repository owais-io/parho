New Jersey transit officials are asking Manhattan commuters to work from home for up to a month because Amtrak is upgrading the old Portal Bridge, which will cut train service and cause big delays. The $16 billion Gateway project will replace the bridge and improve tunnels, but President Trump has tried to stop funding. The slowdown could hurt 200,000 daily riders and lead to more demand on other routes.
