Fulham beat Nottingham Forest 1‑0 after Raúl Jiménez calmly converted a penalty. His 11th perfect spot‑kick keeps him joint top of the Premier League penalty record. The win puts Fulham 10 points above the relegation zone, even with several players missing. Forest struggled to attack and did not create many chances, while Fulham’s defenders and midfielder Tom Cairney helped control the game. Manager Marco Silva praised the team’s resilience.
