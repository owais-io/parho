Richard Orna, 40, an IT manager from Bristol, passed away from a brain tumour. He worked for Callisto, where he helped secure smart‑meter IT and integrated a rival company. Beyond tech, he championed brain‑tumour research, speaking on BBC Radio and raising money with friends and family. Richard was known for seeing talent in his team and treating people well. He is survived by his wife Maja, his father Jonathan, and siblings Henry and Sally.
