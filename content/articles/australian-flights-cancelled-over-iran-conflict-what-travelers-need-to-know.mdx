The Iran conflict has shut airspace across the Middle East, canceling thousands of flights that pass through hubs like Dubai, Doha, and Abu Dhabi. About 115,000 Australians are stranded, and airlines such as Emirates, Qatar Airways, and Etihad have halted routes to and from Australia. Passengers can request refunds or rebook, but waiting for the airline’s notice is safer. Cancelling a ticket yourself may cost money, and most travel insurance won’t cover travel stops.
