The United States and Israel started air strikes on Iran after killing its top leader, hitting nuclear sites and missile factories. Iran fired drones and missiles at U.S. bases in Bahrain, Qatar and Saudi Arabia, and struck the U.S. embassy in Riyadh. Flights in the Middle East were cancelled and the U.S. urged citizens to leave several Gulf countries. The war may last longer than first predicted and threaten oil traffic through the Strait of Hormuz.
