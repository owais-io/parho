Prime Minister Keir Starmer said the UK will not repeat the mistakes of the Iraq war when it comes to future military actions. He told MPs that Britain will not allow US strikes aimed at regime change in Iran and will only support defensive moves that are legal. Starmer also said the UK would protect British interests in the Middle East, but not join any offensive attacks. He warned that past mistakes could affect the region long‑term.
