The BBC’s licence‑fee charter is up for renewal, with the public consultation closing on March 10. The government wants to decide how the broadcaster will be funded, its role, and whether ads or tiered fees are allowed. Many people fear right‑wing critics could try to cut the BBC’s funding and influence its independence. It’s a chance for viewers to tell the government why the BBC matters to the nation.
