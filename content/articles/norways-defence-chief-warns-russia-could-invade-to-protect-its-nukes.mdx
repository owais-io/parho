Norway’s army chief says Russia might invade the country to safeguard its nuclear weapons near the Kola peninsula. He explains that most of Russia’s nukes are close to Norway’s border, so an attack could protect them. The chief also rebuked U.S. President Trump’s comments about Greenland and criticised his claims about NATO troops in Afghanistan. He urged a direct military hotline to avoid misunderstandings.
