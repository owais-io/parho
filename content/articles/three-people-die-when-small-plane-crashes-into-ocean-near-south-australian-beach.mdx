A small Cessna 210 crashed into the ocean off Goolwa South, South Australia, killing the 57‑year‑old pilot and two young passengers. The wreckage was hauled ashore near the Murray Mouth, and authorities are investigating. A second, unrelated crash happened in Queensland’s Normanton, but injuries are unknown. The Australian Transport Safety Bureau is looking into both incidents, urging anyone with footage to come forward.
