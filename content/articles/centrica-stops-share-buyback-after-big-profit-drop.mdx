Centrica, the company that owns British Gas, has stopped its share buyback plan after 2025 profits fell 39% to £1.42 bn. The drop comes even as the number of household customers grew by 1% to almost 8 million. CEO Chris O’Shea said pausing the buyback lets the firm invest in projects like the Sizewell C nuclear plant, Grain LNG terminal and Rough gas storage. Shares fell 8%.
