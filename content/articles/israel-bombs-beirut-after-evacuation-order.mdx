Israel launched airstrikes on the southern suburbs of Beirut after telling more than 500,000 residents to evacuate. The attack targeted what Israeli forces say are Hezbollah sites, following a flare‑up when Hezbollah fired missiles and drones into Israel. The bombing marks a sharp step up in Israel’s offensive in Lebanon, and the broader conflict now involves several other Middle Eastern countries.
