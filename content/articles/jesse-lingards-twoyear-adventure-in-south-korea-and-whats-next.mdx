Jesse Lingard spent two seasons playing for FC Seoul in South Korea, learning basic Korean and trying dishes like live octopus. He faced injuries, tough games, and big fan expectations, but he became team captain and helped younger teammates. Lingard ran over 12 km per game and scored 10 goals last season. Now he’s back home, hoping to sign with a new club in Europe or the Middle East.
