The 2026 World Economic Forum in Davos brings leaders, including former President Trump with a large U.S. delegation, amid war in Ukraine and growing tensions with China and Russia. Trump’s hard‑talk on tariffs, NATO spending and even annexing Greenland sparks conflict, while European leaders, NATO chiefs and the UN push for free trade and rule‑based order. The event also launches an AI panel and faces criticism for its climate impact and declining influence.
