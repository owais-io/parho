A 16‑year‑old protester in Kermanshah was sexually abused by riot police while being moved to jail, says a Kurdish human‑rights group. The report adds to a growing list of arrests, beatings, torture and deaths in Iran’s crackdown on protests that began in late December. Over 20,000 people have been detained and more than 3,700 have died, while an internet blackout keeps families from getting details.
