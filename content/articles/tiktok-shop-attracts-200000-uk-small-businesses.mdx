TikTok Shop, TikTok’s new shopping service, has pulled in over 200,000 small UK businesses and even major brands like Marks & Spencer and Sainsbury’s. Sales exploded on Black Friday, with 27 items sold every second, and grew 50% over last year. The platform lets creators sell products in videos and live streams, with checkout inside the app. Small sellers use TikTok to reach new customers, but the cost of constant content and pricing pressure can be tough.
