Campaigners say Chinese-made cameras guard the Magna Carta in Salisbury Cathedral and the Parthenon in Greece. They argue the firms, Dahua and Hikvision, helped China’s Uyghur crackdown and were hacked by Russia during Ukraine’s war. The World Uyghur Congress and a Ukrainian group have asked authorities to remove the cameras. Some cameras have already been taken down, but the cathedral hasn’t confirmed receiving the letters.
