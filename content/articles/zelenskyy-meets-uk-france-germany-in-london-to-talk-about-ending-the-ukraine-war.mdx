Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy will sit down with leaders from the UK, France and Germany in London to discuss a U.S.‑led peace plan that could end Russia’s war in Ukraine. The talks follow a week of U.S.–Ukrainian negotiations that ended without a deal. U.S. officials say the plan is almost finished, but neither Kyiv nor Moscow is ready to sign yet. The meeting aims to move the process forward.
