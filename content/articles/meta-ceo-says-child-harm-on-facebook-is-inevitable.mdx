In a New Mexico trial, Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg said that harm to children on Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp is inevitable because the platforms serve billions of people. He told the court that some users will always be predators and that the company is working hard to stop them, but no system can be perfect. The case also covers accusations that Meta let kids be targeted, the use of end‑to‑end encryption, and new teen‑only accounts launched in 2024.
