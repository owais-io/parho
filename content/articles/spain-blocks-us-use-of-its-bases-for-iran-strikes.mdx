Spain has denied the United States permission to use its Rota and Morón military bases for strikes against Iran. Prime Minister Pedro Sánchez and Foreign Minister José Manuel Albares said the bases can only be used for operations that comply with international law. Spain says the U.S‑Israel campaign is “unjustified and dangerous.” While a few U.S. jets have departed the bases, Spain insists they were not used for the Iranian attacks and urges a diplomatic solution.
