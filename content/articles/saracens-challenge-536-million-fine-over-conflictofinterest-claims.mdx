Saracens Rugby club, already hit with a record £5.36 million fine and relegation for breaking salary‑cap rules, says a new dispute over the fairness of the punishment is rising. The club claims the accounting firm Saffery Champness, which helped the panel that imposed the fine, was also advising other clubs that profited from Saracens’ punishment. Because the firm didn’t disclose this double role, Saracens wants its case re‑examined.
