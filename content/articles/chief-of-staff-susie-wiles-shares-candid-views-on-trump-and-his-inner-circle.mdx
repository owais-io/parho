Chief of Staff Susie Wiles said in 11 Vanity Fair interviews that she has never been more candid about Trump’s style, calling him an “alcoholic personality” and pointing out his love of revenge. She criticized Musk for shutting down USAID, called VP JD Vance a long‑time conspiracy theorist, and said the new tariffs have hurt more than expected. Wiles defended Trump while admitting she tried to keep the president from stirring up conflicts.
