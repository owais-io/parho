Gianni Infantino, FIFA president, announced a new peace prize. But the person who will set the rules is Zaw Zaw, a Myanmar football boss who was named a junta cronie and is under US and EU sanctions for human‑rights abuses. Many think Infantino is lining up with powerful leaders like Trump and Saudi Arabia to get money for FIFA. Critics worry the prize will favour Infantino and ignore real peace work.
