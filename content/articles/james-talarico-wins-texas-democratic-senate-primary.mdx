James Talarico won the Texas Democratic primary for the U.S. Senate, beating firebrand Jasmine Crockett. The race saw record turnout and big media attention, but voting confusion in Dallas caused chaos. Talarico’s win shows growing energy among young voters and a push to change politics. Republicans face a tough primary with John Cornyn versus Ken Paxton, which could open a path for Democrats in a long‑time red state.
