Trump held a rally in Pennsylvania where he said the problem of high prices is a hoax and blamed Democrats for inflation. He promised lower costs and bigger paychecks, and criticized immigrants, calling them “shithole countries” and attacking Muslim congresswoman Ilhan Omar. Trump also talked about tariffs, a $12 billion farm aid package, and said he would fight for jobs and lower prices. The speech was a mix of economic promises and harsh attacks on immigration.
