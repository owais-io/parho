Many young UK workers juggle huge student‑loan debt, high rent, and a cost‑of‑living squeeze. Because of these pressures, some cut holidays or skip pension payments. A new survey asks people under 30 how they’re saving for retirement, why they may not be contributing to a pension, and how taxes, loan repayments and rent affect their ability to save. Respondents can share worries about future security.
