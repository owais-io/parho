Thousands of homes in Hastings could run out of water during Christmas because a main pipe burst. Southern Water says the loss might start after Christmas Eve, with bottled water being sent to the most vulnerable. Residents are told not to use extra water or hoard supplies. The town has had past outages, and the MP is pressing the company to act fast and protect the community.
