During a CBS Evening News interview, Donald Trump claimed that if Kamala Harris had won the 2024 election, Tony Dokoupil would have lost his anchor job. Trump blamed a “dead country” and warned the pay could be lower. Dokoupil replied that he would still have the job even under a Harris administration. The exchange happened on CBS, now owned by the Ellison family, which has faced editing controversies and political pressure.
