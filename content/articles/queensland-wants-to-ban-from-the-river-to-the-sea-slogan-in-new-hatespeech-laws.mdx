Queensland is set to add the phrase “from the river to the sea” to a list of banned hate‑speech words. The move comes after a 2023 terror attack on a Hanukkah service. The new laws make it illegal to use certain slogans or symbols that could incite hate, with punishments up to two years in prison. The bill also adds tougher penalties for attacking or harassing people at religious events. Jewish groups say it will make them feel safer.
