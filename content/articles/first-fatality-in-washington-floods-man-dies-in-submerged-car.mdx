A 33‑year‑old man died after driving into a flooded ditch near Seattle, the first death reported in the Washington floods. The state has already carried out over 600 rescues and thousands of evacuations because of heavy rain and swollen rivers. Governor Ferguson warned residents to obey evacuation orders and pledged state funds to help with hotels and groceries. The storm is expected to keep flooding until at least Wednesday, and power outages and mudslides continue.
