Israel President Isaac Herzog’s visit to Australia triggered protests because he is accused of supporting the Gaza war that killed my family. Seven relatives died, and I filed a formal complaint to the government, but got no reply. When I joined a peaceful protest, police used batons on demonstrators. The story highlights how the state’s actions ignore victims and undermine Australia’s commitment to human rights.
