Police are looking for 85‑year‑old Chris Baghsarian, who went missing after being taken in a car from his Sydney home. A raid on a derelict house in Dural uncovered evidence that he may have been held there last week. The family says he was mistaken for someone else and that no ransom was demanded. Officers say hope is fading but they are still working hard to find him alive.
