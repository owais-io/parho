A federal judge on Monday stopped the US Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP) after finding its members were appointed illegally. The decision freezes all vaccine guidance issued in the last year and bars the 13 members chosen by Robert F. Kennedy Jr. from serving. The committee still exists, but officials plan to recruit new members while no appeal has been filed, leaving doctors and the public confused about future vaccine recommendations.
