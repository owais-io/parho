Health Secretary Wes Streeting warned that the US raid that captured Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro shows the rules‑based world order is breaking down, which worries Britain. He says a world without rules is unsafe and urges Europe to step up its defense. Streeting defended Prime Minister Keir Starmer for not openly criticising President Trump, who called the raid a “brilliant operation.” The comments come as Maduro faces drug and weapons charges in New York.
