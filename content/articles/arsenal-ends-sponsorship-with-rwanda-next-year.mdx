Arsenal said it will end its partnership with Visit Rwanda in June. The deal, worth about £10 m a year, began in 2018 but drew criticism because Rwanda supports the M23 militia fighting in the Democratic Republic of Congo. Fans protested the logo on the kit, while Arsenal said the partnership helped promote tourism and football in Rwanda. Other clubs, like PSG, keep the sponsor global.
