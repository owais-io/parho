New global survey shows about a third of Gen Z men think women should obey husbands and not initiate sex. Only 20 % think women should never talk first in relationships. The study points to growing inequality and job stress pushing young men to resent women. Social media and the manosphere spread bigotry. While money matters, attitudes will only shift if jobs, wages, and fair living standards improve, plus a cultural shift in how men see themselves.
