Arsenal were 1-1 with Chelsea at Stamford Bridge after a late equaliser from Mikel Merino. Chelsea had taken the lead through Trevoh Chalobah, but were reduced to 10 men when Moisés Caicedo was sent off for a nasty tackle on Merino. Arsenal struggled with the injury of William Saliba but still managed to hold on for a point and stay close to the top of the table.
