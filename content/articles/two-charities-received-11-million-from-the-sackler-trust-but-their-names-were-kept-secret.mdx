The Sackler Trust, funded by the family behind OxyContin, gave more than £1.1 million to two charities whose names were omitted from its 2024 accounts. It donated £3.8 million to arts and education groups that year, with Veterans Aid and the Belvoir Cricket and Countryside Trust each receiving £250,000. Many major arts bodies have cut ties with the Sacklers because of their role in the opioid crisis. The trust, chaired by Dame Theresa Sackler, resumed giving in 2020 after a 2019 pause.
