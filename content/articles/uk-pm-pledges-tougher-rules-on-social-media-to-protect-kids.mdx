UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said he will push for tougher limits on social media to keep children safe after a U.S. court found Meta and YouTube negligent for designing addictive tech. He is looking at measures like banning users under 16 and tightening platform features. The ruling awarded $6 million in damages to a woman who said she was hooked on social media as a child. Tech giants will appeal.
