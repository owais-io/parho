Economy minister Robert Habeck says Donald Trump’s re‑election has made everything a political weapon. He cites the energy crisis after Russia invaded Ukraine, showing how gas can be weaponised, and notes that now tariffs, tech and everyday goods can be used similarly. Habeck talks about Germany’s push for LNG terminals, the nuclear debate, and the shift to heat pumps and electric cars. He also notes the Greens lost the coalition but won a state election, and Germany is still pushing clean energy.
