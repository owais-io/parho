Swedish navy sees Russian submarines in the Baltic almost every week, chief Marko Petkovic said. Moscow is quietly adding more subs and modernising them, he warned. Sweden is tightening defenses, hosting NATO anti‑submarine drills and running Operation Baltic Sentry, which has seen no cable attacks yet. Other worries include drones, sabotage and Russia’s shadow‑fleet oil tankers that could carry drones. The alliance’s cooperation gives Sweden more confidence.
