High pressure keeps the western U.S. in a heatwave, with Phoenix and Denver breaking March records and temperatures staying well above normal. By Thursday the heat could spread east. In Canada, a surge of Arctic air is dumping cold, dropping many cities below freezing. Meanwhile, the Canary Islands faced Storm Therese, prompting a state of emergency, flight cancellations and school closures after heavy rain and wind.
