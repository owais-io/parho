National Car Parks (NCP) has been put into administration, putting nearly 700 staff jobs in jeopardy. PwC was hired to keep the car‑park business running while it explores options, including a sale. The company struggled after Covid hit parking demand, and its high rent and energy costs left it deeply in debt. For now, parks stay open and workers stay on the payroll, but some sites could close.
