A new set of documents released by the U.S. Justice Department contains thousands of photos that show former financier Jeffrey Epstein and his associate Ghislaine Maxwell rubbing elbows with high‑profile people. The images feature royalty such as Prince Andrew, former U.S. President Bill Clinton, and celebrities like Michael Jackson, Diana Ross, Mick Jagger and actor Kevin Spacey. None of the pictured figures has been accused of wrongdoing, and most knew Epstein only before his criminal cases.
