The cost of childcare for kids under two in England fell 39 % last year thanks to extra government money. Parents can now claim 30 free hours a week, saving up to £8,000 a year. But nurseries still charge for meals, trips and holidays, and low‑income families often miss those free hours until the child is three. This means some kids aren’t school‑ready when they turn five. Labour wants to end these gaps, but needs more funding.
