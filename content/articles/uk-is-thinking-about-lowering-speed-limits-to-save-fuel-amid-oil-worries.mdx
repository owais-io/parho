The UK government is studying ways to cut fuel use, including possibly lowering speed limits, as tensions in the Middle East threaten oil supplies. Officials say the country still has enough fuel now, but they’re preparing emergency plans that could limit how fast cars drive and when they can travel. The aim is to reduce demand before prices rise or supplies drop.
