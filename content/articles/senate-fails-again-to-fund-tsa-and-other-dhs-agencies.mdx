The Senate voted 53‑47, missing the 60 votes needed, and once again stalled a bill that would partially fund the Department of Homeland Security. President Trump ordered the Treasury to pay TSA agents immediately, but lawmakers remain deadlocked over how much money to give to the TSA, Coast Guard, FEMA and other DHS parts, while Democrats push for new immigration‑enforcement rules. The shutdown has left many TSA workers unpaid and waiting times at airports rising.
