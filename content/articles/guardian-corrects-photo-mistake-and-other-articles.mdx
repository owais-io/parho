The Guardian issued corrections for several recent stories. A photo used in a Thursday business piece was wrongly captioned – the shopper in the picture was standing in front of a fake street scene on Oxford Street. Other corrections covered the House of Lords' push for an Australian‑style social media ban for under‑16s, Australia’s silence on Chinese boat arrivals, and a six‑month ban on Iranian tourists with valid visas. Readers can send complaints to the Guardian’s editorial office.
