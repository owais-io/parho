Donald Trump’s special envoy is secretly talking to Iran’s foreign minister and other officials, while Pakistan and Oman also mediate. Trump says the talks are “productive” and he may stop plans to bomb Iran’s energy plants. Iran’s officials deny any talks have happened, calling Trump’s claims fake news. The talks aim to ease the conflict, keep the Strait of Hormuz open, and prevent more war. Nothing is decided yet, so the fight’s future remains uncertain.
