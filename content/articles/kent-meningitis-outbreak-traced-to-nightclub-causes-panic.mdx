Kent hospitals are treating dozens of people with meningitis after a club in Canterbury, Club Chemistry, is blamed for a sudden outbreak. Twenty‑nine cases have been confirmed, with two young people dead and over 9,800 people given antibiotics or vaccines. The UK health agency warned students and staff to seek help fast, but many felt the university was slow to act. The outbreak has left students scared and many left campus early.
