Michael Carrick, the new interim boss, has already won two games for Manchester United. He keeps things simple, using a smaller squad and focusing on quick passing and teamwork. Fans are excited after the victories at City and Arsenal. Carrick has relied on key players like Bruno Fernandes, Casemiro, and Matheus Cunha, and has tried new roles for some players. The next matches will test whether this basic approach can keep the team winning.
