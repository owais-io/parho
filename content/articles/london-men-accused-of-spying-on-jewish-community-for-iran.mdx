Two men in London have been charged with spying for Iran. The men, Nematollah Shahsavani (40) and Alireza Farasati (22), are accused of collecting information about Jewish community sites and people in North London between July and August last year. Police and the Crown Prosecution Service say the offence falls under the National Security Act. They will appear at Westminster magistrates court next week. The police say the investigation shows they will protect the community.
