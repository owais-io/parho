In Belfast, a black woman named Maureen Hamblin shares how talking with strangers in a community group, the Circle of Change, helped her feel less isolated. The circle brings together people of different backgrounds to meet, swap stories, and raise money for tolerance projects. Members listen to each other's experiences of racism, learning that many share the same city and want a friendly home. The group says these chats give hope and break harmful stereotypes.
