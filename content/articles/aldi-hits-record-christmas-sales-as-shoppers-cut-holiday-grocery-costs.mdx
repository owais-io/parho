Aldi's UK stores sold a record £1.65 bn during the four weeks up to 24 Dec, up 3 % from last year. The last week alone saw a 5 % jump and the chain ran over 57 m transactions – its busiest period ever. Shoppers bought more than 5.5 m bottles of fizz, 56 m potatoes and 37 m carrots. Aldi now has 1,081 stores, aiming for 1,500. Lidl also posted a record‑breaking Christmas, with a 10 % rise to £1.1 bn.
