If you’re locked out, you might see a locksmith advertising a low price, like £49. These offers are often bait‑and‑switch scams. The workers drill the lock, replace it, and then bill you 10 times more. Legitimate locksmiths ask questions, show ID, and accept card payments. Check for the MLA logo, ask what the price covers, and be wary of ads that say “police recommended.”
