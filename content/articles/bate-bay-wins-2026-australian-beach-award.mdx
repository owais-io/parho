Bate Bay in the Sutherland shire has been named the best beach in Australia for 2026. Tourism Australia’s judge, Brad Farmer, used a 130‑point checklist that looks at looks, safety, parking, access and the beach’s vibe. Local leaders say the win will bring more visitors and shine a light on the area’s natural beauty, Indigenous roots and the need to protect the coast for future generations.
