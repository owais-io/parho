In the New Year Honours list, several TV, sports and comedy figures were recognised. Sir Roy Clarke, the creator of long‑running sitcoms, received a knighthood. Actress Meera Syal was made a dame for breaking barriers on TV. Paul Elliott earned an MBE for ChuckleVision. Sports broadcaster Gabby Logan and former football commentator Clive Tyldesley got OBEs. Other honours went to Phil Spencer, Sally Lindsay, Matt Lucas, Bill Bailey, Richard Osman, and two BBC executives.
