Dame Judi Dench has spoken out to keep London’s parks and green spaces safe from new building projects. She’s worried that over 50 parks, fields and reserves could be lost, including Whitewebbs Park, Wimbledon Park and Green Dale Fields. The actor, who loves planting trees, joins countryside groups demanding stronger legal protection and has called the government’s “grey belt” policy a threat to public land.
