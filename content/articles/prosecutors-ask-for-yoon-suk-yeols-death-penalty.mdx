South Korean prosecutors are seeking the death penalty for former president Yoon Suk Yeol, accusing him of staging a December 2024 coup by declaring martial law and deploying troops to block parliament. The move was stopped, and Yoon was impeached and removed from office. Prosecutors say Yoon planned to seize power long‑term and has shown no remorse. This case is part of a larger series of charges against him.
