Arsenal beat Aston Villa 4‑1 at the Emirates, scoring early and then piling on more goals in the second half. With the win they move closer to the Premier League title and put the former manager Emile's era behind them. Arteta’s team showed confidence, while Villa struggled after losing their first goal. Injuries are a concern, but Arsenal’s strong defence and attack keep them in charge.
