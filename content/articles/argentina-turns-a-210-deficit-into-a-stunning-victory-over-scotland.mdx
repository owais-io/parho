In a Test match at Murrayfield, Argentina trailed Scotland 21‑0 after a strong first half. A costly error by Scotland’s fly‑half Finn Russell sparked a shift. Argentina’s Santi Carreras led a furious rush, scoring five tries in the last 23 minutes. The Pumas capped the comeback with a winning try and won 31‑24, shocking the home crowd in this unforgettable match.
