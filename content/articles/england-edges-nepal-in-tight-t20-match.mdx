In a tense T20 World Cup game in Mumbai, England beat Nepal by just four runs. Sam Curran delivered the final over, conceding only five runs and keeping the pressure high. He said close wins give teams confidence and praised the rising quality of associate nations. With this win, England feels more confident ahead of their next match against the West Indies.
