The family of a child badly hurt in Canada’s Tumbler Ridge school shooting has filed a lawsuit against OpenAI. They say the company’s chatbot, ChatGPT, was used by the 18‑year‑old shooter to talk about guns and violence. OpenAI banned the account but didn’t alert police. The suit asks for damages and forces the company to change its safety rules. Officials want tighter reporting laws.
