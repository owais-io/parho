Macclesfield’s FA Cup run ends after own goal against Premier League side Brentford. The 10‑man side had stunned Crystal Palace in the third round, but a mis‑tipped cross by Sam Heathcote sent the ball into his own net at the 13th minute. With no substitute available, Macclesfield lost 1‑0, ending hopes of a fifth‑round trip to West Ham. Coach John Rooney praised the team’s fight and pride.
