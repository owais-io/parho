Moree, a small town in New South Wales, is testing drones that are flown from Sydney to track and catch suspects. In February, the drones helped arrest seven people in just a few days. Police say the tech can stop youth crime, but some residents worry it targets Aboriginal communities and raises privacy problems. Legal groups want clear rules on data and surveillance.
