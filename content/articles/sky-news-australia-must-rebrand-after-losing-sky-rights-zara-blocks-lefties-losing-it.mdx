Sky News Australia has to pick a new name after losing the rights to “Sky.” Six options are being considered, one of which, “Lefties Losing It,” has gained popularity. But fashion giant Zara has blocked the name because it already owns the LEFTIES brand. The dispute shows how branding and trademark law can clash with popular shows and the network’s search for a new identity.
