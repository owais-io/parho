Stuttgart has broken into the Bundesliga's top four after a hard‑won victory over Köln. Coach Sebastian Hoeness has guided the team to surprise wins against big clubs, but key player Deniss Undav is approaching the end of his contract and could leave next season. The club plans to offer a shorter extension, while fans hope Stuttgart can keep growing and become a lasting top‑team in Germany. Stuttgart’s rise proves they can beat bigger clubs.
