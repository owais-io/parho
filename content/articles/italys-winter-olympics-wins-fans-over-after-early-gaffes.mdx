After a rocky opening and a TV commentator mix‑up, Italy’s Winter Olympics in Milan and Cortina captured the nation’s heart. Over 1.2 million tickets were sold, and TV viewership hit almost five million for key moments. Despite protests against security staff and US officials, the Games ran smoothly and Italy won a record 26 medals. The event is seen as a boost for the country’s economy and image.
