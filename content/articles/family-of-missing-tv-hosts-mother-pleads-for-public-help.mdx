TV host Savannah Guthrie is asking anyone who might know about her 84‑year‑old mother, Nancy, to contact police. Nancy went missing on Jan. 31 near Tucson and police have found no suspects or the ransom note that demanded $6 million. Detectives are still searching the house and the family says they are desperate for any clues. The FBI says it hasn’t heard from the kidnappers, and the sheriff’s office has been silent.
