Nick Sortor, a 27‑year‑old right‑wing influencer with a history of DUIs and a misdemeanor, has built a following of 1.2 million on X by posting violent videos that praise ICE and attack protestors. He’s received praise from the Trump administration, even after an arrest in Portland, and has claimed he drove past protesters in Minneapolis. The Guardian says his content helps push the administration’s hard‑line policies.
