Keir Starmer’s decision to name former trade minister Peter Mandelson as the UK’s ambassador to the United States sparked controversy because of Mandelson’s links to Jeffrey Epstein. Cabinet Office minister Nick Thomas‑Symonds was suddenly called into a Thursday media round, even though he’d been out in Wales. With only a brief note, he had to improvise on the spot. The piece satirises the rushed vetting and the minister’s scramble to defend the appointment.
