Prime Minister Keir Starmer wants Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor, the former prince stripped of his titles, to cooperate with a U.S. congressional inquiry into Jeffrey Epstein. Mountbatten‑Windsor hasn’t answered the committee’s request for an interview. Starmer says anyone with relevant information should share it, even if linked to child‑sex cases. The committee, led by Republicans, wants the former prince to testify but may not be able to force him.
