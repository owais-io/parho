Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem claims the 29‑year‑old Afghan asylum seeker who shot a National Guard member in Washington, D.C. was radicalized after arriving in the U.S. She blames President Biden’s administration for weak vetting and says Trump’s pause on new asylum applications was wrong. Noem also defended sending Venezuelan immigrants back to El Salvador on planes that a judge ordered to be stopped, calling the judge “activist.”
