Rio Tinto and Glencore have restarted talks about merging into the world’s largest mining company, worth more than $260 bn. The deal could see Rio Tinto buying Glencore, but the companies say no offers are made yet and the terms are still uncertain. The move follows recent mergers in the mining world and comes amid high copper prices and a push for industry consolidation.
