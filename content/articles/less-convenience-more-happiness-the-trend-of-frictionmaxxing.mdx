Some people are saying that being less convenient can actually make us happier. “Friction‑maxxing” is a new buzzword for doing things the hard way so you grow stronger and more resilient. The idea comes from a 2026 article in The Cut that criticises gadgets like Uber Eats and ChatGPT for making life too easy. Instead, the trend encourages kids to run small errands and adults to accept a little mess, believing that the struggle builds character and satisfaction.
