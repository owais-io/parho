Jasmine Crockett, the Democratic U.S. Representative from Dallas, has officially entered the race for Texas’s open U.S. Senate seat. She aims to unseat Republican incumbent John Cornyn in a state that leans heavily Republican. The primary will be on March 3, with the general election next November. Her decision follows former Rep. Colin Allred’s withdrawal. If successful, this seat could help Democrats regain a Senate majority.
