Trump began his Davos visit with a fiery speech, attacking European leaders and demanding Greenland. He threatened to hit several allied countries with tariffs. Hours later he backed off, saying he had a “future deal” with Denmark that would give the US limited control over Greenland. European politicians and economists warned that this move could strain relations.
