Anthony Joshua, a former world heavyweight champion, is set to fight Jake Paul, a social‑media star with only 13 pro fights. The match has drawn criticism because Paul is inexperienced and lighter, while Joshua’s legacy is at stake. Some say Paul could bring new fans, but others worry the fight risks damaging the sport’s credibility and could hurt both fighters. The Guardian article compares it to historic mismatches and argues the match is too uneven.
