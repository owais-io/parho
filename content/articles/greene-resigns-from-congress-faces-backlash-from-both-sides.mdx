Greene unexpectedly announced she was quitting her House seat, saying she didn’t want to be a “battered wife” of Trump and was unhappy with GOP decisions. AOC slammed her timing and voting record. Trump also called her a “traitor” but said he’d like to see her back in politics. Other politicians and a Parkland gun‑control activist gave mixed responses, showing the split reaction to her exit.
