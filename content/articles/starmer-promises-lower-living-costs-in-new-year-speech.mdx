Starmer will give a New Year speech to promise lower living costs, saying he will cut energy bills, lower interest rates and end the two‑child benefit cap. He hopes voters and his own MPs will feel better by 2026. To build support, he’s inviting Labour MPs to informal dinners at Chequers. The plan has mixed reception—some MPs are unhappy. He also plans a warm‑homes bill to cut bills with solar panels and batteries.
