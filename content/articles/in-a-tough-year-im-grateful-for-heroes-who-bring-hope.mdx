After a year of violence, the author reflects on moments of courage that bring hope. He highlights a Sydney synagogue shooting where bystanders, like Ahmed al‑Ahmed, fought the attackers, saving lives. He mentions other brave acts—from a Sudanese city to Gaza, Ukraine, and even ordinary people standing up to powerful forces. The piece reminds readers that courage exists everywhere, even when life feels risky, and gives the author’s own gratitude for these heroes.
