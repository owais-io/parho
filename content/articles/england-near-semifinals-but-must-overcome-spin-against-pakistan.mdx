England have won four of five games and are just one win away from a World Cup semi‑final spot. Their next match is against Pakistan, who have bowled almost all of their overs with spinners. England has struggled against spin, losing many wickets to spin bowlers. The match will test England’s batting and team selection, as they look to secure their place in the final stages.
