After a federal ICE officer shot and killed Alex Pretti in Minneapolis, President Trump is under fire. The city and state are calling for fewer federal agents, and a judge will decide whether to end the ICE surge. Congress, both parties, are asking for an investigation, and Trump’s team has softened its tone. The incident shows the growing backlash against the administration’s immigration tactics.
