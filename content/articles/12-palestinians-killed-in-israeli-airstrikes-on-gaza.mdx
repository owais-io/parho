Israel said it carried out airstrikes on Gaza because Hamas was breaking a cease‑fire. The strikes hit a tent in Jabaliya, a site in Khan Younis, Gaza City and Beit Lahia, killing at least 12 Palestinians and injuring many more. Hospitals received the bodies and mourners gathered. The killings come as the U.S. offers a peace plan and $5 billion for rebuilding, while both sides accuse each other of violating the truce.
