Three Democratic lawmakers from Minnesota, including Rep. Ilhan Omar, were allowed into an ICE detention center near Minneapolis but were told to leave after about 10 minutes. They say the move blocks their oversight duties, even though a judge has ruled Congress can visit such sites without notice. The incident happened amid protests over the death of Renee Good by an ICE agent. The lawmakers claim the denial was illegal.
