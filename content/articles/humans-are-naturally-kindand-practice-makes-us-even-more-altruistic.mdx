Studies show that, over time, violence has fallen and most people are generous. Our brains are wired to help others, especially when we feel their fear. Small acts of courage, like Ahmed al‑Ahmed stopping a shooter, inspire awe and spark kindness. Practicing empathy—through meditation or programs like Roots of Empathy—can strengthen our compassion and make us kinder.
