The Green Party won the Gorton and Denton by‑election, taking its first seat north of Herefordshire and knocking Labour’s large majority in the area. 47 % of voters turned out – almost the same level as the 2024 general election – and the Greens secured a 4,402‑vote lead. The win sparked jubilant celebrations, with the candidate giving a tearful speech and supporters throwing a midnight party. Labour and the Reform Party tried to defend, but the result marks a significant shift.
