Donald Trump pardoned Texas congressman Henry Cuellar and his wife after they faced bribery charges. A few days later, Cuellar said he would keep running as a Democrat. Trump slammed him on Truth Social, calling the congressman “unloyal” and saying he was targeted for being critical of his party’s immigration stance. The incident comes amid new Texas district maps that favor Republicans and a Supreme Court ruling that lets those maps take effect.
