Many people want a drink that feels like a real beer but without alcohol, especially after a week of not drinking. The article explains how alcohol‑free beer has come a long way since the first one in 1979. New brewing tricks let small breweries make low‑ABV beers that still have flavor and mouthfeel. Some even add ingredients to make you feel relaxed. However, these beers can spoil if they’re not kept clean, so cans or bottles are best.
