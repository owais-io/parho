Joseph Parker says he didn’t take any banned drugs after a routine test on the day he lost to Fabio Wardley. The test, run by the Voluntary Anti‑Doping Agency, came back positive and Parker is cooperating while an investigation looks into what happened. He insists the result is a mistake and says he will explain everything once the inquiry finishes. The fight left Parker out of the world‑champion shot, and the outcome could affect his future fights.
