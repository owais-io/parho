CBS News announced it will cut about 6 % of its 1,100‑person staff, or roughly 60 jobs, and shut down its long‑running radio network. The decision, driven by changing media habits and cost pressures, comes after earlier layoffs and as the company prepares for a big merger with Warner‑Bros Discovery. Employees were told by the end of the day and have expressed unease, while executives promised support.
