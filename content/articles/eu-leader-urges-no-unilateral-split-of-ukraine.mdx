EU chief Ursula von der Leyen warned that no country can split Ukraine or change its borders by force as talks with the U.S. continue. She said Russia still shows no real willingness to end the war and pushed for stronger pressure. EU leaders are working on using frozen Russian assets to help fund Ukraine, while also urging the return of children taken to Russia. The aim is a lasting peace.
