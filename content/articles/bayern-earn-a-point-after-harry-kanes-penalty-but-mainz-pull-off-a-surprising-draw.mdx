Mainz held Bayern to a point after a last‑minute penalty from Harry Kane, a surprise at the Allianz Arena. The struggling club had the lowest possession in the league and a weak passing game, yet they kept Bayern from full points for the first time this season. Under new coach Urs Fischer, Mainz held a 1‑0 lead before Kane’s penalty evened the score, proving their fighting spirit despite tough odds.
