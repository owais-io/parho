Kushner’s company pulled out of a planned takeover of Warner Bros Discovery after scrutiny. Affinity Partners, set up by the Trump family’s son‑in‑law, had backed Paramount Skydance’s $108 bn bid. The media giant now reviews the offer. Kushner’s ties drew attention, and President Trump has tried to distance himself from the deal. Paramount still plans to offer $82.7 bn for WBD’s key assets.
