This piece invites readers to share personal stories about how AI chatbots, like ChatGPT, have helped them cope with difficult moments. With over 800 million users, these bots are increasingly used for companionship and mental‑health support. The Guardian asks for experiences and offers a form for submissions, also providing links to terms and privacy. It encourages honest sharing and respects privacy.
