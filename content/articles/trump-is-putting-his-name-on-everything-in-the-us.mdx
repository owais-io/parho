Donald Trump keeps using the presidency to boost his personal brand. From renaming the Kennedy Center to adding his name to a new warship class, a $1 coin, and even a navy website, he’s turning public landmarks into marketing tools. Critics say this erases historical names and shows a reckless use of power. Trump’s moves mirror past presidents’ branding tricks, but many argue it’s a dangerous self‑promotion.
