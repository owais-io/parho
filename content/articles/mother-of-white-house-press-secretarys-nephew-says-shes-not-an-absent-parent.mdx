Bruna Ferreira, a Brazilian‑born mother of the White House press secretary’s nephew, was detained by ICE last November. She says the White House wrongly called her an absent parent, insisting she visits, feeds, and supports her 11‑year‑old son. Ferreira was released on a $1,500 bond after an immigration judge ruled she posed no threat. Her lawyers say she has no criminal record, and DHS admits no clear evidence of a prior arrest.
