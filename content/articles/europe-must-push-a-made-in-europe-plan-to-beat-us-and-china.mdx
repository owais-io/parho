European leaders are worried that America and China are pulling ahead in technology and trade. A summit in Belgium will discuss a new plan called ‘Made in Europe’ that would give European companies a boost. The idea is to buy and produce more inside the EU, especially in green energy and tech, to keep jobs and help the single market stay strong. Some worry it could hurt free trade, but many think it’s worth a try.
