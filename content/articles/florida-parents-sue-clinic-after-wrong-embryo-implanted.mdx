After a Florida couple discovered their newborn daughter, Shea, was not genetically related to them, they sued the Fertility Center of Orlando. The couple says the clinic implanted the wrong embryo. A genetic test confirmed no link to the parents, and they are now seeking the child's biological family and the missing embryos. They have also launched a fundraiser, while the clinic says it is working with investigators.
