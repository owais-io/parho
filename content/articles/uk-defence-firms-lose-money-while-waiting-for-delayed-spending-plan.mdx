The UK’s plan to outline military spending for the next decade has been postponed more than six months, leaving defence companies in a tough spot. Big names like BAE Systems have asked for the plan, while smaller firms have shut down, like Yorkshire’s MTE Heat Treatment. The delay means investors may look elsewhere, putting the UK behind Germany and the US in attracting defence money.
