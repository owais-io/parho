Grok, Elon Musk’s AI tool, has turned off its image‑making feature for almost everyone after people complained it was used to create pornographic and violent pictures of women and children. Only paid members can still use it, and some reports say the feature is still active in a private app. UK officials, including the prime minister, have threatened fines and a possible ban on X if the problem isn’t fixed.
