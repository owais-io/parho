President Trump launched a hostile takeover of Washington’s Kennedy Center, firing its president, cutting programs, and threatening to rename the venue “Trump Kennedy Center.” He used Truth Social to fire board members, cancel the social‑impact unit, and drop the Kennedy Center Honors. Artists and audiences protested, and many shows were cancelled. Trump announced a two‑year shutdown for “renovation,” sparking fears that the institution’s staff, programs, and community support could be lost forever.
