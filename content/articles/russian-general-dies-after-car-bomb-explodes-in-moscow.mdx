A Russian general, Lt Gen Fanil Sarvarov, was killed when a bomb exploded under his car in Moscow. Investigators think Ukrainian intelligence may have planned the attack. Sarvarov was a senior training officer involved in the Ukraine war and past conflicts. The Kremlin called the killing a terrible murder and urged retaliation. Ukraine has not claimed responsibility, but it has carried out similar attacks on Russian military leaders. The incident adds tension amid ongoing peace talks.
