S‑RM is a UK cyber‑response team that can join a call within minutes of a hack. They focus on stopping attackers from spreading malware, cutting off damage, and giving clear advice. They also guide companies on whether to pay a ransom, usually steering them away from it. By studying how ransomware groups behave, they provide useful facts for decision‑making. The government’s cyber centre now works closely with firms to warn and support them during attacks.
