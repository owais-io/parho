The AfD says it will support a nationalist revival in Europe after Trump said Europe faces “civilisational erasure.” The party’s spokesperson said it will meet with US Republicans, and even a New York Republican club invited him. Trump’s strategy said the EU is weak and that right‑wing parties should resist. While the AfD is eager to team up with Trump, other European nationalist parties are more cautious, knowing Trump is unpopular in Europe.
