Mehdi Mahmoudian, a co‑writer of the Oscar‑nominated film “It Was Just an Accident,” was taken into custody in Tehran after signing a statement blaming Iran’s supreme leader for recent bloodshed. The arrest came weeks before the Oscars and adds to a wave of crackdowns after protests. Mahmoudian is a human‑rights activist and former political prisoner who helped write the film’s story about former inmates seeking revenge.
