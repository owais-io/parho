Many companies reward you when you send a friend or family member to sign up. Octopus Energy gives £50 credit to both you and the new customer. Banks like Lloyds and Monzo offer £50 or variable rewards, while telecoms and broadband firms give cash or discounts. Fitness clubs and retailers also give perks. The deals can help you save or earn, but you should read the fine print and make sure the offer is right for you.
