A record crop of potatoes in Germany has left farmers with a huge surplus, so a Berlin newspaper and the green search‑engine Ecosia set up 174 free‑pick‑up spots around the city. Residents, charities and even the zoo can grab sacks of spuds, while two lorries of potatoes are heading to Ukraine. The move cuts waste, helps people on tight budgets, and gives the tuber a festive boost.
