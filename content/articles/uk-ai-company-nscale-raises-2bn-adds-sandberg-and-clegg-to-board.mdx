Nscale, a UK‑based AI data‑centre firm, just pulled in $2bn (£1.5bn) from investors, boosting its value to about $15bn. The funding round also brought former Meta chief Sheryl Sandberg and ex‑UK deputy prime minister Nick Clegg onto the board. The company plans to build a super‑computer in Loughton as part of the government’s push to make the UK a hub for AI.
