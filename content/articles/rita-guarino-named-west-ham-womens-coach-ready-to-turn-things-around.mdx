Rita Guarino has been announced as the new head coach of West Ham’s women’s team, signing a contract until 2027. The 54‑year‑old, who won four Serie A titles with Juventus, is excited to join a club with strong values and help the side climb out of the bottom of the WSL. She’ll take charge next month against Chelsea and will keep assistant Steve Kirby with her. West Ham currently sit 11th with one win and two draws.
