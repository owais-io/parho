Senator David Pocock says Israeli bulldozers in Gaza have likely moved or damaged the graves of 146 of the 263 Australian soldiers buried there. Photos show headstones ripped, earth displaced, and boundary walls torn down. Australian families are outraged and want an apology and reparations. Israel says the work was defensive, digging to destroy a tunnel, but no plan to fix the graves yet. The government is urging Israel to stop further damage.
