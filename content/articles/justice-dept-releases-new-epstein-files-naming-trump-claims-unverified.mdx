The U.S. Justice Department released more documents from the Jeffrey Epstein investigation that include FBI notes about a woman who says she was sexually abused by Epstein and Trump in the 1980s. The claims have not been proven and no charges were filed. Trump denies any wrongdoing. The release follows criticism that the department had hidden some files for years. The documents were only released after being mistakenly labeled duplicates.
