Ukraine skeleton racer Vladyslav Heraskevych tried to wear a helmet that honors the 600 Ukrainian athletes and coaches killed by Russian attacks. The International Olympic Committee said it was a political statement and banned him from competing. Heraskevych stood firm, saying the helmet was a tribute, not a protest. The ban caused a PR fight, and the IOC struggled to balance rules and the war’s impact.
