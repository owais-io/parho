Starmer’s chief of staff, Morgan McSweeney, quit after admitting he wrongly advised the prime minister to appoint former minister Peter Mandelson, who has links to Jeffrey Epstein, as ambassador to Washington. The resignation has sparked a leadership crisis in the Labour party, with rivals eyeing a challenge and some calling for Starmer to step down. McSweeney’s departure leaves the PM exposed as he faces policy, election, and scrutiny battles.
