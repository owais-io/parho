South Yorkshire’s mayor, Oliver Coppard, announced a £1.5 billion plan called the “People’s Network” that will put trams, buses and bike‑hire schemes under local public control. The package adds 25 new electric trams, 186 zero‑emission buses and a fleet of electric buses, and will rebrand bike hire services. The aim is cheaper, greener travel and better links to jobs, with plans to keep the airport open and improve rail connections.
