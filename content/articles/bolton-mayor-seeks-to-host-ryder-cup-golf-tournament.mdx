Andy Burnham, mayor of Greater Manchester, wants the Ryder Cup golf tournament in Bolton for the first time in decades. It would be held on a new 18‑hole course at Hulton Park, which isn’t built yet. Local residents and conservationists worry it could damage historic land. Burnham says the event could bring hundreds of thousands of visitors, create jobs, and he pledged £70m for transport. The bid could be decided by 2029 and would compete with other English venues.
