Trump said the U.S. needs Greenland for security, but Denmark refuses to give up the island. The president wants the U.S. to take control, even hinting at military action. Danish officials and Greenland leaders reject the move, saying the territory is self‑governed and part of Denmark. European allies back Denmark, warning that any U.S. takeover would breach international law. Most Americans and politicians oppose Trump’s plan.
