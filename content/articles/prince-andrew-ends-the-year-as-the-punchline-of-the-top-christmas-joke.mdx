The comedy channel U&Gold ran a poll for the year’s best Christmas cracker joke. The winning joke, written by Ben Smith, teased Prince Andrew’s loss of royal status with the line, “Why is Prince Andrew not writing any Christmas books? He hasn’t got any titles.” The joke won £1,500 and a holiday. The top ten list also included jokes about celebrities, politicians and a pop‑band reunion. The competition highlights how Brits use humor to comment on the year’s headlines.
