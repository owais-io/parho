England will ban vaping in cars that have people under 18 to protect kids from secondhand vapor. The new law is part of a bill that also stops smoking, vaping and heated‑tobacco in playgrounds, schools and hospitals. The ban builds on a 2015 rule that already stopped smoking in cars with children. Health officials say even vapour can harm kids, especially in tight spaces like cars.
