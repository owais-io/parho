Japan’s new prime minister, Sanae Takaichi, tried to use her style to push for talks with China at the G20 summit, but the two leaders stayed apart and no deal was made. Takaichi said dialogue was open but didn’t back away from her comment that Japan could help defend Taiwan if needed. China reacted angrily, warned citizens, and raised trade restrictions. Despite this, a poll shows 70% of Japanese people still approve her.
