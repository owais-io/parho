Tottenham just signed midfielder Conor Gallagher from Atlético Madrid for £34.6 million. The 25‑year‑old vows to create “special moments” for the club and has a contract that runs until 2031. Gallagher joined after a slow start at Atlético and is eager to revive his England chances ahead of the World Cup. He’s ready to make his Spurs debut against West Ham and bring energy, leadership, and goal‑scoring ability to the team.
