UK ministers are talking about stopping household energy bills from rising after the Iran war pushed oil and gas prices higher. Energy prices are fixed until July, but a price cap could lift bills by about 10 % next year, adding £160 a year. The government promises to keep costs down, but experts say help should be targeted, not a large nationwide subsidy, and suggest boosting solar and batteries for the poorest.
