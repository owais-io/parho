The UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer has chosen diplomat Christian Turner to replace Peter Mandelson as the UK’s ambassador to Washington. Mandelson was removed after links to Jeffrey Epstein were revealed. Turner, who has worked in the Foreign Office and served in New York, is expected to steady relations with the Trump administration. The move comes amid tense UK‑US ties over Trump’s comments on Ukraine and Europe. Turner said he looks forward to strengthening ties with America.
