New South Wales will set a minimum age for riding electric bikes to improve safety. A review led by Transport NSW will recommend a legal age between 12 and 16, and will look at whether kids can carry passengers. The state will adopt European safety rules so e‑bikes stay powered like regular bikes, and will crack down on illegal high‑powered models. The change follows rising injuries and calls for tighter control.
