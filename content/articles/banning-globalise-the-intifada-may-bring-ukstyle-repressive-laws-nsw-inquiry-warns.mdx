After the Bondi attack, New South Wales lawmakers are looking at banning the slogan “globalise the intifada.” Muslim leaders say the ban would hurt their communities and copy the UK’s harsh rules that could criminalise protest. Jewish groups say the phrase is offensive and want it banned. The inquiry will finish its report on Jan. 31, deciding if the slogan should be illegal.
