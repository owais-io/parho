Alex Karp, the CEO of Palantir, says that AI will cut the economic power of educated women, who usually vote Democrat, and boost working‑class men. He claims this will change the political balance. His comments are being seen as a warning about how technology can reshape voting power, and some critics argue it’s a push to help Republicans. The debate shows how AI might shift who holds influence.
