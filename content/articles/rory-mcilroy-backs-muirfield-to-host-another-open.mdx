Rory McIlroy says Muirfield deserves a spot on the Open Championship route. He remembers a tough 2013 where he missed the cut, but he now sees the course as top‑class and commercially smart. Muirfield is still being considered after the 2027 Open at St Andrews, and McIlroy thinks it could bring a strong crowd and good money. He also notes other links like Portmarnock could host the first Open outside the UK.
