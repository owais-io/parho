The UN’s climate chief, Simon Stiell, says many countries still view security only in military terms, ignoring the huge threat from climate change. He warned that rising heat, floods and droughts can spark famine, displacement and war. Stiell urged leaders at the Cop31 climate conference to include plans to phase out fossil fuels and boost clean energy—yet the draft agenda omits that topic, sparking criticism.
