A minibus carrying nine teens crashed into a car near Tadcaster school at just before 12:30 pm. One of the passengers, a teenage girl, died on the spot, while another teen was badly hurt but not in danger of death. The other passengers, plus the minibus driver, had only minor injuries. Police have arrested the car’s driver, a 40‑year‑old woman, and are asking anyone with dash‑cam footage to come forward. The road is closed, and the school will remain shut.
