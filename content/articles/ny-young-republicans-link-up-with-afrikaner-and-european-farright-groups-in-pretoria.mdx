Stefano Forte, president of the New York Young Republican Club, spoke at a Pretoria conference that brought together Afrikaner nationalist groups and European far‑right parties. The event shows how the club is linking with white‑nationalist movements in South Africa and Europe, and may be influencing U.S. policy. The meeting included leaders from the Afrikaner Solidarity Movement, Belgium’s Vlaams Belang, and Hungarian‑funded think‑tanks, highlighting growing ties between U.S. right‑wing circles and overseas extremist groups.
