Zohran Mamdani was sworn in as New York City’s 111th mayor at midnight in a subway station. He is the city’s first Muslim mayor and a Democrat who says he will govern in practical ways. He promises to stop rent hikes, build 200,000 new affordable homes, give free childcare, make buses free, and raise taxes on big companies and the wealthiest residents to pay for it. He also wants to strengthen community safety and protect immigrants.
