London police are fighting a surge of teenage phone thieves who use Snapchat to get cash rewards up to £380 for the newest iPhones. The Metropolitan Police are rolling out drones and Surron e‑bikes to chase suspects and use facial‑recognition tech. They also want Apple and other makers to make phones harder to reset and courts to stop repeat offenders from being bailed. The new tools aim to cut the 71,000‑plus annual thefts.
