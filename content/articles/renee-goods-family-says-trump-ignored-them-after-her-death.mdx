Renee Good, a 37‑year‑old mother, was shot and killed by a federal immigration officer in Minneapolis on Jan. 7. Her family says no one from President Trump’s team has reached out since the killing, and they are frustrated by false claims that Good was a terrorist. They hired investigators to uncover the truth, held a private autopsy, and have received support from community members. The case highlights concerns about immigration enforcement.
