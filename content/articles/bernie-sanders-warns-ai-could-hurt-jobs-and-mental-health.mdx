Senator Bernie Sanders said AI could threaten jobs and make people rely on machines for help, raising worries about families and mental health. He wants Congress to pause new data centers and study AI's impact. He also criticized tech CEOs who profit from AI. Meanwhile, Republican Senator Katie Britt wants laws to protect kids from chatbots, including rules that AI must say it isn’t a human and ban sexual content. Both sides agree lawmakers must act.
