The United States captured its first Olympic men’s hockey gold since 1980 by beating Canada 2‑1 in overtime at the Milano Cortina Games. Jack Hughes scored the winning goal less than two minutes into extra time, and goaltender Connor Hellebuyck made a stunning 41‑of‑42 save effort. Canada had been chasing a record 10th gold, but the U.S. team ended a 46‑year wait in dramatic fashion.
