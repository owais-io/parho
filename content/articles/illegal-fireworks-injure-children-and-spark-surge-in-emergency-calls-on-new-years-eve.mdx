Illegal fireworks used on New Year's Eve led to injuries for two children in Sydney and a man in Melbourne, while emergency services saw a spike in calls. Police said the night was mostly safe but noted the rise in incidents. Across Australia, several arrests were made and paramedics treated dozens of people, mainly for fireworks injuries and alcohol‑related problems. Most celebrations went on without major trouble.
