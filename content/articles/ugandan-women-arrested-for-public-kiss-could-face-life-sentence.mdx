Two young women, Wendy and Alesi, were arrested in Uganda’s Arua City after police saw them kissing in public. The Anti‑Homosexuality Act says same‑sex relationships can lead to life imprisonment, and the new law has sparked international outcry. Human‑rights groups warn that the arrests are part of a wider crackdown, and that many LGBTQ+ people are now living in fear and danger.
