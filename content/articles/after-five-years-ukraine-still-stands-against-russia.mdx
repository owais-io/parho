Russia launched its invasion of Ukraine on Feb. 24, 2022, and many experts thought the country would collapse quickly. Five years on, Ukraine still fights back, using drones, homemade missiles and tough defence. Russia has lost more equipment and soldiers than expected and can’t take key cities as fast as it once did. Putin now wants to trade territory, but President Zelensky rejects any deal that gives Russia more land. Most Ukrainians support staying in the fight.
