Heavy rain caused a big landslide on New Zealand’s Mount Maunganui, crushing a campsite. A local witness heard a deep crack as the earth fell, killing two people, one of whom was Chinese, and leaving six missing. Rescue teams are still digging through rubble, but progress is slow and the area remains unsafe. Local officials and the prime minister are supporting families, while roads are closed in the region.
