Robert Jenrick’s exit to Reform UK has left Kemi Badenoch in a stronger spot. She fired him as shadow justice minister and removed the party whip, sending a clear message to other MPs. Inside the party, many now view the split as a win and are ready to focus on winning elections. Polls show a small uptick in Conservative support, hinting that Badenoch’s leadership is holding.
