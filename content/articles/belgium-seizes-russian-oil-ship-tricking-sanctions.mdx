Belgium’s navy, with French helicopter help, boarded the tanker Ethera in the North Sea. The ship, flying a false Guinea flag, was heading back to Russia with oil that bypasses Western sanctions. The vessel will be taken to Zeebrugge and officially confiscated. The move signals Europe’s tougher stance on Russia’s “shadow fleet,” a tactic praised by Ukraine but condemned by Moscow as piracy.
