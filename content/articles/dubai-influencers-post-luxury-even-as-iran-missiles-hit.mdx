Iranian missiles struck Dubai, but many social‑media stars kept partying and filming the chaos. Influencers shared videos from clubs, yachts, and hotel rooftops, insisting the city was still safe and life went on. Their posts sparked criticism for downplaying danger and fueling a narrative that Dubai is an unshakeable paradise. The story shows how influencer culture and social media blur the line between luxury and real conflict.
