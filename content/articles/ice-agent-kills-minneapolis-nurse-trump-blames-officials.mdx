On 24 January, ICE agents shot Minneapolis nurse Alex Pretti to death while he was trying to help a woman. Videos show the agents firing multiple shots even though Pretti was unarmed. Trump and senior officials called Pretti a terrorist and blamed Minnesota leaders, claiming they were inciting violence. The article also criticizes ICE commander Greg Bovino’s tactics and mentions a White‑House gala for Melania Trump’s documentary that ignored the killing.
