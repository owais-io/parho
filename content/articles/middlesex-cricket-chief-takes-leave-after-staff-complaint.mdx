Middlesex cricket club's chief executive, Andrew Cornish, has taken a leave of absence after a staff member filed a misconduct complaint. The club handed the case to the Cricket Regulator, which is now investigating. Cornish denies any wrongdoing and says he’s unaware of the complaint’s details. The investigation comes as Middlesex prepares to meet members about new funding and possible club ownership changes. The board will update staff next Monday.
