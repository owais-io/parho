Christopher Hoskin, 49, was a mathematician, programmer and nature lover. He grew up in Glasgow and Somerset, studied maths at Oxford, earned a PhD, and wrote research papers. After leaving academia he worked in IT, helped build university servers, and later joined Overleaf as a senior DevOps engineer. He also built open‑source software, cared for native plants, spotted rare orchids, and photographed wildlife. He died unexpectedly. He is survived by his sister and parents.
