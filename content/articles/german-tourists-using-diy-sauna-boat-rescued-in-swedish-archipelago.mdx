Five German tourists who tried to set up a motor‑powered sauna on an ice floe in Stockholm’s archipelago were rescued after a ferry wave broke the ice. The group was on a makeshift boat with a sauna tent, motorised saw and motor. Ferry crew pulled them to shore near Värmdö, where they were unharmed. Swedish authorities say the incident is rare but shows how ice can be hazardous for DIY projects.
