Tottenham lost 3‑1 to Aston Villa in the FA Cup, with Buendía scoring twice and Wilson Odobert getting Spurs’ only goal. The defeat was the second time this season that Villa beat Spurs, and it leaves Tottenham’s manager Thomas Frank in doubt. Spurs played with little energy, suffered injuries, and a post‑match scuffle involving Rogers, Palhinha and other players. Fans booed and chanted, showing their frustration with the loss.
