UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer called out US President Donald Trump after Trump said NATO troops stayed away from frontlines in Afghanistan. Starmer said Trump’s comments were insulting and hurt veterans' families. The remarks triggered backlash across the UK, with calls for an apology. The controversy adds to tensions over earlier disputes, like Trump’s comments about the Chagos Islands. The debate highlights sensitivity around military sacrifice.
