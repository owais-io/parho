Katie Pavlich, a longtime Fox News commentator, has started her own 10 p.m. show on NewsNation. She plans to use her contacts with the Trump administration to bring in top officials, including Trump himself, for interviews. Pavlich says she will report facts first but will not shy from criticizing the administration when she disagrees. She loves Fox, but sees NewsNation as a chance to build her own platform and reach new viewers.
