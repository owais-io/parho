Usman Khawaja, the 40‑year‑old Australian batsman, has announced his retirement after a steady Test career that earned him 44 runs over 87 matches. In his farewell speech, he spoke about faith, family, and the country’s divisions, showing how cricket can mirror a person’s character. Known for his smooth strokes and calm presence, the commentator praised his classic, patient play and steady influence on the game, noting his decline in the last two years.
