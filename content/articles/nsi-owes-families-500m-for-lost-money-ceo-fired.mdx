NS&I, Britain's state‑owned savings bank, has discovered an administrative error that left around 37,500 bereaved families without money they were owed. The problem, which involves missing payments of about £476 million, has led to the CEO's dismissal and the appointment of a new leader. The bank will contact affected families in May, offer interest and compensation, and promises to fix the system to prevent future mistakes.
