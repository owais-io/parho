Zambia and Mali played a close opening match at the Africa Cup of Nations. Mali went ahead 1‑0 when Lassine Sinayoko scored, but Zambia fought back. In stoppage time, striker Patson Daka leapt for a diving header from a cross and tied the game 1‑1. The late goal earned Zambia a point, ending the match in a dramatic draw in Casablanca.
