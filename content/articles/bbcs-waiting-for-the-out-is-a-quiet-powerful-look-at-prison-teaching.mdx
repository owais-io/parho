Dennis Kelly’s new BBC drama ‘Waiting for the Out’ follows a philosophy teacher named Dan who works with men in prison. The series is quietly powerful, showing how Dan confronts his own fears, past alcohol addiction, and a tough childhood while helping inmates think. It highlights issues men face—mental health, suicide, and the need for better support for prison families—and adds a thoughtful voice to British prison dramas.
