Hannah Spencer, a 34‑year‑old plumber who trained as a plasterer, won the Gorton and Denton seat as the first Green Party MP in northern England. Her win flipped a long‑held Labour seat and could change UK politics. During the campaign she faced jokes, security, and false rumors about a wealthy husband. She talks about everyday problems like cost of living, the NHS, and even stirred controversy by urging Muslim voters to oppose Labour over Gaza.
