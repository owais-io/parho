Ruben Amorim stepped down from Manchester United after a tense season that saw the club struggle to win games and keep fans happy. He had tried to push a new style of play and a different transfer plan, but results stayed poor, with only a few wins in 11 matches. Money is tight after big spending and no European revenue, making it hard to bring in a new manager and turn things around.
