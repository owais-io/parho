A mother from south Lebanon says her six‑year‑old daughter, Narjis, was killed when Israeli forces struck their home on 2 March. Narjis’ mother, Rana Jaber, and her two sons survived the blast but were trapped under rubble. The story shows how the ongoing conflict has taken the lives of many Lebanese children, leaving families in deep grief and children at risk of lasting trauma.
