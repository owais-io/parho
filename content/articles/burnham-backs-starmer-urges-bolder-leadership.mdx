Andy Burnham publicly supports Labour leader Keir Starmer, but asks him to be bolder and more welcoming of ideas from other party members. He urges a united, ambitious government, especially on housing. Burnham also says the party needs a stronger team after a recent byelection setback. Other Labour figures, like Ed Miliband, echo the call for clearer purpose, while internal tensions around the leadership remain high.
