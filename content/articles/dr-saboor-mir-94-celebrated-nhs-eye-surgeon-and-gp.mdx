Dr Saboor Mir, 94, was a respected eye surgeon and family doctor who spent more than 40 years in the NHS. Born in Pakistan, he moved to Glasgow in 1965, then to Hertfordshire where he ran a GP partnership with his wife. He also worked at Charing Cross Hospital and helped raise funds for a local mosque. After retiring, he enjoyed cooking and cricket.
