A huge winter storm swept across the US, killing at least 15 people from Texas to New England. The storm brought heavy snow, ice and record‑low temperatures, causing thousands of power outages and massive flight cancellations. Drivers were forced off roads, and several deaths occurred from hypothermia and sledding accidents. Officials warned of dangerous wind chills and urged people to stay warm and stay inside.
