The UK police say a popular cryptocurrency called Tether was used to move money that helps Russia fight Ukraine. The money comes from illegal drug and gun sales in the UK. One of Tether’s biggest shareholders, Christopher Harborne, donated large sums to UK politicians, including Nigel Farage. Though Harborne isn’t accused of wrongdoing, his ties to Tether raise questions about how the crypto fuels crime and war. The agency has seized millions of dollars worth of Tether tokens.
