Meta’s new oversight board shows what independent oversight can do. Suzanne Nossel, a board member, explains why governments haven’t regulated AI yet and how a board can help. AI can do bad things—advice on suicide, weapons instructions, privacy breaches. Companies chase profit, so safety gets overlooked. The board looks at content, gives public appeals, and recommends policy changes. A similar model could keep AI companies honest.
