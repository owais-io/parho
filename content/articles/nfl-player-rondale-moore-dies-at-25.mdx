NFL receiver Rondale Moore died on Saturday at age 25. His former Purdue coach praised him as hardworking and a joy to play with. Teammates and fans shared sad messages, and police said he took his own life. Moore’s career, marked by injuries, still earned him a spot in the NFL. He was drafted in 2021, played for the Cardinals and Falcons, and joined the Vikings in 2025.
