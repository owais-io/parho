A former private investigator, Jonathan Rees, says he never put bugs on Doreen Lawrence’s phone or cafe after her son’s murder. His new statement contradicts earlier claims that he helped the Daily Mail steal her private data, hurting the lawsuit led by Prince Harry and Lawrence. Another ex‑investigator, Gavin Burrows, also retracted his testimony. The case, involving seven claimants, continues, while the Daily Mail denies all wrongdoing.
