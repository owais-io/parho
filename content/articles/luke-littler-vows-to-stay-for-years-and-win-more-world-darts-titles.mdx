Luke Littler, just 18, won his second PDC world title by beating Dutchman Gian van Veen 7‑1. He said he will stay in the game for many years and hopes to chase Phil Taylor’s record of 16 titles. The young star kept a 106‑average, became the fourth player to defend a world title, and laughed off earlier boos, saying darts fans can be unpredictable.
