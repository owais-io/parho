Rohan Dennis, a former Olympic cyclist, had a suspended sentence for the car crash that killed his wife Melissa Hoskins in December 2023. He recently posted photos of a black Porsche, calling it an “absolute weapon,” and later pictures of his children, sparking outrage. Victims’ rights officials called the posts deeply offensive, saying they showed no respect for the loss. Dennis had already been warned by a judge about his responsibility for the tragedy.
