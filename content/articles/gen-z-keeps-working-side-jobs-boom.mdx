Gen Z workers are turning to side jobs to keep their wallets full and their lives balanced. A Harris Poll shows 57 % of Gen Z Americans run a side hustle, compared with 21 % of boomers. The work lets them stay creative, beat job‑market uncertainty and avoid burnout. Tech layoffs and AI‑driven art cuts push many into gig‑style gigs, while employers are urged to back them and keep learning.
