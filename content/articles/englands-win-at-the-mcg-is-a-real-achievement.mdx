England clinched a thrilling four‑wicket win at the MCG on day two, chasing 175 to beat Australia in the fourth Test. The openers, especially Ben Duckett and Zak Crawley, scored quickly, turning the match into a fast‑paced game. With Australia missing key bowlers, England's aggressive batting – part of their “Bazball” style – proved decisive. It’s England’s first Test win in Australia since 2011 and shows they can finish strong at Sydney.
