Three US fighter jets were mistakenly shot down by Kuwait’s air defences during a combat mission. All six crew members ejected safely and are in stable condition. The incident, which happened after US‑Israeli strikes on Iran, is being investigated by Kuwait. The clash has already caused casualties, with Iran launching missiles at several countries and the region in turmoil.
