The 2026 Winter Games in Milan‑Cortina were a mix of triumphs and heartbreaks. Matt Weston helped Britain win a skeleton relay gold, while Alysa Liu took the figure‑skating gold in a memorable comeback. Norway’s Johannes Høsflot Klæbo dominated cross‑country, sweeping all his events. British athletes missed medals in freestyle, and the skeleton relay was a painful near‑miss. The Games also sparked debate over sustainability and suggested new sports like snow‑volleyball for 2030.
