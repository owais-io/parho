Chocolate prices in Britain jumped 18% last year, while the average cost of a four‑person Christmas dinner fell by a penny to £32.46. Frozen turkeys got cheaper, but potatoes, cranberry sauce and stuffing rose a bit. Supermarkets are offering more discounts to keep shoppers happy, and people still buy small chocolate treats despite the price hike. Grocery prices overall rose 4.7%, and online shopping grew, though most shoppers still go to the store.
