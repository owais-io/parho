Donald Trump was given the first FIFA peace prize after the Nobel went to a Venezuelan opposition figure. At the ceremony, FIFA’s president handed the trophy to Trump, who later talked to Politico. He praised autocrats, urged Europe to send asylum seekers back to Congo, compared Ukraine’s president to a 19th‑century con man, and made confusing comments about Crimea and other politics. His remarks show why many think he’s not a fit for a peace award.
