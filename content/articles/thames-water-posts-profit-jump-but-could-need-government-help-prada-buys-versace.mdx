Thames Water, the biggest water company in Britain, earned £386 million in the first half of its financial year, a huge jump from last year’s loss. The company says it needs big cash to fix leaks and upgrade its pipes, but its debt and funding worries mean it could be taken over by the government soon. Meanwhile, Italian fashion house Prada has bought Versace for about £1 billion, adding a popular brand to its luxury group.
