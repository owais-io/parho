A Virgin Australia flight from Brisbane to Melbourne had to land after a vape device started smoking in the cabin. The crew called a “pan” alert, and emergency services were on standby. Firefighters followed the plane, removed the vape, and the flight landed safely. The incident highlights risks of lithium‑ion batteries in vapes, which can overheat and burn. Airlines now restrict vapes to carry‑on only and forbid charging on board.
