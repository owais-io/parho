The writer shares her own painful memories of childhood abuse and how they echo Virginia Giuffre’s story of being repeatedly raped by powerful men, including Jeffrey Epstein. She explains that when people ignore or gaslight survivors, the trauma stays inside and makes them vulnerable to more attacks. The piece urges society to give these victims justice and support, warning that if we don’t, the cycle of abuse will keep exploding. Resources for help are also listed.
