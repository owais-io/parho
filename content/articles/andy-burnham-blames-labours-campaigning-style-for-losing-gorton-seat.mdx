Andy Burnham, Greater Manchester mayor and former MP, says Labour’s old‑school door‑to‑door canvassing has failed to connect with voters, calling the party’s campaign tactics a “bankruptcy.” He criticised the party after it lost the Gorton & Denton seat to the Greens, urging a rethink of the political system and devolution. Burnham also wants Manchester to become a green leader and hinted he could still challenge Keir Starmer for the party’s top job.
