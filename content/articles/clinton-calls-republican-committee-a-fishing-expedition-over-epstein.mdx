Clinton took the floor at a House committee to defend herself against accusations that she was linked to Jeffrey Epstein. She called the Republican‑led hearing a “fishing expedition” and said it was partisan theatre aimed at distracting from President Trump. She denied ever meeting Epstein. The committee is led by Republican chair James Comer, who threatened contempt charges. Bill Clinton will testify later. FBI memos hint at Trump’s alleged abuse, but he denies it.
