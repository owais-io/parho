Experts say AI‑driven tech stocks may be over‑valued, and a crash could hurt other shares, jobs and pensions. You only lose money when you sell, so don’t panic and dump your holdings. Keep a cash cushion, spread your money across different asset types, and think in years, not weeks. Low‑risk options like gold, bonds and stable companies can help keep your portfolio steadier if the bubble pops.
