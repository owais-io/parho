Hornby, the model‑train maker, has sold its popular slot‑car racing line Scalextric to private‑equity firm Purbeck Capital Partners for £20 million. The money will help the company cut debt and focus on its other toy brands. Hornby will keep supporting Scalextric through its existing infrastructure, while the new owners plan to grow the brand and explore new markets. The deal shows a shift away from public markets toward private ownership.
