Punch is a seven‑month‑old Japanese macaque at a Tokyo zoo who once clung to a huge Ikea ‘djungelskog’ plush. After being rejected by other monkeys, the toy gave him comfort. Now he spends less time with it, playing with adults and sleeping among his peers. Zookeepers explain his early abandonment was likely due to his mother’s inexperience and a hot, stressful birth. Fans and experts praise his progress.
