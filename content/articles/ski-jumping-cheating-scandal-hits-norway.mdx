At the Olympics, Norwegian ski jumpers face a cheating scandal. Two athletes were banned for three months after their coaches stitched extra fabric into their suits. The story has spread rumors that jumpers might be injecting hyaluronic acid to enlarge the fabric space. Ski jumping rules limit suit size, but athletes keep looking for loopholes. The sport’s governing bodies are tightening rules while former stars admit cheating has long been common.
