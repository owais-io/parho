Wizz Air said it expects a €50 million hit to last‑year profits because the Middle East war has pushed fuel prices up. The airline cut scheduled flights to the region and warns it could lose money for the year. Rising oil and gas prices are also hurting households and other airlines, and Europe’s markets have slipped while the UK construction sector keeps slowing.
