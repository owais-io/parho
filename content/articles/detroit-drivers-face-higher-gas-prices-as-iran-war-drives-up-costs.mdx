A new war in Iran has pushed oil supplies lower, making gas prices jump across Michigan. In Detroit, people are paying up to $4.30 a gallon, up 60 cents in the past week. Many drivers are frustrated, especially since Michigan is a key swing state for elections. President Trump says the rise is temporary, but locals feel the pinch on their budgets right now.
