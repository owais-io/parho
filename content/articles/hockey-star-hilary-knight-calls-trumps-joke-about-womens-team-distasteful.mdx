Hilary Knight, captain of the US women's ice hockey team, said Donald Trump's joke about inviting the women to the State of the Union was “distasteful.” Trump had joked that the women would be invited after the men's gold win. Knight reminded everyone the women won gold and deserve full respect. The comments sparked backlash, and teammates said they support each other. Players laughed at Trump's call, which went viral, but later apologized for the insensitive joke. Knight hopes the story will teach respect for women in sports.
