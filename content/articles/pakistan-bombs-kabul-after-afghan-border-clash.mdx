Pakistan carried out air strikes on Kabul, Paktia and Kandahar after Afghan troops attacked Pakistani border soldiers. Both sides blame each other for the deaths, with Pakistan saying 133 Taliban officials were killed. The violence follows months of clashes that have shut border crossings and forced refugees to leave camps. The UN urges civilians to stay safe and encourages diplomatic talks, but tensions remain high.
