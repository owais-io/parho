US and Israel planes hit Iran while Trump’s pause excludes power plants, aiming to calm markets. But fighting keeps going, raising fears that the war could drag on. The UK says it will stay out of US‑Israeli actions, but the conflict threatens oil supplies and pushes up energy prices, hurting British businesses and households. Leaders warn that the crisis could push the UK toward a tougher energy policy and make a cheap green transition harder.
