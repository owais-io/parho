Vladyslav Heraskevych, a Ukrainian skeleton racer, was banned from the Olympics for wearing a helmet with photos of fallen athletes. The IOC said it was political propaganda, but the rider said it was a tribute. Critics say the rule was applied unfairly and called for an apology. The case sparks debate over whether the IOC is too strict about politics in sport.
