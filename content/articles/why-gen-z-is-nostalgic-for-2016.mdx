A new TikTok trend called “Bring Back 2016” has Gen Z longing to live like the year before the big political shifts. The videos show 2016‑style dances, Pokémon Go, old filters, and “vintage” makeup. The writer says it’s a mix of harmless retro fun and a sad reminder that 2016 was the last time many felt hopeful. She encourages people to stay playful and kinder online instead of just chasing the past.
