Estonia has forbidden 261 Russians who fought in Ukraine from entering its borders and is urging other nations to do the same. The ban follows estimates that 1.5 million Russians joined the invasion, many with combat and criminal backgrounds. Ukraine praised the move as a necessary security step. Meanwhile, Russia struck a Ukrainian drone factory and launched large attacks on Ukrainian cities, causing power outages and raising security concerns.
