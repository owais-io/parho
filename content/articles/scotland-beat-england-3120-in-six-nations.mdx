Scotland won 31‑20 over England at Murrayfield. Huw Jones scored a try and was player of the match, with Ben White and Finn Russell also key. Scotland’s forwards dominated the scrum and defence, while England struggled after Henry Arundell was sent off and George Ford missed a drop‑goal try. The win keeps Scotland strong in the tournament.
