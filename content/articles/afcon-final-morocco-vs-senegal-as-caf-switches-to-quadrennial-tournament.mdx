The 2025 Africa Cup of Nations ends with a final between Morocco and Senegal. Morocco, playing at home, is chasing its first title since 1976, while Senegal won in 2021. The Confederation of African Football (CAF) just announced it will move Afcon to a four‑year cycle starting in 2028, a change that many African football associations say was made without their input. The decision has sparked anger and confusion across the continent.
