Trump believes using force can stop America’s decline, but his harsh rhetoric about Iran and threats of military action actually worsen the situation. Owen Jones argues that the U.S. has long used moral claims to justify dominance, yet Trump's heavy‑handed tactics drive anti‑American sentiment and threaten global standing. His approach risks more violence and accelerates U.S. power loss, echoing similar moves in Venezuela that erode credibility.
