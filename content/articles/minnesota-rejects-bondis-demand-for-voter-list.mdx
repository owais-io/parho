Minnesota’s top election officials and state lawmakers say Pam Bondi, the former Florida attorney general, is trying to force the state to hand over its voter list and other private data. Bondi’s letter, sent to Governor Tim Walz, also calls for the removal of sanctuary‑city rules and for public‑assistance records. Critics say the request has nothing to do with immigration and is a political ploy to stir fear and meddle with elections.
