Israel bombed Iran’s giant South Pars gas field in the Persian Gulf, the biggest attack on Iranian energy assets yet. The strike came a day after Israeli forces killed Iran’s intelligence minister, a senior spy chief, heightening the 3‑week war. Iran warned it could hit other Gulf states’ oil plants, while Qatar and the UAE pulled their military staff out. Oil prices jumped and the US‑Israeli assault shows the conflict is moving beyond the battlefield.
