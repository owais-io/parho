MS NOW, the U.S. cable news channel that just changed its name from MSNBC, is now pulling in more viewers, says CEO Mark Lazarus. He says total audiences have jumped double‑digits since the rebrand in late 2023. But the parent company, Versant, is making less money overall – revenue fell 5 % this year, mainly because advertising and cable‑subscription sales are down. The company still plans to grow with new ads for the 2026 midterms.
