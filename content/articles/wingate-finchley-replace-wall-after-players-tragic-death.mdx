On a Saturday in August, 21‑year‑old Billy Vigar collided with a concrete wall at Wingate & Finchley’s stadium and died a few days later. The club and the FA are reviewing safety around pitches. The stadium’s wall was removed and replaced with safer mesh fencing, and extra space was added for players. A fundraiser raised £18,000 for Vigar’s family, and the club is playing away games to honour his memory.
