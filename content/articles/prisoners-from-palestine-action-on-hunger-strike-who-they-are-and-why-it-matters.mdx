Eight men in UK prisons are refusing food while they wait for trial. They say they are activists from Palestine Action, a group that has been banned as a terrorist organization. The prisoners want to be released, end the ban, and get better communication. They are accused of breaking into a military base and a defence company. Several have been hospitalized, and doctors warn that a hunger strike can kill after a few weeks if food is not given.
