A fresh batch of about three million Epstein‑related documents has been released, giving new insight into his network and the investigations around him. The files reveal that Epstein’s lawyers met with prosecutors before his 2019 death, that the FBI received unverified tips linking him to Trump, and that high‑profile figures like Elon Musk, former US Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick, and Prince Andrew had contact with him. Emails also show ties to Richard Branson, Ghislaine Maxwell, and others, while a photo links filmmaker Brett Ratner to Epstein.
