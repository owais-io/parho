Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem, the chief executive of DP World – the Dubai‑based ports and logistics giant – stepped down after emails linking him to sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein surfaced. The UAE‑owned group faced backlash from investors and partners, who halted future deals. DP World, which owns P&O Ferries and several ports, has also been criticised for staff cuts at the ferry business. New leaders have been appointed, but the scandal has raised concerns about corporate oversight.
