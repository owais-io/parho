Sangita Myska, a veteran broadcaster, says she was hit with racist slurs by former Reform UK council leader Ian Cooper, who was kicked out of the party. Myska blames Nigel Farage and Reform UK for creating a “toxic” atmosphere that lets such hate spread. The article links Cooper’s attacks to a pattern of white‑supremacist language and highlights Farage’s own past racist allegations. It shows how the party’s culture is blamed for pushing hate online.
