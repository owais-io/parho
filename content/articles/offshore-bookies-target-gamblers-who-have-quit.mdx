The Long family lost their son Ollie to gambling, and their investigation shows he fell into a network of offshore betting sites that aren’t covered by the UK’s self‑exclusion service, GamStop. These sites use the “Not on GamStop” tag to lure people who have blocked themselves from legal operators. Hundreds of millions of pounds now go to these unregulated sites, and regulators say they’re struggling to stop it.
