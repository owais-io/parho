A Guardian probe shows that the UK’s huge AI spending promises may not be real. The government claims billions have gone into new data centres, jobs, and a supercomputer, but the sites often already exist, the contracts are vague, and the jobs aren’t confirmed. Oversight is weak, and much of the money could end up in U.S. companies instead of boosting the UK economy.
