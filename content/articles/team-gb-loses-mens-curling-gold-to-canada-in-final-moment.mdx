Team GB’s men’s curling squad lost the Olympic gold final to Canada 9‑6, with the last stone deciding the game. The British players, led by Bruce Mouat, fought hard but fell short, leaving them visibly upset and in tears. Despite their strong season and past world titles, the loss stings especially after a similar disappointment four years ago. Mouat remains determined to keep playing and hopes his teammates will continue with him for the next Games.
