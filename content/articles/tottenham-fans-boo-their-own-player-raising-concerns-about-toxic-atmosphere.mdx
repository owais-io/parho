After a mistake by keeper Guglielmo Vicario, Tottenham fans shouted boos at him, showing a growing toxic atmosphere. A long‑time supporter recalls past boos that hurt players. He argues that booing teammates hurts confidence and shows a lack of support. The match ended badly for Spurs, who have only won three home games this season. The writer warns that fans need to remember players are human.
