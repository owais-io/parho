In Clevedon, Somerset, shopkeepers on Hill Road worry that new parking charges will keep customers away. The North Somerset council plans to charge for on‑street parking, but local traders—who enjoy two free hours—believe the fees could hurt sales and even close businesses. More than 3,300 people signed a petition, and some councillors fear the plan could be legally challenged. While a few residents think the charges might help, most traders urge a rethink.
