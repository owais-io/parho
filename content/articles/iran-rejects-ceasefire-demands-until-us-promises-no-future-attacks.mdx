After a month of U.S. air strikes, Iran insists it will not accept a ceasefire unless Washington guarantees it will not strike again and lifts sanctions. Tehran’s officials say any deal must stop U.S. attacks and protect Iranian shipping. The Revolutionary Guard threatens to block the Strait of Hormuz if ships carry U.S. or Israeli personnel. At the UN, 80+ nations will condemn Iran, but some call for a ceasefire.
