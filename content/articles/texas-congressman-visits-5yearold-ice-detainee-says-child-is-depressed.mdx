A Texas congressman went to a Texas detention center to see a five‑year‑old boy and his father, who were seized by ICE in Minnesota. He said the child is sad and has been sleeping a lot. The congressman demanded their release and checked on other detainees. The family, who claim they have a pending asylum case, say they were not illegal.
