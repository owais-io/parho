Nvidia announced its quarterly earnings, beating expectations with a 75% jump in data‑center revenue to $62.3 billion. The chipmaker posted a $120 billion profit for the year and $1.62 a share this quarter, topping analyst forecasts. Shares rose about 3% after the report. The company’s AI‑related deals and high investment in OpenAI remain under scrutiny, but investors are still upbeat about Nvidia’s role in the AI boom.
