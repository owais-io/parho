England are set to play the Boxing Day Test at Melbourne’s MCG after losing the first three games of the Ashes. The crowd of up to 90,000 could be a boost, but the team is struggling. Key players are injured and managers worry about coaching changes. England’s coach, Rob Key, admits he may have changed the line‑up too late and fears the jobs of staff could be on the line.
