Leicester head coach Geoff Parling says the new rules that stop players from sticking to the ball—known as escort defenders—are a positive change. He says the changes have led to more kicking and a freer game, but he’s not altering his team’s plans. Parling also mentioned Ross Byrne’s criticism and that taller wingers are now common. He praised his squad and noted the loss of lock George Martin to Saracens.
