Emerald Fennell’s latest film, a new take on Wuthering Heights, has stirred controversy. The movie is loud, sexual and star‑studded, and many purists say it betrays Emily Brontë’s original story. The director says she just wants to show the book’s raw passion for a new‑generation audience. The backlash has actually pushed book sales up by 469 %. Fans still have older versions to watch, but the film’s buzz keeps people talking.
