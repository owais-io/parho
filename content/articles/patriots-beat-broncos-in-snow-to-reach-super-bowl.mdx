In a brutal, blustery AFC championship, the New England Patriots beat the Denver Broncos 10‑7 on a snowy field, earning a spot in Super Bowl LX. New coach Mike Vrabel guided the team, while 23‑year‑old rookie quarterback Drake Maye led the offense. The win gives New England its first Super Bowl appearance since 2019, setting the stage for a possible historic comeback.
