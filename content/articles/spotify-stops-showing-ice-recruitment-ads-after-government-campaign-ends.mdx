Spotify has confirmed it no longer displays ads for U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE). The ads were part of a Trump‑era recruitment push that ran until late 2025. Spotify said the campaign was separate from its own policies and that users can still rate any ad they see. The move comes amid protests from musicians and listeners who objected to the ads and the company’s ties to controversial military investments.
