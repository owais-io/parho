After the U.S. and Israel carried out airstrikes on Iran, claiming they hit the supreme leader, anti‑war groups held rallies in cities from New York to Chicago. Protesters said the strikes were illegal, un‑approved by Congress and risked endless war. The demonstrations were organized by left‑wing groups and joined by the ACLU and Democrats who called on Congress to stop the President’s unilateral military actions.
