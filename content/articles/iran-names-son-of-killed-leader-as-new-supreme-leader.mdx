After the US‑Israeli strike killed Iran’s long‑time Supreme Leader Ayatollah Khamenei, his son Mojtaba was announced as the new head of state. State TV shows crowds chanting loyalty, while many Iranians online shout back “Death to Mojtaba” or express fear of more crackdowns. Security forces keep tight control and warn foreigners of punishment. Some activists say the new leader will make life harder for dissenters.
