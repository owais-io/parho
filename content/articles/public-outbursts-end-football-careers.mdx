Football stars who air complaints publicly often lose trust and leave clubs. Roy Keane slammed teammates on TV after a loss and was fired. William Gallas was dropped after criticizing the squad. Luis Suárez demanded a contract exit from Liverpool, later moving to Barcelona. Raheem Sterling refused a big offer, then sold to City. Mauro Icardi was stripped of the captaincy after his wife’s comments and left Inter. Cristiano Ronaldo quit United after a harsh interview.
