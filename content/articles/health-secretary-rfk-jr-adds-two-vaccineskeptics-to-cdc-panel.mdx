Robert F. Kennedy Jr. has hired two doctors who question vaccine safety to join the CDC’s vaccine‑advisory committee. The new members, both obstetrician‑gynecologists, have publicly criticized COVID‑19 shots and other vaccines, especially for pregnant people. Their appointment follows Kennedy’s earlier move to replace the entire panel. Health groups warn the changes could spread misinformation and weaken public trust in vaccines.
