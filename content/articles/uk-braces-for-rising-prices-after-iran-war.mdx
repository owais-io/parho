Trump’s attack on Iran triggered a spike in oil prices, sending UK petrol up by 3p a litre. Higher energy costs hurt everyone, especially low‑income households, and the Bank of England may pause rate cuts because inflation could jump again. The government is thinking about protecting consumers and pushing for clean, local energy. The article warns that wars and climate change keep driving unpredictable price shocks that hit the UK hard.
