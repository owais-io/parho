A drone crashed into a runway at the RAF Akrotiri base on Cyprus, forcing the nearby village to evacuate. Residents fled to hotels and relatives, leaving streets empty. The drone was traced to Hezbollah in Lebanon, raising fears that the island could be drawn into regional conflict. The incident sparked protests, criticism of the UK, and calls for better protection for local civilians.
