Voyager probes carry a Golden Record that greets alien life with greetings in 55 languages and Earth music. To celebrate their 50th anniversary, Australians can record a short voice note to be sent into deep space. Through the Powerhouse Museum’s portal, people can upload messages by 27 Feb, sharing thoughts on family, hope, culture or simple wonders. The project invites a democratic snapshot of Australian voices for the universe.
