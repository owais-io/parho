Peter Vandermeersch, a senior editor for the Dutch paper De Telegraaf and Irish Independent, was suspended after he admitted to using AI tools to create and publish fake quotes. He let AI hallucinate words, then posted them as real statements in his newsletter without checking them. The mistake was caught by an internal review, and his articles have been removed. Mediahuis said it will enforce stricter AI use rules.
