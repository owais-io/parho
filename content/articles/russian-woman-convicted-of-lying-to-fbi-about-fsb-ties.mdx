Nomma Zarubina, a 35‑year‑old Russian national, is in a New York jail after pleading guilty to lying to the FBI about her contacts with Russia’s FSB. She was recruited to meet U.S. politicians, academics and media figures, aiming to steer them toward Moscow. In addition to false statements, she admitted to naturalization fraud over past prostitution charges. She faces up to five years per count and will be sentenced in June.
