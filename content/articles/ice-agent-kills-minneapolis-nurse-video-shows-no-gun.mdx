Alex Pretti, a 37‑year‑old nurse, was shot dead in Minneapolis by federal immigration agents during a protest. Footage shows him filming the agents, not brandishing a gun, and witnesses say he wasn’t armed. Yet officials say he was a threat. The incident follows a similar killing of Renee Good. The shooting sparked outrage from politicians, activists, and the NBA, and raised questions about ICE’s presence in the city.
