Quinton Aaron, known for “The Blind Side,” was put on life support after a severe blood infection left him weak and unable to breathe on his own. His wife said he lost feeling in his legs, collapsed, and was intubated at the hospital. Doctors are running tests to find the cause while he stays on antibiotics. A GoFundMe has raised over $37,000 to help cover his medical costs.
