At a 26 January Invasion Day rally in Perth, a homemade bomb was thrown into a crowded square. Police quickly moved in, but the incident left many Indigenous participants terrified and upset. The attack is being investigated as a possible terrorist act, and several Indigenous leaders say the police response was slow and that the community feels unsafe and ignored. Many people are grieving and calling for more protection.
