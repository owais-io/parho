Nissan says it might shut its Sunderland plant if the UK is left out of new EU rules that give subsidies only to cars made inside the EU. UK car makers are worried the rules could hurt £70bn of trade between the UK and the EU. The plan could put UK cars at a disadvantage and might trigger trade fights. The UK is pushing to keep its status as a trusted partner.
