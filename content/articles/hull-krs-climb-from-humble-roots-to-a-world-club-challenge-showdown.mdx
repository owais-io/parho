Hull KR, once a second‑tier club, is now set to play in the World Club Challenge against Australia’s Brisbane Broncos. Owner Neil Hudgell has guided the team from modest crowds and financial trouble to a Super League treble last year. To handle the demand, they moved the match to a larger venue in Hull, filling 25,000 seats in record time. The game could secure Hull’s first world title.
