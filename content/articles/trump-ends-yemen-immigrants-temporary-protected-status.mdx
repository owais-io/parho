The U.S. Home Security Department announced it will stop giving Temporary Protected Status (TPS) to Yemeni immigrants. TPS lets people already in the U.S. stay and work when their country faces crisis. About 1,400 Yemenis have TPS, but the Trump administration says Yemen no longer needs the protection. The change takes effect 60 days after the notice, ending TPS in March.
