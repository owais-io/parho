Two parents and their teenage boys were found dead in a Perth house, police say. The 50‑year‑old man, 49‑year‑old wife and their 14‑ and 16‑year‑old sons died, and a note was left at the home. Police think it was a murder‑suicide, even though no weapon was found. The boys had health challenges and support workers had visited the house before. The investigation is ongoing.
