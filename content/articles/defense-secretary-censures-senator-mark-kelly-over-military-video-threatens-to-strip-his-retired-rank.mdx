Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth formally censured Senator Mark Kelly and started a process that could take away Kelly’s retired navy rank and pension. Hegseth says Kelly’s 2023 video urged service members to resist what he called illegal orders, labeling it seditious. Kelly, a former astronaut and navy captain, blasted the move and said he would fight it. The Pentagon has not commented further.
