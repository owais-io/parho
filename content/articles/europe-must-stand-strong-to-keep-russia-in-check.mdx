The letter argues that Europe has to step up its support for Ukraine because the U.S. is no longer prioritising the region. It warns that without clear U.S. backing, Russia could feel free to test its limits, especially if it wins in Ukraine. The writer points out the risk of European countries not sharing the cost of freezing Russian assets. Europe needs to use its economic and military strength to protect itself and Ukraine from Russian aggression.
