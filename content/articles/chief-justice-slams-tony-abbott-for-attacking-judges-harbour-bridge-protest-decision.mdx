Chief Justice Andrew Bell criticised former PM Tony Abbott for his X post attacking Judge Belinda Rigg’s ruling that closed the Sydney Harbour Bridge for a pro‑Palestine march. Bell said Abbott’s comments were mistaken and ignored the court’s legal reasoning, calling them a threat to trust in the judiciary. He defended the judge’s decision as a lawful balance of free speech and public order, not a political move. Abbott insists it’s an executive issue, not a court one.
