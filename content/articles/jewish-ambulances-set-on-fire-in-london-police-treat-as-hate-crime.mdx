Four Jewish community ambulances were set on fire in north London’s Golders Green, causing damage but no injuries. Police say the attack is an antisemitic hate crime. The mayor and prime minister called it a cowardly assault on the Jewish community. Investigators are looking for three suspects, checking CCTV and online footage, and urging anyone with info to come forward.
