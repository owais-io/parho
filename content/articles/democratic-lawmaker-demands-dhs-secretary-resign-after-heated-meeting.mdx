Illinois Rep. Delia Ramirez met with DHS Secretary Kristi Noem to urge her resignation, calling the meeting “combative.” Ramirez criticized Noem’s handling of immigration enforcement, citing officer use of force, violations of court orders, and a lack of due process. She said Noem lied during the discussion and warned that if she stays, she could face impeachment or dismissal. The meeting comes amid a government shutdown that is affecting DHS funding.
