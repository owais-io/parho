Chelsea and Arsenal played a tense 1‑1 match at Stamford Bridge. Chelsea took the lead with a corner, but Arsenal equalised after a stoppage‑time goal. Midfield defender Moisés Caicedo was sent off, leaving Chelsea with ten men. Despite fighting back and limiting Arsenal’s shots, Chelsea’s discipline slipped, showing they still need to learn how to stay calm under pressure. The game shows Chelsea are improving but not yet a real title threat.
