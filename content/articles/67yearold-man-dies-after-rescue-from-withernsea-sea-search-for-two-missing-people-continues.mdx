A 67‑year‑old man died after rescue teams pulled him from the rough waters off Withernsea in East Yorkshire. Police are still looking for two other people who are missing in the same area. The sea was up to 10 feet high, very cold and windy. Helicopters, ambulances and lifeboats worked in tough conditions. The RNLI warned that cold water can shock people and urged everyone to stay safe.
