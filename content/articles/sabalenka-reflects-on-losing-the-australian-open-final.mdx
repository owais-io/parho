Sabalenka lost to Elena Rybakina in the Australian Open final, dropping her grand slam final record to 4‑4. The Belarusian has reached almost every major final in recent years, but nerves have kept her from closing out big matches. She’s worked with a sports psychologist and admits pressure hurts her. At 27, she hopes to turn mental lapses into titles and become a tennis legend.
