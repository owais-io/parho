In Hartlepool, a group of 21 Labour councillors say the national government has betrayed them by giving too little money for children in care. They need an extra £3 million to balance their budget but have been offered only a small increase that would cover just six kids. The councillors are ready to quit the party unless more funds are provided, warning that key services could close.
