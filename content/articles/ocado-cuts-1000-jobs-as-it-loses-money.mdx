Ocado, once hailed as the future of online grocery, is now cutting 1,000 jobs and reporting bigger losses. Shares fell more than 6%, and the company has struggled to grow its robotic warehouses in the US and Canada. Competitors like Tesco and Sainsbury’s are building their own delivery networks, making Ocado’s expensive model harder to win. Investors doubt it will turn profitable soon.
