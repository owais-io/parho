Casemiro has decided to leave Manchester United when his contract ends this summer because the club chose not to extend it. The move frees up money for United to buy younger midfielders as they rebuild. He earned £430,000 a week and played 146 games, scoring 21. Meanwhile, Chelsea are looking to bring in Brazilian midfielder Douglas Luiz on loan from Juventus, hoping he can fill a gap in their midfield.
