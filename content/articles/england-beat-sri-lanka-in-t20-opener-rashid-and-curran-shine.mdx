England won the opening T20 of their Sri Lanka tour, beating the visitors 11 runs by the DLS method after rain cut the game short. Spin bowler Adil Rashid took 3 for 19, while Sam Curran achieved a hat‑trick. England’s Phil Salt made 46 and Tom Banton added a quick 29, easing the chase. Curran’s hat‑trick was only the second of its kind for England in a T20 match.
