A 31‑year‑old prisoner in Wakefield, Heba Muraisi, has been on a hunger strike for 71 days and says she’s dying. Doctors warn her heart and lungs are failing, and her body is losing weight fast. She and other activists want a fair trial, early bail, and the UK to stop supporting an Israeli arms firm. Writers and academics have signed a letter urging the government to act.
