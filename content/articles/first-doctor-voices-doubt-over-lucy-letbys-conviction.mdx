The Netflix documentary follows Lucy Letby, a nurse jailed for killing seven babies, and shows how doctors and experts now doubt the conviction. It explains that the prosecution relied on a controversial medical study and that many specialists argue the babies died of natural causes. In the film, Dr John Gibbs, a former hospital consultant, openly admits a tiny possibility of a wrongful conviction, the first such admission from a doctor.
