An 18‑year‑old student, Jayden Ryan Wallace, was driving a car after a toilet‑paper prank at a Georgia high school. When the teacher, Jason Hughes, slipped while leaving his home, the car hit him, killing him. The student and four classmates were charged with vehicular homicide and other offenses, but all were released on bond. Both the teacher’s family and the student’s parents say they’re deeply sorry and wish to drop the charges.
