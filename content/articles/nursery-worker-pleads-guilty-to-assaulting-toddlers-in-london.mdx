Vincent Chan, a 45‑year‑old nursery worker, pleaded guilty in 2025 to 26 charges of sexually assaulting toddlers at a London nursery. He filmed some attacks with classroom iPads and took thousands of indecent images. Police discovered him after a whistleblower raised concerns in 2024. About 700 parents have been notified. Families blame the nursery’s safety checks for failing, and an external review is underway before he is sentenced in January.
