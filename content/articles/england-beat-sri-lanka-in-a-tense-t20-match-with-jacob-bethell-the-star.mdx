England’s win over Sri Lanka at Pallekele was a dramatic 12‑run victory. Jacob Bethell, a part‑time bowler, took four wickets for 11 runs and finished the game with a final wicket. Sri Lanka were bundled out for 128, and England’s spinners ripped through the tail in the final overs. England now heads into the World Cup with a clean sweep of the series.
