Three prisoners linked to Palestine Action, who have been refusing food for over two months, are now seriously ill. A doctor says they are past the critical stage and could suffer irreversible damage. The hunger strike started in November, demanding bail, ending a ban, and stopping censorship. Prison officials say they will monitor health but won’t meet the strikers, while supporters fear the government is ignoring the situation.
