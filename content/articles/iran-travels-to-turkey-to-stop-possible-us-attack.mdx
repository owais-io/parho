After tensions rise over Iran’s nuclear program, its foreign minister Abbas Araghchi flew to Ankara to meet Turkish leaders and try to stop a possible U.S. strike. Turkey, led by President Erdoğan, has offered to host video calls between President Trump and Iran’s officials. U.S. officials say they’re ready to attack if Iran refuses to give up enrichment and missile programs, while Iran builds drones and missiles as a deterrent.
