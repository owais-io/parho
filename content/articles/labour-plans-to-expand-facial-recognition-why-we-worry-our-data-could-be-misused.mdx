Labour wants the police to use facial‑recognition cameras everywhere, saying it will help catch criminals and free up police time. But the article warns that online data is never safe and governments can abuse it. Past plans, like a national health‑record database, failed because people didn’t trust the promised safeguards. The author says the state could use facial data to track and target ordinary people, so we should be cautious.
