Chelsea’s new head coach Liam Rosenior is proud of a strong start, winning six of his first seven matches. After a tense 2‑0 home loss to West Ham that ended in a dramatic comeback, fans were still booing. Rosenior says he wants supporters to see him as the club’s best hiring decision and admits his job is to boost morale and win the fans over while keeping the team focused on winning.
