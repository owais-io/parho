Brighton defeated Manchester United 2‑0 in the FA Cup third‑round, with goals from Brajan Gruda and Danny Welbeck. The loss means United, a 13‑time winner, is out of the competition for the first time since 1981‑82, adding to a season already marred by poor results and an interim manager, Darren Fletcher, who may soon be replaced. With their lowest game total since 1914‑15, United’s trophy hopes have vanished.
