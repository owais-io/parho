Ben Sasse, the former U.S. senator from Nebraska, told people on X that he has stage‑four pancreatic cancer and is dying. He said the diagnosis is a “death sentence.” The 53‑year‑old, who left the Senate in 2023, had earlier voted to impeach Donald Trump. He added that the news is hard for him and his family. Pancreatic cancer is a deadly disease.
