An aide to former prince Andrew, David Stern, told Jeffrey Epstein in 2012 to hide his child‑abuse conviction on a Chinese visa application. The plan was to avoid ticking boxes about prior charges. Epstein, who had served an 18‑month sentence in 2008, never went to China. The emails were found in new Epstein files. Police are now looking into whether the royal aide helped breach the law.
