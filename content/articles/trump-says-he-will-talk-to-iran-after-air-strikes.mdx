Trump said he’d talk to Iran’s new leaders after the US‑Israel air attacks that killed Iran’s supreme leader. The strikes, which also hit cities in Iran and Lebanon, have caused dozens of deaths and sparked missile counter‑attacks that damaged airports in the Gulf. Oil prices jumped and shipping slowed. Iran says it won’t negotiate, but Trump thinks talks could start soon. Americans are largely against the strikes.
