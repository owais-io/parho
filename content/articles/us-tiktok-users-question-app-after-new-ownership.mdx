TikTok, owned by Chinese company ByteDance, recently set up a new U.S. company mainly owned by U.S. investors to avoid a ban. The change sparked worries about how much data TikTok collects and whether it censors videos. U.S. users have been deleting the app more than twice as often, and some creators say their posts about ICE or Trump were blocked. The platform says tech problems caused some of the issues.
