Budget 2025 keeps tax rules the same until 2031, so most will pay a bit more of their income. The national living wage rises to £12.71 an hour, giving low‑wage workers a small lift. Energy bills will be cut by about £150 a year and rail fares will stay flat for a year. From 2029, pension contributions via salary sacrifice will hit a £2,000 cap.
