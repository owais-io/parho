Prosecutors say three members of Nigel Farage’s former party were given talking points by an alleged Russian asset and used them in interviews for a pro‑Russian TV channel. The Crown Prosecution Service named Jonathan Bullock, Julia Reid and Steven Woolfe. No evidence shows they knew the script was paid for, and none have been charged. The investigation is still ongoing, and Labour MPs want Farage to help the police.
