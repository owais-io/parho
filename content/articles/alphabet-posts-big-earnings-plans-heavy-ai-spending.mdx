Alphabet, the parent of Google, earned $34.5 billion in the last quarter and expects to spend $175‑$185 billion on capital this year—almost double what analysts predicted. The company is pouring money into AI, building new data centers and using AI tools across its business. While revenue jumped 48% from cloud services and profit beat estimates, investors worry the huge spend could affect future returns.
