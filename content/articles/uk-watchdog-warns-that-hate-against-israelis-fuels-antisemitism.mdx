Jonathan Hall, who reviews UK terror laws, says attacks on Israelis are a form of racial hatred that can spark wider anti‑Jewish hate. He urges police to treat Israelis the same as any other citizens and says ignoring these attacks is a security risk. Some pro‑Palestine groups argue Hall blurs anti‑Zionism with antisemitism. The debate highlights clashes over protests, banning Israeli football fans and police response to anti‑Israeli demonstrations.
