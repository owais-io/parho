Steve Smith has moved into second place on the Ashes run‑scoring list, beating Jack Hobbs by a single run. He reached 3,637 runs and 12 centuries, the same milestones Hobbs had held. The record was set during the fifth Test in Sydney, where Smith scored an unbeaten 129. The achievement underscores Smith’s long‑term dominance in the series, although Bradman still tops the list by a huge margin.
