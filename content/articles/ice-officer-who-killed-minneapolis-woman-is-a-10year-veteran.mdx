An ICE officer named Jonathan E Ross, a 10‑year veteran, shot 37‑year‑old Renee Nicole Good during an immigration sweep in Minneapolis. Good, a mother of three, died in the incident that sparked protests and anger from city leaders. The officer was identified after court records linked him to a similar case in Bloomington. Federal officials say he acted per training and won’t comment, while the governor worries about a fair outcome.
