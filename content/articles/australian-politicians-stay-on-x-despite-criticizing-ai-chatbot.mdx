Prime Minister Anthony Albanese slammed X’s AI chatbot Grok for creating sexualised images of people, calling it “abhorrent” and saying Australians deserve better. Regulators are investigating and some countries have blocked the bot. Yet most Australian politicians keep posting on X, even after the controversy, because the platform reaches a large audience and offers engagement. Some have moved to other services, but many stay.
