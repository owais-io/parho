Former Liberal MP Katie Allen, who represented Victoria’s Higgins seat from 2019 to 2022, has died at 59 after a battle with rare bile‑duct cancer. She was a pediatric doctor before politics, a mother, wife, and teacher. The family announced the death on Facebook. Tributes poured in from the prime minister, opposition leader, and former colleagues who praised her kindness, intelligence and dedication to people.
