Zbigniew Ziobro, the former Polish justice minister from the Law and Justice party, was granted political asylum in Hungary after Poland opened an investigation into 26 charges of abuse of power. He says the case is a political attack by the new pro‑EU government and wants to stay abroad until he sees fair treatment. Polish officials say he’s a “coward,” while Hungary said it has given asylum to several people it sees as persecuted.
