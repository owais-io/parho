UK retail sales climbed 0.4% in December, beating expectations, largely because online sales jumped 4.2%. Online jewelers saw a surge in gold and silver demand, helping lift overall figures. Economists say this rebound shows shoppers are feeling more confident after the budget’s impact was milder than feared. Although 2025 sales grew 1.3%, they’re still about 2% below pre‑pandemic levels.
