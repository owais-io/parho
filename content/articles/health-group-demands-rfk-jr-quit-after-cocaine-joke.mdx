A health‑advocacy group, Protect Our Care, said the US Health Secretary, Robert F. Kennedy Jr., should quit after he joked about snorting cocaine on a toilet seat during a podcast. The comment, along with his anti‑vaccine stance, mishandling of measles outbreaks, push for a meat‑heavy diet, and low public approval, has sparked criticism. The group’s brief “Resign” statement highlights growing distrust in his leadership.
