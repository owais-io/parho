Team GB suffered a third defeat in a day when mixed‑doubles curling lost the bronze medal match 5‑3 to Italy. Chef de mission Eve Muirhead urged the squad to stay positive and build momentum for the rest of the Games. The Scottish pair, Bruce Mouat and Jen Dodds, had dominated the round‑robin but fell to Sweden in the semis, then to Italy for bronze. The team hopes to regroup for upcoming events.
