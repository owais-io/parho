California governor Gavin Newsom said his planned talk at the World Economic Forum in Davos was cancelled because the Trump administration pressured the USA House, which hosts the U.S. pavilion. Newsom had been invited by Fortune for a fireside chat after Trump’s speech, but officials denied him entry, citing program constraints. The governor slammed the move and called Trump’s influence on the event “pathetic.”
