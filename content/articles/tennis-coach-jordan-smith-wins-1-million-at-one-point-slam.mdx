Jordan Smith, a tennis coach from Castle Hill, Australia, shocked everyone by winning a $1 million prize at the One Point Slam. He beat top players such as Jannik Sinner and Pedro Martinez, earning a huge media spotlight and hundreds of interviews. Smith is still figuring out tax and whether he’ll use the money for a home or a life change. He remains relaxed and grateful, keeping his smile and love for the game.
