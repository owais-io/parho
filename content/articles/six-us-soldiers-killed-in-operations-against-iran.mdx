Six U.S. service members have died during the U.S. and Israel's Operation Epic Fury, a series of attacks on Iran. The Central Command says the casualties include soldiers killed in a base in Kuwait, and that three were found in a struck facility. The operation is still ongoing, with U.S. forces targeting Iranian missile sites and the supreme leader's compound. Meanwhile, the U.S. has warned Americans abroad to leave the region.
