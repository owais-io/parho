Former Liverpool striker Ian Rush was discharged from hospital after two days in intensive care for flu complications. He was admitted to the Countess of Chester hospital with breathing problems and was successfully treated. Rush, 64, left the ICU on Monday and is now recovering at home. Liverpool thanked the staff for caring for their legend and ambassador. The 346‑goal scorer had a long career, winning titles with Liverpool and playing for Wales.
