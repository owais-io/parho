Dina Asher‑Smith reclaimed the UK indoor 60m title in Birmingham, running a championship‑record 7.05 seconds. The 30‑year‑old beat rival Amy Hunt (7.15) after a winter of new training in Texas. She said happiness and confidence helped her bounce back from last year’s setbacks. Asher‑Smith will now compete next month at the World Indoor Championships in Poland, while her rival Hunt will focus on longer sprints.
