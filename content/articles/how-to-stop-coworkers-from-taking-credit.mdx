A new book calls people who steal credit “office hookworms.” They sneak in like the parasite, then brag about others’ work. The author suggests you can’t change them, but you can protect yourself: speak confidently, give praise to teammates, and keep evidence ready for HR. The best defence is to keep your own reputation clear and let the hookworm run out of room.
