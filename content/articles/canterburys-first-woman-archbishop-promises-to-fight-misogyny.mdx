Sarah Mullally was officially confirmed as the first female archbishop of Canterbury. She pledged to speak out against misogyny, to listen to people’s concerns, and to open a space for partnership. The move has drawn criticism from some African churches that oppose women in top church roles. Mullally also faces scrutiny over her past safeguarding record in London and will keep a door open for further investigation.
