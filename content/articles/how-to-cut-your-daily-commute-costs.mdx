Buy season tickets to lock in lower fares, or try flexi passes if you only travel a few days a week. Move your work hours to off‑peak times, book tickets early, and grab a railcard for extra discounts. Don’t forget to claim Delay Repay if trains run late. For car users, shop for cheaper fuel, keep tyres inflated, and combine errands. Pack your lunch, use loyalty points, and turn commute time into productive work or learning.
