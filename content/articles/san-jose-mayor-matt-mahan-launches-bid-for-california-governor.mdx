Matt Mahan, the 43‑year‑old mayor of San Jose, has announced he will run for California governor. A former tech entrepreneur, Mahan says his practical style has made San Jose the safest big city in the U.S. and cut homelessness, though he’s also backed a tough‑on‑crime law. He plans to focus on state issues like homelessness and high living costs, rather than national politics, and joins a crowded Democratic field.
