Julian Ingram, 37, was convicted in 2022 for assaulting a family member. He is now suspected of killing his pregnant ex‑partner, her boyfriend and her aunt in Lake Cargelligo, New South Wales. Police granted him bail for earlier domestic‑violence charges, sparking criticism. A manhunt is underway while authorities investigate how the police handled his case and review his history of violence and firearms access.
