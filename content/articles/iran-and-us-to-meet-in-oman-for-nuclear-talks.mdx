Iran and the United States are set to hold nuclear talks in Muscat, Oman, on Friday. The meeting follows last‑minute appeals by Arab states to keep the U.S. in negotiations. While the U.S. wants the talks to cover Iran’s nuclear program, missiles, terrorism and human rights, Iran insists it will discuss only nuclear matters. Tensions are high, with the U.S. moving naval forces to the region and having downed an Iranian drone.
