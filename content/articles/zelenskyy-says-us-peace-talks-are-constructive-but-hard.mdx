Zelenskyy told his nightly address that talks with US representatives about a peace plan were constructive but not easy. He plans meetings in London with UK, France, and Germany to keep the discussion going. Italy’s prime minister reaffirmed support and sent generators to help Ukraine. UK foreign secretary Yvette Cooper will meet her US counterpart to push for a deal. Trump’s team is close to a final plan, but neither side has signed yet. Russia continues attacks, killing four people in recent strikes.
