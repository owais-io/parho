Vladyslav Heraskevych, a Ukrainian skeleton racer, was banned from the Winter Olympics after he wanted to wear a helmet that showed pictures of 24 Ukrainian victims of Russia. His appeal to the Court of Arbitration for Sport was denied, and he flew to Munich to receive the Freedom Order from President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. Zelenskyy praised his courage and said remembrance is not a violation, honoring his stance.
