OpenAI is putting its plan to let users see adult material in ChatGPT on hold. The company says it needs to focus on bigger priorities, like making the chatbot smarter and safer, especially for younger users. It is also working on better age‑checking tools. Meanwhile, a senior manager quit over concerns about a new Pentagon deal that could let the AI be used for surveillance or lethal weapons.
