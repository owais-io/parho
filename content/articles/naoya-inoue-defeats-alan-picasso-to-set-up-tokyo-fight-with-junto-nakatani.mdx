Naoya Inoue beat Alan Picasso in Riyadh, keeping his super‑bantamweight titles. The Japanese star won all three judges’ scorecards, showing speed, power and body shots. Picasso tried hard but couldn't match Inoue’s timing. With this win, Inoue is ready for a highly‑anticipated fight against fellow Japanese champ Junto Nakatani. Fans expect the two undefeated fighters to clash in Tokyo next year.
