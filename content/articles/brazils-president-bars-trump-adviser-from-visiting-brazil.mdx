Brazil’s President Luiz Lula said he would bar former Trump adviser Darren Beattie from entering the country because the U.S. had denied a visa to Brazil’s health minister, Alexandre Padilha. Beattie had planned to visit jailed ex‑president Jair Bolsonaro, who is on a 27‑year sentence for a coup plot. Lula’s move shows the strained ties between Brazil and Washington after Trump’s pressure campaign, even though the two leaders had met at the UN.
