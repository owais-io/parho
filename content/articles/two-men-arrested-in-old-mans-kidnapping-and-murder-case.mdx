Police have taken two men into custody after an 85‑year‑old Sydney resident, Chris Baghsarian, was kidnapped and murdered. The men were stopped at their family homes and later searched for evidence in nearby properties, including a burned‑out car. Baghsarian’s body was found in Pitt Town and is being identified. Police say the kidnappers mistakenly targeted him and expect to charge them with kidnapping and murder. The family wants privacy.
