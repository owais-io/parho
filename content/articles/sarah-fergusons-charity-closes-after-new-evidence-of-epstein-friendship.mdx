Sarah Ferguson’s charity, Sarah’s Trust, announced it will shut down after a trove of 3 million files released by the U.S. Justice Department revealed the former duchess had a close friendship with convicted sex‑offender Jeffrey Epstein. The files show Ferguson calling Epstein “brother” and praising him, fueling criticism that she used the relationship to get closer to Prince Andrew. The trust had worked with over 60 charities and delivered aid worldwide, but many partners have already severed ties.
