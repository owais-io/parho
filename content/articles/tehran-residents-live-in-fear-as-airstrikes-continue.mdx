Residents in Tehran are living in constant fear as U.S. and Israeli airstrikes hit the city. Many have moved to the countryside or are trying to leave. Schools stay closed while hospitals keep running. Despite internet blackouts, people check the skies from their roofs, and some even jog or visit the pool when the bombardment stops. Tension, hope, and quiet celebrations mix as the situation drags on.
