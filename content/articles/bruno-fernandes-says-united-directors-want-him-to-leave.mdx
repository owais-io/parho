Bruno Fernandes says Manchester United directors tried to push him to leave. He said a Saudi club offered huge money, and he felt the club’s bosses were ready to sell him. Fernandes chose to stay because of family and love for the club, but the directors’ attitude hurt him. He also criticized teammates who don’t care as much as he does. He’s still a key player for United.
