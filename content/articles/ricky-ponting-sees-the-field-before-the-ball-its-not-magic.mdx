Ponting seems to see the field before the ball comes, but it’s not magic. Modern cricket uses data and analysts to set field positions and choose bowlers. Experts look at statistics, past matchups, and players’ weaknesses. Captains combine this info with gut feeling to make decisions. The story shows how the sport blends numbers and intuition, making a perfect prediction feel almost prophetic.
