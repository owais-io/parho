Pauline Hanson, a One Nation MP, used taxpayer money to charter a private plane that cost almost $9,000. She flew from Tamworth to Geelong for a ceremony honoring Gina Rinehart’s donation to a private agribusiness college. The trip sparked criticism because it appears more like a personal invitation than official parliamentary business, and Hanson has faced similar scrutiny before.
