UK officials are starting trials that could ban social media for kids under 16. In the first test, about 150 teens aged 13‑15 will either have no social‑media access, one hour a day, or overnight screen curfews. Researchers will track their sleep, mood and activity. The government is also consulting on age limits, addictive features, AI chatbots and gaming. Some child‑safety groups back a ban; others warn it might push teens online.
