A Qatari military cargo plane carrying 104 security staff and vehicles crashed into a lighting tower at Milan’s Malpensa airport while landing. The team is part of Italy’s plan to guard the upcoming Winter Olympics, even though Qatar isn’t competing. The incident happened amid criticism of U.S. ICE agents also in the security mix, and protests against ICE are planned in Milan.
