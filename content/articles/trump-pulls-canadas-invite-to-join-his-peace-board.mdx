Donald Trump just canceled his invitation to Canada for his new ‘Board of Peace’ that he announced at the World Economic Forum in Davos. The board, which he plans to chair, would focus on Gaza’s reconstruction and would cost $1 billion per member. Canada had been open but said it would not pay the fee, and other Western nations have so far declined to join. Trump said the board would work with the UN.
