NYC Health + Hospitals said it will not renew its $4 million contract with data‑analysis firm Palantir, which was meant to last only a few months. The city says it will switch to in‑house tools and keep patient data private. The move comes after protests over Palantir’s UK NHS contract and worries that the company could link de‑identified data to other uses. Activists say the decision shows hospitals can stand up to tech firms.
