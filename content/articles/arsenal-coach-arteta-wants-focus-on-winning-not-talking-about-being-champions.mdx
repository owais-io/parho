Arteta says he’s the most motivated to win the Premier League and wants his players to ignore chatter about being “champions‑elect.” Arsenal are 7 points clear, with a solid defence and good set‑piece work. The team faces Manchester United next, after two go‑all‑draws. He stresses staying in the present and early preparation, noting that injuries are minor and Guardiola praised Arsenal recently. He’s ready to keep the focus on winning.
