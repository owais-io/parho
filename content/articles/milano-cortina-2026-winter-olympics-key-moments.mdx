Milano Cortina 2026 brought the Winter Olympics back to Italy after 20 years. The Games were split between Milan and Cortina d’Ampezzo, featuring 16 sports and 110 gold medals. Fans cheered alpine stars like Mikaela Shiffrin, figure skaters such as Ilia Malinin, and Britain’s hopeful skaters Lilah Fear and Lewis Gibson. Speed‑skating hero Arianna Fontana competed for Italy, while curling’s precision wowed viewers, especially as Britain’s Bruce Mouat led his team.
