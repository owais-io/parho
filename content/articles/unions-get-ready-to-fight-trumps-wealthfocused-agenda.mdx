AFL‑CIO chief Liz Shuler says U.S. unions are getting ready to fight President Trump’s “Billionaire First” plan. They’re pushing to restore federal workers’ right to bargain, filing lawsuits against Trump’s cuts, and backing candidates who support jobs and affordable health care. Unions are also joining strikes, like the Starbucks baristas, to show workers are struggling. With about 68% of Americans backing unions, the movement plans to use this support in the 2026 elections.
