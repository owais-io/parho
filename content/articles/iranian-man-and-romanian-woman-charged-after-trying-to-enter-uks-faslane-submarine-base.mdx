Two people, an Iranian man and a Romanian woman, were arrested after trying to sneak into the UK's Faslane submarine base in Scotland. Police say the pair attempted to enter HM Naval Base Clyde, which houses the Trident nuclear submarines. Both are charged and will appear in court next week. Authorities are investigating the incident to strengthen base security. The incident highlights concerns over security at key defence sites.
