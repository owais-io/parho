In a Gaza town that has been hit by fighting, a woman shares how her Ramadan is very different from before. She lives in a rented room instead of her home, hears drones and the threat of bombs, and has to stretch a far‑larger budget just to feed her family for the month. Despite the hardship, neighbors share food and light a candle each night to keep hope alive.
