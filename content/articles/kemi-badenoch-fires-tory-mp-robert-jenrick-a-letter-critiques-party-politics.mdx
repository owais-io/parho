In a Guardian letter, writer criticises Kemi Badenoch for firing MP Robert Jenrick. The author says the move shows Westminster politicians care more about party drama than real work. He calls for stricter rules and warns that if Jenrick leaves, more Tory defections could follow, threatening a dangerous Tory‑Reform partnership. He urges other parties to unite to stop it politically.
