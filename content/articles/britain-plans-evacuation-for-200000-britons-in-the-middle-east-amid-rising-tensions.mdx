Britain is preparing to pull its people out of the Middle East because of danger, especially in Dubai where many tourists are stuck. The government says about 200,000 Britons are in the region and is asking them to sign up for help. Airspace is closed, so the UK is looking at road routes to safer countries and working with the military to keep citizens safe. Officials say they are not backing US or Israeli attacks on Iran, but are ready to defend Britain if needed.
