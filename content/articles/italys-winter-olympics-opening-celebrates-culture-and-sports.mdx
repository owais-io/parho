The opening ceremony in Milan’s San Siro and Cortina d’Ampezzo celebrated Italy’s art, music, and fashion. Stars like Mariah Carey performed “Volare,” while giant papier‑mâché figures of famous composers danced. The event honored fashion icon Giorgio Armani and showcased Italian heritage. Athletes paraded from around the world, and the ceremony wrapped with a national anthem. Meanwhile, Lindsey Vonn began a downhill training run, keeping her Olympic medal hopes alive.
