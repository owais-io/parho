Russia and China back Venezuela as the US intensifies pressure on its leader, Nicolás Maduro. Trump has announced a blockade of sanctioned oil tankers, causing loading at Venezuelan ports to slow and many ships to sit idle. He urges Maduro to leave power, linking Venezuela’s oil to drug trafficking. Meanwhile, China and Russia criticize the US moves, saying they’re illegal and harmful to shipping. Maduro says the US should focus on domestic issues.
