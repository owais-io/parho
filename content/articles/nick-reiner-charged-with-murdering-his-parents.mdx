Nick Reiner, 32, the son of director Rob Reiner and his wife Michele, was arrested after his parents were found stabbed to death near Los Angeles. He faces two first‑degree murder charges and is held without bail. The medical examiner says they died minutes after the attack. Reiner has a history of mental illness and addiction and had worked with his dad on a film about his struggles. A conviction could lead to the death penalty or life without parole.
