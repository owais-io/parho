Sunderland beat Burnley 3‑1 on a cold Wearside night, with Chemsdine Talbi delivering a stunning hat‑trick that lifted his side into eighth place and within reach of European spots. Habib Diarra also scored, while Burnley’s manager Scott Parker criticised his team’s lack of effort and structure. The loss puts Burnley 11 points from safety in the Premier League.
