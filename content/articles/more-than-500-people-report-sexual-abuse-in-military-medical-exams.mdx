More than 500 people have reached out to police about sexual abuse that happened during army, navy and air force medical exams over nearly 50 years. The investigation, led by Wiltshire police, now covers all three services. Most complainants are women aged 16‑25, and incidents date from the 1970s to 2016. Police say the numbers could rise as more survivors come forward, and they urge anyone with info to speak up.
