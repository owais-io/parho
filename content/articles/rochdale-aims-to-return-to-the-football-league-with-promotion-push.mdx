Rochdale saved from liquidation in 2023 and are now top of the National League thanks to a new owner and strong performances. The club’s recent 2-0 win over Eastleigh kept them at the summit, while a new pitch overhaul is fixing waterlogging issues. They still have a few games in hand and are close with Carlisle, Forest Green and York. If they stay strong, they could win automatic promotion and return to the Football League.
