US and Israel have been fighting Iran for six days. US forces say they’re ready to give air help to Kurdish fighters in northern Iraq if they launch a cross‑border attack. Kurdish officials say their groups are preparing to hit Iran, but the Kurdistan president insists his region won’t get dragged into the war. Meanwhile, Iranian drones have struck an Azerbaijani airport, and Iran claims it hit a US tanker, adding more tension.
