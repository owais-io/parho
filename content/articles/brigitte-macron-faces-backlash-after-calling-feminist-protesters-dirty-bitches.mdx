In a backstage clip at a Paris theatre, First Lady Brigitte Macron used the slur “sales connes” (dirty bitches) to describe feminist activists who had disrupted a comedian’s show. The video sparked outrage from left‑wing politicians, celebrities, and feminist groups demanding an apology. Some politicians called her comments vulgar; others said it was private. The incident shows the tensions over protest tactics and language.
