Fans of Stranger Things made up a theory that the finale was fake and that a secret episode would air on January 7. They pointed to small details in the show as proof, even though many were just continuity mistakes or doctored images. The hype made so many people try to stream the non‑existent episode that Netflix crashed. The show’s creators said the finale was complete and the conspiracy was just misinformation. Fans remain disappointed but some still keep hoping.
