Tennis players’ union, the Professional Tennis Players’ Association (PTPA), has reached a deal with Tennis Australia, which was left out of an antitrust lawsuit against the sport’s big tournaments. The agreement means Tennis Australia will share confidential financial info to help the PTPA sue the ATP, WTA and Grand Slam organizers, while the union keeps its lawsuit going. The move could pressure the other bodies to negotiate and push for higher pay and more player input.
