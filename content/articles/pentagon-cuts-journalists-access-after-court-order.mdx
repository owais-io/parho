The Pentagon announced it will shut the “correspondents’ corridor” and set up a new press space outside the building, sparking backlash from journalists. The change follows a federal judge’s order that voided the department’s new rules and demanded the return of news credentials. Media groups say the policy ignores the court’s decision and limits reporters’ ability to ask questions and get timely information. The court may have to weigh in again.
