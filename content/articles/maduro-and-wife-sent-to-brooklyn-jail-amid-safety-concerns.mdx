Nicolas Maduro and his wife were taken to Brooklyn’s Metropolitan detention center after a U.S. raid in Caracas. The jail is known for holding famous criminals like Diddy and El Chapo, but critics say it’s unsafe, dirty, and overcrowded. Lawyers argue inmates have been killed or harmed, and judges have barred some defendants from being sent there. Despite federal claims of improvement, the couple is awaiting trial and remains in custody.
