The Metropolitan Police said sorry to Commons Speaker Lindsay Hoyle after it accidentally said she was the one who tipped them off that former minister Peter Mandelson was planning to leave the UK. The mistake led to Mandelson’s arrest, even though he says he was not planning to flee. He was later released on bail. The case is tangled with his links to Jeffrey Epstein and is putting pressure on the government.
