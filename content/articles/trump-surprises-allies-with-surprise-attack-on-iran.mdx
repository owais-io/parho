President Trump launched a surprise missile strike on Iranian targets, claiming Israel had been waiting for an opportunity. The attack left U.S. allies, especially in Europe and the Gulf, scrambling because they were not informed and were not asked for permission. European leaders had to rush to condemn the strikes, while Gulf states expressed anger after their airspace was used. The move has raised concerns about U.S. trust and the future of international cooperation in the Middle East.
