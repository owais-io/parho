At 12.33 pm on March 2, a suspected explosive‑filled drone approached Paphos airport, triggering sirens. Cyprus’s emergency services admit most shelters are old, broken or missing, and only about 45% of residents could fit in the current bunkers. The president said action is overdue and plans to set a national coordinator and build more shelters, while the public and opposition demand quick fixes.
