Australia will ban social‑media accounts for anyone under 16 from Dec. 10. Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, X, YouTube, Reddit, Twitch and Kick must shut those accounts and stop new sign‑ups for kids. Platforms must use more than ID checks, or face a $49.5 million fine. Teens can download or archive data and reopen accounts when they turn 16. Adults wrongly flagged can appeal with a video selfie or ID. Some companies will comply; YouTube may sue.
