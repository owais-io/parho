Labour’s top politicians are arguing over whether Andy Burnham, the mayor of Greater Manchester, should try to win the Gorton and Denton byelection. Starmer’s allies say Burnham’s move could damage the prime minister’s fragile popularity and cost the party money, so they want him barred. Burnham’s supporters, including other MPs and trade unions, say he is popular and could win. The party’s national executive will decide.
