Labour’s soft‑left MPs want Prime Minister Keir Starmer to shuffle his cabinet to stop internal fighting. The group, led by Louise Haigh and others, argues that the current team isn’t reflecting Labour values and that policy shifts are needed, especially on the economy and living standards. They also call for the return of Angela Rayner, Louise Haigh and Lucy Powell. The request comes after recent staff exits and a call for Starmer’s resignation from a Scottish Labour leader.
