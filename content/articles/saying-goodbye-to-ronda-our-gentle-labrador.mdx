The author mourns Ronda, a 13‑year‑old Labrador who gave the family endless love and kindness. She was gentle to people, dogs, and kids, even teaching the new pup Olive manners. After a tumor weakened her, the family chose euthanasia. The loss feels as painful as any other, but Ronda’s gentle spirit will stay with the family and inspire Olive.
