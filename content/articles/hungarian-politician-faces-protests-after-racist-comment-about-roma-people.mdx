Thousands gathered in Budapest to call for the resignation of János Lázár after he made a racist comment about Roma people. Lázár said Roma would do menial jobs, using an offensive slur. The outcry comes just weeks before national elections. Roma groups and opposition politicians want him to apologise or quit. The scandal could hurt Fidesz’s poll lead as voters react to the government’s handling of minority rights.
