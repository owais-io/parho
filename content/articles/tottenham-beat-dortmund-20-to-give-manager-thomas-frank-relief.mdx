Tottenham beat Borussia Dortmund 2‑0 at home, a win that gives manager Thomas Frank a break after a rough season. Dominic Solanke scored his first goal of the year and helped keep the German side down to 10 men after a red card. The match was the fourth consecutive home win and clean sheet for Spurs in the Champions League. While the result eases pressure, fans still expect more consistent performances before the next game against Burnley.
