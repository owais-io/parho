U.S. and Israel have teamed up to attack Iran, but their plans are not the same. Trump wants a quick, low‑cost win, while Netanyahu wants to topple Iran’s leadership and change the region’s power balance. The two sides disagree over targets, timing, and what victory looks like. The conflict is driving up costs, hurting oil prices, and leaving U.S. allies unsure how the war will end.
