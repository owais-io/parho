House Democrat Jamie Raskin said the Justice Department made too many redactions in the huge collection of Jeffrey Epstein documents, even putting the names of some victims in the public files. He visited a Washington DC data room, saw the unredacted files, and accused the department of a cover‑up. Raskin wants the DOJ to clean up the files, remove mistakes, and release the remaining millions of documents.
