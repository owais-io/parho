A 41‑year‑old Lebanese‑born U.S. citizen drove a car into the hallway of Temple Israel in Michigan, but security guards stopped him before anyone was hurt. The suspect, Ayman Ghazali, had lost four family members in an Israeli airstrike on his hometown in Lebanon just days earlier. The FBI says the attack was a violent act against the Jewish community, highlighting how Middle‑East tensions affect U.S. places of worship.
