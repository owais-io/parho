On Thanksgiving, SNL had Glen Powell as its first-time host. The show poked fun at Donald Trump, using newly released emails about Jeffrey Epstein as the main joke. The sketches also included a parody of Mac Gruber, a funny “family” sketch, and a gag about AI‑edited old family photos. Powell joked about his acting career and his “Mom” fans, while the episode mixed humor with political satire.
