In a dull Premier League match at the end of the Christmas weekend, Crystal Palace looked listless and their manager, Oliver Glasner, was visibly upset. He grumbled about extra minutes and seemed ready to give up. Palace had already lost six of their last ten games, so nothing much could be done. Meanwhile Tottenham won, with young Archie Gray scoring his first goal for the club, showing a bright side for them.
