In 2020, Derek Chauvin killed George Floyd in Minneapolis, sparking protests. In January 2026, an ICE agent shot Renee Good, a 37‑year‑old mother, near the same location. Good had no criminal record and was simply driving. Video shows the agent shooting even though she posed no threat. The piece criticises ICE’s use‑of‑force rules and urges citizens to demand tighter limits and hold the agency accountable.
