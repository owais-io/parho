19‑year‑old Marcus Casanova, a Santa Clara University freshman, was found dead on California’s Mount Baldy trail. His body, along with two other hikers, was discovered on the Devil’s Backbone path. Rescue teams faced strong winds, using a helicopter and ground teams to reach the site. The cause of death isn’t yet known. Mount Baldy, a popular but risky mountain, will have several trails closed until next Wednesday.
