Storm Leonardo has dumped record rain on Spain, Portugal and Morocco, causing floods, landslides and dozens of evacuations. The slow‑moving system has produced over 700 mm in parts of Andalusia, shut roads and schools, and left a girl missing after the Turvilla river swept her away. Portugal faces a second death after a car was washed away, while Morocco’s Ksar El‑Kebir saw 100,000 residents flee flooded rivers. The weather warns of more rain and danger before the storm moves on.
