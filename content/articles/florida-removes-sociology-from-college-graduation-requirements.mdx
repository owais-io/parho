Florida governor Ron DeSantis has removed sociology from the list of required courses at the state’s 12 public universities. Students can still take the class, but it no longer counts toward graduation. The change is part of DeSantis’s push to curb what he calls “woke” teaching. Board members and chancellor Ray Rodrigues said faculty concerns about a new curriculum led to the move. Some professors say sociology teaches useful skills for many jobs.
