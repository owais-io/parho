Former BBC newsreader Huw Edwards has publicly slammed a Channel 5 TV drama that portrays his alleged wrongdoing. He says the show misrepresents what happened and ignores his input, arguing it unfairly links his mental health struggles to criminal behavior. Edwards claims the drama fails to show the real facts and is produced without his cooperation.
