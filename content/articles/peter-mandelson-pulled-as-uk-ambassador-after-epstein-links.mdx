Peter Mandelson, a former Labour cabinet minister, was removed from his post as UK ambassador to the US after emails showed he helped Jeffrey Epstein get early release. In a BBC interview he tried to explain his role but failed to apologise to Epstein’s victims, sparking outrage. He later issued a brief apology, but the controversy has damaged his reputation and raised doubts about his future in politics.
