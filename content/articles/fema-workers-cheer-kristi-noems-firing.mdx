The U.S. FEMA agency’s workers are cheering the firing of DHS secretary Kristi Noem. They say she micromanaged the agency, cut funding, and even wanted to shut it down. Her tough spending rules delayed rescue teams during Texas floods and stalled billions in aid payments. FEMA staff called her leadership “destructive” and worried the agency will take years to recover. President Trump replaced her with Senator Markwayne Mullin.
