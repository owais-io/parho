Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, has declared an emergency in the energy sector after Russian strikes cut power and heating in many cities. He will boost imports and set up more warming and charging centres for residents. Emergency crews are working 24/7 as night temperatures drop to –20 °C. Kyiv’s mayor said power is almost fully restored, but Zelenskyy criticised the city’s preparation. Trump said Ukraine is ready to negotiate, which clashes with European views.
