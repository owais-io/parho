Accenture is now tracking how staff use its AI tools and will factor that into top promotions. Senior staff must use AI regularly to reach leadership roles. The firm has trained most of its 780,000 employees in generative AI and watches log‑ins to tools like AI Refinery. This move is part of a broader push to embed AI in work and reward early adopters. Some critics see it as corporate jargon.
