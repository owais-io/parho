Puddles are a big part of Britain’s weather, not scary but a bit wet. People don’t have big storms, just little puddles that can splash your shoes. Kids love splashing because it’s fun and curious. While adults worry about getting wet, puddles are more a chance to play than a problem. It shows UK weather is mild and mostly a simple inconvenience. They even appear in stories, cartoons, and poems, making puddles a charming symbol of the UK's gentle weather.
