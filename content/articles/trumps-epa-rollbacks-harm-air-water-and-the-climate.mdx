Trump’s EPA is rolling back many environmental rules. The changes could make the air we breathe and the water we drink dirtier. They also let polluters cut back on safety and could increase climate‑changing emissions. Experts say the moves risk public health and worsen global warming. The agency is also cutting research and staff, reducing its ability to protect the environment.
