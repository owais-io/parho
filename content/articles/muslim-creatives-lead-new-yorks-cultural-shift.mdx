New York’s new mayor, Zohran Mamdani, is backed by a wave of Muslim artists, writers, strategists, and activists who are reshaping the city’s culture. From poets like Hala Alyan to photographers like Dean Majd, they use art to challenge Islamophobia and support Palestinian rights. Community hubs like the Papi Juice collective and Huda restaurant bring people together, while funding bodies and a new magazine give them a platform.
