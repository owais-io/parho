West Brom dropped coach Ryan Mason after a 10th consecutive away loss, ending his 7‑month stint. The club also let two of his staff go. James Morrison will take over in the meantime. The team sits 18th in the Championship, seven points clear of the drop zone and ten behind the play‑off places. They face Swansea in the FA Cup before heading back to league action.
