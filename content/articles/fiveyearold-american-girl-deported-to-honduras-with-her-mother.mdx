A five‑year‑old American girl, Génesis, was sent back to Honduras with her mother after ICE acted on a deportation order issued before she was born. The pair were held in a hotel for almost a week with no lawyer or court hearing, then flown to Honduras. Activists say the case shows how U.S. immigration rules can ignore children’s citizenship and the rights of families.
