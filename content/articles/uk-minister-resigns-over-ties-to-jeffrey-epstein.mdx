Peter Mandelson, a former Labour minister and ambassador to the US, stepped down from the party after documents showed he had close ties with convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein. The papers reveal he gave Epstein advice on banking policies, shared confidential government emails, and received money from Epstein and his husband. Emails show they remained friends for years, even after Epstein’s conviction. The scandal has led to an investigation into the disclosure of sensitive information.
