Donald Trump has blasted Britain for handing the Chagos Islands to Mauritius, calling it “stupidity” and saying the move makes the U.S. want Greenland. He posted the rant on Truth Social while heading to Davos. The UK says it keeps the U.S. base at Diego Garcia and has no plan to hand Greenland to the U.S. The Chagos deal is still contested in Parliament.
