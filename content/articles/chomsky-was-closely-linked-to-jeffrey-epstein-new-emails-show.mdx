Chomsky was found to have had close ties with Jeffrey Epstein. Emails released by U.S. lawmakers show they chatted regularly, shared interests and even discussed vacations. A letter from Chomsky praises Epstein for teaching him about the global financial system and describes him as a close friend. Chomsky says he never received money, though MIT reviewed his ties and donated to abuse‑survivor groups. The new files add to a growing list of academics linked to Epstein.
