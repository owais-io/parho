ICE agents killed Renee Good, a mother of three, in January 2026, sparking protests. A video shows a guard calling her a slur. Experts say this isn’t a one‑off: 13 ICE shootings of civilians since July 2025, 8 people shot and 2 dead. In 2025, 32 people died in ICE custody. The piece argues ICE is built on racial profiling and violence and calls for its abolition and better legal support for detainees.
