Hundreds of UK teens will join a six‑week pilot testing three ways to cut social‑media use. One group will have their apps blocked entirely, another will be cut off overnight or limited to an hour a day, and a control group will keep normal access. The experiment will show how these limits affect mood, sleep, school and family life. A larger Wellcome‑Trust‑funded study will track thousands more teens to guide Parliament’s upcoming debate on banning under‑16s.
