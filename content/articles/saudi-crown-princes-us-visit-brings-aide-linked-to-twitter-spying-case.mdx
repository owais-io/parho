Bader al‑Asaker, a senior aide of Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, will travel with the prince on his first US visit in seven years. Al‑Asaker has been accused by US prosecutors of running a 2014‑15 plan that put spies inside Twitter to find critics of the Saudi regime. The plot led to arrests and a 20‑year jail term for a man who mocked the government. Although the crown prince has legal immunity, al‑Asaker faces scrutiny in the US.
