On Saturday night at the Walcha Motorcycle Rally, a demolition derby car spun and crashed through a fence into the crowd, injuring 13 people. Two were in critical condition with serious spinal, hip, and head injuries; they were flown to Newcastle. The other 11 were taken to hospitals in Armidale, Tamworth, and Walcha, with several suffering serious wounds. Police are investigating the on‑track collision that caused the crash, and local officials are reviewing the rally’s safety.
