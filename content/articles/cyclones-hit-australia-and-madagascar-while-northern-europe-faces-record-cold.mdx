Cyclone Mitchell hit Western Australia, causing high winds, flooding and damage to farms. Cyclone Gezani slammed Madagascar’s east coast, killing 35 and destroying 2,500 homes. Meanwhile, a block of high pressure forced record cold over northern Europe, with temperatures below –40 °C and a frozen Baltic Sea. Flights were cancelled, electricity use spiked, and 38 people in Poland died of hypothermia.
