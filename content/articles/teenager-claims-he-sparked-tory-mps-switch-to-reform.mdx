Andrew Rosindell, a long‑time Conservative MP, announced he was joining Reform UK after a 15‑year‑old schoolboy named Charlie Simpson tweeted that he would defect. Simpson said his exclusive helped trigger the move, but Reform officials say there was no prior conversation with Nigel Farage. Simpson, who runs an independent media outlet with far‑right links, has quickly become a controversial figure in UK politics.
