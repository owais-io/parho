The Guardian’s editorial warns that Sir Keir Starmer’s plan to stop Manchester mayor Andy Burnham from running in a parliamentary by‑election is a mistake. It argues that blocking a popular, experienced politician could damage Labour’s image, alienate voters, and hurt the party in Greater Manchester. The piece says that the move shows an unwillingness to tolerate dissent and could cost the party key support in future elections.
