Arwa Mahdawi says journalists should question powerful figures, yet many media outlets are now owned by billionaires tied to politicians. She recalls leaving advertising for journalism and points out how advertising tricks are used in news stories. She cites the Washington Post’s refusal to show a cartoon about Jeff Bezos and Trump, and how media misreporting helped spread false claims about Gaza. The Guardian remains independent and urges readers to support it.
