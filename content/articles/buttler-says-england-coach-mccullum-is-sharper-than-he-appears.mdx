Former England star Jos Buttler says that Brendon McCullum, the new England coach, isn’t as laid‑back as he looks. Buttler says McCullum is very focused and sharp behind the scenes. McCullum keeps a relaxed public image but uses data, walkie‑talkies and support staff to run the team. Buttler believes the dressing room knows the truth about his hard work and plans ahead for the T20 World Cup.
