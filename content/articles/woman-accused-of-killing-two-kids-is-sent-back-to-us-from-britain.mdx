Kimberlee Singler, 37, was arrested in London in December 2023 after her two children, ages 9 and 7, were found dead in Colorado. She is now being extradited to the U.S. to face two first‑degree murder charges. Singler says she was attacked by a stranger, but police think she did it. She fought extradition, citing life‑without‑parole sentences as a human‑rights issue, but courts rejected her pleas.
