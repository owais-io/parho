Victoria is battling a scorching heatwave with temperatures hitting 42‑45°C, and fire bans are in place because wildfires are spreading fast. The prime minister has warned people to stay indoors, avoid driving in flood water, and heed evacuation notices. Meanwhile, a tropical low is turning into Cyclone Koji and could hit Queensland next. Sydney will also see 42°C, and power cuts and travel delays are happening across the states.
