New South Wales police used heavy force on Muslim men who were praying during a protest against Israeli president Isaac Herzog. The men, led by Sheikh Wesam Charkawi, were grabbed and dragged from the front of the group, despite not blocking the street. Muslim organisations demand an apology, resignation of the police commissioner, and a criminal investigation for the alleged abuse of power.
