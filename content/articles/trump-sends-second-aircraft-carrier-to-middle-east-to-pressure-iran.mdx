Trump ordered the largest aircraft carrier, the USS Gerald R. Ford, to sail from the Caribbean to the Middle East, joining the USS Abraham Lincoln. This move boosts U.S. firepower and aims to pressure Iran amid talks in Oman about its nuclear and missile programs. Iran wants sanctions relief; Israel wants limits on missiles and Hezbollah support. Trump’s rhetoric has shifted, warning Iran that a deal will be swift or tough.
