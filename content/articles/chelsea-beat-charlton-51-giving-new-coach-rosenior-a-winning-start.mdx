Chelsea beat Charlton 5‑1 in the FA Cup, giving new coach Liam Rosenior a winning debut. The team used a second‑string side, showing strong control. Fans, however, continued to vent anger at the club’s ownership, with chants and boos. Rosenior said he wasn’t a puppet and praised his players, but doubts over the club’s direction and squad planning remain.
