Rachel Reeves delivered a calm spring statement saying inflation and borrowing are falling and living standards are improving. But oil prices spiked after US‑Israel attacks on Iran, shaking markets and putting the UK’s economic stability at risk. The Office for Budget Responsibility cut growth forecasts and raised unemployment. Labour promises higher real wages, yet rising grocery prices keep worries high. Immigration policy and the role of the OBR also feature in the debate.
