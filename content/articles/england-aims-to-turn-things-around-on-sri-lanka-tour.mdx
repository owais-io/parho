England’s cricket team heads to Sri Lanka after a season of poor results and coaching turmoil. They’ve lost most recent ODI series and are missing key bowlers. New coach Harry Brook faces criticism. Sri Lanka, unbeaten at home for two years, boasts a dangerous spinner, Wanindu Hasaranga. England’s batting shows flashes but lacks consistency. The tour will test if England can regain confidence before the next World Cup.
