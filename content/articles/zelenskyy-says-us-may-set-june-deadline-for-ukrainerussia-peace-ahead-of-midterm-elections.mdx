Ukraine's president Volodymyr Zelenskyy said the U.S. may push for a June deadline to end the war with Russia, timing it with the U.S. mid‑term elections. He said the Trump administration would likely pressure both sides and that the next talks could be held in the U.S., perhaps in Miami. Zelenskyy warned about a recent Russian strike on Ukrainian energy sites and noted Russia’s weak economy. US military leaders are also joining diplomatic talks.
