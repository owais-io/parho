The 7 am Avanti West Coast train from Manchester to London was almost shut down, but after angry emails, social media, and a rail minister’s comment, the service was kept. Passengers filled the train, and the crew said it’s now fully booked. The train still has problems with delays and a broken microwave, but it will stay on the timetable for now, though its future is still uncertain.
