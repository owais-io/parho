On a busy Saturday, protesters threw custard and apple crumble at the crown jewels case in the Tower of London. The act was carried out by the group Take Back Power, who wanted to highlight inequality and push for a citizens' assembly that could tax the rich. Four people were arrested for criminal damage. The Tower’s Jewel House was closed while police investigated, but the rest of the site stayed open.
