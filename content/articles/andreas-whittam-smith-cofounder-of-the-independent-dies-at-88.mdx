Andreas Whittam Smith, who helped launch the Independent newspaper and served as its first editor, died at 88 on 29 November. He also led the British Board of Film Classification, cleared films like *A Clockwork Orange* for home video, and was a senior Church of England commissioner. A former financial journalist, he earned a CBE, a knighthood and the Canterbury Cross for his service. Colleagues and readers mourn his pioneering influence on British journalism.
