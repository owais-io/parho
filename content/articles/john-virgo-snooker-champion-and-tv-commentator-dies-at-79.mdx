John Virgo, a snooker champion turned beloved TV commentator, passed away at 79. He won the 1979 UK Championship and reached the World Championship semi‑finals, but his real fame came from the BBC quiz show *Big Break*, where he entertained millions with jokes and trick shots. Born in Salford, he learned snooker in local halls before becoming a national TV personality. After *Big Break*, he commentated for 40 years, including major tournaments, until his last call in January 2026.
