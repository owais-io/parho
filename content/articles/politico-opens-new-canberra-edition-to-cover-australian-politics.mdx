Politico, the U.S. political news site, is opening a Canberra office this year to focus on Australian federal politics and policy. The new “Canberra Playbook” newsletter will start when parliament resumes after the winter break, offering in‑depth coverage and global context. Ryan Heath, who ran Politico’s Brussels playbook, will be the launch editor. The move aims to link Australian politics to the wider Indo‑Pacific region and help readers see how local decisions fit global power shifts.
