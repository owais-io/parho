Tottenham manager Thomas Frank says he feels backed by the club’s top officials after fans demanded his sacking following a 1‑0 loss to West Ham. With a thin squad—only 11 fit outfield players—Frank is set to coach the Champions League match against Borussia Dortmund. He remains optimistic, citing a supportive lunch with the club’s leaders and a belief that the team can win even with injuries and suspensions.
