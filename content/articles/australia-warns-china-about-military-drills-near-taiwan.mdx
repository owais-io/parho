Australia says it is very worried about China’s big military drills close to Taiwan. The exercises, called “Justice Mission 2025,” sent dozens of planes, ships and 27 missiles near the island, raising the chance of an accident or conflict. Canberra says the drills could make the region unstable and wants China to talk instead of using force. Australia has told Chinese officials it does not want any sudden changes to the status quo across the Taiwan Strait.
