The Guardian has launched its 2025 list of the world’s best 100 women footballers. A big panel of coaches, former players and journalists, including Vlatko Andonovski, Renée Slegers, and Lieke Martens, weighed in. The ranking covers a full year that saw Euro 2025, Copa América and Afcon. The list is revealed in stages: 100‑71 on Dec 1, 30 more names the next two days, and the top 10 on Dec 4.
