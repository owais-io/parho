Simon Foster, the West Midlands Police and Crime Commissioner, has accused MPs on the Home Affairs Committee of showing bias against Chief Constable Craig Guildford. The MPs allegedly told reporters they wanted Guildford removed while an investigation into the banning of Israeli football fans is still underway. Foster says MPs’ comments risk undermining public trust and wants an apology. He will decide Guildford’s fate after reviewing the HMIC findings.
