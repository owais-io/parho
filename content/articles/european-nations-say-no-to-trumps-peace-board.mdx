European leaders, including the UK, France, Germany, and the EU, declined an invitation from Donald Trump to join a new peace board in Washington. The board aims to rebuild Gaza, but critics argue it is vague, lacks real authority, and is mainly a fundraiser. The Vatican also rejected the invitation. The summit is seen as Trump’s attempt to stay influential after his presidency.
