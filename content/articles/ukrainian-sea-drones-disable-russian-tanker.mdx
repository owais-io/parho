Ukrainian sea drones struck a Russian tanker, the Dashan, in the Black Sea, stopping its oil shipment to Russia. The tanker was part of Russia’s shadow fleet that sells oil to fund the war. It was the third drone hit on such vessels in two weeks, cutting Russia’s oil money. Meanwhile, Ukraine and Western allies discuss peace talks and keeping military support.
