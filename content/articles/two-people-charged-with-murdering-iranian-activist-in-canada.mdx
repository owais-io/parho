A former university maths teacher, Masood Masjoody, who was outspoken against Iran’s government, was found dead in British Columbia. Two Canadians – Mehdi Ahmadzadeh Razavi and Arezou Soltani – were arrested and charged with first‑degree murder. The case has raised worries about Iran’s overseas attacks on critics and has shocked the local Iranian community. Police say the motive is still under investigation.
