President Trump has not yet decided whether to launch airstrikes on Iran. He is waiting for advice from special envoys Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner, who will assess whether Iran will agree to give up its nuclear‑weapon program. Trump said he prefers a diplomatic deal but will act if negotiations fail. The decision hinges on the envoys’ assessment of Tehran’s willingness to cooperate.
