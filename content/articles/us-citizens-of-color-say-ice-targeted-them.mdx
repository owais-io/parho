During Donald Trump's latest immigration crackdown, people of color report that U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agents are targeting them. In Minneapolis, ICE and Border Patrol have focused on Latino and Somali communities, prompting a class‑action lawsuit by the ACLU. Incidents include a citizen being stopped for lunch and drivers asked to prove legal status. Many now keep passports or birth certificates handy to avoid questioning. If you’ve been targeted, report it.
