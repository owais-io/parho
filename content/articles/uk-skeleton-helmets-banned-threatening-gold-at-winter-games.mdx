UK skeleton athletes Matt Weston and Marcus Wyatt had been expected to win gold and silver in Milan. Their new aerodynamic helmets were banned by the International Bobsleigh and Skeleton Federation on January 29, saying the shape did not meet rules. Team GB is appealing to the Court of Arbitration for Sport, hoping the design is safe. The decision could affect the team's chance to win Olympic gold.
