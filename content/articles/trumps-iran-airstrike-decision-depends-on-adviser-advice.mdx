President Trump is still deciding whether to launch airstrikes on Iran. His choice will rely on advisers Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner, who are assessing if Tehran is stalling on a nuclear deal. While the administration prepares for Iran’s latest proposal, Trump could order limited strikes or, if that fails, a bigger attack to force regime change. A large US air‑power buildup in the Middle East is already in place.
