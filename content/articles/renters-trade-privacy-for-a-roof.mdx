In Australia, people who rent are forced to share huge amounts of personal data with digital rental services. Seven popular rent‑tech platforms have serious security flaws, leaving renters’ documents exposed online. Only two companies agreed to tighten security. Renters can feel pressured to use these apps for applications, payments and maintenance, but the data they give is at risk. Regulators have not acted fast enough, leaving many tenants vulnerable.
