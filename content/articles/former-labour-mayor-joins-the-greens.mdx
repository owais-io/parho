Jamie Driscoll, who was the North of Tyne metro mayor and helped start Your Party, has left Labour and joined the Greens. He says the Greens are serious about long‑term plans for workers and small businesses. His move comes after several Labour councillors in London also switched to the Greens. The Greens are gaining popularity and want to win more seats in next year’s local elections, especially in Newcastle.
