On Saturday night, a massive power outage left about 130,000 people in San Francisco without electricity and knocked out traffic signals citywide. Waymo’s driver‑less taxis had to pull over and ended up blocking streets, causing congestion and danger for other drivers. The company paused its ride‑hailing service until power was restored. City officials urged residents to stay off the roads, and police were sent to key intersections.
