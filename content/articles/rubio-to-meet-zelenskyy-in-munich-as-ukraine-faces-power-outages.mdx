U.S. Secretary of State Marco Rubio will attend the Munich Security Conference and meet Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy while European leaders discuss the war. Russia’s recent missile and drone attacks have knocked out power and heating for thousands of Ukrainian households in Kyiv, Odesa and Dnipro. Ukraine’s skeleton racer was barred from the Winter Games for a memorial, sparking criticism. Allies have pledged $35 bn in aid, and Russia’s Belgorod region lost power after a Ukrainian strike.
