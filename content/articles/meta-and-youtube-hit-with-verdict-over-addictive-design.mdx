Los Angeles judges found that Meta’s Facebook, Instagram and Google’s YouTube deliberately built features to hook users, especially kids. The case centers on 20‑year‑old Kaley, who said she can’t live without the apps and has struggled with depression. The $375 m ruling followed a New Mexico case that said Meta misled about safety. Share prices fell, and governments in Indonesia, Brazil and the UK are tightening rules on kids’ use.
