Carlo Soracchi, a former Metropolitan Police undercover officer, is accused of spending taxpayers’ money on a romantic trip to Venice with a woman he deceived into a long‑term relationship. Official documents billed the journey as a visit for socialist activists, but witnesses say only he and the woman were present. The inquiry, led by Judge Sir John Mitting, will question Soracchi over the next four days about the misuse of police funds and his covert operations.
