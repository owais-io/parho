The ABC’s top manager, Hugh Marks, said reporters Laura Tingle and Sarah Ferguson were unfairly attacked after they covered the Bondi shooting. Tingle, on a podcast, said the gunmen’s actions weren’t about religion, and Marks said this did not downplay the terror. Ferguson questioned a politician about his remarks linking the PM to the attacks, which drew criticism from the Liberal side. Marks called the criticism “unfounded” and defended the ABC’s role.
