Birmingham City’s owner, Knighthead Capital Management, is looking to buy a new Premiership Rugby franchise as the Rugby Football Union plans to lock the league at 10 teams until 2030 before slowly adding more clubs. The move would give Birmingham a professional rugby side, filling a gap left by the former Wasps. Knighthead is already investing in a sports quarter that will include stadiums for football, rugby, and netball.
