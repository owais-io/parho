Gregory Bovino, the border patrol commander who has been the face of the Trump‑era crackdown in Minneapolis, will leave the city as the administration reshuffles its immigration team after the fatal shooting of ICU nurse Alex Pretti. Trump says the move will reduce federal agents in Minnesota and move Bovino back to California. Protesters gathered outside his hotel, while officials plan to end the “Operation Metro Surge.”
