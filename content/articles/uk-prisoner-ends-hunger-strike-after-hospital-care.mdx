Umer Khalid, 22, was on a hunger and thirst strike while in Wormwood Scrubs. He was taken to intensive care with organ failure, but now is drinking again and will be slowly refed. His protest ended after a meeting with the prison governor, after which he got his post, clothes and visit restrictions lifted. The case highlights concerns about how the prisoners are treated and the need for a full inquiry.
