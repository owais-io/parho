Frenchie Mae Cumpio, a 21‑year‑old Filipino journalist and radio host, was arrested in 2020 after police claimed they found a hand grenade, a gun and a communist flag in her bed. She says the weapons were planted. For almost six years she has been kept in prison in Tacloban, with a trial still pending. Human‑rights groups call the charges false and warn the case is part of a wider crackdown on journalists.
