California’s richest tech leaders are pouring millions into state politics to stop a proposed billionaire tax. Big names like Google, Meta, and Palantir are funding campaigns for governors, city councils, and school boards, and creating super‑PACs that push for lighter AI regulation and lower taxes. The move also backs a new governor hopeful, Matt Mahan, who has already raised record sums from the tech community.
