A small village in Cornwall called Cardinham has been soaking for 41 straight days. The heavy rain has flooded roads, broken a lane, and made fields soggy, keeping cattle inside barns. Local cafés and small planes are struggling because flights are cut back. Residents complain of gloomy weather, but some still find ways to stay active. Weather officials say it's just a wet winter, not climate change.
