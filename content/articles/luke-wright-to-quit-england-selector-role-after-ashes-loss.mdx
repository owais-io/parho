Luke Wright, who joined England’s selection panel in 2022, has decided to step down after the T20 World Cup, saying the travel demands and his young family make it too hard to keep up. The change comes after England’s heavy defeat in the Ashes series in Australia. Wright was part of a new selection group that included Rob Key and the head coach. He thanked his colleagues and said he looks forward to passing the baton.
