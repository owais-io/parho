Steve Pampalian, 41, was shot by a NSW police officer in May 2023 after a psychotic episode. The police first claimed he was a known offender, but an inquest found he had no criminal record and was never known to the force. The mistake hurt his family and sparked calls for better mental‑health training and clearer police statements. The inquest also reviewed how officers handle mental‑health calls, noting that body‑cam rules and training were updated after the incident.
