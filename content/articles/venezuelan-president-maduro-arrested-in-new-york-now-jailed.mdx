On Saturday, the U.S. seized Venezuelan president Nicolás Maduro during a raid that sparked protests in New York. He was taken to the Metropolitan Detention Center in Brooklyn and will face charges for running a corrupt government tied to huge drug trafficking. Many U.S. residents either shouted against the raid or celebrated Maduro’s arrest. The move has drawn criticism from China and sparked talks about a possible future transition in Venezuela.
