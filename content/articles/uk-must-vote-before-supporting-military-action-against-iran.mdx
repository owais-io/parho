Prime Minister Sir Keir Starmer says Britain will not join an offensive strike against Iran, but it could let the US use UK bases for limited defensive hits to stop Iranian missile attacks. That legal limit protects British interests. If the UK wants a bigger role, MPs must approve it—otherwise the country risks becoming a co‑belligerent in an unlawful war.
