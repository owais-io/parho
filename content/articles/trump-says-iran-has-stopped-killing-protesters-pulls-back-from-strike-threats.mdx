Donald Trump told reporters that Iran has stopped killing protesters and that no executions are planned, so he has pulled back from threatening military strikes. He said he hasn't verified the claims but is listening to Iranian assurances. Despite the pause, tensions remain high, with some US bases in the region on alert and oil prices easing. Trump remains uncertain about any future action while Iran warns of possible retaliation.
