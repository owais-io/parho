Because a war in the Middle East disrupted travel, over a million passengers were stuck as flights were cancelled. UAE airlines Etihad, Emirates and FlyDubai began a few evacuation flights to bring people back home. The US told its citizens to leave the region, and other countries like the UK and Germany set up support teams and offered flights. Airspace closures still block many routes, and airlines keep schedules tight.
