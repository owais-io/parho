London police stopped a pro‑Palestinian al‑Quds Day march and a pro‑Israel counter‑protest from meeting on the Thames. 12 people were arrested, some for chanting hate slogans and for supporting a banned group. A police blockade closed Lambeth Bridge and 1,000 officers kept the two crowds apart. The Home Secretary said any hate or support for outlawed groups would be stopped.
