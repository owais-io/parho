After losing power in 2024, former Syrian president Bashar al‑Assad now lives in luxury in Moscow’s elite suburbs. He’s reportedly back in medical school, brushing up on eye‑surgery, and keeps a quiet life with his wife Asma. The family is cut off from Russia’s political circles, can’t appear publicly, and focus on private comforts. His children, once groomed for leadership, now attend school and shop in Moscow and the UAE.
