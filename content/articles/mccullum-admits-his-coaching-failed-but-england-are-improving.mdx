McCullum surprised everyone by admitting his coaching methods didn’t work in the Ashes, yet England are showing growth. After losing the series early, the team has become more mature and less pressured. Players like Zak Crawley and Harry Brook are learning to play steadier, while Ben Stokes leads by example. With key Australian bowlers out, England still has a chance to finish the series strong.
