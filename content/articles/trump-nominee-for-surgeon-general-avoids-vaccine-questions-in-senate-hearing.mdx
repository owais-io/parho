Casey Means, Trump’s pick for Surgeon General, appeared before the Senate Health Committee. She defended her medical background but avoided direct answers about vaccines. Senators raised concerns about her lack of board certification and active license, as well as her ties to wellness influencers. Critics worry she could support the administration’s anti‑vaccine stance, while Democrats fear she may not effectively fight misinformation. The hearing highlighted a clash between politics and public health expertise.
