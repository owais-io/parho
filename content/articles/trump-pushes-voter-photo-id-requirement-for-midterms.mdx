President Trump said he will force voters to show photo ID before they can cast ballots in the 2024 midterm elections, even if Congress blocks his plan. He wants to add a citizenship check for registration and cut mail‑in voting, except for certain groups. The move faces legal hurdles because election rules are mainly a state job and courts have already struck down similar orders. Polls show most Americans support mail‑in voting.
