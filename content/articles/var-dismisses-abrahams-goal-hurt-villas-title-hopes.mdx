Aston Villa were keen to keep title hopes alive after beating a 10‑man Brentford side, but a VAR review wiped out Tammy Abraham’s goal – his first since 2016 – because the ball had left play earlier. The decision left the score 2‑0 and sent Villa’s championship bid into doubt. Fans were upset, and the match highlighted how controversial video replays can change a game’s outcome.
