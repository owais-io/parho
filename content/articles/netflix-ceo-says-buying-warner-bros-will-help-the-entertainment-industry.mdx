Netflix’s chief Ted Sarandos says its $82.7 billion purchase of Warner Bros Discovery assets will grow the industry and keep it competitive. He argues that a rival bid from Paramount would hurt consumers and creators. Sarandos also defends Netflix’s role in the UK, pointing out the company’s support for local writers and producers. The deal faces criticism for possibly concentrating too much media power in one company.
