Franjo von Allmen won Switzerland’s first medal at the Winter Games, beating Italy’s Giovanni Franzoni by 0.20 seconds in Bormio’s men’s downhill. The 24‑year‑old said he stayed relaxed and simply enjoyed the run. Von Allmen’s rise from a childhood loss and a butcher‑made “Silver Lightning” sausage to Olympic champion shows hard work and joy can lead to glory.
