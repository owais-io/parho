Ousmane Dembélé won the Guardian’s 2025 male footballer award, beating Lamine Yamal by 128 points. 219 judges gave a total of 179,580 points, with 101 of them putting Dembélé first and 33 choosing Yamal. Judges pick 40 players and rank them 1‑40, earning points from 40 to 1. The Guardian releases an anonymised spreadsheet of all votes, letting readers check any player’s history.
