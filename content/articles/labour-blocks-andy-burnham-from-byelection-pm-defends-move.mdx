Labour leader Keir Starmer said the party's executive committee decided not to let Greater Manchester mayor Andy Burnham run in the Gorton and Denton by‑election. The move was meant to keep resources on the upcoming local, Scottish, and Welsh elections, Starmer said. Many Labour MPs and unions are angry, saying the decision is unfair and a waste of time. Burnham said he’d support whoever the party chooses.
