Hurricane Helene hit western North Carolina hard, wiping out many Christmas tree farms and killing people. Even so, growers are feeling positive. Sales are good this holiday season, and farmers are rebuilding—repairing homes, replanting fields, and working to bring production back up to last year’s level. The industry remains strong, with new research on tougher trees, and a local tree even made it to the White House.
