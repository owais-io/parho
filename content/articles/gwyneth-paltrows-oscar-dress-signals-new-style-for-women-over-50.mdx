At this year’s Oscars, Gwyneth Paltrow sparked a debate about how women in their 50s should dress. The article talks about how there are no clear fashion rules for older actresses, and how many choose suits or timeless looks to avoid criticism. Paltrow’s dress was a bold two‑panel outfit that challenged expectations, showing that older women can be daring and still feel comfortable on the red carpet. It raises the question: what style works best for women over 50?
