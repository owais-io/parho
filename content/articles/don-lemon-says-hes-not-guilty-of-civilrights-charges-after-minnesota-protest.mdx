Former CNN host Don Lemon pleaded not guilty to federal civil‑rights charges tied to a protest at a Minnesota church where ICE officials serve as pastors. He says he was there just to report, not to join the demonstration. Lawyers will argue First‑Amendment rights in court. The case highlights growing tensions between journalists and the Trump‑era administration. The trial could set a precedent for press freedom in the face of government scrutiny.
