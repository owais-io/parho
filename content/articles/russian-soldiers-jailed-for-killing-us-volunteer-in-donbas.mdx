Three Russian soldiers have been given prison sentences of up to 12 years for beating and killing a 63‑year‑old US volunteer named Russell Bentley, who had joined the Russian side in the Donbas. Investigators say the men tortured him and tried to hide the crime by blowing up his body in a car. The case has embarrassed Moscow and warned that foreigners who fight for Russia could face brutal treatment.
