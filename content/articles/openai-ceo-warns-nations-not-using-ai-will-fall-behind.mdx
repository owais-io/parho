At a Delhi summit, OpenAI chief George Osborne told world leaders that if their countries don’t adopt powerful AI systems, they risk becoming weaker and poorer. He highlighted the choice between US‑made AI, like OpenAI’s, and China’s tech. The event also focused on helping the global south use AI for jobs, agriculture and health. Some African leaders said they want to build their own AI instead of relying only on the two superpowers, while EU rules were criticized.
