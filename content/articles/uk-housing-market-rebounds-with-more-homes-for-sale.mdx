Spring has kicked the UK housing market into high gear. Listings are up, with a 6% increase over last year, and the biggest mortgage lenders say prices are rising a bit. Low interest rates and easier loan rules are letting more first‑time buyers chase homes. Although more houses on the market could slow price growth, the trend shows confidence is back and moving people.
