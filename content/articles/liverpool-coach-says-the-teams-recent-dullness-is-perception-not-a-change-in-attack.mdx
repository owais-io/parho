Arne Slot says Liverpool’s recent lack of excitement is a matter of perception, not a change in his attacking philosophy. After a brutal nine‑game losing streak, the team has gone nine matches unbeaten but still struggles to create chances, earning criticism. Slot admits injuries and squad changes force him to adjust, but he insists they press high and look for goals. He remains confident the squad can return to a more attractive, winning style.
