João Palhinha, now a Tottenham midfielder, says his football rise is due to hard work. He was the Premier League's top tackler while at Fulham, which led Bayern Munich to sign him for £42 m. After a season‑long injury at Bayern, he went on loan to Spurs where he keeps winning tackles and scoring goals. With Spurs struggling at home, Palhinha hopes the Arsenal derby will boost the team, and he reminds fans his future is still undecided.
