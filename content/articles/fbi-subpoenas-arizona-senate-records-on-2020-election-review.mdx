The FBI has asked Arizona’s legislature for documents about the state senate’s 2020 election audit in Maricopa County. Senate President Warren Petersen confirmed the request on X, saying the FBI already has the records. Trump praised the subpoena, while the state and county officials say they have not received anything. The move follows earlier FBI investigations into election fraud claims across the country.
