TPP Group, owned by Tory donor Frank Hester, paid a £50 m dividend after its sales jumped to £97 m and pre‑tax profit rose to £47 m. The NHS‑software firm has supplied 7,800 organisations and taken in £591 m from public contracts. Hester, who stepped down as director but still owns most shares, donated over £20 m to the Tories and faced backlash after making racist remarks in 2019.
