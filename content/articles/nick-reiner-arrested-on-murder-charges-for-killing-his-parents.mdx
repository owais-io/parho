Nick Reiner, 32, has been charged with two counts of first‑degree murder after police found his parents, filmmaker Rob Reiner and his wife Michele, dead in their Brentwood home. He was arrested Sunday night and will appear in court after a medical check. The case is a shock to the Reiner family and the city. Reiner has a history of addiction and mental‑health struggles.
