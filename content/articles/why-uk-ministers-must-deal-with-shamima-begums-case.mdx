Public opinion still says Shamima Begum shouldn’t return to the UK, but the Home Office can’t just ignore the case. First, many Britons still living in former IS camps need attention. Second, the European court questions whether Britain was fair in stripping Begum’s citizenship when she was a minor and is now stateless. The issue raises hard questions about citizenship rights, especially for people with migrant backgrounds.
