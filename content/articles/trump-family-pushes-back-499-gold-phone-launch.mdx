Trump Mobile, run by Donald Trump's sons, has delayed its gold‑coloured T1 phone, originally due this year. The $499 device, marketed as a proud American rival to Apple and Samsung, may not arrive until later, after a government shutdown hit shipments. Customers must pre‑pay $100 and pay a $47.45 monthly plan. The venture also sells used phones and raises questions about family business interests.
