The Equality and Human Rights Commission is updating its rules about gender after a Supreme Court ruling that says a woman is defined by biology. The new chair, Mary‑Ann Stephenson, wants the guidance to be practical for businesses and not hurt trans people. The changes aim to keep single‑sex spaces while making toilets and changing rooms easier to manage.
