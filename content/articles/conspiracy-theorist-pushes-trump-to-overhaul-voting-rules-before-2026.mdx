Florida lawyer Peter Ticktin, who has long ties to Donald Trump, is urging the president to issue an emergency executive order that would change U.S. election rules. The draft order would require voters to prove citizenship, end machine and mail‑in voting, hand‑count every ballot, and finish counts by midnight. Ticktin cites unfounded foreign‑interference claims, but election experts say the plan is illegal and would restrict voting.
