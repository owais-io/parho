Joe Biden has publicly criticized the U.S. federal agents who shot ICU nurse Alex Pretti and the bus driver Renee Good in Minneapolis. He says these killings betray America’s core values and the constitutional rights of its people. Biden urges a thorough investigation and joins former presidents in condemning the administration’s immigration enforcement tactics. The remarks highlight growing concerns over police brutality and the treatment of citizens in the U.S.
