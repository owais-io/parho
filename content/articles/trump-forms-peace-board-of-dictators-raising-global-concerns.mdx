Trump has set up a “Board of Peace” that brings together more than 20 leaders, many from dictatorships, to shape Gaza’s future and bypass the UN. Allies worry the group is a fee‑paying club run by Trump. Observers like Italy and Greece are attending instead of full members. Freedom House scores show many members score very low on freedom, highlighting the authoritarian nature of the group.
