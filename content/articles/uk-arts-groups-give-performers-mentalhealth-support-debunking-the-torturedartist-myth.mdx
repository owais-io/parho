Performers across opera, theatre, film and TV are getting mental‑health help from new support services, breaking the old idea that great art comes from suffering. Directors and venues like the English National Opera now bring in therapists and wellbeing firms, offering help before, during and after productions. Studies show 84 % of UK actors feel stressed, and a quarter think about leaving. The shift shows artists can be strong, not broken, to create powerful work.
