On Saturday, more than 3,000 No Kings events will take place across all 50 states and 16 countries, drawing an expected crowd of millions. Organized by anti‑authoritarian groups, unions and grassroots activists, the protests aim to block ICE raids, defend voting rights and protest high living costs and foreign conflicts. The day will stay non‑violent, with organizers warning against weapons and potential ICE presence.
