The Guardian article reports that the UK music industry had a year in 2025. Fans of Oasis and Taylor Swift helped lift sales of physical albums, vinyl. In total, listeners bought the equivalent of 210 million albums – a 4.9 % jump from 2024. Vinyl sales grew 13 % and made Swift the top vinyl seller again, while Oasis’s compilation albums topped the charts after their tour. Streaming still dominates, but the revival of physical formats shows a steady rise.
