Pakistan announced a five‑day break in its airstrikes on Afghanistan to mark the end of Ramadan, following a tragic strike that hit a drug‑rehabilitation centre in Kabul. The attack killed over 400 people and injured many more. Both sides blame each other for the strike, with Pakistan saying it targeted military sites and Afghanistan saying it hit a civilian hospital. Aid groups call for calm and better protection of civilians.
