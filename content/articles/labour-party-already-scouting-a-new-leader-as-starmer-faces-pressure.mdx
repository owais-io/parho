Labour leader Keir Starmer is under pressure, and the party has already started looking for a replacement. A fake website announced Angela Rayner might run, and her allies are pushing her candidacy, while others back health secretary Wes Streeting or deputy leader Lucy Powell. Inside the party, accusations of scandal and internal fighting add to the drama. No formal challenge yet, but candidates are being named and debated.
