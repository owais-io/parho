UK Home Secretary Kemi Badenoch says she never urged the government to get involved in the US‑Israel attack on Iran. She clarified that she supports the strikes and wants Iran to stop launching missiles, but she does not want British troops on the ground. Badenoch said the UK should help take out underground launch sites but not join the combat. She also criticized the government for trying to hide details.
