US forces seized a large oil tanker off Venezuela’s coast, a move Trump confirmed. Venezuela calls it piracy. The tanker was under US sanctions for sending oil to Iran and terrorist groups. The seizure happened amid a US push against President Maduro, who has ruled since 2013. The US has also sent ships, jets, and is building a naval blockade. Venezuela says its oil is a national treasure and criticises the attack.
