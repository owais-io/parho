Gavin Burrows, a private investigator, says the confession of illegal acts that is key to a lawsuit involving Prince Harry and others against the Daily Mail’s publisher was fabricated. He claims his signature on the 2021 witness statement was forged and that he never actually wrote it. Burrows argues the case was built on misleading evidence, and he disputes the allegations of bugging and phone hacking.
