El Fasher, a Sudanese city, looks like a huge crime scene. Satellite pictures show thousands of bodies piled in streets and new burial and burning pits. At least 60,000 people may have died, and up to 150,000 residents are missing. The RSF has stopped the UN and aid workers from entering. The city faces famine and urgent help is needed, while experts push for a war‑crime investigation.
