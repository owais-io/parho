Police are looking into whether former duke Andrew Mountbatten‑Windsor abused his position while working as the UK’s trade envoy, after documents from the US justice department showed he may have shared confidential reports with Jeffrey Epstein. Thames Valley police have met with Crown Prosecution Service specialists to assess possible misconduct in public office. No verdict yet, but investigations into related allegations involving other officials are also underway.
