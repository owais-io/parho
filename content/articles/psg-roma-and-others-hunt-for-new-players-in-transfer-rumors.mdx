There’s a lot of transfer chatter. Wolves’ striker Jørgen Strand Larsen could move to Tottenham or Newcastle. Chelsea is reportedly eyeing Trent Alexander‑Arnold. Manchester United’s manager change might tempt Dutch forward Joshua Zirkzee to stay, but Roma could instead target Giacomo Raspadori. Paris Saint‑Germain is watching Marcus Rashford, while Liverpool’s Federico Chiesa is attracting interest from Juventus and Napoli. Other clubs, like Middlesbrough, Coventry, Celtic, and Milan, also have their own targets.
