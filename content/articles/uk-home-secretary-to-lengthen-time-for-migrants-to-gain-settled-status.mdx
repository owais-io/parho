Shabana Mahmood, the UK Home Secretary, is set to extend the waiting period for newcomers before they can become settled, raising it from five to ten years. She plans this even after Labour lost a key election to the Greens. The move risks worsening shortages in health, construction, and education, as many of these jobs rely on migrant workers. Cutting immigration could hurt services and the economy.
