A California jury found Bill Cosby liable for raping 84‑year‑old Donna Motsinger in 1972. Motsinger claimed Cosby drugged her with wine while she worked as a server, then raped her. The jury awarded her $59.25 million in damages after a three‑day deliberation and a second hearing for extra punitive money. Cosby, who had served time and later won an appeal, plans to appeal this verdict.
