Cloudflare, a key internet service that protects and speeds up sites, had a big outage Tuesday. It affected thousands of sites, including X, Spotify, ChatGPT, Facebook, Amazon, Uber, and more. The company said a sudden spike of unusual traffic caused errors and that they were working to fix it. They believe the issue is resolved but are still monitoring. This shows how a few big infrastructure firms can make many sites go offline.
