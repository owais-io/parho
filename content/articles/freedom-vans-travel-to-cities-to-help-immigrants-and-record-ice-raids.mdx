Freedom Vans, painted with the Statue of Liberty, are driving to places like Los Angeles, Chicago, Charlotte and Minneapolis to support immigrant families. Volunteers from both sides of politics give legal help, prayer, snacks, and film what ICE does. The vans aim to show the human cost of raids, gather evidence, and keep people united against tough immigration enforcement. Their work shows a sharp drop in public support for ICE.
