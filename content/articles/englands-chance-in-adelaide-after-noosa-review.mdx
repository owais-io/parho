England are 2‑0 behind in the Ashes, and after a tough defeat in Noosa the teams have sat down for a big review. Coaches are trying to sort training, player pressure and team mood, hoping to fix the problems that caused the loss. A change in the squad is expected for the next Test at Adelaide Oval, where both sides will look for a way to turn the series around. Australia also face selection worries and need to get their bowlers working.
