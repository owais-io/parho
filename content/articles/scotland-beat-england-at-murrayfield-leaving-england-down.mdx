Scotland won a thrilling game at Murrayfield, beating England and handing them their fifth loss in the past six Six Nations matches. The Scots showed strong teamwork, quick attacks and solid defense, while England struggled with ball handling and losing key players to injury. The result is a big wake‑up call for England, raising doubts about their preparation for next year’s World Cup.
