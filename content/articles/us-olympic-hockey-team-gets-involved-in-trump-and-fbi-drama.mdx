During the Olympic gold‑winning game, US men’s hockey players were praised for sportsmanship, but the team’s celebration quickly turned political when FBI director Kash Patel and President Donald Trump got involved. Trump invited the team to the State of the Union, and Patel was photographed with the players, raising concerns about the link between sport and politics. The women’s team declined the White House invite, and some male players are unsure whether to attend.
