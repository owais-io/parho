Paris’s new mayor, Emmanuel Grégoire, won the election and is already riding a bike through the city’s new cycle lanes. He vows to add more bike paths, limit cars, slash officials’ expenses, and stand against the far‑right. Grégoire also promises to overhaul the child‑abuse system in schools, curb Airbnb rentals, and clean up Paris’s air pollution.
