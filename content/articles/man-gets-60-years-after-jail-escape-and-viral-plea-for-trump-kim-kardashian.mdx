A 32‑year‑old man from New Orleans was sentenced to 60 years in prison after a daring jail escape that involved nine other men. He tried to get help from Donald Trump and reality star Kim Kardashian by posting videos online. The judge also found him guilty of kidnapping and raping his ex‑girlfriend, giving him no chance for parole. Authorities say his jailbreak was one of the largest in recent U.S. history.
