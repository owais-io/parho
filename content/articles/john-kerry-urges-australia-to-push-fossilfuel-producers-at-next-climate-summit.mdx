Kerry says Australia, hosting the upcoming UN climate meeting, must confront the world’s biggest emitters—China, Russia, the US, India and Europe—and demand a clear plan to shut down fossil fuels. He stresses a tough, “hard‑nosed” approach, warning that countries like Saudi Arabia and Russia will resist. Kerry also notes the need for Australia to prepare strong proposals and to show how the climate crisis will affect everyday life.
