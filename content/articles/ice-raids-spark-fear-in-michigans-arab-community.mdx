Lorenda Lewis’s husband, an Algerian immigrant, was taken by ICE and is set to be deported from Michigan. The family, already dealing with long detentions, fears financial and emotional loss. In Dearborn, Arab‑American residents are facing rising anti‑Islam protests, a fake ICE officer, and Trump‑era travel bans that threaten their safety and livelihoods. The case highlights how enforcement actions create fear and hardship for whole communities.
