UK MPs want a ban on dangerous Brazilian butt lifts after a report shows unregulated procedures are being done in places like garden sheds and hotel rooms. They say only trained professionals should perform such surgeries and the government must act quickly. The report cites deaths and serious infections from unregulated non‑surgical injections. MPs want a licensing system and stricter rules to protect patients.
