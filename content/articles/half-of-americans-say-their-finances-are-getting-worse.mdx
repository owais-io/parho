A new Harris Poll shows that almost half of Americans feel their money situation is getting worse, while only a fifth think it's improving. Over half (57%) believe the country is in a recession, up from earlier polls. People are blaming the White House more than ever. Democrats, women, Black and Hispanic voters are especially worried, while Republicans and higher earners are less concerned. The poll was taken online in December 2025.
