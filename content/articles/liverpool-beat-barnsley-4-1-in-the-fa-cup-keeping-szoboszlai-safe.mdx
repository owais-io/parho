Liverpool comfortably beat League One side Barnsley 4-1 in the FA Cup, keeping Dominik Szoboszlai’s hopes of a clean sheet alive. Goals came from Florian Wirtz, Hugo Ekitiké and a late shot by Szoboszlai. Barnsley had a chance early, but Liverpool’s defense held firm. The match also saw controversy when Szoboszlai’s goal celebration was seen as disrespectful. Overall, Liverpool’s win secures a spot in the next round.
