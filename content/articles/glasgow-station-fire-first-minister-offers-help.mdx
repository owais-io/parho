A vape‑shop fire next to Glasgow Central station gutted a Victorian building and shut the city’s busiest rail hub for days. No injuries were reported, but the blaze caused huge damage, forced train and bus cancellations, and left the station closed until after Tuesday. First Minister John Swinney said the government would help cover the costs, and the incident has raised calls for tighter rules on vape shops in historic buildings.
