Thomas Frank admits he’s not enjoying his role as Tottenham’s head coach. He talks about a tough, transitional season, injuries, and fans booing after a 0‑0 draw. Frank says the work feels like a marathon—hard but necessary to build a stronger team. He’s focused on long‑term signings and steady improvement, even while the club faces many challenges on and off the pitch.
