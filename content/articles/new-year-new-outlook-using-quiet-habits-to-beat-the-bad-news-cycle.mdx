Each day the internet keeps flooding us with doom‑driven headlines about war, climate, politics, and our own fears. The author says instead of scrolling or turning away, we can change how we pay attention. Small habits—like starting the day by looking at a person, nature, or a book—and reading old prayers or reflections can shift focus from panic to hope. By training our mind to notice good, we’re less swept away by negativity.
