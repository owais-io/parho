The war in the Middle East is pushing oil prices up, which could give Russia a money boost for its war effort. With shipping blocked in the Hormuz Strait and attacks on Iranian oil sites, big buyers like India and China might turn to Russian barrels. Higher oil and gas sales could help Russia keep fighting in Ukraine, while Europe may have to rethink its ban on Russian fuel.
