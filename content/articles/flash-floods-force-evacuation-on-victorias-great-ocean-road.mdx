A heavy thunderstorm dumped over 170 mm of rain on Victoria’s south‑west coast, causing flash floods that swept cars and caravans away from Wye River. The BIG4 Wye River Holiday Park was evacuated, and around 500 campers were moved to a nearby pub by the Country Fire Authority. The Great Ocean Road was closed, and local shops and services shut. Residents say emergency alerts came late, raising concerns about safety.
