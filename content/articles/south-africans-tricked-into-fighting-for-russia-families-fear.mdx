A mother, Mary, is frantic because her son was one of 17 South Africans and two Botswanan men who were lured into fighting for Russia. A former president’s daughter, Duduzile Zuma‑Sambudla, promised training and a better future, but the men ended up in Ukraine’s war. The South African government is trying to bring them home, but many still remain in the front. The case shows how economic hardship can lead young people to dangerous deals.
