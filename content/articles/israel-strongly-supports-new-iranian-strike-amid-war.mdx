After Iranian missiles hit Israel last June, people in Tel Aviv now live in shelters every time sirens sound. Even after the latest attack, most Israelis, including politicians from all parties, back the move to hit Iran again, calling it a necessary step to stop terror. The war has made politics quiet and united the government and opposition, but some critics warn that endless fighting won’t bring real safety. Palestinians and tourists also feel the danger.
