Unai Emery returned to Basel, where he won the Europa League in 2016, hoping to start a new era at Aston Villa. The club, in great form after a win over Arsenal, are favourites for the competition. Emery says a top‑eight finish would let them play later rounds at home. He also discussed player rotations ahead of the match and the uncertain future of young winger Harvey Elliott.
