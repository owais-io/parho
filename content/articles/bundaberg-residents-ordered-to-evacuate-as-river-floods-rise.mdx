Residents in Bundaberg have been told to leave right away because the Burnett River is expected to overflow by more than 7 metres. Bridges will close by 3 pm, and about 280 homes and 120 businesses could be hit. Queensland’s weather department says many rivers are high and warns that flooding may spread. The warning follows record rain across the state and highlights how warmer weather is making floods more common.
