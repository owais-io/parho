Nigel Farage says the upcoming Senedd election in Wales will be a vote on UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer. He and new Welsh leader Dan Thomas launched Reform UK's manifesto in Newport, arguing the election could end Labour's 100‑year rule, especially in the valleys. While Plaid Cymru remains ahead in polls, Reform is gaining traction. Their platform includes cutting income tax, prioritising long‑term residents for housing, ending the 20mph speed limit, and scrapping the sanctuary policy for Ukrainian refugees.
