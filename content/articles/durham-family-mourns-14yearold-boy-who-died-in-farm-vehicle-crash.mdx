On December 21, a 14‑year‑old boy named Aaron Anderson died after a John Deere Gator farm vehicle crashed at a farm in Burnopfield, County Durham. He was taken to Newcastle’s Royal Victoria Infirmary, where he died on Boxing Day. Aaron was remembered by his family as a cheerful, hardworking teen with apprenticeship offers. A 13‑year‑old boy was injured but is recovering. The family thanked the community, emergency services and hospital staff, and asked for privacy to grieve.
