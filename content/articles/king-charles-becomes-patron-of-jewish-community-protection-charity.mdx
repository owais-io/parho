After a firebomb attack on Jewish ambulance vans in London, King Charles was named patron of the Community Security Trust, a charity that protects Jewish communities. The role, announced at the charity’s annual dinner, shows the king’s support against antisemitism. Police are investigating whether Iran‑backed militants were behind the attack, while the government plans extra security for Jewish sites.
