A married couple from Kazakhstan, Dilnoza Israilova and Alisherykhoja Israilov, were arrested in Sydney after police discovered a tiny camera hidden in a Mickey‑Mouse T‑shirt and earpieces that let them see and talk at the casino. They used the camera to record card tables and guide each other on bets, winning about $1.18 million in a few weeks. The NSW police seized the gear, jewellery and €2,000, and the pair were denied bail.
