Three men have been charged by the federal police for sending threatening messages to MPs Lidia Thorpe and Allegra Spender, and a high‑office holder. Joel Davis from Bondi is accused of encouraging hate and abuse toward Spender after she spoke out against a neo‑Nazi rally. Stefan Eracleous was charged for harassing Thorpe and defacing an Indigenous flag outside her office. A third man from Tamworth faces several offences, including harassment and illegal firearm possession.
