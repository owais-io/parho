Alaska former Representative Mary Peltola says she’s running for the state’s U.S. Senate seat, giving Democrats a key candidate in the mid‑term race. Peltola, who won Alaska’s lone House seat in 2022 but lost it in 2024, says she’ll focus on fishing, affordable living, and changing the “rigged” system in Washington. Although Alaska is mostly Republican, voters often choose moderate, independent candidates, so her popularity could make her a serious contender.
