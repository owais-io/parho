A New South Wales minister, Kate Washington, said she will not resign after it was revealed that a convicted triple killer, Regina Arthurell, had lived with two foster children. The children were removed after the story broke, and Washington said a departmental review would look at how the mistake happened. Critics want an independent investigation, saying the system failed to protect the kids.
