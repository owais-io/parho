Sharks won 21‑15 over Saracens in a stormy Durban match. The Sharks, in their second win of the season, were led by new coach JP Pietersen after the previous coach left. Though Saracens started strong, the Sharks scored two tries in the first half and took the lead in the second. Stormy weather made handling hard, but the Sharks’ forwards and good kicking helped them win, keeping the pool tight.
