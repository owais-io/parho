A coroner in Cheshire has begun formal investigations into the deaths of five babies that Lucy Letby, a former neonatal nurse, was found guilty of murdering. The hearings will run in September after a pause while a public inquiry report is awaited. Letby is already serving life sentences for seven baby killings and attempts to kill seven more. The inquests will not reopen the convictions but look at how the babies died and hospital failures.
