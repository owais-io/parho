MPs have found serious problems in the compensation plans for people hurt by the Post Office Horizon software scandal. Thousands of former post office workers still wait for the money they deserve, and the process is slow with low offers and paperwork that can retraumatise them. The report says Fujitsu, the software maker, has not paid anything yet, even though it still gets government contracts. The government will review the findings and keep working to fix things.
