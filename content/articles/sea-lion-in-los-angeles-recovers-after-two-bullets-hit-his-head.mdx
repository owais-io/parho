A sea lion named Confetti was rescued from Ballona Creek in Los Angeles. When he arrived at the Marine Mammal Care Center, doctors found two bullets in his skull and a bacterial infection. He was given medication, fluids, and antibiotics. After a week he could swim, chase, and eat fish. The vets are optimistic he will soon be released back into the wild.
