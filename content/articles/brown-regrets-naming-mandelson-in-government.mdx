Gordon Brown said he regrets putting Peter Mandelson back in government after new evidence links Mandelson to Jeffrey Epstein. The revelations, including documents showing Mandelson shared sensitive information with Epstein, have sparked outrage among Labour MPs. The scandal has led to investigations, house searches and calls for stronger ethics rules. Brown also pushed for public hearings for ministers and an independent anti‑corruption body to clean up politics.
