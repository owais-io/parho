Trump told Ukraine’s president, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, that Russia’s leader is ready to negotiate and that Zelenskyy should act fast. He also said U.S. help for Ukraine is wasteful and praised Putin. While Ukraine is working with Middle‑Eastern countries to fight Iranian drones, the U.S. and Ukraine face other tensions—Hungary has seized Ukrainian bank staff, and Ukraine’s Zaporizhzhia nuclear plant has been repaired after Russian attacks. In Crimea, a drone crashed into a building, injuring nine.
