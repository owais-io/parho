Matt Weston, the top‑ranked skeleton racer, is confident about winning gold at this year’s Winter Olympics in Milan‑Cortina. He won five of seven World Cup races and the world title twice, but after a 15th‑place finish in Beijing he almost quit. Now he has rebuilt his confidence, recovered from injuries and built a strong team, and he says the goal is simply to finish on top.
