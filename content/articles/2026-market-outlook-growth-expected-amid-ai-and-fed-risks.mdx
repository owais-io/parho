Investors think stock markets will keep going up in 2026, but they’re worried about a possible AI bubble burst, a new Fed chair who could slash rates, and problems in private‑credit markets. A poll says 57 % think falling AI valuations are the biggest risk. The S&P 500 could finish 2026 around 7,700‑8,100 points. The UK’s FTSE 100 is also expected to grow, while oil prices may fall and copper could rise. Rates in the U.S. and U.K. are likely to cut in 2026.
