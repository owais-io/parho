California sheriff Chad Bianco, who is also running for governor, has taken another 426 boxes of ballot materials, sparking a legal showdown with state leaders. The move follows his earlier seizure of 650,000 ballots last year. State Attorney General Rob Bonta says the investigation is baseless and has filed lawsuits to stop it, while courts so far have denied his requests to halt the process.
