A new study by the British Retail Consortium says UK shoppers feel panic after the Iran war raised energy prices. 64 % of people surveyed expect the economy to get worse in the next three months, while only 11 % think it will improve. Confidence fell sharply from –20 % to –53 %. Higher fuel costs have already pushed up petrol and household bills, and the government is looking at targeted help for the hardest‑hit households.
