Recent polls show One Nation could get up to 26% of Victoria votes, a big jump from last year. Liberal leaders are worried about how the minor party will split votes and whether they will give preferences to Labor or the Liberals. Former premier Jeff Kennett wants a deal, but party leader Jess Wilson says preferences will be decided later. Some Liberal MPs might defect to One Nation, raising stakes for November.
