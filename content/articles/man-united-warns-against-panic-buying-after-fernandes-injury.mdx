Ruben Amorim told fans that Manchester United should not rush into buying new players after Bruno Fernandes went down with a calf injury. Fernandes is likely to miss the Boxing Day match at Old Trafford, and other players like Kobbie Mainoo are also sidelined. Amorim said the club will look for solutions inside the squad, not rush into the January transfer market. He warned that hasty moves could cause mistakes.
