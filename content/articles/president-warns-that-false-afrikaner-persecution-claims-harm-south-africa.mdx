President Cyril Ramaphosa says false claims that white Afrikaners are being persecuted threaten South Africa’s security. He points to US leaders Donald Trump and Elon Musk who spread a “white genocide” myth. Ramaphosa says these rumors hurt the country’s sovereignty and global standing. The US has boycotted a G20 meeting in Johannesburg and moved its G20 focus away from South Africa. Afrikaners make up about 4 % of the population, but the claims lack evidence.
