On a conservative podcast, former President Donald Trump said Republican state officials should take over elections in 15 states to prevent what he calls voter fraud. He claimed non‑citizens were voting and that the elections need to be nationalised. Democrats, including Senators Warner and Schumer, warned this threatens democracy and said they would fight the idea. The White House said Trump sees widespread fraud.
