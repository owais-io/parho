Gen Xers often feel awkward when they admit they’re ambitious, says the Guardian column. In a culture that now celebrates hard work, older people find it uncomfortable to speak openly about their goals. The piece explains Gen X grew up with a “lazy cool” image, so ambition feels out of place. It also ties the awkwardness to the tough job market, rising failure stigma, and youth fear about the future.
