Water bill complaints have risen more than half in England and Wales as people protest steep price hikes. In 2025 the Consumer Council for Water logged 16,000 complaints, up from 10,600 last year, and the spike started in March. Companies can raise rates by 36% over five years to cover infrastructure costs, sparking anger over affordability, dividends, and bonuses. Regulators face pressure to act.
