The United States announced it will begin the second stage of a Gaza ceasefire, which will include a new Palestinian committee of technocrats to run day‑to‑day affairs while the area is demilitarised and rebuilt. The move was revealed by Trump‑appointed envoy Steve Witkoff on social media, but no details or committee members were named. Egypt says it has already agreed on 15 members, and the plan also calls for more aid and a Gaza‑Egypt crossing to open.
