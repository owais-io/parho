English councils will get new guidance from Active Travel England to make streets safer for women and girls. The advice includes brighter lighting, CCTV, and street‑level crossings instead of dark underpasses, plus ideas from Spain and Sweden where women can ask bus drivers to drop them off at night. A poll shows most women change routes after dark and feel unsafe, so the plan shifts the responsibility from people to the places they travel in.
