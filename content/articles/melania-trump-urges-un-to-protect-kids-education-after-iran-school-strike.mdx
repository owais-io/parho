Melania Trump sat at the UN Security Council’s top seat in New York and spoke about keeping schools open for kids worldwide. She did so after reports that an Iranian airstrike hit a girls’ school, killing many. Trump said education stops war, urged the use of AI to bring learning to remote places, and said the U.S. stands with all children. She avoided talking about the U.S. or Israeli attacks on Iran.
