People using weight‑loss shots like Wegovy, Ozempic and Mounjaro have a very small chance of getting a severe, inflamed pancreas. The UK regulator, MHRA, said it has seen over a thousand reports of pancreatitis, mostly with tirzepatide. It urges patients and doctors to watch for sharp belly pain, nausea and fever, and to report any symptoms. Companies say the medicines are safe for most users.
