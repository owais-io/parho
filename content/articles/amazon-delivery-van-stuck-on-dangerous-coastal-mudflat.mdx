An Amazon delivery van was driven onto the Broomway, a risky mudflat path that crosses the Thames estuary to Foulness Island. Following GPS directions, the van ended up on the mudflats, a route known as the deadliest in the country. Coastguards arrived, found the driver had left the vehicle, and the van was later recovered. The Broomway is only safe when the tide is out.
