Doctors say you should throw out underwear every six to nine months because washing can’t wipe out all germs. Studies show a pair can hold bacteria, viruses, and even 0.1g of feces. Some experts say cotton underwear can survive up to 50 washes. The writer tried to track her own underwear’s age, realizing most of hers are older than recommended. The advice? Replace them regularly.
