Russia’s attacks on Ukraine’s power plants have left most of Kyiv without electricity. In the bitter winter, families use makeshift tents, generators and bubble‑wrap to stay warm. Schools are closed, and at least 10 people have died of hypothermia while 1,469 people were hospitalised. Residents, children and teachers say life has become a fight for heat and basic safety. Ukraine is asking the world for help and a pause in the bombings.
