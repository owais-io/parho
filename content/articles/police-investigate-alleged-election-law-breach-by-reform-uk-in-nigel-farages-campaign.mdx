Police are checking whether Reform UK broke election rules when it ran Nigel Farage’s campaign for the Clacton seat. A former aide says the party spent more than the £20,660 limit and didn’t list all its costs, like leaflets and a bar refurbishment. Reform denies any wrongdoing, calling the claims a disgruntled former councillor’s complaint. Farage and the party are asked to cooperate with the investigation, while political leaders urge transparency.
