Grace Tame, an Australian human‑rights advocate, wrote an essay in Crikey calling Prime Minister Anthony Albanese a coward for backing US‑Israel strikes on Iran. She says the government is a propaganda machine and that Australia shouldn’t tie its security to the US and Israel. Tame, who was named Australian of the Year, accuses the PM of betraying his earlier support for Palestine and of putting Australia in a “geopolitical chokehold.”
