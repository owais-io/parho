Sydney’s new plan will turn the city’s greyhound track into a public park and sports fields, while adding up to 7,300 homes nearby. The move comes after a government inquiry that highlighted poor animal care in racing. The track’s lease ends in 2027, after which the land will be handed to the City of Sydney for open space. The plan also includes new housing, transport upgrades and support for the racing industry to relocate.
