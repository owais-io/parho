The Dargo Hotel, a 144‑year‑old pub in Victoria’s Alpine region, is now on the market. It’s famous for a motorbike hanging from its roof, a tribute to a former biker tour leader who died in 2018. The Hall family bought the hotel in 1993, revamped it, and are now selling after 17 years. They hope a new owner keeps the quirky mementos and the pub’s historic feel.
