After the Taliban banned women from public life, a group of women in Kabul protested by burning a burqa. Three days later, Taliban fighters broke into one woman’s home at night. She jumped from a window and survived. Her sister filmed the attack and sent the clip to a journalist, which went viral and drew global outrage. The video helped them escape, but protests are now hidden or banned, and many women live in exile.
