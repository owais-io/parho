Police in British Columbia say they have identified the person who opened fire at a secondary school in Tumbler Ridge, an 18‑year‑old woman named Jesse Van Rootselaar. The attack killed a teacher and five students, and the suspect’s mother and 11‑year‑old step‑brother were later found dead at home. The shooter also died from a self‑inflicted gunshot. The incident is one of Canada’s worst mass‑shooting tragedies.
