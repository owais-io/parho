An ICE officer shot and killed 37‑year‑old Renee Good in Minneapolis. The local police bureau was asked to help investigate, but the FBI stepped in and took full control, cutting the state agency’s access to evidence and witnesses. Trump and Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem say the officer acted in self‑defence, but videos show Good’s car was moving away. Minnesota officials and protesters demand a fair probe and call for ICE to leave the state.
