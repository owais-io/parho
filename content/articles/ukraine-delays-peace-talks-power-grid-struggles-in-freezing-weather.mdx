Ukraine says a Russian drone hit a bus carrying miners, killing 12. At the same time, Kyiv pushed back planned peace talks to Wednesday‑Thursday, hoping for real progress. While Russia has stopped most energy attacks, it still hit rail and power lines. The country is battling a brutal cold snap, with power outages planned. Elon Musk’s Starlink block has helped, and Russians gained control of a couple villages. Czechs also rallied in Prague to back Ukraine.
