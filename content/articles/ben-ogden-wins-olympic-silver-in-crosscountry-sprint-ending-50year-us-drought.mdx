Ben Ogden, 25, won silver in the men’s sprint classic at the Milan Cortina Winter Games, ending a 50‑year medal drought for US men’s cross‑country skiing. He finished just 0.87 seconds behind Norway’s Johannes Klæbo. Ogden says his love of knitting helps him relax after long races. Growing up skiing in Vermont, he now leads a young U.S. team aiming for more podiums.
