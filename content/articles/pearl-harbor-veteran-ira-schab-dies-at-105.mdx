Pearl Harbor veteran Ira Schab, 105, passed away at his Oregon home. He was one of the last survivors of the 1941 attack. A navy musician on the USS Dobbin, he helped supply ammunition to anti‑aircraft gunners that morning. After the war he became an electrical engineer and worked on the Apollo program. He attended the 83rd anniversary, watched the 84th online, and left about a dozen survivors behind.
