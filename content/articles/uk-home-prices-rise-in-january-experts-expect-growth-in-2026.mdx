In January UK house prices nudged up 0.3%, leaving them 1 % higher than a year earlier at £270,873. Nationwide says the market should keep growing, forecasting a 2‑4 % rise in 2026 as mortgage rates ease and budget worries ease. Demand remains fragile, with mortgage approvals down 9 % last month. The Bank of England’s rate sits at 3.75 %, while inflation stays above target.
