Somali‑born Rep. Ilhan Omar says President Trump’s repeated attacks on her – calling her illegal and accusing her of marrying her brother – are an “unhealthy and creepy obsession.” She reminds people she became a U.S. citizen at 17, faces many threats, and is protected by security. Omar stresses that Trump’s hate fuels violence against her and other minorities, and she vows to keep working on health care, climate and immigration reform.
