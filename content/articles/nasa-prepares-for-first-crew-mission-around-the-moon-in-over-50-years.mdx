NASA is preparing for its first crewed mission around the moon in more than 50 years, called Artemis II. The launch is set for February 6 from Florida’s Kennedy Space Center, using the powerful Space Launch System rocket and Orion capsule. Four astronauts will orbit the moon, test life‑support and docking, and practice emergency procedures. The mission marks a historic return to lunar exploration and a push to stay ahead of China’s plans.
