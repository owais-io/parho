Antonio Tejero, the police officer who tried to take control of Spain’s parliament in 1981, died at 93. His death came the same day the government opened 153 documents that detail the failed coup. Tejero had been jailed for his part in the attack and later claimed he was not sorry. The documents show how the plot was planned and who might have known about it, giving people a clearer picture of that tense period.
