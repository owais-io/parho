United are hunting fresh talent for the Premier League. Their shortlist includes 19‑year‑old Chelsea prospect Tyrique George, and they are eyeing 21‑year‑old Rafael Leão from Milan and 20‑year‑old Iliman Ndiaye from Everton. Other clubs are also busy: Tottenham’s Randal Kolo Muani faces an uncertain future, Juventus could recall their striker, and West Ham is eyeing Chelsea defender Axel Disasi. Meanwhile, Leeds and other teams are looking for new strikers.
