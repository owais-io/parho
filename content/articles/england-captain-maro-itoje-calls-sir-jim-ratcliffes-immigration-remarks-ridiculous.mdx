England captain Maro Itoje criticised billionaire Sir Jim Ratcliffe's comments about immigrants. Ratcliffe said the UK is being “colonised” by people on benefits, sparking backlash. Itoje, who has Nigerian roots and recently lost his mother, said the remarks were wrong and offensive. He added Ratcliffe should apologise. The incident shows how sports figures can speak on social issues in the media.
