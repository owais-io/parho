In Cape Town, rising numbers of baboons are leaping from roads to houses, sometimes attacking pets or people. Residents, activists, and authorities clash over how to keep the primates safe and the community safe. Plans include fences, a new bylaw, and moving troops to a sanctuary, but legal challenges and differing opinions mean the problem is far from solved.
