UK lawmakers will use a new law to fund a permanent memorial for the 72 people who died in the Grenfell Tower blaze. Housing Secretary Steve Reed said the bill gives the money and authority needed to build and maintain the tribute. He also said the government is speeding up building safety changes, policing the fire investigation, and will finish the Grenfell inquiry’s recommendations this year.
