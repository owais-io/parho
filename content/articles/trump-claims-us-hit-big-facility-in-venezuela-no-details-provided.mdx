Trump says US forces hit a big facility in Venezuela, possibly a drug‑smuggling dock, but he gives no specifics and the White House hasn’t spoken about it. The video that appears online is unverified, and the Venezuelan government has not responded. This claim comes as the U.S. has increased military presence around Venezuela to block drug trafficking and oil shipments. No confirmation of a strike exists yet.
