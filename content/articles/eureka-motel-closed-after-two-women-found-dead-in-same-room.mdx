A motel in Eureka, California, was shut down after police found two women dead in the same room just days apart. The first death happened on Feb. 21 and the second on Feb. 26. Investigators suspect drug overdoses and found high carbon‑monoxide levels, with no working detectors. City officials closed the motel until all rooms are inspected, repairs are made, and safety devices are installed.
