A woman in North Carolina, who vanished in 2001, has just been located alive and well after 24 years. Authorities are keeping her exact location private. Her daughter, who grew up without her mother, expressed a mix of joy and confusion. The family confirmed the mother’s husband was cleared of any wrongdoing. This case shows how some missing‑person investigations can finally close after decades.
