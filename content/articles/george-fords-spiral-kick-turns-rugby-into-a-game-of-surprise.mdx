England fly‑half George Ford talks about using his spiral kick to keep opponents guessing. The twist in the ball’s flight makes it hard to catch and can give England better attacking chances. Ford says the key is not just the kick, but what happens after you win the ball back. He also highlights the new rule changes that make catching the ball trickier.
