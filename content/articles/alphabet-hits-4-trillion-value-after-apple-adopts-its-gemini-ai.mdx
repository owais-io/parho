Alphabet’s share price jumped, pushing the company to a $4 trillion market value, making it the world’s second‑most valuable after Nvidia. Apple announced it will use Google’s Gemini AI to power Siri, sparking the rally, though neither side said how much the deal costs. The move shows investors are buying confidence in Google’s AI plans, helped by fast‑growing cloud sales and steady ad revenue. Still, the company faces two US antitrust lawsuits.
