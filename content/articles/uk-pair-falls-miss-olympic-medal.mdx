Team GB ice‑dance pair Lilah Fear and Lewis Gibson made a costly mistake early in their free dance at the Milan arena, losing key points and dropping from fourth to seventh place. Their error meant they missed out on a medal, even though they had hoped to be the first British Olympic skating winners since 1992. Gold went to a French duo, silver to the American Chock‑Bates, and bronze to Canada.
