An escaped goat ran around a Wiltshire village, chasing a woman and eating oranges from a Christmas wreath. Police with riot shields and a lasso caught it and returned it to its owner. The goat even tried to chew on the officers’ safety gear. After a brief escape, the animals were secured and taken home. The incident was recorded on body‑camera footage and showed officers joking about the goat’s mischief.
