US President Donald Trump said he could send troops to Colombia after the US attacked Venezuela. He accused Colombia’s president, Gustavo Petro, of running cocaine factories, even though there is no proof. Petro denied the charges and warned that bombing the country would drive people into rebel groups. Both sides have defended each other, but the United States has also slapped sanctions on Petro and increased its military presence in the region.
