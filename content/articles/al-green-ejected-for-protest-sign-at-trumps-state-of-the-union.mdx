During President Trump’s State of the Union, Democrats in the House largely stayed quiet while Republicans cheered. Texas Democrat Al Green was ejected for holding a sign that said “Black people aren’t apes,” a reference to Trump’s racist video. He was escorted out but kept the sign for cameras. Several other Democrats left early or spoke out, showing strong opposition to Trump’s remarks and policies.
