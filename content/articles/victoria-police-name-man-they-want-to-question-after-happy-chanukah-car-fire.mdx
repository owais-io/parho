Police in Victoria are looking for 47‑year‑old John Argento, who has an arrest warrant, after a car with a “Happy Chanukah” sign was set on fire in St Kilda East on Christmas Day. No one was in the car, and the family living nearby was evacuated. Police say the fire was a targeted attack but there’s no evidence Argento is a threat to the Jewish community. The Premier and local MPs say they’re working hard to keep the community safe and to fight anti‑Semitic hate.
