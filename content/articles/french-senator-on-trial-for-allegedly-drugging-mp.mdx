A former French senator, Joël Guerriau, is on trial in Paris after MP Sandrine Josso says he slipped MDMA into her champagne. Josso survived and has become a leading voice against drug‑facilitated sexual assault in France. Guerriau denies intent, claiming it was an accident. The court will also review his internet searches, and he faces up to five years for assault and up to ten for drug possession.
