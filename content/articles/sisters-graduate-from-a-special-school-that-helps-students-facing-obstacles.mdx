The story is about two sisters Trevanna and Deslie from the Dunghutti community who faced loss, pregnancy, and poverty. They attended Macleay Vocational College, a school that supports at‑risk students with food, transport, childcare, and counseling. The college’s Ginda Barri Mums and Bubs program let them attend classes while caring for kids. After years of struggle, both graduated, thanks to support and each other.
