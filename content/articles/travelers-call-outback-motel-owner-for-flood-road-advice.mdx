Travelers in Queensland have been calling an outback motel owner instead of using maps. Narelle Hetherington runs a motel in Winton, which is now cut off by floodwater. A couple planning a 1,000‑km trip called her for advice, and she told them to avoid the roads. The town has been isolated for a week, but locals have helped each other and some have even been air‑lifted out. Residents say check weather alerts and call locals before traveling.
