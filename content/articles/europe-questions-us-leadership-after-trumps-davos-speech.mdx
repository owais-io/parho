At Davos, Trump said the world couldn’t work without the US, but many European leaders have turned the tables. They say the US no longer acts as a trustworthy ally and that the old rules‑based system is broken. Presidents like Macron, von der Leyen and others call for stronger European power and new middle‑power alliances to counter the US, China and Russia. The move signals a shift toward more independent foreign policy.
