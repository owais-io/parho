Police in Perth arrested a 31‑year‑old who allegedly threw a homemade bomb into a 2,500‑strong Invasion Day rally. The device was designed to explode on impact, but it failed to detonate. He faces charges of intent to harm and making explosives. Police and federal agencies are still probing, with possible extra charges. Prime Minister Albanese said he should be fully prosecuted, and Indigenous leaders called for a thorough investigation to protect future rallies.
