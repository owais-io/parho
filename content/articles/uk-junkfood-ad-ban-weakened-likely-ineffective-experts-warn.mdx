The UK ban on junk‑food ads before 9 pm is weaker than promised. Experts say it only covers about 1 % of the £2.4 bn spent on food advertising, leaving huge gaps. Companies can still use TV, billboards, and their own social media. Even though 1 % sounds small, it’s still millions of pounds, but experts worry it won’t change the overall marketing mix.
