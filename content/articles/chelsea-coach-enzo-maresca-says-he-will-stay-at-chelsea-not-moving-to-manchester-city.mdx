Maresca says he has no intention of leaving Chelsea, despite rumors he could replace Pep Guardiola at Manchester City. He has a contract until 2029 and is focused on the team’s progress, especially after the recent win over Everton. After a brief comment about support, he insisted the speculation is false and that his priority is the club’s performance, not personal moves.
