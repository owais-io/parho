Australia is weighing a visa ban for Israeli TV reporter Zvi Yehezkeli because he once said Israel should kill 100,000 Gazans. The minister, Tony Burke, said he is still considering the decision as Yehezkeli is scheduled to give speeches in Sydney and Melbourne next month. Yehezkeli has a history of controversial comments, and the move comes amid tense Australia‑Israel relations over visa disputes.
