Chinese travelers are skipping Japan for their Lunar New Year trips because of a heated spat over Taiwan. Japan’s prime minister hinted that its military could step in if China attacked Taiwan, prompting Chinese officials to warn against visiting. Tourist numbers in Japan fell almost by half in December and are expected to drop up to 60% this year. Meanwhile, South Korea is set to become the top choice for Chinese holidaymakers.
