Israel launched big airstrikes on southern Beirut after ordering the city’s 500,000 residents to leave. The bombings hit areas the military said were linked to Hezbollah, even covering hospitals and ministries. The move followed Hezbollah’s missile attacks on Israel and has escalated a conflict now touching Iran and the U.S. The strikes have killed at least 100 people in Lebanon, with U.S. and Israeli forces also striking Iranian targets.
