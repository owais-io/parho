England and Australia clashed in the 2025‑26 Ashes, with Australia winning the series. Travis Head and Mitchell Starc stole the show for Australia, taking 31 wickets and scoring a big century. England’s bright spot was Jacob Bethell’s maiden Test hundred. The series saw embarrassing drops and poor fielding from England, while Australian bowlers dominated. Analysts say England needs better coaching, harder bowling and more county play; Australia should keep its fast bowlers fit and develop spin.
