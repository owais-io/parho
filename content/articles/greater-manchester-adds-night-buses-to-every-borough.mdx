Greater Manchester’s public‑owned Bee Network will add night bus services to every borough. Mayor Andy Burnham said the move will help 740,000 people and boost the night‑time economy. Since the network was taken back into public control last year it has seen a 14% rise in journeys and better punctuality. Other northern regions are watching and may copy the model, while the city plans to grow the network further.
