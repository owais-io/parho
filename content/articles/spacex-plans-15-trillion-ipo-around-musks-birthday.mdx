SpaceX is eyeing a $1.5 trillion valuation for a public offering in early summer 2026, timing the IPO to match a planetary alignment and Elon Musk’s 55th birthday. The company would raise about $50 bn. Talks with banks such as JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs, and Morgan Stanley are underway, and the deal would make SpaceX the biggest IPO ever, surpassing Saudi Aramco’s 2019 sale.
