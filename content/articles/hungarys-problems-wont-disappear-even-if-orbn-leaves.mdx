The European Union is holding a summit while Hungary’s prime minister, Viktor Orbán, tries to block a €90 billion loan for Ukraine. Even if Hungary elects a new government next month, Orbán’s influence could still be strong. The upcoming election might not change Hungary’s role in EU politics, and Orbán could refuse to step down. The EU may still struggle to get Ukraine funded and to push back on other issues.
