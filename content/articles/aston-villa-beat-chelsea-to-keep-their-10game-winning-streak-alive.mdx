Aston Villa kept their 10‑game winning streak alive with a 2‑0 victory over Chelsea. After being behind, Ollie Watkins scored twice to take the lead and hold it. The team turned the game after manager Unai Emery made three substitutions at the break, giving Villa more energy and better play. Villa’s ability to win after losing a goal keeps them in the title race, and the next match against Arsenal will be a big test.
