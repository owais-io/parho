Drivers on the M1 are paying record prices for fuel, with diesel up to £1.86 per litre at Woodall services near Sheffield. Prices have jumped the fastest since 2022, driven by a surge in global oil prices after airstrikes in Iran. Motorists, families, and lorry drivers are frustrated, saying the cost forces them to rethink trips or shop for cheaper pumps. The industry, petrol retailers, and the government are debating how to curb the rise and protect consumers.
