After the US and Israel started hitting targets in Iran, the Pentagon said the first six days of the war cost more than $11 billion, but independent analysts put the figure at $12.7 billion. The money goes mainly to expensive missiles, interceptors, and radar. The estimates don’t include extra costs like moving troops, medical care, or fixing damaged planes. The war’s real cost is likely higher.
