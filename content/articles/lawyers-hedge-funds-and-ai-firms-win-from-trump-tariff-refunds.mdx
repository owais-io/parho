After a Supreme Court ruling on February 20 struck down Trump’s tariffs, about $175 billion is up for businesses to claim back. Companies like FedEx and L’Oreal are filing for refunds, and lawyers are in high demand, charging $10‑15 k per case. Hedge funds are buying the right to those refunds, and some AI firms want a share by handling the paperwork. The money mainly flows through lawyers and investors, not necessarily to shoppers.
