A third man has been charged in the murder of 85‑year‑old Sydney resident Chris Baghsarian after a mistaken‑identity kidnapping. Police had earlier charged two other men and a woman. The victim was taken from his North Ryde home on 13 Feb, and his body was found near a golf club in Pitt Town. Arrests were made during a search in Seven Hills, where officers seized a firearm, drug supplies and mobile phones.
