Manchester City won 3‑0 against Brentford to reach the Carabao Cup semi‑finals. Rayan Cherki scored the opening goal, then Savinho added two more. The win gives City a comfortable spot in the next round where they will face Newcastle. Coach Pep Guardiola praised his young players and said the team needs to grow. Brentford’s manager, Keith Andrews, said the game was good and will train for the next match.
