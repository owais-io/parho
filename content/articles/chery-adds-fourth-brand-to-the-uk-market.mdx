Chery, the Chinese carmaker, is expanding in Britain by launching a fourth brand called Lepas. The new line will focus on battery‑ and hybrid‑powered SUVs aimed at younger families. Lepas cars will be built in China and shipped to the UK, where there are no US or EU tariffs, but Chery may eventually build in Britain. The move follows Chery’s growing UK sales, where it already outsold many rivals.
