A detainee in Australia was stabbed by a fellow prisoner while being moved in a plain Kia Carnival that had no safety barriers. The attacker fled the vehicle, sparking a hunt. The incident has raised concerns about the private company MTC’s use of unmodified vans for detainee transport. Staff and unions have warned the cars lack protection, and the government is now adding barriers.
