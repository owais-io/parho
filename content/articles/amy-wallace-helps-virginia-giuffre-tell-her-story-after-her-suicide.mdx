Amy Wallace, the ghostwriter behind Virginia Giuffre’s book *Nobody’s Girl*, faced a huge challenge when Giuffre died by suicide in 2025. The book, exposing Jeffrey Epstein’s abuse, became a bestseller and helped many survivors, especially in Australia, speak out. Wallace keeps the story alive, talking at events and urging the U.S. Department of Justice to investigate the men Giuffre said she was trafficked to.
