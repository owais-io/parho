Arsenal’s women’s team won the first FIFA Women’s Champions Cup by beating Brazil’s Corinthians 3‑2 after extra time. The match, played at London’s Emirates Stadium, saw Arsenal use their fitness advantage, as Corinthians were still in pre‑season. The game went to extra time after a 2‑2 draw, when a last‑minute goal from Caitlin Foord sealed the win. Arsenal’s victory makes them world champions, but the tournament’s credibility still needs work.
