Senegal and Egypt are set to face each other in the AFCON semi‑final, and the story is as much about two star players as it is about the match. Sadio Mané, the Senegal captain who saved his team from penalty heartbreak in 2022, will go up against Egypt’s Mohamed Salah, who struggled in the same shootouts. The two 33‑year‑olds, once teammates at Liverpool, have a long history of on‑field rivalry. Fans expect a tense showdown.
