Colin Boyce, a Nationals MP, says he will run against leader David Littleproud after the party quit the Coalition. Boyce claims the split was a mistake and that staying alone would hurt the Nationals’ chances in the next election. Most colleagues think Boyce has little support, and Littleproud appears safe. The challenge comes after a chaotic week that saw front‑bench members resign and the party face pressure from the right‑wing One Nation.
