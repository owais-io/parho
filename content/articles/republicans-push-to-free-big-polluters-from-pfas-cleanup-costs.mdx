Republicans want big polluters like water plants and landfills to avoid paying for cleanup of dangerous PFAS chemicals, also known as forever chemicals. The EPA has made two PFAS types hazardous and could force polluters to pay. Industry argues they’re not the source and have other priorities. Public‑health groups warn the move would slow efforts to keep drinking water safe and stop PFAS buildup.
