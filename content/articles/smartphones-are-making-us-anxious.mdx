Smartphones have become a major part of our lives, but their constant notifications and social media feeds can trap us in endless scrolling. The article traces how devices that once promised convenience have now turned into tools that push us toward anxiety, depression, and endless comparison. Experts disagree, but the writer admits spending more than four hours a day on her phone, hurting focus and mood. Some politicians want limits for kids, and new AI tools may worsen the problem.
