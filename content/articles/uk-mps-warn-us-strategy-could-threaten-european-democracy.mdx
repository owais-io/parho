UK MPs criticised a new US national‑security strategy that echoes 1930s right‑wing ideas and warns about ‘civilizational erasure’ in Europe. They said the plan could let the US interfere in European politics, which is frightening. Some MPs want tighter defence cooperation, while others defend the US as a friend. The document also calls for ending mass migration and has been welcomed by Russia.
