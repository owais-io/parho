Chelsea manager Enzo Maresca said referees were inconsistent after a 1-1 draw at Stamford Bridge. He praised the red card for Moisés Caicedo but argued Arsenal’s Piero Hincapié shouldn’t have escaped a card after punching Trevoh Chalobah, giving him a black eye. Maresca compared it to a similar incident with Tottenham’s Rodrigo Bentancur and said the decisions were unfair. Arsenal coach Arteta said his side missed a chance to win.
