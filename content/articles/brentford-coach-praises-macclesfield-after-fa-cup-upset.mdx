After Brentford beat Macclesfield in the FA Cup fourth round, coach Keith Andrews paid a visit to the club’s dressing room and praised their determination and defensive play. He said the team’s rise from a phoenix club to National League North champions “fills my heart.” Andrews also highlighted the club’s strong community spirit and the joy of seeing a lower‑league side beat Premier League opposition. Macclesfield will next face West Ham in the fifth round.
