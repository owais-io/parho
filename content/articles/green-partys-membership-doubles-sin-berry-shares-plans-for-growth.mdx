Siân Berry, a Green MP from Brighton, says the party is ready to speak up on every issue because it now has four MPs instead of one. The Green Party’s new leader, Zack Polanski, has almost doubled the party’s members from 70,000 to 140,000, making it larger than the Liberal Democrats and even the Conservatives. Berry praised the party’s new focus on jobs, health and the environment, and said they want to keep listening to voters.
