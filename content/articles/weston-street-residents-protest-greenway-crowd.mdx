Residents of Weston Street are angry about the busy GreenWay trail that runs through their quiet suburb. Since the 6‑km path opened, thousands of cyclists, joggers and tourists spill onto the narrow street, causing noise, litter, traffic hazards and stress. A community meeting called for speed limits, better signage and a possible detour. City officials say they’re listening but stress the GreenWay’s benefits, while residents feel their safety is at risk.
