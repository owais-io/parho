A man at Newcastle’s Bar Beach had big white‑power and Nazi SS tattoos on his leg. He waved a white‑power sign and left the beach. Local resident Ben Parsons took photos and told Crime Stoppers and police. Police arrested the 36‑year‑old in Urunga, charged him with displaying a Nazi symbol in public, and gave him conditional bail until court next month.
