eBay has agreed to buy the UK resale app Depop for about $1.2 billion in cash from Etsy. The move is aimed at drawing younger, fashion‑savvy shoppers, who make up most of Depop’s 7 million users. Etsy sold the app for a $400 million loss after buying it for $1.6 billion. Depop will keep its brand and culture, and the deal should help eBay grow its resale business. Shares in both companies jumped after the announcement.
