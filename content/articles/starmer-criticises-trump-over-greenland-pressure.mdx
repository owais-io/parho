Keir Starmer took a sharp stance against US President Donald Trump during a Commons debate, saying Trump used his comments on the Chagos Islands to pressure Britain on Greenland. He accused Trump of not being sincere and warned the US against imposing tariffs on European nations that support Greenland’s autonomy. Starmer said the UK would not back down on its Greenland stance, while critics in the UK also opposed any US takeover.
