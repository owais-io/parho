UK Conservative Kemi Badenoch said Donald Trump’s repeated attacks on Prime Minister Keir Starmer were “childish”. She criticised the U.S. president’s comments about the Iran‑related war, saying they send the wrong message to Iran and Russia and hurt UK‑US unity. Badenoch, who had once praised Trump’s boldness, is now pushing the UK to stay neutral and keep the focus on talks behind closed doors.
