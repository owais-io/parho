The Gaza peace plan is stuck because Israel wants Hamas to hand over all weapons before any other steps. Israel says it will give Hamas a 60‑day deadline and warns that it could restart war if Hamas refuses. The U.S. has set the deadline but has not detailed how the weapons will be handled or how a new police force will be formed. Palestinian experts are not ready yet, and international troops are hesitant to take guns from Hamas.
