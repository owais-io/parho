Across nine European countries, a recent poll found that almost half of people (48%) think Donald Trump is an enemy of Europe. More than half also say the risk of a war with Russia is high, and 69% believe their own country would struggle to defend itself. Still, 74% want their nation to stay in the EU, and 63% see the UK’s exit as a mistake. Tech security tops the list of threats.
