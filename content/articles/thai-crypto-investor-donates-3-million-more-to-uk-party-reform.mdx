Christopher Harborne, a Thai‑based cryptocurrency and aviation investor, has given another £3 million to the UK political party Reform UK, adding to his earlier £9 million gift. The money will help the party before the May elections, where leader Nigel Farage aims to win seats in Wales and England. Harborne’s large donations, along with similar cash from other wealthy donors, have sparked calls for limits on political giving and a ban on crypto‑based contributions.
