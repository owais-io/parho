Leicester Women are preparing to sign experienced left‑back Ashleigh Neville after Tottenham added new full‑backs Julie Blakstad and Hanna Wijk. Neville, who spent eight and a half years at Tottenham and has made 100 WSL appearances, is expected to get more starting time at Leicester. The move comes as Leicester look to add seasoned players, while Tottenham continue building a squad that’s already finished fifth in the league.
