An independent study says the 2022 fights between Hindu and Muslim groups in Leicester were mainly caused by online lies and misinformation. The report also says city leaders and police failed to act to keep people safe. The clashes involved vandalism, assaults and attacks on homes, and more than 50 people have been charged. The findings urge new community forums and better police training to stop future conflict.
