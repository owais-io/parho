In a move that drew sharp rebuke, House Speaker Mike Johnson unveiled a new “America First” award for former President Donald Trump at a GOP fundraiser. The trophy—a golden eagle statue—was presented while the U.S. faces a war in Iran, a tightening Cuban oil blockade, immigration enforcement sweeps and a partial government shutdown. Democrats, officials and commentators blasted the award as a token gesture that ignores real problems.
