US GDP jumped 4.3% in the third quarter, the fastest growth in two years, according to the Commerce Department. President Trump says the increase is due to his tariffs and says they’ll keep improving. Meanwhile, consumer confidence has dropped for five straight months, the longest slump since 2008. The Federal Reserve is watching the data as it decides on interest rates after a recent rate cut.
