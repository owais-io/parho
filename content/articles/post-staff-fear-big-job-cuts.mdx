Washington Post employees are nervous that the paper may cut over 100 jobs – more than 10% of the newsroom – with sports, metro and foreign reporting most at risk. Staff sent a letter to owner Jeff Bezos warning that shrinking the international section hurts the paper’s reach. A memo from the managing editor cut Olympic coverage, sparking anger. No official confirmation yet, but the uncertainty is hurting morale and the paper’s future.
