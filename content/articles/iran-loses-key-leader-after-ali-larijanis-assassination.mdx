Iran has lost one of its most powerful and experienced politicians after Israeli forces killed Ali Larijani, the secretary of the national security council. Larijani bridged the military, government and foreign allies like China and Russia, and could have helped steer the country after Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei. His death creates a big gap in Iran’s leadership and shows that the regime still faces major internal challenges.
