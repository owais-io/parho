Two top US‑born boxers, Teófimo López and Shakur Stevenson, will face off for the junior welterweight title at Madison Square Garden. López holds the WBO belt, Stevenson is chasing his fourth weight‑class crown. Both fighters are 28, 24 respectively, and have won multiple world titles. The bout will test López’s explosive power against Stevenson’s slick, defensive style. Fans can expect a high‑stakes showdown that could shift the boxing landscape.
