A 12‑foot gold statue of Donald Trump and Jeffrey Epstein was installed on the National Mall to mock their friendship. The artists used a Titanic‑style pose to hint at scandal and secrecy. It is part of a series of guerrilla artworks by a secret group that wants to keep Epstein’s files in the public eye. The White House condemned the piece, saying it shows double standards.
