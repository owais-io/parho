Xi told Trump to be careful about selling weapons to Taiwan, saying the Taiwan issue is the top priority in China‑US relations and that China will not allow the island to separate. Taiwan’s president said US ties remain strong. The US plans a big $11.1 bn arms package, but China has staged drills and the Taiwanese opposition blocks extra defense spending. Trump called the call “excellent.”
