Human rights groups warn that the UK wants to change how the European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR) is applied to asylum seekers. The government says it could broaden the rule against torture to allow more deportations. NGOs fear this could let people be sent to countries where they might be tortured or separated from families. They argue any weakening would endanger universal protections.
