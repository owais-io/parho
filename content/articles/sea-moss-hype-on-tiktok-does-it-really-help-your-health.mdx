Sea moss has become a TikTok craze, with influencers touting it as a cure for acne, gut problems and a super‑nutrient powerhouse. But scientists warn that most studies are on cells or animals, and human trials are scarce. The algae may help blood sugar or cholesterol, but it can also contain iodine or heavy metals. Experts say a balanced diet matters more than any supplement.
