The Democratic National Committee has started a new project called Local Listeners to win back voters who turned out in 2020 but stayed home in 2024. The plan will reach more than a million “infrequent” voters in swing districts. Volunteers will be trained in active listening and aim for 250,000 phone calls and 50 grassroots events by March. The move echoes Trump’s 2024 outreach and comes as Democrats try to regain the House while the GOP holds a fundraising edge.
