Rolls‑Royce has decided not to make its cars all electric by 2030, after earlier promises. The CEO says customers still want the V12 engine, so the brand will keep both electric and petrol models. Other luxury makers like Bentley and Honda are also slowing their electric rollouts, and the whole industry is facing big losses on EV projects. The company is also building more capacity at its Goodwood plant.
