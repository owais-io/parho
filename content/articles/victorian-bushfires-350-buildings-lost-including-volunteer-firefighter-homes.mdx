Victorian bushfires have burned 350 buildings so far, including homes of volunteer firefighters in Natimuk. Twelve major fires still burn across the state, but conditions are easing. Premier Jacinta Allan has launched a fundraising appeal and announced up to $52,250 in payments for uninsured families, plus mental‑health support. The state warns of more heat later in January, and new fire‑trucks are on order. Communities are working together to rebuild.
