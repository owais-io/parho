A new report says Britain's strict safety rules are stopping big projects like new nuclear plants, train lines and reservoirs. John Fingleton, who wrote the report for the government, says regulators need to accept more risk. Prime Minister Keir Starmer has backed his ideas, and some politicians want the rules to change for all industry. Unions argue safety must stay high, warning workers would be put at risk.
