More than 800 tech employees have signed a petition asking their CEOs to pressure President Trump to remove ICE agents from all U.S. cities and cancel contracts with the agency. The petition cites the recent killing of nurse Alex Pretti by ICE in Minneapolis and the previous success in stopping an ICE surge in San Francisco after tech leaders spoke up. Some CEOs have spoken out against ICE, while others remain silent.
