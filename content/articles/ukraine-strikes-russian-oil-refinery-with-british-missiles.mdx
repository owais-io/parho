Ukraine fired British Storm Shadow missiles at the Novoshakhtinsk oil refinery in Russia, causing many explosions. The plant supplies fuel to southern Russia and the army. President Zelensky said he had good talks with U.S. envoys about ending the war. Meanwhile, Russia jailed a left‑wing activist, a French scholar faces new charges, and the Mariupol theatre is set to reopen after Russian‑built reconstruction.
