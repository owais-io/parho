After more than ten years, Malaysia and its partners are restarting the search for flight MH370, which vanished in 2014 with 239 people aboard. Ocean Infinity will look over a 5,800‑square‑mile area starting Dec. 30, paying only if wreckage is found. The company had tried earlier this year but had to stop because of bad weather. Families of victims hope this new effort brings answers.
