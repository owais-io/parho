A huge cloud of Saharan dust is heading over the UK, making sunrises and sunsets look like a warm golden painting. The dust can settle on cars, windows and roads, especially when it mixes with rain – a phenomenon called “blood rain.” Weather forecasters warn that the dusty weather may also raise air‑pollution levels. Temperatures are set to hit the highest of the year, around 19 °C in the south, with a mix of sunshine and clouds across the country.
