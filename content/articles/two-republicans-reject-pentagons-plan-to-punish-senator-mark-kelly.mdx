Two Republicans, Susan Collins and Thom Tillis, said the Pentagon’s plan to punish Senator Mark Kelly was wrong. Secretary Pete Hegseth wants to censure Kelly and cut his pension after Kelly posted a video urging the military to obey the law. Collins said it was inappropriate, and Tillis warned that the move could silence free speech. Most other Republicans stayed quiet while Kelly called the censure “outrageous” and “un‑American.”
