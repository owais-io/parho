A Michigan Democratic lawmaker has filed impeachment papers against Health Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr., saying he’s spread misinformation, cut research funding and raised healthcare costs. Rep. Haley Stevens, who is running for the Senate, claims his policies endanger public health. The measure has little chance of moving in a Republican‑controlled House and isn’t backed by Democratic leaders. Health officials say Kennedy is focused on improving health, while former surgeons general warned his changes threaten national health.
