Dubai authorities arrested and handed over a man to Russia for allegedly shooting and wounding Lt Gen Vladimir Alekseyev, a senior GRU officer. Russian security says the suspect, Lyubomir Korba, was directed by Ukrainian intelligence. The attack happened on a Moscow stairwell and left the general in critical condition. Ukraine denies responsibility, saying it was internal Russian conflict, while Russia calls it a terrorist act aimed at derailing peace talks.
