Ken Weetch, 92, was a Labour MP for Ipswich who served 13 years against national trends. He led a campaign that ended the solicitor monopoly on property sales, getting the change in 1985. Known for his plain‑spoken dedication, he earned respect across parties and was a beloved local figure. Born in a Welsh mining village, he studied economics, taught, and served in the army before entering politics. He died on 5 February 2026.
