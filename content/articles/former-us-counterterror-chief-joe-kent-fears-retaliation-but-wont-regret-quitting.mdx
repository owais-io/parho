Joe Kent, the former head of the U.S. national counter‑terrorism center, quit the Trump administration after the U.S. and Israel launched a war in Iran. He says he will face political backlash but has no regrets. Kent says the war was unnecessary, that he was pressured by the Israeli lobby and Trump’s media allies, and that he was willing to risk retaliation for exposing the truth. He has faced an FBI search and plans to continue speaking out.
