Lizzy Yarnold talks about how arriving at the Olympic village turned her from a lonely skeleton racer into a proud member of Team GB. She explains that wearing the team colours and sharing food halls helped her feel connected. She also shares her injuries, illnesses and the mental focus needed for the Games. Yarnold reminds us that athletes overcome many obstacles and that belonging is key to performing at the highest level.
