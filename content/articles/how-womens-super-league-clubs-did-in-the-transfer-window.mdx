Arsenal is rebuilding, bringing in right‑back Smilla Holmberg and backup goalkeeper Votíkova. Villa paid £450,000 for Jean‑François and loaned Jenna Nighswonger. Brighton kept star Fran Kirby and added midfielder Olaug Tvedten. Chelsea did nothing, while Everton signed Roma midfielder Zara Kramzar. Leicester bolstered its squad with experienced players, and Liverpool's big moves included goalkeeper Jennifer Falk. City added US star Sam Coffey, and United strengthened with Lea Schüller.
