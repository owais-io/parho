The U.S. is planning to expand its travel ban to over 30 countries, covering both immigrants and non‑immigrants such as tourists and students. The current list of 19 countries includes Afghanistan, Iran, and Venezuela. DHS Secretary Kristi Noem said the exact new countries aren’t decided yet, but the ban will grow. Immigration advocates and lawmakers are outraged and plan to stop all applications from the already‑listed nations, amid other controversial immigration moves.
