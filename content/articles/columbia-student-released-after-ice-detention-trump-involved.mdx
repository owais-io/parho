ICE officers raided a Columbia student’s apartment without a warrant, claiming to look for a missing child. The student, Elmina Aghayeva, was taken and later released after a surprise meeting between New York City mayor Zohran Mamdani and former President Trump. The incident sparked protests on campus, raised questions about university security and the legality of the raid, and led to calls for new rules to protect students from federal agents.
