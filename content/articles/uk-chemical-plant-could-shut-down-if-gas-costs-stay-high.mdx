Peter Huntsman, head of Huntsman Corp., said his UK plant could shut down if gas prices stay high for the next three months. The Wilton site, which makes aniline for car seats and aircraft parts, employs about 80 staff. Rising EU gas bills, driven by the Iran conflict, make the plant the most expensive producer worldwide. Huntsman warns that Britain is losing key chemical factories and urges governments to cut energy costs.
