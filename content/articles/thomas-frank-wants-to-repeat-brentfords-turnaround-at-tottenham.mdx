Thomas Frank is sure he can bring the same pattern of improvement he saw at Brøndby and Brentford to Tottenham. The club has lost six of its last ten league games and is under pressure from Champions League matches. Frank believes that by improving habits, rotation and resilience he can turn losses into a winning streak. He is working hard to make the team competitive and hopes the turnaround will happen soon.
