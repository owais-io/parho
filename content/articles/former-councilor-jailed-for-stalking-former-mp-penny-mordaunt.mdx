A 61‑year‑old former East Hampshire councillor, Edward Brandt, was sentenced to 20 weeks in prison for stalking former Conservative MP Penny Mordaunt. Brandt sent dozens of emails and phone messages, and appeared at Mordaunt’s Portsmouth office outside normal hours, leaving her feeling unsafe and fearing sexual violence. He also broke a stalking‑protection order by using social‑media accounts and ignored police warnings.
