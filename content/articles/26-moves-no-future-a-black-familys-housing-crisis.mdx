Na Kaya Godfrey, a 16‑year‑old in Stone Mountain, Georgia, has lived in 26 different homes with her mom and baby. Jaimie, a hard‑working single mother, keeps chasing odd jobs but can’t keep up with rising rent and low wages. The family hits every safety net but still faces eviction, poor health, and school absences. Kids suffer the most, yet help is hard to get and hope for a better life.
