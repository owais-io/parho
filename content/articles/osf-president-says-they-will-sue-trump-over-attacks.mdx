At the UN climate talks, Binaifer Nowrojee, president of the Open Society Foundations (OSF), said the charity would sue the Trump administration if it tries to silence its grantees. OSF, funded by George Soros, backs free speech and peaceful protest. Trump’s team has launched investigations and called for documents from groups linked to OSF. Nowrojee stressed the charity will defend legal rights and protect activists, especially climate defenders in the Americas.
