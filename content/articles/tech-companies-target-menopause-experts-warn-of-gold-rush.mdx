Startups are launching gadgets to help menopausal women, like a cooling wristband that stops hot flashes. While the market could grow to £19 bn by 2030, experts say many products are untested and could exploit fears. Research shows a lot of women feel poorly informed, and health specialists stress the need for evidence‑based solutions. Consumers should check who made a product, look for data, and consider basic self‑care first.
