Five Republicans and their Democratic partners on the House Oversight Committee voted 24‑19 to subpoena Attorney General Pam Bondi so she must testify about the mishandling of Jeffrey Epstein’s files. The motion was led by Rep. Nancy Mace and backed by other GOP members. Lawmakers complained the Justice Department released survivors’ names and redacted others without explanation. Bondi has denied responsibility and even insulted critics in a hearing. The DOJ has not yet replied.
