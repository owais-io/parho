PwC chief Paul Griggs says any partner who isn’t ready to use AI will soon lose their job. He warned that firms like PwC, Accenture and McKinsey are already hiring data scientists and cutting traditional consultant roles. The firm is also rolling out its own AI platform, PwC One, to automate tax and consulting services, which could change how clients are charged. Partners must adapt or risk being replaced.
