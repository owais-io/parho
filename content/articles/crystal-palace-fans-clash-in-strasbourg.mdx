Crystal Palace supporters went to Strasbourg for a European game, but a fight broke out between two groups of Palace fans. One group, the Holmesdale Fanatics, says they were attacked with racial abuse by a rival faction. The club says it will investigate and punish those involved. Some fans were arrested, and the club warned it will work with police before the next match. The majority of supporters said the incident was isolated.
