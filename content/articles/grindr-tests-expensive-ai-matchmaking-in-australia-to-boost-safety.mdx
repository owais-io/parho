Grindr is adding a pricey AI feature called Edge in Australia and New Zealand. For about A$110 a month, the AI scans chats and shows who might be a good match, while also spotting under‑age users. Free users can still see Edge matches but can opt out of AI help. The app wants to stop dangerous meetings and improve user safety, though it raises privacy concerns.
