After the 78‑year‑old director Rob Reiner and his 68‑year‑old wife were found dead in their Los Angeles home, Trump posted a scathing comment blaming Reiner for their deaths, calling him “deranged” and blaming him for “anger.” The post triggered a sharp backlash from celebrities and lawmakers of both parties. Reiner’s 32‑year‑old son was arrested on murder charges. Trump later repeated the remarks at a White House event, deepening the controversy.
