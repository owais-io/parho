Since the 1960s, Columbia’s men’s basketball went up and down, but the women’s team stayed in a losing streak until 2016. Coach Megan Griffith rebuilt the program, turning it into a top Ivy League team. She led them to back‑to‑back conference titles and an NCAA tournament win over Washington. In 2024, the women kept winning, beat Princeton and Penn, and added strong new recruits, keeping hopes high.
