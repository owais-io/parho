Three weeks after an Iranian drone struck the British base at RAF Akrotiri, the UK sent the destroyer HMS Dragon to the eastern Mediterranean. The ship, finished a quick refit in Portsmouth, will join Cyprus’s defence forces from Monday night. The move follows criticism that the UK was slow to respond, and comes as Iran plans further strikes and the Strait of Hormuz remains threatened. Extra UK air‑defence troops have also been posted to Cyprus.
