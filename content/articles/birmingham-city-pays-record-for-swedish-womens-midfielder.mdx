Birmingham City broke the Women’s Super League 2 record by paying about €315,000 (£273,000) for Swedish midfielder Wilma Leidhammar. The move shows the club’s ambition to get promoted while their American owners look to grow both the men’s and women’s sides. It also highlights a surge in women’s football revenue and the trend of Scandinavian talent moving to England. Leidhammar’s arrival could help Birmingham challenge for one of two promotion places.
