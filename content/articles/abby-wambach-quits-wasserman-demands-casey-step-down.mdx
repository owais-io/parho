Abby Wambach, the two‑time gold‑medal soccer star, has quit the Wasserman agency and urged its founder, Casey Wasserman, to resign after emails with Ghislaine Maxwell surfaced in the Jeffrey Epstein files. She said she won’t work under his leadership and called him “a bad person.” The agency, which represents many athletes and celebrities, has seen other stars leave, but the LA28 Olympic board said Wasserman can keep his role after an outside review.
