In the final day of the fifth Ashes Test at Sydney, 22‑year‑old Jacob Bethell batted for six hours and made his first red‑ball hundred – 103 runs off 162 balls. He played with rhythm and confidence against a strong Australian attack on a worn pitch. The innings gave England a rare bright moment in an otherwise tough series and showed that young English talent can still shine.
