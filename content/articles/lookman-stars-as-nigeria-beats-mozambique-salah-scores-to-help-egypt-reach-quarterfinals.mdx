In the Africa Cup of Nations, Nigeria won 4‑0 over Mozambique. Ademola Lookman scored twice and helped with another goal. Nigeria’s attack was bright, but the defence was loose. Egypt beat Benin 3‑1, with Mohamed Salah scoring in extra time. Nigeria’s new faster style shows promise, while Egypt’s win keeps them in the tournament, though they still need to improve for a record‑breaking eighth title.
