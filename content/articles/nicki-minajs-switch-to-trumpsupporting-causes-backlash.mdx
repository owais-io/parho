Nicki Minaj has recently shown support for the Trump administration by sharing his posts, speaking at a pro‑Trump event, and praising its leaders. Fans and critics alike are outraged, with petitions urging her deportation and accusations of “cancel culture.” Some say she may be using the controversy to boost her career, while others question whether she’s truly changed her stance on immigration. The move has already cost her some fans, and her future is uncertain.
