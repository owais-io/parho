Anthropic, the maker of the Claude chatbot, just raised $30 billion in a new funding round, boosting its valuation to $380 billion. The deal was led by Singapore’s GIC and hedge fund Coatue, following a $183 billion round last September. Anthropic’s revenue hit $14 billion, helped by its coding AI Claude Code. Meanwhile, rivals like OpenAI are targeting even larger raises, raising concerns about high AI spending.
