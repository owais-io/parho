Streaming has changed how we watch movies, but the author argues it has made home viewing more expensive and annoying. He compares it to the affordable cinemas and DVD rentals of his youth. Renting new releases online can cost up to £20, and classic films are hidden behind multiple services. Subscription fees rise, and ads creep in, forcing users to pay extra for popular titles. He also criticises the double paywalls and ad‑driven models that make rewatching old favourites hard.
