Trump posted a video saying three U.S. soldiers died after a joint attack on Iran and that more could die before the conflict ends. He thanked the fallen, prayed for the wounded, and said the U.S. will do everything it can to stop the fighting. The Pentagon said the losses were part of Operation Epic Fury. Trump also urged Iran to stop fighting and noted Britain will let the U.S. use its bases for a limited defense.
