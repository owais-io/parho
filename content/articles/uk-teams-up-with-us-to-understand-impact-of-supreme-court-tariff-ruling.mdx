The UK is reviewing how the US Supreme Court’s decision that overturned Trump’s global tariffs will affect its trade. Britain and the EU are studying the ruling, while businesses worry about which tariffs stay and how to get refunds. Steel tariffs likely stay, and some experts fear new tariffs on chips and food. The ruling gave UK firms a boost in the stock market, but the full impact on trade remains unclear.
