UK plans to let AI use weather data from the Met Office and legal records from the National Archives. Researchers will test how this helps councils buy road grit and small businesses get quick legal answers. The government also wants to open other national museums and libraries to AI, while respecting copyright. A pilot marketplace for buying and licensing the public data will launch in summer.
