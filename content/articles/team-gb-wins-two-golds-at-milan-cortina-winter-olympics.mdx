Britons have been wet and cold in January and February, but the Milano Cortina Winter Olympics lifted spirits. Team GB won two gold medals in just five hours: Charlotte Bankes and Huw Nightingale took the mixed snowboard cross, and Tabitha Stoecker and Matt Weston won the mixed skeleton. These were the first British golds on snow, showing how investment in winter sports is paying off and may spark more interest, even though Britain still lacks proper ice tracks.
