Real Madrid lost 2‑1 to Manchester City, after Rodrygo scored early. Coach Xabi Alonso is under pressure, but players like Rodrygo, Courtois and Bellingham say they stand by him. Alonso says the team is united and that results are what matter. The loss has sparked debate about his future, but the dressing room remains supportive as they look to turn things around.
