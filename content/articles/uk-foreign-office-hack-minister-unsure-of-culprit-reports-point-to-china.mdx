In October the UK’s Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO) was breached by hackers. A Sun newspaper report said a Chinese group called Storm 1849 did the attack and may have stolen many visa records. Trade minister Chris Bryant told broadcasters that it’s not clear who was behind it and warned against speculation. He said the chance any person was harmed is low. The government is still investigating the cyber‑incident.
