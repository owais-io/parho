Over 100,000 Britons are stuck in the Gulf because most flights are grounded after Iran’s missile and drone attacks. The UK is exploring ways to get them home: commercial and charter flights, military transport, or buses to Saudi Arabia and Turkey. Consular teams are helping, but finding enough seats is a huge challenge. The crisis could cost millions and shows how fragile travel plans become during conflict.
