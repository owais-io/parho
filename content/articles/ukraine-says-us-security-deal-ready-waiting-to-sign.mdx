Zelensky said the US security deal for Ukraine is ready to be signed after talks with the US and Russia. He told reporters in Lithuania that they are waiting for partners to set a date and location. Ukraine also asked for more air‑defence help after heavy Russian strikes left many Kyiv apartments without heating. Zelensky wants EU membership by 2027 and urged European unity against Russia.
