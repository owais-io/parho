Guardian Australia’s sports writer Jonathan Horn and photographer Chris Hopkins were honored at the Melbourne Press Club’s 31st Quill awards. Horn won best sports feature for his series on the loss of three AFL players, while Hopkins captured best features photograph with a story about a cancer‑suffering mother caring for her son. The club also highlighted other Guardian reporters with commendations for Indigenous affairs, domestic violence investigations, and science reporting.
