OpenAI is losing billions and its market share has fallen, but the biggest worry is how it’s being used to support authoritarian politics. A new grassroots boycott called QuitGPT urges people to cancel ChatGPT subscriptions—over a million have already joined, with celebrities like Mark Ruffalo and Katy Perry lending support. The push points to OpenAI president’s $25 million donation to a Trump super‑PAC, the company’s AI tools helping ICE, and a new Pentagon partnership that critics say backs surveillance and weaponry.
