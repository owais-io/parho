Joshua Bonehill‑Paine, 33, ran the popular Crewkerne Gazette X account that made AI videos of UK politicians. He has a long record of hate crimes against Jewish people and was jailed for inciting antisemitic hatred. After his identity was revealed, he said he was sorry, has worked to fight extremism, and asks for forgiveness from victims. His videos were praised by media and politicians.
