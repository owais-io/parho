The writer’s father kept a huge album of old color slides. After he died, the writer opened them and saw a picture of her mom and herself at Heathrow waiting for a flight to India. The image shows her stylish, dreamy, like a Mad Men character. It reminded the writer of family trips to Kolkata and of her childhood as an only child. The discovery sparked a new interest in photography and old film tech.
