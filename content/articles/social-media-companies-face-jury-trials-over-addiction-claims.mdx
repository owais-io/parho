Parents, teens and school districts have sued Facebook, Snap, TikTok and YouTube, saying their apps are addictive and hurt kids’ mental health. The first trial began in Los Angeles, with Mark Zuckerberg expected to testify. The case will last 6‑8 weeks and could set a precedent for 22 similar trials. Courts may use the outcome to shape future lawsuits and laws on social‑media safety.
