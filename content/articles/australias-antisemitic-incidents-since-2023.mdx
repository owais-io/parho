Australia has seen a sharp rise in antisemitic attacks since October 2023, including vandalism, arson, and the deadly Bondi beach shooting that killed 15. Police say some acts may have ties to Iran, while others are blamed on organized criminals. State leaders have faced criticism over inflated incident figures and are tightening hate‑speech laws, but no major convictions yet. These incidents have sparked calls for stronger protection of the Jewish community and clearer evidence before labeling events as antisemitic.
