US Secretary of State Marco Rubio will meet with Danish officials next week to discuss Greenland, after President Donald Trump expressed interest in the territory. While the US says diplomacy is the first choice, officials acknowledge that a military option is still on the table. European leaders, especially Denmark and Greenland, strongly oppose any takeover and want the decision to be theirs. The move has raised tensions in NATO.
