Johannes Høsflot Klæbo took his second gold in the men’s sprint classic at the Milan‑Cortina Winter Games, moving him one win shy of the all‑time Olympic record. Sweden’s women’s cross‑country team finished 1‑2‑3, with Linn Svahn, Jonna Sundling and Maja Dahlqvist taking gold, silver and bronze. In hockey, the United States beat Canada 5‑0 in the men’s tournament, and the Wranas won mixed‑doubles curling. Norway tops the medal table.
