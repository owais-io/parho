South African rugby star Eben Etzebeth has accepted a 12‑week suspension after a disciplinary panel found he intentionally gouged the eye of Wales player Alex Mann. Etzebeth posted an apology on Instagram, saying the act was a mistake and that other players had changed the situation. He also said he had been hit first and that the eye‑gouge was accidental. The ban, reduced from 18 weeks because of his good record, will keep him off the field until April.
