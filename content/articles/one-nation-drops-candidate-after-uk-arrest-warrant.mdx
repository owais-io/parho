Former South Australian One Nation candidate Aoi Baxter (formerly Trent Baxter) was removed from the party’s ticket after a UK court issued a warrant for his arrest over a sexual‑touching charge. One Nation had done nationwide police checks and found no issues, but the new information forced the removal. Baxter’s website page was erased, and the party will cooperate with law‑enforcement. The state election is coming up.
