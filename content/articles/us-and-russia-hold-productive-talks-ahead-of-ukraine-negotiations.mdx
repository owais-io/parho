The US envoy Steve Witkoff met a Russian envoy in Florida, saying the talks were “constructive” as part of Washington’s push for a Ukraine cease‑fire. The meeting came before Ukrainian and Russian negotiators are set to meet in Abu Dhabi on a Trump‑backed plan. Meanwhile, Ukraine is still suffering strikes and power outages, with a drone attack killing two in Dnipropetrovsk. Zelenskyy says Ukraine is ready for talks.
