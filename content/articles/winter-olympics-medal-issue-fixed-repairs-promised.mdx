Organisers say they have fixed the problem that caused some Winter Olympic medals to crack or fall off. They blamed the medal’s safety ribbon, which is meant to snap when pulled hard, but it broke the medals. Officials will let athletes return affected medals for quick repairs. The fix is in place, and they will check all medals to make sure they’re safe for the rest of the games.
