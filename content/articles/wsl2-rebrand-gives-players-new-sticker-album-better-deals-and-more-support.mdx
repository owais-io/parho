WSL2 has been renamed and given a fresh look, with a new Panini sticker album featuring all 24 clubs and 64 player cards. The change comes with new deals from Nike, Apple and others, and a push to make the league fully professional – salaries, support staff and YouTube coverage. Players say they now feel seen and better supported. The league also offers up to three promotion spots to the top tier and will be shown on Sky and BBC.
