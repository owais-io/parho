The Trump administration has filed a lawsuit against two California cities, Morgan Hill and Petaluma, arguing that their local laws that limit natural‑gas hookups in new buildings are illegal. The complaint claims the city bans violate a 1975 federal rule that forbids local governments from setting energy‑use standards. California cities have been pushing stricter climate rules, but the Trump team says the bans impose high costs on residents. The cities have not yet responded, and state officials have not commented.
