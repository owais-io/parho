Tommy Thompson, a famous U.S. salvage expert who found the 1857 SS Central America ship, was imprisoned for 10 years because he wouldn't reveal where the missing gold coins were. He was finally released after a court decided his continued confinement wouldn't produce the missing information. Despite his release, the coins’ exact location remains a mystery, and investors who backed his search have had long legal battles with him.
