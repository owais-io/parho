A friendly gardener named Kate Bradbury rescued a three‑legged hedgehog called Houdini and keeps him in her garden during winter. Houdini, over five years old, has lost a leg and part of his nose, making him vulnerable, but he’s healthy and enjoys a straw bed. Kate worries about his safety on roads and limited food, hoping to give him more space until spring when other hedgehogs need care.
