Kemi Badenoch said on TV that she agrees with the US about removing Venezuelan leader Maduro and that laws about foreign intervention are old. She thinks the strong should decide what happens. She also said Greenland is too dangerous for US claims. The interviewer noticed her comments were similar to Labour’s Keir Starmer, but she dismissed the need for rules. The piece points out how she mixes fact and opinion and shows a desire for power over diplomacy.
