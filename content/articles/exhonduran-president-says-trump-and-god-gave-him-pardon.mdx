Former Honduras president Juan Orlando Hernández thanked God and former U.S. President Donald Trump after Trump pardoned him for a drug‑trafficking conviction. He posted a video on social media, saying Trump changed his life and he will never forget it. Hernández claims his trial was a political plot by the Biden administration and the U.S. “deep state.” The pardon sparked outrage, with critics saying it undermines the U.S. war on drugs.
