Some Trump backers say the U.S. should control Greenland, citing national security and a desire to block Chinese influence in the Arctic. They point to a secret “Golden Dome” defense system and argue the island is key to protecting America. Critics warn the move could spark international tension, but supporters keep framing it as a strategic win. The idea is still far from official action.
