In Mexico City, over 1,000 people—mostly young activists—marching against President Claudia Sheinbaum’s government were met with police violence. About 120 people, including 100 officers, were hurt, many with bruises and cuts. Protesters demanded better security after the murder of Uruapan mayor Carlos Manzo, who opposed drug gangs. The demonstration turned chaotic when hooded attackers clashed with police, who used tear gas and fire extinguishers to control the crowd.
