Scientists have used a new AI trick to make short movies that show what mice see while watching real videos. By recording the mice’s brain activity as they watched clips, the researchers fed blank screens into a computer program that kept tweaking the picture until the brain signals matched. The resulting clips are blurry, but they hint at how mice perceive sports and people. The work could help us understand animals better, and raises privacy questions about humans.
