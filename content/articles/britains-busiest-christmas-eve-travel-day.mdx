Britain is set for its busiest Christmas Eve travel day. About 4.2 million drivers, 3 million flyers and 37 million road trips over the holiday week are expected. Road congestion will peak on the M25 and M60, with a closure on the M27. Train services finish early; the last London‑to‑Edinburgh train leaves at 4:30 pm. Airports like Heathrow, Birmingham and Manchester warn of record crowds. Buses will also be full on Christmas Day.
