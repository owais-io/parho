The U.S. military says it has taken another oil tanker linked to Venezuela, the seventh ship seized in a campaign to stop Venezuela’s oil from leaving the country. The vessel, named MV Sagitta, was picked up off the Caribbean with no trouble. The move is part of President Trump’s strategy to control Venezuela’s oil and back a $100‑billion plan to rebuild its industry. The U.S. has blocked many ships that use disguised routes to move Venezuelan oil.
