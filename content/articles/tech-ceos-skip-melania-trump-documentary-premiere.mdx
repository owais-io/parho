A new documentary about Melania Trump opened in Washington, D.C., but the event was notable for the absence of several big tech leaders. Amazon’s Jeff Bezos and Apple’s Tim Cook did not attend, even though they paid for the film’s marketing rights. The premiere’s black carpet and star‑filled crowd highlighted the buzz, while critics wondered if the tech giants now see the costs of being tied to Trump.
