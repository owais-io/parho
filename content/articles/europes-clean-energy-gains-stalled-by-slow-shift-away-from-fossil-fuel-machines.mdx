Europe has greatly increased clean power, but it’s still stuck using fuel‑burning cars and boilers. As oil prices rise after the Iran war, households face higher bills. Experts say the slow shift to electric cars, heat pumps and other clean tech is holding back the green goal. EU leaders plan to lower electricity taxes and push for faster electrification, while the war keeps fuel prices high.
