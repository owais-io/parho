Adrian Chiles, a TV presenter, shares a funny story about discovering roast‑chicken flavored crisps. While reading a book about the history and science of chips, he remembered how he only ate ready‑salted as a child. One trip to Portsmouth with his grandmother gave him a bright orange bag of roast‑chicken chips, and the taste changed him. Now he’s a bit of a chip‑addict—just for research, he jokes.
