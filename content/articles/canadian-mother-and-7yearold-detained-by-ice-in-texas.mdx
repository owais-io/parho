A Canadian mom and her 7‑year‑old daughter, both Canadian, were taken by U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement at a Texas checkpoint. They’re now stuck in the Rio Grande Valley Central detention center under poor conditions. The family says all their papers are in order, but ICE still holds them. They’re trying to raise money for a lawyer while the Canadian consulate can only offer help if they leave the U.S. The case shows how harsh U.S. immigration enforcement can be.
