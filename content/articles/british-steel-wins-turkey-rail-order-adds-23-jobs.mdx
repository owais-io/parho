British Steel secured a huge deal to supply 36,000 tonnes of high‑speed rail for a new line between Ankara and Izmir. The contract, worth tens of millions of pounds, lets the company create 23 new jobs and restart 24‑hour production at its Scunthorpe plant. However, the factory still relies on government support after heavy losses, raising doubts about its long‑term future.
