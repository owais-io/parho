Tottenham Women coach Martin Ho says the team is only 15% of the way to where he wants them to be. Since taking over in July, the squad has matched last season’s points by mid‑season and has improved in attack and defence, thanks to new signings and better shot accuracy. Ho focuses on basic standards, punctuality and a strong club culture to build a winning future.
