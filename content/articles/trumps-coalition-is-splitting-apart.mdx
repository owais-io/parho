Donald Trump’s grip on the Republican Party is loosening. After years of dominating the GOP, cracks appear as Trump’s approval falls and some Republicans start breaking away. Scandals, especially new details about his ties to Jeffrey Epstein, have turned even former allies against him. Democrats are gaining ground in recent elections, and Trump’s advisers are fighting each other. The party may soon face a tough fight over who will lead next, as Trump’s influence fades.
