In Arkansas, a father named Dillon Hess was driving his Jeep at 70 mph in a 60‑mph zone while trying to get his allergic‑reaction child to the hospital. A state trooper, unaware of the emergency, used a PIT maneuver to stop the vehicle, spinning it and pinning it to a median. After the stop, officials said Hess would face no charges, while the trooper’s actions are under review.
