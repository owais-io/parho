UK ministers are worried that Trump's war in Iran could hit Britain's economy. They’re debating whether to lower speed limits, give energy‑bills help, or even bail out households as oil prices rise. Trump called UK allies cowards, sparking anger. The government is also preparing to use British bases against Iranian missile sites. With local elections looming, ministers fear higher energy costs will hurt voters.
