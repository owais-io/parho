Clémentine Autain, a left‑wing MP from a poor suburb north of Paris, says the only way to stop Marine Le Pen’s far‑right party is for all left groups to join forces. She plans to run as the left’s candidate in a 2027 primary and wants the Socialist, Green and other parties to pick one winner. While some critics doubt it will happen, she argues that a united left can win a final round of the election.
