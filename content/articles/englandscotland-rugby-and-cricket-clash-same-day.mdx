England will face Scotland twice on one day – a rugby match for the Calcutta Cup at Murrayfield and a cricket test at the same venue. The rugby game is vital for England’s Six Nations hopes and carries a heavy history, especially after Scotland’s loss to Italy. England’s new, confident squad looks ready to beat the hosts, while Scotland must avoid repeating past setbacks in Edinburgh.
