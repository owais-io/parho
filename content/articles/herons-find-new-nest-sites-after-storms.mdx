Storms this spring knocked down many trees, leaving herons without their usual nest spots. The article follows a grey heron couple as they set up a new nest in a beech tree, describing their caring behaviors and the challenges they face. The male fetches twigs while the female builds the nest, all while protecting their eggs from disturbances. The story highlights how weather changes can disrupt heron breeding and how they adapt.
