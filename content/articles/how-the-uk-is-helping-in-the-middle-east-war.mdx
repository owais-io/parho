The UK has let U.S. bombers land at RAF bases and sent its own jets, like Typhoons and F‑35s, to defend Gulf allies from drones and missiles. It also moved ships, a destroyer and an aircraft carrier, to the region and sent air‑defence crews to Cyprus. Some Brits and Cypriots complain the response is slow, and protests have targeted UK bases. Overall, Britain’s military presence is smaller than before.
