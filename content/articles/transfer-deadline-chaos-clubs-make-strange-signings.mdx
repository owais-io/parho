Transfer deadline day in English football was chaotic, with clubs making odd moves. Wolves sold star striker Jørgen Strand Larsen to Crystal Palace, while Palace paid £43 m for the Norwegian forward. Manchester City bought Antoine Semenyo for £62.5 m and secured Marc Guéhi for £20 m. Liverpool splurged on a defender, and other teams swapped players in puzzling ways, leaving fans wondering why clubs made such strange signings.
