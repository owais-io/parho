The UK Prime Minister has told tech companies that any non‑consensual intimate images – including deepfake nudes – must be removed within 48 hours. If a platform ignores a victim’s flag, it could face fines of up to 10 % of its global revenue or even be blocked in the UK. The new rules also cover AI chatbots and make sharing such images a priority offence under the Online Safety Act.
