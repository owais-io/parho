Donald Trump’s move to break the old Western alliance forces the UK to act more independently. Think‑tank chief Bronwen Maddox says Britain must stand up for its values and not just follow the US. She urges the government to defend the BBC, take a firmer stance on China, and look for new, smaller coalitions to keep order. The UK must balance trade, defence and its own principles in a changing world.
