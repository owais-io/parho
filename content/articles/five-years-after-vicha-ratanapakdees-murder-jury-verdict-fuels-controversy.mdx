Vicha Ratanapakdee, an 84‑year‑old Thai‑American, was attacked and killed in San Francisco in 2021, raising fears about hate crimes against Asian elders. A jury this month found the 24‑year‑old attacker, Antoine Watson, guilty of manslaughter and assault, not murder. Watson will face a possible nine‑year sentence. The verdict has upset the victim’s family and community, who feel justice was missed.
