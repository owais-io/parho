The Green Party won a seat in Gorton and Denton, overturning a 13,000‑vote Labour lead and marking the Greens' first byelection victory. The loss has shocked Labour, sparking calls from some MPs for Starmer to shift left or face a leadership challenge. Starmer responded by attacking the Greens as “extreme” and warned he would not lose more seats in upcoming local elections.
