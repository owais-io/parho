American skier Lindsey Vonn finished third in a World Cup downhill race in Val d’Isère, France, but said a small mistake near the bottom kept her from winning. The race was won by Austria’s Cornelia Huetter. Vonn, who returned to racing after a long break, is feeling fast and confident as she heads to the 2026 Olympics. Her third podium in four events shows she's on track.
