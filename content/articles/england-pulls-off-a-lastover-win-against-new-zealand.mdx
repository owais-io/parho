With England needing 21 runs from 12 balls, Rehan Ahmed and Will Jacks turned the game with a series of wickets and boundaries. Jacks hit a six to take the lead, and the team finished the chase in three balls. The win shows England can finish games, but raises questions about their reliance on key players.
