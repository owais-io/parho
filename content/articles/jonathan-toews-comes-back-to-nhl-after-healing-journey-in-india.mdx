Jonathan Toews returned to the NHL after a 2½‑year break, playing his first game with the Winnipeg Jets. He spent two months in India doing a strict detox program to heal his body and mind, helping him recover from chronic health problems. The former Blackhawks captain now plays a key role on the Jets, showing strong leadership and steady play. He says the journey made him feel like a kid again.
