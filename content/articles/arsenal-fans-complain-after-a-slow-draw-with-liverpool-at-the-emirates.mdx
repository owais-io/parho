Arsenal played a 1‑1 draw with Liverpool in front of a frustrated home crowd. The game was very slow, with Liverpool winning most possession and Arsenal hardly creating chances. Fans at the Emirates shouted and groaned over small mistakes, reflecting long‑standing disappointment. The club is trying to calm things by engaging fans and keeping a calm, methodical style, hoping the fans will eventually warm up to the new approach.
