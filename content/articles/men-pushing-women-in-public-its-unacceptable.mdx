Many readers write that men frequently push, shove, or harass women on sidewalks and in public spaces. The letters show how a simple act of stepping aside can feel like a threat, and how men often assume women will move out of the way. Stories from the UK, US, Australia and Europe all call for more respect, awareness, and a shift in how men share public space.
