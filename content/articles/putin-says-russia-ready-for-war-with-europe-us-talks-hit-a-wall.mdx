Putin warned that Russia is prepared for war if Europe pushes the conflict, even as a US delegation meets in Moscow. The talks made little progress and both sides said more work is needed. Russia still demands large territory in Ukraine and claims to control key cities, while Ukraine’s president wants security guarantees and is waiting for US signals. Russia threatened to hit Ukrainian ports if attacks continue.
