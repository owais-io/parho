Hobbycraft has pulled its Giant Box of Craft children’s play sand after tests found asbestos in some bottles. The recall came after a customer noticed the risk when her kids played with the sand. The company warns anyone who bought the set to stop using it, keep it out of reach, and return it for a refund. The asbestos poses a health risk, even in small amounts.
