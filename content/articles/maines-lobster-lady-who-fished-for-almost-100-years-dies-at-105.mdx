Virginia “Ginny” Oliver, known as Maine’s Lobster Lady, passed away at 105 after a 97‑year career catching lobsters. The governor praised her “amazing” dedication, hoping her story motivates future generations of fishers. Oliver, who began working with her family at age eight, stayed active until a fall at 103. Her life was celebrated in documentaries, books, and even a shout‑out from actor Mark Hamill. She was remembered for her humor, humility and love of the sea.
