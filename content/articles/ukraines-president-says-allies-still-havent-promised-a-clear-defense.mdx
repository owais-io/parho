Zelenskyy says European partners haven’t given a firm promise that they will step in if Russia attacks again. Although leaders talked about a European force that could be sent after a cease‑fire, the president still wants a clear, legal guarantee. In the UK, MPs will vote on how many troops could be sent if peace is reached. Meanwhile, Russia has hit Ukraine’s power grid and ports, and the country’s reserves hit a record, while defence spending tops $70 bn.
