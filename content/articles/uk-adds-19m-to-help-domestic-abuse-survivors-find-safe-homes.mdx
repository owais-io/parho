The UK government has pledged an extra £19 million to local councils to help people who’ve been abused at home get secure accommodation. This is part of a £500 million plan aimed at cutting violence against women and girls and includes a new duty for councils to provide safe housing, whether in refuges or secure private homes. It also brings new police rape and sexual‑offence teams by 2029 and wider justice reforms.
