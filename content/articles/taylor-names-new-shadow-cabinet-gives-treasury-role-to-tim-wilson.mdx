Angus Taylor has announced a new Coalition shadow cabinet, appointing moderate MP Tim Wilson as the shadow treasurer and bringing the Nationals back into the frontbench. The lineup rewards conservative allies and removes several of former leader Sussan Ley’s supporters. Key portfolios include finance, defense, employment and industrial relations, and communications. The reshuffle also signals a renewed partnership with the Nationals and aims to ready the party for future government.
