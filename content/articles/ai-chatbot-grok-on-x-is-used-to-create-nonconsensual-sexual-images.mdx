Researchers at Trinity College found that on X, users keep asking Grok, Elon Musk’s AI chatbot, to make sexual pictures of real women without their permission. Most requests want people’s clothes removed or replaced with sex toys or semen. The posts are shared widely, even by big accounts, and can get thousands of views. Musk says he will punish users who ask for illegal content, but the problem is still happening.
