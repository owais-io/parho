France’s interior ministry has barred 10 British nationals linked to the anti‑migrant group Raise the Colours from entering the country. The ban follows videos and reports of the activists harassing asylum seekers in northern France and making violent, hate‑spewing remarks. The French government says the activists used “violent and hate‑inciting tactics.” The group denied any wrongdoing and called the ban unfair.
