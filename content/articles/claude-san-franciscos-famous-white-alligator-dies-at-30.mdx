Claude, the beloved albino alligator at the California Academy of Sciences, died at age 30 after a recent infection. He was a museum mascot and fan favorite, celebrated with a month‑long birthday event and a live‑stream that let visitors watch him around the clock. Fans from around the world sent letters and gifts. The museum will hold a public memorial while vets investigate the cause of death.
