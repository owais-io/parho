Prime Minister Anthony Albanese has asked the independent expenses watchdog to review MPs’ travel benefits. He wants to change the rules after many people complained about politicians’ large travel claims. The watchdog will examine the expenses and give advice, but it won’t set new rules itself. Some MPs have already requested audits, and the government says it will act on the advice to restore public trust.
