Frederik Pleitgen, a senior CNN correspondent, spent a week in Iran, the only western TV reporter allowed to work there amid war. He faced danger, a close call when anti‑aircraft fire forced him and his producer to flee. The Iranian government gave him a visa but kept tight control, asking him to notify them before visits. Back home, he said criticism over his presence was expected, but he believes on‑ground reporting matters.
