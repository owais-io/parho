England and Italy will face off in a T20 World Cup match. England’s campaign has been shaky, losing to the West Indies and barely winning against Nepal and Scotland. Italy has impressed, also losing to the West Indies but crushing Nepal by 10 wickets thanks to the Mosca brothers and spin bowler Crishan Kalugamage. The game starts at 9:30 am UK time, 3 pm Mumbai. Fans expect a close contest.
