Panic Attack, a nine‑year‑old mare, won the Paddy Power Gold Cup at Cheltenham, earning trainer Dan Skelton a big boost for his title hopes. The victory added £91,000 to Skelton’s season earnings, bringing him close to the £1.2 million target and ahead of rival Olly Murphy. Skelton says the win shows his horses are finally peaking, but the biggest tests will come at the spring festivals.
