FBI files released last week show the agency investigated magician David Copperfield for a 2007 rape claim made by a woman on his Bahamian island. The documents reveal the FBI also examined links between Copperfield and Jeffrey Epstein, noting possible shared victims and meetings. Though the case was dropped in 2010 and no charges were filed, new photos, emails and lists suggest the two men were at least acquaintances. Copperfield’s lawyers say the relationship was minimal and deny wrongdoing.
