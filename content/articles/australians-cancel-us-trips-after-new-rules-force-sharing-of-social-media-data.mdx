Australians are canceling trips to the US because new rules would require them to share five years of social media posts when applying for a visa waiver. The policy, set by the Trump administration, also asks for detailed personal data and biometrics. Many Australians have already shifted their plans, even postponing the World Cup trip, because they feel the rules are invasive and dangerous.
