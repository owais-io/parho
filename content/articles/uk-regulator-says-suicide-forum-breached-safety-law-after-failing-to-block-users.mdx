The UK regulator Ofcom has found a suicide forum that has been linked to deaths in Britain to be in breach of the Online Safety Act. The site failed to keep a geoblock in place and even posted a message that helped users bypass it. After years of complaints from families and charities, Ofcom now has 10 days to decide on final action, which could include fines or shutting it down.
