On Jan 8, Philadelphia’s sheriff, Rochelle Bilal, told reporters that ICE agents are “fake” law enforcement and urged residents to stand against the Trump‑era immigration crackdown. She cited the fatal shooting of Renee Good and how ICE agents act in the city. Bilal’s remarks went viral, drawing death threats and calls for her resignation, but she says she will stay in office and defend her community. She urged people to use their First Amendment rights and stay safe.
