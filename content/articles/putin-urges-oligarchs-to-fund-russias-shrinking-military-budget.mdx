Putin has asked Russia’s richest businessmen to give money to the country’s shrinking defence budget as the war in Ukraine drags on. He says the invasion will continue until Moscow controls all of Donbas. Meanwhile, Moscow is raising taxes, including a windfall levy on big firms and higher VAT, to cover the huge cost of the war. US sanctions have forced Russia to sell oil cheaply, widening the budget gap.
