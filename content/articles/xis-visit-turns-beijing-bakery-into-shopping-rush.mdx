During the Lunar New Year, President Xi Jinping stopped at a famous bakery in Beijing’s Dongcheng district. He bought a 45‑yuan bundle of honey‑glazed dough twists, cream‑filled twists and jujube flower pastries. After his visit, thousands of shoppers rushed to the shop to buy the same treats, even buying 16 bags. The bakery had to restock quickly, and the snacks were sold in Xi‑themed packages.
