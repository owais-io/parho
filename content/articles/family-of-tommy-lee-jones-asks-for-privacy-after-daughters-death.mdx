Tommy Lee Jones’s family asked for privacy after his 34‑year‑old daughter Victoria was found dead at the Fairmont San Francisco on Jan 1. Police said it was an overdose, no foul play suspected, but the cause of death is still unknown. Victoria had a history of arrests and drug problems and had been placed under a conservatorship for rehab. She appeared in movies with her dad. The family thanked people for their support.
