Donald Trump stopped supporting Representative Marjorie Taylor Greene after a long friendship. Greene said she got messages from security firms warning her about threats, because Trump said he would not back her in the next election. She posted a chart of rising grocery prices and linked it to the fight over releasing Jeffrey Epstein’s documents. The feud has gotten louder, with Trump calling her a traitor.
