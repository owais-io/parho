Barbados is heading to the polls on Wednesday, and a new poll shows almost 80% of decided voters plan to back Prime Minister Mia Amor Mottley and her Barbados Labour Party (BLP). Mottley, the island’s first female leader, has been praised for her climate‑action work, but voters are worried about high costs and crime. While the BLP keeps its promises, many people feel the government needs to improve living prices and safety.
