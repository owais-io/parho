Frida Kahlo's self‑portrait sold for $54.7 million at a New York auction, beating the previous record for a woman artist. The 1940 painting, showing Kahlo sleeping with a smiling skeleton, sold in just four minutes. It tops the old record set by Georgia O’Keeffe’s work. The sale also eclipsed the earlier record for Latin American art. Some worry the piece will remain hidden after the auction.
