The Home Office says the new rule that requires dual‑national British citizens to show a valid passport, an expired passport, or a £589 certificate before they can board a flight, ferry or train to the UK is correct. Critics say the changes were not well communicated and left some people, like honeymooners and mourners, stuck abroad. The minister said the media had spread the info and that consular help is available.
