Alondra Sotelo Garcia became the head of her dad’s vineyard after he was deported from the U.S. in July. She left her shipping job, moved back to Newberg, and started running the business with a former apprentice and an admin. While she helps her parents settle in Mexico and handle the paperwork, she fights for the local wine community, which is hurt by ICE raids and a weak market. Alondra’s story shows how one family keeps a town’s economy going.
