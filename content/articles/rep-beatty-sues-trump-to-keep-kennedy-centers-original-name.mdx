Ohio Representative Joyce Beatty filed a lawsuit against former President Donald Trump, saying the renaming of the John F. Kennedy Center for Performing Arts to the Trump‑Kennedy Center violates law. Beatty, a board member, claims Congress must approve any name change and says she was muted during the vote. The Trump administration says it saved the old name. Other critics question the legality, noting the center was named by federal law.
