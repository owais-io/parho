John Roberts, who spent nearly 20 years at the Guardian’s sports desk, passed away yesterday. He started as a teenage reporter on the Stockport Express, moved to the Daily Express, and later became a key voice for football and tennis at the Guardian, the Independent, and the Daily Mail. Known for his sharp writing and deep contacts, Roberts helped shape modern sports journalism and left behind a family and several celebrated books.
