Cyprus has taken the EU presidency for six months and wants to bring a fresh, small‑state perspective. The focus will stay on Ukraine and Russia, but Cyprus also plans to bring Middle East issues to the table. It wants to keep the EU united, avoid using the presidency for national disputes, and look for new trade partners like India and the Middle East. The island is ready to act.
