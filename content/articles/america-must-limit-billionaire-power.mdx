America’s richest 900 people spend billions on politics, media and policies that favor them and big‑ticket candidates, especially Trump. They shape laws, cut taxes, push AI, limit unions, and bias the press. Their donations are huge—over $2.6 billion last election—while average Americans have little voice. Many call for public‑finance reforms, a wealth tax and new rules to stop “dark money.” 53 % of voters feel billionaires threaten democracy, so a national movement is urgent.
