Trump said the U.S. had won the war with Iran, while the government prepares to send about 1,000 more troops to the Middle East. Fighting is still going on, with Iranian missile attacks on Israel and Gulf states, and U.S. and Israeli planes hitting targets in Tehran. Iran’s ambassador says over 1,300 civilians have been killed, and Iran denies any talks with the U.S. about ending the conflict.
