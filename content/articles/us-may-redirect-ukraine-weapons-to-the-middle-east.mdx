The Pentagon is looking at the possibility of sending weapons that were intended for Ukraine to the Middle East, especially to help with the Iran war. The decision isn’t final yet, but tensions over munitions are rising. Meanwhile NATO says Ukraine still receives its ordered weapons. Other updates: Saudi crown prince met Zelensky, Russia is seeking oligarch donations, Britain plans to board Russian ships, and Russia banned a documentary. It's a mix of military aid and geopolitical tensions.
