UK ministers will ban private child‑care providers that make too much profit, putting a cap on profits and removing them if they overcharge. They will also recruit 10,000 new foster carers and give £88 million to help homes welcome more children. New rules will keep existing carers, raise their pay, and set up regional hubs to support families and make the system fairer for kids.
