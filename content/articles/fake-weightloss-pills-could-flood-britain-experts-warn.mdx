New weight‑loss drugs are moving from injections to cheap pills. Because pills are easier to copy, experts warn the UK could see a rise in counterfeit versions sold online. Health regulators say people must buy only from licensed pharmacies and keep medicines safe. Companies and the government are tightening rules and using AI to spot fake ads, but enforcement lags behind the growing demand.
