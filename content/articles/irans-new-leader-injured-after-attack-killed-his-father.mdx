Iran’s new supreme leader, Mojtaba Khamenei, was hurt when a strike on 28 February killed his father and other family members. The attack struck the Khamenei home near Tehran’s presidential office. The Cyprus ambassador says Mojtaba was wounded in his leg, hand and arm, and is now in a hospital. He has not shown up in public since and is staying low‑profile amid fears of more attacks.
