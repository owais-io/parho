US Energy Secretary Chris Wright said the sharp rise in gas and oil prices from the recent conflict in Iran would last only weeks, not months. He said the U.S. would not attack Iranian energy facilities and that the price spike is temporary. Oil prices have jumped 35% and gas up 14% in a week, but he expects them to fall back below $3 a gallon soon.
