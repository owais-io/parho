Isaiah Martin, a 27‑year‑old Democrat from Houston, turned social media into a debate arena. In a viral Jubilee video, he faced 20 Republican opponents and used facts to defeat them, racking up 400 million views. His calm style and sharp statistics have energized frustrated Democrats who feel their party is weak. Martin wants more of this online “show‑down” to inspire voters and boost the party before midterms.
