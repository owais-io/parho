In 2020, a teenage girl’s sister warned her about a Snapchat account that posted private photos of girls with their names and addresses. The story sparked the #StopFisha hashtag, which grew into a movement that pressured Snapchat and TikTok to act faster. Now, young people across Europe have joined Ctrl+Alt+Reclaim to demand that social‑media giants let them have a say, cut out hate and junk content, and protect users’ data.
