Ibram Kendi, a professor who writes about racism, says his books have been banned in the U.S. because some people don’t want to read about racism. He warns that the fear of immigrants—called the “great replacement” theory—helps far‑right leaders rise to power. Kendi argues that if we ignore racist policies, we could end up with authoritarian governments instead of open democracies. He stresses the need for accountability and better conditions for all people to stop the cycle of hate.
