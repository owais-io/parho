The UK has pledged to double the number of troops it keeps in Norway from 1,000 to 2,000 over three years. The move is part of a wider effort to strengthen the Arctic against Russia, and the forces will also join NATO’s Arctic Sentry mission. The decision follows talks by UK defence secretary Healey and comes amid rising tensions.
