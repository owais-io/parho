A UN report says producing food and fossil fuels is hurting the planet for $5 billion every hour. It warns that climate change, loss of nature, and pollution are also hurting economies, food and water supplies, health, and even national security. The report urges quick action: cut fossil‑fuel subsidies, tax meat, promote plant‑based food, and price the true cost of pollution. It says these changes could save money and protect people, but many governments and companies resist.
