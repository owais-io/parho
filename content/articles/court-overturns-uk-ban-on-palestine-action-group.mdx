UK judges have ruled that banning the protest group Palestine Action is illegal. They say the ban unfairly stops free speech and protest. The group had carried out small property damage like graffiti and occupied sites. The government used the Terrorism Act, which is usually for violent groups, to ban them. The decision shows the government faced backlash and may have hurt its credibility.
