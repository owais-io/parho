A fake WhatsApp text pretends a delivery driver from “Cleaning Superstore” missed you and asks you to click a link to pay for redelivery. The message looks real because it comes from a verified business account, but it’s a scam that wants your address, bank details and money. Don’t click the link, don’t give personal data, and report the message to WhatsApp.
