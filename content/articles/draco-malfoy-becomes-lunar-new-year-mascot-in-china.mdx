Draco Malfoy, the famous Harry Potter villain, has unexpectedly become the face of China’s Lunar New Year celebrations. Chinese fans love the wordplay: his name sounds like “horse fortune” in Mandarin, a lucky phrase for the Year of the Horse. Memes, fan art, posters, and merchandise featuring the character have gone viral on social media and in malls. Tom Felton even shared the craze on Instagram, while Warner Bros plans a new Harry Potter studio tour in Shanghai for 2027.
