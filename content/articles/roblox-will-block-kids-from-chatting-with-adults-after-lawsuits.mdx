Roblox will block kids from chatting with adults and older teens after a wave of lawsuits claiming the platform lets predators groom children. Starting next month in Australia, New Zealand and the Netherlands, and worldwide in January, Roblox will use facial age‑estimates to let users chat only with others in the same or nearby age groups. The move follows claims that a 13‑year‑old was groomed and sent explicit content by a predator posing as a child.
