Jérémy Jacquet, a defender for Rennes, is the latest buzz in summer football talks. Both Liverpool and Chelsea want him, but Rennes are holding out for now. The headline transfer window is also heating up elsewhere: Manchester United may bid for Cole Palmer, Burnley is eyeing James Ward‑Prowse, and Wolves want Angel Gomes and Lassine Sinayoko. Even Real Madrid is set to bring back Victor Muñoz, while the Women’s Super League looks at Alexia Putellas.
