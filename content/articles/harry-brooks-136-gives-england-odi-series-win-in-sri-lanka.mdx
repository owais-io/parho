England beat Sri Lanka in the final ODI, thanks to Harry Brook's 136 off 66 balls. Brook’s rapid hit‑out included nine sixes and eleven fours, lifting England to a 358‑run total. Sri Lanka chased 358 but fell short after wickets fell. Brook’s performance turned the game, giving England their first overseas ODI series win in three years. The win was a big moment for England cricket.
