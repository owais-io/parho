At the Milano‑Cortina Winter Games, the U.S. team has ten standout athletes. Alpine skiing stars Lindsey Vonn and Mikaela Shiffrin aim for gold, while cross‑country champion Jessie Diggins could win many medals. Ice‑hockey goalie Aerin Frankel and biathlete Campbell Wright bring hope for new medals. In speed skating, Jordan Stolz could sweep sprints, and figure skater Ilia Malinin is a top contender. These athletes lead the U.S. medal hopes.
