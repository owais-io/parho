English teachers say they’re seeing more girls’ and boys’ misogyny toward them, and they don’t have enough help to deal with sexual aggression. The government is launching a new violence‑against‑women strategy that will teach kids as young as 11 about real relationships, consent and the dangers of sharing intimate pictures. A pilot will give teachers extra support. The plan also offers £550 m to protect victims and looks at what Australia has tried.
