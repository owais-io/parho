A U.S. military investigation says a Tomahawk missile fired from the U.S. hit an elementary school in Minab, Iran, on Feb. 28, killing many children. The strike was caused by a targeting error; U.S. commanders used old data to set the coordinates. Iranian officials have video evidence of the hit and claim the U.S. is responsible. President Trump denied U.S. involvement, but the Pentagon says it is still investigating.
