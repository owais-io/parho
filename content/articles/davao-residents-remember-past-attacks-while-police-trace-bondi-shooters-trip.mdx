In Davao City, people who survived a 2016 bomb blast are watching police investigate the recent visit of two men, Sajid Akram and his son Naveed, linked to the Bondi beach shooting in Australia. The duo stayed in the city for four weeks, but their purpose is unclear. Residents feel uneasy but also hopeful that the city’s strong security won’t let another attack happen. Officials say the men were not trained for terror and that the investigation is ongoing.
