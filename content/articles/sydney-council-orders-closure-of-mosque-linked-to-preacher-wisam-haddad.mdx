A Sydney council has ordered the closure of an Islamic prayer hall that the city says is operating illegally. The hall is run by the Al Madina Group but is tied to preacher Wisam Haddad, who some link to the Bondi beach shooting. The council says the building has no approval to be a prayer space and could be used for other purposes. It has issued a cease‑use notice and warns of fines.
