In 2023, over 70 local councils across the UK either didn’t issue any fines for littering or issued very few, according to Clean Up Britain data. This means many people can litter without risk of punishment. The lack of enforcement also costs councils money, as littering fines only brought in about £48m last year compared with £867m for parking. Councils, especially Labour‑controlled ones, face pressure to step up penalties and improve enforcement.
