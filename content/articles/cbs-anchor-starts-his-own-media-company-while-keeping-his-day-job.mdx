As news outlets cut staff, many reporters are launching their own newsletters and podcasts. CBS contributor David Begnaud is launching “Do Good Crew” – a newsletter, podcast and live events series on beehiiv – while staying with CBS. He says working with the network gives him credibility and resources, while his new venture lets him tell uplifting stories and partner with brands. Beehiiv offers creators tools without taking a cut of subscriptions.
