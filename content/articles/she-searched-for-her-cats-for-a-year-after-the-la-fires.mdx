Darlene Hamilton, 66, lost her two cats in the 2025 LA fires and spent 366 days looking for them. She hung posters, visited shelters, and drove across the region, hoping to find them. Despite her efforts, the cats remain missing. After the fire she moved to a rental and has promised to keep looking. The story shows her devotion and the lasting pain of losing pets in a disaster.
