Bodø/Glimt stunned Manchester City 4‑0 in a Champions League group match in Norway. The Norwegian side scored twice in each half – Kasper Høgh netting two first‑half goals and Jens Petter Hauge adding one after the break. City’s top‑class squad was unable to respond, and the result puts Bodø/Glimt into the playoffs for the first time, proving even giants can be beaten.
