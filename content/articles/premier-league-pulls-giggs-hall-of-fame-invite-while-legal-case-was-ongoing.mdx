In 2020, Ryan Giggs was named one of the first inductees to the Premier League Hall of Fame, but the honor was later taken back. The decision happened after he faced charges of coercive behaviour and assault in 2021. The Crown Prosecution Service dropped the case in 2023, but the league kept its choice. Fans and Giggs’ representatives are asking for him to be honoured now.
