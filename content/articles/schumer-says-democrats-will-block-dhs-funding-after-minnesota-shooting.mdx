In a tense showdown, Senate leader Chuck Schumer announced that Democrats will refuse to vote for a federal funding package if it includes money for the Department of Homeland Security, following the fatal shooting of a Minnesota nurse by an ICE agent. The move threatens a partial government shutdown and reflects growing anger over ICE’s violent tactics. Some Democrats have already pledged to oppose the DHS bill until reforms curb abuses.
