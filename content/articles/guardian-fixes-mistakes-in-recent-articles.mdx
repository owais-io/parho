The Guardian’s “Corrections and Clarifications” notice lists a handful of errors in recent pieces: a soprano was wrongly called a mezzo-soprano, a crossword answer was printed wrong, and a word‑search misspelled “cardigan.” It also notes that other stories—about BP, a Ghanaian guitarist, climate growth, teen safety, and a wildlife auction—had minor edits. Readers can email complaints to the editor or call the office.
