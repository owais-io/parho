Starbucks shareholders are campaigning to remove board members Jørgen Vig Knudstorp and Beth Ford because they say the board has let the company stall union negotiations. More than 680 Starbucks stores have voted to unionize, but no final contracts exist and workers went on strike in late 2025. Shareholders argue the board’s lack of oversight is harming workers and the company’s reputation.
