Emma Beddington talks about how adding sea‑moss, hemp protein and other “nutrient‑dense” items to her meals made her feel more anxious than happy. She sees food as medicine, with TikTok and brands pushing supplements, meal replacements and protein‑packed snacks. While some people struggle to buy food at all, the trend turns eating into a constant calculation. She decides to stop obsessing and just enjoy the foods she loves.
