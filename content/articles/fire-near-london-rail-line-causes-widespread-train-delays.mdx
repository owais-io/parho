A fire in north London’s Primrose Hill area shut down a rail line, stopping trains to and from London Euston that go to Manchester, Liverpool, and Glasgow. The blaze forced Avanti West Coast and London Northwestern services to cancel or delay, and even Overground and Southern trains were affected. Passengers were diverted to other stations and could use their tickets on Saturday. The fire, which burned a one‑storey shop, left no injuries and the cause is still being investigated.
