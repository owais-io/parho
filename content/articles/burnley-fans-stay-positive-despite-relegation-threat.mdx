Burnley are in the Premier League but have gone 15 games without a win and only won 3 of 24 matches, putting them 11 points from safety. Despite the bleak odds, season‑ticket holders still enjoy coming to Turf Moor and stay hopeful. Manager Scott Parker is frustrated by poor play and the squad’s lack of depth, but fans keep a positive attitude, even as some grow tired of the club’s recent struggles.
