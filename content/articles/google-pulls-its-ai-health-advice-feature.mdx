Google has pulled its AI‑powered health advice tool that let users read tips from strangers. The feature, called “What People Suggest,” was aimed at sharing experiences from people with similar conditions. It was dropped as part of a search‑page simplification and not because of safety concerns. The move comes amid scrutiny over Google’s AI medical summaries, which have raised safety worries.
