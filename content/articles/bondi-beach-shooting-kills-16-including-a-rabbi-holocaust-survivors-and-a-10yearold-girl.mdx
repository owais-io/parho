At Bondi beach, a shooting during a Hanukkah party killed 16 people and wounded 42. Victims include a London‑born rabbi, two Holocaust survivors, a 10‑year‑old girl named Matilda and others. Police say the attack was terrorist in nature. The incident shocked the Jewish community and sparked calls for stronger anti‑hate measures. Hospitals treated many injured, and support lines are available for those affected.
