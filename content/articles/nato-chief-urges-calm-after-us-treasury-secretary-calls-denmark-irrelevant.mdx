NATO secretary‑general Mark Rutte told the World Economic Forum in Davos that the best way to ease growing tensions is through calm, diplomatic talks. He spoke after US Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent called Denmark “irrelevant” and after President Donald Trump threatened to seize Greenland and slap tariffs on European countries. Rutte said the alliance is strong, that Arctic security must be improved, and that a diplomatic solution can be found.
