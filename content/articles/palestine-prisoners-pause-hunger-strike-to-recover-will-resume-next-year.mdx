Two prisoners in the UK, linked to a banned group, stopped their hunger strike because they were getting sicker. They plan to start again next year. The prison staff said the men are being treated, but the prisoners claim they were denied medical help. Four others still refuse food. The group says they want the same rights as other inmates, including being able to attend classes.
