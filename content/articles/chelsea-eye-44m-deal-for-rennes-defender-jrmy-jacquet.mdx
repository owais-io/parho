Chelsea are looking to strengthen their defence by bidding around £44 million for 20‑year‑old centre‑back Jérémy Jacquet from Rennes. The move could come as the Blues deal with injuries to Levi Colwill and Tosin Adarabioyo, and they might consider selling captain Enzo Fernández to Real Madrid. Other clubs are eyeing different targets, but Jacquet’s transfer would be the headline for Chelsea’s summer plans.
