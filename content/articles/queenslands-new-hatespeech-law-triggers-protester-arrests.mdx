Queensland introduced new hate‑speech laws that ban the slogans “from the river to the sea” and “globalise the intifada.” Anyone using them to menace or offend can face up to two years in prison. Police arrested a man and a woman after they wore shirts with the banned phrase at a protest. Politicians, students and human‑rights groups say the laws are authoritarian and echo past crackdowns.
