Virginia’s governor, Abigail Spanberger, will give the Democratic response to President Trump’s State of the Union address next week. Spanberger, a former CIA officer and three‑term congresswoman, won Virginia’s first female governorship with a focus on keeping costs down for families. Democrats picked her because she’s a pragmatic, moderate voice who can appeal to voters in a swing state and help the party in November’s elections.
