Mary Walsh, a long‑time CBS News producer, announced her departure after 46 years and said she felt the network was pushing a political agenda. She wrote that CBS was told to target a specific part of the political spectrum, a claim that senior producers denied. The comments come as Paramount’s Skydance looks to buy Warner Bros Discovery and may bring new ownership under former executive Bari Weiss. The memo sparked concerns among staff about editorial freedom and potential self‑censorship.
