The U.S. has moved a carrier strike group into the Mediterranean, sparking fears that a joint U.S.‑Israeli missile strike could be launched against Iran. The Iranian government says it is bracing for such an attack, warning that it would try to break public cohesion before striking political leaders. Protests inside Iran have intensified, the stock market crashed, and diplomats say no deal is close.
