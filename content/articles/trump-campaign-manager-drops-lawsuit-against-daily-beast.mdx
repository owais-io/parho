Chris LaCivita, who helped run Donald Trump's 2024 campaign, quietly dropped a lawsuit that accused the Daily Beast of defaming him over the money he received from the campaign. He said the article made it look like he personally profited millions. The Daily Beast never apologized or paid damages, and the case is now over, showing the ongoing legal fights between Trump allies and the press.
