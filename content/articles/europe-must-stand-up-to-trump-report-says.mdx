Europe has realised it can no longer rely on the Trump administration for security and trade. A Munich Security Conference report says European leaders should act more boldly against what they see as the US's authoritarian shift. The report criticises the US for slamming Europe over free‑speech, migration, and trade, and for pushing Ukraine to back down to Russia. It urges Europe to build its own military independence and speak up.
