Duralex, a famous French glassmaker, needed cash fast. The company asked the public for €5m and got it in less than six hours. In two days, people donated more than €19m, enough to keep the factory open. Workers now own the business and plan to grow sales, especially overseas. The move shows how a beloved brand can survive by connecting with its fans.
