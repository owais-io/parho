A new study found that 90 days after the big Palisades and Eaton wildfires in Los Angeles, emergency room visits for heart attack symptoms at Cedars‑Sinai hospital were 46 % higher than before. The same fires also caused a 24 % rise in lung problems and an almost double jump in blood test changes. The researchers say the smoke and heat may have stressed the body’s organs, leading to more heart attacks and other health issues.
