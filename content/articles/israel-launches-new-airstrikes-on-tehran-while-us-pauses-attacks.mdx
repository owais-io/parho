Israel’s army launched a fresh wave of airstrikes on Tehran after U.S. President Donald Trump said he would pause attacks on Iranian energy sites for five days. The IDF kept fighting, hitting military bases and weapon facilities, while leaving power plants untouched. Trump called talks with Iran “productive” and ordered the military to hold off on strikes against energy infrastructure. Iran responded with a new strike on Israel, and the fighting continues in Lebanon.
