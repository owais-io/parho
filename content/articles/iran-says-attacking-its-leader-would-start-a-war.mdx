President Masoud Pezeshkian warned that attacking Iran's Supreme Leader would trigger a war, blaming U.S. sanctions for the country's hardships. He responded to Donald Trump's remarks that the leader should be removed. Iran has faced violent protests over high inflation and a collapsing currency, with thousands dead. The U.S. almost launched air strikes but backed off after diplomatic pressure. Internet access in Iran has partially returned.
