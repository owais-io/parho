A huge winter storm, called a “triple‑threat megastorm,” is set to hit almost 200 million people. It will bring heavy snow to the upper Midwest, strong winds and possible tornadoes to the mid‑Atlantic, and heavy rain that could flood cities on the East Coast. Flights are canceled, roads are blocked, and power outages are already affecting hundreds of thousands of homes.
