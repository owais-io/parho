Dr Randa Abdel‑Fattah, a well‑known academic who studies Arab and Muslim‑Australian groups, was cleared after a 10‑month probe into her $870,000 research grant. The Australian Research Council (ARC) said there was no conflict of interest or misuse of money, so the grant suspension was lifted. Abdel‑Fattah had faced criticism for comments about Israel, but the investigation found no evidence of wrongdoing. She can now resume her work.
