Readers argue the UK Parliament should leave London for good. Proposals range from moving the whole government to a city like Derby, which would cut MPs’ travel costs and let the Palace of Westminster become a museum and tourist spot. Others suggest keeping the Parliament in London but using Buckingham Palace, or even a virtual, online parliament. Critics warn that moving would hurt communication and teamwork between ministries, but supporters say it would bring democracy closer to voters.
