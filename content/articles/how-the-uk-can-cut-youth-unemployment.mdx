A new review shows UK youth unemployment is still high, with 957,000 16‑to‑24‑year‑olds out of work or study. The government plans to spend £820m on work placements for 18‑ to 21‑year‑olds, but experts say more is needed. They call for better health support, wider access to the youth guarantee, higher pay for young workers, stronger schooling, and local job schemes.
