Daily Mail owner Lord Rothermere has been given permission to move forward with a £500m bid to buy the Telegraph. A rival group, led by New York Sun owner Dovid Efune and backed by Axel Springer, says the government’s decision could unfairly influence the sale. They plan to ask a court to review the move, arguing it may harm competition and media diversity.
