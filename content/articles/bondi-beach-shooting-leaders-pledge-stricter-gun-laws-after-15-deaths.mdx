Bondi Beach shooting killed 15 people, including a child, on the first day of Hanukkah. Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese promised tougher gun laws, and New South Wales Premier Chris Minns said the state would also tighten rules. The attack was carried out by a father‑son duo, one of whom was killed by police. The government pledged aid for victims and to fight antisemitism.
