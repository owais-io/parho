Brazil’s former president Jair Bolsonaro, who is in house arrest after being convicted of staging a failed coup, said he had a psychotic episode and used a soldering iron to damage his electronic ankle tag. He claims the attack was triggered by new medicines and that he feared the tag was spying on him. Police and judges doubt his story; an earlier video shows he tampered with the tag out of curiosity.
