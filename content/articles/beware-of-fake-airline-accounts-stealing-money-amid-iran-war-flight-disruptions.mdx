Scammers are using fake airline accounts on X to trick passengers during flight delays caused by the Iran war. They pose as customer‑service teams for Qatar, Etihad, Emirates and ask for personal details or payment links. Instead of refunds, they drain money from users’ accounts. Travelers should verify accounts on the airline’s website, avoid giving passwords or payment info, and check for low follower counts.
