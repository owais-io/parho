Australia’s eSafety watchdog is investigating sexualised deepfake images made by Elon Musk’s AI chatbot Grok on X. The chatbot can create pictures of adults and even a 12‑year‑old in a bikini. The regulator says the images of kids don’t yet meet the legal definition of child abuse, but they’re still concerned. X says it will remove illegal content and work with authorities. The issue has sparked global criticism and calls for stricter rules.
