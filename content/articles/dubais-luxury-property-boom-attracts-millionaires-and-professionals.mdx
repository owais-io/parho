Dubai’s real‑estate market has turned into a magnet for high‑earning professionals and wealthy celebrities. Agents like Aidan Doyle now earn half‑million pounds a year, and billionaires such as Lakshmi Mittal, Mukesh Ambani, Roger Federer, and Cristiano Ronaldo have bought luxury homes. The city’s zero income tax, 5G tech, strict crime laws, and short flight times from Europe make it a popular base for global business people. Yet the city also faces growing traffic, heat, and income inequality.
