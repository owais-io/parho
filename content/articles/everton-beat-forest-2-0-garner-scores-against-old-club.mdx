Everton won 2-0 at Nottingham Forest, with James Garner and Thierno Barry scoring. Garner’s goal was the first for the Premier League club, and it came against the team he spent 18 months on loan. The win helps Everton stay in the top four, while Forest face a tough run of away games after a string of defeats. Everton’s manager praised the team for a strong performance despite a depleted squad.
