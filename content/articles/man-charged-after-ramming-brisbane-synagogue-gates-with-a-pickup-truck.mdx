Police in Brisbane arrested a 32‑year‑old man who drove a pickup truck and smashed the gates of a synagogue on Margaret Street. He was charged with hate crime, vandalism, dangerous driving and drug possession. No one was hurt, and the attack was not treated as terrorism. Officials said the attack targeted the Jewish community, and leaders are calling for protection and support.
