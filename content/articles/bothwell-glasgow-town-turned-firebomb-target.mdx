Bothwell, a wealthy suburb of Glasgow, has seen a wave of arson attacks—27 incidents since 2021—targeting restaurants, cars and homes. Only one suspect has been caught, and no prosecutions have happened. Residents feel uneasy but not scared, worried about possible gang ties and council response. Police say the fires seem targeted, while local groups push for better policing and community support.
