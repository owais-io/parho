WH Smith discovered a huge accounting mistake that wiped £600 m off its value. The UK regulator is now investigating. The company plans to recover more than £7 m of bonuses paid to its former chief executive and finance director. Carl Cowling has quit, and an interim boss is tightening controls, cutting unprofitable US stores, and promising better oversight to rebuild trust. The move follows a review that blamed a target‑driven culture and will also look at other overseas markets.
