Berlin’s new Ukraine museum sits in a WWII air‑raid bunker. It shows real war footage, a drone‑targeted van, and 3D‑printed missiles to make the conflict feel immediate. Curators use recovered gear and photos from Kyiv, reminding visitors of the war’s toll and the need to keep support for Ukraine strong. The museum is privately funded and will stay open until the war ends.
