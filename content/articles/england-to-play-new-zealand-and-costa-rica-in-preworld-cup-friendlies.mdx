England will warm up for the World Cup with two friendly matches against New Zealand on June 6 and Costa Rica on June 10 in Florida. After that, they’ll move to Kansas City, staying at the Swope Soccer Village for the rest of the tournament. The team will travel to games in Texas, Massachusetts and New Jersey from that base. Coach Thomas Tuchel plans the move to avoid long trips after the Champions League final.
