Family of 19‑year‑old student Any Lucia López Belloza says ICE agents surprised them at their Texas home after she was deported to Honduras. She was taken at Boston airport and sent home in 48 hours, even though the family says no notice was given. Agents showed up in unmarked cars, forced open a gate, and entered without a warrant. The family claims this was retaliation for the student speaking out.
