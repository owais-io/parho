OpenAI is raising $110 billion in a new funding round that values the company at about $840 billion. Big names like SoftBank, Nvidia and Amazon are each putting in $30–$50 billion. The money will help the ChatGPT maker build huge data centres and speed up AI services. The deal comes as investors bet on AI’s growth, while worries about jobs and energy use grow.
