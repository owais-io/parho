A UK mother explains how her 4‑year‑old daughter gave a crayon to Greta Thunberg, who was visiting to support Palestinian hunger‑strikers. Greta wrote a sign in protest, was taken to the police, and arrested under UK anti‑terror laws. The piece discusses how the UK treats peaceful protest, the labeling of Palestine Action as a terrorist group, and the impact on free speech. She reflects on the government's harsh stance and the consequences for activists.
