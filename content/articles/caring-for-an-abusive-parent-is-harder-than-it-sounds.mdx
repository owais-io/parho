One woman drove thousands of kilometres to care for her mum, who had never loved her. She slept at hospitals and handled finances. The story shows that when an adult child has a difficult or abusive relationship with a parent, the usual idea that caregiving is simple and natural breaks down. Experts explain how old trauma, guilt, and sibling drama add to the stress and can hurt the carer's mental health. Caregivers need support and understanding.
