Ghislaine Maxwell, convicted of helping Jeffrey Epstein exploit girls, said she would give a full, honest testimony to Congress if President Trump grants her a pardon. She claims only she can explain everything, even naming former presidents. Lawyers for victims call the offer a “trade” and doubt any truth will come out. Trump and his team have said they’re not considering clemency, and the White House says it’s not a priority.
