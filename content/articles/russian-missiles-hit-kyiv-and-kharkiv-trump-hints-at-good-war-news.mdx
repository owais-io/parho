Russian forces launched missile and drone attacks on Ukraine’s capital Kyiv and second‑largest city Kharkiv, causing fires, injuries and power cuts. The strikes targeted energy plants and even a kindergarten. Meanwhile, former US President Donald Trump said the US might have “good news” on ending the war, ahead of talks in Abu Dhabi. The attack came as a ceasefire had just ended, and both sides are preparing for new negotiations.
