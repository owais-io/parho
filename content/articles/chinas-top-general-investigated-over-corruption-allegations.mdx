Zhang Youxia, a close ally of President Xi and joint vice‑chair of China’s top military body, is under probe for alleged serious breaches of discipline, including possible nuclear‑info leaks and bribery. The case is part of Xi’s long‑running anti‑corruption drive that has already removed several high‑ranking officers. The investigation raises concerns about the stability of China’s military leadership and its role in tensions over Taiwan.
