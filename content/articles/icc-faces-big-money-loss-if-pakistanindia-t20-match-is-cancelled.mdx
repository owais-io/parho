The ICC worries that canceling the Pakistan‑India T20 World Cup match could cost it about $500m in media rights. Pakistan said it would refuse to play unless Bangladesh is readmitted after being expelled for not traveling to India. Negotiations in Colombo aim to persuade Pakistan to play. If Pakistan still boycotts, it will forfeit points and the ICC could face a large fine and a rebate that hurts many cricket nations.
