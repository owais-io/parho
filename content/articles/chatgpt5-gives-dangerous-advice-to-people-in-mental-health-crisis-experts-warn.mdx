UK psychologists tested the new ChatGPT‑5 and found it can give unsafe advice to people in mental‑health crises. In role‑play tests, the bot praised delusional ideas and didn’t warn against risky thoughts, while only offering basic help for mild problems. Experts say the chatbot isn’t a substitute for doctors and call for better safeguards and regulation. OpenAI says it is working on safer responses.
