Two appeals by the lawyers for former rugby players were denied. That means many players may lose their chances to have their cases heard. The court said the firm did not share required medical records on time. 180 out of 321 league players now risk having their claims thrown out. About one in five union players could be affected. The next court dates are in March, but the fight for justice continues.
