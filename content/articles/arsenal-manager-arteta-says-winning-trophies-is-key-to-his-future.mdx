Mikel Arteta is marking six years as Arsenal’s manager and says his job will depend on winning trophies. He says every match, every talk in the dressing room, and the players’ support are how he proves he belongs. With the team fighting for the title and injuries piling up, Arteta stresses he has to win games and show progress soon. He looks forward to the Everton match to keep pushing for silverware.
