Brisbane 2032’s three‑word motto – “Believe, Belong, Become” – was revealed after a public survey, but it has drawn sharp criticism. Some people feel the words sound too Christian and weak, calling the slogan “lazy and oddly evangelical.” Experts say a better tagline could have celebrated Australian culture or inspired visitors. The debate shows how a simple slogan can spark a big controversy.
