In 2025, President Trump turned almost every big sporting event into a political stage. He attended the Super Bowl, Daytona 500, UFC shows, and the World Cup draw. His appearances boosted his image, drew donors, and pushed conservative issues like transgender‑sports. Trump treats sports as a quick way to reach crowds, get media attention, and network with allies. He plans to step up even more in 2026 with the World Cup, Olympics, and other events.
