Seventy‑three‑year‑old Goran Radic, an experienced kayaker, got lost in St Vincent Gulf north of Adelaide. He drifted away from the beach and didn’t find the channel he usually uses. He was missing for most of the night, and police, volunteer crews and a helicopter searched the waters and air, using infrared cameras. He was spotted early Wednesday, winched to safety and said the worst part was the mosquitoes.
