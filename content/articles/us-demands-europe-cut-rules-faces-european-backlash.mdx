The US has reiterated its criticism of Europe, saying its new security strategy is meant to push the continent to reform and grow again. European leaders say the language is offensive and that the US is overstepping. Washington wants the EU to cut rules, free capital and talent, and drop tariffs on steel to attract American investment. Meanwhile, Europe’s growth remains slow, while the US economy is expanding faster.
