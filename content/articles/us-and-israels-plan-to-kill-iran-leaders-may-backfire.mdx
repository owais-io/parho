Israel has told the U.S. it can target any senior Iranian official on its hit list. Analysts warn this “decapitation” strategy could hurt more than help. Killing top figures like Iran’s supreme leader and security chiefs may simply promote unknown, hardened successors and fuel resistance instead of stopping the regime. Experts point to past failures against groups like Hamas and argue the plan might destabilize Iran and create new threats.
