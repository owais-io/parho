After federal ICE agents began rounding up people in Minnesota, many residents are staying home and skipping doctor visits because they fear being taken to Texas. Local clinics are stepping in with home visits, telehealth and a rapid‑response unit to give medication and emergency care. Doctors, nurses and community groups call for ending ICE and want hospitals to stay safe. The crisis shows how enforcement actions can make people miss needed medical treatment.
