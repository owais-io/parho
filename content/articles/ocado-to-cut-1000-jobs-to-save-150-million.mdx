Ocado, the British online‑grocer that runs robotic warehouses, will cut 1,000 jobs—about 5 % of its staff—to save £150 million by 2026. Most of the cuts will come from the UK, and two‑thirds will affect tech roles. The company is merging divisions, trimming R&D and using AI to reduce costs. Shares fell 10 % after the announcement, amid other warehouse closures abroad.
