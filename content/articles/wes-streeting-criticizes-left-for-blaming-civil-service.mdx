Labour MP Wes Streeting said the centre‑left is too quick to blame civil servants for slow government change. He called for the government to stop making excuses and to actually fix the system. No 10 plans a new civil‑service overhaul, with changes to pay, training and performance. Streeting warned that the public is tired of delays and sees fewer services for the money they pay. A recent poll shows low approval and big worries about costs, health and immigration.
