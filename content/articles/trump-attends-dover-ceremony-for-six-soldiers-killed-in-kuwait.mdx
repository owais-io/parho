President Donald Trump attended a ceremony at Dover Air Force Base where the bodies of six U.S. soldiers, killed in a drone strike in Kuwait, were returned home. Trump, along with cabinet members, spoke about honoring the fallen and pledging to keep war deaths low. The soldiers were from the 103rd Sustainment Command and had been killed just after U.S. and Israeli forces launched a campaign against Iran.
