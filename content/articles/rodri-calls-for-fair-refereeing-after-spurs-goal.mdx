After Tottenham’s Dominic Solanke scored a goal that City’s defender said slipped off, midfielder Rodri joined his team’s complaints about referees. He said the officials must stay neutral, especially after several controversial calls – from a disputed goal at Newcastle to a handball penalty denied at Wolves. City’s manager Pep Guardiola also voiced frustration, and Rodri said the mounting decisions make fans and players feel cheated.
