Portugal police seized almost 9 tonnes of cocaine from a semi‑submersible vessel near the Azores. The team, working with the navy, air force, US DEA and UK NCA, forced the ship to sink and took 35 bales. Four crew members – three Colombians and a Venezuelan – were arrested. It’s the biggest drug seizure in Portugal, worth up to €600m. Falling cocaine prices are prompting gangs to reuse these boats, as Spain reports.
