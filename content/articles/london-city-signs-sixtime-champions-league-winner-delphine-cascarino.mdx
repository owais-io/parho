London City Lionesses have added French winger Delphine Cascarino to their squad on a 3½‑year deal. The 28‑year‑old, who won six Champions League titles with Lyon, recently left the San Diego Wave after scoring seven goals in 34 games. Cascarino is excited to play in England’s top women’s league and aims to win trophies with London City. The club also welcomed defender Daniëlle van de Donk back from injury after a long absence.
