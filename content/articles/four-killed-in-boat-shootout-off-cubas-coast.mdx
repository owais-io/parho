Four people were killed and six injured when Cuban coastguards fired at a US‑registered speedboat near Cayo Falcones. Cuba says the boat carried armed Cuban defectors planning a terrorist attack, and its coastguard shot them after the boat opened fire. The U.S. is investigating, calling the incident unusual. Tensions are already high because of a month‑long Cuban oil blockade that the U.S. has just eased.
