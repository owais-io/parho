In the small Italian town of Castel di Sangro, the old players keep a WhatsApp chat alive, remembering the club’s shock rise to Serie B in 1995‑96. The story was made famous by American journalist Joe McGinniss in his book *The Miracle of Castel di Sangro*. After a brief stay in the second tier, the club folded in 2005. Today a new team and youth academy are reviving the miracle spirit, hoping to bring football back to the town.
