Just weeks before Christmas, Sydney’s David Jones store swapped its beloved festive window for a display promoting its new loyalty program and a Dalmatian mascot called Domino. Families who normally queue for the holiday show were disappointed, and many posted angry comments on Facebook and radio. The store apologized, saying it had missed expectations, and promised to bring back a more traditional display next year. Meanwhile, rival Myer kept its classic LEGO Christmas windows, drawing large crowds.
