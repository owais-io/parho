Barcelona are upset after 18‑year‑old midfielder Dro Fernández decided to leave the club for Paris Saint‑Germain. The youngster had turned 18 on January 12 and had agreed a new contract with Barcelona, but he chose to sign with PSG instead, paying a €8 million fee. The move has caused anger at Barcelona, with president Joan Laporta calling the situation unpleasant and coach Hansi Flick expressing frustration. Dro only made five appearances for Barça before the transfer.
