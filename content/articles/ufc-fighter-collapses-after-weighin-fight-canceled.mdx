UFC bantamweight Cameron Smotherman collapsed right after his weigh‑in for UFC 324 in Las Vegas. He fell on the stage, was quickly treated, and later regained consciousness, but doctors still needed to examine him. Because of the incident, his bout with Ricky Turcios was pulled from the card. The collapse has sparked fresh concern about the extreme weight‑cutting practices fighters use before fights, a topic the UFC has yet to change.
