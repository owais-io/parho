Manchester United drew 0‑0 with Bournemouth at Old Trafford, leaving many fans puzzled about head coach Ruben Amorim’s tactics. Reports say he trained with a back‑four, but on the pitch his usual three‑back system seemed to collapse, sparking debate over whether he abandoned it. The team lost early leads, but the goalkeeper kept the score level. Meanwhile, star Bruno Fernandes worried about a possible move to Saudi Arabia and said he chose to stay after a talk with Amorim.
