A Toronto fitness fan named Mac Bauer races city streetcars on a rare open road. He keeps winning, proving the trams—used by 230,000 daily—are still sluggish. Bauer’s videos went viral and sparked talk about traffic signals and dedicated lanes. The latest line, Line 6, opened last month but already had delays, and Bauer beat it by 18 minutes. His runs have pushed city councillors to push for better signal priority.
