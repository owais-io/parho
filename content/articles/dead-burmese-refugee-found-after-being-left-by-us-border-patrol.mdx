A 56‑year‑old Burmese man, who was almost blind, was found dead in Buffalo after border patrol left him at a fast‑food outlet. He had been held in Erie County for a year and was released from a holding center following a 2025 arrest over an assault incident. Border patrol dropped him off without telling his family, and detectives are now looking into what happened. The case raises questions about how migrants are treated after release.
