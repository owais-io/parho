French police are asking anyone who might have been abused by former teacher Jacques Leveugle to come forward. Leveugle, 79, worked with children in many countries from the 1960s to 2022 and is accused of raping 89 kids, aged 13‑17. He also claimed to have killed his mother and aunt. Police want more victims and witnesses to help close the case by 2026.
