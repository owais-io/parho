California has started a civil‑rights investigation after residents in west Altadena, a mainly Black neighborhood, were told to evacuate from the Eaton wildfire hours later than those in the wealthier east side. Fire crews also arrived much slower on the west side. All but one of the 19 deaths were from west Altadena. The probe will check if the delay was unlawful discrimination under state law.
