The US has set up a Trump‑backed peace board that plans to send troops from several countries to Gaza and wants Hamas fully disarmed. Trump pledged $10 bn to rebuild the Strip and called for a huge multinational force. The board also wants a 5,000‑person base and 20,000 soldiers, but many nations, including the EU and Israel, refuse to join. Critics say the plan is a shadow UN with unclear rules.
