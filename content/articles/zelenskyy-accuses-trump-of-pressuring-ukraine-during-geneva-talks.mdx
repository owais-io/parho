Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said former U.S. President Donald Trump was unfairly pressuring Kyiv to make concessions in the Geneva peace talks. He warned any deal that would hand back Russian‑held land would be rejected in a Ukrainian vote. Meanwhile, Ukrainian drones struck Russian oil and chemical sites, a Lithuanian airport briefly closed over Belarusian weather balloons, France seized a Russian‑linked tanker, and Russian Starlink outages may have helped Ukraine gain ground.
