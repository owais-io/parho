Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese spoke directly with Israeli President Isaac Herzog about the bulldozing of Australian war graves in Gaza. The IDF destroyed many headstones, mainly those of soldiers who died in WWII. Albanese asked for quick repairs and full funding from Israel. Senators say the government should hold Israel accountable. The Commonwealth War Graves Commission will restore the site when it’s safe, but the process will take time amid the ongoing conflict.
