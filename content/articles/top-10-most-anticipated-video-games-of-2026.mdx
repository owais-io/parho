This list shows the ten games people are most excited about for 2026. From a realistic mountain‑climbing game called Cairn Live to the horror adventure Reanimal, players can expect new thrills. Classic franchises return with fresh twists, such as Resident Evil Requiem and a new Pokémon title, Pokopia. The list also includes the long‑awaited 007 First Light, the sci‑fi shooter Saros, and the blockbuster Grand Theft Auto VI. Other highlights are Marvel’s Wolverine, the new witch‑school game Witchbrook, and a re‑imagined Halo campaign.
