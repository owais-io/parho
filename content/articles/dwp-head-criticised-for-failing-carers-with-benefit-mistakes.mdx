The UK Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) has been slammed by MPs and a Guardian investigation for a culture that left thousands of unpaid carers with huge benefit debts. Senior civil servant Sir Peter Schofield promised to fix the problems but has not acted. The department blamed carers for the overpayments, while an independent review blamed DWP’s own mistakes. MPs want the DWP to overhaul its culture and clear the debts.
