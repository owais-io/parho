Beto came on as a substitute and scored in the 7th minute of injury time to level the game at 1‑1. The goal sent Everton’s 63‑year‑old manager David Moyes sprinting onto the pitch in joy, while Brighton’s players looked defeated. The match ended Everton’s unbeaten home record, and Brighton have only won one of their last 11 games, weakening hopes of a European spot. Beto’s strike sealed the drama.
