Everton manager David Moyes is chasing the tall Moroccan striker Youssef En‑Nesyri from Fenerbahce, offering a loan with a £20 million purchase option. The club is also open to bringing in West Ham forward Callum Wilson. Other teams like Nottingham Forest and Olympiakos are eyeing En‑Nesyri, while clubs such as Crystal Palace and Tottenham look to strengthen their squads with young players. The transfer window remains busy for many Premier League sides.
