President Zelenskyy said Ukraine must set up a new system so foreign buyers cannot buy drones directly from makers. He warned that allowing direct deals could hurt the country’s security and has already warned one company that sold interceptors without checking how they’d affect Ukraine’s defense. With tensions rising over Iranian drone attacks, the US and its allies want Ukrainian interceptors. Ukraine is also waiting for a new peace talk round with the US and Russia.
