Senate leaders and Democrats are concerned that President Trump might use the U.S. military in Iran, which could lead to a big war. After a secret briefing, they said the president should explain his plans to Congress and that any war would need Congressional approval. They are pushing for a vote to hold Trump accountable, stop a possible invasion, keep the nation safe and protect American lives.
