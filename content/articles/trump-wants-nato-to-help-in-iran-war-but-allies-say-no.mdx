Trump says the US is winning its war against Iran and wants NATO to step in, but many European allies refuse. The US has struck Iranian targets, sparing major oil sites, and warns that the Strait of Hormuz must stay open. Iran, meanwhile, is pushing for a wider conflict that could destabilise Gulf shipping and strain US‑European ties. The fight is becoming as much a political battle as a military one.
