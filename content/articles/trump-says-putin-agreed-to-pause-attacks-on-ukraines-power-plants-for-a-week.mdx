Trump said Russian President Vladimir Putin agreed to stop attacks on Ukraine’s power plants for a week because of the cold. He claimed he spoke directly with Putin, but Russia hasn’t confirmed the deal. Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said the pause could help keep cities warm, but warned new attacks could still happen. The ceasefire is unconfirmed and talks continue.
