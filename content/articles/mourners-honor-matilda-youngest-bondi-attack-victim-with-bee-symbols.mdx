Mourners in Sydney are honoring 10‑year‑old Matilda, the youngest victim of the Bondi beach attack, by using bees as a symbol of her life. The family, Ukrainian Jewish immigrants, chose “Bee” to remember her. Bees appear on flowers, candles and stickers at the Bondi memorial and at her funeral at Chevra Kadisha. Politicians and many locals gathered, showing how the attack has touched everyone and how they want to keep her memory alive.
