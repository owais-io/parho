After a string of defeats, Liverpool beat West Ham 2‑0 at the London Stadium. Alexander Isak netted his first Premier League goal for the club, followed by Cody Gakpo’s late strike. Liverpool used a 4‑2‑3‑1 without Salah, with Szoboszlai on the right and Wirtz in midfield. West Ham saw midfielder Lucas Paquetá sent off, but the loss kept their slump. The win lifts Liverpool’s confidence and ends a 71‑day winless run.
