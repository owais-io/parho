A 5‑year‑old boy in northern Japan died after his arm got stuck on a moving walkway at a ski resort. He was trying to get off the 30‑metre travelator that carries skiers from the parking lot to the slope. Firefighters freed him 40 minutes after the call, but he could not be revived. The resort, only 60 cm wide and without handrails, is under investigation for possible negligence.
