A man in his 60s from Stockport is charged with plotting to drug and rape his wife, along with 20 years of ongoing sexual assault. He pleaded not guilty in court, while a co‑defendant from Dundee pleaded guilty. Twelve other men, aged 28 to 73, were also charged. The case is set to go to trial in September, with some defendants appearing on video links from prison.
