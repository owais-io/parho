Police in Moscow say a bomb blew up near their car on Yeletskaya Street at 1:30 am, killing two traffic‑police officers and a third person. The blast happened as the officers tried to approach a suspicious individual who may have been planting the device. Investigators are reviewing CCTV footage and suspect Ukrainian intelligence was involved. The incident comes after a similar attack that killed a senior Russian officer earlier this week. Russian officials are calling for swift retaliation.
