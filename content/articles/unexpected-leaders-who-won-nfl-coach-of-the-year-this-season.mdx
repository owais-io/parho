The NFL Coach of the Year usually goes to the coach who turned a team around the most. This year, many teams improved, so the race is harder. The top favorites are Ben Johnson of the Bears, Liam Coen of the Jaguars, Mike Macdonald of the Seahawks, Mike Vrabel of the Patriots, and Kyle Shanahan of the 49ers. Each coach has shown great improvement, solved tough problems, and helped their teams win more games.
