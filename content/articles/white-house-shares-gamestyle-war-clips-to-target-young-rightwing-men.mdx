White House officials have posted short, flashy videos that mix real U.S. strikes on Iran with action movie and video‑game footage. The clips are meant to appeal to young, right‑wing men who spend a lot of time online. The videos use loud music, fast cuts, and gaming graphics to create a hyper‑macho vibe. Most Americans are skeptical of the war, and many online comments mock the footage as tacky and divisive.
