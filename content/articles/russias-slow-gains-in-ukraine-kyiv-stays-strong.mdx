After four years, Russia is barely expanding its territory in Ukraine, adding only a few square miles each month. Ukrainian forces have stopped the advance, even retaking towns like Kupiansk, and use Starlink to block Russian communications. The West keeps sending weapons, but air‑defence supplies lag. Despite winter hardships, Kyiv remains stubborn and plans to increase Russian casualties to force a pause.
