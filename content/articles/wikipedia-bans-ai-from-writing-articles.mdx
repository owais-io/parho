Wikipedia has decided not to allow AI tools to write or rewrite its articles. The move came after a vote by volunteer editors. Only two uses are still allowed: AI can help translate pages and suggest small copy edits that editors must check. The decision follows concerns that AI can produce wrong or misleading content. Though AI is popular online, Wikipedia’s founder said current models aren’t reliable enough.
