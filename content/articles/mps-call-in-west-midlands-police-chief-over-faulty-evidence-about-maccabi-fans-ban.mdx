Parliamentary committee wants the chief constable to explain wrong evidence that led to banning Maccabi Tel Aviv fans from a Birmingham match. They say the evidence was mistaken, based on false intelligence from Dutch police and an internet search. The ban came after a synagogue attack and during the Israel‑Gaza conflict. MPs demand documents and oral evidence; an investigation ordered by the Home Secretary. West Midlands police will respond.
