Reform UK candidate Corey Edwards stepped down from the May Senedd election after a photo of him making a Nazi salute was published. He says mental‑health problems led to the decision. Party leader Nigel Farage defended him, claiming it looked like a comedy imitation. The incident has raised worries about other Reform candidates, several of whom have faced suspensions or controversies over offensive remarks.
