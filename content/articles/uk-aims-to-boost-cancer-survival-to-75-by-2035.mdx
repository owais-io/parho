UK health ministers announced a new £2bn plan to raise cancer survival to 75 % by 2035. The plan will add 9.5 million more tests, 12‑hour diagnostic centres, and boost robot‑assisted surgery to half a million cases. Every patient will get genomic tests to find the best treatment. It aims to cut waiting times, improve early detection and give more people a chance to live well after diagnosis.
