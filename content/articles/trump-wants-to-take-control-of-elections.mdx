Donald Trump recently said the federal government should take over voting in at least 15 states before the 2026 midterms. He made the comment after an FBI raid seized Georgia election materials, stoking his long‑running claim that elections are fraudulent. Experts say Trump has no legal power to run elections or use emergency powers for that. His comments risk misleading voters and threaten the integrity of American elections.
