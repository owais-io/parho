Trump’s family company World Liberty took a $500 million stake from a UAE royal family member just before he took office. Ethics experts say the deal creates a serious conflict of interest and may breach the Emoluments Clause. Trump says he has no control over the businesses, but he hosted the UAE delegation and later signed a deal to let the UAE import Nvidia AI chips. Congress is urged to investigate.
