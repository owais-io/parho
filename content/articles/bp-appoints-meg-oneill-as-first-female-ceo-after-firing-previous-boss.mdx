BP’s board has fired chief executive Murray Auchincloss and named Meg O’Neill, a former Exxon and Woodside Energy boss, as the company’s new CEO. O’Neill will be the first woman to run a top‑five oil company and the first outsider to lead BP. Carol Howle will run the business until O’Neill takes over in April. The change comes as BP looks to refocus on oil and gas and improve its shares.
