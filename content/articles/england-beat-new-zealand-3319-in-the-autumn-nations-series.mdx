England beat New Zealand 33‑19 in the Autumn Nations Series. A mix of forwards and backs helped the English side. George Ford dropped a 50‑22 conversion, while Tom Roebuck and Ben Earl dominated the forward pack. New Zealand’s key try scorer, Will Jordan, missed a pass that could have turned the game. England’s lineouts and defensive pressure were strong, and their attack was supported by solid support from the backs.
