The Spurs have surged this season, beating the Oklahoma City Thunder twice and climbing to 23‑7. Their new star, Victor Wembanyama, and solid play from Fox, Vassell and Barnes have made the team a serious contender. The Thunder, once on a hot run, are now scrambling to keep up. With the Spurs strong, the NBA feels refreshed and competitive again, and fans are excited and hopeful.
