A surge of flight cancellations is hitting airlines after the US‑Israel war over Iran shut down airspace in the Middle East. Big hubs like Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Doha are closed, leaving hundreds of thousands of passengers stuck and causing airlines to pull many flights. The chaos is hurting airline stocks, raising oil prices, and forcing travelers to seek alternative routes, sometimes by private jets at huge costs.
