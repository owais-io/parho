UN Secretary‑General António Guterres warned that AI is moving so fast it’s already reshaping war. In the Iran crisis the US military used AI from companies like Anthropic and OpenAI to spot targets and help decide strikes, leading to thousands of civilian deaths. The companies say they can’t stop the Pentagon’s use, and the Pentagon wants control left to soldiers, not tech firms. Experts call for strict rules and international treaties to keep human judgment in charge.
