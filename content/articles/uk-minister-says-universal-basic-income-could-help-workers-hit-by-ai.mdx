UK minister Jason Stockwood said that a universal basic income (UBI) could protect workers whose jobs are being lost to artificial intelligence. He said the government is talking about it, even though it’s not official policy yet. Stockwood wants the state to help people retrain and to make sure people aren’t left behind when AI changes work. Many leaders are also saying governments must step in to help displaced workers.
