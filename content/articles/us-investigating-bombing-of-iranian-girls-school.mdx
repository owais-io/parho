US defense secretary Pete Hegseth said the Pentagon is investigating the bombing of a girls’ school in Iran that killed at least 165 students and injured 96 others. He stressed the US never targets civilians, but has launched an inquiry. The UN’s children’s rights office urged a full investigation and accountability. Hegseth also confirmed a US submarine hit an Iranian frigate, and said the US will soon control Iranian airspace.
