Donald Trump made a controversial social‑media post saying that Hollywood director Rob Reiner’s death was caused by the director’s dislike of Trump. Celebrities and lawmakers from both parties called the remarks hateful and unprofessional. Trump also filed a $5‑billion lawsuit against the BBC, claiming the broadcaster edited his 2021 Jan. 6 speech. The news sparks national outrage over Trump’s conduct and media disputes in the United States.
