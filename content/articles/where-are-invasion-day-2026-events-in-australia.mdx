Across Australia, thousands of people will gather on 26 January to mark Invasion Day, also called Survival Day. Events include dawn services, marches, festivals, and community talks. In Sydney, the main rally starts in Hyde Park and a dawn ceremony lights the Opera House sails. Melbourne hosts a march and a free music festival. Canberra, Adelaide, Perth, Brisbane, and smaller towns have their own ceremonies, speeches, and celebrations to honor First Nations histories.
