In Cheshire, council leaders Nick Mannion and Michael Gorman are being asked to step down after they wrote a letter supporting a new town in Adlington, a 1,200‑person village on the Peak District’s edge. The plan, backed by developers and put on the government’s list of potential sites, surprised residents who had no say in the decision. A petition of 19,000 people, a motion from the Conservative councillors, and public backlash now force the leaders to face calls for resignation.
