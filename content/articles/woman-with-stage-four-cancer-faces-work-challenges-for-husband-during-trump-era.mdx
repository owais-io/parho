Michaela, 57, was diagnosed with metastatic breast cancer after a fall in May 2025. She has been undergoing surgery, radiation and medication. Her husband works for the Department of Transportation. When Trump took office, he was forced to return to the office after months of remote work. The agency has cut jobs and a long shutdown added stress and unpaid wages. Michaela worries about future shutdowns while fighting her illness.
