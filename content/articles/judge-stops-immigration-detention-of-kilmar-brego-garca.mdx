On Friday, Judge Paula Xinis stopped ICE from taking Kilmar Ábrego García back into detention after he was freed from a Pennsylvania facility. The order says he can’t be re‑detained until a court hearing takes place. Ábrego, who was previously deported to El Salvador, has lived in Maryland with his family and says he will keep fighting to prove the government’s actions are wrong. His lawyers claim the Trump administration had no right to keep him in custody.
