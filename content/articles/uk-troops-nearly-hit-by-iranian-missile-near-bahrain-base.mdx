Three hundred UK soldiers were only 200 metres from an Iranian missile and drone strike on a US base in Bahrain, but nobody was hurt. The UK is not part of the US‑Israeli attack on Iran, yet its jets have shot down drones and defended allied bases. Iran has fired many rockets at Israel, Gulf states and even the UK’s own air bases, so troops are on high alert.
