England defence coach Richard Wigglesworth says there is no point trying to devise a plan to stop Scotland’s Finn Russell. After watching him on the Lions tour, Wigglesworth admits the fast‑moving fly‑half will simply adjust. England are set to defend the Calcutta Cup at Murrayfield, a place they haven’t won in years, while Scotland were stunned by Italy. Underhill also praises Russell’s defence and physicality.
