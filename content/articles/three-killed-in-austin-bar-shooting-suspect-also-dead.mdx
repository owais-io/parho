A gunman opened fire outside a student‑popular bar in downtown Austin on Sunday, killing two people and injuring 14 before police shot him. The FBI is looking into whether the shooter had ties to terrorism, because evidence and his posts show pro‑Iran sentiment. The incident has reignited Texas lawmakers’ debate over the state’s weak gun‑control rules and the role of immigration.
