Sunderland kept a 1-1 draw with Manchester City, ending City's run of eight straight wins. The result keeps Arsenal a full four points ahead at the top of the Premier League. City were unable to break down Sunderland's defence, and key moments included a close save by City keeper Gianluigi Donnarumma and a late goal from Sunderland's striker. Fans endured cold weather and a long drive home.
