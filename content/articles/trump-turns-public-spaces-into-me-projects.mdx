Trump has put his face and name on many public places, from huge banners at the Justice Department to new names for airports and roads. He added his name to the Kennedy Center and the Institute of Peace, and a Florida airport was renamed after him. Trump is also targeting Penn Station, Dulles Airport, and a new $3.7bn stadium in Washington. Critics say this shows ‘dictator’ vibes and blurs the line between government and personal branding.
