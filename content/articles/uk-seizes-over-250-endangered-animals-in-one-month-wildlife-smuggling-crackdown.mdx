Border Force seized more than 250 protected animals and illegal wildlife goods during a month‑long operation called Operation Thunder. Officers found two rainbow boas hidden under blankets, 2,000 tarantulas worth £70,000, and a car carrying 100 endangered birds that had died in poor conditions. The raid also uncovered ivory, shark meat and other items, aiming to stop organised crime that hurts species and weakens borders.
