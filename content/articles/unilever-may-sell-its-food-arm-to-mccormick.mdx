Unilever, which owns Marmite, Dove and Hellmann’s mayo, is talking to McCormick, a US spice maker, about selling its food division. The deal would let Unilever focus on beauty, personal care and home products while McCormick expands its food brands. Both companies are considering an all‑stock swap, but no final agreement has been reached yet. Shares ticked up a little after the news.
