In Vienna, the city is slashing its culture budget, so several small museums tied to famous composers will close for up to two years. The Schubert apartment, Strauss flat and Haydn house are shut, and others will have shorter opening hours. The cuts also mean public transport fares will rise about 30%. Critics say the city is hurting culture while keeping big festivals running.
