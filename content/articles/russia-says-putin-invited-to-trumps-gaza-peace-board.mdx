The Kremlin says President Vladimir Putin has been invited to join former President Donald Trump’s new “Board of Peace,” which aims to end the Gaza war. Trump’s board would oversee a ceasefire and help run Gaza under a UN‑approved plan. It has already invited leaders from several countries, with a few accepting. Russia wants to discuss the offer with Washington, but it’s still unconfirmed. The move comes as Russia continues its war in Ukraine.
