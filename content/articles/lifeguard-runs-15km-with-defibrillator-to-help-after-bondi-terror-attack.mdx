During a terror attack at a Hanukkah celebration on Bondi Beach, a lifeguard named Jackson Doolan sprinted 1.5 km from Tamarama Beach carrying a defibrillator to help. The photo of him running went viral and showed his quick response. He and other lifeguards joined police and ambulance crews, turning a nearby clubhouse into a makeshift hospital. The incident sparked praise from the community and the council for the lifeguards' bravery and kindness.
