Mexico’s president, Claudia Sheinbaum, asked the UN to stop any violence in Venezuela after the US announced a blockade of Venezuelan oil tankers. She said Mexico won’t allow outside forces to push out President Maduro, who many see as illegitimate. Mexico also offered to host talks. China, which buys most Venezuelan oil, said it opposes one‑country pressure. Meanwhile, the US keeps boosting forces in the Caribbean to fight drug trafficking.
