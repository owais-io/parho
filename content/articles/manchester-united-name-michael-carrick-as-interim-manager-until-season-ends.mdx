Michael Carrick has been named Manchester United's interim manager until the season ends. He brings Steve Holland, Jonathan Woodgate, Travis Binnion and Jonny Evans to his staff. Carrick won the role over former favourite Ole Gunnar Solskjaer after interviews. He must finish the league in a European spot in the final 17 matches. His first game is a showdown with local rivals Manchester City at Old Trafford.
