Oxfam’s new report shows Australian billionaires grew their wealth by almost $600,000 a day last year, adding more than $10.5 billion in total. That equals the yearly income of over 2,000 average Australians. The report urges the government to end tax breaks such as negative gearing and capital‑gain discounts, and to introduce a wealth tax on the richest 0.5 %. It also highlights that 3.7 million people live in poverty, with one‑in‑three families struggling to buy food.
