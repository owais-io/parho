The National Women’s Soccer League saw two top young midfielders swap teams in huge trades. Croix Bethune left Washington Spirit for Kansas City Current for $1 million, and Claire Hutton moved from Kansas City to Bay FC for $1.1 million. These are the third‑largest intra‑league deals ever. Both are rising stars who have played for the U.S. national team and earned Best‑XI honors, and the moves help each club adjust before the new season.
