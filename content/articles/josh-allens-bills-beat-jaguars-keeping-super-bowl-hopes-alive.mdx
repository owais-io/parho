In a close wild‑card game, Buffalo beat Jacksonville 27‑24. Quarterback Josh Allen threw for 273 yards, 1 TD, and rushed for 33 yards and 2 TDs—making him the first QB to complete over 80% of his passes and score multiple rushing TDs in a playoff game. Despite a foot injury and a concussion, Allen carried the team past a strong Jaguars defense and keeps the Bills' Super Bowl hopes alive.
