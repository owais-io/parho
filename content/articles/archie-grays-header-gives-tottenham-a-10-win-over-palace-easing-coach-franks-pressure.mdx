Tottenham beat Crystal Palace 1‑0 thanks to a header from 19‑year‑old Archie Gray, his first goal for the club. The win gives manager Thomas Frank some relief after a slow start to the season. The only goal came after two of Richarlison’s shots were ruled offside, and Palace struggled to create chances. Tottenham’s victory is their first away win since November and keeps them just a point behind Palace in the standings.
