An unknown explosion hit a popular bar called Le Constellation in Crans‑Montana, a luxury ski resort, around 1:30 am as people celebrated New Year. The blast caused a fire that killed dozens and injured about 100. Police have closed the area and set a no‑fly zone while hospitals admit patients. The cause of the explosion is still unclear and an investigation is underway.
