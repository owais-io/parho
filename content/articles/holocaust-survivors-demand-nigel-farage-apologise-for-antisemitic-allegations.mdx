A group of Holocaust survivors are asking Nigel Farage to admit and apologise for alleged antisemitic remarks he made while at Dulwich College. They say he said things like “Hitler was right” and “gas them.” Farage has denied the claims, saying he never meant harm. The survivors want honesty and an apology, arguing hateful words must never be dismissed as playground banter. They also highlight other students who said he used racist language.
