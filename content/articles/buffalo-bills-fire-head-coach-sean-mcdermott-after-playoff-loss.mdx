After a close overtime loss to the Denver Broncos in the playoffs, the Buffalo Bills decided to fire head coach Sean McDermott. The team has made the playoffs every year since he joined in 2017, but never reached a Super Bowl. Bills owner Terry Pegula said the club needs a new direction and has promoted general manager Brandon Beane to president of football operations to start the coaching search.
