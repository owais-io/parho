UN experts wrote a 20‑page letter to the UK and France, saying their ‘one‑in, one‑out’ asylum swap could violate international human‑rights law. The letter cites abuses in detention, torture survivors being held, and people denied help. It gives the governments 60 days to reply and asks them to stop the deal. The UK says the pilot is legal, while refugees and human‑rights groups say the policy endangers vulnerable people.
