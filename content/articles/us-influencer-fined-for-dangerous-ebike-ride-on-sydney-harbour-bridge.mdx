New South Wales police fined US influencer Sur Ronster, 26, after a group of about 40 e‑bike riders illegally rode across Sydney Harbour Bridge, a move seen as dangerous. Police did not pursue riders immediately but later issued Ronster two traffic infringement notices, each carrying a $562 fine and three demerit points. The incident fuels calls for tighter e‑bike regulation amid rising injuries.
