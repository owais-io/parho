Nick Kyrgios played at the Kooyong Classic in Melbourne, a historic grass‑court warm‑up for the Australian Open. He faced Chinese player Zhang Zhizhen, and after a lively three‑set match he won 6‑3, 4‑6, 11‑9. The crowd loved his flair and the venue’s old‑school charm. Kyrgios said the game reminded him why he loves tennis, and he’ll still compete in doubles at the Australian Open and other events.
