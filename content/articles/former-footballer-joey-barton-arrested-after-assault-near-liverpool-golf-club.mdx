Former footballer Joey Barton was arrested after an assault outside a golf club near Liverpool. Police said he and another man were detained for suspected grievous bodily harm. A 50‑year‑old victim suffered facial and rib injuries and was taken to hospital. Officers searched the area and used sniffer dogs. Barton, who played for clubs like Manchester City and Newcastle, has been managing clubs since 2018.
