Brits are swapping beef for pork to save money. Beef prices are up 27%, so many people are buying pork cuts that used to be beef, like T‑bone and rib‑eye. Sales of pork mince and fillets have risen, and recipe searches for pork dishes have doubled. Retailers note that pork costs a fraction of beef, and shoppers are also turning to other cheap proteins like chicken and fish.
