Queensland’s health minister said the state will keep banning puberty blockers and hormone treatment for new teens with gender dysphoria until 2031, while a UK clinical trial runs. The ban was re‑imposed after a Supreme Court ruling and will stay in place until the trial finishes. The decision follows a review that recommends individualized care, but the government wants a pause to gather more evidence. The move has sparked worry among parents, doctors, and LGBTQ+ groups.
