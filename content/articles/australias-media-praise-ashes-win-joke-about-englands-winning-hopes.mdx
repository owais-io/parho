Australian newspapers laughed at the idea of England winning the Ashes, calling England’s “Bazball” attack style dead. They also celebrated Australia’s surprise win, beating England in just 11 days. The papers gave little front‑page space to the match because a Bondi terror attack was in the news. Despite the focus on jokes, the coverage praised the Aussie team’s quick comeback and the historic record it tied.
