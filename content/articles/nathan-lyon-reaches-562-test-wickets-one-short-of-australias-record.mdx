Nathan Lyon has taken his 562nd Test wicket, putting him just one behind Glenn McGrath’s all‑time Australian record of 563. After being left out of a few games post‑Grenada, he returned for the Ashes in Perth and then the England series. In England he struck early, taking two wickets in his first over, and was part of a strong bowling attack that dominated the visitors. The heat and crowd added to a dramatic weekend.
