Actress Nicola Coughlan says she hates being talked about for her body. She says the whole “body positivity” trend feels like another pressure and that constant focus on women’s looks takes time away from work and life. The writer agrees and says we should aim for “body neutrality”—respecting our bodies without obsessing over them. The piece argues the real freedom comes from seeing the body as a tool, not a standard.
