For over a year, Birmingham’s bin workers have walked off the job, stopping most trash pickup. Unite started the strike in January 2025 to fight pay cuts and job changes that could hurt safety and cost workers £8,000 a year. Negotiations stalled in July when the council said it could not offer more. The strike has caused piles of garbage, health risks and a major city incident. Unite says it could continue until September unless a deal is made.
