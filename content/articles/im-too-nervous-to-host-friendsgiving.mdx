The writer says she is a bad host, anxious about guests and order. She explains why she won’t host Friendsgiving: she doesn’t want people in her house, she worries about everything from table layout to guests touching things. She compares herself to the stereotypical high‑energy host who seems relaxed but actually has to manage everything. She feels she’ll never enjoy her own parties and thinks staying out is the best way to be a good friend.
