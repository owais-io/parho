The BBC’s Sports Personality of the Year will let the public choose a team of the year. Three teams are up: England’s Lionesses who won the Euros, the Red Roses who won the rugby World Cup, and Europe’s Ryder Cup side that beat the US. Voting is open until Dec. 18, when the winners are announced. The Lionesses beat Spain on penalties, the Red Roses beat Canada 33‑13, and Europe won 15‑13.
