On Saturday, the United States and Israel launched a surprise air strike on Iran, targeting the country’s leadership. The attack reportedly killed Supreme Leader Ayatollah Khamenei, a claim later confirmed by Iranian state media. Iran responded with a wave of missiles aimed at Israel and U.S. bases, sparking fears of a wider war. Protesters gathered across the U.S., while critics said the strikes were carried out without congressional approval.
