European leaders slammed President Donald Trump’s push to take Greenland, calling it colonialism and a risk to trade ties. They warned he could use tariffs to pressure Denmark and other EU countries, while the EU could retaliate with its own trade measures. The leaders also urged calm and said a military takeover was unlikely. Trump will speak at Davos, but Europe is ready to defend its rules‑based order.
