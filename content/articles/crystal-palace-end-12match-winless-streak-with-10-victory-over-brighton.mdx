Crystal Palace surprised Brighton with a 1‑0 win, thanks to Ismaila Sarr’s late goal. The victory ends Palace’s 12‑match stretch without a league win and puts them back in the hunt for European spots. Brighton struggled all night, with new signings Evann Guessand and Jørgen Strand Larsen failing to make a big impact. The win also hurts Brighton’s manager, Fabian Hürzeler, who faces pressure from fans and a possible future relegation fight.
