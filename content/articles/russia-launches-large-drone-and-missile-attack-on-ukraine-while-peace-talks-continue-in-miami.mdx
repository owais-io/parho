Russia used over 650 drones and 51 missiles to hit Ukraine at night, striking power plants and towns even far from the front. Three people were hurt and sirens rang in eastern Poland. The attack comes as U.S. and Ukrainian officials keep negotiating in Miami, hoping to end the war. Trump wants a deal, but Ukraine refuses to give up land and Russia hasn't agreed to his terms yet.
