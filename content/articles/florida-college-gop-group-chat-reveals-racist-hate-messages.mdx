A WhatsApp chat among Florida International University conservative students was leaked, revealing over 400 uses of the N‑word and hateful language about Black, Jewish and LGBTQ people. The messages, written by student leaders and party officials, prompted outcry from local officials and the Republican Party, with several calls for resignations and expulsion. The incident highlights how extremist views can spread in student political groups.
