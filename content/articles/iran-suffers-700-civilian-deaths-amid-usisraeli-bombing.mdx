Since the US‑Israeli strikes on Iran began, more than 700 civilians, including 176 children, have been killed, rights groups say. Iran’s internet is almost shut down, making it hard to confirm numbers. The government sends texts warning citizens that moving in public could be seen as aiding the enemy, heightening fear. Residents worry about bombs and regime crack‑downs as they try to stay safe.
