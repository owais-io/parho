A 16‑year‑old boy was arrested after a girl was stabbed at a Norwich school. The attack happened at Thorpe St Andrew school, where students were put in lockdown and one student was taken to hospital with minor injuries. Police say the case isn’t connected to last week’s antisemitic chanting. The boy is being questioned at Wymondham police centre as authorities investigate the incident.
