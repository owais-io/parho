In Minneapolis, federal ICE agents killed a US citizen for the second time in three weeks, sparking anger and protests. Residents marched, put up “ICE OUT” signs, and neighbors helped each other with food and supplies as people fear being detained. The governor and local leaders call for an investigation and say the federal government’s actions are unacceptable. Videos show agents using tear gas and attacking witnesses, raising concern about safety and civil rights.
