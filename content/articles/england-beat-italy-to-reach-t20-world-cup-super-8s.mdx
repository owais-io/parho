England's cricket team won a tense match against Italy in Kolkata, thanks to a quick 60 from Ben Manenti and a solid bowling effort. The win puts them into the T20 World Cup Super 8s, even though they had to fight hard to secure it. Italy fought back with a big partnership, but England’s bowlers finished them off. The victory shows England can handle pressure, but they’ll need more smooth play to reach the finals.
