Apple is fighting a UK court decision that said it overcharged millions of customers for apps and in‑app purchases. The judge said Apple took up to 30% commission, far above the 17.5% it should have taken. If the appeal fails, every UK user who bought through the App Store between 2015 and 2024 could get a refund. The case is part of a wave of similar lawsuits against Apple and Google, with total damages over £6 billion.
