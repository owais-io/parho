The Raiders put star tight end Brock Bowers and edge rusher Maxx Crosby on injured reserve before the final two games, sparking rumors they’re trying to lose for a better draft position. Both were dealing with knee injuries, and the Giants beat the Raiders, giving Las Vegas the top overall pick. Fans accuse the team of tanking and criticize weak roster and coaching, but the club denies any plan to lose intentionally.
