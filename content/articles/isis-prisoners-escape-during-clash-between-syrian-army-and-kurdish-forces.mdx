Dozens of ISIS detainees escaped from a jail in Shaddadi, Syria, during fighting between the Syrian army and the Kurdish‑led Syrian Democratic Forces. The army said it lost control of the prison and imposed a curfew on the town. The escape has put the new Syrian government’s plans to take over the Kurdish‑run camps in doubt, and raises concerns about the safety of foreign citizens still held there.
