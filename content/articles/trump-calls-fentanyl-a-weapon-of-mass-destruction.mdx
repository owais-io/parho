Trump signed an order calling fentanyl a weapon of mass destruction. He told the Pentagon and justice department to step up fighting drug makers and smugglers. The move is part of a broader effort to use military power against drug cartels. Experts say fentanyl isn’t used like a chemical weapon and there’s little proof the drug is being weaponized. Meanwhile, the administration has cut funding for addiction treatment while blaming fentanyl on immigrants.
