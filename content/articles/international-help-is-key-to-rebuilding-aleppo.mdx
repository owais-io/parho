The article points out that rebuilding Aleppo isn’t just a local effort. Since 2018 the Aga Khan Trust for Culture has fixed eight parts of the old souk, and UN bodies like UNESCO and UN‑Habitat are also restoring historic sites. Their work has encouraged local traders to repair their own shops. The story shows how outside support gives hope for the city’s future.
