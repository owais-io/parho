Labor MPs are upset that the Australian government is taking too long to ban gambling ads, especially around live sports. They want Communications Minister Anika Wells to act on a report that recommends a phased ban on all online gambling advertising. The government has made some changes, like a self‑exclusion register and a credit‑card ban, but MPs say the rest of the reforms are still missing.
