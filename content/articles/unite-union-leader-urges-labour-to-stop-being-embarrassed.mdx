Unite union head Sharon Graham says Labour needs to act faster for workers before 2026. She says the party’s current plans, like low‑level cuts and hidden taxes, won’t help people and could hurt the party itself. Graham added that changing leaders – Starmer, Rayner, Burnham, Streeting or Miliband – won’t fix the problems unless new policies are introduced. She called on Labour to stop being ashamed of being the workers’ voice.
