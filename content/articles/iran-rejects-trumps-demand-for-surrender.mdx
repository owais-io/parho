Iran’s president Masoud Pezeshkian said the country would never give up, calling Trump’s demand for unconditional surrender a “dream.” He also apologized to nearby Gulf states for recent missile and drone attacks, though Iranian forces kept striking. The comments come amid a week‑long war that began after US and Israeli airstrikes killed Iran’s top leader. The fighting has spread to Lebanon and threatens global oil supplies.
