Starmer has said the UK should not let the US use British bases for strikes on Iran. Labour MPs praise his independent stance, while Nigel Farage and Kemi Badenoch say he didn’t support the US enough. A YouGov poll shows most Britons oppose the military action. The decision has split opinions, with some Conservatives seeing it as a mistake and others supporting it. The story shows how the move reflects Starmer’s foreign‑policy approach and its political fallout.
