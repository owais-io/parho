David Stern, an aide to Prince Andrew, helped Jeffrey Epstein plan trips to London, keep the prince in the loop, and even discuss business ideas. Court documents show Stern sent messages, arranged meetings, and stayed in touch with Epstein until his 2019 arrest. The aide also managed details like formal dress codes and lunches. The story reveals how a royal staffer kept a secret friendship with a convicted sex offender, despite public scrutiny.
