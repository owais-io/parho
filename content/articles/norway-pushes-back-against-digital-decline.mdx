A Norwegian consumer watchdog made a funny video that shows a man turning good things into bad ones. It was part of a campaign to stop digital services from getting worse, a problem the council calls “enshittification.” The video, joined by over 70 groups in Europe and the US, asked governments to give people more power to control, repair, and switch services. They want stronger laws, more competition, and easier choice for users.
