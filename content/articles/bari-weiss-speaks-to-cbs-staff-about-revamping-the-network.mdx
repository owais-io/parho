Bari Weiss, the new editor‑in‑chief at CBS, told staff that she knows they’re skeptical and that she will earn their trust. She outlined a plan to modernise CBS News, adding 18 new on‑air contributors from politics, science and culture. Weiss said the network must reach centre‑leaning viewers and invest in daily investigative pieces. She also addressed a past 60 Minutes scheduling mishap and shared staff worries about job security. Many welcomed her vision.
