Labour says it will give millions of young people jobs. New plans give 18‑24‑year‑olds subsidised roles for six months and offer employers money to hire them. The government will also change the apprenticeship fund to focus on new starters and give small firms a £2,000 grant for each new apprentice. Overall, Labour wants to add 200,000 jobs and set up 360 youth job centres to help the unemployed.
