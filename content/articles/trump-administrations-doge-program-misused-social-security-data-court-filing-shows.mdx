A court filing says employees in the Trump‑era Doge program illegally shared Americans’ Social Security information with a political group. The group aimed to find voter‑fraud evidence and challenge election results. The SSA learned of the data swap in an unrelated review and is now investigating possible violations of the Hatch Act. Lawmakers want the people involved prosecuted and are demanding full accountability.
