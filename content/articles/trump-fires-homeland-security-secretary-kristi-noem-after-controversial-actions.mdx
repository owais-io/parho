Trump fired DHS secretary Kristi Noem after a string of controversies. Noem’s hard‑line raids in cities like Minneapolis, her costly $200‑million ad campaign, and a lawsuit over a luxury jet were all under scrutiny. Republicans criticized her for exaggerating crimes and for personal scandals. Trump said she was “expendable” and moved her to a new post while appointing Markwayne Mullin as DHS chief.
