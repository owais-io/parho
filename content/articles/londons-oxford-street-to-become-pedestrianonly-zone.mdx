London mayor Sadiq Khan has given the official green light to turn the busy shopping strip Oxford Street into a pedestrian‑only zone. The plan will remove buses, taxis, bikes, e‑scooters and pedicabs from the main stretch this summer, with work running until late 2027. The move aims to boost retail, nightlife and public safety. While most retailers support it, taxi drivers worry about extra traffic on nearby roads.
