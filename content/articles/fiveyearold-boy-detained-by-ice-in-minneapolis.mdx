In Minneapolis, ICE agents detained a 5‑year‑old named Liam after his father, an Ecuadorian asylum seeker, was taken away. Photos of Liam next to masked ICE officers sparked outrage. The school district says children are being used to catch parents, and the situation has shaken the community. DHS insists Liam is in custody for his safety, while critics argue ICE is targeting kids in the name of immigration enforcement.
