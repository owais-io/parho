Australia has hit record heat, with temperatures up to 50°C in small towns and record highs in cities and ski fields. The heat stayed hot all night, raising the risk of heat‑stroke and hospital visits. Health officials warn people to stay cool, especially after recent bushfires that worsened air quality. Sporting events like the Australian Open went on, but some races were changed to avoid extreme heat.
