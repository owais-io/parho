New York’s new mayor, Zohran Mamdani, was cheering on Arsenal when former striker Ian Wright sent a video on his show. His excited reaction highlights how soccer is no longer a niche sport in America. Premier League fans grow every year, clubs have U.S. fan groups, and the game mixes with pop‑culture, politics and diaspora communities. Mamdani’s support shows soccer is becoming a shared, cross‑class, multicultural part of American life.
