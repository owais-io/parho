The war is far away but could hit UK finances through higher energy prices and shifting interest rates. The Bank of England may raise rates to about 4.5 % if energy costs stay high, squeezing mortgage rates. Energy bills could climb 10 % next summer, adding around £500 to a household. Stock markets dip a bit, but staying invested usually helps long‑term. Overall, costs could rise, but the exact effect is still uncertain.
