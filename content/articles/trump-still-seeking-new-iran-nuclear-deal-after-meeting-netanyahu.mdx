After a three‑hour meeting with Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, former President Donald Trump said he is still working on a new nuclear deal with Iran. No deal was reached, but Trump said negotiations must continue. Meanwhile the U.S. has moved more forces into the Middle East and may send an aircraft carrier. Israel wants Iran to limit missiles and cut support for groups like Hamas. Trump warned that if Iran refuses, more strikes could happen.
