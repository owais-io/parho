An email from new U.S. Africa bureau head Nick Checker told diplomats to highlight America’s generosity in fighting HIV and famine, even as aid to Africa is cut. Checker wants to show the U.S. is the biggest donor and shift focus to trade and investment. Critics say the memo is offensive and ignores reality. Meanwhile, the State Department plans to close USAID, a move many call illegal.
