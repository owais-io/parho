New documents show Prince Andrew went to a small dinner at Jeffrey Epstein’s New York house in December 2010, just months after Epstein was released from jail. The emails, sent by publicist Peggy Siegal, list high‑profile guests, including Woody Allen, Katie Couric and George Stephanopoulos. The dinner was meant to be “casual” and “fun,” but it raises questions about Andrew’s ties to the disgraced financier.
