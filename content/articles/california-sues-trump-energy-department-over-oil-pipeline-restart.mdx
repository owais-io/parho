California’s attorney general, Rob Bonta, filed a lawsuit against the U.S. Energy Department to stop the restart of the Sable Offshore oil pipeline. The pipeline, which links the Santa Ynez platform to California refineries, was shut after a 2015 spill. Energy Secretary Chris Wright used the Defense Production Act to revive it, but Bonta says it violates state law and court orders. The case pits Trump’s fossil‑fuel push against California’s climate agenda.
