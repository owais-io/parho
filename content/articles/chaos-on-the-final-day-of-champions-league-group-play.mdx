The last day of the Champions League group stage was wild. Benfica stunned Real Madrid 3‑2, then sent their keeper up for a corner and scored a header to win the tie. Real’s Mourinho beat Madrid for the first time, while Liverpool, Chelsea, Arsenal and Tottenham secured top‑eight spots. Portugal’s Sporting Lisbon, Spain’s Inter, and other clubs like Olympiakos and Qarabag also made the cut, setting the scene for the knockout rounds.
