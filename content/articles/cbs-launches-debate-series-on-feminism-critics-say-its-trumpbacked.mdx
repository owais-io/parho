CBS News and Bari Weiss’s new show “Things That Matter” will ask tough questions, like whether feminism is helping women. The series is funded by Bank of America and is seen by many as a way for Trump allies and Weiss to push their views. Critics argue the show is less about real debate and more about promoting a conservative agenda. The article also points out how billionaire owners like the Ellisons are reshaping U.S. media.
