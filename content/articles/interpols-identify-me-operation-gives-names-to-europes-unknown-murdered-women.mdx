In Europe, many women who were murdered or died mysteriously were never identified. Interpol launched “Operation Identify Me” to share details of these cold cases with the public and other police forces. The program helped families, like that of Rita Roberts, find out who their missing loved ones were. The work shows how cross‑border cooperation and DNA data sharing can give dignity to victims who had been forgotten.
