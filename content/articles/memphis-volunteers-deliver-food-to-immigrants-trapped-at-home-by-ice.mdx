When ICE agents started raiding homes in Memphis, many families hid inside, scared to go out. Local activists in Indivisible Memphis decided to help before the raids hit. They created the Immigrant Pantry, a volunteer‑run food delivery service. About 130 residents now drive groceries, from canned goods to fresh produce and hygiene items, straight to those who can’t leave their houses. The pantry has grown fast, with 200 people on the waiting list.
