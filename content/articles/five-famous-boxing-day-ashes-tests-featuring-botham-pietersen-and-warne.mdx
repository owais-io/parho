On Boxing Day in Melbourne, five famous Ashes Tests have happened. In 2017, Tom Curran nearly took David Warner’s wicket but was ruled out for over‑stepping, while Warner scored a century before being dismissed. In 2013, Kevin Pietersen made a patient 67, but Australia won. In 2010, Chris Tremlett broke a record, taking four early wickets. In 2006, Shane Warne claimed his 702nd wicket. In 1986, Ian Botham dismissed five Australians, swinging the game for England.
