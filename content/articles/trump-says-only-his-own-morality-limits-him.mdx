Trump says only his own morality limits him, dismissing international law as irrelevant. In a New York Times interview, he said no outside rule can stop him, only his conscience. The comment comes amid protests after ICE killed a woman in Minneapolis and a debate over a U.S. plan to take control of Greenland. He also briefly touched on ownership and global politics, but the main message was that his power is bound only by his own sense of right and wrong.
