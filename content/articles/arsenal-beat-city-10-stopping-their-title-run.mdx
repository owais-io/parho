Arsenal won 1‑0 at the Emirates against Manchester City thanks to a goal by Olivia Smith. The result doesn’t threaten City’s lead in the Women’s Super League, but it gives Arsenal a mental boost and keeps their hopes of Champions League spots alive. Arsenal’s defence stayed solid, and the team showed they’re on a positive rhythm after a rest period. City had chances but couldn’t score.
