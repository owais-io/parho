Prime Minister Anthony Albanese delivered a heartfelt eulogy for former Labor minister Graham Richardson, who died at 84. The state funeral at St James’ church in Sydney highlighted Richardson’s role as a powerful party broker, his environmental work saving rainforests, and his efforts on Aboriginal health. Despite controversies over alleged corruption and past scandals, many politicians from all sides attended. Critics say giving a state funeral was inappropriate, but Labour chose to forgive and remember his achievements.
