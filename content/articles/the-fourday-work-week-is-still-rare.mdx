After the Covid lockdowns, many people wondered if a four‑day work week could make life easier. Some companies tried it and said workers felt happier and stayed more productive. But others, like Bupa and Unilever, said the idea was too rigid and they stopped the trials. In Australia, city councils and unions are still fighting over whether to try it, and the results are mixed. The idea is still mostly an experiment.
