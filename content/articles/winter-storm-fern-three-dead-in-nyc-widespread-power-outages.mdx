Winter Storm Fern slammed the U.S. from the south to the Northeast, bringing record snowfall, sub‑freezing temperatures and big power cuts. At least 18 states declared emergencies and the National Guard is helping. Three people died in New York City when the cold snapped them. Roughly 135,000 homes lost power, especially in Texas and Louisiana, and over 11,000 flights were cancelled. Travelers are urged to stay home and stay warm.
