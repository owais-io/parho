Keir Starmer has handed the Metropolitan Police a dossier on former minister Peter Mandelson and is moving to strip him of his peerage. The dossier says Mandelson sent market‑sensitive information to Jeffrey Epstein during the 2008 financial crisis. Starmer says the leaks damage public trust and wants quick action to remove Mandelson’s title, a first time since 1917. The move signals a push to hold peers accountable.
