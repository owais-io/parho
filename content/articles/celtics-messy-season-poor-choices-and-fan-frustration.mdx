Celtic’s season has been a mess. The club’s board acted cheaply, firing coach Brendan Rodgers and later Martin O’Neill, then hiring Wilfried Nancy in a rush. Fans were angry at the AGM drama, the board’s rude tone, and the way money was spent. While St Mirren won a cup, Celtic’s poor decisions caused the loss. The article criticises the club’s leadership and the board’s lack of respect for supporters.
