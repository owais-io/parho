Ski jumpers in the Winter Olympics have been accused of injecting their penises with substances to cheat. German newspaper Bild says athletes may use hyaluronic acid or clay to make their suits looser. WADA’s president said he would investigate. Earlier, Norwegian jumpers had suit‑size fraud punished. No evidence yet, but WADA will look into it if it’s found to be doping. The story highlights ongoing cheating concerns.
