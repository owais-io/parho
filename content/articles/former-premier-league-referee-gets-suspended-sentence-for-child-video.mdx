A former Premier League referee, David Coote, was found guilty of possessing a sexual video of a 15‑year‑old boy. The judge gave him a suspended prison term, a 10‑year sexual‑harm prevention order, and 150 hours of unpaid work. Coote had been fired last year after a separate video went viral. His lawyer cited mental‑health struggles, but the court focused on the seriousness of the abuse.
