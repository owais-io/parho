Texas congressman Tony Gonzales admitted in a radio interview that he had an affair with his former aide, Regina Santos‑Aviles, who died by suicide last year. He says he has taken responsibility, stayed married, and will not resign. The House Ethics Committee is investigating his conduct, while Santos‑Aviles’s family claims he tried to demand money. Gonzales denies any role in her death.
