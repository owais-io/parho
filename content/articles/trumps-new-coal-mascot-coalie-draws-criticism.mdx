Trump’s administration has introduced a cartoon coal mascot called “Coalie” to cheer up coal mining. The picture, made with AI and shared by Interior Secretary Doug Burgum, shows the coal lump in a helmet and boots saying “Mine, Baby, Mine!”. Climate groups slammed the move, saying it glamorises the dirtiest fossil fuel and ignores its pollution and health harms. The agency says the image is only an educational tool.
