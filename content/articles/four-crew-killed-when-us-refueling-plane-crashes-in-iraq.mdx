On Thursday, a US military refueling plane crashed in western Iraq, killing four of the six crew members. The remaining two survivors are still being searched for by rescue teams. Officials say the crash was caused by a mishap involving another aircraft, not by enemy fire. The US military is investigating the cause and will release details soon.
