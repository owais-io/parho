Brighton’s poor run in the Premier League has left fans upset. Since winning just one of four games last season, the team has struggled, losing to Fulham and Palace. The 32‑year‑old coach, the youngest manager in the league, is under fire after a 1‑0 loss to Palace where a substitution decision sparked boos. Brighton spent over £200 million on new players, but results haven’t improved, and the club may change the manager before the season ends.
