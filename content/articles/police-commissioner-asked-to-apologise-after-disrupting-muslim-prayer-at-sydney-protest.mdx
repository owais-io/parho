In Sydney, police officers moved a group of Muslim worshippers during a protest against the Israeli president's visit. Muslim leaders, including the Australian National Imams Council and the Australian Federation of Islamic Councils, want the NSW police commissioner to issue a public apology. The commissioner says the police were clearing a crowd, but the Muslim community says the actions were aggressive and uncalled for. The incident sparked calls for a public inquiry.
