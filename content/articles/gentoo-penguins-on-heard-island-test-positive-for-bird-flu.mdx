Gentoo penguins on Heard Island have tested positive for the H5N1 bird flu, the first time birds in an Australian territory have the virus. Scientists found the virus in seals and penguins after earlier tests on seals. The government says Australia is still free of the disease, but it has invested $100 million to boost readiness. The spread could threaten other unique species.
