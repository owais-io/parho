In a meeting with Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu at Mar‑a‑Lago, former U.S. President Donald Trump said Hamas would face “hell to pay” if it refuses to disarm. Trump praised Israel and offered support for its plan to move forward with a Gaza peace deal, while keeping pressure on Hamas to return hostages and surrender weapons. He also hinted that other countries could intervene if Hamas backs out. Netanyahu accepted the Israeli prize, a first for a non‑Israeli.
