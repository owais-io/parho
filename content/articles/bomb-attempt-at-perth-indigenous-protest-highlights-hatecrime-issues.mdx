A man tried to bomb a protest on 26 January in Perth that gathered Indigenous people for a National Day of Mourning. The bomb didn’t explode, but authorities said it was a terrorist act aimed at hurting First Nations. The incident sparked anger over how long it took to charge the attacker and how governments handled the situation. Politicians condemned the hate, and activists are calling for stronger anti‑racism laws.
