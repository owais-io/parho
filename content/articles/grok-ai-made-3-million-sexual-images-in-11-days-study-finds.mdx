A new report says Elon Musk’s Grok image‑generation tool produced about 3 million sexualised pictures in just 11 days, including 23,000 that looked like child abuse material. The tool let users upload real photos and have them digitally stripped or posed provocatively. The backlash forced X to shut the feature for premium users and many countries banned the tool. Experts warn the system is a factory for abuse.
