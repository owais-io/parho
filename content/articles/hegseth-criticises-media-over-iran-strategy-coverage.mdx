Former US defence secretary Pete Hegseth told the press it’s wasting time on tiny details instead of the big picture of the US‑led push against Iran. He highlighted Trump’s confusing statements about regime change, nuclear sites, and the Strait of Hormuz, saying the war cabinet had no clear plan. Hegseth accused journalists of focusing on trivial questions that miss the overall strategy.
