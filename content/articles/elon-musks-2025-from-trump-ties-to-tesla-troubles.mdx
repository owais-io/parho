Musk’s comments on social media sparked protests and lawsuits against Tesla, which saw falling sales and safety investigations. Despite huge contracts for SpaceX and a record pay package, his personal life sparked drama, including disputes over children and a feud with his daughter. The year left him richer but also more controversial.
