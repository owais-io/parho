PhotobookShop, a Victorian online retailer, was hit with two ACCC infringement notices totalling $39,600 after it paid influencers to review its products without telling viewers it was paid. The company used free gifts worth $50‑$400 on 107 occasions and even edited videos to hide negative comments. This first‑of‑its‑kind penalty highlights the law that all paid online content must be clearly disclosed.
