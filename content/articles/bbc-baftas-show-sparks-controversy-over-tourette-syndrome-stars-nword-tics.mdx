BBC’s Baftas broadcast showed John Davidson, who has Tourette syndrome, utter the N‑word during a live award show. The BBC didn’t cut the clip, sparking outrage. Davidson said the word came from an involuntary tic, not a conscious choice, and he left early, upset by the distress it caused. The Guardian asks people with TS, especially those who coprolalia, to share their experiences and thoughts on how the public should react.
