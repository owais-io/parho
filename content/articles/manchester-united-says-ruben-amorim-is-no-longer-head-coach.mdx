Ruben Amorim has been fired from Manchester United, ending his role as head coach after a heated post‑match incident at Leeds. The club confirmed he is no longer part of the team and that Darren Fletcher will lead the squad for the next game. United, sitting sixth in the league, will look for a long‑term replacement, with names like Maresca, Glasner, and Southgate mentioned. The move shows the club’s impatience with results.
