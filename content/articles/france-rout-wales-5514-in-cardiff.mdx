In a Cardiff game, France beat Wales 55‑14. The French team, full of young talent, scored eight tries in the first hour. Wales managed only a single try and struggled against France’s fast play and good set pieces. The match showed France’s strong line and Wales’ defensive problems. It was a big disappointment for Wales and a strong start for France as they look for a grand slam.
