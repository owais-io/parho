Bank of England will change the pictures on the £5, £10, £20 and £50 notes to show wildlife instead of politicians. A panel of wildlife experts and the public will choose the animals. Suggested faces include a red fox on the £5, a common toad on the £10, a beaver on the £20 and a swift on the £50. The move also helps fight counter‑feiting and raises awareness of Britain’s native species.
