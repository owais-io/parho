The band performed at London's Roundhouse and projected a video that put a swastika inside a Star of David over the heads of Netanyahu and Trump, ending with a claim that the government is complicit in genocide. Critics called the image antisemitic; the venue apologized. Primal Scream said it was artistic debate, but a Jewish security group warned it could stir hatred and reported it to police. The incident drew strong backlash from the Jewish community.
