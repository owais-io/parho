In a secretly recorded call, Senator Ted Cruz told former President Donald Trump that rising prices could cost the GOP a “bloodbath” in the 2026 midterm elections. Cruz warned that if people’s 401(k)s fall 30% and grocery costs jump 10‑20%, the Democrats could win both the House and Senate and Trump might face impeachment. Trump’s angry reply was recorded as “fuck you, Ted.”
