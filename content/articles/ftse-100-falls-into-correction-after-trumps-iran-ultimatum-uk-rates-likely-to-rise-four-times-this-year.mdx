UK stocks fell sharply and the FTSE 100 has slipped into a correction as fears grow over Trump’s ultimatum to Iran. Bond yields are at a 17‑year high, and traders expect the Bank of England to hike rates four times this year. The Middle East flare‑up has pushed energy and metal prices up, hurting UK growth and making investors nervous about inflation and the economy.
