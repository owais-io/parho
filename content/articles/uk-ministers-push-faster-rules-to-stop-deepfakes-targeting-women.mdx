Liz Kendall, Britain’s tech secretary, said the government must keep up with rapid tech changes, especially deepfakes that threaten women and girls. She wants yearly reviews of online‑safety rules and is pushing a ban on social media for people under 16. Kendall also plans to bring AI chatbots under the Online Safety Act after X’s Grok bot spread illegal images. Parliament will debate these measures next week.
