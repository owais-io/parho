Unions, faith groups, and community leaders in Minnesota plan a full‑day economic blackout on Friday. They will refuse to work, shop, or go to school to protest the Trump administration’s deployment of around 3,000 ICE agents and the killing of 37‑year‑old Renee Good by an ICE officer. The protest aims to pressure local businesses and the state to end the violence and the federal crackdown on immigrants.
