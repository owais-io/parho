Trump said in a Wall Street Journal interview that he takes more aspirin than doctors recommend and that it makes him bruise easily, but he insists his health is perfect. He explained he thinks the extra aspirin keeps his blood thin and he’s been doing it for 25 years. Trump also talked about his work schedule, his dislike of exercise other than golf, and his frustration with staff’s health advice. He added that a recent scan showed he was fine.
