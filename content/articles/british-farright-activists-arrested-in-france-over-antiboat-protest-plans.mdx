French police arrested two British far‑right activists in Calais while they livestreamed hateful comments about migrants and a planned protest called Operation Overlord. The men were filmed cutting up small boats and encouraging violence. They were taken into custody for inciting hatred and for being part of a group that could prepare violent acts. This is the first time British far‑right activists have been arrested in France for these offences.
