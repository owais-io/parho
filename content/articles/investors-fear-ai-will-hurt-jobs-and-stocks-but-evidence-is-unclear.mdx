Investors worry that new AI tools could replace white‑collar jobs and squeeze company profits. This fear has helped push tech stocks lower this month. While big companies are pouring $660 billion into AI, the real benefit to the economy is still unproven. Some analysts say the hype is overblown, and that the job loss story is a stretch for now. The long‑term impact remains uncertain.
