They first met when the narrator posted a poster about a Dalek‑like set, and after a few texts they started hanging out. They fell in love, she moved into his flat, and after 18 years they live together, have cats, and share hobbies like dressing up and gardening. The story shows how a chance meeting turned into a lifelong partnership.
