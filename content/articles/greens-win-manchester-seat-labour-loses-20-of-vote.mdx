In Manchester's Gorton and Denton by‑election, the Greens won a seat that had been a Labour stronghold for over a century, slashing Labour’s vote by 20 points. The result shows voters drifting away from Labour and gives Reform UK 29% of the vote. It signals a bigger split in Britain’s politics, putting pressure on Labour’s leader to rethink its message and leadership style.
