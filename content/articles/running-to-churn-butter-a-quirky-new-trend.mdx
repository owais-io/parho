A YouTuber called Libby Cope and her partner started putting cream, salt and a zip‑lock bag into their backpacks, then running while stirring the mixture to churn butter. The trend has spread online, with other runners carrying their own “butter‑bags.” Commentators joke that this bizarre mix of exercise and dairy prep is the latest “on‑the‑go” snack craze. While some find it silly, others see it as a creative way to combine fitness and cooking.
