Lewis Hamilton has said the 2026 Formula One season will be the toughest change he's ever faced. He tested Ferrari’s new SF‑26 car at Fiorano and praised the work of the team but warned that the new hybrid engines, active aerodynamics and driver‑management system will demand a huge learning curve. Williams isn’t ready for the first test, and a dispute over engine compression could give rivals an edge.
