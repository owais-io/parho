An 11‑year‑old boy in Pennsylvania is accused of shooting his 42‑year‑old father to death after the father had taken away his Nintendo Switch. Police say the child found a gun in a safe while looking for the Switch, loaded it, and fired at his dad in the bedroom. The boy was taken into custody, denied bail, and faces homicide charges. A court hearing is set for 22 January.
