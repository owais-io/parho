The Guardian Weekly asks whether Iran will talk to the US after President Trump’s hard line. Iran says it’s open to talks and a meeting between Trump’s envoy and Iran’s foreign‑minister is set for Istanbul. The piece notes that Iran’s recent crackdown on protests has put pressure on its regime, making it more willing to negotiate. It warns that if talks break down, a wider regional war could flare up again.
