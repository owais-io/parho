North Queensland will get heavy rain for the next few days, with some places getting as much as 500 mm this week. The Bureau of Meteorology has issued flood warnings for the gulf, Townsville and Cairns areas. Rain could damage farms and flood rivers like the Diamantina and Flinders. Police are ready to help with livestock and road closures. The storm is expected to move east by Tuesday.
