Raymond Hager, a 95‑year‑old from Wichita Falls, Texas, is the world’s oldest active bus driver. He began driving city buses in 1998 after a life on the farm and as a cropduster pilot. Hager still takes his bus to work, thanks to regular health checks, and says he’s “ready to go” after a few hours of sleep. The city honored him with a proclamation and he earned a Guinness World Record for his lifelong driving career.
