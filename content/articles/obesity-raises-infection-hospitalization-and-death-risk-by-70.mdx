A new study shows people with obesity are 70% more likely to be hospitalized or die from infections such as flu, pneumonia, COVID‑19 and other illnesses. Researchers followed over 500,000 adults for more than a decade and found obesity raises serious infection risk by 70%, possibly contributing to 600,000 of the 5.4 million yearly infection deaths worldwide. The study urges better weight‑management policies and up‑to‑date vaccinations.
