Police in Melbourne arrested a 47‑year‑old man after he set fire to a rabbi’s car that had a “Happy Chanukah” billboard on its roof on Christmas Day. The car was parked in St Kilda East and was damaged, but no one was inside. The accused faces up to 12 criminal‑damage charges, theft, burglary and more. The attack follows other recent antisemitic incidents and has left the Jewish community on edge. Victoria’s premier condemned the act.
