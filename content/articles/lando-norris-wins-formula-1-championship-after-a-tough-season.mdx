Despite a rocky season full of crashes, bad races and a fight with teammate Oscar Piastri, Lando Norris kept his cool and won the Formula 1 World Championship. He started slow, struggled with the car, but the McLaren team upgraded it and Norris improved. In a tight finale at Abu Dhabi he finished third, beating Max Verstappen by just two points. Norris became the first British champion since 2020, proving grit and humour paid off.
