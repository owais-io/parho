On January 20, the Women’s March is asking Americans to leave work, school and shops at 2 pm to protest the Trump administration. More than 600 events are planned across the U.S., with participants turning to local leaders or community gatherings afterward. The walkout is a new tactic to oppose ICE raids, healthcare cuts and National Guard deployments. Researchers will measure its impact.
