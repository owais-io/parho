Human rights organisations say FIFA must step up to keep fans and locals safe during the 2026 World Cup in the U.S. They worry that immigration raids, arbitrary detentions, and racial profiling could happen at match sites. The groups sent seven specific requests to FIFA, demanding guarantees that everyone can enjoy the games without fear of unlawful enforcement. FIFA and the U.S. government have yet to reply.
