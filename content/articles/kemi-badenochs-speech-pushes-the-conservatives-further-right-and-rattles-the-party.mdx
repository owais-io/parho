The Tory leader Kemi Badenoch gave a short, stark speech that declared the Conservative Party is moving strongly to the right, dismissing moderates and calling the party’s future bleak. She urged MPs who do not share her hard‑line stance to leave, which caused confusion and concern within the party. Her comments have raised worries that the party’s appeal and poll numbers could fall sharply.
