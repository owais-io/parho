Britain’s roads will be very busy on Friday before Christmas, as people head for holiday trips, work or shopping. About 72 % of drivers plan to go, and major motorways like the M25, M6 and M1 may have jams. Rail services will slow because of maintenance, so trains might run late or be replaced by buses. UK airports will also be full, with around 460,000 passengers leaving on Friday and 3 million flying over the festive period.
