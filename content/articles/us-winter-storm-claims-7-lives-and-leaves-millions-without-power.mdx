A brutal winter storm has killed at least seven people across the U.S. and knocked out power for hundreds of thousands. Schools and roads are shut, flights canceled, and many states are still under warning. Temperatures have plunged to as low as –20°C in parts of Texas. Authorities are asking those affected to share how they're coping, but safety comes first.
