Werder Bremen’s coach Horst Steffen was dismissed early on Sunday, even after a last‑minute equaliser by 21‑year‑old Keke Topp. The goal only pulled a point from Borussia Mönchengladbach, keeping the team out of the relegation playoff, but Bremen remain 15th and 10 games winless. The board said financial limits mean a quick coaching change is needed. The club’s future coach will be chosen soon.
