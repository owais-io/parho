Morocco, the host and favourite for the Africa Cup of Nations, are under a lot of pressure. Their coach, Walid Regragui, was booed at a match, and fans worry that huge money spent on a 2030 World Cup has made the team too cautious. The quarter‑finals feature big names like Ivory Coast vs Egypt and Algeria vs Nigeria, while Morocco must stay sharp to avoid disappointing fans.
