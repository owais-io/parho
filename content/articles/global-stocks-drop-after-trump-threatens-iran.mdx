Stocks around the world fell sharply after former President Trump warned he would destroy Iran's power plants if the Strait of Hormuz stays closed. The threat pushed oil prices up, sparked an energy crisis, and made investors nervous, causing markets in Asia, Europe and the US to slip. Prices for gold fell too, and the UK government is preparing to address rising energy bills and a higher interest‑rate environment.
