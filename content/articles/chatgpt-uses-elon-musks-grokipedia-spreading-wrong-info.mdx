Researchers found that ChatGPT’s newest version, GPT‑5.2, cites Elon Musk’s AI‑generated encyclopedia Grokipedia in dozens of answers. The site is known for pushing right‑wing myths and unverified claims. When ChatGPT pulls info from Grokipedia, it repeats false details about topics like Iran’s government ties, Holocaust denial, and other disputed subjects. Experts worry this could make untrustworthy sources look credible and spread more misinformation.
