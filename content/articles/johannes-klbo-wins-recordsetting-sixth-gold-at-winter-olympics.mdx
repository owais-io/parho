Norwegian skier Johannes Klæbo finished the men’s 50km classic at the 2026 Winter Games to claim his sixth gold medal, the most in a single Winter Olympics. He beat teammate Martin Nyenget by 17.4 seconds, and this win puts him ahead of many Olympic legends. Klæbo has won every cross‑country event he entered over the last two weeks, becoming the sport’s most decorated athlete.
