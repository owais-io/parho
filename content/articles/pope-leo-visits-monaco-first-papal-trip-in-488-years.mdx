Pope Leo will fly to Monaco for a one‑day visit, the first time a pope has been there since 1538. He will meet Prince Albert, celebrate mass at the stadium, and talk about protecting life. The trip shows the pope’s focus on small European states and Catholic values, even as he keeps the Vatican’s agenda global. The event draws thousands of Catholics who line up for free tickets.
