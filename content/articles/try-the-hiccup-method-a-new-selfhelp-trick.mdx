Miski Omar jokes about a new self‑help trick called the Hiccup Method. Inspired by Mel Robbins’ “high‑five yourself” tip, it turns hiccups into a quick mindfulness practice. The method says you can deal with a hiccup by ignoring it, scaring yourself, or holding your breath for seven seconds. The piece pokes fun at a future app, retreats, and quirky merch that turn everyday hiccups into “spiritual energy,” satirising the self‑help craze.
