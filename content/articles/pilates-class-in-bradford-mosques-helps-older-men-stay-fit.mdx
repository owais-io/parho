In Bradford, a new pilates class in mosques lets mostly older men mix prayer with light exercise. Starting in one mosque, the sessions got viral on TikTok, attracting up to 30 men each week. The workouts improve strength, flexibility and mood, helping those with arthritis or heart conditions. The classes also build friendships and aim to make the mosque a community hub for people of all ages.
