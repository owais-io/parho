MP Al Carns, a Labour veteran minister, claimed about £3,000 for 17 promotional videos showing him with local businesses and firefighters. The videos were made with a PR consultant who cost £14,000. All expenses were approved by the Independent Parliamentary Standards Authority (IPSA), and Carns said they were part of constituency work, not personal promotion. IPSA has also issued a new, shorter rulebook giving MPs more claim flexibility.
