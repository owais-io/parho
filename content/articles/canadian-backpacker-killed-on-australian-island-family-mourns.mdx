Canadian backpacker Piper James, 19, was found dead on K'gari (Fraser Island) in Queensland, Australia. Her body was discovered near a pack of dingoes at the Maheno shipwreck, but police are still looking into whether she drowned or was attacked. The incident left her family, friends and the local community devastated. An autopsy will determine the cause, and authorities promise a thorough investigation.
