Emails released by the U.S. Justice Department show a model scout, Daniel Siad, worked with Jeffrey Epstein to find and send young women—some as young as 15—to Epstein in Paris and other cities. Siad claimed the women were for legitimate modeling, but prosecutors say Epstein used the industry to abuse and exploit them. The documents reveal Siad’s regular contact with Epstein, payments he received, and allegations that the scout helped arrange sexual encounters.
