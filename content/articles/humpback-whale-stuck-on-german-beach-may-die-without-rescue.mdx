A 10‑metre humpback whale is stuck on a sandbar in northern Germany’s Baltic Sea. Rescue teams, including coastguard and fire crews, are trying to help it get back to deeper water by clearing netting and making waves. The whale, likely a young male, is getting weaker each hour. If it stays stuck, experts warn it could die soon. Locals are told to stay away, and some have tried to help or criticize the rescue.
