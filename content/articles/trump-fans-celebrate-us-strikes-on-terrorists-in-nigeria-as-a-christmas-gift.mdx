On Christmas, the US launched missile attacks on Islamic State sites in Nigeria. Trump’s supporters shouted praise, saying the strikes help protect Christians and are a “Christmas gift.” Several Republican lawmakers on social media congratulated President Trump and called the hits a decisive blow against terror. The attacks follow similar US strikes in Syria earlier this week.
