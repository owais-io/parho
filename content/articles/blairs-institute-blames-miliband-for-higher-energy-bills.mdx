The Tony Blair Institute says Ed Miliband’s push for greener energy is raising bills. It criticises the government’s 2030 clean‑power target, saying it’s too costly and out of touch. The report urges the energy secretary to scrap some green plans and keep gas prices low, and warns that delaying action could hurt jobs.
