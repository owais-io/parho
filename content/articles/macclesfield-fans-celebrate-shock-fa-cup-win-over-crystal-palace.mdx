Macclesfield FC, a part‑time team of teachers, podcasters and property developers, shocked the FA Cup by beating Premier League side Crystal Palace – the first non‑league club to beat the cup holders since 1909. The win comes after the club was rescued from liquidation and is a huge moment for fans, players and owner Rob Smethurst. It also follows the death of 21‑year‑old Ethan McLeod, so the town celebrated with pride and hope.
