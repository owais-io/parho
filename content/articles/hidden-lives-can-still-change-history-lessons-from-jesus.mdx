About 57% of Gen Z in the US want to be influencers, chasing fame and money. But the article argues influence doesn’t have to be flashy. Even Jesus, born in a quiet manger, changed history. His humble story reminds us that small, hidden acts can create lasting good, and that Christian ideas of equal human dignity helped shape modern rights. The piece urges us to value quiet, everyday contributions and to see the overlooked people as central to change.
