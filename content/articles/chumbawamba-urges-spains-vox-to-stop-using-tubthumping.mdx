British band Chumbawamba has asked far‑right Spanish party Vox to remove their hit “Tubthumping” from a Facebook post that condemned migrants. Vox’s leader, Santiago Abascal, used the song to support an anti‑migration message during a town visit before regional elections. The band says the song celebrates resilience, not hate, and has already threatened legal action for similar misuse by politicians in the past.
