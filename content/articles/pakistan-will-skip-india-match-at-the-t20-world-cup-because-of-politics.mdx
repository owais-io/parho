The Pakistani government has told the national team not to play India on 15 February 2026 at the T20 World Cup. The decision follows years of tension between the two countries over security and politics. Cricket fans worry that dropping the match hurts ticket sales, TV deals and the sport’s money. The International Cricket Council says the change will hurt fans worldwide, especially in Pakistan. The move shows how politics can overtake sport.
