Elon Musk has merged his space company SpaceX with his AI firm xAI in a $1.25 trillion deal. The move combines rockets, satellite internet and AI tools like the Grok chatbot, aiming to build space‑based data centers to power future AI. Musk plans to take the combined company public in the summer. The deal follows criticism of xAI’s content and raises questions about the future of AI in space.
