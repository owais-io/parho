Ferrari has decided to replace Lewis Hamilton’s race engineer, Riccardo Adami, before the 2026 Formula One season. Adami will move to a role in the driver academy. The team says the new engineer will be announced later. Hamilton’s first year with Ferrari was tough, with the car slower than rivals and many disagreements with Adami. He called the season a “nightmare” and wants changes. Ferrari hopes the change will improve results.
