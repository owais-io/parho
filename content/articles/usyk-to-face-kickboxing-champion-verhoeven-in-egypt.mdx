Usyk will defend his WBC heavyweight title by fighting kickboxing champion Rico Verhoeven on May 23 at the Pyramids of Giza in Egypt. The bout, called “Glory in Giza,” is the first boxing title fight in Egypt and will stream live on Dazn. Verhoeven is a 36‑year‑old Dutch fighter with a long kickboxing career who has sparred with top boxers. Both fighters say the match will be a special clash of champions.
