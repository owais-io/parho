Newcastle, newly promoted to the Premiership, lost a close match against Bristol. Despite a fast start and two tries by Oli Spencer, the Reds could not earn a bonus point. Bristol scored six tries, with Louis Rees‑Zammit and Matías Moroni making a big impact. The game ended with Bristol winning, but both teams missed the extra points that could have changed the standings. Newcastle's lock Joe Batley was sent off, and defender Ravouvou was sin‑binned.
