The U.S. and Israel are pushing hard against Iran, but their attacks may spiral into a bigger, costlier fight. Even after killing Iran’s top leader, Iran still holds nuclear weapons and can hit the Gulf and shipping lanes, raising global oil prices. U.S. officials worry the war could spread to Lebanon or even involve other powers, turning a tactical win into a long, messy war.
