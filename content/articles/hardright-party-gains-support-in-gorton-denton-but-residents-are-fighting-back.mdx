Hard‑right candidates are winning votes in Gorton & Denton, a diverse Manchester area. Reform and other nationalist parties push anti‑immigration and racist rhetoric. Many residents, led by a grassroots group called Local Voices, are organising community meetings, training and hustings to counter division. The group stresses the need to protect solidarity and prevent the erosion of progress made against racism. Their hope is that unity will win over the divisive right.
