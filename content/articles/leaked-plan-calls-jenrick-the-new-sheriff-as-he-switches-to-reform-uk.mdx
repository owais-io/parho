A leaked media plan for former Conservative minister Robert Jenrick’s move to Reform UK describes him as the party’s “new sheriff” and claims he will bring experience and political heft. The memo, produced by his aides and later shared with the press, also calls him the most dynamic Conservative. Jenrick announced his defection after being sacked, and Reform UK says his arrival could boost their chances in the next election. The move has sparked mixed reactions among party members.
