Chemical giant Ineos, owned by billionaire Jim Ratcliffe, is looking to sell pieces of its vinyls business to raise hundreds of millions of pounds and shrink its £18 billion debt pile. The move comes as the company faces higher carbon costs, weak trade protections, and a tough global market. Ineos is also talking to lenders about refinancing its upcoming bond payments.
