Newcastle won 2‑0 at PSV, with Harvey Barnes and Anthony Gordon scoring. The win puts them in the Champions League playoff, but captain Bruno Guimarães has a bad ankle injury that may keep him out of the next game against PSG. Newcastle’s quick, high‑pressing play rattled PSV, who struggled to build from the back. Manager Eddie Howe praised the team’s performance.
