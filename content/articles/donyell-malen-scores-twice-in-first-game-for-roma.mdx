Roma’s new striker, Donyell Malen, arrived from Aston Villa and made an instant impact by scoring twice in his first 30 minutes against Torino. He worked closely with Paulo Dybala, who set up both goals, and Roma won 2‑0. The win highlights Roma’s need for a goal‑scoring centre‑forward as they chase Champions League qualification. Malen’s deal is a €25 million loan that becomes permanent if he meets certain playing and club‑performance targets.
