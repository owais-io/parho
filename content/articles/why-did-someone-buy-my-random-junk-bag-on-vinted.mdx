The author posted a bag of cheap jewelry and small trinkets for £2 on Vinted. It sold in minutes, surprising them. They share their family’s split opinions: the son thinks older people overvalue stuff, while the daughter is happy to sell through the author. They describe the bag’s contents in detail and wonder what made the buyer value it so highly. The sale was canceled later, leaving them back to start over.
