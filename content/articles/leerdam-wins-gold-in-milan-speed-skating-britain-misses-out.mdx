Jutta Leerdam won gold in the 1,000m speed‑skating event at the 2026 Winter Olympics in Milan, beating world‑champion Femke Kok and breaking the Olympic record. British athletes were disappointed, with only a silver in men’s freeski slopestyle and a fourth‑place finish in women’s big‑air. Other highlights included a triple‑cork win in snowboard slopestyle, mixed‑team curling where Britain missed bronze, and an Italian win in alpine combined. The medal table kept shifting as nations fought for top spots.
