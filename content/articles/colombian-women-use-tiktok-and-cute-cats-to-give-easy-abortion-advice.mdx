Jacarandas, a Colombian abortion helpline run by an all‑female team, uses TikTok, Instagram, and even a cartoon cat to give young people clear info about safe, legal abortion. They answer every WhatsApp question with facts and support, aiming to make abortion normal and less scary. The service, which grew after abortion was legalized in 2022, now has 400,000 TikTok followers and helps hundreds of people each month.
