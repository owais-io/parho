At Newcastle’s biggest coal port, climate protest Rising Tide has stopped three ships from docking. Over the weekend, police arrested 141 people, most charged under crime and marine safety laws. Greenpeace activists even chained themselves to a ship and were taken into custody, but no charges yet. Protesters demanded the government cancel new coal and gas projects and end Newcastle coal exports by 2030. The blockade caused a brief pause in shipping, then resumed and drew international attention.
