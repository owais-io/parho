Labour’s chief strategist, Morgan McSweeney, who helped the party win a huge 2024 landslide, has quit after a scandal linking him to former UK minister Peter Mandelson and Jeffrey Epstein. McSweeney also faces criticism for creating a toxic culture in Downing Street and for alienating left‑wing supporters. His resignation could hurt Prime Minister Keir Starmer, who relied on McSweeney’s tactics to win the election.
