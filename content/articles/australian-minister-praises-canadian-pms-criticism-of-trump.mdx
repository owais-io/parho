Australia’s treasurer Jim Chalmers said Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau’s strong criticism of former US President Donald Trump was “stunning” and is a hot topic in Canberra. Chalmers praised a speech by former Bank of Canada chief Mark Carney at the World Economic Forum, noting how the old rules of international order are breaking down. He urged Australia to cooperate and stand up to bullying, echoing former PM Malcolm Turnbull’s call for the new leader to do the same.
