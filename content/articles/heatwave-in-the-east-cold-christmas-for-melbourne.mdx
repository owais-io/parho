A big heatwave keeps the eastern coast of Australia hot into Christmas, with temperatures hitting 40°C in parts of Sydney and up to 41°C in Perth. While the east remains scorching, storms and rain are expected in the northern and western states. Melbourne will be unusually chilly for Christmas, with highs around 17°C – the coldest since 2006. The rest of the country will see a mix of hot, mild and wet weather.
