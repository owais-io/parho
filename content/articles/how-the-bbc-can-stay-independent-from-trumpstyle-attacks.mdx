The letter warns the BBC is under a new kind of attack called ‘entrumpification’ – when a powerful figure turns mistakes into a fight against trusted institutions. A small error was quickly fixed, but critics used it to launch costly lawsuits and call the BBC ‘fake news’. The writer says the BBC should admit the slip and keep its evidence‑based work alive, standing firm on independence. People are also urged to donate or join together to protect the BBC.
