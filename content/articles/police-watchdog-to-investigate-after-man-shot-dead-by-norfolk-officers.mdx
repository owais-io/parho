An investigation has started by the police watchdog after a man in his 60s was shot dead by Norfolk officers. It happened after two cars crashed on the A11 and the man, thought to be a driver, left holding a gun. Officers fired twice and the victim died. Police and the watchdog will review call logs, video footage, and the weapon. Families have been told and the incident is being examined carefully.
