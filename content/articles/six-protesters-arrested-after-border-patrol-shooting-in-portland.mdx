In Portland, two people were shot by US border patrol agents outside a hospital. Protesters gathered outside the ICE building, calling the agents “thugs” and “Nazis.” Police arrested six protesters as the crowds grew. City leaders urged calm and said investigations were underway. The mayor and state officials demanded ICE stop operations in Portland until a full investigation is done. The shooting adds to outrage over federal immigration enforcement.
