At 5 pm, a Chanukah celebration started at Bondi’s Archer Park. Around 6:40 pm, a father‑son duo, Naveed and Sajid Akram, fired rifles from a footbridge over a nearby car park, shooting at the gathering. Police and civilians rushed in; a by‑stander disarmed one shooter. The two men fired until police opened fire and stopped them. Over 100 ambulances treated 42 injured. Police later raided the Akrams’ home, found a bomb, and called the incident a terrorist attack.
