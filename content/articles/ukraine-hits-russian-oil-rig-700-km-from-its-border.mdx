Ukraine’s drones struck a Russian oil platform and a patrol ship 700 km away in the Caspian Sea, part of Kyiv’s push to hit Russian targets far from its own borders. Ukrainian forces also damaged a radar in Crimea, destroyed two fighter jets there, and hit a bridge in Odesa that supplies fuel. Russia fired many airstrikes and drones on Ukrainian towns, killing civilians, while the US is trying to arrange talks between the three sides.
