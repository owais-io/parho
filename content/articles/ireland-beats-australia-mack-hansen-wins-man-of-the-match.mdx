In a one‑sided game, Ireland beat Australia in the Autumn Nations Series. Mack Hansen scored a hat‑trick and was named Man of the Match. Ireland’s forwards and backs dominated the line‑outs and kicks, while Australia struggled with set‑pieces and defense. The win shows Ireland’s strength and gives them confidence for the rest of the series, while Australia will look to improve before their next fixtures.
