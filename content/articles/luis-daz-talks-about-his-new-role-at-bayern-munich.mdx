Luis Díaz has settled in at Bayern Munich after leaving Liverpool, quickly making 18 goal contributions. He says the club’s trust and welcoming atmosphere helped him adjust, and he loves the freedom to create “chaotic” moments on the pitch. Díaz is eager for Bayern to win the Champions League, enjoys life in Munich with his family, and looks forward to the World Cup, where Colombia will face Portugal and has strong fan support.
