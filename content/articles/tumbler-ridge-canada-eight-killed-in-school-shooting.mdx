On Feb 10, a gunman opened fire at Tumbler Ridge Secondary School in northern Canada, killing eight people, most of them teenagers, and killing two adults. The town of 2,400 was shocked, and residents are still coping with grief. The shooter, identified as Jesse Van Rootselaar, had a history of mental‑health issues and a now‑expired gun permit. The tragedy has drawn national attention and community support, with politicians visiting to mourn.
