Trump pulled the US out of the Paris climate agreement for the second time. Now the U.S. is the only country that has left the pact, joining a small group that isn’t part of it. The move may slow global progress, but renewable energy is still growing worldwide. China is leading the shift to clean power. Experts warn the U.S. exit could make it harder for poorer countries to get climate help.
