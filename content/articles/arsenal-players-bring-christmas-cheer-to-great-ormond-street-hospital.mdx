Arsenal players, including Bukayo Saka and the three Gabriels, came to Great Ormond Street’s Koala ward just before Christmas. They wore bright red jerseys, carried gift bags and played games with children and their families. The visits give the kids joy, a chance to move around, feel normal and boost recovery. The club has long helped the hospital and the players say the experience inspires them to keep fighting. Their visits spread holiday cheer.
