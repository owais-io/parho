The MV Dali, a Singapore‑flagged cargo ship, lost power and slammed into Baltimore’s Francis Scott Key Bridge in March 2024, killing six workers. The ship’s senior crew—captain, engineer, electrician and second officer—are still held in Baltimore for investigation, even though no criminal charges have been filed. The NTSB report blamed a faulty electrical wire and wider safety gaps. The crew’s employer is paying for them, and lawsuits and a settlement are ongoing.
