Trump told federal agencies to immediately stop using AI from Anthropic after a disagreement over safety rules. The Pentagon wanted looser restrictions so it could use the tech, but Anthropic said it wouldn't allow mass surveillance or autonomous weapons. OpenAI signed a new deal with the military, keeping the same safety limits. Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth called Anthropic a national‑security risk, cutting contracts.
