The FDA has pulled more than 3,500 gallons of salad dressing from stores after black plastic pieces were found in the onion mix. The recall covers large, deli‑sized bottles of Hidden Valley Ranch and other brands made by Ventura Foods, a California supplier. The products went to many U.S. retailers, including Costco, and one shipment reached Costa Rica. If you bought one of these sizes, throw it away and ask for a refund. Regular small jars are not affected.
