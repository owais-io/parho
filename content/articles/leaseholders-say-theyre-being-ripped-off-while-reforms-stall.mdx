Leaseholders across England are fighting sky‑high bills from their building managers, with one lady in Birmingham being asked for £14,000 after paying £800 a month. The government promised to overhaul lease‑hold rules but the new law is still missing key details, leaving residents stuck with huge service charges and ignored repairs. Many say the reforms are slow and the companies are “milking” them.
